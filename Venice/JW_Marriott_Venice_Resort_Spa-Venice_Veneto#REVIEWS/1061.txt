Booked this on a Room and Breakfast deal with Fleetway Travel via Travelzoo, upgrading from 2 to 3 nights and tweaking flight times provided by Easyjet. Still not cheap, but what an experience.  My wife decided our trip from Venice Airport would be by private water taxi which at 130 Euros was pricey but set the tone for our stay. The Hotel is located on an island off Venice waterfront, quite a way out, but takes only tops twenty minutes on the 1/2 hourly water taxi direct from the hotel to S. Marco Giardinetti where the hotel has a shared mooring with another.
