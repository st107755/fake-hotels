We arrived on the private island via the free 20-minute shuttle boat from Saint Marks square. I believe the hotel only opened earlier this year - bur apart from the odd bit of landscaping everything is complete and looks amazing. The staff are extremely helpful and welcoming. Although we had booked a luxury room, we had the fortune to be upgraded to one of the loft maisonettes with a private pool [maybe they recognised us as internationally famous Trip Advisors?]. Everything is very well spaced out and considering that it was quite full - extremely quiet and peaceful, which is very nice after fighting the crowds in the city. You would want to make sure breakfast is included since the charges are a bit exorbitant.
