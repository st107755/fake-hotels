My wife and I recently stayed at the Marriott, Isola Delle Rose, for 4 nights as a birthday treat for her. Our private transport (speedboat) from Marco Polo airport burbled, James Bond style, into the Marriott's berth and they took over. Bags were spirited away and delivered to our room, check-in was an effortless delight and we were unpacked and ready to explore within an hour of arriving.  Our room (238) was quite spacious and reasonably well equipped - though the bathroom was a little on the small side. (We've holidayed numerous times in Abu Dhabi and their 5-star bathrooms ARE 5-star.) The bed was extremely comfortable - one of the best hotel beds we've ever had.
