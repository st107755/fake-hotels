This is a fabulous hotel and a real treat...the standards are top class and the service is simply excellent. Getting to and from the hotel is easy with the hotels own launch, which takes you to San Marco in 25 minutes. We stayed in one of the Loft rooms in the La Maisonette building. Not a cheap option but we thought was best for our family of 4 with two children. The room was amazing but I would say be prepared to sleep on a sofa bed. It had a double bed on an upstairs mezzanine floor but we had to give that to the kids as they would be first to bed and so we needed the lounge area...not sure I would therefore choose this option again in hindsight as was uncomfortable for 7 nights.
