A wonderful experience at the JW Marriott Venice Resort.  From the moment I checked in, the service was top notch.  It was nice that I am a platinum member, but I get the impression that everyone was treated extremely well. Enough staff that you are never left wanting or waiting for anything. World class food, stylish hotel.  The free shuttle to St. Mark's is handy.  Only thing I would suggest, is for the price, the hotel should provide an airport shuttle.  The rooms were clean and modern, very comfortable. The grounds were well cared for.  Reservations for the restaurants strongly recommended, but the food and service in the bar was exceptional.  A few places the carpet looked like it was poorly installed, but that was the only fault I saw on the whole place.
