I spent 4 nights in metro-Venice and all the tourist chaos that is Venice. Then I spent 4-nights at the JW-Marriott & Spa Venice. Wow, tranquility. I originally had booked two nights with points plus $$$ then added an additional two nights to my stay. It is not a cheap stay, but to escape Venice but still view San Marco from the private island and the 4th floor roof top bar and pool is just spectacular. And, do not miss dinner at Dopolavoro - the best meal I have had in 52 years - seriously, this is a wonderful dining experience. And, the spa was good for two days of treatments after 5 days of walking around Venice. Hotel staff's attention to every detail is totally world class. It is a seasonal hotel Open April-thru-October.
