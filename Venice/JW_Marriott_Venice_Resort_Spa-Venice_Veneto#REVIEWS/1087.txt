If you want to be right in the middle of the craziness of Venice at this time of year, this is not the place for you. How else would you arrive into Venice? We got a private taxi from the cruise ship ($160 Euros for 6 people and luggage booked a day ahead by email). It took 20 minutes although they told us over an hour to arrive. Not sure what that was about but you can see the island from the ship. We were greeted like we were the only ones there. Staff could not do enough for you. We stayed in the main building but the rest of our family stayed in a villa.
