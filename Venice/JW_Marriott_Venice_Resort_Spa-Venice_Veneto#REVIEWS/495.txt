Booked the J W Marriott hotel as a last minute get-away to celebrate our 25th wedding anniversary and couldn't believe how much of a find this hotel was. We arrived by private water transfer into the hotel entrance and was greeted with a warm welcome and congratulations on our anniversary. We were taken to the private check-in desk and told that our room wasn't available until later in the afternoon but they would ring once the room was available. We didn't mind as it was still lunch time so after a quick drink in the rooftop bar we took the complimentary water taxi into Venice. The water taxi was around 20min journey but took you straight into St Marks Square which was ideal for getting around to see the sites.
