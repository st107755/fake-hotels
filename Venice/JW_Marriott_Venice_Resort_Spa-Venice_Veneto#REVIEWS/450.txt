I love this hotel and it is probably the biggest reason I love Venice now as much as I do.  When we booked the hotel, we arranged a private water taxi from the airport to the hotel. This option costs more (140E), but is the only way to go. You are taken through the city and then to Isola Delle Rose, the hotel's own private island!  The water taxi entrance is like a scene out of James Bond (have your cameras ready!).  From there we are met by the porter who takes your bags from you to have them delivered to your room.  There are two main room types at the hotel, the rooms in the main building and the private villa rooms. Each have there own reception. We were taken by golf cart to the private villa reception. Check-in was speedy and efficient from Oscar and Andrea.
