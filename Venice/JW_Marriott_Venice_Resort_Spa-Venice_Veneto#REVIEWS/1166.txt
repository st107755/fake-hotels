Like a stay in paradise  The location of this hotel is painstakingly beautiful- situated on the private island in the lagoon, with beautiful gardens and lots of white roses (thus the name  Isola delle Rose). You arrive in a true Bond –sh style- with a boat (Airport water taxi costs around 130 € and can be pre-arranged with the hotel). The hotel  lobby is beautifully decorated- very modern, lots of white and occasional bold usage of strong colour (I read somewhere that it was done by Mattheo Thun ??) You are greeted warmly- the staff is, in general, very nice, welcoming and helpful. The rooms all bear typical Marriott signature- very nice and spacious. The only problem for us was that in the bathroom there was not enough space on the counter to leave your toiletries.
