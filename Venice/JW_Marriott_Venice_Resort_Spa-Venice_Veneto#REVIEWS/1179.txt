Amazing hotel where nothing is too much trouble. Set in beautiful grounds on its own island the roof top has 360 degree views, probably the best views of Venice itself. There is a large sunbathing area for relaxation in peace and quiet and  a great restaurant. We ate here the first night......wow it was excellent.   Special mention to Viola who looked after us on arrival, we paid for an upgrade to a room with a patio which gave us our own area to sit in privacy, soak up the sun, sip Prosecco surrounded by sweet smelling white roses looking over the island's olive grove. Access to Venice is via the hotel's complimentary shuttle service, this is prompt every half hour and comfortable and we met some lovely people.
