I travelled in August 2016 and stayed for 5 nights with my husband and 2 children aged 12 and 9. We booked the previous December through Expedia and paid a bargain £1,870 for 5 night’s bed and breakfast including scheduled BA flights from Gatwick to Marco Polo Airport.  This is without doubt the best hotel I have ever stayed in. Located on its own island a short boat trip from St. Mark’s Square it allows you to combine total relaxation with the hustle and bustle of Venice, my favourite city in the world.   All of the staff were extremely attentive providing outstanding service throughout our stay.   We pre-booked a private water taxi from the airport to the hotel and I would thoroughly recommend this to anyone.
