As soon as we pulled up to the hotel I was greeted by the doorman who took my luggage to the front desk. Check in was swift, Julia was charming, accommodating and professional. The receptionists and concierges are welcoming and highly professional. The room is excellent as well as the always punctual boat transfers to the city. The hotel has to improve the room’s delivery going over the different features and two of the restaurants, the one at the rooftop and the one by the bar. Service is very amateur, the waiters are not well trained and disengaged. I won’t go in to more details. Breakfast’s service on the contrary is very good.
