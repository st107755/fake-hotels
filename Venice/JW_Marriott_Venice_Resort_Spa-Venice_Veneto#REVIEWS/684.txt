Like most of the other reviews, my wife and I loved our stay at JW. The staff was exceptional, well trained and engaging with guests.  Particularly the main hotels breakfast and restaurant staff. They all seemed very happy. We enjoyed 3 days in one of villas, La residenizia(spelling). It was very private and perfect if you have a dog. The main hotel was more lively than the villas area, villas were 2 minute walk to main hotel.   The pool in the main hotel offered great views and was also luxurious. Very good pool side service and food. The pool is small in the main hotel and seats were limited so arrive early. The family pool was very large with many seats and a snack bar as well.
