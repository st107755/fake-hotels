We enjoyed our stay at JW Venice. Spent two nights but wish we stayed longer. This is a unique property as you can see from other reviews. It’s a piece of quiet paradise a short distance away from the center of Venice. Service was solid. As a Lifetime Titanium member I was able to take advantage of an upgrade and my family was pleasantly surprised with a two story suite and a private outdoor hot tub. Thank you Marriott! Had dinner at the rooftop restaurant (Sagra) as we arrived late. Definitely not a place for the budget minded, but I believe the unique location justifies it. Andrea took great care of us. The rooftop Pool seems to have limited seating so get there early to get a good spot.
