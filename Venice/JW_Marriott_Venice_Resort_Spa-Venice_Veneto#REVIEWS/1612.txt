My fiance and I recently stayed at the Marriot for 4 nights and can honestly say that we had an absolutely fantastic time. The hotel is stunningly beautiful with incredible views across the lagoon. The staff are exceptionally friendly and always willing to assist. Our room was clean and comfortable. The restaurants in the hotel are all fabulous and this is certainly one of the key selling points of the hotel. The rooftop pool is a beautiful feature and enables you to enjoy great views across Venice. My only quibble (if I must have one) is that there is no free wifi in the rooms. Given that this is a 5* resort, this should really be included in the price of the room.
