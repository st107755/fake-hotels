I debated between staying in Venice and Isola Delle Rose.  I was afraid if I did not stay right in the heart of Venice, I would be "missing something".  I decided to stay at the J. W. Check in was efficient and welcoming as we were escorted from the boat dock to the check in area.  We were seated in comfortable upholstered chairs.  Although our room was not ready (we were an early arrival from a cruise ship) we were offered a tour of the property by Luca.
