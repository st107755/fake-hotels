During end of Juli 2017 my wife and I stayed for 4 days in the JW Marriott resort. Upon arrival at the Marco Polo airport we were awaited immediately at the gate by a transportation service. In a comfortable SUV we were driven (20 mins) to an exclusive boat close to the cruise ship terminal. From there we arrived the Isola delle Rose in a 20 mins speedy but secure boat ride. At the elegant hotel’s boat pier we were very friendly welcomed and checked in. Our room no. 203 had a view to the lagoon, was cosy, perfectly maintained and equipped. Breakfast was included, served inside and outside and offered a large variety of tasty cold and warm delis. The Michelin-starred “Dopolavoro” is recommendable. This restaurant offers - amongst others - a 10 course tasting menu.
