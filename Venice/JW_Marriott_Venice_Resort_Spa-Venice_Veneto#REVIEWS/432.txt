This property is beautiful. An island, far away from hustle and bustle of central Venice, offering an oasis of peace & relaxation. Rooms, restaurants, bars and swimming pools are dotted around, surrounded by olive groves and views of the lagoon. Arrival by private water taxi is pretty special as you motor into a James Bond style entrance beneath the main property.  We booked a La Residenza De Luxe room, with its own private garden, which was a great place to relax. Check in was far from smooth though. Having booked through Amex we were entitled to a complimentary upgrade, when available. On checking-in we were told that they hadn't looked for one for us and would get on it straight away.
