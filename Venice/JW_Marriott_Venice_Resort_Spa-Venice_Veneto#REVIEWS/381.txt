This hotel and staff exceeded our expectations! So friendly and catered to our every request, I've never been treated better by hotel staff. As it was our first time to Venice, I was worried about having to take the shuttle to/from San Marco, but found it very convenient and comfortable. They even offered us a private boat to Murano with a personal tour of a glass factory for only €20\person, completely worth it. Afterwards they dropped us off at San Marco for some more sightseeing.  They also have a shuttle to the train station which is very nice and convenient. The spa is also very beautiful and peaceful. The only thing to note is that the food is very expensive and it does feel a little like a hospital in the halls, but everything else outweighs this by far.
