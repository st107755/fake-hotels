Could not have asked for a nicer surprise than Marriot’s island spa. A shuttle leaves San Marco square every half hour to your own oasis away from the crowds. The staff was exceptional and the rooftop pool overlooking venice can’t be beat. Taking a private taxi in for your arrival will make you feel like James Bond through a private waterway into the hotel lobby. The children’s pool is another oasis away from the main hotel.
