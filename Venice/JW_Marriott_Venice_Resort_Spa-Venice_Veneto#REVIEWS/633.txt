The location excellent, staff extremely well trained and service a delight, and the ferry to St Marks square every half hour a pleasure and makes trip exciting with boat ride. The hotel has many variable room and suite options, with a church, gym, spa, bikes to ride around island, deli, cooking school, local tours, stunning pool bar on roof and much much more. Having stayed at many of the top 5 star hotel groups this was my first Marriott experience and it was excellent. The reception and the training of hotel staff from hotel schools I think fantastic and a credit to Marriott group, and the breakfasts were best I experienced in Italy over 21 days.
