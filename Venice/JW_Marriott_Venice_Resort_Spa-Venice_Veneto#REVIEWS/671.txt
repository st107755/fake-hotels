Having been to Venice several times before we were looking for a quiet retreat that was close to the centre of town.  We found this at the JW Marriott on an island, Isola Delle Rosa, in the lagoon. We arrived by water taxi into the spectacular entrance where the concierge immediately recognised that we had booked a room in the Residence, a small unit of rooms located in the grounds of the hotel.  He showed us to the special villa reception where we were warmly welcomed by the villa reception staff.  Checking in was very easy.  Our room was exceptional, beautifully furnished with all the amenities we expect from a 5 star hotel. The room also had a small, well maintained, private garden with decking and sun loungers. chairs and a table - a perfectly quiet retreat.
