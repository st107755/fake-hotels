We stayed here at the end of June / start of July 2015 and I kept meaning to write a review ... better late than never I suppose!  This hotel is a real gem. When visiting the hot, sticky and busy Venice it's great to have an Oasis like the JW Marriott where you can retreat from the madness of St Mark's Square and the claustrophobia of Venice's narrow back streets. Sip a cooling drink by the rooftop pool in the main building; doze in the shade of the cabanas by the garden pool; or if you want to treat yourself, get a room with your own small pool in La Residenza (well worth it, with your own private garden overlooking the lagoon).  The Spa is exactly as promised in the promotional material, and more. Superb.
