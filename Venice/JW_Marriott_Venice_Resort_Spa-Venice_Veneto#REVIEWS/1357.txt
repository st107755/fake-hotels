This resort was absolutely fabulous from the moment you step off their shuttle onto the dock. The check in experience was fabulous and very welcoming. You can sit in a comfy chair while they check you in! The room was absolutely stunning with a balcony view of Venice in the distance. The staff were all very friendly. The shuttle boat was convenient, but could stand to be more frequent during the afternoon. The rooftop pool was smaller and shallower than expected, but had an incredible view. One thing I would mention is that the pool floor and steps were very slippery and felt rather dangerous- it would be smart to add some texture to prevent slips and falls. The breakfast was fantastic.
