My husband and I stayed here for a week. Shuttle to Venice was easy, food was amazing and the spa and heated pool were incredible. We even did the Chef’s Table which was €120 per person and completely worth it. The only negative thing was housekeeping. On the third day there, we realized our used coffee cups and wine glasses were not being replaced with clean dishes but merely being rinsed and wiped out. This was evident by the old espresso and lipstick smears on our cups! We were so grossed out, we didn’t get room service for the remainder of our trip.
