This was my first time to Venice and chose JW Marriott to escape the hustle and bustle.  On first impressions pulling up to the hotel was very impressive. When we arrived the room wasnt ready, so got the free water shuttle into Venice. The Shuttle service runs mostly every 15 minutes and takes about 20 minutes to get you there.  The hotel room was lovely, great size, nicely decorated and good air con. Breakfast was included in our rate and was delcicious with plenty of selection. The only negative i have is that on arrival in the breakfast room, there were always ques to get a table and it was a bit chaotic.
