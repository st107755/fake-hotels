One year later, back to the JW Marriott on the Island of Roses (rechristened from the original “Sacca Sessola”), which is a private island about 20 minutes from St. Mark; free shuttle boats go back and forth every 30 minutes. Being on your own island is part of the pleasure, but you must factor in the additional time when you want to go to Venice.  Some things have definitely improved, some have not. Last year the resort had just opened and about one half of the island was still closed, now everything is up and working, including a couple of outbuildings with quiet rooms, some with private patios, and starred restaurant Dopolavoro.
