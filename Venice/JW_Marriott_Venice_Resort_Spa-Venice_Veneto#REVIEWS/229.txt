I thought the rooms were lovely with outdoor courtyards where we had our morning coffee. I felt the hotel was a bit snooty and sterile and you have to travel by boat from San Marco (which has plus and minuses). I would say that I was disappointed that we could not get a reservation at their Michelin Star restaurant. I think that people staying on property should be moved up to the top of the list.  Their other restaurant was, however, inviting, the bartenders were friendly and the food was fantastic. We had a charcuterie plate that could have fed an army. I purchased some lovely (expensive) olive oil that they make right on property so that was a great souvenir. I also bought some apple butter that I haven't yet tried but I'm sure will be delicious.
