We stayed at this island resort about 15 minutes by boat from St. Mark’s Square twice, the visits book ending a week-long Mediterranean cruise.  The first time was a late-night arrival after a delayed flight from London, the second at 9am some six hours ahead of check-in time.   Both times we were struck by the sheer warmth and slickness of the check-in process carried out in a spacious and airy lobby by a group of smiling and clearly-well-trained young women sitting behind tables with guests on comfy armchairs in front of them. We were particularly impressed by Titiana, who welcomed us the second time.
