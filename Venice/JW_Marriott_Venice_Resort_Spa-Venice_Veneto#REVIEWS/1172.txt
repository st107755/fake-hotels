According to the day barman, "Mateo" (great Bloody Marys & Venice information), Marriott International purchased this island location that was originally used as a sanitarium for respiratory illnesses during the 1950s. Gravel paths wind through beautiful, serene gardens, leading to among other things an isolated lap pool, and lovely chapel. From the hotel's dock, it's about a 20 minute boat-ride to St. Mark's square, the shuttle is free, and pick-ups/drop-offs are every 30 minutes. As a former Marriott manager, I was extremely impressed by the quality of the food & beverage; excellent service; beauty and tranquility of this property.
