My family spent a wonderful 3 days at the JW Marriott Venice Resort and Spa. The hotel is truly an oasis tucked away on a small island about a 10 minute boat ride from St. Marks Square.   We arrived at the hotel by private motor taxi from Marco Polo. On arrival, our welcome was extremely friendly, and we were escorted to the check in area.   Check in was quick, and were went to our 2 rooms. The rooms were fabulous and were modern, spacious, and spotless. The beds were comfortable with high quality linens. The bathrooms were also beautiful with all the amenities you could require.   The hotel grounds are beautiful and offer a peaceful atmosphere to get away from the hustle and bustle of Venice.   They have a beautiful rooftop infinity pool that overlooks Venice.
