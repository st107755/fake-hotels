We spent a long weekend here having travelled from the UK.  When you reach the hotel - it is an oasis.  The hotel is lovely and the reception staff were very welcoming and efficient.  We received a free upgrade to a suite which was lovely - massive bed, sofa, chairs, large bathroom and tea/coffee facilities.  We have a Marriott rewards card so receive the basic wifi free of charge.  The weather was great.  We had breakfast included as part of our package and it was brilliant - everything you could imagine and nothing was too much bother for the staff.  There were plenty of staff available.  The boat shuttle from St Marks Square is good - just get to the boat docks in good time as the maximum number allowed on the boat is 40 and it can get really busy at peak times.
