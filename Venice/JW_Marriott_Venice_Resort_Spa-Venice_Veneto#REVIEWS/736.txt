The JW Marriott is a sleek and stylish property located in the Venetian lagoon and is a hidden treasure waiting to be discovered.  This property is located a short 20 minute water shuttle ride from the foot of San Marco but feels removed from the throng of tourists flooding St Mark's Square.  The hotel is modern with beautiful amenities and the staff is efficient and ready to assist with any questions. The rooms are well appointed and very comfortable...a welcome relief from some other properties in Venice.  There are many fine dining options on the property (named Rose Island by the developers) as well as a beautiful spa and conference center.
