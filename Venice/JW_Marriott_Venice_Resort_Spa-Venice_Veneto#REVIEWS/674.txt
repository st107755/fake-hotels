We stayed at the JW Marriott for 6 nights this past week.  It was truly an amazing experience.  After a very long flight, it was great to arrive to a truly James Bond-ish experience. The grounds and all the hotel facilities are in immaculate condition.  But truly where the hotel shines is with the staff.    Since this was our first trip to Italy and Venice, we really wanted to immerse ourselves in the Venetian lifestyle.  We met with Jacopa, one of the concierges at the hotel.  He was incredibly helpful, friendly, patient, just top notch!
