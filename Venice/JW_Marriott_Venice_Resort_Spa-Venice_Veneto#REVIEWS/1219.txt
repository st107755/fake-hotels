This hotel is gorgeous! After a busy day in crowded Venice it was always nice to return to our own little peaceful island to relax! They offer a free shuttle that runs every half hour and is quite convenient. My only complaint was that the first one didn't leave until 9am, which was rather late. The grounds of the hotel were magnificent as well. The weather was cool when we stayed but we still enjoyed walking around the island and relaxing in the heated rooftop infinity pool and looking out at Venice. One night we ate dinner at the restaurant located in the main hotel (there is a separate restaurant on the island as well) and everything we tried was presented beautifully and tasted delicious.
