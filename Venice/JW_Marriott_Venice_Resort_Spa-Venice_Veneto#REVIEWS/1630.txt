The hotel is fabulous. Located on a private island with a complimentary shuttle water service for San Marco Piazza (from 9am till midnight) and Piazzale Roma (3 times a day). On the shuttle they provide complimentary water (still and sparkling) and apples. The hotel is new and modern, some territories were still under development (spa, dining room, family swimming pool and grill). The hotel provides a complimentary boat to Murano island if you like to visit one of the Murano glass factories (actually only one which have a negotiation with the hotel). The breakfast was very good with variety of hot and cold meals. During our stay we visited a gym and heated swimming pool on the roof. The water was very warm and comfort for swimming.
