We stayed here for our "luna di miele" and couldn't have asked for a better place.  The property was beautifully designed, landscaped and well-maintained.  The buildings were exquisite and the setting felt like being on an Italian country estate.  A lap around the island is about a mile so it's not very small.  Our upgraded room was amazing with all the amenities included.  The spa was tranquil and luxurious.  The staff all over was incredibly friendly and everyone spoke English, though we tried a little Italian.  The food was excellent and thoroughly enjoyed their exclusive Prosecco.  The fitness "room" (was actually it's own building) was incredible with fitness gear uncommonly found in other hotel gyms.  There was also a steam room and sauna in the fitness room.
