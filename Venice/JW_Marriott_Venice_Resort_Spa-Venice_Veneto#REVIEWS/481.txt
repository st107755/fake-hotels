Just returned from a magnificent holiday at the JW Marriott on Rose Island. The experience from arrival to departure was other-worldly.  Yes, it's an effort to get to the resort- I chose the hotel's private transfer for 210 euro.  It was worth it in that the staff meets you at the train and handles everything.  The beautiful boat takes you on the 20 minute trip to the resort's "entrance."  Staff there greeted me warmly and escorted me to reception where the staff works its magic.  It's all about the staff here.  So kind in a helpful, sincere attitude. I wish I remembered all the names of the staff but be certain Holden, Erica, Simone, and the others will work their magic.  The junior suite was spacious, beautiful, peaceful, and relaxing.
