The JW Marriott in Venice was a nice start to our honeymoon in Italy. We stayed for three nights. The staff were all attentive and helpful, in particular Romeo in the lobby answered all of my questions about touring Murano and Burano while I waited in line to speak to the concierge. After I was finished talking to him I didn't need to wait anymore to speak to the concierge, so that was really nice.   The hotel building and grounds are beautifully kept. Everything is new and clean. The view from the rooftop is amazing.  Be aware that the hotel shuttle over to San Marco does take some time (approx. 30mins), they seem to speed up at night which is nice so you can get back to the hotel faster.
