We have just returned from a 3 night stay in JW Marriott Venice. I booked on a 3rd party website and saved considerable money in doing so. We arrived by private water taxi, this cost €128 from Marco Polo and was totally worth it, we didn't fancy spending up to 2 hours travelling around on public transport. The boat ride and arrival was an experience. When we checked in, I advised it was a special occasion as I had done in a previous email to the hotel. We were asked did we want to upgrade to a junior suite at a cost of €150 per night, which we were told it was normally €400 a night so we were getting a deal. I declined this. I had read previous reviews of guests being upgraded free of charge from a deluxe room to one of the top rooms.
