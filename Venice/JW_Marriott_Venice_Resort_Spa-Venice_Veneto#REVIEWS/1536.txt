This new hotel was a lovely place for our Venice break. It is situated on its own island which is accessed via an efficient shuttle service from San Marco.   Our room was a good size and our A/C worked fine. The bed was extremely comfortable, just what you need after walking around all day. Not much to watch on TV however this is not really a big deal. The WIFI is not complementary which is a glaring mistake! Not sure I have had to pay per day for access anywhere else in a 4 /5 star hotel?   The rooftop pool area is lovely and a nice place to spend a few hours. The drinks were not reasonably priced which is unjustified. Evening sundowners would have been possible except for the heavy prices.
