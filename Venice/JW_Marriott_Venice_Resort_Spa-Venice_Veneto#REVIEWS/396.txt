While on a trip to Italy, we spent 3 days at this luxury resort.  We wish we had spent the entire vacation there.  This resort is located on a private island, Isola delle Rose about 20 minutes from Venice.  The hotel runs a ferry service all day long to Piazza San Marco making sightseeing fun and easy.  We drove a rental car from Bulgheri which we dropped off with Hertz at the Piazzale Roma.  We asked the hotel to arrange a private transfer making for for an easy luggage transfer.  It was a spectacular ride in a water taxi to the James Bond check-in dock at the Resort.  This is the coolest check-in we have ever experienced.  You could also opt to handle your bags yourself and take a water taxi as the dock is just across the street from the Piazzale Roma.
