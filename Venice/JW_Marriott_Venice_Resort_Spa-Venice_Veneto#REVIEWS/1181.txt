This is a beautiful 5 star hotel on it's own Island. The grounds and rooms are what you would expect from a hotel with this rating but what sets it apart from anywhere else I have visited were the staff. They were fantastic. They were professional, helpful, proactive and friendly, but most of all they seemed to be very genuine and were in no way pretentious. I would like to thank the lady who took me on a tour of the island on the golf cart and gave me the full run down on the history of the place and also the porter who carried my take away breakfast down to the boat when I was running late. (sorry I have forgotten your names). Thank you again to all the staff for making my stay memorable. You are what sets this hotel apart from others.
