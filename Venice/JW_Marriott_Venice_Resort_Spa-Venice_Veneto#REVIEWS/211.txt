I stayed here for 3 nights with my husband.  From arrival everyone who had assisted us whether it may be around the hotel or in their restaurants were extremely friendly. We stayed in the Uliveto building and it was very clean and spacious. We were lucky enough to get one of the rooms with a patio as well. However one of the nights our room keys stopped working for some reason but this was quickly resolved at the main reception during after hours where our go to reception was unavailable (10pm).  We went to the spa twice during our stay for two different treatments and I cannot fault it. It’s so clean, and the guys who attended us were fantastic.
