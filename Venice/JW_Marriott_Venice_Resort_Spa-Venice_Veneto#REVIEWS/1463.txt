My fourth trip to Venice and the first for my children aged 13 and 15.  The location, far out in the lagoon is a perfect antidote to the hustle and bustle of Venice in summer and the frequent and fast, free shuttle service between the hotel and San Marco is handy for day trips into the City and beyond.  Rooms are spacious and well appointed and the choice of restaurants is good.  La Cucina serves an excellent buffet breakfast but our firm favourite is the rooftop restaurant albeit a little more expensive.  Service is friendly and plentiful.  Staff are sometimes a little green for a five star hotel but, they make up for this with a cheery demeanour and eagerness to be helpful.  They are great with younger children, very friendly and the family pool is well thought out.
