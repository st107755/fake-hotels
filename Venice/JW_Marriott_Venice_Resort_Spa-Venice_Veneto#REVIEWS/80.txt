Our family booked this property on one of the outer islands as we wanted to give the kids some downtime (pool time) and have always appreciated the service levels at the JW brand of hotels.   We arrived to the hotel via private taxi around 12:30pm after a long flight and although our room was not ready the hotel allowed us to use facilities. We opted to have a leisurely lunch and stroll through the gardens on property.   We booked a Junior Suite and the size and amenities were perfect for our family. We are Bonvoy gold members so we were a bit surprised at our first floor location (which is actually the 2nd floor of the hotel), but we realized Venice is so crowded and the hotel was near full occupancy, so we were fine with our room location.
