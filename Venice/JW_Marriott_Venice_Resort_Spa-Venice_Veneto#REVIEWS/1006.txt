This new hotel located on an island at 20 minutes from San Marco by boat enjoys a very nice situation. We stayed in the rooms of the Uliveto building, they are very quiet, well insulated, big enough and with a terrace on the olive tree field. There are two restaurants, one on the roof top and one Michelin one star rated, which we haven't tasted. The roof top one is expansive for what it is and the pasta we ordered were salty and hardly eatable. Four main courses and mineral water for 160€ !!!  The pools are two small for the size of the hotel and you have the choice between the kids (garden pool) or David Guetta music (roof top pool), none of them is quite and calm.
