My family & I had the best stay at this resort this past week. The resort is on a private island but it was easy to leave with the complementary water taxi provided by the hotel. All the other islands we went to were only 20-30 minutes away & the ride there was breathtaking so we didn't mind.  The staff was what made our stay worthwhile. They were absolutely incredible, always happy to help & very positive. The concierge was extremely helpful with accommodating tours & helped us navigate the island effortlessly. The staff at the rose bar was efficient and attentive with us at all time. Our bartender, Albert made our nights at the bar very special with the creative drinks he made every which were all detailed & beautifully executed.
