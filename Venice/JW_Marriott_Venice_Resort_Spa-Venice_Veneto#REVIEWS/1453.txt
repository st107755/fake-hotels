This hotel is located a short boat ride away from San Marco Square.  The ride takes about 15 minutes.  I didn't find the driver of the boat to be as helpful with the bags, but sucked it up as I heard this was a very nice property.  The biggest attraction to this hotel is that it is NEW - nothing in Venice is new - so that is very appealing.  The JW essentially has it's own island in Venice.  We were upgraded to their nicest room/villa on the property and for that I can only give this resort a 5 star review.  Maybe it wasn't crowded, maybe they were just being nice or maybe I was just lucky - regardless, it was incredible.  The grounds have cool art and most rooms are very well decorated.  Many rooms have their own plunge pools.
