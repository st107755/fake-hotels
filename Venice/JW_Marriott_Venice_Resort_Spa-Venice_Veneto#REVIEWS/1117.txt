I recently stayed at JW Marriot Venice  hotel/resort. It was wonderful. The staff were professional, warm and ready to do anything to make you happier (if that is even possible!).  I wanted to specifically mention 2 scenarios.   The first one, I have a dietary restrictions and cannot eat gluten or dairy. I ate at the hotel multiple times and found plenty at the buffet for breakfast and restaurant menus. The wait staff was happy to guide me through the menu on what was safe for me and ask the chef to modify the items on the men for me. Fantastic service!   The second one, on checking out (around 9pm), I had asked about an item in the resort store. It was a bottle of vinegar I had at dinner. At the time the store was closed so I was unable to see the price of an item.
