Beautiful resort away from the hustle and bustle of Venice. Booked a Maisonette room and was told we were "upgraded" to a Residenza room with a pool. This room did have a nicer view but was half the size and actually at a lower rate than our booked room... Not so sure if that's an upgrade or not. Either way, room was nice and clean and large for a Europe hotel. Had a great treatment at the spa which also had beautiful facilities.   Housekeeping was average. Didn't change our glasses we used and didn't always take away our used towels at turn down. Nonetheless, it was still clean.   Shuttle service was hit or miss. There were too many guests for the amounts of boats they had to shuttle us to San Marco and back.
