A lovely hotel to which it is a treat to return after a day in Venice.  Unlike another reviewer we weren't offered another room when the air conditioning failed in ours but this did not detract too much from our stay as we just opened the windows.  The staff were endlessly obliging but service was somewhat slow even though the hotel was obviously only less than half full.  One wonders how it will cope when it is full.  The roof top bar and pool is a wonderful place to relax with a drink, or would be if loudspeakers did not pump out continual noise - you can't really call it music.  It's a shame that the city of Gabrielli and Vivaldi could not find better music to play than synthesized muzak.
