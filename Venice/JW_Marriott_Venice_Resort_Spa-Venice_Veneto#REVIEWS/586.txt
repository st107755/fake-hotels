The moment you arrive at JW Marriott Venice Resort you realize you're in a very special place. Your boat drops you at the dock, your luggage follows you as you approach the main building. But there is so much more. There are villas, guest houses, a conference center, adult only pools, family pools and heaven only knows what else there is on this "fantasy island". The entire Resort is beautifully appointed in a most sophisticated manner, modern and at the same time welcoming.
