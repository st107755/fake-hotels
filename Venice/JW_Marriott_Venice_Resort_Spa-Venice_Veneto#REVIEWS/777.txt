We decided on visiting Venice for a quick getaway with our kids (age 6, 8) before back to school, however we wanted a place where we could relax as well. This resort was the perfect combination. We upgraded to a suite which included some great perks including a private pool/terrace. We didn't use the pool that much though as we found the larger pool funner. We took advantage of the private tour to Murano, as well as the wine tasting. The wine tasting was one of the highlights, sommelier Simone Celeghin was fantastic. My husband and I were the only ones at the tasting, and he was really knowledgeable. We learned a lot and it was as though we were spending time with an old friend.
