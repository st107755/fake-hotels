We stayed here prior to leaving on a cruise and I used Marriott points for the stay.  Prior to arriving I contacted the hotel and let them know that we were celebrating our anniversary on this trip - so I'm not sure if that helped with the upgrade. We also received a bottle of prosecco and chocolate covered straweberries.      The location is both a good and bad thing.  While the hotel is initially a pain to get to with luggage (we opted to have the hotel arrange a private transfer for 120 euros), it is also nice to be away from the crowds.  The shuttle service was always punctual and the trip was quick to get to Venice.  We arrived from a trans-atlantic flight and got to the hotel around 10:30am.  We were hoping our room would be ready, and indeed it was.
