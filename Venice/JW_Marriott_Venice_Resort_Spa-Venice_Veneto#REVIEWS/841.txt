The hotel is a haven due to its location as a private island in the beautiful city of Venice.   We arrived in Venice in the evening after a long, stressful day in Verona. We boarded the shuttle boat service that would transport us from San Marco to the island, and thankfully, the dock where the boat is held was not hard to find.   Upon arrival, we were kindly greeted by many bell-boys at the entrance who took our luggages and guided us to the reception who was also very approachable and friendly. After knowing how stressful our day was in Verona, she was very reassuring about our stay in Venice and even gave us lots of useful tips and general knowledge about the area.   Our room had a lagoon view, so we faced the distant city of Venice and the private island's dock.
