Situated on Isola Delle Rose, the hotel is essentially on it's own private island, with recently landscaped gardens; it's a lovely setting to allow yourself to simply unwind after a days sightseeing.  Transfer from Venice, near St Marks Square takes around 20 minutes on a JW Marriott boat, this can be a bit of a chore, but is also a pleasant experience, just make sure you note the timetable, as there are occasions where there are 45 minute gaps, which would be irritating if you just missed one.  A nice touch was the complimentary bottled water in the boat departure / arrival area on the island.  Arriving at the hotel by boat you can't help but be impressed with the hotel and you do feel quite privileged to be staying on your own island.
