My husband & I only stayed at the JW Marriot for 2 days and we wish we stayed longer so we could relax a bit more! We truly enjoyed this magnificent newer hotel which was situated on its own private island. Can't get much better than this! The complimentary shuttle boat takes you to St Mark's Square every 30 minutes and we had no trouble whatsoever going back & forth. It was a nice 20 minute boat ride. We stayed in a villa and not in the main hotel. So I don't know what those rooms are like. This room was spacious and decorated beautifully. The bathroom was large with 2 double sinks and a tub/shower. The toilet was in a separate room with another sink. It was a very quiet bldg. which we enjoyed.
