The property was gorgeous. The grounds were very green, well manicured and you could here the cicadas/crickets chirping throughout the day. The views of the sea from every direction were fabulous. The shuttle boat to Venice was convenient but required planning as it left on the hour half hour and reached capacity quickly. The ride itself was better than any taxi ride you'd encounter to a city.   The rooms were comfortable and lovely. We stayed in a deluxe room in the main hotel which was spacious and perfectly adequate for what we needed.
