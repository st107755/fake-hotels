I have been a business traveler and traveled the world for fun.  So I have some experience with hotels and my stay at the JW Marriott in Venice is one of the nicest experiences I have had.  Important to me are a beautiful enviornment, rooms with views, great food at the hotel and a feeling of being pampered by the staff.  Under Paolo Parlavecchia's watch (Night Manager) - I experienced all of these.  It's a nice water taxi ride from the center of Venice and the moonlight rides at night were beautiful.  We splurged for the water taxi when we arrived and departed, arranged by the hotel  and worth it.  The workout facility was nice and the spa was beautiful, although a bit expensive.  Next time I will do the spa rather than taking the trip to some of the  outer islands.
