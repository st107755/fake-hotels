Just returned from a 4 night break in this wonderful hotel on its own private island just across from St Mark's and all the sights of Venice.  After the crowds and bustle of Venice it was heaven to go back to the peace and tranquillity of this wonderful hotel to recharge our batteries for another foray the next day.  The transfers were by private launch from its own jetty at St Marks and were a regular half-hour shuttle all day and until 1.00 am - very comfortable and safe even for us oldies (both in our late 70's).  The hotel was beautiful and the staff could not do enough for you, always smiling when greeting you on your return from your outings.  The room was fantastic with the skyline of the city across the water from the Windows.
