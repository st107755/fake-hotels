My husband was mad keen to book this hotel following a business trip there in the summer and I could see why when we arrived, it was a delight. To have your own island so close to the heart of Venice! To be able to jump on a hotel boat back and forth across the lagoon to St Marks was fantastic. They upgraded us on arrival and gave us a fantastic apartment with it's own stylish front garden. We didn't spend much time there though as we were bound for the mainland but I will be back hopefully one day to make better use of the wonderful setting and facilities. The food was very good, the pool on the roof stunning, the hotel's grounds and interiors very smart.
