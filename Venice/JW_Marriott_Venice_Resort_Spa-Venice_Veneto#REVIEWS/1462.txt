Located on a small private sland opposite Piazza San Marco, this beautiful hotel is  the perfect place to relax after a hectic day in Venice. The  free ride into town is a pleasure in itself. The shuttle is scheduled about every 20 minutes until late at night. The hotel provided useful information on how to reach the property from the airport. Check-in was very friendly and welcoming. Platinum status was acknowledged with an upgrade to a very nice suite. With an outside temperature of 34*F, the room was cool, however, during the night it warmed up considerably. The engineer who was sent up found that the (large) heated towel rack was turned on ;-). Problem solved. Room, hall ways, public areas, pool, water shuttle: everything was spotlessly clean.
