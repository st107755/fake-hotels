This was our first time in Venice. I have mixed feelings about our stay and the property.  The property itself is absolutely amazing.  If you like being on a secluded Island far away from the tourist chaos, then then is for you.  The downside to that is that you really are trapped for all of your meals if you do not opt to take the 20 minute free water shuttle off the island.  The other downside is that the meal options are extremely expensive at this property.  Although we heard of other guests having their breakfast included, as a Platinum Premier, ours was not.  We decided to skip the pricey breakfast and visited the local Coop grocery store in Venice (which was amazingly cheap with great quality food), and do breakfast in our room.  It helped.
