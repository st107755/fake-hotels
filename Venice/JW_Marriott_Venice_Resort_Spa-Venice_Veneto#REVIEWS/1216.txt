I have given this hotel a 5 star rating however that is based upon overall experience as if I was being critical it does have small things missing which should be there for the price. We did struggle to find the free shuttle when we arrived in San Marco and we had to phone the hotel. It should say on the hotel booking that the shuttle for the hotel is behind the shuttle for another hotel as it was hidden so we ended up walking 15 minutes in the wrong direction. Check in was great with staff not being able to do enough to help. They gave us a booklet of hotel activities. There is lots to do here but only in the daytime. Even the rooftop pool was only open until 6/7pm which was too early in my opinion.
