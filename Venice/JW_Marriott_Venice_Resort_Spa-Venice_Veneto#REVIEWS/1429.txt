I stayed at this hotel in April 2015 and very much enjoyed it, but was aware some parts of it were still being built. So I stayed here again in July 2015 to try out the new amenities and also because I wanted to visit Venice during the Festa del Redentore and the Biennale (both of which I'd highly recommend). I now regard this hotel as my little island getaway in Venice and have only fond memories and a desire to stay there again. The gym at the hotel is now finished and, as I was training for an exercise event, it was wonderful to be able to use the equipment to keep up my fitness level.  The gym is open 24 hours a day, so there is absolutely no excuse for not keeping fit.
