This is a wonderful resort hotel. New with beautiful ground which are still being developed. The view from the roof top bar at sunset is by itself worth staying at this hotel.  The hotel is very new and certain facilities are not as yet available and the main deficiency at the moment is the 4th floor restaurant is the only evening meal restaurant and it is well below the standard of this hotel. The menu is limited, the service patchy, their is a lack of control and organisation. Even when the restaurant was not busy service was slow. On one occasion  we were in the restaurant for 90 minutes to have a simple meal. I would not eat at the restaurant again. The breakfast restaurant was the complete opposite.
