WOW - this is an amazingly beautiful property! The architecture of the old buildings with the new is stunning. The spa building is breathtaking from the outside (inside is nice too). Staff was extremely friendly and accommodating. The room were very nicely appointed. We stayed in larger room in U'Liveto building.  The second floor room boasted a deck - view of the church and water beyond.  We did not want to stay on the ground floor of the U'Liveto building - very little outdoor privacy. If you are a foodie, be sure to make a reservation at the resorts Michelin Star restaurant - Dopolavoro Dining Room - a magnificent experience. For day to day eats, SAGRA on the rooftop of the main building is FANTASTIC!
