I stayed here for three nights for a conference in June. The location on an island separate from historical part of Venice is great to avoid the crowds of tourists, and the staff have been all helpful and friendly, the rooms are large and modern.  Arriving to hotel by water taxi from mainland has been a truly special experience, but might be difficult logistically to arrange transportation from mainland for over 100 people. Conference facilities can easily host 150 people, but the service for conference facilities could have been better. For example, there were no glasses for water provided in meeting rooms, and the staff did not come to check the cleanliness of meeting rooms during the day - small things that oftentimes make quite the difference.
