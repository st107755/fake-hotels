We wanted to get out of Venice proper for the last leg of our babymoon. We transferred from the Hotel Gritti straight to JW Marriott. Definitely different type of hotels. But JW Marriott has a lot to offer. I am happy we were able to split the time between two hotels because JW provided us with the lovely distance from hustling, busy and chaotic Venice. JW felt very modern and Americanized not a true Venetian feel...but that was fine with us. We checked into our junior suite we booked and unfortunately we were very turned off by the smell in the carpet. Seems the hotel knows about this and has some issues with the carpet smells. The junior suite was nice if not for that.
