We booked this hotel before it was finished and therefore had few expectations. As reviews began to appear we could see there were some teething problems which are to be expected in any new resort. Thankfully we had no real issues. The location on a private island close to Venice affords stunning views from the rooftop terrace bar and pool and provides a quiet haven after a day exploring. Of course this does mean that you need to get a shuttle boat to and from San Marco but we enjoyed our 20 mins of relaxing views and they always ran to time. Our room was a "basic" one but was still luxurious. The bathroom with walk in shower and separate bath was idyllic and the bed was extremely comfortable. The air conditioning was controllable, quiet and efficient.
