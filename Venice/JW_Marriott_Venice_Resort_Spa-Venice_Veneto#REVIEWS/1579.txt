This is a superb hotel and part of the JW line of Marriott Hotels. It is a brand new hotel, and like other luxury hotels in Venice it is a renovated palace. The hotel is located on a private island and the grounds are beautifully landscaped. The rooms are large by European standards, especially when compared with the tiny rooms you typically find in Venetian hotels. Prices are high, but it is definitely worth it and very comparable to other high end luxury hotels in Venice.   The hotel has a dedicated shuttle taxi every 30 min. to St. Mark's and it is about a 20 min. ride. We were a little apprehensive initially, but the shuttle service is excellent and goes on until midnight. They also have a morning shuttle to Santa Lucia Train Station, which is very convenient.
