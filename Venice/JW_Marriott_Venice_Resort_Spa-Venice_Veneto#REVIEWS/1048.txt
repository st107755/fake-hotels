We had five lovely days at this hotel, celebrating my birthday and our wedding anniversary.  When we checked in, Marco upgraded us to a junior suite, which was spacious and comfortable, with everything you could need.  Breakfasts were delicious, the shuttle boat which runs from the island to St. Marks Square was excellent, and so was the service in the hotel.  All the staff we had contact with were eager to help, very smiley and upbeat.  Which brings me back to the marvellous Marco.  He was so kind and helpful to us, and really helped to make our stay there very special.  Our only slight disappointment was the two swimming pools.  The rooftop pool has stunning views but is very cramped, and is not intended for swimming.
