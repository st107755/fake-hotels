We spent two wonderful days at JW Marriott in Venice. The hotel is on a private island a 20 min boat drive from St Marco's Square (complimentary to hotel guests). The room was tastefully decorated, voluminous with a big bathroom (we had a junior suit). The rooftop pool on the fourth floor with its adjacent restaurant is truly amazing, it enjoys wonderful evening sun as an icing on the cake. The SPA is the biggest in Venice and has an other pool. There is also a third pool on the grounds aimed at families next to a huge lawn with football and volleyball goals and a playground. The hotel also has a Michelin star restaurant that we didn't have time to visit. First class facilities all in all.
