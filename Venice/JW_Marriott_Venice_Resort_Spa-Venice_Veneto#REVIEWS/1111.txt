We've recently returned from a 4 night break at the JW Marriott Venice Resort & Spa so thought we'd add our review to the many on here already! I'd done my research about the hotel and Venice in the weeks before our trip so was aware of the 20-25 minute shuttle boat trip to & from the hotel - I have to say we both thoroughly enjoyed it and didn't find it an inconvenience at all. It was nice to get away from the heat and crowds around St Marks Square and get back to the tranquillity of the island/hotel.  On arrival at the hotel our luggage was dealt with and taken to our room. We had a 'deluxe' room but I think this is their 'standard' room, it was more than adequate - plenty of space, spotlessly clean, very comfy, firm bed, large bathroom with huge shower cubicle (no bath).
