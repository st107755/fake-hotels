We were initially nervous about staying on an island off of Venice because we really wanted to spend a lot of time in Venice.  However, the boat shuttle was reliable and made that a non-issue.  It was actually much nicer to have an escape from the crowds of the city when we wanted it.  The grounds, rooms, and service are all fantastic.  Would absolutely recommend and hope to return.
