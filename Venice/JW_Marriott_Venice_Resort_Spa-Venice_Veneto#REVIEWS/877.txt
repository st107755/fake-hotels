Stayed for 5 nights B&B in Sept 2016. Check in was very quick and efficient. We had room 202 which is a double/corner room with a view over towards St Marks. All staff we encountered were extremely professional, pleasant and helpful in every respect. The regular motorboat service runs every 30 minutes and was very comfortable.  The room itself was pristine, well equipped and housekeeping excellent.  The bed was lovely and comfortable and the hotel was quiet at night.  The breakfast buffet was very good and varied.  I particularly liked the smoked salmon and cream cheese with lovely fresh bread.  My partner had fresh eggs cooked most days by the chef as the scrambled eggs on the buffet were not always warm!
