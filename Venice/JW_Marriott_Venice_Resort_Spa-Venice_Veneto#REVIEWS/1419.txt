My wife and I arrived at the hotel for a one-night stay as part of a fly-cruise package.  The actual arrival at the hotel is extremely impressive as the water-taxi slides straight into a dock under the hotel frontage.  We arrived at around 11.30am knowing that check-in wasn’t formally until 3.00 pm – but we were, as ever, hopeful of our room being available earlier than that.  We stood in one of four long queues and eventually spoke to one of the staff who confirmed that our room wasn’t yet ready but that we would be prioritised – so please check back regularly.  We were offered the option of upgrading to a suite for 150 Euros – but didn’t think that that was worthwhile so declined.
