My family and I just returned from 2 nights at the JW Marriott in Venice.  For the most part, it's a great place.  The physical environment (your own private island with relaxing breezes and beautiful landscaping and buildings designed by a talented architect) are wonderful and I can't imagine any way you could improve it.  The island is just beautiful and so relaxing ....especially after the hectic pace of Venice's tourist areas.  The free water shuttle is a huge treat and we enjoyed every ride.  Plus, the pools and workout room and the hotel itself are wonderful.  Also, our hotel room and our beds were very comfortable.  Finally, the staff are for the most part very competent.  All in all, it's a wonderful place and we will go back.
