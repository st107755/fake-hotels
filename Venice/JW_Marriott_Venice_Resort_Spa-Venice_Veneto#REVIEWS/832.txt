I have been to Venice twice, the first staying right on the Grand Canal, this time at the JW. The property is lovely...nice large comfortable rooms with great beds, bathroom and coffee maker, refrigerator, and a couch for lounging. Breakfast was top notch. The property is large with many buildings, a park, free bikes to use and a rooftop bar and pool (the bikes, pool and roof were not open or not really usable given the temperature during our visit.) They have a free boat shuttle to St Marks square every half hour, which is essential as a taxi is $70 euro. I learned that as one day they changed the schedule and we were holding tickets for something we had to go to at a set time.
