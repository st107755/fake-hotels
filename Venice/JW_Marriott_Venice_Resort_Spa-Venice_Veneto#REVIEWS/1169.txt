Spent a week at JW Marriott  Venice recently. When you visit iconic places like Venice, it's rare to think that probably the most impressive part of the visit would actually be the hotel.  JW Marriott Venice is that hotel. Being met straight off the plane, escorted through customs & immigration straight to the private speedboat that delivers you Bond-esque into the Hotel Lobby just set the mood for an  incredible holiday experience .  Venice is a beautiful, busy, crowded, noisy, tiring experience on a good day, so catching the shuttleboat  (that goes every 1/2 hour) back to the tranquility and relaxation of the private island that is Isola Delle Rose, was something to look forward to at day's end.
