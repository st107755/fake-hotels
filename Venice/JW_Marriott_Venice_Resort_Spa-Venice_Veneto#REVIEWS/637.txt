We first visited the JE Marriott Venice just after it opened in 2015 and had a fabulous time. We have just visited again and I have to say it's even better now. In 2015 they were still working on teething problems, like getting staffing levels right and the facilities perfect. Don't get me wrong, even with those teething problems we loved it.  So we were excited to return and see if anything was different and a little nervous about whether we should go back, as we didn't want to be disappointed.  We needn't have worried, in fact it was even better this time around.  It started well with an upgrade to the "La Maisonette" which is a 1 bed apartment with its own garden and hot tub. Very kind of them!
