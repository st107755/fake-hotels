We stayed at this hotel for 10 days. The grounds are beautiful and access to Venice is very easy. The rest... is not so good. We went for our honeymoon and the hotel did absolutely nothing to make it a "special" stay. In fact, when we checked in we were downgraded from a deluxe room in the gardens (which we had booked) to a junior room in the main building. The main building was loud, kids were running down the halls and you could hear footsteps outside our door at all hours of the night. When we requested to be moved into the room we actually booked they said sorry we're at capacity. There was also a bottle of champagne in the room, nice! Except it came with a note that it was provided because of my platinum elite status and didn't even mention my wife or our honeymoon.
