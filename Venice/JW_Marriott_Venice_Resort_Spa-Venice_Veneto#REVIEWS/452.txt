Being away from the madness of Venice in the summer was invaluable. We stayed in the Maisonette and Uliveto buildings which have their own concierge. The rooms were beautiful and include the breakfast which was plentiful and delicious as well as included prosecco! We took advantage of the resort package and visited Murano and Burano. In hindsight, we wouldn't have added on that fee as the visits to those islands were nothing more than a merchandising connection. Better to visit on your own so you can have time to wander those gorgeous islands at your own pace. We felt rushed and hurried to buy and then quickly shuttled back to the hotel. Great service all around, but very expensive for hotel food even at hotel prices which are always steep.
