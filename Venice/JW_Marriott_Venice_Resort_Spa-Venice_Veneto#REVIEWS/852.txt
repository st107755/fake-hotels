I stayed here a week & a half ago with my boyfriend. The property is just as beautiful as the website photos. I slightly dreaded shuffling back & forth to the main island by boat, but the complimentary shuttle boat they have was great & on time with a lot of availability until midnight. The only thing is the earliest is not until 8:30AM, so when we left to make our flight from the Marco Polo airport, we took a private boat to the main island.   The JW brand is one of the more upscale Marriott brands, so it evokes a higher standard. Upon arrival, we found out our room wouldn't be ready for another hour. End of the world? No. Slightly irritating? Sure. They gave us a ticket for our bags with the concierge, who is situated at a counter right next to the front desk check in.
