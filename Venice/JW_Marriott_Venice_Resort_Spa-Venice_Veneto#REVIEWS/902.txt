We booked as part of a package with BA and came here to celebrate our 20th wedding anniversary.   We were not disappointed.  The hotel is stunning and immaculately kept and it was lovely to get away from the hustle and bustle of the city.  The hotel has its own boat which runs from 8.30 AM every half hour to 12.30 AM.  We used to go in for the day, come back late afternoon for some R and R, and then go in again for drinks and dinner in the evening.  We booked the water taxi transfers through the hotel - these cost 130 euros each way, but you do get your own private water taxi to and from the airport dock and frankly, it is the only way to arrive and depart in Venice.
