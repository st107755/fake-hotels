After arriving at Stazione di Venezia Santa Lucia (Train Station), it took almost 35 minutes to get to St. Mark's Square by the shuttle taxi's, €7.50 each.  The complimentary boat shuttle to the hotel is poorly signposted, but I think all hotels suffer from this.  The shuttle is every 30 minutes and quite a nice ride around 20 minutes to the island where the J W Marriott is located. So it took almost 2 hours to get to the hotel from the train station.  The initial room booked was a deluxe at €350.00 per night including breakfast, however, I chose to upgrade to a junior suite for an extra €200.00 per night.  (staying for 2 nights).  I was told a complimentary glass of Prosecco would be sent to the room.  This never emerged.
