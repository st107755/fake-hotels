We stayed 4 night at the JW Marriott Venice, it was such a fabulous experience. The resort has its own private island, just some minutes south from Venice and they provide free shuttle from early to very late in the day/night. The hotel is beautiful and the facilities brand new, modernly designed, and reflect the luxury and peacefulness of the place. The staff is friendly and helpful, they make their best to go the extra mile.  The gastronomic restaurant Dapolavoro is a real gem, the food is sublime and the service impeccable. Highest highlight: the sommelier, Octavio, who is so passionate and dedicated, he made amazing recommendations and made us discover fantastic local wines and 'smaller' wine-makers that produce high-end quality wines.
