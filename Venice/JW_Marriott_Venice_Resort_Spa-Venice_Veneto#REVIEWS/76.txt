I booked a last minute 3 night break to this hotel for my daughters birthday. It couldn’t have been a better memory for both of us. On arriving to Venice we took a water taxi direct to the hotel reception and was greeted immediately by all the staff, in particular the extremely busy reception and concierge staff who always had a friendly open welcome. We didn’t have long to wait for our room and boy was it worth waiting for.  We had the most amazing suite looking out over the gardens and lagoon.  The roof top pool albeit small was perfect for a few hours looking out from the infinity pool to the main city and other small islands.  The breakfasts were fabulous and the omelette station was superb.
