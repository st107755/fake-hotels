I have to say that I was in Marriott Venice under specific conditions. Hotel was opened just for our group and our conference. So I can't comment on their regular service and from a typical tourist point of view. The place - private island Isola della Rose - is just amazing. Across the Venice, in a very unique surrounding you can enjoy privacy and tranquility. I believe that you can't find too many place like this in Europe. Maybe in Asia and Central America, in a typical beach resorts.  Hotel is very new and you can see that they should fix some minor details. But rooms are amazing - big, comfortable, nicely decorated. Bathroom and amenities are just perfect. Service is good but as I said they are fresh so they need some time to get into to groove of perfect 5* service.
