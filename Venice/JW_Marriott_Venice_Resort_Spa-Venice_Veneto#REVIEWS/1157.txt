My husband booked our room here for my birthday. We were excited by the idea of a hotel on it's own island and the Marriott is a standard pleaser anywhere in the world. I have to say that we weren't completely sold on the experience.  It was expensive and inconvenient to stay on a private island with all the boat taxi's. The hotel gave us mixed messages on what taxi's they provided and what we had to pay for and we ended up paying more than expected. The restaurant was too expensive and not great at all and I felt as though I was missing out on the magic of Venice. The plus side was that we were away from the crowds and in a safe, clean and definitely luxurious environment which was nice because we had our two small children with us.
