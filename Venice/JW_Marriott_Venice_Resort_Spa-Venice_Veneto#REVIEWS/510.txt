There is a reason you arrive at this location by boat....because you need a boatload of cash to stay here.  This is a beautiful location and the property is gorgeous, however there is a stifling sterile feel as soon as you arrive.  Everyone is attentive, almost overwhelming.  The rooms are very nice and comfortable.  My problem with this location is you are captive here, there are some shuttles that take you from the island at no cost, however every time you go out its half hour 45 minutes out and back to Venice only.  If you want to go anywhere else you have to get a water taxi.  Biggest reason for my rating is the food, extremely expensive, myself and 2 others we had burgers and two beers each, the bill was over $180 and it was awful.
