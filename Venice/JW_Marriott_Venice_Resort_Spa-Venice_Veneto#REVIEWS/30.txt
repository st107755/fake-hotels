Beautiful hotel. Loved the speed boat from the airport, the roof top pool and spacious room (great to receive an upgrade). Less great experiences though were the Sagra restaurant - very bright lighting for dinner made it lack any atmosphere, food average and extremely expensive at €200 for two courses (main only for kids) and a couple of drinks each.  Worst experience was the murano glass factory tour. Salesman became very passive aggressive once he realised that we weren’t up for purchasing a statement piece of glassware and seemed unwilling to arrange a taxi back to the hotel (this was advertised as a return trip). Had to wait over an hour for the taxi in the end and the factory said they’d only drop us on San Marco.
