The resort is absolutely stunning. Design by Matheo Thun is chic, reduced to the max and truly calming. Service is extremely accommodating and genuinely friendly throughout the resort even tough it shows at times that not all associates seem to have the same training standard (quite evident at breakfast, where service consistency is somewhat lacking at times...e.g. 2nd service of coffee, busing of used dishes, replenishment of buffet). The only drawback is that recognition of status is almost unheard of. Whilst I do understand that it is a resort with it's own set of terms & conditions, such as no guaranteed late check out, it seems somewhat petty minded to decline a Gold member a 1 pm check out (vs. 12 noon regular check out time).
