Loved the "James Bond" entrance when we arrived via our private boat! Upgraded to a lovely room in one of the olive garden buildings with our own private patio. Very quiet and beautiful. Had dinner at the Sagra rooftop restaurant not much to say about that however. Breakfast buffet was excellent. We enjoyed the adults only roof top swimming pool although it was always crowded with limited pool side seating. On those busy days we were directed to the side areas which was unfortunate and really hot. Amazing that the hotel has so much grounds that they only have space for two small pools...roof top and family. It would seem that they would have room for a HUGE pool with swim up bars, etc after all they have "resort" in their name.
