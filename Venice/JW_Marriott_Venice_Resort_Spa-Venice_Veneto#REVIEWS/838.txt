As a very frequent traveller, staying in Marriott hotels all over the world, I know what I'm going to get. The down side, for some, is that things are so similar wherever you are, there is little to differentiate the locations from one another, making things less interesting. This Marriott couldn't be more different.  From the moment you approach the island and the impressive entrance at the private motor boat landing stage, you enter a world that couldn't be more removed from the Marriott standard.
