Beautiful private island 2km boat ride from San Marco square. Maybe not the best location for sightseeing as the boat takes 30 minutes each way and the boat only goes every 30 minutes and is sometimes to full to take everybody. Private taxi boat from the airport costs almost 200 euro each way.   The rooms are quite nice, sadly ours had an old and strange looking bathroom and the mini bar was not cold.  The service is quite slow and poor though, they forget a lot and you have to cue for checkin, cue for breakfast, cue for checkout. And a lot of things are charged extra; no free wifi, using the spa is 35 euro for hotel guests, even using the bicycles to make a small ride around the island cost 20 euro. Not really what you would expect.
