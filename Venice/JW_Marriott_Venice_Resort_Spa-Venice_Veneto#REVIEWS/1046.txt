This hotel makes you feel like a king (or queen)!  You drive the water taxi through a moat right into the lobby.  The island itself is impeccably kept and is spacious and quiet, unlike the rest of the city.  This is the only greenery you will get to see, unless you go to one of the outer islands.  We did not eat at the restaurant, but did spend a day on the roof deck!!!  What an amazing view.  You look right at St. Mark's square with a vanishing edge pool.  It really felt like a dream.  Definitely order food by the pool.  This is no ordinary pool food selection.  Perfect spot for an Aperol Spritz.    Being on an island does mean you have to take a water taxi / shuttle, and the shuttle  come less frequently, but definitely saves you a lot of money.
