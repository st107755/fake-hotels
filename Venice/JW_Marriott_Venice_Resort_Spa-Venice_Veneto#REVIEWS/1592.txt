Stayed a full week, which meant we intended to stay approx half a day in Venice and half a day in the hotel each day. Overall all this is a stunning location, with what seems to be now an excellent shuttle service, (although no shuttle boat service between 1.30 and 2.30 during the day.) Everything is nearly finished or finished except the gardens which are ongoing, although when we arrived we were disappointed that the family pool was not yet open! Breakfast was good and I can say that as it was included in our room rate, for some however it was $45 a day and that would be too much for what was on offer. Other than this the hotel a luxury hotel with lots of places and spaces to go. Staff were pro-active and accepted when things were not right and attempted to make good.
