We are here now. We have a suite - it's 1200 US a night. The hotel rooms are nice (to be honest at this price point I think expect allot more) but they are nice rooms. Service is generally excellent and better than most Marriotts. BUT if you chose this resort (as we did) because you have kids it a very very poor choice. There is a kids club, but it looks pretty grim, the family swimming pool would be described by people from the US as a "lap pool" no more than that. It's about 18 meters long and about  3-4 wide. Remember the hotel is huge. Imagine allot of kids (like an entire school) in a bath and that's pretty much it. At this price point it's a disgrace. There is also a small adults only pool. They serve ice cream at the pool and the hotel service standards are high.
