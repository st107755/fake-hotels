I am just back from a marvellous stay at the JW Marriott resort in Venice. It really was everything I hoped for. The hotel is on a private island in the lagoon and is reached either by private water taxi or by the free hotel water shuttle. The shuttle runs very regularly, has wifi and complimentary cool water (still and sparkling!) and takes around 20mins. Arriving was a joy. We were welcomed by staff on the private jetty and taken to reception for check in. The hotel is newly opened and has just been refurbished and is gorgeous. We were travelling as a birthday celebration and at check in we were given a room upgrade to a fabulous 2 floor loft room, which was amazing. This has happened to me before and is one reason why I stay in Marriott hotels whenever I can.
