We stayed here in June, shortly after the hotel opened and just never got round to the review.  We booked on an offer as the hotel was not open when we booked, and therefore prices were discounted to encourage bookings. Having always wanted to see Venice and having a birthday and Anniversary in one week in June, we took the chance and booked in. The arrival in Venice is dreamy enough, but when we were picked up at St Marks by the little boat and taken across to the hotel we couldn't believe what a lovely setting the hotel has.  The island is far from the crowds and hustle and bustle, but a short 15 minute trip across the lagoon.  The trip any time of day or night is just magical.
