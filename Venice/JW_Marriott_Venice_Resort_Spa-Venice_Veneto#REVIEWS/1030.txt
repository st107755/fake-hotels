Located on an island behind Giudecca, the JW Marriott is a good option to spend a couple of summer days in Venice. I would not recommend to come for a short trip, since the commute to San Marco takes almost thirty minutes. We spent a lot on taxi boats to Giardini, Giudecca & Co since it is a bit complicated to reach these destinations otherwise.  The hotel itself is quite lovely. Large garden, two outdoor pools and one indoor pool, gym, spa, running track. Be prepared that there is a wedding almost every weekend day, so a lot going on (it is not a quiet retreat, the hotel is huge with a large number of guests). I would give it 4.5 stars. Very relaxed way to experience Venice.  The weakest spot of the hotel is service.
