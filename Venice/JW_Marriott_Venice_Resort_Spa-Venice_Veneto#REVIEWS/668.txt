Our stay was a wedding present from my parents after getting married the weekend before. They'd booked us into a deluxe double, but on arrival the lovely staff upgraded us to a garden room near the spar.   We'd treated ourselves to a taxi from the airport €130, which dropped us right to the door through the imposing dock, a great way to enter, very James Bond. After check in, which was seamless, and took less than 5minutes, we were shown the room by the hotels trainee Romeo, he was very helpful and courteous.   I can't fault the room, it's perfect. I run a high end building company, we work in some of the most prestigious properties in London, the rooms decor and finish is on par with anything we do. An ideal place to spend down time in Venice.
