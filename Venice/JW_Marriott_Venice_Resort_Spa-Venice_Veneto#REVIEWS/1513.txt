We were very excited as we had booked the JW Marriott for a night in Venice.  Our expectations were not let down by this hotel except for some small hiccups. We took the motor boat in San Marco from JW Marriott's private dock as we stayed in San Marco for a night.It was an amazing experience and it was completely free of charge.  It took about 20 minutes to reach the Hotel from San Marco. They have shuttle to san marco which runs every 15 -30 minutes depending on the time. The check-in was smooth with Francesca checking us in.We had requested for inter-connecting rooms and they gave them to us which was very important to us as we had 3 children. Wi-fi was free only at public areas like the reception. The breakfast was amazing and it was upto the standard of 5 star hotel.
