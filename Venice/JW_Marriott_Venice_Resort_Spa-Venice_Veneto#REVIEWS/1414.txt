We have just finished our 5 nights here, and the hotel is beautiful, location wise it is top notch and hotel itself is very nice. We had our 11 month old with us. Rooms were a very decent size and good for a little one to roam around with no danger. They also have a lovely kids room with a ball pool and soft play, great for little ones. The resort has two pools booth which accept kids, although the 'family' pool is too deep for toddlers and so the roof top pool works best for them. There is also a small playground with swings greaat for little ones. All the staff were also very friendly. So far so great, however a few things let the hotel down from what we expect from a jw marriott.
