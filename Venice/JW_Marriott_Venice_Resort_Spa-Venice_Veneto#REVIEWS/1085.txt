Wonderful service. Loved our room with a private garden & dipping pool. Easy boat transit to & from Venice, left every 30 minutes.  Great returning to beautiful resort with lots of land & space after so much sight seeing  My only real complaint was the food. At night we were too exhausted to return to Venice & ate dinner twice on their rooftop restaurant. View & atmosphere was wonderful, along with service, but the food was disappointing. They tried too hard to over complicate the dishes (i.e. the carpaccio would have been better without the out of season chewy artichokes hearts) versus serving delicious Italian dishes with fresh ingredients. Even the caesar salad was below standard. The dressing had no flavor and the croutons tasted store bought.
