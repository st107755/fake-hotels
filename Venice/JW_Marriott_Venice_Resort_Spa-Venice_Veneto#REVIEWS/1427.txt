We stayed at this hotel for two days prior to our cruise.  It was perfect for what we wanted.  Built on a private island in the lagoon, it is a lovely and modern respite away from the crowds in Venice in August.  We arrived early afternoon on a Saturday, the check-in was quick and efficient (if you download the Marriott app you can do this online in advance and simply pick up your room key upon arrival).   We didn't use many of the amenities that the property has to offer however we were extremely satisfied with what we did experience.  Shuttle:  efficient, excellent location on Saint Mark's side, regular service (download the schedule from their website in advance), roughly 15 minute transfer time.
