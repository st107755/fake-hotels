This is wonderful hotel and so well appointed. The rooms are spotless, very comfortable and well equipped.  Being on its own island, getting there is an event in itself and adds to the whole experience. You can catch the Shuttle Launch from St Marks Square and takes you out across the Grand Canal towards the hotel. When you arrive, it is like an occasion and, for me, quite a surreal experience. The staff were extremely friendly and helpful. Couldn’t really ask for more and nothing to much trouble. When you explore the island, there are some notable places around. As you would expect, there are leisure facilities of quite a high standard. There is a chapel as well. It also has a separate Michelin star restaurant with its own gardens for produce.
