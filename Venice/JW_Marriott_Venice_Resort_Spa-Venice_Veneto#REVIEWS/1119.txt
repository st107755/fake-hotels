The Sagra restaurant gives guests the option to dine either outside with views of the grounds or inside in the main restaurant area with floor to ceiling windows. The restaurant is well light, simply decorated, has a relaxed atmosphere and is not too formal. The food drew positive comments from my 16 year old and 21 year old children resulting in us returning on three occasions during our stay. The beef did just melt in the mouth and the veal was very special. The kitchen were even willing to go off menu for my daughter  who is vegetarian. What added to the experience was the speed and quality of service. Nikolina, the restaurant's hostess always provided a genuine warm welcome.
