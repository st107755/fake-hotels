We have recently returned from spending 4 nights here, booked using my Marriott Reward points. I was a bit worried about the water shuttle provided by the hotel as I can get seasick but it was never a problem.  On arrival at the airport we bought a 7 day water bus ticket including return bus transfer from the airport for €72 each. It's cheaper than daily tickets and excellent value for money especially if you get out to the islands on the water buses. They run very regularly, and Burano is a must see with its colourful houses. When we got off the bus at the main bus station in Venice we made our only mistake. We saw a sign for San Marco where the hotel shuttle bus goes from and headed that way, thinking it was close, cases in tow.
