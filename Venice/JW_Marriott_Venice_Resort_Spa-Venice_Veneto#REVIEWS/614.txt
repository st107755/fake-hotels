We just left this resort a couple days ago and overall our experience was wonderful. Only one issue that irked me a bit.  We did a private water taxi to the hotel and it was 130 euros. While expensive, it was an amazing way to travel into Venice to see the city from the water.  Our room was spacious with a bedroom, bathroom, and living area. We were on the first floor with a "view of Venice." The view was not worth the money from there since the trees blocked most of it. I imagine something from a higher floor would be better if you're paying for the Venice view. In my opinion don't bother though as there are plenty of places at the hotel to get better views.  We ate at the main restaurant downstairs the first night and had some cocktails.
