Prior to taking a Mediterranean cruise, Regent Cruises put us up in the Venice Marriott on Isola De Le Rose, the sole occupant of a beautiful islet off Venice proper.  The hotel itself is a mixture of modern white exteriors/intereriors and old world service.   Upon arrival at Marco Polo Airport we were taken by water taxi to one of the most unique entries to a hotel we have ever seen; a water tunnel leading to the interior of the hotel.  The rooms are Italian modern, well appointed with large bathrooms with all the expected amenities. To offset the modern design the walls are covered with black and white photos of Hollywood greats of the past that have visited Venice.
