We chose to stay at the JW Marriott Venice for a long weekend to celebrate my 40th Birthday as we wanted somewhere a bit special and with some outdoor space of our own. We chose the Residenza rooms away from the main hotel with their own private terrace and garden. We paid a premium for a private boat transfer to and from Marco Polo airport which was well worth the money for the arrival journey as we were taken directly from the airport to the hotel and arrived at the 'James Bond' entrance - it was a great start to the holiday. We arrived early but our room was ready - again another bonus. The room was lovely and spacious with a lot of natural light and felt quite secluded so we weren't overlooked by anyone - just what we wanted.
