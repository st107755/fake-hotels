Me and my wife were recommended to stay at the Jw Marriott in Venice as it was a new hotel that offered something unique to other hotels in Venice. It did not disappoint!! An amazing hotel! You arrive in style into their impressive dock and are greeted warmly. Jw marriott is set on a huge private island and the grounds are stunning! What a location. Yes it is far from the centre of Venice but they offer a free shuttle to San Marco square every 30mins. The staff were great - very welcoming and friendly. You could tell that they are trying hard as this is a new hotel. We stayed in a junior suite and were not disappointed! It was a lovely room with a large balcony overlooking the gardens and water of Venice. The bathroom was large and as always JW beds are amazing!
