Visited Italy in August, only now finding the time to review. Stayed 3 nights at the J.W. Marriott which is on its own private island, Isola delle Rose, in the San Marco Lagoon. Venice in August is a bit like Disneyland, it is so full of tourists that you can literally run in to tourist pedestrian traffic jams. Staying at the JW, with its wide beautiful old growth gardens, was magical respite from the crush of Venice. They have a shuttle boat that runs back and forth to Piazza San Marco every 15 minutes or so. which was super convenient.
