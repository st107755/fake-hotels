I recently stayed at JW Marriot as part of my family holiday to Venice. It has been an amazing experience, one of our best holidays. Venice is a beautiful place as it is but the added stay at this wonderful resort hotel makes the experience extra special. There is a complimentary boat ride between the hotel and Venice throughout the day and into the night. The restaurants within the hotel are superb. The views from the roof top pool are stunning. A very special feature of this hotel is the friendly and helpful attitude of the entire staff, I would especially like to mention Mr Marcello at the breakfast lounge who has been brilliant with his hospitality towards us. Also Teresa at the reception desk was very helpful.
