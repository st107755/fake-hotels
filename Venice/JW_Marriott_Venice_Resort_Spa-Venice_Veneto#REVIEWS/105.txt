Stayed at this hotel for 3 night with our kids of 3 and 5 years old. The beginning of our stay was somewhat not promising: when we arrived at the hôtel at midnight, we found all the guests evacuated at the conference center  because of fire at the hotel apparently set by one of the guests. Despite the overall chaos and our tiredness, i must say the situation was being handled very well by the staff. They were efficient, offered a multitude of beverages, towels, and were genuinely concerned and trying to help as much as they could. The rest of the stay was great. We stayed at the main building. The room was very spacious, bathroom was very clean, but quite minimalistic, beds were comfortable. The rooms were made up several times a day.
