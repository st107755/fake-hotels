It is a very impressive entrance as the water taxi takes you right into the hotel which is of a smart, modern style with cool and calming colour scheme. Reception staff were very helpful on arrival and took us to our pool loft suite. Spacious accommodation with added benefit of views to Venice and small garden area with loungers and plunge pool. Breakfast was excellent with choices to suite all tastes and ages. We ate in Sagra on the rooftop each evening and by 4th night, a bit more variety would have been welcome, particularly in terms of pasta and providing 'taster' or starter size portions. The standard of food is excellent.
