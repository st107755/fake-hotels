We spent 6 nights at the JW Marriott in one of their Duplex Suites - family of 4, two children aged 10 and 18.  The hotel is located on a beautiful island in the lagoon of Venice. It takes about 20 minutes to get to San Marco from there and the hotel has shuttle boats operating the whole day.  From check-in to check-out we felt very welcome and received fantastic service.  The head-concierge Vittorio and his team helped us organize some fantastic experiences during our stay.  Most memorable highlight was spending a day with Chef Micki and learn about Venetian food and cooking. The hotel has a fantastic cooking school and Micki is a wonderful and special guy. We all had a really special time together with him.
