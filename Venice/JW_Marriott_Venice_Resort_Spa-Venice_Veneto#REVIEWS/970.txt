This is the second time we have stayed at the JW Marriott in Venice. We initially came for two nights in May and decided to return in August with friends. The hotel itself is great for families. It has activities to keep children (and adults) entertained and close enough to frequently visit Venice itself.  The highlight has definitely been the service. All the staff have been very welcoming and attentive and many remembered us from our 2 night stay in May.   In particular I would mention Titziana and Gianmarco at Reception, Gabriele, Nicola and all the team at Giardino and also Simone at Sagra. We also had another fantastic Pasta Cooking class with Chef Micki and Raphael. I can't recommend the classes enough. Such a great experience and fun for all the family!
