The JW Marriott Venice Resort & Spa is amazing. It is truly 5-star. I booked 2 rooms with points. 40,000 Marriott Rewards (13,333 Starpoints) points per night, which was an amazing deal. First off, the 17-minute shuttle to and from San Marco is a breeze. I thought it would be annoying, but it's not. It runs every half out from 7 am (I think) to 12:30 am.   At check in, both of our rooms were upgraded due to our Marriott Gold Elite status. One room was a 2 twin DeLuxe room (Room 110) with a huge terrace and larger area of space, and the other was upgraded (complimentary/free) to a Studio Suite (Room 109). The Studio Suite name is misleading in a good way! It wasn't one large room, but was a huge 1 bedroom suite with a large bathroom and sitting area.
