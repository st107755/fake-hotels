We booked the JW Marriott Venice months in advance and were extremely excited for our stay. Even though the location of the hotel on an island off Venice was slightly remote, we believed that the luxurious resort experience promoted on the hotel website would more than make up for the travel time.  At the time of booking I believed the price was reasonable for a hotel of that calibre, especially considering the amenities provided throughout the resort. What I didn't know when I booked was that many of these amenities came at an additional cost and even wifi was extremely expensive. I travelnfrequently and cannot remember last time I had to pay for wifi.   The island itself is a great concept and the free boat transfer to and from Venice was excellent.
