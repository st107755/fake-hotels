This beautiful hotel is located on a private island, Isola Delle Rose.  I stayed for just one night following a Mediterranean cruise, and booked with Marriott points.  You reach the island through a dedicated shuttle boat which has multiple power outlets, comfortable seating, and capacity for 39 passengers.  Your luggage is stowed dockside and brought to your room after check-in.    The sprawling, and meticulously manicured grounds make a tremendous first impression. The lobby is immaculate, expansive, beautifully decorated, and scented with just a hint of rose fragrance.  The check-in experience is wonderfully different, and brilliant in its simple ingenuity.
