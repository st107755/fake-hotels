5 star service. 5 star facilities. Boat back and forth to Venice was timely and dependable. Staff were incredibly friendly. Room was well sized and appointed. Fitness facility was superb.   All of that said, I would not stay here again. It is simply too isolated and prices on food and beverage are insane. 34 euro breakfast, 40 euro pizza buffet. Could not believe the prices on beverages.   For example, bottle of Moet & Chandon Ice Imperial Champagne during our Spain/Italy trip was 35 euro in Nice airport duty free, 50-60 euro at stores in towns, and 65-95 euro at other 5* hotels/resorts during our trip (including Fairmont, Ritz Carlton, and Le Meridien properties). At the JW *market* the price was 165 euro.
