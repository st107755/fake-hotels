This is a new hotel on a private island in the Venetian lagoon. It is clean, bright, airy and over air conditioned as you might expect, and has retained some of  the old buildings, converted to resort usage, but there is little on the island  to do apart from walk around the formal lawns or sunbathe. The huge surprise on such a large island is that the pools are tiny, so it is not an option to swim in them! The mature trees have been retained, so walking is pleasant.
