My husband & I spent 3 nights at this hotel & had very high hopes. Given that it was the most expensive hotel we stayed at on our trip through Europe, we expected it to wow us. Unfortunately, it did the opposite. Our room was clean, bright, & large. The bathroom was the same, but we found the lack of counter space & lack of shelving of any sort in the shower annoying. We had to put everything on the floor of the shower. Tea, coffee, & bottled water were complimentary. And the hotel is on its own private island. We liked the idea of this but actually found it worked against us in the end. Because it was a 20-minute boat ride to the city of Venice, we killed a lot of precious time simply riding to & from the hotel.
