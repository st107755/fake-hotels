Family of 5 stayed here in May 2017 for 3 nights prior to a cruise.  Great hotel!  The hotel is located on its own island where the grounds are beautiful and landscaped well. The service was top notch and very friendly.  The rooms are definitely on the small side, especially considering the per night cost. Saying that,  the rooms are well appointed and very clean. Only issue I had was the very limited English television channels.  When relaxing at the end of the day it was hard to find any English channel other than news. The interior of the hotel is very modern, very clean and welcoming.      I suggest a private water taxi to the hotel.  It was quick from the airport, but it was a little difficult to locate the actual taxi location at the airport.  This is a great hotel!
