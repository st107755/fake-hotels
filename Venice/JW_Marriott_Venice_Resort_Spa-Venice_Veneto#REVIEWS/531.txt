I give this resort 5 stars because it truly provided superb service and amenities. I would give it 4 stars for location. This resort absolutely does not feel like Venice. If you are visiting Venice for the first time I would want to know how modern and clinical the property feels. The spa was outstanding and the spa facilities are out of this world! The ride to the island is a lot of work. We took the train then had to find our way to San Marco to get the JW boat. Our options were get on a ferry, equivalent to a crowded city bus, or pay a water taxi 60 euros to get to San Marco. We were lucky to find another couple who split it with us. Once you get to San Marco, it is a slow ride to the island. It took about 20 to 30 minutes to get back and forth.
