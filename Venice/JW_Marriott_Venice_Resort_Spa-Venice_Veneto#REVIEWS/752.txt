We came to Venice in October for 4 nights to celebrate my husband birthday. We have visited this beautiful city several times before but this was the first time when we decided to be away from the centre and it was a great choice. JW Marriott is a very new hotel situated on its own island 20 minutes shuttle ride from the St Marks Square and about 40-50 minutes from the airport. We had a privet taxi transfer from Marco Polo airport and it was the best way to start our short holidays. You enter the hotel form the water by a privet entrance short walk form the main lobby. The hotel consists from the main building and few separate complexes throughout the island that offer different type of accommodation.
