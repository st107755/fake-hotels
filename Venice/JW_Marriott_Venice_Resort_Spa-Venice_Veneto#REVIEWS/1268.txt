Very nice JW hotel in the island of roses in Venice. The hotel has full facilities, ideal for a couple to relax. Unfortunately it is 30 min by boat from downtown Venice so you not practical if you are planning to visit Venice every day. When you book, do not forget to reserve the transfer from and to the airport otherwise you may end up having to pay a small fortune.
