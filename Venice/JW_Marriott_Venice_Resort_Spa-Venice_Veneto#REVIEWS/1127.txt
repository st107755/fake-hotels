While this is a very impressive hotel in a wonderful location, this wasn't the 5-star experience for which I was hoping.   There was a mix-up on check-in: our double room wasn't ready so we were offered a different room. Great. Until we reach the room and it was has two single beds. As a gay couple, I found this insulting - a straight couple would never have been offered the room. I hope it was just linguistic mis-communication but it was at best, disappointing and at worst, discriminatory.  My main problem with this hotel is that rather than offering a special, indulgent experience, it felt a bit like the hotel was trying to drain every last Euro from their guests.  The rooftop bar has stunning views over the lagoon, but there's a €7 cover charge. To use the hotel bar!
