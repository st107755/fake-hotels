My husband and I stayed at the JW Marriott Venice resort recently and we must say we had a fantastic trip. The food is amazing and the staff could not be more accommodating. They really went above and beyond to ensure my husband had a great birthday during our stay.  The trip on the shuttle boat takes around 20 minutes and the hotel being away from the centre of Venice means you really do get away from the hustle and bustle.  This hotel is really worth a visit and we would return due to the impeccable service however my only negative (hence 4 stars and not 5) relates to the rooftop pool. This is a beautiful pool and the views are amazing however unless you get up there for opening it is difficult to actually get a sun lounger on the top deck due to the lack of them.
