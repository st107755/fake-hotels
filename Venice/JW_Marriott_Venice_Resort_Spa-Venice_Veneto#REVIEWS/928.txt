The JW Marriott is a gorgeous property and a relaxing place to stay away from the hustle and bustle of Venice. It's on its own island and a short boat ride away (there are convenient boat shuttles every 1/2 hour to and from Venice). The property is peaceful and well-manicured. The views from the hotel are amazing—especially from the roof-top pool and the Spa. The hotel is beautifully decorated and serene. It's a good place for couples or a family as there is both a family pool and adult pool. Felt the rooms were well appointed, clean, modern, comfortable and restful. We worked with Sara Scolaro, the guest coordinator, to create a special stay. She did an outstanding job organizing customized trips to the nearby Prosecco region and the islands Murano, Burano and Torcello.
