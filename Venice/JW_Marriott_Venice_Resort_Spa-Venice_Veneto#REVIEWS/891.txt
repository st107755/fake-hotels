This property is exquisite. Expansive, well designed, elegant service. Rooms are well-appointed. Very good breakfast. Roof-top bar/pool (over-looking the water) gorgeous. Free water shuttle service on time and convenient. We sat at the bar each night and opened our own aged Brunellos/Napa cabs without hassle or corkage fee. Sagra restaurant average and over-priced. The property needs more dining options. Service professionals were uniformly elegant, proactive, responsive and helpful. Concierge english-speaking, intelligent and excellent.  As a new resort this property had a "corporate" feel to it that I suspect will evolve and soften over time. The JW "formula" is very apparent.
