This property, like most JW Marriott's is located in an isolated and beautiful area that leaves you captive to its restaurant, beverages and other pricing.  The property is on an island that you access by private water taxi from the Venice airport or by public transportation to San Marco dock and then catching the complimentary JW Marriott boat to the hotel.  We contacted the hotel in advance and they arranged the private water taxi  through Venezia Turismo at a cost of 140 Euros one way.  Upon arriving in the airport it was difficult to locate the Venezia office.  After asking several people who worked at the airport we finally found the very small sign and desk.
