This is a great new hotel, set on its own island, 20 minutes by boat from San Marco and the centre of Venice.  We can discuss the tourist over-population of this wonderful city elsewhere; if you're staying here you've joined the 28m visitors due this year alone.   What this property offers is a calm and welcoming respite from the crowds and you'll be able to mix both if you stay here; given that no one would come and not spend time in the hubbub of the Grand Canal.   The owners have converted some very substantial older buildings and built some smaller, new ones on this island.  The main hotel is good, rooms really nice, but walk around the grounds and enjoy the more rustic views across the Lagoon.
