Had five days in Venice last week and loved everything about our trip, especially this hotel. We emailed them and explained that it was a special occasion and were upgraded to a suite which was a fantastic surprise as were the strawberries and fizz which awaited us in the room.   Staff incredibly helpful and friendly and nothing was too much trouble. Boats to San Marco ran regularly and the complimentary bottles of water and apples in the boathouse were a nice touch. Fantastic breakfasts set us up nicely for each day's sightseeing but equally, you could quite happily spend the day on the island enjoying the hotel's facilities.  The real gem is the rooftop pool and bar and there is no way you could ever get bored of the most fantastic panoramic views of Venice.
