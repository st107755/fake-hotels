My husband and I recently spent 3 nights here when we came to visit Venice. They have a very convenient free water shuttle from the San Marco dock area and the ride takes about 20 minutes to the island. It runs on time and often.  When you step off the dock area from the water taxi from the airport you should walk down to your right and look for the black sign that say JW Marriott to board their shuttle.    Our room was not ready when we arrived so we sat near the pool and enjoyed a light lunch at their grill nearby.  You can enjoy a nice view of Venice from the grounds near their spa there. One part of the resort was having a private function so one of the hotel buildings were not available.  We were in the smaller building on the grounds.
