My wife and I spent five days in April 2016 at the new J.W. Marriott Resort Hotel in Venice, Italy. The facility occupies an entire island in the Venice archipelago. It is located just 20 minutes by water taxi from St. Marks Square. The hotel provides free service every 30 minutes during the day, thereby enabling an escape from the large crowds in Venice to a relaxing time at the hotel resort in the evening.  We have travelled for many years; and, stayed at Marriott facilities all over the world.The Venice Resort is certainly in the top class of our stays. However, what impressed us the most was that although the hotel has only been operational for two years; the staff is extremely well trained, friendly and accommodating.
