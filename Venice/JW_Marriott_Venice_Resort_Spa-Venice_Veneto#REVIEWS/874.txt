First, the arrival to the property (via a private water taxi) was very nice.  The hotel assisted in making the reservations and everything did go as planned.  We were greeted by a warm staff.  The front office agent offered an upgrade (for a charge) to a suite, which we opted for.  The room was nice and clean.  Note:  the hotel is only a few years old, but I was surprised at how worn the carpets are... but everything else is fantastic.  We had a coffee to go from one of the outlets, had the lovely breakfast buffet in the first floor restaurant and a wonderful dinner with drinks in the Sagra Restaurant.  The food and drinks were excellent.  I must point out that we opted to sit at the bar and have our drinks and dinner and Franco was the bartender.
