We arrived in the midst of a wedding... to be followed by three more!  The hotel sits on an island with constant private water shuttle to and from San Marco.  It sits on acres and acres of olive groves, cultivated gardens and constant treats for your eyes or lens in the form of outdoor art displays.   The white gleaming lobby is a bit stark and doesn't do anything to evoke a sense of Venice but opts for vintage Art Deco photos and curiously cheap carpeting.     The mirrors everywhere, privacy and expansive grounds make it an ideal wedding location so if you're not part of a wedding call and make sure you understand what portions of the hotel may be closed down for private functions.
