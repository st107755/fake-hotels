This hotel is on a small island accessible by a 20 minute Marriott shuttle boat ride that runs every half hour to and from the mania that is downtown Venice! An expensive option is a private water taxi. This may be your only choice if leaving Venice by air and it will cost you a hefty 140 Euros for a 30 minute fare to the airport. The property used to be a hospital and there are several buildings comprising the campus-like atmosphere. There are pluses and minuses to this set up. You must plan your time in Venice carefully as it's not very convenient to go back and forth several times per day. On the other hand, it's nice to seclude yourself in a quiet, comfortable enclave in the evening. Our room was nice but hardly luxurious.
