We arrived at the hotel on a Friday Morning about 1000am.  Our room was not available.  We sat in the lobby for about 3 hours awaiting our room.  There was no FREE wifi, even for Marriott rewards members unless you were the highest status. Wi Fi was overpriced.  The lobby bar charged $6euros for a coke and that was the only place to purchase a beverage.  The hotel did not have a gift shop with sodas and snacks.  The room was quite large but had a horrific odor of sewage.  The water in the shower did not get hot.  We did not have bathing towels or toilet paper or regular coffee.
