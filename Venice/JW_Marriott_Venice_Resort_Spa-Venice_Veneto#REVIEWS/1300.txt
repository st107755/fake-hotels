JW Marriott Isole delle Rose is an amazing option for visiting Venice and staying away from the excessively touristy areas. The hotel is in its own private island only accessible by its private boat, a 20 minute ride from the San Marco.  I stayed here with my mom, the room was adequately sized and we also had a great balcony/terrace. The property is truly fabulous, there is beauty every corner you turn.  Bare in mind this is still a very new hotel, possibly Venice's newest, so there are some minor glitches.  While the food at the rooftop bar was good, it took nearly 40 minutes for two sandwiches. The place felt understaffed. I had to go get the check myself at the counter since I could not longer wait for it at my table.
