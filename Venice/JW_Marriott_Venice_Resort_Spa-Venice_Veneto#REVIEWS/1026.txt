My husband and I recently stayed at the JW Marriott on a short trip to Venice.  My husband had reservations about staying off the 'mainland' and that we would get fed up getting a boat backwards and forwards but this couldn't be further from reality.   On arrival at Marco Polo airport we organised a private water taxi from the information desk to take us to the hotel.  On arrival it was very busy in the reception area as there was a big party checking out but when we were seen the service was excellent, which continued throughout the holiday.  Everywhere is immaculate!  We made our way to the rooftop terrace and was greeted with fantastic views across to Venice.  Walking around the rooftop outside the restaurants is a must.  The views are spectacular.
