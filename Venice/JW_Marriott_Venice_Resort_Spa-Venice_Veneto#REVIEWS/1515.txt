Stayed here for a few nights back in May, the Hotel is almost like a retreat. Its has large grounds to walk around, a little church and a tranquil pool just past the wooded area for when you want a little peace and quiet. In addition to that it has a great bar and pool area on the top of the Hotel with a beautifully warm pool over looking the Canal. There are also daybeds up there and a really nice bar/dining area. They provide a regular water service to St Marks and back again which take around 20 minutes. Our Bedroom was very nice, it was a superior suite with a large balcony that provided a great view over the water and the gardens. It had a large bathroom, living area and walk in closet to get ready in.
