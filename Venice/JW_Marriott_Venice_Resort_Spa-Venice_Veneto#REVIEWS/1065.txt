Three friends stayed one night after a cruise.  Took the water taxi from the cruise pier to St. Marks.  The Marriott boat pickup is on the left side of St. Marks in front of the treed area. We had one/two bridges to navigate with luggage.  Watch you head on the boat!  We were early and left our luggage and returned around 6:30 PM.  We were on the 4th floor with a balcony facing Venice.   Requested twin beds and a rollaway.  The room was nice with a shower and separate tub.  After an exhausting day of Venice we checked out the property, had a drink and retired to the room.  No issues with beds, linens, water pressure, noise, etc.  The buffet breakfast was awesome with several items cooked to order.  We had a choice to dine outside or inside and chose inside.
