Just come back from the most wonderful weekend in Venice.  The location of the resort was perfect.  We were easily able to enjoy the hustle and bustle of the city and then return to the resort for some peace and tranquility.   The Island is like an oasis.  The 20 minute shuttle transfer gives you a chance to see Venice from a different perspective that others staying the city itself are not able to enjoy.  We had time to enjoy some well needed R&R around both the rooftop pool and the family pool.    The buffet breakfast was worth every penny and the food in the restaurant was superb.  The resort staff could not do enough for us.  They were all immensely knowledgeable in their roles and provided first class advice and assistance when required.
