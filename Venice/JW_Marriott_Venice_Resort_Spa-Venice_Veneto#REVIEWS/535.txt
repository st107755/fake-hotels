We visited Venice during a hot and humid trip. The JW was the perfect respite from the unbelievable crowds in Venice.   We arrived via water taxi (€80 from the train station) at the private dock. Felt like something out of a James Bond film.   There is a hotel guests only shuttle to and from San Marco that takes about 20 minute and the beautiful lagoon.   I felt like without being able to escape to the JW Marriott, Venice would have been claustrophobic to me. It was my first time visiting Venice and I could not believe how crowded it was. The narrow streets made me feel like I was in a sardine can.   The resort was beautiful and contemporary. Everything felt clean and fresh. The pools were great. We tried both the adults only pool and the family pool. This was an oasis.
