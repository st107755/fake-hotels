We stayed in La Maisonette pool suite which had a little pool in front of the room - not big enough to swim but great to relax after a day around Venice. Also used the rooftop pool on 4th floor of hotel one morning which while small was lovely and gave a nice view into Venice.  Room was great - split level with many comforts. Well designed.  Getting into Venice is pretty straightforward and free on the hotel boat which seats 39 people and runs on the dot every 30 minutes both ways. You don't need a timetable - just remember, on the hour and half hour all day from 9.00 until midnight. Takes about 20 minutes.  Ate at Cucina for breakfast - good but expensive if not included - and the rooftop restaurant which was in line with Venetian prices and high quality.
