We recently stayed at the JW Marriott while visiting Venice and celebrating a birthday. The property is absolutely beautiful with the buildings set amid gorgeous (and well-tended) gardens. After a day navigating the tight streets and crowds of Venice, you'll welcome the chance to escape back to calm of this island retreat!  Our room was in the L'Uliveto which is a smaller, building set apart from the main property. It had a more intimate feel with its own reception staff. Thank you to Rafael, Gabriele, Anna and Ieva who made us feel so welcome at check in and throughout our stay.   We enjoyed dining at the hotel for breakfast each morning before starting the day.
