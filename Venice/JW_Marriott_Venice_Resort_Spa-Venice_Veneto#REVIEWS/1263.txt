Stayed at this hotel as a part work part pleasure trip to Venice. Very grand as you arrive but also quite corporate looking in decor. The staff are all very charming and do all they can to please. Our room was very good with all you could need for a good stay including dressing gowns and slippers and good toiletries in the bathroom A nice point is the fruit bowl in your room as you arrive. Unfortunately the weather wasn't great so sitting on the balcony wasn't an option but with 3 windows you do get plenty of light in the room. Breakfast was very good with plenty of choice and you are not rushed to eat quickly and leave. Draw backs are only two restaurants which if you are a veggie cuts your choices down a bit. And they are expensive!
