We were here for 3 nights staying in a Junior Suite. The room with separate bathroom and dressing room was sumptious and we had a spacious balcony area. The room tended to get very hot and it didn't seem to want to turn down lower than 18 degrees. We were fortunate to get wonderful weather and enjoyed an afternoon at the pool but sadly it became overrun with children. The area for the pool is quite small and I wonder if it would be a better idea to keep that for adults as people were drinking alcohol in glasses (not plastic ones) which could potentially be dangerous and it was not particularly relaxing with lots of children milling around. A separate family pool area would seem to be appropriate where children can relax in a larger area.
