Highly recommended! Beautiful place in all aspects, a true aesthetic experience. And a real relief from the crowds in Venice... Shuttle service to and from San Marco square is efficient and takes only twenty minutes each way. Excellent service and delicious food (although a bit expensive) in all three hotel restaurants that we had a chance to eat in (Dopo Lavoro, Giardino and Sagra). Breakfast at Cucina is also worth it. Don't miss the infinity pool at the roof top, you will get incredible views. By the way, the hotel has the infinity pool with a quite and exclusive experience (adult-oriented) and a separate one in the gardens for families and kids (with lots of activities for kids to have fun). Very convenient!
