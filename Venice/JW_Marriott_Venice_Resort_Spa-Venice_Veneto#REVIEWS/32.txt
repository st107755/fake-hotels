I had my reservations staying in an isolated island hotel during my first time in Venice, but the 20-30 minute shuttle ride really didn't make much of a difference. There is a shuttle that runs every 30 minutes from San Marco square to the resort and vise versa. The property is beautiful and quiet, and the people who works there are very professional and courteous. I had emailed the property prior to our arrival to reserve a water taxi upon our arrival, and our ride was expecting us when we landed. There is an amazing view of the city from the rooftop infinity pool. There is also a charming little park surrounded by olive trees where I enjoyed running laps around.
