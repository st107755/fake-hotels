We spent 5 nights here on our first trip to Venice.  It really is a beautiful hotel complete with really nice grounds.    We flew into Treviso and took a taxi to Piazzale Roma which cost €75, then a water taxi direct to the hotel which cost €80.  Just watch taxis back to the airport - return journey was €100, not sure if this is the norm or we were had!  In my opinion the perfect hotel if you plan on spending a slightly longer time in Venice than just weekend as you have the luxury of space, two swimming pools, nice gardens and a very convenient shuttle service into Venice every 30 minutes.    The shuttle service was very efficient and always ran on time, although it can be very busy at peak times on weekends so get to the dock early if you want to guarantee your space!
