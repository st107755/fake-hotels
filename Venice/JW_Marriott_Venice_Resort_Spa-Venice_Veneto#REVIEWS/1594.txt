We stayed at this hotel for 1 night and absolutely loved the luxurious grounds - the rooftop pool, family pool, fitness center, views of the city, walking around the island itself; everything has been impeccably done. We upgraded to a suite, which I highly recommend - we had our own private deck looking at the church and surrounded by flowers. The rooms are luxury at its finest.   If arriving in Venice by train, can either take the public boat shuttle (7 euro per person) to St. Marks, where the JW has a complimentary shuttle to the hotel. We attempted to do this, but the crowds and wait for the public boat shuttle were too much - thus opted for the 70 euro taxi from the train station directly to the hotel.
