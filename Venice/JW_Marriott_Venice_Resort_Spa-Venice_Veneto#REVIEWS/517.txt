Spent ~ 3 days at this Hotel.   It is on an actual island ~ 10 minutes by boat (free shuttle till midnight) to Venice (drop off near Dodge's palace).  The resort is pretty spread out and the rooms are very large by Italian standards (this was a pleasant surprise).   Check in was a bit long as hotel was a bit busy on day of arrival.   The breakfast was great!!  Resort grounds were very beautiful / well maintained.  Stayed w/ little kids and felt safe to have them run around on the grounds and not worry about cars etc.- only had to watch out for the occasional golf cart / bicycle.  Did not do much on the resort but saw families w/ bigger kids exploring with bikes.  This is not the hotel if you want to be in the center of hustle / bustle of Venice street scene 24/7.
