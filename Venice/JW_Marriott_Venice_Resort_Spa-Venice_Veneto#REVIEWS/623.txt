We (me, husband and son) stayed at the JW Marriott Venice for a week in June. World class service from all the staff from the moment our water taxi pulled up to the dock until we left. The rooftop pool is a wonderful retreat after a busy day touring Venice itself. The rooftop Sagra restaurant was fantastic - incredible view, very relaxing and excellent service - we ate there several times. Our waiter Frederico was attentive and accomodating, and Guiseppe - who I believe is the floor manager - made sure that each evening was absolutely delightful - he engaged all the customers, nothing was a problem and clearly he was enthusiastic about the food. The pasta della Nonna was a favorite of my husbands - and Guiseppe said it's just like that of his grandmother's.
