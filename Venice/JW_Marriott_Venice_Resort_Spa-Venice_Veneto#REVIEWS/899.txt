Just recently returned from the JW Marriott in Venice on the private island Isla de Rosa. We took the public water transit going over and connected with the Marriott shuttle they offer every half hour. It was a bit long in comparison to the water taxi we took when we checked out two days later but fun to go slowly down the grand canal to get a feel for the city and take a few pics. The hotel offered us an upgrade since we are gold elite one for no added cost and one for an additional 100.00 euros. We actually did the euro upgrade but not sure it was necessary since we didn't spend much time in our room. It was very nice though. We ate at the restaurant up top by the pool the first night since we arrived kinda late and it was good.
