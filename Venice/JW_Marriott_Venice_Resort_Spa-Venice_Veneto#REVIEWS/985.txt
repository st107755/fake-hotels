From the moment we stepped on this beautiful island of Isola Delle Rose which belongs to JW Marriott , we knew that we were going to stay at a very special hotel. From check in to check out the service was superb & flawless! Well almost. During check in, even though had already done via the Marriott App for 11.00am, the room was not ready until 14.30pm. Long wait but was well worth in the end! The Junior Suite was huge with wonderful high ceilings & with top class amenities. The rooftop swimming pool has the most stunning views we have ever come across. You can swim & see almost whole of Venice form here. Wow! Buffett breakfast was very good as well. We found the shuttle service very good & operates every 30 minutes until 12.30am!
