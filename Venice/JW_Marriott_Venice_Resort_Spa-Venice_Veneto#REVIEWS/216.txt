Before you even arrive, the service at the JW Marriott Venice begins with contact from the concierge with details related to the hotel, transportation options from the airport and dining options on property as well as any planned special events.  We arrange for a private water taxi upon arrival at the suggestion of the concierge and it made for a perfect arrival experience.  It is a 45 min boat ride from Marco Polo, but with fresh air, sunshine and a view of the various islands, every minute is enjoyable.  When you arrive at Isola delle Rose, you enter the boat cabana as if you are an old time movie star or traveling with James Bond.  It's fantastic.
