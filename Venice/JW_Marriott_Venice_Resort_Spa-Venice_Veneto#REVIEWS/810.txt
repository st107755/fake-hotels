This hotel by far was one of the most captivating and beautiful hotels I've stayed at. They have a free private shuttle that takes only guests to and from the hotel. It's about a 20 minute ride from San Marco to the hotel but the view from the boat is incredible. As soon as we stepped on the island it was an impressive experience. The employees at the hotel were very friendly and helpful with any questions we had. They even had a golf cart to take us to our room. We didn't take advantage of them but they had bikes you could use to ride around the property. I would recommend going to the rooftop bar for sunset or sunrise, you can see San Marco and most of Venice. It was one of our best pictures/experiences of the stay. The room was great and very quiet and private.
