My boyfriend and I stayed here while traveling through Italy. I'm a frequent Marriott patron and we were able to stay here completely on points, which was awesome (and free). We were greeted at the dock and a valet took our bags and had them brought to our room. Check in was simple and someone showed us up to our room. We had a HUGE corner room with a beautiful view of the lagoon and Venice. We quickly migrated up to the adults only pool, which was so quiet and relaxing. Drinks from the bar will run you about 15 euro a piece, which was ridiculously expensive. But the bellini was incredible! We ended up purchasing a bottle of vodka while in Venice the next day and just drank in our room for much cheaper.
