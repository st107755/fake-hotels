Visited with my husband for our first wedding anniversary and I am so glad we made the choice of Venice and this hotel. We took the boat from the pier at San Marco it was very easy to find. The boat took about 15 minutes to get to the island. Being away from the main island was great for us as made the evening more relaxing. As soon as we arrived the staff could not do enough for us to make our experience perfect. The breakfast at the hotel is delicious and eggs can be made fresh on request. One evening we had dinner at the roof top restaurant I would recommend booking here. The hotel also boasts a wonderful spa that is state of the art and an art gallery.
