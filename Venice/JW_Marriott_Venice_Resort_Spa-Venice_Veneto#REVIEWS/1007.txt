The location is beautiful, the view of Venice from the pool unbeatable. However, I was glad we only booked one night. Staff is not up to Marriott standards at all. We waited almost an hour for our suitcases to arrive, had to call in the end to get them delivered. We managed to get chairs at the pool and enjoyed a great lunch near the pool. I was advised to book a table for dinner so I did. When we arrived for dinner we were seated next to 4 guests who clearly had too much to drink, even though there were plenty of other tables availability. It was very uncomfortable and we finished dinner in 45 minutes. Service during dinner was poor and even though I emailed the manager as soon as I got back to the room, the issue was never addressed.
