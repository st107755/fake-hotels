New stylish hotel on its own island. Shuttle service is great and on time (takes about +-15min from San Marco to the hotel - at night a bit longer as it takes a different route).   The island itself is quite big and can be used for a short jog/run. Great fitness room although its in a separate building a bit away from the hotel. Free bottled water everywhere on the island (room, fitness center, shuttles waiting area). They also have a table with candy near the reception (liquorish and gummibear-like sweets)! Breakfast included with hot and cold items (and eggs/omelets to order).  They have 2 pools which was a nice thing during the midday heat. They are a bit on the small side but the rooftop pool has the greatest view of Venice. It also has a jacuzzi function which was nice.
