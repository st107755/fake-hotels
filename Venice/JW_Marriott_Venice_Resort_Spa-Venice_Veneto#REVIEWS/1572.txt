We booked this hotel for a short break via a Travelzoo deal.  We did find the check in process a bit ponderous however once we got to the point of checking in we were very happy to learn that the hotel had upgraded us to a loft suite. This was a pleasant surprise and we were even more impressed with the suite.  We particularly liked the personal show round of the property and the escort to the room. We, like other reviewers experienced air conditioning problems however a technician responded very quickly and it turned out our windows were open a very small amount. We had not realised that one of the myriad of switches in the room operated electric windows.
