We had a wonderful stay at this fabulous hotel. We had been planning the stay for some to celebrate my partners 40th Birthday. It was a rare few days away without the children- though they would have loved the hotel!  The hotel grounds and facilities are really good and as expected. I agree with other comments that the rooftop pool is quite small and very difficult to get a bed by the pool on a sunny day. That said- there is a lovely large family pool which we used on our second day. The food was of a high standard, though you do need to be culinary liberal to enjoy the many raw dishes ( i.e. The lobster salad). We ate at both the rooftop restaurant and the Michelin starred restaurant- and both were excellent. The breakfast was also of a high standard.
