We spent 5 nights in July at the JW Venice. We had booked a junior suite with breakfast included and had a great stay.  As Gold Elite members, we were not upgraded at check-in, but I already knew that the chances were very slim since the hotel was full. The junior suite was very roomy, a large bathroom, and a very large terrace, where we enjoyed a glass of prosecco every evening listening to the cicadas.  The shuttle to and from St-Marco is very easy. The boat is always on time, and there was always plenty of room for everyone. It was like being on your private island, a beautiful island and a luxurious hotel. We much preferred the tranquility and extensive gardens on Isola Delle Rose to the frenzy of Venice.
