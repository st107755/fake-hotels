Having been to beautiful Venice before I thought this island holiday would be a nice surprise for my wife's 70th. We were not dissappointed it's a great experience to spend time exploring Venice city and then being whisked away to the wonderful peace and quiet of Isla del Rosa island. The hotel although quite large compared to other Venice hotels is superb. The staff are amazing and the grounds are peaceful and beautiful. We had a fabulous junior suite in the third floor in the main building with great views. Although it was mid April the larger pool in the grounds was really warm and very quiet. The roof top pool can get a little busy at times but has unbelievable views toward the city.
