I do not regret having spent 120,000 Marriott points on 3 nights at the JW Marriott Resort and Spa in Venice, Italy.  The standard room we booked costs around $400/night in the summer.  This resort is like no other, in that it is is situated on its own private island (Isola della Rossa), which is a 25 minute boat ride to and from St. Mark's Square in Venice.  The feature of this property that sets it apart from other hotels in Venice is its retreat-like quality.  After spending a hot summer day in Venice touring the Doge's Palace, riding the gondolas, or fighting the crowds in St. Mark's Square, the JW provides a quiet atmosphere that would appeal to both families and couples.
