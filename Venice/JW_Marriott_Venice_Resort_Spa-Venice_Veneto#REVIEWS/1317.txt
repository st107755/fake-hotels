My first review of a hotel online and this hotel certainly deserves it. I was sceptical at first given the reviews from its first few months of opening but decided to book because of the complimentary shuttle service, no extra supplements for one child sharing and a good deal on secret escapes.  The service was excellent from pre-arrival arrangements for the private water taxi to the fastest & most efficient checkout I've ever experienced.   We upgraded to a junior suite for a very modest rate - worth every penny and during our stay there was a free minibar promotion that we didn't even use.  It's worth mentioning again how friendly all the staff were and how warmly they greeted my 5 year old niece.
