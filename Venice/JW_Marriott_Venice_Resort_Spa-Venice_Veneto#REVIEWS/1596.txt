The new JW Marriott resort in Venice had been open only 2 months when my wife and I visited for 3 nights at the end of May 2015. Our impression was very positive. We took a private water taxi transfer from the airport (€230 return booked with VeniceLink). This may seem a bit expensive to get to and from the airport, but it is very enjoyable, and we thought well worth the money. It takes about 35 minutes. You arrive at the dock at the hotel, and your luggage is collected for delivery to your room, and you are welcomed and accompanied to reception. Check in is very welcoming, and we were brought glasses of iced water.
