This is the place to stay when visiting Venice.   Don't bother with the cramped city hotels when you can stay here by the pool and go to Venice by boat (free of charge) whenever you want. The journey is about 10-15 minutes  and goes every 30 minutes. Arriving by boat is like being in a James Bond movie because it docks inside the hotel.  The rooms are beautiful (junior suite) with balcony and lounge area. The style is impeccably modern. The staff were warm and welcoming and incredibly attentive, a man by the pool asked if i'd like my sunglasses cleaned (thats a first) and you are offered ice cold flannels every hour.  There is lots of room round the pool even in busy times so you don't need to sit near anyone else.
