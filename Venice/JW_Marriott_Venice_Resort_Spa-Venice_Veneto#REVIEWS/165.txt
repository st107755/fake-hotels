My wife and I enjoyed four days of our 30th wedding anniversary trip at the JW Marriott in Venice. Before arriving we were a little concerned that it was located on it’s own island, however their regular running complementary water taxi actually added to the experience.  The property itself is stunning. Everything you would expect from a JW Marriott. First class all the way.  We too contracted through the concierge to be picked up at the airport and have a private water taxi bring us to the hotel. While you can do this on your own without much trouble, being greeted at the airport and shuttled to a gorgeous teak wood boat with your own driver was a magnificent start to our trip.
