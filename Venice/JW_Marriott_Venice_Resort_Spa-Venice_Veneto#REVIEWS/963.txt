I spent 3 fantastic nights at this hotel last week with a friend. I have to admit before flying out for my short trip, I was a tad bit apprehensive about having to grab a water shuttle every time I wanted to head into Venice and returning once I was done exploring, but, you really don't notice it if you plan when you grab the shuttle. I think later in the evening the shuttles can get away with travelling quicker within the lagoon.
