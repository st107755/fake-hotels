My family of 4 decided to visit Venice for a few days.  Of the four of us, I was the only person who had toured Venice so when we considered hotels, I was in favor of staying in one of the historic hotels in Venice proper and focus on touring the city sights.  My family convinced me to stay at the JW Marriott because it offers not only easy access to the city via water shuttles that leave every 30 minutes like clockwork but also a roof top pool, fitness facility and a new, relaxing environment away from the the Venice crowds.  Marriott did a nice job converting what was once a facility dedicated to restoring respiratory health to a 5 star hotel.  I am glad they convinced me to stay the Marriott.
