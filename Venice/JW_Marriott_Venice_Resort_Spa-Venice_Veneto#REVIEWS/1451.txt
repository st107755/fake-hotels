There have been many reviews that have summed this hotel/resort up nicely, both good and bad.  Our experience was incredibly positive, but I understand why some have expressed concern.  The staff were all very nice; although the housekeeping staff are not what you expect from a 5 star hotel. The breakfast was first class, for service, quality & choice. The pool is excellent; although not large and as others have pointed out, it didn't come with many lounges  We experienced no problems with the shuttle to San Marco, but did speak to some people who were turned away, because they were full. (they seat about 40)  Before we left, I thought the isolation from Venice itself might be bothersome.....it wasn't, it was a blessing to be away from the craziness.
