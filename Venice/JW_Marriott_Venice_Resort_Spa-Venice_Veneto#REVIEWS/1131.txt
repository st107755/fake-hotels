I stayed at this hotel using Marriott points and received an upgrade to a villa outside of the main hotel building.  My room was beautiful with a super comfy bed and luxurious linens.  I was disappointed that I did not have a sea view.  The room did have a patio area, but the greenery was a bit overgrown and buggy with lizards, so I did not spend any time there.  The breakfast buffet was pretty good, but even with Marriott Gold, it is not complimentary (although after complaining, I did receive mine for no charge.)  The rooftop pool on the main building is beautiful with wonderful views of Venice.  It is a bit difficult getting to and from Venice from the hotel, but they provide a complimentary boat shuttle that runs throughout the day and into the evening.
