We stayed at the JW Venice for 3 nights while we were in Venice.  The hotel is located on a private island that's a 20 min boat ride to Venice. The hotel offers a shuttle service that runs every half hour from both the hotel to Venice and from Venice back to the hotel. The shuttle docks at San Marco right by the palace and San Marco Basilica. It also runs until 12:30 AM, so it's super convenient.   The staff is also very friendly and helpful. Romeo and Fabio were especially helpful in showing us around the island and helping with recommendations in Venice.  The island itself is very beautiful. There is a garden of olive trees, a church, and the entire island is full of flowers (Jasmine, Roses, etc). There are also spa services and a gym on the island.
