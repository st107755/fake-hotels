It was a 6 day trip to see all the sights in Venice.  The hotel certainly helped. Located on a nearby island, access to and from the City is by Hotel shuttle.  One every half hour each way, and taking around 20 minutes.  Very comfortable and a good place to chat to other guests.  The rest of the island is laid out as hotel grounds where one can always walk, swim, or visit the restaurants and Spa. The welcome was first class.  All the staff were very friendly and keen to please.  The level of service was also excellent. The room was very comfortable, but there were occasionally some strange gurgling sounds coming from the ceiling area of the room at various times, a matter of which the Hotel Management was 'aware'. The breakfasts were good.
