We booked this hotel as our first stop on our tour of Italy with 2 kids aged 9 and 11. When you land you can take a private water taxi for about 140 euros or you can take the shuttle taxi to San Marco and then the free shuttle from San Marco to the actual hotels private island. We opted for the private water taxi as we landed quite late. You are dropped off at the dock entrance right at the hotel.  From the second we arrived to the moment we left, the staff was amazing. Everyone says hello to you and is more than willing to help with any request. Alexandru specifically in the Rose Bar went out of his way to answer our questions and help us with Italy tips. He was an absolute gem! The grounds are amazing. Our balcony opened to the harmony of crickets every am.
