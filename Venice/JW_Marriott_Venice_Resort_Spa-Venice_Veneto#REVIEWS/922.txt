This is a stylish well laid out resort hotel, an oasis from the hordes crowding out Venice in mid summer. The hotel runs its own water taxi service twice an hour from San Marco and the journey is about 20 minutes.  There are excellent facilities in the complex from a kids' pool to a well equipped gym and spa. Restaurants abound from the grill bar, and also a Michelin option, in the grounds to an open rooftop location next to the main pool overlooking Venice in the distance. The staff were uniformly attentive and excellent in every way from reception to concierge to restaurants.   Our room was functional, clean and modern but it lacked obvious shelf storage spaces at bedside and in the bathroom. Strange.
