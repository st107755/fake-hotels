We stopped here at the weekend for 3 nights with our 15 month old. Not the easiest of places to get to but once there it is worth it!!! We took a public water ferry to St marks square but would advise taking a private taxi as it took us 1 hr 30 mins to get to st marks square then the 20 minute private shuttle to the hotel. When we got to the hotel we were greeted really well by the staff who could not do enough for us. We were upgraded to La Residenza part of the hotel which words could nt describe how good our room, view and back garden area was !!! It was a different world !!! Food and drink was expensive but it is expected in a 5 star resort.
