My husband and I spent a few nights here last week. The resort is new, and it shows. The grounds are beautiful and meticulously maintained. The service is incredible. Gabriele and Flavia at the Dispensa are ready to make your morning or afternoon special with an espresso and a snack! We also took the pizza and beer cooking class with Chef Micki. It was a wonderful way to spend an afternoon learning the secrets of Italian bread! He was fun, relaxed, and we learned a lot!  Our only complaint about the hotel is its inaccessibility. We stayed there because we had hotel points, but I wouldn't recommend it for people looking to be close to Venice. A shuttle runs every 30 minutes between 9am and 1am. It takes about 25 minutes to get to St. Mark's Square.
