This review is longer than normal as the Marriott Venice is a new hotel and there is a lot to talk about. My wife and I had been considering revisiting Venice for a while and when a very attractive, excellent value, opportunity arose to revisit the city arose we took it at once and booked in at the 5 star Marriott, which had once been a hospital for chest complaints but has been completely renovated and many other accommodation bocks built on an island. The Marriott is unusual in that it is sited on an island all to itself in the Venetian lagoon, the Isola delle Rosa. The location of the hotel has many benefits but it does mean that it is more difficult to get to that other hotels in the city.
