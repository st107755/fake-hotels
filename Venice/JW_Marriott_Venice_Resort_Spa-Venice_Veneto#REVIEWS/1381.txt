This hotel is located on its own private Island and takes approx 30mins by water taxi from the airport to the hotel which I organised through the hotel which cost 120 euros. It was well worth the expense compared to using the public transport. The private water taxi enterance was special. Staff met us and assisted with our luggage as we checked in. Our room a Junior Suite with a large balcony with garden views and very quiet. I had previously read on Trip Advisor that some guests complained about the air conditioning at the hotel. Venice during our stay reach 32deg and our room was lovely and cool and the rest of the hotel was comfortable.
