We stayed prior to joining a cruise. Approaching the entrance via water taxiing a very different way to start your visit. The hotel is a 20 minute boat ride from Venice. There are frequent free ferries to Venice and back, dropping you near St Marks. This adds to its charm but also reduces the time you have to explore Venice itself if you have limited time available. The hotel is very modern, with public areas very attractive and very good breakfasts. Free wifi in the public areas but expensive in the rooms. There are a couple of main restaurants including one with a Michelin star (Dopolavoro Dining Room) which I heard was excellent but I did not eat there. Food at the non starred Sagra restaurant on the roof top was very good, but pricey.
