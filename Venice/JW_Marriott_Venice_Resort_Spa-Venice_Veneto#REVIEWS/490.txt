Being located on a separate island, the hotel is much quieter than the craze of Venice. This is good as long as you don't mind a 20-minute boat trip every time you head out. The grounds are huge but a little empty. Strange that the pool is so far from the hotel. The key drivers of this rating are the fact the staff, although always very nice, were very trained to upsell you with each conversation. It was really over the top. Plus, even though I was well aware that it would not be a bargain, the level of prices for meals and extras in the hotel were way over the top. $25 for a salad, $15 for a beer - hard to justify. And the meal came with the extra of a large cover charge.
