The J W Marriot Venice Resort and Spa opened in March and was still handling some "shakedown" issues such as a non working elevator for 3 days and lack of air conditioning in the main hotel one night. Overall we enjoyed our 5 day stay on Reward Points very much. The 400+ acre grounds are oustanding with many creative modern outdoor sculptures. The shuttle launch which goes to San Marco Square maintained a convenient and dependable schedule throughout the day and evening. The 20 minute return ride to the hotel was a welcome transition from the heat and crowds of Venice. Our standard room was somewhat smaller than expected, but did have a split bathroom with shower and double sink in one and toilet sink and bidet in the other.
