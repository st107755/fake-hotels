A interesting choice for Venice.    On the plus side the Hotel is located on an island that is a 20 minute shuttle (free) from San Marco square so you can enjoy a break from the crowds of Venice.    The downside of course is you have to plan your travel into the city.    For a family with kids and teenagers it's a good tradeoff.    The hotel is expansive with an adult pool and restaurant overlooking the city, and excellent family pool with a small restaurant and and bar adjacent.      There is a fabulous fitness center and Michelin star restaurant in it's own building.    The property is contemporary - very little distinguishing about it relative to its location.
