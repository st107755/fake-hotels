I love Venice but it can be overwhelming with the crowds. A short boat ride away is Isola delle Rose and the JW Marriott. Plenty of space to walk and enjoy the outdoors, allowing one to catch a break from the crowds of people. The rooms are much better than those I have experienced in Venice, quite comfortable with all of the amenities of a 5-star hotel.  The service and staff are outstanding! Perhaps the best service and friendliest staff in all of Venice. The free shuttle runs from 8:30 am to 12:30 pm to provide one plenty of opportunity to enjoy shopping, sightseeing, and food around Venice. On property there are multiple restaurants and bar locations, including a Michelin star restaurant, and a rooftop restaurant/bar.
