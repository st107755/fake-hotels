After landing in Venice, we secured a private water taxi from the Marco Polo Airport to the JW Marriott. This is definitely worth the additional spend. After 30 minutes of viewing the outer areas of Venice and then a quick jaunt through one of the neighborhoods, we could see the San Marco Square skyline. The JW sits about 15-20mins off Venice on it's own private island, do not call it small. Think James Bond as you pull into their private docking area, checkin was superb, the staff was amazing. There is the main hotel with several smaller hotels (neighborhoods) around the property; all a quick walk. Our room, a little small, great bathroom, came with it's own private patio with garden. Very nice gym, great spa, and the restaurants were all amazing.
