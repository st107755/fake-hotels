This is a very beautiful hotel...what makes it so exceptional, though, is that it is on an island just 5 minutes from Piazza de San Marco (with service every 15 minutes 24/7).  It is literally an island of calm among the craziness of Venice.  We had a beautiful suite (upgraded from a Jr. Suite) with a lovely view of the sea.  The hotel has a huge, beautiful garden and an enormous pool (apparently the builder mistook the "feet" for "meters".  The service is excellent...especially the concierge and the people who man the boat dock.  Very expensive..but worth it to be so close from Venice...and yet so far away.
