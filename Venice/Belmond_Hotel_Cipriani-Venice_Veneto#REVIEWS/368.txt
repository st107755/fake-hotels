We love to holiday in Europe and the States but this was our first time in Venice. A good friend 'in the know' suggested the Cipriani above the rest and we took her advice. We journeyed in on the Orient Express and arrived early evening. We stayed 3 nights and had the most incredible time. We were upgraded, without asking and placed in a lagoon view suite. This place is amazing in every respect and the staff cannot do enough for you. We dined at the Cip's Club each night and the view across to St Marc's square was something to behold. We shall definitely be returning and next time take in the beautiful pool and its surrounding gardens. Without doubt this is the place to stay in Venice with their own motor launch to take you across to St Marc's Square whenever you need.
