This really is a wonderful hotel and highly recommended. The setting is superb, being immediately across the water from St Mark's. The hotel has its own regular ferry service to and from St Mark's. The service is discrete yet attentive. Breakfast was excellent. Our room was extremely comfortable. If you want to spend a morning away from the crowds, you will find the pool large and the pool area well supplied with good loungers and a plentiful supply of towels. My tip: do not eat out unless you have had several independent recommendations about the proposed restaurant. The one flaw in our holiday was when we ate in a "Venetian"  restaurant recommended by the hotel. It was terrible.
