Superior service in a legendary hotel.  Amazing staff and attention to your needs.  Location is a beautiful get away - just a short boat ride from Venice.  Do not be discouraged by it if you have not stayed here before - it is no inconvenience.  Room in our experience was not up to the standard we anticipated and perhaps parts of the hotel need some refreshing - some of this is personal taste and some of this is the hard work all hotels have to constantly undertake in order to stay relevant.  However, as regular travellers, we felt Cipriani is further behind the average on the curve.    Other factors compensate for this.  Cip’s and Oro were great and we enjoyed Walter’s company at the bar!
