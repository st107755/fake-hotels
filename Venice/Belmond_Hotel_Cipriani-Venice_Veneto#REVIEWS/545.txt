This hotel is breathtakingly beautiful, the rooms are the height of luxury, no expense has been spared and we loved and appreciated all the local glass, the decor and all the extra touches that made each area special. The pool is warm and inviting, the gardens well kept and beautiful and the breakfasts are the best in the world. But, we got the impression that the level of service is directly linked to the size of your tip. This was a holiday for our retirement which we had planned and saved for for many years. We aren't rich or famous and can only tip with €5 not €50 and felt we were treated accordingly. We decided to really push the boat out on the first day and book a meal at Cips restaurant.
