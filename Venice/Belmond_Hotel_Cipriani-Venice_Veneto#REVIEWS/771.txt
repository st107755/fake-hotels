We recently spent three nights at the Hotel Ciprini and had a fabulous time there.     5 star hotels often have staff that provide an ‘us and them’ feeling but the Cipriani is different – you are made to feel part of their family and yet the high standard of service is maintained throughout.  The room was of a high standard with beautiful flowers and a welcoming bottle of Prosecco provided on arrival.  The breakfast provided was excellent with friendly waiting staff and we even had the pleasure of eating outside on our last day as the sun was shining.  We also had an evening meal in the hotel’s Cip’s Club restaurant and their Risotto was one of the finest we have tasted.
