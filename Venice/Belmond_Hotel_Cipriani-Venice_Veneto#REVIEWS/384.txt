We stayed for three nights after travelling from London on the Orient Express.  The Cipriani was a wonderful experience: everything is done so well and the staff took the greatest care of us.  We ate in all three restaurants and they are all excellent in different ways.  A special trolley just for different types of olive oil at the Oro was impressive!  The restaurant beside the pool is great for pasta or pizza on a lazy day and the Cip's Club was great fun in the evening and has a really wonderful view.  The pool area is a lot of fun: immaculately kept and attended by lovely staff who make sure you have everything you need and bring the occasional sorbet to keep everyone cool.
