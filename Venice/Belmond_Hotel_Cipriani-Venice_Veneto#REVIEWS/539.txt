Upon arriving at Hotel Cipriani we were a little worried we were a little too out the way for a city break and we might loose some of the experience. When we took the hotels boat (runs 24 hours a day for free) to St Marks sq we arrived and the place is madness, it's packed with tourists and you have to battle your way everywhere. When you return to Hotel Cipriani with it's beautiful gardens and tranquil setting it really is a beautiful place to recharge and take in the true beauty of the area.
