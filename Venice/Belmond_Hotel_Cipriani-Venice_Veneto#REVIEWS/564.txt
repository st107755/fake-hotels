The Cipriani is located on the island of Guidecca, just a short boat ride from San Marco (Piazza San Marco).  The grounds of the hotel are absolutely stunning and the hotel is right on the water's edge so you can see all sorts of boats coming and going.  The hotel itself is lovely and we stayed in a room that had a flower enclosed balcony where we could sit and enjoy a lovely glass of wine and read!   The only big negative and the reason that this lovely place receives only 3 stars is that it was chronically understaffed when we were there.  If you went into a bar there were maybe 2 staff attending to several customers, resulting in waiting for service.
