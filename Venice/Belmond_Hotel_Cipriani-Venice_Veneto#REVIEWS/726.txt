We stayed here for our honeymoon after taking the Orient Express to Venice.  Although only here for two nights it was quite magical.  We had booked a double garden view room but when we arrived late at night we were given the most beautiful suite overlooking the lagoon complete with chocolate heart shaped  cake, pink champagne and a lovely note from the manager wishing us a romantic honeymoon.  The service was second to none and nothing was too much trouble for the staff.  We enjoyed a full cooked breakfast in our room both mornings we stayed and enjoyed cocktails and coffee in the pool bar of an evening.  Although we did not eat at the hotel we heard from other guests that it was second to none.
