If you have seen our other reviews, you will know that we have been lucky enough to enjoy high end corporate and personal travel over recent years. Bearing that in mind, you will appreciate how good this place is, when I say it was possibly the most perfect short stay we have ever had.  From being met at the airport and privately escorted to the hotel by water taxi to the beautiful suite, everything was polished and classy. Our favourite memory of this hotel however was taking a wonderful breakfast overlooking the canal with the sun glistening off the water. Tranquility and peace of mind. Idyllic. Must return.
