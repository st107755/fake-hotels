We decided to splash out and stay at the cipriani when booking our trip on the Orient Express from London to Venice - so pleased that we did.  It totally lived up to our expectations.  We arrived a bit late as there were a few problems with the train.  We were shown to our rooms which were splendid.  We had been upgraded along with our friends to rooms looking over the lagoon.  They were very large and beautifully furnished.  Breakfast was just lovely every morning. Tabled are laid out on the Terrace overlooking the lagoon and the spread of food is amazing. What is so great about this hotel is the enormous swimming pool (apparently the only one in Venice).  The hotel gardens are immense and you feel like you are in the countryside.
