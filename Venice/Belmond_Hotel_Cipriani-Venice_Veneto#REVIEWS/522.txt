This was our second visit to Hotel Cipriani. We were last there in 1999 to celebrate our Silver Wedding; this time we went back for four nights to celebrate our Ruby Wedding anniversary. It was stunning then and it was just as magnificent this time.  From beginning to end it was perfect. The jasmine-scented garden, our room and bathroom, the service, the concierge and the housekeeping were all faultless. Two minutes after being shown to our gorgeous lagoon-view room a waiter arrived with a complimentary bottle of Prosecco, a congratulations card and two beautiful heart-shaped desserts. A small touch which shows this amazing hotel's attention to detail.
