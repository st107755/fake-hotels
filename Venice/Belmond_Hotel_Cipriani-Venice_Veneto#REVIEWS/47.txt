The Belmond Cipriani is one-its-kind. Impeccable service from the moment you fly in until you leave. Professionnal and personalized, the staff is very attentive and kind. Rooms are generously sized, appointed with antiques and have nice bathroom amenities. Restaurants offer a wide range of meal types from a Michelin-starred dinner to a casual lunch by the pool. Pool is the most generous of Venice.  The location of the Belmond is to mention as well. Located on Giudecca island (facing San Marco), it has great views of the city, is outside of the main tourist areas (which is a blessing!) without being geographically far from the main attractions either especially considering that the private boat takes guests back and forth 24/7 to the main island.
