Stayed at a lot of luxury classic hotels and modern new ones- not always do they live up to their exalted reputations or reviews- and rarely do they surpass expectations- can count them on one hand- where everything, seemingly everything just clicks. Add Cipriani to that short list of places you must stay. Suffice it to say this is the place to stay in Venice hands down. Sure could stay somewhere posh on the Grand Canal like the Aman, but you wouldn't have the private boat and view surrounding San Marco every time you come and go, nor the gardens or the pool or the peace of mind being out of the tourist hub bub otherwise. It is like you're on your own island but just outside of the central areas, and if you like a walk down Guidecca to the Fortuny showroom is lovely too.
