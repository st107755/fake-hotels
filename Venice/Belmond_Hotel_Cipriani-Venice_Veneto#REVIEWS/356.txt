The Belmond Cipriani has the notorious distinction of being one of the most expensive hotels in the world.  It is worth every penny! From the personal welcome as we stepped out of the water taxi on arrival, the daily delivery of breakfast on our balcony overlooking the lagoon, to the perfect recommendations by the concierge staff-- this place is absolutely stunning. Naturally, the room was spacious and bright, with an enormous bathroom and excellent quality linens and robes. We did not dine in the formal restaurant, opting rather for the more casual Cip's club, with its deck right on the water.  Always, the waitstaff was highly attentive, and the food was superb.
