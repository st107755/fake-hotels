We stayed here recently for 2 complimentary nights before travelling on the Orient Express. This hotel has a reputation as one of the best in Venice frequented by celebrities etc. The room we stayed in looked like an older room as I believe some have been refurbished. The room was spacious but looked very tired and in need of updating. I appreciate that it was the cheapest room tariff but still expensive for what it was. You do get lovely toiletries etc but I think the hotels best point is where it is. Away from the crowds with free boat to shuttle you back and forth, a large swimming pool and attractive gardens. This is what made this hotel special, not the room.
