My wife and I have been regulars at the Cipriani for many years and have always enjoyed the hotel and the highly personalized service one experiences there. However until this Easter when we have traveled to Venice with our children (three under ten) we have stayed elsewhere as we found the Cipriani unwelcoming to young children. Since the change in management, all has changed and the Cipriani has become the exemplar of a child-friendly hotel. I never thought I would be able to take my children to dine in the Fortuny Restaurant for dinner but this year we did. All in all the experience has been transformed and the chintzy  room designs of the past consigned to history.
