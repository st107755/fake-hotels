Like another reviewer we had always wanted to try this property and finally stayed here recently to celebrate our wedding anniversary. I have to say it lived up to the hype and it was more or less as we expected it to be - prices included. The location is stunning and having breakfast and lunch by the water every day was wonderful. Food and wine were as expected, eye wateringly expensive but very good. We thought the food in Cips particularly good. The staff were professional and pleasant in all the restaurants. The famous pool didn't disappoint and it was nice to get the little treats of homemade ice lollies and fruit etc. The only fly in the ointment was the mosquito problem (sorry!) - they didn't just come out at dusk but struck during the day.
