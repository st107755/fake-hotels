My Wife & I are fortunate to have stayed at a number of Belmond hotels over the last few years and all have been excellent. The Cipriani is no exception and possibly stands out.  It is an icon of charm and elegance located in the tranquility of Giudecca just a few minutes in your complimentary personal taxi from the bustle, noise and queues of the great city across the water. It’s classy. There’s an air of understated competence here.  There was not one member of staff - whatever their station - who could not be applauded for their expertise and the friendly way in which they dealt with our needs. Our room was true Italian chic and beautifully appointed. We were fortunate to have direct access to the pool area with its staggeringly huge, heated, salt water bath.
