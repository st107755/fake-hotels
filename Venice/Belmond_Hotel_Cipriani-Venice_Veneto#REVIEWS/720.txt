Having had a wonderful journey to Venice aboard the Venice Simplon-Orient Express from London, the beautiful Cipriani  was waiting our arrival.  We were met outside central station by private launch and taken to this wonderful hotel. The view of Venice were just incredible.  Upon check-in we were taken personally to our room which was perfectly appointed with a private balcony overlooking Venice grand canal. This hotel is five star and so very civilised. It has the biggest lane pool of any hotel in Venice. The staff were very attentive and nothing was too much trouble. We had a very relaxing time here in wonderful Venice.
