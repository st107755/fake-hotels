We have stayed at two of the other well known Venice hotels however nothing compares to the Cipriani.  Even when it was looking a little worn and shabby some years ago (totally not the case now) it had a charm and unique atmosphere which was unsurpassable.  Cip’s restaurant is a delight, in fact we so enjoyed it last night we are going back again tonight. The walk to Cip’s through the gardens and hotel’s farm is always lovely. The entire property is a joy to behold.   I particularly recommend Suite 209 on the second floor which is absolutely perfect in every way. The Cipriani has never been better.  Belmond has been our favourite hotel group for some time and LVM become involved with the perfect selection of hotels and trains. A brilliantly managed property on every level.
