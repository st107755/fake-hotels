We will continue returning to the Cipriani: a beautiful, tranquil haven from which it is easy to get to Venice, Murano, Burano, Verona... The cuisine is remarkable and the staff (both at the hotel and in the spa) most gracious and helpful, whether organizing restaurants or drivers.
