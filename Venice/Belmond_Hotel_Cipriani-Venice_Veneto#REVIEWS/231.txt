The one... the only... the Cipriani!  Located a (private 24h) boat launch across St. Marks Square, the Cipriani is an absolute mythical place and a safe haven amongst one big tourist Disney land that Venice transforms into during the summer months. Because of this it is the Best location in the city, close enough to the action but far enough from the action.   We arrived in the Cipriani after staying at the equally Magical Belmond San Michele in the rolling hills of Fiesole outside of Florence. Having such a great experience at the sister property, set the bar higher for this hotel. And it met each and every expectation.
