My wife and I always wanted to take our 3 children to magical Venice. We decided to go back to the Belmond Hotel Cipriani where we stayed some years back without the children in connection with our trip on the Orient Express. It did not disappoint. The hotel was still lovely. We only stayed 2 nights. The location is ideal for us as you can escape the busy "streets" of Venice and also enjoy the 33 meter outdoor swimming pool. (It was heated to 26 degrees which was just perfect for this mornings swim!) We had a few issues with the booking of the rooms but Arnaud Sourigues, Reception Manager, sorted it out with ease on arrival. I was also pleased to see the gym had all the latest Technogym equipment which is excellent. The rooms are nice and and the beds comfortable.
