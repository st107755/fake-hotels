We had no hesitation in booking the Cipriani based on its reputation alone despite some slightly negative reviews on this site. Main complaints seem to refer to the size and quality of the rooms. Based on our experience this is entirely groundless. We had a lovely standard room which was more than big enough for two people and a bathroom which was not huge but perfectly well appointed. The standard of decor in both was absolutely excellent with no complaint whatsover - although obviously you have to understand this hotel is decorated in a very classical style and there are no smart, sleek, modern, minimalist features.
