It took me 56 years (I’m 56) to eventually get to Venice and I always promised myself that when I did I would only stay at the Cipriani. It’s international reputation proceeded it on all fronts, with recommendations also from family, friends and clients. It’s unique exclusive location on the tip of Giudecca opposite St Marks Square accessed by the Cipriani’s own private boat as well as having the largest outdoor saltwater pool in Venice made it beyond compelling. The quality of its restaurants, it’s reception areas, bedroom suites, grounds and of course it staff are also world renowned.   The arrival by boat from Venice airport arriving at their carpeted quay is like something out of a Bond movie and the warm welcome from Luiz, from the management team was wonderful.
