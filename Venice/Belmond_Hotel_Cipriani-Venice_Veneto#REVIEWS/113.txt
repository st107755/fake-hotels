Let me start with the pros. The location of this hotel is amazing. It is on a remote island away from the crowds of venice. The hotel runs a free water taxi to St. marks Square every 5 minutes so you get the best of both worlds. Quick access to venice but you get to escape the crowds and come back to this quiet, peaceful island when you need a break. The grounds are spectacular. The hotel has a very nice pool as well which is great in the hot summer time. The service is amazing and everyone is quite nice.   The only cons for this hotel is that it needs an update. For the prices charged for this hotel - not cheap - the rooms are woefully out of date. They are not "run down" and are kept quite clean and tidy but the overall decor needs an update.
