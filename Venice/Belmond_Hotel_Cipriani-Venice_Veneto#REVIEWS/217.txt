Had a wonderful 2 night stay in this beautiful hotel that is perfectly situated for reaching St Mark's by the hotels own regular, complimentary shuttle launch. We also ate in Gabbiano, enjoying a sumptuous club sandwich & burger with delicious cocktails for a more relaxed informal evening.
