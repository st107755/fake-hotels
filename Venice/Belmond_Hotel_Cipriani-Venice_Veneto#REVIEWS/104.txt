This hotel is from another era entirely, and that may well be why we loved it. What we particularly appreciated was its peaceful location just a five minute boat trip from Venice. We had the best of both worlds: a serene setting and an exquisite pool minutes from the rich flavors and history of Venice. Our room was palatial, and it was "just a garden room." We loved the small balcony overlooking a lush garden, the bed was too comfortable for words, the marble bathroom, oversized tub, the closets finished to perfection. Our breakfast was exceptional with eggs to order, mouthwatering ham, delightful pastries, and truthfully a less that perfect cappuccino, our one "complaint".
