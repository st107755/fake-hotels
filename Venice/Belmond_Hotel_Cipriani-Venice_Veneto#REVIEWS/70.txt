It’s true that the rooms are old fashioned and need a renovation, some of the public areas also look glamorous but a bit stuck in the 1970s, and the layout of the hotel is a little strange....  However, from the moment we arrived the service in the hotel was brilliant - the incredibly lovely front office lady that met us at the dock, checked us in, sorted out our quick lunch and transport to our first museum was gorgeous (inside and out!!).  The concierge team were super helpful with every minor request and the team in the restaurant at breakfast were happy and smiley and full of life and laughter.  But overall it was just this feeling like we were at home....  I haven’t really felt that before at any of the many other 5 star hotels I have stayed at.
