This is truly one of the great hotels of the world. The location on the Giudecca a few minutes boat ride from Piazza San Marco enables one to all of Venice's cultural highlights while also escaping back to the total peace and quiet of the Cipriani. There one can recover at the delightful swimming pool, walking through the lovely gardens or over an excellent seafood buffet lunch at the poolside restaurant. The service is generally excellent and a trip across the lagoon in one of the Cipriani's boats is a truly memorable experience. The Cips Club (the hotel's more informal restaurant) is my favourite in Venice with its excellent food and fantastic views across to San Marco.
