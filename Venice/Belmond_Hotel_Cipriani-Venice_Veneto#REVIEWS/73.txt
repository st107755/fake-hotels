Generally considered THE best place to stay for the rich and famous, Cipriani (pronounced "chip:-riani") is, as advertised, a quiet retreat from the intensity of Venice.  Located on Giudecca island, just across from Piazza San Marco,it can be reached by the hotel's dedicated water taxi in less than ten minutes.  I stayed here with my wife in honor of my 70th birthday, and decided to blow some real money on a two room suite with private deck ( about $2,200 per night).  This would have been perfect but for the fact that three of our four days here it rained, no, poured.   When the sun finally shown on the morning of our departure, it was glorious to behold. The hotel staff is impeccable, rooms clean, breakfast excellent. Avoid the Michelin 5 star restaurant, " Oro".
