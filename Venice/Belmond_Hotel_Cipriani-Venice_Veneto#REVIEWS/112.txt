My husband and two daughters and I stayed at this classic hotel which is now run by Belmond.  We finished a Crystal yacht cruise (which was lovely), and it ended in Venice.We had a few days before we were taking the Belmont Orient Express (which was disappointing), and loved our stay at the Cipriani.  I will start off by saying that although I think Venice is beautiful, it has become sooooooo touristy in the summer, that combined with the heat, its not very enjoyable.  But, the Cipriani, which is a seven minute boat ride away , is pure luxury.  If you want to be in a glamorous environment, with very nice food and service, and beautiful surroundings, then this is your place.
