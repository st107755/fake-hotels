The Hotel Cipriani is really a one of a kind place due to the backdrop of St. Marks square across the canal waterway.  The rooms have a Hollywood glam to them you do not usually find in Italy.  We found taking their private boat service over to St. Marks to be especially refined and only on one trip were we with another couple so it felt very intimate and grand.    We stayed there before a trip on the Orient Express and there is a check in area solely dedicated for you if you are part of the train experience.  Before the departure to the train the entire group meets in the lobby and the trip and stay is extremely well executed.
