We stayed 4 nights having travelled to Venice on the Orient Express. We had heard of the reputation of the Cipriani and having stayed there we now understand why it is so highly rated.  Firstly its location is fantastic for anyone who is visiting Venice for a sightseeing holiday. The hotel is located on Giudecca Island directly opposite St Marks Square and accessed by the comfortable and free Hotel Cipriani boat which takes about 5 minutes to take you to its privileged landing place at the entrance to St Marks Square. The boat operates 24 hours a day and there is a never a wait of more than 10 - 15 minutes. Being on Giudecca takes you away from the crowds and bustle of the centre of Venice.
