Venice is amazing anyway and the cipriani is the best. The only pool in Venice and definitely the only hotel combing the hustle and bustle of Venice with a truly peaceful relaxing away from it all feel. However you pay for it.  I had always wanted to stay here and my wonderful husband booked it for a weekend to propose. However on first impression I was honestly a little disappointed. Our room was nice but quite small and even a little dated looking, for over 1000 euro a night! (we had a standard lagoon view room) However the bed was exceptionally comfy and the view beautiful. The thing with the cipriani is it grows on you. The general feel of the place is wonderful and it feels so exclusive. The pool is lovely and food and drinks are great.
