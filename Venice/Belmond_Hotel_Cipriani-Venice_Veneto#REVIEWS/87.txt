My partner Rose and I stayed at Belmond Hotel Cipriani for 2-nights in early-May.  This being our first time to Venice (and in celebration of our 7-year anniversary) we wanted to do it right, so it was simply a no-brainer to choose the much-celebrated Cipriani as our host for the trip.  Flying in to Venice Marco Polo, it’s important to pre-arrange your hotel transfer.  I used Venicelink’s private water taxi meet & greet service (€304 for a 2-person return ticket). We met our hostess at arrivals, who drove us by car to the boat, which then took us on to the hotel. Seamless journey, no queuing, friendly staff. The boat was spacious enough to seat 6 quite comfortably, as well as hold our large suitcases.
