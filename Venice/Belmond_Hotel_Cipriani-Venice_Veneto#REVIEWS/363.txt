The hotel stay was part of a wedding honeymoon on a Belmond Simplon Orient Express Package.  At Verona a member of the hotel staff boarded the train.  She introduced herself to us and completed all the check in formalities, thus speeding up getting to the room on arrival at the hotel. Your overnight bag from the train is taken to the hotel, and arrived within the hour.  The check in bag was already in the room. On arrival at the hotel you show your card and you are given your room key.  You are shown to your room, to find a celebratory bottle of champagne and massive portion of cake, and greeting card. I am not going into too much comment about the room, as it met what you expect of quality hotel.
