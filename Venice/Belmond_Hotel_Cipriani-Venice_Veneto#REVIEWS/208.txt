This a good five star luxury hotel, but not a great one. Its location on the island of Giudecca means that unlike other Venice hotels, it boasts a huge swimming pool and extensive gardens. Access to the main island and its tourist attractions is via the hotel launch, which runs efficiently every 15 minutes day and night to and from a dedicated landing pier close to St Mark's Square. Service throughout the hotel is as superb as you would expect. Cip's Club restaurant is a disappointment; average food at extraordinary prices. Breakfast served in the Oro restaurant is excellent. The swimming pool is a real benefit.  It's enormous and has sea water, rather than chlorinated fresh water.  The pool bar area is a lovely place to relax with a drink and chat.
