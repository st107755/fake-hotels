We spent threee nights here and loved it. Even though we had a special deal it is very expensive but for a significant anniversary we thought it was worth it: we were upgraded to a suite complete with complimentary prosecco and flowers in the room on arrival and the setting and food were outstanding. There were only two small drawbacks: firstly it was Venice Film Festival week and there was a handful of noisy, inconsiderate and frankly vulgar media types staying there which was tiresome; secondly the service, while generally excellent, was occasionally condescending and the front-of-house manager was quite offhand to our friends who had a complaint about their suite.
