I paid a lot more for this hotel than I could have paid elsewhere but in the end it was worth it because the hotel really made the difference in giving my family a great Venice experience. We had two deluxe interconnecting rooms facing the lagoon. The rooms are bright and cheerful and decorated very carefully. Except for the lack of a standing shower and feeling very scared balancing myself in the bathtub, they were basically perfect. I was told when I mentioned it at checkout that if I had stayed on a lower floor, I could have had a standing shower. The kids club is excellent with its own pool and our kids had a great time there and they enjoyed chilling out there during the hot afternoons when we took a break from the city.
