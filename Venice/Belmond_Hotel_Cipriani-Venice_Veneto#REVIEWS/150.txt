My wife and I made a quick trip down to Venice for two nights and decided to try Cipriani based on reviews. The property is situated 10 minutes by boat from the main square of Venice. The hotel has a complimentary shuttle that runs 24 hours a day and drops you at the entrance to the square. Very convenient.  When we arrived we were greeted warmly and graciously by all the staff. This theme continued throughout our entire stay. As an added bonus we were upgraded to a suite with a lovely terrace and views over the lagoon. The property is situated on waterfront on guidecca island with expansive gardens to explore. It feels like an oasis from the hustle and bustle of touristy Venice but yet is so close to all the sights.
