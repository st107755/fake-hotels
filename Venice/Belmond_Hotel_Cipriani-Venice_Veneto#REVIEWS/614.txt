First time to Venice and first time to the Cipriani and expected the best given the price. Let me start by saying this is the best place to stay when visiting Venice. It allows you to escape to a little piece of paradise after battling the hoards that cram into Venice proper. The Hotel is located a short (complimentary) ride across from San Marco square which allows you to transit back and forth as you please. The rooms are small but well appointed (except for the showers which are a joke) the staff are on the whole are eager to please but be prepared to pay over the top for everything. 10 Euro for a small bottle of water and 18 Euro to have a shirt ironed (not cleaned ...that's extra) and the food is good but not great.
