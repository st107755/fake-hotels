This hotel is absolutely stunning. Arrive by boat to the reception, get taken to your room, so open and light, still traditional, but not stuffy like some hotels can be. A little claustrophobic in the lift, but the stairs are quick and easy to access. The grounds here are beautiful, a vineyard was our view, and the pool was the view of our friends. Wonderful outdoor area, very quiet, barely anyone ever about in the day if you want to lounge by the pool, great pool bar restaurant with wonderful pizzas for lunchtimes over looking the water. Their on site restaurant is quite expensive and on the strict side with men having to wear long-sleeved shirts or dinner jackets but our waiter was fantastic and helped the children choose an off menu dish to their liking.
