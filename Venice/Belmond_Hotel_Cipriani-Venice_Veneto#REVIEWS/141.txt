My husband I had the pleasure of staying at the Cipriani for 2 nights before boarding the Orient Express to Paris .  We had arranged to be collected at the airport by the hotel , found the representative who took us to the moorings where the boat was waiting to whisk us to the hotel , an enchanting meander through the back street canals of Venice before arriving at the hotel .  Our room wasn’t quite ready so we were escorted to Cips where we has a beautiful lunch of white asparagus and fresh fish washed down with a very pleasant Rose . By the time we were finished our room was available.  Our Junior suite overlooked the lagoon , lovely to sit on the balcony and watch the world go by .
