The Cipriani is a really lovely hotel.  The staff are wonderful, we were so well looked after, the room was not large but comfortable and luxurious, not really outstanding though, for the money, especially when you can hear your neighbours through the walls.  The food was delicious but ridiculously expensive for what it was.  It has an amazing olympic sized swimming pool kept at 28 degrees. The boat service backwards and forwards to Venice was great and very frequent.  However, the hotel was full of very pretentious nouveau riche people, mainly British, talking loudly and showing off - it sort of put us off, which was sad.  The hotel is full of history.  If you really want to splash the cash, then this hotel is a good one to do it at.
