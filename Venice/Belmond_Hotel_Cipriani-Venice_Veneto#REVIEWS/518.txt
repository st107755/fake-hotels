We have just returned from our holiday at the Cipriani to celebrate a special birthday with our friends. The hotel was in a stunning location with beautiful gardens and décor.  The rooms were excellent and very well equipped. We had a delicious meal when we arrived at Cip's Club where the staff couldn't have been more helpful.  Lunch the following day at the Portocciolo Pool Restaurant was really nice again with excellent staff. But on the night before we left our friends booked Oro Restaurant as a belated wedding present to my husband and I. What a disappointment! The vegetarian starter of asparagus was woody and inedible and then the main course of pasta was nearly cold and the only taste apparent was of chemicals.
