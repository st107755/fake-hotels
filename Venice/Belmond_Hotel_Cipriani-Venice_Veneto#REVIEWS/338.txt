The Hotel Cipriani is perfect in virtually every facet. The hotel itself is magical & the staff make you feel like you've known them all your life.  A very minor point would be that I would have liked more variation in the food served in the restaurant, but it is Michelin starred, so what do I know? The regular complementary shuttles by private launch to StMarks Square simply enhance the experience.  We stayed there after travelling to Venice on the Orient Express; we will definitely visit again with our young family.
