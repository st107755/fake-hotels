My husband and I stayed at the Cipriani recently and I must admit, the location puts you close to St. Mark's Square, but in a very quiet setting that lets you forget the proximity once you arrive there.  The grounds are very nice and the room we had was very well appointed although the sheets were made of linen- very scratchy and quite uncomfortable.  If you decide to stay at the hotel, you can call in advance and they told me that they can provide different bedding upon request. They do not provide complimentary transportation to the hotel so it cost us over 220 Euro to get to the airport on top of the 1500 Euro we paid for the each night's accommodations.   Breakfast is included in the room rate and that was excellent.
