It has been a privilege to stay in some of the best hotels in Italy - the Hassler in Rome, the Hotel Splendido in Portofino, the Sireneuse in Amalfi, and Via San Michelle in Florence - and I would probably say the Danieli in Venice is quite good as well, having stayed there twice, but the Cipriani is right up there with the best. Your trip begins as you arrive by a beautiful teak boat at the Hotel's private dock. The launch takes you regularly into Piazza San Marco, and returns you to the hotel until late at night. The rooms are not luxurious, but many face directly onto the canal, some with balconies and tables and chairs for breakfast in one's room.
