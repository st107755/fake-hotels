We stayed at the Cipriani as part of a tour which ended in Venice.    The fact that the Hotel is located on a separate island with complementary motor boat taxi makes it a very special location.  Although this does mean that just popping out of the hotel to explore is not as easy, with the launch operating 24h a day, and scheduled every 10-15 minutes it is not too onerous.  The pool facilities are amazing.  Unfortunately we didn't get the time to use the Olympic sized pool, but the night time illumination makes it a great photo opportunity, and i just wish we had the time to make use of it.  The hotel has it's own gardens, and with the launch using a private landing pontoon it means that it is very secluded and quiet, and relaxing.
