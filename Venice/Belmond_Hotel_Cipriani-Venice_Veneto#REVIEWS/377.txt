Depends on if you can afford it! £800 for garden view room with breakfast per night. Good points: excellent location accross the lagoon from St Marks square on the peaceful island of Guidecca - 24 hour free boat transfer always running which is fantastic to escape the crowds of San Marco; rooms serviced every day to high standard and very nice though balconies small; excellent swimming pool and pool service for beds, towels, drinks etc Less good: Cips restaurant - great view but service average and food expensive without being legendary! Room mini bar outrageous (eg bag of crisps 8 euros; peroni 11 euros, half bottle Chianti 33 euros, half bottle water 8 euros).
