On a recent tour of Italy, my wife and I stayed at the Belmond Hotel Cipriani for the Venice portion of our trip. It was the first of three Belmond properties in which we stayed as part of their Italy package. Set apart from the bustle of the main tourist areas but only a free shuttle boat away from St. Mark's Square, Cipriani provides a quiet, luxurious base for your Venice adventures. Our visit began with a friendly greeting and the news we had been upgraded to a beautiful suite, complete with a balcony and wonderful lagoon views. The Hotel grounds are beautifully landscaped, including a huge and well heated outdoor pool. The Hotel and our room were immaculate and well maintained.
