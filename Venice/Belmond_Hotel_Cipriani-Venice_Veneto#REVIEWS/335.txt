There are a number of very good hotels in Venice.  Some will swear by the Gritti, others praise the Bauer, or maybe know of a relatively small gem hidden somewhere in the city.  But for me, it is the Cipriani, if only because, at the end of the day, it is a soothing ride away from the throng, arriving at an excellently managed hotel nestled in greenery by the sea.  Few hotels lift my spirits; the Cipriani, just by being there, does so.
