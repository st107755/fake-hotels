We spent 2 nights at the Cip in Sept.  Room was very comfortable and large and we had a massive bathroom with a jacuzzi in the bath.  I agree with some previous comments, the rooms do look dated  - some of the fixtures and fittings look like they have come from the 1970's.  Breakfast on the terrace is the best that I have ever had in a hotel and we enjoyed room service breakfast the next morning.  The hotel restaurant is very expensive, so we ate out.  Regretfully, most restaurants are expensive in Venice and the quality is poor, and service charge is high.  Everything closes early in Venice, so we had some cocktails in the bar when we returnted to the hotel on our last night.  Bar was closing after midnight, so we took our drinks outside.
