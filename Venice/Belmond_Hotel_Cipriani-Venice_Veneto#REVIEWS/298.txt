 As the Belmond says the Cipriani is the Royal box in the theatre of Venice. Located on stunning Giudecca Island with lush gardens, beautiful rooms and an amazing pool the hotel should set the standard to which others should aspire.   The hotel offers an outstanding facility in a unique location but truly great hotels must afford superlative and anticipatory service that respects all guests and consistently delivers the best of the best at a rational cost. But beyond the kindness of our female check-in attendant on the first day , one room service waiter and the room maids (who were excellent) it fails the test on service and respect.
