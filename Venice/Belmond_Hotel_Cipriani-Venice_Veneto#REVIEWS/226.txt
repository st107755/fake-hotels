We were lucky enough to stay at the hotel Cipriani before travelling on the Orient Express.  From the minute we arrived we were totally blown away by the style & beauty of this Hotel, From the moment we stepped off the private jetty we were greeted by friendly & efficient staff. We were given a full tour of the hotel & grounds before being shown to our room. We originally booked a standard lagoon view room, but were upgraded to a balcony room with stunning views. Our room was large & furnished in traditional opulent Venetian style. One quirky feature we loved was the pop up tv at the end of the bed.
