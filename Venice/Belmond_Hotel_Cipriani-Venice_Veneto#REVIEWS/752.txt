After a small initial fumble, the staff at Cipriani have surpassed all expectations. Without exception, they have been friendly (everyone knows you by name), knowledgable and efficient. Every recommendation they made turned was perfect and no request was too much.  We had originally booked in a double room in the Palazzo side but received an upgrade into a beautiful suite looking back over towards St Marks Sq - it's almost like you're living in a Canaletto. We were fortunate enough in our timing to coincide our arrival with the first peaches of the season, allowing us to indulge in their exquisite Bellinis and enjoy the start of season party.
