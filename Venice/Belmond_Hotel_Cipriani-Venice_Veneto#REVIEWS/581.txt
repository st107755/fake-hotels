We arrived at Venice Station after travelling from London on the Orient Express and it was like arriving into a painting by Canaletto.  We were taken by water taxi to the Hotel Cipriani.  Our room 131 was enormous, with his and hers bathrooms.  No wonder it is one of the best hotels in the World.  Eating breakfast on the outside terrace is a memory we will never forget.  No wonder George Clooney stays here, unfortunately not when we were there, we had a not very sociable Michael McIntyre.  We met some wonderful people on the OE who were also staying there, so nice to see friendly faces every morning.  Staff are friendly and very friendly. The private water taxi too and from the hotel makes you feel like a movie star.
