We stayed for a couple of nights with a large group who like us had arrived on the Orient Express.  The hotel is beautifully situated with wonderful views over the lagoon, and does feel a special place.  Shame George C wasn't around, the nearest I got was drinking a cocktail he had named in the hotel bar ...  We had a fabulous huge room/suite with a wonderful bathroom containing the biggest bath I've ever seen and our own private terrace.  We have stayed at some very good hotels all over the world but this was definitely the best room we've ever had.
