Despite the unfortunate cancellation of my Orient Express trip in June 2014, I decided to book a two night stay at the Cipriani before embarking on my Queen Elizabeth Mediterranean cruise.   I had arranged a boat from the hotel to meet me at Marco Polo airport. Unfortunately, the day I flew was also the day of the French air controllers' strike, which meant a delay of over 8 hours! While waiting at Gatwick airport (I had intended on taking the far nicer VSOE) I emailed the hotel to explain that I was unsure when I would be arriving. They responded promptly, informing me they would monitor my flight number and to rest assured a boat would be waiting on my arrival.
