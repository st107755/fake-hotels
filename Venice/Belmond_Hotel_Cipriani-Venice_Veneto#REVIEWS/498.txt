We arrived with several others but were welcomed warmly and the check-in process was quick and efficient.  Our room was very large and, with a garden view, had a spacious private terrace with sun beds, table and chairs.  Throughout our stay, staff were friendly and efficient.  A complimentary boat service runs across to a private landing in Piazza San Marco or there is a near-by vaporetti stop close to the hotel from which you can reach the other islands.  Food and drink in the hotel is very expensive, even by Venetian standards.  The food is excellent but there is a lot to be said for exploring the city - a mini-bar price list would have been nice as it would allow guests to decide if the wish to spend more than €10 on a small bottle of water or beer!
