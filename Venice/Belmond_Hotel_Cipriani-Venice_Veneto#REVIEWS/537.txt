After a wonderful train trip onboard the Orient Express, it was only made more perfect staying in one of Orient Express Hotels in Venice, which is on an island within Venice. Venice is a very beautiful city built on canals, but very little vegetation, however this hotel was the complete opposite, with the smell of Jasmine plants hitting you when you get off your James Bond style boat, where you are looked after by very friendly staff.  The gardens in this hotel are the selling point, and well worth a walk after diner, in one of the two restaurants, although we choose the outdoor restaurant, that overlooks Venice.
