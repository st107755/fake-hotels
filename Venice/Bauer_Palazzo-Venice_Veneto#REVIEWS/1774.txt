Stayed here late October for 4 nights to celebrate wedding anniversary. Had a superior room - no view, but we were told that when checking in. Room itself was fine - a small entrance hall, bedroom spacious enough for two chairs and writing desk/chair as well as large bed - large bathroom, and walk-in wardrobe. Also, attention had been paid to our anniversary and a bottle of prosecco awaited our arrival. I loved the public areas, and was very pleased to see so much original art deco furniture and the beautiful staircase. Service was friendly and efficient - the greeting when we arrived by water taxi, reception and concierge staff, and the waiting staff at breakfast every day. Overall, a very professional staff.
