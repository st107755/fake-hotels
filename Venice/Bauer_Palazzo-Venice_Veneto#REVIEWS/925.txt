This is a 5 star hotel.  Much of the 'award' must be down to the staff.  The concierge staff are excellent (especially GianLuca) - very knowledgable and helpful and nothing is too much trouble.  The rooms are a little dated.  The pillows are poor and offer little in the way of real support.  Even the additional ones only made things a little better.   The bathroom - having a bath with a good, strong shower -  was spacious and well set-out. Overall the room size was fine.  TV was limited to mostly Italian language channels - only English was on business channels or CNN and BBC. The Breakfast buffet was very generous and wide-ranging. Maybe think about the room you want.  Ours had no external view worth speaking of but was quiet.
