We selected this hotel for the last two nights of our 5 nights in Venice. We moved from the Palazzo Vitturi (which we loved) to join other family meeting us in Venice. The Vitturi was more authentic old world in a quiet square away from the tourists. It contrasted greatly with the Bauer which is on the grand canal and in the heart of the tourist hub. The Bauer is close to all of the very high end designer shopping and a quick stroll to St. Marks square. We took a water taxi right to the front door- literally you step out of the boat into the lobby. It cannot get easier or more convenient than that! You can easily travel with all of your luggage from the airport or anywhere else in the city this way. The Bauer is a beautiful building and caters to a higher end traveller.
