The Bauer is a nice luxury hotel in Venice, but seems to be managed poorly. We loved the location of the hotel and the waterfront breakfast terrace is unbelievable. However, when we checked in, our room had twin beds, not a king, as we had booked it. The front desk clerk said she would send somebody immediately to combine the beds (to make a king) and change the bedding to accommodate the change. Unfortunately, hours later, after dinner, we discovered that our bed had not been fixed. I am not sure why, but for some reason, they couldn't fix it? Eventually, they moved us to a better room (larger with a better view) to make up for the mistake, which was very nice. However, I wish that was the end of our problems, but it was just the beginning of our issues at The Bauer.
