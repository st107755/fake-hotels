The location of Bauer Palazzo was ideal, very close to St Mark's Square, restaurants, shopping and of course the gondolas. The reception area is quite large and the staff were pleasant and helpful. Once checked in we were shown to our room. We were told we could either leave our key at reception or take it with us when we went out. We had a room with a canal view, the room was large with a nice décor and a small balcony, TV and DVD player, mini bar, slippers, robes,  in room safe and plenty of cupboard space. As with a lot of hotels the items in the mini bar had a highly inflated price to them, a 200ml coke cost €8. The bathroom was done in a lovely marble design and lots of mirrors.
