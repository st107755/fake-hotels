My husband and I stayed at the Bauer for a long weekend. We used Ryan air and flew into Treviso. It could not have been easier. Ryan air arrange courtesy transport into Venice which costs 9 euros return. It took about 45 minutes and we arrived at Piazzale Roma. This is the furthest point that cars/buses and trains can travel. We then caught the water bus, which costs 5 euros for a one-way journey. The Bauer is quite a few stops away from Piazzale Roma and you need to get off at S. Marco Valaresso. Walk down the alleyway in front of the stop and turn left at the bottom. Walk for a minute and reach the hotel on the left side before the canal bridge. Walking takes a couple of minutes from the stop. The staff were very friendly and we were showed to our room which was a suite.
