We stayed at this truly delightful hotel for four nights recently with our family.  Our rooms were located the "Il Palazzo" wing of the property: My husband and I had a deluxe suite; our teenagers stayed in a double room close by, with a king split into twin beds.  First: the Bauer Il Palazzo's location, near San Marco Square, is fabulous.  Most major attractions are within convenient walking distance.  The hotel is easily accessed by water taxi and public transportation (a vaporetto stop is nearby). There is even a gondola stand next to the hotel. We were extremely pleased to discover that the hotel's front entrance opened onto a square, and not onto one of Venice's narrow and crowded streets, which is a luxury when arriving with luggage and bags.
