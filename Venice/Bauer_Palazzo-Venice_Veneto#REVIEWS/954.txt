It was great staying on the quiet side of the canal, and the hotel was very clean and had nice amenities in the room.  The staff was very accommodating and helpful (they did a rush laundry job among other things).  Our room was two levels, which would seem nice, but it meant that the only natural light was from one window on the second level.  Our bed and bathroom were on the first level, and we really didn't have time to hang out on the second level.  The room was comfortable, but aesthetically austere.  Our son had a smaller room that was less forboding.  The closets in both rooms were huge.  We opened the window on the second level at night, and within minutes our room was swarming with tiny fleas, which was obviously annoying.
