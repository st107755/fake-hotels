This hotel was perfect! We stayed for 2 nights. The staff couldn't be more helpful, especially the concierge (he helped us to rearrange a water taxi, gave advice on how to get to F.Nove vaporetto stop on the other side of the island - even taking the time to draw a route on the map).  The rooms were excellent and had everything you could possibly need. We stayed in rooms that overlooked the rooftops - which was not a problem as when you are in Venice there is so much to see and do you really only use your room to sleep. This hotel has the added advantage though of having the breakfast room overlooking the southern entrance to the Grand Canal - sitting outside on the terrace drinking your morning cappucino watching the boats go by was a great way to start the day.
