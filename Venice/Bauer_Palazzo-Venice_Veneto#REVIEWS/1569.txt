We arrived to the Bauer after a long train ride from Munich and at 2 pm, our room was not ready. After waiting for about 20 minutes, we ended with a dungeon of a room with a view of a wall and barely any light coming through even though it was a bright sunny day. After complaining that we paid for a canal view room, we were eventually moved. They said they would bring our luggage up but after waiting an hour, we went and got it ourselves: we were anxious to start enjoying our day in Venice and didn’t want to waste it waiting on poor service.  No iron and not even a clock. We were surprised by how hard the beds were and the pillows were like sandbags. Don’t even think about walking into the room and collapsing onto the bed because you might give yourself a concussion!
