Reservation - The Bauer hotel came recommended by a friend. My initial interactions over the phone were good. The agent was very professional and my request for an upgrade was granted which I found a nice gesture. This however was tainted by the fact that our room (Casa Nova Suite) was in another building adjacent to the Bauer Hotel and not the hotel itself. This was quite disappointing however all hotel facilities were on offer and it was only 20 metres away. This however should be very clearly indicated when booking.  Location- The hotel is very centrally located and only a stone's throw from St. Marcos square. You access it by water taxi €60 or water bus €6.50. There are lots of Ristorantes and exclusive shopping nearby.
