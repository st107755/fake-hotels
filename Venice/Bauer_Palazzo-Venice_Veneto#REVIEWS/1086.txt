The Bauer Hotel is positioned right in the heart of Venice in a perfect position for visiting the city. It is an easy walk from the airport boat services but perhaps a private water taxi right to the door is a better way to arrive. The hotel is elegant and well appointed. The rooms are comfortable although not all of them have a view to the outside world. Most of all the Bauers is about service. From check-in till check-out the staff are attentive and accommodating. The concierge service is excellent and all three restaurant recommendations provided to us were excellent, and each perfectly tuned to our particular requirements. Their knowledge of the city and ability to adjust an itinerary to our needs was exceptional.
