Hello, we just stayed 6 nights in the Bauer Il Palazzo in Venice. overall a very good location:  Room: We had a room on the 7th  floor (748) which was pretty perfect. It has a side view to channel. In the beginning we were a little bit disappointed about the view, because it is really a side view, but after a couple of days we were  sure that this was the right choice. The room comes with a big terrace and is sunny from 11 am up to 8pm. Although you are right in center of Venice, max 5 minutes to San Marco, this place is absolutely peaceful. I would always prefer this room from any room directly at the Canale Grande as there it is quite noisy, a lot of traffic, many people around and not half as much privacy as in 748 (749/50 and 747 have the same view).
