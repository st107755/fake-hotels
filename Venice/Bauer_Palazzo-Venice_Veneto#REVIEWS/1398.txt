We have stayed in all of the best hotels in Venice -- Gritti Palace, Danieli and now in the Bauer Palazzo. We  had stayed many years ago at the Bauer Grunwald, as it was then called. They have now made two Bauers, and we are at the time of writing this at the Bauer Il Palazzo. It is a very good hotel, but I am not sure that separating both has been a good idea, since the entrance to the Palazzo is not palatial at all, much better is the lobby of the Bauer. The rooms are nicely redone, and the bathrooms are marble and with everything one would need.
