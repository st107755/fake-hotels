We stayed in the Bauer despite some less than glowing reviews.  Some said that the staff was rude and the rooms were just OK.  I don't really see how that could be.   We had wonderful staff with an exceptional concierge who made us feel as if we were the only guests in the hotel.  Every time we entered the reception area he greeted us by name and had suggestions for things to do for the day.  He drew maps for everywhere we wanted to go and had accurate estimates of the time it would take to walk to those locations.    We booked a deluxe room with a view (just under the suite class of rooms) and it was a nice room, well furnished with a limited but very nice view of the grand canal.  All in all, we had no reason to complain about anything at this hotel.
