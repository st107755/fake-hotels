We knew when we booked , Venice was expensive but we were more than satisfied with everything we experienced at Hotel Bauer.  The room was large, elaborately decorated and very Venician. The bed was enormous and  comfortable.  The bathroom was small but more than adequate with excellent water pressure in the shower, high quality products and enormous fluffy towels.  The concierge, Marko, was so helpful with advice of times to visit attractions to avoid extra large numbers of other tourists.  His advice on dining places in Burano and in Venice, were affordable with excellent quality food.  Nothing was too much trouble for him.  Breakfast on the Terrace was amazing watching all the traffic on the Grand Canal and gondolas in the canal next to the hotel.
