Spent 3 nights here and could not really fault the hotel. Very comfortable, excellent and professional service, I would return any time.  We had dinner here on our first night and were disappointed. Italy is home to some of the most fabulous food in the world but the chef here seems to be experimenting rather beyond sense. I could not fault the ingredients or quality but some things just don't work. For example, marinated anchovies with barbeque sauce! The anchovies were delicious, quite subtle but sat in a very artistic square of barbeque sauce which of course completely overpowered everything. Veal sweetbreads with avocado. The sweetbreads were lovely, milanese style but the avocado puree and slices just did nothing, it was almost an exact reverse of the starter.
