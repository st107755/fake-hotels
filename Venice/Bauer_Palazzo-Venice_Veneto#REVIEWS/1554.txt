Excellent location on the corner of the Grand Canal, a few minutes from St Mark's Square. We stayed in a deluxe suite which was of a good size (for Venice where space is in short supply) and very nicely decorated. Air conditioning worked well, bed was huge and there was no problem with noise. Great views of the lagoon from the breakfast room on the 7th floor. Where the Bauer really wins, though, is the quality of service: friendly and attentive without being either patronising or obsequious. When booking dinner, I mentioned it was my wife's birthday. When we got back to the room that evening there was a complimentary bottle of fizz waiting. Couldn't get the bath plug to work on the night we arrived but told the porter to leave it till the morning (it was 10pm).
