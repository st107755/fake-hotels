This was the first stop of our honeymoon in Italy (we were arriving from Paris). We arrived by water taxi, which was great. The friendly staff promptly took our bags to the room, while we waited in the lobby enjoying a nice coffeee. The rooms are very spacious and comfortable, although the first one had a problem in the bedroom's window. We only noticed that a cold breeze was entering the room when we went to sleep. The staff promptly checked the window (at my request), and since they would not be able to repair it at that time, they moved us to another room (a little bit smaller, but more modern). Mr. Paolo (the manager) and his staff we very sorry for the inconveninent, and offered us complimentary drinks in the bar. We believed they dealt very well with the situation.
