We had booked a room in the Bauer Hotel, but were allocated the Bauer Casanova, which is just around the corner from the main hotel, with it's own entrance. This did not bother us at all, we did not actually see any other guests in this part, just something to be aware of when booking as some people would not like this arrangement. The location of the hotel is perfect, easy to reach the main attractions and at the heart of designer shopping. The gondolas pass the hotel entrance so it definitely has the Venetian vibe.  Our room was beautiful, lovely decor and an extremely large, comfortable bed. The bathroom was spacious with good quality towels and bath robes.
