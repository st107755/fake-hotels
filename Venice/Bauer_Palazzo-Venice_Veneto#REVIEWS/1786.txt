Getting There  From the airport turn left out of arrivals and follow the signs for the water taxi (about a 10 minute walk). Take the Alilaguna shuttle boat (blue line) to the San Marco (Giardinetti) stop (13EURO each one-way and you can buy a ticket on the boat for no extra cost). This takes about 1hour 20 minutes. When you depart the boat turn left and walk past the Tourist Information Centre on your right, over a small bridge and then you will see Henry's Bar on your right, take the next right, walk up this street past the likes of Gucci until you come to the end and the Sisley store is on your left. Turn left, you will pass a book shop on your left, Fendi on your right and then into a square where you will see Prada on your right and across to the left the Bauer hotel.
