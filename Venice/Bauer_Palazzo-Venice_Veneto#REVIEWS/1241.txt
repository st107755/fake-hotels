We visited this hotel to celebrate my boyfriend's 40th birthday in style. We arrived by private taxi and entered the beautiful hotel at it's private dock.  The receptionist was correct, but not a warm person. I said it was my boyfriend's birthday, and he just said 'congratulations' in a cold tone. Not a nice start. Another staff member brought us to the room, we had a luxe room on the 3th floor with 'street view' ( a canal offcourse), but the street was narrow, so we only overlooked the hotel across the street. No problem for us, who stays in the room when in Venice :-) The room was very luxury, everything you need was there. Except free wi-fi; but now and then we could go on the internet for free ( no idea if they know that). Rooms are clean, and twice a day housekeeping.
