This hotel is of a very good standard and we would definitely go back.  We arrived at about 12pm and our room was ready for us for us to check into straight away.  The room was clean and comfortable, and the bathroom was of a high standard.  The hotel's location is excellent - very close to St Mark's Square and the other main sights.  It's also very near the public boat stop which is handy.  We had breakfast at the hotel, which was included in our room rate.  The breakfast is of a good standard and there was a selection of ham, cheese, pastries, fruit, cereal etc, as well as hot food.  The service was good.  We ate in the restaurant one night, and also had a drink in the bar. It is expensive to eat or drink in the hotel, but to be honest Venice is an expensive place.
