A Christmas present from my husband I returned to Venice after many years and stayed at this wonderful hotel. They organised our transfer from the Airport to the Hotel by means of luxury car and private boat so we arrived gently and elegantly via the Grand Canal to the door of the Bauer. Check-in was smooth and within 10 mins we had been shown our lovely room and were in the bar enjoying a drink and a light supper.  Our room was 352, a Canal Suite with balcony on the corner overlooking the mouth of the Grand Canal and the Lagoon. The narrow channel alongside the Hotel is 'central station' for the Gondoliers which gave us much amusement with the singing, whistling, accordion playing and general chaos on the water of 'parking'.
