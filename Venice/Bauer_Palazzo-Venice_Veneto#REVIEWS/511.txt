Stayed at the Bauer hotel for 4 nights. Overall impression, very fancy hotel, great location, let down by service.  Booked because it is one of the few central hotels that has a gym only to discover once we got there that the gym is an additional 25 Euros per person per day. Definitely not worth the money. Considering how expensive it is to stay per night and that in most hotels the gym is complimentary, I was quite shocked at this hidden extra cost.  The front of house staff (men not women) were very curt. They failed to deliver a pre-paid bottle of bubbles to my room that was a surprise by family members for my birthday.
