As has been said many times before, the location is perfect.  The hotel has its own water taxi stop and gondola station. He is a local, and very proud of his city, and the depth and breadth of his knowledge is quite impressive.  He effortlessly arranged for many excursions, meals, and more for our group of 9.  His personal service was unmatched in our travels.  Management is very lucky to have him, and he made our time in Venice exceptional.
