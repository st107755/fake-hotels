After reading many mixed reviews i checked into the hotel with low expectations. The hotel exceeded my expectations and i would recommend it as long as you know this is not a newly renovated hotel. The hotel is in one of the best locations in Venice. I am sure it was a world class hotel at one time, now it could use a little face lift. Yes it is aging however the rooms and bathrooms are super clean and the service is excellent. It’s just not up to date for example the only wall outlets in rooms are behind the bedside tables making it hard to charge your phone because you have to move furniture to get to the outlets. Again bathrooms and rooms are clean but small, have TV and excellent AC which is important in the summer in italy.
