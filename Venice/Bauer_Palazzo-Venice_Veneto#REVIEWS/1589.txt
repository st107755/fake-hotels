Thhis was my 3rd time at this hotel, it continues to be my favorite in Venice. This time we had a great room with views of the rooftops and St. Mark's campanile from the bedroom and bathroom.  Having stayed there more than a couple of times (and multiple rooms with family) I can assure you that there are many different rooms, some with different charms than others.
