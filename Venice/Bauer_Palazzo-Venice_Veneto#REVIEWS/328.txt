this hotel is located on one of the best spots in Venice. A few steps away from San Marco square and with a private boat pier, near de Canal Grande. The staff is superbly friendly and efficient. The rooms however, although the very high room rate, are completely obsolete. They put you back 50 years in time or more without any historical value. The bar is as cozy as a Mac Donald. On the other side, the restaurant "De Pisis" is outstanding, as well for breakfast as for dining. Marvelous seating with sight on the canals and the traffic. Weather permitting, a large terrace enables breakfast and dinner outside with the gondola's passing a few yards before you. Besides the restaurant, the hotel is overpriced for the quality delivered. You just pay for the location.
