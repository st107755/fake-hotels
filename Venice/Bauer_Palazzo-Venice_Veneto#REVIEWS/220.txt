Stayed few days after our cruise. Been to Venice many many times but we always decided to stay in the hotel my husband and I stayed when we were both students and we had a student budget over 20y ago. This time decided it was time to grow up and stay in a nice hotel. We had a perfect time, the view over breakfast in the terrace seems to be taken out of a movie set, gondolas, boats, singing gondoliers, and the hustle and bustle of the Gran Canal right in front of you, the food was also very good with lots to choose from. The room was big, with a great view of San Marco bell tower and Venice roofs. The bathroom was big. The only drawback is the few electrical outlets in the room, only 3, and all European plugs. But it’s an old building so we worked around that.
