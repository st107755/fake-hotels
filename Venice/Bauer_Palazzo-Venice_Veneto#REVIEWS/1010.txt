We stayed at the Bauer during a July heatwave. Our room was small but nicely decorated, no view at all, and the bathroom well appointed but as another reviewer mentioned they really need to add shower curtains. Our AC worked fine and the room was quiet. Laundry service was excellent as was the location of the hotel. Breakfast was fine.  The Bauer charges extra for everything. Wifi, and use of the gym all cost extra. We were there during the fireworks festival and the Bauer has a great location to watch the fireworks. But to do so you had to pay 500 euros per person for a full gourmet dinner.
