Stayed here for three summer nights in August. The hotel is great and I think we got a great value (however, it’s expensive but along other 5 stars in Venice it was a good deal). Location is the best, next to San Marco and close to my favorite place in Venice – Dorsoduro.   Bauer is absolutely one of the better hotel I never stayed in, and I have usually high expectations. Everything was to the point great with efficient and friendly staff, well equipped and charming rooms, clean, great views, sober atmosphere and a breakfast buffet of your dreams. We had a Deluxe Room in the new part with a small balcony overlooking the tiny canal and lively street with gondolas, roomy marble bathroom and a great bed.
