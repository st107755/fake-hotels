This is our second time to Venice, and first time staying at the Bauer Palazzo. This is by far one of Venice’s best hotels, beautifully situated at the end of the Grand Canal with water taxi service to and from the airport & train station.  From the time we arrived by water taxi, the service was first class.   We were lucky to be upgraded to a lovely large room located on the 7th floor of the old Palazzo overlooking the Grand Canal and Santa Maria Salute. The room was exquisitely decorated with lots of wonderful amenities including excellent Wi-Fi.   The Terrace Bar is amazing. The views of the Grand Canal will take your breath away. Guests staying in the Palazzo have access to the terrace for breakfast, drinks and snacks throughout the day.
