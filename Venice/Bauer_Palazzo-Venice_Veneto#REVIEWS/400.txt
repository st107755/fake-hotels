The Bauer Hotel exudes style and tradition. Arriving by water taxi we were impressed by the care the staff provided to get us and our luggage safely into the foyer and up to our room. The room was very comfortable and spacious, however as in many older Hotels the shower was over the bath which is inconvenient. Being right on the water afforded some excellent views from the dining room and terrace. An extensive buffet was offered for breakfast although there were minimal choices if you wanted a cooked breakfast. The Hotel contained a number of very high end shops and was surrounded by more. It was so well located that St. Mark's Square was minutes from the land entry to the Hotel as were many other places of interest.
