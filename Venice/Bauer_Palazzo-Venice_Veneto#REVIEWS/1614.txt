Our first visit to Venice and a 25th anniversary treat so we booked a room with a view at the Bauer II Palazzo with Elegant Resorts.  The arrival and transfer by private water taxi was fantastic along the grand canal, although the private Bauer II jetty (and reception) was closed due to refurbishment work.  Nevertheless the welcome at the Bauer's more contemporary reception instead was warm and efficient.  We were shown to our room which had a view but not much.. a tiny glimpse of the grand canal and a large scaffold clad building opposite which was clearly undergoing refurbishment.  Still it was a very grand room (102), spacious, comfortable and warm.
