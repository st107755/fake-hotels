I had to write after reading the last few reviews of Bauer Il Palazzo.   I couldn't disagree with their experience more! I booked my room with American Express, and was given an upgrade to a beautiful room overlooking the canal.  The room was very large, in great condition, and the bathroom and walk in closet were both huge!  My only negative about the room was that the linens could be a bit softer/higher thread count... The room was a bit warm, but I simply shut off the heat using the in room thermostat.  Problem solved. The shower was one of the best I've ever had in Europe - hard pressure!   The breakfast is wonderful, and has one of the highest views of Venice. The restaurants recommended from the concierge were expensive, but honestly worth every penny.
