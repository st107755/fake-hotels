We stayed in a huge room overlooking the Grand Canal in the Il Palazzo part of the hotel for 3 nights in early September as part of an anniversary celebration. WOW. I don't think we've ever spent so much money on a hotel, but this place was just amazing and was worth every penny. The location is probably the best in all of Venice -- a few minutes walk to St. Mark's Square, sitting on the shores of the Grand Canal.  You arrive by water taxi from the train station, and gondoliers go floating by each evening. The breakfast buffet (included) was terrific and could be eaten on the hotel's patio overlooking the Grand Canal. The concierge was fabulous -- very helpful in arranging whatever we wanted/needed.
