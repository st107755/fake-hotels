In general we were satisfied with our stay and would stay again.   The older part of the hotel (Il Palazzo) overlooks the Grand Canal and offers spectacular views from some of the rooms. If you book the hotel on another website, you are certain to get a room that has a view of the building that is 5-10m away (and that is the best case, since some rooms face miniscule internal courtyards).
