We stayed at Il Palzzo in late September for 2 nights. It is a gorgeous historic building right on the Grand Canal. Our room on the 7th floor was spacious and roomy. We lunched at the hotels water front restaurant on the first day. The meal was delicious, the location superb and the wait staff very professional. The breakfast on the 7th floor terrace was a real highlight. I made the mistake of booking an early train on the morning we left which meant we only had breakfast once. Rookie mistake. Book a later train and enjoy this lovely experience as much as you can. Venice as a whole is beautiful and unlike anywhere else, but it's also expensive compared to other parts of Italy. When you consider that everything needs to be bought in by boat you can understand the cost.
