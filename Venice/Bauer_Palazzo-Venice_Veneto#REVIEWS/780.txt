This is the second time we have stayed here, the hotel is in a perfect location, has a lovely terrace over looking the canal, and you can enjoy a great breakfast or lunch in the sun..  We had a super lunch, of fish and squid and pasta, great service and lovely sommelier.  The breakfasts are nicely presented, with a good wide variety, and great coffee.  The hotel is very close to St Marks Square and whichever direction you set off, you will come across great boutiques, restaurants cafes and bars to stop for an Aperol Spritz.  Our room did not have a view, we looked into an inner small bright green courtyard, however this didn't bother us as we didn't spend any time in the room, If you're booking check your view if it bothers you.
