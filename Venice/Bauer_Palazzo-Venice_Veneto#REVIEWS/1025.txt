The Bauer Il Palazzo is a beautiful hotel perfectly situated just off St. Mark's Square and is a fine alternative to the Cipriano for those who can afford the best but don't want to have to take a motoscafo every time they need to venture into town. We were with a group of friends and for some reason got a great upgrade to a 6th floor double suite with a canal view. Others of our group got rooms more cramped or with no views so their experiences were decidedly different. The service in every sense was first rate from the housekeeping staff to the concierge and front desk. Breakfast on the roof was lovely with everything you would want and served by a very friendly and professional crew. Great eggs and granola as well as the usual Italian meats and cheeses.
