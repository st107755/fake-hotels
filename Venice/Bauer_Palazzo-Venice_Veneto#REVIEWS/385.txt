To be completely honest the best part of this hotel is the rooftop terrace for cocktails or a bottle of wine in the evening.  We got amazing views of the sunset and it was really romantic but 48 euro for 2 bellini cocktails?!  The breakfast was also very good, decent selection and a really nice view of the canals. WI-FI is reasonable and the hotel has a great location for touristy stuff or if you enjoy shopping in the likes of Louis Vuitton or Gucci.  Now that I've got the positives out of the way here come the negatives: Understandably I booked the standard classic room but it was incredibly basic.  It just didn't feel like it was worth £100's per night. I booked a double room, even though we received a twin.  They did offer to change it but we didn't bother.
