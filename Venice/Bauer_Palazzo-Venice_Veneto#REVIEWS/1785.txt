We were coming off a Windstar cruise and needed to stay overnight in Venice to catch an early flight the next morning. At 360E a night for 2 rooms we were located in the CasaNova annex. We were not upgraded as the hotel was fully booked for the Historical Regatta, but that was fine. It never hurts to ask. The man at reception said "Follow me..." and we proceeded to follow him out the doors and into the small square in front of the hotel.
