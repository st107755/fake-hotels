My wife and I just came back from a 3 night stay in Venice where we stayed at the Bauer Hotel.  Overall we thoroughly enjoyed the weekend and the Hotel was great, especially the location which cannot be beaten, but just some tips which might help ensure you enjoy it...  1.  Make sure you get breakfast included in your rate.  It is marvellous to sit on the terrace right on the grand canal, but if it's not in your rate...wait for it...it costs €50 per person...!!!  2.  My wife and I sat on our last night on the same terrace and had a couple of goodbye Venice drinks...wait for it...€70 for two vodka and coke and two Spritz...  Lastly, the room was really nice but lacks some amenities you would expect in 5 star hotels such as wifi, iron etc....
