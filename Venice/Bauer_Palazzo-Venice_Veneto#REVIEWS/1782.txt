Wow! We arrived on a private taxi from the train station, our first trip to Venice proper.  What an arrival!  From the private dock off the lobby, to the excellent service from the concierge, we were swept away by the romance that is Venezia! Our room didn't have a view, but the accommodations were so outstanding, and while a view would have been nice, simply going to breakfast and enjoying the buffet next to the Grand Canal was absolutely amazing and certainly allowed us to enjoy the sites! Watching the Gondolas, listening to musicians play their tunes for all of us to enjoy...charming!  The hotel has a lounge/club with DJ and small band that was fun to enjoy since Venice does shut down fairly early.  We had such a wonderful time wandering through the streets of Venice!
