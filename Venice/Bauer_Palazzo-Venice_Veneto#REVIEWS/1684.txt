Just returned from four nights at the Bauer hotel, Venice.   I must have read most, if not all of the trip adviser reviews before booking this particular hotel, as well as some from other review sites. I was a little anxious before arriving, in fear of being put into one of the back rooms which may have been older and smaller. We stayed in room 327 and 328, next door to each other which was a bonus. (and something we had requested of time of booking).  POSITIVES; Most of the reviews give much hype about the wonderfull location, so i was not at all surprised to find this is the case. I dont think it is posible to get accomodation anywhere better in Venice.   Buffet breakfast; We found there was a good selection and it ran from 07:00 until nearly 11am.
