This hotel was full of surprises, A fabulous terrace directly overlooking the Grand Canal in one of the best Venice positions and staff who were impeccable and hugely courteous. The Art Deco lobby is impressive and connects with the adjacent somewhat grand Bauer Palazzio. Together they share the terraces, bars and dining rooms so why stay at the more expensive Palazzio?  Shuttle boat connects across the canal to the Spa and the water taxi from the airport arrives directly at the hotel entrance. Our room (suite) was spacious and very ordinary in tems of decor.Excellent bathroom and sitting room off the bedroom. 80 euro was the intended charge for a dip in their roof top jacuzzi - Ridiculous price.
