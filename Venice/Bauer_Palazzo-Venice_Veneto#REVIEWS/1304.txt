Bauer Il Palazzo has luxurious rooms and a quite wonderful breakfast terrace but there are one or two aspects which detract from a magnificent experience. First the high points. We had four nights in a de luxe room with a balcony looking out on the Grand Canal and the room was marvellous and beautifully furnished. The literal high point is the terrace on the 7th floor which seems to be the highest such terrace in Venice and has quite wonderful panoramic views encompassing the Campanile, the Grand Canal and especially Santa Maria della Salute. The concierge and other staff were all extremely helpful. Another special feature is the shuttle over to the sister hotel Bauer Palladio on the Giudecca which has delightful gardens and where we plan to stay on a future visit.
