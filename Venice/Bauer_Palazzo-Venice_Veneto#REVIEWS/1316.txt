We went to Venice to celebrate my fifteenth birthday and to visit the Venezia Film Festival. We arrived at Aeroporto di Venezia in the morning and then we took a watertaxi to Bauer. We were welcomed by two friendly guys on the private jetty who helped us getting off the boat smoothly. Then already we were blown away by the magnificent views on Venice and her Grand Canal. The location of Il Palazzo couldn't be better to get to know the city.   After checking in, we were brought to our room, which wasn't really big, but well-equiped and clean. The room was lovely designed and had royal allures. The classical Venetian style of the hotel with golden, ancient green en red details created an atmosphere that I liked.
