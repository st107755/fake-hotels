We booked a short, 4 day, break in Venice staying at the Bauer Pallazo Casa Nova. The hotel is located about 75 metres from St Marks Square. The street between is full of shops which delighted my wife, but not my wallet. When we arrived at the hotel we found that due to weather and the Corona Virus outbreak the city was quite quiet, as was the hotel.   The reception process was as smooth as at any hotel I have stayed at. Due to the situation we were upgraded to a room in the main hotel. We were taken to our room and had the facilities explained to us. Room was smallish (but the hotel is in a 19th Century building) so it is to be expected. The bed was large and extremely comfortable. The bathroom was well fitted with everything we required.
