We have stayed in quite a few hotels in Venice over the years but nothing and nowhere that has the sheer elegance and discrete luxury of the Bauer. The hotel has an amazing location - overlooking the Grand Canal and literally two to three minutes from Piazza San Marco.  If you are staying in one of the Palazzo suites you will have the pleasure of breakfasting at roof terrace level on Floor 7 and be tended by the charming and highly knowledgable Lucia.If you don't have this benefit breakfast is in the De Pisis Restaurant downstairs and is equally delicious ( it's the same food ) and you can look over the canal, which is a superb view. The staff are wonderful and a joy to deal with. They are friendly and tread that fine but important line between over-famliarity and aloofness.
