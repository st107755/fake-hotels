We were initially disappointed that the view from our room on the fifth floor was of a wall, even though it was very attractively painted! The bed was very comfortable and towelling robes and slippers were provided. The marble bathroom was attractive and very spacious, however due to the design of the bathtub not so easy for those with dodgy hips to get in and out.
