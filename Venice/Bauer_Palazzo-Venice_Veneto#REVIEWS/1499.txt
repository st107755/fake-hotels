The Bauer Il Palazzo is located well at the beginning of the Grand Canal and very close to San Marco. The Bauer Il Palazzo is the 300 year old hotel located on the canal and connected to the newer (100 year old) Bauer. Unfortunately, it rained all the time that we were there, otherwise the terrace would have been a lovely location to take advantage of. I was there to celebrate my daughter's 18th birthday with her, so it was great to be upgraded to a suite - albeit that we were located overlooking the alley, so no view at all, but the suite was very spacious and comfortable with 2 bathrooms. We also received a surprise and complimentary bottle of Prosecco on my daughter's birthday.
