I had initially believed I had booked the Hotel Bauer. It was only after booking I realised I was in the casa nova annexe. One little moan here at British Airways holidays - they do not make it clear at all that these are the rooms on offer. I then had a bit of a panic and wanted to switch hotels but they refused. Anyway, it's actually really lovely and I'm not just saying this but I'd rather be in here than in the main hotel, which is very grand but is pretty big. It depends what you like, but I prefer smaller hotels, but love a bit of luxury too.The casa nova annexe is out of the door of the main hotel and literally 20 steps away. The rooms are beautiful, very traditional and a really good size. Bathroom is huge, all marble, with large bath and separate shower.
