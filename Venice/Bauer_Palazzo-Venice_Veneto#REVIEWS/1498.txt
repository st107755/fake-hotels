This old palace is perfectly locates (few minutes walk from St. Marks Square, La Fenice, Musica a Palazzo..).  The Bauer is really a special place and the staff makes you feeling you are special. I celebrated my birthday in Venice and stayed with friends at the Bauer Il Palazzo and everything was just perfect.  The small cozy lobby-level lounge has a wonderfully relaxed library ambiance with tea, water and spumente. The ideal venue for waiting the water taxi.   Having breakfast at Settimo Cielo overlooking la Salute is just heaven on earth… Though the weather was cold and unfortunately the terrace was not open, the view was a pure delight.  The beautiful rooms have high stucco ceilings. I had a lovely room on the 2nd floor with both a view on the rio de S.
