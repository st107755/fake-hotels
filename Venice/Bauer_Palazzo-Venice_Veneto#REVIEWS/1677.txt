Arrival by water taxi was wonderful! and being ushered into the quaint lobby was great.  We had booked a deluxe room with a view.  We were taken to room 103 on the first floor.  The room was very elegant, but the view was only really available if you had your head out of the window!!  Shortly after arrival water started pouring through the ceiling of our bathroom.  (looking at the ceiling this was not the first time) and the management moved us to (?upgraded?) a room on the 8th floor.  The room was not as "grand" with low ceilings, but we did have views of the roof tops of Venice which was lovely.   Breakfast was served on a terrace on the 7th floor which was fantastic, with amazing views. We had dinner one evening in the hotel restaurant, outside right on the Grand Hotel.
