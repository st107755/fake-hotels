We booked a four night stay on the Palazzo side of the hotel in a deluxe room with partial view of the Canal Grande.    The hotel is easily accessible by walking from San Marco or by water taxi with its own water taxi stop.  The front desk staff are friendly and professional, and the hotel overall feels very well kept despite the age.   There are two distinct sides in this hotel.  The palazzo side is the original historic building with extremely tall ceilings and exquisite details.   The other side is the newer building that has many more floors and a lot more rooms.  We were lucky to be assigned a room on the palazzo side with a balcony.  Only a few rooms on the palazzo side have balconies.
