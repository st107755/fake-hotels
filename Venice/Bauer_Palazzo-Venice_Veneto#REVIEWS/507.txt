Not knowing it was in the middle of carnival when I planned my arrival, we took a water taxi from the airport in the morning and in no time arrived at the hotel where suitcases were quickly off loaded and we were checked in. Our junior suite faces part of the San Moise canal and the Grand canal and it was very well decorated and roomy. Breakfast was taken at the top floor of the hotel with great views of the city.
