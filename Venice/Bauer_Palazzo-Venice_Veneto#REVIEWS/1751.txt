We have just returned from a fantastic 3 night stay in the Bauer Hotel.  There are 3 'parts' to this Hotel - Il Palazzo (the original and most expensive part of the building overlooking the Grand Canal), L'Hotel (the 1940's section adjoining the rear of Il Palazzo overlooking a side canal and square) and Casanova (an annexe adjacent to the Hotel).  We stayed in the 1940's section - a little cheaper than Il Palazzo, but essentially the same building.  We had booked a 'deluxe room with a panoramic view' and upon arrival, were invited to view a selection of rooms to see which one we would prefer - not an easy task as each room offered something a little different from the others and all were well equipped to the extent of having scented toilet tissue!
