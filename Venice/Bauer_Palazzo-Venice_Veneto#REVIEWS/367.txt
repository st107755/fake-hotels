So we were in Venice for 3 nights and we booked the Bauer due to the location and fairly solid reviews.   Firstly, the hotel is beautiful and situated very close to the important sites like St. Mark's Square, Doge's Palace, and St Mark's Basilica. Everything is walkable.  However, there were some issues. Firstly, our assigned rooms were below the roof so you can constantly hear the chairs being dragged around.  But the hotel did change our rooms a few floors below as we requested. So that was good.   The major problem was when I requested the complimentary water as the housekeeping may have forgotten. One room in our group received theirs but mine did not. I had to ask nearly 3 times in a span of  6 hours it took for the water to be brought up.
