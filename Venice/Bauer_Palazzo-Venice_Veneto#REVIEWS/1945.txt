Every once in a while if you are lucky, you get to stay at an wonderful world class hotel.  The Bauer Il Palazzo is one of those cases.  We stayed here for 2 days during carnivale this past February and had a gorgeous room overlooking the side canal with a great view of the grand canal from our terrace.  The bathroom is a palace unto itself and the staff is first rate.  This is one of the finest hotels I have ever stayed at, if not the best and my girl friend and I were treated like royalty.  When you go for breakfast here you should get there first thing and watch the sunrise from the roof.  One must do is to have them call a private water taxi to go to and from the train station in off of the hotels private dock on the grand canal.
