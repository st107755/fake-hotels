We were booked into the annex and were upgraded on arrival by very pleasant desk staff. I'd e mailed beforehand to check we could leave luggage, as we were on an early flight. I received a very polite and helpful reply. We were given our room key and our luggage was taken up for us by a porter. On arriving in the room we were amazed to find we were in a room overlooking the canal at the side of the hotel. It was much larger than I had expected and beautifully clean. As we had booked 3star we were very pleased to find ourselves in 5 star. Staff were very pleasant and polite, always willing to answer questions.  Breakfast was quite good but not worth the 50 euros they were charging. Luckily we were staying on a B&B basis so didn't have to pay.
