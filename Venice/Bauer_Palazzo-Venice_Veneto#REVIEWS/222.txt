I booked the Bauer Palazzo after having carried out some research on the locations of various hotel, the types of rooms and the ratings. The hotel was in the perfect location! 5 minutes walk from St Mark's Square, on the Grand Canal, within walking distance of the Rialto bridge, great restaurants and gelaterias, and Vaporetto stops.   The hotel itself was quite big, with a big lobby with seating and a number of check in staff and a concierge.
