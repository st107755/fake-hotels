After researching hotels in Venice I booked Bauer Palazzo hotel which is situated on the grand canal. This hotel’s location is fabulous. You are a 5 min walk from St Mark’s Square.  The water bus stations are also a 5 min walk,  and the hotel has a water taxi service any time you require a taxi. If you walk out of the main entrance you arrive on a road filled with designer shops.  The hotel rooms are clean and a good size with the bathrooms also a good size. We had 3 rooms, 562-564. Located on the 5th floor. The views from these however look onto other rooms within a very close gap in between. The facilities in the rooms are basic. The TV’s do have one English Channel and one American channel and unfortunately reception on these are grim.
