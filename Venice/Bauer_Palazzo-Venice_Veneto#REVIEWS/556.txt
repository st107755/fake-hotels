I arrived here as a surprise from my husband, our 5 years anniversary.  The hotel is very grand, and the location is very central. The hotel is 2 minutes walk from Saint Marks Square.  Hotel room 653 is where we stayed no view, the room was clean and the bathroom room except from the water marks on the black tiles. Our room was cleaned daily, and sheets and towels changed everyday. We stayed for 4 nights. We had breakfast which was part of our booking. Bauer has 4 buildings, we stayed in the main building. One night, I could here the room next door snoring away, which was irritating, but with room walls pretty thin, this was to be expected. As a complementary visit, the hotel offered us a boat ride to Murano island and visit to a factory, this is pretty amazing.
