Went to this hotel for our 25th wedding anniversary. We were not in the main hotel but we're in adjoining accomadation. Initially we were a bit disappointed but that was soon irradicated. I loved the room. My husband felt we should have a better room and asked and staff said they would upgrade and give us a reduction on the room but I was more than happy were I was. Our room was lovely it reminded me of an old fashion ship, it was spacious and comfortable. I was a bit confused with the small kitchen area with a microwave and a 2ring stove and  sink but no cups or saucepans, no kettle and no tea/coffee facilities, so maybe that could be upgraded. Staff lovely, decor of hotel beautiful. Breakfast excellent. It was quite expensive but we were in the heart of Venice.
