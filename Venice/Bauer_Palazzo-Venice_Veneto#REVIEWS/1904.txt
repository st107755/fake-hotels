We went for the Bauer hotel as it was central, near St Marks Square and good value compared to their normal rack rate.  The entrance was rather Soviet looking and a bit stark.  We stayed in room 242 a deluxe room – and it cost £415 for 3 nights inc breakfast. (We could have got a slightly cheaper rate if we booked last minute)  The room was decorated in rich blue fabric on the walls and gold drapes and bed covering. The main wall was clad in a modern style wooden panelling that housed a TV – no DVD player.  Not that we went to watch TV but the only English language channels were BBC world News, CNN and Sky Cinema.   The mini bar had the usual at medium – high prices.  No complimentary bottles of water in the room.  No additional blanket or pillows in the room.
