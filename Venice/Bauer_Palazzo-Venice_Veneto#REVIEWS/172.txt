Really looking forward to returning to Venice and staying in our Superior room.  But what a disappointment.  Front desk staff unfriendly and aloof. When I queried this with front desk I was told it was part of an extension building and it was a Superior Room, Superior to what!  I would have made a fuss but we were very late checking in and tired and it was only a 2 night stay and we were out all the time.
