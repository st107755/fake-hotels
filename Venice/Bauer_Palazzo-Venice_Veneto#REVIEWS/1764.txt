My husband booked the hotel as a birthday treat, a nice relaxing weekend exploring Venice, a destination I had wanted to visit for a very long time. The hotel was excellent, they upgraded us to one of their suites - beautiful room, two bathrooms and a separate seating area and bedroom. They also placed a bottle bubbly in the room as well. We arrived quite early, taking a river taxi from the airport to the hotel (this a must if you are going to Venice, a nice fun way to arrive, especially at the Bauer as it has a private pier) and we could go straight to our room. Breakfast was superb, and on the second day it was served on the terrace overlooking the Grand Canal which made it all the more special. Good selection of pastries and fruit.
