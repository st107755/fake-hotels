This is a great hotel. The location is perfect. The rooms are perfect. The staff are perfect. We stayed a week and the only thing which annoyed me was the fact that house keeping wanted to initially  charge 5 Euros to deliver a kettle to the room, which was simply crazy given the 5 star status of the hotel. I think they soon realised the absurdity of this delivery charge themselves and I got the kettle with "free" delivery included! Other than this strange experience, everything else is wonderful about this hotel.   My main reason for giving it 5 stars is because of the superb staff and management which this hotel has. They are VERY welcoming, friendly and helpful. In a city like Venice, despite being tourists, we were made to feel and be special during our one weeks stay.
