My fiancee and I have just spent two wonderful romantic nights at the Bauer hotel in Venice (16-18 September 2011). We originally reserved a four star hotel elsewhere with impeccable reviews, but decided to risk (because of some mixed reviews) a change to a deluxe room at this hotel, due to its superb location, 5* rating and a marginal price advantage (606 euro B&B for the two nights vs 740). We are glad that we did. From the onset the staff were helpful and friendly. The concierge provided us with excellent advice and provided complimentary boat services. The grandeur of the lobby area and the wide corridors to the rooms made you feel that this was once a grand place indeed. The room was comfortable, clean and well maintained. Housekeeping kept it that way.
