What a beautiful hotel old and chic, fabulous decor!  Dropped off from the airport on a private water taxi costing €110 but a lovely couple asked to share so split the cost and took under half hour to the hotel.. otherwise there are public taxi which we got on our return at €15 each but took 1 hour and half approx with several stops!  Stepped off the taxi to the door to a beautiful reception, the concierge and hotel staff couldn't do enough took our luggage to the room, told us about the 'Jazz at the bauer' which is every Friday night In the hotel.  We stopped in the deluxe room beautiful huge bed, huge wardrobes, bath and shower over bath, shutters that were black out so had a lovely sleep! Beds are quite firm which I liked but could be too firm for others!
