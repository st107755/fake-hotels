Though not staying at the Bauer I booked a table for their "Jazz athe Bauers" concert on Aug. 14th. Unfortunately as the band were just into their second number the heavens opened and heavy rain forced the concert to be abandoned. Staff were everywhere trying to clear tables of drinks,snacks,amplifiers, instruments etc etc. Pandemonium. I had bought 2 drinks , but because of the melé was unable to find someone to pay  immediately so left with the intention of returning next day. On my return next day I went tothe head barman on duty (Daniele) and explained I wished to pay my bill of the previous night (22 euro), and of course explained why I had not previously paid.
