First, there are less expensive hotels in Venice and there are far more expensive as well. Simply put if you are on a limited budget there are better options. But if you might want to splurge just a little or if you have unlimited funds and would like to save a few euros, this is the place for you. The Bauer Palazzo has a room or suite for almost any budget and they are all wonderful rooms. Our room was small but if you’ve traveled around Europe most likely you’ve experienced a little smaller rooms sometimes when you were expecting something larger. Our room was very well appointed with a small but elegant bathroom. Our view out the window was spectacular even though we were not facing the Grand Canal.
