This hotel was within about two minutes walk of St Marks Square - the perfect spot for sightseeing and shopping. We booked a Deluxe Double room with a City View. The room was large and we had the bonus of our own private not-so-small balcony which was nearly as big as our room (we think the balcony was around 7m x 4m). The balcony looked out over a square and an old church and, complete with deckchairs, was a lovely place to sit on a sunny afternoon. The furnishings in the bedroom were lovely and we had the added security of shutters (closed at night by the staff who turned the bed down) on the balcony. The windows could still be opened if your preference was for fresh air.
