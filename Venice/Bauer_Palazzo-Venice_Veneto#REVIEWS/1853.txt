The hotel was not at all what I expected. The hotel was bit shabby and dated.   We arrived and were told we were upgraded to a better room. I would hate to see what the room I paid for looked like. At almost 600 Euros a night the hotel was not worth the 5 star cost.  The lobby was in great need of a buffing and polishing. The staff was arrogant with no reason to be so. The elevators held only 4 people at a time and were similar to the old freight elevators.   The room was small - very small (remember this is an upgrade). The bathroom was so small the commode was hidden behind the door. The shower/tub was raised so high I needed help getting in and out of it. I am not sure what an older person or child would do. I actually fell getting out. The service was not great either.
