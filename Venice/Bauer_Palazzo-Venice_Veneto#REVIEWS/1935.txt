We have just returned from 3 nights in Venice at the Bauer Hotel. The location is second to none - literally a minute from St Mark's Square and, despite the rather odd modern appearance of the city side of the hotel, it overlooks a tiny pretty canalside square in Venice where the gondoliers congregate. The piece de resistance is undoubtedly the terrace - it is better than you imagine - even after reading the reviews and looking at the photos! The highlight of my weekend was watching the bustle of the grand canal in the early morning sunshine over breakfast. That in itself makes the Bauer the best choice in Venice. Don't be put off by the high prices - shop around - we got a great deal. The standard price for our room was 680 euros per night - we got the 3 night trip for 840.
