Luxurious hotel in great location Review of Bauer Palazzo Pending review  The Bauer Palazzo is a beautiful and luxurious hotel situated two minutes from St Mark's Square. The Doge Palace is 3 minutes away and the major sights such as the Academy of Art, Peggy Guggenheim, Opera House and the Rialto bridge are short walks away. There are countless excellent restaurants close by but to get the best prices avoid the main squares and go down the narrow side streets.  The staff are exceptional and couldn't have been more helpful. The rooms are clean, spacious and comfortable and I couldn't find any faults. This is a 5 star hotel so the extras are expensive (150 euros for a Jacuzzi!) but if you avoid the mini bar and champagne in your room you'll be fine.
