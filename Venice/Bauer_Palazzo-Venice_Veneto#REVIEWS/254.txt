Arriving from the Orient Express (far from shabby!) we arrived at the Bauer Palazzo and were whisked to our suite overlooking the Grand Canal - which began an absolutely delightful 3 day stay. Breakfast on the 7th floor terrazzo - with its unparalleled views of Venice, and the Grand Canal, unfolding in all its color and movement - was fabulous. The Palazzo is right next to St Mark's Square - and is surrounded by shops, which for my treasure seeking wife , were a constant magnet. The entire staff could not have been more helpful, friendly and courteous.We were constantly wishing our Italian was half as good as their English. But it was the soul of the Palazzo which won us over from the very beginning. It's not at all like your typical hotel.
