We stayed here in April 2011 for five days. Took a river taxi from the airport and delivered to the hotels dock. The lobby is very nicely decorated, definitely something you would expect from a 5* hotel in Venice.  We were immediately upgraded and had a choice of rooms that they showed us. the room we chose had a view over the canal and we could hear the Gondoliers singing to their passengers.  The hotel is located in the heart of Venice. You couldn't get a much better location. A stones throw from restaurants, shops and the Piazza San Marco.   Breakfast is buffet style but you can sit outside overlooking the Canale di San Marco. The view is awesome.  You can enjoy the same view from the outside patio bar as well. Make a note the bar staff are brilliant.
