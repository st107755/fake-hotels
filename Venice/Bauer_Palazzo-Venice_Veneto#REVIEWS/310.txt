Spent 4 amazing nights at the Bauer Palazzo this September (my second stay here). This is a truly magical hotel, which is one of my very favorites in the world.  A room on the grand canal here is just sublime--particularly if you get a room with a balcony, as we did for our most recent stay. What is especially lovely is that you don't just have views of the Grand Canal (as you would from the Gritti), you also have views of the lagoon, and San Giorgio and Giudecca.  Being able to sit on your own terrace watching the sunset over San Giorgio and Santa Maria Della Salute and listening to the birds, or waking up to watch the quiet grand canal at sunrise when it's just the vaporetto and the working boats, is a truly amazing experience.
