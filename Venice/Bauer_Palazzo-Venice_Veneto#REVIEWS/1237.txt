If you have the urge to splurge, this is the place for you. A old style, classic 'palace' hotel, with absolutely the best location one could have in Venice. We had a room on the 5th floor with a terrace overlooking the grand canal.. views of magnificent monuments, boats and gondolas, even the occasional ship..You step out of the completely silent and peaceful hotel and the crowded, noisy San Marco square is precisely a 2 minute walk. Breakfast on the rooftop is the perfect way to start the day, the views of the city are awesome..The staff was very hospitable and service excellent.. Our boat trip to the Bauer spa across the grand canal was a bit of a disappointment- maybe we should have stayed longer..
