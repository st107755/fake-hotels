Lovely hotel 5 minutes from San Marco.  Great base for all the touristy bits but easy to navigate off the beaten path and explore 'hidden Venice'.   From the moment we walked in every member of staff we had contact with could not do enough for you. All staff from reception to restaurant to housekeeping greet you with a cheery smile and Buongiorno! You can fill up with endless delights and coffee and no need to eat til evening.  A terrace and verandah with what must be one of the best views in Venice.
