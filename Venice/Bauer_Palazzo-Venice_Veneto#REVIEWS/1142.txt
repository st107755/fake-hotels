We stayed at the Bauers for three nights for my husband's birthday. I think the word best to describe the hotel is opulence. The hotel is very centrally located, your are less than 5 minutes walk to St Marks Square. Our hotel room was fine, quite small, typical traditional style. I would of loved tea and coffee facilities though which are not provided in the room. Bathroom was a little dark. The cost of room service is extortionate, I am in no way tight but felt it was a little OTT, although obviously typical of Venice. We had a very basic ham toastie which came with a bowl of crisps and I think it was about 70 euros for us both (and I have to say it wasn't very nice either).
