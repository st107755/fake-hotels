We stayed at this hotel in October 2014. Very good location at the entrance of the Grand Canale providing a beautiful view at its main restaurant where breakfast is also served. The hotel is at the same time just a few steps walk from the Piazzo San Marco, hence very convinient for historical attractions as well as shops/restaurants. (An impressive Michelin star restaurant Qaudri/Alajmo at San Marco and Harry’s Bar – where “the Cipriani” story first started are both a short walk). Certainly only a few rooms have the Grand Canale view, but the ones with an internal view (where we stayed) are big and comfortable though limited daylight. The hotel’s number one attraction (superseeding even its location) is the Concierge!.
