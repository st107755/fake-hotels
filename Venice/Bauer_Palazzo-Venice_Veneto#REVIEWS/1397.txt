My wife had a special birthday due in September, so we deided to celebrate in Venice. We choose the Bauer il Palazzo, near San Marco Square. From the outside it does not give you the impression of a five star hotel, but once you walk through the door, that changes. Marble floors and very expensive furniture soon tells you why this hotel is rated so high. The Hotel has two sections, the very old section and a newer section. Staff at the first check in were very helpful and guided us to reception in the older part. The rooms in the older section look over the Grand Canal and are a little more expensive. If you decide to stay at this Hotel, go for the views in the older section. Just looking over the Grand Canal, with all the activities going on, was a delight.
