Just returned after staying over Easter at the hotel. Arrival at the hotel was by water taxi to the private jetty of the hotel which was an excellent way to arrive and also leave.  We arrived earlier than the recommended check-in time but this was no problem as they had a room ready in 10 minutes.  Room was excellent, large and roomy with an equally large bathroom with corner bath and shower room.  There was no noise at all apart from the bells of the nearby tower but that was soon forgotten.  The hotel situation made for easy access to the Square and only a 15 minute to the Rialto Bridge.  Will definitely be staying at the hotel again and hopefully will get to try the newly furnished Spa.
