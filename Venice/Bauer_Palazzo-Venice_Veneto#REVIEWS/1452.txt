Before getting to the negatives, let me begin by commenting about the positive aspects of this hotel: (1) The hotel is located just steps away from St. Mark’s Square, and sits on the Grand Canal where guests can dine for lunch or dinner alongside the gondolas and engaging activity of the Canal; (2) The rooftop terrace, Settimo Cielo, where breakfast is served offers an amazing view of Venice; (3) The three servers in Settimo Cielo are delightful women (Roberta, Lucia, & Manuela) who welcome guests with a smile and an eagerness to be of service.  (4) The guest room in which we stayed (652) was well decorated and reasonably spacious (by Venice standards), and was decorated with expensive fabrics, bedding, and towels.
