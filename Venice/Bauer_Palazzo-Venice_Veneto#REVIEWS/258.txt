Wonderful location, directly on the waters of the Canal Grande, opposite the Dogana and the Chiesa di Santa Maria della Salute. Even though it is located right in the middle of the luxury shopping area (Cartier, Hermès, Chanel, etc.), steps away from Piazza San Marco and a short walk from the Ponte dell'Accademia and the Guggenheim Collection, maybe because of its recessed position on the Calle San Moisé, it gives you the feeling of being out of the crowd, in a peaceful heavenly retreat. Everything here exudes comfort: the vast imposing hall, the unpretentiously kind staff, the spacious, old style but immaculate and elegantly appointed rooms, the rich breakfast buffet.
