The BAUERs is truly an exceptional concept and hospitality experience. To begin with, the location lapped by the waters of the Grand Canal with the splendours of Piazza San Marco a stone’s throw away is hard to fault. We have had the pleasure of staying both at the hotel and the Palazzo, both of which are the last word in elegance and luxury. The Venetian soul just seeps out of the fabrics, glass artworks and general ambience – testament to the passion and care with which the complex was renovated and infused with all that is chic. This is not ‘just’ luxury; this is history lovingly brought to new life.
