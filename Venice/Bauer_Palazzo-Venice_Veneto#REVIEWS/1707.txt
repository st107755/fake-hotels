The last time we were in Venice we stayed in the Bauer Palazzo. This time, the Palazzo was nearly full so the only rooms remaining were very high priced. Since Venice is already super expensive, we elected to stay in the Bauer. We booked a Deluxe City view room which was nice but very small (not unusual for Europe) but they gave us a room with a huge deck. It had a table and 4 chairs and looked out on the campo below in front of the San Moise church and the Prada / Versace/ Bottega Veneta stores. Wow! We got carry out food every day and ate outside on the deck. We had views in 3 directions of all of Venice. Just incredible! The hotel would have been fine without the deck but it was spectacular with it.  It is an old hotel in Venice and is just what I expect.
