From the minute we walked into this hotel, the day before New Years Eve it was magical.  The foyer was beautiful, there were Christmas trees everywhere and the Staff couldn’t do enough for you. Our first breakfast was a delight, sat in a window overlooking the Grand Canal and watching the gondolas.  The staff at breakfast were so helpful and genuinely welcoming. The whole hotel had a lovely ambience about it. The view from the top terrace, which we visited, was amazing and we took some remarkable photos. Our room was not large but was warm and beautifully furnished and kept spotlessly clean, the wi fi was a bit hit and miss but this is Venice.  Although we didn’t have a Canal View we could hear the gondoliers singing in the smaller canal below us.
