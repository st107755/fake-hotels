My husband and I stayed at the Bauer hotel and had a great experience.  Venice is a VERY expensive city with many places to stay.  We chose the Bauer Hotel and booked a Casanova Suite.  From the name, we weren't really sure what to expect.  But, for the price we were willing to take a gamble.    Even though we did not have a view of the canal, our room was great!  Pleasant suprise!  The Casanova Suites are meant to be more like apartments.  Our room had a small fridge, a microwave, and a small cooktop stove.  (even though we didn't use it, this is great for travelers on a budget, especially in Venice) Our bed was large and comfortable.  The bathroom was large with the biggest shower of all the hotels we stayed in during our trip.  Housekeeping came to our room twice a day.
