The Bauer Il Palazzo is one of two Bauer hotels in Venice - both housed in the same building.  However, the Il Palazzo is predominantly housed in the old palazzo, facing the Grand Canal,while the Bauer L'Hotel is at the back of it looking at a piazza.  The hotel is a 5 mins walk from Piazza San Marco.  We chose the Il Palazzo because of its location and style (oh, and the fact that it had a gym - a must have for my husband).    We visited Venice in early April 2014.  We chose to use the limousine and private water taxi service on arrival at the airport (and on our return) - bookable through the hotel - it is well worth arriving in Venice and at your hotel in this style - magical.  The Il Palazzo has its own jetty, so you arrive straight at the front door - perfect.
