We approached the Il Palazzo for our first trip to Venice, via a private water taxi. It was a spectacular arrival, enabling us to see the full, stunning frontage of the hotel, at the mouth of the Grand Canal. We received a really friendly welcome and were shown to a lounge area and offered a drink while we were checked in. The reception area of the Il Palazzo was beautifully designed in Art Deco style. The room, by comparison, was a little disappointing. The bathroom was gorgeous, fitted in Italian marble with a spa bath and large shower stall, but the bedroom was a little underwhelming. We did however, have an amazing view stright on to St Mark's Sqaure with the tower immediately in front of our window, so it was a perfectly situated bedroom.
