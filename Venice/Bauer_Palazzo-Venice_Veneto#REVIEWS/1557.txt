This hotel sits directly on the Grand Canal and is a boutique type of attachment to the main Bauer Hotel. It is possible that we were in the same towers as the Bauer as you could access our room through the same elevators shared by the Bauer.  Our suite had a very small balcony with two chairs. We had a view of the canal, but were set back above and overlooking the bar restaurant area.  The hotel across the side canal was under renovation, so the side view wasn't so great. Our suite was spacious and clean. The bed was not the most comfortable nor was the small couch. The shower was on the small side and slow draining but great water pressure. The front desk person was also the concierge. We were always addressed by our names.
