This is a really bizarre hotel.  When we arrived, we came in through the very grand entrance at the sister hotel – The Bauer.  Two lifts and a long walk later we had been led (by a very nice lady) to the not very grand entrance of Il Pallazzo.  Our first room (653) was quite nice, but the view looked directly into five other rooms with less than two metres between us and the other room.  Now, this room was hot.  I mean temperature.  It was mid winter, but the room was a good thirty degrees celcius.  We immediately turned off all heat and opened the window and hoped that it would cool down.  The next day we complained and were told that the head of maintenance would look into it.
