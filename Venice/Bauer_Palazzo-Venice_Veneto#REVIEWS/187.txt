Wow. You can’t knock this hotel on location.   It was perfectly placed right next to the Grand Canal and St Marks Square. Plus you could step into a Gondola literally just outside the front or back doors of the hotel.   Add to that the hotel has its own private launch that will take you ‘free of charge’ to its sister hotel on another island or very importantly to the Murano Glass factory - this is a trip not to be missed as you get a private tour and display of glass making.   The views as you have breakfast are stunning and the staff are super helpful and knowledgeable; restaurants, sights and making bookings for you.   The room itself was in the ‘Old Section’ of the hotel and so had the stunning views.
