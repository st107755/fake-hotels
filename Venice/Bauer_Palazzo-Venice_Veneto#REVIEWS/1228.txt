We were told the Bauer Hotel was a 5 star hotel. We booked the Casa Nova suite annex.  Which is not connected to the Bauer Hotel at all.  You have to walk to a separate alleyway to get to the building.  There is no security or hotel personnel in the lobby.  The only 5 star thing about the hotel was the view of the canal from the breakfast area.  We did have windows to our room.  But the only thing you could see out of them was the building next to us.  There was no clock in the room.  There was no ice or room service.  There was a Mr. coffee but no coffee or accompaniments to go with the coffee. Including cups.  The hotel said there was Wifi but we could never connect.  The breakfast was the same every morning.  Cold scrambled eggs, pancakes, potatoes, bacon, sausage.
