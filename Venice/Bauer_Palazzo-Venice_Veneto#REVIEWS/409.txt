We came to Venice for our honeymoon and stayed in Bauer Palazzo. It was expensive but the service and friendly nature of the staff were fantastic, helping to arrange getting us to the hotel via a chauffer service and then water taxi to the front desk weeks before we even departed to get to them.  We had no problems with our room, large sleeping area with air con, large walk in closet, decent bathroom and a lovely little terrace overlooking the bar/side canal and grand canal. it is perfectly located next to Saint Marks square and breakfast was nice sat up on the roof top bar, despite the birds.  Their evening restaurant on the mouth of the grand canal was beautiful and the food was very good, though again rather pricey.
