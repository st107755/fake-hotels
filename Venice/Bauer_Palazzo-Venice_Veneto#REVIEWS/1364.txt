When we arrived in Venice I found it overwhelming and claustrophbic.  Luckily, this hotel that we booked in advance was on the Grande Canal and we had a room with a view of it.  The room was large, stunning, quiet and a great location; the hotel is elegant.  The breakfast was superb and the coffee was the best we had on our 3 week trip to Turkey, Greece and Italy. The concierge was helpful. In fact, all the staff were always friendly and helpful.  There is a sister hotel on another island and the hotel has a free boat shuttle service to the other hotel. This was a bonus that gave us a chance to get away from the crowds and see another quiet part of Venice.  My only complaint is this: the sheets were linen and dreadful to sleep on.
