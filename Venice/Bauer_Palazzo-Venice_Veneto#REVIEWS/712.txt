Having reached Venice via the Orient Express the Bauer Il Palazzo had a tough act to follow however we were not disappointed.  Although we had booked a deluxe room we were told at the time of booking that it was in the annex of the hotel therefore our expectations were not raised. As it turned out the room was excellent.    We looked out onto a side canal and to the left you could see the Grand Canal. When we opened the window you could hear the Gondoliers signing and chatting below us - fabulous.    The location of the hotel is excellent - two minutes walk from Piazza San Marco with a Gondola stance right beside the entrance.  The terrace bar looks out over the Grand Canal as does the gourmet restaurant (De Pisis), both with ample outside seating (weather permitting).
