We were upgraded on arrival to a luxury room with a side view of the Grand Canal which was a welcome start to our honeymoon. The room and bathroom were clean and spacious. The hospitality and service from all staff encountered was probably the best we encountered in Italy. The breakfast venue on the hotel's roof is spectacular and they serve a good spread of the healthy and not so healthy. The position of the hotel is surely one of the best in Venice - being able to retreat away from the crowds was a great relief but being only 3 minutes walk from San Marco square was very convenient.  My only negative about the hotel is the amount of light bulbs that were out. Each chandelier has two to three lights out which wasn't expected from a hotel of this calibre.
