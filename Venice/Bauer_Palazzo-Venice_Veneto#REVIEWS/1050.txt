This was our third visit to Venice and we wanted something special for this one. After some research I chose the Bauer Il Palazzo and what a choice. The visit began with a   water taxi arrival to the hotel's private wharf opposite the tip of Dorsoduro. Reception is literally 10 yards from there. The reception staff were all extremely helpful and we were shown to our room by a delightful young lady.   The room was stunning with beautiful satin walls and a complete marble bathroom. The bed was extremely comfortable and with the shutters closed we overslept until nearly 10.00am! We dashed down to breakfast for our first experience of the stunning views from the 7th floor terrace.
