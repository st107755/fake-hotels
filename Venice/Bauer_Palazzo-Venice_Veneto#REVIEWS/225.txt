This is a beautiful hotel located on the Grand Canal, and only a hop skip and a jump to St Marks Square.  My husband and I (early 40’s) stayed in a suite on the contemporary side of the building which was huge, particularly for Venice! Whilst our room faced the side canal and the square which is generally quite busy, we did not notice the noise from our room.... who spends much time in their room when in Venice anyway!!!!!!  The staff were extremely helpful and were able to assist with our every needs. The buffet breakfast was lovely and is served on the deck overlooking the Grand Canal, a treat in itself! The cocktail bar on the rooftop affords beautiful views and was certainly a favourite for a late night cap!
