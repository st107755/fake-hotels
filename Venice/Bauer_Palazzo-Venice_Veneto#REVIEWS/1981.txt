For $570 a night the Bauer put us in a superior room so small you hardly can turn around. There is not even place for a wardrobe to put your clothes in, you can hang your clothes above the mini-bar and put them on a board on two meters high (but no stairs to reach that board). The television and remote control (heavy!) belong in a museum, and better ignore the telephone altogether. Shower did not work properly. Only curtains seemed to be thought of during renovation. This surely is not 5* hotel quality. The front desk person told us that this room was were we paid for. Only after insisting and asking if he would like to spend that amount to stay in a cubicle we eventually were upgraded. The deluxe room is much better although not worth $780 by European standards.
