All the staff were exceptionally helpful and courteous.  I visit Venice every two years and this was the nicest hotel I have stayed in.  I read a couple of complaints from other guests and it's true that the hallway (I was on the second floor) was a bit 'cold', no complimentary water, not enough electric outlets for chargers.
