My wife and I stayed here for just one night in July 2011.  We stayed in a 'Superior Suite' which was quite expensive but wonderful.  The cost was partly influenced by it being Biennale time.  Our suite was very large, very tastefully decorated and furnished.  The bathrooms (there were two) were beautifully fitted out and with a great shower.  The king size bed was extremely comfortable.  There was a safe in the room and free WiFi.  The breakfast room and balcony looking out over the Grand Canal was beautiful and the breakfast offerings were excellent.  Breakfast was included in the price of the room.  Service by staff on arrival and throughout our stay was impeccable and warmly welcoming.  So - we should have been happy because we treated ourselves to a nice room.
