We arrived at the Bauer Hotel by Boat Taxi - the staff greeted us with a hand shake and made us feel extremely welcome.   The decor and style of the hotel is grand and spectacular. We were upgraded to a Junior Suite - the room was spacious with a king sized bed, beautiful furniture and a jacuzzi bath! There is air con but we had a balcony with opening windows so there was no need for the air con - which was an advantage.  The view from our suite was amazing - looking out on one of the canals, Prada and Versace!!   We had dinner in the restaurant which was a real treat - the Steak was amazing and all the food was outstanding. We also had breakfast included in our room rate - 50 euros otherwise - American buffet style and all food was of a high quality.
