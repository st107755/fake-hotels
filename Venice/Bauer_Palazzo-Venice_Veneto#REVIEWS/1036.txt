Stayed here for 4 nights on business, but with time to explore and use the hotel facilities. This hotel has the best location for al fresco drinking and dining, or just watching the to-ing and fro-ing on the Grand Canal. Expensive, but wonderful.  But the wait staff don't pressure you if you just want to sit.   Some of our party arrived by motor-launch from Marco Polo airport, others via the 7 euro vaporetta from Piazzale Roma - which takes you to San Marco vaporetta stop adjacent to the hotel. The hotel is situated in a small square with gondolas adjacent, and a short walk to Saint Mark's Square. However, cutting through the calle you can find very reasonably priced meals and bottles of water.
