A property holding the St. Regis banner and placed on its own island in Venice just off of the main center of the city should be a dream come true.  Unfortunately in this case it is not (unless you want to call it a nightmare).   We arrived as a group of twelve on a Sunday in June.  We came by private water taxi arranged by the concierge (who did not respond to several emails until finally I called the hotel manager).  When we docked at the hotel we felt quite alone.  There was not a single staff member there to greet us.  So we took our bags off the boat onto the dock (which was a challenge considering eight of our twelve were either children or grandparents).  When we walked to the lawn we saw a staff member being barraged by guests trying to get onto boats.
