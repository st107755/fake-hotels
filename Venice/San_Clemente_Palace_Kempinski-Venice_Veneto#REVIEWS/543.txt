We have stayed at other 5 star hotels and San Clemente Palace beats them all.  The entire staff bend over backwards to serve their guests at all times, under all circumstances.  We had an incident (unrelated to the hotel) with our luggage prior to our arrival.  Within minutes of speaking to the concierge, the problem was under control.  Our first room was above the stone/brick courtyard across which, very noisy laundry carts are wheeled beginning very early morning until way past our bedtime.  We called the front desk in the morning and we were in our new room within 30 minutes.  We had a large corner room with a wonderful view of the lagoon and Venice.  My only disappointment was, we could not open our windows more than a slight crack.
