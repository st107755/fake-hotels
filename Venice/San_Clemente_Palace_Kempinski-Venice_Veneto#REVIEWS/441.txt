The trip from the airport to the hotel is the best. No high speed taxis or sitting in traffic. Just a Private boat transfer which was totally relaxing. The hotel is on an island with with a regular taxi service to St Marks Sq. The hotel itself is lovely with great grounds and well appointed rooms. The only area it drops below the luxury level is service quality. It's not terrible but it's not consistently great . Around the pool I don't think I was asked once whether we like a drink. You always had to get the attention of the waiter or go to the bar.
