It turned out to be a remarkably smart choice to stay at the St. Regis San Clemente since, despite being the tail end of the holiday season, Venice was packed with sweaty, rude and unsavory throngs of tourists. This place though was like an oasis of serenity. Comfortably away from the hustle and bustle of the city, the calming breeze always blowing across the small island is a joy. Add to that the historic and beautiful architecture of the palace renovated into a 5 star hotel and my wife and I just felt like we were in heaven. They were kind enough to bump us up to a junior suite and although the food here is expensive it's not really much more so than the exorbitant rates being charged in venice.
