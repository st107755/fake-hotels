The San Clemente Palace Kempinski is a truly magnificent hotel.  To this day it continues to serve as the ‘gateway to Venice’ for the those lucky enough to stay there. And we were one such lucky couple at the end of October.  However, the imposing grandeur of the hotel is surpassed by the friendly welcome and attentiveness of the staff.  We were delighted to be told that on arrival we had been upgraded to a junior suite. This was so we would have more room as I use an electrically operated mobility aid to get around.  A very thoughtful and much appreciated gesture.  The concierge team were also so supportive.  Nothing seemed to be too much trouble for hotel staff.  They ensured that I could get in and out of the building with ease and down to the hotel’s shuttle boat.
