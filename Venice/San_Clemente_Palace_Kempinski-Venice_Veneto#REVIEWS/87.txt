We stayed in this hotel as part of a package from Costco travel.  We chose the 'medium' option for a room in Venice.  This hotel was so nice it was hard for us to imagine what option could have possibly been of a higher grade.  The location is great, with views that are stellar.  The shuttle service was reliable and frequent (morning to late night) from the island back to Saint Mark's square.  The staff were welcoming, polite and helpful.  The rooms were clean, well decorated and well maintained.  I would highly recommend a stay here with one caution:  the on site food and drinks are *expensive*.
