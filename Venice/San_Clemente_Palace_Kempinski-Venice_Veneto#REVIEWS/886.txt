Just returned from a three night stay at the St Regis San Clemente Palace and it has been a lovely experience.  This was our first trip to Venice and we wanted something a little quieter, as many reviews mention the noise and bustle of the city.  We weren't disappointed. We were celebrating a special occasion and having booked a deluxe room we were delighted to find the we had been upgraded to a junior suite. The service and attention to detail is second to none. Staff are really friendly and the style of the hotel, although very grand with the enormous corridors and staircase, still feels very comfortable. Prices in the hotel are high, but if you book an inclusive breakfast and then eat in Venice itself, you won't break the bank.
