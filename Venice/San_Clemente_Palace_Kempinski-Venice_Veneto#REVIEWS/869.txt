Secluded on a small island just a stone's throw's away from San Marco's square, the hotel was once one of my favorite hideaway while in Venice. Then it closed down a couple of years ago (because of bankruptcy of the parent company, I believe). It just reopened thanks to The St. Regis. A light renovation, better food and impeccably trained service make it a very welcome come back!
