We only had two nights to enjoy the hotel and grounds, which was not long enough. Lovely large room and enormous bathroom.  We had mixed experience with service. Check in was a delight. Check out was OK, but at least two charges which shouldn't have been on our account needed to be removed.  Trying to find something for my son (with allergies) to eat was very difficult.  We couldn't have coffee in the grounds at breakfast time. You had to go to the breakfast restaurant. The concierge advised it was possible but the staff at the bar/restaurant out side, said they didn't open till 10am. That was a bit annoying as we spent 30mins wandering around trying to get a coffee, but ended up going back to our room to get our bags to check out. Note: no coffee machine in room.
