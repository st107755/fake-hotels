Echoing remarks by other posters, the St. Regis San Clemente Hotel is a beautiful property located on a secluded private island.  The grounds are picturesque with numerous places to sit back and take in views of the Adriatic sea and the main part of Venice.  Walking the halls, it's not difficult to imagine what it would have been like as a European aristocrat roaming their palace.  The hotel definitely has an upscale, European feel.    Location: As one could guess, there are pros and cons of being located on a private island.  I particularly enjoyed the sense of privacy, which seemed like a world away when compared to the madness the San Marco side of Venice.
