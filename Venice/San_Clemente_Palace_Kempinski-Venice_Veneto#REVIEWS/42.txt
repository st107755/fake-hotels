I have been to venice several times.  This is a nice hotel, average rooms but in my opinion better suited for a wedding or meeting as opposed to a hotel that makes you feel like you are in venice.    Location.  On a private island. It is not a 5 minute ride to venice. Each day we took the transport into venice and it averaged 15 minutes depending on traffic and weather. Going home we took midnight book which was much shorter, about 7 minutes   Transportation to and from:  we we arrived into venice via train. We asked the hotel the cheapest option to get there and they kept quoting private transfers. We took a water taxi right from the station which was only 60 euros and easy. It would take you well over an hour in swarms of people to take the vaparetto to the hotel boat.
