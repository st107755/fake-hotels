This luxurious hotel is located on it's own private island which is large enough to feature extensive, immaculately kept grounds; with plenty of bench, sofa and lounger seating to allow gazing over the lagoon, 3 hole golf putting course, large outdoor heated (was freezing when I tried though) swimming pool or gardens, wooded area and reed covered pond.  There is also a tennis court, conference centre and most exclusively a medieval church, lots of white wash to go with all the marble; where you can get married.  Being a former historical building, all the hallways are wide and expansive and the large rooms are more than double height. The hotel is only 3 storeys, so forgo the lift and take the central double staircase to admire the large white glass chandalier.
