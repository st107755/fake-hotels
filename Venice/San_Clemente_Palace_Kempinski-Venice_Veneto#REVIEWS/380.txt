If you want an outdoor pool in Venice, there are only a few choices, and I can recommend this hotel.  The good:  - generous sized rooms and nice bathrooms - pretty island, big, lovely old buildings - attentive service - prompt and frequent boat shuttle.   The bad:  - rip-off prices knowing you are isolated on the island, see later on for examples - smoking permitted at outdoor breakfast in the confined courtyard. I expect this in public in Italy and I don’t mind it at the hotel bars, but a luxury hotel with focus on its spa and island should take the lead and prioritise the health and comfort of other guests in my opinion.
