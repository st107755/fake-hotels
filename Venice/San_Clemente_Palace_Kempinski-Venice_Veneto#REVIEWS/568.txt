We spent five nights in the San Clemente Palace Kempinski late September and early October. It was a wonderful combination to be on a quiet island a 12 minute trip by launch from St Mark's Square. Surrounded by lapping water, beautiful and extensive grounds, thrilling views and being in such easy reach of Venice - a great base for a few days in our favourite city.  If you only have a single day or even two in order to explore Venice, it is probably better to be in a centrally located hotel in Venice itself but if you have the leisure to be there for several days, the San Clemente Palace is a great option.
