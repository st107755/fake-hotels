We has booked the San Clemente as a 'get away from it all break' as a couple, we were excited by the website promise of the hotel being a 'retreat' and somewhere to relax away from the hustle and bustle of Venice. Unfortunately during our stay this was not a place of tranquility, this was a place where families travelling with children to Venice were staying. As a consequence in the afternoon the pool resembled that of a holiday camp with screaming children jumping in and out to their heart's content - no doubt letting off steam after being paraded around Venice in near 40 degree heat all morning. We pointed out the problem to reception staff who simply told us that they could not be responsibe for people's children.
