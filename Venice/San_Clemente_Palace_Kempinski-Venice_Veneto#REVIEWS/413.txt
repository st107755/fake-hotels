This was our base from which to explore Venice for a week. The building was stunning and the island really is 10mins away from San Marco by regular shuttle boats.   Our rooms were spotless and the beds very comfortable.   There was minimal drawer space so we had to keep the suitcases open for the week.   The breakfast buffet was good but there was no printed menu so you had to guess what other items were available. Some of the waiters were clearly in training which resulted in delays and mistakes but it didn't matter much to us, except we felt embarrassed trying to sort out the orders every day.   Room service was an extra 8euros on top of every order including tea (no tea making in the room because it's an old building).
