For our first family visit to Venice, we chose the St. Regis Venice San Clemente Palace, which entirely occupies a small island near Venice itself (10 minute water taxi ride).  Our choice was greatly fortuitous, since we found Venice to be very busy and full of tourists (especially when the cruise ships arrived).  Though we managed to experience Venice thoroughly, the highlight of the trip was definitely the hotel itself.  They have redefined luxury on this oasis of calm (the property is very quiet, especially at night, so you will sleep deeply and awaken refreshed).  The staff is extremely attentive and friendly, often addressing you by name, and no request of them seems impossible to fulfill.
