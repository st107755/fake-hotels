Our friends thought we were insane when we told them that we were going to start our annual summer vacation in Venice—everyone told us the summer crowds in Venice would drive us mad!!! They would have been right except, we chose to stay at Kempinski’s San Clemente Palace which is on its own private island and only a 10 minute private boat transfer from Piazza San Marco—the Kempinski boats run back and forth between the private island and Piazza San Marco every 20 minutes and they are very efficient!!! We ventured into Venice every so often but, we were always so happy to come back to San Clemente and be greeted by the warm and friendly Kempinski staff. Tho hotel is lovely and has been renovated with thought and care and the gardens are huge and well laid out.
