We arrived by private water taxi on the afternoon of 14 June 2015 around 5pm and although there were St Regis staff around at the dock, no one took our bags or offered us umbrellas and we had to walk to the hotel in the rain dragging our bags behind us.  At reception, we were told our room was not ready and offered a coffee in the bar and told we would be notified when the room was available.  After 40 minutes, no one had appeared and I returned to reception to be told we had an upgrade (room 215: quite a dark room with tree view). There was no cold water. After several calls, a maintenance technician appeared and quickly confirmed what I had already told reception and the butler: the taps could not easily be mended  and we would need to change rooms.
