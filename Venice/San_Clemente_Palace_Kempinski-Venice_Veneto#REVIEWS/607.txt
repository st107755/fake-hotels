Situated on its own island  about 15 minutes by launch from St Marks Square the San Clemente Palace is one of the best kept secret vacation locations in Venice.This was our third stay here. The journey by motor launch over the lagoon is part of the experience. They go back and forth every 30 minutes. You pass all the snobs staying at the Cipriani knowing that your hotel has more space and elegance than almost all others in Venice. Beautiful, landscaped setting, with an oval pool, tennis courts, running track  gym and some spa facilities ,all served by attentive and caring but not obsequious staff, makes this the ideal hotel choice for the holiday traveller .Capacious rooms, high ceilings and discrete charm is the essence of the place.
