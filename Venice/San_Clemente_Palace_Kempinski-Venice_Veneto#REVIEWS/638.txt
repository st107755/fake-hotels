This is easily one of the best hotels I have ever stayed in. The location away from the hustle and bustle of St Marks is ideal and the short transfer by one of the hotels beautiful wooden boats across the canals is the biggest selling point for me. The boats are beautiful and luxurious - not just a ferry like most other hotels in Venice that offer a boat transfers but real James Bond style! The Palace is exactly that - just stunning. Rooms and Service were amazing and we felt like we were staying in someone's private residence rather than a hotel. Breakfast was another highlight. Not your standard hotel buffet but several notches above. Friendly staff who all genuinely enjoyed their jobs and pampering their guests at this beautiful hotel.
