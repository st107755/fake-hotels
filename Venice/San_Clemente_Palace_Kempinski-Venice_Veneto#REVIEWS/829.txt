Absolutely gorgeous gardens, lawns and pool area. Great to come back to the tranquility and chance for a swim after a busy morning sightseeing amongst the crowds. The property was once an asylum. The hotel is vast and incredibly spacious, beautifully decorated and not at all stuffy or over formal as some luxury hotels tend to be. The rooms are great, but the main attraction is the fabulous outdoor spaces. Fantastic buffet breakfast can be eaten in the lovely courtyard. Lunch is served outdoors by the pool terrace. There is a lovely walking track around the island through the stunning, lush gardens. Very good service. Half-hourly hotel boat service to and fro St Mark's square.
