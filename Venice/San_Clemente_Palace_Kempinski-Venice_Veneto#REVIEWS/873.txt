My husband and I stayed four nights in May at this hotel. The good bits are- Water shuttle service is brilliant - on time and lovely staff. Eduardo provides a first class welcome - a real asset to the hotel. The pool area is serene and relaxing. The island provides a great escape from overcrowded Venice. Room for improvement - breakfast was a bit of a disaster service wise - a conference and too many clients for the dining room caused chaos. The next morning the hotel tried to solve the problem by serving breakfast in an additional location - this ended up with a more limited choice - no eggs!!! Generally I would suggest for breakfast the hotel needs to: Provide toast (scrambled eggs no good without toast). Have an egg station to solve the egg issue generally.
