We stayed here for one summer week a few years ago. It was a birthday present stay for the 14th July fireworks, which were magnificent to view from this hotel on an island during a dinner which had shockingly bad food. The F&B Manager, however, was very apologetic and understanding and we were offered free drinks at the bar as a compensation, which was nice. The bar and drinks were excellent. Nevertheless, the raw material for food, even breakfast, was poor quality. Frozen bread, for instance, is unacceptable. Other than that, hotel service was excellent, it was very easy to get in and out of Venice town centre. And it was great to be far away from the maddening crowds.
