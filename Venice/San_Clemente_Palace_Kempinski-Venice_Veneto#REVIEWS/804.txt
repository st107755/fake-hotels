Despite an uncomfortable 2 hour delay check-in, this is a beautiful hotel, wonderful grounds, very tranquil, fairly good service (slow by the pool), generally good food, our room well located on ground floor.   This is an excellent option, especially in the summer, where you can escape from the heat and crowds of Venice.   I am not certain if I would recommend it in the winter months.   The 15 minute boat ride that leaves every 30 minutes to/from Venice is pleasant although maybe the hotel should provide some seating arrangement on the dock in Venice so the guests don’t have to stand waiting for the next ferry.  All of this was overshadowed however by our request to one of the concierge to arrange taxi service to the island of Murano.
