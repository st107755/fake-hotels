I stayed at the St. Regis San Clemente Palace in late August, with my daughter. It was with great excitement that we arrived in Venice - I had originally booked at the Westin Europa & Regina but decided to splurge and upgrade to the St. Regis, and I have to say it was a little regrettable. No doubt, this is a lovely and beautiful property.  You can walk the grounds and take it all in and be simply grateful to be in such a beautiful place.  The private water taxi to and from the property is superb - punctual, reliable, enjoyable. However, I've come to expect the best from St. Regis - because they always deliver - and this wasn't it.
