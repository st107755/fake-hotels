We stayed at the San Clemente palace for 4 nights as part of a birthday celebration. We were greeted with champagne and chocolate dessert and a lovely tin of cookies. Very impressed with the hotel, the grounds -- not so much the room, which was small with an offensively small shower even for the slightest stature -- and the ease of traveling via shuttle to and from Venice proper. But above all, it wasn't so much the quality of the hotel as it was about the staff. The "Ladies in Red" is the team charged with customer relations and they were the ones who truly elevated our experience. Wow. Professional and friendly they went above and beyond to ensure we felt welcome and well taken care of.
