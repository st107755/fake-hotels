There is a very limited number of iconic 5* hotels in Venice, but the renovated Kempinski / San Clemente Palace located on the private island of San Clemente facing the Canal, is definitely a landmark hotel which can compete with them.  We spent there 10 days on a private program, and we can only recommend to stay there. The Venice Kempinski offers multiple facilities, a private boat shuttle to San Marco, a team of  4 Concierges, all Clé d’Or, which are extremely helpful, a very friendly reception staff, as well as special guest service called "The Ladies in Red", which will help you to resolve anything you may need and make your stay very pleasant. Further facilities are a very large pool area with a very big lawn, as well as tennis courts.
