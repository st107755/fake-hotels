This is a proper resort experience in Venice and in my opinion the best way to experience Venice. The hotel is located on a private islands. Therefore the only way to access the hotel is by a boat.   The property itself is plush and luxurious. The hotel grounds are green and serene. The pool is lovely. And the restaurant besides the pool is magnificent. We spent an entire afternoon eating Pizzas and Pasta, and drinking lovely Italian wine in the poolside restaurant.   The major apprehension most people would have is the location, at least I did before I visited. But I say don't worry about it at all. The location is the best part of the hotel. It takes 10 odd minutes to get to the main Venitian Island, the hotel has punctual, clean and convenient shuttle service.
