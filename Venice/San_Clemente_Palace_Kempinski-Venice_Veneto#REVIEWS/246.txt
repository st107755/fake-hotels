The hotel itself is great - fantastic rooms and interiors, well decorated. The rooms are very spacious and comfortable, especially when compared with hotels in the the rest of Venice. The wifi also works well and they give you daily complimentary water bottles and a welcome mini bar.   The only advice I would give is to remember that this hotel is located in the private island of San Clemente. There is no public transportation connecting it. This means that food choices are limited to the hotel's own restaurants which are quite overpriced at around 30 euros per dish on average with a quality that does not justify such prices.   Importantly, you are also limited to leaving and arriving to the island by the complimentary boat that the hotel offers.
