My husband and I celebrated our 25th wedding anniversary last August 17, 2016 so we embarked on an Italy & France tour. It was our first time to Venice and admittedly with the hundreds of hotels on the island, it was confusing to look for one let alone visualize its location. Luck came to my side when I discovered San Clemente Palace Kempinski. After browsing the hotel website, right on I wanted to book our stay. At first, my only apprehension was that the hotel was located off the main island of Venice but after reading they had regular boat schedules to ferry guests to San Marco it was a go for us!  It took us 10 minutes from San Marco to San Clemente Palace on board their gorgeous private boat.
