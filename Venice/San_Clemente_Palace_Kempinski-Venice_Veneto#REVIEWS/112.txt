Easy water taxi transfer to this beautiful hotel from the airport. We were really glad we were staying on the island to escape the hustle and bustle of Venice. The complimentary water shuttle to St Marks Square was great, on time, never too busy.   The superior room we had was small and quite dark with high windows but we asked to move to a bigger room which was organised quickly and was lovely. Although all staff were delightful the service left a bit to be desired...asked for an ironing board and iron on the second night and despite 4 calls and assurances it would be delivered shortly it never arrived.   The breakfast buffet was plentiful and great quality but ordered breakfast items took a long time to arrive and had to be chased up.
