The San Clemente Palace is a 5* hotel on its own island 12 minutes by free hotel boat to St Marks Square. It is a beautiful hotel, our room was excellent and the breakfast was first class. We arrived at night and couldn’t get to the hotel due to severe weather, the hotel took care of everything and arranged to put us up in another 5* hotel before collecting us the next morning. We booked through Travel Republic who were also excellent as they phoned the next day to make sure we were ok and the following day called to tell us that the hotel had instructed them to refund our first nights money + this was appreciated and unexpected as the problem was totally out of their control. When we arrived the staff could not be more apologetic and helpful and they also upgraded our room.
