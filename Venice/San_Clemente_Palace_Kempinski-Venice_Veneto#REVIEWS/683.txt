Being Venice my favourite place in the world, I love spending a few days every year in the lagoon. I love the best hotels and the first time I stayed at the San Clemente Palace was in 2004: my impression at that time was more than positive but now...SIMPLY FANTASTIC!  I have been told that there is now a new management running the place from the beginning of 2016 but I could not believe my eyes: the Kempinski has done an excellent job with this dreamy island which is just 10 minutes far from St. Mark Square with a golf course, outdoor swimming pool, tennis fields, surrounded by gardens, wrapped in a magical mantle of peace.  The quality of the service has truly impressed me. Nice and smiling employees everywhere, attention to every detail, in other words: perfection.
