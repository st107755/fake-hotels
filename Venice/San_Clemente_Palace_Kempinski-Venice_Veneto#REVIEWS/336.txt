This was my first time (my wife's second time) at the San Clemente Palace Kemplinski - we stayed for three very enjoyable nights. I defy anyone to find a better/more luxurious hotel in Venice! It is on a island in the lagoon housed in a former monastery. The setting is beautiful, as are the buildings and the gardens. The hotel provides a regular complimentary and reliable launch to/from Piazza Sam Marco. All the staff we encountered were courteous and efficient. Of particular note were the men on the concierge desk - "helpful" would be too small a word (e.g. restaurant recommendations, assisting with luggage etc). Our room was superb, comfortable and spotlessly clean. We ate in the main restaurant one night - excellent; and the breakfast was very good and varied.
