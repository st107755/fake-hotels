An amazing location, hotel & property with so much history, San Clementes is the perfect place to relax from the over populated tourist streets of Venice. The reception staff & concierge are very friendly & helpful. Our room was not large however it was comfortable, extremely clean & had glimpses of the mainland. This hotel is large & there is always a nice surprise around the next corner with its glitz & glamour, it truly feels like a palace. It is both a mixture of new & old blended together.The private shuttle was an excellent way to visit the canals etc of Venice & then escape back in the afternoon from the crowds, relaxing by the pool in the peace & quiet was truly a blessing in disguise.
