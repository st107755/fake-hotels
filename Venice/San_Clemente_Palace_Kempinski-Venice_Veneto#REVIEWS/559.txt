This hotel is elegant and spacious. It has high ceilings, fabulous chandeliers and provides a luxurious experience. The grounds are wonderful and the peace and quiet after the hustle and bustle of venice are to be treasured. Unfortunately the price of the drinks and the fine dining restaurant are disappointing. The restaurant would certainly not get a Michelin star and it is far too expensive for its quality.
