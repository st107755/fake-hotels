We spent a wonderful 4 nights in San Clemente Palace in the run up to Easter.  The hotel is an oasis of calm.  Far from the crowds, but a mere 15 minutes water taxi ride (which is free of charge and departs every half hour) between the hotel and piazza San Marco.  We travelled with children aged 8,10 and 12 and they all loved the wide open corridors, spacious grounds and warm welcome. Breakfast buffet is huge and plentiful with very attentive staff.  Service outside the informal bar area was mixed but the views are amazing and it didn't spoil our trip.  As with any luxury hotel be prepared to pay high prices for onsite drinks and snacks and ask for the kids menu if you need it.  We didn't find out there was one until we'd had a very expensive dinner on our first night.
