I stayed in a Sestiere Suite for my brief stay in Venice.  Taking a water taxi from airport is wonderful way to arrive at the hotel via their private dock. My room had 2 large windows over a side canal which allowed the evening singing from passing gondolas to pass in, it was quite memorable.    I enjoyed dinner and breakfast on the terrace overlooking grand canal, the breakfast buffet was great and very attentive service with many servers while the evening service was a little lacking on my stay, not quite enough people to keep up with occupied tables.  The decor and furnishing throughout the hotel were distinctive and high quality. There are tons of interesting historic photos of past guests on the walls and the entire place exudes history.
