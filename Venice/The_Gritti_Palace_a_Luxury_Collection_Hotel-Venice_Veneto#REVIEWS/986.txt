My husband and I began our silver anniversary trip with two nights at this beautiful property prior to a cruise in June.  We arrived late morning after flying all night, and were immediately shown to our Grand Canal facing room.   Rooms are stunningly beautiful, with beautiful frescos, art, and murano glass chandeliers.  Bathrooms are all marble and are elegantly appointed.  A lovely note, chilled bottle of spumante, fresh berries and chocolates welcomed us, which was an appreciated touch.  The morning -- and sunset view -- of the beautiful church Maria D'Astute is worth ensuring you have the Grand Canal facing rooms -- we threw open our balcony doors to soak in the sights of that and the gondola traffic.
