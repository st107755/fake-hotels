I have always wanted to stay at the legendary Gritti Palace and decided to splurge for my 45th birthday weekend.  We arrived by train from Milan and had arranged for the hotel to pick us up at the hotel with a  water taxi which all went smoothly.  We were given a very nice and large room which the front deck man assured us was one of the best.  It indeed was a very lovely room and I think is probably large for a Venice room.  The hotel itself is lovely in a classic European way and you can feel its legendary presence.  However I still think there are probably other very nice hotels in Venice that are  cheaper - a better value.  A few complaints.
