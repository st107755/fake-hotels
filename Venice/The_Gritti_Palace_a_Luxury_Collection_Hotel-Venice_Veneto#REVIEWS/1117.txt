I have to admit that my expectations of the Gritti Palace were extremely high and if anything they were exceeded. The check in process was more welcoming and personal than any other hotel I had stayed at, to make things even more special we then found out that we had been upgraded to a Grand Canal view room. There were so many little touches such as the complimentary wine and chocolates that just made the hotel seem special. The staff were always friendly and obliging but never intrusive. We were so lucky with the weather for our late October stay which was unseasonably sunny and warm and it was just amazing to have breakfast on the terrace overlooking the Grand Canal each morning.
