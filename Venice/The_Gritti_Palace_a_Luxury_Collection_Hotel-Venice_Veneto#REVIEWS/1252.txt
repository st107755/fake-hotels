A stay at this hotel is without doubt a great privilege.  This was our second visit to Venice and I have somewhat mixed views about the destination. Although an obviously enchanting city, regrettably it undeniably sacrifices much of its charm with the hordes of tourists and especially day trippers and all too of often has the ambiance of a trashy over priced theme park!  Fortunately the Gritti Palace exudes unspoilt charm and provides to a repeat visitor a delightful sanctuary to sample in small doses all that the city has to offer from an incredible location.  Much praise has been appropriately heaped on the Gritti, and for me it's high kudos was generally justified.
