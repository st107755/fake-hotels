My husband and I stayed here recently and while I echo much of the favourable comment of others, we had a few less than positive experiences. When we disembarked from our water taxi I expected a great welcome with people rushing to greet us but this didn't happen and in fact we had to carry our own bags into the lobby where we were then approached and directed rather indifferently to the check-in desk where the young lady was very pleasant and efficient. The rather unwelcoming feeling was not enhanced, for me anyway, by the letter of welcome in our room from the manager which was addressed only to my husband! The room was a canal view room with of course stunning views and a little balcony.
