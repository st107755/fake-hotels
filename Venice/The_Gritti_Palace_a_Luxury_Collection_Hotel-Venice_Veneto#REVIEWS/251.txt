The Gritti Palace is a beautiful hotel. It definitely belongs in the highest of hotel categories. The rooms are opulent, although clothing storage in our room was lacking. The hotel location is ideal with unique canal views and close to most of the major sites. Here is some specific information in case you decide to stay here:   SPG Recognition - We unfortunately we’re not upgraded to a suite since the hotel was full, but were assigned a cute room on the second floor with high ceilings. Platinum breakfast on the terrace included a generous buffet with made to order eggs. Two complimentary bottles of water were provided daily.   Customer Service - Overall customer service was very good. Roberta at check in was outstanding. Breakfast service was a bit lacking.
