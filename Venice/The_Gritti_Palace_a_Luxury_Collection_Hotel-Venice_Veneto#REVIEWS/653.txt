The Gritti Palace is located on the Grand Canal. It's centrally located or as centrally located as you can get in Venice.   The hotel itself is beautifully decorated and very intimate.  We had a Landmark Suite with a Grand Canal View room.  The room is tastefully decorated with a large bathroom decorated in marble.  The room was big and the bathroom was bigger. Perfect for the wife and I.  I would defiantly suggest you book a room with a Canal view. Once you close the windows you can barely hear any noise.  If you like tall ceilings make sure to request one. Our room ceiling  was 14-15 feet tall.  From what we were told these rooms are mostly located on the second floor. That would be the third floor in American hotels. All other rooms are eight to nine feet tall.
