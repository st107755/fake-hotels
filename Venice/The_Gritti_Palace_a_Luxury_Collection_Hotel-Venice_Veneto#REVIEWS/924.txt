From the moment we stepped off the water taxi until we departed, this was a special experience.  Our canal view room also had a small balcony on level 2 from which we gazed upon and shared in the city's morning awakenings.   The room was not huge but was adequate whereas the bathroom was marble lined and spectacular with a large prolific walk in shower.  What distinguishes this from almost every other hotel is the large canal front deck restaurant and lounge.   There is a gondola station next door and a vaporetto stop 50 metres away. A short walk takes you past fashion and jewellery shops and cafes to Piazza San Marco.  Morning room service for coffee was prompt although it took more than a day for staff to retrieve the tray and provide fresh and complimentary bottled water.
