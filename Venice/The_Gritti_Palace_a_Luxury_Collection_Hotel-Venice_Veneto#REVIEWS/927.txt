We have recently returned from a wonderful trip to Venice, to mark our 30 years together.  I don’t think we could have chosen a better hotel than The Gritti Palace to celebrate this occasion.  It was a far cry from the hostel that we stayed at when we last visited Venice 26 years ago! This is a truly beautiful hotel with a warm, welcoming atmosphere and its location is nothing short of perfect.  All the staff were extremely courteous and helpful – nothing was too much trouble. We booked through Grand Luxury Hotels who helped with restaurant recommendations and made advance reservations on our behalf.   We booked one of the Grand Canal landmark rooms that was beautifully furnished, and provided a fabulous view of the canal.  I have never slept in such a comfortable bed.
