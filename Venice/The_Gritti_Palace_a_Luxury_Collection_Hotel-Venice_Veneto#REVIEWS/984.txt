Just prior to our arrival at the Gritti, we had been pampered for days with extraordinary service and luxury at the St. Regis Florence. Needless to say, our expectations were set very high by the time we got to Venice. Anyone who has had the privilege of staying at the Gritti, however, will not be surprised that this historic hotel not only met our expectations, but exceeded every single one of them. The Gritti Palace is truly an exceptional experience. It comes with a very high price tag, but if you’re willing to pay it, you won’t be disappointed. We actually did start off a bit disappointed that all the Grand Canal view rooms were sold out for our stay, but once we settled into our suite, we completely and immediately forgot about the view.
