The Gritti Palace Hotel is one of Bonvoys luxury selection and it is a luxurious hotel, full of very Italian old world charm. It’s position on the Grand canal is ideal being only 100 meters to San Marco square and about 200 meters from the Teatro Di Fenice (Venice’s opera house) which is well worth a visit just to have a look inside. it also has a gondola station right outside and water taxis at the door. The room we had on the third floor overlooked the canal and was obviously double glazed as the room was very quiet. The restaurant and bar were outside and right on the canal and given we had beautiful weather it proved superb.
