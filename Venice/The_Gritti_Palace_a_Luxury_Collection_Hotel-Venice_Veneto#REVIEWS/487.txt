We stayed at the Gritti Palace for five nights at the end of March and would definitely recommend it, primarily for its incredible location on the Grand Canal and just ten minutes walk from St Mark's Square. Opt for a water taxi transfer for the most glamorous and stress free arrival at the Gritti's private landing at the canal side entrance to the hotel. We were lucky enough to have great weather for the time of year and the fabulous terrace and Riva Lounge were open for three days of our stay - the heaters are amazing and it was possible to sit outside in the evening with a lovely glass of wine and probably one of the best views in the world.
