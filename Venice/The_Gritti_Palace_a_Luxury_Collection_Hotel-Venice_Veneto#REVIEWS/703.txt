This hotel is the ideal location to stay. It is a short walk to all the main attractions and has the most beautiful outdoor seating area.   With that said it comes at a cost.  It is very expensive and in my opinion it is not worth the money. The location and bar are the only thing the hotel has to brag about. The hospitality is lacking and there is a staff disconnect.   We were staying here for our honeymoon and we disclosed this on our reservations and dinner reservations at the restaurant.    Never once did any employee acknowledge the special occasion. I don't need my horn tooted but after staying at other Italy hotels for our trip I expected at minimum a congrats.   Our room was average. Small as usual for European hotels but clean.
