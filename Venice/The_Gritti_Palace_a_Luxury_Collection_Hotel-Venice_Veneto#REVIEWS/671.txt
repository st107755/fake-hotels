My wife and I spent three nights at the Gritti Palace for a milestone anniversary.  From the moment we walked in, we felt like we were well-cared for.  Our luggage was immediately taken, and we were ushered to the reception area.  The reception clerk acknowledged our special occasion and tried to place us in a room with the best possible view.  The room was not ready, so she offered to call as soon as it was clean.  An hour before the promised time, the reception clerk called to inform us that our room was ready.  Tall ceilings, beautiful furniture, elaborate wood trim, and a view of the Grand Canal made this one of the best rooms I had ever experienced.  It was plenty large by European standards.
