We stayed at the Gritti after a cruise around Italy. The Gritti is exactly what anyone could hope for from a great hotel in Venice (or anywhere else). We were there 32 years ago and the service was even better than we remembered (although, unlike the first visit, we didn't order a cocktail in our room, so we don't know whether they still roll a bar into your room when you do). We are SPG gold members, so received an upgrade to a wonderful, large bright room with one of the most beautiful marble bathrooms I have ever seen (grey and mauve, from top to bottom). The women whe check you in are lovely and so very friendly. And thank God for the concierge who arranged for what would otherwise have been a nightmare of an experience picking up a rental car.
