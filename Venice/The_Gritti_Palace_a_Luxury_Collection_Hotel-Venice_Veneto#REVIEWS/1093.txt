We stayed here in September for three nights and our only regrets were that we did not stay longer. The location and views from the terrace restaurant were magical, we would just sit and watch the canal traffic for hours it seemed. We had dinner on the terrace overlooking the canal and the beautiful 600 year old Basillica di Maria della Salute church across the canal from us. It was a stunning view and the food was world class 5 star. We purchased the breakfast package and we ate breakfast in the same spot every morning and never tired of the food or the views. The service was fantastic from every one we came into contact with, from the doorman to the concierge desk we were treated like old special friends.
