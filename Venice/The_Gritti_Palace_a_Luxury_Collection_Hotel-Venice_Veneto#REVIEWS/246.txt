I stayed at the Gritti Palace for 2 nights after spending 3 nights at the Londra palace. I stayed here after reading all the wonderful reviews and wanted to have a great memorable stay whilst renewing my wedding vows for our 10 year anniversary.  We stayed in a basic deluxe room with a view of the internal building walls. Yuck. If you have the money to spend, then I wouldn't bother staying here if you cant have a view of the grand canal. Staff didn't go out of there way knowing we were celebrating our 10 year wedding anniversary. A room upgrade would have been nice. I didn't feel the personal warmth of the staff in this place. Perhaps if you didn't "Look" wealthy to them or famous then they didn't really bother.   Its a nice hotel, the food is lovely and so are the drinks.
