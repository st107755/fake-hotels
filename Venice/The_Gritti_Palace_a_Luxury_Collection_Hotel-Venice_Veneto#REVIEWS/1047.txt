This was our first time in Venice. We were encouraged to go by friends who had visited last year and wanted to return. They found a good deal through BA holidays. We were immediately made to feel at home by their friendly and courteous reception. We had rooms overlooking a canal to one side of the hotel. Not the Grand canal but you would not expect that from the price we paid. The hotel staff took a great interest in making sure we were happy. I advised them of my wife's birthday and without prompting provided a complimentary beautiful bouquet of red roses and a birthday cake at an evening meal.
