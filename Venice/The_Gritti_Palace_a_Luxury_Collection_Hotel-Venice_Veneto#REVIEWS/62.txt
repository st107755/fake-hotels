We chose to celebrate our silver wedding anniversary with a special lunch at The Gritti Palace Terrace Restaurant whilst on holiday in Venice. We made a reservation by email in advance of our trip and received a very prompt response from Elena.  We were treated like VIP’s from the moment we arrived.  The Gritti Palace is beautiful. We were offered a table overlooking the Grand Canal and the staff were so attentive without being intrusive.  Elena (whom we had been in contact with by email to make the reservation) came to meet us to wish us a happy anniversary, a gesture we really appreciated. The food and bellini’s were devine.
