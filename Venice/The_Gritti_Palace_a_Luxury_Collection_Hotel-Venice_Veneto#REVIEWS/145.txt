Beautiful hotel which is very well staffed. Service is exceptional and also understated / not intrusive. Very helpful concierge who gave great dinner recommendations so we could avoid the tourist traps. The decor of our room was perhaps the best I have experienced as it was so unique and artistic. The bed was very comfortable indeed and the bathroom was very well appointed with plenty of towels.  The cocktail bar has an extensive menu - the measures are good and some of the cocktails are quite strong! We thoroughly enjoyed looking over the canal sipping away and watching the world go by. A perfect location.  Breakfast is plentiful with a very good quality buffet, with the option of cooked food to order if you wish.
