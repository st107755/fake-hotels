Perhaps it's because it was recently refurbished, but everything in Gritti Palace brings a classic feel to its guest. It's not the nearest luxury hotel to St Mark's Square (the main square in Venice), nor is it the closest luxury hotel to the branded shopping streets, instead it's located in a quiet square that's approximately 7 - 10 mins walk away from St Mark's Square. If privacy and a bit of quiet is what you are looking for but at the same time without being too far away from the busy crowd, Gritti Palace is a clear choice. Their breakfast selection, although limited, was something that my wife and I looked forward to every morning.
