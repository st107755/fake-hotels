Newly renovated, this is one of the best Five Star hotels we’ve stayed in for some time.  The entire staff from the time we arrived to the time we left was attentive and helpful.  Our dinning experience was excellent as well.  Breakfast was included with our rate and you could take advantage of the buffet with fresh fruits, various meats, cereals, pastries, and of course eggs and breakfast meats.  You could also order from the menu.  Over the course of the five days we were there my wife and I did both.  They added a porch area above the canal since we last visited and it’s a great place to have lunch or a cocktail before dinner.
