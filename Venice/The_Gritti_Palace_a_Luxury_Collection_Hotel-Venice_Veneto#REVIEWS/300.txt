What's left to say about this hotel that hasn't already been said? We visited the hotel in September 2016 as part of our babymoon. The suite we were upgraded to was absolutely beautiful. The hotel is well maintained with modern amenities. The breakfast spread is so divine that it's worth a splurge. Eating breakfast on the water while watching gondolas go by is a wonderful way to start the day. Every day, I looked forward to our breakfast!   The warm and helpful staff will assist you with all your needs, including reserving a private boat to take you to the airport. Word of caution regarding the boats to the airport: confirm the price of the trip before you board so that if there are any discrepancies you can get one of the hotel staff to set the record straight.
