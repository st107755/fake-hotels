The Gritti Palace is unlike any hotel I've every stayed in which is fitting considering how unique Venice is in general.  The hotel just recently reopened after an extensive 1 year renovation.  I can imagine it was an incredible undertaking, but one that most definitely paid off.  Our room was probably their lowest level room and it was out of this world gorgeous.  It's hard to imagine that every room had as much attention to detail, but they must.  From the hand painted headboard and Venetian glass chandeliers to the complete marble bathroom (huge, whole slabs of marble), I was in heaven.  I fantasized about redecorating one of our extra bedrooms exactly like it.   The only downside to this hotel was the price for the breakfast buffet and for a single drink at the bar.
