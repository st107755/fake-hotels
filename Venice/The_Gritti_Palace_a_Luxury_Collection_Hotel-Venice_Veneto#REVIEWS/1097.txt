I went to Venice with my wife and three daughters for two nights just before Christmas. As a frequent traveler, I have stayed in many nice hotels, but the Gritti Palace stands apart. The atmosphere of the hotel is like no other and gives the feeling of the opulent life of the Venetian aristocracy. We were upgraded to a suite with an adjoining room for our children, and the complex was filled with beautiful marble work and Renaissance oil paintings of the city. The marble work of the master bathroom was exquisite, with book-matched slabs on every surface. The service was impeccable, even though it was Christmas. The breakfast buffet was delicious, and the dining room had a beautiful view of the bustling Grand Canal.
