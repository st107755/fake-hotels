Just when I thought our trip was complete visiting our beautiful family in Southern Italy, we arrive at The Gritti Palace for an overnight stay in Venice only to depart the next morning to the States. The Gritti has been on my bucket list for many years. I have stayed at their sister property , which was nice, but The Gritti is suberb! The Gritti is situated right on the Grand Canal so you have spectacular views as well as a feeling of remoteness, relaxation away from the hustle and bustle but yet within walking distance from Piazza San Marco, Gondolas... The rooms/suites are palatial, some with Murano chandeliers, mosaic tiling, beautifully appointed furniture, hand painted ceilings, oak parquet floors.
