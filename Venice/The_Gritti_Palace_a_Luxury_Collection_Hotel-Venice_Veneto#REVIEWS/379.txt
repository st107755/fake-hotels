My Wife and I were fortunate enough to spend three lovely nights in this beautiful hotel next to the Grand Canal.  The website photos do not pay it justice enough. It’s absolutely gorgeous inside and the decor is old Venetian.  Every member of staff from the moment we checked in until the time we left were attentive and couldn’t do enough for us, without being overbearing.  The food was delicious.  We’ll definitely be back.
