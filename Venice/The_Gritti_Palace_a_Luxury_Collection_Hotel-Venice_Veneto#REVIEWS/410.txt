We stayed at The Gritti Palace for two nights following a cruise that ended in Venice. Wish we could have stayed longer. I had been at the Gritti over 25 years ago and thought it was a very fine place. It is now beyond any set of expectations one could have. A true gem in Venice--a city with many fine hotels. We were upgraded using the Andrew Harper Hotels program to a spectacular suite fronting the Grand canal. I have never been in a more beautiful suite.  We ate at The Club del Doge the first evening and it was superb. A romantic table right on the Grand Canal on a beautiful evening. The food, wine and service were all exceptional.
