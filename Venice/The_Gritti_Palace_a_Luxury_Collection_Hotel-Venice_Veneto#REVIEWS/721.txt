We split our time in Venice between the Gritti Palace (2 nights) and the Westin Europa (1 night), both of which came very highly recommended. We arrived on the same day as a national strike in Italy, and based on my Platinum status with SPG were upgraded to a Sestiere Suite (no Grand Canal view rooms were available).  Overall, we had a nice stay. The room was reasonably spacious, and the hotel’s history is impressive. When you stay at the Gritti Palace, you feel like you’re in Venice thanks to the thoughtful interior décor throughout the hotel. The drawbacks were that we did get some outside noise in the morning from the narrow canal we were overlooking, and staff didn’t seem as eager to help as in other hotels we visited in Italy.
