The Gritti is Old World all the way. But then, that's why you go to Venice. White glove service, the fabulous bar, the "Wall of Fame" (photos of luminaries who have stayed their over the last century or so) the luxurious breakfast and the breathtaking views of the Grand Canal are one of a kind.
