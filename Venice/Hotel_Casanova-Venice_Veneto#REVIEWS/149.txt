The location of Hotel Casanova is fantastic. Literally around the corner from San Marco. We took the water bus from the train station and the hotel was easy to find. The hotel is obviously very old but the rooms definitely are showing their age. Pieces of furniture are certainly showing their age and the beds (in our room 119) were nothing more than what I would consider to be cots. The fridge in our room was not functional. The wardrobe in my brother in laws room (227) was held together with duct tape. The carpets in the room were in rough shape. There is only wifi in public areas but being on the 1st floor and close to the breakfast room, we were able to get wifi sporadically. The shower was nice and the breakfast had a good selection.
