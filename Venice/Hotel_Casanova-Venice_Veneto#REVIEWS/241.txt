I visited Venice last May.  It was a beautiful experience to visit Venice and Milan.  In my four night stay, I stayed in Casanova Hotel.  This is hotel is centrally located in Central Venice.  It is a few metres away from Piazza San Marco and a few metres away from line 1 and line 2 of the vaporetto.  Not far away from the hotel, there are good restaurants which can be reached by walk while admiring the carnival masks displayed in various shops.  The hotel is a clean hotel, spacious, comfortable and the ambiance is attractive.  The staff was very helpful and friendly.  The service is great and while some of the reviewers stated that the breakfast is limited, the breakfast was very healthy with some bread, coffee/tea, yogurt and a delicious croissant.
