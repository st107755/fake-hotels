I choose this hotel as we were on a limited budget, we paid €62/night. I did read that they charge between €62-€300+ not sure I would be so happy paying the higher amount but it's all relative.  The hotel is in such an excellent location and was very easy to find. We arrived very early and popped in on the off chance the room might be ready. It wasn't but the staff were really friendly and let us leave our bags so we could go exploring.  Our room was on the top floor looking straight down the street out on to the water. Very useful for checking whether it was flooding as it was expected. The room it's self was pretty much the same as everything  in Venice, I bit tried and could probably do with some TLC but it was clean and functional and a bargain.
