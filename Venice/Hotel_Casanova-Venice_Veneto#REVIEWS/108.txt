After reading a lot of reviews for other hotels of a similar price in Venice I didn't expect much from the hotel but was very pleasantly surprised by what I found.  I will start with the location of this hotel which was just perfect and very easy to find.  It's a 2 minute walk from water bus, less than 2 minute walk into St Mark's Square and right in the middle of the designer shopping area.  The hotel looks like it has just been completely refurbished as everything looks new and clean, and has been very well done.  Our room was much bigger than I expected for an old building in the middle of Venice and had very good soundproofing.  Everything was spotless and the décor was really lovely.
