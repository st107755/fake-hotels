We stayed here for two nights in July. It seems like a comfortable place to stay for a couple of nights. The rooms are small but not cramped. The shower cubicle however is rather tiny, and it is difficult for a person of average built to take a shower without banging your arms against the cubicle doors. Wifi is available in the rooms too although signal is weak. Breakfast is basic-just coffee and a bread basket. The staff at the front desk are warm and were helpful with directions and our other queries. Also, there is no elevator to the fifth floor of the hotel so if you have a room on that floor you are going to have to carry all your heavy luggage on your own up the flight of stairs.
