We booked this hotel because of the location. I am handicapped and it is close to San Marco square. Later we figured out the hotel is in an yellow area accessible for electrical scooters and/or wheelchair. Considering that Venice is not an accessible city for handicap people this location was really good. The hotel is very simple, the breakfast is good, but internet and TV connection were terrible.
