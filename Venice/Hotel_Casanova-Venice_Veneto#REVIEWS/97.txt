Location of this hotel is just perfect! Just a few steps and you are on St Mark's Square, which basically means that everything is nearby.  The hotel has very nice atmosphere and so has a room, I really loved it. Not every room has a balcony, our had (number 231) and the view was quite nice (partially visible Grand Canal). The room was surprisingly very quiet, considering location. I didn't like that the safe was small, smaller than my MacBook 13" and that the shower didn't have proper isolation from the wall, but both are pretty small issues. What I was really missing is ability to order a coffee and sit in the lobby. It looks like it used to be possible, but currently is not, too bad.
