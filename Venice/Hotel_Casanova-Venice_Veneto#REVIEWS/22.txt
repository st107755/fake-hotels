The hotel was very easy to find from the water bus stop at St Marks Square (there are 2 stops and either will get you to the hotel quickly). Because of the fantastic location it was impossible to get lost, which we did many times over.   First impressions of the room were great, it was clean, comfortable but not the best views overlooking the street (hey, it’s a central hotel, what did we expect?). It was a view that really grew on us as it became great fun to people watch and tourists would wave at us coming up the street. The soundproofing in the room was phenomenal. As soon as we closed the window we heard nothing.   Staff are ‘ok’. We didn’t find them rude or unfriendly but they were hardly super helpful either.
