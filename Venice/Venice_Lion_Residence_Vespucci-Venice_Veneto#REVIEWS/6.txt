This B&B is very clean and the decor in my room was charming and beautiful! The price was a great bargain. The staff are very kind. The location is about a 10 minute walk to the streetcar stop which will take you right to Venice for 1.50 Euro. The trip back is 7.50 Euro. It took us a while to figure out the streetcar even though the owner of the B&B provided directions to the stop. Getting tickets to the streetcar is complicated as not all stops have a kiosk (and the app wouldn’t work for us) but if you go to the “mestre central” stop, you can buy tickets there.   My only two complaints: check out is early 10:30 am. I asked for late check out and was simply told “no” with no explanation. I would have paid extra!
