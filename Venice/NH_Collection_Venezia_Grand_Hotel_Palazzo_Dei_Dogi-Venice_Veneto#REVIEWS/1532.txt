We stayed here for a long weekend - a surprise birthday present for me from my husband. Like others we loved the impressive building and the garden which is lit up at night and stretches right to the lagoon.  We had a lovely big room looking over the canal furnished in traditional style with antique furniture and chandeliers. We liked being in  a quiet backwater away from the tourist crowds. When we arrived the staff were friendly and helpful and recommended local restaurants.  It seemed very quiet when we were there; a pianist played in reception in the evenings but we seldom saw any other guests.
