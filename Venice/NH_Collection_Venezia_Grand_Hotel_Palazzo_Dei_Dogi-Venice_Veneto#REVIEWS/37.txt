This is an exceptional hotel that has much to recommend it. We arrived by cruise ship and had no problem getting to the hotel via water taxi.  The building is absolutely gorgeous and superbly maintained. It is not a large property, which suited our needs quite nicely. The onsite bar is typically not busy and we were able to relax there with our fellow travelers. (We were there in November, a period of lower tourism.) The bar manager made each visit enjoyable.  The staff is very attentive, friendly, and speak excellent English.  We ate breakfast each morning in the hotel restaurant and found it to be excellent. There was a generous spread of food with something for everyone. Everything was fresh and hot (or cold). Yum!  The only downside of this hotel is the location.
