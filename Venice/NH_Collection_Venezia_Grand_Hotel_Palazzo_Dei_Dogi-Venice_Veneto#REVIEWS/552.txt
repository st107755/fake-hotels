This place was gorgeous . Unbelievable chandeliers of beautiful murano glass . The staff can't do enough for you . Restaurant recommendation were spot on. You never felt like you were imposing on staff with questions(and we had a lot) . We had a beautiful room in rear of hotel . Downstairs were comfy couches to lounge on . Word to the wise the prices are insane there . Beautiful but totally ridiculous. Plenty of choices off island . Hotel located away from main drag . Located near Jewish ghetto . It was nice to be away from hustle and bustle . FYI you prob only need two days here to see everything .
