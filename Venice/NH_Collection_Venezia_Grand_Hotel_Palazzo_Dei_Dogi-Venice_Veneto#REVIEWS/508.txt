A real gem of a place. Set in a lovely quiet area. Beautiful building - was a palace, then French Embassy ... Beautiful garden at rear - we arrived by river taxi from the airport at the front entrance and left from the rear entrance by river taxi (low tide that day) when we departed.  Very nice and helpful staff. Lovely room service breakfast. Didn't want to spend 25-35 euros per head on the buffet breakfast and the meals and drinks were all very expensive but we knew that would be the case - it's a 5 star beautiful hotel.  And there are plenty of places to wander out to for breakfast, lunch and dinner - even on Christmas day! 24th and 25th December were a bit quieter than usual so a great time to be there!
