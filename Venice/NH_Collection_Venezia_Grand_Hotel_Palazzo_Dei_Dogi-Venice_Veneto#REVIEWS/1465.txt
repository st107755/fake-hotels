We stayed at this hotel for three nights in October 2011.  The hotel decor is magnificent and our deluxe room was spacious and beautifully appointed as was the bathroom. The hotel gardens at the back of the hotel are beautiful and well maintained.  We had to wait three hours for our room as we arrived early but the receptionist did not inform us that there was tea/coffee available which would have been appreciated after a long trip.  Nor did we realise that there was a complimentary launch to San Marco and only found this out from another guest.  This is an excellent service and the two men in charge of the boat were charming and gracious.   The luxury spa was not working during our stay which was very disappointing.
