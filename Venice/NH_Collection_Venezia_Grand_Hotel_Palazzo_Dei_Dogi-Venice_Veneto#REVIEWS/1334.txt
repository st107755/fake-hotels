My husband and I recently went to Venice for my "Dream Trip". Our hotel, the Boscolo Venezia, did not disappoint. The staff was so pleasant and welcoming...as well as patient. Two mornings in a row the canals overflowed and we were flooded. As my husband and I waited in the lobby for the water to go down, (which it did by noon each day) the staff graciously explained the flooding in Venice over and over....and every time they were asked a question, usually the same question, by any hotel patron, they responded as if it were the first time they had been asked. They were without exception lovely. The room was fantastic. The breakfast was delicious and full of options.
