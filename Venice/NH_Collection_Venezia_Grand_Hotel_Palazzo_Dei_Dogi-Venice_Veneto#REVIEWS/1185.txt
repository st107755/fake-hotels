This was our first trip to Venice and we chose this hotel because of the Venetian architecture inside and out and because it was said to be in a quiet neighbourhood. We were not disappointed on either of these points. The location is certainly off the beaten path which is no bad thing being well away from the tourists but be aware that it is quite a long way from St Marks Square which is where everything either happens or starts from. You can walk but probably need 45 minutes or longer first time you do it as it is easy to get lost. The tours and boat trips all seem to leave from St Marks square and so we found ourselves there at least twice every day!
