We just returned from a three day break at this Boscolo hotel in Venice.  Having stayed at the Boscolo Exedra in Rome last year our expectations were high and I am pleased to report that they were generally met.  The hotel is located in an old classical building and it boasts lots of marble, high ceilings and fancy chandeliers.  It has 60+ rooms and ours was on the top floor and while not massive it was very comfortable and beams in the ceiling added a certain character.  Other guests we spoke to however complained of smaller darker rooms on the lower floors so take care.  Breakfast is served in a gazebo in the garden, which is lovely on a sunny morning but was a bit cold on a wet and windy May morning.
