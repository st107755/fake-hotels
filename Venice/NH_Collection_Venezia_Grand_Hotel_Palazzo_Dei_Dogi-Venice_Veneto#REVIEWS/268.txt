This hotel from the outside is unassuming and when you enter the lobby looks nice,but dated.  We checked in for 3 night stay.  The staff were pleasant and though it was before check in we did get a room after about 15 minutes.  The room we were given was on the second floor ( they refer to it as the first floor) facing a garden area overlooking a tent.  The room was very large but very dated.  The carpet, walls, furniture need updating and replacing.  In April they said they don’t turn on the AC so asked for a fan as the room was very stuffy and warm.  During the days we kept the windows open but did not at night for fear of mosquitos.  The fan was sufficient to keep the room cool enough.  The bathroom was a nice size, but again dated.
