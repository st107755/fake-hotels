During my first night before I joined the rest of my Rick Steves' tour, I stayed for one night at this wonderful hotel in the heart of the Cannaregio region of Venice.   It's a bit of a hike to get here from Piazzale Roma (so if you have large bags, I honestly don't know how'd you get here other than via water taxi), but with small, carry-on luggage, it wasn't too horrible. By the time I arrived, I noticed that the neighborhood was exceptionally quiet, with most residents in their apartments, leaving the canals and alleyways completely deserted. It was a bit disconcerting, but I never once felt unsafe.  Check-in was smooth as silk and the room itself was very spacious. The bathroom was pristine and the gardens surrounding the villa were absolutely wonderful.
