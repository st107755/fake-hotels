The Hotel has a nice old fashion charm to it.  The interior is quite elegant with many nice chandeliers.  The rooms are clean and the furnitures are comfortable.  We had a nice corner room with a king size bed and they provided an additional twin since we had two young children.  The breakfast had a good selection and provided a good start to the day.  The hotel is located in a nice quiet section of Venice, about a 15-minutes walk from the Santa Lucia train station.  The hotel provides free water shuttle service to Saint Marcos and there's also a free water shuttle to/from Murano Island for a glass factory tour.  We did have a problem with the water temperature for the shower.
