Arrived on very hot day from public water taxi. I had never arrived Venice by airplane so was not aware Venice was so far  from airport and that it took so long eventhough our hotel was the first drop off point. I mention this since in all fairness i was not in the best of moods when i arrived at the hotel. So when our request for adjoining rooms and canal views were not honored my family was immediatley disenchanted with the place. The receptionist was very patient and kind and we were to move to the canal view the next day when two rooms on canal side became available. After settling in with garden view rooms we decided to stay put since the rooms were vey spacious and beautiful and to be honest the canal wasn't the Grand Canal by any means.
