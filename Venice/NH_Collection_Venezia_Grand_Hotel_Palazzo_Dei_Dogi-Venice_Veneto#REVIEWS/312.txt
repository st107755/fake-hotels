This hotel is situated right at the far tip of Venice, with Orto being the nearest stop. So we were first off the river boat from the airport.  The hotel used to be the French Embassy in Venice so is an elegant, classical building with an abundance of charm. The garden is one of the largest private ones in Venice. You feel like you are staying in an exclusive, luxurious palace.  The service is excellent with each member of staff being more helpful than the last. Breakfast each morning is a delight with a use choice of quality dishes and a buffet. Each night we had delicious cocktails in the bar, with some delicious snacks.   Our room was functional, quite small but not a matchbox! However the main reason for a 4 * rating is the walls are so thin.
