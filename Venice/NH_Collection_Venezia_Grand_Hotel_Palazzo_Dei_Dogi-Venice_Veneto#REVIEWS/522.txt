I stayed only 1 night at the Boscolo Venezia, but it was fantastic in every way.  Location: For those who haven't been to Venice before, it's a very small island and very walkable. In fact, I find that walking around the city and getting lost in it is one of the most enjoyable things. It's entirely safe and I've never once felt uncomfortable walking around at any time of day. So, don't be put off by this hotel being in Cannaregio. In fact, I think it's far superior to staying in San Marco, which is overrun by tourists. Cannaregio, in contrast, has a very neighborhood feel to it. And never fear - you can get to San Marco with a 20-min walk.
