Looking for an elegant hotel, with great service, in a quiet neighborhood of Venice? This is your place.  The hotel is in Connaregio, the northern most island that makes up Venice. Put another way, it is not near Saint Marks square. But, fear not, free shuttle service to St. Marks square connects you to the major sights. Plus, the ride to and fro is an excursion in itself.  The hotel itself is in an old mansion. It has beautifully appointed rooms. The gardens are beautiful, and given their size, unique in Venice.  The service is great, the staff most accommodating.   We did not have dinner at the hotel. But the staff found great small casual restaurants around the area (10 minute walk) that were wonderful.  When we return to Venice, we will most certainly stay here.
