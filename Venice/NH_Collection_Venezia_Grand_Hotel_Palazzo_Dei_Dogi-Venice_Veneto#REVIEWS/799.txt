This hotel located in a very strategic location of Venice island. You can reach the hotel from Venice airport by Alilaguna ( orange Route ) at first stop ( Orto Station ) about 25 minutes. Then walk straight from the jetty and turn right at first junction. Walk towards the river side and you will see the hotel at your right hand. Just take about 5 minutes to reach from the jetty. The hotel is very classic, room is very spacious with good and quality amenities. Breakfast serve at ground floor garden area which you will enjoy a good start from here. The taste of food is good. You can walk across the river ( few minutes walk ) and you will find many shops, restaurants, cafe around. Nice, quite and great hotel to stay.
