We enjoyed three nights here at the end of our 2 week vacation in Tuscany and Venice.  We took a water taxi from the airport that takes you right to the front of the hotel which is on a small canal which was great but expensive. However, we have a tip we learned and wanted to share about after that. You can take the Orange Line Water Bus from the airport which is only 16 euros a person so a lot cheaper and get off at the Dell' Orto stop which is right near the hotel, just a short walk away. The hotel was a palace and is beautiful inside. Our room was nicely decorated with a beautiful bathroom and comfortable bed. The staff was friendly and helpful both the concierge and the staff in the restaurant. The food in the restaurant was delicious.
