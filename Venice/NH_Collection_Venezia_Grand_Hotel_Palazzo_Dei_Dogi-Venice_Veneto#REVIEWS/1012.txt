We stayed in the Boscolo Venezia for 3 nights before our Adriatic/Aegean cruise and were pleasantly surprised to be upgraded to the Presidential Suite.  The room was beautiful with the living room overlooking the lagoon and the dining room facing the beautiful garden of the hotel.  The hotel is a 20 minute walk to St Mark's Square, while passing small bridges and residential homes.  We love the quiet location, as it gave us a nice break after a full day of touring the city. The hotel provides free motor boat service to/from the square. It was such a nice service as the boat passes the canals and gave us the chance to take some great photos.  The stay in this hotel and the excellent service provided by the staff truly made this visit as one of our most memorable trips.
