There are many good things one can say about this hotel.  It’s greatest asset is the staff— all highly professional, warm, friendly and eager to please and assist with any questions you might have, or arrangements you might need.  The public areas are comfortable and homey, with a smallish bar, nice dining area, and beautiful spacious garden in the back, with sun chairs put out on the lawn.  There is also coffee and tea for guests throughout the day and night.  The rooms are a bit “hit or miss.” Our first room was very small, with no sitting area and a tiny bathroom (you could not open the door without hitting the toilet).
