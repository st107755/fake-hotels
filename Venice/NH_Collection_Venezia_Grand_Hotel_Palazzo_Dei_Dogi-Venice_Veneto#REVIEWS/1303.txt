We stayed at this luxurious Hotel only a few days after the terrible floods in November 2012, the Hotel had been flooded but you would never have known. We took our Wellington Boots but fortunately they were not required!  There was an extensive garden with plenty of private leafy areas.  In summer months this must be a lovely retreat and a very rare facility in a Venetian Hotel! This Hotel is only a short walk from the ‘Orto’ wharf and with no bridges to negotiate; it was much easier to reach than some Hotels we have had to drag our cases to!  A magnificent building, formerly a Palace, beautiful marble floors, lovely decorative features and spacious  grand salons.
