Our first visit to Venice and a quick one - just 2 nights. Due to the biennial art festival - rooms were super high prices in general in Venice. We had the "bigger" rooms - now I am not sure what the category is called. Particularly comfortable and large beds. Tastefully decorated. Large comfortable bathrooms.   The reception and lobby area had a lot of character.
