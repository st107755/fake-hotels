Situated on the northern edge of the city this hotel escapes the hustle and bustle of the tourist spots and provides a tranquil respite from the busier parts of the city, especially the beautiful hotel garden. It is very well furnished and our room (in the main hotel building) was comfortable and spacious with a huge bed and very well equipped bathroom. The decor around the hotel reflects Venetian style with frescoes and Murano lights / chandeliers and is very sumptuous. The hotel's restaurant is small but is extended with a tented Gazebo in the garden (It seems to be a permanent structure), where the standards of food and service were pretty high which was reflected in the prices thus the value for money is questionable.
