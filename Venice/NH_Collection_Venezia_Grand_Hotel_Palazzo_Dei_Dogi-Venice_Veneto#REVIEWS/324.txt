First of all the location of this hotel could not be better if you're arriving from San Marco airport. It's a super short ride on the Orange Alilaguna line and the boat drops you off a couple of blocks from the hotel. The hotel has its own boat shuttle that goes to piazza San Marco several times a day and also picks up hotel guests from there to bring them back to the hotel. That said, San Marco is a super pleasant 25-30 minute walk from the hotel, going through stunning neighborhoods and crossing picturesque bridges.   The service was impeccable -- we were offered prosecco on arrival while waiting for our room. Staff were friendly, cheerful, and always ready to help.
