The hotel has a lovely Venetian atmosphere and a very good location in a quiet neighborhood. It is the first stop with the vaporetto Arancia (Orange) line directly from the airport (stop Madonna del Orto) and from there few steps to walk to the hotel entrance. The reception staff was extremely polite and helpful. As mentioned before, they organize a couple of boat rides daily to and from San Marco square, but you can also walk and get into the bustling main road of Venice in a few steps. There are quite a number of nice eating facilities around. The hallways and rooms make you feel like living in a Venetian Palace.
