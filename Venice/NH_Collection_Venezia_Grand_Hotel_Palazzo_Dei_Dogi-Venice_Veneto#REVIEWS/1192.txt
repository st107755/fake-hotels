We saw the pictures of the hotel on the Marriott website and were not disappointed. While my taste in decor tends toward the modern, this hotel was true to my expectation of what a Venetian palace should be. It seems recently renovated in a way that keeps the charm but also provides the modern conveniences--including good water pressure and hot water almost immediately.  The hotel staff were equally charming and accommodating. The concierge, Ivan, was very helpful and friendly and offered recommendations on restaurants, sightseeing and modes of transportation. The free water shuttle to St. Marks's piazza and Murano are also nice touches.  We took advantage of the hotel breakfast every morning. (We were three and had two rooms.
