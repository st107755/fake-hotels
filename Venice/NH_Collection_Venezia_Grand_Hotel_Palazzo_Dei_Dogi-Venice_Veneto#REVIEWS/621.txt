Located on the north shore this hotel provides a sanctuary just far enough away from the tourist hubbub but is a short walk to the vaporetto and thus perfectly located.  The orange line dock is just a few yards away providing easy and cost effective access to and from the airport. Built in a beautiful historic building with one of the largest gardens on the island, the building abounds with beautiful details.  The lobby bar is a pleasant retreat where the bartender is quick to replenish the delicious olives, nuts, and other treats to go along with well-mixed cocktails most any time of the day. A starred Michelin chef serves up delights not to be matched for any meal of the day.   Take a free private (to hotel guests) boat trip to Murano to visit a glass factory.
