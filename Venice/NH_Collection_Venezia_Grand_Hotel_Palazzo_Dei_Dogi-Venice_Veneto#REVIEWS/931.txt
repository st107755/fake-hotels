If you like to be a little off the beaten path, this is the place for you. This charming hotel is not around all the hustle and bustle of the main tourist attractions. However it is better because you can take the hotel boat to the places you want to go complementary.  They have a boat to the Murano glass factory and St. Marks Square. It was as if you had you own private transportation. The staff is very helpful with booking tours. They booked a gondola for us, one that was the cadillac of gondolas. I would say it had the most gold trim I had ever seen on a boat. People looked at us it had so much gold on it. Our room was on the canal with a lovely view.
