This hotel is located a few minutes walk from the main connection between the bus/train terminal and St Marks Square.  It is well worth the little walk over a few bridges to reach.  In addition to the quiet location, they have a great staff and the breakfast was fantastic.  Further, they offered a shuttle boat that went to St Marks Square a couple of times a day as well as a boat to take guests to Murano Island for a tour of a glass factory / showroom.  If you have never been to Venice, please note that the entire area after the bus/train station is pedestrian-only (except for the boats in the canals).  It is a great change of pace from having to avoid traffic in most cities.
