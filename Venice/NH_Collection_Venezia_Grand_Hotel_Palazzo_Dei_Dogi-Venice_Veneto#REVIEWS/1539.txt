Spacious, quiet and bright in a city where all three are hard to find in combination, this property has consistently impressed us over three visits in as many years. The rooms range from the relatively inexpensive and small (our first stay in one of them featured a view of Madonna del'Orto from the marble bathroom!) to grand, garden suites with original frescoes; all have access to a beautiful garden where an impressive breakfast buffet is served daily. The best feature by far is its isolation in the Cannarregio district, which is peopled by locals, not tourists. The walk to the Rialto Bridge and beyond is only slightly longer than it would be from better known hotels at the center of things to their detriment.
