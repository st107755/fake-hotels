Anyone going to Venice should be prepared to become part of the huge mob of tourists. It's inevitable, which is why we really enjoyed staying at the Boscolo Venezia. Situated in a quiet corner of the Cannaregio quarter, this hotel provides the perfect escape at the end of the day from the crowds and madness around the major tourist sites.  From the moment we arrived, we were made to feel very welcome, and the receptionist was meticulous in explaining all of the hotel's features to us, and kindly showed us how everything in our room worked.  After unpacking, and with a complimentary drink in hand, we explored the gardens, which are extensive. I can imagine them being extremely pleasant in the summer.
