My wife and I stayed here to wrap our last 3 nights of our two week vacation in Italy. The hotel was a short 1.2 km walk from the train station and is off of the busy traffic areas.   We were given a room on the back of the property on the second floor (top) over looking the bay that heads to Murano, the airport and other locations. Our room had lots of character with a large king bed, the ceiling had wood beams with brick.   We dined in for breakfast every morning found it measured up with expectations. On our last night we opted to dine in and were pleasantly surprised by the service, quality of the food and the overall prices as they discounted for guests.
