Stayed here for only 1 night as we were cruising the Greek Isle but the hotel and staff were fantastic.  From the airport we took a private water taxi to the front door for 110 EUR.  This is the only way to go, if you take a public taxi you will save money but could be hours before you get to the hotel based on stops.  Our room was ready at 11:00am and it was small but very clean and everything worked properly.  This is the only hotel in Venice with grounds to walk on and it is beautiful. They supply a free water taxi back and forth to St. Mark's square which is very convenient but it stops at 7:00pm.  You are on your own at that point but still very easy to get back.  Had breakfast in the hotel and it was good, not awesome but free which made it great!
