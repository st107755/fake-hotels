This was our first trip to Venice and I spent some time looking at many hotels and reading the various reviews. After taking everything in to consideration I chose the Grand Hotel Dei Dogi and I cannot tell you how thoroughly pleased I was to have made this property my choice in Venice. We arrived by train and it is overwhelming if you visiting for the first time but its easy when you know how the "Vaparetto" - public water boats/taxi works. If you leave the station and head towards the water transport ticket office, purchase a fare to ORTO and then find the departure gate for boat 4.2 or 5.2. I needed to call the hotel for directions and spoke to a most helpful lady, who later checked us in by the name of Clarissa. We caught the water Vaparetto and disembarked at Orto.
