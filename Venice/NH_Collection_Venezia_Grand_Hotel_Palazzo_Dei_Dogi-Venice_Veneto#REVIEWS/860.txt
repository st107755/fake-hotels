I research every hotel we stay in prior to booking.  I knew exactly what we were getting before we got there.  Our first contact with the hotel was via email asking the best way to arrive from the airport.  They provide clear and concise options for us.  We chose to take a short walk through the airport towards the water and take the Orange line which drops us near the hotel.  It is an easy short 5 minute walk from where you are dropped off and easy to do with luggage.  We arrived early in the day so there wasn’t a room available for us but they held onto our luggage for us to start our day in Venice.  The lobby is absolutely amazing to look at it which was expected in such an old city with so much charm.
