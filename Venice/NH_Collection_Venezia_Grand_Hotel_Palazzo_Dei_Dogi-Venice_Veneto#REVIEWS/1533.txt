We stayed at the dei Dogi as part of a package, and were very impressed with the standard of accommodation and (particularly) the wonderful, quiet location in the Canareggio.  Our room was sparkling clean and very well furnished, and the staff we met polite and helpful.  Restaurant and minibar prices were steep - but we found plenty of good local restaurants just a couple of minutes walk away.    Being away from the centre of the action was a good thing - it gave us another perspective on Venice, and meant we didn't stay immediately around St Mark's but saw much more of the city.  It really is the best place in the world to get lost, and being away from the middle increased the chances of getting lost immensely!
