Having read somewhat mixed reviews, we were a little anxious before we checked in at the Boscolo Venezia.  All our apprehension was immediately put to rest when we arrived at the hotel and were greeted by a friendly and professional staff.  The lobby was beautiful, with glass chandeliers, marble floors, and gracious furnishings.  We had booked a standard room using Marriott Points, but we were upgraded at check in to a Junior Suite.  Our room (403) was perfect!  Large floor to ceiling windows opened to a small balcony overlooking a canal and the main entrance.  The room was beautifully decorated and included a comfortable king size bed, end tables, and dresser.  In addition the room included a sitting area with a settee, two chairs, and a table.
