An elegant, quiet and restful hotel that's not too far from the hub of Venice (about 20 minutes walk to St Marks Square).  Its the nice touches like cold water in reception with a hint of flavour from different fruit types placed in the jugs that makes the difference.  Breakfast had a huge selection/choice and the cooked options did not take long to arrive.  Our air conditioning was just right and we didn't need to adjust it even though it was 30 degrees outside (we normally find the air conditioning within hotel rooms on the cold side though and have to turn them down).  The shower was powerful and toiletries provided lovely.  The garden area was a bonus and had great views over the lagoon from the back of the hotel.
