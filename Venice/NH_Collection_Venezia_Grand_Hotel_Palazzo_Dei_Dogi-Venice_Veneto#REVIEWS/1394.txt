Thsi hotel is situated on the north side of Venice, by the vaporetto stop, Madonna Del orte. This is the first stop for the boat from the airport, taking about 25 minutes, 16 euros each. It is a large old house, full of period furniture and paintings, but modernised and very comfortable. The hotel provides a free boat shuttle to St Marks Square and to Murano to visit the glass works. There is free we fi in the hotel. It is stylish and comfortable.  The rooms are quiet.  One should use the plug in insect repellant in the room to reduce insect bites.
