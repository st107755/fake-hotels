We stayed three nights as part of Marriott's Autograph Collection.  The hotel is situated on the northern edge of the city facing the lagoon. Yet it is only 5 minutes walk from the Grand Canal through charming and safe back alleys and over bridges.  Staff were good but our concierge Ivan was excellent. Great personality and always helpful.  The free shuttle boat/launch to St. Marks Square was probably the best free boat ride in Venice. Taking almost 25 minutes around the northern part of the main island it is not to be missed - do both directions often !!  Our room was at the bottom of the long garden and adjacent to the shuttle boat pontoon. The room was spacious and beautifully appointed.
