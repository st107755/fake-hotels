The Boscolo Dei Dogi Hotel in Venice, Italy was a fantastic hotel. The service from the hotel staff was personable and professional. GM Giuseppe Pontrendolfo was always there to greet or assit his guests. The location of the property was extremely convienent for airport bus or taxi (First stop) and was only a short walk from the Grand Canal. The hotel is a former French Embassy and provides it's guests with a luxurious decor in an intimate setting. The hotels gargen is very beautiful and the largest garden in Venice. The food service is terrific. The hotel is located in a residential section of Venice which really makes you feel part of the neighborhood.
