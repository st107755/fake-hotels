This hotel is in a relatively untouristed part of Cannaregio, so you are able to enjoy Venice without always being in the midst of the mobs that are so typical of much of the city.  It's easy to get to from the airport by Alilaguna (first stop on the orange line is a three or four minute walk from the hotel).  The hotel and grounds are beautiful and spacious.  Our room overlooked the lagoon, on the other end of the garden from the main building, and was quite large and well-appointed.  Our breakfast was included in the rate, and included a very large cold buffet and hot options that could be ordered from a menu.  Excellent restaurants are nearby, and the main shopping corridor toward San Marco was about a seven minute walk away.
