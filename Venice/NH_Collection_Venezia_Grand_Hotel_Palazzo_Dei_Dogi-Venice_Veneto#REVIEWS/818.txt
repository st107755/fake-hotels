The Boscolo is around a 20 minute walk from things like St Marks Square. Its an easy and interesting walk around Venice so this is manageable. Having been to Venice before this was what we were after but this will be for some possibly just a smidge too far out of the action - check your google maps to be sure. The immediate (5 mins) around the hotel is pretty quiet and bordering in parts on what would pass for residential. A great little garden in fact not little by Venice standards which if the weather permits provides a nice outlook to the little outdoor restaurant area. We were there in July and during a period when it was unusually hot (circa 38 degrees) and very very humid so outdoors wasn't really an attractive option.
