My wife and I were fortunate to stay six nights at Boscolo Venezia. The hotel is in a quiet residential area, a 15 minute walk from the hectic tourist areas.Transit from the airport to the hotel was simple. Just purchase the 25 Euro ticket (round trip = andata e ritorno) at the airport machine, and follow the walkway to the Alilaguna Linea Arancio vaporeto. Get off at the first stop, Madonna del' Orto. From there it is an easy 5 minute walk to the hotel. We started each day with the ample and delicious breakfast buffet, which also included a variety of hot dishes to order. The restaurant staff, Bane and Codu, were delightful. We initially stayed in a beautiful room, looking out over the lagoon. Unfortunately, the mosquitos (zanzari) were pleased to join us.
