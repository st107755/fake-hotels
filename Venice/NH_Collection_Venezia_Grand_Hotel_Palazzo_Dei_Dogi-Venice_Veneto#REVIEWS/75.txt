This was my second experience with the Grand Hotel Dei Dogi, and I was very pleased once again (if not, more so this time). We booked a Junior Suite that was very spacious and elegant (highly recommended!! as the regular 1-king bedrooms are quite small...) and the rate included breakfast. WONDERFUL breakfast that comes with your choice of omelette, pancakes, or waffles made-to-order in ADDITION to the full breakfast buffet.   A trip to the New Murano Gallery is a must, and the reception can coordinate that for you when you arrive. Additionally, the boat service to San Marco square is great (although it is quite long...) if you want to experience Venice by boat. We did this once just to do it, but then walked back as it is faster to walk.
