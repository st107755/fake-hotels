This hotel had its positives and negatives for me, and in the end I wouldn't stay here again. On the positive side, the front desk staff were very helpful. In addition to answering any questions and dispatching the porter quickly to help with bags, they gave a nice orientation to Venice upon check-in and offered their complimentary shuttle to San Marco square when they heard I was heading right back out after check-in.  The food at breakfast looked excellent, but I skipped it because the tables were set up outdoors and there were pigeons all over them.  The food was set up inside, so if you don't mind using linens that some pigeons just danced on, then you probably will enjoy the breakfast.
