Just returned from a four night stay and loved the ambiance of the hotel. Our room was slightly cramped, but nicely furnished and a decent bathroom. The hotel is very charming and the neighborhood is quiet, yet only 7 minutes from a lot of nice neighborhood restaurants and the nearest ferry stop. The hotel also offers water shuttle service to San Marco Plaza that is near most of the museums, high-end shopping, and other sites. As has been referenced in other reviews, Ivan the concierge is wonderful. Breakfast was terrific, yet the only disappointment was the hotel dining room. We normally don't eat in hotel restaurants, but after a day in the rain, we just didn't feel like dealing with more weather for dinner.
