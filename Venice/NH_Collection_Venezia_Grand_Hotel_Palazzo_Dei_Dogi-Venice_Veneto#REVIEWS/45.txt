This is a lovely hotel away from the bustle but in easy walking distance to many restaurants and shops. Definitely 5 star service throughout. Check in was very friendly, although as we arrived really early from a cruise the room wasn’t ready. No matter as we had a coffee in the lovely refined bar and then wandered through the hotel’s extensive gardens and then the canal network. We were upgraded to a suite as I am a Titanium member - nice touch. The hotel has kept it’s olde world charm throughout. A peaceful haven. We took the hotel’s free water taxi to and from San Marco, it runs every 90 minutes or so. Breakfast was very good.
