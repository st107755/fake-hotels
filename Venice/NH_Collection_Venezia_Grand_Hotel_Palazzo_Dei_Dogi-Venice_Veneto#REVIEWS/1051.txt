The hotel structure is beautiful with some beautiful rooms that are under-utilized as the building at one time was an embassy.  It was a delight to browse.  I am a lifetime platinum with Marriott and my room was way too small and a view that was not attractive.  I expected more. Also, the hotel is away from the tourist sites and that has it's disadvantages, but it's also quiet.  Everything in the hotel was expensive, but they did offer complimentary transportation to St. Marks Square, but a long boat ride.  They also offer complimentary transportation to a Murano Glass Factory. (I did not know that in advance and had booked a tour.)  The complimentary breakfast was just better than adequate.
