A wonderful old hotel, lavishly furnished.  It is located in a pretty neighbourhood in Cannaregio, pleasant, and not subject to the vast crowds in the area of Piazza San Marco.  The walk to San Marco is not onerous (about 20 minutes) and the hotel provides a free shuttle; reservations required. As an aside, the neighbourhood near the hotel also offers restaurants, many more reasonably priced and seemingly more authentic than so-called tourist traps (though in a historic city of 55,000 residents that sees 20 million visitors annually, I'm not sure there is any local business that does depend on visitors).  The breakfast buffet at the hotel (hot and cold) was the most varied and interesting of the seven Marriott hotels we visited during out Italian holiday.
