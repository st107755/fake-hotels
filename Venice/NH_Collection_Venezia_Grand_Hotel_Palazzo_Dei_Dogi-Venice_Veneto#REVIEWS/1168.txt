We stayed in this beautiful old hotel for a two night stay. The place retains many of the palatial charm making one of the most unique hotels I have had  the pleasure to stay. The staff were all courteous, friendly and helpful, no evidence at all of the indifferent or snobby attitude noted by other reviewers. Our room was spacious and large with a well appointed bathroom. We were lucky enough to get a garden room well away from the main building. The hotel is located off the beaten path, well away from the throngs of people streaming through the city. This was quite a welcome feature, as it provided a quiet respite to have dinner at a local restaurant with out having to elbow our way through the crowds. The locals were friendly and pleasant.
