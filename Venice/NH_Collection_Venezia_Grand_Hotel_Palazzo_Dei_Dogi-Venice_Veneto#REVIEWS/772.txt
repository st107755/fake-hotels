This hotel is a 5 minute walk to the main streets of Venice and a 15 minute taxi/boat ride from San Marco Square, which means that this hotel is away from all of the tourist crowds.I personally found this to be a very positive attribute, but some may want to be closer to all of the tourist.  The hotel itself is perfect for a place like Venice. This is a Marriott Autograph hotel. We had a Junior suite, which was the most spectacular room that I have ever had in all my years of travelling. The hotel does not have many guest rooms, so get a reservation early. The restaurant in the hotel is small - but Excellent! Great food and personal attention.
