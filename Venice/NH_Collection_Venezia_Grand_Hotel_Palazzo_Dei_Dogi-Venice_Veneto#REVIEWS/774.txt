Staying here made me ponder, what makes an expensive hotel (and this is an expensive hotel) expensive) and is it worth that to me?  This place has some very beautiful attributes, is located away from the hustle and bustle of Venice and the hordes of tourists, the hotel grounds are immaculate and beautiful, and the lobbies are filled with beautiful murano glass.  Is this what makes a hotel expensive and worth staying at?  Or is it the service an amenities?  If so, this hotel failed miserably.  From the moment we checked in to the moment we checked out, everything that we tried to do or ask for was a hassle.  A day prior to checking in, I received a text via the Marriott app asking when I was going to check in so they could be ready.
