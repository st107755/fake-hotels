Having been to Venice many times, we generally stay on the Canal Grande. It was an unexpected delight to discover this hidden gem away from the hue and cry of Plazza San Marco. Sure, we will be strolling in that area, par for the course, but the lodging in this quiet, peaceful hotel on the other side is both a respite and an easily accessible location. Just four stops from the S. Lucia Railway Station(Ferry D, 4.2, a short walk at Orto.   Our garden room is superb, staff are very friendly, the full buffet restaurant for breakfast and for dinner under the veranda is excellent.
