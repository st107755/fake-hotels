We decided to stay here on Marriott points and because it is away from the very crowded parts of Venice.   Location: It is about a 20-25 minute walk from the hotel to St. Mark's Square but the Boscolo has a complementary private boat that will shuttle you back and forth several times a day. We found the schedule to accommodate our needs getting to St. Mark's but we always either walked back or took the vaparado public transport boats back. This hotel is near some truly hidden gems in the "Jewish Ghetto" part of Venice. We found this part of town to actually have the best local bars and restaurants with a completely different vibe from the tourist traps near St. Mark's.
