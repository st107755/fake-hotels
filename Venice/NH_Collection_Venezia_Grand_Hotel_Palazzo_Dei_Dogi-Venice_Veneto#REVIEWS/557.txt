The best thing about the Marriott Boscolo Venezia is its location . Located along a canal in a residential district , it is within walking distance to many excellent local restaurants. For $130 , four passengers or less can take a 30 minute private water taxi from the international airport to the front door .On the other hand, the public taxi is about $15 per person, and docks a six minute walk behind the hotel. The train station is a 15 minute walk away where you can take a high-speed  2 1/2 hour train to Florence . If you plan on going to the Uffizi Gallery in Florence, please get your ticket and tour time in advance online. I took a train there for the day and returned to Venice for dinner that evening.
