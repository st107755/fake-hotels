They have great people working at this not so great 5 star hotel. I'll begin by saying that the staff did go out of their way to do what they could...which unfortunately was not as much as we would have liked.  The hotel itself is situated in a quiet part of Venice, away from the large crowds that can, at times, feel overwhelming. We stayed here for only one night as we had returned from Florence to fly out of Venice and decided to try the quieter area.  The lobby of the hotel is nicely done, and upon entering we could feel the charm and old history of this old building. Unfortunately, once inside the room, all we felt was the old.
