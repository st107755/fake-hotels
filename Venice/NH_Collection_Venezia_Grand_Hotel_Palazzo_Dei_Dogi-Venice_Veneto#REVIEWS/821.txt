My wife and I spend 4 days at the Boscolo Venezia hotel as we have accumulated a number of Marriott Points over the years.  It's smaller size certainly falls into the boutique style type of hotel and the charm that comes along with hotels of that personality.  The staff was inviting and was very helpful when attending to issues.  They provided a number of good options for dinner and the ride to Murano was entertaining (although we found the island of Murano to be a bit touristy).  The rooms are on the smaller side (when compared to what you would normally find in a US hotel) but they are charming in décor and has most if not all of the amenities you would require.
