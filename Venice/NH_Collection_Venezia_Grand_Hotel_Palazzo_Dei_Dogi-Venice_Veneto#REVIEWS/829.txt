We spent two nights here, capping off a two week journey through Italy. I booked this hotel primarily because of the Marriott connection, but also because of reviews. It did not disappoint. The property is off the the well beaten tourist paths, yet only a few blocks from those arteries. Although the hotel offers a shuttle (boat picks you up at the front door) to St. Marks Square, we chose to walk approximately 20 minutes and explore the neighborhoods along the way. The area near the hotel offers a neighborhood feel rather than a tourist area. Nice local eateries that come alive in the evening. The hotel itself is a former French embassy and reflects the elegance of the period.
