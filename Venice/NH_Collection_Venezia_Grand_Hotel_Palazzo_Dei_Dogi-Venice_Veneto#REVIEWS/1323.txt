The boat service to St. Mark's was super on that rainy Friday and provided a dry start to the day.  We had no issues with Wifi (room 219) and they provided a log-in which allowed multiple devices which we appreciate.  The breakfast service was good and the choices were very typical for Italy and priced like most European five star properties.
