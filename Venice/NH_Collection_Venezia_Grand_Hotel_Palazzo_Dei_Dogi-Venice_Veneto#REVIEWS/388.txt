I cannot rave enough about this hotel.  From the minute you check in, they provide you with lavender towels to freshen up, a cold pitcher of water with fresh berries and there are even containers of candy on the piano!  We are platinum so we were upgraded to a room similar to what we've seen while touring the castles of Europe.  We felt like royalty!  Breakfast is served in a beautiful garden and the food is plentiful and delicious.  Each morning we were greeted by 2 adorable rabbits who live in the garden and eat fresh sliced apples that the wait staff brings out.  The garden has lovely walking paths and places to sit and relax, and in the back of the hotel there is a dock where you can catch a complimentary boat to take you to San Marco or Murano.
