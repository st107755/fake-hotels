At the heart of Cannaregio area, wonderfully located away from the crowds of San Marco this is a traditionally furnished Venetian palazzo with a garden,although the weather was a little too chilly to use it. Only blot on our stay was a wedding party with music and loud singing that occupied the entire hotel public rooms for a whole afternoon and early evening. We had invited friends for a drink in the bar and could not hear our own conversation.
