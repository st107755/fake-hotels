My wife and I had a wonderful overnight stay at the Boscolo Venezia.   I highly recommend the hotel if you're visiting Venice. The trip to Murano was very nice and sales pressure free.  The boat trip to and from St. Marks was very convenient.     The Boscolo is located about a 15 minute walk from the Train station.  It's located in a quiet neigborhood that is about a 25 minute walk to St. Marks Square.   We loved the fact that the neighborhood was quiet and didn't have the crowds and noise of being near St. Mark's square.
