Getting to the hotel from the airport is easy by public boat on the orange line to Madonna dell'Orto  and it is a short walk from there.  During your stay make sure you visit the beautiful church.  Entering the doors of this hotel you enter a different world which is just what we needed after days spent sightseeing in Venice.  The reception area is stunning with fantastic Murano glass chandeliers and reception staff to match.  Nothing was too much trouble for these staff who seem to know the answer to everything.
