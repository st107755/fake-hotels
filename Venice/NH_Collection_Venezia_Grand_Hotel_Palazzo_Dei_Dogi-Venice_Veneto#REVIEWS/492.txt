We had a 5 night stay here from 13th Feb.  Beautiful, classy hotel in a stunning city.   Staff on reception are excellent, particularly Paolo and Phillip.  Had great recommendations and advice.    We didn't eat at Hotel as it was expensive and we preferred to get out and about.  There are a host of eating establishments to suit any taste within easy 5 minute stroll. We stayed in Room 209.  Excellent housekeeping.  Had a bit of trouble regulating water temperature when showering but got "the nack" and it really wasn't enough of an issue for us to complain.  Would have liked coffee / tea making facilities in the room, that was unusual but so may positives that it didn't matter. The gardens really are beautiful and that's out of season.
