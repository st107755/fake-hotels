First and foremost Venice is expensive. In most of the places you will go to, your eyebrows will leave your face with surprise at the extremely poor service and horrendous quality/price ratio... The Boscolo Venezia Hotel is NOT one of these place. With exquisite service and a genuinely friendly reception (our receptionist, Georgia, was one of the most efficient and kind people we had the pleasure of meeting) the Boscolo Venezia is the ideal setting for a true Venetian experience.  As we were travelling as a family me, my wife and our son, we had a junior suite in the hotel which was an impressive room in traditional Venetian style with amazing decor throughout. This was a slice heaven and incredibly relaxing after walking around the streets and boutiques of Venice.
