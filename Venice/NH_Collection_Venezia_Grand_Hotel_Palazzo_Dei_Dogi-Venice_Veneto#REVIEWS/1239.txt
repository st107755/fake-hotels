The Boscolo Venezia is a treasure.  The setting, with its large and magical gardens that extend from one canal to the sea, is magnificent.  The service at every level was fabulous.    Easily the best and loveliest hotel during our 16 days in Italy, I wish we had stayed at the Boscolo Venezia for more than four nights!    The concierge staff took time with us, made reservations, and gave us directions on the easy walk to shops and restaurants.  The bar and restaurant staff were most attentive.    The rooms (we had one for our daughters 20 and 22 and a second for ourselves) were perfect with tons of closet and drawer storage and the highest, most magnificent ceiling I have seen.
