We stayed in the Boscolo Venezia for two nights before a cruise.  It’s defining advantage was its location in a beautiful and quiet space away from the hordes of tourists coupled with a free shuttle to the St. Mark’s Square.  We were impressed with the spacious room we received as well the breakfast buffet.  Staff were generally friendly and helpful, though we did have difficulty with one person (described below).  Overall, we would recommend the hotel and would stay again should we plan another trip to Venice.  Room The room we received (305) was located on a part of the hotel that did not have full elevator access.
