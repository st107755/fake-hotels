We got back yesterday from a three night stay at this beautiful hotel. I had undertaken a fair amount of online research when planning our trip as I was seeking something luxurious and romantic.   Arrival: We arrived by water taxi directly to the hotel where we were met by the concierge and escorted to check in. Polite and friendly and welcoming. We were checked in but as we were early our room wasn’t ready so our bags were taken in and we went out for a walk and some lunch. There are plenty of good restaurants and bars within a few minutes walk in most directions. But as many reviews have said, it’s nicely away from the hustle and bustle of the more well known areas such as Realto or St Marks. The walled garden is beautiful.
