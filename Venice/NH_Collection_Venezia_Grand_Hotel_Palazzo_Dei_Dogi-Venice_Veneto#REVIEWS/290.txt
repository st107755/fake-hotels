We recently stayed at the Boscolo Venezia during our recent visit to see the Carnivale in Venice.  There are several items we really like about this hotel:  1.  The Cannaregio location is well removed from the chaos surrounding St. Marks Square,  It's quiet, the neighborhood is pleasant (mostly locals) with good, well-priced restaurants.  2.  It's location is near a rarely visited church with wonderful paintings inside by the Italian masters.  Also, a block or so away, the city transit system offers a boat to the airport for 10-16 euros -- a major savings from a private boat from or to the airport.  3.  The service staff at the hotel is warm, friendly and anxious to please.  4.
