My wife and I spent three very enjoyable nights here in early June. The Boscolo Venezia is located in the Cannaregio neighborhood in an historic 16th century palazzo that was later used as the French embassy.  The hotel fronts on a quiet canal well away from the main tourist areas and enjoys the largest private gardens in Venice. The walled gardens open out onto a covered dock on the lagoon with a view of Murano across the water.  In other words this is old world luxe with a level of peaceful privacy that seems almost impossible in the frantic tourist mecca that is much of Venice.  Our upgraded room was smallish but pleasant and comfortable, the bathroom was just fine and- in our room at least- there were adequate outlets for all the electronics that needed charging.
