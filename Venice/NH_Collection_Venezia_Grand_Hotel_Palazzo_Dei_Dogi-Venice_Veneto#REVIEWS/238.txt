We visited Venice for four days total.  Two days prior to the start of a 10 night cruise were spent at a different hotel on the Grand Canal just by San Marco Plaza.  At the end of the cruise, we opted for the other side of Venice, so we chose this hotel.  It was a wonderful stay on a quieter side of the island.  They have a lovely garden, and the staff is very helpful and friendly.  We were in room 208 which is called a Panorama room on the website.  It was a corner room with a view of the canal from two sides.  Very nice, large, quiet room, with a good view.  The breakfast at the hotel was nice, and you can choose to eat outside in the garden area or inside.
