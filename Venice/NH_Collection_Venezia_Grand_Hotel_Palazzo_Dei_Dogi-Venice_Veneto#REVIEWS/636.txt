I did not stay at the Boscolo this trip so cannot comment on the room facilities, but I did dine there twice and also use the spa facilities twice.   The Boscolo provided me with excellent service despite the fact I was not a hotel guest.   I ate items from both the bar menu and the restaurant menu. Both were excellent.   I had a pedicure at the spa and returned the following day for a detox special. I was treated like a queen both times.   I am so impressed with the Boscolo I would stay there should I return to Venice. The location is on a quiet street far from the grand canal but close to the vaporetto station. A short vaporetto ride and you are at the grand canal.
