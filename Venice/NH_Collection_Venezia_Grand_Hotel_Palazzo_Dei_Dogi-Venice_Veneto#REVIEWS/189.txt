Accommodations at this hotel did not disappoint.   I arrived at Venice Airport for a 3 day stay in Venice then onto Florence and Rome.   I took public water taxi to hotel area. (Take orange line from Venice airport to Orto stop- first stop from airport via water taxi).  Purchased a 1 way ticket in airport after exiting baggage claim from booth that cost 15 euros.  There were electronic ticket booths in baggage claim but wasn’t sure on what I was purchasing so I headed out of baggage claim and found the ticket window to purchase.  Also, please note that there is an ATM machine in baggage claim just next to the Travelex currency exchange booth which I opted not to use to change American dollars due to fees and used debit card at ATM to withdraw euros for my stay.
