My wife and I stayed here for 2 nights in early October while we were traveling around Italy on a bus tour (Globus - Best of Italy tour).   The location of the hotel is on the north side of Venice.  Its about a 30 minute walk to St Mark's Square.  We thought the hotel was very nice.  Our room was located on the 4th floor and was enormous.  We had a very comfortable king size bed, a large seating area with two chairs and a couch, and a balcony that overlooked the canal in front of the hotel.  The room was equiped with an air conditioner but it didn't seem to work (we think they turned it off since the temperature outside was moderate and a/c wasn't necessary).
