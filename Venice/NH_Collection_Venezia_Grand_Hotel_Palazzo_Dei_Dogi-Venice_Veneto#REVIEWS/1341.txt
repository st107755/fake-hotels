At the end of a Mediterranean cruise we spent an extra two nights in Venice. On our first day of Venice visiting we were still on the ship We spent most of that day in the beautiful but hectic St. Marks Square area. The hotel is in a quiet section of the city, but convenient. Our water taxi dropped us at the front door.
