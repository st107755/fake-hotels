This is a lovely hotel - very comfortable and great staff. There's a regular shuttle to St Marks' Square which is definitely worth booking and you can also visit Murano.  The concierge was really helpful in recommending local restaurants, and you simply must go into the bar for one of their Spritz - delicious and refreshing (and you'll certainly be wanting to buy a bottle to take home!).  Some comments have been made about small rooms, but we were perfectly happy with ours - after all you don't go to Venice to sit in your room all day even when it is raining.  Breakfast on the terrace each morning was a lovely relaxing start to the day - but watch out for the pigeons.
