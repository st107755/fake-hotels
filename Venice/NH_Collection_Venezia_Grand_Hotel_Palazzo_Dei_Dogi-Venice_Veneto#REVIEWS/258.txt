This hotel is located in the Canareggio neighborhood, truly an area where people live. Children play outside, the church next door had services while we were there, people shop at the local vegetable market, and you can watch the deliveries on the canal and people taking their own boats to go to dinner in the evening. The morning is special when you can watch the neighborhood wake up and people going about their daily routines. The streets are narrow and interesting walking home in the evening. It is 15 minutes walk from the train station. There are approximately 4 bridges to cross which require stairs to haul bags up. It’s doable and we preferred that over the $70 euros it would have cost for a water taxi.
