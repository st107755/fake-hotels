The new St Regis is perfectly located on Canale Grande vis-à-vis Santa Maria delle Salute church, and is the beautiful transformation of the former Westin Europa & Regina, one of the grand hotels of Venice in very busy San Marco district by the canal (alongside Gritti Palace, Bauer, Monaco, Luna etc.); it used to be a bit stuffy and worn, however, always had the vast terraces overlooking the Canale Grande as USP. The hotel is a bit secluded from the very busy main street Calle Larga XXII Marzo with all the upscale boutiques and the hotel cannot be seen from the street.   From the outside, the building has not changed much.
