St.Regis Venice opened a few months ago which sparked my interest and I took a chance and booked there.  My overall take-away is that StR has several strong positives particularly the top notch room, while service is still a work in progress as is the case for many new hotels.  As Marriott Ambassador Elite guest I received a fantastic upgrade to a Grand Canal View Suite; in terms of loyalty recognition that’s hard to beat.  Our suite at StR was not very large (63 sq meters) but it’s a proper suite with the living room separate from the bedroom although only 1 bathroom so no powder room unfortunately.
