The moment we arrived at the drop-off point we were wafted into our opulent hotel with amicable grace. The staff were extremely welcoming and took the time to give us the tour of this magnificent Palazzo hotel. It is only about two years since this old building was given the restoration it so deserved and converted into a hotel: the glory of old Venice with 21st century living. It is so well appointed and is sited right on the grand canal but is set back from the waterfront; one of the only grand buildings with its own front garden.
