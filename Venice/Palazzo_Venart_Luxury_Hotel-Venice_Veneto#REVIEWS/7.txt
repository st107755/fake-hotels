Finding the right hotel in Venice wasn’t easy. It was our first time in Venice and we wanted a luxurious hotel but yet not too far from the train station (just in case we are caught in the floods!) Palazzo Venart hotel was a random pick in the end as we have searched through several hotels and we were trying to balance  between luxury and price. The hotel has a very Venetian feel. (Toiletries were from Etro!) Service was prompt and good. Breakfast is of top quality served in Glam ( Michelin starred restaurant !) Location wise, it’s not as close to the St Mark’s square but it’s just a short walk away. In fact, we were lucky as Venice experienced one of its worst floods in history as we were leaving the hotel!
