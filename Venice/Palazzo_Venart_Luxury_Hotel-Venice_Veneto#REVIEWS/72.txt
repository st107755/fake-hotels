This is a really lovely 5 star hotel (and I have been fortunate enough to stay in some of the best in the world). It is quite small (which we prefer), probably about 20 rooms and so likely to be classed as boutique. It is set in a great location just far enough away from the busiest tourist areas but these are all very easily accessible by foot and canal (boat stop right next to the hotel). The decor/furnishings throughout are stunning and simply ooze class. Some of them are breathtaking, for example the Murano glass in our bedroom windows and all fittings/furniture of the bar room. Our spacious, superior room was very stylish, beautifully decorated throughout with a king size, really comfortable bed and a bright modern bathroom.
