The setting of this hotel is truly its best feature. Housed in a remodelled Venetian palace, it really takes you back in time. It lies quite central in Venice with its own dock from which you can take water taxis and of course, gondolas. It was very easy to get to and from from the water bus dock as well.  The downside of this location is that the rooms were quite dark and you can feel that the property is quite old (not a complaint, just information). I found it very romantic. Beyond the setting, this hotel does not stand out in my memory as very special however. The concierge was competent and set up dinner reservations and a gondola ride for us, however interacting with them was always a bit awkward.
