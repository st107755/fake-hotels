My fiancée booked this hotel as a surprise trip to Venice for my Birthday. This hotel is pure luxury. We arrived by private water Jetty (booked through the hotel and paid for direct with the taxi upon arrival at the hotel. And he did accept a credit card)  The entrance is directly off of the grand canal. The hotel is very Venetian, very grand and opulent. We had a classic room which was fabulous. The decor was stunning and I think it had the most comfortable bed that I've ever slept in!  Bathrobes and slippers were provided as you'd expect and they even had umbrellas hanging in he wardrobe for us to use if needed.  Don't bother packing shampoo and conditioner as the ones provided by the hotel are great and a decent hairdryer was also provided.
