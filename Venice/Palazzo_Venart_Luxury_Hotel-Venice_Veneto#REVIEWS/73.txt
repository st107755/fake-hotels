Early April, I decided to go back to Venice (my 35th trip) for my birthday and decided to opt for Palazzo Venart as I've wanted to experience a new Leading Hotel but a small property. I did not regret it as it was a wonderful birthday break. We stayed in 2 different room. The first night in the Murano which is a classic room (located of the last floor). The room was small but well appointed with a pretty large bathroom for the size of the room and a wonderful little terrace. The second and last night we were in the Marco Polo room (prestigious room located of the 1st floor) a large spacious room. The hotel location is so convenient (only 3 stops from Piazzale Roma which is, according to me and my experience the quickest way to reach Venice. Take the bus then the vaporetto.
