Just returned home from a wonderful short break staying at the Palazzo Venart hotel.. From the moment we booked the hotel, prior to arrival the staff were accommodating with advice and ideas for our stay. We opted for the private water taxi at the cost of €170, this was well worth the cost as we were met at the airport by a driver who then took us to the waiting boat. The trip in the boat was about 30mins but what a way to arrive at the hotel gates! We were welcomed by the hotel owner and his team, which felt very personalised. We had a classic room (Summertime Room) which was small but well appointed, however we had a terrace which greatly opened up the feeling of available space. The bed was huge and comfortable and made up twice a day.
