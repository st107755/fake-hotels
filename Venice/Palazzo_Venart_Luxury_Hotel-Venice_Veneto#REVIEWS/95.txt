This is a beautiful hotel set in a quiet part of Venice.   Firstly to the room, our room was a classic room on the ground floor just behind reception. This was a bit of a surprise when we arrived and we didn't like the idea that our bedroom door was 2 metres from the reception desk. We did ask if there was another room available and the staff were very accommodating in trying to meet our needs even though the hotel was virtually fully booked. They did offer us another room on the 1st floor but it was not nearly as nice and we had already unpacked, so we decided to stay. However, if you are sensitive to noise I would advise you to request a room not on the ground floor.
