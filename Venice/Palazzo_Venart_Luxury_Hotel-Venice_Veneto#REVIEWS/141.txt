We booked the Palazzo Venart Luxury Hotel for our 25th wedding anniversary visit to Venice.   From the moment we arrived to the moment we left we were treated to exceptional service by all the staff in the hotel.   The hotel is located on the banks of the Grand Canal opposite the Venice Casino. We stayed in room 302, which was beautifully appointed, with a stunning private terrace.   The hotel has a large quiet garden that looks onto the canal, which has a combination of modern and historic artefacts.   Although we never ate in the hotel in the evening, the breakfast was excellent with a range of eggs, pastries, juices and fruit.   The attentive staff are a real credit to the hotel and were always courteous and respectful of our privacy.
