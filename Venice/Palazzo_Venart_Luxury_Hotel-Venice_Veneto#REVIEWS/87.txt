This was our second trip to Venice and one thing one cannot avoid to say is the place is an amazing feat of human ingenuity and architectural splendour like nowhere else. Whether it rains or not one is always in awe of the place. We stayed at Palazzo Venart as it was a member of the LHW and were very kindly treated to a wonderful room upgrade as it was the tail end of our honeymoon travels. The hotel has been renovated to extremely high standard and it was a very comfortable stay. The receptionists were very helpful and attentive on arrival and departure and in between and the only slight let down was the restaurant staff at breakfast which seemed overly ‘busy’ for such a small venue, and maybe it was a language issue, not terribly effective.
