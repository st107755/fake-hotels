This is a wonderful out of the way boutique hotel in Venice where we had the pleasure of staying for three nights over New Year 2015/2016. We were well taken good care of by helpful friendly staff and bar personal. They even opened their exclusive Krug lounge especially for us. The elevator opened directly into our suit which was large enough and having been designed by Phillipe Stark had is quirks with mirrors everywhere. Even the mini bar offered a few extras you would not normally find elsewhere! The New Year’s Eve event was excellent, organised by “Madame Ema” again it was not your usual NYE event, the food was lovely and the wines well chosen and unlimited with Champagne at midnight.
