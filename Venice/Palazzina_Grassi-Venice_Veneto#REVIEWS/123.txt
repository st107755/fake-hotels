We had a recommendation for the Palazzina G as a modern Venetian hotel in a good location but the prices were higher than what our travelling companions wished to pay. We found the G House through the hotel's website and were very pleasantly surprised both with the pricing and the amenity . This is a 3 story 2 bedroom/2 bathroom apartment just across from the hotel with full use of the hotel's facilities available to guests.It has a small kitchenette, a separate lounge/TV room and even a small rooftop terrace with seating. It is stylishly and comfortably furnished and was perfect for our  2 couples to enjoy the delights of Venice.
