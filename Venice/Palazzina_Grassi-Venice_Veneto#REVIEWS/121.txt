Leave the airport by following the Public Transport and occasional water taxi signs. Across the car park you'll see a half-covered walkway, follow that and it will take you to a dock. There you can get the public transport water bus that stops at about ten stops before getting vaguely near the hotel or pay €110 to a individual private water taxi. Don't worry about bartering or overpaying, the prices are fixed municipally and there is a chart in the taxi. On arrival don't go into the museum which shares a very similar name; go left up the lane past the museum and then first left. The hotel entrance has only the building number above the door and no glitzy sign (as do all Venice hotels). Someone will greet you and take you into the restaurant for registration.
