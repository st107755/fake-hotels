When trying to decide whether to book this hotel or not I did check Trip Advisor and was nearly put off by some of the negative reviews.  I have to say, don’t be put off, this hotel is incredible. I find that a lot of hotels in Venice are more traditional in their décor and I struggle with that. This place is a sensory experience from the moment that you arrive.  It was my birthday and so I was a little spoilt because my husband had arranged for the private water taxi transfer from the airport. It you’re celebrating something special, I’d highly recommend it for the whole rock star experience. We did get the ferry on the way back and it made me appreciate the incoming experience all the more.
