I don't normally write reviews, but the stay at Palazzina G warrants travelers knowing how great this hotel is. Above all the normal expectations of a top hotel (ultimate cleanliness, comfort, beautiful setting, convenience), the staff here made it the true goal to make our stay perfect. Upon arrival we were given complementary drinks and were able to relax in the lounge after our long flight. We were escorted to an upgraded room overlooking the canal- the decor was a fabulous mix of old Venice and modern touches, none sacrificing the great comfort of the room. The concierge was top notch and offered great suggestions for food and touring that allowed us to avoid the more touristy spots- finding some real local charms.
