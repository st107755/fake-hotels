My partner and I have stayed at the Danieli on a number of occasions over the years and have always loved it. The hotel does have real character and charm.   On this occasion however we booked the same category room as ever but were given a room that was clearly a standard size room and not of the price we paid. It appears that there is a huge discrepancy in rooms in different buildings of the hotel. The main old building is lovely, however the attached building we were put into this time was old and somewhat tatty and the rooms tiny. The windows open out to a brick wall. The room felt dark and pokey. It really was unpleasant. It made the holiday a lot less enjoyable than on other occasions when we have been in the main building where the rooms are beautiful.
