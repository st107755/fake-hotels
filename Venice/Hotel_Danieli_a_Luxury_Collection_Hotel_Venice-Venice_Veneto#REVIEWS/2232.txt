We have just spent four nights at the Danieli. The accommodation in a standard room was very handsome and well-appointed and the house-keeping excellent. However the experience was marred by the outrageous cost of food and drink. In particular the 50 Euro buffet breakfast was a disgrace - nothing out -of-the way by the standards of any large international hotel. Foil wrapped butter pats and tired scrambled eggs are not what you expect in a top class hotel! Ordering from the menu was very slighly more reasonable but still far too dear.We had planned to dine in the hotel at least once but changed our minds when we saw the prices.
