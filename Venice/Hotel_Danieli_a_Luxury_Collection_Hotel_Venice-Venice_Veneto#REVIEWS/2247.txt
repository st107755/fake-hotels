First impressions We took a water taxi from the railway station to the Danieli. There was something unique travelling by boat to a hotel. This was our first stay in Venice and we did not really know what to expect. Unlike other hotels the water taxi actually drives right next to the hotel entrance in a private canal. I had mixed feelings about the decoration of the hotel and at first I didn't really like it..  The room We were shown to a newly renovated room. The new room was rather small but very "luxury collection" in decor. Alas no canal view so we decided to upgrade.  The new room was in another building. The decoration is dated (as you can see from the photos) however the room is very spacious and the view was just fantastic.
