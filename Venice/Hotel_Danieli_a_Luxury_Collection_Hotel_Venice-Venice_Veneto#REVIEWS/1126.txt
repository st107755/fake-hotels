We took a Vaporetto  water taxi with all our luggage. There was a lot since we were off on a cruise after this stay. We wanted to arrive at the hotels doorstep  on the canal so we could have the porter help bring things up to our rooms. Even in November Venice is crowed and the entrance to the hotel canal was suffering from a  major Gondolieri-Jam. The Vaporetto had to wait too long so we gave up and moored beside the water taxi station. We did get help to treck across the pavement to the hotels, so full marks for the taxi captain and the porter for their service. The Danieli is a landmark hotel  for very good reason. it is old world elegance  to the extreme.
