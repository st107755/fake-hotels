This hotel was on my wife's Bucket List, so we chose to stay here (well "she chose"). It's difficult to rate a hotel like this since you go in knowing that it's expensive and can never live up to the price vs. value. That being said, the hotel experience was better than expected. First of all the room was quite nice...sort of Italian cheezy opulence. My wife loved it. The room itself was kind of small, but the bathroom was decent size and the bed was quite comfortable. The room had all of the amenities you could ask for (except for free internet...at least at these room prices). We knew going in that we would not get a great view (since those rooms are even more expensive), but it turned out to be not too bad (a view of the old jail). The service in the hotel was good.
