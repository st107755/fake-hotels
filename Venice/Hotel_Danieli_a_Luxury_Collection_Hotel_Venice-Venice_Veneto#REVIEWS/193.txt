We arrived for our three night stay at the Hotel Danieli via water taxi which dropped us of fifty metres from the hotel .Walking into the hotel you are struck by the fabulous reception and lobby which is awe inspiring. The welcome and checking in was professional and friendly by the hotel staff.We received an upgrade and were shown to a fantastic room (326) on the second floor by reception staff, which was overlooking the canal. The room was spotless with a huge double bed which was very comfortable and all amenities you require. As mentioned in previous reviews there was no tea and coffee facilities but that’s a minor point. The hotel also kindly provided a bottle of wine in our room to celebrate our wedding anniversary.
