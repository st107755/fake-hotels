There is very little I can add in terms of how opulent the Danieli is. Always considered one of THE hotels in Venice in which to see and be seen!   If you can I would advise booking a premier luxury room which tends to have more space especially for those who are non-smokers. The inner rooms are very good value (off season) but if you want to splurge the lagoon view rooms are magnificent.  Unless you wish to spend 50 Euro per person for breakfast I would highly recommend ensuring that breakfast is included in your rate. The views in the morning are stunning (even in fog!) but beware that the restaurant gets very busy on weekends!
