My wife and I booked the Danieli on its reputation as part of a celebration trip that started on the Orient Express from London to Venice. The train trip was all we hoped it would be. Our stay at the Danieli to round the trip off did not disappoint. We had a room in the (relatively) modern annex which others have been disappointed with but it was a large, comfortable room, great bathroom, quiet with a view over the Doges Palace to St Marks Campanile. The staff were polite and helpful and entering the cool and calm lobby of the Danieli from the tourist-thronged lagoon-side entrance was a nice experience. The hotel’s central location was a great feature allowing us to easily get back for those late afternoon naps essential before hitting the tratorias and bars in the evening.
