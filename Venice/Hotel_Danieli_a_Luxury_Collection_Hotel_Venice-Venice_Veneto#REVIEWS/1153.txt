Great location as it is right by one of the water bus stops and next to St. Marks square. However, everything nearby is extremely expensive, especially restaurants.  We had considerable trouble connecting to the premium wifi, but found the "free" basic wifi worked just fine for our needs, including allowing us to stream Netflix, so we weren't too negatively effected.  The elevators are small and the hotel is extremely traditional and clearly old.  Our HVAC was sufficient to keep the room cool but never could get is as cold as we liked it, and the bed was firmer than we usually like. We found the location of our room could be a little noisy at night as I think the alley it was beside could be busy.
