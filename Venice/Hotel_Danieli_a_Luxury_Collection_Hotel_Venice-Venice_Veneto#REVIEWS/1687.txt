What I liked the most about this hotel is its location. It's close to the center of Venice, which is in the St. Mark's Square area. It's not hard to get around Venice from here since transportation (water vaporettos, gondolas) is just across the hotel.   Breakfast at the terrace was also a nice experience. The food was good and varied. There was also a chef cooking eggs. It would be a nicer experience to have a table nearest the edge of the restaurant where you could eat while having a non-obstructive view of all kinds of water vehicles passing through the Venetian Lagoon.   Though we paid a higher price, our choice of a room overlooking the Venetian Lagoon was very worth it. We could see the Venetian Lagoon and all the boats coming and going through it the whole day.
