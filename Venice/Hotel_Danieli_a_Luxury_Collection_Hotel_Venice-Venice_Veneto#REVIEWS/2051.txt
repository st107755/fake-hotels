Venice was never top of my list as a holiday destination but I have never been more wrong in my life. I now try to go there 3 times a year and the last time we stayed in the Hotel Danielli, wow what a lovely place. It was like being taken back in time. We visited in December and the day we arrived it was overcast and drizzling but still beautiful.  They were putting the huge Christmas tree up in the big lounge area which was still in keeping with it's days as a Palazzio.   The hotel itslef has a quiet calmness about it, the staff are wonderful and they really cannot do enough for you and they all speak perfect English which helps in my case!
