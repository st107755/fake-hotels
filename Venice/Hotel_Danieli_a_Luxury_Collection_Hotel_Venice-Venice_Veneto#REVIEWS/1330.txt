This hotel was just stunning from the moment we entered via the boat entrance.  Murano glass chandeliers everywhere, carved dark wood and cozy lobby bar that felt like 1950s Hollywood. Live entertainment at the bar each night that we were there.  Located on the Grand Canal, right next to St Mark's Square. The minute you step out onto the grand canal, the crowds were crazy, but this hotel provided access to every thing we needed:  museums, restaurants, exhibits, shopping, cocktails, music as well as the private water port for easy pick up and drop off from our Murano and Burano day excursions.   The rooms were large, gorgeous with large showers and tubs.  All rooms appointed with beautiful Murano glass fixtures/sconces.
