Hotel Danieli, what a gem!  14th century historical hotel. It’s a lavish building decorated in the neo gothic style, with a very dramatic original staircase and rich fabrics which decorate the rooms. We were fortune to have stayed in a waterfront suite facing the lagoon. Each room comes with a large gothic style key which you keep with concierge when you leave everyday. It’s a very private hotel as well which was nice since due to its popularity many people try to come in to take photos. Unless you’re staying at the hotel or have a dinner reservation you cannot get in. I actually enjoyed this feature because with 60,000 visitors a day coming from cruise ships you’ll appreciate coming back to your hotel to peace & quite.
