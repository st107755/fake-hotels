The Hotel Danieli began the 19th century as the Royal Danieli Hotel and today it is certainly fit for royalty.  This hotel began in the 14th century as Palazzo Dandolo and has been wonderfully preserved and restored.  We stayed at the Danieli the last week of June and could not have had a more wonderful experience.  Arriving from Marco Polo airport via water taxi we were escorted through their canal side entrance into the most wonderful and ornate lobby I have ever seen.  The check-in process was quick and professional with a great multi-lingual staff.  We were escorted to our rooms by one of the front desk professionals and our luggage arrived promptly.
