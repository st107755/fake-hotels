It was a choice between the Danieli and the Grizzi Palace. We looked at both and chose the Danieli for its views. And these were magnificent from the terrace at breakfast and our room facing the lagoon. But the service was extremely poor and unwelcoming. Rather than list the issues I'll give one example which sums up the attitude of many of the staff. We stayed three days and were lucky enough to have sunny mornings on two of them. On the second day we went for breakfast and found the doors to the open air terrace closed. The maître d said that it had rained and they had moved people indoors. It was however sunny and no sign of water on the tables or ground. I asked twice and was told everyone had been moved indoors and perhaps the terrace would be opened for lunch.
