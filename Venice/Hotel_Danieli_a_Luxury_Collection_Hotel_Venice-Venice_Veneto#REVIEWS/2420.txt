We stayed in the Danieli for a Valentines long weekend.  When we arrived our booking had not been sent through by the company we had booked with.  We had booked a suite overlooking the lagoon and there was none available for that night. The staff could not have been nicer or more helpful.  They put us in another room for the night and gave us complimentary champagne and chocolates and the next day took our bags to our new room while we went sight seeing.  The rooms were perfect and beautiful with wonderful views of the lagoon.  Again there was an other bottle of champagne and chocolates for us.   We ate in the restaurant one night and it was expensive but the food was lovely and the staff were extremely friendly.
