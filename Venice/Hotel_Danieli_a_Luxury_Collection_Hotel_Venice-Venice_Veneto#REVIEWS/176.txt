I just spent Easter weekend in the hotel. The staff is great, very helpful and friendly. I sent a few emails to the concierge for some last minute reservations and recommendations, and they were very quick to reply every time. The two main selling points for this hotel are the location and the rooftop terrace. Breakfast is served on the rooftop. The view is STUNNING, that is really what you are paying for. The food is good but nothing memorable. The lunch was definitely better than the breakfast, but look to be spending easily 250-300 euros for a 2 person lunch. The rooms are quite comfortable with the exception of the no A/C. I feel like if you are paying close to 700 euros a night for the simplest room, you should have A/C.
