We had a long weekend and we weren’t disappointed with the hotel. Yes it was expensive there but you go to any major city and stay in the finest it has to offer and you will pay for the privilege.  We couldn’t fault any part of it. Bedrooms superb and very clean and the bed I give 5* for comfort. We took breakfast in the roof top restaurant and found the quality superb and all very fresh. The staff where all very pleasant and couldn’t do anymore for us. We didn’t have any problem getting a table outside and would recommend it as the view fomm the roof top is spectacular. We had an evening meal on the rooftop on the Sunday night and there are no complaints there either. The menu was well put together and the food was high quality and well very well portioned.
