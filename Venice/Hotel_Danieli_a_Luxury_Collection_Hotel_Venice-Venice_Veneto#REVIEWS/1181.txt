I cannot recommend this hotel more highly if you want a luxurious treat in Venice. Entering the lobby all the photos of this grand, old hotel really do not do it justice!  Antique furniture, glass chandeliers and a magnificent staircase gave us a preview of what to expect. After a warm welcome and speedy check-in we were taken up to our room where a complimentary bottle of sparkling wine and chocolate coated strawberries were waiting for us. [It was a very special anniversary we were celebrating].  Our room overlooked the Lagoon and the church of S.Giorgio, and although it faced the Lagoon, it was well insulated against the noise of the continual pedestrian traffic.  Our enormous bed was the most comfortable we have ever slept in - we wish we could have shipped it home!
