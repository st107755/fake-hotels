I stayed at the Hotel Danieli on a business trip that was also my first visit to Venice.  I received an upgraded room with an excellent view.  The room was very clean and the internet signal was fast and stable.  The TV was excellent and had HD signal.  The hotel staff went out of their way on every occasion to help make my stay enjoyable.  The air conditioning system in the room was great and kept the room at a satisfactory temperature.  The hotel does not have a fitness center.  There is one within a 5 minute walk that costs 20 Euros per day.  The hotel restaurant was excellent and merits a return visit.  One note about this property.  When booking on the Starwood web site the rooms do not specify smoking or non-smoking.
