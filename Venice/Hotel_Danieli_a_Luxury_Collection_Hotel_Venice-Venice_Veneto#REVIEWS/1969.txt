I was a little nervous to stay here after reading some of the reviews, but I left the hotel wowed by my experience. The room had Bang & Olufsen TVs, an amazing white and black marble bathroom, and a great historical feel.  The hotel had it's own line of toiletries in red Danieli packaging which was a very nice touch.  This was my first time in Venice and I agree that Venice is a little too crazy at times, but we ended up loving it.
