I decided to write a review of the oldest and grandest 5 star hotel in Venice because I saw some weak reviews - The Danielli can never live up to its reputation as you are experiencing a world gone by. But isn't that what you want in Venice? The Grand hotel is fine wine where the the moment in time dicates the the unforgetable experience or just a glass of house wine. The rooms are from another era - big and grand. We  walked up the staircase all day to experience "the full MONTE" - but  what it lacks in moderization the Danieli makes up in its time capsule  "a time past where glamour was a life style". Years ago we wore a Tux and long dress to go to the The Grand Casino in private water taxi and returned big winners to create the most amazing feeling of ole world Venice.
