Have breakfast on their 4th floor terrace overlooking Grand Canal while tug boats one on bow, one on stern bring cruise ships right in front of you. It is a high end hotel, previously a merchant's residence right next door to St. Mark's Square with a water entrance which you will arrive at from the rail station or airport and a land entrance. Murano glass light fixtures in lobby. Spacious bedroom and washroom. Pretty good water pressure in shower and hot water. There is a difficulty locking/unlocking the room entrance door. WiFi is 15 euros for 24 hours. 99 TV stations.
