We stayed for 3 nights before boarding a cruise from Venice and loved the hotel. It is an incredibly beautiful building and the lobby is breathtaking. We had a standard room with a view of the lagoon. The service was wonderful and helpful. The room was much larger than we expected and based on previous experience with European hotels. The building is in excellent condition given it's age - it is centuries old.   Our standard room included a vestibule, a large dressing room with closet, full length mirror and shelving, a marble -clad bathroom, larger than most bedrooms in my home (and complete with not just a multitude of terry toweling, but also linen towels), and a bedroom that  held a kingsize bed, as well as a full sized rollaway bed brought in for my daughter.
