Central location, which is great, though the waterfront gets so busy with huge groups from cruise ships that at times it can be overwhelming. Tourists come into the hotel lobby, making it very busy. Hotel was also hosting a number of events (wedding parties etc) which increased the foot traffic.  The hotel is actually spread across three different buildings, which are linked by bridges across the canals, which is lovely.
