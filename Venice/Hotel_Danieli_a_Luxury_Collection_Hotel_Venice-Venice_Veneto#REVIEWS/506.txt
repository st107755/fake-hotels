We stayed at this hotel for five nights. The hotel had been recommended and booked by our travel agent when our usual hotel was not available on our chosen dates. This is a difficult hotel to review so I used its own literature and discourse as the measure for seeking evidence of its success in achieving its own standards. The hotel is beautifully decorated and under constant maintenance check ups. The staff are immaculately dressed in smart uniforms, efficient and friendly. The views are wonderful and the rooms are spacious and beautifully decorated. The beds are comfortable and ensure a good nights sleep. The bathrooms are huge with showers over a bath and a walk-in shower. The meals are of a high standard. So far so good.
