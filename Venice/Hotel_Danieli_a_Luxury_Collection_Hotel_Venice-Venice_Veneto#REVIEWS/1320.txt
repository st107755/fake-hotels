After being in Venice for a day or so and after dealing with the crowds of tourists and tourist traps i.e every single restaurant and store, the Hotel Danieli was a breath of fresh air. Being a luxury hotel, I already assumed I would be in the land of the rich and famous but never imagined such a hospitable and curteous wait staff and straight forward menu. I felt relaxed, comfortable, and enjoyed my quaint little table surround by the bustle  of the bar area. Guests were enjoying their drinks and whispering sweet nothings while my friend and I sipped our cocktails and delighted in a plethora of salty snacks which were complimentary! The best part was there were no hidden seating charge or service charge at all! What you got was what you paid for...awesome!
