We spent three nights at this hotel between Christmas and New Years.  If Hotel Danieli wasn’t the best stay I’ve ever experienced, its near the very top.  This is an outstanding property, with an excellent staff, great location and true Venetian style.  Room. We booked a Deluxe Room but applied a SPG Suite Night Award, which cleared 5 days before arrival. We were assigned a Lagoon View Suite at check-in. The room was spectacular.  It had a large living room, furnished in classic style, with an expansive view of the lagoon. There was a small sitting area in the entry and separate bedroom. The marble bathroom included a stand-up shower, tub and dual sinks. There was a very thoughtful welcome gift of biscuits and champagne waiting for us on arrival.   Property and Location.
