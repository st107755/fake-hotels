After looking longingly at the Danieli for many years, we finally visited to mark my husband's retirement. I can't understand why we have waited for so long for such a treat. The hotel is luxurious and the staff were so friendly, welcoming and helpful that it made our stay even more memorable. We had a fourth floor room (thank you very much for the upgrade) which looked out towards the Lido; it was beautiful, elegant and tasteful as well as classically Venetian. Perfect. It was also warm and comfortable without being over-bearingly hot like so many hotel rooms. We took the breakfast "package" for our four nights. This is one of the best hotel decisions we have ever made.
