We stayed at the Hotel Danieli twice in May for 2 nights each before and after a cruise. The hotel is full of old world charm and whilst faded we found it clean and generally comfortable and very close to all the attractions. Our room was good, great bed, great bathroom, old and faded but clean and comfortable except for the air conditioning. More about this later. The bar was excellent and the breakfast upstairs was also excellent with good service. Everywhere the lighting was minimal, not sure if this was "mod lighting" or whether the policy was just to use very low wattage bulbs. The foyer was always extremely busy and the system of check in and check out on both occasions seemed lengthy and disorganised.
