I stayed at the hotel as part of a small conference and would definitely choose Danieli again for business or leisure travel.  The location is very convenient, close to St. Marks and right in front of the water taxi.  When I travel, I want to have an authentic (albeit luxurious) experience; I don't want to stay in a sterile chain hotel.  When you stay at Danieli, you are immersed in the history and art of Venice.  The hotel is filled with Venetian glass and it all sparkles; not a speck of dust.  Even the rooms feature Venetian glass chandeliers.  My room was large and elegantly appointed.  I even had a separate dressing room with a small vanity.  The bath was large and luxurious with double sink, marble shower, separate deep soaking tub and nice quality toiletries.
