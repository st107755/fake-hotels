We have just stayed at the Danieli for the first time and although we liked the hotel I doubt we would stay there again or near ST Marks Square as the location was too busy for us as we prefer a quiter and less busy location. You will have to check with the hotel as to which one you are staying in as there are 3 Danieli so you may think you have paid for the one as pictured but you could be put into a different part of the hotel as this happened to us although we were moved but the room we stayed in was very old fashioned and smelt musty. The breakfast is excellent and the view from the terrace is amazing so it was worth staying there for that.  We did eat an evening meal there as well but it was extremely expensive so we didn't repeat that experience!
