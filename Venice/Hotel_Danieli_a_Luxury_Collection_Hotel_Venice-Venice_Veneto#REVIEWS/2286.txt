Having visited the Danieli previously to view the breathtaking staircase and lobby we saved to stay at the Hotel Danieli to celebrate our 25th Wedding Anniversary.  Having read some of the previous reviews (post booking) we were prepared to be disappointed in our expectations.  However, from the moment we arrived until the moment we left the staff could not have been more friendly or helpful. The hotel had arranged a lagoon view room in the beautiful former Dandolo Palazzo part of the hotel . Within minutes of arriving we were treated to a bottle of Champagne and an amazing fruit basket as a gift from the hotel.  The breakfast and views from the terrace restaurant were to die for and it was difficult to tear ourselves away in the mornings to explore Venice.
