I've been here before. 25 years ago and was concerned that the dream of the Danieli I carry in my fondest memories would have been thwarted by being a part of a Global hotel group. The Danieli is a haven in the madness. Breakfast is wonderful, you live the view. The bar after 22:00 is an experience in itself. Again attentive and the pianist plays to his audience.
