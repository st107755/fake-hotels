Do NOT come to this Hotel on a budget. If you want a nice couple of days in a beautiful hotel in a great location , and money is not a major consideration in your plans then this is the place for you. Arrive by water taxi to the private water entrance is very stylish. Our room at the back was a bit noisy at times, due to trolleys for the street stalls being stored here and people getting to a small hotel at the back, but otherwise very good. Restaurant on the 4th floor has fantastic views, and if the weather is good there is a lovely terrace to view the lagoon and Grand Canal.
