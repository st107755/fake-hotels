To arrive in Venice is always wonderful, to arrive at the Danieli in a private launch under cloudless skies is a lifetime ambition fulfilled. A very warm welcome from the receptionist, who escorted us to our room, was a splendid start. The bedroom, in the annex alongside the main building, was huge, with a separate dressing room and lavish bathroom. The enormous bed probably the most comfortable we have ever slept in.   Unfortunately, on and off rain made the rooftop restaurant terrace unusable, but the views towards the Giudecca are still classic, and the breakfast choice of food and beverages enormous.
