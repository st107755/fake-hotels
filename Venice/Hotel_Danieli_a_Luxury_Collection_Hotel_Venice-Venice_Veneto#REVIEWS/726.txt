I spent many hours browsing TripAdvisor reviews before selecting the Danieli for our three-night February break. There were many hotels sitting above the Danieli in the “charts” of the best hotels in Venice, but the location and history of the hotel won us over. On arrival via water taxi the service was friendly and efficient. I had grabbed a very good rate online, so we were not surprised to be in one of their most basic (and small) rooms (93 - in the main building on the third floor). Despite being at the bottom of the range, the room was still well equipped and very comfortable. Housekeeping service was very good. Room was serviced at least three times a day, with a turndown service as expected in a Luxury collection hotel. The breakfast is a highlight.
