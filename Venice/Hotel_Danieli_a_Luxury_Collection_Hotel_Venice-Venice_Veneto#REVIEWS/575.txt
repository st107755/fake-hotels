The Danieli is part of the Starwood Group which has recently merged with Marriott. We booked the Danieli as our gold status with Marriott is also accepted by Starwood. The overall level of service from arrival to departure was excellent and helped by being upgraded to a room with a view overlooking the lagoon. If arriving at Venice Marco Polo Airport it is worth the extra cost to take a water taxi which will drop you (and more important your luggage) right outside the Hotel. This is a Hotel for a special occasion with relatively high room rates but worth it for the views and you also must eat in the terrace restaurant for breakfast and lunch or dinner.
