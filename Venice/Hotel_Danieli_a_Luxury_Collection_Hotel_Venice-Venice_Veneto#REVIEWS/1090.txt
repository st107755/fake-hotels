We have just stayed in Venice for our 20th wedding anniversary and as a treat we booked afternoon tea at Hotel Danieli, wasn't cheap but was a great experience. If you want to book afternoon tea their website just suggest that you book in advance which we did, we booked direct with the hotel the day before. The tea consists of canapés, scones, cakes and tea, you can pay extra for a glass of champagne but we were happy to have tea.  We sat in the bar area and the bar man came over and set the table for us.  The canapés are tiny but very tasty. Plenty of cakes, perhaps too many, we would have preferred more canapés. The bar man kept coming to check everything was ok which it was, we were never rushed, just left to enjoy and relax.
