We have stayed in Venice many times and in better places at a fraction of the cost. We were so disappointed when we arrived. The transfer from the airport was great fun and special. But then we were shown to a rather down at heel room. The view was indeed magnificent but the room really did need upgrading. We didn't feel looked after. We came back after a long day and our room wasn't even made up. Breakfast was ok but the buffet wasn't replenished and the site of guests queuing for eggs is not something a hotel should be proud of.  The only plus point, location.
