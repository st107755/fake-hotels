I just stayed at this hotel and absolutely loved it. The location is perfect about 100 metres to St Mark's Square and near all the great shops and restaurants. It is right on the water and easy to get to by boat. The hotel has real charm and is in typical Venetian style. The rooms are spacious by European standards, bathrooms are large and the beds are comfortable. The hotel has a really nice lounge and bar area to chill out and have drinks and a great rooftop restaurant. I would definitely stay at this hotel again because of its location, atmosphere and service.
