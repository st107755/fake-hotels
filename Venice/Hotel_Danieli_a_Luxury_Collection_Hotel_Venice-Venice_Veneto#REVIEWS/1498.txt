We stayed here from Oct 16 thru Oct 18, 2014 during our Italy vacation.  The interior is simply gorgeous, breathtaking, and its just beautiful to view.  Our room was a canal delux room with a decent size very nice bathroom.  They really try to maintain the old style architecture of all fixtures right to the old fashioned room keys.  The dining is on a much higher scale.  The rooftop restaurant is nice but they do not give many selections for those that do not eat seafood; however, the breakfast buffet is very enjoyable.  The concierge staff are all quite friendly; however, the receptionist/checkin staff could use a little more training on making their guest feel welcome and as though they really want you to be there.
