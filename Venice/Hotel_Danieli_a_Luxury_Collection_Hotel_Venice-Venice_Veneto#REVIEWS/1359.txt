I have long wanted to stay at the Danieli in Venice, so when my husband booked it, I was very excited.  He used a combination of Starwood points and cash for the room, which is close to an astronomical $1,000 US per night. We justified it as a splurge for our single night in Venice, on a holiday where we booked other very reasonably priced hotels. The location is good and bad. Convenient when coming off the vaporetto at San Zaccaria with luggage, but filled with throngs of tourists in groups that seemingly have no end. Not surprising given the hotel's proximity to Piazza San Marco.   We found out when we arrived that for that price we would not even have a view.
