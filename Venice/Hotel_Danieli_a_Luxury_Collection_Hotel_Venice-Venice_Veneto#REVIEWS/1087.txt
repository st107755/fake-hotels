While planning a 4 day trip to Venice during 2016 Carnival for my girlfriend and I, my research pointed me to the Hotel Danieli as the "must visit" and most authentic Carnival hotel experience in Venice. The best I can describe it the first time you walk in is like momentarily walking into the palace of a Disney movie!  For this reason alone (especially during Carnival) going to this hotel is a must.    The service was absolute top notch also.  The concierge recommendations for the opening night of La Traviata at the Teatro Venice was one of the highlights of our trip!
