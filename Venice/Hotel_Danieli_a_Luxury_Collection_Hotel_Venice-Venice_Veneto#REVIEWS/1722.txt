As I am writing this review, I am still here at the Danieli, sitting in room 291. After a great stay at the Savoy in London and then two days on the Orient Express to Venice, we got a room at the Hotel Danieli. First of all the location. Perfect, hands down. Two minutes walk from Piazza San Marco and with a Vaporetto station almost in front of the hotel. Does not get easier and better than that. Also even though there are other great hotels on the same island, the Danieli is simply right there once you get to San Marco. No need to walk through the maze that Venice is, like you have to do with many other hotels. So yes, it is convenient and again in an amazing location.
