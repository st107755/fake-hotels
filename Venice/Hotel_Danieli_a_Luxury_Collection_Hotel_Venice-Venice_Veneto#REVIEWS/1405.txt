Given its history, the Danieli, indeed, is quite an amazing hotel!   However, the service and furnishings did not match my expectations. Staff did not behave in a manner that one would expect from even first class stewards. They rushed us out of the check-out line to avoid a congregating crowd, and then did not return to provide us service as promised. Consequently, we had less time to catch our boat transport to the train station.   Additionally, when one entered the hotel, there was no "presence". At one point, the front desk was unstaffed. Room keys were just left in a pile with what seemed like a lack of security and order. Although hotel staff were not rude, the hotel just did not feel luxurious in its level of service.
