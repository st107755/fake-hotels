After reading mixed reviews about the Danieli this morning I felt compelled to share my experience from May 2013 as it was wonderful.  The location gets no better than this as the hub, "St Mark's" square is a few buildings away. The common spaces are beautiful, it is like being inside a jewel box. Our suite, on the first floor was above the desk and the odd time late at night you could hear someone speaking. This by no way deterred from our experience and we will even request this suite on our next visit as it was perfection.....very large, consisting of living room, bed chamber dressing room and marble bathroom.  But the BEST part was our two little window seats with shuttered windows that opened overlooking the entry and the Grand Canal.
