Stayed here a few years back on my first trip to venice, we have since stayed in Venice 4 more times at 3 different hotels. All the hotels were 4/5 star rated and depending on what you want out of Venice there is something to keep everyone happy. We found the room ok fitted out in an old style which matches the style of the hotel. The hotel it self is beautiful and we stayed on an upper floor room on the side of the hotel with a canal view which you could see the lagoon front if you looked to the right. The mini bar was not touched as was very expensive. We ate breakfast at the hotel every day and it was a buffet with a fantastic choice of food could not fault it.
