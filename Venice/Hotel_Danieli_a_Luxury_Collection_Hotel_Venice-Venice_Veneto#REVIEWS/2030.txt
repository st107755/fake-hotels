The Hotel Danieli is probably my second favorite hotel stay ever (the first is Ballynahinch Castle in Recess, Ireland, outside Galway). It absolutely fulfilled every wish I could imagine. The location is superb, a few steps away from Piazza San Marco and you can access a gondola from the side of the hotel. The old world charm, furnishings and atmosphere took my breath away: I can't imagine a more perfect hotel. The view from the terrace offers an amazing view of the Grand Canal and our meals were all quite delicious. We stayed in a small suite and it was so charming. Wherever I turned, I would see another beautiful, meticulous detail in the hotel millwork, glass, etc., that would knock my socks off! The staff is fabulous and will attend to your every need.
