First off, Hotel Danieli is a historic hotel, and a 5 minute walk away from the main attraction, St Mark's Square. The rooms are beautiful and the front desk staff is extremely friendly. Everyone there speaks english, and it is a very romantic hotel. If you are staying at the hotel, you need to visit the terrace at least once. The view is fantastic and is something that must be experienced. The staff tried to turn us away for breakfast because they had not finished setting up the terrace. Since we had an early train ride to Florence, we were insistent that we be seated on the terrace and we were. Unfortunately, they were not very happy with us so they gave us glares throughout our breakfast and provided us with minimal service.
