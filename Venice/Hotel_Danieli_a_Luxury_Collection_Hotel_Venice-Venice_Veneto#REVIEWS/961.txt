We stayed at the Danieli for two nights. The hotel is located in a perfect place only a bridge away from St Mark's square. The common areas are beautiful and luxurious. The room we had (facing the water) was nice but because it was on the 2nd floor it got a little noisy in the mornings and late at night. There was a little living room facing the water (someone might want to remove the rugs on the floor as my husband and I almost fell three times walking in) and the bedroom and bathroom were off the to the side (very nice size but on the dark side). The front desk was extremely helpful (especially Ennio) when my mom found herself stranded at the hotel because of strikes in France and accommodated her for one more night.
