The Hotel Danieli is very convenient to San Marco Square, situated as it is about three doors behind Doge's Palace, fronting on the Quay.  We stayed there with a cruise/tour, and based on previous stays in Venice, the area of San Marco is not my favorite place to stay, since it is so crowded with tourists.  Having said that, the Danieli is a fine hotel.  the inner atrium is drop dead gorgeous.  On the ground level, there is ample public space for gathering large groups.  The lobby also has two internet machines available to hotel guests.    The room was of medium size.  The air conditioning was very effective (we were there in hot mid-September). The beds were just about the most comfortable of any hotel we have ever stayed in.
