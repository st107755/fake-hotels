Staying at the Hotel Danieli is like living in history. The Doges Palace and adjoning ancient prision were yards away from our bedroom window and St Marks Square just round the corner. The hotel itself has it's own illustrious history and as you enter you're faced with a spectacular renaissance inspired lounge and bar area.  Our room, whilst at the back of the hotel, was well proportioned, 19th Century inspired in it's decor and a balcony with that view of The Doges Palace. The modern technology (wifi) worked particularly well, and, as you would expect, the room was well appointed. The bathroom was on the small side but decorated with beautiful marble and, gain, well supplied with all accessories.
