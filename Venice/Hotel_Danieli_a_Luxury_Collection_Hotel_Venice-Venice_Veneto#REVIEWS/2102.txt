Stayed in ther Danieli purely because the idea of using the same bathroom and bedroom as the lovely A. Jolie was too tempting. Made a number of calls to the reservation centre and got myself a 4night stay including 1 night in the suite used for the movie.   Arriving in Venice i was met at the airport and escorted to my private speedboat (more James Bond than the tourist). Arriving at the hotel you are taken to a private side entrance instead of having to walk the 10metres from the Gondola moorings to the main hotel entrance. Walking into the hotel you are immediately struck with the scale of this old palazzo building and the huge sweeping staircase looks as grand as it does in the movie.
