My girlfriend and I stayed here a couple nights in Feb 2016, 2/25/16-2/28/16, and had a wonderful time overall. The hotel is great, has an amazing ambience to it the second you walk in..just gorgeous on the inside. The staff across the board was very pleasant to interact with, from the doormen, to the concierge, to the check-in staff. Obviously this is the norm when in a place like Venice, but how can you not love pulling up to your hotel in a water taxi!? :-)  Very neat experience.  Great location being right next to Piazza San Marco and the Bridge of Sighs. Water bus stop pretty much right outside of the hotel as well (if you don't feel like walking anymore).  One personal, small note I would make is that everything was extremely expensive at the hotel & restaurant.
