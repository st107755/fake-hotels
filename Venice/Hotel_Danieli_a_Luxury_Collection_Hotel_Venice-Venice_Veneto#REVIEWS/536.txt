The Danieli lived up to all the hype.  As soon as you walk-in, the hotel foyer takes your breath away.  The staircase known to be from medieval times is the showpiece of the hotel and so it should be, it is absolutely amazing.  The entire foyer, waiting room, bars, and internal balconies and hallways are incredible, and knowing the age of the palace and the history it holds brings it to a new level.  The staff and concierge were all great, although check-in could have been a little faster.  We requested a complimentary room upgrade but was told the hotel was full, however soon after I overheard the reception staff overing available rooms to others, so I would have preferred to have just been told the truth in the first place.
