I have decided to travel to Venice at end of July and the Daniel's was a complete surprise for me. Best location in Venice, about 50 meters from Dodge Palace with an amazing sight above Grand Chanel and Laguna. The big surprise is the building, from XV or XVI century, real amazing! The room was a surprise, large room with all the commodities from the XXI century. After a nice building and one wonderful room the cherry in the top of the cake! The breakfast in the balcony at last floor with a huge variety, very professional and friendly staff. The sight from balcony with an excellent breakfast make me feel like one person in a “fair tale”. As a SPG Platinum member I can tell that this hotel is one of the best I found in the world!
