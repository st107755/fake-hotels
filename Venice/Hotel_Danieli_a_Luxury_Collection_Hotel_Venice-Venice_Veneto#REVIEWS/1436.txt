After 25 years of marriage we returned to the place of our honeymoon and chose to stay for 2 nights at the Danieli. It proved to be the perfect place for a special celebration. Everything lived up to our expectations. All staff were absolutely charming- including ALL the concierge who had some rather bad press in other reviews. People fell over themselves to help us and we were charmed in turn. I cannot fault the experience , food was lovely, drinks divine and the complimentary bottle of prosecco the icing on the cake. We love Venice during Carnivale and it only helped to make our stay even more magical. The hotel is the perfect setting for a costume extravaganza . It is not a cheap option by any means but if you want to feel like special then it is the place for you.
