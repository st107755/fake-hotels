The Danieli has always been my favourite hotel in Venice, perhaps anywhere. We recently stayed for our 25th Wedding Anniversary after travelling on the Orient Express. We particularly asked to stay there rather than the Cipriani as we love the beauty and ambience of an old palace which it has retained. The staff are always courteous and helpful and were very patient with me when I confessed at reception that I couldn't find my way to our room. The charming young man escorted me to my door and was kind enough to say that he'd had the same problem when he first arrived there which made me feel less stupid. We had a De Luxe room with Lagoon View on the second floor which was stunningly beautiful and made us regret that we were only staying two nights.
