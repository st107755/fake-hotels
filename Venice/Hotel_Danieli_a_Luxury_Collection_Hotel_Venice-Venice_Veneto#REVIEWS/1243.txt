A magical hotel set in the heart of Venice near all the sights, perfect for site seeing and drinking in St Mark's square! The hotel is beautiful old school Venetian style with class!   We also ate at the restaurant on the terrace which turned out to be our favourite meal of the trip. However it is worth noting that we often go to restaurants in London/ france/ spain etc and travel frequently and found the hotel Danieli terrace on the expensive side but it is worth noting Venice in general is expensive and the Terrace is an amazing way to spend a long lazy dinner with the bustle of Venice down below and incredible views of the water, catherdral and hotel Cipriani. It was certainly a night to rememeber!  I only gave four stars as the staff are so rude and unhelpful!
