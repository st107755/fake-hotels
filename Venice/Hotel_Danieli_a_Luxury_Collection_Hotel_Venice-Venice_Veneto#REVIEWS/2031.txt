What a location!! It does not get any better than this. Firstly pre book a water taxi online and enjoy the transfer from the airport straight to the jetty outside the hotel. You are doing yourself out of a magical experience if you don't. Checked in with my gf and young baby,staff could not be more helpful. Stayed in a lagoon view room which was a first for me and well worth the expense. As my room was in the old part of the palace it was beautiful. A little tired maybe and could do with freshening up.Bathroom was clean, spacious and luxurious. Lovely. Breakfast was fabulous. Great choice of everything you could want and good coffee. The view from the terrace is probably one of the best in Venice and worth having breakfast just for that.
