My husband and I visited the Danieli, Venice in February without the kids.  It was incredibly romantic and an unforgettable experience.  The private taxi pulling up outside the Danieli was extremely exciting and felt very special.  From the moment we arrived, the service at the Danieli was attentive, non-intrusive and thoughtful.  The hotel is so much more beautiful than the photographs could ever depict.  Our room was small but beautifully fitted, very clean and quiet allowing a good night sleep after a lot of walking.  The bar is exquisite with the best pianist/vocalist.  The cocktails are excellent and accompanied with tasty snacks.  The breakfast at the Danieli is set over the most unbelievable views.
