My husband and I were going to Venice for the first time following a cruise around Italy.  We asked our travel agent for her recommendation and, because it was our first visit, she suggested the Danieli.  Arriving by water taxi was fun ,but walking into the lobby literally takes your breath away.  The front desk staff was polite and efficient.  It was not check in time so we went up to the terrace restaurant for coffee and pastry.  It is expensive but the view alone is worth the price.  The restaurant manager (Christian Iannone) was charming and funny and apologized that he would be out of town for the rest of our visit.  We took the short walk past the Bridge of Sighs and the Doge's Palace to St. Marks. Literally a five minute walk.
