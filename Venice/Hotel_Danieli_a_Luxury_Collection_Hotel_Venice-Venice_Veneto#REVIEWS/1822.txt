One should certainly allow four days here, and the Danieli hotel is perfectly placed to explore all the sights. The Danieli arranged to meet us at Marco Polo Airport where we were whisked away in a private boat directly to the steps of the hotel and where we were met and registered in to one of the most romantic hotels we have been fortunate to stay in. Our room was located in the more modern wing with perfect facilities and facing west, very quiet. We forgot our adaptor but within minutes the Concierge sent one up to our room. The staff were absolutely fantastic, polite, helpful and the Concierge team excellent. The location of the hotel overlooking the Grand Canal could not have been better.
