The Danieli is rated a five-star hotel and certainly lives up to that.  We just spent four nights there, and it was a wonderful stay.  The hotel doesn't look much from the outside, but the interior is absolutely wonderful - truly a jewel of a hotel.  Our room was spacious and beautifully decorated in blue and gold, with a huge and very comfortable bed and every amenity you could think of - even a walk-in dressing area.  On the first evening I rang to ask for a rubber shower mat - a few minutes later a smiling maid delivered it and five minutes after that Housekeeping rang to check it had been delivered.
