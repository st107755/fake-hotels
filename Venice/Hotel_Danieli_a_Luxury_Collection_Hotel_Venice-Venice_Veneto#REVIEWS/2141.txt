Having been to Venice before, you know it's going to be busy, sometime unwelcoming and rather expensive. The Hotel Danieli embodies these traits in bucketloads I am afraid to say.   On the positive side our room had a lagoon view in the modern part of the hotel and the vista is simply stunning. The room is well appointed, modern and was kept clean. However, the balconies on these rooms afford no privacy with screens between neighbours barely at waist height.     Part of the attraction of staying here was to experience a dinner in the La Terazza restuarant overlooking the lagoon. Our tour operator booked a table for us for two nights a good six months in advance.
