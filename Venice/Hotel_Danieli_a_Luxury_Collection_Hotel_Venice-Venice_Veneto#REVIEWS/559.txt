My family of four had a corner suite and an attached room with balcony for our three night stay in Venice. The location was ideal for the touring we had planned, literally steps from St Marks square without being right in the middle of the noise and chaos. The beds and linens were very comfortable, the bathroom and amenities appropriately luxurious.  It is definitely the case that the hotel could use a refresh, though it has that old-Europe feel that I find appealing, with rich red velvets and gold toned accents.
