I took a short trip to Venice with a friend off season for 1 night. My agent put us in a hotel outside the city so we had to find something central. We walked past the Danieli and decided to inquire. They gave us a room for the night for just over £200 which seemed fair for one of the best hotels in such a location. We were grateful to check in early.  Fortunately we got the old part of the building. It was the Italian equivalent of staying in Claridges in London. It was warm, clean and the staff were helpful. We reduced our bill by not eating at the hotel.  Food and drink prices were the same as in London. The location is perfect. The interior is stunning.
