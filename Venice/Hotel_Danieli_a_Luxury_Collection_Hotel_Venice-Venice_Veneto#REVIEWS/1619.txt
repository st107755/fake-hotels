Having wanted to go to Venice for as long as I can remember - I finally booked it for our 18 wedding anniversary.  I based my decision of hotel on close proximity to the water taxi drop off point and for style.  The Danieli Hotel is a beautiful hotel.  The only reason I wouldnt give it 5 stars is because the price of the breakfast - we didnt use this facility - based on the continental being around 48 Euros each.  We were upgraded to the refurbed side.  I had heard it was better in the old part, but it was fine.  The bed and pillows were amazing and  it was only noisy when we had the window open, but when we sorted the air con out - we kept it shut and heard no noise at all.  The Daniela is in a wonderful position, near St.Mark's Sqaure.
