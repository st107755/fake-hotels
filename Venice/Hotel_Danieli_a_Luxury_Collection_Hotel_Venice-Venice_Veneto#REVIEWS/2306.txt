Booked the Danieli because of its history and luxurious image. Whilst the hotel certainly does embody all that Venice is about, its massively over priced. Granted the Euro means that travelling in Europe is costly at the moment, but the Danieli makes it so you cannot even afford a drink at the end of the day. With a small cup of hot chocolate priced at £8, sadly its not possible to spend any time in the luxurious surroundings of the hotel. We had to spend our time between exploring and going out for dinner, in the bedroom. As there were no drink making facilities the only thing I could afford to do was relax in the luxrious bath.
