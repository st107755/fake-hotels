My family and I stayed at this hotel for three nights in September. I assumed that for the price and the 5 star rating, this hotel was going to be the best hotel we would stay at during our two week vacation in Italy. Well, I was wrong. The hotel and room itself was fine. Other than my room’s window facing a brick wall and windows with bars on it. The bed was comfortable and the bathrooms were nice. However, the staff that worked at this hotel were very rude. They seemed like they were too bothered by my family and I. The concierge was helpful for the most part but every other part of the staff, from the check in to the bartenders to the wait staff at breakfast, were all rude and unhelpful. I like to use the word bougie.
