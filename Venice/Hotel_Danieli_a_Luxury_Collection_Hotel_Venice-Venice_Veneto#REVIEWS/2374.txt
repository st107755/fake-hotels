We spent a long weekend at the Danieli and loved both the hotel and Venice.  We got a lagoon view room and what a view - it can only be described as breathtaking!  We kept the shutters open all the time - although the street below is the main thorough fare for tourists so you may want to keep the glass windows closed to cut down the noise!! The room was small but beautifully furnished and the standard of service we received from all the staff - from the front desk to the cleaner was great - they were all really friendly. The hotel is an old hotel but that is what we went for.  It's location is wonderful and is right beside the Water bus station where the No 1 takes you right along the Grand Canal.
