The hotel has a very dramatic interior which suits the whole Venice experience. The location is perfect right next to St Marks Square. There are three Danieli hotels attached but the central one is the original and the most historic.  The terrace on the top floor has some of the best views across the grand canal, so breakfast is pretty special.  However our room was very noisy and with a cramped bathroom.  We had paid for an upgrade so thought there would have been a little more space.  The decoration was very tasteful and the room had everything we needed.  Also the cleaning staff were caring and helpful with any requests. The big disappointment was that on our anniversary we thought we would eat dinner in the Terrace Restaurant, which did not live up to expectations.
