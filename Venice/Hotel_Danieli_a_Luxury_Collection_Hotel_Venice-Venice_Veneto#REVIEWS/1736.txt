Arguably the best location in Venice, a few steps from St Mark's Square, Harry's Bar etc and with S. Zaccaria and San Marco vaporetto stops right outside. If you can afford the lagoon facing rooms take one and if you can't - take one anyway! Life is too short to count a few pounds against the lasting memories and views from your window and the Terraza restaurant at breakfast.  The Palazzo Dandolo is exactly as you see in all the pictures and the Excelsior wing also have great views and facilities etc. The staff are fabulous, knowledgable, courteous and polite - they even humoured me and my terrible efforts at speaking italian!  Look be warned, this is not cheap.
