If you are looking for the perfect hotel to spend a romantic getaway in Venice, Danieli is the place. Located on the Canale and besides the Dux Palace and the Piombi, it is in the center of the historical sites. The view is incredible. Do not miss the terrace. The hotel comprises three palaces. The oldest was the home of the Dandolo family. Three duxes belong to that family.
