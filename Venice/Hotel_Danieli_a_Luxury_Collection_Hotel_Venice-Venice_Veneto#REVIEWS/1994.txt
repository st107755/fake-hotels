In our 20 day trip through Europe, this was our favorite hotel.  We even stayed at the St. Regis Rome but were disappointed by service issues there.  The Danieli's lobby was like nothing we've ever seen before and our suite was amazing.  Service was excellent, as well as the housekeeping.  It's quite confusing to find your room as you can't take any elevator, different elevators go to different levels and different parts of the hotel.  Eventually we learned we had to go the same way in and out and were fine.  We didn't eat at the hotel, but found numerous places to eat within a short walk.  St Mark's Square is very close and one morning I woke up early to photograph the square without the crowds of people.
