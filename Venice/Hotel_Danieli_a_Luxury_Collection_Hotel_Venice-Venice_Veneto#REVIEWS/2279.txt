My wife and I spent 3 nights at the Danieli and from the moment a member of their staff met us at our train, we knew we were in for a treat! We could have stayed anywhere in Venice but chose the Danieli based on its location and reputation. If you are looking for a romantic stay in Venice, I cannot think of another hotel that could compare. There may be newer and arguably nicer hotels in Venice, but none have the history, ambiance and location of this hotel.  Our host greeted us at our train from Florence and immediately took us to our water taxi. Upon pulling up to the entrance, we were greeted in a manner one would expect from a top hotel.
