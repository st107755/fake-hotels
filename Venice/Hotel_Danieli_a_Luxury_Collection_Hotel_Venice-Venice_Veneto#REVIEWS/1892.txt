This was the third time we had stayed at the Danieli and it continues to provide its guests with comfort and a sense of luxury.  Our room looked out over the water and gave us one of the most romantic views in the world. St Mark's Square is 100 metres away and the hotel is easy walking distance to everything that one comes to Venice to see. The staff are efficient and helpful. And the hotel, an old palace, is beautiful; the entry lobby is one of the grandest rooms I have ever seen and was beautifully decorated for Christmas. We had our Christmas Eve dinner in the hotel's Terrace Restaurant- an excellent and considering that it was Christmas Eve,reasonably priced meal.
