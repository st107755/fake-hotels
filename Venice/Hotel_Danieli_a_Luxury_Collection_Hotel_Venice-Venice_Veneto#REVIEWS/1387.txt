My wife and I were in Venice celebrating our 25th wedding anniversary and, by chance, I came across the Hotel Danieli and its roof terrace restaurant and decided to book a table for lunch.   And I am so glad I did. Despite the understated exterior, the interior is magnificent in the truest sense of the word. When we arrived at the rooftop restaurant (on a Sunday lunchtime) we were told that lunch would be a "buffet-style brunch" comprising a variety of Italian dishes.   The concierge undersold this in a huge way as the food was nothing short of spectacular. But even that was put in the shade by the restaurant itself.
