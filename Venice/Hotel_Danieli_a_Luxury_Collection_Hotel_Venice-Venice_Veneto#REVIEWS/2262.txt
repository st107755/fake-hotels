Taking a water taxi from the airport to the Danieli (Euro100 and very easy) is one of life's great experiences, being ferried directly to the landing of the hotel and met by the Concierge.  The staff are exceptional, and respond well to amateur attempts at  Italian language.  I found they could not do enough for me, and responded immediately to any requests. The hotel has centuries of style and class emanating from every inch of the building, with a spectacular foyer that is worth visiting even if you are not a guest. The location makes the splurge worthwhile, and one must stay overnight in Venice when all the crowds have returned to their cruise ships.
