We were so excited to stay at the iconic Danieli. Unfortunately it did not live up to all the hype. The hotel and decor is dated and while to some degree, that adds to the charm, on the other hand, the hotel really can do with a big makeover - the interiors appear tired as do the staff. There isn't the zip nor enthusiasm of a world class 5 star hotel and many aspects of the hotel are just not cared for the way one would if there was really pride of ownership. As an example, the business lounge (if you can even call it that), is in the main lobby/ bar area. So while one is enjoying a cocktail and listening to the piano, there is also frantic business people in the same area; working away on the hotels computers in a way that detracts from the atmosphere.
