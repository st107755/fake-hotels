The Danieli is quintessential Venice and a must see for all who visit.  We were lucky enough to be upgraded to a lagoon view room in the oldest most oppulant part of the hotel.  The room had a little age on it, but was comfortable.  I did worry how much the room below heard us on the creaky wood floors.  The sumptuous breakfast on the terrace with a view of the lagoon was PRICELESS...I couldn't wait to start my day.  Cocktails in the lobby in the evening felt like I was a visiting dignitary or possibly a hollywood celebrity.
