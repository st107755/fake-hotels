I had seen this hotel in various movies, so I wanted to experience it. Luxury charming centrally located next to St Marks Plaza.  The suite we has was spacious with a little patio looking over the grand canal and the side canal that you get off when you arrive. Expensive but worth ever penny if you are going to do Venice once then go for it. But now I have decided I love Venice and will go back. Maybe try another luxury palace.... We had dinner at the hotel on there roof restaurant over looking the grand canal. The food was amazing. We had a prixfixe meal one night with culinary magic from the chef. Watching the cruise ship float by was fun especially since we had gotten off the windsurf that morning. Breakfast was good.
