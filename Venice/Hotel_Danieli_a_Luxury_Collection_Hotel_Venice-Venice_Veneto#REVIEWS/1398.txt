We stayed at the Danieli from 5-8 April where we had two rooms, one for our two older teen girls and one for my husband and I. My husband informed the lady during check-in that we would like the rooms to be on the same floor if possible as it would make it easier for us to see the girls. The lady was very helpful and she made sure we had two rooms next to each other. She accompanied us personally to our rooms. On the way there she met a colleague and he asked her see thing in Italian. He disappeared and then re-appeared again just before we entered our rooms giving us all a pack of chocolate Easter eggs each! What a lovely surprise! The lady told us a bit of the history of the buildings as well. We were very pleased with our rooms.
