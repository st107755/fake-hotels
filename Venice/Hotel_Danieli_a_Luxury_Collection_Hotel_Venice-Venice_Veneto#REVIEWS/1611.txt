I recently stayed at the Danieli for 3 nights with my husband and 2 teenage daughters to celebrate my 50th birthday. Having read some previous reviews I was slightly apprehensive that the hotel would not meet our high expectations but am glad to say these fears were unfounded - this traditional gem of a hotel did not disappoint. When the water taxi from the airport swept into the grand canal and I caught my first view of the Danieli right beside St Marks Square and overlooking the canal, it was even more impressive than I had imagined. Our rooms were in the refurbished Excelsior wing and were very well appointed with good bathrooms and a very comfortable bed. The rooms were inter-connecting and perfect for families.
