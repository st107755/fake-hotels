My boyfriend arranged a surprise holiday for my 40th birthday in May  to the Danieli Hotel.  When you first enter the hotel, the decor just takes your breath away, it certainly left me speechless. We had a wonderful room, the sheer luxury of fabric on the walls and a spotlessly clean bathroom. The hotel even arranged champagne and chocolates to be brought to our room for my birthday. The level of customer service we experienced was faultless, nothing was too much trouble for any of the staff. If you take the time to engage in a little conversation with the staff, (we tried a few Italian phrases,) you will find out about the history of the hotel and of the famous faces that have stayed there over the years.
