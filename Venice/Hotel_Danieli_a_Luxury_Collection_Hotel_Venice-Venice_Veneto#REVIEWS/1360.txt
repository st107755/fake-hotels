Our third visit to Venice and decided to stay at the Danieli.  Major plus - the location of the Danieli is just 5 minutes walk to the Doges Palace and San Marco Square.  This makes it super easy to see Venice and get to the tourist spots early as the queues can get quite crazy after 9.30am.   Service is generally good with the staff being helpful and friendly especially reception staff. Hotel looks beautiful at reception area and was once part of the Doges Palace.   Rooms are a little dated and seriously needs a refurbishment. We were "upgraded" to a room with a view of the canal - nice view with a small balcony to people watch.
