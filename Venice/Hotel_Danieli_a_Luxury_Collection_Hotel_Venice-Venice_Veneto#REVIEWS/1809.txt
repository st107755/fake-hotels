We used Starwood points to stay one night in mid May before a cruise.  The location of the hotel was very convenient. We took a water taxi which we arranged ourselves from the airport and were dropped off just in front of the hotel entrance.  The hotel lobby is as beautiful as the pictures.   The front desk check in gentleman could not have been nicer. We arrived around 11:30 am in the morning but were able to check into our room after just waiting about 15 minutes.  He personally walked us up to our room while a bellman took our bags. Our room was surprisingly large and was very well appointed, as was the bathroom.  The bed was very comfortable.
