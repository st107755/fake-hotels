We stayed for two nights at the Hotel Danieli. The hotel is magnificent and offers a glimpse of Venice of the 15th or 16th century. Our room was nicely decorated. The hotel arranged a private boat trip to Murano to see one of the glass factories which is a must to see the ancient art of glass making. We also enjoyed the breakfast in the morning on the terrace overlooking the bay of Venice and San Marco. We truly enjoyed our time.   However and having stayed at many luxury hotels, there is a "but" that management should really take into consideration. One afternoon and we were thirsty from walking around the city and used the elevator to reach the terrace of Hotel Danieli and ordered Lemon Sodas. Each tiny bottle was Euro 15.---  Excuse me! This is a total rip-off.
