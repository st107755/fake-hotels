We booked the Danieli hotel in Venice for three nights as a part of our Italian honeymoon. This decision was made purely on the basis of the hotel's reputation as a high end, classy hotel. We therefore had high expectations, especially when paying the rate for a 5 star hotel, we expected class and quality as a minimum. Sadly we felt a little disappointed and rather let down that this hotel has clearly lowered its standards, in particular to dress code. In the Terrazza restaurant for example, 'jacket required' didn't seem to apply, as some customers were there in shorts and t-shirts wearing flip flops in the evening! In the bar area the majority of customers were dressed as if they were in a Disney World resort hotel and not in an establishment with any class!
