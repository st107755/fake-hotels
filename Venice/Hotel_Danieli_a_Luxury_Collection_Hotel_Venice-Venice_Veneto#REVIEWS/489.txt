As SPG members, we have been sampling hotels and restaurants around the world, and Hotel Danieli will be added to our favorites list if/when we return to Venice. A warm welcome by the Reception Staff, and the ease of having our boat pull right up to the hotel's side entrance, both coming and going, minimized bag-drag trauma that most people face in Venezia.
