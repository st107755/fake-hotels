At the end of this past April and before I joined a Tour of Itlay, my daughter and four sons secretly booked a room for one night at the Danieli for me.  From the moment I walked through the revolving door into the marbled, fairytale lobby it was perfect.  When my daughter, who was my traveling companion, just happened to mention to Marco (the young man behind the reception desk) that it was my birthday, he upgraded the room to one with a view of the lagoon. Staying at the Danieli had been my fantasy for years, and I was not disappointed.  Although the room was not large, the view of the lagoon with the Lido in the distance and the picturesque rows of gondolas tethered below me like teams of black and silver horses more than made up for it.
