We stayed here for seven nights towards the end of October 2016.  The hotel is in a fantastic location, right on the Riva Degli Schiavoni, just a bridge away from the San Zaccaria ACTV Vaporetto and Allilaguna (Linea Blu) stops.  The Arsenale stop is only 5 minutes away in the opposite direction.  So, it couldn't be more convenient - only 5 minutes or so walk to St. Mark's Square.  We had booked a Standard Double Room, but we're upgraded on arrival to a Deluxe Double. Our room was at the front of the hotel overlooking the little canal at the side of the hotel and the Laguna itself. The room was extremely comfortable.  The hotel is located in an ancient building and the decor suits the property with fans and heavy drapes.
