We have just returned from a three night stay at the Metropole and what a treat it was. A beautiful location between San zaccharia  and Arsenale, right on the waterfront and a short walk to St marks square, so ideally situated for the main sights.  Hotel itself is warm, sumptuous, cosy, beautifully decorated and styled and clearly the owners have spent a great deal of time getting the feel right. We loved the rooms, beautiful bathrooms, opulently decorated and good linens and very clean. We particularly admired the owners collections on display around the building, especially on the second floor.  The staff were very professional and attentive, remembering your name and room number goes a long way for good customer service.
