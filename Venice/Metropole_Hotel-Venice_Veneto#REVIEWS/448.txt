We stayed for one night after a Greek Isles cruise at this beautiful hotel. We would definitely love to return for a longer stay. It is a perfect location along the canal with public water taxi piers very close. We had Alilaguna blue line tickets and we had to walk over just one bridge (whole walk maybe 3 minutes). The front desk staff was immediately welcoming and took out a map to show us where to walk to avoid crowds. We were very early for check-in, so they stowed our bags with no problem. We had the Garden Annex room that was just lovely with a modern bathroom! The garden area is  gorgeous and we enjoyed walking through it on the way to our room (we had perfect weather which helped).
