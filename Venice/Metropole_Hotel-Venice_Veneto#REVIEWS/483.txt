Our recent trip to Venice was dreamy and magical, and our home while there was the sumptuous Hotel Metropole.  We came to Venice on the occasion of our 25th wedding anniversary to renew our vows and brought along our 9 year old niece and 12 year old nephew (who served as our maid of honor and best man respectively). The memories from this trip and, in particular, the experience of staying at this hotel will remain with all of us forever.  Five stars is simply not enough for the Hotel Metropole of Venice. And I would add that it was worth every Euro to stay here. The hotel, and all its furnishings and decor, is Venice not as cliche, but rather as the epitome of what Venice is, was, and should always be.
