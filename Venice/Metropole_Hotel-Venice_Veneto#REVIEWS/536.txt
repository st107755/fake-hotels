Centrally located close to San Marco - the Metropole Hotel was a great base for us to explore Venice.  It is right next to San Marco square, so you will never get lost trying to find your way back to the hotel :) The staff team, from reception to concierge to housekeeping, were very professional and helpful at all times - which really differentiate it from many hotels in Europe.  The rooms were nicely presented notwithstanding that this is a rather old building.  The collection of art objects along the corridors are interesting to browse although some may find them a bit too overwhelming, although they do add to the character of the hotel.
