We just returned from a seven day lovely stay at the Metropole. As we are hotel owners ourselves in the California wine country we are naturally overly picky about other properties we visit. Alas, we could not find a single fault with this hotel.   The accomodations were spacious, clean and extremely comfortable . The decor of the hotel was beautiful; a combination of Venetian antiques (not overly done) combined with modern amenities. The staff was attentive, helpful and very friendly. The breakfasts were consistently fresh and the service outstanding. The food and service at the Met restaurant was, as expected, first class.  We would not hesitate to recommend the Metropole again as the experience we had could not have been better.
