Arrived in style with a private water taxi from the airport taking us to the hotel's own jetty, expensive, but definitely worth it. Excellent location compared to some of the other hotels we had considered and pleased we had chosen the Metropole. Check-in was immediate and staff very friendly. We had asked for an upper floor room and found ourselves on the 1st of 3 floors. The room was small, but comfortable and had plenty of wardrobe and drawer space. The bathroom was also small, with a shower over the bath, a shower curtain and not much room to move as the shower head was in the middle of the bath rather than at the end.
