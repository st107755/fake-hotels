For our return trip to Venice after many many years, we wanted a full-on luxury Venetian experience and picked this hotel.  Our tastes lean towards the clean modern look so the hotel was a lot to take in but we did not find it overwhelming or oppressive as we have with other historic hotels.  That being said, we will not be taking any of their design ideas home with us.   The hotel has an excellent location overlooking the main lagoon within a short distance from San Marco and conveniently right next to a vaporetto stop.  However, the area can get very busy during the day with numerous tour groups and vendors parked outside.  Walking into the hotel amidst such frenzy can be quite the oasis with its dark interiors, period furnishings and signature scent.
