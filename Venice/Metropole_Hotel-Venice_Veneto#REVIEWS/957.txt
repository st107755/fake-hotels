The wife and I stayed at the Metropole over the Valentine weekend and here are our thoughts....  Location - excellent. It is very close to San Marco square and looks over the waterfront, which in the morning, can be quite stunning.   Food - I can't comment on the Michelin-star lunch and dinner as we didn't try it but we did try the free inclusive breakfast. Personally, I thought it was good, but not great. Why? Because I would have liked a selection of hot foods for breakfast but you'd have to pay for these. Instead we were offered the standard continental choices -  meat slices, croissants, fruit etc.   Service - on the whole, pretty good actually.
