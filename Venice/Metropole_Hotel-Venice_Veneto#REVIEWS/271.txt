Stepping off your private water taxi into the foyer of the Metropole Hotel is like stepping back in time. The boutique style hotel of only 67 rooms is situated in the heart of venice and is crammed to the rafters with character, charm and sophistication. Upon arrival the concierge will suggest you explore each floor of the Hotel in turn to discover a myriad of original artefacts and antics on display which all add to the ambience of this amazing 15th century building.
