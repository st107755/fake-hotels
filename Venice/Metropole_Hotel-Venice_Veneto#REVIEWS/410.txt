In Nov we stayed at this hotel after travelling on the Orient Express. We arrived by water taxi and were delivered to the hotels own dock at the side. We booked a junior suite with Grand Canal views (Room 119). The room was lovely affording magnificent views from two aspects; two front windows and one on the side. As we were on the first floor we were concerned about noise in the eve but this was not a problem as the turn down service closed the shutters keeping out the sounds of Venice. In the morning it was lovely to open the shutters to the hustle and bustle! We slept well in the large comfortable bed and enjoyed breakfast in the garden room.
