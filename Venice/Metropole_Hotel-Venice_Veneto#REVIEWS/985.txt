We stayed at the Metropole in May of this year- after a lot of research- to celebrate my husband's birthday. Firstly, yes, Venice is as wonderful as everyone says it is, despite the crowds and the expense. Just to stand on the Rialto bridge and view the Grand Canal took my breath away. So if you get a chance to visit - take it.   The Metropole is well located and quite ordinary looking from the outside. However inside it is a lot more pleasing. We found the communal areas of the hotel to be fantastic - very atmospheric and interesting. Outside is a beautiful garden in which you can chill out and relax -read a book or order coffee or cocktails.
