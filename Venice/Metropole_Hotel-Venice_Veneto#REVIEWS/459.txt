Beautiful hotel facing the Grand Canal and the basilica of Santa Maria Della Salute the Hotel Metropole is an elegant reminder of the comfort and styles of the past with a focus on modern expectations in accommodation and technology. From a simply delicious buffet breakfast in the morning to a complimentary birthday persecco at the end of the day, the highly professional staff addressed all our questions and needs. We had two suites - next door to each other (suites 203 and 245) - which simplified the task of monitoring and communicating with the kids. The rooms were simply stunning. Antique cherubs graced the headboard of our bed while rich red tones dominated the room. Shutters opened to a view of a canal just below.
