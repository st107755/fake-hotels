This is a luxury hotel that delivers a very patchy experience, some great and some bad. It is VERY expensive and when one splurges at 550 Euros for a room, one would expect an incredible stay. Not so. The only luxury hotel I have checked into in years without hearing a word from the concierge in advance of our arrival. No email communication...nothing! Not even an inquiry about our possible time of arrival.  We had a direct view of the lagoon from our room which was attractively furnished but VERY small for the price. Very ordinary bathroom for today's luxe hotels.  Dinner at the Met restaurant was SUPERB! Original food, expertly prepared and well served. Kudos to the restaurant team.
