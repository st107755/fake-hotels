We booked a night at this hotel in Venice on line as we couldn’t get into the larger better known hotel we wanted to stay in . What a welcome surprise , from the attentive staff at check in to the breakfast staff ... all couldn’t do enough for us .  The hotel is literally dripping in history and if you are an antiques buff like me , it was eye candy on every floor ! I spent an hour looking at all the private collections displayed around the hotel ... from the antique fan collection , the calling card box collection to the antique hand bag collection . The decor made me feel like I had stepped back in time . The breakfasts are served in a quaint cool garden at the back of the hotel which is also beautifully appointed .
