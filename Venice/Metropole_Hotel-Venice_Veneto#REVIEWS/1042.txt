We stayed at the Metropole for two nights in April 2004. The holiday had started badly as we missed our flight and had to travel the following morning. We phoned the hotel at 5pm on the evening that we were due to arrive to be told that the hotel's cancellation policy dictated that 48 hours notice of cancellation was required but the manager would 'see what he could do'. When we came to pay the bill at the end of the stay, we discovered that we had not been charged for the first night which we thought was very benevolent of the hotel as we had given such short notice. Our room, a junior suite was fantastic - very tastefully decorated with antique furniture and carved marble pillars surrounding the massive bed.
