My boyfriend and I stayed only 1 night in Venice. We found the Metropole online at hotels.com. We wanted a room with a view or balcony and we managed to find 1 room (one of the junior suites) at the Metropole with a nice little balcony view overlooking the Rio della Pieta. The junior suites are all decorated differently and we loved ours the most (we had a chance to see a few other available rooms). The hotel was small yet beautiful and has a luxurious feel with rich colors and  gorgeous decor. It is decorated with elegant antiques.The all-you-can-eat breakfast was amazing!
