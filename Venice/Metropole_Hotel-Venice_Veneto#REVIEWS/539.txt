Just came back from Italy on a week long birthday trip, after much research and debating , settled on the Metropole Hotel for our short stay in Venice. It was the best value for the money, we booked directly through the hotels website and the rates were dramatically less than any other site featuring this hotel , the other perk was that our rate included breakfast which otherwise would have been 30 euros per person ! I booked a canal view room, which was beautiful and elegant in a modern taupe and gold color scheme. My brother in laws room was dark red and luxurious too. From the lobby, to the garden, to the restaurant the hotel had a great vibe and not "dark" as described by others on this site.
