Although the Metropole Hotel at first does not seem like your typical luxury hotel, it deserves a second look.  Located directly on the Grand Canal, a 3 minute walk to San Marco Square the location is unbelievable. When we arrived me and my friend booked two rooms, hers a standard, mine a junior suite.  Upon entering my room all I saw was stairs leading up, stairs leading down.  The down stairs were for the bedroom/living space area.  This space felt tired and worn out and in dire need of updating.  The "up" stairs on the other hand led to a small but beautiful balcony with side views of the grand canal.  My friends standard room was smaller, but beautifully updated and furnished.
