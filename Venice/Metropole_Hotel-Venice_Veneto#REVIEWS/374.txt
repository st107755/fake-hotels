Spent 5 nights in the Metropole hotel on our recent trip to Venice. The best thing about it is its location- less than 5mins walk to San Marco square and literally right next to the vaporetto stop (San Zaccaria), making it easy to get there from the airport (even if like us your budget doesn't stretch to water taxi!) and also to visit the neighbouring islands.  Our room (245) was lovely, very quaint layout with a few steps up from the entrance and bathroom area to the bedroom, great furnishings but would have liked it if there had been a proper shower cubicle as bathroom floor ended up permanently wet!
