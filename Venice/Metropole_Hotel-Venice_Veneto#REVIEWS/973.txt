Before taking a cruise around the Greek Islands we had an overnight stay in the Metropole. The hotel has a wonderful location just a few hundred yards from San Marco square. First impressions are excellent as you can pull up in your water taxi right at the hotels door. The staff were very attentive and went out of their way to be helpful. The décor was rich and luxurious with wonderful warm colours. We were a little wary after reading previous reviews but our family room turned out to be excellent, plenty of room with wonderful views. Unfortunately we couldn’t get a table in the restaurant that night but as we were returning in seven days time we booked for our next visit.
