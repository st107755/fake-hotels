Initially when my Husband & I looked at going to Venice for our anniversary we had wanted to stay in the Hotel Danieli (the one from the film 'The Tourist'), but after doing some research I actually preferred the look of the rooms in the Metropole and it was considerably cheaper.  The hotel itself is wonderful and is in a great (but expensive) location. We upgraded to a Deluxe Lagoon View room, however if i'm honest i'm not sure it was worth the upgrade. It was incredibly noisy having a room at the front of the hotel as there is a bridge with a ramp on right outside and the noise of people walking over it constantly disturbed me throughout the night.
