Sometimes the minute you walk into a hotel you fall in love. The Metropole is very different and its this uniqueness that makes it special. The owners are into collections from crucifixes to cigarette cases so the hotel has the feel at times of an antique emporium. It has charm and style while being intimate and welcoming. Opt for anything above the classic rooms which while being beautifully furnished are quite compact and pay extra for a view as the hotel is right on the water front facing the lagoon and St Giorgio and a short walk from St Marks Square. The staff could not have been kinder or more helpful during my stay and I regret that I didnt have the opportunity to eat in the 2 star Michelin restaurant.
