I visited metropole hotel for two nights with my girlfriend and we had a great experience. The hotels location is only a 5 minute walk from st marks square and when arriving by boat its only a 30 second walk from S.Zacharria port. Another main attraction being the Rialto bridge, this is about 15/20 minute walk depending on which route you take.   Greeted with 5 star service, taking care of our bags and answering all our questions. We were taken to our room which was beautifully decorated. Our rooms window looked over the canal so we could see those gondolas passing every so often if we wanted to which added to the whole experience.
