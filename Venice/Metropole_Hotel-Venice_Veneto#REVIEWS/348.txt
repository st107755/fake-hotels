The hotel looks old (everything in Venice does) but was very well maintained. The room had a unique design and the view was very good. We didn't spend much time indoors. Their breakfast selection was good and sumptuous.   The hotel is very close to St. Mark's square and right across the dock. The restaurant and bar were pretty good too. The service offered by the receptionist was exceptional and they even went out of the way to decorate the room with a wine bottle when I mentioned it was our honeymoon. That said, one of the bartender/concierge seemed arrogant and racist but the rest of the staff made up for it and apologized on his behalf.
