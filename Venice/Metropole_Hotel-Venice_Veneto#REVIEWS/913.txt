We have just returned from the most amazing weekend in Venice after staying at the Metropole hotel. Our suite was on the top floor looking out across the Grand Canal which provided the most amazing views.  As someone who normally likes a minimalistic contemporary atmosphere this hotel most definately took me outside of my comfort zone which was immediately apparant from walking into the opulant foyer. However I could not help but marvel at the decor throughout the hotel. Indeed I even wandered the corridors of the hotel in order to take in the various displays as one would within a musuem. This was to be a special weekend for us as I had arranged the trip to propose to my girlfriend and was not disappointed in anyway.
