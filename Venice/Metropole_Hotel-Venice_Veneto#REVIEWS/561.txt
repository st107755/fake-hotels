We stayed here for 4 nights before our Azamara cruise. We chose the hotel based on Tripadvisor reviews, location, amenities and general "star ratings" etc. We were not disappointed. The property is a nice "boutique" hotel in an old European style. The décor is "as advertised" and filled with the owner's antique collections. Very different but charming... Rooms are a bit small (typical of properties several hundred years old) but very well maintained and immaculately clean and comfortable. Wonderful beds and linens. Bathrooms were comparatively large and nicely renovated. Service in all respects was excellent. We particularly liked the central courtyard and garden- a lovely quiet spot for breakfast or a drink at the end of the day.
