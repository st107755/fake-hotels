Honestly, this is one of the best hotels I have ever stayed in. The staff are fantastic and the rooms superb.  We didn't eat in the hotel, though the menus looked really good. The highlight for me though was the extra mile the staff would go to.On our last day there, it was my girlfriend's 28th birthday. The staff must have noticed this when they took a copy of our passports as on her birthday they had left a card for her in our mail slot wishing her a happy birthday and inviting us for a glass of prosecco in the bar.  We had it in the lovely courtyard garden and it came accompanied with olives, cured meats, breadsticks and other nibbles. It really was something else.
