We spent 3 days at The Metropole Hotel in late August 2008.  We booked this accommodation as a late booking and could not have been happier with our choice.  It is an ideal spot on the very edge of Venice - - literally a few steps away from where all the boats stop and just a short walk away from San Marco.  We did much travelling in our 3 short days and when we returned from our journeys it was wonderful to be so close to the hotel. The hotel is beautiful inside and quite grand in Venetian style.  Our room seemed huge in size even though we are a family of 2 adults and 2 children.  There was ample storage space for all of us and we had a side sea view out onto the lagoon where you could see and hear all the boats arriving. We stayed on a bed and breakfast basis.
