The hotel Metropole strikes by its historical flavor. Everything reminds you of the past centuries of glory: the entrance and its preserved style, the collections exposed in all parts of the hotel (the fan and nutcracker collections are especially unique), the delightful garden where you can enjoy a peaceful break after all the walking across Venice, etc... Also great is arriving directly in the hotel reception from your taxi-boat! In addition to a beautiful lounge and a vast outdoor (with heaters) smoking corner, the hotel features a Michelin-starred restaurant, a great bar and a generous breakfast. We had a room on the second floor, and this was good because we found the ice machine quite noisy (on the first floor).
