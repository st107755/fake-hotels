I have just returned from an excellent weekend in Venice with my partner.  We took a water taxi from the airport direct to the hotel’s private dock.  This was a memorable experience and the perfect start to our break; it was expensive, but it was worth every penny.  On arrival at the Metropole the reception staff were welcoming and friendly.  Our room was brilliant.  Each room in the hotel has a different theme, ours had Chinese theme.  Superb silk panelling on the walls.  We also had a small terrace with views of the canal and over the lagoon.  Breakfast was served in the garden.  This was a real treat in the centre of the city.
