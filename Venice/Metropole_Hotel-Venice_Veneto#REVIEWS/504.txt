We stayed here to celebrate our 40th Wedding Anniversary. It was recommended to us by our travel agent as we wanted a very memorable and trouble free holiday. Even though our room wasn't ready the Hotel stored our bags for us, there was no offer of refreshments after our twelve hour journey though.
