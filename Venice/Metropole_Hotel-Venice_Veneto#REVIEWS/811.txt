Stayed here between the 8-10 June. The hotel like most other Venetian buildings is old but very decorated and maintained. Like most other Hotels in Venice, the lobby is tight with a single long reception and concierge desk. We stayed in a Superior room and it was reasonably comfortable for Venetian standards although the bathroom was a little tight. The room was well furbished. Breakfast was nice in terms of overall spread and taste but has some scope for improvement. The staff especially the concierge on the first day was polite and helpful. The hotel has some nice dining options and an oriental bar.
