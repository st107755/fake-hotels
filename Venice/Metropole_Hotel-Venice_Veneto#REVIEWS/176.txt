We stayed here for four nights in July 2017. We returned following our first visit four years previously. The hotel remains as beautiful as ever and the staff are as ever attentive and on the whole I am happy that we returned. However, a couple of points disappointed us. I had paid for breakfast as part of our stay and I requested toast each morning which only arrived on two of the four occasions, and on the other I waited interminably. On our last evening we were looking forward to having a few drinks in the lovely gardens only to find them out of bounds due to a private (noisy) function that we were unaware of. Surely they could have informed customers of this beforehand? Lastly, there is now a huge carbuncle outside the front doors of the hotel on the steps of the bridge.
