We had a triple room and a double room to accommodate our family. Both rooms were distinctively different in style. I guess when you have a place that dates back to the 1800's there will be no cookie cutter room designs. It was so fun to arrive at our hotel lobby via water taxi from the airport. How cool! The porters and reception folks were very welcoming and spoke English readily. The cozy lobby decor reminds me of the the parlor of an old world bordello. Red velvet and fleur de lis everywhere. Really interesting collection of antique fans, wine openers, etc. Our room was the same decor. Everything was functional and very clean. The bathroom was typical for European hotels.
