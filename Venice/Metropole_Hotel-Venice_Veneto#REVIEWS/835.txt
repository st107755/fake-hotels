The hotel was full of antiques and very charming. The staff were warm and welcoming offering advice and assistance when requested but not intrusive. Our room was beautifully furnished and had a small balcony (but not big enough to walk onto), it was certainly furnished very comfortably and was beautifully decorated. The staff could not have been more helpful when I had my mobile stolen during a walk around venice and were keen to assist me. The hotel had a beautiful garden with organge trees and it is possible to eat breakfast there when the weather allows. The location of the hotel is perfect and I disagree with previous comments that the hotel is not 3 star - I consider that it certainly is.
