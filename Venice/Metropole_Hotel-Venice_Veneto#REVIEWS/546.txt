Booked this hotel as a deal with flights, so our accommodation was reduced in price. However, we were disappointed that this meant that we did not have breakfast included and at 15 or 30 euros each this would have been quite an addition to the bill for four of us staying 3 nights.  I contacted the hotel direct and asked them to book the water taxi transfer from the airport direct to the hotel and our reservation was there when we landed, which made things nice and easy.  The hotel is in a great location, within easy walking distance of San Marco Square or you can cut into an alleyway and find yourself wandering around the streets and canals of Venice.
