This was our second honeymoon in Venice after the Royal Crescent Hotel in Bath.The Metropole is a wonderful hotel. Our reception via water taxi was fabulous and the room with its steam shower was fantastic with lovely glimpses on the Lagoon in the evening sunshine. Very comfortable bed (not two pushed together as is sometimes the case) and beautiful towels and robes etc. This is a food lovers paradise, the taster menu is very special indeed with every course carefully explained and of course great wine.Yes it is expensive but who cares when it is this good/The staff were outstanding and nothing was too much trouble. The atmosphere in the bar was simply magical, great bar snacks and music made it a haven after a long day seeing the sights.
