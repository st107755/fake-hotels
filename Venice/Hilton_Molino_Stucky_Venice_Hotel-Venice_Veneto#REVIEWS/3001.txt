Stayed for 3 nights. Very helpful polite staff throughout. Good room with all mod cons, except WiFi which is an issue. That aside we found all the hotel amenities clean and tidy and the breakfast was very good with a wide range of choice. Visited the skyline bar up on the roof. Again like most drinks at the hotel it was rather pricey. Stunning views across the Guidecca canal to Venice. However I did have an issue with the service. Waiter took my order only to be told after ten minutes that they had to restock and would we like a water for free...!! seriously. After 25 mins we left, with the waiter in pursuit. Told him what we thought of the service and left.   As mentioned there is no free WiFi anywhere apart from the ground floor. If you want anywhere else then you pay.
