Stayed here for 2 nights in Feb and had an excellant stay. The room was gorgeous. Cute, clean and confortable - everything that I look for in a room! I loved our stay here and will stay again if I ever return to Venice. The location is not on the Venice mainland, but the hotel has a ferry that will take you there so its still very convenient.  The customer service here was exceptional. Upon checking in, we were upgraded to a suite with separate living room. The concierge desk was extremely helpful. Venice is a strange city to get around in, but the staff at the desk provided us with helpful info and maps.
