We were delighted by the quality of our room, of the breakfasts, of the public areas and of the lounge.bar/restaurant area.  More even than those plus points, we were impressed by the professionalism, courtesy and good English language skills shown by staff.  These comments are made deliberately to contradict the few hostile reviews that in our experience are simply wrong.  Venice 'proper' is of course utterly amazing but it was a special privilege to away from the bustle by being on the tranquil island of Guidecca.  There are several good restaurants along to the Palanca and beyond and a great bar too, full of non-tourists, so good atmosphere and prices!  The shuttle service across to the Zaccaria stop next to St Mark's Square worked perfectly.
