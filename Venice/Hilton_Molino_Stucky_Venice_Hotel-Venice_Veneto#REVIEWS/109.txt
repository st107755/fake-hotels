This is my 3rd stay at the Hilton Molino Stucky. I like the location of the hotel away from the craziness of Venice. I am a Hilton Diamond member.  I was assigned an “upgraded” room which was a junior suite.  When we got to the room and saw that it was old and had a poor smell we went back to reception.  The receptionist was not friendly and certainly did not make me feel like a valued guest.  I was assigned a regular king room that had been updated but had no view.  The room was small but adequate. We had breakfast 3 mornings in the breakfast buffet which felt like a cafeteria.  The service was basically non existent, we were offered coffee and that’s it.  On the second day I asked if eggs were available and found out yes I could order from the kitchen.
