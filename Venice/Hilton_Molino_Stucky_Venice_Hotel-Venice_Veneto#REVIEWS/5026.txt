This hotel and its customer service is high above other hotels I have been. From the first moment we entered the hotel from water taxi ( which was by the way very nice ride directly from the airport to the main door of the hotel..1,5 hrs) we were treated like VIP guests. Our room wasn't ready so we were invited to wait at executive lounge. We were escorted to our room later on... and we were upgraded... to a tower suite. What a nice suite it was.. beautiful with amazing views over the city and canal. Free shuttle boat service from hotel to S Marco and Zattere was very fast and efficient. Worth to use it. Don't miss the pool area with beautiful views. Spa is also a very nice experience and the dressing room had everything a woman needs for refreshing herself.
