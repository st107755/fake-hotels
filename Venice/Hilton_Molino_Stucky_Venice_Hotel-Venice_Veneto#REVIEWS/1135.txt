Overall this was a great hotel. Air conditioned in the heat of the summer but beware that it appears it is only used in the room as halls and public areas were still stuffy and hot. The room was a bit small but nicely decorated. Bathroom was great and nicely appointed. The bed and pillows were very comfortable, especially after a long overseas flight. We did find a cricket in the room and wonder where that came from.  The fridge in the room was a minibar of which I was not a fan. One of those where you are afraid to touch anything because it is triggered by movement. There was a shelf for personal items but there was not too much space even to put your own bottle of water. As for ice, probably best to ask in lobby. The ice machine did not work during our visit.
