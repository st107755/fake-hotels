The hotel itself is impressive and staff who greeted us were lovely and very helpful.  The room was a large family room, however the views were disappointing.  However the rooftop bar made up for this with exception to the prices.   The room came with air con, hairdryer and dressing gown and slippers which was a nice touch.    The hotel provided a shuttle service and umbrellas (which we needed).  The pool area is a great treat but was exceptionally busy and you struggled for a seat but worth it.    I booked in for a massage with Elemis products and I have to say I have had better massages as I like deep tissue massages to have a decent pressure applied but it wasn’t bad, the price however was far more expensive than Four Seasons.
