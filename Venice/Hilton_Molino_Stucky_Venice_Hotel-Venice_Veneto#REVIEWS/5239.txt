First a note on me so you can judge my review. I am a Hilton Diamond member so usually get an upgrade. This time I certainly got an upgrade -- to a fabulous side room with two separate views of the city across the water. Nonetheless, regardless of your status the entire hotel is worth seeing and worth staying at. It is an amazingly renovated hotel, with beautifully restored beams everywhere, high ceilings, and a totally great atmosphere. The staff are all friendly and the breakfast (if it is included) is excellent. They keep the hot food hot, which is not always the case in some hotels. The Executive Lounge is good but not one of the best. Finger foods are fine, cheese is excellent, and okay choice of wines (and spirits later on in the day).  Are there any drawbacks?
