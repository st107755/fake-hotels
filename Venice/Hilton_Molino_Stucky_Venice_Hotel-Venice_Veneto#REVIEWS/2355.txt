We stayed at the Hilton Molino at Venice to celebrate my 2016 Anniversary / Valentines.  We were offered an upgrade at 50 Euro's per night which entitled me to a room at the front of the hotel with water view as well as Free Wi-Fi and Breakfast and access to their executive lounge. A little expensive but well worth it since I did not have any of these included within my booking.   I have to say both me and my partner thoroughly enjoyed our stay here. It was a great hotel, in a great location which provided an excellent service. I must I was unsure about the location of this hotel but glad I chose to stay there. I was initially wanted to be closer to San Marco Square with all the hustle and bustle but this proved to be a great choice at the end.
