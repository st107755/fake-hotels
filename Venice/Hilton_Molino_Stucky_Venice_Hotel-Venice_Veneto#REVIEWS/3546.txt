Hard to fault such a lovely hotel!   The deluxe room we stayed in was comfy, extremely clean with good bathroom facilities- can't fault this aspect of the hotel.   The breakfast was outstanding with loads of choice for everybody such as cooked, pastries, cereal, meats, fruit, bread and more.   Pool area was really nice with gorgeous views however pool temperature was extremely cold. Also, not enough sun beds/ seats for around the pool.   All staff very friendly and helpful! The hotels courtesy umbrellas were very much appreciated for rainy August days that we didn't plan for!  Something we didn't know but is worth knowing is that there is an airport water bus that regularly stops at the hotel- the cost was 16 euro per person.
