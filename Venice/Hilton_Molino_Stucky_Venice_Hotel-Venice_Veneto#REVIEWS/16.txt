Stayed here because we like the quietness of the island and restaurants directly on the water.  Check in took an eternity even though it was after 3pm. Check in queue was constant and staff were unable to cope with the queues of people trying to check in. Eventually got to our room which was nice with view over the water. Breakfast both days was chaotic and on the second morning we left without ever eating anything as our food took so long to arrive. Staff delivered food to the wrong tables and it looked completely unorganised and chaotic. Roof top bar was half empty but we were told we could not have a drink the entire evening. One side of the bar remained closed. Charged us for water which were complementary and we were relieved to check out.
