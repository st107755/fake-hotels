The Hilton Molino Stucky is hands down one of the most outstanding Hilton’s in the world. A 19th century mill was carefully restorated to now offer the scenic back drop of a splendid 5 Star premium Hilton location. The best way to reach the hotel was by water taxi. 75€, but well worth the trip through Venice by night. During the stay the hotel had very low cost water shuttle available, which connects the hotel with the city center.  Service and personnel where really excellent, very friendly and attentive staff during all aspects of the stay. We had booked a suite and were upgraded to a tower corner suite with spectacular views of the candle grande and San Marco square.
