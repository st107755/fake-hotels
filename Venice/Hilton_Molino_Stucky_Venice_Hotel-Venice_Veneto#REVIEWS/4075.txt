Great hotel, not in the middle of the touristic centre, but on the island of Giudecca. That mean tghere are no shops or restaurants nearby. However, there are regular (free!) water shuttles going across to Zaterre (5 minutes) and San Marco (which takes about 15 minutes), taking you right into the heart of the city.  Hotel is very large, with restaurants, bars - including a great rooftop Sky Lounge with great city views, espceailly at night. There is a spa and a rooftop swimming pool but I couldn't use the latter as it was closed for the winter.  The buffet breakfast is extensive with several rooms to sit down.m Nice executive lounge, which is open all day (but alcoholic drinks and a small food selection after 5pm).
