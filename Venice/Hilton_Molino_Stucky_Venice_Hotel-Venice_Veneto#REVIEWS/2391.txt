We stayed here for 3 nights as we were visiting for the Carnivale and attending a masquerade ball on the Friday evening.   Hotel is a beautiful building, we arrived by taxi boat which although is €120 the trip was great and much better than the 1hr and 40min public boat option.   We were given an upgrade to a suite and had amazing views over the canal. The staff in reception are very helpful and friendly. Also we had emailed prior to arrival on a couple of occasions with queries, and they were responded to within an hour every time.   There is a shuttle ferry with regular service to/from San Marco and with the heavy crowds in town on Saturday I can't tell you how nice it was to escape!! We booked into the spa for some relaxation which was nice too.
