Modern hotel with excellent facilities.Located in Guidecca Island, 15 minutes boat ride from St Marks Sq. From Airport: Alilaguna boat 1h35 minutes 15 euos, direct service to Hotel Jetty. From Train/Bus station: Take Vaporetto(water bus) to Guidecca, stop 10 minutes(two bridges) away or Private water taxi(70 euros). Hotel Check In: Can be crowded, It helps to take a print out of of your reservation. Lobby: clean, comfortable sitting area and a coffee shop.
