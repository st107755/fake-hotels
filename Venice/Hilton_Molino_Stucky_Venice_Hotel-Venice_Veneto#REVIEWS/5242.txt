VERY expensive. Professional but unfriendly staff. Good buffet breakfast but for €30 it should be. Sat down in Lounge on arrival and asked for tea.  Waitress said only the "specialist" teas on the menu were available, no "ordinary" tea. Not wanting Gunpowder/Lychee or similar, I tried asking for "English breakfast tea" (not on the menu).  She reluctantly agreed to see if this was possible and eventually reappeared with the right tea. The teapot had a saucer on the top because the lid was missing. My husband's camomile tea was served in a Japanese cast iron/metal pot which didn't improve the taste of the teabag. This cost Euros 20.
