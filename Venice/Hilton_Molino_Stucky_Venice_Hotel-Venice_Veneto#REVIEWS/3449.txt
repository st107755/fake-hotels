We really enjoyed staying here, as the hotel is in a lovely old building, far from the madding crowds of San Marco.  But full disclosure, we used Hilton points and had access to the executive lounge.  I'm not sure I would have been so pleased had we paid the full freight.  The room was generic Hilton, although the public spaces were lovely, and it was a busy hotel with a somewhat institutional feel.  Very helpful concierges.  The rooftop pool and bar are gorgeous.  We enjoyed being on Giudecca, but be aware that you are bound by the hotel shuttle schedule to get across the canal.  We never had a problem getting on the shuttle, but others we spoke to had to wait and then couldn't board because it was too crowded.
