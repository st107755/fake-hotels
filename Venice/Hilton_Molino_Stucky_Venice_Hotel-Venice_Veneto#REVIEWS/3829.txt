The hotel has a good location, away from the crowds, and has a scheduled boat service to the main island side. In the evening and at break times the service is infrequent. The last boat coming in is around midnight.  Some of the rooms have a low ceiling and are not very well lit. The lounge for Hilton elite members is a small area by the side of the bar. The lounge breakfast is continental along with a lightly cooked yellow liquid paste presented as scrambled eggs, and the beer/wine/snacks in the evening are not pro-actively restocked. I understand that the service used to be very good but I found it be less than that now.
