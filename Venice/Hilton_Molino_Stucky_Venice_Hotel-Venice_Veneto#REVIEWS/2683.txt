Lovely position, which didn’t make up for a poor room and lack of service.   This building on the edge of one of Venice’s busiest waterways used to be the biggest flower mill in Europe, owned by the Stucky family, and it’s now the biggest conference centre and hotel in Europe- or so they tell us guests. The best thing about the hotel is the wonderful roof terrace with a reasonable swimming pool and views to die for from up there. The best view from the roof is at sunrise with the sun behind you, around 7am, so it’s all locked up until 8am and no, they don’t want to open it for a guest to take a photo! Not because there would be disturbance to guests rooms nearby- there aren’t any.
