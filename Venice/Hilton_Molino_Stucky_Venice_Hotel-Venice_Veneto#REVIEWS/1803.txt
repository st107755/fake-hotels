We stayed here for 2 nights, and had pre-booked a standard double room. The room was even better than expected, spacious with a king size bed, 2 closets, a large flatscreen TV and a desk with chair plus an armchair. Large bathroom with a bathtub. Our room was quiet, we never heard a sound from our indoor or outdoor surroundings.   The hotel breakfast was amazing, one of the best we have had ever. Our reservation did not include breakfast, but after having a look at what was offered in the breakfast room, we decided to pay for it. Important to note that if you pay the breakfast at the hotel reception, it cost "only" EUR 19 p.p.. instead of the EUR 30 p.p. you have to pay at the breakfast room if you have not prepaid it at the reception.
