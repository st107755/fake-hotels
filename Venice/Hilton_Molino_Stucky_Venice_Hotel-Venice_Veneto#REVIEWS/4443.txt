I will preface this by saying that I stayed at this property using Hilton Honor's points so I can't really comment on value because I didn't pay anything for the room.  That aside, this property is beautiful.  It's across the harbor from Venice but there are free shuttles that run all the time from the hotel to St. Mark's Square. Venice is a beautiful city and you can really appreciate its beauty from the rooftop bar in the hotel.  They have great drinks and you can overlook all of Venice from there.  The rooms were very nice - what you'd expect from Hilton. We ate at the restaurant one evening because it was raining in Venice. The food was just okay...a bit overpriced and I thought the quality could be better.  Service however was excellent.
