The hotel itself was spotless and well maintained. Breakfast was buffet style, offered all you could ask for and was constantly kept topped up. You could ask for omelettes, eggs cooked how you like etc  The barman in the Skyline bar recommended and booked a wonderful restaurant for us, he even suggested a local bar for pre dinner drinks and I would say that if you want to try local food at reasonable prices you should visit one or two (The horse steak was lovely and not supplied by Tesco's!). The free shuttle boat only takes 40 people, and whilst it runs frequently, you can not book it or be guaranteed a seat - the public water bus costs 9 Euros one way so not cheap for a short stretch of water. This is a lovely old hotel and I would not hesitate to stay there again.
