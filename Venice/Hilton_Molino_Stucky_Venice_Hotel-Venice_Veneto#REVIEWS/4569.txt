Our family stayed in two executive rooms facing Venice. We paid for upgrade for view and larger room. It was nice to look out large window to watch boat traffic on the canal. Great unfortunate situation was that the size of the lounge was way too small for the number of people wanting to use the lounge. They did not want people to take drinks and food from the lounge to sit in the lobby and were somewhat rude about it. One night we wanted to sit in the lobby and order food for our children. They would not allow us to do that...we had to move a few feet to sit at a table in the restaurant.
