The hotel is situated on the southernmost island, La Giudecca. A free hotel shuttle boat is traversing the canal and continues over to San Marco. We had a very nice room overlooking the canal and Sestiere Dorsoduro. Picture-book view out of the window! The bathroom featured a tub and a walk-in shower stall. The room's interior is charming, very classical and nicely in shape. Excellent breakfast buffet. Great roof-top bar (go to the eastern terrace for a better view of the city). Would have given 5 points, but at check-out the staff neglected to inform us that the water busses were on strike.
