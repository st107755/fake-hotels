A very nice American style hotel, located on the island of Giudecca, far enough away from the hustle of the Grand Canal. The hotel is composed of several buildings so it is possible that you could be placed, as I was, in a room with a view not over looking any water. The amenities are all first rate and I you won't go wrong with this hotel. I did have an issue with the transportation cost. To get to hotel, via private water taxi from train station, was 60 euros which I knew of before hand. That trip takes 15 minutes. However, I was in Venice to start a cruise and my ship was located across the Giudecca canal, a trip that would take 2-3 minutes in the water taxi. I was informed that to cross the canal in a water taxi was 70 euro.
