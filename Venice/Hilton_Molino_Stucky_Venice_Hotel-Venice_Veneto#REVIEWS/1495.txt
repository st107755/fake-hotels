I've previously written glowing reviews about the Molino Stucky hotel having stayed several times since our first visit in 2012. This Hilton has always been absolutely top of our list - in Venice and around the world as we have stayed loyal to the Hilton brand in quite a lot of countries. But for this stay, we came away rather disappointed and will probably choose the San Clemente next time we're in Venice.  We once again booked a couple of rooms for our family but in summary, felt we were an unimportant part of the clientele in what seems to have become a massive conference machine.
