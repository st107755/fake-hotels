On our recent trip to Italy we chose to stay at this location because it's a trusted name in lodging and the available water taxi's, which were included in the room rate, take you directly to the Grand Canal.  The staff was knowledgeable and friendly and more than willing to help with locations and directions.  Wifi was available free for gold and diamond members.  There may be a cost involved for others.  The hotel itself was upscale, classy and had a European flair.  The location on the corner of two canals provided us with beautiful views.  The breakfast buffet had something for everyone including eggs, breads, cereals, meats, fruits and juices.  The dinner restaurant had a limited menu in case you didn't want to go out for food but a bit pricey.
