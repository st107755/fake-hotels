We stayed at the hotel in May for two nights before we took a cruise. The hotel itself is very large. There are several sections to it. We had paid for an upgraded room. The room itself was fine. We really didn't have much of a view though. We did get a rate with breakfast included. I would recommend doing this. They had a very nice large selection of items. You won't go away hungry. They have a free shuttle boat as others have mentioned. You need to look at the schedule or you can be waiting for some time. When we arrived in Venice we took a bus and then a vaparetto to a dock where the shuttle boat was to pick us up. Unfortunately for us, it was on a lunch break and we waited over 45 minutes and gave up.
