For our first visit to Venice we chose the Hilton Molino for our stay.  We arrived by vaporetto on a weekday to Sacco Fisola, suggested by the hotel, as we only had carry on luggage.  Check-in staff was friendly and attentive.  The room was spacious, clean, and decorated nicely but with no view of Venice (Skybar had the view, so no worries there).  Although the rooms are well appointed, the one surprise was that we could hear guests talking through the walls quite clearly.  I would have expected better insulation!  The Skybar on the top floor offers beautiful views of the city on one side, so it's worth a visit for a drink.
