We stayed here to celebrate our 30th wedding anniversary and were generally very impressed with the hotel and friendly staff.  We booked an Executive room which was beautifully appointed and entitled us to use the Executive lounge and its benefits including an excellent buffet breakfast, and all day snacks and drinks.  Our only gripe was that the lounge was located on the ground floor next to the general bar/lounge, separated by  railings, nowhere near the bedrooms (usually these lounges are on the same floor as the bedrooms making it far more exclusive and convenient) resulting in a noisy atmosphere - hopeless if you are on business and wish to work quietly as it seemed to be more like a family room!
