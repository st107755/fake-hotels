Stayed here as a family over the half-term holidays for 3 nights. It was the children’s first trip to Venice.  Our first sight of the hotel was from our water taxi, all lit up beautifully in the evening. We were excited to be staying there. As we were celebrating a family birthday, the General Manager was very kind and accommodating. He and his staff made us feel very welcome and well looked after. We loved our suite, with a spectacular view of the canal and St Mark’s Square beyond.  Great location; fun riding the hotel taxi across to St Mark’s. The leather seats and wood all felt very luxurious! Worth the nominal fee, which allows one to take as many journeys as you’d like. Do check timings, particularly off peak though.
