During my Biennale visit I chose to stay at Hilton Molino stucky in order to have a place a little away from the crowds. Location of the hotel is good, they have a shuttle boat that runs regularly to San Marco Zaccariae and in the evening hours another one to Zatterre (Dorsoduro). Its EUR 8.50 per person per stay, which is slightly questionable considering that hotels like Kempinski San Clemente offer a free shuttle service.  The hotel as such offers the typical atmosphere of a big chain hotel fully equipped with a hotel shop and a counter to book boat tours. Rooms are clean and practical and in case that you’ve booked an executive room you have all day access to an executive lounge area where you can get drinks, take away morning coffee and snacks.
