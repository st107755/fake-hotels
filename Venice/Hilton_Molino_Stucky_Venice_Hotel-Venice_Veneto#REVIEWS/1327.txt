This Hilton property has tremendous character and counts with a privileged place within the islands that surround Venice. The hotel stands tall in La Guidecca island and counts with a shuttle that takes you every 10 minutes to two different destinations in Venice, being San Marcos the most popular. Amongst the most attractive features are the roof top pool and their Sky Lounge; both unique in a city with few taller buildings that the hotel. The suites are generous in space with lots of windows offering natural light; the service is friendly and welcoming, and the staff is helpful.
