This hotel had the feel of an American hotel even though it is in Venice. Hilton did a great job renovating this old building into a beautiful hotel. The rooftop pool is definitely a plus as it has a fantastic view of Venice. The cruise ships also sail by this hotel so that was quite a site from our room window. The reception and concierge staff were very professional and friendly. The bell hop staff were excellent as well. The rooms were clean and comfortable. The bathrooms had marble vanities, although it took some time for the hot water to come through. One criticism is the executive lounge which is quite small compared to other ones we have been to in other Hilton locations. The selection of food items was as not as extensive as well.
