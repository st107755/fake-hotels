This hotel is a monster, in a good way. The old flour mill has been lovingly restored and now offers a great facility. The rooms are magnificent, with high ceilings, and marble clad bathrooms as highlights. Unfortunatley we had a courtyard view, but I can only imagine the incredible view that the sea view rooms must have.  The hotel offers a free shuttle boat service to and from St Marks via Zaterre, which saves a fortune on taxis/vaporetto. I'm sure this is subsidised by the cost of the rooms though, which was around £200 on a deal at the time we booked.  We also loved the rooftop bar, which offers amazing views towards St Marks in both sunlight and after dark.
