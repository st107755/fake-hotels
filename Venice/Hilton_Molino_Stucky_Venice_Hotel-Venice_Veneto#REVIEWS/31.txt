Gorgeous hotel in what used to be a historic flour mill -- the architecture is outstanding. Do note it is on the island of Giudecca, so you'll have to take the Hotel water taxi-shuttle to the main island to sight see at St. Marks, etc. We appreciated the location, as it was away from the crowds.   Incredible staff and service, particularly the very helpful travel concierge that helped us navigate how to get around Venice --- don't worry about trying to google ferry times. Avoid the headache and just ask the concierge - they will coordinate it all! They fully coordinated how to get to and from our (what seemed far-off) Carnival Gala on the island of Murano. Good restaurant in the lobby with decent wine list and cocktails - really loved the Carbonara.
