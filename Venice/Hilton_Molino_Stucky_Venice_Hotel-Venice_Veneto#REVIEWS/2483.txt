I had seen this hotel advertised by Hilton as a destination hotel and when the thought of visiting Venice came up I immediately thought of this hotel. Finding the hotel was pretty easy, although it is not on the main island and required a short boat ride. Check in was easy and our room was ready. The lobby and common areas are beautiful, the lifts are cheap and have some tacky rope lighting in them not what you expect in a five star hotel, and there are bleach stains on the carpet in the corridor, not a great start. The room had a wonderful view, however,.... Was filthy. I moved a chair to sit down and watch TV and the amount of dirt and dust behind it was awful. The cleaning team clearly do not Hoover or dust! The amount of dust in the room was comical.
