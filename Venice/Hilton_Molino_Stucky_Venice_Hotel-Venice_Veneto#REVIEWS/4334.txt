The hotel is on the opposite side of the Grand Canal from St. Mark's Square, so we had to take a water taxi.  Unfortunately we had just gotten off the train, and we had a lot of luggage.  Otherwise, we would have taken a public taxi.  The private taxi cost 60€!!!!!  From then on, we were able to take the Hilton's shuttle boat free of charge.  The rooms were beautiful with excellent views, and the staff at the front desk were extremely helpful.  I was waiting for my husband to check us in, and there were two men standing near the front desk in suits who worked at the hotel.  I asked them about the best way for my daughters to get to our hotel since they were coming from Mestre, and they gave me explicit directions in perfect English.  They were so nice.
