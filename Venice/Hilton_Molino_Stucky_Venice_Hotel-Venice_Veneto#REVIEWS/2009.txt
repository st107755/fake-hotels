This was my maiden voyage to Europe and it was exquisite.  We did a lot of research and decided to indulge ourselves and stay at the Molino Stucky.  On arrival we had already arranged a shared Water Taxi from the airport and arrived at the Hilton in style.  With Viator it was like a tour through the water ways as we dropped others off.  On checking in we upgraded to an Executive room with a breathtaking views of Venice as it woke up and as the sun set, and the cruise ships coming in an out.  The Executive Room came with access to the Executive lounge which always had nibbles, some better than others and wines and other beverages.  There was also a full breakfast there in the morning but sometimes space is limited.
