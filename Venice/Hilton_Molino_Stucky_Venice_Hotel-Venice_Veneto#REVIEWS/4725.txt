The Hilton lived up to usual Hilton expectations, in a good position, looking across the canal to St Mark's Basillica. The hotel offers a free shuttle boat over to the attractions.  Breakfasts were extensive and well presented, and the Executive lounge was staffed with amazingly helpful people Andrea being the one who stood out. All the staff were courteous, helpful and kind. We had to change rooms, and this was done efficiently with minimal fuss, as we'd complained about the noise generated from the interconnecting room! All in all, one of the better Hilton hotels we've used.
