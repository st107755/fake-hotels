We stayed at the Molino Stucky Hilton 16-19 October 2015. The hotel itself is lovely with great rooms, facilities etc but it calls itself five star and we didn't have the consistently high level of service you'd expect.   The little touches expected of a five star experience were missing: no robe & slippers in the room, a broken hairdryer that I had to request was replaced three times, only one milk per person per day in the room's tea/coffee making facilities, being offered eggs at breakfast one day but not the next, each morning at breakfast having to complete the table layout ourselves by taking items from other tables, a temperamental tv, a 3.5Euro charge per person for the 'free' shuttle to the main part of Venice, an offhand concierge.
