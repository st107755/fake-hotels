The hotel is situated on a separate island opposite San Marco which is very near to all the main attractions of Venice but not convenient as you have to stick to water bus timetable. The hotel is very large and busy, has a modern reception areas and great facilities for business conferences but the guest rooms are small and in need of reservation. They offer continental breakfast with a good choice of food and fresh fruit but very expensive if you decide to dine in one of the hotel's restaurants. One of the main things to think of before going to Venice is transportation- a trip by public water bus takes around 2.5 hours and is not overall a nice trip is you suffer from seasickness, it might be worth taking private water taxi instead which costs 70-120€.
