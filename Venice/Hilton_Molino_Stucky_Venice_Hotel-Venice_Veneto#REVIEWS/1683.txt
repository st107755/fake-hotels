Although the Hilton Molino is located across the Grand Canal and you have to take a shuttle to the main island, the shuttle is efficient and the hotel is well worth it.  Large rooms, helpful staff, multiple dining options, and very reasonable prices make this a wonderful option for your Venice stay.  I was reluctant to stay here previously because of the location.
