The Hilton Molino Stucky Venice Hotel is a modern hotel with fantastic facilities located on the island of Giudecca in Venice. It sits across the main canal (not the Grand Canal) from Venice. The Hilton runs a free ferry shuttle service from the hotel to San Marco every 20-40 minutes from 8am - 12:40am. If that doesn't work with your schedule, grab a ferry ticket and take the Vaporetto from Palanca which is just a 5 minute walk along the waterfront. The ferry and shuttle take about 15-20 minutes from Giudecca to San Marco.  The pool at the hotel has the best skyline view of Venice, bar none. There's a great bar up there and you can watch the sun go down over the beautiful city. The pool looked a little dirty, but that didn't stop anyone using it.
