I have stayed in Venice before now and was a little worried as this hotel is sited off the main island of Venice, So I was worried we would be isolated from Venice itself while we attended the conference.  However it is a 5 minute boat ride provided free by the hotel every half hour (except for a long lunch break) but twice we could not get on the boat as it was too full already.  I stayed with a work colleague in a twin room as this was the hotel where the conference was taking place.  Our initial room (115) was not good as it was on the first floor with a walkway right in front of our room. There was also the roof of an entrance right by our window, so anyone could have easily broken into our room if we left the window open.
