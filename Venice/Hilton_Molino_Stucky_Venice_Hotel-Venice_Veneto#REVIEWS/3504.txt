My husband and I chose to stay at the Hilton for the first three nights of our Italian vacation. We chose the Hilton because he is a Diamond Hilton Honors member and we wanted a hotel that was easy to find as our first stop (we flew in and out of Venice). The hotel is very easy to get to from the airport via private water taxi but the taxi is a little expensive. There is public transportation but it does take a little longer. The hotel itself was very nice. We had a very big room that included a living room and bedroom plus a fantastic view. The hotel breakfast was very nice and a great way to start our day of exploring Venice. The hotel staff was extremely nice and very accommodating of our requests.
