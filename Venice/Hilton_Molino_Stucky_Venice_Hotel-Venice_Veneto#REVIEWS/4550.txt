We were very happy with our four night stay here.  From the airport we took the Alilaguna Blue line boat (15 euros one way) which after 90 minutes or so dropped us right off at the hotel dock.  We got to see parts of Venice we did not see later in our stay so it was worth the extra travel time.  Easy check in, upgraded to an executive room on the 6th floor overlooking the canal with fantastic views of Venice.  We used points to stay here but when comparing prices at the time of booking using points seemed more worthwhile.  As others have noted in previous reviews the executive lounge is on the first floor.  Choices were OK for breakfast but the buffet in the restaurant were much more extensive.  The lounge was much quieter for breakfast though.  Service was good too.
