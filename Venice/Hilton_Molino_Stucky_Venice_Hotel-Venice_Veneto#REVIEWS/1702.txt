Nice clean spacious room with a comfortable bed. We were upgraded to a beautiful room on the seventh floor which was in a private area of the hotel. The breakfast each day was crowded, but very good with a nice selection of food to choose from. We used the water transportation provided by the hotel each day to get to Venice and it couldn't have been more convenient.  The pool on the roof has spectacular views with limited hours and $8 beers. The front desk staff were very friendly and spoke good english. Ilaria the front office manager did a wonderful job working with me to redeem a voucher I had for free nights and I appreciated her help.
