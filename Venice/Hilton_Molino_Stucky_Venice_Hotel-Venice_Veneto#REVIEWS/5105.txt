This is a nice hotel - it's very modern, rooms are clean and reasonably spacious (especially for Europe) and it's close to most of the action. There is a reasonable choice of restaurants/bars and facilities. I would say that their internet access pricing is an absolute rip off, however, and sometimes the service was a little surly and inattentive. The rooftop bar is rated highly by the hotel for events such as New Years, I'd say it's a little overrated. The hotel was very clean and most things were of a high standard. It's a good place to stay overall.  Some might prefer locations on the main island as they may want to limit travel time, however the hotel provides a free boat to the main island and the journey time is no more than a few minutes.
