We had stayed at Molino Stucky before so we knew what to expect.  As returning guests we were upgraded to a suite and that was a great start to our three day stay. It is a quiet and calm hotel after a session in the crowds of Venice.  Breakfast was ok but you serve yourself from the buffet on such small plates. The variety on offer is good and food items replenished often. The staff is pleasant but were a tad slow at times during breakfast.  However overall I found the staff pleasant and very helpful.  The executive lounge was well used and the food on offer is definitely superior to what had been on offer during previous visits. The range of drinks is good but the lounge does not offer any spirits now.
