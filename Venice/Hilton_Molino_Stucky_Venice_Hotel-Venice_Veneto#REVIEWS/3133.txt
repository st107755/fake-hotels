Traveling with my daughters on what was billed as a value minded Italian tour, we made a last minute booking at the Hilton Molino Stucky and were surprised to land five star luxury at a three star price. Our arrival from the train station to the hotel by water taxi was unexpectedly exhilarating. Once inside the Molino Stucky, we found the grand sense of arrival in the Rialto Lobby which doubles as a bar.  The three of us found our room to be well-appointed, and comfortable, but its location down a maze of hallways may have qualified it as "the Expedia room" (I admit to getting lost twice). But to be fair, we never expected to stay in such a fine hotel on our value minded budget.
