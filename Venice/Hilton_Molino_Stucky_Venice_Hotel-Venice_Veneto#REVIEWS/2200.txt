Beautiful hotel and decor. Staff welcoming, breakfast choices extensive. We were rather overwhelmed by the hotel lobby/lounge drink and bar food menu prices - these in our opinion are far to overpriced even in comparison to some of the top bars and restaurants in Venice. Room house keeping could be a little better - coffee and tea / milk was not restocked properly daily - This is the only downside.
