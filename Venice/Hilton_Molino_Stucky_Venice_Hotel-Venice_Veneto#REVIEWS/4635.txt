The Hilton is located on an island and requires a boat transfer to the main tourist attractions, the hotel provides a regular free service to take you back 'n' forth. Roof top pool is shut during the winter so you may not get a chance to use it, however the hotel has a cocktail bar on the 8th floor with good views if you want to look down on Venice.
