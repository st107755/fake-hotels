My wife and I chose to stay here for two nights during the Venice portion of a tour across Italy; however, it was a regrettable decision.  First, let me say that if you've ever stayed at any of the Park Hyatts, Mandarin Orientals or St. Regis hotels, etc. and are expecting comparable accommodations and service, your expectations will more certainly not be met. This is a 3-star hotel boasting a 5-star rating.  Our check-in and the views from the pool and and bar were the ONLY enjoyable experiences we had at the hotel over the course of our two days there; however, we didn't let that spoil our time in Venice.
