Excellent service with a smile and a willingness to go that extra mile. The room was good as expected, but I regret not paying for a room with a view. There was excellent choice of food at breakfast and the hotel managed the large number of guests very well.   The shuttle service was fine for going out in the morning and returning in the evening, but I did feel cut off staying on Guidecca. I would very much have liked to pop back to the hotel during the day to freshen up, but it was a little too far to bother. Similarly I would have liked to go out for dinner in the evening, but it was just too far.   The hotel restaurants were v pricey in my opinion, and being on an island meant that they had a captive audience.
