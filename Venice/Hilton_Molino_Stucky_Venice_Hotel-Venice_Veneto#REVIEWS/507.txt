1st stop of a 3 city tour. Venice was one of the few things left on my bucket and Hilton Molino Stucky was a great place to stay. My wife and I enjoyed our stay in a King Executive suite with a view of the Giudecca Harbor and the City of Venice. The room included access to the Executive lounge. The entire staff was very friendly and always willing to provide assistance with any needed assistance. Food and drinks in the Rialto Lobby Bar and Lounge was great. The Roof Top Bar was a nice spot to enjoy cocktails and nighttime views of Venice. The pool is very nice but the weather wasn't quite warm enough to enjoy the water.  Venturing into the city center was easy.
