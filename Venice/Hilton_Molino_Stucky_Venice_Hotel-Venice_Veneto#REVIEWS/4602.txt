We had an enjoyable stay in this stylish and comfortable hotel which has been converted from a historic building into a modern hotel on the Guidecca with great views towards San Marco. Room was comfortable with views over the Lido. The Sky Bar on the roof was excellent ( but not cheap of course) for a drink at dusk and we were lucky to have a great sunset. Breakfast was good with a large selection in 2 large rooms.  Hotel provides a great alternative to staying in the more central parts of Venice and might therefore suit someone returning rather than staying for the first time.  The free water bus shuttle to San Marco and Zattere was really convenient but watch the departure times as they are only every 40 minutes or so with gaps at lunch and supper time.
