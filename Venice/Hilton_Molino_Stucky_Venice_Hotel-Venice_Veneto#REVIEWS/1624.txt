My wife and I spent 2 days at the lovely hotel in early December. The entire experience was excellent from arrival to departure   Key to my rating was the service of all staff including the check in team, concierge, bar service, breakfast and restaurant. These were all top notch.   We also had a couples massage at the spa. Again service was excellent.   The hotel itself and the grounds has quite an interesting history (you can see some great photos around the hotel) and the restoration of this grand building must have been a complex task that required a lot of vision.  We enjoyed walking around it.   View from the room was spectacular!  Only point to note is that you either need to use the hotel shuttle or the vaporetto to get to San Marco or elsewhere.
