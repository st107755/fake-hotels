We stayed at the Hilton Hotel for 2 nights during a short break to Venice. Upon arrival we were greeted by very nice reception staff and checked into our room very quickly and with ease. Our room was very spacious and spotlessly clean, the bathroom was amazing and very large compared to other hotels we have stayed in, in Venice. The common areas of the hotel are very elegantly furnished and lovely to sit in whilst taking advantage of the free internet down stairs.  Even though the hotel is a little isolated from the main land of Venice it has a regular water taxi service to ferry people to and from the main touristy parts of Venice.
