Great location. Away from madness of San Marco or Grand Canal.  Good size rooms for Europe and especially Venice.  All modern amenities including pool ( we did not use it, though). Free wi-fi for hhonours members.  We had room facing the lagoon and great views. Executive lounge option helped a great deal to have access to drinks and snacks round the clock and finger food in the evenings. Convenient and punctual waterbus shuttle service to San Marco-San Zaccaria, every 30 min or so. More importantly shuttle boat has adequate capacity. Last return from San Marco is about 10:30 pm, if I recall correctly, which even allows dinner near San Marco or main Venice area. Nearby vaporetto stops are available ( with 5 to 10 min walks, if the shuttle is missed.
