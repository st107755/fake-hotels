Excellent hotel. Outstanding in each and everyday. Staff, room, bars, breakfast and overall experience were all outstanding. Please note that when arriving to Venice, the odds are you will arrive in Piazzale Roma like all other travelers. From there, you can only walk to the big Canale splitting the north side from the south side where the Hilton is actually located. We spent close to an hour trying to figure out how to cross the Canale and reach the Hilton which was frustratingly across from us. Eventually we learned of a free service shuttle from 2x mini ports where a Hilton free shuttle operates from. One is called Zaatri and the other is Sant Marco. Once there, a Hilton shuttle will eventually take you across the Canale and to the hotel.
