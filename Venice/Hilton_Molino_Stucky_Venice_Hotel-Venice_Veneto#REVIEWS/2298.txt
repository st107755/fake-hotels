We visited Venice for 5 days last week and stayed at this hotel with my wife and 4yo daughter.   Landed at Marco Polo and booked a private water taxi to the hotel for €99. The hotel charge €120 for this. Taxi takes 30-40 minutes from the airport straight to the hotel.   The hotel is not in the main centre of Venice, so it is very quiet and you can relax from a long day of touring.   The hotel offer a complementary shuttle service that runs every 30-40 mins, this is very reliable and drops you to the centre of Venice in 10 minutes.   We booked a deluxe Exec room that we found extremely small on arrival -- immediately offered a bigger room (Upsell!) for €90 a night extra to an Exec Suite - this was huge - we took this and paid the extra.
