Very few hotels can accommodate 3 or 4 people in your party in 1 room.  Due to the massive size and location this hotel does it easily.  It's a beautiful property, easy to get to if you take a water taxi or water bus (and don't mind waiting 90 minutes).  The Hilton shuttle from San Marco to the Hilton ran every 20 minutes or so and took 15.  The first ride from the hotel provides awesome views of Venice.  We found being on Guidecca a peaceful rest from the craziness of the main island.  The interior of the hotel was exquisite, especially the main lobby area.  The food in the lobby was good, especially the caprese salad.  The rooms were well appointed with free power adapters if needed.  The bathrooms are huge by Venetian standards with everything you need.
