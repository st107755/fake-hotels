Starting my fiancce and my anniversary trip to Italy in Venice at the Hilton Molino Stucky was a fantastic choice. The hotel has preserved the historic character of the building while sacrificing nothing in the way of modern conveniences; the room we had was spacious and very comfortable with spectacular views. Being set a bit outside of the tourist centers, the hotel is quiet and relaxed, though a convenient and frequent shuttle boat drops you in the middle of the action.   The staff impressed me even before we left the US, cheerfully making a correction to an error I made on our reservation and suggesting options surprise my fiancée. On arrival, as promised, chocolate covered strawberries and prosecco  were in the room to delight her.
