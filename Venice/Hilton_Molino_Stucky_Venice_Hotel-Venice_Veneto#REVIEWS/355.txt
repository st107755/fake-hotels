The Hilton Molino Stucky is located on the waterfront, on Giudecca Island. It is on the opposite side of the wide Giudecca Canal from St Mark's Square and the main part of Venice. Giudecca Island is relatively quiet and mostly residential, and although it took some extra time getting back to the hotel by boat, we enjoyed getting away from the crowds and returning to this quieter area at the end of the day. Transportation across the canal is provided by a regularly scheduled hotel shuttle boat, at a cost of 8.50 euros per person per stay, or by a nearby Vaporetto (public water bus). The Vaporetto runs more frequently than the shuttle, and it can be used to reach locations throughout Venice, but it's a more expensive option.
