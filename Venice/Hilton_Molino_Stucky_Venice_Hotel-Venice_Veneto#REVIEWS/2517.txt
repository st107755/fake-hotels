We spent 3 amazing nights at this hotel. The room was spacious and very comfortable and the special offer of breakfast for 15 Euros was a bargain! The hotels location is great and the shuttle is cheap and regular. Just be prepared to stand your ground when queuing for it as other guests seem to like to push in!!!  The best way to reach the hotel is by water taxi and there are online offers available. We paid £50 for two of us and travelled by speed boat with 4 other couples- it was great! Take an hour or so to wander round giudecca and the little canals. Also there is a small supermarket on the waters edge just a 15 minute walk towards redentore church which is great for getting last minute bags of Italian chocolates to take home for the kids!
