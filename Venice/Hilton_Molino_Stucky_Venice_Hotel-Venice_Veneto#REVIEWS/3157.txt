Just across the water from Venice on the isle of Giudecca is the Hilton Molino Stucky Venice.  A former flour mill, this luxury hotel is all that you would expect from the Hilton brand, and more.  Situated right on the water, we dined outside with spectacular views, enjoyed evenings at the rooftop bar, and were in awe of how they managed to keep so much of this original factory while transforming it to a hotel.  We upgraded to a fully-equipped one bedroom suite and the large pull-open windows allowed for a beautiful breeze and view.  The hotel has everything you need including a business center and shops.  Their breakfast lounge had fresh fruit and pastries and the restaurant food was good but pricey.  We were happy to dine outside for the ambiance and view alone.
