Just back from a weekend in romantic venice. Where else would we stay other than here?! My wife had let them know it was a special occasion and the hotel had upgraded us to a Venice view room, a bottle of bubbly was waiting along with a cake. Well done the management of the Stucky. The building when arriving which of course has to be by boat is amazing and never fails to impress. The staff are eager to please and always polite. The room was of a good size and that sought after Venice view including the canal. The bed is large and very comfortable. Breakfast which is a buffet including Prosecco was very good value. Lots of choice and well cooked. Top tip include the breakfast in the room rate as its an eye watering 30 Euros each otherwise.
