We stayed for 3 nights in May and from the time we arrived to the time we checked out, we received excellent service.The hotel itself is a beautiful converted factory and there are still some of the old beams visible which gives this property lots of character. The rooftop pool and view is stunning - but there are not many sunloungers so get there early if you want one.  Drinking and eating in the hotel is very expensive though! We stayed in a suite on the 4th floor and it was really great, we were there as part of our honeymoon and the hotel arranged for a bottle of prosecco and chocolate dipped strawberries to be in our room on arrival - very lovely touch.
