We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky for a pre-cruise visit for 3 days.  I had been apprehensive because of the location and my unfamiliarity with the Venice hotel scene.  We essentially booked it because we felt comfortable with it being a Hilton brand.  We were very pleasantly surprised, and found the location to be a real asset and not a drawback.  The Giudecca location allows you be in the midst of Venice and all it's wonderful sights, and jump on the water taxi and be out of the crowds and clamor in a few minutes.  The rooms were excellent, and the converted flour mill added real charm to the stay.  My wife still talks about it, and is insisting to go back.
