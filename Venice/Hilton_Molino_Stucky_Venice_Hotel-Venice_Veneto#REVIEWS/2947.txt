We stayed here in late March on Hilton points as I am a Diamond member.  We were allowed to check in early and upgraded to a corner suite, which was absolutely amazing.  The views were incredible. We used the hotel shuttle boat once to get over to the main island and then bought 7 day vaporetto passes for all our other travel as we knew we wanted to go to Murano and San Giorgio Maggiore.  The breakfast buffet was crowded, but as this is a huge hotel property that is expected. Even so, we never had issues with the service there or at any other place in the hotel. We did not visit the bars or the restaurants (other than breakfast), however we did do room service once or twice.
