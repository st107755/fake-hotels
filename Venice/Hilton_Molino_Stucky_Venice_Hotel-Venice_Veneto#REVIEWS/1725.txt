On a first visit to Venice for my birthday we decided to stay at the Molino Stucky.  First impressions were good, check in went smoothly and we excitedly went up to our room - that's were things got disappointing.  We paid over €250 for a room that we'd asked for a good view and also stayed it was my birthday - so we got a room with a tiny window and a view of some industrial building and nothing for my birthday. The room decor was ok but basic - no slippers, no robes, 1 bedside lamp, no coffee machine, no free water, only news channels in English. All pretty disappointing for the cost.  So the hotel had a few restaurants and was a great base for exploring Venice without the craziness of the main island but for that cost, it really was a let down.
