This is a lovely but very large hotel -even the staff gets confused(bags can be delivered to the wrong building).   That being said nice rooms and bathrooms, generous breakfast and a true Venice location.  You are across the canal from the main island but the free hotel shuttle operates from early am to after midnight so that is no problem.  After you have done the obligatory and crowded and perhaps flooded St. marks Square use the hotel shuttle to Zaterre - runs more often, much quieter, you can visit the Guggenheim and the Academia and true artists' galleries.  Be aware about the only dining option in this location is the hotel and it is pricey.  Eat on the main island.
