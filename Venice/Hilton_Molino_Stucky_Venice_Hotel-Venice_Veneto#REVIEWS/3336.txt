Lets start with location. Nice enough area, off the main Venice island which i'll leave to you whether you'd like or not. We did, no massive groups of tourists, very quiet. We took a private water taxi to the hotel on arrival (€110 for a boat which takes 6ish) which was lovely under the stars, only 30 mins and the water taxi back (€16pp) which took 90 mins ish.  The hotel itself is very tastefully converted from an old flour mill I believe, with most of the original brick walls and wooden beams exposed, albeit renovated. Very well done. We just had a basic King room. Well decorated, spacious and a well kitted out bathroom.  Breakfast was everything a westerner could ever hope for. For a change at these types of hotels, the breakfast staff were happy and very attentive!
