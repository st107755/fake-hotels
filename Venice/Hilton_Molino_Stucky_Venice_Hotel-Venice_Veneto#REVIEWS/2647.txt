For me this was a surprise anniversary trip organised and booked by hubby!!. The Hilton was a good hotel with all mod cons (even if you had to pay for them. Breakfast & Wi-Fi to mention 2). Other reviewers have said that the hotel boat is free but we were told it would be 7 euros. Our room was on the top floor at the back of the hotel, we heard no external noise at all. The lift is super efficient.The room was spacious and the bed very comfy, the bathroom large but the shower cubicle was a standard size which seemed lost in the room, the door did not fit at the bottom, so water leaked during showers. The location was great away from the hustle & bustle and so easy to get to using the public water buses.
