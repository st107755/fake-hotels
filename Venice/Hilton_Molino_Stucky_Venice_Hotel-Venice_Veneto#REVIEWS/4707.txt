Stayed recently with my Wife for a long weekend. Check in was slow as although there were a number of Check in desks only 2 were staffed. At least when we reached check in desk the process was quick & efficient.(Robotic)  Exec Room overlooking the water to Venice was clean &  comfortable but not as large as some of the Exec Rooms within the Group. Marble bathroom was clean with adequate toiletries provided.Plenty of hot water.  We ate in the hotel restaurants during our stay and although the quality of the food was excellent we felt it was somewhat overpriced. Service was efficient ( Robotic) and we did have problems in getting the Waiting Staff to differentiate between Prosecco which was served and Champagne which was actually ordered.
