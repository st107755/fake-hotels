Arrived for a four night stay over Easter weekend. We got a shared water taxi, pre-booked online and it was £100 return for two people - cheaper than €100 one way from airport. Takes around 30-40 mins from airport, easy to find but hotel is on a separate island, 20 minute water shuttle from all main attractions. Hilton provides this service but expect this charge on your bill at checkout (€9 for two). Hotel and rooms are lovely, worth the price, bed very comfortable and clean. Sky bar is lovely albeit pricey (€17 for a disarrano sour). Things you would expect to be included are extra. Spa is €25 a day and the pool was closed our entire stay.
