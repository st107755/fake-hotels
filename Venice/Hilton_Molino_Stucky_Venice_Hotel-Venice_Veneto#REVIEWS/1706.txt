An amazing very large hotel on the island of Giudecca rather than the main Venice island. It was originally a four mill and pasta factory which fell into ruin in the 1950s but was completely refurbished at enormous expense in 2007. The brick shell and some iron columns, girders have been retained, but everything else is absolutely top notch showing Italian design at its best. The hotel has a boat which goes across to Venice at least once an hour and more often at busy times. Don’t make our mistake of taking the red bus; it drops you at the other end of Giudecca which is about 40 minutes walk from the hotel; the blue bus stops just outside the hotel entrance for EUR15, or you can pay much more for a speedboat taxi.
