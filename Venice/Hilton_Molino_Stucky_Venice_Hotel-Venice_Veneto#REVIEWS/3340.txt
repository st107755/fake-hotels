First of all: the Hilton is a good Hotel and you will have a decent stay. But there are a few service aspects which need improvement OR having the right mindset.  Pro: - Rooms and beds are good. - TV works and has enought international channels. - Very fast Internet in the Lobby (where it is free) - Good and punctual (and free to use) shuttle boats to the main Island (two different stops).  Con: - The Pool is on the rooftop and therefore not allways open (e.g. Mid-October already closed). - Bathrooms could be a bit cleaner and lime stone removed. - WIFI in the guest rooms is not free. - The gym is - for a Hotel in that category - a disappointment. - For all Photographer trying to take photos of Venezia: the Rooftop is closed in the mornings and during the night.
