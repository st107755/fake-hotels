The driving directions to the hotel were incomplete, and we ended up quite confused about which boat shuttle to take from the parking garage. Staff is mixed between helpful and unhelpful people.  When we check in the staff was divided over whether to be helpful or not--after much persistence, we were allowed to change our room (which was prebooked for 3, but too small).  The second bellhop we worked with gave us most of the information we needed about the hotel that the front desk staff and first bellhop did not give us.  Same at breakfast and in the hallways--some helpful, some less so.  The restaurants were fine, but limited and not spectacular or memorable--you can special order things at breakfast like eggs, but they don't make this obvious.
