The hotel was lovely inside and out and our room was perfect for our stay. The hotel shuttle service was ideal and as long as we arrived a few minutes early we were guaranteed a seat, we never had to wait for the next boat. The pool was closed for the duration of our stay and we were upset that no one informed us of this before we arrived. We also felt a five star hotel should have a leisure pool for its guests to use. On our last night in the hotel the skyline bar was closed for an event and when we returned from the mainland the noise from the bar upstairs kept us awake until late into the night. Even a call to the manager didn't help and we were told that nothing could be done. We finally got to sleep but missed out on a full morning in the city as a result.
