We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky Hotel in Venice on Guidecca Island in mid November. The hotel is very modern and clean and the staff is fluent in English. The front desk offered us an opportunity to upgrade our room and use the Executive Dining room, which offers free breakfast in the morning and drinks/snacks in the evening. It was totally worth the additional price. There is a shuttle that takes you to and from the main island. At first, I thought staying across the canal  would be a major inconvenience, but it turned out to be wonderful. We enjoyed the quick ride across the canal and got some great photos of the Venice skyline. Venice is always extremely crowded so it was nice to get away from all the chaos and recharge at our beautiful hotel.
