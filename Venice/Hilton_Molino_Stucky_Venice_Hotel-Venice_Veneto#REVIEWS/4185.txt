I stayed here for 2 nights with my friend and my two sons during the summer.  We had connecting rooms that were very well decorated with wooden beams and nice furnishings.  The room with two twins was definitely larger than the room with a king sized bed.  The bathrooms were spacious and very clean and modern.  The building is very unique (an old flourmill) and the rooftop pool was amazing.  I can't believe what a fantastic view you have from up there.  So relaxing after a long day of sightseeing.  The complimentary shuttles were easy to catch and very convenient.  They ran often and I enjoyed having the hotel outside of the busy Venice city-center.  It was very quiet and calm around the hotel.
