Overall, the hotel itself is impressive. I'd asked for a room upgrade/canal view as it was my mum's birthday. They were unable to accommodate my request but offered half price on the executive upgrade price which I declined.  They did however arrange a card and some mini biscuits as a gift.  Our room was facing the canal on the opposite side to Venice, which was still quite nice as I'd read other reviews which said they had views of the building.  Positives are the room sizes are decent, we had a standard twin and it was well appointed with a lovely bathroom. Coffee making facilities was good too and complimentary shampoo etc always a plus. Buffet breakfast was really good, even though the options never changed over the 4 days we were there.
