Stayed here following a cruise.  Hotel is accessed by water taxi although they do have a free water taxi that runs from the hotel to San Marco.  Hotel is a converted mill and has kept some of the features from the mill.  We were in Room 232 that looked directly out over the water to Venice.    Staff were very helpful with anything you asked for as well as providing information without prompting to assist you to enjoy your stay.  Only downside was the fact we decided to eat at the restaraunt Aromi which is attached to the hotel.  Found it to be overpriced and although the service was good, the food waqs not worth the price.
