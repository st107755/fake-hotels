I booked three rooms for 2 nights using points and it was well worth it! The hotel itself is great. The views we had out of our room were beautiful as the hotel sits right on the river. Getting to the hotel isn't all that bad. Everywhere you go in Venice requires waterway travel. We arrived at the train station and took a vaperatto from right across the street right to the hotel. The staff was wonderful. We even had dinner one night at the restaurant. While it was a bit pricey, it good.  The hotel staff helped us get around as we were planning a trip to Murano and Burano from the hotel.  Having the hotel shuttle is also a plus, making going places easier. Just keep in mind that you cannot buy tickets for the vaperatto at the stop located in front of the hotel.
