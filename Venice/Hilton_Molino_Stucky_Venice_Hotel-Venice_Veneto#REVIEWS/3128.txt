We enjoyed our brief stay at the Molino Stucky - a hotel with history, beautifully revamped. The hotel provides an excellent boat service to and fro central Venice so no worries that you are not on the Grand Canal itself..Boatmen are courteous and will help you on board. The main public areas of the hotel are beautifully done and staff are very attentive and friendly. Our deluxe room seemed a bit smaller than expected but had a lovely side view of the canal with windows that open, which we liked. We do prefer walk- in showers but this was over a bath but room was very clean.  We were surprised to see no complimentary water in our room and when I enquired they said this was normal. ( NOT in the deluxe Hiltons we had previously visited it wasn't!)
