We stayed at the Hilton Stucky Venice Hotel for 3 nights on the tail end of a 2 week European vacation.  The building is an old flour mill that has been brilliantly converted to a 5* hotel.  We were upgraded to a tower suite and had an incredible view of Venice and the main waterway.  Our room was probably the roomiest if the 4 hotels we stayed at during the trip and was quite modern - much like a Hilton would be in the US (which is a great feature to us since we have been disappointed with European hotels in the past).  The location is great - off the hectic cruise-passenger laden main island but just a short 10 minute ferry ride away.
