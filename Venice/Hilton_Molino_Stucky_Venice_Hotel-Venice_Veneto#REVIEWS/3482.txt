We stayed for three nights and the hotel is very nice. Although it is not in Venice proper--it is across a large waterway--they provide a free shuttle to St. Mark's square and one other stop so it is not really a problem. The shuttle does stop at 10 or 11 pm so you do need to be back or you will have to get a private water taxi which is expensive. They had a free water service to the Murano glass factories and a tour ( 35 min each way) that was worth taking (check with front desk). You are captive at one factory, however, and then straight back. But it was interesting and a good side trip. The Hotel was nice by all standards. the spa was good as well. The restaurants are average at best.
