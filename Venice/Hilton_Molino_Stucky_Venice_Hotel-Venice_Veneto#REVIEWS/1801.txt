We stayed here 3 nts as part of our Italy trip.  It is a little harder to get to, but it was a nice hotel to be out of the hustle and bustle of the city. This made it nice and quiet at night, and had great views.  Water taxi dropped us right at the front door and during our stay, we used the water shuttle to get to and from Venice.  There is a one time fee for the shuttle.  The room was very comfortable, spacious and clean.  Staff was very friendly and helpful.  Breakfast was outstanding!  If you want to get by a little cheaper, instead of the restaurant and bar, walk down along the Guidecca Canal and take in one of the little restaurants there.  The pool at the Hilton was really nice, although a tad chilly, but did have excellent views all around.
