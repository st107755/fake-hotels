This Hilton is not cheap but we just love it. Stayed there on two separate occasions. You should be very particular on specifying where you want a view. There are some rooms with views of the courtyard but if if views are important, I would ask what you see before you accept a room.  Located across the canal from all the hustle and bustle is a plus. Ferry runs very frequently and just takes a couple of minutes.  There are some extremely nice restaurants on the Hilton side of the canal within a very short walk from the hotel.   The restaurant at the hotel was also surprisingly good and reasonable. We ate there the first night as just too tired after the long flight and boat ride from the airport.  HIGHLY suggest a Private water taxi from the airport to the hotel.
