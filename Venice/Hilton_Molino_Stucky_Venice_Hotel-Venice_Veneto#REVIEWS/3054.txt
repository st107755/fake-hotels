I've given this 4* and on FB 5*. Ideally would rate it and 4 and a half. Overall it was a fantastic stay and I hope to return one day and hopefully when the pool is open ( we missed its opening by days and one of the reasons I chose the hotel) I arranged this as a surprise for my mums 70th. I had requested a room with a view but unfortunately didn't get. I could have tried for a swap but my mum didn't particularly want to and Im guessing canal facing rooms are double rooms /suites as opposed to a twin room. The hotel is huge and a bit of a rabbit warren, first time you will need directions as not all the lifts go the same floors. Still staff were so helpful and approachable and were throughout our stay. Room although not with a view was lovely, great bathroom.
