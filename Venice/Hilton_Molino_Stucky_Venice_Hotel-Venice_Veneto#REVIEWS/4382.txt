We were looking for a short break away in June and decided on Venice as the flight time from the UK is great and were drawn by stories of the beauty and history of the city. Having looked at numerous hotels we finally decided on the Hilton Molino Stucky as we had had stays in other Hiltons hotels such one in Hong Kong- we believe in sticking to a chain hotel as more often than not you known you are assured a certain standard. The hotel is it has to be admitted a fair trek as it is on an island away from centre of city - you have to take a ferry to reach it and from airport to hotel takes over an hour and journey like the one we had can be choppy and test the stability of your sea legs ( and stomach).
