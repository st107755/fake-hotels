I stayed there with my wife for 4 nights to celebrate her 40th birthday and our 21st wedding anniversary. We arrived by private water taxi and we were astounded at the magnificence of the building which was stunning. Bell boy took our cases and escorted us to the check in desk. First impressions were very satisfying and impressive at the granduer of this hotel. At the check in we were congratulated on our upcoming anniversary, a very nice personal approach. We arrived in our room to be greeted with champagne and chocolate covered strawberries, looked out our 5th floor bedroom which overlooked Venice, an oustanding view which took our breath away, you could see every tower, chapel, st marks square, the whole island from our window. Magic.
