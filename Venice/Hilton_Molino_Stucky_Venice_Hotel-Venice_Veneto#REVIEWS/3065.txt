We stayed in Hilton Molino Stucky  for 2 nights in April. It was a very pleasant stay. Prior to our visit, hotel was prompt in responding to our queries on how to reach the hotel etc. At  time time of check-in We were upgraded to a family suite due to the Gold HHonors membership that I have and were given access to Exec Lounge. The suite was really spacious and comfortable with 2 bedrooms and a living room.  Though the hotel is across the canal from the main attractions(San Marco square, Cathedral, Doge's palace etc) ,there is a very convenient water shuttle to and fro every 30 min or so from the Hotel to Zettere and San Marco. The breakfast at the hotel was very nice as you would generally expect in a Hilton hotel with plenty of hot options .
