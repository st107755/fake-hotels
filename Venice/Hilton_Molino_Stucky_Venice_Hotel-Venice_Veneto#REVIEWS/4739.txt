We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky for one night prior to our cruise from Venice.  We had some trepidation about booking a hotel on its own island and so far from the “action” of Venice.  We were blown away at the Molino Stucky! The water taxi took us right to the dock and the door man was waiting for us! We were whisked to the beautiful lobby and the Hilton Honors desk lady was super nice and even though our room wasn’t ready, we could/did enjoy the executive lounge breakfast.  The Molino Stucky is HUGE! Let me say that up front and we did get lost a few times finding our way to our room but the work they have done to this property is unbelievable.  We had suite B533 with a sea view, king bed, separate living room, dining room and a bath and a half.
