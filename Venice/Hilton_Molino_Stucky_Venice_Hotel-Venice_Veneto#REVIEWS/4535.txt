Hilton is always Hilton but this property is new and actually everything - rooms, furniture, accessories are in a very good condition. Absolutely no complains to sevice too. To my mind the location is very suitable for Venice - it is far from noisy and crowdy tourist routes but you have a possibility easily to go San Marco by shuttle boat. We did't have any problems with it in April but it might be busy during the season. Please be aware about two things which greatly disappointed me: 1) food and drinks are extremely overpriced even for Venice, 2) the quality of the food at McDonalds level. I do not know how they managed to do this but hotel food has absolutely nothing to do with Italian food. It's a shame for 5* hotel.
