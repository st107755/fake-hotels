While we usually opt for less commercial hotels for couples trip , I am glad we were advised to stay at the Hilton ,Sticky Molino. This updated industrial building has been well restored to a 4-5 * hotel a  short distance away  from  bustling Venice. The accommodations  were well done, comfortable and clean.  The bathtub had copious water to soak away our tired feet.       The barman ,Orfeo, was a true gentleman and helped weary travelers unwind and relax. The proscecco and other concoctions were prepared with a gracious smile.       The high lights  though were the fabulous panoramic views from the Skyline bar. A ethereal place to soak in the sites form afar.        The concierge , Elena, helped with transfers and made you very comfortable with any request.
