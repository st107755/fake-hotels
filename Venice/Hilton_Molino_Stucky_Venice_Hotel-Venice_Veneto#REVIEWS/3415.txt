The Molino Stucky has great boat connections anywhere in Venice. Would recommend the top floor bar and swimming pool with great viewing of the entire surroundings. Consider the hotel as a business related stay, more romantic and Venice style - to stay on the other side of water. The price of hotel is quite high compared to quality, especially when thinking about the room.
