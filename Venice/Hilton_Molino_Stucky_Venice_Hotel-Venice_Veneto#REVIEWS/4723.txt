Great location for a great service hotel. Real gem on Guidecca, provides frequent shuttle service to Zattere and San Marco (free, timesaver, convenient) and is the last stop on the airport shuttle waterbus (real inexpensive option). Front desk staff is outstanding and can assist in multiple languages. Customer service and friendliness are a priority - when you've just landed in a foreign country, the comfort factor is key. The Hilton is a couple minutes walk from the Palanca vaporetto stop and nearby local restaurants. (The Veneto wines are great.) Also, the hotel is a respite from the tourist crowds - you can have a drink at the top floor bar and plan your next excursion to a Venice sight.
