The selection of the Hilton Molino was our mistake.  We were sightseeing in Italy over Christmas and Venice was our last stop.  As a resort, the hotel was very nice.  You have all the comforts of an American Hilton hotel.  My only complaint about the facility was the fact that there is a charge for in-room wifi access.  I was surprised because all the places we stayed in Italy had complimentary in-room wifi.  If you have Hilton Honors gold status a lot of stuff is complimentary, but since I only had silver I was like everyone else.  The location of the hotel required a vaporetto ride to get to any of the Venice sightseeing areas. The Hotel provided a complimentary vaporetto that ran from 9AM until 11PM every hour (in the winter).
