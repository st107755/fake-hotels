We stayed here recently for a conference and were very happy with the accommodation. The hotel is in a group of buildings which were once a disused and neglected flour mill. The restoration is absolutely top notch and no-expense-spared, and the hotel is archtecturally wonderful. They have a small gallery of photos of the mill and staff back in its working days, which is worth a look.   We had a room on the eastern side with a great view along Guidecca and across in the direction of San Marco. The room was spacious and very comfortable, and the bathroom was well appointed with a shower-over-bath set up as well as a bidet.  The hotel is on the opposite side of Guidecca Canal from the rest of Venice, so you have to get water transport to go anywhere touristy.
