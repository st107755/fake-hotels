The Hilton Molino Stucky Venice is a nice spot to spend a few days; I visited with my wife and two boys (5 and 3) last month.  Situated on the adjacent island of Guidecca, it allowed me to enjoy my trip to Venice much more than previous visits due to being able to retreat to a much quieter area after doing the "touristy" things for the day, yet it was just a quick (and cheap, relatively) hotel ferry ride away from the action.  The hotel has all the expected amenities, and while I wouldn't say it was the best variety or pricing I have seen for room service, they did do an excellent job catering to my wife's gluten and dairy allergies, which put us at ease.  Breakfast was excellent, and every staff member we interacted with went above and beyond to help us.
