Hotel is Not perfectly located but does have good views if you get upgraded or pay for the upgrade yourself. The Breakfast is not superb but does have a moderate selection of fruits and other choices. Hard to find things if you are eating healthy though.  Somewhat overpriced and having to wait for shuttle to take you across becomes somewhat of a nuisance but better than dealing with crowds on the main island.  Venice is the Disneyland of Italy but okay if you spend a few days exploring the sites and relaxing. Does get somewhat old though after two to three days. Be prepared to walk and walk and walk. I am in great shape but walking around in circles does become somewhat old.
