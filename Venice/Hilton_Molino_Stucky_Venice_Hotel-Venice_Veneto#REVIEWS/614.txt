The Molino Stucky Hilton was the final hotel on our tour of Italy. Our room was large and had a huge bathroom with nice toiletries (e.g., the usual items plus toothbrush and toothpaste, a comb, shaving kit, etc.) and washcloths (first hotel on the trip to have them). The breakfast buffet has become our new standard for great spreads, surpassing our previous standard, a hotel in Prague. My wife pronounced the spread "humongous." I noted the following items, among many others: granola with many toppings, several different types of milk including soy, many different types of cut fruit including watermelon, turkey sausage, several different cheeses, and honey in a real honeycomb. The only other time we've encountered this in our travels was in Amsterdam.
