We were lucky enough to be upgraded to the presidential suite when we went which was quite spectacular. The bedrooms were a good size and very big for Venice. The bathroom was a good size also with a shower and bath separately. The suite also had a lounge, gym, kitchen, and a hot tub and your own personal viewing tower. The room was well decorated with out being over the top and had a very modern feel. There was also spectacular views as the hotel was not on the main island of Venice but rather looked out on to it. The hotel is a renovated factory as so is therefore very big and spacious compared to other hotels on the actual main island.
