We stayed here last September and were so impressed I've booked again for this coming July.It's pretty much everything I expected from a five star hotel with a famous name. Our executive room was spacious and clean, with a comfortable king size bed and lovely bathroom. It had all the usual and expected amenities - safe, minibar, flatscreen TV, table, chairs, air conditioning. Maid service was excellent. There is wifi available in the room, not free unfortunately but reasonably priced. All the hotel staff we encountered were courteous, friendly and helpful.  The breakfast buffet is superb, one of the best hotel breakfasts we've had in Europe. There is loads of choice and the quality of the food is excellent.
