Insight Vacations put us up in this hotel as part of our Spotlight of Europe tour.  This was by far the best of all our hotels in the cities we visited.  The location is the island opposite the old city of Venice and San Marco square (where most tourists end up).  The hotel provides a free water taxi once an hour on weekdays (until late afternoon and evening then every 40 minutes) and every 40 minutes weekends.  It is a 10 minutes ride to Venice proper.  The hotel is an old flour factory that has been restored and converted into a hotel.  It rates 5 stars in my opinion.  Marble floors, rich wood doors, arched doorways and ceilings.  It covers several buildings and has a spa and rooftop swimming pool.  The service is excellent.  The rooms went well beyond our expectations.
