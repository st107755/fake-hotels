my wife and I stayed here for 3 nights in Oct 2014 during a 2 week trip to Italy. Getting to hotel was fairly easy with a water taxi from the airport.  had a few stops but got to see a lot on the way.  The hotel, I think, is unaware they the taxi has a stop right at the hotel as their instructions were to disembark and catch the Hilton water shuttle. Any way, the Hilton does have a water shuttle that is regular, free and very helpful getting to the best stops in Venice. The concierges were also extremely helpful, especially Elena. we are inexperienced travelers and she was very patient.  There is a nice executive lounge if you're allowed access(Hilton Honors level) with breakfast as well as snacks during the day and wine in the evening.
