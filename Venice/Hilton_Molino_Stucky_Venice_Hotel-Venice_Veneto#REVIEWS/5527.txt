Not sure what to expect from previous reviews of this hotel but thought a 5 star would deliver far more (and indeed SHOULD). Previous reviewer who commented that this hotel is typically 'American Hilton' but set in Venice is spot on. Hotel is huge and you can easily get lost (they provide a map of Venice with a plan of the hotel on the reverse to help locate the many areas! Some areas accessible only via outside routes (Aromi restaurant - which is great in the summer but not so good in December). All extras have to be paid for - Spa, sauna and even printing for aeroplane boarding passes, after also paying for expensive internet access to download prior to printing.
