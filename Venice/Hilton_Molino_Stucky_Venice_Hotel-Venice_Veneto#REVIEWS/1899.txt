If you are looking for a lovely hotel whilst in Venice then look no further than the Hilton Molino.  Whilst it is clearly part of a large hotel chain, this is probably the best Hilton I have stayed in and due to business travel I have had to stay in quite a few over the years.  Executive Lounge is great if you have access.  Rooftop Bar and Pool areas are brilliant.  Only downside to the rooftop pool is that it isn't that big and gets quite overcrowded at peak times - note you may have to sit on your towel for a while before a sun lounger becomes available - however the views are well worth it as you have a birds eye view of Venice in the distance.  Staff are very attentive and the hotel is immaculate.
