We love the location of this hotel, on Giudecca Island, with it's own shuttle into Venice (4.5 Euros per person per stay) and vaporetto stops to the left and to the right. With all the water traffic, you would think it would be noisy, but it is surprisingly quiet. We loved that at the end of the long day in Venice proper, we could get away from the crowds. The walk along the channel is lovely, with side streets and real people living real lives, bakeries, hardware stores, office supply stores, and a gondola repair and making shop we found just wandering around. Giudecca reminds me a little of what Venice used to be, with neighbors greeting one another, shaking hands, stopping for a drink on the way home.
