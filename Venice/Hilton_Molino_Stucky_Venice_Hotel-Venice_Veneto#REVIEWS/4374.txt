I booked this hotel on Giudecca, a small island just off Venice, as it was one boat stop away from the cruise terminal, we were taking a cruise the next day. Our room was lovely, the staff were very helpful and attentive. We dined in Venice that evening as there was a particular restaurant we wanted to experience, but on return to the hotel we noticed tables by the water edge and their own restaurant which looked quiet and very romantic. Maybe on a future visit we will try it! The breakfast buffet was all you could want and of very good quality, with very attentive service. I asked if there was any gluten free bread available and the waiter returned swiftly and very happily with warm freshly baked gluten free rolls!
