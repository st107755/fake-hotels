What an absolute pleasure. This hotel provides beautiful, spacious rooms which leave you feeling as though you are floating on the canal. The convenient and oft running shuttle made this a far more convenient hotel than others. we had stayed a few years ago at the Best Western Premiere, which is a great place but far less convenient.  The staff was great, David checked us in and gave us room upgrades. He patiently explained that the shuttle would only cost 9 euros for our stay... one time charge for a 3 day/ 2 night stay,,, unlimited use (2 adults, 2 kids). We used it often, and it only took 15 minutes to get you to San Marco square. Otherwise the hotel is conveniently located to 2 vaporetto stops which we used getting to the hotel from the train station and leaving to it.
