Stayed here for a three night break. Fantastic building and amazing to get the water bus from the airport and see the hotel all lit up. Great room with a view across the canal. We went to the spa, very small, it can only really fit 4-6 people and you have to book - which we didn't realise although they were very accommodating and managed to get us in. Great roof top bar and can imagine that in the summer it is delightful. Unfortunately the pool was closed as it is winter so didn't get to see that. Big range of food for breakfast. Restaurant food was nice although not a cheap meal, the cheapest bottle of wine (or at least that we could see) was €38.
