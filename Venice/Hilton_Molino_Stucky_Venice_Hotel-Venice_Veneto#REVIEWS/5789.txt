By far the best looking hotel in Venice. It was our first time to Venice and we chose this hotel for a little luxury after spending a week travelling around the Tuscany region. The hotel is breathtaking, its own website gives a pretty accurate description and it certainly lived up to our expectations and beyond. The concierge was helpful, reception staff friendly, lifts a little confusing until you get the hang of it. Our room overlooked the canal and Venice. It is very very expensive but for that extra special trip i highly recommend this hotel. The rooftop pool is lovely but there is a lack of sun loungers so get there fast. The Skyline bar is also very nice and staff friendly.
