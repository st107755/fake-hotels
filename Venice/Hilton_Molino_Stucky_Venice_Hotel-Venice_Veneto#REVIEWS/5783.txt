My boyfriend and I stayed at this hotel for one night at the end of May before leaving Venice on a cruise the following day.  What a wondeful location this hotel is in. On entering you immediately think WOW.  We headed straight for our room with the bell boy carrying our luggage.  Our room was lovely even though our view was only of a fire escape but Venice and the hotel in general are so beautiful you don't want to spend too much time in your room.  My boyfriend and I headed straight for the bar on the roof and what a view.  I have never seen anything like it.  The pool is also on the roof and is just amazing. I would recommend this hotel to anyone as it is something special.  The buffet breakfast was amazing.
