My wife and I stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky in Venice for the second time last month. As with our previous stay, we chose to lodge at the Hilton prior to embarking on a Med cruise at the nearby port terminal. I have to say that our most recent experience was even better than our previous stay. Although we opted for a less expensive room overlooking an interior courtyard at half the price of the room we had before with a canal view, the room was larger, the bathroom bigger, and the furnishings were nicer. Maybe we just lucked out. Our fourth floor room was very clean and quiet.  We had a strong Wi-Fi signal, comfortable bed, and decent ventilation. For the money and location, we were very satisfied with our room. We rarely saw or heard anyone in the hall outside.
