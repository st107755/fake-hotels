We could not have asked for a better place to stay that cold, windy, wet night when a storm struck Venice. Our ship, MSC Musica could not dock in Venice.We were taken to another port, and transported by bus back to Venice. Daylight gave way to darkness, and with it our hearts were filled with fear. By the time we reached Venice, a foreign land, it was past 10 pm. Not a soul in sight. Winds howled through our bodies into our bones, Luckily, my organized husband had printouts of our bookings and the hotel's telephone number. He called them and they happily organized a water taxi to come and get us (of course it was not complimentary).  When we landed at the Hilton, its very warmth enveloped us into its welcome midst. Our rooms were beautiful, different and nice sized.
