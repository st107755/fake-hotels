My wife and I had been planning a trip to Italy for 3 months. We are Hilton Gold Members and it seemed like this hotel would be perfect for us on our first overseas holiday. It was!   We booked a private speed boat from the airport which took about 30 mins ($130). If you’re into photography, the ride is very scenic! When we arrived, check-in was a breeze, and we even got a complimentary upgraded room and was invited to the executive lounge. The front desk agent (he had longer hair, don’t recall his name) was courteous and worked with us, as we know zero Italian.   We had drinks and shared lasagna as an appetizer after checking in, at the main dining area and the bartender was fantastic! Gave lots of recommendations.
