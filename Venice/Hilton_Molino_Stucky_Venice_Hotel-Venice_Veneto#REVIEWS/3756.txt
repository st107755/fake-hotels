We have just returned from a few days in Venice and were delighted that we chose this hotel.  At the time of booking we had looked at a hotel close to St Marks Square but were so glad we chose this hotel as at the end of a busy day sight seeing, it was lovely to get back to the peace of the island of Giudecca.  The hotel run a regular shuttle boat to the main island which was always reliable.  We stayed in an Executive room and we had a view of the gardens at the back which made it really quiet.  The room was large and the bathroom was really special with a jacuzzi bath and separate shower which was lovely and powerful.
