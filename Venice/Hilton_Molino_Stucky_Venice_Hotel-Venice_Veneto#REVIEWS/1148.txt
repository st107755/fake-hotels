This property is across from the “main island” of Venice, it sits looking back over Venice on the island of Guidecca. So if you’re looking for peace and quite from the hustle & bustle of Venice, this is a great hotel choice! There is a water taxi for the hotel that operates throughout the day, also there is a public water taxi if needed approx. 2 blocks away, if all else fails the hotel will call you a private water taxi but these can be pricey.   The property has seasonal air conditioning, so in April it was 70+ outside but we were informed it was not in season to operate so we were instructed to leave our windows open.  So bring bug spray in your bag if you are not there during the time of year A/C is available!  The property is very nice inside and outside.
