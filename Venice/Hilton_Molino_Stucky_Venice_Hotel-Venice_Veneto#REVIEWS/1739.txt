The good points about this hotel are the size of the rooms, comfortable king-size beds, quiet location and room, windows that open, good range on the breakfast menu and room to cater for large numbers. The negatives far outweigh these advantages. My main gripe was the cleanliness of the room. Our phone was hidden under a layer of dust, thick enough to embarrass the worst housekeeper. There was dust over every other surface in the room, but at its worst behind items that had obviously not been moved for cleaning. There was a very distinct layer of dust around the edge of the room and the legs of furniture. The toilet bowl wasn't clean. On the second day I noticed the tea cup I had used had been washed, however, there was a dried drip of tea down the outside of the cup.
