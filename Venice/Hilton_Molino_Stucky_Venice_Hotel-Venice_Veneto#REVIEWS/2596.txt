Stayed here for 2 nights in August. on the upside - Hilton Venice has very spacious rooms and bothrooms are simply big. There's a 6 cardio machines fintness room (fitness rooms are rare in Venice in all categories of hotels) and the rooftop pool is gorgeous. The food and beverage program is up to par with the brand and concierge people were very helpful and accommodating. The water taxis service that the hotel is running for almost free from the hotel to main "land" is major plus.  However and enerally speaking, it seems that the stars rating in Venice is slightly too generous. For a 5 stars hotel of a major international/American brand a 6 cardio machine "fitness room" is significantly below standard (there are remote Hampton Inns with a better gym).
