Luxury---slightly more affordable than other luxury hotels in Venice.  We stayed in a suite (executive suite? Junior suite?) which ended up being cheaper for our family of 5 than two rooms would have been.  There was a large queen sized bed in a separate room, a kitchenette, living room with sofa bed and a good sized marble bathroom.  Everything was clean and beautiful, gorgeous renovated old mill turned into a hotel.  Complimentary shuttle with frequent pickups and drop offs.  It is located on guideca island, so you didn't have to leave the island for dinner---you could just walk along the waterfront and look for a restaurant.  When you book a suite, you have access to the executive lounge---complimentary snacks, desserts, soft drinks, wine and beer.
