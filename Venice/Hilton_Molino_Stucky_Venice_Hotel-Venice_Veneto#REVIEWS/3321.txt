This is quite a difficult one to review.  The hotel is undoubtedly beautiful and has its own character despite being a chain. The shuttle boat service was excellent, extremely reliable and never a minute late.   The Skyline Bar was fabulous, a slick team in operation led by the quite superb Franco.  There simply cannot be a better view in Venice to enjoy your cocktails by.   Unfortunately the same cannot be said about the rest of the hotel.     Of course no-one cares for criticism and this establishment certainly doesn't seem to even want to comprehend the fact they do get things wrong.
