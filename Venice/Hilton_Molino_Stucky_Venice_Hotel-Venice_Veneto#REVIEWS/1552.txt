Stayed in this hotel  in December as part of a pre cruise extension from Viking Ocean Cruises.  Many staff speak fluent English.  Hotel has excellent accommodations; however, if you are used to bathing with a wash cloth do bring one from home. There is a possible issue for guests with mobility handicaps.  As with many European hotels, the rooms have bathtubs which one must get into to shower.  I do not recall a grab bar or fall prevention strips in the tub in our room . I was able to shower, but would have experienced difficulty if I did not undergo knee replacement surgery in April, 2016.  Our accommodations included a more than adequate breakfast with ample choices. We ate one one of the hotel's restaurants, Barcarromi, during our first night.
