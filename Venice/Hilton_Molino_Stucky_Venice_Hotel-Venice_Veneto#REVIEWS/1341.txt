This is a great hotel, particularly if you want peace and quiet.  In an old industrial building, it has great views of the canal and a fabulously warm and comforting bar in the lobby.  The room was comfortable and quiet, although the view onto a disused courtyard not particularly scenic (not every room can have a view!) and it did seem strange that slippers and bath robes had to be requested rather than included.  My wife claimed that her massage was the best she has ever had (and she has had a lot of massages in her life!) and the staff went out of their way to help me to provide a surprise for her at breakfast by playing out a table with some sparkles and candles I had bought to celebrate her birthday.
