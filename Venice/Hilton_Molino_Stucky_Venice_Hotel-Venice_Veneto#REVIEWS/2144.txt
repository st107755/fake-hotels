This building is a huge old grain mill which has been converted to a luxury hotel.  The inside is fantastic  It is beautiful and imposing in both day and night. The service was very good, and for the most part people were helpful and friendly.  The hotel was absolutely sold out, so the staff was pretty busy and stressed.  There was apparently a conference going on there at the time. The bar on the roof (Skytop Lounge?) is a must.  We had a drink up there overlooking the city, and it was great.  We had breakfast one morning, and the breakfast was good.  It was a bit pricey (because we neglected to book it in our room rate), but had a very good selection of food. The rooms were large, quiet and very comfortable.  Everything inside the room worked well.
