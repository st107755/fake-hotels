We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky for 4 nights during our 24 day holiday through Europe.  The hotel is very beautiful with great rooms and high ceilings.  Our room was located on the 6th floor in the arsennal building (over looking the canal).    The free water shuttle to the Zattere stop (5 minutes) and then to San Marco (15 min) is a wonderful addition and takes the hassle out of having to figure out transportation.  Get a copy of the Hilton's shuttle schedule and you will be set.  If you are coming here for the pool, you will be disappointed because there are only about 20 chairs and you must be up there at 8am in order to snag one.
