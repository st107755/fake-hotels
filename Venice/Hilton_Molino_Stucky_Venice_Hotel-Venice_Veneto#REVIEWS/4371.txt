Loved the hotel. Although a large hotel it is very good, situated quietley just away from the main island, the hotel provides and free shuttel boat. The roof top pool is a must and the skyline bar in the evening is essential (although expensive). Breakfast very good and although we had to leave early on our last day for plane they provided a great 'packed breakfast' for collection free of charge which was a nice touch.
