Stayed for 4 nights in April.  LOCATION AND GETTING TO AND FROM CITY: Molino Stucky is located on the island of Guidecca. We arrived from Treviso airport from which you can board 40 minute bus to Tronchetto Car Park. From here it is 1 stop on boat to Guidecca Island (stop: Sacca Fisola) and about a 5 minute walk to the hotel from there. Alternatively you can get off at Palanca stop which is 2 stops further (the middle stop is on the other side of the water) and again it's about a 5 minute walk from there too. The hotel offers a "free" shuttle service to Piazza Saint Marco (..well as free as free can be- I think it's about 10 euro charge per couple per stay). The boat leaves approximately every 40 minute from morning until about midnight which is fantastic.
