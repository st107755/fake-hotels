We stayed at this hotel while in venice and had a wonderful time. I would definitely go back. We booked a family suite which was more than enough space for a family of 4 (children 7 & 9). The shuttle was tricky to find from San Marco they could use better signage. The hotel will provide a map and shuttle schedule. We didn't have a problem with the frequency since we planned around it but it does take a longer break around lunch and around 8 PM. We had access to the executive lounge which is absolutely excellent. The roof top pool is beautiful but very cold. The service is at this hotel is wonderful. The only things that could be improved are the signage/frequency of the shuttle and pool temperature.
