A nice, large, modern and spacious hotel on Giudecca island across the channel from the main Venice island, San Marco. When you stay there, the hotel has a "free" (one time charge of less than 10 Euros covers entire stay) shuttle boat to take you back and forth. Service was friendly and good, although we never got a call that our room was ready since we arrived early, and the bell never gave us a ticket for bags, so we had to search the luggage room to find them.   Rooms are large, comfortable and functional. The bath had a tub with a half-door of glass that swung in and out and sometimes let the shower spill out to the floor. Room had a wardrobe with limited hanging space. There was also a dresser that had the mini-fridge and coffee as well as a couple drawers.
