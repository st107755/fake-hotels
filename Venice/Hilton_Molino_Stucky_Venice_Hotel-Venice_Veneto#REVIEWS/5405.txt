This hotel is very nice and must get to it by boat.  They have free water taxi's to Venice regularly.  The first room we had did not have a view so we changed and had a wonderful view.  The staff was very nice and friendly.  Breakfast everyday was great.  We filled up and just did a light lunch and a nice dinner.  The wifi is VERY expensive and they do not provide any WiFi hotspots even in the lobby.  There are two computers past the bar with printers for boarding tickets, etc.  These are free but do not have timers.  I had to wait for over 40 minutes to get on one as the other people stayed on forever.  For the price you pay for this hotel, they should give you a hotspot.
