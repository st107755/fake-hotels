I took my family to Venice and stayed for 2 nights at the Hilton Molino Stucky Venice. The rooms and flights were paid for using air miles and Hilton Hhonors points that I had collected through business travel.  The rooms are very well decorated and clean and well kept. The amenities are typically Hilton and they have a small Executive lounge which is a glass walled sectioned-off part of the ground floor lobby lounge, controlled access via your room key. The Executive lounge is small and cramped, long and thing but it does the job adequately enough and the free food and drink is quite good.  The hotel room we had did have a very nice view over the canal to Venice (room 233) and the room was air conditioned and the bed very comfortable.
