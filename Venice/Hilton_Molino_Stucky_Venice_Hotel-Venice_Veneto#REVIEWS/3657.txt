This was our first time in Venice and despite requiring a 10-15 minute boat trip from the main attractions this is a great place to stay. The hotel is styled in a fairly contemporary way but is in keeping with the original flour and pasta factory that dates back to the late 19th century.  Rooms are spacious, comfortable and modern but room cleanliness was not at the highest level.There were stains on the headboard and I complained when I found an eaten prune stone on the floor and was assured that this would be rectified immediately. 18 hours later it was still there so I again mentioned this at checkout - The receptionist thanked me again for bringing this to their attention and would discuss this with housekeeping, which really felt just like "lip service".
