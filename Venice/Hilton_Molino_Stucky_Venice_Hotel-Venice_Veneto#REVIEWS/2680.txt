Having previous visited Venice in month of August, I know just how crowded and hot it can get. We specifically pick Molino Stucky for its location on Guidecca to avoid the crowd and its pool for its heat. I have to say having the right hotel choice make all the difference to our visit to Venice this time during our late August/beginning of September stay.   Our flight to Venice was delayed by 5 hours. It doesn't help it took almost another hour for the baggages to arrive at the baggage claim belt. At least travel from Marco Polo airport to Molino Stucky was issue free. The express bus to Roma piazale was quick & easy and the Vaperatto line 4.2 to Molino Stucky (Sacca Fisola)  took only 3 stops, less than 10 minutes.
