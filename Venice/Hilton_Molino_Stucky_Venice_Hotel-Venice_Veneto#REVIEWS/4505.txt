Brilliant hotel. Make sure you ask for a room high up. You won't stay in a room in front of the canal (those are more expensive), but you *may* get a room on the side with a view of the Giudecca canal. Stunning view from the Sky Lunch / Bar and the roof top swimming pool, although a bit small, is a great place to enjoy the sun.You can also request free 24h WiFi vouchers for access in the lobby / lounge which is a plus when you have to pay for WiFi in Venice.Last but not least, it's located in a less crowded area of Venice, giving you a bit of a break from the noise and "tourist stress". There's a free shuttle across the canal to Zattere and San Zacharia (San Marco).
