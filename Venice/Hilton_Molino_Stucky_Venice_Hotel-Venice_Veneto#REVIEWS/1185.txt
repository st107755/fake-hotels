I stayed at this hotel last week with my boyfriend to visit the Biennale.  We loved the location on the Giudecca. It was such a relief to be away from the crowds in the more touristy parts of Venice. I cannot emphasise this enough!  There is a shuttle offered by the hotel to cross the canal and there is also a vaporetto station a stone's throw away which we also relied on.  In the mornings we took breakfast at a café down the street toward the vaporetto station (has a wood bench outside... do not have the name right now...), which was a highlight and which we highly recommend. Well priced and the most delicious panini (also fresh-squeezed juice and coffees).  In the hotel our room was small, but the bed was very comfortable.
