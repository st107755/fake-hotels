First, the positives. The hotel is gorgeous. We were given a huge, upgraded room. The executive lounge is large and well-stocked. The wifi is fast. The shuttle to Venice itself was quick and efficient. Now, the negatives. Complete misinformation about how to get to the hotel. We rang ahead and asked, as we were driving to Venice. We were told about the Tronchetto parking area, which we found just fine, but it went downhill from there. We were misinformed about the hotel shuttle service. When we finally learned we would have to take a taxi boat to the hotel, we were not told clearly where to find these taxis. It was dark and rainy, and the area was under construction.
