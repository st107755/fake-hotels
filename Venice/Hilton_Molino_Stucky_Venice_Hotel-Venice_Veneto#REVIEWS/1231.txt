The Hilton Molino Stucky was in a great location with easy access to St Marks' Square by the complimentary water shuttle.  However, some of the timings are 40 minutes and sometimes in the evening queues can be long getting back so make sure you arrive in good time to catch it.  Also, allow for flooding in the square..The selection of food at breakfast was excellent and the service was very good, particularly from Victoria.  However, bananas, apples and blueberries would have been welcomed.  The furnishings were luxurious, although I would expect this from the cost, and the pool was pleasant although it didn't seem to be heated which was disappointing because of the cost of the hotel.
