We returned yesterday from a 4 day stay at this hotel over New Year. We had 3 rooms with 4 adults and 3 grown up children. The hotel is not situated on the main island but you have to take the hotels own boat which runs every 40 minutes all day/night (costs 3.50 euros per person which is a one off charge). The main island is 'VERY' busy so actually the hotels location is ideal. The hotel breakfast is 'Amazing' so much choice and quality, you will not be disappointed. We also had an evening meal at the hotel on one night which was also very good. The rooms have everything you need and are decorated and furnished to a high standard. The staff from receptionists, to catering were all 'Very' polite and nice and went out of their way to make us welcome.
