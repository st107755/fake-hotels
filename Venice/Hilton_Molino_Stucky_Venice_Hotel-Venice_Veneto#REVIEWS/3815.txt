The good: The initial impression of the hotel was wonderful.  A quick and efficient check in, by a surprisingly delightful trainee, who was thorough and friendly.  The hotel entrance and lobby set expectations high.    The room was nice, although not terribly big.  The bathroom is quite large, especially relative to the room.  The side of the hotel facing out over the boat landing has an incredibly nice view.    There's an executive lounge for Hilton HHonors members which has a modest, but very nice, selection of snacks and breakfast foods.  The bad: The hotel has a free water shuttle that runs to two locations across the water, Zattere and San Marco.
