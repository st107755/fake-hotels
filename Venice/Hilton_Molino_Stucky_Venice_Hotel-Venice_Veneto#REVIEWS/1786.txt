We stayed for 3 nights as part of a post cruise stay.  I was a group leader and had 7 other rooms booked for my guests.  The Good--Away from the crowds on Venice Island.  Inexpensive hotel shuttle boat ran on time and was convenient to use.  Easy access from the cruise port.  The Not So Good -- Overpriced  Our Executive King Room with view was $500 per night.  One of my guests had a Standard King Room at $370. The rooms were identical except for the view.  Don't bother paying extra for "Executive" privileges, it's not worth the additional expense.  Breakfast buffet was extensive, but unfortunately all the hot foods were cold.  I thought perhaps it was because we arrived around 8 a.m., but on our last morning we ate at 7:10 a.m. and everything was cold then as well.
