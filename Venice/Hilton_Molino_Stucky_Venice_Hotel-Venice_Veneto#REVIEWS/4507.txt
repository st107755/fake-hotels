We had a 3 night break at this hotel early May. Upon arrival we received an upgrade to a suite, which was large, clean and had excellent facilities.  It didn’t have a view although this didn’t bother us as we were out most of the time exploring Venice.    As we arrived early the room wasn’t ready though - we had expected this and as such this didn’t pose any problems as we left our bags with the concierge.  The bags were delivered promptly to our room when it was ready.  Given that we didn’t have long to wait for our room we explored the area around by the hotel and were able to grab a quick drink nearby.
