Coming back to Venice for the Festa del Redentore we decided to stay at the Molino Stucky for the 4th time. As HHonors Diamond member, we were pleasantly surprised to be upgraded to a suite. Although we did not have the view on the canal, the room was fantastic with a king "heavenly" bed. Thank you to the cleaning ladies doing a fantastic job, the rooms are spotless.  The service at the reception was a bit slow but this is a very busy period and it was not a problem.  The Hilton breakfast is renowned to be excellent, but at the Molino Stucky, it is simply fantastic with all kinds of foods for pleasing anyone.  The Executive lounge is far too crowded with guests staying there for hours stuffing themselves as if there were no restaurants in Venice. Not very pleasant.
