We stayed here a night before our cruise and a night after our cruise.  My husband is extremely loyal to Hilton so there was no convincing him to stay elsewhere though I would have preferred closer to city center.  It was very easy to get to the cruise ship port (one stop on Alilaguna line).  The shuttle to the city center ran approximately every 30 minutes, but if full, you'd have to wait for the next one.  I didn't like that you had to pay for it regardless of whether you used though if you stayed there for multiple days,  you'd likely get your use out of it.  The rooms were nice and spacious.  Two hours at the spa was included for Hilton gold members and my mother and I took advantage of before boarding our cruise ship.
