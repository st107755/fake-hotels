This was our 5th trip to Venice and our first stay at the Molino Stucky. In the past we have stayed in Dosodoro, near the Rialto, or near St. Mark's, My wife and I much prefer Guidecca Molino Stucky for the end of day quietness away from the thousands of tourists ( yes, such as myself ) who pack the other areas of Venice. At times quiet is good in Venice. We also wanted a quiet spot to discuss with our Venicescapes private art/history guide our itinerary. We never had a problem with the hotel shuttle because we looked at the times and the locations when and where the shuttle would be. They were always, and I mean always, on time. Arrival to and from the airport is so, so simple and inexpensive if you have a vaparetto pass ( ACTV ).
