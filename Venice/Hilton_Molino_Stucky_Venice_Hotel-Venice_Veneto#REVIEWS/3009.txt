We stayed here for three nights in April 2013. We like staying in the Hiltons and the Molino Stukey is on Guidecca Island which was perfect for us. We wanted a good location that is still close to everything but also away from all the crowds. You need to get a taxi boat to get to Piazza San Marco but it's a free service as a Hilton guest and is only a 10 min ride. The taxis come regularly and the staff give you plenty of information on arrival with the times etc. We ate lunch in the hotel once when we arrived and it was ridiculously expensive, but I guess you are in Venice! We had complimentary drinks and snacks as Hilton honors members but generally we wanted to eat out so can't really comment on the food but it looked tasty enough.
