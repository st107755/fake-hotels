The hotel Molino Stucky is by far the most magical experience you can possibly ask for. My daughter and I were very weary after the 14 hour flight from lax. It was an easy walk to the private water taxi that whisked us away to our enchanted hotel. The staff were so welcoming and tended to our every need. We especially want to thank the exceptional bartenders at the rooftop bar. They are so cheerful and masters of the best cocktails we've had in Italy. They even brought out blankets to us at the terrace overlooking the grand canal. The complimentary private water taxi will take you to the Main Street in Venice as well as Saint Marks square. The hotel is located in giudecca serene and peaceful away from all the touristy crowd.
