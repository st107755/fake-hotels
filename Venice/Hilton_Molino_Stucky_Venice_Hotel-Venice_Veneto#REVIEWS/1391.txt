The rip off prices of the water taxis are renown and most of them should have the decency of flying the skull and crossbones but that was an unfortunate start to the trip.  I wish the hotel site was much clearer on how to get to the hotel like do not take a taxi and then have to transfer to a local water taxi you may as well book before and pay over 100 euros because that is what it will end up at least costing you.  Now as to the rest of the stay faultless the room was excellent with views over the canal and really light giving a fresh feel to the space.  The executive lounge was well run and made breakfast with a view.  The island does have a few restaurants and bars but remember to walk further in there are some hidden gems.
