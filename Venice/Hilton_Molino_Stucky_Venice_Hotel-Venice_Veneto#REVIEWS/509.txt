Recently stayed as a HH Diamond member, and received an upgrade from a Deluxe Room to Executive with View.   The view from the room was excellent, and the room was a very good size, clean and well kept.   Only concern with the room was the bathroom, which was shower over bath. Unfortunately this meant the shower space was quite small and uncomfortable, especially as the shower head was too low for me. This was a bit disappointing given the space of the room which meant a proper shower could have been included easily.  Turndown service was offered daily, and general service in the hotel was good.   Breakfast buffet in the restaurant was very good, however a cooked to order egg station would have been good.
