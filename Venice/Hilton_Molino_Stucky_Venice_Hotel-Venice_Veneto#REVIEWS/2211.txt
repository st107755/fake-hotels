As diamond HHonors we have stayed in Hiltons all over the world so Venice was a must.  David at front desk gave us a very welcome upgrade as we had a lot of luggage for our 4 month world trip.  The suite was nothing short of spectacular with water views to die for.  Watching the traffic going to and fro from our windows was mesmerising.  The hotel has a shuttle boat which transports you to and from San Marco at regular intervals. There is a very small fee for this which covers your whole stay.  We were catching a cruise ship out of Venice and found this location perfect with a short, 18 Euros water transfer directly to the cruise terminal. The hotel porter took our luggage from our room and on to the boat for us very efficiently and conveniently.
