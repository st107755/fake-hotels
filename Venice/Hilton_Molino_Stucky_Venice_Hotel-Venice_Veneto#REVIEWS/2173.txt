No doubt about it, the City of Venice can be crowded with tourists almost any time of year.  Originally, I had booked a different hotel, on the Grand Canal; photos of the rooms showed them to be rather small, but -- hey! it's Venice! -- they're all small.  Then, I took a look at the Hilton Molino Stucky.  It is located on Giudecca, a short 15 minute boat ride on Hilton's own shuttle from the hustle and bustle of Piazza San Marco (St. Mark's Square), and a short walk from the ACTV (water bus) stop.    Located in the former Stucky flour mill (Molino Stucky), it's clear that this Hilton was designed to save as much of the original factory's structure as possible.
