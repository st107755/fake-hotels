Normally one would expect to rate the hotel a 5 star given the great location away from the grounds of St Mark's Piazza, supported by a regular ferry service across to St Mark's, well appointed rooms with a very good buffet breakfast. However, there were some negatives for me during this visit:- 1. To get free wifi you had to join the Hilton Honors scheme which was just a hassle to work through added to check-in and receiving more unwanted emails. 2. Poor telephone service for the mobile phone. 3. Lack of hot water. Needed to get up early to make sure you had a hot shower. 4. Expensive to eat/ drink with a captive audience with two Grand Marniers at E32 (A$55), a club sandwich at E26 (A$45) and a can of coke at E8 (A$13).
