This place is a converted flour mill and pasta factory, not on the main island but the free hotel shuttle to Zattere (5mns across the water) and to St Mark's Square (a further 5-10mns) runs 2 or 3 times an hour and is excellent.  The hotel itself is huge, very well appointed and clean.  Rooms spacious and comfortable, although I hope (for the rest of you) that not all rooms have a toilet for the handicapped like ours did.  It's ideal if you need such a facility but when you don't, it's an uncomfortably high perch...  Initially we were a bit disappointed to find that, despite specifically booking a Kingsize Deluxe Plus room (the Plus bit means a view of the water), we were given a twin.  They changed it, but a little apology for their mistake would have been nice.
