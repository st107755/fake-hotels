This is not your typical Hilton but it is not a 5 star hotel. It cannot be compared to great hotels in Venice like The Gritty Palace. Though large, the Hilton has a great facade and yet it has been graciously updated with good but not great amenities. It is on Giudecca island which requires a shuttle to St. Mark's Square where all the action is in Venice. The shuttle is provided free by the hotel and is offered with generous frequency. At first, we thought this would be a negative but it is actually quite nice to be slightly separate from the tourism madness of Venice. The trip is short. Giudecca though has very little appeal with nothing to see though so you'll have to take the shuttle. Don't waste your time going anywhere but to Venice or the Murano glass factories.
