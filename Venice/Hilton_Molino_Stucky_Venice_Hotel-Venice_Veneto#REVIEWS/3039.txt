What a beautiful hotel! Was perfect location in that it wasn't right in the middle of the hustle and bustle of Venice but was on an island across the Guidecca Canal.  The hotel provided shuttle water bus service to and from two locations, Zattere and San Marco so we never needed to hire a taxi or use the city Vapporetto.  The shuttle ran until midnight so it gave us plenty of time to eat a late night dinner, gorge on gelato, and stroll around without the crazy crowds during the day.  Hotel staff was incredible and the concierge service outstanding.  Our room was a little noisy (7th floor) due to it being directly under the bar but we stayed out so late at night that by the time we hit the bed the bar was closing so it wasn't that big of an issue.
