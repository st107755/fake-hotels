Great Hotel with superb views of Venice.  All the front desk staff were very friendly and professional. I have to particularly thank Annimi Schifferegger, Gloria Bevilacque and Marko Ludovici for all their help to my wife and myself during our stay.  The hotel provides a complimentary water bus transfer from the hotel to Zattere and St. Marks Square, again brilliant service. On the roof was a lovely pool area with again marvelous views of Venice and Giudecca Island.   We are Hilton Gold members but unfortunately no upgrade! Fortunately we had already paid extra for an Executive room which gives access to the Lounge with a very good breakfast and evening hot snacks with drinks. The lounge is a little bit small.
