My girlfriend and I spent two nights at the Hilton Molino, the hotel has an amazing roof top pool/bar and stunning decor inside the communal areas. The first room we were given was a bit shabby looking, stains on the valence, scuff marks on the walls but still quite big in terms of European size rooms. We spoke to the desk manager and they were able to move us the next room was a lovely room with a great view, still feel there is a few issues that a keen eye spots walls had minor scuff marks, TV was too far from the bed, picture quality on the TV was not great. The one window had stunning views of the canal the other viewed onto a pile of bin bags really.
