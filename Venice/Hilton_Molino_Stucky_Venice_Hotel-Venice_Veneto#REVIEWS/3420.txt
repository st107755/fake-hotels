We live enough close to Venice not to need a hotel when we travel there. But I have been dreaming about staying at the Hilton since it opened some years ago. A business trip which required an evening in Venice plus enough HHonors Points allowed us to book a junior suite for the night. Oh my God. The hotel exceeded my highest expectation. The room was simply awesome. And the executive lounge in the lobby was very good too. We enjoyed some light snacks and morning breakfast there. The weather wasn't much nice so the pool terrace was closed. I wished my stay was much longer than a night!
