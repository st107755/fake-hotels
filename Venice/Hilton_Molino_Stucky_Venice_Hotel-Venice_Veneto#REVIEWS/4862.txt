Everything was great about the stay. It was absolutely beautiful to come up on it from the Grand Canal any time day or night. They have a free shuttle around every 20 minutes or so from 2 different locations which was so convenient. In the room, the bathroom was beautiful, the bed was SO comfortable, & the layout was great. The weird thing was we had a fuse box in the room which we thought nothing of...until the power blew out when we tried to plug in a phone... It almost shocked my boyfriend & had a really large spark. Pretty inconvenient since we couldn't charge our phones but I guess it's good the fuse box was right there?
