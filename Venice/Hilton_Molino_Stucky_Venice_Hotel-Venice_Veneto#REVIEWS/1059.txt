A very impressive achievement to have turned such a big old flour mill into this very classy and modern 5 star hotel. Very impressed with the lobby bar and lounge, the sky bar is lovely with great views over the islands and canal.    The staff were all very helpful, polite which is always a bonus. We arrived by private water taxi and our luggage was quickly taken off the boat and looked after, check in was swift.   My room was very clean, the bed amazing along with a big bathroom with double sinks and a separate shower unit. There was plenty of shower products and shampoo, and even the bar of soap was replaced twice. The TV was not used much but had BBC/Sky news channels and an excellent picture.
