We stayed here for 1 night at the end of a Mediterranean cruise, and loved it!  We found all the staff very helpful, the breakfast was wonderful and the room very comfortable.  Our only trouble was finding our way around the hotel!  It is a very large complex with several wings, so to get to the rooftop pool (which has an amazing view!) almost requires a compass or GPS.  However, that was part of the charm and meant you explored more of the hotel than you would otherwise.  Because the hotel is on its own little island, there is a free shuttle boat to and from the main Piazza in Venice.  This runs frequently and is a great service.  We also caught the airport shuttle from the hotel.
