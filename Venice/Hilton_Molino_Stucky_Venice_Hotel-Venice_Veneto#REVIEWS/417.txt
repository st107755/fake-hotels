My wife (a diamond member) and me (a gold member) stayed here for a Carnival. We got an upgrade, a free breakfast and an access to an executive lounge. The hotel is on an island cross the channel from the main Venice, and provides a frequent shuttle to St. Marco at a meager 7.50 euro per person for whole stay. It worked for us until it did not. On a Saturday night we tried to get to a last boat to a hotel but were denied  boarding as it was full. We did see a note that a capacity is controlled and were on a pier 15 minutes before a departure but there were a lot of people ahead of us and we did not get in.
