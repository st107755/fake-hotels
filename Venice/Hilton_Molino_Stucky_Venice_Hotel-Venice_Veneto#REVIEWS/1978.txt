Our family stayed at this hotel for 2 nights - 2 adults, 1 19 year old. Our room was spotless and had a beautiful view over the water. The rollaway bed for our daughter is the only reason for a less than excellent rating - it was seriously uncomfortable - and she had been on 2 others in other hotels to compare it to. The bathroom was very good - lots of counter space and towels with a very nice shower. The hotel is big - it has it's own docking area right out front with a hotel shuttle boat that has 2 stops over on the main island (the second stop is right near San Marco plaza). You need to arrive early for the boat during the busy morning and evening hours - otherwise you might not fit on.
