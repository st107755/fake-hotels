We were somewhat reluctant to stay at the Hilton Molino Stucky because Venice has so many "old world" charming hotels. In the end, we decided to divide our stay between two hotels and to try the Hilton for one night. We were not sorry.  Of course, this hotel is a Hilton and, therefore,  a bit "corporate" in feel. That also means that the rooms are very nice, the beds are great and the bathrooms are well appointed. The developers did a terrific job transforming an old mill into this large hotel.  The roof bar and pool have great views (our room  had a good view) and a drink on the roof bar is a wonderful idea.   Some people may think that the Guidecca location is a disadvantage since you must take a  shuttle or vaporetto to get to St Marks.
