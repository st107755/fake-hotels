The Hilton Venice is a great hotel. We booked a 3 night city break in febuary, and most of the facilities were available even though we were out of season. The hotel was very luxurious, and nicely furnished. After reading bad reviews of the hotel we were slightly concerned, but found it to be comfortable. The rooms are very nice, good furniture, large and clean. The views from some of the rooms are critised, and if you are wanting a water view you may be dissapointed with some of the rooms described as having a view. The hotel is located on a Island away a short boat ride from the main attractions and the building is impresive. There is a free shuttle boat provided by the hotel.
