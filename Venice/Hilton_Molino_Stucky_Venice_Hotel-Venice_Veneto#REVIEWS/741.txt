We booked a triple room at the Venice Hilton & it was fantastic.  The only problem we had was the ac did not get as cool as we like.  The breakfast is very pricey so be prepared to pay a lot!  It’s plentiful & looked delicious but we opted to leave on the hotel shuttle boat for San Marco square & ate at small cafes in Venice ALOT cheaper than our hotel.  The triple room was great & spacious & the bathroom unbelievably equipped for being able to clean each & every body part possible!  The decor top notch too.  The front desk was very helpful in all situations although their broken English was hard to understand.
