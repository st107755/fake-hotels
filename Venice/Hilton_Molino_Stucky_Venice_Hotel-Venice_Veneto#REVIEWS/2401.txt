The Hotel was quite splendid although not without some flaws. We had some electrical fitting problems in the room which were probably a little dangerous. We were told that we had been given a free upgrade to a better room but I am not certain what we were upgraded to as the room we were given looked the same as advertised on the website but I think with the addition of a mini bar. They were also a little mean with the coffee and milk on offer as there was only the option of decaffeinated coffee and 2 very small containers of milk. However they did replenish these on request. We chose to spend our days on the mainland of Venice and eat in the hotel in the evenings.
