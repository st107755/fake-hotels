We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky Venice Hotel as part of a post cruise extension.  The Hilton is a beautiful property and truly reflects the Hilton brand.  I would recommend getting a canal view upgrade and the executive lounge upgrade.  It cost us €100 extra per night, but it’s Venice, so don’t be cheap.  We made it back by a liberal use of the executive lounge.   The informal restaurant is fairly good, but pricey.  However it’s not any worse than any other upscale hotel in a tourist destination city.  The staff was very welcoming and took good care of us.  Room service was fast, efficient and the food was okay, but pricey.  The room was very well appointed and clean.
