On checking in we discovered that we had been allocated a very small double room.  The room had not been properly prepared as it seemed to have been allocated as a single room - one set of towels, no bath robes etc.  The front desk dealt with this first complaint very elegantly and moved us to another "up-graded" room.  This was about up to the standard we would have expected from a 5-star, however, there were many small problems that indicated a poor attention to detail.  The toilet was so close to the wall on one side that it was very difficult to sit comfortably.  When the lid was lifted it became impossible to access the toilet tissue as it was trapped behind the lid. The show, whilst nice had a very slippery marble floor which caused me to fall into the door.
