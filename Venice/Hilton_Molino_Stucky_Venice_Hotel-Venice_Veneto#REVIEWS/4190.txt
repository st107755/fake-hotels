This hotel has such charm and elegance. Lobby is decorated with all the local glass making results. So pretty and welcoming. Hilton Honors Executive Suite is right off the main lobby with access via room card. Well stocked all day but items only can be consumed right in this room; nothing allowed to be taken back to your room which was a little inconvenient.   Our room was so unique with very high ceilings crossed with dark wooden beams and a pretty chandelier. Nice touch of providing gratis a bottle of Bellini, delicious chocolates and mineral water right next to the bed. Huge window opened out overlooking onto their courtyard below and the other rooftops which make up this unique hotel. It is a former flour making factory from what I understand.
