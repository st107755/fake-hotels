On our arrival at Zattere vaporetto stop while waiting for the hotel shuttle boat we got our first proper view of the Hotel Molino Stucky. We had noticed it as we floated past on the vaporetto from the car park but had not realised it was a hotel. In fact my husband did wonder if it was a power station having two big chimneys! But the three days that we were at the hotel going back and forth on their very handy shuttle boat, we actually came to love this Deco/ warehouse style building.  Check in was good and we were shown to our Executive room. Unfortunately the room was not very nice, as soon as I entered it I noticed that it had an unplesant smell, the carpet was stained and some wallpaper was coming away from the wall under the window.
