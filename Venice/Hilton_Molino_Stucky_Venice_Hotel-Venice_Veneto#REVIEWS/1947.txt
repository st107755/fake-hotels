Absolutely gorgeous hotel.  Sits right on the water with amazing views.  If you are looking for a quiet, quaint hotel, this is not it. It's bustling and lively.  The waterway gets moving early in the morning.  But it was worth it for us.   The fitness center is okay, though a bit limited. 3 treadmills, one stepper, one elliptical, one regular and one recumbent bike.  They have 3 weights machines, one for chest, one for legs and one for back.  They also have a sit up bench. We did not go to the spa, though it seemed really nice!  Went went to the lobby restaurant for lunch and found the menu rather limited. We had breakfast in the breakfast restaurant and had the buffet. Did not enjoy the experience.
