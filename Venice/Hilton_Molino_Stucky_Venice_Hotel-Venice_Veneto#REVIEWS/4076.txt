Stayed here last week. It is beautiful from the outside, the public areas and the hallways. The check in was amazing and Tito is one of the best hotel representatives I have come across! However, the room was nondescript, had no character and had grime in the carpet by the wall. There were very few outlets and the pillows were so tired that as soon as you laid your head down, they flattened to the mattress. The hair dryer was no where to be found at first, but eventually found in the wardrobe vs the bathroom. The room did not have an iron, but instead we had to walk to an "ironing room" at the end of the hallway.   We booked the room for 3 people, but apart from the bed being made, the room was still only set up for 2.
