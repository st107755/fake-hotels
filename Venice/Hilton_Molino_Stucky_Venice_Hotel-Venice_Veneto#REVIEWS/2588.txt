A premium property that is spacious and superbly comfortable on the quiet island of Giudecca across from Venice.  Obviously the premium rooms are the ones facing the Canal from which you can see the busy boat traffic and the main island of Venice.  However, do not count out the courtyard view rooms because they are absolutely silent without any noise from the canal traffic intruding.  It's not that the canal side rooms are noisy but some engine noise does get in.  Good restaurants from breakfast to fine dining.  There are also good restaurants and a snack bar close by on the island.  I recommend Osteria ae Botti for a casual meal and La Palanca Snack for a superb light breakfast next to the canal at the Palanca Vaporetto stop.
