We stayed here while traveling on a tour of Italy.  The hotel was beautiful and our room was spacious.  The bathroom had plenty of counter space, a roomy stand up shower, and a tub.  The bed was very comfortable too.  We did not eat at the hotel restaurants though because the prices were outrageous. We were going to try the Nutella stand but it had closed early due to rain and never reopened.  While there definitely are more shops/restaurants near St. Mark's square, we enjoyed dinner a restaurant within walking distance one night, as well as pizza from another place.  We also found a grocery store within walking distance that sold bottles of wine and packs of beer for less than $3! We did not have any problems taking the hotel water shuttle.
