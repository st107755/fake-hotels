We stayed for three nights as a family with teenage daughter and had a small but good view of lagoon  room. The staff were very helpful and were happy to arrange a water taxi tour of the Grand Canal on our last day as well as recommend restaurants and places to go. The hotel shuttle water taxi was excellent value for 4.5 euros each for our whole stay and we made very good use of this.  The building itself is well restored &  historical, retaining some original features. Guidecca itself was a perfect place for us as it was away from the crowds of the main tourist area.  The skyline bar was excellent - great cocktails and nibbles and the view was fabulous. The restaurant was a bit overpriced as we had equally as good food at a local Guidecca trattoria for half the price.
