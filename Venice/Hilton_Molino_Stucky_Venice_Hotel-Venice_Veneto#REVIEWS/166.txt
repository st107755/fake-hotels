I have stopped at a few Hilton hotels but I must say this is my favourite so far, the way the architects kept a lot of the original features of the old building give an excellent blend of old and new. The standard of the room, decor and public areas was all beautifully built. The pool area is a really nice place to unwind if you have had enough of sight seen for the day, be warned the water is very cold. Staff all did their jobs to a very high standard in all areas of the hotel. It’s well worth the €8 each for the use of the Hilton boat it runs every few minutes to a couple of stops on the main island. Breakfast was very good with a good selection.
