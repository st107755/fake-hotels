This is a great hotel overall.  The staff was extremely helpful and courteous.  We arrived early in the morning from the USA, but there is a hotel baggage storage area where we were able to leave our bags while we enjoyed touring Venice and Burano.  There is a shuttle from the hotel to the main island of Venice, but it is a very slow boat and the timetable has certain periods of breaks throughout the day.  So be aware of the departure times and plan your return carefully.  The breakfast is a bit expensive, but has a vast selection from which to choose.  There is a rooftop bar and pool as shown on the hotel web site.
