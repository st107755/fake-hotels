Stayed just one night at this hotel before our cruise departure, the overall verdict was a lovely hotel situated on the island of Giudecca, we arrived late afternoon by water taxi from the airport which took around 30 minutes. The staff member who checked us in was very friendly and kindly upgraded our room being a Hilton HHonours member.  Lovely King Executive room (no view though!) nice comfy large bed with a quiet night’s sleep and good bathroom.  We dined at the Aromi Restaurant at the hotel, had a moderate two course meal for two with one glass of wine (a thimble amount) overall cost of the meal came to €118.00!  We were disappointed not to have seen the rooftop bar and view as it was closed during our stay in April.
