My wife and I stayed here four four nights as part of an anniversary trip.  We used points for the entire stay but were blown away by how well they treated us as Gold members.  We were upgraded to a high floor executive room with an amazing view.  The breakfast is absolutely incredible, and the rooftop bar (while extremely high priced) is worth a visit.  Every staff member we met was friendly.  The spa was excellent and my wife really loved her massage.  I was a little worried about the location because it isn't right next to all the action, but the convenient shuttle was easy and I actually really enjoyed the ten minute ride there and back.  It gave you a chance to get away from the main tourist crowds and to enjoy a great view from the water.
