We enjoyed our stay at the Hilton Molino Stucky on Guidecca Island in Venice.  We were a bit nervous about the booking as we had been concerned about ease of access to Venice Island and if we would be too remote.  Guidecca ended up being a perfect location for us!  Super easy to get to from the Airport.  The hotel has their own shuttle to Venice Island which runs every 20 minutes.  You can also catch the public water bus which is about a 3 minute walk from the hotel (Palanca stop).  We found the Hilton location to be in such a peaceful area - which was welcome after days with wall to wall tourists at the big sites during the day.
