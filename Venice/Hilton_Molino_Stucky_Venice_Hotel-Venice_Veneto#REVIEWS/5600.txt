Great Hotel - wonderful location on Giudeca across from the crowds but easily accessible.   The Hotel's shuttle is wonderful and gets you into a non crowded part of Venice near Zattere, although it also goes to the central tourist spot of St. Mark's Square, although less often.   The staff was very friendly.   The hotel is an oasis of quiet elegance.     The pool is awesome, great views of Venice.  Some nice restaurants within walking distance.   Concierge was great with recommendations.     Our bathroom did have a strange smell which did not bother me but my wife really found hard to deal with.   The hotel maintenance people came by but could not figure out what was wrong.     For some reason the hotel website does not tell you how to get there by the Vaporetto.
