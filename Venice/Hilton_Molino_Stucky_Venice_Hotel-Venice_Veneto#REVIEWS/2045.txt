Stayed here for our wedding anniversary. First time to Venice. Friends recommended this hotel and we wanted one with a pool as July is hot so a dip in the pool is needed!  Boat taxi over to Saint marks twice and hour which is great takes 15 mins. Rooftop pool is lovely but get up early and reserve a bed as they go fast!  One big double bed by the bar seemed to be the one everyone wanted .. We managed to get it once.  Breakfast room service was ok they messed up a bit but came back with the right food in the end.. If you order the American breakfast be sure to name every item you want even if you think it automatically comes with the meal.. It doesn't.
