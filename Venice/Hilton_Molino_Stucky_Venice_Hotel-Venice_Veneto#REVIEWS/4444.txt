Arrived the hotel via no 41 water taxi, alighting at Palanca. From there a mere 8 mins walk and crossing two bridges, we arrived at the hotel at 12.30pm. We were upgraded to a corner suite (diamond status), but the room was still being cleaned and we were ushered to the executive lounge while waiting. The reception told us that the room will be available in 1 hour's time but she promised that she will try to speed up in checking us in. To our surprise, we got our room within 20 mins. Kudos to reception.  The corner suite was huge with a king bed and separate sitting area. When we entered, a roll away bed was already in place as per our request. The bathroom was spacious and the whole suite has a luxurious feel to it.
