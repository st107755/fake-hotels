It's a Gorgeous looking building as you pull up outside on a water taxi. Stylish reception area with very helpful reception staff. We arrived early, too early to be given keys to our room, but we were checked in nevertheless and given access to the executive lounge until our room was ready.   Executive room with view:  Gorgeous room with lovely view across the water. Room was large, as was the bathroom and the huge comfy bed. TV channels in English language are limited to news channels, BUT you are not going to Venice to watch tv, right ?! Your bed is turned down each evening, with a little sweet left on your pillow, which is a nice touch. Slight negative is that the room had a slight smell of rotten egg each time you walked in, not major but slightly unpleasant..
