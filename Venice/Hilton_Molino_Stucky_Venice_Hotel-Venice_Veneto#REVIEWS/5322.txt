The Hilton Stucky looks out across the beautiful Venice skyline. This is a fantastic repurposing of a former industrial building in to a well maintained hotel with great facilities. The hotel is on the blue line directly from the airport so is very easy to get to without the need for a private water taxi. Once you've checked in you can make use of the free, regular shuttle boat from the hotel to St Mark's Square. It's a great place to stay as you can enjoy the lovely view of venice, get across the water easily but also escape the tourist crowds at the end of the day. Staff are friedn;y and helpful, the roof top bar is a great place to unwind, and the leisure facilites can also help you relax.
