I stayed at Molino Stucky in March 2013, and have been meaning to review it since but haven’t got around to it. But here it is at last!  I arranged the stay for me and my long-term girlfriend, as I wanted a romantic location where I could propose to her. Molino Stucky is clearly one of the nicer places to stay in Venice and I found a really good value room so snapped it up. The hotel is stunning, as was our room. Can’t say we used the facilities much as when not in our room we were out and about in Venice, though at a glance they all looked excellent.  The location is not an issue at all, the boats run regularly and I actually enjoyed the experience of crossing the water daily, it added to the Venice experience overall.
