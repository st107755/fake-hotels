We were in Venice for 3 days in May - our first trip. We were enchanted by Venice, and the Hilton Stucky Molina added to the romance and luxury. It's bright, clean, and just grand to look at!  The front desk personnel were so helpful and sweet.  When we told them that it was our second honeymoon and 25th anniversary, they sent up champagne and chocolate-covered strawberries- such a lovely touch. They also recommended a nearby restaurant, Il Cacciatore, and we loved it and went back twice. The room was very large, with bathrobes, slippers, and a big bathroom. As with many European hotels, the walls are thin and we could hear people talking at all hours of the night, but at least the corridors and rooms were carpeted, so we didn't hear them walking around also.
