The Molino Stucky is on Giudecca island with views towards the main Venice Island which is perfect to get away from the crowds in St Marks Square. The shuttle boat is good and free! The hotel is the largest in Venice and feels spacious. The rooms are luxurious with high quality wood and marble. Views across the Giudecca canal are the best for atmosphere. There are some good restaurants, especially liked the Bacaromi traditional cuisine.outside the hotel near Palanca area there are good local restaurants. The executive lounge in the hotel does not offer a good food selection for such a prestigious Hilton hotel, service in the evening is from 5  to 7 pm only so definitely not similar to other major European Hilton Hotels.
