Huge hotel with a feel of open, vibrant and relaxing environment. The service in this hotel is fantastic and the staff are welcoming and friendly.  The room was plush with everything that you would need for a short stay or business trip.  The I only had breakfast in the hotel but plenty of choice for everyone from tots to the older generation.  Plenty of staff on hand to deal with any requests swiftly and efficiently.   Taxi boats were expensive but water buses were inexpensive and frequent.   The sky lounge bar was a nice place to relax for a drink and a chat with some great views. It was €15 for a G&T, but then again you are in Venice.   The only downside was that the swimming pool was shut as it was out of season. It was 20+ degrees and could/should have been opened.
