We love Hiltons--they are generally very reliable in terms of cleanliness, service, room amenities, etc. We spent one night in this hotel, which is beautiful. We are Gold HHonors members, so we were upgraded to a suite, which should have been nice considering this hotel is very expensive. The two room suite was well appointed, but faced a brick wall so...no Venice view at all! In the bathroom, there were no washclothes and the makeup mirror was broken and falling off the wall. Room service was fine, though.
