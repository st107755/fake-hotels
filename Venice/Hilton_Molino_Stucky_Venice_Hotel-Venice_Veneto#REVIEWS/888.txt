We stayed at the Hilton on two rewards nights vouchers, so our stay was free and was our only option. I'd looked at this hotel for a previous stay in Venice, but I wasn't keen on it being on another island - it turns out I was wrong! It being away from the chaos but with a magnificent canal view made it all the better for us.  It being a Hilton means the rooms are excellent (big, comfy, spotless). We were upgraded as I had Diamond status, giving us access to the executive lounge too. This was different to other Hiltons though - it was a sectioned-off part of the main bar area (which was fine, just different). The building is fantastic and very interesting for a wander.  The island itself is great.
