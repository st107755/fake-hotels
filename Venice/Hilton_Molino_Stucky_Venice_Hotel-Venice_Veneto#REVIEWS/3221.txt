Have just returned from a long weekend in Venice, staying at the Hilton - in one word, brilliant! We probably chose the worst weekend to experience Venice, arriving in snow, which developed into hail, rain and very strong winds, but there is nothing one can do about the weather! We caught the water taxi from the airport which takes about an hour and twenty minutes to the hotel, priced at twenty seven euro for a return ticket it was excellent value. The staff on reception when we arrived were very friendly, efficient and spoke excellent English. We had booked two rooms on the executive floor and were upgraded to a junior suite and a larger bedroom which had an amazing view of the canal. Breakfast was delicious and certainly set us up for the day!
