Having stayed at the Molino Stucky for several days, I am of the opinion that there is much truth in both the good and the bad review on this site. This is a huge hotel and there are always queues for reception and the concierge.  Both are pleasant enough when you get to speak to them, but  you really do need a reason to stand in line for five or ten minutes. Our check-in was efficient; however there was an error on the bill at checkout which took for ever to resolve (a good job we had left plenty of time to catch the airport shuttle) and throughout I felt as though I was being accused of lying about us not having ordered or received the service charged - and there was no signature to back up the dispute.
