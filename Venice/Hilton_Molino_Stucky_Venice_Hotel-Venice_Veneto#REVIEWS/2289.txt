We stayed at this hotel over Easter based on the reviews on TA and to be fair the hotel is very nice, it's a great historic building but it has a few negatives which spoilt the stay for us.  As nice as the hotel is it is just in the wrong place and having to plan ahead at both ends for the boats took away a lot of spontaneity and did nothing to help us relax. The time between boats is around 40 mins so if you can't get on one (they are busy) you're stuck for 40 mins or have to get the water bus, which gets expensive for a family and should not be necessary in what is an already expensive hotel.   Which brings me on to my next gripe, wow everything is so expensive, 18 euros for a cocktail is bad enough but for me 8 euros for a small cup of coffee is ridiculous.
