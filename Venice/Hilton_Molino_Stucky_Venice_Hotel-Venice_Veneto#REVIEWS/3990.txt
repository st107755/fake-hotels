Our first visit to Venice, for two adults and two teenage boys, and the trip proved to be a truly memorable experience thanks not only to the stunning beauty of the city and its magical Carnivale atmosphere but also the comfort, attentive staff and good facilities at this hotel. Any worries about not staying in the heart of the city were dispelled by the fun of travelling by the free and frequent hotel shuttle, taking in the extraordinary sights along the way. It was also great to get away from the hustle and bustle and enjoy the calm of the hotel's surroundings. One of the Molino Stucky's many highlights is the excellent breakfast buffet - a great way to start the day.
