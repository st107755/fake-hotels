We found this fantastic hotel after a bad experience at Palazzo Guardia. Palazzo Guardia had turned the air conditioning off and our room was boiling hot until about 2am. They wouldn't even come and check the room to see what was wrong and refused to refund us for the next two nights we had paid for. The final straw was when they had an industrial concrete cutter operating indoors just below our room. Their response was it is day time so what's your problem.  We double checked with Hilton about their air conditioning (which worked perfectly) and booked in. The hotel was fantastic. It is a short boat ride from the mainland and the boat seems to run about 18 hours a day. The breakfast Buffet was very nice with a good mix of Italian and American food.
