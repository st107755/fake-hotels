This hotel was the best of both worlds. Being on Giudecca, you are close enough to all the activity in St. Mark's but without all of the noise of the tourists.  It is about a 15 minute boat ride via the shuttle to get to St. Mark's Square. I noticed there was some confusion about the price for the shuttle on other forums, but it is 6 Euro per person for the entire stay to use it. Well worth the price.  Hotel itself is beautiful, We splurged for a room with a view and we were not disappointed, even if you don't get a room with a view, make sure to head up to the roof top pool or the bar and have a look. Rooms were large, unlike most European hotels.
