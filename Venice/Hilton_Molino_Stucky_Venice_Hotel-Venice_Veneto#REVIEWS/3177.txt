It was our first time staying in this landmark in Venice. We arrived by boat in front of the hotel and our bags were promptly taken to the reception. As Diamond members we got a beautiful Executive Suite with Canal View including access to the Lounge of the ground floor.  Our room was beautiful and very elegant. We had stunning views of the Canal and Venice.   Breakfast was very extensive but the restaurant was often full. Service could improve a lot when times are busy. The concierges are very helpful and provide excellent assistance.  We had a couple of issues during our stay. The internet did not work at all during a transition between two operators we had two days without any Wifi access.
