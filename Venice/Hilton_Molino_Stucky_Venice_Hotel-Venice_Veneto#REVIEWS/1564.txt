Before you read my review, a few caveats. We were booked into this hotel at the last minute as our flight back home had been cancelled and the hotel was for the most part fully booked.  Our arrival and initial greeting were ok, although there didn't seem to be many staff to deal with all the guests checking in or out.  On our last day went to check out only to be told we'd signed for a drink in the bar which we hadn't and looking at the signature it was nothing like the one I'd provided at check in. Somewhat reluctantly (I felt) it was agreed I'd not be charged, but was left feeling like I was the one in the wrong. This is a shame as two other staff members were very helpful.
