First off getting to the hotel is actually very easy! After leaving the airport you take a left and walk for 7 or so minutes until you reach the jetty. Here you can take the public water bus straight to the front of the the hotel (Molino Stucky). This takes about 1.5hours and costs €27 for a return ticket, can be bought from inside the airport or the counter beside the jetty.   The hotel also offers a free shuttle boat from the hilton to zatterre and to san marco. This is really useful for hopping across to the areas with the main tourist attractions. On our second day here the 9am boat was full so the hotel put a small group of us into a private taxi (free of charge) and took us to where we wanted to go!
