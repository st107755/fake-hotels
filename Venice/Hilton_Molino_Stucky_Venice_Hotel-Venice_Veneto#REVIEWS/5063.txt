The Hilton Molino Stucky Venice remains magical--the service is first-rate and the rooftop pool with its view of Venice makes the hotel worth the price.  The location is quiet and relaxed, but the hotel's free water shuttle takes you to San Marco in 5 minutes, so you have the best of both worlds--a peaceful and relaxed retreat, and Venice at your feet!  The breakfast buffet is not to be believed--fresh squeezed juces, every bread, meat, cheese, fruit, and pastry imaginable, and excellent coffee.  Our room was spotlessly clean and comfortable, the bathroom was modern and pristine, and the canal view from the room was pleasant.  Oh, the hotel is next door to the famous Fortuny headquarters--we keep forgetting to go in, but will the next time we visit.
