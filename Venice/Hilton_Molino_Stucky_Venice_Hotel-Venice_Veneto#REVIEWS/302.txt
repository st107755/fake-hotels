In terms of organisation, convenience, staff and facilities the Hilton Molino Stucky was a brilliant discovery and as an Hotel in Venice, I cannot recommend it highly enough  I asked Google to find me a Hotel in Venice with a swimming pool. I phoned the Hilton in Stucky that evening and spoke to a charming Italian who took me through the cost of the room. I then asked him if he could help with transportation from Venice Airport. Not only could he help but I was given a number of options. I chose the basic meet and greet  launch service which collected us from the Airport, took us by taxi to the pier and then by private launch across the waters directly to the Hilton. It was a brilliant service, costing little more than our taxi from Home to Heathrow.
