First of all the outside of the hotel is beutiful. But the hotel is on Guidecca Island so it is hard to get back and forth from Venice. They do have a free water bus be it on comes ever hour and fifteen minutes which in the hot sun in august was a while for a ginger. The lobby is very nice and so was the front desk staff. It was very expensive for every thing. But the complementary breakfast was very nice. I ask one on the maids a question in spanish (which Italians usally understand) and she ran away laughing.
