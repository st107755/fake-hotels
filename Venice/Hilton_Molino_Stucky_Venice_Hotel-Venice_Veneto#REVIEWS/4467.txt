Hilton's investment in Venice is in an old mill on Giudecca island with beautiful views of San Marco. The wood-beamed ceilings, Italian glass chandeliers, and comfortable guest rooms with windows that open to complete the Venice experience, all create an exceptional experience. It would be hard to believe that there are rooftop pools elsewhere in the world with views like this one. The Skye Bar and restaurant is a popular spot to see the sunset that creates the beautiful Venice glow. The hotel has its own water taxi to transport you to San Marco -- if you want to deal with those crowds. Giudecca has its own canal walk with small cafes and shops. We had a delicious meal in the Bacaromi restaurant -- risotto with grilled shrimps and asparagus and tagliatelle with braised lamb.
