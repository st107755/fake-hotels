We stayed at this beautiful hotel for 2 nights. Unlike a previous reviewer I must say we as HH Diamonds were treated very well - welcome amenities in room, water, complimentary use of the spa and a fabulous room upgrade. The views and location are sensational and we were absolutely delighted to have chosen this hotel and Venice for our break. Check in was efficient and staff very helpful. Breakfast was good in the restaurant (wide choice). I agree with other reviewers that the executive lounge is quite limited in terms of size and selection of drinks and snacks, but again lovely views.   After a morning at the gorgeous pool on day 2, I returned to the room for a shower before we would head in the hotel's water taxi to St Mark's square. My husband remained at the pool.
