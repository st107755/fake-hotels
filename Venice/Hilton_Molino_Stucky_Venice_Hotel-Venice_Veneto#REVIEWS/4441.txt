I recently stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky with my family on a side trip to Venice.  I used Hilton points to do so.  We stayed in Marriott's throughout our trip to Europe but this hotel, was by far, the best hotel we stayed in. It's location is exceptional for the views of Venice.  The Pros: - Large, spacious rooms (I had 2 rooms for my family), that are steeped in luxury. - Helpful and friendly staff - Amazing rooftop infinity pool that overlooks Venice - Beautiful views of Venice - Amenities in the room are a step up from most hotels. - Convenient and free water shuttle from hotel to San Marcos square.  The Cons: - Expensive water taxis to and from the hotel.
