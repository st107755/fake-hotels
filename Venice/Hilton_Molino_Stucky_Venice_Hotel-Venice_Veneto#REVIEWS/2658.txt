My husband and I booked this hotel based on the fact that it worked well with our points advantage. We weren't sure if staying off the main island would be a plus and were very surprised to find that it was. I looked forward to coming back to the hotel every night. It was clean and inviting, with staff being friendly and willing to help. The interior is well done with some modern art accenting a renovated traditional building.  The room was good, not exceptional, but I wasn't expecting amazing with 4 stars. It definitely isn't 5 stars. But it was clean and well appointed. Some travelers didn't have the same experience and I think from reading through he reviews maybe Hilton needs to improve their maintenance but we did not have any issues.
