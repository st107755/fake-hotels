I stayed at this hotel for one night only on a business trip to Venice.  Upon arriving at the hotel I was pleasantly surprised as it is in an old industrial building that was once a mill. The staff were very helpful and we were checked in very efficiently. Once I got to my room I was impressed until I looked out of the window where all I could see was a fire escape. For the price we paid I would have expected a view towards Venice!  The following morning we had to get up very early to get some shots of a cruise ship arriving and we had decided to shower and breakfast after the shoot. The shower only afforded cold water, however long I let it run for. Not a great experience.
