A massive, un impressive complex that feels like a Dysneyworld hotel. Rooms are like any other business hotel so you could be anywhere in the world. Poor service and very bad breakfast in a noisy/ shabby room with mediocre food complete the picture. Do not hope for anything better if you have access to the Executive Lounge: tiny, overcrowded, with barely anything worth eating/drinking. How did this place got 5star rating is a Mistery in our view. There are dozens of better  and more authentic hotels in Venice = do not waste your money at the Hilton.
