This historic building/location has been transformed into a modern masterpiece. Unlike most of the hotels in the main part of Venice, the Hilton is large, the rooms spacious, and the atmosphere contemporary. They offer all the amenities you could ask for, the breakfast is a gorgeous feast and the staff are all impeccable.  It's quiet and there's very little foot traffic, other than Hilton guests and the locals. Several restaurants nearby, as well as a nice little bakery/shop just three bridges away. When you are ready to venture across to the "real" Venice, there is a boat at your service, on a regular schedule.
