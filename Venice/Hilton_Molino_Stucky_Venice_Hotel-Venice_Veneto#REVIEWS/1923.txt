The hotel is situated on the island of La Giudecca just a 15min water bus from St Marks Square. The hotel's courtesy water bus is very good value and automatically added to your bill (€4.50 per adult per stay). It's frequency meant you could loosely plan your day before returning at some point in the afternoon for a well earned swim in the Hilton's 8th floor open air pool, which has exceptional views across the city. In the evenings we took a courtesy bus back across the water to eat in one of Venice's many restaurants. Venice is rammed during August therefore the Hilton's location provides that feeling that you have somewhat escaped from the crowds.
