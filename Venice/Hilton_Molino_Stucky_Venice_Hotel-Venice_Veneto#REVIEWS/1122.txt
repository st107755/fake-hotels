We visited Venice for the first time in early August 2017. My Diamond status got us a very nice, large upgraded room.  The property was very well maintained and the employees were very courteous during our entire stay.   The rooftop pool is awesome and provides an incredible view of the lagoon and the city. The pool lounge chairs were very comfortable and included adjustable face sunscreens. (Best sun lounge chairs I've ever used). The other outdoor "club" chairs    Need to be replaced. They were very uncomfortable. The pads were so thin, you could feel the metal support bars underneath.   The hotel has an awesome rooftop bar that has great sunset views. Drink prices very very high but not surprising.   The executive lounge offered a respectable breakfast.
