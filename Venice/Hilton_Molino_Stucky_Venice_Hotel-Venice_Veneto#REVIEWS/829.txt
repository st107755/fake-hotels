It was my first time in Venice and have read so many reviews not to book this hotel of you are a first timer as it is away from all the action. I’m glad I decided to stay here as after going to 4 different cities I chose Venice as my favorite city as it wasn’t as chaotic as the other 3 where we stayed at hotels right in the center of the city.     We took the Alilaguna blue line from the airport where it drops you off right at the front of the hotel. It was a long trip but it made me feel like I got a free tour of the islands.  They have their own water taxi which currently runs every 40 mins (i guess their winter schedule.) if your schedule is really tight, that might be a problem but we didn’t mind as we like the laidback feel of being away from the main island.
