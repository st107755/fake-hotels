I stayed at this hotel as a part of my Adventures by Disney tour of Italy. This hotel was beautifully decorated and the staff was very nice! The rooms were very spacious and the courtesy boat to cross the canal was convenient. My friends and I loved going to the Nutella bar and enjoyed the views of the entire city from the pool deck. There was  a large selection of foods at the breakfast buffet. However, the problem I had with this hotel was that the walls were paper thin and I was able to clearly hear my next door neighbor's entire conversation and TV almost as if I was in the same room.
