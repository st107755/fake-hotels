When you think of Venice, the thought of staying in a Hilton hotel is not the first thing that comes to mind. But, this Hilton is truly Venetian, surrounded by water and convenient to most destinations. The Hilton has its own water shuttle to San Marco,  has a dock for private water taxis and is close to two stops for city water taxis that can get you just about anywhere, including the airport and other parts of this beautiful city.   The Molino Stucky Venice Hotel is very large and has conference facilities for meetings. It is in a converted flour factory and is in a quiet and charming part of Venice, the island of Guidecca. Steps away from the hotel are a Cipriani Dolce location, a family-owned bakery and the famous Fortuny shop for luxurious Venetian fabrics.
