Hilton Molino Stucky exceeded all my expectations. I was a bit worried about reaching Venice, as the hotel is in Giudecca, in front of the Island, but the shuttle boat every hour works like clockwork, it takes you to Zettera in 5 minutes or San Marco in 15. Considering I got a good deal, it was a pleasure to stay at a modern hotel, with all possible comforts, instead of staying in the island itself, in old hotels. The room was very confortable, the bathroom was exceptional, the bed, linens and pillows were excellent... We did not have the view to the Island, but the view to a channel and some gardens was very pleasing. We only ate at the hotel's Nutella Piazza, excellent crepe. The concierge recommended a nearby restaurant in Giudecca, good value and food.
