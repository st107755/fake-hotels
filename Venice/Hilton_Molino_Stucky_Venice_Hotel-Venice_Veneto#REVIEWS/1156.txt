Just back from a long weekend at this fantastic hotel.   Best things- Brilliant exec bedroom with lounge area, great bed and very spacious. Worth the extra and also gives access to executive lounge next to the dining/ bar area for breakfast, nibbles etc. Check in staff very engaging as were all the staff we met. Shuttle boat over to main St Marks costs 6 Euros per head for all of your stay. Runs all day long every 30 mins or so. Great value and adds to the experience of your trip. Breakfast is up the the usual Hilton standard which is VG, dinner however is fantastic. Hardly surprising that the pasta dishes are out of this world. Rooftop pool looked very nice but a little small so quite busy. Rooftop bar is a must see, awesome cocktails and views to die for.
