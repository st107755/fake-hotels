We stayed in this hotel in the first week of May 2014. Overall it was a very pleasant stay, subject to couple disappointing moments described in paragraphs 5 and 6 of my review.   The hotel building is stunning. We had a room with the view and it was breathtaking. I felt like a movie star or a princess getting up every morning and opening the curtains.   We had no problems with the shuttle boat. It was convenient and we always had space for us. Although I need to mention that we did spent a lot of time in bed and we only went out at 1 or 2 pm (off peak hours).  We had an access to the business lounge where free drinks and some nibbles were served every early evening. It was also possible to use the wifi there (that was not available at our room).
