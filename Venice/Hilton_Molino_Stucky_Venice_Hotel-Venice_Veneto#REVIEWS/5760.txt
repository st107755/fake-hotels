Just returned from two nights at the Hilton Molino Stucky with my sister. Overall the hotel is very impressive. The building is spectacular and is beautifully decorated. Our room - an executive twin room - was also lovely and well appointed. We enjoyed the hotel's pool although we felt it's cleanliness left something to be desired as the water looked cloudy and there was lots of mould/algae around the sides.  We also very much enjoyed the free shuttle service to Zattere and St Mark's Square which was never overcrowded as previous reviews have stated and was always on time.   With our room, came access to the executive lounge - this was a great perk and we visited the lounge on several occasions to take advantage of this.
