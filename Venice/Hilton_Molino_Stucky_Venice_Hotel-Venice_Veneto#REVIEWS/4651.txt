The hotel is on its own island. There is frequent shuttle boat. Very reliable, very easy. From, train station, water taxi comes in 20 minutes to hotel. Location was great. Room has no view but very roomy. Oberpriced Aqua Panna water bottles little bit annoying. I think in a suite, Hilton can provide some water bottles. When we arrived, there was no one to carry the luggage. Check-in by a Russian front desk staff was little bit mechanical but efficient. Hilton Honors lounge is at the lobby level. Long and narrow and you can have access with your room key. I found the staff, little bit pretentious and funny enough not so well informed. (not knowing what is a bloody Mary!) However, they dared to warn my wife not to put his leg up on an ottoman.
