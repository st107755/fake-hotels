It’s a great hotel in a great location but the service is not great.   As a Diamond member I was very disappointed that the hotel refused to recognise this because I had used air-miles to make the booking. It also seemed like they were less helpful because I had used air-miles.   The location is perfect because it is close enough to the St Marco (by shuttle boat) but also away from the crazy crowds so it’s a little more relaxing.   The roof-top pool was good, as was the roof top bar and the building is clean and extremely well presented.   Food at breakfast was generally good and well presented although I was disappointed that the Fresh bread on Saturday morning was then re-served as “Fresh” on Sunday. It clearly wasn’t fresh.
