I booked this hotel using points and upon check in was upgraded to a beautiful suite.  The room was well appointed with a large bathroom, (tub and a walk in shower) the living room was a nice place to sit and have coffee in the morning  and the bedroom was very large with a large king bed (probably a California king).  Nice big screen TV - but they could due with an update to the channel selection.  CNN was about the only thing I could watch. The 8th floor is where the pool is located which was great to lay out and relax.  Also the 8th floor bar was awesome.  Great views of Venice.  We sat up there every evening having drinks and just watching the water traffic on the channel.
