We just returned on Oct 15 from our second trip to Venice and our first stay at the Hilton Molino Stucky.   It was a true pleasure,  the Hotel is located across the canal and provides a shuttle to and from San Marco which was very convenient.  We had read previous reviews concerning being able to hear voices from ajoining rooms but did not find this to be a problem at all. I would suggest that you include the Breakfast buffet  when booking as the choices were great and more affordable than purchasing seperately.  I would also recommend the Executive lounge as this was a great place to unwind with a cocktail and refreshments. The rooms were a nice size for Europe and very clean.
