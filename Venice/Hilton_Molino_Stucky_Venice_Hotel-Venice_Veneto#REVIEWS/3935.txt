Hotel is on Giudecca Island which is about a 10-15 mins. barge ride from St. Mark's Square across the canal. Shuttle boat rides are frequently and free of charge provided by the hotel. Water taxis (vaporetto's) are also nearby. To me this is preferred to escape the at times insane hustle and bustle of the 'main island' after a day of sightseeing. The hotel offers consistent quality throughout with little 'surprises' in a historical setting of an old mill. Many of the main island hotels can be hit and miss in terms of room quality, accessibility (especially coming with suitcases, etc.) and many do not have elevators.  The Hilton has a fantastic rooftop bar with great views and the ideal spot for a sundowner.
