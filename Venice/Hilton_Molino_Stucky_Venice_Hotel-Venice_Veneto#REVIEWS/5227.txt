Outstanding property with amazing location, imposing architecture and grand galleries and rooms.   This was the last leg of our Italy trip. We were given a free upgrade to a canal view room at the reception and the hotel chef sent special chocolate / strawberry platters for us. The rooms were outstanding - grand and royal. The finish is very palatial.  The food is great !!! The breakfast spread is pretty good and would suit people from all countries - The omelet was great and so was the hot chocolate and the lunch time risotto :)  The Alilaguna cab service from the hotel jetty to Zattere and St Marks is very convenient and runs every half till midnight so it is very convenient to travel to the city. The reception staff are well informed about the good places in Venice city.
