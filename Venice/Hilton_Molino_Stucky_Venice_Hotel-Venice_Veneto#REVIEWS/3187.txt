Stayed here in February Half term with wife and 14 year old son, the hotel is converted from a old flour mill and may not have the architecture of some of the older hotels in venice, but is well designed. We stayed in a family suite which was very god, with separate living room area, with sofa bed for my son. The sofa bed wasn't the most comfortable, but the main bed was very comfortable.  Staff in the hotel were very friendly and service generally was excellent. Bar prices were a bit expensive, but acceptable.  the view from the Skyline rooftop bar was superb, and a drink in the bar early evening is recommended.
