We stayed at the Hilton Molina Stuckey Hotel (an old converted flour mill) for the night before our cruise out of Venice. We landed at the Venice Airport and took a shuttle bus from the airport to the port and a water taxi from the port to the Hotel. This took only about 30 minutes total versus about an hour strictly by water taxi from the airport. Upon arrival at the hotel our room was not available (2:00 pm check-in) but as a Hilton Honor member we were given a pass to the Executive Lounge and told to check back in an hour. We were upgraded to a suite with a terrific view facing the Giudecca Canal and the room had a large bedroom, bathroom with a bath tub and separate shower and a living room with a dining table.
