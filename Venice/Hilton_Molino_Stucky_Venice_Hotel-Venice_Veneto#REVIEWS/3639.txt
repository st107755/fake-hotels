Stayed at this wonderful hotel for 2 days as part of our Europe trip to Paris, Venice and Rome. The location is breathtaking and our room had amazing views of Venice canals and skyline. The cruise ship ports were also near the hotel, we could see some sail past our window!! The hotel had great amenities like the spa and roof top bar. Breakfast was delicious and filling. The best part was that  the hotel provided free  and frequent shuttle service to and from Venice proper which was was about 10 mins away, yet was away from the hustle and bustle of crowded tourist places. Rooms were large and comfy. The hotel building used to be an old flour mill so they kept some parts of the building intact, which gave the hotel a rustic old world charm.
