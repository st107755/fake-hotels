Reading some of the reviews, I was slightly concerned about the location and service before going there. But after my three-night stay from July 3-6, I thought I made the right decision staying there. Here are my two cents:  Location:  This hotel is away from the central area such as St. Marco and Rialto. However, the hotel offers frequent free shuttle boat from early morning until midnight (a bit after midnight on weekends) and it takes 15 minutes to San Marco. The transfer service is efficient and reliable. While you can't walk to the popular spots, you get the terrific review from many of the rooms and rooftop pool and enjoy quietness.
