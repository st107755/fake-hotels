This hotel is very quiet as it away from Venice itself, but with spectacular views across to Venice. A courtesy hotel boat shuttles guests back and forth to Venice all day and evening for free, the trip takes 5 minutes to Zattere and about 10/15 minutes to St Marks. The rooms were clean, but we had no view at all, just a brick wall of a small courtyard which was disappointing. Breakfast was really excellent, just about everything you can imagine both hot & cold. We had free wifi and access to a premium lounge with free soft drinks & snacks throughout the day, you could have breakfast in there too but the choice is very limited compared to the main restaurant.  The service and the staff were very friendly which you don't always get in Venetian hotels.
