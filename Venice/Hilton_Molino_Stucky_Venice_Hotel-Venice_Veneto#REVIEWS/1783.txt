You will take a ferry or water taxi to get anywhere if interest in Venice and we did not find the island of Giudecca itself to have much to recommend it.  On the plus side, the hotel is well maintained, clean, the rooms are spacious and comfortable but not luxurious.  We joked every time we came back to the hotel that it was like returning to the United States because everything about the hotel is very American except for the staff, including the size and layout of the hotel, the portions of food served at the restaurant.  One drawback: they advertise multiple restaurants being open but only one restaurant appeared to be open.  The hotel feels very quiet and almost empty even when we stayed there and we were told it was at 99% capacity!
