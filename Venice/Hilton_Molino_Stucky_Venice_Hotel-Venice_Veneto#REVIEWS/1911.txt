Sadly just came home from our fantastic holiday here.  We booked our holiday through British Airways and added the "executive" package... It's a must!  You get access to the executive lounge, this is where you can have breakfast (you can have breakfast in the main restaurant too) snacks, drinks throughout the day. They also bring out alcohol in the evenings. You also get included an executive room- King Hilton Executive- which was incredible. The rest of the hotel is lovely, the pool is amazing but very expensive if you want a drink for example a bottle of water was €5 (especially if your a executive customers and you get soft drink for free downstairs).
