We had an awful room that had two large windows. One window literally looked out on to a brick wall while the other window faced a courtyard that had very bright lights that shone into our room. No blackout curtains were provided. The heat was unbearable in our room and there did not seem to be any temperature control.  When we were greeted by staff they failed to tell us about any of the amenities of the hotel. The shuttle the hotel runs does not really run that often.  The items at the breakfast buffet were reheated and not tasty. The only tasty item was a made to order omelet. I am baffled by all the positive reviews about this hotel. It was actually quite run down with threadbare carpets near our room. We booked this hotel through Viking and it is supposed to be 5 star.
