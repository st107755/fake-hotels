My boyfriend and I spent 2 nights at the Hilton Molino Stucky; March 17-18.  The hotel is a series of interconnected buildings.  We stayed in the Foscari building, room number 545.    ROOM: We found the room very large for Italian standards, as expected given a US hotel chain.  We had a large king size bed, desk, 2 chairs (one for the desk), large night stands, a sitting table, and several lamps.  The room was still rather dark when we had all the lights on in the room.  There was a wardrobe with a safe in the separate entry hall, I liked having the entry hall as we could take off our shoes and coats before entering our room.  The carpet seemed a bit warn and could use a cleaning or replacement.  The bed was the most comfortable bed we used during our 11 day trip.
