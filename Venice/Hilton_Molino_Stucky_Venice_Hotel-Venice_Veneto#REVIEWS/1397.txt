We arrived in Venice by train and arrived at hotel in style by river taxi (beware of porters carrying your luggage 30 metres to taxi rank - €10 tip was not enough!) as the hotel is across the Guidecca Canal away from the main throng of the 'main' Venice island.  The hotel, a converted mill I believe, has been beautifully converted but has an unassuming reception area. Staff spoke excellent English which was handy as my Italian is not great! We stayed here for three nights. The room on 1st floor was clean, quiet but rather dark. No amount of switching on lights helped. Weird! The bed was firm and comfortable, the room well equipped and had free wifi. You would need to ask for ironing equipment should you require it.
