My corporate travel department booked this hotel for me for a recent trip to Venice.  Being a Hilton brand, I knew it would be a nice hotel - however, I had no idea it would be so absolutely incredible.   The building itself is amazing - it's an old renovated flour mill that has been creatively restored and transformed into this gorgeous modern hotel.   So, as you're walking through the hotel, parts are very luxurious and modern, and other parts feel like a medieval Venetian fortress.   Hilton has done a wonderful job of maintaining the historical aspect of this 13-building compound, while allowing all of the luxuries of a 5 star hotel.
