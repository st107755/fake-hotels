This hotel has to quality staff and clean rooms. The food isn't the best, but when visiting Venice, eating out is the best thing to do. The owner is very polite, and is happy to help you if you need it. It isn't very expensive, which is quite important, according to me, so you can have more things to do thing in the city. Speaking about the city:  The city has a lot of interesting shops and museums to visit, one where carpenter carves out sculptures of clothes in wood, and it looks real! You need to be careful, though, as it is easy to slip by the edge of the pavement. Especially if it is wet.  Venice has, like all of Italy, really good ice-cream. There are many shops dedicated to selling ice-cream, and it is seldom bad.
