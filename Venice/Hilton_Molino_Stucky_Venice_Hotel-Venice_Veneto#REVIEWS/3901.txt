Visited Venice for two days and stayed here because I prefer Hiltons when possible. This hotel is well located and the shuttle water taxi is a great perk. I got good written instructions from the Hotel for arriving at the train station. It was easy to get there. We were offered a room upgrade which provided a nice room with a wonderful view of the canal. It was foggy the day we arrived so the view early on was not too got but when the day cleared we got a fantastic view of Venice and even a large cruise ship coming in. We were also offered a special for the breakfast for 15€ each which turned out to be a great as the offerings were fantastic.
