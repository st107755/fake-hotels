We are back in Venice for the Carnival and are staying again at the Hilton Molino Stucky on Giudecca island. We’ve spent two weeks here at this hotel every year for the last seven years or so.  We love it here in the Molino Stucky, because it’s only a little way from the bustle of Venice but you get great views of the city from the hotel in a tranquil setting. Giudecca itself is a great place to explore with several restaurants from the cheap and cheerful to the outstanding. The Hilton has an hourly-shuttle from its front door to San Marco and you can even come directly from the airport to the hotel’s front door by waterbus.
