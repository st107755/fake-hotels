Hotel was spot on. Grand, great facilities and comfy, clean rooms. All staff were attentive and polite and it has a lovely rooftop bar.  2 things to be aware of.  One. There is a shuttle boat that takes guests to San Marco every 40 minutes. It runs on time and is relatively hassle free. However there is only roof for 39 guests on the shuttle and their is only one. Luckily we went in the March shoulder season so we were only told once there was no room but this much be a nightmare in the summer. If you miss it you have to wait another 40 minutes. Surely another boat can be brought in to make this as convenient as possible for guests.  Two.
