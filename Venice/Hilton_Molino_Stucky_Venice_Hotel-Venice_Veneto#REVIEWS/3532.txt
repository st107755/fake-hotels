Just spent a long weekend in wonderful Venice at the Molino Stucky. I had specifically chosen this hotel for its location which I thought would provide some calm from the hustle and bustle of the streets of Venice. This it certainly did!   The hotel is a factory conversion which I thought had been unimaginatively renovated. The exterior of grand red brick contrasted with the low level generic marbled interior which lacked a focus and was all a bit corporate. Our room was a good size, clean with a nice big bathroom, all done out in a classic style.  Our three nights stay was pleasant and the staff were courteous and always helpful.
