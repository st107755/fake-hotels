I am a Hilton Honors member and stayed here for 2 nights after our cruise. The vaporetta (water bus) from the Cruise Ship Terminal was easy and quick, about $15 euros each. The vaporetta stop is literally on the front door of the hotel.   The hotel is an old mill (molino) that has been refurbished and is a beautiful structure, especially light up at night.Check-in was a little slow as everybody arrives at the same time but the staff is absolutely fantastic and friendly. They will welcome you to Venice with a sincere desire that you thoroughly enjoy your visit.  Since we checked in early (10:00 am), they could not upgrade our room since they were not ready. Still we welcomed the chance to get in a room and relax and unpack for our too few days in Venice.
