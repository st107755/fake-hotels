I had the pleasure of staying here with my family in March.  The property is a converted flour mill and is rich with history.  This has to be one of the best Hilton’s I have ever stayed at. The hotel manager personally delivered our room keys to us.  Once in the room, we received deliveries of orange juices and breakfast biscuits for the next morning.
