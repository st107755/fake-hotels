We stayed for 2 nights at this hotel, to celebrate my wifes 60th birthday. This hotel is beautiful, and we very much enjoyed our stay. There were one or two things that I would change if I stayed again. Firstly, would not bother with the executive room and lounge. The little on offer in the small lounge hardly made it worth the extra cost, and people would wait outside the lounge until someone used their pass, and would casualy walk in behind, this was very noticable by us, but the staff did not seem to care.  We also thought the breakfast on offer in this lounge was poor.  However, the food that we ordered in the restaurants, was very good, although a little on the expensive side, but I expected that.
