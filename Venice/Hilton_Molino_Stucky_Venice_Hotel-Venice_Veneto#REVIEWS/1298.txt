The Hilton Molino Stucky is a jewel in the Hilton Hotel chain.  It is located on the Giudecca Island, a quiet and beautifully tranquil residential island on the Giudecca Canal, within sight of Piazza San Marco and the Grand Canal.   The hotel opened in 2007, after an exquisite remodeling of a former flour mill, which was abandoned in the 1950s.  The original architecture is in tact, and the interiors are simply wonderful.  Upon arrival, we were upgraded to a large, deluxe king room facing the canal with sweeping views of the water.  The room was well appointed, although the air conditioner wasn't allowing adjustment for lower temps.  Bathroom shower was wonderful, as was the bidet, rarely found in any hotels.
