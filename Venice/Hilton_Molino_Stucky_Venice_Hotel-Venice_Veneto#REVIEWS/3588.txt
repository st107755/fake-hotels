This was the third hotel of a tour of Italy.  It was supposed to be the highlight of the tour with its 5* label.  However, very disappointed.  Reception is understaffed for such a huge hotel.  I know this because I had to queue three times.  Twice to fix the room key card which had mysteriously stopped working and a third time to ask about the pool party which other reviewers have mentioned and went on until 2am.  Reception staff were uninterested in our lack of sleep.  Room was very spacious and beds were comfortable.  Breakfast was poor quality and again appeared understaffed with only a few waiters in there, all of whom were working as fast as they could but were overwhelmed by the sheer number of guests.
