My family and I stayed at this lovely hotel in Venice on a very hot sunny weekend in August. The hotel is very new, the executive rooms were clean tidy and large. We unfortunately had a room over the back of the hotel we should have asked for a lagoon facing room. The rooftop pool was well used by our teenage girls.  One point to make is that the sound proofing in the rooms on the third floor were not great!! The shuttle water boats operated by the hotel were very efficient so it was very easy to get to the main attractions and we actually loved being far away from the crowds on the grand canal. As a diamond hilton member i do have to say the arrangement of the executive lounge on the ground floor was not a good idea.
