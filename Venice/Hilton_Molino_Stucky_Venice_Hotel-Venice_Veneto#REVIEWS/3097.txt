Starting with the positives, the public areas of the hotel are nice, breakfast good and staff all pleasant.  The room itself was spacious enough, but really 'gloomy'. Right at the bottom of the gap between 2 buildings, overlooking a flat roof, it hardly got any natural light. On discovering this we did ask for an alternative room, but we were told none were available.  Later I checked on Hilton's website and funnily enough I was able to book the same configuration room for the durstion of our stay, so it appears reception dicn't check very thoroughly.   We discovered when someone booked into the neighbouring room the next day, how thin the walls are. Not just voice noise, but we could hear every word of conversation.
