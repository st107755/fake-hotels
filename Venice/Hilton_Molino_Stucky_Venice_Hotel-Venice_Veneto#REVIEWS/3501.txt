We stayed in this hotel for 3 nights on our way to Malta!  The hotel is not far away from the airport but to get there is a bit complicated! We took the taxi (private water boat) but it was very expensive! The public boat ride takes 1,5 hours so it is very inconvient!  I liked the hotel overall but wasn't too impressed with it! The building which is a former prison certainly looks very imposing! It is situated on Giudecca island about 10 min boat ride from San Marco square. The hotel provides shuttle service every 20 to 40 minutes so it is not a problem to get to the main Venice attractions!  I stayed in the Deluxe Room and was very pleased with it! It is very spacious, the bed is very comfortable with excellent pillow choice! The bathroom is huge with both bath and shower!
