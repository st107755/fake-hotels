Stayed here for a technical conference for 6 nights.  The hotel is charming and the staff are friendly and helpful, but there were problems.  The lock on my first room failed 3 times.  They offered to let me move rooms after the 2nd failure (twice in one day) but I didn't want to pack my stuff so instead opted for the maintenance man to put in new batteries.  Next day the lock failed again and I did move rooms.  In the new room the ventilation seemed to be working better, the door lock worked though the door stuck and was hard to get shut and locked.  The TV however only sort of worked.  In spite of these problems I still found the hotel to be charming - so I am giving it a rating of 4.
