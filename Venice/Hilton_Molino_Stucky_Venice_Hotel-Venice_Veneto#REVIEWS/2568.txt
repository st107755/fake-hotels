My wife and I stayed here for a very special anniversary in September. We had been recommended this hotel by family and were not disappointed at all. Getting there by water-bus from the main terminal was a doddle. 15-20 minutes and we were there at reception checking in. This is a huge hotel but the staff looked after us very well. We took a room overlooking Venice. The hotel is on its own island. Not a problem as the frequent hotel ferry gets you across to all the sites in a few minutes - although it can get busy and on two occasions we had to wait 20 minutes longer for the next ferry (not so good!).  Our booking allowed us into the executive lounge where we had breakfast each day.
