My boyfriend and I stayed in the Molino Stucky for four nights. We had a beautiful room on the forth floor with a gorgeous view of Venice! The room was spacious with everything you could possibly need. The hotel was also helpful in providing in us an adaptor and extra tooth paste. Our room was cleaned and tidied twice daily which was a nice extra touch!   The hotel was gorgeous, staff very helpful and Christmas decorations lovely. We had access to the executive lounge during our stay which was great, we had breakfast there every day (eggs and bacon or continental option), free drinks including tea, coffee and the majority of soft drinks, snacks and between 5-7pm alcoholic drinks (limited time period for this was a little annoying).
