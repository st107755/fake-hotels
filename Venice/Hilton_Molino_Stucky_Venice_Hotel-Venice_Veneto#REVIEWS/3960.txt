I think it's a great standard Hilton hotel with nice and clean rooms and great stuff but the lack of any kind of charisma makes it difficul to rate it with the maximum points. The location itself is very peaceful, but if you want to get to th center of Venice with the free shuttle boat it can take up to 30 minutes. The timetabel is a bit hectic so it's better to plan ahead if you don't want to wait 2 hours between noon and 2pm.   The rooms are nice and clean but really nothing special, more suitable for a conferences than a romantic getaway. As a couple we found this hotel a bit boring and it's a shame, as the hotel has great potential. I think it would help a lot, if they could change a little bit this "corporate" kind of feeling.
