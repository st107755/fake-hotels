I spent four nights at this hotel with a friend.  It was a great stay but NEVER to be confused with a 5 star establishment.  I had a twin deluxe room on the 2nd floor, and would say that the decor was at best sufficient.  However, after reading about the 7th floor and the noise from the sky pool bar I feel lucky.  The bad bits are, firstly the bar prices in the room and in the hotel, which are frankly ridiculous.  €6 for a small bottle of water! However, you can take a free bottle of water at breakfast, as people were doing. There is nowhere in the hotel to eat snacky food unless you're hooked on Nutella which again is disappointing. The staff are very hit and miss and looked overworked.
