We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky for our first wedding anniversary getaway. Venice is absolutely gorgeous and arriving at the hotel pier, we were immediately impressed. On checking in, the staff were friendly and efficient, giving  us all the info we needed. The shuttle between the hotel and the main islands is punctual and frequent - we had no problems getting on but I heard of several people being left behind as the service was oversubscribed.  The room was well appointed - the view could have been a bit more exciting (we looked onto the courtyard where the Nutella Piazza is) but we were only there to sleep so no problems for us.  Breakfast was brilliant, plenty to chose from, lots of different breads, meats, fruits, juices, cereals... Really impressed.
