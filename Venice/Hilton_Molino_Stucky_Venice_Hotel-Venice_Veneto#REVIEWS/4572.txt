Somehow, I don't think that this hotel was ever 5 star. Size isn't everything. It has to be the biggest hotel we have stayed in. Maybe it was the housemaid staff, but, generally, on a day to day basis, we had to go down to Reception, to have tea bags, milk and sugar replenished in our room. Is that 5 star? There was also a dud light in the bathroom when we arrived, not to mention the wall socket in the bathroom that was hanging off the wall. Do not even think of opening the Mini-Bar! If you as much as MOVE anything in there, I guarantee it WILL register, and they will charge you accordingly. It takes some convincing when you are checking out. The one good thing is the private water taxi from the island, which is incredibly efficient, timewise. Venice was wonderful.
