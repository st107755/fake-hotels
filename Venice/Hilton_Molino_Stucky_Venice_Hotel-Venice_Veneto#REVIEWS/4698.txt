As Hilton Diamond members we were upgraded to a suite in the Bienalle building with a Giudecca canal view.  The hotel is a very large old building which has been refitted as a hotel, but they preserved the big wooden beams of the original structure.  It is definitely a quieter location than being on the main island, but has the inconvenience of having to take a cab/shuttle/vaporetto to get almost everywhere, as there is very little on the island of Giudecca and there isn't a bridge.  The hotel shuttle is pretty prompt, but the schedule on the web page was not the one they were running (seasonal change).  Some of the vaporetto routes available in summer were also not available in the off season.
