It is rather huge hotel at Guidecca island very close to all the Venice attractions. Hotel itself and rooms are nice and providing good value for money. We have appreciated free water shuttle service (San Marco) operating from the morning till late night 2-3 times an hour (with some breaks during day). There is great swimming pool on the roof. The cocktail bar was fine but service slow and prices high. The hotel flagship restaurant Aromi is fine but both food and service are just average and so not great value for money. In case that you arrive by car you can read a very basic advice at the hotel web site (I would certainly appreciate more detailed advice!!!).
