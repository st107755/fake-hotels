The hotel is located just across the water from the madness which is perfect you can retreat to peace and quiet when you’ve had enough.   The boat transfer the Hilton provide is fantastic it runs from early morning to late and is consistently on time. For €7.50 per person for the duration of your stay it’s a bargain.  After reading other comments on the executive lounge I wasn’t expecting much but was pleasantly surprised. While it is a narrow room off the main lobby it is quiet and there are great views. I really enjoyed the breakfast selection and found the staff very attentive.  The room was spacious, clean and although we didn’t get the upgrade I’d tried to book it was still a fabulous room.  The fitness suite is small but well stocked and absolutely spotless.
