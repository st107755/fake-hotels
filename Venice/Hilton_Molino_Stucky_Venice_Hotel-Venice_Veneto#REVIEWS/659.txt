We recently stayed at the Hilton Molino ... the night before our cruise and the night after our cruise.  From the Marco Polo Airport, you can take the Public Water Taxi (ALILAGUNA - Blue Line)  directly to the hotel.  It took about 1.5 - 2 hours to get from the airport but the sights we saw on route were great.  The Hilton has it's own stop, which is really convenient.  The Hilton also has it's own private water shuttle which takes you across the canal to St. Marks Square and one other stop.  The cost for their shuttle is only about 7.5 euros for the duration of your stay and you get unlimited trips.    We did not eat at the hotel so I cannot comment on the restaurant(s), other than the prices seemed pretty high.
