We visited Venice in November for a short break and received a warm welcome upon our arrival in the hotel. Our room was not yet ready so we made the most of our time by a trip to the hotel bar for some nibbles and to sample the local Aperol Spritz. The staff were friendly and the atmosphere in the bar relaxed. We used the extensive knowledge of the fantastic concierge team to get around the city and make dining recommendations, and we weren't disappointed.  They are a credit to the hotel.  Our room had a fantastic view of the Grand Canal which I would highly recommend requesting. The rooms aren't huge but are perfectly formed with all the necessary amenities.   The complimentary shuttle boat across the water is excellent value for money and operates a regular service.
