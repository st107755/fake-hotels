I travelled to Venice with my wife, 4 year old boy and my mum. As such, I wanted to ensure we stayed somewhere where we were properly looked after.  We weren't disappointed. The hotel was great and the staff amazing.  The hotel is located in an old flour mill on Giudecca, with views across Venice (it is worth making sure you get a room with a view onto the canal - well worth it).  One of the good things about the hotel is that it has a half-hourly boat service to St Mark's, which costs just a few euros for the entire stay. This was amazingly convenient.  The hotel itself was lovely - it has a rooftop bar with amazing views and a rooftop swimming pool as well (closed during winter so we didn't visit).
