Molino Stucky served as a great home base for our week in Venice. This hotel is located on La Giudecca which is across the water from lively San Marco Piazza. However, the hotel has its own complimentary vaporetto service and there are other water taxi spots nearby, so access around Venice is no problem. The professional staff take great care to welcome you with Venetian hospitality. And our little 2-year old girl always got attention! Breakfast consisted of a wonderful layout of European meats, breads, cheeses and the freshest fruit anywhere. Our room was spacious by American standards with high ceilings and a large bathroom with a separate shower and tub. We did notice the room felt a little damp, but we attribute that to Venice.
