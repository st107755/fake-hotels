We stayed at the Hilton in Venice for 2 nights.  The hotel is located on an island next to Venice. From the airport you can take a boat directly to the hotel. The boat has many stops so it takes 1 hour and 40 min. You can also take the bus from the airport and then the boat no 4.1 or 2. It is faster. When you are at the hotel, it is easy to get to Venice  with boat no 2. The hotel also its own shuttle to Venice. There are only a few restaurant close to the hotel.  The hotel is really nice. We got an upgrade to a suite, which was huge and clean in typical Hilton decor. From the window we had an amazing view at Venice. The wifi was ok, but you are logged out all the time.
