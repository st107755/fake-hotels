We stayed at the Hilton's Molino Stucky hotel in Venice for a long weekend. We were impressed with the building, which is a converted mill, and is one of the tallest buildings in Venice. The rooms are impressive with high ceilings and Murano glass chandeliers, superb bathrooms, mini bar and all the 5 star conveniences you would expect, the cleaning was also good. The free water shuttle service was punctual and frequent, we never had any problems getting a seat. The only thing I would say is if you are arriving by plane at Marco Polo airport don't get the public water bus to the hotel as it takes 1 hour 40 minutes, it is the very last stop!
