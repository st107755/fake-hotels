This is my 3rd time staying at the Hilton Molino Stucky.  We stayed on the 1st floor with a beautiful view of the canal.  Room was large (even by American standards) and immaculate, bed was comfortable and bathroom spacious with plenty of vanity space. My only negative comment about the room was the poor air conditioning.  Even at the high setting, the room was uncomfortably hot at night which made it very hard to sleep.  The "members only" snack area in the lobby is wonderful. Plenty of choices for breakfast and afternoon snacks.  Beer, wine, soft drinks, water all included with delicious food offerings.  Staff is superb. Front desk professional, very accommodating and courteous. Concierge is fanatastic.   Beware of the less formal trattoria attached to the hotel.
