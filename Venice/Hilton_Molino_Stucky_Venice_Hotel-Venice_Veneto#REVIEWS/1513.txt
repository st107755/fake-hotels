This is our seventh, two-week stay at the Molino Stucky in Venice, so I guess they must be doing things right. We always come for the spectacular Venice Festival to photograph the amazing costumes.  The Molino Stucky is a former industrial complex ... and that makes this hotel very special. It’s a living museum and a testament to the industrial age. The conversion from mill to hotel is excellent and both the guest rooms and public rooms are excellent.  I have a diamond honors card and this is a Hilton where you do seem to get the full advantages of honors membership; for example, we got an excellent  room upgrade, the breakfast is in a beautiful dining room and the food is excellent.
