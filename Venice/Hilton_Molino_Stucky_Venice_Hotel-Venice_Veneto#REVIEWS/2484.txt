We spent three nights here and had an excellent stay. We booked a roundtrip private water-taxi from the airport, and it was about a 20 minute ride, and the hotel has its own dock right by the entrance. While not the lowest cost option, the comfort and convenience is great, and booking an online roundtrip from Venicelink costs less than most other options, for sure less than what most hotels offer.  The hotel's location is a bit away from the city center, but its not an issue as the hotel has a complimentary shuttle boat that runs to San Marco every 40 minutes. The benefits of being away from the city center are greater. Firstly, its a bit secluded from the crowds.
