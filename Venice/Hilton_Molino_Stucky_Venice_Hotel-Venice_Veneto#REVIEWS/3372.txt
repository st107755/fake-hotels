We enjoyed our 2 night stay here although sadly we would not chose this hotel again for a few reasons.   Our room was facing the shuttle boat and while we had a wonderful view of Venice, we could hear the shuttle boats coming and going (as well as other boats at 6am) and the noise from the guests waiting to board the boat carried. The walls could really do with soundproofing, we could hear the guests and their children in the room next door, as well as them using the hairdryer and flushing the toilet.   WiFi - seriously Hilton, in this day and age how can you justify charging such a ridiculous amount (€20 for 24hrs) for WiFi when cheaper hotels are providing this for free?!
