We booked just two nights in this spectacular hotel to accommodate us for our final leg of our holiday to Italy and the only things negative I have to say about this hotel is that we couldn't have stayed there longer!  Everything from the service of having our bags taken inside from the taxi, to the incredible service by all the staff, and the beautiful building itself were just breathtaking.   We ate in both of the hotel restaurants which were both unbelievably tasty as well as spending both evenings in the roof top bar which has the most spectacular views of Venice and rather delicious cocktails.   The hotel is just out of the business of the town center which is perfect and gives the place a quiet air.
