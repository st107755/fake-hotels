Very nice, large, hotel on one of the larger islands of Venice.  Transportation to all of the tourist sites and Grand Canal was easy - either by free boat transport (usually every hour) from the hotel dock to San Marco or via water bus a short walk from the hotel.  If you have heavy or big bags, however, be aware that the walk from the water bus stop requires you to cross over 2 bridges with stairs only (no ramps).  There is direct water service from the airport to the hotel dock, but not from the train station.  Hotel is huge, but layout makes it seem quiet and quaint.  Rooms are large by European standards, and executive rooms are very large and nice (worth the upgrade if you are looking for space!).  Bathrooms are fairly standard Hilton quality, and well maintained.
