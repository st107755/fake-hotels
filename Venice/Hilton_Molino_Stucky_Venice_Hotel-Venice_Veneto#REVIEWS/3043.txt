Really glad we choose this hotel - which is located on Giudecca just across the canal from Venice.  We thought the complimentary shuttle (which hops across to Zattere, then onto San Marco) was fantastic.  Unlike some other reviewers we never had a problem with it being over-crowded, but perhaps we were not travelling at peak times.  Our hotel room was of a very good standard being a dual aspect corner room (Executive Twin), with fabulous views over the canal on one side and the Molino Stucky clock and outside restaurant area on the other.  The only negative I can think of is the lack of free wi-fi in the rooms - which really should be available and I think Hilton should rethink this point.
