We mostly selected this hotel because we could use Hilton Honors points.  US travelers will not be disappointed with the very familiar amenities and accommodations of a Hilton property.  The rooms while a bit smaller than the US (it's Europe, get used to that) but very comfortable. Don't miss the view of the city from the rooftop pool and bar in the evening.  Spectacular.   The only small downside is the location.  You have to take the hotel's free water shuttle  to the main portion of Venice.  Just be aware of the schedule and plan accordingly.  Don't bypass a pleasant evening stroll along the waterfront near the hotel.  Some nice little places for dinner or a glass of wine.  Be advised of free wifi in the hotel common areas only.
