I loved this hotel. The staff were superb, the room was great. We had an executive room through voyage prive web site. The views from the roof were awesome. The boat service was extremely good. We even managed to see George Clooney and his wedding party on the grand canal and captured some fabulous photos. My only bad comments would be that the murano glass factory experience provided free by the hotel was not good as the salesman at the factory was a very unsavoury character whose sole aim was to sell glass at hugely inflated prices ie 400 per cent more than in the shops in Venice and his attitude was appalling, he even said to me, please stop asking me the prices?  My other unfavourable point would be the breakfast which was dull and wifi needs to be included.
