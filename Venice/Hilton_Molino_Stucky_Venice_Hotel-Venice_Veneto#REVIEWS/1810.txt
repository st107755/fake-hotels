We chose this hotel based on the reviews and were not disappointed.  On arrival at Marco Polo airport, we got the water bus Alilaguna (blue line) which stops at the hotel. €15 each one way and takes about 1 hour 45 minutes but it's a great way to see the sights without the hassle.  A 7 minute walk to the dock from the arrivals hall and easy to find. The staff at the hotel were courteous, helpful and very efficient.  We stayed in a deluxe room which was clean and functional.  The hotel was quiet as were the rooms.  Occasional groups of cruise tourists took over the space but in general it was quite refined and relaxing for a huge corporate hotel.  Breakfast had a good buffet choice and save yourself €5 each per day by booking in advance.
