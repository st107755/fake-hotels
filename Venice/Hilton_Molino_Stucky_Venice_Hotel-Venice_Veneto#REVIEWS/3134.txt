Beautiful hotel off the tourist rush of Venice.  Tucked away in a nearby island, this hotel is a nice place to stay if you don't mind taking the shuttle (provided by Hilton) into the main islands.  An old flourmill, it is an incredible feeling as you walk by the halls and imagine how it looked in the old days.  The rooms we stayed in were on the inside and did not have sights of the river but they made it convenient for us since we needed connecting rooms with our children.  The front desk is helpful so are the concierge.  Wireless is patchy in your rooms (unless you want to pay a premium for it).  The lobby and breakfast places have good connection.  The breakfast spread is very good and specially worth if it is included in your room rate (which it was for us).
