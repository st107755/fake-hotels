A simply marvellous experience. A birthday trip for my wife was met with outstanding service from the concierge, front desk and breakfast restaurant staff. Our one niggle would be the housekeeping staff service where some lapses of service contrasted with everything else.   A heart-shaped array of petals on the bed, a plate of chocolate dipped strawberries with a bottle of Prosecco on arrival and a 2nd bottle with a birthday cake on my wife's birthday all set the tone for a great stay. The concierge arranged good value transfers and changed them when the disappointing BA cancelled our flight at the 11th hour and we had to re-arrange our route.  The quiet location is great - with a superbly reliable boat service to both San Marco and Dorsoduro.
