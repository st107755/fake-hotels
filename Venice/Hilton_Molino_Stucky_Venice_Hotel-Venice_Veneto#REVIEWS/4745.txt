My husband is a Hilton Honors diamond member so we always try to stay in a Hilton property when we travel.  We found the Hilton Molino Stucky to be a very nice Hilton property, with all the amenities we have come to expect.  Our suite was very nice with Murano glass chandeliers in two of the rooms, a spacious bathroom, comfortable bedroom and large living/dining room along with a fully stocked kitchenette.  The staff was friendly and helpful, particularly the bellmen who stored and managed our luggage the day we checked in and the day of our late evening departure.  The complimentary shuttle was easy to use, and the times we missed it, we walked to the public ACTV shuttle about 10 minutes away.  The only time we used a private taxi was on our way into Venice.
