We stayed here last week as an add on weekend for a business trip to Milan.  From the train station it is a 60-65 Euro water taxi ride (worth it in my mind).  The hotel itself is really nice, we opted for the 60 Euro per day upgrade to the executive level with a water view from our window.  The executive level includes drinks and snacks most of the day, a good European style breakfast each morning, and a nice afternoon reception including wine, beer, etc and some nice appetizers!  This is not a busy area but we found the free water shuttle to two locations across the water more than sufficient to take in Venice!  Beautiful churches, nice shopping, and great little bars and restaurants to find. My only gripe is that the room did have an odor to it the was not pleasant.
