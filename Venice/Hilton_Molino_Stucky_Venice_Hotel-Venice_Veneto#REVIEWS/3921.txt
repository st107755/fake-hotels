Visited for three nights on last weekend of March. Despite being early season with some of the hotel facilities being unavailable it did not detract from the fact that this is an exceptionally good hotel. Previous reviews have commented on the advantage of residing in this renovated flour mill on Giudecca island and escaping from the tourism trap of Venice city and this should not be understated. I found the facilities and service impeccable even though the in house prices are somewhat high, but in keeping with a hotel of this standard. During our stay we sought the opportunity to visit the 5*L Cipriani hotel which is also on Giudecca and found no reason to consider the Molino Stuck to be any way inferior.
