A very pleasant stay at the Hilton. We had an executive room with view and the view was smashing across the lagoon. I had expected that staying at the hotel would be a lot quieter than when we have stayed on the Grand Canal but it was not. The shuttle boats caused a bit of noise for us and bigger boats went by frequently, but it was fun to watch the cruise liners being towed in front of the hotel. The breakfast was excellent. Great choices, full english, continental with some particulary excellent local cheese and meats and fruits and cakes. . We also enjoyed the Executive Lounge with its great views. The pool was spotless when we attended. I agree that a few more loungers around the pool would be appreciated.
