We stayed here during our 1st ever visit to Venice this month. It is pricey to get to though, we hired a private water taxi from Marco Polo Venice airport to drive us & our luggage directly to hotel dock for about $100 USD, then hired them again when leaving to return to airport for another $100 USD. Front desk staff was very pleasant. As Hilton Honors/Diamond we received an upgrade to a beautiful suite with fantastic view of the waterways/canal. The rooms were very nice, clean with many amenities. We had executive lounge privileges and that was nice, too. The main restaurant offered a nice extensive breakfast buffet.
