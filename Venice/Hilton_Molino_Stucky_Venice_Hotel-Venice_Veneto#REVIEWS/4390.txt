My wife and I stayed at the hotel for 2 nights on the 19th and 20th of June 2013. The hotel was everything that we have come to expect from Hilton Group. The rooms were comfortable, the bars and restaurants very good and air-conditioned.  Of particular note is the roof bar which is superb for a drink after a night out in Venice or, as was the case with us, a masked ball!  We found the staff to be very helpful and customer focused.  Unfortunately, our stay was marred by my falling whilst alighting from a water taxi and fracturing my shoulder and badly damaging my ribs. The staff was efficient in calling a doctor and an ambulance to take me to the local hospital.
