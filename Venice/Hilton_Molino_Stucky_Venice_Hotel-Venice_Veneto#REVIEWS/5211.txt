We started our 2012 European Spring break cruise with a couple of days visit to Venice before our cruise left from the Port of Venice.  We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky because it is located off the main island of Venice, on the island of Guidecca.  After arriving from the airport on our private water taxi from the airport (a 100 euro fare which was a HUGE thrill for our 7 year old), our adventure began.   We were given a nice room on the 5th floor which faced the quiet courtyard (we used a hotel voucher using miles from AAdvantage so we didn't expect a canal view) and we were immediately given a roll-away bed for our young son without asking or reminding staff that we had a reservation for 3.
