What an incredibly wonderful city to visit!  Venice may be one of the more expensive places in Italy but it is one of the most unique and fascinating places in the world.  To see it you have to walk alot and by the end of the day you want a nice place to come back to and relax.  The Hilton Molino Stucky is just to place.  It's right on the water, across from Venice and it provides free shuttle service by boat.  Molino is a fairly quiet island away from the noise of Venice.  The hotel is modern, beautifully decorated, and the rooms are very comfortable.  The staff are well trained, very helpful and plentiful.  The view of Venice from the rooftop pool is amazing (the pool is nice too).
