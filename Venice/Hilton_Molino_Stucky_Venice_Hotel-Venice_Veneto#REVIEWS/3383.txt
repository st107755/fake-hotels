After deciding last minute to have a girls weekend in Venice, we chose a room at Molino Stucky due to the previous reviews.   We were not disappointed - we had a lovely room even at very short notice, we were in an executive room so also had access to the lounge area with complimentary drinks and snacks throughout the day and wine/beers and canapés between 5 and 7. A nice pick me up after a full day of walking along the canals!   Our room was lovely and spacious and the bathroom was large enough for two girls getting ready at the same time!   The hilton water taxi goes every 20 mins, however be careful coming back as they only allow 39 people on each time even if there are spare seats.
