This hotel is by far one of the best I've stayed at.  The staff were so helpful, prior to our stay, organising flowers and a cake and dinner reservations for a birthday party on the night we arrived.  Following this, we were met with nothing short of charm.  Our kids couldnt wait to get back to the hotel to enjoy the delicious crepes made by the wonderful Vencentio. The place is so children friendly, even though it exudes elegance and luxury.  When we posted our pictures from the room view to social media, people message to ask if we'd taken them or if they were off a website!  While this hotel is not on the 'main land' in San Marco, it was so easy to get the other side with the shuttle boat provided by the hotel.   The buffet breakfast is expansive and delicious.
