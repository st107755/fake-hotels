This was our first trip to Venice and we were well aware that the hotel is located on an island away from Venice.  It’s an amazing huge hotel with plenty of history right in the middle of quaint neighborhood. It’s peaceful and quiet at night away from the tourist center. We absolutely love it and it’s a wonderful place for a family. Our room is extremely large for European standard with spacious bathroom and plenty of drawer space. Since it is a family suite, the room has three fridges, a sitting room, dining room and small kitchenette. There are many MANY reviews that provide misleading information on forum related to this hotel. The first one is the restaurants. There are many great local restaurant around the hotel if you are willing to walk for within 0.5 a mile.
