The hotel is in the perfect location for a stay in Venice as it is not on the main island- the regular free shuttle boat will take you there and back. Whilst we very much enjoyed our stay here I do have mixed emotions about the hotel. At £750 for two nights I find it rather annoying to have to pay€9 for 24 hours of wifi. The wifi service is also very poor as signal only works in the lobby. The service is outsourced so you have to call a seperate company to get resolved. We did visit the rooftop bar on or stay which was great if not slightly over priced. You are paying for the view only as service is so-so.  The reception staff however are great, very helpful and even arranged for a welcome bottle of prosecco for us as we were on our honeymoon.
