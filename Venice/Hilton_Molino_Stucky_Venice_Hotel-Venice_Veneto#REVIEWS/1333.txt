We tend to look at Hiltons first when booking hotels, and probably would never have looked at this one otherwise due to its location off the main island.  I had some concerns about the location, due to the time it takes to get to the main island. Overall, I would say that it has pros and cons. I would stay there again, but many people will prefer the convenience of being more central.  We booked an Executive Room when the rate was good and on arrival we were upgraded to a suite, which was very nice. The room was comfortable with plenty of room, although still a little smaller that suites in other hotels.  The Executive Lounge is separated from the main lobby area by a glass wall, and it goes round two sides of the room, making a long and thin area.
