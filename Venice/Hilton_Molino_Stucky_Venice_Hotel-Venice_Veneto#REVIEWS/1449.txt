We arrived in Venice and took a water taxi from the airport to the Hilton. If you don't mind the taxi stops, this was a delightful way to arrive in Venice. The taxi pulled right up to the dock in front of the Hilton. The taxi service and the Hilton shuttle run daily from the hotel to the Grand Canal so it's an easy way to stay and yet get around Venice. The room was really comfortable and well-appointed. The bathroom was just elegant - marble, heated towel rack, luxuries! The hotel has a really good breakfast buffet. The hotel has two restaurants and a rooftop bar. We did so much walking during the days that we just came back to the hotel in the evenings for drinks and dinner. We were only here for three evenings prior to a Viking Cruise.
