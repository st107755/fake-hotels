I'm not the biggest fan of large soulless chain hotels ...but Im prepared to make an exception for the Hilton 'Molino Stucky ' on the south west of the Giudecca island in Venice .This  trip was our 2nd to this specific Hotel and  I'd gladly return again . A restored flour mill, the Stucky has been developed sympathetically into a huge Hilton .The internal geography of the hotel is a little confusing to begin with ...but once you find the right lift to access the spa and pool all is well !  The hotel is positioned across the Giudecca Canal from the cruise ship terminal in Venice and this distance from St  Marks Square  can be seen by some as an inconvenience .
