We took the train from Florence to Venice. It was a very easy walk to the water taxi that we took to the hotel. Upon arrival we received help getting our luggage off of the taxi. We were greeted at the desk and were very pleased to find 2 of the 3 rooms were ready even though we arrived earlier than check in time. The hotel is beautiful. Our room had a view of the water and was decorated beautifully.  The hotel’s shuttle boat was great. We took this several times during our stay. It ran on a regular schedule to St Marks Square.   We were able to walk to many restaurants nearby the hotel. The hotel has a great rooftop bar with beautiful views. There is also a large pool that 1 of the couples we were with took advantage of to relax.
