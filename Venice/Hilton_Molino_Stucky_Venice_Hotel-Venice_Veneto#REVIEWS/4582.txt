I can't say enough good things about this hotel and can't wait to return.  My daughter and I stayed here for three nights.  The room was an excellent size with a luxurious bathroom, the only thing it lacked was bottled water which seemed odd as they provided other luxuries like thick towelling dressing gowns and slippers. Our executive room gave us unlimited access to the executive lounge all day long with the use of our room key. There, you have access to drinks and snacks all day, including unlimited wine and selected spirits between 18.00-20.00.  The location of the hotel was not a problem in the least to us, as the hotel's free shuttle service to Zaterre and San Marco operates so well.
