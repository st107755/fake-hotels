We stayed for three nights in a Family suite. The rooms were huge and well furnished. We had a bedroom, living room with sofa, desk, dining table and wet bar, and the bathroom was especially large with a shower and separate bathtub. We took a vaporetto from the train station to the hotel, which took about 30 minutes. And the shuttle to St. Mark's was convenient and took only 15 minutes. We ordered room service a couple times and it was delicious and prompt. The beds were comfortable and we didn't have any issues with noise. The concierge was helpful with reserving a private water taxi to the airport for us. There are a few restaurants nearby and we took advantage of the hotel's offer to shuttle us to a glass factory on Murano one afternoon.
