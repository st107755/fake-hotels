We arrived on 29th September 2012, at the Molino Stucky Hilton in Venice after a 20 hour journey from Singapore to to Venice, with a five hour transit time in Doha. From the Marco Polo airport in Venice, you take a water taxi costing about €25 per person, to reach the Hilton hotel on Giudecca island. It’s a 8-9 minute walk from the airport exit to the wharf pushing your luggage to where the water taxis are located. The pathway has a overhead cover except for one place where you need to cross a small road.  The Molino Stucky Hotel is interesting as the hotel was formerly a flour mill built in the industrial architecture of the 19th and 20th centuries.
