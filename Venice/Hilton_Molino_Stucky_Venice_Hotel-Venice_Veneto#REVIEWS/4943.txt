If you are an American and have stayed at a Hilton property you will feel at home.  It is very clean and the service is what you would expect at a Hilton.  It is a large property but rooms are average size but everything you need is in your room.   Large breakfast buffett simular to a Sunday brunch in most places.  It is off the main island but that is nice when you are tired of the crouded tourist filled streets and want to come back and enjoy the calm of the pool or spa.  The have a selection of resturants on property but they are a little expensive but most of Venice is.  The have a complementary suttle boat that runs often to the main island.  Staff is very accomidating and speaks good english.
