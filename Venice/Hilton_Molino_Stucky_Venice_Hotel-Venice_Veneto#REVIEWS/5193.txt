My husband and I stayed here for 3 nights in April; I couldn't fault the hotel in any way.  We stayed in a 'Thats Amore Suite' which was really nice and spacious, good quality furniture and exceptionally clean.  We ate breakfast in the hotel a good selection was on offer and omlettes were available on request.  Drinks were a little pricey but nothing more than expected. The water shuttle was very regular, although we did struggle to find where to catch it back from at St Marks, so please ensure you take note of this.
