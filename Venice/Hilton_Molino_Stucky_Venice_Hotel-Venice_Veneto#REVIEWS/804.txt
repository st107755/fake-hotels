We ended up at the Hilton to escape from a not-so-good hotel in Mestre. Even though we booked less than one day in advance, the room was quite cheap for Venice, at 23.1 Toblerone bars (the Toblerone bar is the unit of price in this review, see below), probably because it's not located on the main island. We had to wait a little bit for check-in, maybe 10 minutes, as the front desk was busy. Apparently, you can not pre-check-in using the Hilton app, like in some other Hilton hotels. The front desk clerk suggested an upgrade for 10 Toblerone bars, for an "executive room" with a view, access to the executive lounge, and breakfast.
