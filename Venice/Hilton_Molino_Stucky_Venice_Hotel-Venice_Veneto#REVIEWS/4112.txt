I booked the Hilton after hours searching through TA for a decent hotel for our first family visit to Venice.  I chose the Hilton over the more rustic hotels with more character on the main island simply because I didn't know Venice at all and I wanted a hotel I knew would be of decent quality and easy to access.  In the end I made the right choice.   I booked a private water taxi from the airport (expensive but easily the only way to enter the city) and it pulled up right outside the door.  The route from the airport to the Hilton takes you right through the Grand Canal so well worth splashing out for the private taxi for the jaw-dropping first impression.   We were upgraded to an executive room which meant we could use the free executive lounge for drinks and snacks.
