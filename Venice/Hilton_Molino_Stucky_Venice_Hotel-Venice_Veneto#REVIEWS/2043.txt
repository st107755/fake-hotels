This Hotel certainly lives up to its rating, quite simply everything was truly superb, from the moment we arrived.  The location in my opinion is excellent, as although it is on the small island of Guidecco, this means you can escape the hustle and bustle of the main part of Venice. The hotel provides a shuttle over to ST Marc's, so it easy to get around. Getting to and from the airport is also very easy, the Hotel website instructions are a very good guide, using a land bus then water bus, very quick, easy and cheap. We did use the blue line water bus to travel back, which takes longer, but does provide a tour around the outer limits of Venice.  On our arrival, we were checked in very quickly and given a warm welcome.
