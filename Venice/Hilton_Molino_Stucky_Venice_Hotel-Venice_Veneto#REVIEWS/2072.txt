The hotel is beautiful.  You have three options to get to this hotel private water taxi, public water taxi or the hotel water taxi.  The hotels only run 8am-11:30pm - outside those times you have to use public or private.  Private will cost you $130 euros each way to the airport or $80 Euros to the train station.  Even if you do not use the hotels water taxi they charge you $4.50 euros - so check your bill before you check out.  The public water taxi only runs every hour in the off hours.  It is hard to figure out which dock to go to, to find the correct one - but don't ask the hotel, because they will give you all the wrong information, and leave you stranded or force you to have to get a private water taxi in which is expensive because they take about a $20 euro cut.
