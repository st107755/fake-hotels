Service:  The women working the front desk were not welcoming upon our arrival and could not provide accurate information regarding our reservation. First, she couldn't locate my HHonors account information which entitled us to free wifi and second, she tried to argue with me and tell me that breakfast was not included in the reservation I selected (ultimately, we did not end up getting charged).  Location:  The hotel is located on Giudecca which is not the main part of Venice. From the train station to the hotel, it is a about a 30 minute water bus ride and 10-minute walk. The hotel offers a free shuttle that runs pretty frequently so getting to and from the major attractions is pretty easy.   Food: We used room-service twice and also ordered cocktails at the Skybar.
