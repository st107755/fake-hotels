As you take the boat towards the hotel, especially at night, you think, is that really the hotel.  It is a  converted factory turned into a very nice hotel.  The rooms are what you would expect at a 5 star hotel.  Nice beds, decent size, nice bathrooms, etc.  The breakfast buffet is very good, better than most hotel buffet's, so if you have time, enjoy a nice breakfast.  The hotel offers free transport to San Marco every 20 minutes or so until midnight, so it is great to be able to get off the island.  However, getting to the hotel from the train station is not as easy.  There are public boats for €7 a person or you can have the hotel pick you up in a private boat for €110/120 for up to 4 people (€150/160 for up to 8 people).  Either way, the ride is about 25-30 minutes.
