We stayed for 4 nights in this beautiful hotel on Guidecca using Hilton points in late October.  After a great deal of research and confusion about what the best transport option to the hotel would be, besides an expensive water taxi, we opted for the Alilunga.  Our hestitation with the length of time it took to get from the airport (about 1 hr 20 min).  We definitely made the right decision.  The vaporetto was crowded the Tuesday we arrived, and we could not picture ourselves standing the entire way after a long TA flight, carefully watching our luggage, then dragging our bags over 2 bridges to the hotel (although it was only a 5 min walk from the Palanca stop).  Our Alilunga ride was relaxing and offered a great tour of the city.
