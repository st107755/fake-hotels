We arrived at the hotel after staying in central Venice and were expecting good things. Check in was unimpressive no smile or warm greeting, no information about the hotel, restaurants, bar, breakfast or pool.  The room was very dated and in desperate need of refurbishment but a nice canal view. We had booked a double, requested a King and were given an executive twin so had to wait for staff to join the beds. We went to the bar to allow them to complete this task where the staff were much friendlier and the view of Venice is fantastic.   On returning to the room we found that connecting the beds resulted in only one bedside table and the usual dip in the centre of zip and link beds. Not great.
