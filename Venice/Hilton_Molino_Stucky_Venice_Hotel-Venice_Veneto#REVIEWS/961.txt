We stayed here as part of an Insight Vacations tour and had been told to expect a lovely surprise for our final destination. All our hotels had been good but my goodness, we were all completely blown away when we reached the Hilton Molino Stuckey, first viewed in all its splendour from our water taxi. What's not to love as you pull up to the hotels own pier and are immediately helped up and out, while your luggage is whisked away to your room.  This stunning historic building was once a flour mill and there are many wings and levels but it's very easy to follow the directions to find your room. Every detail has been taken care of and the luxury appointments as you traverse the corridors are fabulous. It's like a feast for the eyes.
