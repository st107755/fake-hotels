It's our first time in Venice and it won't be our last. Our hotel is one of the furthest being 1hr and a half on the river bus or 30 minutes by private taxi but personally I think it's worth the journey.  The hotels staff are excellent and happy to help with anything, I received a complimentary birthday cake and card. We didn't pay the extra for the lagoon view however we received one, the room was bigger than expected and has everything needed.  The shower wasn't the most powerful but you can't expect everything in a old building.  The bed was great, big enough to sleep with lots of space between your partner.  The breakfast had a good variety and we really enjoyed the block of fresh honey still with the comb.
