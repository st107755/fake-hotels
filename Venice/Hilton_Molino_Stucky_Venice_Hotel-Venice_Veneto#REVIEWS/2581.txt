We stayed one night at the Hilton Stucky Venice. While the room was beautifully decorated, we paid for a double room and were given a twin share. It also overlooked the Nutella bar so not much privacy. The staff spoke English well but we're not as friendly as at other hiltons we've stayed at. We also waited an extraordinary amount of time for our meals at one of the hotels restaurants - however the staff were very apologetic and offered a free dessert which was nice. Don't get me wrong, it was a nice hotel but you come to expect more from the Hilton. The hotel did offer a shuttle boat for 7 euro per stay which takes you to the main attractions which was very convenient.
