Not enough roam to say all the great things about this five star hotel!!! Hotel is a converted old flour mill and they keep many of the old beams and supports in the hallways and lobby area! Nicely done! Can be hard to get to location on ones first visit to Venice using the Vaporetto! Used the directions from the hotel web page but there is some walking involved from the nearest stop! Hotel shuttle boat is great but watch near end of the day; they got very busy and will leave from Venice St. Marcs stop with a few empty seats due to they make another stop before the hotel and you might have to wait till the next one hour later. So give yourself some extra time! Pool on the roof is great and offer great views of Venice!
