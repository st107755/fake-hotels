Venice was the first stop on our honeymoon through Italy and we absolutely loved the Stucky! Yes it's on it's own little island, but it is so easy to get to the city of Venice that you shouldn't let that stop you. There is a hotel shuttle that runs back and forth and the public water taxi is a 5 minute walk from the hotel. The hotel itself is absolutely beautiful. We loved our room - was it smaller than a US hotel room? Perhaps but it was lovely. We could not hear anyone above or to the side of us. The hotel is super clean the staff is very accommodating. They have a great breakfast every morning with anything you could want. There's a beautiful rooftop pool where you can rest after a long day and have a beautiful view of the city.
