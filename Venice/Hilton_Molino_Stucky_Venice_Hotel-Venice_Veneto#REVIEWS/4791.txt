The courtesy of the staff of all levels is remarkable. Training and professionalism are all in place. Beds are comfortable and rooms are tidy.  What could be improved: We attended a buffet dinner on a big convention and the level of the dining room, food, and presentation of it were only average, plain and ordinary. We were told that ravioli was over pretty much at the beginning of the meal where 1/3 of the guests were still standing for the first serving.  One of those mistakes one would not expect from an Hilton were some extra ravioli ( not truffle!) should be on the 'to have' list for any contingency plan.  It felt like having a meal on any in-company cantine one could think of.  Room was v. small and it was not possible to change it.
