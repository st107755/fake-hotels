We booked a long weekend in Venice and stayed at the Five Star Hilton Molino Hotel at the end of August. The weather and temperature was perfect and we did not experience the 'smell' that some have reported in Venice during the summer months.  Arriving late in the evening at Marco Polo Airport we had not pre-arranged any transfers to the hotel but had decided to pay for a private taxi. These 'should' cost 110E, if you are willing to walk for 5 minutes from the terminal building. A similar transfer from the Departure area will cost you about 20E more, and the short walk is worth it! There are others ways to make the transfer and are less expensive too, so research them before hand if your flight lands earlier than ours did.
