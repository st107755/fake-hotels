We booked a 3 night stay through Expedia secret hotel so we got a bargain price. From Trevisso airport we caught the bus which was 11Euros return each, it took about an hour. From P.Roma we took a vaporetta to Planca (20 mins) which is  only a 5 minute walk to the hotel. The hotel shuttle is very prompt and about 2/3 an hour. My husband is coeliac and they made him G.F pasta. The staff are so happy in their work. The cocktails in the Skyline bar are very tasty and a must for a great view of Venice.  The room have wonderful beds large and firm. The bathrooms as also lovely. I would defiantly recommend this hotel and it is good to be away from all the crowds.  Across the canal on the shuttle boat is Zattera.
