I've been fortunate to visit this hotel on a number of occasions and just love it, originally a flour mill its been converted into a gorgeous quality hotel. The renovation work has been done to a high class and the quality of the hotel rooms linked to the old features of the mill are a joy. Although the hotel is on the Giudecca side of Venice you can catch the hotel boat across to St Marks or walk to the local water boat stop and travel across from there. The hotel now charge 6 euros for use of their boat throughout your stay which is peanuts although it use to be free. Whilst you are not in the centre of Venice there are some great restaurants now on the Giudecca side if you want to eat out there or its a quick trip to St Marks from the hotel.
