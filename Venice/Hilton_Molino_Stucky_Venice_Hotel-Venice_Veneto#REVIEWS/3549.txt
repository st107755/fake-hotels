A few tips that might make you decide to stay here or not.  - i don't buy the 'its off the beaten track' and therefore 'it is nice to be away from the hustle and bustle' -  so if you like convenience of popping out when you want don't fall for that selling point.  - the shuttle is ok, i think we used it 9 or 10 times and probably 4 or 5 we had to wait 20 minutes for the next one as it was full.  I think we were on it and others were left behind 2 or 3 times, meaning almost all the time it was full.  we ended up getting to the stop 15 minutes before it was due just to ensure we got on - meaning many wasted minutes hanging about.  if you are staying here you can get the water bus (stop is 5 minutes away, cost is 7 euro or you can buy a daily, 2 day etc pass).
