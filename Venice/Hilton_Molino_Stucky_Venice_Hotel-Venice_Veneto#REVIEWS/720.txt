This hotel is across the water from the crowds and madness of main Venice - this side has a number of lovely places to coffee and lunch but the hotel is a disappointment.  I think this is due to the volume of people coming through the property there is little attention to detail.  We were there pre cruise for three nights and were given a room on the lowest floor - which smelt damp and fusty in the hallway.  On the first night the air con was broken and we couldn’t open the window due to mosquitoes.  We nearly cooked.  The room had stained carpet which was not in keeping with the Hilton name. As like many of the Europe hotels there was little or no English television and no option of movies in house.
