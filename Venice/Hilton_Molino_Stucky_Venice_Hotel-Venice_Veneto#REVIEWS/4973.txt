We booked this hotel for £120 per night (excluding breakfast), which was stated to be 50% off the standard rate for the time of our visit. If returning we would not want to pay any more than this rate as it probably represents fair value for this hotel. Upon arrival, we were offered the option to add breakfast to the room for €15 per day per person, which we did.   All in all our stay in this hotel was a pleasant one. The room was clean, spacious, comfortable and classy. It benefitted from a large marble bathroom and large bed.  The roof-top pool has a great view over Venice. The pool and surrounding area is small for the size of the hotel, but we did not find this a problem and managed to get sun beds every time.
