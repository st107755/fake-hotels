We are avid travellers and have supported the Hilton group on all our travels, but the Hilton Stucky has made me question my loyalty!  Yes, the views are awesome, but, the Stucky lacks a personal touch when it comes to service.  Staff are unhelpful and generally devoid of hospitality.  Hotel charges are extortionate when it comes to internet and mini bar facilities!  I felt like the hotels primary aim was to extort as much money from its clients as possible!
