We've just spent 4 nights at the Molino Stucky Hilton and were highly impressed by every aspect of the hotel.   We occupied a Tower Suite which had a fantastic panoramic view of the lagoon across to the Zattere promenade and to San Marco in the distance. The room itself was magnificent with quality furnishings throughout, a lovely bedroom with enormous bed, superb marble bathroom, a lobby area, second bathroom and huge lounge with dining area. There were flat screen TVs in the lounge and bedroom which unusually provided BBC1 and 2 - top marks Hilton!   The hotel is based around the expansive lobby area which is opulent and well served by plenty of willing staff.
