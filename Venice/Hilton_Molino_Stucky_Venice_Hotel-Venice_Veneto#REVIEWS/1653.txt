We arrived here from Rome and loved the hotel from the very start.  The building is a converted mill, and once inside, the reception and lobby area oozes class.  We had booked a Deluxe room, and had a pleasant view of the sea and hotel complex.  The bathroom had similar views, too.  The hotel has a wonderful, frequent shuttle (boat) service to the heart of Venice (St. Mark's Square), in the evening another stop is made closer to the hotel (very convenient for seeing other parts of Venice which are less touristy).  The schedule runs from very early in the morning to very late at night, so only those staying out into the small hours would experience any problem returning to the hotel.
