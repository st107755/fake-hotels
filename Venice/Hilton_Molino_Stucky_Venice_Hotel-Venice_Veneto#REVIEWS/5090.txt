We stayed in this hotel before our cruise last May 2012. The PROS:  they have a private dock for the water taxi from the airport, which I'm thankful for since  I have seen so many people dragging their luggage around the main island! Clean, modern hotel and surroundings, rooftop pool with a nice view of Venice, great breakfast buffet, there is a public shuttle that takes you to the cruise port for 8 euros per person and there's a shuttle to take you to Zattere and San Marco. I had more fun walking around this island and took a lot of pictures without a hordes of tourist in the background!
