We stayed in room A105 an executive king room. Room size was average with an average bathroom which included a bathtub and shower combination (no  separate shower). I immediately realized the wifi was not complimentary which is not acceptable for an executive room, or any room for that matter! Wifi was 9 euros a day. Free wifi was available in the executive lounge on the 6th floor. View from the room was great, room had a front view. The noise from the vaporetto stop right in front of the hotel may be bothersome for some but I am a heavy sleeper. We could hear the people in the room next to us late at night and in the morning, soundproofing could be better.
