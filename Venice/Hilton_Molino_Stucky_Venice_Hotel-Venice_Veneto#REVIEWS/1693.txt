This hotel is located on the Giudecca island opposite the main island of Venice. It has a regular free shuttle service across to the frontage near St Marks Square. For us it had 2 huge plus points. Firstly it was a wonderfully quiet island to return to via a very pleasant 10 minute water crossing to escape the crowds and bustle of Venice. Secondly it had a fabulous roof terrace with surely the best view across to this lovely city. There is a swimming pool up there and nice furniture to sit and enjoy the sunshine even in October. Our room in this huge converted flour mill was spacious and comfortable.
