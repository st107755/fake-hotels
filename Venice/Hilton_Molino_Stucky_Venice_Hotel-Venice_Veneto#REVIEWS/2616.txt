We stayed at this hotel the night before departing for a Costa Cruise. Most guests seemed to be coming/going from the Cruise Terminal.  The ALILAGUNA water taxi Blue Line can pick you up from the Venice Airport and bring you right to the doors of this hotel. Take this instead of the private water taxis, they are much cheaper and really do the same job!  When leaving for your Cruise, get on that same ALILAGUNA water taxi Blue Line, and you will go 1-stop across the canal to the Cruise Terminal. So easy.  We ate at the ROMI restaurant for dinner. Dine al fresco (outside) if you can and soak up the sunset over the canal. Try the Chef's Pasta Interpretation and the Beef Carpaccio... expensive, but both are delicious!
