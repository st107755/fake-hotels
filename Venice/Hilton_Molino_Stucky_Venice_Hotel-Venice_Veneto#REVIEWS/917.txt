My boyfriend booked this hotel as a surprise mini getaway and it was a lovely surprise. The hotel is huge, the building has a lot of character which makes it even more special. The hotel has a shuttle boat service which takes you to and from Guidecca to Venice main land which was very useful. At first there was a lot of confusion as to how, where and the costing of the shuttle service. So basically, you can get the Hilton shuttle boat service from directly outside the hotel the hotel will charge 6euros per person for your stay. At first, we though that meant 6euros each for every ride. However, we took the shuttle boat numerous times and were charged at the end of our stay when checking out 12euros. This would make us come back and stay at the Hilton Molino Stucky again.
