I've stayed here several times and have never been disappointed... in my room, the excellent staff, the location, and the food --  including the wonderful breakfast buffet.  The views are spectacular; it's an easy 15 minute boat ride to St. Mark's or the train station as well as a direct stop on the boat line to the airport.  The hotel is a piece of Venetian history with modern amenities including newly upgraded rooms although my not-yet-upgraded suite was full of old world charm.  The full service gym and spa were great (I had an very good massage at a very reasonable price.) The location, on Guidecca a terrific part of Venice in its own right, suits me perfectly.  Close enough to everything but also a peaceful sanctuary away from the hordes of tourists.
