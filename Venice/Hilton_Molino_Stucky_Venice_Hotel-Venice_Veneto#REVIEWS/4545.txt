We stayed for one night at the Molino Stucky Hilton Hotel in Venice.  When we arrived to check in, we had been upgraded to a beautiful suite (we had booked an executive room with lounge access).  It had a huge bedroom and living room and a kitchen area with a view of the river (room 119 - highly recommended!).    Breakfast was lovely.  Loads of choice.  Concierge was excellent and recommended a beautiful restaurant.  Everything in Venice is expensive but with executive lounge access at the hotel you get free drinks and also snacks in the early evening.    The sky bar at the hotel is on the 8th floor.  Fantastic bar but there was a private party when we were there which meant one of the roof terraces was out of use for hotel guests.
