I stayed here with my husband and four children ( age 10-15) before a cruise in July 2012.  I booked the hotel after reading a Telegraph review recommending it for children as it has a pool!  Well not only does it have a pool - but I discovered - and I dont think Hilton advertise this enough ... but if you are travelling with children then each room with those children in are reduced by 50% of the first room - eg if two adults and 1 child have a room at 160 euros a night then the next room with 3 children in is just 80 euros!  This made Venice a very viable proposition to us and since BA fly there from London at very reasonable rates and include 23 Kilos of luggage per person this made it an ideal cruise location for us!
