Three nights in Venice which was one of our best trips ever. The water taxi ride from the airport has to be one of life's most exhilarating ways to arrive ar any hotel! Check in staff absolutely charming but we arrive in our room to find no bed for our daughter. Disappointing when you have an excited 7 year old and although very swiftly remedied is a poor start of not getting the basics right. Room was immediately too hot; keep fiddling with the AC to no avail and door window stuck solid so could let no fresh air in. On speaking to reception am told that they cannot turn the AC on by law until May. I understand the reasons for this but if sleep quality is compromised any traveller needs to be aware.
