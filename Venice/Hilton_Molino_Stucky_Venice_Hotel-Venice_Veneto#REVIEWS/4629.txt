An excellent hotel essentially conceived for hosting conferences and events but also an excellent option for tourism too, if one accepts to be a little far away from the centre of Venice. I was there with my wife and my youngest daughter.  The room was ample also for three people and very nice. I was in Venice with my wife several times. It is one of the most wonderful cities of the world. Every time we stayed in different location ranging from San Marco to the Lido. This location, on the island of Giudecca is interesting and provides another view to approach Venice even if I would not choose it as a first option if it is your first time in Venice because I prefer staying around San Marco but this is a matter of taste.
