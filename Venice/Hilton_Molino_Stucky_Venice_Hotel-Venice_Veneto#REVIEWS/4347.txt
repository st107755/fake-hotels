I booked this via the BA website as part of a deal, and I must say it was very good value for the price.   On the plus side:  - the staff really make this hotel. Very friendly and helpful. From the concierge to the breakfast staff. I was impressed.  - this is one of only TWO hotels in Venice which has a rooftop pool. Despite there being 394 rooms, and the hotel running at a constant 80% occupancy (so a member of staff told me), you could still get a chair or lounger around the pool if you were patient mid-afternoon (this was peak season - July).  - the hotel shuttle boat service. Free and efficient, you're in San Marco in minutes. There are also two other water boat stops on the island the hotel is located on.  - the views of Venice.
