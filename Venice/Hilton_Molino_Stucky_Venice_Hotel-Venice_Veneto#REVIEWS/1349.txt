We arrived by private water taxi to the hotel's own little docking station. Warmly greeted by the concierge, and likewise the reception staff. The hotel is on an island a few minutes water ride from St Mark's Square and is served well by its own water shuttle. We never had to wait more that 10 minutes for the shuttle and always had a seat. The island itself has many restaurants and is very pleasant to walk around in the evening away from the hustle and bustle of Venice city itself. The hotel is a converted flour mill, and has been beautifully restored. Our room was very good, with an excellent shower and bathroom facilities and a very large comfortable bed. The roof top bar is exquisite and the views of Venice from up there are really breathtaking.
