If you want to get luxury hotel in Venice , stay in the Hilton. Old building with high maintenance, nice and clean rooms, view to the main canal,  excellent breakfast, and nice roof with nice pool. We warry at the beginning from auther reviews about the timing of the water taxi to the main island,  But there was many water taxi, all over from the hotel and from a nearby station. People was very helpful with directions. The best way and the cheapest is to buy 3-4 days pass, additional to the hotel shuttles.  I sugest also to use the VIP lunch at the Hilton, and add the money to the reservation because there is no many places to buy snacks or drinks on this island, the only option is to take the water taxi to the main island, it will save you money and time.
