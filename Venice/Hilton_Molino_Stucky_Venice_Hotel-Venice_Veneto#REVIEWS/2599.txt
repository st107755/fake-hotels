This property is located just across the Guidecca canal from Zattere and is an easy walk to St. Marks Square.  The hotel has their own boat taxi and it runs frequently.  We never had to wait more than about 5 minutes. This property worked out well for us because the Venice Film Festival was going on and Venice was packed with people. The distance was just far enough to still feel like Venice but we were able to avoid the crowds. I believe the shuttle ran until 11 PM so you could still enjoy the Venice restaurants and night life.  Our room was small but we also got a very good rate so didn't feel we could complain.  Our air wasn't working well either so it was small AND stuffy.
