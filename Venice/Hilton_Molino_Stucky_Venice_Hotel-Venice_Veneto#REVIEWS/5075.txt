First warning -- this is a big hotel with big hotel service.  The flip side is that it had big hotel rooms which were much more spacious than rooms in historic city centers.  The rooms were clean, quiet, and everything was well run.  The hotel itself had tons of amenities including an AMAZING rooftop pool and Skybar.  This place easily had the best views of Venice.  The only potential downside is the location.  It's a little ways from Venice city central and maybe not ideal if you want to walk at night.  The flip side (and positive for us) is that the island of Guideca is absolutely charming and we'd stay here again if going go Venice.  The hotel also has a really great shuttle to San Marco square that runs until 1 am!!
