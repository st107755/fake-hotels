Very good, if not excellent, Hilton style service in an impressive converted food factory and mill on an island across from the main attractions of Venice. Rooms are large, very comfortable , pay extra for the Venice view, it is worth it. Buffet breakfast has huge choice, but this is a big hotel and at breakfast it feels like it as guests spread across 3 dining rooms. Internet is charged for in the room, free in the lobby, which is not really acceptable today and prices in the bar are high. If you want to exercise their is a gym - small for the hotel size and difficult to find if out of hours.  Concierge is good for restaurant recommendations and were always helpful.
