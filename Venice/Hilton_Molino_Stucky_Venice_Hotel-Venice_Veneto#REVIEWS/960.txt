We stayed here recently as part of a tour around Italy; we chose this hotel mainly because of its quirky location.  It's on the island of Giuadecca which is separate from the main part of Venice, so the hotel can only be reached by boat.  The hotel organises a shuttle launch on a very regular basis which is charged at six euros per person per stay, I'm just a bit surprised that this can't be absorbed into the total cost of the hotel because it's not really a cheap experience!
