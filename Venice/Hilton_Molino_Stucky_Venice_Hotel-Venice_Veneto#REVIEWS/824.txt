This wonderful Hilton Hotel provides a complete Venice experience from its canal side arrival onwards.  Tastefully converted from a flour mill, Molina Stucky is beautifully presented throughout. The details enhance its industrial heritage without making it feel anything other than luxurious. Well finished and if the bedroom chandelier was not Murano it was an outstanding replica. Their excellent staff maintained a high, welcoming standard during every part of our stay.  Excellent selection in the breakfast buffet although freshly prepared eggs to order seemed unavailable. We enjoyed dinner in 2 of the hotel’s restaurants.
