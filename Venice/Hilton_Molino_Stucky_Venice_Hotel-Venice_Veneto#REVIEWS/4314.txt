The hotel is large so it's easy to lose your way inside but it's a beautiful hotel on the island of Giudecca with a free shuttle across the canal.  The service was wonderful - we were greeted with the offer of a cold drink as our room was not quite ready so a nice cold beer was very much appreciated.  The rooftop pool offers wonderful views of Venice but it can be crowded later in the day when all the crowds get back from their day trips so try to use the pool in the morning or early afternoon.  At night, the rooftop bar is a wonderful place to take in the night lights of Venice and we ended most of our evenings there.  The breakfast buffet was excellent although the coffee leaves a lot to be desired.
