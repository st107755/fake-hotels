The hotel is really excellent. Most of the staff members (with some exceptions) are polite, well trained and extremely helpful. They were always ready to help with any questions. When we asked for recommendations for restaurants, the staff was never pushy and their suggestions really were spot on. The actual rooms are spacious and nicely decorated. A Venetian theme can be felt subtly throughout the hotel (without being tacky or overwhelming). The bath care range was lovely. The pool overlooks the water and the bar offers a good selection of cocktails (even if it is visited by one too many pigeons which the waiters and waitresses consistently shoo away).  If I were to be extremely picky, there are a few things which I would point out.
