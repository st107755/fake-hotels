For visiting Venice, the Hilton Molino Stucky is a smart hotel with easy shuttle boat access to the main island of Venice. Checking in was a bit of a battle having asked for an upgrade to a room facing the Giudecca Canal and views of Venice. After rejecting two rooms which had barely any view at all, I concluded that an upgrade of 44 euros a night wasn't worth having so we took a room facing the courtyard instead. You would probably need to pay considerably more for a good Canal facing view, but given that you probably wouldn't spend much time in your room in a place like Venice - and much of that unconscious - it probably isn't worth going for an upgrade. The room itself was fine, the beds very comfortable.
