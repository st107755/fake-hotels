I have stayed twice at the Molino Stucky, which is a converted 19th century mill. The hotel is modern, beautiful and comfortable. The staff is helpful and polite. It is a boat ride away from the central locations, but I don’t see it as a problem because the hotel offers a free shuttle boat service that runs from morning to around midnight, with a pause at lunchtime. On our first time there, we got a room with a lateral view of the canal. On our second stay, we had an executive room, on the 7th floor, with a stunning view overlooking the Grand Canal. The room is spacious, with a very comfortable bed, a LED TV and a good-sized wardrobe. The bathroom has a big counter space and is well illuminated.
