I stayed in this hotel for three days in late September. It is certainly spectacular, with particular attention paid to the small details (in true Hilton fashion). The shuttle boat to and from San Mark's square was very convenient and always on time. My only problem was that it was possible for me to hear EVERYTHING that went on in both adjoining rooms, where people were not being specially noisy.
