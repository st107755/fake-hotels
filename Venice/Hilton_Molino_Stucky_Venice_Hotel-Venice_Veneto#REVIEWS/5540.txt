As others have mentioned, the hotel requires a boat ride.  Rather then being reliant on the shuttle (which is always on time and works great, just not as often) we bought Vaparetto tickets.  We then took whichever boat came first.  With this solution I don't think we ever waited more than 5 minutes for a boat.  The Molino Stucky is large and mostly full of Americans.  Those, to me, are it's downsides.  On the upsides the building is beautiful.  The views of Venice are stunning (especially from the rooftop pool).  As a Hilton Diamond member I got an upgrade to a giant suite -- it really made our stay much more enjoyable.  Also, we quite enjoyed the executive lounge being in the lobby.
