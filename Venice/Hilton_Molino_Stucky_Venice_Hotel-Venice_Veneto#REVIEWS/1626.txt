Took the private shuttle boat to the hotel from the stop on San Marco. It was included in the price of the room and the shuttle could be taken as much as you want to get almost anywhere in Venice. My first problem was that we had booked a room for 2 with breakfast and paid for the room in advance. a third party ended up going with us, so the hotel upgrades us to a suite, for a price of 100 Euro, which was a deal. Thee room was fabulous! The restaurant service however, left much to be desired. They got our orders wrong and were very slow in correcting it. The spa, which was an additional fee, had a cold "hot tub", which was the main reason we went, however to their credit they immediately refunded us our entrance fees. The Concierge, was unbelievable.
