We were a little worried about staying here as it is based on the Island of Guidecca, but it could not have been more perfect! After visiting the crowded, touristy St. Mark's square, we were relieved we had an oasis to retreat to that was away from the crowds. It was very easy to get here on the public water bus from the train station, and the free water shuttle (with two separate stops) through the day/evening was so convenient. The room was beautifully decorated with marble and high ceilings, but the real beauty is from the roof top pool or bar. WOW! It was such a unique way to view/experience Venice. The breakfast is expensive (15 Euro each) but was very good, and had some of the best coffee we had in Italy.
