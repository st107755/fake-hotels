When you first arrive to Venice, it seems like a nightmare trying to get to this hotel. The best way to get here from the train station is to catch the water metro line #2 (located right outside the train station) and get off at Sacca Fisola.   The hotel is located about a 20 min boat ride from the main area in Venice but it's actually quite nice because it allows you to appreciate the canals every day as you are getting to the main area. We always got on line #2 and never went on the hilton shuttle as it was less frequent and doesn't even take you to a main area.   The hotel was very large and quite nice. Beds are comfy (including the foldout).
