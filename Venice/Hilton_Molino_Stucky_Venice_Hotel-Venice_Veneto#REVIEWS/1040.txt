Despite being a Hilton Elite member, I'd never stayed at the Molino Stucky despite many trips to Venice because it just seemed so far away across the Giudecca Canal.  I am so, so glad that I finally made the decision to stay there.  During my four-day stay, everything came as close to perfection as I can possibly imagine.  I'd written ahead in my so-so Italian, and was greeted in Italian by a very enthusiastic and wonderful reception desk.  I managed to make out everything they told me, then went to my room on the 2nd floor.  This is important -- get a room on the 1st or 2nd floor because when you open your window, it seems like you're right on the sea.
