My husband and I stayed at this hotel as part of our Europe trip summer 2011. Neither one of us had been to Venice before and didn't know what to expect. We thought we'd book a bigger name like Hilton. We drove from Tuscany to Venice. After we parked the car and were getting to the hotel by boat; we thought we probably should have booked a hotel at the main island. It turned out this hotel had great shuttle boat services so getting from and to the main island was pretty easy. The hotel itself was very nice. Our room was clean and had everything we needed. The bar and pool at the roof top were super cool! The staff was all wonderful. I have to especially mention a customer service person named Constance.
