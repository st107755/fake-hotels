I Visited Venice for the first time.  I chose this hotel because it had a roof top pool.  This hotel was more expensive then I wanted but every time I kept checking other hotels I couldn't get the pool out of my head. So I booked it! When we arrived from the train station, I hopped on a private water taxi for 60 euros .  That was the best water taxi ride ever.  I felt like I was in a movie cruising down the canals of Venice.  We are a family of 5, so we needed two rooms. When we arrived the staff was there to greet us and handle our bags.  The rooms were super clean! The pool was amazing, the view from atop left me speechless. This hotel provided a free water taxi to bring us back and forth to the main island, about 10 mins.
