For our Venice trip we choose this Hilton hotel. As we have 4 kids, we looked for some space and this hotel offered a nice junior suite with separate bedroom (that has a door) while two kids sleep on a sofa bed. For the other two elder kids we had another room close to our room. The hotel is nice, what you can expect when choosing a Hilton hotel. The first night we had dinner in the lobby. Fair food for money but nothing too special. Venice has a lot more to offer. Very nice though is the terrace with a hip bar on the roof of the hotel, with spectacular views over the city and water. As it was too early in the year the roof pool was not yet open. Breakfast is very well done with a lot (!) of choice.
