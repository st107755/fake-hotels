My wife and I stayed here for one night while we visited Venice and we were not disappointed! We booked a private transfer from the airport to the hotel which included a 20 min drive via private car and then a 10 minute water taxi ride (Only way to the hotel, we did not realize that until a few days prior!) This was the easiest way to go although a little more expensive. If you want there is a public shuttle to the island but it is a 10 min walk from the airport, with bags would be a pain, and it takes almost an hour and half to get there where as this was 30 minutes. Once you actually arrive at the hotel they have a Hilton water bus that takes you to the city center for only 8.95 I believe as a one time fee and you can go back and forth.
