This Hilton wins the price for the most beautiful location in our experience. The hotel is beautiful inside and out, and our room was perfect. The downside was that the public areas were stiflingly hot - we complained to the manager who made the excuse that it was 35c outside with 90% humidity- really? We live in Florida where those temps and humidity are normal but never have we experienced this level of discomfort in a Hilton hotel.  Also the executive lounge is very limited in both size and service - breakfast service and then evening hors  d’oeuvres for 2 hours  only.  In defense of the hotel, I have to say that some of the guests using the lounge were incredibly greedy and rude, but other Hilton  hotels offer far more service in their executive lounges.
