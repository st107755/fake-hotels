I am not a big fan of this Hilton as I don't think it lives up to the brand. I gave it an average rating mainly because it is just an average hotel for the price.  We knew it was not on San Marco when we booked but wanted to give it a chance even after reading some of the reviews. I believe we also stayed in one of the smaller buildings of the hotel that was not as updated so my review may be a bit skewed due to that but I can only review what I experienced.  First, the lobby was very nice and check in was speedy and efficient. The restaurant and bar area were very nice.  Our only meal there was a late night meal just after we arrived and the food was good but service was just average especially since we were the only ones eating at the time.
