This is one of the true 5-star hotels that lives up to its billing.  It is by far the nicest hotel experience that I have had in visiting over 30 countries and hundreds of cities around the world.  The service is first rate to every person on the staff.  There are more nooks and crannies in this hotel for meetings and socializing as well as all the well-adorned expected amenities, spa, restaurants, etc, that you would want in business or leisure travel.  The only downside for some people would be the extra water taxi ride to cross over to the main sections of Venice.
