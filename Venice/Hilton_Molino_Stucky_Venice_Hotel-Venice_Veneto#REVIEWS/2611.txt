Unique hotel created from an old flour mill, so unusual design and some history.  We stayed in September 2015 for 3 nights.  It was quiet away from the hustle and bustle of Venice.  There is a regular boat shuttle with two stops in Venice for which a small charge is made by the hotel.  The staff were always helpful and informative.   The wifi was free.  The best feature for me was the swimming pool on the roof, especially after a day of walking around Venice and it was so refreshing and nice to cool down.  The view from the roof was amazing and you could see for some distance and make sense of all the islands.  Our room was good, a nice comfortable bed, TV, safe, desk etc.  The only thing that was negative was there was a small amount of mould around the bath seal.
