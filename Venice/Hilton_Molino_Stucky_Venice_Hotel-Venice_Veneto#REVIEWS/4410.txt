We arrived in Venice on Friday night - tired but excited about our first visit to Venice. We were only staying one night and then to a cruise.  We were dropped off at the side entrance by a river boat to the Hilton. No one to meet us. We then carried out own luggage to the front entrance. On arrival one gets the impression you have entered a railway station. At reception we were checked in, and direct to the lift. Finding it was a challenge whilst dragging our luggage behind us. None of the usual "did you have a good journey" or "breakfast is served at" etc. Having got to our room 40 mins passed and no luggage. I  called the concierge and after a short abrupt conversation he said he would check and call back. No call back so we called again.
