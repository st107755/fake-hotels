We arrived in Venice on the 16th of July after spending a week in Cortona. It was a family reunion and had four rooms booked at the Hilton for 8 people. One family came with us decided to check into another hotel as they were not interested in sharing a room. Next morning we met with the Ricardo Ranzato, Guest relations manger. who in my view is one of worst person to deal with. He is rude, lacked professionalism and a ruffian.
