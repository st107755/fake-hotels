This Hilton can be rated as one of the nicest I have stayed at. As a long term Gold member, I was impressed with the quality of the rooms and common areas.   The executive lounge was a little "different" as it was located right beside the bar and lobby area where the lounge could be viewed in it's entirety from the "common areas" (Like being in a fish bowl.)   The lounge occupied a wonderful space right beside the canal, with window seats and comfortable chairs.   Personally I don't like the feeling of being "privileged" and having the "un-privileged" guests looking in on me.....just an uncomfortable feeling that others would think that I am better than them, which is something that is simply not the case.
