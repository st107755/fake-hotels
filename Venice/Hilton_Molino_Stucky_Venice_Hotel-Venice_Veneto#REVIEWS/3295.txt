I had a tough time deciding between 2 Venetian hotels that I was getting comparably good deals on online -- the Hilton and a boutique hotel on San Marco. I went with the Hilton for the brand name and because I wasn't that familiar with Venice itself. Looking back, I probably would have chosen the boutique hotel - but it's all based on what experience you want.   There are some people that would opt to stay away from staying on San Marco. True, it's crowded, maybe a bit noisy (though actually very quiet in the evenings when I visited in Dec.). However, it's perfectly safe, and walking through San Marco, I saw tons of cute little places that I'm sure would have been a lot of fun to stay in, being so close to everything.   The Hilton is removed - on the island of Giudecca.
