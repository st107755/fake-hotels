This is a beautiful property on a sleepy island in Venice.  The rooms are big and comfortable and very clean.   Here are the cons: the hotel is far from the main islands and the shuttle books up in the morning, when you are trying to get out for your tours.  The next shuttle is 40 minutes later.  The concierge Federica (see below) immediately took care of the problem. The rooms have TERRIBLE sound proofing.  You can hear the alarm go off in the room next door, the people talking, coughing, sneezing, and blow drying their hair. Bring all of your booking documents with you -- they told us they did not have the rooms we reserved but I had printed out my emails with the reservations manager from 8 months ago and suddenly without explanation the rooms became available.
