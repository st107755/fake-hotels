I have stayed at this hotel twice and thought i had reviewed it before, but apparently not so here goes.  First off, the hotel is not in the main touristy parts of venice, but close enough for me.  I have been to Venice many times and have stayed in the central locations close to the sights before so that was not a big confer for me.  Also, the hotel has a water shuttle that will take you to st mark's square in no time at all.  If you have never been to Venice before then maybe you might want to stay on the main island, but honestly, the accommodations in the touristy areas are not great and anything comparable to the Hilton will cost you 2-3 times more than the hilton.
