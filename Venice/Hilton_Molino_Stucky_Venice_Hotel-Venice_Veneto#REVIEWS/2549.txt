Hotels in Venice are expensive.Some tourists stay in surrounding area and just visit Venice for the day. It is a great mistake, as staying in this beautiful dream like city is an experience you should not missed. If you decide to come, Venice is not the place to save the pennies. It cost 3.50 euro for the whole duration of your stay. The boat pass the custom house, San Giorgio church before decked a few feet from the Bridge of Sign.
