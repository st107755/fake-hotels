We booked this hotel as part of Hilton's annual sale, so the rate we paid was 140EUR p/n. When I checked the room price on the door, it was 895 EUR! Mind you, as Hilton Diamond members, we were upgraded to a Executive corner room on the 5th floor in Arsenal. I don't think we would ever be this lucky in high season! The room was beautifully furnished, with luxurious curtains and very comfortable beds. The bathroom was also very posh.   The only drawback was that wi-fi did not extend to rooms and you need to plug in for the internet. On the positive side, free wi-fi is available in the lobby and there are also a couple of complimentary PCs for guests to use.
