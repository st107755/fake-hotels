Having stayed a few years ago at a very expensive hotel on the Grand Canal, I found it the location very hectic, so wanted to try something a little quieter this time around. Staying on Guidecca was great as the Hilton runs a shuttle  boat that makes getting back and forth to St Mark's Square easy. The hotel itself is well set out and has what I would call "American style service" meaning they leave you to do some things yourself. For example, a coffee maker in the room and an iron (with a long cable) and ironing board. No need to call room service for such simple things. A couple of issues: (i) I would have liked to have seen smoked salmon at the breakfast buffet. All hotels I have recently stayed at seem to have it so I was surprised the Hilton did not.
