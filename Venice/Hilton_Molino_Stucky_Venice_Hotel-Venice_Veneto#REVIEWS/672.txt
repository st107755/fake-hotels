If you are flying into Venice, I would recommend contacting the hotel beforehand and purchasing their personal airport pickup service. You will be glad you did. We were greeted at the airport by a Hilton representative who helped us with our luggage and escorted us to a van, which drove us to a dock where a private water taxi was waiting.   The pickup service and transportation to the hotel includes a tour of the Grand Canal on your way to the Hilton, and it’s a fantastic way to start your stay. The hotel is off the shores of Venice, so a boat ride is the only way to get there, and this way was effortless.   The hotel rooms are very small, and even though we were upgraded and had a room on the water with a view, it’s a tiny window.
