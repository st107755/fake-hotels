If your chain of choice is Hilton you'll be at home. I was amazed to learn that the conversion is relatively new as the rooms feel incredibly dated with dark veneer furniture and bling fittings. The bed was very comfortable, sleep quality great and the bathroom functioned perfectly so it ticks the most important boxes. Wifi in the communal areas is adequate though slow and ludicrously expensive in the rooms. The hotel's shuttle operates a limited service, if you want to get into Venice at other times it's a 10 minute walk to the public boat through a less than salubrious (though seemingly safe) part of the city.  If you want to visit Venice from a sanitised distance and feel safe in an international chain, I recommend this hotel.
