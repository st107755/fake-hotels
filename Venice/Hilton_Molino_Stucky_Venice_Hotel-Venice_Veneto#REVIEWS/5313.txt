We really enjoyed our stay at the Hiltom Molino Stucky in Venice.    The location was great.  The views of Venice at night were really excellent.  You are just a free boat ride away from St. Marc's and the hustle and bustle of the tourists' Venice ... but you are far enough away that it feels more relaxed somehow.  We didn't have problems with the free shuttle.  It was always on time.  It worked for us.  If we were in between shuttles and wanted to get back, we just took one of the city water taxis.    The staff was great.  Always friendly.  Good recommendations and service.    Executive lounge was good.  Not as good as some Hilton International Exec Lounges (like Cavalerie Hilton Rome, Bangkok Hilton, etc.) ... but it was good enough.
