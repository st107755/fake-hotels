We stayed at this hotel 9 years ago for our honeymoon and wanted to return again. The property remains well kept and just as charming as I remember.  Quick boat shuttle into Venice. There were a few times the shuttle was full and we had to wait for the next; the only drawback. May is not peak season so if you have somewhere to go, consider private water taxi or getting in queue early.  We ate at the rooftop bar Skyline and the Aromi patio. Skyline is great for drinks and maybe small plates- we were underwhelmed for the price paid. Aromi was worth the price. Outstanding!   Loved the rooftop pool but lounge chairs can be hard to find.  At minimum head up to enjoy the views of the city with a drink from the bar!  It’s a big property so it was a little noisy.
