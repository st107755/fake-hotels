Unlike other reviewers we did our homework before staying at the Hilton. we were aware that it was on an island and that we would have to use a shuttle boat to get into VEnice. That was fine and worked well for us. We were also aware that Venice was an expensive city, and were prepared to pay. We also knew that this was a converted Mill and not a conventional hotel and as such had a unique appearance and interior. The hotel was huge, one of the largest that I have ever stayed in. Rooftop bar was excellent. food good, as was bar food downstairs in the main bar - comparable in price to Hiltons everywhere. Executive lounge good for a quick snack and relax when returning from sightseeing in Venice. Large choice for breakfast.
