As people who read my advice myself and fiancé are both diamond members at Hilton's separately and hence try and stay in them as much as we can. For our loyalty Hilton are very good to us and this time was no exception by upgrading us to a water front room. The employees are extremely helpful in the hotel and even though the Hilton, free, shuttle is needed to go over to the main island it did not cause us any problems. The executive lounge is small and during our stay busy but another great benefit. Some watch outs though - laundry on a Saturday and Sunday has a 50% surcharge which made our laundry some of the most expensive I have ever had - we have stayed at the Waldorf in shanghai and Conrad in Tokyo so be warned!
