1st ever time to venice, initially worried about being off the mainland on the guiedecca island. However we booked the shared boat transfer there (very easy connection at Marco Polo airport.) and were fortunate to only have one other couple sharing, meaning it was quick easy and we were alone for half the trip so a magical start. On the return having not booked a transfer and having a flight at 1850 we planned to take the free boat to St Marks Square and then have a walk to the coach station 45mins but a beautiful walk.. So asked for a late check out and were amazed when the hotel accommodated us till 2pm for no charge..!! All I can say is the staff were first rate from start to finish.. Provided bath robes and extra towels within 10 mins of asking..
