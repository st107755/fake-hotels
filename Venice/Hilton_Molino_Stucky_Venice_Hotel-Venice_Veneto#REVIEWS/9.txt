This Hilton was by far the best we’ve ever stayed in. We were in the new part of the hotel, in the premier king room. The room was gorgeous, very spacious with lovely views and sunsets. It was beautifully decorated and spotless - you felt at ease without constantly being reminded of covid.   The hotel sits on Guidecca island, and is walking distance from several great local restaurants and bars. The Hilton has its own private shuttle boat for guests, which runs hourly to San Marco and takes just 15 mins. The roof top pool was absolutely stunning, with 360 degree views of Venice and beyond. At the moment, you have to book an hour slot each day, we recommend you do this at the start of your stay, for the week, then you don’t have to think about it each day.
