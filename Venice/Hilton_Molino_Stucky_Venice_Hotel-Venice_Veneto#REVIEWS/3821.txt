The Hilton Molino Stucky is a nice, clean and modern hotel with American standard 5-star amenities, but other than that, we don’t have anything else good to say about this hotel.  Services were rather disappointing.  While some of the staffs tried hard to serve us, most of the personnel had an indifferent attitude.  We noticed there were several big groups at the hotel during our stay.  Due to the considerable size of this hotel, companies often place their customers in this hotel as they can book a large block of rooms.  Maybe that is why the hotel staffs were so uncaring as they were just tired of dealing with so many guests at the same time.    The location of this hotel is simply horrible.
