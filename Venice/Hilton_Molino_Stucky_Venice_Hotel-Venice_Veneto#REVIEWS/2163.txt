I stayed here as the last leg of an Italy tour. Being last we decided to take a break from B&B's and pamper ourselves and it turned out to be the best decision we took.   The hotel is on a separate island a short couple of minutes from main touristic parts which i took as a benefit since this meant it was quiet and not crowded. Venice itself is too crowded so a break from that was great. Also the shutttle ride back and forth was a a great experience in itself.  The rooms are excellent. Breakfast was great with lots of variety. The staff is very helpful and friendly. The free shuttle service was our main way back and forth and it was very timely and frequent.
