I stayed here for two nights pre-cruise and there cannot be a better choice. The room behind the historic façade is very nicely decorated and well-equipped. It is quite spacious with a King bed, unusual for Venice. The service is as good as a Waldorf-Astoria above many Hiltons that I stayed, everyone is helpful. The rooftop pool and bar is really spectacular: you should come for a drink even if you do not stay here for an incomparable view over Giudecca Canal. It has been a perfect stay to recover from jetlag for the cruise, and it is great that there is a waterbus stop right outside the hotel for direct transfer to the cruise terminal at only 7 Euros. Some people questioned the location and the frequency of the free shuttle, but these do not bother me a bit.
