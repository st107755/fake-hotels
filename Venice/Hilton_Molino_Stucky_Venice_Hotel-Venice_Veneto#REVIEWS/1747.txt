Very big Hilton hotel - an old flour mill.  We (2 American couples, 60's) stayed as part of a Viking cruise 'city stay.'  Viking had about 200 people on our cruise staying here, and there were several other tour companies who also had large groups staying there.  All of these groups got (1) free breakfast buffet, (2) free use of the Hilton water taxi over to main island (every 20 minutes), (3) King size beds, and (if you were a Hilton Honors) free wifi in room.  If you're a 'book it on your own' person, all of these things might cost you extra charges.  Rooms were big, spacious, lots of amenities, decent views of canal/city.  View from rooftop pool was extraordinary.  Good restaurants within walking distance down along the large canal outside the hotel front door.
