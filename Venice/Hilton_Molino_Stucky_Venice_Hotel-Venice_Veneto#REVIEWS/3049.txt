We stayed 3 nights in late April in a king deluxe room with a view. It was a very good stay. Room was large enough for the two of us and clean. As you might expect from a Hilton brand, the bathroom was larger than you typically find in Europe with both a shower and separate tub, which is a plus. We could see Piazza San Marco from our window!  As noted on most reviews, it is a bit removed from the tourist attractions on the main island, for me that was a benefit. That being said, if you don't have breakfast at the hotel options are limited on Giudecca island. I would suggest just getting passes for the vaporetto instead of using the hotel shuttle boat. The closest stop is only a 5-7 minute walk from the hotel and is served by two routes that run frequently.
