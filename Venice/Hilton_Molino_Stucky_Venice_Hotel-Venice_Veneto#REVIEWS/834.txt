We stayed here a few years ago and had a great time, so when it was time to return to Venice we booked the Hilton again. The check in was fast and pleasant and we got an upgrade to a family suite as a Hilton Diamond. The name family suite is some what missleading as it is actually a 2 bedroom suite. The room opens into a hallway and then there is a bedroom to either side. The livingroom/bedroom has a large table, a wetbar with stove and fridge, an an additional side table. Both rooms have big TVs and the beds are very comfortable.  The breakfast is outstanding and ranges from eggs with bacon and sausages to croissants, pastries, numerous types of proscuitto, hams, salami, cheeses, cereals and of course cappucinos. The hotel is located on an island across from Venice proper.
