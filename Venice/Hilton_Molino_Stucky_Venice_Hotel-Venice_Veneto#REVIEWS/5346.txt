Free is always good and I used my Hilton Honors points for a 3-night pre-cruise stay.   The hotel provides all the charm of the better hotels along or near the Grand Canal but without the closed in feeling of a lot of people in a small area.  A free water taxi runs frequently from the front door of the hotel to two popular spots across the channel and the cruise terminal is just 5-minutes away via the Alilaguna water taxi which also picks you up a the front door every minutes.  One evening my wife & I had a great waterfront dining experience sharing a nice Italian Pinot Gris and a specially prepared platter of local meats & cheeses.  The tables are nicely spaced to allow a private conversation with beautiful views across the channel.
