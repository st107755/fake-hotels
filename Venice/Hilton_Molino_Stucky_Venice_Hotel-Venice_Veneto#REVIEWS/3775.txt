We enjoyed a four night stay here recently, on first sight the hotel is very impressive and dominates the headland as an old converted mill - we were amazed by it's sheer size! Once there we were met by a man who walked us in under an umbrella as it was raining, after that check in was smooth and we were led up huge marble corridors to our well appointed sumptuous room. The room had everything we needed & was completely quiet, spotlessly clean with a huge comfy bed. We had a problem with our suitcase being damaged & would not open on arrival, the maintenance team arrived in minutes & sorted it out for us, indeed when we ordered room service, again, they were very quick.
