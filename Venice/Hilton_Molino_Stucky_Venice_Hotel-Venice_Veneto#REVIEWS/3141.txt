Hilton molino stucky was exactly what I expected. It is a great property, a little out of main venice tourist area, but great.  The bedroom was pretty good for italy. It is larger than the average hotel room and I was allocated to a room that had a million dollars view. I am one of those who do enjoy a nice eye candy view at all times, but Venice does add the charm. No complaints about the staff. Overall the hotel was what I expected. I would mention 2 things for future travelers. The first being that if you plan on staying more than 3 nights, the shuttle boat into and from San Marco will get boring. It does work well and the timetable is quite generous, but the need of relying on a boat all the time did get on my nerves.
