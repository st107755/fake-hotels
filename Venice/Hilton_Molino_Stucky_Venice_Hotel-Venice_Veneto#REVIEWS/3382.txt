This hotel was part of a cruise package.  We would never pay this much for a hotel.  It was amazing!  Beautiful roof top pool and view of the city.  It was a very comfortable room with extremely high ceilings, lots of space, and a large bathroom.  The shower was thin, but it worked just fine. The breakfast was delicious and the gluten free crackers and cookies were the best!  A fellow cruise passenger who was gluten free said they made him his own eggs for breakfast.  I was not offered that.  I was offered gluten free rolls that were really good.  Lots of fruit, cereal, glutened breads rolls croissants, cake, sliced meat, potatoes, bacon, etc.  Very nice.  The concierge service was superior.
