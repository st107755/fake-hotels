Ah the Hilton. We got a fantastic deal on our long weekend to Venice. As you approach the hotel, it stands out with the fairy lights draped across the entrance of the building, we immediately felt very excited and couldn't wait to get checked in. The hotel was clean, the staff were exceptionally friendly and I was very happy with the Champagne and Birthday cake I had arranged to be in the room -  which was a lovely surprise for my Boyfriend. The room we had was spacious, clean and everything worked.  You MUST go to the Skyline bar in the hotel, it has a really cool vibe and the cocktails are amazing. Make sure you take a look in the day, the views are incredible. We also ate at the downstairs restaurant (sorry I can't remember the name) on the first night.
