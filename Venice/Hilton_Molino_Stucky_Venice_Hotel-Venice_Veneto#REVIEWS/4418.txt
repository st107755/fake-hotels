My wife and I stayed at this hotel in June 2013.  We were given a corner room on the third floor overlooking the Canale della Giudecca from two sides (I am HHonors Silver, but that usually doesn't help me, so I think it was luck of the draw).  We had asked for a quiet room, and when we checked in the girl at reception told us we had been given a room with a view.  She told us she could give us a quieter room.  I give her a lot of credit because receptionists at most hotels, especially in Europe, just tell you it's a quiet room regardless (we had that experience in Florence).  We chose to take the room with the view and were very happy we did.  The windows have double-paned glass, which keeps out most of the noise (although not all).  The views, though, were extraordinary.
