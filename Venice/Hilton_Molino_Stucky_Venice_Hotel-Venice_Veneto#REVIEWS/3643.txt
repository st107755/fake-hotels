Our family of four stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky for a few days before leaving on a cruise.  The hotel itself was once a flour mill, with the resulting renovation being both impressive and unique.  Pros: 1.  Location.  This hotel offers the best of both worlds.  With free shuttle service to/from Venice-proper (two different drop-off locations) all of the tourist attractions and restaurants visitors can hope to see are very, very convenient.  That being said, at the end of the day, you get to leave the EXTREMELY crowded streets behind and return to the mostly-residential Guidecca.  Also, the view from the roof-top pool area offers a great photo-opportunity of Venice's "skyline." 2.  Executive Level.  We had two rooms on the executive level.
