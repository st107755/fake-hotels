I really enjoyed my stay here. The heat was unbelievable so the pool was absolute chaos but we always managed to find a place to sit. The room was nice, small, but well maintained. We were on the back of the hotel so didn't have the best view but thats what the rooftop bar is for. We spent all 4 nights drinking up there. Enricho the manager is an absolute gentleman and his team were awesome. Davide down in the lobby bar was also super helpful and very nice. The boats get pretty busy, we got turned away so make sure you're at Zattere early to avoid a full boat. The jazz on a Thursday is a must see and if you're stuck for places to eat across the canal I suggest Rossopomodoro Pizza - San Marco is AWESOME.
