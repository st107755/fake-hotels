This is a beautiful five star hotel on the island of Giudecca just 10 min away from San Marco Square on the free shuttle provided by the hotel. The only annoying aspect was no free Wifi in the room which I feel we all expect as the normal now. It was lovely getting away from the hustle and bustle of Venice, the hotel was the quietist I have ever stayed in. Room was large, good air con and very comfortable bed, bathroom large and good shower. View from rooftop bar and swimming pool was fabulous. Hotel staff were very helpful , shuttles ran every 40mins but be aware some only go to the stop before San Marco which we got off at a few times and had a lovely stroll down quieter areas to the Grand Canal.
