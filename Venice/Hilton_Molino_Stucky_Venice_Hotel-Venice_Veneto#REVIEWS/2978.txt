After getting the boat for 1.5 hours from the Airport, we panicked that the hotel seemed further out of the city than we'd liked.  Once we arrived at the hotel, we need not of worried (or cared!). The hotel is beautiful inside and out.  Firstly, they provide a private and free shuttle boat to the main island every half an hour or so (they provide you with a timetable). This worked brilliantly and we never waited more than 10 minutes for a boat.  Secondly, the room was large and beautiful. The decor is slightly old fashioned and not to my taste but it went with the Venetian feeling.  The bed was large and very comfortable.  The first night we arrived, we ordered room service as we was tired from travelling and our wedding two days before.
