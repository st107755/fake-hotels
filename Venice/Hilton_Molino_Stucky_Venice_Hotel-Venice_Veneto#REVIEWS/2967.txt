The Hilton Molino Stucky is probably the fanciest, most different and expensive Hotel in Venice.  We arrived and received a room on the 7th floor, right below the pool on the roof. (Recommended if you enjoy the pool, it's 2 minutes away, request room 705 for an 'outstanding' view). We were given room 707, but within 12 hours our TV went bad and we were transferred across the hall to an even better room, 705 (request this room, guaranteed gorgeous view you will never forget toward San Marco and St. Sofia). For our inconvenience, we were given a free dinner and a bottle of wine. The shuttle is great, but learn the time schedule and route. The view from the pool is indescribably awesome.
