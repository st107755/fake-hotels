Stayed here for five nights with family during the Easter break.  I booked a family suite which we found tiny compared to the advertised space of 60 square meters. Moreover, it's location at the back of the hotel was not convenient, as the various hotel buildings are not connected.  On the morning of the second day the shower wasn't working, there was no water pressure.  After complaining, we were upgraded to an executive suite with view, which was much more spacious with a second WC and an excellent view of Venice.  This certainly helped to make our stay.  The biggest let down was the lack of decent dining choices within the hotel.  From breakfast to dinner, the food was mediocre at best.
