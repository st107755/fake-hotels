The Molino Stucky is a very impressive former mill building on the island of Giudecca, so it isn't in the main part of Venice.   There are regular boats to the main island including a free hotel shuttle and some nice local restaurants and cafes just along the waterfront, plus some small shops for other supplies.  I stayed in early November as part of a conference and many people complained the rooms were too warm and stuffy, but at least the windows can be opened.  Food options are excellent at breakfast. It included a range of cereals, plain and flavoured yoghurts, fruit salads, cut melon, pineapple, compotes and also fresh fruits like pears and apples.
