Highly recommend this hotel if staying in Venice.   It’s easy to get to from the airport, we took the public transport water taxi on the blue line. You can buy tickets easily from the airport. The journey is around 1h30mins (slower than it says online) but was great value and it stops literally right outside the hotel.  With this Hotel you are out of the way slightly being across the main canal however for €7.50 for the duration of your stay you can make use of the hotels taxi boat. It goes backwards and forwards all day between St Marks square to right outside the hotel.  We found the hotel food prices a bit over the top but a short walk from the hotel are several small supermarkets and loads of small coffee shops, bars and some amazing little restaurants.
