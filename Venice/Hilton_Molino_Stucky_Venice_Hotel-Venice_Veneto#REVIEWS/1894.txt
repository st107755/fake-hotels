Super hotel if a little quirky to find your way around due to the fact it is a converted mill. The hotel itself is spotlessly clean and our room which was a king guest room was also of a very high standard. The room also had a very efficient air conditioning system which due to the temperature in Venice was a boon. The buffet breakfast was as expected with good international choice. We ate for two nights at the hotels Aromi restaurant where we had two delightful evenings where the chefs, maitre,d  and waiters could not have been more helpful given the fact my wife is allergic to garlic. The food itself was to a very high standard although as like most things in Venice slightly expensive, but this was a special weekend after all.
