I was a bit worried about staying here because of some of the comments on TA made the transportation issues sound iffy. I needn't have worried! My husband, daughter and I stayed here for several days at the end of September. We loved the hotel!! The boat ride to the main island was extremely convenient. We used the public boat to the train station on our last day and it was so easy. We enjoyed being on the more deserted island- it was so nice to walk around in the evenings after exploring Venice during the day. The view from the pool deck was incredible. We are Hilton gold members and were upgraded to a suite with access to the executive lounge-- it was fabulous. Some nights we never even made it out to dinner after the snacks and drinks.
