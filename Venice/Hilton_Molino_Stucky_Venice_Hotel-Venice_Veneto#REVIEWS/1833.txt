We were very impressed with the public areas of the hotel but the room itself was very disappointing. The room was very small and dark and dingy. No view to speak of. It was also really hot in the room and the air conditioning was not working. When we spoke to reception we were told it is turned off in winter so there is no air conditioning. Our bed had a badly torn sheet on it and we had no towels.  There was also a picture missing from the wall in the corridor outside our room. All in all it just doesn't feel like a good quality hotel other than in the entrance areas.  Breakfast was good with a large choice available. The roof is closed in winter.
