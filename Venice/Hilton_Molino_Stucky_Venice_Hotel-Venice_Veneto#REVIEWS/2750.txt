Hilton has created a beautiful property in this formerly abandon pasta factory on the Island of Giudecca, just across the canal from St Marks Square.  Giudecca is very quiet, and a very pleasant residential island with a few art venues and  hotels, compared to the congested tourist crowds of the main island, but with all the charms of canal-central Venice.  The day and night view from the Molino Stucky of ongoing activity along the Giudecca Canal is entrancing.  Hilton offers continuously running water shuttles from the Molino Stucky to St Marks Square and Zattare just across the canal, making access to central Venice very convenient throughout the day.
