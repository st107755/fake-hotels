We booked Hilton because we thought the hotel's complimentary shuttle boat will meet our needs to island-hop and we were glad we did. There is a standard regular boat service to Hilton from the airport but use the more costly and faster water taxi if you prefer to arrive in style. If you are thinking of upgrading from economy class to business class with certain airlines and the business class comes with complimentary chaffeur-drive from airport to hotel then this is definitely the flight sector to upgrade. Not all hotel in Venice has a pool as the buildings are tiny but beautiful hence Hilton's rooftop pool gets my vote. But check the pool closing time to minimise disappointment.
