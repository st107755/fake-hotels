The Hilton Molino was fantastic.  The hotel is isolated from Venice by the canal but that isn't a bad thing as they provide a free water taxi to a few points in the city.  Our rooms were beautiful and well appointed, the rooftop bar is the place to be with evening city scape views and is free for guests of the hotel.  The hotel is a unique complex and its architecture is amazing, I believe it is a converted flour mill.  The hotel has many dinning options, we opted for the more casual location in the hotel's courtyard, the staff there provided tourist friendly service as we are not fluent in Italian. The $15 euro buffet breakfast is a must, easily one of the most comprehensive breakfast buffets I have ever been too.
