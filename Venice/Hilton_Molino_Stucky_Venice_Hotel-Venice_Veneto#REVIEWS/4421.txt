This really is great hotel and it is hard to find fault with anything. The place is stunning, the rooftop pool and Skyline bar with their breathtaking views of Venice are fabulous and breakfast is awesome. However unless you have very deep pockets don't plan on spending too much time in the bars. We had a drink in the Skyline bar on arrival and were charged 22 euro for 2 spritz, a drink you will pay 2 or 3 euros for in a lot of Venice bars. Also, consider this, although the hotel's free shuttle boat is very convenient, if you would like to enjoy Venice's late night social scene without clock watching then this hotel is probably not for you.
