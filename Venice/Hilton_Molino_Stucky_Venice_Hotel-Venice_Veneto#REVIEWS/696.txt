This was the last place we stayed on a 3 city European honeymoon. Arriving from the Orient Express we were whisked to our room after a glass of prosecco (I travel for work and am lifetime diamond with Hilton), to be greeted by a bottle of prosecco compliments of the manager.   Breakfast was included, as was the executive lounge. We found the buffet enough to get us through the day to dinner, with an occasional appetizer and glass of wine from the lounge.   Service was exceptional. The gym is better than many hotels and the spa was excellent if European in terms of cost. Bar staff were attentive and welcoming.   The pool is to die for, if a little cold in June. On the roof it has stunning views over Venice and again a fantastic bar with complimentary ice tea.
