Just returned from Venezia after spending three nights at the Hilton Molino Stucky. We arrived by using the public water bus from Piazzale Roma ( 7 EUR per person). It is a lot cheaper than the water taxi. Take nr. 2 or 41. They stop on the channel about 5 minute walk from the hotel. Unless you have hoards of heavy luggage, it is no problem walking this stretch...  Donatella at the check in counter was very nice and making us feel welcome. The rooms we had were deluxe king size, and quite small, but clean and nicely decorated. We had a glimpse of the canal from the room. Bathroom was good, bath tub and separate shower. Thick good towels and nice soaps, shampoo etc. I only missed bath robes, found it strange they didn´t have that in a hotel of this standard.
