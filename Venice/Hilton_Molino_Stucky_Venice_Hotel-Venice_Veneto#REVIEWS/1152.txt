This weekend we stayed at the Hilton Stucky hotel as part of our brother in laws 40th bday celebrations.  The hotel didn't let us down.  The roof top pool had stunning views and the rooms were great.  We went for a family suite which included a huge queen size bed, separate lounge and dining areas as well as a little kitchenette.  The bathroom was well stocked and featured separate bath and shower cubicle.   We had a couple of issues with the TV remote not working and the lack of a sofa bed.  However both issues were rectified within the hour with new remotes being provided and two additional beds provided for our children.  The hotels offers a shuttle boat to San Marco which costs only 6 euros per adult per stay which saves lots of money on individual taxis.
