We visited Venice for the first time in May 2012 and we absolutely fell in love with everything. The travel from the airport by a water bus was painless and rather exciting. We arrived at Guidecca at one of the water bus stops and we walked to our hotel for probably a few minutes, which was not a problem whatsoever. As we were walking towards the Molino Stucky, we were gob smacked by its magnificence and unique beauty of architecture. The moment we walked in, we smelt a divine scent of orangey fragrance mixed with something more exotic, which was incredible, and as being a guest of so many other Hiltons before, we have never had such an amazing experience!   We were welcomed at the reception by very nice staff, which quickly accommodated us in our 1 bed Deluxe Suite.
