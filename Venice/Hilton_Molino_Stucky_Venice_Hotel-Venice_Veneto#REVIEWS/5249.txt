This hotel was perfect the first time we visited three years ago and it was perfect now that we took our three small kids to Venice for their first time. When reading reviews please keep in mind that what might be perfect for me it might not suit your needs. That been said I want to mention that the hotel location for us is ideal. Giudeca is right in front of Zattere and St. Marks and the hotel offers a convenient free shuttle service to both islands every 20 minutes, not a minute late, it takes 5 mins to Zattere and 10 to St. Marks. We also took the vaporetto to get to Lido, there are vaporetto stations everywhere.
