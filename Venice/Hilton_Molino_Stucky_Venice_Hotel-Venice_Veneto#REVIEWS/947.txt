We just spent five nights at the Hilton Molino Stucky in Venice and were very pleased with the location, staff, restaurant, executive lounge and accommodations.  For us the location was perfect because we never like to stay in the center of the action, preferring hotels away from the crowds.  The Hilton rests in a quiet neighborhood and is a quick, pleasant ride across the water to St. Mark's Square where you can access transportation to all parts of the city or outer islands on the vaporetto.  The staff was courteous, pleasant and helpful at all times.  The restaurant offers a very good breakfast but my only suggestion would be for them to vary the selections from time to time.
