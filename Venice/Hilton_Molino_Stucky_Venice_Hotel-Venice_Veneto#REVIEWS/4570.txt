We arrived for a 4 day break to Molino Stucky over the bank holiday weekend and very impressed with its size and location. The reception area is roomy and clean. We were checked in very quickly both had to wait for until 2 before we could go into our rooms. Some of the staff can be a bit abrupt. We booked executive suite for the kids and king room for us, wich were opposite each other. The rooms (461/460) were OK nothing special. If possible try to get rooms in the tiwer as ther are more modern. Breakfast was really good with wide range of foods available. Food at the hotel is very expensive (club sandwiche 29 euro!!!). We had use of the executive lounge which is brilliant as its open 7am-11pm for tea, coffee, snack ets and alcoholic drinks between 6.30-8.30pm.
