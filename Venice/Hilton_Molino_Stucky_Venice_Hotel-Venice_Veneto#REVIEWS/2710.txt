We stayed at the Molino Stucky for a couple of nights in early September 2015. The building is fantastic, and it is wonderful that it has been renovated, retaining many original features, and put to a new use as a hotel.  We arrived very late, at 2:30am,  due to a delayed flight - obviously no fault of the hotel.  However the check-in process seemed to take an eternity, which we could have done without given the hour.  Our room was on the 3rd floor and was small and lacking in natural light (just a small window facing a brick wall of an adjacent building).   The following morning we were surprised to find clothes we had placed on a side chair soaking wet - the overhead a/c vent was dripping water.
