We stayed at this hotel for a few nights in august 2012 as part of our honeymoon. The hotel itself is quite an impressive building, and the location good, across the water from the main town and quieter because of this. Service was good and breakfast was excellent. The views from the rooftop pool are amazing, but the pool is not big enough for the number of guests using it. The rooftop bar is lovely, again with incredible views over Venice. Food was very expensive in the hotel's restaurants. Our bedroom was ok but nothing special and quite small. The hotel's water taxi provides a good, reliable service. Clearly prices in Venice do tend to be on the high side, but I do feel that this hotel is very overpriced and would not stay again for this reason.
