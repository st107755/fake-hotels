One issue that I think a lot of travelers mis-judge is planning for the transportation from the railway to the hotel.  If you are coming from the airport not an issue the public boat will drop you off at the hotel.  Yet, if you are coming from the railway I recommend use http://www.motoscafivenezia.it/eng/ for a water taxi from the train station to the hotel at 55 Euros it is the easiest and best way If not you will have to take two boats or walk with lungage for 10 min and over two bridges.  Once we got to the hotel we were greeted with happy and co-operative staff (team) members.  They checked us in and we went to the room.  The room was the largest I stayed in at any European hotel and had a very nice view.
