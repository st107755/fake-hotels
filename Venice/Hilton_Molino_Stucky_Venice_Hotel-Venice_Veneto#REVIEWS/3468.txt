Excellent room in quality and finish, our room 167 overlooked a small canal to the left of the hotel, room was fully a/c with controls easy to operate. Bathroom was well fitted and clean as you would expect.  Staff were all very friendly and helpful from the front of house reception and those on the door to the cleaners, the Skybar staff. We opted not to take breakfast based on cost we do not each much and prebooked it would cost 15 euro and 30 euro each if paid for on the day. we just found a local cafe/bar and had a pastry and coffee which cost a total of 5-10 euro depending on where you ate. We used the Nutella bar which is reasonable in cost and well worth a visit. We did not use the pool as it would mean walking to and from our room with out a bathrobe.
