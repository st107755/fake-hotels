As a Hilton Diamond member I can say this is one fine Hilton!  Upon arrival our room was not ready but when it was we were pleasantly surprised as we were upgraded to a corner suite facing Venice on the next to the top floor.  The executive lounge breakfast was excellent and the front office and concierge staff were helpful although their restaurant suggestions were pricey.  Not being actually in Venice is a pain but the boat ride is tolerable except for the peak times when you might not get a seat on the first boat.  This was our first visit to Venice and I was concerned about not being in the city proper and navigating the boats, etc. but if one is reasonably intelligent it will do fine.
