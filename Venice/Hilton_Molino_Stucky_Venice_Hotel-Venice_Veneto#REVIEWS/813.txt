If you are planning to stay for longer than 2 days in Venice I could highly recommend this hotel. My boyfriend and I were scared that it was in a separate island but it ended up being part of the charm and one of the things we loved the most. We had a lovely stay at a room with a wonderful panoramic view to Venice (as romantic as it sounds!). Felt incredible to open the window every morning and see one of the most beautiful sights just outside your window!   The service was very good and we did not encounter any issues. The hotel itself is beautiful and there are some nice restaurants/ pizzerias and bars close to the hotel which make your life easier if you don't want to cross over to the other island.
