We stayed here for 2 nights using my hilton honors points.  I was a Diamond Elite member at the time so I was upgraded to a nice room with a view of the canal.  The staff also left a half bottle of wine in my room which was a very nice touch to show that they appreciate loyal members.    Breakfast was good but I wouldn't pay the amount they were charging if you didn't have free coupons for being an elite member.  Luckily, I didn't have to pay. The selection was extensive.  There were fruits, bread, bacon, sausage, eggs (not a big fan as they were soggy), cheese, fresh fruit juice, etc.    The executive lounge was one of the highlights of my stay here.  There was free soda, alcohol, and appetizers.
