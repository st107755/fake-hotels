Let's be honest. There are only two types of hotels in Venice: a) two and three star independent-owned hotels (being promoted as 3 and 4 stars, beautifully presented in their websites, but with some photoshop), in the middle of San Marco / Grand Canal área, (with service standards closer to B&B rather than a hotel); and b) Expensive hotels. Period. Both categories have pros and cons. Independent hotels show "the Venice experience", but they lack of room space, amenities, etc. And most important, if you choose one of those hotels and it’s not "exactly" in a large canal you may find yourself strolling your luggage for some blocks. I honestly think that Hilton Stucky Venice is expensive. But I also think it’s worth it. Why?
