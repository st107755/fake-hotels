We had a weekend to spend in Venice and decided on the Hilton Molino Stucky. It is located on the island of Giudecca which is a short boat ride from Zattere vaporetto stop.   Love the hotel. We got upgraded to a nice executive king suite (guess they had the room?). It had a view of the interior courtyard of the hotel--not great. If you want a view of Venice or the lagoon, better ask up front. The suite was gorgeous. Bathroom was lovely.   Executive lounge was awesome. Breakfast had some hot food, cheese, cold meats, yummy croissants. Happy hour in the evening was hot appetizers and other snack foods plus beer and wine. Soft drinks and water all the time.
