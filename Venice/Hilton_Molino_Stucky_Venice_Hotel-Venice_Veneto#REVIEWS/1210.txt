Beautiful hotel located in an old flour mill. Hotel provides a water taxi for 6 euros per stay. Truly amazing the refurbishing of this large building!  Check-in was fast & Anna was very helpful. Our two bed accommodation was very large with a great view. Quiet air conditioning and lovely, big bathroom. Mini bar fridge even had a shelf allowance for your own drinks. Security is throughout the hotel monitoring- we felt very safe 2 women traveling here.  The pool is amazing as well as the 2 roof top bars. The breakfast bar is truly one of the best! Executive lounge 4pm-7pm provides many appetizers and drink choices. 2 lovely restaurants on the property. Aromi restaurant was fabulous.
