This hotel is located across the canal from San Marco and is away from the amazing crowds that Venice is becoming so famous for. The hotel runs a shuttle across the canal approximately every 20 to 30 minutes which is a pleasant trip into town. The hotel has a beautifully presented entrance/reception area and presents really well. Getting to your room can be a challenge with the building having been added to and added to. My suggestion is a trail of popcorn. The service was poor, especially for a hotel of this standard. There was never anyone at the concierge desk and we always had to ask at reception for someone. We had 3 people staying in our room and one person slept on the sofa bed in the lounge room.
