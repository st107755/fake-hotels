We have visited Venice on numerous occasions: always choosing a different hotel. Now we will never again choose a different hotel. The Hilton Molino Stucky will always be our choice for a trip to La Serenissima. At other hotels, we have had rooms which claimed to overlook a canal. They usually overlooked a small service canal: equivalent to a land-based alley. At the Hilton, we overlooked the Giudecca Canal: a wide main artery where ocean-going cruise ships sailed into port.  The Hilton Molino Stucky is more than a hotel. It is a destination. It is a sympathetic, yet luxurious, conversion of a 19th century flour mill.
