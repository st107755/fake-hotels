The Hotel itself is nice and extremely spacious, it has all the restaurants and facilities you would expect. However the rooms are looking tired and could do with updating. Stayed in an executive suite which had an amazing view but was noisy due to passing boats and high wind. The hotel spa is nice but I expected the jacuzzi etc to be included in the stay (not treatments obviously) so to be charged £20 for a pre booked 2 hour slot to use a small jacuzzi and sauna seemed a bit unreasonable. Not as unreasonable however as the £150 room deposit that needs to be paid on arrival (this amount seems to increase to bizarre levels now) an amount I’m still waiting to be refunded 1 week on despite 3 calls and an email.
