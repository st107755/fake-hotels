The hotel is a lovely property, rooms have modern appointments, our bathroom was quite large and the windows opened, which was a plus to take photographs of Venice from across the lagoon.  We also found the staff to be very pleasant and helpful.    We had planned on visiting the pool, but decided against it - the pool itself is quite small with limited deck space - it felt "busy" very quickly.  We were thwarted at the Sky Bar the evening we wanted to watch the sunset there due to a private party, so best check the schedule upon your arrival.
