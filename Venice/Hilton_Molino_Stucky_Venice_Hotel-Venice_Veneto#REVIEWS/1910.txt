I know I had only booked a standard room but the room I was given (359) must have been one of the worst in the hotel. The very small window looked onto a brick wall and the room was so dark the light had to be on all of the time. Left hotel after last night with bug bites, that had come up over night, all over arms and legs. Not convinced they were mosquito bites but can't say for definite they are bed bug bites. Drinks and food very expensive. In restaurant, even though I was spending a lot of money on meal was again given a table with virtually no view. Not enough beds round pool and the pool itself was absolutely freezing. Did have good view of Venice though from pool.  Hotel waterbus was very convenient although I do think it should be free.
