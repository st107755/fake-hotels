The hotel overall was good, but certainly didn't give off a warm welcoming feeling when staying there.   From a functional standpoint the hotel was fine. The beds ok and the rooms were very large by European standards. Its size is what I would typically expect in an American hotel. The bathroom was huge with both a stand up shower and separate tub. While handy to have both, I would have preferred to have constant hot water - not capping out at luke warm or going from hot to cold and settling on warm.  The staff was ok, but never went out of their way to make me feel welcome to the city or hotel.   The hotel does have a shuttle which runs to two stops.
