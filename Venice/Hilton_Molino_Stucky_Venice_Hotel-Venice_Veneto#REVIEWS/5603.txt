We stayed here for 2 nights and were very excited when we were upgraded to the suite. The only inconvenient part was the boat trip across the canal to get to main venice area but to be fair their free water shuttles every 20 minutes worked out alright. It's only 5 minutes from Zattere by boat and another 5 minutes to San Marco. From the airport, there's a direct airport water shuttle that comes to Hilton's doorway (it does take well over an hour, though).  The complimentary private boat to Murano(available on request) was a delightful touch. The rooftop pool has a beautiful view of the main canal and the city of Venice. A must-do atleast once during your stay.
