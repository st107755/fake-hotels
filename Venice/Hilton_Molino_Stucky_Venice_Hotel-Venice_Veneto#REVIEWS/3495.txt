My husband & I stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky & were disappointed with the location, staff & amenities.  The room was only okay.  It seemed small & cramped compared to the other Hilton Hotels we stayed at in Italy.  The executive lounge was right past the lobby & very small.  There were few selections & the drinks were warm.  We couldn't believe this was what they deemed the "executive lounge" after experiencing such excellent food & wine etc. in other Hilton Hotels in Italy.  The staff was extremely slow in replacing empty platters.   We had to wait an hour for the hotel vaporetto to pick us up & we were lucky to get on as there were so many other guests also waiting for it to take us to St. Mark's Square.  The hotel did have a rooftop pool & restaurant.
