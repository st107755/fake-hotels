This hotel is off the main island and St Mark's Square.  It is connected to the touristic part of Venice by a 10 mins hotel shuttle boat ride.  Even though it is not as convenient as hotels on main island, it gives you the breathing space away from the noise.  We enjoyed this small distance.  Our suite had the water view which was very nice.  Soundproofing was OK and we were not affected by the busy traffic.  Service in general was good.  One of the concierge team members was exceptionally good in recommending restaurants while another one was too cool and impatient with our enquiry. Executive lounge was moved to ground floor with a nice view over the waters.  Drinks selection was good but snacks selection should be better.
