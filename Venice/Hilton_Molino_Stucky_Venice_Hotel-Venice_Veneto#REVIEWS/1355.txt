One of the nicest hotels I've ever stayed at. I purchased the room on a Flash Sale in the Hhonors app as a prepay. The great experience started with an email from the hotel a few weeks before we arrived saying that we had been upgraded to an Executive King with lounge access and 2-hour spa access per day due to me being gold Hhonors. Once we arrived, the stay was even better. Beautiful corner executive room with a fantastic view of Venice and the canal. Staff were extremely courteous and nothing was too much trouble. We used the sauna and steam rooms in the spa. The executive lounge was kept stocked with snacks and drinks all day long and the cappuccino in there was fantastic. The water shuttle to St Mark's was great, with departing times exactly as displayed.
