Great location and great views from rooms on the higher floors, particularly at the front of this imposing red brick converted warehouse. Pool on the roof along with the bar also provide a grand view of Venice. Handy hotel shuttle boat service delivers you to the opposite shore in minutes where you can walk to some fine street cafes and restaurants should you wish to venture out although the dining choice in the hotel is excellent. A short run to the second stop in the shuttle boat drops you at St Marks which is usually thronged with tourists and in complete contrast to the tranquility of the island where the hotel sits on the main waterway.
