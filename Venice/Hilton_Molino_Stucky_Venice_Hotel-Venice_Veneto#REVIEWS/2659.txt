I stayed here for two nights on our honeymoon in October 2013.  I can't speak to the price as I stayed here on Hilton points but we had a great experience and were upgraded to a suite with great views of the dock and Venice across the water.  The hotel is located on an island across from the main island where St. Mark's square and primary tourist attractions are located. Some may not like that you have to take a water shuttle to get to these areas but we actually enjoyed the Hilton shuttles which ran every half hour from the morning until 11:30-12:00 at night. The shuttle trips were relaxing and provided great views of Venice that we really enjoyed particularly at night.
