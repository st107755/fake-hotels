We are frequent travelers and Hilton Diamond members, often staying at European Hiltons. The Hilton Molino Stucky, a restored grand flour mill, met or exceeded our high expectations in almost every way. It is situated just across the Guidecca canal from Venice proper, overlooking the city -- a bit quieter and less congested than the city and the crowded St. Marks area. The hotel shuttle boat, for a small one-time fee, provides fairly frequent service to the heart of Venice. Other boat transport, called Vaporettos, are also widely available to key points, so avoid the expensive private water taxis if you can. While guest service overall was friendly and very responsive, the most outstanding aspect of our stay was the service provided by the Concierge, Carlo.
