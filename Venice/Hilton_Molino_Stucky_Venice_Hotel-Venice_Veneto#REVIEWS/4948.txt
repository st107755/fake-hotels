Stayed here after our cruise for my Fiancés birthday.  This was our first experience of a hilton hotel.  On arrival our bags were immediately whisked off by the doormen and we were taken to check in, however we could not check in as it was too early (about 10am) and our room was not ready, so we waited about the hotel until about 1pm and we asked if our room was ready yet and it had just become available.  We were showed to our room which was clean and spacious.  I asked for a nice view as it was a birthday present for my Fiancé, but we got a view of a metal staircase.  I also thought the hotel may have left a birthday note or something as most hotels do when your celebrating a special occasion, but there was nothing.
