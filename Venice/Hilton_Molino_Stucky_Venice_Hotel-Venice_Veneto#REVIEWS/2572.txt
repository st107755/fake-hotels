We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky in Venice in October 2015. We were luckily enough to get upgraded from King Room with views to a Executive Suite with views. This was a GREAT welcome. When we got to the room there were some Italian cookies there with a note to wish me a happy birthday. Now this was AWESOME.  The views were beautiful and although the weather wasn't that great, the views were incredible. Breakfast was lovely and great choice of food. Boat service was always on time. Staff were friendly. The sky bar was our favourite place and spent way too much money there (couldn't help ourselves as the drinks were so good and the staff were wonderful).
