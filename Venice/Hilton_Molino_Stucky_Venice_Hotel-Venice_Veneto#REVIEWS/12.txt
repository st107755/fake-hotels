Worst things first: We stayed in a family suite - and didn’t love our first room. There was no view and it was very dark. But we asked to move and it was excellent. Bright, spacious, airy and with a wonderful view over the canal. The room was comfortable and clean.  That’s our only niggle. We had such a great time.   The best thing is the rooftop pool. It’s spectacular. You have to book a slot of an hour, but the pool staff were very sensible and let people stay for longer as long as the next session was not busy. It meant we could feel safe and enjoy the most amazing views over Venice. If I could change anything, I’d suggest earlier opening hours for the pool.  The rooftop bar has a vaguely sterile atmosphere but great drinks and views.
