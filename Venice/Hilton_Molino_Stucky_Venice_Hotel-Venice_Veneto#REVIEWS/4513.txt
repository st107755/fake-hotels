This was the second hotel we stayed in during our honeymoon. The contrast could not be marked. This hotel seems to feel that 5*s are earned if the place looks pretty and the prices support it. I believe 5*s are earned through great service and that is not what we experienced here at all.  Service at breakfast was almost comical. My wife's simple request for tea was finally fulfilled correctly on Day 4 (after two coffees and a cappuccino!). Plates were rarely cleared away.  Even though it was only early May, the shuttle service across to Venice was clearly inadequate  so this added time and expense to the simple task of going out for the day.  The television did not work at all well. The reception was poor and changing channel was an art I never quite mastered.
