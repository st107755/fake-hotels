I stayed for a night back in May, and although we had a pleasant stay I wouldn't go as far as to say it were 5* worthy. While I know star ratings are very different between the UK/US and Italy/Greece etc, it just didn't have the finesse I'd expect. Saying that, the staff were fantastic and very helpful. Breakfast was one of the best hotel buffets we've had, great choice and all very fresh. The rooftop bar was a delight, but sadly very pricey and busy, we felt a little rushed for our table.  Unfortunately the pool wasn't open when we went, which was disappointing as it was one of the reasons we'd picked the hotel. The room was clean and comfortable but very dated with bizarre low ceilings.
