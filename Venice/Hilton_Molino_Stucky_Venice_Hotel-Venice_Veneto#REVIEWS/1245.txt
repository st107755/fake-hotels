We stayed here previously 5 years ago so this was our second stay. This is a gigantic hotel with a lot of cruise passengers and Americans. We like staying on the island because San Marco is a mad house between 9am and 6pm. There are also some good restaurants on the island at a fair price. The room is large and the bed and linens are comfortable. The shower configuration is one of the dumbest things ever. Both visits here we were baffled by the shower design. My biggest issue at this hotel is the concierge who is flat out rude. This is my 5th trip to Venice so I know it well. We wanted to visit the Lido which I had visited before well aware that beach cabanas are expensive. When I asked her to recommend a beach club she said she couldn't and paused.
