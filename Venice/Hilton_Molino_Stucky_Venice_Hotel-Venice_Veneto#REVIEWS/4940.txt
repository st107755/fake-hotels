I almost didn't bother to write this review after reading what others have said, since the location and the quality of the hotel are well described already.  However, I didn't notice anyone raving as much about the breakfast as I thought it deserved.  The lovely Anna will greet you with a warm smile as you enter.  There is an enormous variety of foods and drinks, including spumanti, and everything we tried was surprisingly good quality, especially for a buffet.    The concierge was extremely helpful in planning our time in Venice.  The shuttle boat is easy and reasonably comfortable, and generally we did not mind being away from the center of Venice.
