The Hilton Molino Stucky is a stunningly beautiful building which has been converted into a fabulous luxury hotel.  Take my advice, and get a private water taxi direct to the Hotel from the airport - these taxis are sleek and powerful speed boats big enough to carry 10 people in comfort, and at considerable speed across the water, and when you enter Venice, they take you on a route through the beautiful water ways including the Grand Canal, right up to the steps of the Hotel.  We took the taxi back to the airport too.  You will pay €110 each way, but well worth the money - think of it as an excursion rather than a taxi ride!
