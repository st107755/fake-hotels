Just before our trip to Venice, we stayed in Hilton by Lake Como and the experience was very different... We actually really enjoyed the hotel in Como, so we were expecting similar quality from the Venice one, however, were hugely underwhelmed.   The hotel is formerly a mill, the building feels old and dark. Our room was not as big as we hoped. The style is worn out Hilton, which looks and feels like an old conference facility, not very inspiring or fresh. No USB chargers in the room.  The breakfast place looked a lot like a canteen. The quality of food was average. Despite the staff's best attempts to make us feel welcomed, the place looked tired. We only had breakfast there once and decided to eat elsewhere for the rest of the trip.   About the room...
