A great hotel with character a short boat ride from the mayhem. The beautiful site of the hotel is close by lots of local restaurants and bars, off the tourist track and well worth a visit, but so is the hotel restaurant- amazing! The down side is the small bedrooms and the queues at concierge and reception, constantly !
