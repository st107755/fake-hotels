My wife and I booked a trip to Venice for a short break and plucked the Hilton Molino from one of many listed and recommended on the web. It turned out to be an absolutely 1st class choice. It was great value for money and the location was great for our purposes. The hotel runs a great free shuttle boat to the heart of Venice and is very reliable. The staff were excellent and were as helpful as anywhere I have been. The hotel was beautiful, well laid out and the breakfast spot on. There is a great local bar about 300 yds to the right of the hotel on the canal if you want the true feel of Venice at a good bit less than the cost you will pay on the "mainland". We ate there on two occasions and the staff and management were really welcoming and friendly.
