We loved this hotel for a variety of reasons.  It was beautiful on the outside when we pulled up.  We took the public water bus from the airport which took almost two hours.  Since we got in to Venice at 11 am, and check-in time was around 3-4, we didn't mind the trip.  It also helped us get a sense for the area.  We loved the free shuttle every 40 minutes from Guidecca Island to San Marco Square.  The food was marvelous - we ate in the restaurant twice, and it was one of the best meals we had.  The executive lounge offered adequate snacks and great wines.  The breakfast was truly first class - which as a Diamond member was free.  Our rooms were great - they were overlooking the canal.  Our view was perfection!  We also had treatments at the spa which were wonderful.
