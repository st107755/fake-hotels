In my small experience, there is a wide range of service and quality levels within the HILTON hotels range. I have seen the best and the worst. So when I was booked to the MOLINO STUCKY I wasn't expecting a six-star hotel. The description on the site does try to give this impression, by who doesn't? I wouldn't have written a review because overall it is a "standard" Hilton, but, upon my arrival, the very polite young men at the reception offered to upgrade me, for free. I should have know better, but I fell for it, mainly because I thought that during a dead season hotels do upgrade you to "tease" you into the luxury for the next visit. I was upgraded to a family suite. It had a full-size living room with a kitchenette, a large bathroom, and a rather small bedroom.
