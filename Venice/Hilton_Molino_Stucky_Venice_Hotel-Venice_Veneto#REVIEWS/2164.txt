My husband and I stayed one night prior to a cruise.  This hotel is beautiful...the restoration amazing.  We used HH points to book our room and were pleasantly surprised with an upgrade to a room with a view of the canal.  The room was nicely appointed and had a lovely window which opened onto the canal.  We were delighted to watch several cruise ships go right by our window!  The bathroom was large with shower, tub and bidet.  Beds were very comfortable.  Only negative was that we could hear the people next door very clearly through the connecting door. The Executive Lounge was nice for evening treats and breakfast.  It did seem a little odd that the Executive Lounge was a divided part of the main lobby, but the food and drink were good and the staff friendly.
