Firstly I think the hotels' location is excellent - on a large island next to the main island with lots of frequent water taxis. Rooms were very comfortable  and actually had a bit of character compared to the usual  bland decor of international hotels . Breakfast  was very good , especially the fresh orange  and the freshly squeezed  apple car,rot and Ginger . I was disappointed with the executive lounge - lay out ,furniture , food and choice of drinks .
