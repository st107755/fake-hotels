We have Oceania Cruise Lines to thank for our stay at the Grand Hotel Molino Stucky on Giudecca Island. First off, the unobstructed views of the main Venetian island across the canal, sunrise and sunset were unparalleled and breathtaking, especially from the roof-top pool area. The hotel provided regular water taxi service to points across the canal including the Piazza San Marco area. The August weather was quite warm for the Italians and Europeans but spring-like for those from the US South like ourselves. The Hilton staff spoke English so I saved little, passable Italian I speak for venturing out. Word of warning - August is vacation-time for most of Europe so anticipate crowds.
