Stayed here for three nights on a recommendation from a colleague and it. Retail you didn't disappoint. You have to get to the hotel via a boat which my life don't overly like but the actual hotel was fantastic. Super clean, comfortable, friendly, lovely breakfast and good restaurants. We had an evening meal at the attached Bacaromi restaurant which was a typical Venetian meal and was excellent. It seems everything in Venice is expensive so come prepared as even a quick lunch in the tourist areas will cost you £50 to £60 but it's worth every penny. I would highly recommend this hotel and after seeing a few others this is surely one of the best around.
