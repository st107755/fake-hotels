I really liked this property and the staff that I encountered during my 3 night stay.   Getting there from the airport does take a while but in Venice you quickly get used to this.  We were generously upgraded to a one bedroom suite on the first floor with a water view. The design of the hotel has tried to stay true to its factory origins and it looks lovely. It is getting slightly tired in places but I believe they are about to embark on somewhat of a refurbishment. The small things which we noticed were a lack of plug sockets and slight musty smell but nothing over powering, perhaps just the moisture.
