Stayed 3 nights at this beatiful Venice hotel before a RCL cruise. The weather in Venice was nice, sunny but a bit chilly which is normal for the end of october. The hotel is a busy conferance hotel, and most guests were attending a conferance. Our room was great, large room, actually a livingroom with a separate bedroom. Had access to the executive lounge where we could enjoy cofee, softdrinks and snacks during the day. Also wine and hot snacks in the afternoon. Hilton Molino Stucky is not at the sunny side of Venice. It's an old, large brick building, and there were no place to enjoy the sun at the hotel. The pool on the roof was closed, and there were no access to the sun deck by the pool. Missed that.
