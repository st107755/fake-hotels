My wife & I had a few days here that we added to the end of a cruise that finished in Venice.  Venice is one of those must see cities and if you like Hilton this must be on your list.  I have been a loyal Hilton customer for around 20 years and this has been on my list for at least 10 of those.  Mostly this lived up to my expectations with a few exceptions. The check in and check out process could be better. Especially for Gold & Diamond HH Members.  On checkout, you shouldn't have to ask for help getting into the water taxi's. Especially when you struggle to walk and have a walking stick like I do.    That said, all of the service in between was flawless.  We had 1 lunch & 1 dinner in the hotel and the food was great as was the service.
