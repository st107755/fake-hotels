Spent a night in late November 2014 while in Venice. Considered other fine hotels in this one of a kind town and found this renovated former flour mill building to be equally impressive and charming. The room was spacious and well decorated with Italian accents. I enjoyed having a high ceiling (12 plus?) room and a descent size bathroom w/ a tub and a separate shower. The main hotel restaurant was acceptable but could have been better in terms of presentation and taste, mainly since there are so many good places nearby. Fortunately, service made up for whatever was lacking. The excellent service appears to be the common thread as I found other hotel staff to be just as friendly and attentive, all with excellent English.
