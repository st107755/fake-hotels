Venice was the second stop on our two-week driving tour of Italy. The picturesque drive from Milan took an uneventful 3 hours. We opted to park at the Venezia Tronchetto parking garage (Isola Nuovo del Tronchetto). The garage appeared well monitored, but you can opt for the VIP parking with caged extra secure parking spaces if you plan to leave objects in the vehicle. Transportation to Giudecca for the Hilton Molino Stucky was easy. Exit the rear of the parking garage and go left to the water taxi station. Do not take the people mover. Routes #2 and #4.1 will take you to the Palanca stop. It's a short easy 5 minute walk to the hotel.  The Hilton Molino Stucky is a grand hotel with complete anenities, and a friendly professional staff.
