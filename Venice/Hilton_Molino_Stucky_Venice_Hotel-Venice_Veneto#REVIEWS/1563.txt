The Hilton Molino Stucky Venice is a beautiful 'grand dame' on the northern banks Giudecca island with hospitable staff, but lacking behind some serious maintenance and long overdue renovations.   Upon entering the hotel, the main entrance is not very welcoming and has quite weathered wooden doors, which are hard to open due to the salty air (which has weathered the wood) and can definitely undergo some renovation. When you do manage to get inside you are greeted by a powerful hotel fragrance, which is smart seeing a human nose, is the fastest way to the heart. Although it should not replace a staff member greeting you upon entering, which was usually the case.    Having stayed at this Hilton years ago, I thought to give it another try in low season.
