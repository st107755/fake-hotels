Just returned from Italy where I stayed 4 nights at the Hilton Molino Stucky on Giudecca.  We stayed in an Executive Room with a view.  The view was spectacular.  We had a great view of Venice.  The room was very clean and very nice.  The wooden ceiling was a piece of art.  The lobby is very nice and welcoming and the lobby's restaurant, while having a limited selection, was very good.    I travel a lot and get tired of buffet breakfasts but this one was very good.   It had a comprehensive selection of items to choose.  The wait staff were very attentive and the hostess remembered my name every morning.    The shuttle boat was a bit inconsistent but maybe it was because it was carnival.   One of the greatest things about the hotel is Carlo, the concierge.  He was fantastic.
