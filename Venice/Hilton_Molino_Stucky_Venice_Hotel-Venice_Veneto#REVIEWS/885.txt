There is no doubt that the Hilton Molino Stucky is a beautiful hotel in a great location. They've done a wonderful job in renovating the old Molino Stucky flour mill. As far as location, we preferred being across from the mayhem of Venice on the island of Giudecca. The Hotel's shuttle boat and nearby vaporetto stops made it easy to take the short trip across the canal to Venice.  However, what separates the Hilton Molino Stucky from other hotels is the people. The service is what you'd expect from Hilton, but it was the extra care that was exceptional. My wife and I were waiting at a nearby vaporetto stop with our luggage to get to the train station when we found out that there was a transportation strike.
