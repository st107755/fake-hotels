We recently stayed at this hotel for 2 nights. We took a shared water taxi from the airport which was convenient and took around 25-30 minutes. The hotel is located on the island of guidecca which is a 10-15 minute water taxi ride from the touristy part of Venice. The Hilton had a very convenient shuttle that went back and forth regularly for a nominal fee per stay. We never had a problem catching the shuttle when needed, however I would recommend getting to the pickup point a bit early from Venice  as there were larger crowds that showed up moments before the shuttles left. We are Hilton silver members and received a complimentary upgrade to a king room with a lovely view!
