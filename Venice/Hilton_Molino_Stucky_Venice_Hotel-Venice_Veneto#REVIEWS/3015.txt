My Wife and I went to stay at the Hilton in Venice as she likes Venice and I had accumulated enough points for us to stay there for a reasonable price. To get to the Hilton requires a boat journey; whether this is a long or short one depends upon whether you travel directly from the airport or you travel on land to Venice and then get a boat across to the hotel. Once at the hotel, there is a regular dedicated boat service to st marks square from the quay outside the hotel. We had a really good room that looked out onto the canal and relaxed to the sight of the river traffic going up and down the canal. The rooms have to be experienced to understand how different they are to the usual hotel fare.
