We stayed here in April 2013 for a surprise birthday trip to Venice and it was fantastic. The Hilton operates a free boat service throughout the day from outside the massive historic building to the main island which was just a stones throw from the famous Basilica and San Marco Square - what more can you ask for?  The hotel itself was stylish and beautifully decorated, and the rooftop swimming pool with a view of Venice to die for. There is also a very trendy bar on the top floor balcony which served some really tasty cocktails against a stunning evening backdrop, but it wasn't exactly cheap! Saying that, nothing in Venice is so I guess it wasn't that big a surprise.  The rooms were clean, spacious and we had no complaints.
