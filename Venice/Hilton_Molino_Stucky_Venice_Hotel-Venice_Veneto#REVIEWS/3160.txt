My wife and I stayed here using my Hilton points and we had a great experience, which started with the lovely girl at check-in, who I think was called Ammini or something similar. We upgraded to an Executive room with a lovely view of the Venetian mainland. The complimentary breakfasts were high quality and the happy hour was a welcome restorative at the end of busy days walking. The boat shuttle worked well but I guess at peak times of the year, it must get pretty crowded at the busy times of the day. We sampled the rooftop bar, another great feature of the hotel. We particularly liked the Egg Lights on the tables and having done some research, I realise these are too expensive to have at home!
