Just returned to the US after a 3 night stay at the Hilton Molino Stucky Venice hotel.  Was one of the most amazing hotels I have ever stayed  before.  We chose the private meet and greet service from the train station...that was a mistake...it is way overpriced for the service you get.  We ended up cancelling the service for our return to the train station and just did a private water taxi arranged by the concierge.    Much more reasonable!  Check in was a breeze, our room was amazing and well appointed.  The complimentary breakfast exceeded our expectations!!  Although the charge of over $5 euros for a coke at breakfast was a bit much.  The executive lounge was great as well!  The highlight of the hotel has to be the Sky Bar on the roof!
