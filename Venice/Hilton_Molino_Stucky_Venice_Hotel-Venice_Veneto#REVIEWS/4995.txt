We just got home from a cruise in and out of Venice and decided to stay an extra night in Venice at the end. This hotel was heaven, had a executive room for two adults and one child. Got a great deal including breakfast that I must say was very good. Room was large by Venice standards and well appointed. This is Venice one of the most expensive cities in Europe food is going to be expensive and I have stayed in other Venice hotels and paid far more for less than at this place. Yes it is on another island but that is part of the experience of Venice if you don't want to take a boat everywhere don't come! I felt the free shuttle to St. Marco sq. very easy to use and this was July it was busy but we all got on. Bell desk got our bags no problem both directions. Beds were fab.
