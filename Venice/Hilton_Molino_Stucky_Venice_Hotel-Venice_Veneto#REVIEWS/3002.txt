This hotel surpassed all of my expectations. Arriving by boat from the airport at this beautiful building was very exciting. We loved the location of the hotel and spent our first afternoon exploring the island (Guidecca) surrounding the hotel which has some great, inexpensive restaurants nearby.  The hotel shuttle boat to the main island was frequent and never over crowded.   Our room was spacious, bright and extremely comfortable.   We visited the rooftop pool and SkyBar every day which has the greatest views in Venice. I highly recommend the cocktails in SkyBar!  The staff in all parts of the hotel were incredibly welcoming.   TOP TIP: the boat (blue line) direct  from the airport to the hotel was a great way to arrive as the views were fantastic.
