The Hilton Molino Stucky Venice may not be a perfect hotel, but it sure was the perfect place for my wife and I to stay during our recent visit to Venice.  Once a flour mill, Hilton has transformed this long abandoned property into a gorgeous hotel, and staffed it with some of the friendliest folks we've encountered in our extensive travels.  We were impressed with this hotel even before we'd checked in, as we'd disembarked from our cruise ship well before our room would be ready.  However, when we arrived at the Molino Stucky we were warmly greeted, and told that not only would they hold our bags for us, so we might explore the lovely Giudecca area, but they'd put a rush on getting our room ready - which they did.
