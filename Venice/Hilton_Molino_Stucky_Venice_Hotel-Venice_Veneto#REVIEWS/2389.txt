I chose this hotel for one reason location. My first time in Venice and I didn't want to get lost in Venice at night where I didn't speak the language so figured a hotel on its own island with a massive sign couldn't be missed :).  This was actually a pretty good choice, the food was good, staff were friendly. It was my birthday weekend so I was uber excited and pleased with my choice. The free shuttle takes you across to the main island at pretty decent times if you have tours and you can always take the aliguana line to the  airport.  The view from the room wasn't that great but it was a nice room overall. Overall I enjoyed my stay.
