The hotel is on the quiet side of Venise, which is good, on the water. Check in was fast as we are diamond member with Hilton. Our room is spacious with a big bathroom and it has a view on the cannel.  We were supposed to get 2 complimentary bottles of water every day but we only got them 1 time.  The 2nd time i had to ask the lobby.  Anyway we bought a bottle of water outside.  So i did not bother calling for complementary water bottles.  Breakfast: avoid the executive lounge, choice is too small and not comfortable.  Alternatively, join the others at the main restaurant. The choice is elaborate, staff is friendly and efficient.  Nothing bad that i can report. Staff cleans tables fast, so no dirty tables.
