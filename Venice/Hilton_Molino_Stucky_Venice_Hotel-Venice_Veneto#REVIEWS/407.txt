We stayed at Hilton Molino Stucky for 3 nights recently. The position is very good, on the Giudecca island, just overlooking the main island of Venice, The hotels offers a water shuttle to and from the premises to San Marco square, which is surely very convenient. AT first glance, the hotel looks sumptuous, but this is only an impression, as a lot of issues awaited us during our stay. Reception staff was polite, but not very trained and a bit unsure of all the information we requested. The room was certainly spacious, but looked a bit outdated, with no amenities (I am a Hilton Gold member), like complimentary water, which we had to request twice. Also, we requested a dental kit, which took good 40 minutes to be delivered, and only after a second call to reception.
