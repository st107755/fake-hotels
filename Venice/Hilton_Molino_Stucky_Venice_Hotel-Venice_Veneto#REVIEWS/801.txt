We spent two days in this hotel at the end of our cruise. It is housed in an old flour mill, and its conversion to luxury hotel has been well developed. It is a large hotel, and often requires lengthy walks and up and down elevators to get to various parts of the building but it has excellent facilities and we would happily stay there again on a return trip.  The Executive Club facility is well-appointed and given this is a conference hotel, it is sometimes nice to get away from the general melee of conference delegates, good people that they no doubt are. The Skyline Bar has spectacular views over Venice and although the weather wasn't particularly good while we were there, a visit to this bar is well worth the effort.
