We stayed here for one night, family of five in two rooms, before leaving Venice after a weeks stay. We came primarily for a swim in the rooftop pool, and were really disappointed to find out it had been closed for the winter season a few weeks before. There was no information anywhere about this on the website. Very difficult to understand as we'd just come from the Lido, where the pool remained open during the sunny autumn days.  The rooms were really lovely, beautifully furnished and the beds AMAZING, the bathrooms were fantastic. The height of luxury. The staff were really helpful, locating us in rooms next to each other without me having to ask, and an extra bed appeared for my son.
