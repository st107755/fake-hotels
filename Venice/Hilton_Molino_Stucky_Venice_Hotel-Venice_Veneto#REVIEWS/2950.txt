My Partner and I stayed here in March from Friday - Monday. Everything was fantastic. The bars, food (especially the sea bass risotto!!), spa, boat transfers, view, facilities. Only shame is that free wifi isn't provided in the room but it works great in the lobby area and is free there. The Nutella bar was fab too! Overall, no negative comments about this place. The hotel is on a little island opposite Venice itself where there are other gorgeous restaurants and hotels. It's lovely to stay just over the water from Venice centre - great views and it takes 5mins in the Hilton boat to get to St Marks Sq etc. I would NOT go to the Lido! There is nothing there and good luck trying to find the beach!! The hotel also has a lot of character and is warm and friendly.
