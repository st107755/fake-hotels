My husband and I stayed at the Molino Stucky Hilton for 2 nights before we departed for a cruise from Venice. This hotel waa beautiful. Although it wasn't right in Venice, but across the canal on an island, the shuttles every 20 minutes made the 5-10 minute ride quick and easy! Check in was easy and all the hotel staff were friendly and always had a smile. We never made it to the pool but we heard it was beautiful and had great views. The rooftop bar was very cool, especially at night, but drinks were very expensive. The hotel was very clean and immaculate. The elevators sometimes took a while and are extremely tiny, but we were on the first floor so we took the stairs a lot. The rooms were very nice and clean. Bigger than what I had expected.
