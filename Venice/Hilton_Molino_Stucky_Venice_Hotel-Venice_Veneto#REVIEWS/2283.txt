We love the location of Molino Stucky on the Giudecca Island. The hotel has a shuttle that goes to San Marco every 30 minutes aprox. with a longer break for lunch. After 6pm, it also stops at Zaterre, which make it easier to go to Academia Bridge, which we strongly recommend. There is a charge per person/per stay at the hotel of 4.50 euros to use the shuttle. You can also use the water bus from Palanca stop, which is only 5-10 minutes walk from the hotel, and it takes you to multiple stops throughout Venice. Room 309 was beautiful, the view is memorable and we were fortunate to have had the same room 7 years ago, during our honeymoon.  The Sky Lounge is highly recommended. The staff is very friendly and professional (Michele). The views are beautiful day and night.
