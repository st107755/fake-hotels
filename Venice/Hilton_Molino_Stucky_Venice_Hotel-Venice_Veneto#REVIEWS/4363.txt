The position of this hotel right on the lagoon is perfect. The frequent  free shuttle across the lagoon makes the hotel perfect for visiting the main sites of Venice. The staff are friendly and there is a warm friendly feel about the hotel. The restaurant prices are rather high but there are many good restaurants in Venice to go to as well. A water taxi to the hotel from the airport is well worth the investment. It is not cheap but we were taken on a trip around the canals to get to the hotel. That was a magical start to the holiday. Our only bad experience was with our first room that had a connecting door to the next room. You could hear every word from the room next door as the were obviously from USA and were using Skype to call America at near midnight!.
