My wife and I stayed at Molino Stucky 8 nights and I was impressed from check-in to check-out.  This hotel has a few very small quirks but overall has its act together.  We booked on Expedia and found a special for approx $200 USD per night.  Check-in was very easy and the front desk even upgraded us to an executive suite free of charge.  In full disclosure, I don’t typically at Hilton and have absolutely no elite/executive status.  They saw we were on our anniversary trip and totally took care of us.  We were also granted access to the executive lounge which saved us a ton of money on food/drinks.  The lounge provided a full breakfast and snacks throughout the day.  The lounge also has a variety of alcoholic & non-alcoholic drinks.
