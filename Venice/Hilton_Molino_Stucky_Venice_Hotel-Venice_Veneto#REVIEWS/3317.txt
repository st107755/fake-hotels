Location.  Location.  Location.  In Venice, location is the name of the game, and this hotel is fabulous in that category.  Because I am a diamond member, they upgraded me to a fabulous suite with a view of the boat- traffic outside of the living room, dining room and bedroom of my suite.  It was restful, quiet and elegant.  The room makes my rating excellent, because if you stay where I did, you would be delighted!  The executive lounge was ok.  They ran out of food (the shrimp) both nights I was there after a few minutes.  Other drinks, cheeses and goodies were fine.  Breakfast was standard.  The view from the Executive lounge is eye level with the canal, so you can eat breakfast and watch the boat traffic.  We saw a huge cruise ship en route to the port on Sunday morning.
