This hotel is located on the island a short (shuttle boat) hop from San Marcos square. It's as you expect from Hilton, clean, friendly highly efficient staff and on site restaurants and spa. We ate out, and were too busy to use the spa (Hilton I'd be happy to go back and 'secret shop!' :)) The shuttle bus is not free (no idea how they know but they invoice you at the end of your stay, so must have a way of checking who you are and how many times you get on and off, electronic keys maybe?) The pool on the roof is gorgeous with a life guard on duty but it was just too cold, shame. The views were amazing from up there, but the drinks are expensive. Mind you Venice is costly so I guess they cash in on the fact that you're on the island and not too near to other places.
