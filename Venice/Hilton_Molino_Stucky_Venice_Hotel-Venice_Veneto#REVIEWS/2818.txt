Perhaps we have unusually high standards, but the hotel was 4 star at best from where we sat. It lacks the polish that you would expect from the Hilton marque. The location is a positive and the hotel trades on this. While the lobby areas and the breakfast courtyard were nice, the remainder of the hotel was decidedly average.  Our King Hilton Deluxe Room was pretty run down with scuffed furniture, worn/stained carpet and a view of next door's rubbish (see photos) that did not live up to the "Deluxe" tag attributed to the room. I'm not sure what could be done about it (as this is Italy), but we would have preferred a brick wall than the overgrown weeds, rubbish and construction materials we could see from our window. Not the most romantic view.
