The hotel is very big and was a let down after the charm of the boutique hotels we had enjoyed in Florence,Ravello and Venice. When we checked in we were given the wrong elevator number on our room key packet and could not locate the room. My husband had to go back to the front desk,wait in line again and we had to drag the luggage back downstairs and wait in line for a different elevator. Then when we got in the room the trash had not even been emptied and their were greasy fingerprints on the nightstand. The room was small and our view was the side of the hotel. When I told the front desk about our problems I was told it was a big hotel. I know that it is. I walked half of it looking for our room. I hated this hotel. It should be called the Hotel Stuffy.
