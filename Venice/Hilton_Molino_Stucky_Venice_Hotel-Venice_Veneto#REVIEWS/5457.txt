This hotel was in a perfect location for sightseeing, with a free hotel shuttle to Zattere and to St. Mark's Square. The room was small but nice, with a somewhat cramped bathroom and too little storage space (and no iron and board). The prices for everything were high--but they actually provided room for your own stuff in the refrigerator ! The staff were helpful, especially the young woman who checked us in who got us a room earlier than the check in time, and found us a very quiet room as we requested. Breakfast every day was included, and good (although it would not have been acceptable at 30 euros per person). One dinner was also included in our rate; the dishes ranged from acceptable to good, although the posted prices were very high.
