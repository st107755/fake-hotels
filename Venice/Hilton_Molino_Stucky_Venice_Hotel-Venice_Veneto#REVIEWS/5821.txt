This hotel far exceeded my expectations. Being on Giudecca, an island a short boat ride away from the main sights, was actually pretty nice. It was quiet and a perfect place to get away from the tourist crowds. The Hilton boat over to the main island was reliable and didn't take too long. The vaporetto stop was also very close so you have plenty of options. Our room had a view of much of Venice including St. Marks, it was absolutely beautiful. It was a little small, but only by American standards. Plenty comfortable.  Food at the restaurant was very good, some of the better food we had in Venice. Service was mostly good, we had a few hiccups with them misplacing our reservation and such, but they were dealt with relativlely quickly and professionally.
