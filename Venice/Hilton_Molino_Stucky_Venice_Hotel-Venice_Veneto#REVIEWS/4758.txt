From the moment we arrived until the moment we departed, we could not fault the hotel. Cannot understand some of the negative comments on this, and other sites relating to the hotel. We are not what you would call 'avid travellers' but we always do our homework when going abroad. (Just because a hotel has been awarded 5 stars, some people expect 6 or 7 star service). From this research it became obvious that the hotel was situated across the water from the main sites, therefore transport by boat would be needed. Dont forget, Venice is by its own nature an island with canals and waterways!! The shuttle boat ran on time and although it ran at approx 30-40 minutes intervals, it is not hard to plan your journey.
