Took a speed boat directly to a waiting landing next to hotel, where a bellman greeted us and took our bags 20 steps to the lobby. This is important because many Venice hotels- even expensive ones,can mean you walk- with your luggage- for blocks over rough cobblestone streets. The private speedboat cost about twice what the larger boats do- but cut 1/2 the time of getting to the hotel and guaranteed hotel side delivery. The hotel is well appointed, spotless and all the employees were polite and conversant in English. The family suite was recently redone and great for two adults and two teenagers. Two rooms with an entry hall, wonderful bathroom w separate shower and tub. Very high ceilings w windows that opened and lots of extras.
