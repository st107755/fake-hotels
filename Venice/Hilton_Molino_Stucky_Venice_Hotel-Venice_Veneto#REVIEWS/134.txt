I was a bit concerned that the property was across Giudecca Canal from the main island, but that turned out to be *great*:  1) The views are spectacular.  The rooftop bar offers a sunset view of the Venice skyline that isn’t possible to see elsewhere.  2) Convenient, inexpensive shuttle to main island makes coming and going fun, easy, and cheap (8.5 euros for unlimited use during your stay). 3) Uncrowded. Because it is the only major property here, we had our run of Giudecca Island.  It was nice to not have to deal with crowds when we didn’t feel like it.   The hotel itself is lovely, occupying a beautiful, old, brick mill building. Nicely decorated with a modern Italian vibe, our room was well-appointed and large-ish for a prominent European locale.
