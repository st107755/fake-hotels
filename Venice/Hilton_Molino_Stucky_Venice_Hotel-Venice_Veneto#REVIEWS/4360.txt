I was hesitant to stay at this hotel due to the location across the lagoon from most of the main tourist areas in Venice, but it turned out to be a non-issue. The location was actually great!  The views from the hotel are wonderful - especially from the lobby area and rooftop pool.  I am assuming that the views are probably equally wonderful from any front facing rooms over the lagoon(which mine was not).  The beds are super comfortable, too.  I was upgraded to a suite due to Gold Hilton status, and my room was huge - large marble bathroom with separate shower and tub, large bedroom and separate living area. The huge room size made up for the lack of view - which was only of other rooms across the corridor.
