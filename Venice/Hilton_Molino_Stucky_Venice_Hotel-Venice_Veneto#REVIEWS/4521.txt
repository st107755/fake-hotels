Background We enjoyed a six night stay at the hotel on our first trip to Venice and I would point out that husband is a regular Hilton hotel user so the comments are in part in that context. We arrived at the hotel via a shared water taxi from the airport (and I strongly recommend pre-booking) and were dropped off at the side of the hotel.   Room I booked a Deluxe Room with view and it was absolutely worth the extra. However if our son had been with us, the room would have been too small for three but was nice for two.   Our home for the six days was on the sixth floor (627 I think) and although we later moved to an Executive Room, we asked to move back to our original room which although smaller had amazing views of the Guidecca Canal and the Campanile in the distance.
