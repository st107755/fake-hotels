We stayed here for 2 nights to celebrate our first wedding anniversary. The building and location are ideal if you are happy to be away from the crowded parts of Venice. The hotel has been wonderfully renovated from an old mill and manages to retain it's charm, especially from the outside. The staff were all excellent, especially at breakfast when they were very busy.  The free shuttle is good, although could do with being a little more frequent during busy periods. I would recommend getting off at the first stop and walking to the main attractions as the small streets, buildings and boutiques are beautiful in the Academia area and it's much less crowded than the main tourist attractions.
