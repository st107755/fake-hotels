If you want to visit Venice in style, this is the place.  Our flights were scheduled to arrive quite late in the evening, so I pre-booked a water taxi due to the considerable time saving compared to the water bus. I was delighted with the purchase as I excitedly stepped into our private speed boat at the airport dock and zoomed off past the water bus! It also enhanced the arrival experience at the hotel as we were met at the dock by a porter to take our bags.  There was a short wait at check in, but we were delighted to find we had been upgraded to an Executive Suite, even Silver HH status can pay off! The room was fantastic with a separate living room and a spacious bathroom.  The experience was slightly let down by the outrageous prices for food and beverages.
