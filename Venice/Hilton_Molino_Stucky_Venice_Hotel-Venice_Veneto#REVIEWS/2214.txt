We stayed for 3 nights at Molino Stucky in a standard kingsize room. I would have liked a few over the water but having said that the room was lovely. The bed supremely comfortable with the biggest feather pillows I've ever seen. The bathroom was very nice although they really need to put some sort of shower screen as it's impossible not to get water all over the floor and sink area. The hotel offer a shuttle boat service which runs every 40 minutes low season and every 20 minutes high season. It's always on time and the best way to get to St Marco. We didn't eat in the hotel apart from breakfast, which was great every day with a decent choice and you can order omelette or fried eggs if you want.
