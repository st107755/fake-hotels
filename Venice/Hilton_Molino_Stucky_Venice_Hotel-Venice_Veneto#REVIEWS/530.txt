I stayed at Hilton Stucky for 5 nights as part of a business conference. The hotel is fabulous, close by but quietly away from the madness of Venice. Breakfast is a real treat - huge choice, healthy options, all beautifully displayed. The roof top bar and swimming pool were fantastic with far reaching views and great service.
