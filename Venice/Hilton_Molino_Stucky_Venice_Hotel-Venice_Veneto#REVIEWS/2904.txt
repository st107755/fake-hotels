The location of the hotel is great. Situated on the island of Giudecca, it is only 15minutes in a boat from St. Marks, with the hotel providing a shuttle service, in which you pay 7 euros for at the end of your stay but is worth every penny. Giudecca is an excellent location to escape the hustle and bustle of the tourists, but still contains some excellent restaurants with a beautiful view looking back at Venice.  The hotel facilities where excellent. Everything you could need was available. The hotel pool was great, although not big enough considering the size of the hotel, it was the perfect place to go to, to escape the warm temperatures.
