While our room was large & attractive, with a separate desk area and large bathroom with whirlpool tub, I had a hard time getting past how poorly the room had been cleaned.  Finger prints and smudges on the light switches, grunge in the corners, and spots on the mirror made me question the overall cleanliness of the room.    Otherwise, the room was very quiet, nicely decorated, and overlooked a courtyard.    Pros:   -Large, tasteful room near the spa and breakfast areas.   -Roof top bar and pool area (with infinity pool) provide spectacular views of Venice with excellent original cocktails.   -Hot breakfast included.   -Concierge very helpful with restaurant, train, and opera reservations.   Cons:   -The hotel is on an island across from Venice.
