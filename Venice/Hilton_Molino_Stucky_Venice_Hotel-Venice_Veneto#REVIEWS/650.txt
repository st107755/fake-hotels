I will say we are Hilton people.  When we stayed in Venice I originally wanted an "authentic" local hotel as we had in Paris.  I found many were sold out so I booked this Hilton Property. It was amazing! It was the perfect mix of "American" amenities and Italian touches.  The rooftop bar offered breath taking views. There were fabulous restaurants within walking distance and a complimentary Hilton water taxi over to the main island when we wanted to go.  Our room was very large, clean and private.  The staff was very accommodating for anything we wanted.  A nice bonus we found was they offered a taxi to the Glass Factory and back.  And on the morning we left for the airport, we splurged for a private water taxi to the airport. They arranged it for us.
