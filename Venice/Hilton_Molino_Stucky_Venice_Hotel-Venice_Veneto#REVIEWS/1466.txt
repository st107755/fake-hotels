My wife and I travelled to Venice and stayed three nights in this hotel to celebrate our wedding anniversary. We stayed in an executive King Sized room with a view of the city, and on the whole were delighted with the hotel. Thew down points - they have a great rooftop bar with a fantastic view out over the city of Venice but it is not open until 5pm, and in March it was dark after 5pm so you couldn't see the view; The breakfast bar was generally cold and if you attended within the last 30 minutes of breakfast, they have pretty much run out of most things; the executive lounge is supposed to be open until 11pm but the staff wouldn't let us in after 1050 to get a coffee. There is a great shuttle boat service which runs every 40 minutes but it is NOT FREE.
