My fiancé and I stayed at the hotel the week before our wedding, what a superb hotel! The staff were helpful the professional, the standard room was furnished to a an exceptionally high standard! With large comfy beds and plenty of space, breakfast was good with a large variety. The only draw back we found was the cost of the water taxis we needed to use to reach the centre of Venice/St marks square... The hotel did run a free shuttle every 40 minutes but it only went to one of the two places and sometimes waiting could be a pain so we would need to use the water taxis... This isn't the hotels fault just a curse of the hotel being located on its own island.  Overall a fantastic hotel, we thoroughly enjoyed the sky bar with views across the city!
