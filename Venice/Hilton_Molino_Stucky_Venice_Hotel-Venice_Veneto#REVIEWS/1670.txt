We added two days to our Italian vacation at the Hilton Molino Stucky Venice using Hilton Honors points.  We expected the usual nice Hilton accommodations but were completely surprised to arrive in a fantastic, renovated 1500's flour mill.  The old touches of the building were evident everywhere and featured Murano glass artwork in the lobby and on every floor.  The rooms were updated and very comfortable, and we were upgraded to a beautiful water view of the City of Venice before we even arrived.  The ability to throw open the windows and watch the canal activity was amazing.    We used the rooftop bar after dark, the free spa accommodations and executive lounge, and breakfast in the restaurant.
