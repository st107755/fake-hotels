My daughter and I stayed for a week in June and tended to pick up pointers as we went along. First tip is from Marco Polo Airport take the Alilaguna Vaporetti which will take you straight to the front door for 15 Euros. It's a new service and so some of the other Travel Reviews which talk about expensive Water Taxis etc should be disregarded. The journey goes around a good bit of the lagoon and is very scenic - not direct like the Water Taxis but much cheaper - also stops at Murano Island where they make the glass if you want to go back another day.
