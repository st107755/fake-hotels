Spent 2 nights at the Hilton Following a cruise of the med. Hotel was easily accessible from Cruise port by using Alilaguna bus service to Giudecca, maybe 7 Euro one way.  The Hilton was the first stop on the way and the water bus actually arrives at the hotel dock. I believe this company offers an airport service from the Hilton as well.  This company appears to have the free hotel water taxi service to San Marco and the other stop of which we found reliable. Check in exceeded all expectations with a VIP of the day upgrade to an Executive room as we were celebrating our 25th wedding anniversary. Thank you to the Guest Relations Manager Riccardo for this nice touch. Chocolate topped strawberry's awaited us in the room.
