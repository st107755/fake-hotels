This was our second time visiting this hotel.  We adored it the first time because there's lots to love:  great location on Guidecca Island (quiet and away from the sweaty throngs of San Marco & surrounding areas), wonderful rooftop pool, great breakfast and quiet rooms.   So what's not to like?  The $7.50 per person charge for the boat shuttle between the Hilton and San Marco.  Maybe I simply did not notice this charge on any of the materials I received when checking in, but my issue is that this extra fee was not clearly disclosed.  Not only that, but during our two-day stay, we only used the shuttle once (we had an Alilaguna pass).  We also witnessed plenty of people using the shuttle and then simply walking down the island (not into the Hilton at all).
