My husband and I booked this hotel recently as we were celebrating our Silver wedding Anniversary whilst I wasn't sure at first because it was not actually located on the main Venice Island I can honestly say that when we arrived in Venice I quickly realised that it was the best decision we made. The hotel is located just 10/15 min short boat ride from St Marks Square which is the main drop off point in Venice and you pay €6 per person on checking in for the hotel shuttle boat which runs every 20 mins back & fore from early morning until late at night so it was no problem getting over to the main part of Venice at any point.
