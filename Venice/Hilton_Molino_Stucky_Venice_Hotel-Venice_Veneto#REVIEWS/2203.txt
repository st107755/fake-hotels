Venice is a charming city – yes it is absolutely crawling with tourists, but the unique sights and sounds give it a feel that one must experience at least once in their lifetime. I did a fair amount of research before booking this Hilton – I often judge a hotel by how quickly they respond to my requests for information before I make a booking. The Hilton Molino cannot be faulted in this regard as my numerous queries were promptly responded to by a number of their staff (thank you Federica and David). The water taxi ride from the train station to the hotel took a mere 20 minutes and was a breath-taking introduction to the city as we first went through the grand canal and then crossed the open Adriatic sea to get to the island of Giudecca where the hotel is located.
