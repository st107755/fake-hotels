We had a nice stay at Molino Stucky despite the high water and blizzard-like weather conditions. The hotel is not on the main island of Venice, but it was still easy to travel to and from.   Pros: The rooms were spacious and comfortable, and the bathrooms clean and grand. I loved the classic decor - it really felt like we were staying in an old castle. To start the day off right, the breakfast was superb! An entire room was dedicated to the buffet and had every breakfast food imaginable. The ferry to and from Venice was fast and easy.  Cons: We had VERY rainy weather. If you are traveling in the Winter, I would recommend bringing your own rain boots to combat the high water.
