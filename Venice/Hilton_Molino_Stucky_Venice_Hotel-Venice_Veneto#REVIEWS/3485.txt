I have stayed at the Molino Stucky a few times now,and was last there a week ago. The Hotel has maintained its easy and relaxed atmosphere and good service from staff. Being on Giudecca island....it is great to get away from the hussle and bustle of the central part of Venice.....and the free and regular shuttle boat is only 10 mins away from St.Marks square.  There is a lovely rooftop bar with a great view of Venice and in the evening a great place to relax and look over Venice lit up at night.  The bedrooms are roomy with the most comfortable serenity beds,I have always slept really well there.  The buffet breakfast is a good way to start the day with a variety of options which caters to any taste.
