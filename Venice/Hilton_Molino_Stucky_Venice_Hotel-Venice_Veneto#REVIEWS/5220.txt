We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky in Venice for 2 nights in mid March.   Prior to our trip I emailed the Hotel asking, if possible, for a room with a view and on arrival they offered us an amazing free upgrade to the 'Thats Amore' Suite which not only provided views over the water to Venice, but also had its own seperate living room area, 2 large flat screen tvs, a huge comfortable bed and a very well appointment bathroom (with a heavenly shower). It was a wonderful start to our trip and made our stay very memorable. The suite was located in the Arsenele part of the hotel (the hotel is so large they provide you with a map to get around) and it seemed to us to be one of the nicest wings.
