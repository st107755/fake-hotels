Stayed for three nights in September. We had a Junior Suite (£230 per night booked through Expedia). The rooms were spacious and very comfortable, with lovely toiletries and comfy bathrobes. We had the usual safe, minibar/fridge.  The views to Venice are fabulous, especially from the rooftop pool and bar (which serves great cocktails).  We prebooked a private water taxi to take us from the airport to the hotel – 100 euros from motoscafi venezia – and it is certainly the way to arrive. It went part way along the Grand Canal as well, so we had a great tour of Venice. It took about 25 minutes from Marco Polo Airport. The room we had gave us access to the Executive Lounge which was very handy.
