We have stayed  at Hilton properties in the past and anticipated the Stucky would be our best and safest experience in Venice.   We stayed 1 night as part of our pre cruise.  Since we were celebrating our 35th wedding anniversary, our friends their 25th, we decided to go all out and paid the $750.00 each for the executive king room experience.  We have traveled to many places over the past 10 years some excellent and some not so great.  This is the very first time I felt the need to make a public comment on our experience. To start off our party was split up and sent to different buildings we were not grouped together as requested. That place is huge ...It just went down hill after that.   Reception staff was neither friendly or helpful upon arrival or departure.
