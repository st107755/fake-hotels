We stayed at the Hilton recently, about 8 years after our first stay. If we were totally blown over by the majesticism, great service and excellent food in 2011, this time hasn't been so pleasant. First, the hotel used to offer a FREE shuttle boat service from Zattere and San Marco vice versa, as it is not located on Venice mainland but on the Guideccea island. We arrived at Zattere at 1300hrs, hoping to get the shuttle to the Hotel, but was told by a skipper that the service from Zattere runs as from 1900 pm till midnight. Had the option to walk to San Marco to take the shuttle or pay normal waterbus (ACTV) to the Hotel. We chose the latter because of the inconvenience of walking again with luggage and children.
