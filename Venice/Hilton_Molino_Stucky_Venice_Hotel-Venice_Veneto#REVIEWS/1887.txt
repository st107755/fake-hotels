We stayed at the Hilton following a recommendation. We upgraded to a King Executive Room with a view at a cost of an additional €90 / night. The views were wonderful and the Windows opened to allow you to really soak up the atmosphere of Venice. I would recommend a private boat from the airport it was a wonderful experience travelling into Venice in a speed boat, it cost us (€195 Euros) for the return trip (pre booked in the UK). The Executive lounge wasn't brilliant and the hot food selection poor and lacked appeal. The lounge is small and poorly situated. We had breakfast in the main dining area which was excellent there was a really good choice appealing to all tastes.  The soft furnishings and decor were good and the main areas of the Hotel warranted the 5 star rating.
