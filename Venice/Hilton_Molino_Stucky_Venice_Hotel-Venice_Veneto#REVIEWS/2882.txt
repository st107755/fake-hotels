We were in Venice for 2 nights recently to celebrate our wedding anniversary. There are so many hotel to choose from, but we have never been failed by a Hilton all over the world so decided to stick with what we know.  The hotel is in a beautiful restored Mill, with all the usual modern conveniences inside. We were offered the choice of 2 rooms, and chose one on the fifth floor (510) which had an absolutely fantastic view of the water from 2 windows. We had been told that it would be 2 single beds made up into one large bed, but this wasn't a problem. The room was clean and the space very adequate for our short stay.  We didn't spend a whole lot of time at the hotel, but did manage a few hours by the rooftop pool - great pool with amazing views of Venice.
