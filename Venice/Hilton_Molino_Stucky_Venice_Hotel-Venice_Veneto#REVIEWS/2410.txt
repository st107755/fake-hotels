We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky in November 2015 before getting on an Oceania cruise. We are both Diamond members of Hilton and used points for our stay. What a wonderful night we had at the hotel- we were given an executive room facing the canal with the vaporetti and other ships sailing past our open window. I have been to Venice 15 times before and always stayed on the Venice/ San Marco side across from Giudecca so this was a different experience. As we had only one night we did not cross over the canal but stayed on Giudecca for the evening. We had drinks at the truly lovely bar on the top floor of the hotel with a view of the sun setting over Mestre.
