My husband and I just completed a 2 night stay at this beautiful hotel. We were offered an upgrade to a deluxe room with a view at check in, which we were happy to take. This hotel has been beautifully renovated yet has maintained all it's unique charm and details. The breakfast was delicious and offered a little bit of everything, the location is further away from the business of Venice, yet a quick Hilton water taxi is available to take you to San Marco. My husband and I spent a lovely afternoon in the spa and enjoyed a delightful couples massage. The rooftop bar is not to be missed and offers incredible views of Venice. We enjoyed our stay from start to finish, and the staff were always available to make sure our stay was a pleasant one.
