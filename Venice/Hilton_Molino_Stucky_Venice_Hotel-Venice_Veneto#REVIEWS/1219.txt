Nice hotel - for the rating and the chain it is exactly as you'd expect and as described. Wicked rooftop views. The pool is smaller than it appears in the pics - but very welcome when the weather is hot.  One word of warning - you can hear everything in the room next to you. I had a family next to me and could hear people's conversations and children playing (not muffled - clear as a bell). Moved rooms - same thing.   Things to think about - you rely on the shuttle to get back and forth so have to wait around (they're pretty frequent).  You may want that though as the city is packed...although San Paulo seemed quite peaceful. but if you're hot & tired after a days of walking then its another step.
