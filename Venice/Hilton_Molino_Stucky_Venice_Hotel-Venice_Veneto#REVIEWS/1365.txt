We stayed one night after a trip to Padua in this luxurios hotel. During e-check in we dicovered as Diamond members that we were upgraded to a Tower suite. On arrival the friendly lady at the reception told us everything we needed to know and the conscierge booked us a table at the Bacaromi restaurant and also booked us one hour at the wellness center. The Tower suite was an amazingly decorated big suite with an view on beatifull venice. We enjoyed every minute being there. Spa was also great. When we entered the Bacaromi restaurant we felt that the next door restaurant Aromi was more our style and it was no problem to change. We even were offered a free glass of proscecco. Diner at Aromi was super and the Brunelo wine was also perfect in combination with the Food.
