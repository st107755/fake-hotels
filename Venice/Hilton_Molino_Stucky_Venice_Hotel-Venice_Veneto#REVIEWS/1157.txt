My husband and I were of two minds about our hotel for Venice, he wanted to stay on the main island somewhere historic and with character, I wanted to stay somewhere familiar but easy to get to and from the main island. As the Molino is built in an old historic mill, we compromised. I booked us spa treatments at the same time that I booked the hotel, and was happy to see that the water taxi drops off right in front of the main entrance which was easy with our luggage - though TOP TIP: pack light when you go to Venice as water taxis are the only way to get to/from the airport and light packing is the way to go! Having been before, we knew this - but my heart went out to the tourists who clearly were seeing Venice as part of a longer trip and had lots of big bags.
