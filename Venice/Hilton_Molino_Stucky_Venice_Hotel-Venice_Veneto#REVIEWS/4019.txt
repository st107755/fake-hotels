First time stay in Molino Stucky. Nice location on a separate island (Giudecca), nevertheless, easily to reach (via waterbus or private and complimentary hotel shuttle).  Given my Diamond status, we were given a room upgrade to an executive suite. Large room with a 'normal' channel view (nothing special or scenic).  Unfortunately, that already summarises the good impressions. Negative highlight was that the features / package I had paid for were not considered / provided. These included a (i) dinner on the second day of your arrival (ii)  welcome gift and (iii) complimentary Venice city guidebook.  Upon arrival, I was negatively surprised by that fact that basically none of the above 'additional' features were provided / considered / were prepated.
