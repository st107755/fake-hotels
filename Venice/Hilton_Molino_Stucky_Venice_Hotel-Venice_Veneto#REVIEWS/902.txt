My husband and I stayed 3 nights at the Hilton Molino Stucky Venice Hotel. The location of this hotel is just across the Grand Canel and felt private and secluded.  The Hilton Water Taxi was a great way to get back and forth to where all the action is near St. Marco Square.   The hotel it self was very clean, just as you would expect from a Hilton with a beautiful lobby lounge area to sit, read and have a drink.  The breakfast buffet was beyond expectations.   We spent one full day at the Spa to relax and recharge.  The roof top pool area was closed as it was October and we had just missed the cut off.   We loved the history of the building. We were very happy with this hotel, location and would stay here again for sure.
