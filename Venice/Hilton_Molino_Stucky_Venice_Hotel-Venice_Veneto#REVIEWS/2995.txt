Stayed here recently (May15); a very good & large hotel on Guidecca Island, away from the hustle of Venice main but easily accessible by the hotels private shuttle boat or by ACTV waterbus from Palanca (5mins)  Its a big hotel & has lots of conferences; Kaspersky & Westinghouse whilst we were there but it doesnt spoil things.   We booked an Executive double room / suite & got one on the 5th floor; I would say, check your room prior to accepting it........ours didnt have a bath just a shower & was on the side view of the Arsenale building.......there are about 5 blocks! we changed it to another room overlooking the courtyard with a bath but no view of any water. The main rooms at the front overlooking the Guidecca canal had all been booked out to conferences.
