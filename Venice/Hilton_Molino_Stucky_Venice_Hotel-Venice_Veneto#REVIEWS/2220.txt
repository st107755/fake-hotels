Just spent a family weekend in Venice. An Italian friend recommended the Hilton Molino Stucky. While we originally planned to stay in the city, we opted for the Hilton, which is situated on one of the islands. However, easy and efficient boat shuttle service (full at peak times though; might have to opt for public water system), getting you to St. Marcus within 15 minutes. Spectacular view over the canal from the hotel. Some nice little trattorias also situated close to the Hilton. The Hotel itself is very new with very friendly, caring staff. We had a family suite, which was great value for money when traveling with four. Don't miss out on the excellent roof top bar. The roof top pool was unfortunately still closed (opened during the summer season).
