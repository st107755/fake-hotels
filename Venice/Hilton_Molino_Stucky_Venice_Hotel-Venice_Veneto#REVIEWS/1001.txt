Hilton's experience in running top hotels, together with a great conversion of an old flour mill into a modern hotel, coupled with its location right on the Giudecca Canal, makes an unbeatable winning combination and shows what can be achieved as compared to other older hotels constantly trying to update existing old layouts. Hilton leads the way in showing Italian hoteliers how to run a hotel in Italy. The room and bathrooms were a generous size, with a separate shower stall from the bath, but shower area was a bit small and shower pressure was low. Great air-conditioning and tea/coffee facilities in room - other Italian hotels please take note. Could do with more power points at desk height for accessory charging.
