Location We arrived at the Hilton via a private water taxi from the airport. At €110 it seemed a little spendy, but it turned out to be a ‘must do’ (in our opinion at least), even if the driver was a bit of a misery and subsequently didn’t get a tip (this isn’t the USA and means you don’t have to tip if you get rubbish service).  The hotel has an imposing location and it’s a beautifully industrial building (an ex factory).  I’d seen a previous ‘moaning minnie’ on here complaining that the location was poor (on the basis that you had to get a boat everywhere). I’m guessing that they hadn’t read that Venice is on water!   For us, it was a welcome respite from the maddening crowds.
