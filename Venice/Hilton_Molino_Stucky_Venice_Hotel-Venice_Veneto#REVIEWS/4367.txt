My wife and I stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky in July 2013 for 5 nights as part of a city break to Venice. Our booking was based on a bed & breakfast basis, and was booked via Expedia who offered a special rate at the time of booking. As most readers will appreciate, Venice is a unique city with no cars whatsoever – all journeys are either made on foot, or on water. The Molino Stucky is situated on the Giudecca island and is reached easily from Marco Polo Airport by using the Alilaguna Blue line ferry service. This service runs from the airport, via several intermediate stops, to a jetty right at the main entrance to the hotel.
