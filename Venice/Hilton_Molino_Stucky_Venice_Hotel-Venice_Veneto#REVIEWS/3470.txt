After staying in 2 smaller hotels in Rome and Venice, it nice to stay a large, full featured hotel.  The location was conveniently located.  All though not on the Island with St. Marks Square, the free hotel water shuttle was very efficient, and it was nice to get away from the more crowded areas.The room was large and the AC worked seamlessly. The bathroom sink had plenty of counter space to spread out our personal items, and the bidet was an interesting experience. The tube had not enclosure or shower curtain, which made it difficult to keep the floor dry.  We had to use towels to mop up the floor.  The staff was very helpful, and assisted us in making restaurant reservations.  The view from the roof top lounge were impressive.
