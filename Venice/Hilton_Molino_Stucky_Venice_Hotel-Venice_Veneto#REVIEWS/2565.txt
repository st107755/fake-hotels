We arrived by private water taxi late afternoon from the airport and the Hotel looks stunning when coming in to stop. It was definitely worth the extra 120 Euros if you can afford it. Check in was quick straight forward and simple, although we did get a bit lost trying to find the correct lift.  Positives  We had a standard room and it was very big, en-suite with bath and separate shower also large, very clean.  All staff were very helpful and polite.  Sky Bar cocktails were superb.  Hotel Boat always on time and apart from one morning there was always room. We did however buy an ACTV pass to use during our stay and the stop is only a short walk away.
