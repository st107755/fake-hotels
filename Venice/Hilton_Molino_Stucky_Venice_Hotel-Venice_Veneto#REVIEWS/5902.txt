We stayed at the Molino Stucky as a 3-day add-on stay in Venice following our Italian cruise on the Oceania line.  Although the room was very nice and quite spacious by European standards, it overlooked a small interior courtyard.  Since we were on a low floor, all the natural sunlight into our room was blocked by the surrounding hotel structure - we had to literally put our heads out the window to see if it was sunny or cloudy!  Also, since cell phones are apparently not permitted in the public areas of the hotel (a plus for most guests, if this is truly their policy), it meant that cell phone users were stepping into "our" courtyard to make their calls - we were treated to one-sided conversations in a variety of languages at all hours of the day and night.
