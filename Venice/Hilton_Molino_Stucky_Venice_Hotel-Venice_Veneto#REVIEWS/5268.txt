This was a great hotel.  We stayed there using points but were still given rooms with views of Venice.  It was just an awesome view.  It was a sold out weekend we were there but they were still able to give us 2 connecting rooms.  The only meals we ate were breakfast using the concierge lounge as a Diamond Honors Member.  It was by far one of the best breakfasts in an executive lounge I have had.  We also used the lounge for some drinks and light snacks in the evening.  Granted the Hotel is huge and sometimes things are hard to find, but when in Italy, why rush and get worked up over small stuff?  Overall it was great and we can't wait till we go back to Venice.  We'll definitely be staying in the Molino Stucky next time.  PS.
