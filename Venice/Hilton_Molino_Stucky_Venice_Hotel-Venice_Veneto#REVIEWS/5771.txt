My husband and I stayed at the Molino Stucky recently for 3 nights.  I wanted to stay somewhere special as a treat for his birthday and thought that the Hilton would be the Ideal choice, as they are renowned for being good quality hotels with good service.  However, I was bitterly disappointed.  Not only is the hotel over-priced but the level of customer service was particularly poor.  The hotel was a nice easy boat ride from the airport (although this costs about £100) but there wasn’t the usual Hilton welcome on arrival – the staff weren’t forthcoming with offers to help with luggage and the reception staff weren’t exactly welcoming either!
