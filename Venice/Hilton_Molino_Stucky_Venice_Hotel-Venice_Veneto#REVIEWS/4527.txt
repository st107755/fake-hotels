We just finishing spending three nights at this wonderful hotel. Yes, it was pricey, but traveling with two children (ages 5 months and 4 years) we wanted to stay in an American-style hotel to assure ourselves that the room would be big enough. The roof-top swimming pool was incredible and our 4-year son especially LOVED it. I highly recommend this hotel! My recommendations:  - Definitely pay for the breakfast at the time of booking - you won't be sorry. It is also a lot less expensive to pay for it ahead of time. - buy the 'umlimited' vaporetto pass for the duration of your stay in Venice. (Kids under about 5 or 6 are free.)
