Stayed as a couple on honeymoon. Extremely impressed with the building which is beautiful with so much character. The inside is luxurious and perfectly clean. It is much better than other Hilton or other 5* hotel chains I have been to and the 'feel' of the hotel is so much more individual without sacrificing quality. We loved the roof top bar and the service and staff throughout the hotel was attentive and polite. On the roof top bar there were complimentary nibbles before drinks were even ordered but what amazing views from the top of the building. The frequent complimentary taxis really made this an ideal place to stay with Venice less than 5 mins away. We dined in the restaurant and the food was very good. We definitely will be back.
