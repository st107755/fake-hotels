This was our first stop on a multi-city visit of Italy.  For a hotel that was built from a converted factory, it really was a nice stay.  *Suggest you prearrange a water taxi transfer from the airport with the hotel.  It was a great start to the visit having a quick tour through the grand canal before heading to the hotel.  The hotel will meet you outside baggage claim and escort you over to the water taxi pier and then go with you all the way to the hotel.  The trip was about 45 minutes.  *Hotel is somewhat "disconnected" from the rest of Venice.  However, they have a shuttle that runs continually throughout the day (except for two, 1-hour blocks of time for a "break").
