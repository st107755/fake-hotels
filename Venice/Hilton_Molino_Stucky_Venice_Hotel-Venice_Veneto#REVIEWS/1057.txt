Excellent location. We arrived via the hotels's arranged private ( boat obviously) transfer from the airport. It is around £450 but the most amazing way to arrive. It took us down the Grand Canal and was perfect. We had booked an exec room with view, which was charming with a lovely view but as our teenage daughter was with us, the additional bed placed under the window prevented enjoyment of the view and meant our side table could only have one chair, so we couldn't sit and enjoy an aperitif together. After the 2nd night a family suite became available which the hotel moved us to complimentary. That was extremely spacious ( but no view). The exec room is fine for 2 people. The exec lounge is a small area sectioned from the main bar.
