Just returned with 5 friends to the above hotel.  It certainly deserves its 5 star ratings.  The only downfall to that is that the price of eating and drinking is very expensive.  But do not despair, there are lots of bars and restaurants we have discovered for you.  Just a few tips for when you travel.   When you arrive at the airport, turn left when you come through arrivals and there is a Public Ticket information box.  Ask for a return ticket for the Airport Express coach to Marco Polo (bus station) it costs 11 euros return. Also ask to buy a 3 day boat pass (imob.venezia Actv) for 35 euros which is well worth the money.  You can travel all over Venice (including the Grand Canal) on this ticket up to your return to the airport (just reverse your journey).
