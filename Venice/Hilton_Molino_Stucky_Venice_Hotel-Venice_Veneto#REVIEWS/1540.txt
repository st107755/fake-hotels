An imposing building in a former mill with a great history. 30 min by water taxi from the airport and 15 min from the main square. In a peaceful location away from the hustle and bustle. I was part of a large group attending a workshop and the hotel had no problem accommodating us all. It is a classic decor throughout the hotel, my room and bathroom was fairly spacious overlooking the beautiful canal, the bed was comfortable, I had all the mod cons and toiletries I would expect in my room. The meeting rooms were excellent, the food was very very good both in our private area and in the main restaurant. And they had an excellent roof top bar which was great, although I would not eat up there, the food in the lobby bar is better.
