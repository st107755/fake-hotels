My husband and I recently stayed for 4 nights at this hotel when celebrating our wedding anniversary. Everything about the hotel was wonderful, but I particularly noted the calm atmosphere everywhere. Even when it was busy, everyone seemed to be happy with the service they were receiving. This is not always the case with this class of hotel, where expectations are high.  The staff were friendly and helpful, but not obtrusive. Naturally, the room was great, as you would expect from a 5* hotel. There were many small touches which you simply do not find elsewhere.  The building is a fascinating place that has been very tastefully, and interestingly renovated.  The shuttle boat service was efficient, punctual and an enjoyable part of our stay.
