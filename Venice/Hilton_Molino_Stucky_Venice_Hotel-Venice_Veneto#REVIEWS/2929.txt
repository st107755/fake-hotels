Location: This hotel is across the Grand Canal, which means you need to take a shuttle to get to St. Mark's or anything else you may want to see. We took the hotel's free shuttle twice and did not have a problem -- the shuttles ran on time and it was a short trip. If there are more than 39 people on the shuttle, however, you will need to take the next one, and another family told us they took a water taxi because of a problem with a shuttle. Take the schedule with you and keep in mind that while the shuttle normally runs every 20 minutes, there are "breaks" when the shuttle doesn't run.   Rooms and Views: The views from our rooms and the 8th floor rooftop pool were stunning and the rooms and baths were large and beautiful.
