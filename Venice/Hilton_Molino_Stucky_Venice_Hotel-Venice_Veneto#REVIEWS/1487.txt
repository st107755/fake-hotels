We have just returned from a 2 night stay as a surprise for my husbands 40th birthday.  Reached the hotel by private water taxi, 20 minutes from the airport, which was fantastic. We had a delux room not booked with a view but had a gorgeous view of the Grand Canal and overlooking Venice.   I had read many reviews of hearing neighbours through inter-connecting doors so asked for a non connecting room, which we were given and had no problem hearing neighbours.  The most comfortable bed I have slept in during a hotel stay, it was amazing. Room was great, we had no concerns and would stay again, room 523 for ref.  The breakfast was outstanding...we had buffet which has ample choice and I ordered an omelette and porridge from the kitchen too.
