My wife and I just spent 5 nights here and we truly enjoyed our stay. There are 2 ways to stay in Venice, one is where all the action and the crowds and noise are or you can stay at the Hilton Molino Stucky, and I'm glad we stayed here. The hotel is situated away from all the hustle and bustle of the crowds, but the free shuttle boat that the hotel offers runs until 11:30 at night and every half hour or so you can catch a ride to and from San Marco Square. The overall service was great and our room had a spectacular view over looking the water. Like any other 5 star hotel expect food and beverages to be pricy, but that's what we work for, to enjoy that small window of down time where you try to break the budget rules.
