Valentines was made even more special as we stayed here. Amazing room with a glass chandelier and full marble bathroom the breakfast is served from 7 am until 10:30 am monday - saturday and is a self serve breakfast. You can choose from cereal, fresh fruit , deli counter , pastries and of course cooked breakfast. Several juices are served also and fresh coffee at the table. The hotel has a 24hr free gym with access to the spa pools / sauna 25 euro for 2 hours. A free bus will take you to zattere or san marco and this runs frequently and is a fast service also. (Only for hilton guests) All the staff are multi lingual so communicating is no problem although it is best to learn please and thank you.
