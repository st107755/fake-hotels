This is a large, impersonal hotel that caters to business travelers, conference attendees and pre/post cruise stays. If your travel falls into one of those catagories, this may be the place for you. It was a terrible choice for a romantic Venice stay. It would actually be almost funny if I hadn't spent 300 euros a night for what was to be one of the worst hotel stays in my experience. I had purchased a package that included tickets for the Guggenheim gallery. I did not notice until later that I had not been given them upon check-in. I went to the concierge, who told me I had to wait until the next day to receive them.
