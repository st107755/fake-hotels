I just spent 3 nights at this hotel.  I think the location is great.  It is a 10 minute water taxi ride to St. Marks Square and the hotel provides this service on a printed schedule.  So what you get for this is a quiet hotel, no loud noises at night.  The views from the hotel are great.  Lot's of boats going by including cruise ships.  The breakfast buffet is well stocked.  The internet is fast.  The rooms are a reasonable size and the bed is very comfortable.  You get great advise from the concierge desk as to places to eat.  So what's not to like about this place?  Nothing.  I booked my room thru Venere and was surprised as to the  low price.  I had to wait till the last minute to book my room and as I waited, I watched the price go down.  I was surprised.
