For anyone that is well travelled I would say that this hotel is not the typical 5 star standard, it's overpriced and the rooms are tired and in need of a refurb.  Service in the hotel is great and can not fault that at all.  Exec lounge access was included in my booking and really was great. We visited for snacks during the day and probably saved over a hundred euros just there!   The skyline bar was ridiculously overpriced, I payed over 25 euro for a double vodka soda. Living in London I have seen expensive drinks before, this was a joke.  The shuttle service was great, use the timetable and you are fine ... Takes some organising but no problem at all.
