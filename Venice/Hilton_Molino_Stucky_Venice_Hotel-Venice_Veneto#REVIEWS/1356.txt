It was a joy to arrive at this hotel via the private water taxi from Marco Polo airport to see the huge imposing building and pull up outside, felt a bit like James Bond! We used the shared option with Viator which meant we dropped off a few other people on the way so it took longer but cost 55 euros - a private one is 130 euros!  I would definitely recommend this way of arriving from the airport because coming by coach or  train and then vaporetto would take a long time and require lots of hulking bags up and over bridges on the main island or by Alilaguna there was a big queue.   The hotel itself is a good quality Hilton. We used 2 of the three restaurants and I would especially recommend the Aromi.
