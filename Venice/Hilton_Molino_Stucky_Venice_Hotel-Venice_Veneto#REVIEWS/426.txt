We spent 5 days here in January. We found every aspect of this hotel lovely. The staff is topnotch, with everyone speaking English and Italian (and many other languages) fluently. Our welcome as Diamond members was exceptional and we received a gorgeous, spacious room that looked out onto the Giudecca Canal. The hotel was a re-purposed pasta and bread factory that was made over in an elegant and modern style but retained the charm of exposed beams and the beautiful brick facade. Breakfast was included in our rate and had a pretty good assortment of international breakfast styles. We ordered eggs from the kitchen and they came out perfectly cooked, whether poached, fried or as an omelet. Attentive, helpful staff, great management team.
