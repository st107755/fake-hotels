Really good hotel. Stayed in an executive room with view which had great views over the grand canal. Room was well equipped, very clean and bed was very comfortable. Ate in one of the hotel's restaurants on one night and food was good. Breakfast was generally more than adequate other than cold tea and coffee on one of the days. Skyline bar offers fantastic views over Venice and well worth a trip up to the top of the hotel. Check in and check out at reception was swift and efficient. All the staff spoke excellent English and were very helpful The only disappointing feature was the executive lounge. Having stayed in plenty of Hiltons over the years I found the executive lounge to be small, not very comfortable and selection of food and snacks was very sparse!!
