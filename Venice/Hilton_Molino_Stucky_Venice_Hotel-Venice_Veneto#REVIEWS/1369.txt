We were really impressed with our stay here both in terms of the hotel comfort / facilities and what it offered the visitor to Venice in particular. It does not have the charm of olde world charm of more traditional Venetian hotels, but has a lot to recommend it.   The hotel was generously staffed, those staff were friendly and efficient but never overly deferential  which is a particular dislike of mine in many  of the upper range hotels . Check in/ out  was quick and easy with an efficient system of looking after your baggage after check out.  Our room was a decent  size with large comfortable twin beds and  bathroom . It was blissfully quiet over night too both from adjoining rooms and the corridors - an excellent night's sleep.
