My husband and I Stayed here for 3 nights, with 2 other couples and we had a fantastic time.  My friend booked the rooms, which were executives with lounges.  Unfortunately our room was not with a lounge, so my friend who booked explained and the next morning we were put into an executive with lounge and view of canal with a bottle of prosecco and apology note.  Huge TVs  in both lounge and bedroom, toilet off lounge and bathroom off bedroom, it was huge.  As were both other couples, fabulous.  Also with executive rooms you get executive lounge from 5 pm till 7.  With drinks, nibbles, sandwiches and a couple of hot snacks, all very nice.  Breakfast was good, very nice choice, and they take orders for eggs at the table.
