Looking for a hotel in venice can be difficult however after  much research, we decided on the Hilton hotel. This was our first visit to venice so we were unsure of the lay of the land. I personally prefer contemporary, modern hotel rooms and while searching for venice hotels, most of them seemed very old fashioned. The Hilton hotel didn't disappoint. The rooms are spacious with beautiful marble bathrooms and huge, comfortable beds. Full continental breakfast is available.   The only disappointment is that the wifi is quite expensive.  The hotel is on a separate island (guidecca )away from the main San Marco area, however the hotel offers a free shuttle to and from San Marco square.  From the airport, we took the Alilaguna shuttle boats which have around 10 stops.
