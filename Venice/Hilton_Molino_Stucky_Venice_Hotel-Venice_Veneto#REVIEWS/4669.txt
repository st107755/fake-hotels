This is a fantastic hotel with a courteous and friendly staff.  They were very tolerant of my attempts at speaking Italian and graciously helped me along in finding the correct words.  The hotel is located on the island of Giudecca and has a continuous shuttle boat that runs between Giudecca and two stops on either side of the Gran Canal.  My family (wife, 18yr old son, 12yr old daughter and me) found Giudecca to be a great starting and ending point for the day.  It is much quieter and less touristy than the main islands of Venice.  The hotel has fantastic views from the guestrooms and the breakfast was over-the-top!!!  Make sure to take in the sights from the rooftop pool deck.
