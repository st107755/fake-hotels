This hotel has become my go to place to stay in Venice (I have been there 3 times in the last year).  The experience is first class, quiet, and restful.  The hotel is across the canal from San Marco Square (on Giudecca Island) providing beautiful views of Venice without the crush of people and tight accommodations.  The free hotel shuttle runs across the canal every 30 to 60 minutes depending on time of day.  I think the hours are from 08:00 to 24:00. If you want to party after hours off San Marco square, this is not the hotel for you.    It is a sprawling complex built from what I understand was a flour mill.    The rooms are very large by Venetian standards and luxurious.  The standard room featured 2 double beds, desk, dresser, sitting table, and closet.
