We have visited Venice many times over the years, usually staying in the middle of the hustle, bustle and noise of Venice. This visit we stayed at the Stucky. It certainly was a different experience. I travel frequently for work in the Asia Pacific area and use Hilton hotels a lot and so I thought using Hilton for a holiday stop in Italy would be interesting.  Our room was clean, as quite as a tomb and the bed comfortable. The bathroom was very generously proportioned with a separate bath tub and shower. Alas, no face washers were provided.  We tried to use the room phone but it wasn't working. Also there was no hotel information in the room nor was there a minibar price list.
