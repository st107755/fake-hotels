I stayed here with my mum in April. The hotel is beautiful and as others have previously mentioned, it is a converted flour mill.  We arrived by taxi which cost 120 euro each way and took about 30 minutes from the airport. I had booked the hotel as a package through BA holidays and had previously upgraded our room to an executive room. I would fully suggest doing this if you can afford to do so. We decided to do this as breakfast was not included in our package and the hotel informed me it was 30 euro per person per day for breakfast so it actually worked out cheaper to upgrade.  We loved the fact the Hilton wasn't in the hustle and bustle and loved the boat journeys across to San Marco!
