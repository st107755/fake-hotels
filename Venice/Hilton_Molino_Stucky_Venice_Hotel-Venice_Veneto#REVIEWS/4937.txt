I stayed with my wife and two teenage kids in exec rooms. The rooms were ok, if a little small and dark, but not sure if the upgrade to 'Venice view' at 42Euros per night per room was worth it. We had access to the executive lounge, but having stayed at Peabodys Orlando and the Waldorf in Rome, it is poor in comparison. Limited drinks and no food until 6pm. They allow access to basic spirits between 7pm and 8.30pm when most people are out dining. I know it's the Hilton, in a major tourist city, but the prices are high end and 25 euros will just about get you burger and chips in the bar restaurant.
