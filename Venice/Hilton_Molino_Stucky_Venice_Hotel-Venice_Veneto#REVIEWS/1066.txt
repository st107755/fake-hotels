Nice hotel, as you would expect of Hilton.  The location gives a lovely view of Venice, particularly from the rooftop pool.  Rooms are nice.  Staff are competent and polite.  Food is good.  Exec lounge seems a little too small for the size of the hotel.  I wouldn't hesitate in recommending this hotel.
