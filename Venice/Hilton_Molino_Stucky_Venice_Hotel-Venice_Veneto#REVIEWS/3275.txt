The Hilton Molino Stucky is situated across the canal from St Marcos Sq, in which you can get a free water boat (provided by the hotel) across to the other side of the canal (2-5min) or stay on the boat to get to St Marco (approx. 15-20min). We found this very useful and they run late into the evening so if you go out for food/ drinks you do not need to worry about getting back to the hotel.  The hotel itself is beautiful inside. Immaculately presented, with grand hallways filled with interesting décor. There is a large lounge/bar area downstairs and the bar up towards the top floor is highly recommended where you have great views out over Venice. There is a pool on the roof however we did not get to see it as we went in May, a couple weeks before it opened for the summer.
