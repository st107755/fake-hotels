We recently stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky Nov. 19-21 2011.  The hotel is very new and modern.  The price for the room was amazing for a 5 star hotel of this caliber.  We did have some issues with our original room (I-Pod player will not work without requesting a non-battery operated clock radio, hotel room hair dryer needs an adaptor from front desk before use, and voltage converter in the bathroom malfunctioning).  The Hilton Molino Stucky made up for these issues by giving superb customer service.  Thank you to Constance the amazing "Guest Relations Manager" for resolving our issues and making our stay absolutely amazing.  We will stay here again on our next visit to Venice without question.
