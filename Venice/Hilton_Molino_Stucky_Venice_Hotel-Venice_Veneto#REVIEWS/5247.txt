My boyfriend and I stayed here for two nights, one of which was Valentine’s Day. I’ve stayed here on a previous visit to Venice, as well, and have had no complaints. It’s a five-star hotel and it’s a Hilton. You can’t really go wrong. The staff spoke excellent English and were very helpful and friendly. Even though I booked the room through Hotwire, we asked for a room with a view and we were kindly granted our request, which was a nice treat for Valentine’s Day. The rooms were spacious and almost impeccably clean. The hotel bar on the seventh floor offered a spectacular view of Venice and the sunset (my favorite!), but few cocktails on their menu are under $20. It may be worth it, if only for one drink.
