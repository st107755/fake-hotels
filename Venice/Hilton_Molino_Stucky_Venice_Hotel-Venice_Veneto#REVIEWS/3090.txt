It was my fifth time in this magical city and I stayed for 2.5 weeks in the comfort  and hospitality of Hilton Molino Stucky and I can not praise enough the hotel and its personnel. It was my first time staying in the island  of Giudecca, which is across Giudecca canal from Venice, which is a great advantage, because after a busy and often exhausting day in Venice it is a pure pleasure to get to the tranquility of Giudecca by the hotel's shuttle, which is very reliable, runs often enough and costs practically nothing (I had to pay only 2.50 euro for the entire stay and unlimited use of this shuttle). It takes about 3 minutes to cross the canal and get to Zattere and 10 minutes to get to San Marco.
