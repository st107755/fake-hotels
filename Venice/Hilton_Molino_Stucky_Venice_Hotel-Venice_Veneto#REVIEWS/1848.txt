We stayed there pre-cruise in July, 2016, primarily because I'm a Hilton Gold member.  This is a very good choice in Venice, as it is away from the craziness of the major tourist attractions and you won't have to schlep your luggage through the crowded stone-paved streets.  The shuttle boat is both frequent and reliable.  The bldg. is magnificent, as well, and the amenities and service are both generally good.  Now, here are some issues to consider, particularly if you are a Hilton HHonors elite member.    First, the Exec Lounge is the worst I have seen worldwide! Cocktails are only served during a very narrow window of time in the evening and, even then, the selection is very limited.
