Our family of four (kids aged 9 and 12) just returned from three nights at the Molino Stucky. We liked the location outside of the busiest part of Venice. The hotel breakfasts were good and the dinner at the hotel restaurant enjoyable (while pricey). There are many nice little restaurants along the river for dinner.   During our first night, there was an incident with ants having occupied our family suite. We complained and the staff responded promptly and professionally and moved us into two new rooms. The following night they moved us into yet another room (a larger suite), so that we could stay together. That was very nice and appreciated.
