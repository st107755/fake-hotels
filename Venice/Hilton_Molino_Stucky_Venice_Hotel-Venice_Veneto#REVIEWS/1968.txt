We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky for 3 nights, three adults and 2 children under 4.  Requested adjoining rooms which was provided along with a free room upgrade. Rooms were a good size, enough space for the children and very comfortable.   Staff were excellent, well informed and welcoming.   The pool is superb, excellent if you are visiting Venice with children, just to allow them to burn off some energy.  The view really is as amazing as photos suggest, especially when a cruise liner is passing!  We stayed during the Rendatore festival and had the opportunity to view the fireworks from the rooftop, a unique view.   Room service was quick and a decent amount of choice.
