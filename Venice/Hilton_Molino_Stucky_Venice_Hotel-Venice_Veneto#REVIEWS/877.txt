Excellent location. Hotel not busy , staff cool rather than welcoming and not terribly flexible or accommodating. For complex reasons had 3 different bookings for 3 separate nights different grades of rooms. Comparing them they all seem to be the same size. Note rooms with a view may not all have the front facing view towards Venice . Executive lounge is a bit strange with its glass wall separating it from the main downstairs lounge, they are sticklers re access we were 15 mins later than checkout time and were unable to get in while awaiting our alilaguna ferry. Not sure it is worth it. Be aware that they will put WiFi charges on your bill unless you are a member of their loyalty programme. Breakfast excellent with superb coffee and overall excellent variety.
