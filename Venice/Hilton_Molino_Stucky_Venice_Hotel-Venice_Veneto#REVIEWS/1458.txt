We were placed here by our cruise line to extend our stay in Venice for two nights.  The Molino Stucky is an upscale hotel, but more of a 4 star rather than 5 star.  It's located in an industrial area on Giudecca island across the large shipping channel from the main islands where the primary tourist attractions are located.  The rooms were well appointed and furnished with luxury style toiletries and bedding, however the reason for my rating is that our room on an upper floor was very hot with no air conditioning.  They admitted they don't turn it on until April?  We were then given an upgrade to an Executive room on the channel side second floor.  It was warm also, but there were two windows we opened.
