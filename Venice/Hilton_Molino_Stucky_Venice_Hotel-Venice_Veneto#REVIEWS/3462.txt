Excellent location for a stay in Venice. You can get to the hotel by water bus directly from the Airport, which takes about 1.5 hours and is a lovely introduction to the waterways of Venice. You can also get the water bus from the hotel to the port, if you're off on a cruise like we were.  The hotel looks very impressive as you approach it from the water bus, and continues to impress when you get inside. It was a mill originally and it has been beautifully renovated.  The staff are friendly and helpful and there was a good choice at the buffet breakfast. The hotel also has a regular courtesy boat that can take you across to San Marco (St Marks Square) and back again, which was great.  Lovely rooftop pool with great views over Venice.
