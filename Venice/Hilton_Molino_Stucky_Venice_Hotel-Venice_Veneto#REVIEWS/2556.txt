This is a very easy review to write because the Molino Stucky is a perfect place to stay in Venice. My wife and I have visited Venice on 7 occasions and this recent visit is our second at the Molino. The location on the Guidecca island is easy access to St. Mark's and the Doges Palace by the Hilton private shuttle which takes you to San Zaccaria  and also makes a stop at Zattere for a short 4 minute walk to the Accademia for a visit to the gallery or to catch a vaparetto going back toward Ferrovia train station. It is definitely quicker to take the Hilton shuttle to San Zaccaria than to catch a vaparetto from Ferrovia or Ca d' Oro or even the Rialto stops.
