The hotel is impressive, from outside as well as from inside, it is an example of industrial archeology recovered.   The hotel is at the Giudecca island, much quieter than Venice crowded streets, a 15 min ride from St. Mark in a convenient (free) boat shuttle, and 5 min from Zattere, which is on the other side of the Giudecca Canal.   And from the hotel, Venice deploys its magnificence.  Having said that, I must say that the rooms (at least ours) are outrageously small (they are small as per european standards, that usually are half the size of american standards), no WiFi on our room ("all rooms should have"), no worktable, and the TV set was perpendicular to the bed. Bar prices are a ripoff (22 euros for a Club Sandwich), and there is no alternate solution.
