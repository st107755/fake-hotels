This hotel was a great choice, especially for Hilton Diamond members.  There is a water taxi exclusive for the hotel that transports you to and from St. Mark's Square (€6/ guest for entire stay). Friendly and responsive service, spa (2 hours daily included for Diamonds), fantastic breakfast and access to Executive Lounge.  You can easily save quite allot on dining if you enjoy evening refreshments (wine, beer, salad and appetizers from 5-7PM) before venturing out to all the marvelous restaurants across the canal near St. Mark's square.  The view of Venice from the Skyline Bar is spectacular.  The first night we arrived it was raining and we were tired from our flight from the US.
