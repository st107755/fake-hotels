My family went to Italy this summer and the final part of our trip included Venice at the famous Hilton Molino Stucky.  I want to make this review short and helpful so I'll go to the point.  If you have enough money to pay for this hotel, then seriously consider finding a different one. The transportation arrangements from the hotel are not the best: it sits on an island and you have to make sure that you get to Venice when the hotel is running their boat shuttle to take you across, otherwise, you will walk with your suitcases all over town trying to catch a water taxi that will take you there (not fun).  The service stuff at the hotel is not friendly (as everything and everyone in Venice),  meals are overpriced (49 Euros for a continental breakfast !!!).
