Stayed at the hotel on business trip.  Hotel is very large and indeed can take a while to gain your bearings, particularly if your room is accessed from elevator 1, or 2 or maybe 3...  Room itself was very very spacious and some original features of the building remain with a lovely beamed wooden ceiling.  Room was very comfortable and bathroom also well appointed. Only negative would be that corridors/elevator etc, always had smell of food as my area of the hotel (elevator 3) was located above the breakfast room. Other comment would be the price of a latte at the hotel bar.
