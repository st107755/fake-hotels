My family and I stayed at this property in July.  It is located in an old flour mill factory building and on the island just across the canal from the main island of Venice. So the building has character and history and is a bit away from the throngs of crowds around St Mark’s Square. They do have a great shuttle boat service that leaves from rt in front of the hotel to take you across several times a day for a minimal daily fee of, I believe, $8.  Service was great, everyone was friendly.  We had our room upgraded because we are Diamond level members and the suite was gorgeous!  So remember to ask if you are a Hilton Honors member.  Our main window looked rt out the front over the canal with great views of the boats going by!  Fabulous!
