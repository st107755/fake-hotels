On arrival I thought it was lovely. Beautiful building, amazing canal side location and the staff were really friendly. The hotel lobby and bar area was cool and welcoming. We had a lovely room overlooking a canal with fantastic views and a huge comfortable bed, however there was nowhere in the room with a socket and mirror to dry your hair – this is a common problem in many hotels.  During our stay we enjoyed the Skyline rooftop bar which was wonderful, very chilled out and relaxing. I also used the roof top pool but I was disappointed that a hotel of this standard did not provide ether somewhere to change or robes in the room. The pool was also crowded and sadly there were no sun beds or chairs available.
