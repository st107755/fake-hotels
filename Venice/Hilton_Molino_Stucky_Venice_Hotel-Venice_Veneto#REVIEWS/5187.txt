Writing this review, I realise that I never enjoyed Hilton hotels at all, and try to avoid this mainstream, standard type of comfort. I prefer smaller or locally owned hotels with a face rather than a distant property which is made to please americans abroad.  For a Hilton, this one is pretty much OK, and you can stay in this place without feeling in Venice at all. One could be anywhere in the world.  The shuttle service is good, and the fact that the hotel is not on one of the main islands is fine. Giudecca allows one to see Venice without many tourists, too.  Nonetheless, I will not stay at this hotel anymore, as there are many properties in the city which are much, much nicer, at this price level.  Provide italian rather than filter coffee at breakfast.
