The only good thing about this hotel was the terrific view of the Giudecca Canal from our bedroom. We stayed here for 2 nights after a cruise. The bedroom had an inch of dust on the carpet around the skirting board. The edge of the surface surrounding the bathroom sink was thick with dust. The small safe would not work, so I had to carry my handbag everywhere . The first morning at breakfast, the dining rooms were full, so we were in an "overspill" area. Fruit consisted of single grapes & very small pieces of chopped apple. Hot breakfast not very exciting.Large plate for toast had 1 piece on it. Halfway through breakfast, tables were cleared & chef appeared, pushing a trolley & started loading metal containers containing hot food onto it. He then left. It was 9.30am.
