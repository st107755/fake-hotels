We stayed here from the 14.02.15-19.02.15.We found the room to be a reasonable size and very clean and tidy but we did have a poor view. The bed was extremely comfy and one of the best we have slept in. Breakfast was good with a fair amount of choice. The  overall facilities in the hotel were good except you have to pay to use the indoor pool and spa. During our visit we did use the gym however quite a few of the pieces of equipment were out of order. During our stay we had to request milk and tea every day as these were not being replenished. We did book a meal at the restaurant however the choice was very limited so we cancelled it.
