We had an excellent experience at the Hilton Molino Stucky.  The location across the Guidecca Canal from Venice was nice and quiet, offering a little breathing space.  A private shuttle boat runns to Zattere and San Marco, and the Alilaguna blue line (running from the airport and cruise terminal) stopped right in front of the hotel.  No trucking bags through the narrow streets of Venice!  Being one stop away from the cruise terminal was extremely convenient on our embarkation day. The rooms were spacious and well appointed.  We even were lucky enough to get a two room suite on our final stay, which was fantastic.  They also have a rooftop pool that has a breathtaking view of the city.
