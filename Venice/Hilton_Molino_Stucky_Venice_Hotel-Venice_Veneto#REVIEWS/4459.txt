We booked this Hilton for two nights as the last leg of our Italian vacation. As is the case with almost all Hilton hotels, the rooms are extremely comfortable, spacious and clean. After walking around so much during our trip, their comfortable bed was exactly what we needed to rest up and relax.  It wasn't hard for us to find this hotel, as we simply followed the directions given by the staff via email using the Vaporetto service. This Hilton isn't on the main Venice island but they provide a complimentary shuttle service that goes at least once every hour to the main center of the city. This turned out to be a huge advantage as we didn't have to rely on any other form of transportation, hence saving good money!
