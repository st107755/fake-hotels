We stayed at the Hilton on our first visit to Venice for my wife's birthday. Ours was an Executive room so we had access to the Executive lounge which is on the ground floor next to the reception. The room was nice and we found the executive lounge to be a great way to keep us hydrated during the summer heat, besides the lounge being a good place to relax while watching the canal outside (food and drinks at the lounge are complimentary).  Although the hotel is not centrally located but they have a free boat service which departs every 15-20 mins and runs like clockwork. You can use the boat to go to Zattere or San Marco and come back.
