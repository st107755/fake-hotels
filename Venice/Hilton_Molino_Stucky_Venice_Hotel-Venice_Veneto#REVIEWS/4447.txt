Stayed for 4 nights at this lovely hotel. Beautiful property away from the hustle & bustle of Venice proper. The hotel shuttle water bus to San Marco runs on time & regularly. Although we enjoyed our stay very much, we were surprised that our executive suite only comes with 2 bottles of complimentary water on the 1st day, unlike other Hilton hotels we had stayed in the past which replenished on a daily basis. We were simply told by room service staff  that the tap water is safe to drink if we do not wish to order from the mini bar. The executive lounge is a good place for a light breakfast & snacks & drinks throughout the day. There are a couple of nice restaurants in Giudecca, near the hotel but the prices are slightly higher than what Venice offers.
