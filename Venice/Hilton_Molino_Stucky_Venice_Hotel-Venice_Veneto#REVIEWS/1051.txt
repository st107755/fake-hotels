As a Hilton Honors Gold member I booked loyally...but I have to say that whilst the location is stunning the customer service and general layout of the hotel is seriously lacking - it is almost as if Hilton are trying to do too much. I fell for the monthly rooftop dinner, about €120 per head without wine etc. The service was amateur (served champagne in wine glasses, food order 'lost' - even though the chef was 2m behind me!, and the staff had no idea what they were serving)...5* service it was not. I sent an email pointing out the shoddy service but received a totally inadequate reply (not even a "have some HH points, now go away")...my next booking was NOT a Hilton ('LA CHARTREUSE DU VAL ST.ESPRIT' which is an SLH hotel in northern France, amazing service).
