Although we only stayed one night at this hotel, it made quite a lasting impression on us. Everything from the hotel entrance to the check-in procedure to the rooms and dining was superb. I actually liked being away from the "main part" or Venice as it was a peaceful retreat from the crazy busy-ness of the summer peak tourist season.   We stayed here the night before we got on our cruise, and it was the perfect location. The morning we got on board, we simply took the 10 minute Aliguna waterbus that dropped us at the cruise ship terminal. No dragging our luggage required! The morning breakfast was simply delicious - the only negative was that the staff was having a hard time keeping the food replenished due to the crowded hotel.
