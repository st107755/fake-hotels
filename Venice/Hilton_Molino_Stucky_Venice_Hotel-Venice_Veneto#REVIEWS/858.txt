This hotel is fabulous.  The staff is extremely attentive and professional.  The rooms are very comfortable and the food was amazing.  Bed was very comfortable and the surroundings were welcoming.  The hotel provides a shuttle boat to San Marco and other destinations and was included in the price of the hotel stay.  The shuttle leaves and arrives multiple times during the day and up until 11:45 PM, to return from San Marco, at no additional charge.  The shower could have been a little better with not much pressure but hot water always.  Cleaning service was very consistent and good.  My company hosted a convention here and their services were amazing for the support of the company.
