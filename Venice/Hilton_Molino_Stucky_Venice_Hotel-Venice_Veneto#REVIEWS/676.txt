In planning a trip to trek the Dolomites, I included a 48-hour stay in Venice and I chose to use my excessive Hilton Honor points for once at a nice hotel. The Hilton Molino Stucky did not disappoint!! This was my third trip to Venice and while I’ve stayed in the heart of the energy of Piazza San Marco and enjoyed every minute, I also loved staying off the main island. I found navigating the ACTV public transportation and the freedom of having a Hilton shuttle if need be, fantastic additions to my time in Venice. The rooms were spacious and the inside facing rooms are quiet. We slept for 9 hours without interruption our first night and it was wonderful!!  The rooftop bar and pool are beautiful and the service and staff were truly wonderful.
