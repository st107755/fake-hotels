Stayed here for three days and as my partner is a diamond member, we were upgraded from King room to Tower Room Suite which had panoramic views of Venice. We enjoyed the fact that the hotel is on the south side of Venice and away from the hustle and bustle. The free shuttle across the water was a nice bonus but I don't understand why people moan on here that they had to wait due to capacity issues - there is a public water bus only a few minutes walk away at Palanca which goes straight over to Zattere or further up to San Marco. It's no big deal! Anyway, the hotel is extremely large covering 7 separate buildings and I really like how Hilton have been sympathetic to the origins of the building with lots of metal struts, brickwork and the like.
