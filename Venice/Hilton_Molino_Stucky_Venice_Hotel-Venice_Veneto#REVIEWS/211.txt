I am a Hilton Diamond member so do expect a bit more. By and large the hotel delivered. We arrived about 1PM and our room was ready. The couple we were travelling with have no status and their room was also ready. I was using points and we got a nice room but no upgrade. I see this at Marriott and Hilton where I have the highest status and when you use points, they don’t treat you as well a cash customer. So, this is not to be critical, just a mention. Our room was overall nice and the AC worked quite well during the heat wave in Europe.  To get to the hotel we took a private water taxi from train station – I think 75 euros. To the airport I think it was 120 euros for private water taxi. Two couples and 5+ bags.
