We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky for 5 nights prior to an eastern Mediterranean cruise that was part of our 25th anniversary celebration.  This hotel is located in a beautifully restored mill, which has been renovated into a stylish retreat away from the hustle and bustle of the main island.  We arrived and left in a private water taxi, which is money well spent and in my opinion the best way to arrive into Venice, especially after close to 30 hours of travel time.     The good news was that our room was available and despite what I was advised at check-in, about it being an upgrade (it was the one I booked and expected to be assigned when we arrived, as I called to make the booking with Hilton’s reservations).
