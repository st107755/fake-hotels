My husband and I stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky for our honeymoon. We booked a deluxe room with a view and we were not disappointed. Our room was spacious and well appointed with a fabulous bathroom. The king size bed was the most comfortable bed I have ever slept on! Our room was on 7th floor just below the rooftop pool and we had a stunning view over the Giudecca canal towards San Giorgio. There was a staircase at the end of the corridor which took us directly up to the pool area and rooftop terrace bar which was really handy.   We booked our transfers direct with the hotel and the service was impeccable. We booked a private water taxi so we had a hostess meet us at the airport and escort us to our boat via car. It wasn't cheap but I would certainly recommend it.
