To start off getting to the hotel from the airport I would definitely recommend  hiring a private taxi.  This was easy and drops you directly in front of the hotel and is very direct. It is a great way to arrive at the hotel by water. The hotel is located on a small island form the main area but runs a taxi service regularly.  This was fine for our family needs.   There are a few restaurants that are within walking distance from the hotel on the island that are very good and have outside seating area.  However, be careful as when the water level is high you can get wet if your table is very close to the edge.  We arrived when there had been a lot of rain and so the water level was high and we did see people having to move from their tables.
