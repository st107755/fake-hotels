My family and I - two daughters, 16 and 20, stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky Venice Hotel for one night for the start of our Italy vacation. We flew into Venice and took a water taxi directly to the hotel - water taxi is very much worth the cost, especially with luggage. The Hilton Molino Stucky Venice Hotel is located on a smaller island - Giudecca - directly across from the main island of San Marco. Hilton runs a water shuttle back and and forth, with plenty of time options throughout the day and evening. It was wonderful to stay away from the crowds! Giudecca island also has a few nice restaurants where we enjoyed dinner one night and espresso one morning - again no crowds! The hotel also had a very nice roof top bar with an incredible view.
