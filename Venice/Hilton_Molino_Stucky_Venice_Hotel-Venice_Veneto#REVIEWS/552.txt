We stayed here for 8 days in the beginning of August, and the hotel itself was not bad - but below the standard of other Hilton hotels in my opinion. We had some issues with our minibar (it did not cool) so we contacted the reception, but the people they sent up did not understand a word of english. In total I think we ended up with 4 visits to fix this one thing, which I believe is unessecary and really bad for customer experience.  The breakfast was good and you could order whatever you like - such as eggs benedict and a cappuccino. Nevertheless, there was no information about this on the tables, so this was something I discovered after several days at the hotel by watching other tables.
