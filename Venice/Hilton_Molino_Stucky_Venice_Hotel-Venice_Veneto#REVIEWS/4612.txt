We stayed here for 2 nights before our cruise. We took a private water taxi from the airport which was about 100 euros and took about 30 mins. Although the room we had was facing a small canal with an unimpressive view, everything else about the room was comfortable. The bed was very soft and the bathroom was spacious and they provide you with bathrobes. All the furnishings in the room seemed brand new. We ordered pasta and a hamburger from room service and it was quite good although pricey. They have a complimentary breakfast which was also very good and offered a wide variety of items to choose from. The hotel is on a island by itself and you have to take their complimentary shuttle to get to san marco or zattere. The cruise port is about a 10 minute boat ride.
