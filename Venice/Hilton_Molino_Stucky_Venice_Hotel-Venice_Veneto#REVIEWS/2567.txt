We visited this hotel as a family with a 6yr old staying in a club room.  Overall the hotel was very nice and staff friendly and helpful. We didn't mind the hotel being on a separate island and had ACTV passes so had no problems with transport.  The club lounge however was the worse we have used. As others have mentioned it is a strip sectioned off of the main lobby bar. The selection of drinks and food are very limited. It is certainly not worth the cost of upgrading.  The other main issue which affected our holiday was the fact that the pool and Nutella bar were both closed - both of which our daughter was looking forward to using. Despite literature advising when the outside Nutella bar closed, the facility would move inside, they do not do this until January!
