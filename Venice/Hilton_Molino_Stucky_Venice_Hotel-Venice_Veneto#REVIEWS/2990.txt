Stayed at the Hilton for a romantic weekend for my wife's 40th birthday. Having never been to Venice this was the perfect place to stay. The boat service is excellent, it does get busy! (some people think the boat is only for them and some Americans got very annoyed when they turn up 1 min before the boat leaves and they don't get a seat, but the hotel staff have seen this before and are prepared for the complaining.) The rooms are very good, ours was in need of a few small things ( loose ac vent, wallpaper a bit old) but did not affect stay at all. Breakfast is excellent very polite and helpful staff. The skybar is the perfect place to end an evening and the staff here are very polite and professional. The only grumble I would have is that the WIFI is not free.
