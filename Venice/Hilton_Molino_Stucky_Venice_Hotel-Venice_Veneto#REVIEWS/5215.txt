We had a wonderful weeklong stay at this beautiful hotel.  But let's get the biggest issue out of the way: it is NOT on the main islands of Venice.  The Hotel is located on Guidecce island, across from the other cluster of islands that make up most of Venice (about a 10 minute boat ride).  The hotel does provide a free shuttle boat every twenty minutes to either of one or two locations (except for when they are on "pause" for an hour plus), but you have to make amends with this inconvenience.  To be honest, despite our best planning, there were two nights when we found ourselves waiting up to 30 minutes on the dock for the shuttle in the rain [you can always take a public transport boat or private water taxi to get you near or to the hotel].
