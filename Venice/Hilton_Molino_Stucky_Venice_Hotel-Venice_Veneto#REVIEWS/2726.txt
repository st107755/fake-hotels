This a lovely Hilton property off the beaten path and across the canal from the major tourist spots. The service for the most part was first class, Carlo was especially helpful but knowledgeable and most importantly accurate. Our room was lovely, ask for a canal view.  Be aware that this property is very spread out, so if you will need to use different elevators to get to different areas of the hotel.  If you want to use the pool, get there early, park for the entire day.  We were very happy with the boat shuttle that ran back and forth to two points each day, the cost is 3.50 euro per person per stay.  Do not accept a free private taxi to Murano, this was the most frustrating part of our trip.
