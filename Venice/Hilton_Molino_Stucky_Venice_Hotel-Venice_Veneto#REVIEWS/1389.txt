The only weakness the Hilton Molino Stucky has is that it is located on Giudecca Island, across the Giudecca Canal from the main attractions of Venice, and so requires water transport to visit these. However, given the usually tremendously overcrowded nature of Venice during midday, and the availability of shuttle service, this is actually a good thing; the Hilton offers an island (literally) of calm to retreat to after having your fill of the chaos of Venice. The Hotel itself is very attractive, the rooms comfortable, the service efficient, and the restaurants and rooftop bar (reviewed separately) excellent.
