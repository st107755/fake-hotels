Whatever time of year you plan to visit, make sure you bring insect repellant! Both me and my boyfriend were bitten alive by mosquitoes, my reaction being extremely bad. Our room smelt stale and of stagnant water - without having opened any windows or doors - and the air-conditioning did not work. The plugs provided to 'kill' the insects did not work, neither did the change of rooms at 3am and two complaints to reception. I have returned home with 17 bites across my face and arms, simply from mosquitoes in the rooms. I even had to leave a note for the cleaners asking them NOT to open any doors or windows as after 2 nights of no sleep due to being bitten, I was scared to sleep at all.
