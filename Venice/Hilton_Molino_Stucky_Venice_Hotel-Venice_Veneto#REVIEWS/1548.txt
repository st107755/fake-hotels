We found this hotel's location splendid - 15 minutes by hotel shuttle to St Mark's, also on vaporetto routes with stops 5 minutes away, with stunning views of the rest of the city, across the Giudecca Channel. There's also, unusually for Venice, a waterfront to walk along with small local shops, and residents rather than just tourists about.  The island of Giudecca itself was well worth exploring for a different experience of Venice. There were also some pleasant eateries of various sizes though opening hours of some were a problem. Pizza and pasta at the Chinese run pizza bars were quite acceptable, though. Our room was stylish, clean and comfortable though lighting could have been a tiny bit brighter.
