My wife and I spent a couple days here during our honeymoon.  Overall, this was a relaxing stay for us. We stayed in a room with a river view which was quite romantic at night. The staff was cordial and we did have a decent experience at the rooftop bar one night, even though we did feel we were being ignored by the staff at some point. We took advantage of the Hilton executive lounge for the breakfast as that was quick and easy for us. One evening something rather disappointing happened. We returned to our room to find that someone (obviously staff) had taken out a couple drinks from our refrigerator, where at least one was consumed and then whoever did it had the audacity to leave them all on the counter.
