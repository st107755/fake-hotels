This large converted warehouse on the quiet and island of Giudecca is home to one of the nicest Hilton properties. Don't be put off by having to cross the canal. For those willing to make the short crossing, what awaits you is something simply not available on the main island, a large hotel with big comfortable rooms, many with views, air conditioning that actually works and a fabulous rooftop pool and bar. I noticed lots of cruise ship passengers of all nationalities on my last stay. This is wise as the proximity of the hotel to the ship port is just a short boat taxi away. You purchase you ticket in the hotel (€8) and the taxi drops you right at the port. I tried the room service and was incredibly impressed. The prosciutto was delicious as was the melon.
