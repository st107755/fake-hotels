Traveling on our honeymoon we wanted somewhere really special, and out of the chaos in Venice.   Unfortunately, on the whole, Venice is a bit of a waste of time now. It is just so busy that to go around the major tourist spots is so hard, it takes the joy out of the day.   The Hilton was a lovely old building, renovated well, and the staff were extremely helpful. Some negatives; - For what we paid for our room, we weren't blown away.  - Prices for drinks and food in the hotel are mad. We had friends come to meet us at the skyline bar, and four drinks cost me €67. - Breakfast is average, and the location in the building is too small for the amount of guests that go every morning.  - The water taxi service from the hotel is unreliable.
