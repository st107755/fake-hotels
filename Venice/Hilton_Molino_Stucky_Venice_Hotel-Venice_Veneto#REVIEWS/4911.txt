We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky for two nights after a cruise.  As an Elite member, we were given an upgrade to a two-room suite and access to the Executive Lounge.   The room was big, and the bed was very firm.  The second room had a dining table and a pull-out sofa our son used.   The bathroom was large with a separate shwoer and bath.   The room included a water heater and plenty of coffee supplies, and we had two small refridgerators and a stove.    The Executive Lounge was okay.  It looks like they have it in a temporary location at the edge of the lobby.  We chose to eat breakfast in the dining room as it gave us more choices than the lounge.  In the evening there were hot and cold appetizers, but the wine quality (A Merlot and Soave were poor).
