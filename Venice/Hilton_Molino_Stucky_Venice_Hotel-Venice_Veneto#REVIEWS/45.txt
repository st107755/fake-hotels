My daughter and I took a break to Venice recently and decided to stay at the Hilton, mainly because she works at the Hilton in Belfast to supplement her uni costs and therefore we got a staff discount. The hotel is breathtakingly beautiful, although looking at it from outside you wouldn’t be able to tell. The staff were all fantastic, the concierge through to the housekeeping staff all were very helpful and welcoming.   Breakfast is as good, possibly better than any other Hilton we’ve stayed in... And the large volume of Asians (presumably staying there for the Chinese New Year) were also catered for, with a part of the buffet I believe was called the ‘Chinese corner’ or something like that.
