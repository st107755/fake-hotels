I stayed 3 nights 17-20 June 2013. Our company had organized a partner conference, hosting about 250 guests. The hotel infrastructure is very modern, well maintained and luxuruous. Guests enjoyed the accomodations, as well as the restaurant and bar. Food and beverage quality and service were at the level you would expect in a modern 5 star hotel. The hotel sits on an island opposite the main attractions of Venice, and you need to take a boat to get across. A shuttle operates every 20' and is free of charge for hotel guests. Other transportation is not needed in Venice, if you need to travel outside shuttle hours, water taxis are available of course.
