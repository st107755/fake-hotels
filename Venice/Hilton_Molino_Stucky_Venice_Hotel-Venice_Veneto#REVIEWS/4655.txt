My family and I stayed for 2 nights at the Hilton Molino Stuckey Venice right after New Year's. It is a wonderful hotel although the location is away from the main tourist sites in Venice. There is a hotel boat shuttle which takes you to Piazza San Marco and runs every 20 minutes or so. Personally, I found it much easier and more relaxing staying at the Hilton Molino Stuckey Venine than the more quaint hotels on the main island where I have stayed before.  The breakfast buffet was the most lavish in all of Italy, and the rooms were large and comfortable. I would highly recommend this hotel for business or leisure travelers who do not need to stay on the main island in Venice.
