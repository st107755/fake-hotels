We stayed two nights in the Hilton Molino Stucky, a converted mill on Giudecca. The conversion is excellent and the rooms are comfortable and well appointed. Particular highlights are the rooftop pool and bar, which offer amazing views over the city. We stayed in an executive room and were therefore able to use the executive lounge. As others have pointed out, there is a problem with the lounge. It is small, narrow and with a barred partition from the bar/lobby, gives the distinct impression of a cage. There are not enough tables for all the guests at busy times and breakfast was a bit of a scrum. Notwithstanding this, the staff were pleasant and helpful, and breakfast and snacks were enjoyable.   The shuttle to Zattere and San Marco was regular and very efficient.
