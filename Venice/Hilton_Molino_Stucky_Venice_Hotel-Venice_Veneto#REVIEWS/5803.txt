This is an amazing hotel in an amazing place. We had a fantastic room and loved having access to the executive lounge for snacks and drinks and the wonderful breakfast buffet. Beautiful, large, clean hotel with friendly and helpful folks. Relaxing lobby and bar area. Internet access in the lobby is by the minute and is charged to your room if you like.   The hotel is across the water from the sites but they do have their own shuttle boat that picks up near St. Marks.  We took the public boat from the train station and had a bit of a challenge finding their dock which is a few bridges past St. Marks (shared with a public boat station).
