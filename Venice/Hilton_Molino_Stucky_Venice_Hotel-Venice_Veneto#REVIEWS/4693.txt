This was our third time to the Molino Stucky Hilton and it never fails to be an excellent adventure.  We took the Alilaguna Blue line from the airport which stops at the Hilton so there is less transferring of luggage.  Check-in swift, staff always pleasant and courteous.  We are Diamond HH so our room was on the executive floor overlooking  Guidecca canal.  Executive lounge has had some beautiful work done since our last visit.  The lounge staff were very welcoming and the food wonderful.  Breakfast in the il Molino great.  Always a large selection of hot and cold foods, juices and prosecco :-)  Wait staff there very accomodating!  Hotel shuttle to San Marco or Zattere is timely but you can always walk to Palanca to catch the ACTV if needed.
