My wife and I stayed here for our silver wedding anniversary (8-12 March).  My first choice was a 16th century palazzo near St Marks but when I read the details for the Hilton I had no hesitation in changing my mind.  The Hilton is cheaper, offered a fantastic breakfast (not just continental) and the Executive Lounge as well with its snacks, canapés petit fours and free drinks all day. I am so glad I changed my mind.  On arrival we upgraded to a room with a view (405)and as soon as the girl on reception realised it was our anniversary the price of the upgrade was reduced.  On the day itself we came back from our day on the main island to find a note from the manager wishing us a happy anniversary along with Bellini's.
