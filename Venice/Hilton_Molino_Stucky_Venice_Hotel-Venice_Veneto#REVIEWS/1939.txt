We stayed here for two nights for our 20th wedding anniversary. The hotel is large, but doesn't feel intrusively so except for at breakfasts. The staff were welcoming and helpful (as would be expected at the grade of hotel). The room was large, comfortable and well equipped.  The shuttle takes you to San Marco or to Zattere, although San Marco gets crowded it is a perfect place to start walking around Venice. You can cover most of Venice on foot without too much effort.  The rooftop pool is beautiful, the restaurant that we used one evening (Aromi) was lovely and would recommend it.
