I was unsure at first whether booking a massive hotel in an international chain was a great idea. However, our fears were soon allayed on arrival at the hotel. The staff are welcoming and go out of their way to accommodate any requests. The building is beautiful - the site of an ancient monastery and decor in public rooms and bedrooms is tasteful and complementary to the hotel. Although the hotel is across the water, the taxis are regular and plentiful. Just a word of caution - don't miss the last one back, because you really are stuck (no pun intended!). The rooftop swimming pool and champagne bar is very chic and cool, but getting a sun lounger not so easy! One restaurant not to miss: Locanda Montin. Inexpensive, fantastic food and great service.
