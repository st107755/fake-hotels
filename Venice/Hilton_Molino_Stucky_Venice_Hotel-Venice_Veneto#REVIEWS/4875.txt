Beautiful hotel located across the grand canal from the heart of Venice. The free shuttle across the water made the hotel easily accessible to Venice but allowed you to be out of all the crowds. Excellent restaurants within walking distance without having to eat within the hotel. Paying a little extra for executive lounge access is a great thing since it has outstanding snacks and excellent free wine and beer. Water bus to airport is inexpensive but takes 90 minutes. Private water taxi to airport is about 100 Euros for up to 4 people.  Internet access is expensive and very slow. You pay per device. Computer connectivity was 22 Euros per day and iPhone access was 9 Euros per day.
