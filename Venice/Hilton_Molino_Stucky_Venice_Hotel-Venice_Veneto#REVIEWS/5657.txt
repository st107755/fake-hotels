Our choir sang at St Mark's on Sat 10th April and the company organised the Hilton as one of the accommodation options.   The hotel is convenient from the airport about 45 mins by water taxi.  It is in a beautifully refurbished old mill building and the rooms are very well decorated.  The staff are extremely curteous and we enjoyed an excellent breakfast.    The only downside was being charged 6E for a small bottle of water from the mini bar.  Shame the hotel has to"rip off" clients.   It would have been a thoughtful touch to actually provide a bottle of water each day.    I am sure they could cover the cost somewhere in their pricing structure.
