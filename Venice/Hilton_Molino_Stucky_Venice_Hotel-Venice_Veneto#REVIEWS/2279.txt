We stayed here for two nights before our Viking cruise.  A beautiful hotel; an old flour mill that has been very creatively turned into a hotel.  Our room was very comfortable and quiet.  We looked out over a small interior courtyard.  The bed was quite comfortable.  The bathroom was large and well-laid-out.  Good storage space in the closet, but not quite enough room to leave suitcases open during the day.  The breakfast buffet was huge with something for everyone.  One comment - the scrambled eggs on the buffet were probably runnier than most Americans would like, order an omelet if you prefer your eggs cooked a bit more.  The rooftop bar has beautiful views of Venice across the canal - a lovely sight to take in after dark.
