Our stay at the Molino Stucky Hotel was great. We had a fantastic big room with a king size bed and a marble bathroom. The hotel is very quiet even when it is fully booked. The breakfast at the buffet restaurant is pretty good too, thinking it is Italy evven Prosecco is served every morningand omeletts can be ordered as well. The service is very attentive and customer oriented. onlythe kitchen is sometimes low to replenish the buffet when too many people are eating at the same time. The free shuttle service is a very nice offer too and we used it several times. It goes to St. Marks and stops at the Zattere vaporetto station. Overall a great location on the Guidecca island awayy from the hustle and bustle of Venice but close enough to enjoy the goods parts of Venice.
