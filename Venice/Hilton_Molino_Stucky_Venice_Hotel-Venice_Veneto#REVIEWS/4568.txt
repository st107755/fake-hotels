Me and my boyfriend stayed at the Molino Hilton for 4 nights from the 1st to the 5th April. Despite the long journey on the airport water bus (3 hours getting there and strangely only took an hour and half on the way home) the stay in this hotel was absolutely wonderful.   From the moment you set eyes on the hotel it is impressive, with dark wood furniture and cream marble everywhere. Our room was beautiful and quite large; however our view was pretty rubbish. The air con didn't work throughout our stay, but the weather wasn't great anyway for it to matter.   The hotel provides a regular shuttle over to St. Marks Square and to Murano if you fancy it. We didn't bother with Murano, but heard good things about it. The journey over to the square takes about 10 mins.
