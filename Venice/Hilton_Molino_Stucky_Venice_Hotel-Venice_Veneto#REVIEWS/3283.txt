Stayed here in November,wonderful setting on Island just across from main venice attractions therefore quieter place to stay. Water taxi to and from airport added to the fun! Free hilton shuttle taxis take you over the water at regular intervals so short trip to reach st marks,bridge of sighs etc. Excellent choice of restaurants within the hotel and a lovely building retaining features from when it was a mill. Executive lounge is always worthwhile in hilton hotels as it allows you free drinks and snacks plus breakfast. We had a junior suite and it was beautiful plus a good size,although notthe best view from the window. Standing outside by the water watching the sunrise was wonderful.
