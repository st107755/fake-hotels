We stayed here for 4 nights over the valentines weekend To my surprise to get engaged. The hotel itself is beautiful both inside and out. The rooms are cosy and clean the only thing is they could do with some more English channels on the TV. The breakfast was delicious serving both cooked and continental food. There was a wide range of variety. The hotel restaurants were limited to what they served and very over priced for what they were serving. We went to bacaromi and ended up leaving our entire meal. There are about 6 dinner options at both restaurants. The free shuttle was excellent and accessible at all times providing it wasn't too busy.
