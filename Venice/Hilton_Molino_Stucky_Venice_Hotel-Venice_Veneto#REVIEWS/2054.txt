This hotel is hands down one of the nicest hotels I have ever stayed in. Ever. I am IN LOVE with this place.   Let me get to the pros: Amazing location. Yes, you have to take a ferry to from the main part of Venice to the island this hotel rests on, but it is worth it. The ferry only costs a few euros and it drops you off at one spot near San Marco Square during the day, and offers two spots at night (for more night life opportunities). But you are away from loud crowds, it's so incredibly peaceful at the hotel that you just get a real chance to relax and exhale. I'm very glad we decided to stay here, despite having to take a ferry to and from the main area.  The rooms are stunning. Gorgeous. Large! Comfy beds.
