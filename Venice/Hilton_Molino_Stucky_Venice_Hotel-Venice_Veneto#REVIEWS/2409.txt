We stayed here 3 days for New Years Eve 2014/2015. The hotel itself was beautiful, but the room had a really high ceiling, which made for a bit of a cold feel. The view from our room over the canal and Venice was absolutely spectacular.  We had parked our rental car at the garage the hotel had suggested and taken a ferry boat to the hotel. Whenever we went over to Venice we took the shuttle boat across, but to avoid the wait time after an exhausting day of shopping, walking and sightseeing we took a taxi to get back to the hotel, which got quite costly. We had dinner twice at a fantastic little restaurant nearby, called Osteria Ae Botti. New Years Eve we went up to the roof terrace and enjoyed champagne and fireworks.
