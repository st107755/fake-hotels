The view from the rooftop pool has to be about the best in the world.  Spectacular.  But will your schedule and the weather even allow you to use the pool?  It was rainy-ish on one day and the pool was closed.    Obviously I can't speak for all of the hundreds of rooms in the hotel, but our room had multiple maintenance issues -- trim stripped off the door frame, water stains on the ceiling of the bathroom, cabinet containing tea & dishes collapsed when we opened it and was not repaired during our three night stay.  Wifi was not consistent.  Otherwise the view from the waterfront room was spectacular, the beamed ceilings throughout are cool, and the AC was quiet and worked great.  As for mysterious charges at checkout, it was not as bad as many resorts.
