I have a room with a beautiful view across from Giudecci island to Venice for 2 nights . Transport there via shuttle service is convenient in the day, but not so in the night as there is a long gap after dinner time.  The room is reasonably well decorated in the Venetian style, but don't expect attentive housekeeping service. They are almost invisible. I asked for a hair dryer and it never came. On my last day out, I have to go find the housekeeper myself and collect the hairdryer as I really needed one. The reception staff too appeared robotic in their service level. The European staff especially do not have an international service standard and tend to have preconceived ideas of your needs. When I asked for taxi service to airport, they assumed I wanted public transport.
