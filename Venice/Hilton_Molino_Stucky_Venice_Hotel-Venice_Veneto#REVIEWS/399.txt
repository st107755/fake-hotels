The hotel itself is nice enough, sitting on the small island of Giudecca which faces Venice.  Giudecca is well worth exploring if you want to get away from the crowds in Venice itself.  It has some nice restaurants, art galleries and churches of it's own.  We spent one of the days of our stay just on this island.  The Hilton shuttle is convenient for getting onto the main islands, although it does tend to get full very quickly and you can be left waiting around 40 minutes for the next one.  We stayed in a king deluxe room, which was clean and reasonable in size even if the decor is a little dated (they are in the process of refurbishing the rooms, and our was one of those yet to be refurbed).
