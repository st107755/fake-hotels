Just returned from a week long trip to Italy with my husband and 2 teenage children.  Our first stay was at the Molino Stucky.  It is a beautiful renovated hotel.  We had a large suite in the tower over looking the canal with views towards San Marco and the Zattere.  I was hesitant to stay here as I was concerned about needing to depend on a shuttle to get us over to all the sights.  Since we were staying on points, we decided it was worth the inconvenience.     Overall, this is a very nice hotel and the service everywhere was excellent.  The wait staff, front desk staff and concierge were all extremely friendly and helpful.  The location is easy to get to by water taxi..a 15 minute ride to the train station and about a 20 min water taxi ride from the airport.
