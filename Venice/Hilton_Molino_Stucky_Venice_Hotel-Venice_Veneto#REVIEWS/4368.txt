I stayed in this hotel for 2 nights with my family. I think this is a great hotel!   We had a beautiful room facing the canal. The room was large, and clean. The bed was comfortable. The walls are a bit thin so we could hear the people beside us, but it did not bother us. This is just a heads up  for those who are sensitive to noise.   The hotel is located on a different island than the touristy part of Venice. For us, this was a benefit. The hotel has a free boat that takes people back and forth to the tourist locations, so we got the best of both worlds. Make sure to line up for the boat early, because it does fill up. In the event that you miss it you can get back to Giudecca by the public boat system.   The service at the hotel was pretty fantastic!
