On re:booking this stunning hotel this week I noted I clean forgot to review the Molino Stucky, following an amazing stay back in August 2016 last year. My wife and I loved the hotel, and found it perfect for our needs, being away from the crowds but still epic views of Venice.   In August naturally it was very warm, and the roof top pool, amazing though the view was, really came into its own on a warm day, for a lovely soak and sun bathe after several hours pounding the streets/maze that is Venice. But enjoying a sunset dinner on the rooftop was a true highlight. In fact we ate at all restaurants on site during our stay, and everything was great.  Our room, an executive king with a view, really had a great view looking out across the large canal to venice.
