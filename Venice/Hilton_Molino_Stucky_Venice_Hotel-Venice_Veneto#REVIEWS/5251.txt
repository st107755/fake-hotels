Our trip of a lifetime.... Carnivale in Venice, and a 10 night stay at the Molino Stucky.  It should have been perfect, but there is always more to the story.  To be fair, it was probably the busiest time of the year for the staff of the Hilton, and they seemed to put forth a proportional effort, but sometimes that effort seemed to go nowhere.   It started a little rocky when trying to arrange a shared taxi from the airport through their concierge.... you can only arrange this 24 hours in advance of arrival, and flying from the west coast it meant we were in transit at this 24 hour mark.
