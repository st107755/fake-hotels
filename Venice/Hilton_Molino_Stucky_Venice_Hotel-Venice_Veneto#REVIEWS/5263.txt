We have just spent 3 nights at the Molino Stucky Hilton in Venice and are in love with it!! The hotel is wonderful and great attention is paid to details. Set on the island of Giudecca, it is easily reachable from Marco Polo airport. If you want to save money, catch bus number 5 outside the terminal to Piazzale Roma for €5 per person and then the boat line 4.1 or 4.2 to Palanca for €6.50. The hotel is just on the right! Another way is to use the Alilaguna boats @ €15, which stop just outside the hotel. The journey time is 1.40 hours, whereas the bus option is a lot quicker.
