Stayed here for a two night post cruise visit to Venice. First impression of room (714) -  it was a bit claustrophobic as fairly small with low ceiling and small windows but bed very comfortable and good view over to the port, but no face flannels/cloths. Excellent breakfast (included in our stay) with extensive buffet selection which catered for all tastes. Courtesy shuttle service much appreciated and worked well for us but make sure to arrive in good time as takes only a maximum of 39 people. Unfortunately it rained during our stay so couldn't full appreciate Skyline bar and pool area.
