We visited this hotel for 5 days during the holidays for NYE, upon arrival the hotel looks fabulous. There is a vaporetto which takes 1.5 hours from the airport and drops you right at the front door. We were welcomed to the hotel however were asked to pay €150 prepaid, we made them aware we didn't have this amount so they charged us €15 which we have still not been refunded. Our room was spacious and comfortable, the bathroom was spectacular however dirty (we had to ask twice for this to be cleaned) the beds were comfortable however the rooms were not cleaned daily and the coffee and tea was not replenished, we asked 7 times for more milks and sugar. The free taxi ferry was brilliant and departs on time every time!
