I have chosen Hilton Molino Stucky as I am HHonors card holder without prior knowledge of its position and facilities and I was pleasantly surprised. Despite its former industrial use, the hotel looks great and is well equipped for travelers, especially for tourists that abound in Venice. A free shuttle to San Marco is run every hour, even twice an hour at rush hours in the evening. Even more, the public transportation to/from airport has a stop at the hotel door. The room was what I expected with a splendid view on the Venice. Guessing from their database that during stay I will celebrate my birthday, nice flowers and champagne greeted us in the room. The reception and concierge were very responsive to our various requests.
