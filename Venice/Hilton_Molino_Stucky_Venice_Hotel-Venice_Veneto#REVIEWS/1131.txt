We booked the Hilton Venice, The hotel is very large and very grand. Kind of has the modern Hilton look with a classic Venetian twist. The location I thought was great...ok is is away from the main area but on the plus side it was lovely getting away from the crowds and also on our side of the canal we had a lovely view of the sunset over venice. The shuttle boat is very easy so don't be put off by the location.  We were upgraded to a Exec suite due to our Hilton membership, the view of the canal was lovely. Exec lounge isn't the greatest I've been to but none the less was still good.   We made use of the spa which was heaven... the roof pool was lovely and beautiful but water was freezing!   We had drinks in the sky bar with a lovely view of venice.
