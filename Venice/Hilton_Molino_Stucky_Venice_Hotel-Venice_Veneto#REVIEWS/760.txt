This hotel is the perfect location for seeing Venice, it is on the Guidecca which is just across the canal from San Marco and Hilton provide a shuttle boat that you can use throughout your trip which takes you straight to the Piazza San Marco. As the hotel is across the canal it benefits from being slightly quieter meaning you can sleep well.  The sky bar is amazing and gives your beautiful views across Venice and the lagoon, the pool wasn't open when we visited (as it was winter).  The restaurant had great traditional Venetian food, although would recommend booking as it is very cosy! The seafood is fresh and the pasta is made on site and delicious.
