Recently completed a wonderful one-night stay at the Hilton Stucky Molino.  We were provided a fantastic room upgrade on account of Hilton Diamond status, as well as a bottle of champagne for the special occasion.  The hotel design and architecture is stunning!  As others have mentioned, the Hilton is located across the cannel from the main island.  For us, this meant an escape from the crowds of Venice, but easy access to the main island via the complimentary water ferry whenever we needed the main island.  We loved the views over the canal, especially as the parade of cruise ships goes back each morning/evening.
