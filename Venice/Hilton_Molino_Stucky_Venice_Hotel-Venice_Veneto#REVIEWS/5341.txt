On this trip we stayed only one night at the Hilton Molion Stuckey on vacation.    Great: This hotel is very clean, has all the amenities you could ask for, and staff are very helpful and want to make sure that you have a great stay.  As a Hilton Diamond member we enjoyed free snacks / drinks in the lounge area.  The hotel in general has a lot of character in design.  Only Issues: The room that we were upgraded to had a view of a brick wall, and not the beautiful canal.  The room however was very well appointed with a separate sitting room.  Make sure that you ask if/when upgraded if the room will still have a view of canal so that you can make an appropriate decision of what matters most to you.
