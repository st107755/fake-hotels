The hotel itself was very new & beautiful. The hotel did a great job restoring the building.   Our family suite was very spacious, clean and  comfortable. At first I was a bit concerned about the property being on the Giudecca Island but it was not bad at all. We were able to take Hilton water shuttle over to Venice fairly easily. The room wifi was strong, had no issues. The room didn't have AC but we had cool breeze with opened windows.  Breakfast at the executive lounge was delicious. A special thank you to Nicole and Carlo at Guest Relations for their excellent service. They went above and beyond to make our trip extra memorable. Our kids really enjoyed the glass blowing demonstration.   Tip: We had purchased 24 hour unlimited water bus tickets for 20 Euros each.
