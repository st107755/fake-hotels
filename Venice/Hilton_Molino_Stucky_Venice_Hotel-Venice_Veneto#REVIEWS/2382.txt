This was our fourth or fifth two-week stay at the Stucky Molina to photography the Venice Carnival. Venice is probably the most beautiful city in the world and the Hilton provides an excellent base for exploring.  The Stucky Molina is on Giudecca Island, a few hundred metres across a canal from Venice. Hilton provides a free shuttle from the hotel door to St. Mark’s Square about twice an hour, and you can also take the nearby waterbus to a wide range of destinations including the airport). Giudecca is fairly quiet, nice to explore and has several restaurants. 	The hotel is in a converted mill and is built with a 19th Century brick ‘solidness’. I rather like the conversion. I am a Diamond Honors member and was upgraded to a nice room with a view across the water to Venice.
