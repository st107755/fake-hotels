This was our second time staying at the Molino Stucky; it really is the perfect pre or post cruise hotel when visiting Venice.  Its location away from the center of Venice makes the Hilton very convenient for water taxi transfers to/from the Cruise Port (only about a 15 minute ride) and VCE Airport (about 30 minutes but far quicker than transferring from a hotel near the Grand Canal or San Marco.)  The hotels convenient shuttle boat service to the San Marco runs day and evening so one can be in the touristic center of Venice in minutes; and its a pleasure to escape the crowds when returning to the hotel.
