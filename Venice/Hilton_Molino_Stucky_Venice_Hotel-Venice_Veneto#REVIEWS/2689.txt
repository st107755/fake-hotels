Just returned from a 3 night stay at this beautiful, grand hotel.  The hotel rooftop terrace, swimming pool and views over Venice are truly outstanding and memorable.  The breakfast selection is excellent and will keep you going well into the afternoon.  Yes, the drinks and food menu are on the expensive side but if you are wishing to stay somewhere of a very high standard, then I can't rate the Hilton highly enough.  My stay was an early 50th birthday present and worth every penny.  So if you want to celebrate or spoil yourself - you won't regret this choice of hotel.  The hotel staff were very friendly especially the young lady who showed us to our breakfast table each morning.  As for Venice itself - one of the most beautiful, quaint and unique cities in the world.
