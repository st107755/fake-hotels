This property is on the other side of the canal to the center of Venice and was a perfect fit for our trip.  The water bus and the hotel shuttle ran on a regular basis so getting into the center of Venice was very easy.  Everyone spoke English so no problem there.  Hilton did a nice job on this property and I would recommend it.  Not cheap but worth it. The room was quiet comfortable and everything worked!  Good wifi reception and free if you are a Hilton Honors member which is free to join. Good size room for Venice and from our fifth floor we had a cool minor view of Venice. Our breakfast was included and it was a decent variety.  The staff are young and eager but failed to mention when we sat down you could have eggs to order.  The omelets were good and filling.
