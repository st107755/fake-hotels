We stayed here for 2 nights at the beginning of December. Lovely friendly welcome by Lucrezia at check in. All staff were attentive and courteous.  Our room was nicely decorated, clean and with an amazing view overlooking the canal.  It was very handy having a Hilton shuttle boat provided for the price of 6 euros/ stay going to Piazza San Marco, which we used quiet a lot ;)  Breakfast was sumptuous, fantastic assortment of food and the muffins were delicious, not good for my waistline though.  We had dinner once in the Bacaromi restaurant and eat Venetian food, which was delicious.  Overall, our stay was as expected a 5 star hotel, with a first class service.  The bonus to us was that we were in Venice for our anniversary, and we couldn't have asked for more.
