We stayed here for 1 night before a cruise.  The hotel is a stylishly converted former mill and it is located away from the main islands on Giudecca Island, with a hotel shuttle waterbus available (7.50€ per person for the entire stay at the hotel) to take you to St Mark´s Square.  You can also access the hotel using water taxis (useful when arriving with luggage) and there is a public waterbus terminal about 10 minutes away crossing 2 small bridges.  Reception staff are very friendly and efficient and we were promptly checked in to our room – a very comfortable and spacious suite overlooking the canal and the main island of Venice.  We had booked an Executive Room with a view but were upgraded due to Hilton Honors status – very much appreciated.
