Hilton Molino Stucky Venice is a Category 8 (3rd highest category) Hilton property located on the island of Giudecca, a short boat ride away from the main tourist attractions of Venice. The hotel provides a complimentary shuttle to St. Mark's Square (Piazza San Marco) as well as another water-bus stop, Zattere.  The hotel used to be a flour mill that was renovated and converted to this wonderful property. It sits right on the water (Giudecca Canal). You can sit on the bench in front of the hotel by the canal and literally spend hours just watching the boats, taxis, and water-buses go by.  As I mentioned above, the hotel provides courtesy shuttle to the St. Mark's Square and Zattere stop. This is the single best hotel amenity I have experienced in all of our Europe trip.
