I would thoroughly recommend this Hotel for a stay in Venice. The staff are virtually all Italian with excellent English & all very pleasant. The room was large, clean and well appointed with a very large and comfortable bed. Breakfast was very good & plentiful, but the black coffee served is nowhere near as good as the coffees in Venice itself! The location to some is a detriment, but to us it was a bonus, being in a huge converted pasta factory away from all the hustle and bustle. The shuttle bus was no inconvenience - but we were a reasonably large group & it was winter & never a problem with getting on & very punctual.  The hotels has a couple of letdowns: 1)Everywhere is too hot (November).
