All the practicalities relating to getting to and from the hotel are covered by many of the other reviews, as are the quality of the facilities, which we couldn't fault, other than the very faint hint of ordure, which is just a fact of life in Venice and a lot worse in other parts of the city. The location may be inconvenient for some, but for us it was ideal; a complete contrast to the seething mass of visitors in the city. We visited in April, and couldn't countenance coming to Venice in high summer; more visitors and more heat, for sure, so all the more reason to stay in a place which affords comfort, respite and has helpful and efficient staff if you plan to come in July or August.
