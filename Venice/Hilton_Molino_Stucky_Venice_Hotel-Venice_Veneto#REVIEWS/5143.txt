My Husband and I were celebrating our honeymoon. Everyone at the hotel was incredibly helpful and sweet. Upon check in we had champagne and strawberries in our room with rose peddles. It was a very sweet gesture.   We had booked a room with 2 twin beds (cheaper) but they had pushed them together for us. The room was on a corner and the views were stunning! LOVED IT!  The roof top bar was great for a late night drink. The fresh air felt good after a long day of travel.   The only thing that was a bummer was it's location. You have to take the hotels ferry or a water taxi (which is 60 euros+) to get to the main island with all the main attractions. However it was nice to get away from the tourists. We were there during a holiday so there were a lot of tourists.
