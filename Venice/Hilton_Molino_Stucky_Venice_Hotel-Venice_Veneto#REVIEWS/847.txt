This is a very large hotel in front of Venedig. We booked a suite which was not as big as we expected but the bed was comfortable though. We didn’t spend much time in the hotel so the room size wasn’t really an issue. Breakfast was ok. The bar on the rooftop was also nice. I think it was a good option to the hotels on the other side as it was quiet and less crowded.
