Great hotel, amazing space, lovely staff, restaurant on the quay, and totally epic rooftop pool and bar.   Not cheap, but then this is not either a cheap location, or a cheap brand. Some of the less positive reviews, I suspect, are based of the cost / benefit rewards. However, the executive lounge gives you access to free drinks etc, and at tea time, some wine and food (we highly recommend that pay for this option). Similarly a cheap upgrade option can give you a room with a view (however you run the risk that if they are full, you might have a do a few days in a normal room).  A free water taxi runs to the other side (Zattere), as well as to the main tourist Piazza San Marco.
