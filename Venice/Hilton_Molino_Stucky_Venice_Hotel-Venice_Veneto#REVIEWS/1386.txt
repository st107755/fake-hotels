We loved our experience at the Hilton Stucky. Please know that the hotel is not on the main Island, but this is not much of a worry. There are several ways you can get to the hotel.   1) Go to St. Marks Square and take the shuttle (aprox. every 45 min) back to the hotel. 2) Go to the Zattere water bus stop and take the shuttle (aprox. every 45 min) back to the hotel. 3) Take a water bus the Guidecca Palanca stop and walk a short ways back to the hotel 4) **A little difficult to navigate, but not impossible ** Take a water bus to the Sacca Fissola stop and walk a short ways back to the hotel.  We found that it was very easy once we got there, and we actually loved all the opportunities we got to ride the water bus and shuttle.
