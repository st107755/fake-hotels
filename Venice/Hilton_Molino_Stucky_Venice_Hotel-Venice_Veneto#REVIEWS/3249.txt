We stayed here for 2 nights as a surprise trip for my 40th birthday in January 2015. The hotel is beautiful and most of the staff were friendly and helpful. Unfortunately our room (537) was right next to a very noisy lift that needs looking at so it wasn't as quiet as it might have been - fortunately it wasn't used very much through the night so was only woken a few times but the lift should definitely be fixed! On the plus side the super king size mattress was very comfortable.
