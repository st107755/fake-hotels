We were happy that we chose this hotel as it was comfortable modern environment in which to relax and cool off after hot, busy days of sightseeing where we really tried to pack it all in.  We found the executive lounge to be worth it, being able to drop in and quickly have something to eat and drink at various times of day but especially for breakfast.  One tip that I would highlight for future travelers using this hotel is that you can easily take the Vaporetti to and from this hotel.
