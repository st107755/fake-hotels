We stayed at the HMS for 5 nights in September and would definitely do so again if returning to Venice.  Several months prior to our arrival I emailed the hotel and requested an upgrade in honor of a special occasion we were celebrating.  At check-in I was thrilled to hear we’d been upgraded to a better class of room as well as a highly coveted water view.  Check-in was simple and efficient.  We left our bags for the bellman to deliver while we went for a walk around Guidecca. On entering the room, we were tickled to see an ice bucket with chilled Bellini’s and a few sweets waiting for us.  The room had modern accoutrements and an iron and ironing board is available on request.   We had breakfast each morning and evening cocktails in the Executive Lounge.
