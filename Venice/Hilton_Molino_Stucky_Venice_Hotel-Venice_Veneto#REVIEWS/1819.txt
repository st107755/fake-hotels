A wonderful stay for a 2 day mini break. We had a view over the canal and it was stunning waking up to watch the boats. I could have led there all day just watching the canal come to life. Check in was quick and the man on reception was friendly and helpful. He also there when we checked out and remembered us. The hotel has a roof top bar overlooking the city which has the most amazing view and it's worth staying here just for this. Breakfast was nice with a large selection. The hotel has its own water bus which picks up every 20 mind and drops you off at the St Marks square vaporetto stop and is only €4.50 pp for unlimited use during your stay. There is also a vaporetto stop right next door.
