Located in Guidecca, the Hilton is not exactly in the center of Venice but their shuttle boat runs hourly to put you where you want to be.  If you don't want to plan your goings and comings in advance then this is not the place for you.  And if you don't want to spend 30 Euros each for breakfast, then either buy a baguette as we did, or look elsewhere. One other caveat, the concierge clearly has an "attitude" and you won't get a smile from him.  The rest of the staff, however, work hard to make you feel welcome.  Our room was lovely with a Murano glass chandelier and heavy beams on the ceiling. Nice amenities and plush towels.  Wonderful pillows.
