I stayed 3rd to 6th August with my husband and 2 other couples. We arrived at the hotel at midday and were quickly checked in although only 2 of our rooms were ready. No problem as check in isnt officially until 2pm. We headed up to the Sky bar and were greeted and served by Lorena. We left the bar to freshen up before going back up for lunch which was delicious if rather expensive at EUR14 for a toasted sandwich however the views from the bar are well worth the extra cost and it is a lovely chilled out space. My only really complaint was with the Sky Bar in the evening. The 6 of us returned for a night cap and were pretty much ignored despite being seated by the staff.
