Stayed at the Molino Stucky on the last three nights of an eleven day holiday to Italy. We were originally supposed to stay at a different hotel but after the hotel agent our travel company booked it through went into administration we were given a choice of alternatives and we chose this one.   It is on a little island just over the lagoon from the main tourist area but the hotel offers a free shuttle service which takes around 15 minutes to get to San Marco. It's not that frequent but there is an Alilaguna stop right outside the hotel or alternatively a public water taxi stop about a five minute walk down the main street.
