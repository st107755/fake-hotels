This is a beautiful hotel with wonderful service.  We booked a regular (cheapest) room and were given this gorgeous mini suite.  Idk if it's bc we were Hilton Honors members, but they didn't say they were giving us an upgrade, so maybe all the rooms are this spacious and beautiful.  The room had a sitting area with a tv, huge amounts of storage, very large bathroom and a great bedroom with tv and very comfortable bed/bedding. One very positive is the hotel has a water taxi shuttle that will take you to St Mark's square (about 20 min ride) where most of the action is.  If you've been to Venice recently, you know how difficult and very expensive it can be to get anywhere across the canals.
