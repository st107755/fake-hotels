My family and I stayed here for one night of our 7 day trip to Venice. The building is really attractive and is set in a perfect location away from the crowds but in a position where you can see the beauty of Venice. The family room we stayed in was really good. Spacious and soooooohhhh comfortable. The rooftop pool has one of the best views ever. Absolutely amazing.  But a few things let this hotel down. I had to wait in a queue to check in with only one member of staff on the desk. This happened again and again when waiting to speak to the staff on reception. You don’t expect this at a Hilton! When we arrived we ordered some coffee from the lounge bar by reception.This was expensive and scored 1/10 for quality. the 3€ add on for service charge was excessive.
