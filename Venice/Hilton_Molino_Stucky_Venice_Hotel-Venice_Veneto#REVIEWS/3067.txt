We have booked 2 nights in Hilton Mulino Stucky after some thinking and revising other hotels at Venice's city center (around Ponte Rialto, San Marco's Square). This was a great decision. It is a real value for money hotel.     Hotel is located on Giudecca Island and in a very high season this would be his only disadvantage. You have to catch Hotels courtesy boat to get to and from the city.  The boat is pretty full when you are returning back to the hotel, but if you are not in a hurry, walk to the Zattere station and wait there.  We have booked the Deluxe room Lagoon view and got beautiful room with beautiful view over Venice and Grand Canale.  The size of the room is really good, bed is great so we got a really good night sleep.
