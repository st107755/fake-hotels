My husband and I stayed in this hotel for four days during our honeymoon and from the private transfer from airport to the Hotel and then back four days later I was in awe and thoroughly enjoyed every moment.  The staff are helpful the breakfast was varied catering for all palates we upgraded to executive and used this lounge frequently it is worth the upgrade.  The water taxi the hotel provides runs every 20 minutes and there was never a time when we were waiting too long, or couldn't get on the taxi which was a concern of mine as I had read through some of the more negative reviews prior to our visit. You will be given a timetable when you check in so try to pre plan.
