The hotel shuttle boat only costs €4.50 for your entire stay but only runs every 40 minutes.   Instead buy a 24, 48, 72 hour pass for the water buses and travel where you like whenever you want.   We bought the 72 hour pass for €40 and our trips included a slow boat up the grand canal (boat #1), Murano glass (boats #4.1 or #4.2), from the airport and back.   Factor this in when booking this hotel.   In terms of the hotel: - Make sure your booking includes breakfast or the standard rate is €30 each or on offer at check in for €19 each. It is very nice but overpriced.  - Wi-Fi in the rooms is free if you sign up for Hilton Honors, also free. Would have been nice to be told about this at check in!
