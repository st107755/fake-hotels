We are Diamond with Hilton so our stay for three nights (2 pre-cruise and 1 post) was using our Hilton points.  We were hoping for a canal view when we first arrived and they indicated they were all booked, but put us into a suite which was such a beautiful room!  I loved the decor in the room with beautiful chandeliers and lovely furnishings and the arched windows.   The executive lounge was open from 5 PM to 7 PM and served some hot hors d'oeuvres and cheeses with wine, beer and soft drinks which was great.    We enjoyed the transportation provided by the hotel to the main areas but wish it ran a bit more often during the weekdays.  The alilaguna blue line runs to the cruise port and is a great way to get to and from the ship as it stops directly in front of the hotel.
