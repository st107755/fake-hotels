My husband and I stayed the end of August. Had a king suite with a view.  It was spacious and well appointed.  As a Hhonors Diamond member I had complementary access to the lounge with free drinks and snacks so I did not experience the high priced water others mentioned in their reviews.   Rooftop pool and bar were a nice plus, however if you're really interested in laying by the pool (which I don't understand in Venice) you need to get to the pool early as there is limited seating.    The complementary shuttle was very convenient. Since we did a lot of walking there was little need for any other transportation.
