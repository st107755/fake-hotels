From the moment we climbed out of the water taxi at the hotel and greeted on the waters edge to the day we left, the staff at the hotel made every effort to make us feel welcome and quite special. The location of the hotel really helps you to relax as it is located across the Giudecca canal from the central square which means you can use the complimentary launch the hotel provides to take you over and back throughout the day. Leaving the crowds behind you.   The hotel maintains the character of the original flower mill, with oak beams, high ceilings and spacious rooms.
