We absolutely loved this hotel. We stayed at this hotel last week for our 10 year wedding anniversary and was pleasantly surprised with a room upgrade when we arrived. The hotel decor is quite contemporary and the staff were very polite and helpful. The view from the rooftop terrace is absolutely breathtaking. There is not an awful lot to do on the island where the hotel is located but the free shuttle service to San Marco comes in handy. Also there are water taxis waiting just outside of the hotel in case you want to venture out a bit further. . The only negative point I would say about this hotel is the restaurant. The all day dining restaurant is actually quite good but the more expensive restaurant that was open for evening dining was really not that impressive.
