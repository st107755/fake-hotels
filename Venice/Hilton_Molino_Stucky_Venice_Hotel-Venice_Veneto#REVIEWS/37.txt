My wife and I have been coming to the Hilton Molino Stucky in Venice every year for the best part of a decade to spend two weeks at the Carnivale. I don’t think there’s another Hilton that we visit so frequently or enjoy so much. Why? The Hilton Molino Stucky stands on three pillars. The first pillar is its great architectural beauty. It’s a very large old mill and it is solidly built. Indeed, it’s almost like staying in a living museum. They have done a great job in converting a factory into a remarkable place to stay; its wooden beams, red bricks, and iron columns are quite amazing. This year we are staying in a newly renovated part that combines the best of the old with modern décor (and the latest technology like lots of USB sockets).
