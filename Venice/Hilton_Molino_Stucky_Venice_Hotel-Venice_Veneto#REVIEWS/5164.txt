We went to Molino Stucky for our wedding anniversary.  Flew into Marco Polo airport and took the blue line of the Alilaguna Airport - Venice roundtrip ticket through http://www.venicelink.com/index.php?lang=english.  Very reasonably priced and a slow tour of the city for ~ 90 minutes.  The second to last stop is the Hilton and it is sensational to arrive at after a long plane flight on a nice sunny Venetian day.  We are Hilton Gold members and were upgraded to a room overlooking the Giudecca Canal (in between the hotel and St. Mark's square).  We also had lounge access.  Their is a boat from the hotel to St. Mark's and back for free and we used it strategically to take advantage of the lounge.  The room was beautiful, the staff were very helpful and spoke great english.
