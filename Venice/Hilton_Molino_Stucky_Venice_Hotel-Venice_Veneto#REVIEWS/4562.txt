We stayed at the Molino Stucky in March 2013 and had a good time. Our biggest concern before we got there was the location of the hotel and how easy it would be to get to the centre of Venice. We needn't have worried as the free shuttle boat was very good; they stuck to the schedule and we were never turned away due to it being full.  In fact, we quite enjoyed the short boat trips to and from the hotel as we got good views of Venice and the boat was something of a novelty.  Our room was very small but large enough for a weekend city break because it was just a place to sleep.  The room was very well furnished and the bed was very comfortable and the tea and coffee were replenished daily.  The marble bathroom was well presented.
