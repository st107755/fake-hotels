I stayed here with my girlfriend in January 2014. We got a pretty good deal to stay here through a third-party site, so though "Why not?". The room was the nicest we stayed in on our trip through Europe. Not the best or the one with the most character, but the "nicest" if that makes sense. I guess that's expected though, because it was also the most expensive.  The highlight was definitely the comfortable king size bed. We're both pretty tall, so staying in that bed with plenty of room was a real novelty for us. Like I said, the place was really nice too, but in a sort of generic, hotel-chain type of way is probably the best way to describe it.
