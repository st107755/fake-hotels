Just back from 4 wonderful days in this Hotel, fantastic from the moment we got of the water taxi to the day we left.  This was for a birthday treat for my friend Jackie.  We were upgraded to a canal view which was fantastic, had a lovely surprise in the room when we got there, a nice chilled bottle of prosecco, and a lovely birthday cake.  The receptionists were great both in and out, and very informative about the Hotel facilities, the Hotel was immaculate and the room with its glass chandelier was gorgeous.  The island of Giudecca  was nice and quiet, away from the hustle and bustle of the main island which was only a 10 mins taxi ride away, nice to get back after a great day touring St Marks square etc.It was also nice to see all the conservation going on in the area.
