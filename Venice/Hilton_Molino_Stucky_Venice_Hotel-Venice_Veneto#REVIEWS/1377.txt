We arrived via car, parked at the main Tonchetto garage and took the public bus over to the hotel.  It was not difficult- you do not have to get the $$ water taxi. We got the 48 hour bus pass and it was worth it. Loved pulling up by boat to this beautifully restored old mill.  There is an automatic 6 euros charge per guest for the Hilton shuttle boat. Between the 2 boats it was easy to get around. Shuttles are almost always on time.  The hotel is very unique and they have done a nice job- a few restaurants there we did not try. Staff were all courteous.    We are Hilton diamond members and stayed for 2 days in an executive room with city view. The room was spacious and the view was worth it.
