If you are going on a vacation to Venice then I would suggest that stay at Hilton Molino Stucky. Its quiet calm and a perfect place to relax. We took a Water taxi to the hotel (its the best way to commute with heavy luggage) and upon arrival we were helped by the staff for a quick check-in. The staff were quiet efficient and knew what exactly we needed - before we could ask for a baby-cot for our baby - they had already put a request for the baby. Breakfast was awesome - a huge spread and you get almost what you need for your breakfast. The sad part was the Skypool on the terrace doesn't open in the winters and we couldn't enjoy that.
