My wife and I stayed at this hotel during our recent trip to Italy.  It was by far the nicest hotel that we stayed at. The rooms and beds are very comfortable.  The breakfast buffet was really good.  There was a beautiful grand piano in the lobby that I played a few times.  The concierge named Carlos helped us one morning with a problem we had with our airplane tickets.  He worked for about 1/2 hour to make sure that it got sorted out.  He was so helpful and in the end he got us an upgrade of our seats at no cost.  This hotel is across the water from the main part of Venice but there is a free and regular shuttle service back and forth.There are some restaurants and shops within a short walking distance to the hotel.  (Don't go to Venice if you don't want to walk).
