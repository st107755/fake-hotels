We have just returned from a weekend in Venice staying at this hotel.  The hotel and staff were fantastic.  We had a suite facing the water in the 6th floor which was huge and spotlessly clean.  The hotel is a boat ride from the main tourist attractions but there is a regular shuttle boat which take around 15 minutes and costs only €4.50 per person for the whole of your stay.  Note a private water taxi charges €60 one way!  The hotel also runs complimentary trips to the glass factories at Murano which are well worth a visit.  There are good restaurants only a 5 minute walk away if you want to eat out and a few small shops too should you want fruit, cakes or ice cream.
