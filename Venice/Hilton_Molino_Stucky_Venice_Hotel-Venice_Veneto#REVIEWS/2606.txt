We were upgraded to an executive suite that overlooked the lagoon. So fabulous to watch the boats and cruise ships go by. The view of Venice from the room and the Pool and Skyline bar is beyond compare. I usually would not spring for an executive room or a view, but I will next time. We came back to refresh late afternoon before dinner and it was a pleasure to have this view. Was worried when we heard they were fully booked because of some cruise ship clients. But the hotel handled everyone easily and it is such a big "flour mill" we never felt crowded. The rooms can have a bit of a mildew smell but I think that may be the water that is everywhere in Venice. There was a plug-in aromatic product in a drawer. We plugged it in, and it worked great.
