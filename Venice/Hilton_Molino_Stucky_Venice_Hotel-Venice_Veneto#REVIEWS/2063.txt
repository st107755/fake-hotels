You know what to expect from a Hilton, and, by and large, the Hilton in Venice delivers. The hotel is clean and the staff are courteous and professional. It is in a fantastic location......on the banks of the Guidecca Canal....which is so much quieter than Venice itself....plus the hotel runs a frequent shuttle to San Marco throughout the day and until almost midnight. It's also close to La Palanca if you want to pick up the water bus network and travel out to Murano, Burano and Torcello.....highly recommended.
