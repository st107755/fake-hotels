I stay at Hilton hotels all over the world for business. I have come to expect reliable but basic service and amenities. Hilton Molino Stucky is definately an exception in a good way. It's been built in a historic restored building which gives it a very authentic Venetian feel. It's right on the water and the roof pool has a spectacular view. Although a 15 minute boat ride from the town centre, the hotel provides a free shuttle service which runs frequently. Very comfortable and well appointed rooms overall hotel decor. I just have two complaints. I upgraded my room to a king deluxe with a view that was advertised as breathtaking. The view was okay but from the side of the hotel rather than the front.
