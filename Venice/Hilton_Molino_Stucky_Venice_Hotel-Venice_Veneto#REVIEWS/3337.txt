Some may feel not being in the centre of Venice a hindrance but I think it was very good.  It was, after all, only a very short water taxi ride to St Mark's square.  Admittedly we were on a tour so it was all arranged for us.  That is the huge advantage of a tour.  The hotel is fabulous inside.  Very plain from the water frontage but beautifully fitted out.  Our bedroom was huge!  The bath room equally so.  It was very easy for one person to be in the bath whilst another was in the shower.  Décor superb.  Our breakfasts were very well presented with a huge selection to suit all comers.  The only flaw was the free Wifi (arranged as part of the tour) which only worked down in the lobby or outside the front door.  I believe Wifi is quite expensive if not included.
