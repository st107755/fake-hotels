Being on Giudecca allows one to escape the hordes, and yet a quick free ferry ride away you are in St Marks or other parts of Venice.   The hotel conversion from a former flour mill is original and impressive,and this Hilton location offers very fair value in Venice. To get here by the public water taxi from the bus or railway station is an easy simple affair, but the place comes into it's own at night. The few restaurants located a short stroll from the hotel cater to locals, they pop in for a chat and a glass of whatever with their dogs, feminine companions or mates, and thus you do not feel like a tourist.
