I stayed at the Molino Stucky Hilton in February. It was my second stay at this hotel. I fell in love with it after my first stay the previous year, and I knew that I wanted to return as often as possible. All of the staff at the Molino Stucky are people who take great pride in their work and define themselves by the level of professionalism that they bring to their work each day. These people do their work so well that they make it look easy and they make a lasting impression on people like me, who then feel compelled to write about their experience. For my own part, I will always measure other hotels by the standards set at the Molino Stucky Hilton.
