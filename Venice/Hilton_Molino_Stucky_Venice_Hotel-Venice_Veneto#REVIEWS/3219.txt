Absolutely stunning hotel. When you pull up to the hotel via the hotel shuttle it is breath taking. Also the fact the hotel has its own shuttle is brilliant and it savea you a fortune. We walked into the hotel the reception and bar area is nice and spacious. Our room itself was a good size, bed was very comfortable and bathroom was nice and spacious. Hotel bar and in room fridge for drinks was quite expensive but is to be expected. The hotel is very large and can easily get lost, the hallways are very nice decorated and wide so will have no problems with suitcases. I also visited the gym which had basic equipment but has what is needed. Spa didnt use but gym was located there and it seemed very nice.
