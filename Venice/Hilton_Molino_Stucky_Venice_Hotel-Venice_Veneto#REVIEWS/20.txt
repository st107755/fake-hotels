I spent a week in Venice 8-15 August. As a HH Diamond member I got upgraded ot a Guidecca room on the 6th floor with a view along the Guidecca canal with great views of Santa Maria de Salute and San Giorgio and the Campanile of St Mark's square was also seen. The sunrise views were amazing and during the day it was breathtaking. Breakfast was excellent  with a wide variety of hot and cold items served by the great staff who remembered my name. The pool has to be prebooked ( the first day was not available but I had left it to the same day to book- for the rest of my stay I booked a day in advance and got a slot) The gym is nice but only used it once as I spent most of the day walking in Venice.
