We stayed at the Venice Hilton, the Molino Stucky, last year and the first thing we did when we got back was to book another 13 nights this year. We enjoyed it last year and we’ve enjoyed it just as much this year.  Last year, we were reticent about staying at the Hilton because it’s on Giudecca Island about 300 m from Venice proper and the only transport is by boat. We were worried about transport in case it was too infrequent or full. The Hilton provides a free shuttle hourly to Saint Mark’s and we’ve never had any problems getting it. Giudecca is relatively quiet (it’s a real place full of real Venetians unlike the region round St. Mark’s).
