As a renovated flour mill the hotel exudes warmth. The floors in the old mill have been replaced by  beautiful marble floor while the wooden ceiling beams of the mill have been refurbished. The staff is as warm and welcoming as the hotel and even though they are all young they have been with the hotel since it opened a few years ago thus they are experienced and very helpful. There is always someone at your elbow to help with any question or issue you may have. If you decide to book at this hotel you will love the rooms. They are lovely and spotlessly clean. And you will adore the views if you book a front room as the front of the hotel faces the lagoon with its busy boat traffic. The colorful buildings of Venice provide the backdrop of the view.
