We stayed here last minute due to our original hotel not having the AC turned on yet in Mid May. It is a huge picturesque building across the bay from the main island. If you take the vaporetto it is the palanca stop & a 5 minute walk to the hotel. We just bought a day pass & took that instead of using the hotel's shuttle- which came less often.  The hotel is giant, with a huge fancy lobby, several restaurants, spa, gym & skybar on the roof.  We declined the€15 per person breakfast, which was way too expensive.   Our room was beautiful & spacious. This is definitely an American hotel. The pillows were fluffy, bed firm but mot rock hard like Italian hotels, & the bathroom had an american tub/shower instead of those mini corner showers. The sink had a long counter too.
