We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky Venice during a 3-day visit to Venice.  The hotel itself is beautiful.  The building was an old factory, but it's been renovated inside to suit the original architecture and Venetian styles in general. Numerous nice decorative touches remind you that you are staying in a unique Italian hotel - at no point does it feel like a boring chain property.     The views from the hotel are incredible.  It sits on Giudecca island, just across the canal from Venice proper.  From our room we could see all the way to San Marco. And if your room does not face the canal, you can go up to the rooftop bar for spectacular views.  Getting to Venice proper from the hotel is easy.
