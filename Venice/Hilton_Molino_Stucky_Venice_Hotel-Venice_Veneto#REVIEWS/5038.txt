Stayed in the Molino Stucky for 5 nights at end of June and can only say good things about it. What an impressive hotel, stands out from the waters edge. We paid extra for an executive room and was really glad that we had as we had the most amazing canal view and also entrance to the executive lounge with free drinks, alcoholic and soft ,tea and coffee and snacks all day and night including very fresh salad! The staff were friendly especially in the breakfast room and on the desk to the entrance, they couldn't do enough for you. We arrived by private water taxi which whilst expensive (€105) was arranged by the Hotel is the best way to arrive into Venice especially for first times, very exciting!
