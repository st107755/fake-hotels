Spent four nights at the Hilton Molino Stucky on a short holiday.  This is a former flour mill, which has been transformed into a luxury hotel with seemingly no expense spared.  The hotel had been open for three months by the time we arrived and any teething problems had been overcome.  The staff were very friendly and efficient and set an impressive tone from the moment of our arrival.  The hotel is vast – one member of staff told us that it is the biggest in Italy – and it still wasn’t full.  That will take some time, but I feel sure the Stucky will prove highly popular as a conference destination, as well as catering for holiday trade.
