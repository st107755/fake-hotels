We stayed 3 nights at the Hilton Molino, I booked a king executive room with view, the room was large with a view of the Lido, tea and coffee in room plus slippers/robes. The club executive was a big benefit, complimentary soft drinks and tea and coffee and snacks all day, then 5pm til 7pm they offered hot/cold snacks and wine, amazing olives, nice to relax after day on main island.  The skyline bar was great and amazing views off Venice Island, also they make amazing cocktails ... The skyline pool is lovely too, two afternoons we sat on roof by pool enjoying the sun with a glass of prosecco or two.  Breakfast was good, however the plates are tiny, so your back and forth as you can't put what you need on plate at one time!
