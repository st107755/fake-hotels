You are going to pay for this hotel so it is not for the faint of heart, and I don't just mean the rooms. First, it is located on the island of Giudecca, a short free boat ride over to the main part of Venice (the boat drops you right next to Piazza San Marco). This is good and bad. Good because you can easily avoid some of the tourism madness of the primary Venetian spots, and Giudecca is kind of underrated as a place to stroll, jog, and get a feel for a legit neighborhood, if such a thing can be said to exist in Venice. Bad because the last hotel boat returns at about midnight and after that you will need to wait for the much diminished early morning services of the vaporetti (about 7 Euros without the pass).
