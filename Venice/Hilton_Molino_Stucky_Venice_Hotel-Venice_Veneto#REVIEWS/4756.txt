My wife and I stayed at this hotel recently for a two night visit to Venice.    Since I am a Hilton Gold member we got upgraded to quite an impressive suite.    The suite did not have a view, but that was not really an issue since we were up and about most of the day anyway.  The executive lounge offered a limited breakfast, basic pastries and some hot food.  While there are pots of coffee, my tip here is to ask the lounge staff for a cafe latte, cappuccino, or espresso.   They will gladly make one for you.   Overall the staff was extremely friendly and helpful.  We drove into Venice from another city and parked our rental car in one of the many parking garages.
