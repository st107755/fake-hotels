Travelling w/sister, on a tour, very excited that we would be staying here 2 nights. Something different from previous stays in Venice.  But must say I was not overly impressed; maybe I expected too much.  Room size good, comfortable; breakfast, I've had better at less prestigious hotels, albeit a nice touch was a server walking around refilling your OJ. The building has an interesting history as a former mill; huge but navigable. Not inconvenient, as the free shuttle to/from Venice proper is a plus/$-saver, and ran timely, although we only took it 2x going not returning.  Also accessible by vaparetto.  Giudecca island is nice & quiet, not crowded, off the "beaten" Venice track.  Every day we saw a local mom walking her twin babies in their pram.
