This was our 3rd visit to Venice in 5 years so we didn't feel the need to be in the middle of things.  We couldn't have asked for a better place to be.  We arrived after 24 hours in transit and were provided an upgraded room (suite) overlooking the Grand Canal.  It was lovely, comfortable, and quiet.  The hotel provides regular shuttle boat service to the piazza directly across the canal (where we ventured around back areas for dinner--comfortable, not too crowded, and good food), as well as regular service to St Mark's Square.  Getting to the hotel from the airport wasn't exactly convenient but that can be said for any of the Venice hotels, and after that, we had no issues.
