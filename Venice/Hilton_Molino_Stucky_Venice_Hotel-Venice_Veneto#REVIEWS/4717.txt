Beautifully restored and appointed, this hotel certainly stands out.  Great if you're about to embark on a cruise (the port is very close) but not such a great place if you want to be in the middle of the action, although the regular shuttle to San Marco is a bonus.  The rain was lashing down so much when we arrived that we decided to have a light lunch at the hotel rather than go out (we did try but were soaked after five minutes so turned back).  This wasn't so good... the order had to be repeated three times (the waiter just couldn't get it) and the food was so-so and extremely expensive.  And the wine... well, when a hotel charges €50 for a €10 bottle of wine, that's a real sting so we chose to have a Spritz instead.
