From the moment we checked in, the staff at the Molino Stucky couldn't have done  enough for us. We were a couple travelling last-minute and received first-class service all the way.   We thought the room facilities were excellent, with a chilled bottle of Prosecco awaiting us in our room. Mini-bar was well stocked, and the view was stunning. Room was cleaned to an exceptionally high standard too, which was lovely. The hotel used to be an old mill (the "Molino") belonging to a man named Stucky (see where it gets its name from!) and the decorations in the common areas, as well as the rooms, reflect its old heritage with wooden beams and cast iron supports sat alongside the more modern, opulent furnishing.  We used the main "Rialto" bar a couple of times, both great.
