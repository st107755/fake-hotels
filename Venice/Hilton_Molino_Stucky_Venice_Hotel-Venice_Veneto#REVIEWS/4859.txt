The Hilton Molino is not the usual standardized Hilton hotel, but a true luxury experience, with large, comfortable and elegant room, facing the laguna. The service is effective and helpful. The view over the city is nice. The only negative aspect is the location. on Giudecca island, which forces to take the hotel boat shuttle each time you want to go out for a walk.
