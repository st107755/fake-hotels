The Molino was great. I went off other reviewers that suggested staying on one of the surrounding islands in the summer months. The Molino is located on the island of Giudecca right across the channel from Venice. They provide a (nearly) free shuttle that runs every 20 minutes and drops you right near San Marco. The ride takes about 10 minutes. I said nearly because the hotel adds a 4 euro per person charge onto your bill. Considering a private taxi is around 70 euro, I think this price is more than fare. FYI the last ferry leaves San Marco at 11:40pm, if you miss the boat you will have to get a private water taxi back to the hotel.   The hotel has a rooftop pool which we never had the chance to go in.
