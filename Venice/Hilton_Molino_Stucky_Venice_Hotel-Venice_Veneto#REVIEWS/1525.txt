My wife and I had a wonderful relaxing respite while staying at the Hilton Molino Stucky Venice Hotel. We enjoyed the spa and my wife had several massages whilst we were there. We had never had the occasion to travel to Venice, so we had nothing to judge our stay against. We opted for the Molino Stucky because it was away from the center of all the action, even though we knew it wouldn't be very crowded this time of the year, we wanted a place where we could stretch out a bit. As I understand it some older hotels in the main city area are quite old and much smaller. The Stucky Molino is newly renovated (in the last 15 years I think, thats brand new by Venice standards) so the rooms are bigger, much bigger.
