We took the airport boat bus to the Hilton Molino. We were so impressed from the moment we walked into the hotel. The reservation staff was friendly and our room was ready although we arrived early. We were weary travelers after our long flight. The room is large for Europe and the bed was perfect and so plush. I loved this place and the Guidecca Island. We enjoyed a wonderful romantic dinner just a stroll down from the hotel. There are cute restaurants along the water. The views are spectacular here. Don't worry - they have a private shuttle bus that takes you San Marco square and runs late at night- so you can access busy Venice at your conveinence. We also enjoyed the rooftop bar in the evening at the hotel overlooking the boats and beautiful city of Venice at night.
