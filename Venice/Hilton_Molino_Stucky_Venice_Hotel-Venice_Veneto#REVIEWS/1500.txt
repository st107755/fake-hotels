This hotel has such history and has been restored to a high standard.  Stayed one night and was upgraded to an Executive suite.  What a wonderful room!  Ample space, with beautiful marble bathroom and good views.  Loved that it had a walk in shower which can sometimes be rare in older hotel buildings.  Getting to and from the main Venice island was easy enough since they provide a water shuttle at a cost of 6 euros per person per stay.  Breakfast was plentiful and catered for all tastes.  Executive lounge was small, however, it had sufficient snacks and drinks throughout the day, the cappuccino was particularly nice. Staff were friendly and helpful, always smiling and efficient.
