This is a  old pasta factory redesigned into a very nice, full service hotel.   We stayed here for a couple of nights before embarking on an Adriatic cruise.  Got a little concerned after booking when I began to see some critical reviews -  but they were proven to be unfounded.  The lobby / common areas are very attractively appointed; check in was quick and efficient; and our room was beyond satisfactory.  We experienced excellent service at every encounter, and enjoyed both the breakfast and dinner meals.  The rooftop bar offers a spectacular view of Venice, and enjoying an evening cocktail or glass of wine is a must.
