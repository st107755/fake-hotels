Beautiful hotel with lots of restaurants and great rooftop bar and pool.  The downsides: expect to pay heavy price for all drinks and food. Also be prepared to take the hotel shuttle (10-15 minutes) to the main island. Finally, service is pretty bad. Took over 1 hour to get an iron to our room at 23:00. Took over an hour to get a hair dryer for my wife.
