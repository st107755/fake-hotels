Escape the hustle and bustle of "downtown Venice" at this grand old warehouse! Facing the Giudecca Channel on Giudecca Island, the front rooms have a magnificent view of all the comings and goings to and from Venice! If you are a ship spotter, it's the place to stay! King Executive Rooms with a view are well worth the extra money! The hotel was originally a flour mill and pasta factory, which lay empty for 50 years before being restored in the 1990's.  The restoration has been skilfully done, with no expense spared. The rooms are huge, with high exposed ceilings and roomy bathrooms. My only "gripe" is that the hotel faces North, so gets very little sun at the front. The breakfast is your usual huge American circus (!), and was NOT well managed when we were there!
