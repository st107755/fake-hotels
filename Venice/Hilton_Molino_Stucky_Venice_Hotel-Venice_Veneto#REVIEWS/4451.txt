In many ways, just what I expect from Hilton - both the good and the bad. On the plus side, it was beautiful, good quality furniture/bedroom and excellent service. The rooftop pool and bar had the most beautiful views which were a great place to unwind after a day in Venice before a night out.  On the down side, some of the food was atrocious: EUR25 for a burger was extortionate, but at least it was tasty. The club sandwich however (same price) was horrible. The Venetian restaurant was much better, although not amazing. Similarly, the so-called "world famous breakfast" was a mediocre affair (both executive and standard). As with other Hiltons, it felt like a bit of a hierarchy there: the nice views in the breakfast areas blocked by the executive lounge.
