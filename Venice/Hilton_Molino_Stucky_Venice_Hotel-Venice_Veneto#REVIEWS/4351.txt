The hotel is located in a beautiful restored mill on an island with some transport facilities. The standard room ( 200 euro per night booked via hilton.com) was spacious and nice. Strong wooden furniture - decent bed. The furniture is starting to tire and a few shelves collapsed. Pillows were terrible (all supersoft) but the head of housekeeping made me a pillow of sorts and it wasn't too comfortable but I appreciated it. Room was very clean, although I discovered two of the towels we hadnt used were dirty, clearly used by the previous occupier and not seen by the cleaning staff. Breakfast was plentiful and of reasonable quality. The tv remote didnt work at all and got stuck on the adult channels (!!).
