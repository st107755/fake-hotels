We immensely enjoyed our stay at the Hilton Molino Stucky in Venice.  We stayed 4 nights at the end of May.  We arrived in Venice via rail, and took the vaparetto water bus to the stop nearest to the hotel.  The Directions page on the hotel’s website states that you can take either line 2 or line 4.1.  Line 4.1 is definitely the better choice.  Line 2 takes a long loop around the islands, makes frequent stops, taking a considerable amount time to make it to the Palanca stop.  Line 4.1 was quicker and less traveled by tourists (which meant more room on the boat for your bags.)  It’s not a bad walk from the vaporetto stop nearest to the hotel (Palanca), maybe a half mile in a straight line along the water.
