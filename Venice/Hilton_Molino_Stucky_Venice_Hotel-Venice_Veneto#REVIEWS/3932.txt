We spent 3 nights with our two teen kids and we all enjoyed it a lot.  First a few good things and then a few items for improvement:  - Service - Amazing.  My husband is a diamond and we were upgraded without asking to an Executive King Suite on the tower building.  It was like our own little apartment with breath taking views of the canal.  They brought two individual beds for my kids.  Breakfast included in the main restaurant without too much negotiating.  Have never seen this service even with the status my husband has at any other Hilton, Marriott or SPG properties.  - Location -  Beautiful and Quiet.  Short complimentary ferry ride away from St. Marco.  Yes you have to wait on the boat schedule and there are other water taxi option but it is not an issue.
