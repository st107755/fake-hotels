We stayed here only one night at the beginning of November. Hotel was spacious, clean, nice, up to date. The room was really good, we actually got an upgrade and we were very happy with this. Francesco at the check-in area was very attentive, service was outstanding. The shuttle service is very convenient, easy access to Venice, we didn't mind having to take the shuttle to get to Venice, you are right there within 5 to 10 minutes. If you are looking for a hotel with an old charm this might not be for you but if you appreciate new, spacious, modern, westernized hotel this is a great option for you. We loved it! Would definitely stay there again.
