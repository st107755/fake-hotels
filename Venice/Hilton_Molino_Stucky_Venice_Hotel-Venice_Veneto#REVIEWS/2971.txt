Had a lovely 2 night stay here - which couldn't have been better. Highlight for us really was the rooftop pool which provides really the most amazing views over Venice ...   Don't listen to a previous reviewer about shuttle boat - these run every 20 minutes (although not all go all the way to San Marco - some only go across the channel to Zattere, but that's no big issue, as Zattere is also a very useful stop). And you are only charged this if you actually use it - 7 Euros for us for our whole stay ... which is nothing when you consider that a single journey on the public vaporetto costs 7 Euros per person! So we saved a lot of money with this - although we did also buy a 24 hour ticket for the public boats as we wanted to travel a bit further afield.
