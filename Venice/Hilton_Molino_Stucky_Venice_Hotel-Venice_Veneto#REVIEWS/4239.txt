My girlfriend and I travelled to Venice December 2012 for our anniversary and stayed at the Hilton. It was also for me to propose to her and thought what a better city than Venice. I called the Hotel prior to our trip explaining my plan. They were very helpful with prompt replies via email. Upon arrival, we were upgraded and entered the room with champagne and chocolate strawberries (as per my request). The view was of the canal opposite the Hotel, which was wonderful. We would wake up to mist across the canal, which always looked great. The rooms were spacious and cosy and the beds were were comfortable. The breakfast was a treat! We would eat so much and get slightly excited with the Prosecco to start our day.
