Stayed here second week of February 2014. This is a very striking hotel, lots of places to sit on the ground floor and different restaurants, not all open at the same time. Giudecca is interesting to wander around and the hotel shuttle was never full. We ate in the Rialto and the Bacaromi. Food and service excellent. Prices compared to other Venice restaurants of this quality, drinks expensive but it is a Hilton in Venice. This is the best Hilton that I can recall staying in. The staff are, without exception, helpful and friendly. One of our coats was soaked one day and they quickly took it and dried it, no charge. Breakfast was excellent and they cook many things to order. We did not use wifi but there are 3 computers available for use and this was helpful.
