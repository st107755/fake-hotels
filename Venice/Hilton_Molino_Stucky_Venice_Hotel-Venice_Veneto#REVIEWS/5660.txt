Beautiful hotel housed in a converted grain mill.  Though room rates are steep, you can see where all of the money goes--excellent staff, facilities, and amenities.  The concierge staff was most helpful, especially Irene.  The executive lounge is not much to write home about but manager Francesca made sure that I was well taken care of.  My room was too small and I was not upgraded as a Diamond member--I was given the stock response that the hotel was "full" and that no rooms were available on the executive floor.  I found this a bit incredible.  The water shuttle to San Marcos was very prompt and reliable and it added to the pleasure of visiting other areas frequently throughout the day.
