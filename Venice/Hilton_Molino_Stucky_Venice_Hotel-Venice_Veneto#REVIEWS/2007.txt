Just completed a stay on July 4 and had my doubts about staying across the Guidecca canal. I was afraid that it might not be convenient to the sights and activities in and around Venice. My fears were unfounded. The hotel shuttle water taxi dropped us off right at San Marco Square and ran on a very reliable schedule. The times are published and the hotel provides a copy at check in of the schedule. I shot a picture of it on my phone for handy reference so I didn't have to fumble with paper and its posted at the dock on both ends as well.  Check in was a little delayed because our flight arrived so early. I cant really blame Hilton for that and they were able to accommodate us in the lounge for a cup of coffee on arrival.  The hotel was fantastic.
