I was a little concerned staying at Hilton Molino Stucky because it is on Giudecca Island instead of on the main island with all the attractions, but quickly realized the benefits outweighed the negatives. First, the hotel overlooks Venice just across the water, so you have an awsome view of Venice that you would not have from hotels located on the island itself. Second, it is much quieter in the evening, as you are away from the late night party people and volumes of traffic. It was like escaping to my own private oasis after the hustle and bustle of a long day of exploring Venice. I found the hotel's complimentary water shuttle to be reliable and efficient and will take you to either Zattere (just across the water near the Accadamie and Guggenheim) or to San Marco itself.
