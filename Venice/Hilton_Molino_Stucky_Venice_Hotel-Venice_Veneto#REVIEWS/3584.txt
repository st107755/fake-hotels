We spent 3 lovely nights in Venice and called the Hilton Molino Stucky our home. This is a modern renovated old factory in the Guidecca area - across the main shipping channel from the main tourist areas of Venice. Depending on what you're looking for, it could be perfect, or like we found, it could be a bit inconvenient.  The Good: we found our room to be modern, clean, and big. There is a beautiful pool and skybar on the roof (and hotel pools are hard to find in Venice). There is a beautiful view from here. The hotel has all the amenities you can imagine (several places to eat, fitness, spa, etc). We had no complaints about the hotel itself.
