After staying in some of the smaller hotels on our trip to Italy we found the Hilton to be a let down. We knew it was a larger hotel but assumed the warmness and hospitality we have found all over Italy would continue here. They weren’t unfriendly but just not warm and welcoming. I could’ve been checking into any Hilton anywhere in the world. Nothing about this hotel makes you feel like you are a special guest or that you are even in Italy. If you are the type of person that likes a chain hotel because of its consistency then the Hilton is for you.   We also found the shuttle service to the main island to be inconvenient. The shuttle is not large and if it is too full you will have to wait for the next shuttle which could make you late for a reservation.
