My wife and I stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky for one night prior to boarding a cruise since it was only ten minutes away from the cruise port.  This hotel is on an island opposite San Marco square and has frequent free shuttles to two or three drop off points for sightseeing purposes.  Though the hotel is probably the biggest in Venice and may feel crowded prior to cruise days, nevertheless, after check in, the hotel facilities were top notch, especially if you were a gold card (or higher) member for free drinks and appetizers throughout the day.   The breakfast buffet was impressive and the staff efficient to ensure clean tables.  Our room was large and looked out on the canal.
