Converted from an old flour mill, this 380roomed hotel (the largest in Venice) situated on Guidecca island, when visited in July, was very busy.This meant queues for check in,for the concierge, and often for lifts.Nevertheless the well trained staff coped very well.It is a fussless 5star which means that yout take luggage to your room yourself- help is available if requested- and forego the evening bed turndown ritual.Most guests stay only for a few days during which the city is explored and may miss out on some of the hotel facilities - the small pool or the spa for example. What everybody needs is a comfortable bed and well equipped bathroom, and here the hotel excels.
