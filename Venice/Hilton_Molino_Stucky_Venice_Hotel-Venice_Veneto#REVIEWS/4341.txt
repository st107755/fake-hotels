Stayed 1 night als we had to cover one extra night between our appartment Residenza Grande Veduti and Lago Maggiori.  The island of Giudecca is - for us the perfect Venetian hideaway.  Just across from the busy main island of Venice.  Hilton is a typical huge congress hotel. Rooms, restaurants are picobello. Shuttle service to San Marco and Zattere across the rivier are very convenient.  The rooftop swimming pool is TOP.  2 things that bothered me: 1/ roomrate is quite high, and still Hilton finds it normal or necessary to charge for WIFI in the rooms.  In the Public areas they offer free WIFI for 8 hours. For the rest you pay 20€ per day?!  Sorry Hilton, but that is for me a reason to think again.  2/ the Public areas are very busy.
