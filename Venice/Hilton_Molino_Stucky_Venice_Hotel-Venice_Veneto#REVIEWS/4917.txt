We are having a lovely trip to Venice. The hotel is well situated despite being located on Guidecca island. Most of the main attractions are accessible via the free Hilton Shuttle boat to Zaterre and St Marco.   We arrived from Marco Polo on the AliLaguna Blue Line which seemed to take 1.5 to 2 hours to cover the distance from the Airport but the great plus is that the boat stops right outside the hotel so this is effectively door to door service. Tickets can be bought on the web for a small discount.  The hotel is in amazing condition having been restored in 2007 and we were given two adjoining rooms. Unfortunately the second room had one double bed and we have teenage children so this was not desirable.
