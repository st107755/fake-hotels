Had a lovely long weekend in Venice with my wife. Very romantic setting. We enjoyed lovely weather. The sun was out throughout our stay. The Hilton brand is what attracted us to this hotel and we were not disappointed. The hotel was immaculate. The staff very friendly. We upgraded our room from standard to a room with a view of the canal and city beyond. The hotel offers a free taxi shuttle boat which ran frequently. Probably best to visit in October, as we did, rather than the height of summer to avoid the crowded queues for the taxi boats. The gondola ride was expensive but needs to be done so prepare yourself for the price.  Good walking. Good shopping. Great history. The hotel buiding had a history of its own as a former flour mill.
