My wife and I stayed briefly before an Adriatic cruise. This was our first stay in any Hilton, but our third trip to Venice, which we love - there's nowhere else like it. Favourite tip; take the water bus (Alilaguna?) from the airport - it winds through the centre of the city, while the far more expensive water taxis seem to take the shortest route round the less-than scenic landward side. Second Venice tip; buy a good map - I find the AA one invaluable as it even has a street index. Third Venice tip; have a plan for what to do in case of good and bad weather, and buy 12- or 24-hour tickets at least for the water-buses when you decide on an itinerary that warrants them - the multi-day passes are even better value if you'll be using them throughout.
