We had 1 night at the Hilton post-cruise and at first glance it looked a good choice.  However to get to the hotel from the San Basilio terminal which is directly opposite we could either get on a waterbus to somewhere then change buses to get to the Hilton - which we decided was a bit too faffy with all our luggage - or get a water taxi across.  Minimum charge for water taxi was 50 Euros so a 2 minute crossing of the lagoon was outrageously expensive albeit the most convenient way.      There is a lovely rooftop bar at the Hilton, but we noticed a sign by the lift saying that it was opening at 8pm instead of 5pm that night due to a private party for something to do with the Venice film festival.  Disappointing as we wanted a drink before dinner up there.
