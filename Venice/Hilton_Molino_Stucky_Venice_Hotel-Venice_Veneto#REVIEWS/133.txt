Overall we enjoyed our stay at the hotel, however there were a few flaws!  On the plus side we loved the location as it gave great views of Venice and it was very easy to get the water buses (we didn’t use the hotel one ) to the main attractions. The hotel rooftop bar and pool are also very nice. We had a refurbished room and found it very modern in comparison to other hotels we had looked at in Venice. We were also able to check in to the room early which was a bonus. The staff at both reception and in the bars were very helpful. There was an iron, tea/coffee making facilities and free WiFi in the room which again are big plus points !
