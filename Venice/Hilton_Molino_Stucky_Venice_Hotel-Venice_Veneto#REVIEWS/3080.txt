My friend and I travelled from America to tour Italy and stayed here in December last year. The hotel is beautiful and it's always nice to stay with a chain your familiar with when the city is all new. Our room overlooked the inside courtyard but we were hardly in there it was spotlessly clean and well equipped. The bathroom was my favourite! We visited the hotel lobby bar for crepes most days as they were so delicious! Skyline also has stunning views out over the canal! We visited there most nights for a drink where the staff are very attentive. The taxi from the hotel to the main island is usually every half an hour and always has space for everyone.
