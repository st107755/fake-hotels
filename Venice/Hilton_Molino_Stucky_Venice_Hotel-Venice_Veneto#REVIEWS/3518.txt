This is a beautiful hotel located across the waterway from Venice.  The rooms were clean and the bed comfortable.  We paid extra for a "water view" & thought we would be facing the water.  Our room actually face the side with a view going toward San Marco.  The view was beautiful but not what we expected.  The tub was scary for my husband who has mobility issues.  The bottom of the tub was curved.  In other words, there was only a very small flat area on the bottom of the tub with no grippers so it was hard for him to balance in the tub.  We put a towel in the bottom for him to shower.   The cost of the room was not exorbitant but beware if you plan to eat at the hotel.  Our room wasn't ready when we arrived so we got lunch while we were waiting.
