Just enjoyed another Christmas break at the Molino Stucky Hilton. This Hotel has soul, 13 former flour mill buildings brilliantly converted into a 5-star hotel. The wooden beams and steel supports on view everywhere convey a feeling of the enormity and importance of the enterprise in the 19th and early 20th centuries. Great staff, can't do enough for you, and several recognised us from our Carnevale trip in February 2015. Food is great, albeit a little pricey, but you don't have to spend a fortune to be well fed. Have enjoyed 6 great stays at different times since Christmas 2008, and never been disappointed. Rooms are perfect for a short break, well-appointed with comfortable beds.
