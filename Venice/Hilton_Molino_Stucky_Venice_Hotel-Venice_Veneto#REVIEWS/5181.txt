We stayed at this hotel for two nights pre-cruise.  I was a bit reticent to book at first given it is not on the main island.  However, the free hotel shuttle transportation to Venice proper was convenient and easy.  Plus, you have a beautiful view of Venice from the hotel rooms (ask for a canal view).  We had also arranged for transportation from the airport through the hotel.  For those (like us) who are visiting Venice for the first time, I would highly recommend to avoid confusion.  The hotel representative was waiting outside of customs and a van was waiting outside which took us to the private water taxi and then to the hotel.  Very easy although it is somewhat pricey.  We stayed on the Executive floor which included drinks and food, including breakfast, in the lounge.
