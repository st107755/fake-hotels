We had a wonderful stay in November for 3 nights. The hotel upgraded us to an Executive room in advance of our arrival.  The rooms were very nice, and we had a great view of Venice.  We also really enjoyed the rooftop bar and the service was great.  We arrived into Venice quite late from Milan, and the bar manager went out of his way to look for some food to serve us, even though the kitchen had closed.  The executive lounge was on the first floor, where the hotel sectioned off a part of the first floor restaurant.  The breakfast was pretty good, though we would have liked a bit more variety.  In the evening, the lounge was too small to handle the number of people there to enjoy the appetizers and beverages.
