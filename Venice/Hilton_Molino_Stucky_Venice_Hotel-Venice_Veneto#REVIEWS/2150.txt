My wife and I spent two nights at the Hilton Molino-Stucky and fell in love with this property, it's staff, attention to detail, and location in the heart of Venice.  St Marks Square, Basilica San Giorgio Majorre, Rialto Bridge, .... in fact everything is just a short water bus or water taxi ride away.  The hotel has it's own water taxi and charges E $4.50 a day if you use the service (and you should).  If you prefer endless rides throughout Venice via water taxi I suggest you buy one full days pass for E $20.  You can go to Murano, Burano, and anywhere in Venice using the ogi/day water bus pass.  Two stops and you've essentially gotten your money's worth.
