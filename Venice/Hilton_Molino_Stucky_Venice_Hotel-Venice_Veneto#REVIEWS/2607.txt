We stayed in a “King Hilton Deluxe room with City View” for 7 nights in September.  It’s a nice hotel with both pros and cons.  Our room was nice and spacious, with a magnificent view. The breakfast was delicious, and the lady that helped us find a table at breakfast every day was very nice and service minded. Other than her I can’t really praise the customer service. The staff was OK, but it could be hard getting a smile out of them (I smile all the time so I do find this a bit sad!).  The roof top pool and terrace area were beautiful with a breathtaking view. Be aware that the sun beds gets occupied very fast on nice days, so you have to get here early if you want to be guaranteed a sun bed.
