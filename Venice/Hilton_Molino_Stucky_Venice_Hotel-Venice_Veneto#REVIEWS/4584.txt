We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky over Good Friday/Easter weekend and had an excellent time.  As Diamond level members we were upgraded to a beautiful suite overlooking the water. The hotel is very comfortable and the room had every possible amenity.  We had breakfast each morning at the breakfast buffet, which was very extensive  The hotel's location is a positive and a negative.  It is certainly nice to be able to get away from the bustle and crowds of St. Marks but it can be a bit inconvenient to have to take a boat each time you go out (though you are in Venice so that shouldn't come as a surprise).  You can't run back to the hotel in a few minutes if you forgot something or want to drop something off.
