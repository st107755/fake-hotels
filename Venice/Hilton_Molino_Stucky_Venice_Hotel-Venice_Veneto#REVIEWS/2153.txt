I love this hotel. We first visited here 5 years ago, and didn't hesitate to book again for this most recent trip to Venice. We took a private water taxi from the airport to the hotel, but you can easily take the public vaporetto as it is a short walk. This stay was on points and as a Diamond member we were upgraded to a suite with a lagoon view (see pic from the room.) Absolutely fantastic! Another perk is the free breakfast which really stood out from our last trip and didn't disappoint this time either!  Guest will love the Hilton water taxi shuttle that stops at St. Marco and Zattere, and its usage is included for guest for a small free.  Definitely take advantage of this.  There is also a lounge near the lobby for gold and Diamond guest.
