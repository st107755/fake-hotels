This trip we travelled with our 13yr old and 10yr daughters.  The hotel is located in Guidecca on the opposite side to the main attractions and cruise terminal.  We booked the family king suite.  This consisted of a king beg, separate room, a dining table, small kitchenette and a sofa bed.  One bathroom, two toilets.  Fabolous layout for us.  The girls shared the sofa and we were able to close our room off at night.  The fridge in the kitchenette is empty which allows you to purchase drinks etc in Venice.  The breakfast was a typical standard affair, not the world famous breakfast that they advertised.  To get to San Marco you need to catch the hotel shuttle boat which leaves about every 30 minutes.  The downside is that at about 8.30pm they stop for about 1 and half hours.
