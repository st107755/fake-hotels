Loved the property and service. We chose the location due to the roof top pool, yet never had enough time with all of our site-seeing to actually hang out there. We enjoyed taking to water taxi to & from the main land-- although it was a bit difficult to coordinate site seeing with the boat schedule. Had a lovely dinner at one of the hotel restaurants, Bacaromi, when a huge thunderstorm rolled in and prevented us from gettting back to Venice. The concierge easily set up our reservation and gave us a voucher for 2 complimentary glasses of delicious Prosecco. Executive king suite was very spacious for our family of 4. Only objections were the lack of convenient outlets for charging electronics, especially bedside, and the lighting in the room was pretty weak.
