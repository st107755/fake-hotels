A spectacular hotel to enjoy Venice's beauty and splendor. While you may have to travel the water to get to the square, you simply can't beat the service, quality and beauty of this hotel. The complimentary water taxi is always on time and makes for a perfect way to embrace Venice in the way she was meant to be enjoyed - on the water. The rooms are spacious and opulent; opt for a suite if you can (we went with the Amore suite) and you'll receive access to their wonderful lounge which serves a fantastic array of complimentary food, beverages, cocktails and more throughout the day looking out onto the water.
