I went to Venice with my girlfriend and booked this hotel as I wanted to use some Hilton points. I had mixed expectations as I had read various, conflicting reviews, so wasn’t quite sure what to expect. From the photos I had seen and from the hotel website it all sounded and looked amazing, and I was really looking forward to visiting Venice for the first time, but I had nagging doubts, as I had read a lot of bad reviews about the service. Having booked on Hilton points, I was allocated a standard room, but on arrival at the hotel I discovered that, as an Honors Diamond member, I had been upgraded to an executive room, which was nice.
