Great place to stay in Venice, it is not located near the tourist hotspots, but the shuttle from the hotel runs every 20 minutes and takes you straight to Piazza San Marco. I honestly enjoyed the shuttle, we got to experience views of the city that visitors normally pay for with canal tours. Be aware that the shuttle can fill up fast during peak hours (dinner time, early morning, end of day).   Our room was immaculate, the best part was the massive window over looking the canal.  If you want the fresh canal evening breeze to lull you to sleep you will have to endure the sounds of the canal traffic. Nothing beat the views of the historic city though, it was very inspiring.
