We stayed at this hotel as a family (11months old) for our 5th wedding anniversary. The hotel is absolutely stunning! The rooms are a good size and very clean. The complimentary water transfers to St Marks Sqr are great and the location is very good and not too far away. The pool is gorgeous and one of the reasons we stayed here. The only issue was the fact there is absolutely no shade there so we couldn't stay long as our baby is 11 months and it was too hot for her.  The main thing that lets the hotel down are the prices of the food and drink. We paid €19 for a cocktail which is think is unjustified. We would have more time drinking and dining at the hotel if it was more reasonable. You expect to pay more but they really do take the mick!
