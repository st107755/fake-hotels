This hotel is not up to the standard of other Hilton's we have stayed in unfortunately. Whilst the room itself was nice, clean and comfortable, the service we received could not be further from that. The hotel made an error with my credit card that was being held for pre-authorisation, meaning that they emptied my bank acount of all funds over our trip, and did not return this money for over three weeks after our return, despite constant phone calls. I was disappointed in their attitude towards the problem, constantly that they would 'sort it later'.  The hotel is not in the main square area and is in fact on it's own little island, Giudecca, however this is not a problem as the shuttle boat from the hotel takes passengers over to the main spots every 10/15 minutes.
