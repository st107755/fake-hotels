We stayed at this hotel for two nights during our trip to Italy.  From the minute we arrived we were made to feel welcome and the receptionist gave us all of the information we required during our stay. There is a lot of information because this hotel is a very large combination of buildings making up this wonderful place. We were staying in a King Deluxe room which had amazing views of Venice and the grand canal. The room was freshly decorated with a good air conditioning system. My only fault with this room was the size of it and I would expect a Deluxe room in a 5 star hilton hotel to have a  separate seating area and this would have been greatly appreciated as I do not like sitting on hotel beds whilst in my room.
