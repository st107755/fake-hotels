Stayed here for two nights. It's on a separate island, which is also picturesque with a few cathedrals to look at. Away from the busy summer tourist congested main island it was quiet and serene yet had an amazing view of Venice. With a frequent shuttle service going to and fro from there ( ten to fifteen minutes ride) there wasn't any worry about missing out on anything. It cost us about 9€ for the duration of our stay and was definitely worth it.   Tried one of the restaurants ( bacaromi ) and was so glad to have done so. Great food. And the visit to the spa for a massage was also amazing.
