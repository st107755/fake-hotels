A great hotel, we were very happy with our choice to book here when we arrived. A much quieter area to stay but with very easy access to the tourist busy areas over the water. Exactly what we wanted! The building is quite something, very striking, with nice touches inside of photos of its history. Skyline bar is definitely worth a visit - it’s pricey but good for both drinks and food. My only note to the hotel would be to add blankets on seats for the evening - we ended up leaving because I was freezing, moving to the more standard bar downstairs. Our room was smaller than expected but comfortable. Only downside was the view, into another building, but we totally understand not everyone gets Venice views!
