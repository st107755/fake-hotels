As a seasoned traveller, I tend to avoid hotels if at all possible and rent flats/apartments etc. Not only is it usually significantly cheaper, you get a lot more bang for your buck and no calls at the door of "Housekeeping!" when you least want them. So I admit some possibility of negative bias in this review in advance.  The main reason we stayed at this hotel was for a week long conference. We had been to Venice before but had not stayed on the Guidecca which is across a stretch of water from the main cluster of Venice's sestiere or quarters. The hotel seemed a sensible choice rather than crossing backwards and forwards on the Alilaguna, especially as the conference had a special room price that was very good.
