A lovely hotel.   We decided on the private taxi transfer from the airport which was arranged by the concierge and they were there waiting even though we first off the plane and straight through as we only had hand luggage. The journey was about 30 minutes long but unfortunately it was dark. We decided on paying the extra to get us to the hotel quickly as it was a Friday night and we didn’t fancy hanging around for a shared taxi or the water bus. This did give us time in the Executive lounge which we would otherwise have missed. Check-in was efficient and we were told that as a diamond member we had been upgraded. And what an upgrade. Separate bedroom, lounge, dining room, kitchen, bathroom and second toilet! Massive and very elegant, as is the rest of the hotel..
