Travelled here on the 18th of MAY, 2012.  Had an overnite stay, before me moved to another cruise ship provided hotel.  Funny thing is many of the people on our cruise-Regent Seven Seas Mariner were being put up at this hotel.  We stayed here because the room was reasonable by Venice standards ($480 euro w/brkfst).  We took a water taxi thru Viator to the hotel--great way to get there and very reasonable as well  $78american for two for the 45 min ride across the lagoon from the airport docks.  Taxi dropped us off at hotel's private dock and we were met by bellman who took and marked all bags.  We did have to wait on check in as it seemed they were short handed or did not appreciate a crush at the time we checked in about 1pm.
