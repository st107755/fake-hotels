Stayed for 3 nights in October, 2015 and this hotel was the highlight of our 2 week Italy vacation.  As a Diamond member, I am partial to Hilton properties and we paid for our stay using Hilton Honors points.  That said, I am still critical of service and amenities even though the stay only costs me the room taxes. Received an upgrade to a King Executive room, with the sitting/living room area and separate bedroom on a top floor.  While the room did not have a city view, I was more than pleased because let's face it...if you go to Venice only to sit in your room and look out the window, save your money and go someplace else. The bathroom was a great size with separate bathtub and shower and away from the bedroom, which was a nice touch logistically. The bed was comfortable.
