We stayed at the Hilton Stucky for 3 days and let's just say... it's HUGE. The layout is also a little confusing but you learn to find your way around. We stayed in the Executive Room with the water view which was really beautiful. The sky pool is amazing with wonderful views of Venice. The only tricky part about getting to this hotel is the transportation. As most of you know, the Hilton is on it's own little "island" and you can only get there with a water taxi. Coming in late at night we went straight to the water taxi counter in Venice airport and it was about 120 Euros for both my husband and I which is pretty price.. especially since we weren't seeing anything on the way there. The taxi ride takes about 30 minutes but it was worth the wait.
