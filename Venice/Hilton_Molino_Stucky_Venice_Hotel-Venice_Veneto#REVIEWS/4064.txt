Was a little confusing to get to at first, but if you arrive by train, you will need to get the #4.1 or #2 actv water bus, costs 7 euro per person, ride is good for 60 min and 1 article of luggage or you can buy a 24, 48, 72 hr or more unlimited pass depending on number of days that you are there. Hilton Molino Stucky is no longer a stop on the 4.1 or 2, so you will need to get off at Palanca Guidecca, and walk back to the hotel. This walk will include crossing 2 bridges, which will require you to carry your luggage so be prepared.   Executive lounge is on floor 6, open all hours of the day, has water, soda, nuts, little tea sandwiches, at approx 1730 adds beer, wine, and hot snacks.
