Stayed for 5 days at this delightful hotel and had a first class experience.  The executive lounge, restaurant, pool deck upstairs, spa, were great.  The staff was delightful - welcoming, friendly, and wanting your stay to be the best it can be.  Transportation was a snap, took a water taxi to San Marco.    We had a problem with our mattress when we checked in, it appears the hotel room cleaning team overlooked a large spill on the side of the mattress on our bed.  It was gross.  The hotel sent someone up immediately who was clearly horrified.  They went above and beyond to move us to a new room quickly and smoothly. The hotel manager called and apologized and sent us a large fruit basket.
