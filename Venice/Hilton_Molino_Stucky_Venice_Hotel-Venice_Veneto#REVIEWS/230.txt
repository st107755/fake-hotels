We stayed here one night pre cruise, the accommodation being arranged by the cruise company. First the good points. The rooms are a decent size and spotlessly clean. The bathroom was very good with nice toiletries. Breakfast was good, despite being very busy. The food choice at breakfast was superb, and the quality excellent. The service for hot drinks wasn't great, but the place was heaving and the staff were working hard.   Now the bad. The hotel is so big and not purpose built, so finding your room can take some time, several sets of lifts in different places.  Our room faced a brick wall and we couldn't see the sky.  We had to eat in the hotel as it rained heavily when we wanted to go out, we chose the lobby bar.
