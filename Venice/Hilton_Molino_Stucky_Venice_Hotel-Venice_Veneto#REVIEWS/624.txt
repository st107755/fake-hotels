I stayed for night with my wife. The hotel is built in a converted warehouse. The decor is lovely and the staff very helpful. Don’t let the island location put you off this is a real plus point!  I couple of tips if you chose to stay.  1. We don’t need to use the hotel boat, the ACTV ferries stop at Palanca , we had a 48 hour ferry pass so used this to go direct from Palanca. The hotel did not charge us for their ferry no issue.  2. There is a Coop supermarket on the island, ( it’s a street or two back from Palanca ferry stop, not quite where google map says) it charges normal prices and all the islanders shop there, we got a load of supplies there, save paying the crazy Hotel prices.  3. The restaurants near the hotel are good value with good food.
