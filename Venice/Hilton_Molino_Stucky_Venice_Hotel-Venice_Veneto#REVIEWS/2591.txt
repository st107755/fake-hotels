We stayed here for a couple of nights at the end of a cruise. The hotel has most of the facilities which one would expect from a five star hotel. Our room was spacious and well appointed, and the rooftop pool was magnificent with superb views of Venice and the lagoon. However, some of the public rooms on the ground floor were rather dark and gloomy.  The reception staff were friendly and helpful, but we were not entirely pleased with the attitude of the restaurant staff. One morning when we came down for breakfast, we were told curtly by one of the waiters that the dining area was full, and we would have to go somewhere else, without being given any directions or assistance. That evening, we were sitting in the bar area, having a drink with some friends.
