While I originally planned to stay in a 15th C. gothic palazzo/hotel on the Grand Canal, I could not turn down the opportunity to use points for a 4 night free stay at the Hilton.  I hadn't even realized there was a Hilton in Venice.  Apart from it being free, we were absolutely delighted with the hotel and wouldn't stay anywhere else next time.  It's fun and convenient to take the hotel boat over to the Zattere or San Marco, so don't be put off by the fact the hotel is on the Giudecca and somewhat removed from the city center - it's actually an advantage.  One criticism I do have, however, is about the executive lounge.  As a Hilton Diamond member we had access to the lounge for breakfast and evening drinks and snacks.
