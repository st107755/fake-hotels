This is the kind of place an outfit like Royal Viking Cruises will book you in Venice (yes, they have a tour desk in the lobby).  It caters to high end package tourism, and does a good job at it.  But should independent travelers stay here?  If, like me, you have high status in the HHonors program and can use your loyalty points, you should, as they throw in free breakfast (decent but not great buffet, absurdly priced at 30 euros) and a nice executive lounge in the lobby.   But if you don't have status and won't get a bunch of free perks, I'm not sure.  The location is good, but not great.  It's on the island of Giudecca, which is a few minutes ferry ride from what most people would consider "Venice".
