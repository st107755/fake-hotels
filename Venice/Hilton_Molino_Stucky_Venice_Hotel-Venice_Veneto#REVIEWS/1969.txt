We were extremely happy with our stay at the Hilton.  We were uncertain whether to stay at this hotel due to the fact that it was not in the center or close enough to main sites.  Staying at the Hilton Molino was the best choice we could have made. The city center is overcrowded (at least at this time of year) and we were very happy to take our shuttle back to our lovely hotel where we could relax and look at the views from across the water.  It is more peaceful there and the property is lovely. The hotel has a shuttle boat service that takes you to three separate stops. It takes approximately 15 minutes to get to Piazza San Marco. The shuttles run continuously.  It is actually a pleasant boat ride and it does not feel as if it is a burden to take the boat.
