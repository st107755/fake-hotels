It only took 45 minutes from airport landing to find ourselves standing in the hotel reception; the staff were courteous and eager to assist. The conversion from a flour mill was superb. The water bus to the main area was regular....and free! We ate in the hotel and the food was excellent with more staff than normal at breakfast sittings. We started in a room on the 7th floor with a view of Venice, but a cautionary note is that the rooftop bar has a self indulgent DJ who plays the music too loud. However, the hotel compromised by relocating us to a suite for our final night.  Both accomodation's were excellent. Room cleaning was prompt and well done. Never seen a hotel with so many smiling and genuinely friendly staff.
