We stayed a couple of days at this hotel after a long cruise trip and it was a perfect choice. Good place to rest and relax but at the same time close to where the action is in Venice. The Hotel provides an efficient scheduled  free shuttle boat to the famous San Marco Square. The Hotel is modern, clean and very big, it is not easy to be familiar with the several buildings that conform this Hilton Hotel and there are not enough directions/signals  to find the rooms and facilities. The room was small but fine,   looking the great channel so it was great to see all boats and cruises passing by.  The Executive Lounge serves a basic breakfast, but in my opinion it is small for the size of the hotel. The coffee machine was out of order for two days.
