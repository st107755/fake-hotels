Normally we would get a taxi from the airport but in Venice it had to be a combination of Bus/Taxi, walk and a boat. River taxi from the airport was expensive so we tried the blue line boat. Journey took nearly 1 hour 40 mins but it took us direct to a stop outside the Hotel plus it was only 15 euro. The Hotel is excellent, very modern but built within an old warehouse type building. We got a nice upgrade to an Executive Suite, the room was really nice with exposed beams and metal work. The living room area had a small kitchenette, but we only used the kettle for cup of team. The executive lounge had "happy hour" between 5 and 7 with free beer/soft drinks and wine (no spirits). The food on offer was the same both nights we stayed.
