My husband and I stayed here for 2 night in May 2009 prior to boarding a Mediterranean cruise.  We both thoroughly enjoyed this hotel for a few reasons (1) the breakfast was very good with both hot and cold choices as well as sparkling wine, (2) our room overlooked the Guidecca Canal which was beautiful and highly recommended location, (3)  transportation to/from Saint Mark's was free, and (4) the room was large by European standards with a huge bathroom (even by US hotel standards).    The hotel itself is either relatively recently remodeled or new so everything was in pristine condition.   The mattress in the room was very comfortable and the staff at the hotel was quite accommodating.
