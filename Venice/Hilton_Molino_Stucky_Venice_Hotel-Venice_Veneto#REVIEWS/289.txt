Many positives here.  Stayed for three nights pre-cruise arranged through Celebrity.  My biggest complaint is that even though we paid for the room, because it was through Celebrity we were not recognized as Hilton Honors members.  While other reviewers mentioned perks we got none.  Oh well, a nice room but little issues.....hair dryer didn’t work, no washcloths, phantom $300 charge..... To their credit all were taken care of when we pointed them out.  The water shuttle is great and plenty of nearby places to eat.  Felt the food and drinks were very high priced, even by Venice standards.  Very comfortable bed but we had no view and very normal sized room.
