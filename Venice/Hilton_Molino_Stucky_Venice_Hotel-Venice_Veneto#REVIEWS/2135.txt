I checked into the hotel on 30th May 2016 and checked out today, 1st June 2016.  I had a lovely time here!  The hotel is located in Stuckey, a 10-15 minutes boat ride from San Marco (the Centre). The hotel arranged a shuttle boat service to and fro San Marco and the services were very very prompt. It was therefore very easy to get around.  We had no problems with this at all. When we arrived at the hotel, however, we had to take a taxi from the Train Station to the Hotel. This was because we did not know how to get around and it was our first time in Venice.  Immediately we checked in, however, getting around was easy, thanks to the efficient and properly run hotel shuttle service provided to us.   The hotel room was very nice and very clean.  The staff were friendly.
