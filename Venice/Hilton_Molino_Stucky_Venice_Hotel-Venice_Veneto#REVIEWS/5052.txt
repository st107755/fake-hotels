We stayed for three nights at the Hilton Molino at the beginning of July.  We had a lovely room with marble bathroom (bath and shower) and a side view of the canal and St Marks in the Arsenale building.  The room was spotlessly clean on arrival and each day all the toiletries / hospitality tray were re-stocked. One thing worth a mention is the fact that although this is the biggest hotel in Venice, the corridors were extremely quiet with no noise from banging room doors or lifts - a real bonus if you are a light sleeper. The hotel is, as many other reviewers have stated, on the island of Giudecca which is very quiet with just a few small restaurants on the waterfront. There is also a small co-op supermarket about a ten minute walk from the hotel.
