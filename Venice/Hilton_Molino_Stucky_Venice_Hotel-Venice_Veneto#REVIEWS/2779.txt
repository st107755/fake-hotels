We got the blue line boat to the hotel from the airport which took about 90 minutes. Check in was a bit slow with a wait. The room was smart with the usual Hilton decor, ask for a cue of Venice. Air conditioning was effective which was essential as it was hot. The roof top pool allowed unrivalled vues of the city although waiting for a chair to become free could be frustrating. The Skyline bar was also beautiful for sunset cocktails which were pricey but delicious. The boat to St Marco was reliable and there were local restaurants available including Harry's Dulce a spinoff of Harry's Bar. Breakfast was pleasant and comprehensive. Checkout was also slow as there seemed to be half a cruise ship checking in and extra counters were not opened.
