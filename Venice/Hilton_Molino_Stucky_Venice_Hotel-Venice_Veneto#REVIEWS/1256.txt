When booking, I had concerns about staying on Giudecca rather than San Marco but it was the right decision!  We stayed for 2 nights and had rooms with a view of the canal.  The hotel is beautiful and the history of the building is very interesting.  The shuttle to the main island was comfortable and always on time.  The website says that the shuttle is complimentary but they actually charged us 6.50 Euro per day for it, so it is not free but still a great deal.    We took a vaporetto from the train station to the hotel and the walk was easy with one suitcase in tow.  Two small bridges to climb.  We had the hotel book us a water taxi upon departure and were able to charge it to the room.  The breakfast buffet was extensive and delicious.  Service was good.
