I stayed at the hotel on a weekend break in January. The hotel is located on giudecca island which is a 15 minute boat ride from the heart of venice. The hotel put on a regular, complimentary shuttle service which worked very well. It actually turned out to be quite pleasant not staying in the hustle and bustle (noise, smell, cramped rooms) that most Venetian hotels have as standard. The staff were very pleasant and welcoming.  The hotel itself is in a large renovated mill which is an impressive building in itself. The rooms are spacious with comfortable beds. Two gripes are that they don't offer towling robes (which I would expect of a hotel of this quality) and there is no mineral water in the rooms unless you fancy paying €8 for the mini bar.
