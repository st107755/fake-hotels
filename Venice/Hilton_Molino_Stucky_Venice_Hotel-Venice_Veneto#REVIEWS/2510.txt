This has to be the best Hilton I have stayed in and in my job I stay in many. The rooms are well appointed, clean and actually very cosy. Also, the curtains can be closed such that it is really nice and dark if that's how you like for sleep. The beds are comfy, neither to hard or soft. The main thing about this hotel is that Hilton have kept many if the original features from the buildings time as a flour mill. It is a fascinating building both outside and in.  The staff are very friendly and helpful.  If I'm being completely honest, the Skybar was a little disappointing as it didn't open until after dark so the view couldn't be seen, but I was luck enough to have a very similar view from my room so shouldn't complain.
