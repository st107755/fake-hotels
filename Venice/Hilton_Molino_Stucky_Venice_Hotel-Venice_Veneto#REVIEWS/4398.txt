Here's what no one tells anyone about Venice--it's not an easy transport.  And, if Hilton has one black eye it's that it does not provide airport or train station transportation in a place that doesn't allow cars.  The alternative is that many get sucked into a $100 ride from the airport or a $8 group bus ride to the confusing boat taxi's, another $15 to get to the station, a quarter mile walk with luggage over two bridges with no ramps.  (God help people with disabilities going to Venice.  This place is not friendly for those, who can't walk and lift baggage.)  At $400+ a night, the hotel should provide an alternative to the $80 boat ride they contract.  But, what about once you get there?
