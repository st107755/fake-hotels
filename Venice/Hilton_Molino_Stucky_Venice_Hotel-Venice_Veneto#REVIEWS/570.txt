My wife and I loved this hotel. It used to be an old factory that had been abandoned before Hilton restored it. The property is beautiful. It is located on the Giudecca island and looks across the Grand Canal at Venice and you can see the top of San Marcos Basilica. They offer a shuttle that will taxi you to the big island of Venice and back. It runs every 20 minutes in the morning and every 40 minutes in the afternoon/evening/night...until about midnight.   There is a pool and bar on the roof of the hotel that provides the most amazing views of Venice. The drinks are expensive, about 18 Euro each. You don’t have to order a drink to sit and take in the views, but it’s a nice place to drink one and absorb the beauty of Venice.   The room was very comfortable and clean.
