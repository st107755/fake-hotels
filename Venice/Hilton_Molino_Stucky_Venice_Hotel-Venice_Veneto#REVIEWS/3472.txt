We stumbled upon the Hilton Molino Stucky, when we decided to stay an extra night in Venice, and the hotel that we had booked was not available for the extra night.  We ended up going on Hotwire and finding the Hilton, a 5-Star Hotel on the island of Giudecca. We had a reservation for dinner back in the main part of Venice for that evening, but loved the calmness of Giudecca and decided to stay there and walk around. We went to the local grocery store, and took some amazing photos of Piazza San Marco, and the walkway along the canal between Giudecca and Dosoduro. The Gallery 795, the Fortuny Gallery and some laid back restaurants, inches from the edge of the canal all make it a great place to stay with full access to Venice.
