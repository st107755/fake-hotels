The hotel has a great location, across from the hustle and bustle of Venice and yet with the free Hilton boat (they actually charge you 3.50 EURO per person on check-out but its still a deal) its easy to get to Zaterre or St Marks Sq. The hotel is modern inside and when we visited it was always extremely busy, always long queues at the reception and concierge desks we assumed this was because it was high season and the hotel was 100% full, no excuse really as they had 4 spare spaces with PC's at the desk, they just needed more staff. Overall impression of public spaces was hectic and more like a transit area, a little thought given to some of the available space could improve things dramatically and make a calmer environment.
