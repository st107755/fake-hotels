We stayed at this place from the 11th to the 14th ofJuly 2012. After our arrival in Tronchetto Interparking Garage, we called the hotel and they sent us a private taxi boat, extra charge (60 €) but it was very helpful with our heavy luggage. The staff were professional and  spoke well English. We took the ''regular deluxe room'' which was spacious with an enormous and luxurious bathroom. The breakfast buffet was also impressive, everything for everyone, from American to continental and it was very good and there was also Asian food. The breakfast staff were very welcoming and offered a good service. The shuttle boat service is totally useful and free.
