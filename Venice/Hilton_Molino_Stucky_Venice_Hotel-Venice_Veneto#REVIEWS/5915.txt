My fiancee and I had a wonderful weekend stay at this hotel. It is located on a separate island to the St Marks area but the hotel taxi service is frequent & efficient. The hotel was very quiet and it seemed we had the place all to ourselves.  This recently opened hotel is certainly worthy of its rating - our room was immaculate and the offerings for breakfast were extensive and would cater for anyones taste. The skyline bar was also a pleasant way to round off the eveniing - unfortunately it was too cold to sit on its pool terrace during our stay, but the views were fantastic and perfect for a warmer evening.  The prices seemed to equate to other establishments in Venice (i.e nothing is cheap - but it wasn't too expensive - and you felt you were getting value for money).
