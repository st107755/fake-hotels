Xmas in Venice is always magical , combine this with a Luxury hotel like Molino Stucky Hilton and you just a have wonderful city break in middle of Winter in lovely romantic city like Venice.  Apart from being across the Venice channel , this hotel stands proudly on Giudecca island in complete isolation from the main city and there was a hotel boat shuttle that ran frequently between the hotel and Piazza San Marco Jetty. So when and where you needed to hop on and head to the Main city centre it was just a 15 mins boat ride away.  They also served Full english breakfast everyday while we were there and we made the most of the City and even headed into Padua for a day trip.
