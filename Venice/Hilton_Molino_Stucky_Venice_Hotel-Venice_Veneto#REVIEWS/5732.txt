Hallo everybody, I just thought I could share my experience with Molino Stucky. Well, It’s not that I have been at many five-star hotels, but naturally my expectations were very high, especially because I decided to spend half my salary on a weekend outing. When my girlfriend and I arrived at St Lucia railway station, we thought it would be best to take a water tram to St Mark’s, from where the hotel shuttle boat leaves, which was… a bad idea. It’s very difficult to find the location of the boat service around there. Perhaps it’s wise to follow the directions on the hotel website, or email the hotel for precise directions, and have the hotel phone number ready.
