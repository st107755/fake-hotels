My husband and I stated at Hilton Molino Stucky for 3 nights prior to a Mediterranean cruise. We were here as part of a group, but we arrived a few days early. This hotel was chosen because of its proximity to the cruise departure point, but we also realised it's easy to get around Venice with the ferry from wherever you are.   I want to thank Chiara and Gabriele for the help they gave me in obtaining a good rate for the extra couple of nights that we spent here, making it easy for us to choose to stay on at this hotel (rather than finding another hotel).   The room itself was one grade up from a standard room. It was average in size, looked out on a courtyard (because the river view rooms are reserved for higher room types).
