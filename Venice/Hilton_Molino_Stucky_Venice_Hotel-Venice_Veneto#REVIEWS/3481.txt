The positive is that this hotel is modern and well equipped for Venice. It is located across the grand canal from St Marks with a free shuttle so it's quiet. The chief concierge was excellent, couldn't have been more helpful. Also the bell staff were excellent.  This could really be a great hotel if they improved the overall customer service which is really patchy, sometimes great, sometimes very average.  The Aromi restaurant staff were fantastic. The bar and pool area staff were very ordinary.  The pool is well situated on the room but has too little area for the size of the hotel and the staff provide no assistance to guests whatsoever.  The hotel could make a simple improvement by getting the shuttle boat staff to simply greet guests when they board.
