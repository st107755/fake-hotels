We stayed for 4 days and loved it. It's a great location and the private hilton boat runs till 11.30ish at night and takes you to St Marks  ( and 1 other location eves ). There are three bars, one for most tastes. The skyline pool bar is a great place to see night time venice and has a great atmosphere. A sandwich up there is 16 to 20 euros n the average alcoholic drinks 10 euros, but worth it for the view n complementary snacks. We didn't use the other bars as we loved skyline. There are lots of great places to eat on giudecca island with a view across to Venice.
