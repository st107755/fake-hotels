We took a private water taxi to the hotel from airport which took 30mins.   We had a free 3 course meal on the 2nd night as part of our voyage prive package, food was nice but drinks are completely overpriced! especially the wine! 2 bottles and 4 drinks at bar cost €146, the price of a dinner and dinner should cost!   The maid also walked into our room without knocking, we didn't have sign up, but thats not the point, you don't just walk in! Some days we did not receive more milk or teabags, if its empty we are clearly using it therefore it needs to be replenished!    Hotel shuttle bus was always on time and we always got on, we always got there 10mins before.   Breakfast good.
