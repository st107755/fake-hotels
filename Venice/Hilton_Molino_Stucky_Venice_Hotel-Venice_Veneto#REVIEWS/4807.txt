We stayed here using up some Hilton Reward points my boyfriend had clocked up while traveling for work and were really impressed. The staff were really helpful, even though we seemed to arrive while around 150 or so delegates of a conference were also trying to check out.   It's a lovely big old hotel in a beautiful building. Our room was lovely, with a wooden ceiling and chandelier. The bathroom was huge with free standing rainfall shower and a separate jacuzzi bath.   We had breakfast in the hotel which had a huge selection and Prosecco which was a nice touch!  My only slight reservation about returning is the location. It's not a massive problem, but you are off on an island away from the main areas which means you have to get a water taxi everywhere you go.
