Our stay at this hotel was almost perfect. It is a large hotel with an with an old world elegance to it. Check in was easy, and we were delighted when they upgraded us to a large suite with a full view of the waterfront. The room was a dream, and made it hard to leave it each day to go site seeing! The rooms were very clean, and the large, well equipped bathroom was a treat. We did not use the spa or any of the restaurants here, but enjoyed the breakfast buffet each morning that was included with the room. Breakfast had a nice selection of both hot and cold foods with a little something for almost any taste. You can even order a tasty cappuccino or espresso with your breakfast. Unfortunately, it does get very busy at times and the waitstaff does get stretched thin.
