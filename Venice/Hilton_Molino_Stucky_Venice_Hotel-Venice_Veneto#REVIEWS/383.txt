Wonderful , Meravigioso and in any other language you would like to use to express my feelings for this hotel. Getting to the hotel is easy from the airport and don’t take either an expensive private shuttle or the direct service which takes one and a half hours. Take the private bus 20 mins and 8 euros then from Plaza Roma square the water taxi 7.5 euros and it will take 30 minutes , much quicker and easy to do. On arrival at the hotel it’s a short walk over 2  small bridges then you arrive into a hotel steeped in history! Check in was easy and we were fortunate to try one of the new suites on the second floor which had just been completed. One small teething issue which was quickly sorted and the room was a haven of tranquility.
