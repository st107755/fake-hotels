Not got time to read all this? There’s a summary at the bottom!  As the title suggests, I have mixed feelings about this hotel. We spent three nights here on bright, but chilly, November weekend. As we landed reasonably early, we decided to take our time getting to the hotel from the airport and use the Alilaguna boat service, which at €15 per person (one way) didn’t seem too bad given that you got to see a lot of the outside of the city and are dropped off at the entrance to the hotel. However, after nearly 1 hour and 40 minutes on the boat I was ready to get off.  The Hilton building is a very impressive structure from the outside, arguably one of Hilton’s finest (and it looks great from the other side of the water as well).
