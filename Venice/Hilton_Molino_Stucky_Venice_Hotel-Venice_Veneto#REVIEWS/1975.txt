This hotel, a converted mill and pasta factory, was the base for friend's daughter's wedding.  The ceremony was over the water, at the municipal offices, but the hotel was the base for staying for wedding party and for the associated meals. The light buffet lunch and the wedding breakfast on the day itself were excellent, beautifully presented and very high quality.  Perfect setting for a brilliant day. The hotel is very American, as might be expected, but none the worse for that: roomy building, large bedroom, high quality all around. The breakfast had probably the widest range of options I have ever seen.  But note, the bacon is not typical American, but is clearly described as pancetta!
