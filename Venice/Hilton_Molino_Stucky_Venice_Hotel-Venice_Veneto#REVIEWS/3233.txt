We stayed here for a 'milestone' birthday, and were upgraded to a water front view. The room itself was a bit tatty (plug hanging off the wall, frayed lamp shades, and worryingly when I put the dressing gown on there was a box of matches for a different hotel in the pocket!), however, the room was clean and has a great outlook. We went in late December and it was minus temperatures, but the room was always warm and extra blankets were provided.   The wifi was very slow in the reception area, and upgrading was expensive.  The concierge was always available and very thorough, they recommended a great restaurant for our celebration. The bar at the top of the hotel has amazing views but we could only justify one cocktail as it was pricey, but very tasty!
