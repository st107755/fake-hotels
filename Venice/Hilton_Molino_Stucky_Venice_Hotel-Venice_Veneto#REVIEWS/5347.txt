Well on first impressions the hotel is lovely with fabulous features of the pasta factory it used to be.  However, it really was a little too far away from the main tourist areas of St Marks Square / Rialto Bridge. There was a shuttle service but it wasn't as regular as you would expect and it was not made clear that there is one stop before St Marks Square which led to a rather confusing first night trying to find a restaurant!!  It would have been a good idea if the hotel gave the shuttle times to you on arrival along with a city map as these were only discovered after asking the next day.
