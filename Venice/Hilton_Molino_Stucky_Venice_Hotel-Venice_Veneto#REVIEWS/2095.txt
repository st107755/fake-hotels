We stayed here on May 20, 2016 prior to taking a cruise. We highly recommend this hotel to others. The hotel is located on an island opposite Venice.  The staff were very nice and the woman who checked us in, Alessia Martire, was extremely gracious and friendly.  She gave us an upgrade so that we had a room overlooking the water.  She also gave us recommendations about restaurants, including a very good one on the island, as well as thoughts about what to visit in Venice.    Venice can be reached easily by a free boat from the hotel.  One can also easily get a boat to the cruise terminal.  Getting to the hotel relatively easily necessitated hiring a boat at the airport, which required a bit of a walk from the terminal.
