First impressions when we arrived at this hotel was how big and spacious it was compared to other hotels on the canal!   At first we checked into the newly renovated wing with modern style interiors. Although, the view was not as good so we instantly upgraded to the suite facing the canal, and what a great decision that was!   The hotel has everything you need, one of the best infinity pools with an incredible view of the canal. The hotel provides shuttle service to st marks square at a nominal charge of 8 Euro per person. They have a private dock which allows hotel shuttles and private water taxis only. For public transport you have to walk 2 blocks and cross 2 bridges to reach the hotel which was a bit inconvenient.
