This is a luxury hotel; it has all the amenities, including a spa, gym, restaurants, rooftop pool and bar, and more. I wish I had been in Venice longer to experience it all.  Most convenient of all was its shuttle bus, that left every half hour. It connected to the San Marco stop, close to the Doges Palace; very convenient.  However, the executive lounge was being renovated and the make-shift lounge was horribly cramped, understaffed and ran out of food.  A similar problem occurred at breakfast, though I don't know why. The staff were always run off their feet and making mistakes. Weird.  On our last day, as we were leaving, there was some strange communication issue and our cases ended up being put into storage instead of on the taxi that had come for us.
