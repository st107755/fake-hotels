Our travel to hotel from San Marco hotel was about 1 hour and 50 minutes by the Blue water taxi and cost 15 euros each. It was a handy way to get our bearings around the different islands and lagoons. A quicker but much more expensive option would have been a private speedboat taxi.   Room options - we had an Executive room which was comfortable with a lovely bathroom - however, it had a connecting door ( locked) to an adjoining room and the walls were paper thin and we could hear every conversation the couple in the next room were having and asked to move as it would be impossible to sleep and by 24.00 on our first night we were packing up our belongings to move to a 'quiet room'.
