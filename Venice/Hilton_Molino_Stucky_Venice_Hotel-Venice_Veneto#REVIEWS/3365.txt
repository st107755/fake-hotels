We spent three nights at the Hilton Molino Stucky in Venice. At our request, the hotel made all the arrangements for us to get to the hotel from the airport. A car met us at the airport and drove us to the water taxi, which then took us to the front door of the hotel.  We had a small room on the second floor with a waterfront view. It was really nice to all of the boats going by when we were in the room. The hotel has a boat shuttle that runs all day and into the late evening which is a huge cost saver for getting around. It has two different stops, and then goes back to the hotel. So getting around was very easy.  The staff at the hotel were all very friendly and helpful. The food at the restaurants and bar was also very good. We had no complaints.
