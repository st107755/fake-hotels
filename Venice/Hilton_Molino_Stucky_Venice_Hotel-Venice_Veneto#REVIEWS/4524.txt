We stayed at the Molino Stucky for four nights having obtained a good deal through the internet. Having initially wondered whether a hotel away from the main island of Venice was a good idea, we were so glad we had chosen this hotel. It is of a very good standard, large, airy, quiet and modern yet retaining some character having been created from a former historic mill. The complimentary shuttle across the short distance of water to the main island means that transport is available on a regular basis from early in the morning until after midnight, allowing guests to stay on a quiet island yet have easy access to the business and bustle of the main part of Venice. Public water buses are also available. The service was very good, friendly and unobtrusive.
