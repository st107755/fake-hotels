This was our second time back to The Molino in Venice.  It was as wonderful as we remembered.  A fantastic location with a very convenient free water taxi to the centre of Venice.  The swimming pool on the roof is magnificent with incredible views down the canal.  The rooftop bars are a must for pre-dinner cocktails, try the prosecco and mandarin juice, divine.  To sit on the sofa's in the evening sun sipping cocktails is about as good as it gets.  Magnificent breakfasts in beautifully restored surroundings.  If you are fortunate enough to be admitted to the executive lounge then the afternoon patisseries are a must!  I strongly advise catching a private water taxi from Venice airport and not the public vaperetto, for your departure and arrival.
