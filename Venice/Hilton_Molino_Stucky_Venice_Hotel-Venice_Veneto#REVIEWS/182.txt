Knowing Venice is expensive, we but the bullet and are glad we opted for an executive room with canal view.  Price included executive lounge and buffet breakfast.  Hilton offers their own limited boat taxi service to St. Marks (8.50€ pp per stay) which relieved the stress of waiting for correct taxi and having a seat.  We arrived by train, private taxi was 60€ per boat to hotel- great if you have large luggage.  Staff extremely friendly and helpful, being off of main island allowed for more relaxed and quiet settings. Room was spacious, and quite soundproof which windows closed ( canal gets a lot of trafffic, including huge cruise boats).
