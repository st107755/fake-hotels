We paid for room with view of Venice but when checking in were offered a room with a better view for 50 Euros - a bit cheeky so we refused. Our room was lovely, modern, comfortable, clean and a good view. We didn't use the restaurants but had drinks in the ground floor lounge bar and sky bar, service in both was excellent and prices as expected. View from the roof bar is fantastic. We had issues with the room safe and the thermostat which were both sorted as soon as we notified staff. For the safe, they were in our room dealing with it 5 mins after we spoke and had it dealt with 5 mins later. Had to ask housekeeping for extra tea, coffee etc and they left plenty. All staff in the hotel were brilliant, couldn't fault them.
