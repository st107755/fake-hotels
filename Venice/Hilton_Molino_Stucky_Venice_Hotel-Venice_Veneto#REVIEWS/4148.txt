My husband and I stayed here for 4 nights during out honeymoon and had a very pleasant stay. It felt rather luxurious after 2 weeks of travel. This was the only "American" hotel we stayed in during our Italy vacation and it really did feel like an American hotel: big rooms, big bathrooms, multiple restaurants and spa onsite, great linens. Also similar to American hotels: no free wifi in the rooms, only in the common area but we we're there to surf the internet anyhow... The location was a bit removed but there is a frequent free water taxi which was very convenient so location is no complaint. Front desk service was 5 stars. View from the rooftop bar was wonderful.
