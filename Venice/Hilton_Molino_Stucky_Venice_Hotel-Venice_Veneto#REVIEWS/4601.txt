This hotel has all the hallmarks of an American hotel chain in Europe.  It’s full service and has American-sized rooms. And it’s quite unique being across the canal requiring a ferry to get across to Venice proper. The building was an old mill, adding even more charm to the hotel. The service was excellent from both the front desk and Concierge. We are diamond members with Hilton and had access to the lounge which was in the lobby, separated by a glass wall. It had a light breakfast, treats throughout the day and free beer/wine happy hour at night. The rooms themselves were clean and neat, beds comfortable, and a very spacious bathroom with bidet. The TV had numerous channels, including many in English.
