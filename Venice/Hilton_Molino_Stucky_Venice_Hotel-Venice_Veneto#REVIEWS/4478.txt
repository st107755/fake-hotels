Had a week in April using my Hilton points. Very easy to get to from the airport as the Alilaguna Blue line stops right outside. I was upgraded to a room overlooking the Giudecca Canal so had fabulous views, even from the 4th floor. The room was very comfortable and spacious. An added bonus was the huge original(?) beam in the ceiling. A word of warning - staying on Giudecca is very different from staying more centrally. The hotel does provide a free shuttle boat to Zaterre and San Marco but these run to a timetable. So no spur of the moment walks to "the sights". That said, Giudecca itself is well worth exploring. Far quieter than the main islands and a lot more residential. Still lots of canals and old buildings to see.
