In a town of beautiful views and many choices, we decided to go for an American hotel, which we try not to do when travelling abroad. This hotel is absolutely wonderful...we got upgraded to a suite with a view of the canal and it was wonderful. Clean, (HARD) beds, lots of room, English TV, modern lofty- look to the inside lobby and halls etc. as it was built from a factory or some other pre-existing building. Staff was very nice and helpful, the executive lounge was nice and always stocked with food, Can't say enough about this place. The only drawback is that you aren't in the thick of things in the downtown area of Venice. You are across on another island away from it. But the Hilton shuttle was great and FREE. Brought us back and forth.
