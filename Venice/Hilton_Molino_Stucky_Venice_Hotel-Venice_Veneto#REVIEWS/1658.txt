In Depth Review from a Hilton HHonors Diamond Member.  My fiancée and I booked this as an Engagement treat, a brief 2 night stay to enjoy the magic of Venice… we expected to leave exhausted and ready for home. However it couldn’t have been further from the truth, we left feeling refreshed sad to be leaving and thoroughly spoiled as a direct result of this Hilton. So if you’re planning to stay in Venice and celebrate something special, look no further than the Hilton Molino Stucky it’s simply incredible.  We arrived by Alilaguna Water bus (Blue line) direct from Venice Airport VCE and it took approximately 1 hour and 40mins door to door. Tickets were bought in the Baggage hall via the card only ticket machine at a cost of only 27eur per person return.
