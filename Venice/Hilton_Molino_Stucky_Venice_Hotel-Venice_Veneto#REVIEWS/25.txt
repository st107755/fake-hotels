We booked a tower suite for our 6th visit here. This is normally way too expensive for us but given COVID it was a good deal. The room on the 6th floor was huge. We were welcomed with a box of biscuits and a bottle of good quality prosecco.  We would not stay in this room again; clearly it's designed for private parties and more guests and could easily sleep 4. Surprisingly unlike other Arsenale rooms there was no bath, just a walk in shower.  The rooftop pool reopened while we were there and with booking you could get an hour in the sun - and the normal refreshing mohitos   Breakfast was good but obviously not the normal buffet, we were served at a table. It was great to see Victoria a long serving staff member in the restaurant again.
