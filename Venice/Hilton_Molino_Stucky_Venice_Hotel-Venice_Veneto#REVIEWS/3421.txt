This hotel is what to be expected from a Hilton. The room was big, it was comfortable, and clean.  The best part of this hotel is the roof-top pool. It had the most amazing view of Venice, and the pool itself was remarkable. There were plenty of lounge chairs for everyone who wanted one. The staff were very efficient - when someone left, it wasn't long until someone came and checked the chairs for left towels, cups, etc. I had accidentally left a bracelet on the roof, and when I had returned a half-hour later, it was already picked up by the staff and stowed safely away.   The free shuttle to the main island was useful, though it does get very busy in the morning and you may need to wait to get on it. The breakfast was good.
