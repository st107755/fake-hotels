Excellent facilities and very efficient staff. We were a little concerned about the hotel being set on a small island and whether this would affect our experience -  but with the combination of the free hotel shuttle bus and the public water taxi stopping right outside the hotel, we were always able to get to where we wanted to go readily. I guess the free shuttle bus may get very busy in the summer months as it does limit its passengers to 40 on any trip. As the island depends on water for transportation, always allow plenty of time if you have deadline to meet. It takes 1hr 40 mins to get from the hotel to the airport using the 'alilaguna' public water taxi. It is lovely to admire the surroundings as you wind your way though.
