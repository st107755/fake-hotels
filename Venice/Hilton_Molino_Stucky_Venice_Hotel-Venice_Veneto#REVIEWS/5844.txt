My wife and I have stayed in high class hotels good and bad across the world, but the Stucky Hilton took our breath away with the enormity of the project and the fabulous mix of old and new. We arrived late on Christmas Eve for a 3 night stay, and it was surreal to be just two of a very small number in such a large complex. Nothing was too much trouble for the staff on duty over Christmas, and our first floor room overlooking the Giudecca Channel was absolutely perfect. Had we been staying for longer than three nights the amount of hanging space in the surprisingly small wardrobe might have been an issue. The carpet in our room was also a little grubby, and again, it was a surprise that a hard floor material had not been used in construction instead of carpet.
