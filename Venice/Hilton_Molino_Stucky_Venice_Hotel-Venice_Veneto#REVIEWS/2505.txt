Room's hotel are great, the location is an advantage and a disadvantage as well.  Indeed you are far from the center activity (so calm) in the same time your are also far from the center activity ;-)  So if it's your first trip in Venice better choose an hotel near the St Marco place. As the vaporetto is really not necessary to visit Venice it's a good plan if you want that make Vaporetto in Venice for a cheap price (less than 10 euros/pers/stay). The only issue is when the pass at the winter schedule the shuttle are not enough and can be very full (so they refuse you).
