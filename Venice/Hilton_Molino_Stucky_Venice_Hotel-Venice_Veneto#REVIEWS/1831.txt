My boyfriend and I booked a long weekend here over Valentine's - it was absolutely fantastic. Ideally located, it's just over a canal from the main town of Venice so it's lovely and quiet. I ordered a surprise romantic package of champagne and strawberries to be waiting for us and it was beautifully arranged and chilled. I don't know how, but my boyfriend secretly arranged an upgraded room for us - the room was fabulous. The black out blinds meant we could have slept in every day, and the bed was very comfortable. The ceiling has open beams which added a nice old fashioned touch.  It's well worth getting a weekend pass for the river bus, it's so simple to get into Venice - but bring your walking shoes as it's very easy to get lost (but very fun!)
