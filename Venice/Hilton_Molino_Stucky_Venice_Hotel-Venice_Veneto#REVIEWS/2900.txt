This is a top class hotel and arguably has the best views of the city from the rooftop pool and bar. I brought my wife here for her 40th birthday a couple of weeks back and it's an ideal place to enjoy a big celebration.  We arrived at the hotel via a private water taxi I had arranged. Please don't bother with this - it's completely overpriced and with the hotel only 5-10 minutes from Piazza Roma it's also pointless.  As for the hotel itself it isn't exactly cheap but the facilities are great. The rooftop pool is lovely but can get very crowded on sunny days. The rooftop bar with it's two terraces on either side of the hotel are a lovely place to end the evening with a few cocktails (some of the best I've tasted may I add !) and take in the stunning views.
