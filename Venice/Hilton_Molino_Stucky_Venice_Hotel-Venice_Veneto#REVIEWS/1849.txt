It’s a huge hotel but we loved it. Ideally positioned for getting to the cruise terminal, which is just a short trip on the water bus (8E/person if purchased from the machine in the lobby).  The waterbus from the Airport to the hotel takes a while (over an hour) but is fun if you are not in a hurry.    We found the hotel shuttle to St Marks very good - never had to queue,  it was free to use but there was a small charge added to the room (13.5E in total) for its use -  we were there two nights, there were three of us and we used the shuttle for 3 return trips, so it was excellent value.  The rooftop pool was great - it was a bit shallow but the views were brilliant.
