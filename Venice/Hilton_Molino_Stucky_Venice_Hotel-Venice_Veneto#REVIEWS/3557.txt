This is a stunning hotel, but if you're on a budget (especially a tight one) then this hotel is not for you. We paid around $450 a night for the deluxe sweet with a view. The room was gorgeous, with a decent view of the Canal, but the first thing I found when I went into the bathroom was huge toe-nail clippings on the floor. Yes, I know this doesn't happen everyday, but it's still pretty disgusting. When we complained they offered no compensation and it took an hour and a half for a maid to come clean it up. The restaurants (apart from the Nutella patio) were decently priced, depending on what your budget is, I guess.
