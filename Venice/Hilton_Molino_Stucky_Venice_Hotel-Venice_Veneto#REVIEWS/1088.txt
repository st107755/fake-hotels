Visited here for 3 nights at the end of August/beginning of September.  The hotel is stunning, they've spent a lot of money on it to make it look as good as it does.  There is a water taxi provided by the hotel for €6 per person for the whole stay which was great. I've read reviews where people think it should be more frequent which would be pointless, there's one pretty much every 30 mins which you can plan to and that's great to take you right to the centre.  I felt the staff were very hit and miss. You could get one person who couldn't do enough for you, and then next somebody that really didn't want to help you and would act as if they're doing you a favour.
