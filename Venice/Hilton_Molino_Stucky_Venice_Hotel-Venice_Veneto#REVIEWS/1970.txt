Executive room with a view across the canal to the city booked in the sale for a pretty  reasonable rate. The executive status gives you access to the executive lounge with free soft drinks, tea and coffee all day and free wine and beer 5pm to 7pm. The shuttle boat provides easy access to the city itself but the position of the hotel gets you away from hustle and bustle which can be pretty significant when the cruise ships are in. Staff were all friendly, polite and helpful. We had a late flight home and were given a courtesy room for the whole last day. The rooftop pool and skyline bar were excellent fun.
