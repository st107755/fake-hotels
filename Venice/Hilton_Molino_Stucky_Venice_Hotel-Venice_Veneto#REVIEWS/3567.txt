I took my partner to Venice for our 5 Year Anniversary. I booked the flight and hotel as a package with British Airways which breakfast included.   The hotel is an old Flour Mill factory on the Giudecca Island.  I know from previous reviews people have moaned about the location but what is there to moan about? It’s away from the madness the crowds, the hustle and bustle. It offers a free shuttle service and has all the modern amenities of a cosmopolitan hotel in a very traditional Venice!   Check In:  We arrived about 12:00 and our room was ready right away. The lady on reception was very friendly, wished us a Happy Anniversary and told us about the island and hotel.
