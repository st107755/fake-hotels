Although not resident of the hotel, we decided to cross the canal to sample breakfast there on Sunday morning.   This is clearly not something that people in Venice attempt often, based on the unwelcoming (but not hostile in any way) welcome we got.   We are asked to leave our bag at reception but could not do this without a hotel room number. Catch 22 (not being resident) resolved by taking our luggage into the breakfast dining room (this is Italy where rules, surely, are to be bent).   Food very nice - especially the cheese selection.   Room stuffy and uninspiring.   Paying also proved an experience: very resourceful maitre d’ redirected us to the front desk where we had a room created and closed so we can pay by credit card.   Worth 30 euros?
