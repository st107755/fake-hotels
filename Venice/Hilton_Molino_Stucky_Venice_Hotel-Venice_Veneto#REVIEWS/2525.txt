My wife and I had a 3 night stop over in Venice before returning home after our last cruise.  We got a really good bed & breakfast deal on the hotel which was idealy located for exploring the city. The the hotel itself was absolutely lovely, the room we had was spacious, well appointed with ample wardrobe space which we'd been warned wasn't that great but the room we had was fine. The breakfast was great, a perfect way to start the day which we made the most of. However if you plan to eat and drink in the hotel be sure to have a large bank balance, I don't mind paying a bit over the odds if I feel I'm getting value for money but I find 8 euros for a coffee and 18 euros for a gin & tonic quite frankly a rip off, for another 7 euros I could have bought a whole bottle of gin!
