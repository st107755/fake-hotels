This years New Year's Eve was in Venice and we booked the Hilton for our 4 day stay. The location is perfect as it is an easy 5 (Zattere) or 15 ( St. marks) minute boat ride to the center. The shuttle is free and frequent which makes it work flawless. Getting to the Hilton from the airport is either via the Blue Alilaguna line or the private water taxi (much faster and fun). However the room we had was a slight disappointment which is mainly related to the bath. The shower in the bath tub had no protection at all, so you had to be very careful not to shower the entire bath room. Even worse the BD sink was smelling very nasty which was spreading to the entire bathroom. Not sure wha this is, but it was awful.
