We stayed at the beautiful Molino Stucky for a long weekend at the end of August. The hotel was very busy but we were well looked after. The rooms were well appointed and comfortable. We weren’t lucky enough to get a room with a view as such but the room we did have was light and airy. The bathroom was great, with two sinks, large bath (not small US size) and a walk in shower. There is no iron in the room but when we requested one it was delivered promptly. The hotel itself is gorgeous and has lots of character. It was originally a flour mill and I like how you can still see many of the original details  The location is great – away from the hustle and bustle in the centre of Venice.
