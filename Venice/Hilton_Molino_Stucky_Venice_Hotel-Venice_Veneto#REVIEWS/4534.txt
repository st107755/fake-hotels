In Venice, even if you pay a premium to get to the luxury five stars, you might get very disappointed by many things, especially the size of your room, the sometimes rude service, the lack of recent refurbishing and well... the smell, especially during the summer months. By selecting Hilton Molino Stucky, you will know what to expect.  The Hilton offers a decent size room even if you select the basic category. The bathroom is clean, well maintained and of decent size with a proper glass door shower! No sticky shower curtains here! Beds are clean and comfortable. Most likely you will get a good night sleep here, away from the noise of Venice busier places.  The buffet selection for breakfast is good as one would expect in a Hilton. The service is efficient and friendly.
