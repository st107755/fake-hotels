The hotel's location is amazing, especially as you arrive in a boat and dock right in front of it.  However, our disappointment started with the room.  Getting to it, through a maze of corridors,  there was a horrible smell of damp which lingered in the corridors. Despite booking an executive room (we were hoping for a higher floor and a bit of a view) we ended up with a pretty regular room on the first floor (how is this "executive?") overlooking a wall - yes, a wall! Or rather, should I say, someone else's bedroom! To ensure privacy, we had to be careful that the curtains were always drawn. It is unfortunate that due to the layout of the hotel (as it's not just one building),  a lot of rooms would be facing the building opposite and therefore have no view.
