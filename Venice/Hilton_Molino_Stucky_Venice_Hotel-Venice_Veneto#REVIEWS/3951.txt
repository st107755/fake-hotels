We have been Hilton fans for about 20 years and we really love the consistency of the brand.  This hotel was exceptional.  Every detail from the exterior, hotel layout, restaurant options and shuttle service well exceeded our expectations.   We had a decent view but it is important to consider this hotel was once an historic flour mill and views were not likely a consideration at the time of that construction.  Some views may be better than others but we found the entire experience was unique.  Our stay was made even more exceptional by the interactions with Enrico in the bar and Lorena in the bar and restaurant.  Both Enrico and Lorena were super friendly to us and we loved sitting at the bar or dining in the restaurant when they were working there.
