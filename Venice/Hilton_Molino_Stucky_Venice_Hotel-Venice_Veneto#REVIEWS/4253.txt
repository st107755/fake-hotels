An excellent location, with a free shuttle that made getting around Venice to see all the attractions much easier. The hotel is well staffed, clean and has a nice pool area. The food in the hotel is average hotel food with no Italian charm and quite expensive. The inclusive breakfast was nice, a pleasant breakfast, but you could be anywhere in the world. There was not much telling you that you were dining in Italy or even that you were sleeping in Venice. It is a very nice hotel that is clean, well staffed, good location and has all that one needs for a pleasant stay. It just lacks charm and a certain uniqueness to feel like you are in one of the most  beautiful cities in the world.
