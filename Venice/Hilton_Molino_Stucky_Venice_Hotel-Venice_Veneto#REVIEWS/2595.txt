My wife and I stayed at the Hilton for one night prior to joining the Queen Victoria on a med cruise. We spent time prior to our trip sourcing the right hotel and the Hilton lived up to all expectations. Formally an old flour mill, the hotel is quirky in its design and very functional.  Opting to take a private water taxi from the airport, you arrive at the hotels private pier. Our bags were quickly unloaded and taken to our room - check in was quick and we were allocated a room overlooking the Grand Canel (fabulous). The room, in keeping with what we had heard, was not the biggest but was functional.  A cocktail (or two) up on the Skyroof Bar is a must .... We were blessed with perfect weather and the view you get looking back towards Venice is fantastic.
