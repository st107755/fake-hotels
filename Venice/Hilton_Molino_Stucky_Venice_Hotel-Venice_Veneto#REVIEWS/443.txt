I keep coming back here, and aside stunning surroundings, there’s no doubt the team at Stucky are the most friendly and helpful and simply the charming people on Earth!!  Whether it’s bellboys or exec lounge waiters, reception or porters you meet in corridors; everyone is just amazingly friendly without being intrusive and utterly graceful with whatever nonsense we, guests; come up with.
