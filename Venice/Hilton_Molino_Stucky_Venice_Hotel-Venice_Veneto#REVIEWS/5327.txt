We spent 3 nights in Hilton Molino Stucky in october 2009. It was a romantic week end with my husband. Due to his dimond hilton card we were upgreaded and got a room with a view to the channel. It was a really nice excperience, I would easily recomend this hotel to anyone. Please, note that the hotel is situated quiet far from the city center of Venice but the navette boat of the hotel (free of charge) provides the guests of the hotel to the easy access to the city centre. I really have nothing negative to tell about this hotel. It is a standard Hilton hotel resembling to some extend to all the others hiltons around Europe. The room was nice, spacious, well decorated with all the necessary facilities. High quality service.
