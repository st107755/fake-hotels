The Hilton had large rooms (we stayed in the family suite which was pricey but had more than enough room for 4 and privacy for Mom and Dad), nice facilities and a helpful staff. Breakfast buffet was reasonably priced and much needed for the kids (Italians aren't big on breakfasts). The gym was solid by European standards but just okay for a high end hotel (not a ton of weights but enough to get a work out). 8th floor bar was nice but the pool was ice cold (don't count on using it). Our biggest issue with the hotel was location. Its across the bay from the main part of town and a minimum of a 15 minute boat ride each way.
