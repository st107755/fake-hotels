This is by far my favorite hotel in Venice! The hotels in Venice are all small hotels but the Hilton is huge with a great location on a small island with a shuttle boat about every hour! Breakfast was really good with a wide variety of food to satisfy all different. It was honestly just a beautiful hotel and rooms were standard size for American hotels so big for European standards. I was a gold member at the time so I received breakfast.
