My wife and I stayed here for 3 nights to celebrate her b'day. It is the second time staying in venice but i was apprehensive about staying on the Giudecca as we were closer to the action in Santa Croce the first time - i pleased to say that my apprehension disappeared as soon as we checked in!  We had an executive room on the 6th floor which had an amazing view directly overlooking the venice and the canal - you could see everything from the cruise terminal to st marks square.  The view itself was worth every penny!  The room itself was spacious with a large bathroom and the shower was fabulous - no issues with water pressure there!  Having the exec room also meant that we could access the lounge.
