We stayed 3 nights at Hilton Venice. Celebrating our honeymoon we decided to go for the 100 EUR per night upgrade. The room was really nice and clean with a very comfy bed. The view was great as the room was facing the canal.  What we found very annoying was the lack of free wifi in the room. Hilton offers free use of internet in the lobby and bar areas, but in the room a ridiculous amount of EURs are charged for use of wifi. It should be mentioned that for Hilton HHonors members on a certain level, internet usage is free of charge in the room. Anyway, in 2014 free internet is no longer regarded as a luxury benefit in hotels. It is just something you take for granted, at least in Europe.
