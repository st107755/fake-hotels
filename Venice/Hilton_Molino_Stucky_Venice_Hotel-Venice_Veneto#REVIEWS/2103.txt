In real estate selling the 3 key words is location, location, location and that also applies to great hotels. This Hilton is a great hotel for many reasons and let's start with location. Located in an old factory building that has been completely remodeled to superb taste, this hotel sits on a island just across the bay from St. Mark's Square. The hotel is conveniently accessed by both the city boats and the hotel's own private boat. The unique location gives this hotel views that are worth a million! At night the view of the city is beyond any words that I could write. You just have to be there to truly appreciate the view. From the hotel's rooftop you can see all over the city of Venice. The staff is another reason why this hotel is great.
