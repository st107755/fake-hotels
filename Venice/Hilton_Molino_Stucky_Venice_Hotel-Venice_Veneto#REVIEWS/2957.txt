This was our second visit to this hotel, it is lovely, good sized comfortable rooms, large bathrooms.  Very good shuttle boat service over to Zatere and also San Marco. The last time we stayed was September 2012 and since that time standards in the breakfast room have definitely slipped; little things really but still annoying. My daughter was given a teapot with a 'saucer' as a lid, on two mornings out of three, hardly what you would expect from a 5* hotel. It was hunt the salt and pepper 'yourself' the same with orange marmalade and orange juice, you had to keep asking and all delivered eventually, in the staff's 'own' time. The biggest disappointment was that 'unlike' 2012 when there were bottles of Prosecco available, sadly no longer!
