At first I was a little leery of staying at a hotel on an island away from St. Marks, but saw that this hotel was a relatively new 5-star Hilton, and that made we want to try it. Went there in April with my wife and spent four nights in a King Executive with View. Very nice room, not huge, but certainly ample for 2 people. Bathroom was clean and new. But the view was amazing! We looked down at the water, and across to Zattere, which is another wonderful Venetian island. A few minor problems in the bathroom, though. No washcloths were provided so we had to call for some. The next day, same problem, had to call for them. Nothing major, but it wouldn't hurt to leave 2 or 3 washcloths in each room each day.
