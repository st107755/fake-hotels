My wife and I visited for a long weekend in the height of summer. Despite the heat the hotel was perfectly air conditioned throughout. All the staff were extremely helpful and friendly, and service was uniformally excellent. The hotel itself occupied a superb location on the island of Guidecca, just a 5 min ferry from Zaterre and 10-15 mins from San Marco. The hotel had it's own shuttle service, and dedicated stop on the Alilaguna. The location allowed for lovely views of Venice in the morning and evening, but some escape from the throngs after a day's site seeing. The hotel decor was top notch throughout. The roof top pool allow a vista of the Venice skyline without the hassale of queing for the Campanile.
