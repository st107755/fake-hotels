This is a beautiful hotel, situated right on the water front in a peaceful part of town. The hotel is clean and the rooms are spacious for a European city. Breakfast is also excellent with a large choice, including cooked and continental and prosecco and take away coffee.  We did have a couple of issues, the free shuttle is not really a shuttle, there are only 40 seats so some boats are too busy to get a space on and you have to wait 30 minutes between boats so advise you get to each timed boat 15mins early to be at the front of the queue. Also they have a break between 8:30pm-10:20pm, which would have been ok accept we were waiting in the queue for the 10:20pm boat to find it never showed up, apparently this can be a regular occurance.
