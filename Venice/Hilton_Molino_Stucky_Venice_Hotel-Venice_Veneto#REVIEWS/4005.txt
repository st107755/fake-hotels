It was our first visit to Venice and we were worried about its location not being on the main island where most tourists stay. Soon after we arrived, this worries vanished as we realised that the hotel was just across from San Marco and there is a complimentary shuttle boat every half hour. From S.Lucia train station, just take water bus ACTV no. 2 and alight at Palanca station, turn to right and after crossing two bridges, you will reach the hotel. To me, the hotel is considered one of the landmarks there, as it was converted from an abandoned flour mill factory previously with a long family business history dated back as long as 100 years ago (you can get a copy of the history brochure from the hotel concierge).
