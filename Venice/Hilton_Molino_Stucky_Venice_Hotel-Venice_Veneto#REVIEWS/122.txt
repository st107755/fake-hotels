The property itself is magnificent. The rooms are incredible with the wood detail. Stayed on HH points and was upgraded to the Executive Floor overlooking the canal from a corner room. Much larger room than expected with a large bathroom.   The property is clean, the front service desk and consigere incredibly helpfull and friendly. The skyline bar personnel were excellent as well.   However, the Rialto bar and grill in the lobby, the Executive Lounge personnel, the breakfast buffet personnel and the layout of the Executive Lounge detracted from what I experienced several years ago. Service was simply not acceptable in these locations. After experiencing incredible Hilton hotels in Singapore and Sydney, you'd think a Hilton in Venice would be beyond expectations. But no.
