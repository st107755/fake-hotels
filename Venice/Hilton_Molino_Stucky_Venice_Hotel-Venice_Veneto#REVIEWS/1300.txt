This was our first visit to Venice for my birthday.    We were a group of 4 ladies on a weekend trip from 25th - 30th May 2017.  We chose the Hilton Stucky hotel, as it was on the island of Guidecca and away from the hustle and bustle of Venice island and we were not let down at all.  This hotel is like an oasis - we so loved being able to come back and relax in the hotel bedrooms, rooftop bar, restaurant, pool bar and outdoor rooftop pool, during what was quite a hot weekend in late May.  Staff were very friendly at front desk and the Concierge helped us out when luggage didn't turn up for 3 of the ladies.  Breakfast staff were extremely helpful and on my birthday, presented me with a small cake with a candle - what a lovely touch I thought that was.
