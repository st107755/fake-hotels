Stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky in September 2013.  Overall an excellent stay, with usual Hilton standards, in a wonderful converted mill building.  Plus points included the rooftop terrace, where we had lunch with a stunning view of Venice; the shuttle boat to and from St Mark's Square which ran promptly and frequently through the day and evening; and the main restaurant, where we had a wonderful evening meal overlooking the water.  Only negatives were breakfast rooms, which were OK but had a slight canteen feel and the Executive Lounge, which could only seat a few people and had pretty restricted food choices.  Service was excellent and room quality was good - particularly as we had a view of the City.
