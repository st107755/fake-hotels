This was our first trip to Venice so, we had many questions especially on how to get around. Every person we asked was super helpful. The Hilton has a shuttle that runs throughout the day to St. Marks Square (about 15 mins) which was very convenient and there's a public water taxi station nearby as well. The Hilton is across the water from the main part of Venice which I found to be an advantage as it was quiet and not crowded. There are a few restaurants nearby so if you want a quiet evening you can just walk down the way for a good place to eat. Our room was beautiful with high ceilings, large bathroom and a view. The complimentary breakfast was decent with a nice selection of food. The rooftop bar is a must see when staying here.
