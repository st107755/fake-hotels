We stayed at this amazing hotel for two nights on the 12th/13th November and were blown away by the hotel, the staff, the facilities and the rest of Venice.   The building is beautiful, and we were upgraded to suite 218A which had stunning views of the city.  The water shuttle was brilliant, and the timetable truly idiot proof. The one day we missed it we instead caught the water-bus which was a fun experience in itself! The hotel was quiet for an uninterrupted sleep, breakfast was filling, and the only criticism I had was the cost for wifi although from other reviews I had expected this and have read that it is soon to become a free service.
