We arrived by water taxi to the Hilton’s private pier, where our heavy bags were taken care of by the porter.  Check in was quite quick. Our room had plenty of floor space and the luxurious feel you would expect from a Hilton. A light in the shower was not working and this was fixed promptly after a phone call. The Aircon was excellent;  the room was very quiet;  and the TV was large and modern with only a few English channels. We enjoyed a well-presented room service dinner, that arrived in good time. The bed was very comfortable although we found the pillows too soft. Breakfast selection was excellent and catered for many cultures.  We also had dinner in the Bacaromi Restaurant specializing in Venetian meals.  Note that there are nearby small cafes and restaurants.
