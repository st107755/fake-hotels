So this is an upmarket tourist hotel aimed at a broad spectrum of tourists from groups to families to couples. It is the biggest hotel in Venice and located on the island to the south of Venice Venice (so to speak). It is exactly what it seems to be; so should you choose to stay be aware of some things that are not readily apparent until you arrive.   Getting here by private taxi is probably worth it from the airport but not from the train. The taxi takes you right to the hotel but the water bus takes you to Palanca about 100m to the east (right) of the hotel. If you dont have much luggage or you are young and fit then that shouldn't be a problem.  The exterior of the hotel is not much to look at but the inside is very nice indeed.
