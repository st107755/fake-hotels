We spent 3 nights at this lovely hotel which overlooks the main island of Venice and is a short 10 minute boat trip across to the main island. The hotel is a fascinating conversion of an impressive industrial pasta mill which adds character, setting the hotel apart from the usual corporate feel of a large hotel.  On arrival we upgraded to a front view room - a bit pricey at €100 per night, plus the local tax, however, looking at Venice is a once in a lifetime experience and we felt it was just about worth what they were charging. The upgrade also entitled you to enter a separate coffee/snack area where you could have complimentary drinks throughout the day and evening, so the upgrade did save money on these items, which otherwise would have clocked up during the stay.
