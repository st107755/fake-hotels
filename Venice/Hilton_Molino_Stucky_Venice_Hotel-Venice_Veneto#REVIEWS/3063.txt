This is an excellent quality well run hotel. We had a junior executive room which was a good size with a fabulous bathroom. Super comfortable bed and luxurious bed linen. Breakfast was delicious and the staff were all very professional. The Skyline bar has the most stunning views across Venice and is a glorious spot for an early evening aperitif - at a price!  Minor niggles. No free wifi for the majority of guests. The exorbitant cost is not clearly advertised and in this day and age the majority of hotels offer free wifi. Think again Hilton.   The hotel is located off the beaten track. There is little to do on Giudecca island and whilst the shuttle boat seemed efficient it should perhaps be complementary for hotel guests?
