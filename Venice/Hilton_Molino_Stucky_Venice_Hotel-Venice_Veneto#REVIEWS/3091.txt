We stayed recently for three nights at the Molino Stucky hotel in Venice.  It was booked for us as we were working in Venice.  On arrival we were allocated room 424 (a deluxe room with a separate shower and bath).    The room had a view over the side canal and we could see the main islands of Venice if we peered to the left.  The room was very quiet as it is well away from the lifts and also had black-out blinds so a good nights sleep is ensured.  I was surprised that there were no bottles of water in the room or bathrobes and slippers, I normally expect to find these in a 5* hotel and I also had to call down for a hairdryer which was missing for whatever reason.
