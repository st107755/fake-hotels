We really loved the hotel.  It is very impressive.  The boat trip over to Venice, which takes no more than 10 minutes is very reliaible and runs every 20 minutes.   We were upgrated to the Executive Lounge which was very good and saved a fortune on prices for drinks in the bar, which are very high.  10e for a bottle of sparkling water and 48e for the cheapest bottle of wine.  Between 6.30 and 8.30pm there is free wine, beer and some spirits and a nice selection of canapes in the executive lounge and we really enjoyed our time in there. Our room was very comfortable and quite large. Not a great view though.   For a great view the roof top bar and terrace was lovely and there was a very good atmosphere in there in the evening. Breakfast was very nice too.
