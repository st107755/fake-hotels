We went on to Venice from Rome via the high speed train which was very enjoyable and for me much less hassle than travelling all the way back to the airport for an internal flight.  The Hotel is set in a wonderful location with fantastic views across Venice. All round this is an excellent hotel with very good facilities, excellent staff, very good and well equipped rooms, clean and a wonderful adapted building, but! On the downside for pleasure trips they do have a very busy corporate entertainment schedule as well and this can be detrimental to the pleasure trippers. For example, for our stay due to a very large global courier company having their annual jaunt there it meant that we could not use the rooftop bar for 2 nights out of a 3 night stay.
