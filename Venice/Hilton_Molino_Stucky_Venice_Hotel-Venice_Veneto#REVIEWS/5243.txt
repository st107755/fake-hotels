What a beautiful hotel, inside and outside, a real pleasure to stay here. We were staying for Valentine's night so really wanted the hotel to live up to our expectations as it looked stunning from the pictures I had seen online. The instructions for arrival by car to the hotel given on the Hilton website were accurate and very helpful. We arrived from Tronchetto parking and took the vaporetto to Zattere and then the free shuttle to the Hilton jetty. All ran smoothly. Upon arrival the concierge and FOH staff were professional and efficient. Our room was stunning, spacious and in trend with the style of Venice. Lovely, quality robes and slippers in the room which I think is a nice touch.
