This hotel is located in a beautiful old flour mill that has been interestingly modified as accommodation.  The bedrooms are very nicely equipped with high quality furniture and wonderful Murano chandeliers.  The staff are quietly efficient and friendly, especially in the dining rooms.  The only reason I am not giving this hotel an Excellent rating is because we were forced to change rooms late in the evening because of the fact that the people in the next room were smoking and it was seeping into our room via the interconnecting door.  Although I was assured that the hotel was 100% non smoking, having spoken to other guests, smoking in the rooms is still a problem to other guests who have interconnecting doors.
