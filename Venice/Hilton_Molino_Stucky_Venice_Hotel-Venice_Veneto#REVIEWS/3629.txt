For my wife's 70th birthday I booked a cruise from Istanbul to Venice and part of the cruise package was one night at the Hiltonn Molino Stucky Venice Hotel post cruise. As a surprise for my wife, friends of ours from Canada came to Venice and also booked into this hotel and consequently I booked an additional night at Aus $590 for the additional night which is the most I had ever paid for accommodation. The room was classed as a standard room but I was expecting a fabulous experience. On entering the room we were surprised at the smallish size and the minimal facilities it offered for the price. There were no bathrobes provided.  No slippers were provided. No complementary bottles of water were provided. The view out of the window was over the Nutella Café.
