We just got back from a 3 night familytrip at this enormous Giudecca Island property . We arived at 9.30 am and all three rooms were ready for check in . Rooms with marble bathrooms were all good size , unfortunately with no water views , but who cares when you're all day long up and around the Venice canals . Unfortunately for our 3 and 8 year old , there were no TV channels (Nickelodeon,Disney,Cartoon Network,...) available to them .The breakfast buffet featured everything imaginable plus made to order pancakes , omelets and so on. The rooftop pool with amazing views over the Venice skyline was fun but crowded at all times.
