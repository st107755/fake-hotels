We needed to book a hotel for one night post-cruise, and had a really hard time finding availability (who knew at the time that George Clooney was getting married in Venice this day).  We were lucky to find a room at the Hilton Molino Stucky and enjoyed our stay.   We paid extra to get our room prior to check-in time and we felt it was worth the extra money.  While the check-in line was horribly long, when we finally checked in it was nice to get settled in early and then head out to enjoy more of Venice.  The Hilton offered a free shuttle that dropped you off near the Doge’s Palace, and we found it to be very convenient to get back and forth from the Hilton.   After being on a cruise ship for a week, the room seemed gigantic.
