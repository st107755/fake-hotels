At first I was unsure that I wanted to leave the main part of Venice to take a ferry to get to our Hotel.  Waiting for anything is not my strong suite.  However, as soon as we arrived at the Hilton Molino Stucky I knew we were in the right place.  The view of the city from there is beautiful.  No worries about the shuttle, it runs constantly (even by my standards).  And it was a nice getaway from the hustle and bustle of the city.  The rooms were beautiful, the bathrooms large, and the beds were like falling into a cloud.  This was at the end of our trip so Something wonderful was much needed.  The variety of breakfast food surprised me with everything you could think of and more.
