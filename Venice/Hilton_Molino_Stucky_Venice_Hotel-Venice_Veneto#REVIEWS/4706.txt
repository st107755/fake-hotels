My husband and I stayed at this hotel for a 2 night break - from the moment we arrived we had the most fabulous service from all the staff. We enjoyed the breakfasts on both days, we opted for the executive lounge on the first morning, which had less choice I guess due to the numbers and space available, and the main restaurant on the second morning which was full of everything you could possibly want or need to start your day.
