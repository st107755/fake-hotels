We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky Venice from June 11 to June 17th.  We are Hilton Diamond members so were able to enjoy breakfast in the main dining room and the executive lounge the rest of the day. The hotel is located across from the main island of Venice with a free hotel boat running every 20 minutes.  In addition, the Hilton is a regular water bus stop for other destinations.  We were given a room with a beautiful view of Venice.  The room was beautiful and the bed gave a both a great night's sleep every night!  A Pleasure! The breakfast at the restaurant is full buffet with made to order eggs and pancakes.  The breakfast buffet spread was tremendous!   Afternoon drinks and snacks in the executive lounge was very nice.
