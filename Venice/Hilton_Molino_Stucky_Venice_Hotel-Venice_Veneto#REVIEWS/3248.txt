We stayed here prior to a cruise departing from San Basilio and very much enjoyed the stay.  I've been to Venice many times but this was first time on Giudecca and I really liked it.  Less busy and touristy than other parts of Venice and the views looking back toward the main part of Venice were beautiful, particularly at sunset.   There is very easy and relatively inexpensive access to the hotel from the airport using the Alilaguna service.  The boat was a very easy walk from the airport terminal even with luggage.  The crew helped load the luggage.  The trip took about 90 minutes and we enjoyed the sight seeing.  A taxi would have been faster but vastly more expensive.  The boat dropped us at the Hilton dock.  The same service connects to the main cruise terminal.
