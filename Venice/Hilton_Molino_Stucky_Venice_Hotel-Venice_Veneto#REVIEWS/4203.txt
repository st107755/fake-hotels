Booked 3 nights with my girlfriend in a "King Deluxe with view". Came out of the city on a private water taxi to see the impressive hotel but from there it was downhill. Be careful what you book from the many different room categories and also apparently who you book through - my booking was via Lastminute (5/5).   The room had a side view of the hotel where, if you twisted your neck you could see the water but were otherwise faced with a view of rubbish on waste ground. At the price being paid I went down to complain, saw the duty manager who had a very well prepared script of what the various room categories were, drew them on a map and refused to help at all. I suspect I was not the only person to have complained.
