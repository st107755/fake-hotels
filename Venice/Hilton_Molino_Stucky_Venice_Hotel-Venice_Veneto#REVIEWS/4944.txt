Actual enough said after tons of reviews and the average tripadvisor score is generally true: Molino Stucky is a very good Hotel, but definitely not top end lodging. And first of all it’s a tourist lock, wherefore the slightly out of the way location is not a serious problem for most guests. Once arrived on La Giudecca Island, the view over the Canale de Fusina compensates a potentially time consuming journey with a not invariably effective Shuttle boat, north- or eastbound ‘view’-room strictly provided. Room itself is snuggery, cozy and warm colored. More ceiling height than bathroom footage. Perfect bed and fittings, only a construction related small window disrupts this overall positive inside picture.
