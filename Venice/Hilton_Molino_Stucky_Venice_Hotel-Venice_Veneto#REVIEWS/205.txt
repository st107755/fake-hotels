The hotel is an easy commute from Marco Polo airport and whilst we used a Water Taxi on the nighttime inbound, we truly enjoyed the Vaporetto (water bus) ride for €15 each on the daytime return.  I can affirm that there are ‘cultural clashes’ at this hotel which is operated by Italian staff but predominantly filled with American origin residents, who will rarely comprehend the European pace of life nor we their need to shout and dress casually even in the evenings. An example being the Executive Lounge, a small area with views over the Giudecca Canal absolutely ‘rammed’ with transatlantic guests between 5pm and 7pm loading their plates high with hot food and kids staring at teabags, blocking access.
