As other reviewers have noted, the hotel is a stunning refurbishment of the old Stucky Mill, and many of the original features have been preserved. Being on the island of Giudecca, you have to get a boat to the main islands of Venice—the hotel runs two of these free per hour, but only one per hour runs to St Mark's Square; the other runs to Zattere in the borough of Dorsoduro (the other side of the Grand Canal from St. Mark's Square). Otherwise, you can take the waterbuses or a water taxi. My low score is based on the fact that the hotel staff were completely unhelpful with printing boarding passes for my flight home. Because the business center charges for all printing, one of the staff suggested I used the internet to get my boarding passes, and email them to him.
