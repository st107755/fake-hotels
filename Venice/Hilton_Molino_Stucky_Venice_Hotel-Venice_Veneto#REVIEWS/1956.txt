This hotel is a bit far from everything (on a separate island from Venice proper), but has a convenient water shuttle to San Marco.   The pool is small and crowded and the food and drinks are crazy-expensive, but the views are nice.  The check-in process was efficient and the woman at reception was lovely, giving us an upgrade for our HHonors status. The concierge was curt but helpful when we asked how to take the train to Florence.   Getting to our room required a long walk through a maze of old hallways. Our "executive" room was a little dated, but quite large.  Unfortunately, we soon realized our room (133 in ducale bldg) was infested with mosquitos.
