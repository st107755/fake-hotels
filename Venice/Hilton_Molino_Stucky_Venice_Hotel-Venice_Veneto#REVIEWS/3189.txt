Stayed here 20-22 Feb as a birthday treat for the wife. Wow what can i say, the hotel manager (Ilio) arranged an upgrade for us and we were met at the room with a really nice birthday cake fresh cream not a sponge :-)  The hotel itself offers a really efficient transfer from San Marco and Zattrre, the times on the timetable you can set your watch by. (and it's free)  We arrived at Marco Polo airport be prepared for a 10 minute walk to the pier for a water taxi.... and booked a shared transfer via "Share Venice" after speaking to other travellers i can confirm this was an excellent deal at €68.40 (£52) return, we shared with two other couples so with two stops the transfer time from the airport was around 35 mins.
