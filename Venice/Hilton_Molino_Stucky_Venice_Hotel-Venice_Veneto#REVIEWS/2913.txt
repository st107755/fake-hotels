we stayed in this hotel for one night to break up a two week stay in lake Garda. The hotel is fantastic, we stayed in an executive room with a view overlooking Venice. The room was very spacious with beamed ceilings, there was a stocked mini bar ( to pay for) and tea/coffee facility's, bathrobes and in the evening they came in and closed the curtains and put slippers and a sweet by our bed.  We had a meal in the restaurant which was delicious,the staff really can't do enough for you, when we left we were unsure about which boat to catch to get back to the car park, so they showed us the way and then gave us a voucher for money off the car park charge.  Staff top quality and extremely friendly.
