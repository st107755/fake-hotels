This is one of the nicest hilton's I have visited to date. First I'll start on transportation from the airport to the hotel, it seems to be a confusion for a lot of people. I highly recommend booking a private water taxi and going directly from the airport to the hotel. If you take the intended public water bus you will waste precious time in your day. After you walk out of baggage claim at Marco Polo you will see a stand selling water taxi tickets. Let me warn you it is expensive so be prepared, a one way ticket will cost you €120 for 4 people. The hotel is located on giudecca island, about a 15 minute boat ride to San Marco plaza.
