This hotel is in a very impressive building and has fantastic views over to Venice. It is nicely decorated however we did have a few issues with our room such as leak in bathroom ceiling and from the tap and a limescale encrusted shower head. This was a bit surprising that it hadn't been picked up on considering it is 5 star hotel. I haven't given a low rating however because I understand things happen and it is how it is dealt with that is important. I was directed to the manager who was very professional and arranged a new room for us pretty quickly. The next room we got was lovely and had no issues and a much better view out the front of the building so we were happy. The staff overall were very friendly and helpful.
