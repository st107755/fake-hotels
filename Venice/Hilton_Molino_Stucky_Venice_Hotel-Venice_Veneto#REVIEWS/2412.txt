I spent three nights at the Hilton for three nights this January and had what I can only describe as the most magical weekend. The hotel has fantastic facilities, the staff are extremely friendly and always available to help.  The shuttle boat that runs to Zaratte and San Marco are very quick and efficient although we had a couple of tight calls with timings!The hotel food and drinks are very expensive but as its the only amenity on Guidecca its understandable. The rooftop bar gives you a panoramic view of Venice and is a must for anyone staying here, stay away from the very expensive drink list. When on the main island we found places selling wine for 2.50 euros and free snacks so its worth saving your money!
