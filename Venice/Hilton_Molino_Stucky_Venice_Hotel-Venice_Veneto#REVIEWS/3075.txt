The hotel is fantastic, very modern.  As a diamond member, we were upgraded to the 4th floor corner suite.  We were only staying 1 night. All of the staff were wonderful and up to the usual high standard of all the Hiltons in Europe that I've stayed.  We had great views from suite, so the Skylight bar was not as stunning for us.  The island itself is residential, so all of the action is on the main island.  There's a Hilton shuttle, but there is a charge (not too much).  At San Marcos, the signage is small and we walked past it many times.  It's by the monument.  We took a water taxi to the airport, which cost 120Euros, but paid through the hotel so there were no haggling after the fact.  This has occurred on many previous trips to Venice.
