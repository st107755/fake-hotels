The hotel overall was OK.  I enjoyed being on the island of Giudecca, and the hotel's shuttle boat made access to everywhere else a breeze.  The rooms were adequate, but somewhat Hilton-like.  They seemed a bit generic and outdated.  Getting simple problems fixed, though, was bothersome (broken remote control was not replaced until I had to contact the manager; telephone in the room did not work, so calling the desk was impossible).  The breakfast there is a total gyp--THIRTY EURO.  That is simply outrageous and speaks to a level of food and experience that this hotel cannot provide.
