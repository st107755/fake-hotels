When I arrived at the train station in Venice at night, I had no idea what to expect.  GPS said to take the #2 ferry, and I wound up on the wrong one, but it got me to the hotel anyway(just a little longer trip).  The hotel is quite a sight.  A huge, historic, building located just a stones throw from the main areas of Venice.  It does require a $7.50 ferry ride each way, or the hotel has their own shuttle which you can use as many times as you like for $6 total!  Quite a bargain!  The hotel gives you the ferry schedule when you checkin.  The public ferry is a 10 minute walk, the hotel ferry is right outside the lobby.  The ferries will have you in the center of Venice in 10-15 minutes.
