We stayed here for 3 nights in the middle of the August tourist season--it was amazing! A little off the beaten path requiring a shuttle boat to get there, we enjoyed this unique hotel immensely! It was a former pasta factory that was converted into a Hilton, and it sprawls all over one side of the island of Guidecca. It was a maze to get around, but that was OK because it was just so nice. The service was excellent, and the Spa rooms were really big and quiet!!! The best part of the hotel was the rooftop pool, which was a real happening place on those sunny hot Venetian days. It was very difficult to get a lounge and required strategic stakeout because the hotel was full. But once you were settled in, the views and sunsets were amazing!
