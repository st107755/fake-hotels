Very enjoyable stay 1-3 February. First plus point is that you can get the water bus (blue line) direct from the airport to the hotels own landing stage. The journey takes time but you get to see a lot and really get a feel for the layout of the city. The Hotel itself is a very imposing building with good facilities. We upgraded to an Executive room which was well worth the additional cost as it included cooked breakfast in the Executive Lounge, snacks all day and a Happy Hour between 5-7pm with wine and beer available. Shuttle water bus to St Marco was efficient and regular - good value for money at 3.5 euro each for the stay. What real made our stay was the friendless of the staff. All were pleasant and professional. We really enjoyed talking to them.
