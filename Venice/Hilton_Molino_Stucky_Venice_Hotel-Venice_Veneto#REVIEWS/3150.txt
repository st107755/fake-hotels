My husband and I visited the Hilton Molino Stucky hotel in Venice in July 2012. From the moment we got to Venice we had trouble navigating the public transport water buses. We followed the guidance from the hotel to get to the correct jetty and waited for 3 hours for a hotel ferry to collect us and take us to the hotel but it never arrived. I had to use my UK mobile phone to call the concierge to ask how we could get there and they instructed us in broken English to get on a public bus as the last ferry had sailed. We were very disappointed and tired at this point - surely they were expecting us???  We finally got on a public bus to the other side of the water and then had to struggle with our cases to walk along in the dark up and down stairways to get to the hotel.
