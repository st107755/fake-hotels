 The Venice Hilton is a beautifull upgraded factory building . It is huge!! As diamond members we were upgraded to a Executive Suite with an amazing view across the waters to Venice.   We had access to Happy hour which included alcohol and canepes.  We decided to go to the main restaurant for breakfast which was well catered for all nationalities.   The water taxi ran regularly and was prompt. We also invested in a ticket for the Public water taxis which gave us the flexibility to hop on and off around Venice and see all the sights. We felt this was good value travelling the Grand Canal 3 times. We bought it at the kiosk at the bus station and it cost us £10 a day each.   Another tip is to get to Basilica San Marco early as there is a Que.
