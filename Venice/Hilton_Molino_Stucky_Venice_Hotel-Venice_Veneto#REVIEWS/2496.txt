This hotel is situated on the island across from plaza san marco which is the main square and the main land of Venice. The hotel is beautiful with large and well maintained rooms with a gorgeous view of the ocean. The hotel staff is very courteous and the on site restaurant and the breakfast are just incredible. The only issue is the 10 min ferry ride back to the main land. If you can plan your day around it its not much of a bother, but, easy access to the main land isnt there due to its location. Having said that their virtually free shuttle is very prompt and regular and doesnt bother you unless you're habituated to living in central downtown with quick access to everything.
