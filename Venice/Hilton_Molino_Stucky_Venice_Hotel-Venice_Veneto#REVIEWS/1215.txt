I booked this following a recommendation from a friend. My wife and I cannot walk around Venice all day and having somewhere so beautiful to return to and view the City from the comfort of the rooftop pool with Bar at hand was very helpful, indeed invaluable.  The boat which ferries you to and from St. Marks Sq is excellent and helped us get over to the action nice and early. 6 Euros each per stay is probably the best value of the whole trip!  However, I was more than disappointed with Breakfast service. This is where the Hotel stops being 5 star. The 1st morning when asked if we wanted Tea or Coffee, we asked for Tea which didn't arrive. Then when prompted it was made with cold water.
