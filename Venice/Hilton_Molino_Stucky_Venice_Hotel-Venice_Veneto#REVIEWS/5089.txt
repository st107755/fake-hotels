This hotel really is stunning, inside and out. Right from the moment you arrive at it's own jetty we were amazed and impressed. The hotels free shuttle boat is the best way to get to and from the hotel as it is literally on its own island, don't make the mistake we did on arriving and not having lunch before arriving at the hotel otherwise expect to pay €14 for a sandwich which you could get for €3 the other side of the water. We stayed in a deluxe King room which was beautiful. We were on the end which gave us a large window on two of the walls and the wooden beamed ceiling was a nice touch. As you would expect, it was all entirely spotless and very well equipped, except my one grumble is that you have to pay for wifi.
