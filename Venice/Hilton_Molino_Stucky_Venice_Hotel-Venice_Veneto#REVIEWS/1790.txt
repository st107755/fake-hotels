Obviously when looking at where to stay in Venice, one wants to stay in the heart of the city, where everything is on walking- or gondola- or water-taxi distance. Well there is of course a very large choice, but one never actually knows exactly how big the rooms,  and how good the beds are. Venice is after all an ancient city, so not all can be exactly as you like it. Our choice to stay in the Hilton Molino Stucky proved to be an excellent choice. You can get there by water-taxi directly from the airport, but that takes a long time as the taxi stops everywhere. We took a private taxi and in 40 minutes you are there. Service all the way was great. The rooms exactly how we liked it and superb view over the water.
