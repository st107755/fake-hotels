This hotel is one of those places that was so comfortable and was in such a great location (in my opinion), that I would stay again in a heartbeat.  It all depends on how you like to travel.  If you like to stay in the middle of all the action, this may not be for you.  However, if you like to stay off the beaten path and like to get a feel for how locals live, this is a great place to stay.  First:  location.  As others have noted, this hotel is across the lagoon from the main city of Venice.  We came in by train and had no problems taking a vaporetto - even with quite a few suitcases in tow.  The vaporetto stop is Palanca and it lets you off a short distance from the hotel.  So, again, no problems hauling the luggage.
