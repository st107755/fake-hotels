A friend and I stayed for 2 nights and the overall experience was a good one.  As a Gold level Hilton Honors member, I was upgraded to a two room suite, which was a nice size and had a beautiful and spacious bathroom; we were also able to check in early, which was helpful.  The wallpaper was peeling some at the seams and the room was above a restaurant and at first had a strong smell of garlic and for the remainder of our stay had a general stale food smell.  Not what I expect when paying $600+ a night, but not unbearable either. The Executive lounge was small, but we had no issue with seating availability and the food assortment and quality were decent.
