Well, I was slightly wary about this hotel after reading a few negative reviews. But my partner and I absolutely loved the hotel. Being slightly away from the hustle and bustle of venice suited us and taking a boat trip everyday added to the venice watery feel. The staff were ok, some where pleasant, others slightly rude and moody, but I was on holiday so ignored it. The rooftop pool was a great added extra, we went mid September so there was always a bed to lay on and plenty of room in the pool, however in the height if summer I can imagine it's quite packed. This is the same for the boat journey to the main island.  No other country knows how to queue, so be prepared for this.
