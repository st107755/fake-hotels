Second visit to this hotel. It has many plus points but one or two issues that made the stay not quite perfect although the hotel was good in recognising this hence I still give it five stars.   The location is fantastic on the island of Giudecca. The courtesy transport to and from the main tourist area of Venice is great and the small charge the hotel adds to the bill is perfectly reasonable given what it’s costs in fuel and staffing to run these boats.  Also note that a number of water buses including those to the airport also stop at the Hilton. For example a water bus from the Hilton to the airport cost €14 per person if you use Venice Link (great for tickets and excursions) otherwise it’s €15 which you can pick up from a sales rep within the hotel.
