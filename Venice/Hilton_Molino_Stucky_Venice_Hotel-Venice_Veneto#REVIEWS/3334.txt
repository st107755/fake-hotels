My sister and I decided to go over and spend a couple of days in Venice before we set out on our cruise to Greece and Turkey.  Being a Hilton Honors members Hilton properties are always my first choice and we were not disappointed by this property.  The hotel fits the old saying "looks can be deceiving".  From the outside it is very plain but once you step through the doors it has it's own elegant flair to it.  The room was large compared to other rooms I have stayed in in Europe.  Although we arrived in the morning our room was ready for us, the staff was very friendly and accommodating. The complimentary water taxi with the short ride over to St. Mark's square was an added bonus.
