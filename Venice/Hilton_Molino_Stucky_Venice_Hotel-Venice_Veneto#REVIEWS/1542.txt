What a fantastic hotel. Visited early February for three nights and have to say it's fantastic. First view was from the water taxi and it is quite an imposing building. Set on an island opposite the hub of Venise , means you can return to it to relax after the hustle and bustle of the city.  It is as beautiful inside as out. From the entrance to the rooms, a 5* hotel.  Breakfast is an ever ending supply of fruit,meats,cheese,healthy eating and cooked. The rooms are spacious with a huge bed and all the facilities of a top end hotel. We had a meal in the Bacaromi restaurant, wonderful food, on the first night and one in the Rialto bar , again food beautiful, the second evening. Sky bar is a must,although it could end up rather expensive if you stay too long !!!!.
