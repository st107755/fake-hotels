We stayed 3 nights here which were quite restful, although the rooms aren't exactly insulated very well so you can hear pretty well what is going on in the room next to you. The bed was VERY comfortable though, so much so that it made it difficult to actually go see the sites. We were lucky that we were there for a festival type weekend with fireworks and a gondola race. They have the shuttle buses going to the main areas quite often (Zittare and San Marco), although make sure you make it before the 1800 mark (small break in service, usually was when we were about to go out to dinner though). We did eat dinner at the Bacaromi Bar one night and the waiter was attentive and very nice, food was quite delicious.
