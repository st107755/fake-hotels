Turned out to be just as nice as we hoped. Old Venice is in dire need of repair and maintenance - but still that is of cource part of the charm. Very nice then to be able to enjoy both authentic Venice AND retract to modern and luxurious Hilton at the end of the day. Old Venice is also a tourist machine churning on and for the first time unprepared visitor it might be a bit too much and unpersonal. Very nice then to live at Hilton where service is unimpeccable and eveything works. A very nice two days was topped off by conciege-service to ease the next leg of our vacation. One thing to improve: they should have informed us that you can take almost ANY boat service across and then walk over the bridges to the hotel. No need to wait around for the Hilton shuttle.
