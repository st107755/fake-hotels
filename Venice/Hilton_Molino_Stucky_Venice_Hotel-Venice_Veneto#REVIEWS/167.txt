I am always dubious about the Hilton brand. In London where I live, they are often tired and overpriced, living off their central locations and really not much else. This Hilton was a pleasant surprise however! The building is an absolutely massive fort, so if you’re looking for boutique then this is not for you. However, that said, there are some excellent advantages for Hilton to have chosen this hotel outpost in an old flour mill and pasta making factory. For starters, the building has a great history. The view is totally sublime and up top you have Venice’s only roof top bar with uninterrupted views down towards Venice from Giudecca.
