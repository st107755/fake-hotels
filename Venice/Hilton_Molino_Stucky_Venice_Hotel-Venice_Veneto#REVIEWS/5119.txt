i have to say I am pretty happy with my stay at the Hilton Molino Stucky.  Overall, it was a good experience.  I wouldn't rate the hotel quite as high as it is though.    Probably the best feature of this hotel is the free water shuttle service that runs on a fixed schedule.  Getting across the channel in to the San Marco area was very simple and very convenient.    The room was very clean and the bed was Hilton comfortable.  The bathroom was very nice, aside from the typical European problem with not providing a barrier to keep shower water from spraying all over the bathroom.    The lobby lounge and bar was very comfortable and service was good.  However, if you are not HH gold or higher, you feel a little out of place and ignored in the lounge.
