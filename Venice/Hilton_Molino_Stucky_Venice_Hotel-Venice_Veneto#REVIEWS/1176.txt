After a bad experience elsewhere we transferred last minute to the Hilton as both my fiancé and I regularly stay in Hiltons with work so know it is a reliable brand. This hotel is in a great location out of the chaos the grand canal but equal distance between San Marco and the train station. Whilst they do offer a direct shuttle to San Marco we walked 5 mins along the canal and hopped on the water bus and were able to get anywhere very easily. The front desk staff kindly organized a deluxe room for us which had an amazing view looking down the canal and meant we could lie in bed and watch the boats coming and going and the cruise ships coming in and out.
