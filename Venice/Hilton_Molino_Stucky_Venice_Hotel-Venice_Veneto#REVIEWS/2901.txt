We four stayed only one night in the hotel but it was as everywhere. Big hotel, functioning as a big machine. But you know what you get.   The location is lovely but a bit isolated from the "city centre". That was seen in the moments when free water bus (Hoter's own service) was full and we could not get on the first one we wanted - not in the evening, not in the morning. So we had to wait another 20-30 minutes. When checking in the staff said waterbus costs 3,5 per person, but everyone else told it was complimentary. No one asked any money anyway but they charged us when we asked about it. I am sure we were the only ones paying.  We stayed in two rooms family suite. The size was good but rooms were outdated with dirty carpet, but great firm beds for adults.
