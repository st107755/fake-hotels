My family and I stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky hotel for 3 nights in early September. From first viewing the hotel from our water taxi to the last day, it was fabulous! The hotel sits on an island across from the Zattere area of Venice (down the canal from St. Marks Square). It offers incredible views of Venice across the water. Looking out our window, we were able to watch lots of boats and cruise ships go by. If possible, get a room overlooking the canal. Just beautiful. The service and concierge were excellent. They offered up excellent suggestions for restaurants, helped book water taxis, provided umbrellas when it rained. and were friendly throughout. Not knowing about the mosquitoes, we accidently left our room window open top enjoy the fresh air.
