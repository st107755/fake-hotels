I have stayed for four nights in this hotel while attending a medical conference in this town. The hotel is in a very beautiful location, just at the west side of Giudecca island, looking over the Giudecca canal. It is a wonderful former sugar factory made in red brick and iron dating from the beginning of the 20th century. The rooms are spacious and well decorated, unless we did not have a very nice view (a building site) but it was a very reduced rate so no complaints there. There was no noise and no bad smells at all. The shuttle boat is extremely puctual and brings you in no time to the main Venice islands. The staff was quite helpful, spoke good english (and some even good spanish) and was very polite.
