My husband and I spent a lovely three days/nights at the Molino Stucky using our Hilton Points I'd accumulated during work travel.  Here's some pro's and cons and a few tips:  Con's:  -Every time you want to get into the heart of Venice or go back to your hotel from the heart of Venice you have to take a water taxi.  You don't have to wait long as there are a lot of departure and return times, but every 15 minutes in Venice counts.  -If you are prone to sea sickness, or drank too much at dinner, the rides back and forth can be, well, a bid nauseating.  The lagoon, albeit amazingly beautiful, is a bit choppy.  -Pro or con depending on how you feel about it:  It's not within walking distance of much.
