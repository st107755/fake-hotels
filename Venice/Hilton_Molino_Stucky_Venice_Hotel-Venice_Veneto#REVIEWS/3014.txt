Huge superbly renovated old corn mill dominating the shoreline of the Guedecca canal on the south side of the city. A free private launch runs regularly to and from the centre of town ten minutes away. Airport water bus stops outside. Hotel service is friendly and rooms well-equipped. The big draw is the rooftop pool and terrace. This must be one of the finest views over the city you can enjoy, quite spectacular especially at sunset. Decent breakfasts. After the jostle and claustrophobia of downtown Venice it is very pleasant to return to this vast, airy and spacious building. One moan: dear Hilton bosses, it is the twenty first century and free fast wifi is what we guests expect in our rooms in return for your high prices.
