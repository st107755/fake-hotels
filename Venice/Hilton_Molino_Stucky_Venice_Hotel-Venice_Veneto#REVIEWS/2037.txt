I originally booked an apartment via an agent and after discovering the air conditioning was broken, we quickly pulled up stumps and checked into the Hilton.  Having stayed at other Hilton's throughout the world I did not expect to be "wowed" but the Hilton team achieved it this time around. Concierge Carlo was very helpful in organising activities, the restaurant team was really fun yet professional and the seafood was locally sourced and just great. We ate in the restaurant outside watching the sun set two nights and I couldn't have been happier. The wine flowed, laughs were had and it was a very relaxed experience both nights.  The room was a King Superior View, which obviously had a water view. The room was small but it didn't matter really.
