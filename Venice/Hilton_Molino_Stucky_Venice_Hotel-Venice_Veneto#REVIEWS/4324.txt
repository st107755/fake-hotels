Stayed here in May 2013 for 3 nights w/points.  We are Diamond members so we received free WiFi, free breakfast and access to the Exec Lounge which was nice.  There was a nice, large variety for breakfast.  Exec Lounge offerings were good too but not spectacular.  We also received free access to the spa and gym.  The gym was a bit on the small side, but sufficient.  The spa had a small steam room and nice jacuzzi along w/a relaxation room.    Our room was facing the canal so we had great views of Venice and lots of cruise ships would go by.  That was pretty neat.  The carpet in the rooms could use some deep cleaning but otherwise, it was fairly clean.
