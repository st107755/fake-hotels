Great location just off the main island and away from all the hustle and bustle of St Marks. Convenient boat transfer across the canals to the centre. Large hotel geared up mainly for groups and cruise passengers but still has a cozy feel to it thanks to being in a historic flower mill building. Rooms are a good size and have all the mod cons but not as luxurious as some of the other hotels in the city but perfectly adequate. Breakfast was a bit disappointing and the breakfast room has the charm of a waiting room at a bus station. Service is a bit slack and the decor could do with a bit of TLC and a general tidy up. This is where it felt most like a group hotel where the focus is on feeding large number quickly.
