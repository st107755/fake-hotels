After spending nearly a full month traveling around Sicily (which lacks fine hotels in the class to which the Hilton Molino Stucky belong), it was a delight to check-in to Hilton Molino Stucky in Venice.  I was immediately impressed at the spacious and elegant lobby, a designated concierge area, a bell station, and multiple bars and restaurants—all of which should be the norm at a five-star hotel, but hotels in Italy do not always deliver on such metrics.  The only critique I can offer about the lobby area is that the front desk is understaffed.  They need more employees to help with check-in during peak times, as the wait times can be excessive for a hotel that strives to be a luxury one.  The Molino Stucky staff is very friendly and helpful.
