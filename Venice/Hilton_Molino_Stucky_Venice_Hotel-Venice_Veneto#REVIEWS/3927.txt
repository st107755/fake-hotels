This Hilton on the Guidecca just across the Giudecca canal from Venice proper, a journey  which takes five minutes on the free shuttle.  The hotel was a originally warehouse and flour mill and amid the luxury retains a bit of an 'industrial' feel. The rooms are comfortable, the roof top pool and bar and lounge have great views over Venice and watching the cruise liners glide by is great fun.
