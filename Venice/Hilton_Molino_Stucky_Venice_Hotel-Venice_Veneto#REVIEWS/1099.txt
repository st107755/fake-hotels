This is an absolutely beautiful hotel removed from busy Venice center! We visited as a family of four in early August.  We loved this hotel and the wonderful renovations of an old foundry.  It is easily accessible via water taxi from the airport and had a very easy water shuttle service to the main area in Venice.    The hotel pool was a welcome break from the heat of the day.   One note that the pool is a bit smaller then pictures depict and did get very busy.  But again, even as busy as it was, it was great to cool off during the day and refresh ourselves!  I would have loved to give this hotel five star review but the hotel staff held me back.   When we arrived after an overnight flight our room was not available.  I completely understand that that sometimes happens.
