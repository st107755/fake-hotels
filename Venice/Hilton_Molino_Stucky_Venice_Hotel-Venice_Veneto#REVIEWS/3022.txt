We had a 4 day break at the Hilton Molino Stucky Venice. Great hotel, nicely converted from an old flour mill. We had wanted an upgrade to a suite or higher level room, but unfortunately it was a late booking for us, and the Hotel was pretty full. Room 222, exec room, nice enough, but the view from the windows was terrible. In fact no view at all. Fortunately the roof pool, and sky bars made up for this. If the weather is nice you will not want to move from the roof! Special mention to RICCARDO RANZATO, who emailed us before hand to check our needs on arrival, for arranging the wine and chocolate strawberries, and generally being there. Excellent man.
