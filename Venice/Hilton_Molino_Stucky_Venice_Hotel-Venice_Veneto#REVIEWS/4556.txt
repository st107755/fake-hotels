First it should be noted that this property is on Giudecca island across from Venice. No worries, they have excellent water taxi service shuttling you across the lagoon. Before I rate, I should note, Giudecca is worth exploring if you have a few hours. It is most famous for Michelangelo's voluntary exile to this island.  The Molino Stucky is a large property, tastefully done and kept up to standards. The rooms are very large and request a lagoon view. The windows open wide for spectacular views of Venice.   We had breakfast each day at the Hotel; it was a typical American breakfast, good but nothing over the top. The Hilton executive lounge is quite small. A good job was done keeping it stocked but do not expect to fill up here on food.
