We were concerned about the location of this Venice Hilton because it involved a boat ride to the main part of the city however those concerns were unfounded. It was so easy to take the free shuttle from the hotel to two main stops that put us exactly where we wanted to be for the day's itineraries. Everyone at the hotel was welcoming and friendly and though the location of the Executive Lounge left something to be desired, there was always lots of food. Breakfast was plentiful with an array of hot foods as well as a nice selection of meats, cheeses, fruits, cakes, croissants and more. There were nice sweets for afternoon tea or cappucino and plenty of evening appetizers. Wine and beer as well as soft drinks and Pellegrino was provided and plentiful.
