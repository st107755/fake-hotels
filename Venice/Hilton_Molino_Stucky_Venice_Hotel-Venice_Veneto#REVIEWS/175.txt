My husband and I stayed here during a quick trip to Venice. I was skeptical about the location on Giudecca and easily making it to the hotel from the airport. We contacted the hotel concierge by email prior to arrival, and they quickly responded with the options to get to the hotel. We opted for the Alilaguna water boat (blue line) which goes from the airport and stops right in front of the hotel. We arrived a little bit before checkin time, and we were delighted to be upgraded to an executive king room with water views of Venice due to our Hilton honors status. The room was decently large for European standards, but the bathroom/shower situation was a little small and disappointing.
