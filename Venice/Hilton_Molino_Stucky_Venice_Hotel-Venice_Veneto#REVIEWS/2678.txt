This was our 5th visit to Venice (and that's relevant when choosing the location of your hotel). We stayed for two nights ahead of a cruise. We had seen all the major sights on earlier visits but hadn't previously stayed on Giudecca island where the Molino Stucky is located. If you're hoping for some Venetian character - don't book this hotel; if you're looking for a large and modern hotel then this could be it. Whilst the hotel has been built within an old mill and has some history, quaint it ain't. If you'd like to stroll out of your hotel's front door and see the sights then this location isn't for you; but if you want some peace and quiet away from the bustle and crowds of St Marks Square etc., then the Molino Stucky will work.
