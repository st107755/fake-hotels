Recently visited Venice for the first time on vacation.  We chose to stay at the Hilton Molino Stucky mainly because we had some points for a free stay.  I did have some concerns about the location but thought it worth the risk.  What a great place.  Yes, it is on Giudecca which is separated from the two main islands but the hotel operates a free taxi across to Zattere and San Marco and there are frequent water buses so it really is no problem.  In fact we enjoyed retiring to the relatively quiet surroundings in the evening.  As Giudecca is mainly residential the local restaurants are cheaper and less touristy and, if you want to push the boat out, there is always Harry's Dolci just a 5 minute walk from the hotel.
