Transfer from the airport to the hotel was smooth. We purchased round trip alilaguna tickets, blue line, from the ticket counter right outside of baggage claim, 2 for €54.  There was a very short line for tickets and for entering the boat, but a lengthy walk after ticket purchasing. The Stucky was one of the last stops, so it took an hour or so to get here, but was time well spent looking at the various stops/people watching on the way. Upon arrival, hotel staff were wonderful, friendly, willing to immediately help, which was wonderful considering that we had almost 300 pounds of luggage.  Because we had to wait a couple of hours for our room to be ready, we took advantage of the exec lounge.  You must ask that this be included in your bill...we found it very worthy.
