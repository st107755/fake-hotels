I was concerned about the location of this hotel because I couldn't seem to find a good map of its location. It is across the canal from Venice. The hotel has a nice shuttle that runs frequently across to Venice. There were no shops on the island where the hotel is located, except the gift shop inside the hotel. There were a few restaurants within walking distance, but they were few and far between. The hotel is very nice and clean.  The hotel also offers several restaurant options, but we opted to eat lunch and dinner in Venice to enjoy the atmosphere there. Hotel staff was very nice and extremely helpful. We were upgraded to a suite because my husband is a Hilton Honor member. Large tub, separate shower, living and bedroom area. Bed was very comfortable.
