Stayed in this hotel for three days. There were several things I did not like about this hotel which are good to know: 1. the hotel is located on Giudecca, a separte little island which is very inconvenient. The hotel offers a shuttle but it only goes to the very crowded area of San Marco. This means that every time you want to go somewhere, you need to take the shuttle and navigate through the super crowed tourist area of San Marco and also take into account the hours of the shuttle and waiting for other passengers. It is much more convenient to stay in Venice itself. 2. the hotel is massive which we found very unpleasant. The breakfast area feels like a cantine and there is practically no space to eat outside.
