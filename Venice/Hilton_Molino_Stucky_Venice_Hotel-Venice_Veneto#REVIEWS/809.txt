I have frequently looked at this hotel and been put off that it is on Giudecca and not in Venice directly however recently I had the opportunity to stay here for work.  It was a perfect location!  I had amazing views across the water and the hotel's shuttle waterbus went across to St Mark's Square regularly and on time so there was no detriment whatsoever.   The building is beautiful, the standard of the rooms was high, higher than many in Venice itself and I always appreciate the  standard and variety of Hilton breakfast offerings.  Due to being at work and the time of year I did not have time to try the rooftop bar and terrace. The only criticism I could have is that the walls are very thin.
