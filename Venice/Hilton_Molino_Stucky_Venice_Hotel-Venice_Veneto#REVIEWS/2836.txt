Stayed here for two nights before a cruise. Enjoyed all the comforts you would expect of  a Hilton of this quality itself but for me the two best features are the location and the Sky Bar. Because you are on Giudecca you are looking back at the main centre and enjoy fantastic views of the city (especially from the Sky Bar) which was pleasantly full and buzzy but not so packed as the St Marks environs.  The private water shuttle takes you to Zatterre or St Marks in minutes and is a lovely trip in its own right. Zatterre is a delightful area packed with restaurants and interesting shops charging less than the area around the Square but offering all the magic of Venice at its best.
