I haven't contributed to a TripAdvisor before but felt that this hotel was worthy of comment. My partner and I stayed here over the Easter weekend and it surpassed our expectations in every way. All the staff were very accommodating, providing helpful information on shuttle boat services, ordering water taxis and offering tips on where to find good local restaurants.  The room we had was well appointed and, whilst the hotel is still relatively new, the decor and room furniture, carpets etc were in immaculate condition. The only thing that could have been better (and I really am pushing it with this) was that the shower could have been a fixed head in order to make it more powerful.
