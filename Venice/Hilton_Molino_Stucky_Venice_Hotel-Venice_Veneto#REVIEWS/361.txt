We stayed here the last weekend of April 2019.  When booking this looked a very nice hotel. However, first impressions tend to stick. Our check-in was not that great. There was 2 staff members caring for the check-in and they were not very efficient. Other staff members stood in the background not offering to help.  The hotel décor is beautiful and our room was very nice although we looked over the internal courtyard. It would have been nice to have a water front view, but our room was nice and quiet. We found out that the air-conditioning was not turned on across the hotel until sometime in May/June when it get warmer. Personally, I think a/c should be available and let the guest decide when to use it or not.  Breakfast was included and there was a great choice of food.
