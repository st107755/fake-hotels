Booked this hotel based upon a recommendation and as a HH member.  Was a little put off at the challenge of getting there after a night flight but just bit the bullet and booked a water taxi, expensive (€120) but the trip there from the airport was a great experience, once we got over the stress of trying to find where the water taxi was supposed to wait for us. The hotel is a lovely conversion of an old building and a lot of original features have been retained. Check-in was super friendly and helpful as were all of the staff we met. Room was very comfortable, quiet and with a nice water view.
