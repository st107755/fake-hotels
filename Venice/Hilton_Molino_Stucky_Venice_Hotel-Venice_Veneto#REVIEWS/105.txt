Came to Venice for 2 day stay before biking in Pulia, booked this hotel via trip advisor reviews, and have to give it 5s for location (5-8 minute walk from bus station and taxis, 10-15 minutes from the trains, and same for the docks for valporetto (boats going up and down the grand canal and out into lagoon). The staff was attentive and efficient. 345 am wake up all happened at 345, by a person; taxi for 4am was there 355 as promised by staff. Breakfast staff was meticulous with settings and service was discrete quiet and seamless, want to take them home! Room was well appointed, excellent light and space overlooked a garden and was dead quiet although hotel was full. Located on a small canal, access to water taxis and stores and restaurants were 5 to 15 minute walks.
