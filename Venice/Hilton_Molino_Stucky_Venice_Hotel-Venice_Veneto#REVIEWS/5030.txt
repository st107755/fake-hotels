We chose the Hilton as we wanted a modern hotel with a pool and liked the idea that it was away from the mad crowds of mainland Venice, and we were not disappointed. We arrived mid afternoon our room was ready, then we headed straight up to the pool as sunshine has been in very short supply at home we were keen to make the most of the warm Venetian sunshine. The pool area although small is clean and tidy and they will bring your drinks to your chairs for you, even at 3pm in the afternoon we were able to get chairs. The view from the roof is breathtaking.
