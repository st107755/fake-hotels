Second time to Venice, and that I wish we had come to the Molino on our last trip pretty much says it all. Venice hotels are notoriously expensive and some of marginal quality; however, look out for a good deal with Hilton and, although it will look a little expensive paying that little bit extra will pay off with a really solid hotel (literally), really good location and overall great staff. Rooms, bedroom and bathroom all to a very good standard and the common areas nice for sitting and planning the day. Breakfast food was of a pretty good standard and range; although at busy times the table service left something that the hotel needs to improve upon. Rooftop pool is closed until summer months, but do try the Skybar with impressive views to the City.
