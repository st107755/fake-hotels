Hotel was excellent,  the beautiful old building is kept in fab condition. Its clean and bright and modern with the old venetian twist! The rooms were a good size very comfortable and the bathroom was lovely and big.  At first we thought the location wasn’t great as it’s a little out the way but then we decided that actually after a busy day in the main square it was nice to retreat to the quietness of the Stucky Molino. The hotel also provide a free shuttle boat service in the hour  which makes it cheaper to get arund. The staff were lovely so friendly and very helpful making you feel very welcome.
