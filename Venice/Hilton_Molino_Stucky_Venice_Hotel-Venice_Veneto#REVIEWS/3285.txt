Just come back from a stay at the Molino Stucky. Beautiful hotel, set off from the main Venice which is nice to get away from the hustle and bustle of the more tourist hotspots.  Massive old converted flour mill that has tried to keep the overall aesthetics within the new surroundings and the internal staff were always very polite and helpful.   There was however an issue with the bathrobes being taken away after day 1 even though we were booked for three nights. A friend had tried to arrange a bottle of Champagne to be placed in the room before we got there but was never replied to (fortunately we had brought our own), the room was like a sauna regardless of trying to turn off the radiators and put the aircon on (and yes we had the windows closed before trying).
