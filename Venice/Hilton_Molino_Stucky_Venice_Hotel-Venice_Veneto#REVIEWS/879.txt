This is one of my favorite Hilton properties!   The hotel-- A renovated mill, opened as a hotel in 2007, this hotel is not only charming with history, but modern. Its the largest hotel in Venice, and by passing by the awards cases, looks to be very highly regarded! The location I thought to be ideal, away from the hustle and bustle of the city center. It is located on a more so residential island, but I greatly appreciated the charm that comes with this. Being able to stop by great restaurants just a few minute walk away, La Palanca was great, to dine with the locals for lunch. The hotel boat was perfect, only 7.50€ per person for the entire stay. The boat drivers were kind, courteous, and most importantly-- punctual.   The room-- Definitely spring for the rooms with a view!
