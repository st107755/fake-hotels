Spouse, adult son and I spent five nights here, on points.  Hotel upgraded us to a suite.  Bedroom had doors closing it off from the sitting area, where a separate bed was brought in.  Son thought that bed not comfortable, so, guess what, mother spent next four nights there.  It was fine.  I slept fine.  Son shared king-size bed with father.  OK with me, as, when I can't sleep, I like to turn on lights and read.  As to room, it really was roomy.  Bathroom had separate tub and shower.  Roomy, deep tub is wonderful for working out kinks after a long day of walking, wandering streets of Venice.  Water was hot, pressure was good, amenities were nice.  As spouse is Hilton Diamond, we had access to Executive Lounge.
