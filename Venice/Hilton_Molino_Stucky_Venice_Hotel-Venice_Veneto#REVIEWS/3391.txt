The hotel is in a lovely location and the free shuttle service provided by the hotel across to San Marco Square is very good.  The hotel is also very beautiful in an old building that has been totally transformed.  The room we had overlooked the water and was very clean and the bed very comfortable.  The bathroom was gorgeous.  The hotel is very large and we wandered around to find the rooftop pool and other amenities,  I found an ironing room which was very useful  as we were travelling round Italy and Greece from Australia. We booked a club room with access to the Club Lounge the lounge was a long narrow lounge set at the front of the hotel with lovely water views.  The breakfast was a bit of a disappointment as the tea and coffee were cold.
