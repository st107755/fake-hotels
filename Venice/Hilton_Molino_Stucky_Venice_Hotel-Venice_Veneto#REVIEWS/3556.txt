The Hilton Molino Stucky proved to be a pleasant surprise. I was concerned about its location on Giudecca, across the lagoon from Venice proper.  However, the hotel's private water shuttle eliminated any concern I had.  It ran twice per hour with two convenient drop off / pick up locations on Venice.  When the shuttle filled up, the hotel paid for a water taxi to transport the overflow. A nice gesture.  The Executive Lounge was a bit small and crowded for breakfast and happy hour.  However, it had an excellent selection of snacks and drinks.  The rooftop pool was appealing, offering spectacular views of the surrounding city, but the pool deck was very crowded.  The staff was friendly, receptive, and spoke English very well.
