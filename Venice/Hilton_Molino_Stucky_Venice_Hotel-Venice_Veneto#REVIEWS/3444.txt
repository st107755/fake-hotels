My partner and I stayed here for 2 nights recently and must say that after reading some of the reviews on TA we were a little worried about what our money was going to get us.  But all of the worry was for nothing, all of the staff we encountered couldn't of been more helpful, the bar staff polite, and the service exceptional. Having stayed at the Hilton in Milan during the same holiday I can say without hesitation that the price for our room in Venice was fully justified, if not just by the amazing views from the roof.  The boats were always on time, and but for 1 exception we never struggled to get on the boat of our choice. The staff on the boats aren't as courteous, but I think these are run by a 3rd party.
