First off I must say this is a beautiful hotel, it is newer, and the staff were generally helpful.  Reception desk was never busy and the concierge was great.  I was upgraded to a room with a view over the water which was excellent.  Venice is a crowded old city, so many other hotels have small, cramped rooms.  This sets Hilton apart because its rooms are all large with a spacious bathroom.  That being said, I travel quite often and I research my hotels extensively before booking.  I have bypassed the Hilton Molino on the last 2-3 trips to Venice primarily due to it's location.  A quick look at a map shows that the hotel sits on an island too far away from the main part of the city to be connected by bridge.
