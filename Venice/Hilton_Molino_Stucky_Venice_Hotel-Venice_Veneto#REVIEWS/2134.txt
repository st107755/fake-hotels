We stayed for 2 nights at the hotel and had a lovely time. As I an HHonors member we also had an upgrade which was fantastic.  The room was a great size, very clean and comfortable. As is the case with lots of old European hotels, things aren't always practical (I.e the magnifying mirror in the bathroom doesn't extend to a point where you can look into it! And hand towels rails are not by the sink!) but I guess this is part of the charm! The bed was very comfortable. The towels had seen better days but of course were white and clean. Just a small point for improvement. The breakfast was good, one day the service was very disorganised and slow but the second day it was prompt and helpful (I think the bulk of the bank holiday traffic had checked out!)
