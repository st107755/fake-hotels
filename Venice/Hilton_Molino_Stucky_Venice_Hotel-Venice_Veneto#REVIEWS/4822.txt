The Hilton Molino Stucky exceeded our expectations from the moment we set eyes on the property from our vaporette. The building is a renovated flour mill, which was left in decay from 1955 until the early 1990's.  Hilton has restored the property making it the largest hotel in Venice.  While our room does not have a view of the canal, what it does have is all the comforts one could imagine. I explained, or really I kind of begged for an upgrade that included two rooms.  I had not acclimated to the time changes and was waking up in the middle of the night while we were in Vienna. I could not go back to sleep and since we were in one room, I could not get up without waking my husband.  Hilton was nice eough to give me an upgrade to a suite.
