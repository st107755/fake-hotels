We stayed in the hotel for 3 nights.  I would have liked to stay longer!  Our room was great.  A huge, comfortable double bed,  plenty of space to relax.  The bathroom was superb with a double sink unit, huge bath and separate shower cubicle.  The hotel is fantastic, an excellent and sympathetic restoration/renovation of the original building has transformed it into a superb hotel.    The service was friendly, courteous and professional.  The breakfast buffet was freshly prepared and varied.  Although breakfast was busy there were always cleared, clean tables to sit at and the waiter/ress service was prompt and efficient.  The hotel has a rooftop pool which, although not huge and therefore busy, offers a lovely relaxing way to finish off a day of sightseeing.
