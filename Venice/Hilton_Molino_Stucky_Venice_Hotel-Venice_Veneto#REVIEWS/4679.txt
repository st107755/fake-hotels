We stayed at this 5 star hotel while visiting Venice and waiting to board a cruise ship.  We arrived at the airport and took a water taxi, Alilaguna, directly to the private Molino Stucky dock.  It was after dark and raining, so this convenience was much appreciated.  The hotel itself was created from an abandoned large flour mill from bygone years, while providing the guest with a 5 star hotel experience.  Located across the narrow Guidecca from  the main attractions in Venice such as St Mark's square, the hotel offers free shuttle service back and forth to the main islands of traditional Venice.  There is also availability of picking up the Alilaguna water taxi which can take you to the cruise terminal or back to the airport.
