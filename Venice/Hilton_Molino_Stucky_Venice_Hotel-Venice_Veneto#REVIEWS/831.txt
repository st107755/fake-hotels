We stayed here for a few nights in early November 2017.  The hotel was fantastic, it was easily one of the - if not the - nicest hotel we had ever stayed at.  We were fortunate to get an upgraded room overlooking the grand canal and the room was fabulous.  We saw cruise ships being towed back out to sea and going by our room by what felt like 100 feet away.  really cool.  The hotel and staff were very accommodating.  Nikkos at front desk was awesome.  The hotel is a little tricky to get to with public transport ferries but only a short walk away from 2 stops.  If nice weather, no worries but it was raining hard when we arrived.
