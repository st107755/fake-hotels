You expect a certain level at Hilton hotels and this didn’t disappoint.  The staff were very good, the room was lovely and the position overlooking the main island was perfect.  You could leave the busy tourist centre of Venice and take refuge on your hotels island.  A very short, straight walk from the hotel are a varied choice of restaurants. And you can have that after  dinner evening stroll without getting lost!  They have their own launch which takes you from directly outside the hotel to San Marco and back again for 7.50€ and was well worth it as no hassle or long queues.
