Hilton has done a briliant job converting a massive former mill into a first class hotel.  It's a stunning landmark on the the island of Guidecca offering stunning views and air conditioned cool, vital when it's 35c (92f) outside.  This was our second stay and so we knew the drill with the shuttle boats and the times when the rooftop pool was likely to be less busy (middle of the day when most people are out sightseeing).   The rooms are very comfortable, good beds, pillows and bathrooms.  Breakfasts, given the number of people being served, are pretty good.  It is true that the bars, restaurants and all extras are very expensive (up to 8 Euros for a simple coffee) but something has to pay for the amazing conversion from mill to hotel.
