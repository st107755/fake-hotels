On arrival the hotel seemed lovely.  It is a very attractive building.  At first it seemed exactly what my husband and I expected based on the website.  Unfortunately travelling in February means that some of the restaurants and the heated roof top pool were closed which was disappointing.  The boat that took us to San Marco was also infrequent which could again be attributed to the time of year.  The room was comfortable and clean.  However it was very hot and the air conditioning wouldn't work- luckily the windows opened.  We were disappointed to discover that nearly all of the hotel's services were only offered as an additional service.
