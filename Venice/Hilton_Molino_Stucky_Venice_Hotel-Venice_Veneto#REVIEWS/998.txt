In August, I was in Venice, Italy. I had pre-booked a room for my family using a Hilton-HHonors card. The lobby seemed nice and so did the rooms. Rooms were well cleaned and furnished. Spacious but terrible view of the room in the building over. Hotel is kind-of a maze considering it’s size (used to be a factory). The lobby of the hotel is quite nice featuring a sitting area, restaurant and bar. A business center is also conveniently located for travelers to ship packages, use computers, etc. The rooftop pool is absolutely beautiful and refreshing. There is another restaurant and bar at the pool. There could be more seating at the pool but it is otherwise nice. There is a water shuttle to St. Marco Square as the hotel is on a separate island.
