We stayed in the Hilton Molino Stucky for nights.  The hotel is large and looks fine from the exterior.  The rooms are not up to Hilton standards. The exception to this is that the bathroom was very nice. Overall the room, while clean enough, was small and not comfortable.  There was only one chair in the room. The lunch and breakfast restaurants had mediocre food. For example, the scrambled eggs in the breakfast line were lukewarm and not tasty. This restaurant was also a serve yourself breakfast  buffet.  The lunch room restaurant food was not up to Hilton standards.  The burgers were not tasty, though the fries were okay.     The halls leading to the rooms were substandard. They were not well cleaned and the walls were in badly need of a paint job.
