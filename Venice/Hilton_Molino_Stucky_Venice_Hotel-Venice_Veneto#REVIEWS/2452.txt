Hotel with a character. Staffs are generally hospitable and helpful. Check-in was seamless and we got a suite with 2 bedrooms.   Although not one with a full view of St Marks, being able to view the canal from the room was a plus. The rooms are well-kept and housekeeping did a great job in cleaning the rooms everyday.  Daily breakfast can be taken at the main cafe or the executive lounge which is located on the ground floor. We consumed ours in the main cafe and the spread / selection was relatively good.  Although the hotel is located on another island, we did not find it exceptionally inconvenient as the shuttle boat from the hotel as well as the public water taxis provided more than sufficient options to get to other places quickly.
