Venice semeed to be a very confusing place to travel, but that is no fault of Hiltons. After arriving at Marco Polo, you need to take a bus (#5) to the public water bus stand (which takes about 35 minutes). Once arriving and getting on the water bus, it is about 50 more minutes to make it into the heart of Venice. Very long and confusing.  The hotel has a free boat to two separate stops in Venice, and runs pretty regularly. The hotel boasts excellent views, and excellent restaurant/executive lounge, and the sky bar is truly awesome. The rooms are big (especially for Europe) and clean. Comfortable beds and pillows as well. The front desk was amazing in helping us with maps, and restaurant suggestions.
