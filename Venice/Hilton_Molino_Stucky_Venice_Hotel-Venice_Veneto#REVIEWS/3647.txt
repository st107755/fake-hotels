Spent a weekend here and loved every minute. The hotel is immaculate as you would expect from the Hilton brand and staff are polite and extremely helpful.   Room was very clean, spacious with an excellent bathroom comprising both bath and shower and thankfully the room was equipped with air conditioning as the weather was very humid.   We only ate at the hotel once, food was pricey and to be honest average, although when in Venice it should be said that perhaps the hotel is the last place to eat as there are so many local restaurants to take in the culture of the city. There is a Coop 5 minutes walk from the hotel and it is recommended that this be used to stock up on drinks and snacks, the concierge will gladly provide directions.
