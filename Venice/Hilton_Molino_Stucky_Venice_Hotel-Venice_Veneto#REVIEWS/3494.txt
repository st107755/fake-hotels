This property is amazing. It appears to be a restored building tastefully redone to a shining 5 star hotel. Check out the rooftop pool and restaurants. Amazing views. The rooms are comfortable and clean. Large shower and tub. Plenty of towels and pillows.    The location is away from the crowds and nice to retreat to at the end of the day. The free water shuttle is a huge bonus.  It's located on a quiet island which has quaint delicious restaurants. Ask the front desk staff for a recommendation. The breakfast is for sure 5 stars and the staff is pleasant. Overall value excellent. Would recommend. Tip from airport: Highly recommend taking a private taxi with another couple or party of four. You pay a couple euro extra then public transport and save an hour of your time.
