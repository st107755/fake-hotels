Stayed at Hilton Molino Stucky in mid October and it was fabulous. Upon check in, I didn't realize that my prepaid reservation was for a twin bedded room. The young lady was able to fix that right up and gave me a room with a king bed.   The room was pretty big compared to other rooms that I have stayed in during my travels to Europe. The bathroom was very spacious. The bed and pillows were like sleeping on clouds.   The breakfast was very good and offered TONS of choices. The even had coffee and OJ situated outside of the restaurant just in case you wanted to take some to go.  The hotel is located quite some ways from the main section of Venice but that is not a problem with the shuttle service the hotel provides.
