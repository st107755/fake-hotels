For us, the hotel really did make the holiday. It was the perfect sanctuary to escape the hustle & bustle of St. Marks Square after a morning of sight seeing.  -The building looks great,  -the staff were friendly and knowledgable,  -the roof top pool area was divine  -and the free shuttle boat over to the mainland ran every 20 minutes like clockwork.   Overall, can't recommend this enough. It's not the cheapest but would definitely recommend upgrading to a canal view room if you can. Our 2 small criticisms would be: 1) The pool water is literally freezing! More akin to an Arctic Plunge Pool than a Mediterranean spring.
