For many many years I saw this derelict old flour mill as a perfect hotel, and finally it became one thanks to Hilton, who have done an outstanding job renovating and converting the old site. Let us say at once that the ONLY and MAIN drawback of staying here is that you are a long way from the St Marks/Rialto area, requiring a lengthy (free!) hotel shuttle ride to near San Marco. Every 40 mins both ways. So you cannot just walk out and be in the heart of Old Venice.   However, that aspect apart, the hotel is excellent,  from the reception to the public areas, the lovely rooftop pool and panoramic lagoon views,excellent A/C, good elevators and porter services.
