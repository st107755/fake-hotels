We stayed at the Hilton Molino  Stucky  for only one night following a cruise.  The hotel is large and has a wonderful location on Giudecca Island.  The hotel operates a regular shuttle service to and from St. Mark’s Square which is very convenient.  There is a nominal charge of €4.50 per guest per stay for this service. We had club lounge access but the lounge is quite small- located just off the main lobby with dining tables and chairs and lacks the ambiance of the usual club floor lounges. The choice of canapés in the evening is a bit restricted and caters more to an Italian clientele than an International clientele. The complimentary wine was also very mediocre.
