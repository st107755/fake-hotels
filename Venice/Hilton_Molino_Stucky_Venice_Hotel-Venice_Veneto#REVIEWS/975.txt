We stayed here after a Viking cruise, as the post cruise extenstion.  When I first saw that we were at a hotel on Giudecca I was not sure if I would like that.  Well, it turns out I did.  The staff was very pleasant, all spoke English fluently, and check in was a breeze.  We were very pleased with our room, the super high ceilings and even a metal support post in the bath reminded you that the building was once a factory, but that was were the similarities ended.  The room was very clean, and well appointed.  Comfortable king bed, large windows, big TV, bath with separate shower & tub, double vanity and bidet.   The honor bar fridge even had an empty shelf on top for our use.   Ice machines were just down the hallway.
