We stayed at Hilton Molino Stucky Venice Hotel for two nights prior to leaving on a cruise. This was a reward stay on HHonors points.   Upon arriving to the airport there are basically two ways to get to the hotel. The first, is the water bus. While much cheaper, the Hilton is the second to the last stop, it takes about 1:15+ total travel time. The other option (that we chose) was the private water taxi. After nineteen hours of traveling we wanted to get to the hotel and get settled in. The cost of the private water taxi was 120 euros but takes around half an hour. The cost of the water bus I believe is 10 euro per person.    While we were unsure of staying across the grand canal on Giudecca, we are 100% happy that we did!
