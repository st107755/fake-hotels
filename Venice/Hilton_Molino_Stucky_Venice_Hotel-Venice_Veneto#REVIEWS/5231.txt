My husband and I just returned from a 12 day vacation to Italy.  We began our visit in Venice and stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky Venice.  We flew into Marco Polo airport and took the Alilaguna public water taxi from the airport to a stop directly in front of the Hilton. It was efficient, inexpensive and offered a nice introduction to Venice.  The exterior of the hotel reflects its former life as a flour mill while the inside has been nicely redone with a modern, spacious lobby and several restaurants.  Check-in was easy; however, the staff were uninformed that Hilton Gold members receive complimentary breakfast and it took three staff members to set this right.    Our room was supposedly an upgrade, but it only had a partial canal view.
