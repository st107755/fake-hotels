The Hilton is on Giudecca, the island below the the lagoon.  I was initially skeptical because it wasn't right bang on the center of the action, but it turned out to be the best thing about it.  It is easy enough to get to via public transport, and the hotel has a shuttle to the San Marco.  Beware if you have bags though, there are a couple of bridges to cross to get to the hotel.  The hotel itself is beautiful.  It is a converted flour mill, and inside, a lot of the architectural detail has been preserved, making for a very pretty place.  The room is spacious, and the bathroom is awesome.  Only downside is that value-wise, it is a bit pricey.  We got breakfast with our room, which was excellent, but otherwise it would cost a lot.
