We heard good things about this hotel so we decided to stay during a month-long European trip.  I think Hilton needs to do a better job of marketing exactly what constitutes a "Hilton" property in the brand.  I've stayed in Hilton's by airports that seemed like one step above hostels.  This place gives properties in the Waldorf Astoria collection a run for their money, and in many way exceeds their amenities.  The hotel is situated right on the Giudecca Canal and offers boat transport to and from St. Marks Square and everything else you would want to do in Venice.  The concierge lounge looks right out onto the canal, and the food and drink selection is immense.  The staff, especially the lounge and restaurant staff are extremely friendly and helpful.
