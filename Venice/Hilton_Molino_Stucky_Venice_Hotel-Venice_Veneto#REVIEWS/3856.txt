We stayed at this Hotel recently for 4 nights.  The building is a beautifully restored flour mill and our room was fine overlooking a garden square at far rear, but initially difficult to find from Reception as there were no proper directions given.  One of the porters took us to the room and advised us the quickest way was via the Hotel Spa, but unfortunately this wasn't always open. We were disappointed that there was no free Wi Fi available, and could get no English language channels on the TV apart from news channels.  No iron in the room.  We had dinner in the Hotel one evening and the main course was served up cold and we sent it back.
