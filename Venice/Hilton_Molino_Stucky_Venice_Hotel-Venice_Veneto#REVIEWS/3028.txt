We thoroughly enjoyed our 5 nights at this hotel. The hotel staff were unfailingly helpful, especially the breakfast room staff and the concierge. Upgrading to a canal view room is a must. The breakfast was the best selection I have ever encountered in a hotel and set us up for the day, so ensure this is included in your room rate. The shuttle bus was excellent and never too crowded in late March, but might be a problem in high season.  Giudecca is very relaxed and made a welcome break from the more touristy areas of San Marco etc.  We found some excellent, friendly  and reasonably priced places to eat on Giudecca, as recommended by the concierge. Do have a pre or post...or maybe both..dinner drink at the Hilton Sky bar for the most wonderful views.
