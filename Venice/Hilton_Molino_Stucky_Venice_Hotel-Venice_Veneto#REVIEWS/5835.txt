We stayed at the Hilton Molino during our first visit to Venice, we were travelling through Italy on the way back to Sofia, and chose to stay right in the heart of venice rather than one of the many hotels outside. On arrival in venice we had to park the car at the station and take one of the many waterboats to the hotel. Arrival itself was a small adventure but well worth it when we arrived.  Reception was first class, they gave us a very nice room, sightly small but well equipped and extremely comfortable, quiet and with a view over the canal over to St Marks square. What a wonderful view, maybe one of the best in the world?  Breakfast was excellent with a wide range of food, excellent service and good value.
