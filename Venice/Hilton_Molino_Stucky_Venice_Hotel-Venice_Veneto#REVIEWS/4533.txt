Hilton has made a very good job of converting this industrial building into a luxury hotel. The staff were very welcoming and friendly and the rooms are comfortable. I can't say too much about the facilities since we didn't eat here and the pool was closed for the winter ( which isn't mentioned in the website) even though it was a warm and sunny April week when we were there. The prices in the hotel are steep (30 Euros for breakfast, 9 Euros for a 25cl beer from the mini bar).   The main problem with the hotel is the location as it's situated on the island of La Giudecca away from the main sights. There is a free shuttle bus, which runs hourly to St Marks Square, or twice per hour to Zattere.
