Great trip for me and wifey. Room is nice, my water view was partly blocked based on the location of the windows but, could see much of Venice. I was on top floor room 705, any lower than 5th floor you probably won't have a great view. Price for quality is ok. I felt after the conversion to dollars it was on the high end (compared to an executive suite in Paris). Hotel feel high class, with doorman and ambiance. The food is a little overpriced however, they have a free* shuttle that goes from the main part of Venice ( Marco Polo) to the Hilton.
