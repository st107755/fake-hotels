AS part of a trip to Italy, we booked two nights in Venice as our final stop. Walked in and were blown away - the hotel is absolutely stunning.  The staff are professional warm, welcoming - across the board, from the chap who drives the water taxi to and from the hotel, the the Bell Hop, the barman - everyone was amazing and a real credit to the hotel.  We decided immediately we wanted to stay an extra night and the wonderful girl on reception booked us in and even managed to get us a reduced rate.  Out room was lovely  - we had paid extra for a view and it didn't disappoint.  There were complimentary Bellini's and chocolates in our room with a handwritten welcome card which was a great touch.  It is far from cheap though - especially alcohol!
