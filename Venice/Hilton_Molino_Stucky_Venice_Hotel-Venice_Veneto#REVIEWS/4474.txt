This was my sixth visit to Venice which is a wonderful place to visit.This time we stayed here for a couple of nights after finishing a cruise.The hotel was efficient and met our requiremnets , but for me it was too big and impersonal.There was a large queue at reception at check in , and in the huge lobby there was a constant flow of guests with some events and large parties taking place. There was free wi-fi for 24 hours and a computer available at a cost.In other 5 star hotels we have been used to having this facility free. Bedrooms were roomy , but were just like Hilton rooms anywhere - no special Italian feel or decor!
