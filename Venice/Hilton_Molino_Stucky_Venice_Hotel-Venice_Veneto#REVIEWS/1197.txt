The Hilton is nicely located, about 15 mn away from St Marc's square by water shuttle. The hotel offers a convenient and punctual shuttle service for 6 EUR per person per stay. This is a really good price compared to the water bus that would cost 15 EUR per person per way even if you'll take it for only 1 stop. The Mediterranean restaurant overlooking the canal is really nice, friendly service and good food. We ate there every day.  It was so easy to get to the city center with the shuttle boat. We took it every day to St Marc's square and started our adventures from there.  Getting to the hotel and from the hotel to the airport is not so easy, we were offered two options, either a 30 EUR ride that takes 90 mn by water transfer to get to the airport or a 130 EUR water taxi.
