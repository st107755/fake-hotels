This really is a lovely hotel but it is located on an island that requires you to take either the Hilton Shuttle or a water taxi. This requires some planning and often we were too tired after coming back to the hotel to think about going out again on another boat trip. Our executive room was lovely with a stunning view of the canal. A warning, though, the rooms facing the canal are very loud Friday and Saturday as you can hear people partying on the canal until the early morning. We laughed about it but it may not be right for everyone. You have to pay for the wireless, which is absurd, considering what one pays for this hotel. The connection itself was maddening! The executive breakfast is nice and the staff reminded me of the larger buffet available to me as well.
