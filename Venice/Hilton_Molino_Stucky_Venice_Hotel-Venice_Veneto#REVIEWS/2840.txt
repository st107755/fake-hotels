We got a special deal and were able to stay here for 4 nights. An amazing hotel and very welcoming. If you arrive in Venice by train and decide to take public transport travel to the Zattere stop and walk to the adjacent jetty for a free shuttle to hotel. DON'T catch the boat to the Giudecca Palanca stop as you will have a 500m hike to the hotel with your luggage. Not fun in the 36 degree heat as we found out. Prices are slightly high but you are at a Hilton. The roof top pool has to be seen to be believed. Although on the small side the views are to die for. There is plenty of sun bathing space but it does get crowded especially in hot weather where it is a lovely place to cool off, relax and have a drink at the roof to bar.
