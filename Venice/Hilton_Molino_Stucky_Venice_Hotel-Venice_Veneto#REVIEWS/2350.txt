I am writing this having just arrived home… The hotel first off was immaculate, excellent service from all staff, very helpful and always looking to see if there is anything you need. The bedroom and bathroom was excellent., We stayed on the 5th floor and had views over Venice which were great. The only slight drawback of the room was the poor tv channel selection…however who comes to Venice to watch tv? Another slight drawback was that you had to pay for Wifi which was quite pricey (40 euro for 3 nights).
