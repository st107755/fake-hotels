Large hotel in old factory building. Classic style rooms with good beds.   Breakfast is served as a rich buffet with lots of choice. Very cosy bar & lobby. Staff however is not an example of friendliness nor hospitality.   Amazing roof top pool & bar/restaurant with incredible sights over the city of Venice.   Shuttle between the hotel & pier near San Marco square.
