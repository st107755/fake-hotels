We were bit apprehensive about staying here after reading numerous reviews about it but as it was part of our package deal we had no option.  We did wonder if we had got the same hotel as the negative reviews as we found it really good! The price was not too expensive by Venice standards and our room was great with a view of the canal. The hotel was originally an old granary - lovely. The breakfast buffet had delicious and varied foods catering for a wide clientele.The roof bar and pool were lovely but  it was too busy for us.  The view from there was fantastic.  The hotel was clean, free wifi in the general areas but not in the room, ample comfortable seating areas.
