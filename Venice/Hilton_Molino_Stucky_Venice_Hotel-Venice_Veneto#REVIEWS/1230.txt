If you are looking at this hotel as a possibility for your Venice stay, the big question you're asking yourself right now is whether you can live with the location.  The answer is yes.   The Hilton Molino Stucky is located off the main islands of Venice - on the island of Giudecca - virtually across the water from the Zattere vaporetto stop in Dorsoduro. That means you'll need a boat to get to Dorsoduro, or St Mark's Square, or any of the key sights of Venice from the hotel.  The hotel provides a regular shuttle boat (every 20 or 40 minutes depending on time of day) to St Mark's. In the evening, there's a second stop at Zattere so you can get to the Dorsoduro district. The boat is clearly marked with a Hilton Molino Stucky sign, as are the piers it departs from.
