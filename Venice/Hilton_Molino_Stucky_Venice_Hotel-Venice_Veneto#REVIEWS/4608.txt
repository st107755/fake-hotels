If you want to stay bit far from the major tourist attractions and noise, this is the hotel for you. It has class and very attentive staff catering to all your needs. The hotel has its own shuttle throughout the day and until late nite and also can be reached through public water transport (about 5 min walk from the hotel to the station). The rooms are nice and as you would expect from a Hilton brand but we have got the view of the side canal leading to the main canal.  The roof top view is priceless with the pool. You can relax in the lazy sunchair and watch the cruise ships and boats go by... so relaxing!!!
