We visited Venice the first week in December and stayed at the Hilton to take advantage of our points.  We were upgraded to an executive suite which was very nice and comfortable.  It was in the back part of the hotel so it was very quiet but did not have a view of Venice which was ok but something to be aware of.  We used the complimentary services of the executive lounge quite a bit especially for breakfast which saved time and money and the view from there is awesome.  The shuttle boat was so convenient and always on time, and being off season we were sometimes the only people on it.  We did not at all feel we missed anything because we were not staying on the main part of Venice.  Service was friendly but professional.
