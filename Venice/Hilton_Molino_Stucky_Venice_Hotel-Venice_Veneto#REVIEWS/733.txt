We stayed here for 4 nights and enjoyed every minute.  The staff were very welcoming and helpful from the time we walked through the door until the day we left.  They were very attentive and ensured that everything was to our satisfaction.  The hotel itself is an old flour mill conversion and beautifully done.  The bedrooms are spacious as are the bathrooms.  We had an Executive Double Room and the view was amazing looking out over the water.  The hotel is on an island and runs its own shuttle boat service for which there is a small charge but well worth it as it takes 20 minutes to get to the busier places and the boat runs all day until late at night. Breakfast was plentiful and a good choice.  The hotel is on the quieter side and away from the hustle and bustle.
