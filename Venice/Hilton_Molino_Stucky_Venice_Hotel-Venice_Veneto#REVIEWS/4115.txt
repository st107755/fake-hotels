We booked a 5 night reward stay at the Hilton Molino Stucky and with Gold Hilton Honours status were upgraded to a Jr suite, received complimentary breakfast and executive lounge access, and free wifi. Room 422 is on right side of hotel with view to Giudecca Island and an okay side view to the Giudecca channel (but if you look down the view is pretty ugly). It had two narrow but high windows that open - one in a small living room and one in the bedroom - in-between are the bathroom (shower only) and large closet area. Each main room had a wonderful Murano (?) glass chandelier - the one in the bedroom with 10 arms - very pretty. Each room also had a stocked mini bar, and one room had a kettle to make coffee and tea. The linens were lovely and the room was very comfortable.
