We arrived at The Hilton Molino Stucky - after taking the Alilaguna Blu Line boat from Marco Polo. It takes about 1hr 40 mins but its is a bargain at 27 Euro return and you get a lovely tour of Venice on the way through.   We were welcomed warmly to the Hilton and the downstairs lobby is really quite something. Check in staff were very friendly and helpful with information regarding the free shuttle boat etc. I had requested a room with a canal view as it was my mothers 50th birthday -we did not receive this but I was very happy with the beautiful room they gave us with super comfy beds, a gorgeous marble bathroom and a nice view out to the court yard.
