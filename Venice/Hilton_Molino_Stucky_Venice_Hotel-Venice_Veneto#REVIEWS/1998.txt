Decided to visit Venice for Redentore this year, and had a great time. Stayed at the Molino Stucky Hilton for the ninth time and as ever were looked after in immaculate style. So much character in the Hotel with all the wood beams and the 'yesterday' pictures of the complex in and around the breakfast room. Staff were impeccable as usual, and several of them recognised us from our Christmas 2015 visit. Abdel in the Bar area is a perfect host. Love staying on Giudecca island, away from the Venice mainstream, and coming across the locals in the bars and Trattorias along the front. On the Saturday of Redentore the whole place was buzzing, with the front packed with families and friends enjoying a picnic waiting for the 11 30pm fireworks - it was heaving!!
