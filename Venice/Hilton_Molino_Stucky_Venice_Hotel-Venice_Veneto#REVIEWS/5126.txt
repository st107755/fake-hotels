As a Hilton Diamond member I am always in these places - and I have to say this is the best yet! Recent trips have included the Beverly Hilton, Paris and Toronto but Molino Stucky beats them all! The benefits were unparalleled; free spa entry (usually €25/2 hours), breakfast (not just continental) and a lovely room upgrade. The hotel is wonderful. It is a huge converted flour mill with several different buildings housing the rooms- all exposed beams and brickwork. There are several restaurants to choose from and the Executive Lounge is stocked from morning until night. The on-site spa is amazing and offers good value treatments. The rooftop pool is just fantastic and offers amazing views of Venice and you could spend all night in the Skyline bar.
