My husband and I were here for a six-night stay, the start of an Italian vacation. This is a five-star property at a fraction of the cost of similar properties in Venice, I suppose because it is located on Giudecca, across the Giudecca Canal from San Marco which boasts the city's main attractions. From my perspective this is a plus, however. There is a free shuttle boat that runs frequently and drops you almost at the hotel's door. You can access it upon arrival from the same pier that the Vaporetto (water bus) from the airport docks at. Cost of the water bus is 15 Euros per person. We saw many people who were staying in San Marco lugging their suitcases through the labyrinth of alleys and up and down the bridges that cross the numerous canals, we did not have to do that.
