As loyal Diamond customers, our stay at this property was less than what we expect from Hilton.  We were "upgraded" to a canal view room, however it was located on the first floor adjacent to the water taxi dock.  This meant that our stay was less than peaceful, although the view was nice. Avoid first floor rooms in the A building.  We traded a king bed for 2 single beds for the view and the beds are horribly firm and uncomfortable, even for the spouse who did not have a recent back surgery.  The walls are paper thin and we could hear the partying ways of our neighbors, along with their mundane conversation.   We had to request the amenity for our stay, although as Diamond status it is clearly stated on the reservation. We waited until the last night to make this request.
