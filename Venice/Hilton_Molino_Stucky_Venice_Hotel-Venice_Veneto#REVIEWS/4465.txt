Stayed at this hotel during our recent first trip to Venice.  We arrived by train to Venice and it was a bit tricky to try to figure out how to get to the hotel as they did not have directions if arriving by train.  Once we made it, we were thrilled.   The location, across the main waterway, overlooking Venice was amazing.  The hotel is an old mill that has been resoted an rennovated into this magnificant hotel.  There is great charm and sophistaicated elegance about this property.   The room we had was gorgeous and our window opened onto the view of Venice.  What more could we want?! The rooftop bar is amazing, however we could only enjoy for one evening as it was closed for a private function on our second night.
