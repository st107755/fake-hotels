We stayed for 5 nights, before and after a cruise. The building is beautiful and common areas are well maintained.  The main issues for us were very poor service, dirty room with a faulty TV and the need to ring several times to get an extra towel or shampoo. Hilton has a policy of charging you when things are moved in the little bar fridge. We were caught in this trying to accomodate a cake given by the airline to celebrate one of us 60th birthday, even though the fridge had leftover food from the previous guests!! We were charged more than 120 euros for it.  We were given a "de luxe room" which was in disrepair ,very dirty and with a faulty TV set. As the pictures show , the amount of dust on the air conditioning was appalling.
