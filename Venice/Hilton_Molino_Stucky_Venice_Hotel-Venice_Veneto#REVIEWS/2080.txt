My wife and I stayed at this lovely quiet hotel for one night prior to a Mediterranean cruise. I have gold status and so we were upgraded to the exec floor, with exec lounge benefits, which included all day snacks and soft drinks, plus a light evening buffet with wine and beer for two hours.  Our room was magnificent offering fabulous views of Venice from its location on Guidecca Island, a short ferry ride across the Grand Canal. We had bottled water and every luxury one could think of.  One little moan was poor water pressure in shower.  We opted for breakfast in main dining room and there was a fantastic selection of hot and cold food.  The hotel has a timed ferry service to St Marks Square.
