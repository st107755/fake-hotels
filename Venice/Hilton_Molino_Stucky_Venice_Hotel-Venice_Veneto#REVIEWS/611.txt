The Hilton Molino Stucky is on the western end of Giudecca island, near the ancient village of Fortuny and approximately 1-hour ride on the Vaporetto, (water taxi) from Marco Polo Venice Airport.   This hotel has it all, starting with a long, rich history,  a picturesque rooftop bar,  dining by the waterside, and much more. Our room had a traditional design with authentic touches of Venice; the bed was cozy with plenty of fluffy pillows.   We are happy that we chose this hotel as our pre-cruise stop and we feel that it was worth every cent. We suggest you take a stroll down the canal towards Planca Vaporetto stop and experience a meal at any of the local family-run restaurants while in this area.
