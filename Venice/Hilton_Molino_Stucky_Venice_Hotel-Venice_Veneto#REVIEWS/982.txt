Just back from a 4 day stay at this lovely hotel.  I’ve dropped one star because we really wanted to use the pool and sit up on the deck but we weren’t told when we booked that it was closed, though to be fair on our last day we were given a tour of the presidential suite on the top floor and were able to take some fabulous photos.  The executive lounge is far too small and I would have liked a gin and tonic rather than beer or wine on an evening but maybe I’m being picky.  Although we could have taken breakfast in the lounge we preferred the main breakfast area.  There was a fantastic choice and the food was of a great quality.
