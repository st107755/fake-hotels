I am a fan of Hilton hotels and try to stay at one wherever I travel. However, I was less than impressed with this one.   The Stucky is located on Giudecca, a boat ride is required to get to Venice proper. The hotel offers a shuttle that runs every 20 to 40 minutes, depending on season. The shuttle drops off near San Marco. The exact location also varies by season.   The hotel is nicely appointed in common areas - roomy, open, plenty of spaces to sit and relax. The room was small. Upon check-in, we were advised that we were upgraded to a deluxe room. It had a king bed, bathroom, desk, armoire and night stands. It also had two available outlets in the bedroom and a "razors only" outlet in the bathroom. None of the outlets were near the bed. There were no USB chargers.
