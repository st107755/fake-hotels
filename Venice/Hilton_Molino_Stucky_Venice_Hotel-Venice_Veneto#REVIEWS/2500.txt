We rented a suite here in late October 2015 and had a very pleasant stay. Whilst the hotel is a little away from the bustle of St Marks, there are plenty of boats to take you over to the other islands. The hotel launch over to Dorsoduro takes but a few minutes. We used to get off here and walk over the Accademmia Bridge (about 10 minutes away) from the landing.  The room was good although we were invaded by mosquitos one night which ensured we kept the nets over the windows after that. Breakfasts are good and there's quite a lot of choice. The executive lounge is a bit disappointing compared to other Hilton's and there's a very small window of opportunity if you fancy a glass of wine. Staff are very friendly and helpful.
