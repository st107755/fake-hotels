We spent a long weekend in Venice at the Hilton Molino Stucky and enjoyed the luxury of the Hilton brand. Although the hotel is not situated on the main island, it wasn't really an issue as the hotel provides a shuttle boat for a one-off charge of €4.50 for the duration of your stay, which runs approx every 30 mins. When we arrived at the airport we transferred directly to the hotel by the Alilaguna Blue Line water bus, which took about 1 hour 45 min but we enjoyed the sightseeing trip.  Cost €25 return (if you book in advance on-line).  For a change, we went back to the airport via the Orange line from San Marco through the Grand Canal. Anyway, back to the hotel. Our room was a large double situated on the 4th floor and was very spacious and clean.
