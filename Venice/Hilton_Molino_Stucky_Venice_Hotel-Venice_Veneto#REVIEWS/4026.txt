If it were not for our Hilton Points we would not have stayed here. The hotel was nice but really made more for corporate gatherings as it was really packed and loud. While the shuttles were convenient during the day, at night they should have gone later as Venice is more of a wandering island and if you want to get back to Giudecca without having to pay a water taxi then you need to be there promptly and cut your evening short. The building was great and the bar was nice but we didn't have any food because frankly - it's a Hilton and who wants to eat at a hotel restaurant when the food looked just average.
