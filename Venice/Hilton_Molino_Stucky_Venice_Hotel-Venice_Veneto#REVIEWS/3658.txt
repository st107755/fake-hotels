I just got back from a weekend at this hotel. Loved everything about it. I'm a seasoned traveller who finds fault in small detail and had read all of the reviews for the hotel before going. Everything was great from the service (the really nice girl at check in gave us loads of information and gave us a really nice canal facing room, going out of her way to find one) to the beautiful rooftop bar and pool. The room was really big, really modern and had a great view, air conditioning was brilliant, powerful shower, little touches like turndown and sweets are always welcome. We had an executive room and it is definitely worth paying extra as you can have soft drinks in the exec lounge all day and wine/beer between 5-7pm.
