Stayed for 4 nights as the final leg of our honeymoon in Italy. Our room was nicely furnished with a very comfortable bed and the bathroom was lovely. Sad not to be given a room upgrade as we are gold members and were on our honeymoon. We did ask and were told we had been given a very beautiful room but it turned out to be the room grade we had booked and paid for anyway. Very different to the Hilton Cavalieri in Rome who gave us a fantastic upgrade and made us feel very special. Staff at the hotel were helpful and efficient - from the young man who helped us from the water taxi with our cases to the concierge and waiters. The lady running the breakfast room was super efficient. Went to look at the spa and fitness room but didn't use them as they were small.
