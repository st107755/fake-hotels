We spent the night before our cruise at the Molino Stucky in a room overlooking the canal separating the island of Giudecca from St. Mark’s Plaza and the rest of Venice’s tourist center. Having flown all night and after a four-hour layover in Paris, we were ready for the comfortable bed and the effective air conditioning. We arrived via private water taxi from Marco Polo Airport which took us to a dock next to the hotel. The half-hour trip was €130 for up to four people. It’s a fun way to start your trip to Venice.   The only downside in our room was an extremely narrow tub with no shower curtain or rubber shower mat. Although I managed to spray most of the floor and sink area with the handheld shower head, I happily didn’t slip in the tub!
