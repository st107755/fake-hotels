We stayed here for 3 nights for a family wedding at Il Rendetore Church close by. Giudecca itself is just so much nicer than mainland Venice with such a calm, relaxed feeling. Close to some lovely restaurants and Harry's for lovely evening drinks. The restaurants/bars in the hotel are superb. The Sky Bar has an incredible view for a relaxed lunch. My only beef (hence 4 stars) was we were in a family suite and on returning from the wedding we attended, there was a pool party that went on until about 4/5am and it was LOUD. We could see the pool from our window, so not quite sure why they didn't have the sense to put us further away given our children's ages! We only used the hotel shuttle once but it was a great service.
