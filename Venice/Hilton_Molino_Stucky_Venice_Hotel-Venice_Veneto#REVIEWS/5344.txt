Have just returned from Two nights, three days in Venice, staying at this beautiful hotel with my husband and thirteen year old daughter. First impressions were fantastic, when you see the impressive red brick building as you round the corner onto Guidecca Canal. Check in was smooth, and when there was a problem with our access to the executive lounge it was sorted out without fuss. Rooms are as to be expected and the breakfast was fantastic. (Included in our booking, but usually 15 Euro). The staff were always friendly, helpful and proffesional. The front desk and conciege especially so.  The hotel run a shuttle boat to and from St. Marco via Zattere.
