This was our first trip away without the children in almost 11 years so we decided to spend a long weekend here. We arrived early at the hotel so our room wasn't ready, We went for a drink in the main bar of the hotel. My wife and I were made to feel very welcome and were certainly treated in the manner we'd expect from a high quality hotel chain.  The island of Giudecca where the Molino Stucky is located is a residential area a 2 minute boat ride from the main island. Everybody we encountered while exploring seemed friendly, but be warned the residential buildings do look fairly run down and is quite an eyesore in places.  One our room was ready we settled in and took a look around, the room was nice, but maybe not quite as luxurious as I expected.
