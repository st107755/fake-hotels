My wife and I stayed here for a 3 night break to Venice in November and despite floods before we came and some rain whilst there, we really enjoyed our time in Venice and staying at the Hilton. As a Hilton Diamond VIP member, we were upgraded to an Executive Room after inquiring about the availability. The first room we were in had an adjoining door to the next room and as such, the sound proofing was less than satisfactory and we were disturbed by the people in the next room. I went to the reception and asked if we could be moved and they very quickly arranged for us to move to another executive room, and this one had a dual aspect view over the canal.
