My husband and I recently stayed at the Molino Stucky for two nights on our Italian vacation.  The hotel is beautiful.  I used Hilton points for one night stay and paid for the other night stay, which made it much more affordable.  The hotel staff was very nice and the hotel water shuttle to the main tourist area was convenient.  We never experienced an issue with the boat being full (first come basis).  The hotel room was very clean with a view of the water.  The bathroom was very spacious with a very nice tub/shower combo.  It was nice to take a hot bath after a long day of sightseeing.  We had paid for an executive room that gave us access to the executive lounge.  This was  a very nice added benefit.
