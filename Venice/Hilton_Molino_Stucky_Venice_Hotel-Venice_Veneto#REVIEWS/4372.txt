Stucky Molino is not your typical (usually relatively charmless) Hilton Hotel. This place has a style to itself - very industrial, very "SOHO" or even "DUMBO" in NYC. It's truly a great option to visit Venice with a very practical boat shuttle that runs ever 30 minutes or so to Zattere or San Marco.  We stayed in a Suite located on the 2nd floor. While relatively small, it was perfectly clean and comfortable with  view on the canal. The AC was perfect, the bed comfortable. There even was a small kitchen – that we didn’t use, of course!  A quick + and -:  PLUS: great design, comfortable, clean, roof top pool, quiet, great views (exceptional views), staff is mostly impersonal but fine.
