My husband and stayed at this hotel the beginning of Marco 2013. The room we stayed in was amazing (we had a suite). The view of Venice from our room was incredible!! Some of the staff at the front desk was friendly, others would pretend they hadn't noticed you standing there.  The restaurant food was tasty but very pricey for what you got. I would recommend eating in Venice.  The water shuttle to Venice was always on time. There is only a tiny window of time you have to catch the shuttle before it leaves so plan on getting to the platform early.  My only negative comment I have is about the front desk staff. Several we're not helpful at all. They would pretend to not see you and you would have wait until one on the few friendly staff members would become available.
