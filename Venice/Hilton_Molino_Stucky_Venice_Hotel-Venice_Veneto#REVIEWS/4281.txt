Arrived late in the evening (11pm) as this hotel is the last stop from the airport on a shuttle boat taxi. (worth taking a private taxi!) Service was quick and efficient as you would expect, but no service to take our bags to our room which I thought a five star hotel would provide.  The room was very well presented and clean. Unfortunately we had a view of the inner part of the hotel but to be honest, we didn't care much as we didn't come to Venice just to view it from the hotel room window!  We ordered a late night snack as we had arrived so late and there was a decent menu to choose from including toasties and french fries. Expect expensive prices for the food as with any hotel.
