My husband, daughter and I, stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky for 4 days.  It was our first trip to Venice and it was a very nice short break.  The hotel is on the Guidecca island which is about 5-10 min water taxi ride from San Marco which is the main square of Venice.  The hotel has a complimentary water taxi from right outside the hotel which runs every 40 min.  We couldn't get on one in the morning as there were so many people waiting for it so instead of waiting another 40 min., we got on a public taxi for €7 per person.  The hotel is very clean and spacious.  We stayed in one of their suites as we had our daughter with us and wanted space.  The room had a separate seating area where they put the extra bed so it worked well.
