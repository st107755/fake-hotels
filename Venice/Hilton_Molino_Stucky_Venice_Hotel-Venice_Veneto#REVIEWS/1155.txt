After staying 2 amazing nights at the JW Marriott we booked 2 nights at the Hilton Molino.   Reservation through booking.com and possibly worst room on hotel. Right at the back of the hotel, in desperate need of upgrading and renovation. Windows on each wall so room like a sauna.   Air con poor so requested the temperature be lowered - didn't happen.    Decided to head for the pool area which had approximately 6 sunbeds in total, guest actually lying on towels poolside. Pool very cloudy, would it pass a hygiene inspection?  Told pool area closing at 5pm for private party...we arrived at 4:30. Decided this was by no means a 5 star hotel.   Discussed concerns at front desk with guest relations manager. Not interested one bit.   We left immediately.
