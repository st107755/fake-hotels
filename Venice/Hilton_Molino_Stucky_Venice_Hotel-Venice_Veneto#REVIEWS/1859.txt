Venice in the summer can be overbearing with the crush of the crowd and this hotel offers a peaceful oasis. Located in former flower mill on Giudecca Island across a canal form the main part of Venice this hotel offers great views and peace. If walking to sites the sites is your thing, don't stay here. If you want to get away from the crush of the crowd then you will love it here.  I had an executive suite with a view and I loved watching the boats traverse the canal and when the cruise enter and depart Venice they go right past this hotel and it's quite the site.  Breakfast is fantastic with lots of choice and made to order options. Had dinner in the hotel one night it was fantastic. Did drinks on the roof top terrace and that was cool too.
