We stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky Hotel for 3 nights in May of 2018.  I used Hilton Honor points and had a room on an higher floor.  The room had a wonderful view, but head up to the Skyline bar and the rooftop pool and the view is AMAZING and AWESOME! The staff was helpful, but we did not have much interaction.  The room was nice, clean and comfortable.  The restaurant is pricey, but this is the case for many restaurants in Venice. This is a Hilton, so there is room for conventions but we did not have any issues with this during our stay. I was worried with transportation to and from the hotel.  Water taxis can be expensive. This really was not an issue with just a bit of organization.  We came in to Venice via the Santa Lucia train station.
