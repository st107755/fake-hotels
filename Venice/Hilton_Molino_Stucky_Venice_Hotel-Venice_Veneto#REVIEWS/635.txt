With literally hundreds of hotels to choose from, it can be a daunting challenge to decide where to stay in Venice. Firstly, it is a beautiful hotel, after having been converted from an old flour mill, it has the touch of the old with all modern conveniences. The rooftop bar gives you great views of Venice.  It is conveniently located.
