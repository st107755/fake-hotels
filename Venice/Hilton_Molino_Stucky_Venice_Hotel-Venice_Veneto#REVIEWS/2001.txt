This property is located on Giudecca Island which allows you to get a much needed respite from the frenetic energy across the canal.  The hotel is beautifully appointed and our room was quite comfortable.  We took the Alilaguna water bus from the airport which, conveniently, has a stop right at the hotel.  It takes 1 hour and 45 minutes but is a pleasant ride in and a lot cheaper than a water taxi.  It is very easy to get your luggage to this hotel.  We got a great deal on the booking and declined an upgrade offer to a canal view as it was quite expensive.  We did not miss the canal view.  The rooftop pool is incredible.  There is a walkway on the left hand side of the pool that will take you to the lounge chairs.  This can be hard to spot when you first get up there.
