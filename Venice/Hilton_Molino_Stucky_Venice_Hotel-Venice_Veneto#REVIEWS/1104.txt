We enjoyed a 4-night stay at this hotel.  We were welcomed as a gold member with an upgraded room, breakfast and access to the lounge.  My only complaint is that there is no shade at the pool so impossible for me to spend any time there.  Since this was one of the key reasons I selected this hotel I was disappointed.  Certainly some umbrellas could be made available!  Other than that it was perfect.  Breakfast was delicious, all the staff we encountered were helpful and friendly.  Access to the lounge was nice for snacks, drinks and appetizers/salad in the evening.  The hotel has a ferry service over to the square and we had no problem with it.  It was very convenient.
