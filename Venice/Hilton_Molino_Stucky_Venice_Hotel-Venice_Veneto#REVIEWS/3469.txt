Just about everything we experienced in Venice was magic.  From the time we exited the train station and saw the speed boat taxis zooming up and down the 'street' in front of the Termini to the last lick of gelatto when we left for Florence - it was a blast.  We enjoyed three days, 2 nights at the Hilton Molino - thanks to a recommendation from a friend. Due to a glitch, or [likely] my error in requesting a Hilton upgrade, we did not get the room I requested and the management wouldn't budge.  However our room on the side of the building was beautiful with a view of both sides of the island and quiet as requested.
