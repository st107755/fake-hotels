Spent 3 nights at this hotel in February. This is a good hotel but location is not perfect although the complimentary shuttle does help. We had a problem with our booking (not the hotel's fault) which meant that although we booked at the same time as our friends and paid the same amount, we ended up not having breakfast included. The hotel flat out refused to budge on this and we were told that breakfast would be an additional €20 a head which we thought was a bit expensive. The following morning at reception I overheard breakfast being offered for €15 which I wasn't impressed with. After checking in, we discovered that our friends' room was far superior to ours although apparently the same category.
