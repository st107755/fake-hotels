My wife travels for work and stays at Hilton hotels, so we were able to use points for the room.  Everyone at the hotel was very friendly and helpful.  The room was great.  It was our anniversary and we received a heart made out of silk rose petals in our room in the evening.    If you have access to the Executive Lounge take advantage of it!  It has a great breakfast and wine and snacks in the evening.  Some say the lounge is small, but we really liked the design.  We were there in off-season (something I highly recommend to avoid crowds), so in peak season you'll want to get places early or you'll put up with big crowds.    The shuttle is great, but if you are in Venice for more than two days consider getting a several-day pass on the Vaporetto to give you more options.
