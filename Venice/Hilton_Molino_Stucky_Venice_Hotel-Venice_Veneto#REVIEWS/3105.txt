Hilton Venice looks jazzy in pic and from far you would believe it too. However once you enter the hotel the impression takes a beating. Entrance is stuffy and not welcoming.  No one to offer to carry your baggage. Checkin was easy with an upgrade offer to executive room at best price thrown in, but we declined. Rooms, however are bad - both classic and deluxe - unless you are not opting for executive room. Rooms have low ceiling and seem suffocating. Partial lagoon view is a waste. Executive lounge is also pretty bad - both in shape and spread. Best part of the hotel was its free shuttle service to the Centre every 15 minutes which is a saver. One can reach hotel by opting for normal taxi at €7 from station.
