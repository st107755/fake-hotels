Not sure why there are some negative reviews on this place, as it is a marvelous hotel. As mentioned in previous reviews, it is true you will not find a wash rag here. Folks, this is Italy and most, if not all hotels are without wash rag. I found the hair dryer to be top notch and worked well in our bathroom. It was a standard hair dryer, not the "hose type" units you will find attached to a wall in most Italy hotels. We did have an alarm clock, bathrobes, closet safe, good internet connection and all amenities you would expect from a Hilton Hotel. As with any property you will always find your pros and cons. I will admit that the bed situation is the usual European problem, as we had two over sized twin beds rather than a large queen or king.
