An absolute five star hotel for several reasons but ever so slightly let down by the porters. When we arrived they took our bags to the wrong room and we had to wait 2 hours for them to find them which wasn't a great start when travelling with a baby and unable to leave the hotel without several of the items in the bags. The porters also aren't that great at opening the doors so might as well not be there.  That aside, it is a lovely location with easy access to Venice, Lido and the rest of the lagoon. It is directly on the (sloooowww) Blu route from the airport but don't be cheap: pay the extra for a water taxi and arrive like James Bond.
