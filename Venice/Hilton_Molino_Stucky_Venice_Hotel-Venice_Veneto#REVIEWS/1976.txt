Last stay of the 16 day trip and we were happy to splurge after many days on the road and in different hotels.  I will say upfront my experience with Hilton chain has been hit and miss, but I was willing to give the chain another go.  Its not cheap and so the expectations are there.  We booked two rooms ( one with a view for adults) and one with courtyard view for kids ( 3 aged 17, 12 and 9) spending an average $650 AUD a night per room for a 2 night stay.  There is no parking on the island so you have to park and then catch a ferry/water taxi over - suggest doing some research beforehand as its not easy to trek all the luggage from the car park and its not easy to see where or what to catch.
