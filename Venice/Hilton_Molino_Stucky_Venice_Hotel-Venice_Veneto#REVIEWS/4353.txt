Stayed here at the end of April for 5 nights. Arrived by train (italotreno) from Rome where we just disembarked after. 15 night transatlantic cruise....just stepped out of the station and into a water taxi straight to the hotel...rather pricey but no hassle.Arrived there to be met quayside by hotel porters and didn't touch our luggage till we were in our lovely hotel room. The room was quite magnificent with soaring high ceilings,beautiful bed linens and a superb bathroom with large tub and separate glass shower.....all quite luxurious. The hotel offers a free water shuttle to Zattere and to Piazza San Marco which we found very convenient so never had to use the crowded vaporettos. We also had a free trip by taxi to Murano provided by the hotel.
