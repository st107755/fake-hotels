We booked 1 stay here using Hilton points, and we were not disappointed. We used a bus and boat shuttle to get here from the airport. Once we arrived by boat, it was a short walk to the hotel. There were several areas with steps, so beware if you have heavy luggage or need assistance with walking. The hotel was very nice. Our room had a canal view. The bed was comfortable, and the room was large. The breakfast buffet that we received for free was the largest and most delicious buffet I have ever been to. We also had drinks at the rooftop bar which had a fantastic view of Venice at night. We did use the hotel boat shuttle which runs several times an hour to the city center. This service is automatically added to your bill at the end of your stay.
