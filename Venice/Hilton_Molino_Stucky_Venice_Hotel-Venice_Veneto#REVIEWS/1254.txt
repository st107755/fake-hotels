Loved this hotel! It is very nice (and expensive, but so was everything else in Venice).  They have convenient shuttles that take you to and pick you up from the st Marco piazza which was very convenient since there's no way off the Giudeca island without a boat. The rooftop pool was great and has panoramic views of Venice.
