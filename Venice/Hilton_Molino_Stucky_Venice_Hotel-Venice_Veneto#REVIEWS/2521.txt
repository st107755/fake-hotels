From the moment we arrived by the hotel shuttle  boat we were made welcome by David on the reception desk. He ensured that we had a superb suite overlooking the canal. Lovely food and service, if a little on the expensive side, but it suited us well at the time. The boat service to and from Venice, St Marks Square, around 15 minutes is again excellent and regular throughout the day and evening.
