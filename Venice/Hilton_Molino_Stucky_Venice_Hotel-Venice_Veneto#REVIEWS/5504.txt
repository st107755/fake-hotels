Had a pleasant stay with my wife the weekend after attending a conference. I had some free nights with Hilton Honours so I used these otherwise I may have chosen a hotel closer to the centre of Venice. The only to reach the hotel is by water taxi (not a surprise some may say in Venice) but you can find hotels and be able to walk from the railway station to most places in Venice but only if you travel light. The Hilton has its own scheduled water taxi to two points closer to the heart of Venice, this service is free of charge we visited in October so the hotel wasn’t crowded but I guess this taxi service could get very busy during the summer months.
