We recently stayed at this hotel for 3 nights prior to leaving on a cruise.  We got a great rate through Hotwire.  Upon arrival, we were given a chance to purchase the buffet breakfast which wasn't included in the Hotwire price.  While the buffet seemed expensive, it was far and away the best buffet breakfast we've had in our European travels.  It was more like an American breakfast buffet with scrambled eggs, sausages, bacon, etc., in addition to the usual European fare of cereals, cold-cuts, hard boiled eggs and juices.  Our room, on a corner overlooking the canal couldn't have been nicer - nice size, comfortable bed, over-all a good experience.
