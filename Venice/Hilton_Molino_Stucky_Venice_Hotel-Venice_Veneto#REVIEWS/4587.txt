This is a great option for families.  The rooms are larger, like American hotels.  Great concierge and service.  We were traveling as 2 adults and 3 children (ages 14, 11, 8) and slept comfortably in an Executive Suite with King bed, large pullout sofa, and an extra roll-away bed.  There were 1 1/2 baths in the suite.  Kitchen, too, although we did not need it.  The hotel has a frequent free shuttle from Guidecca (hotel location) to the either Zaterre or San Marco island.  We used this every day!  It is also a quick walk to the public water boat (vaporetto) stop...Palanca.  I highly recommend this hotel for families and would stay again!  There is an outdoor pool, but was too cold when we were there.
