We spent two nights here in July.   The rooms were super with fantastic views toward Venice.  The hotel provides transport to/from the main island regularly though we booked taxis as we had an elderly relative with us.  The actual amenities at the hotel were excellent but, it was woefully understaffed in all service areas.  We arrived and sat in the main bar area awaiting the rooms to be readied.  No one came to ask if we wanted drinks.  Eventually, we went to the bar and were told to take a seat and someone would come to us.  Ten minutes passed, still no drink.  Next morning at breakfast we were shown through to the lovely large dining area but to a table covered with the previous guest's dishes.
