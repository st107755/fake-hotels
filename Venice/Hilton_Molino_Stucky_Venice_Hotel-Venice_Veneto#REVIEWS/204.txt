This is a lovely hotel. Big rooms, friendly staff and a swimmingpool with great view. You should deffinetly book this hotel. Here are some tips:  Parking: park your car at Tronchetto parking. No reservation needed. 21€ per day. The hotel will give you a 20% discount voucher.   Arrive at the hotel: 50-100 meter from the parking you can take a public boat for 7,5€ per person which takes you 5-10 min of walking from the hotel. We were so lucky that they stopped right outside the hotel.   Go to the center from the hotel: The hotel has its own boat that takes you from the hotel to San Marco every 30 min. It costs 8,5€ per person and then you can use it as many times as you want. Great service!  We booked an executive room with access  to the executive lounge.
