My partner took me away for a romantic 4 day break to Venice. The Molino Stucky is pretty much the last stop on the taxi service from the airport - seemed to take forever but when we arrived, everything looked really nice - very clean, nice big bar area attached to reception and a lovely rooftop pool and bar area - although we never got to use this as it was always full by 10am with people that appeared to be from the cruise ships rather than hotel guests! We decided to eat in one of the hotel restaurants on the first night - DON'T bother.  The food was very nice but the wine was astronomically priced - £45 for a bottle of house white!!  Every other meal during our stay was much cheaper and much better quality.
