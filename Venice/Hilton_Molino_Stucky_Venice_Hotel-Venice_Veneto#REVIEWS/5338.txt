This is an above-average Hilton with some extra amenities.  Great value for the luxury-like quality if you don't mind a less convenient location. I would categorize this is a business hotel with some nice extras that made it more personal/leisurely (i.e., excellent Philip Thomas Roth bath products and beautiful rooftop bar with excellent views).  In short, if you are looking for a "Western-style" hotel, this is perfect. If you are looking for an authentic, typical Italian or Venetian-style hotel/palazzo, you will be disappointed.  Our room was right along the water, so we had great views of Venice in the morning.  Excellent service all around.  Beautiful, roomy, clean rooms.
