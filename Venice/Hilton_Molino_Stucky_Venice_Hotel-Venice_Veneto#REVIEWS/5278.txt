We had just come off a cruise and chose to stay in Venice for a few extra days.  We decided on the Molino Stucky as one always knows the quality they will receive from Hilton.  We were definitely not disappointed in our choice.  We had a beautiful, quiet room that overlooked the city and the dock where the Hilton's private launch picked up and discharged passengers.  The launch ran on a specific timetable and could be relied upon to be punctual.  It was quite comfortable.  The concierge arranged a trip for us to via a private water taxi to Murano for a tour of one of the Murano Glass Factories.  It was a totally enjoyable experience.  I highly recommend the Molino Stucky.
