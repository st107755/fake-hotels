My husband and I traveled to Italy and France in September of 2011.  We flew from the U.S.A. and landed in Venice.  We took the water taxi (linea blu) from the airport to the Hilton Molino Stuckey.  The Hilton was nearly the last stop on the water taxi.  I think it was approximately a 45 minute boat ride with 5-7 stops before we arrived at the Hilton.  This was about the last thing I wanted to do after a long flight and the time change, but the price was too good to pass up.  A private water taxi was something like 120 euro and we couldn’t justify it.  I think our tickets were something along the lines of 10 euro each.  The taxi ride was quite beautiful as we were able to take in the many sites of Venice via water.  It was amazing and my first taste of Europe.
