Fantastic hotel away from the crowds and yet close to everything thanks to the free shuttle boat. Large clean rooms, well decorated and confortable. Housekeeping doing a great job except for the replenishment of the hospitality tray or the complimentary bottled water (is their anybody checking?). Beautiful bar and excellent service at the top floor for a relaxing night cap with Venice at your feet. There is no "executive lounge" as such but only part of the restaurant/bar by the lobby. It is far from being relaxing with people litterally throwing themselves at the small dishes as soon as they were brought. The hotel should consider having a separate executive lounge for more relaxing environment.
