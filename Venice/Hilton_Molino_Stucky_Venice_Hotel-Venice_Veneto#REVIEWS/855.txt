We stayed here for 3 nights, rooms were good and breakfast fantastic. The shuttle to and from the mainland were frequent and convenient. I would say standards were what to be expected from the Hilton brand. My only complaint was the noise from ongoing building work outside our hotel room - after spending long days exploring the city and late nights in the skyline bar, sometimes you don't want to be woken by drilling and banging from 7am. Presumably the hotel manager can be seen walking around at breakfast making conversation with guests - we mentioned this to him and he said the hotel was full with a doctor's conference - almost as if to say there was nothing he could do. I would have appreciated prior warning of the building work or even an apology.
