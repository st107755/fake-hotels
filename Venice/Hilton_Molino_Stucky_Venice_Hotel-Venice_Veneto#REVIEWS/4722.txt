This was my first stay here, did not know what to expect after reading some reviews. Been to Venice before and had used the water taxi's before but this was going to be my first time to take one to a hotel. About 2 weeks prior to my stay I get a e-mail from Annimi with the options I had to get to this hotel, no problems. I took the city bus to Piazza Roma and then the ACTV shuttle to Palanca, then a short 10 minute walk. This hotel is large but they did a great job of restoring it. Very classy and romantic, the rooms are great,  they gave me the room with a view of the canal. As a Diamond they did give us the option to eat in the restaurant or Exec Lounge, the restaurant had a wide range for breakfast. The concierge was very helpful as was the rest of the staff.
