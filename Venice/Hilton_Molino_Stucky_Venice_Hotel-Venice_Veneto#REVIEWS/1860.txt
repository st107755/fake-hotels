In general we loved our stay. The girls at the reception were very nice. Both rooms (yeah, stayed at two different rooms during our visit) were quite spacious with high ceilings. Bathrooms were quite clean. Buffet breakfast was reasonable with a lot of varieties, but for freshly cooked items, i.e. omelette, you need to order from the waiting staff. We were told once that it took 30 minutes so never tried it. The executive lounge was not very big given the size of the hotel. The roof top swimming pool was amazing. They serve Nutella ice cream there, as well as the Nutella Stand in the courtyard. The shuttle boats were frequent enough with a fee of €4.5 per person per day.   The only downside of the hotel is sound proofing.
