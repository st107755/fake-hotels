Located on the island of Giudecca, the Hilton Molino Stucky is away from the hustle and bustle of the main island. However - it is only a v. short boat trip (free from outside the hotel main entrance) away from the centre of all things that are good about Venice. The first boat stop directly across from the hotel is within a couple of minutes walk from the Academia area - the free shuttle also takes you to the prime location of St Marks Square.  As far as the hotel is concerned we arrived early and, not unexpectedly our room wasn't ready so we took a stroll along side the canal - a variety of shops and restaurants are on hand and within easy walking distance.
