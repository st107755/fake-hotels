We stayed here for 3 nights in July 2018. Beautiful hotel, lovely staff, gorgeous rooms. We had use of the executive lounge which gives you breakfast, snacks and soft drinks all day, with 2 hours of alcoholic drinks and more substantial hot and cold snacks/food in the evenings. It’s nice to be away from the busy hubbub of the main land, the roof top pool is awesome, the barman up there was so friendly and took great pride in mixing his cocktails to a high standard. There is a spa and various bars and restaurants, plenty of choice. The water bus provided by the hotel is (I forget the exact amount something like) 15 euros each which covers the whole time you are a guest and use as many times as you like, and that takes you to 2 stops across the water to the main land.
