I had, prior to booking a short break, looked at tripadvisor for tips and locations with regard to choosing a hotel. I was not disappointed. We arrived by water taxi to the pier where a bellhop was waiting o collect our bags and show us to reception. Check in was quick and efficient and so we headed up to check our room. Having only booked a short break I upgraded to the deluxe view room, sadly this was not on the hotel front however I was not disappointed. 7 floors up and on the side of the hotel, 5 windows looked out with a breathtaking view over the skyline of the Island of Venice. The room itself was not large but was very clean, a lovely bathroom and a deluxe size bed (well we werent going to be spending much time in the room so id consider this ideal).
