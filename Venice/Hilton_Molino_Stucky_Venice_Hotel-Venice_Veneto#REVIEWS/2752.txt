We have just returned from 3 nights  here having got a great deal through British airways. This was the perfect place to stay, all staff were helpful and room and bathroom was large and very clean. Breakfast was included in our stay and had a good selection. We never had a problem with the shuttle boat being too busy and it always arrived at the scheduled times so there's no need to be getting private taxis which can be very expensive. The pool area is lovely but a little small, it gets quite busy but the longest we had to wait for someone to leave and get beds was 10 minutes. The only criticism I have would be that drinks and food is very expensive around the pool and rest of the hotel, €12 for a glass of coke and fanta.
