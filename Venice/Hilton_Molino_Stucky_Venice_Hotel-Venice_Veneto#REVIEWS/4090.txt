Me and my wife ended our recent cruise to the Mediterranean by staying one night at this magnificent former mill on the island of Giudecca. It was easy to spot the huge building far away while the cruise ship was slowly entering Venice and eventually passing the hotel along the Giudecca canal on its way to the port. It was an easy one stop way from the cruise terminal to the stop right at the hotel by Alilaguna blue line boat. The hotel has its own complimentary shuttle to the Zattere stop across the canal and a second one at San Marco. There is also a vaporetto stop nearby. In addition to Venice proper (our 4th time) it was interesting to see this side of the Giudecca canal. We had a nice cup of coffee in a small corner café admiring the sights across the canal at dusk.
