The moment you arrive in the Hilton Molino Stucky you are aware of the standard of the staff, very efficient but still friendly. The executive room was big, well furnished and very comfortable. I might recommend that more attention is given to cleaning items like the chandeliers which looked as if they had been missed. We had dinner in the aroma dining room, a selection of tables at the end of the lounge bar. It lacked any atmosphere and given the size of the hotel I would recommend that they allocate appropriate space for dining. However the food served was excellent. Breakfast is the only place where the hotel fails. The breakfast is served in an institutional type area with the food laid out in another room which make serving oneself a bit like being back in school.
