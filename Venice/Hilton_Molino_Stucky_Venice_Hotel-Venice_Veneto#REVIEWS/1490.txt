We enjoyed our stay at the hotel and found it up to usual Hilton standards that still had a touch of Venice , rooms were decorated tastefully and had the basic necessities. Courtesy water shuttle to st Marco square for 6 euro per person per stay which was ideal and a nice touch ! Gorgeous roof top bar with friendly staff adding a cool vibe to our trip . The hotels roof top pool was not open at this time of year and access to the spa costs 20 euro for 2 hours unless you are a gold Hilton honours member .
