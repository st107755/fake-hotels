We stayed at the Molino Stucky Hilton with 2 adults and 2 children after a Mediterranean cruise. To get to the hotel from the cruise port you have to take a shuttle boat that is operated by a separate company. The fee for this is 8€ plus a additional surcharge if you have over sized luggage, tickets can be purchased in ticket booths located at the end of the pier before you exit the port, the Hilton is the first stop on the blue line. Since we disembarked the ship at 8:00am the room was not ready therefore we had to store our luggage, the hotel has a secure area that you can store your luggage in. The hotel staff delivered our luggage to the room as soon as it became  available.
