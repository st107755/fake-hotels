We were fortunate enough to be invited to a friend's wedding in Venice, we chose to stay at this Hilton because it's easy to get to, the rates are cheap compared to 5 star hotels in the city, and it removes you from the busy-ness when you want.   It's on a little island called Guidecca - a lovely peaceful place with some exceptional restaurants (including one of the three at the Hilton, and the amazing Majer). There is a water bus that runs either twice or three times an hour, depending on the time of day, from 8:20am until 11:45pm. It shuttles you to San Marco, a trip that takes about 15 minutes.   Our suite was dual aspect, spacious and clean. The bed was large and very comfy.
