Forgive the title but discovering the hotel has normal coat hangers and not those fiddly things with no actual hook was a great bonus! Spent 4 nights here in April and it's a fantastic place to stay.Was fortunate to have a room overlooking the canal and could actually lie in bed watching the boat pass by set against the backdrop of beautiful Venice. We had a water taxi direct from the airport to hotel and I would not recommend any other way. When you see people lugging suitcases through the narrow streets of Venice and onto water taxis you realise that it was the right decision to book a direct boat trip. The taxi takes you from the airport direct to the door of the hotel.
