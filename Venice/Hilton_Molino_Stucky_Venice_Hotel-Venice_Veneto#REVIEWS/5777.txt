This hotel is absolutely beautiful. I'll start with the gripes to get them out of the way. The hotel staff was not as friendly or professional as I would have expected for such an upscale place. The concierge was distracted and seemed annoyed at our questions. When checking in, one front desk staff became irritated with us and raised her voice a little. To their credit however, when the one staff member became annoyed, her coworker took over and was friendly. There is no free internet access. One other thing, the hotel is large and has an odd layout.  On to the good things. The water shuttle that went from the hotel to the main island of Venice was fast, frequent, clean and convenient.
