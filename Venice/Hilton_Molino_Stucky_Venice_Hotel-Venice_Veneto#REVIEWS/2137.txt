Had a fantastic stay at the Hilton for our short 3 day trip to Venice. Were upgraded to the Presidential Suite for the first night, which was incredible!! Massive suite with own gym, sauna and jacuzzi and lovely views across Venice, and our very own private elevator! The other two nights were in the Tower Suite which was again very comfortable. Lovely breakfast every morning, the staff at breakfast was very welcoming and very friendly. The free water shuttle to and from San Marco runs every 20 minutes and was very convenient. Felt nice to come back to the hotel away from the crowds and relax at the room in the evening. Had dinner at Bacaromi restaurant one night, which was good, but didn't have too many vegetarian options.
