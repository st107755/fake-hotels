Billed as a 5 star hotel, but not quiet up to scratch. I actually am a fairly easy going person but I was aggravated enough to want to write a review. I know this hotel has 100's of rave reviews and one review won't make a difference, but here's my two cents worth anyway.   The location is not too bad, but pretty far from the airport. Don't ask for advice at the airport. They put you on a water bus that takes around 100 minutes. Once you're there, it's not to bad to get around.   The problem is that there's nothing else around the hotel. It's totally dead for a city that has so much going on.   As a result you may wish to relax on the sofas in the lobby and have a snack or some room service. Well don't bother.
