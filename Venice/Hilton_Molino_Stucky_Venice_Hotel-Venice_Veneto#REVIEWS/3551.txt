Stayed at this hotel during our honeymoon and my wife and I were very impressed.  Hotel is not located on the main island in Venice, but that’s a plus not a minus.  Upon check-in, my Hilton Gold Status was recognized.  We were upgraded to a room facing the canal and provided with access to their executive lounge.   The lounge was one of the nicer Hilton lounges I have visited, offering breakfast and snacks all day with cocktails available in the evening.  Our room was very nice with a unique charm, including exposed wood beams and a chandelier…not your standard Hilton room.    The hotel provides frequent complementary shuttles to the main island and is also a stop for 3rd party shuttles going to the airport and cruise ship terminal.
