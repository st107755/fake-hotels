I stayed for 5 nights with my wife in the late spring this year and the stay was fantastic. As a Diamond level frequent traveler, I was initially given a great room overlooking the canal on the front of the building, and then upgraded to a tower suite when one became available. Mr Rodoni and staff were very responsive on site as well as by email.  I have seem other reviews indicating dissatisfaction with the breakfast buffet- we thought it was excellent- food selection and quality, staff responsiveness.   The executive lounge while not the most spacious was very acceptable and the service was very good. Especially given that breakfast was offered in the restaurant the lounge was fine.  Dedicated water shuttle was on time and a quick trip to the square.
