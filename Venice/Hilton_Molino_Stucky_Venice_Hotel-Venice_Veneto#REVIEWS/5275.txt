I booked a suite with a view for my 20th wedding anniversary. The hotel staff do their job in a professional manner. The hotel is clean and it was clearly rebuilt with quality in mind. The downstairs lobby area is very roomy although finding a trash can is a feat. The mini-fridge has sensors for everything.. if you move it, you bought it and no extra room for anything. The water busses stop about a mile on either side of the hotel and you have to walk to get there. The hotel has it's own water bus that goes to 2 sites besides the hotel and is fairly easy to catch. The hotel is so large it has it's own map. I expected a much larger room than what I was given and was disappointed with the over all experience.
