Not knowing Venice, I decided to "play safe" and book the Hilton as a known standard. I was impressed by the building (an old flour mill) as I approached by Water Taxi (Euro 50 from near where I dropped off the hire car).  Shame that it is not on the main island, but I knew that when I booked, and I was pleased to hear that there is a free shuttle boat to and fro the main part of town.  The problem started when I was shown to my room. The smell as we got into the elevator just got worse when we got to our floor and then entered the allocated room. I know Venice is built on the water, but other areas of the hotel did not smell like this, although the staff claimed not to notice. Really?
