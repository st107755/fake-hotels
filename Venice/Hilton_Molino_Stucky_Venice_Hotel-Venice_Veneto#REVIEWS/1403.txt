We stayed here the night before a cruise departure. The hotel is on an island across a canal from the main Venice island. It is much less crowded and very peaceful as opposed to the touristy area of Venice. There is a free Hilton water taxi that will take you across the canal.  You can walk along the waterfront which has plenty of restaurants and bars. There is a grocery store called the Coop a few blocks away, but note is closes at 7:45 pm.  We received a Lifetime Diamond upgrade to a wonderful corner suite which included a living room, dining room and powder room.  The executive lounge was nice but very narrow almost all tables on seated two. As we there was four in our party we went to the Skyline bar on the rooftop which was very nice and offered light snakes.
