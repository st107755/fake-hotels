Some of the negative reviews on here really stuck in my mind when I arrived but after staying here for a few nights, I can say that I did not experience any of the negative things that others have written about.   First of all the complimentary shuttle boat is fantastically convenient. But be wary that it can be difficult to spot which is the correct spot. What I did find strange is that they do not say that you can catch a shuttle boat from the airport straight to the hotel (only takes about 1hr 30mins). The staff were all very friendly. At reception we were told that we could have breakfast at a discounted rate of €15pp as opposed to €30pp but what they did not say that this only applies for the first day that you stay.
