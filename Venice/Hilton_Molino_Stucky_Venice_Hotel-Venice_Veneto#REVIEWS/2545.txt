It's very simple : until the Hilton Molino organises a real permanent shuttle ( meaning going back and forth all the time ) between Zattere ( San Marco can be less regular may be ) and the hotel, guests will feel isolated from "main" Venice.  You don't want to have a schedule, you don't want to risk the existing shuttle being full, you don't want to try to understand how the public boats work when you are there for a few days. That's it.  The hotel in itself is nice, good service, good gym, nice room etc….. happy with all this .  I would have canceled my stay had I not made my booking via a travel agent. I have to stay for 4 more days and put up with this isolation feeling, shared by many of the other guests I spoke too.
