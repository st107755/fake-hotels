Ah Venice in August. It’s hot, it’s crowded, and it’s expensive. But a stay at the Hilton Molino Stucky is a great way to beat all of the above. Service is great. We were warmly welcomed on our anniversary and received sweet biscuits and proseco. The concierge is wonderful. They can direct you easily and tell you the best way to get places. The 7.5€ per person per stay for the water shuttle is well worth it. It’s a 15 min ride to St Marks and in the evening they have a stop 5 minutes away. Getting there is best done by private water taxi. It’s either a 30 min boat ride to your hotel directly from airport or an hour and forty min bout ride on a crowded bus. (Like I said, Venice is expensive) our room A422 was very roomy by European standards.
