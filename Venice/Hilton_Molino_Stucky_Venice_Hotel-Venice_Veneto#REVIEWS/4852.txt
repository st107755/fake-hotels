This is the second time my wife and I have stayed at this hotel and it's every bit as excellent this time as it was the first.  Not being on the San Marco side is a huge plus, i.e., no crowds.  The hotel provides it's own "vaparetto" that runs a couple of times an hour to get you across the canal, when you absolutely have to get to the other side.  I'd give it's location a plus, far more than a minus in this regard.     I think someone at registration erred slightly in our favor when we checked in.  We were given a suite for the entire five days of our stay!    And, if you start your day off grumpy before your breakfast, the receptionist at the buffet breakfast, Anna, will certainly default your attitude to a much more pleasant one.
