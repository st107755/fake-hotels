My wife and I stayed here Sept 3-7 pre cruise. Lovely hotel across from San Marco's.The main part of Venice was easily accessed from the hotel by shuttle boat which is free and runs every 20 minutes with stops at Zaterre and San Marco's . Hotel was formerly a flour mill and had framed historical photos on the walls of its previous life,fascinating.  The room was very nice,warm with character.Has a coffee/tea maker and in closet safe. Unfortunately we did not get a room with a view of Venice but the room was nice and quiet with a view of an inner courtyard. Also has 2 matching clothes closets,both with drawers,so plenty of room for clothes. Nice not to cram clothes in drawers. Bed was firm yet comfortable and the best pillows we have come across in our travels.
