We stayed at this fabulous hotel for 4 nights on a family break to Venice. We transferred from the Airport using the Alilaguna waterbus service (blue line). It took 1hr 30mins and was 15€ each. We arrived at night and the hotel was lit up & looked amazing. Welcoming staff on check-in  & very efficient. Our room 371 was very quiet in fact the only noise that woke us was the water taxi drivers chatting to each other. The complimentary wifi in the room for Hilton Honours members was really good. We loved the buffet breakfast there really is something for everyone! It certainly filled our teenager so that was great. We visited Venice during Carnival so it was quite busy. The hotel run a water bus to mainland Venice for a one off charge of 6€ per guest.
