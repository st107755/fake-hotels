The Hilton Molino Stucky is very nice hotel. The hotel complex is very big and new. The rooms were clean, offered a good amount of space, and the bathroom was very spacious. Our room was located in the front of the hotel (although the hotel tried to take me out of the room even though I‘m a HiltonHonors member) so we had a view of the main waterway and Venice. We didn’t eat at the hotel but we did go to the SkyBar twice for a drink and that was a good spot to start the night. For your stay if your looking for a newer hotel in Venice with bars and places to eat this would  be a good choice. If you want the smaller European hotel atmosphere with the free breakfast you’ll want to stay somewhere else.
