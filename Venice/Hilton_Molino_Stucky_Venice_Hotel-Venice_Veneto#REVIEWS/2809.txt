My boyfriend and I arrived at the Molino Stucky for a one night stay following a cruise on the Celebrity Constellation. We took a water taxi (EU 70) from the port, expensive but we wanted to get there quickly and it took around 6 to 7 minutes.   We arrived at around 0930 so our room was understandably not yet ready. Our suitcases were handed in to the drop off area and we headed to the rooftop pool. The views over Venice were stunning and the area was spotless. A lovely rooftop pool/bar area. There were lots of loungers and given that we had turned up early found one just down the steps from the pool area.   Each sun lounger has a small movable shade which is handy if you want to lie and read or just keep the sun out your eyes in general.
