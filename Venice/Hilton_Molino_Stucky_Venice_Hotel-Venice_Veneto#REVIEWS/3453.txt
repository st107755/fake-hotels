Wow! Stunning hotel. Very big... biggest Hilton I have stayed in. Everything was perfect from the beautiful pool area overlooking Venice to the fabulous view from our room. We stayed in an executive room and also had access to the executive lounge which was lovely after a day walking around Venice. The hotel has a lovely feel to it as it seems to be an old warehouse and there are features throughout the hotel which set it aside from other Hiltons.  Shuttle boat takes you across to piazza san marco every 30 minutes and takes less than 5 minutes to get across. Lovely touch in the room - a mini bottle of Bellini which my friend and I enjoyed whilst watching the sunset over Venice.
