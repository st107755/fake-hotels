On the whole this hotel provides a very good service and facilities.  Our 1st room was disappointing & noisy, but the 2nd room was perfect.  The breakfast in the buffet area on the ground floor is very good, with a very good service.  Aromi restaurant was excellent. We recommend it.  The water shuttle is fine. No problem.  Wifi is available free of charge in public areas but not in rooms.  The real issue of this hotel is with the Executive Lounge (for those who book executive rooms). The executive lounge is only a restricted area of the noisy lobby-bar of the hotel.
