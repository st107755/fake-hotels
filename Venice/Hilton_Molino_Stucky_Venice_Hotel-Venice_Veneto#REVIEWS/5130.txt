Really enjoyed this hotel. Worked out at €300 per night but we got the upgrade as Hilton Diamond members. This is actually very good value in Venice where the accommodation is expensive.  The Exec lounge is a bit strange - Hilton normally put them on the upper floors with great views but this one is a partitioned area of the downstairs lobby/ bar, accessible through a gate. Nonetheless the food, wine and service were fine.  The room was excellent and the hotel is very well managed. this is not a character property, although has some history associated with the lovely building. The problem with many Hiltons around the world is that they are large, lack cultural integration and you could be anywhere in the United States.
