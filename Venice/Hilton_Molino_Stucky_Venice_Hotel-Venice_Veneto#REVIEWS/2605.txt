Had 3 nights here, the bed is amazing so comfortable. Lovely room with fantastic facilities and bathroom. Hotel itself is very large, no free wifi in rooms which is a big shame, but you can sit in the lobby to use free wifi there. Free ferry to San Marco is appreciated but allow extra time at peak times of the day as it gets used a lot. The breakfast is excellent, however do yourself a favour and don't eat at the bar. We ordered a hamburger for $40 AUD and it was overcooked and tasteless and not worth the big price tag. The other negative was getting evicted from the rooftop pool bar at sunset due to a 'private' booking. This happened 2 nights of our time there, the views are amazing from this area so we were disappointed in the staff having to do this.
