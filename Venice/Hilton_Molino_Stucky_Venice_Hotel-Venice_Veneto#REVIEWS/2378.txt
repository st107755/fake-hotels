I have read the previous reviews regarding difficulty of getting to the hotel.  Fortunately we have been to Venice several times and know our way around.  You can take a water taxi or the Aliguna from the airport if you want to arrive by water. The blue line Aliguna stops right at the front door of the hotel but does not operate 24 hours so check the timetable on the web.  They had stopped running when we arrived. They are currently upgrading the airport and what was always a fair walk to water transport is now much longer so, for the first time, we opted to take the bus from the airport to Piazzale Roma. This leaves directly outside arrivals, costs 8 Euros and takes about 20 minutes.
