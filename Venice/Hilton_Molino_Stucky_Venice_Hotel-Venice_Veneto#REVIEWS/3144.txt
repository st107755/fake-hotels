We booked this hotel after staying in a Hilton in Iceland which was excellent, but overall was left a little disappointed in this hotel.  The adverts on Expedia showed a fantastic roof top pool, but failed to mention it's only open in summer months and you can't even go out onto the roof. To be fair it does say this on their website (which we didn't think to check) but would have been nice to know this in advance as this was the primary reason we booked this hotel.  Stayed in room 161. Was mediocre at best. Room was clean and tidy but that's about it really. Bath was too small in length and depth, aircon didn't seem to do anything at all and the pillows were shockingly thin.
