I booked this holiday to Venice last Feb in order to stay over Christmas and celebrate my husbands 50th birthday. From start to finish the service and quality we experienced were fantastic and i would not hesitate to go back and stay. Due to some of the negative comments i was a little worried as i wanted this to be really special for my husband and we had never spent Christmas abroad before. We were upgraded to room 602 which had a stunning view of the Venice skyline, canal and distant mountains. The room was perfect, very clean and we sat with the windows open every evening watching the boats go by. The shuttle was great always on time. There is a public boat about a four minute walk away uf u didnt want to stick to the hotel timetable.
