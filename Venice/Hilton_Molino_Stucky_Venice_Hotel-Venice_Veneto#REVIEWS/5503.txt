We stayed at the hotel for 3 nights in September for our anniversary. The hotel buildings are certainly impressive and we were made to feel special on arrival at the hotel, with it impressive reception. The renovation has been very sympathetic to the building's former use, with lots of exposed wood and steel beams, and I cannot fault it at all in that respect. As HHonors gold members we were given use of the executive lounge on the ground floor which was excellent, serving a wide choice of snacks and canapes throughout the day and also a good choice of complimentary beverages. We could quite easily have gotten by on the food and drink in there alone!  The hotel did have one or two downsides however.
