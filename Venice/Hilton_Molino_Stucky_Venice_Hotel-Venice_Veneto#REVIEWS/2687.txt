My sister and I backpacked across Europe and we chose this hotel as our place to crash while in Venice. We were SO HAPPY that we chose this hotel. From Venice, you take a little boat over to the hotel (which I was a wee bit terrified of as it's a bit rocky), which adds to the whole adventure.  Once there, the staff was amazing and our room was incredible. It had a beautiful window that opened nice and wide, which overlooked a little canal and some charming buildings. The room was beautifully decorated and really, really comfortable. For two backpackers, we couldn't be happier!  The bar in the hotel is excellent and we met some other travellers while there. The entire hotel's decor is amazing and the best part are the views from the restaurant on the top floor.
