Just back from 3 days with my wife. We were upgraded to a deluxe room for 25th wedding anniversary which overlooked the canal and the room was top quality. Staff were very attentive. We skipped breakfast at 30 Euros per head, not because we could not afford it but we wanted to get out into the city. Turn right and walk 100 yards and you can get a espresso and chocolate croissant for 6 Euros. The rooftop pool was amazing and made our stay in Venice. Never overcrowded and with glorious views. We were a little disappointed by the price of drinks in skyline bar (10 Euros for a small glass of wine, 8 Euros for a beer, 12 Euros G&T) and there really is  no where else to go if you want a late night drink.
