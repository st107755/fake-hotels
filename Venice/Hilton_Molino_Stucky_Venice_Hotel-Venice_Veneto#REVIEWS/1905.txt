Where do I start with this review?  Well I will start from the train station as we traveled from Florence by train to Venice.  Now I was not going to pay the high prices for a private taxi/transfer as you can buy a single for €7.50 from the station which takes you to the Guidecca/Palanca stop.  The instructions given by the hotel are spot on - you get to Palanca and walk about 500m and you are there.  The shuttle is about 30 mins long and is pretty much hassle free.  Not sure if you can pre purchase tickets but there are machines around but there can be a queue.  Check in was simple and a little tip, get an Honors card, you get free wifi in the room and we managed to get a special rate due to this so it will be worth it in the future.
