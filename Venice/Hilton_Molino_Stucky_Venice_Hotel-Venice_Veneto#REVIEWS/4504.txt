Brilliant hotel away from the tourist masses.  A calm across the water from Venice with easy access to all the sights and sounds on the main island.    We were given a room on the 6th floor with a view of the main Giudecca Canal.  Quiet, comfortable and with all ammmenities needed.   My only comment is that the lighting could be impoved to make the roomer brighter when needed.  I have seen on trip advisor complaints about rude and off-hand staff.  The lady on check-in was one of the most helpful I have ever come across in any hotel.  All the staff were helpful and cheerful.  The breakfast was good.  We did not eat in the hotel for lunch or dinner as we were touring Venice.  Prices for food were high but no more so than most of the establishments in the city.
