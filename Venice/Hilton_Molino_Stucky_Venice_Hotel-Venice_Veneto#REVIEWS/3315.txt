My wife and I stayed at the Molino Stucky for three nights in November.  The hotel is a converted flour mill and is a bit of a maze, however the occasional disorientation we experienced was worth the historic feel of the property.  Getting to and from the train station was easy, please note that the non-private boat to the airport takes about 1.5 hours.  Overall, we enjoyed our stay with a couple of minor exceptions.  Despite it being the low-season, the hotel seemed quite busy.  I think the issue is that the common areas on not particularly laid out well and quickly feel crowded.  This particularly true in the "Executive Lounge" which is located next to reception, around the lobby bar.
