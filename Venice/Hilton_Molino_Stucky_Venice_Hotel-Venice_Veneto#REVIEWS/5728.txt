We stayed for two nights pre cruise and were not disappointed.  Our executive room which  overlooked the Guidecca Canal towards San Marco was large with very high ceilings that added to the space.  It was very well appointed and comfortable.  We only ate breakfast and this was of the usual Hilton high standard with the added bonus of the availability of Prosecco which was lovely to toast each other the morning of our wedding anniversary.  It was also very nice to have the option of eating breakfast outside in the gardens and we noticed that many of the restaurants in the hotel had an outside option.  The service we experienced overall was very good however on our last night after waiting for over 20 minutes in the nearly empty rooftop bar for a drink we gave up!
