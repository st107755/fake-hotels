Stayed for 4 nights in an Executive room with Executive Lounge access. We took a private water taxi from the airport to the hotel, which has a flat charge of €130, and although quite pricey was well worth it if you’ve not done it before. The hotel is amazing from the outside. Check in was good, and although I had emailed prior to our arrival about upgrade options, this wasn’t replied to. At check in, Anna told us she could ask her manager for specials as it was a special occasion, I asked if I could use points to upgrade but was unable to, so we declined.  The room we were assigned was ok, nothing special.  The Executive lounge was decent with a happy hour between 5 - 7pm every night with snacks too. We had breakfast in the main buffet on the first morning.
