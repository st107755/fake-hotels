No need to duplicate accolades from previous posters.  This was our fourth trip to Venice so we were looking for something that Venice had to offer other than the crowds at St. Marks and Rialto. We stayed three days.  As a Hilton Honors guest, we have been pleased pretty much all over the world with Hiltons where we have stayed.  However, this Molino Stucky has got to be one of the crown jewels for Hilton.  We were given an upgrade to a beautiful executive room with a breathtaking view of Venice across the way and quietly passing cruise ships from various cruise lines.  The area around the hotel is a nice little neighborhood with restaurants/bars right on the water. With a vaporetto stop nearby it took us pretty much anywhere we wanted to go.
