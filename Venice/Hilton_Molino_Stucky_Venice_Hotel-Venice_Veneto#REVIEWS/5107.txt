My husband, my six year old daughter and I just stayed at this lovely hotel. We had adjoining rooms which were enormous, there was a separate living room with dining table and a small kitchen (hot plate, sink fridge) attached to the main bedroom. Decor was lovely, room and bathrooms were immaculately clean. The most amazing aspect about this hotel was the incredible service from the porter to wait staff in the restaurants, reception, housekeeping and even the life guard at the pool. All were courteous and helpful. I have never experienced this level of service outside of the Aman, Mandarin and Four Seasons hotel groups.
