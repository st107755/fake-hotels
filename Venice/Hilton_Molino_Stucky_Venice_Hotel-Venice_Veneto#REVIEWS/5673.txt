Using Hilton Honors points, I stayed here for two nights in April 2009.  Now I'll be using he last of my points to return this April.  Notwithstanding the difficulties mentioned by others with the timing of the shuttle to the hotel, this was by far the best hotel experience I've ever had.  First, the staff greeting was warmly welcoming--not at all officious or snooty as in some other Hiltons (Milan, for example).  I was given an upgrade; I understand that now there is a charge for the upgrade, even for "gold" members, but i'll be satisfied with an interior room.  The bathrooms are a work of art even in the standard rooms (I peeked); the beds are clouds and the decor is bright despite the dark wood, which itself is warm and elegant.
