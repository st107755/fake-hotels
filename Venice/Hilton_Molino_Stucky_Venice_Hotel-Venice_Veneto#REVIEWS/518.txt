The Molino Stucky is a great hybrid of a large conference hotel with a more curio style local flair.  The rooms are large and well appointed.  The staff were friendly and helpful and all services are available.  The rooftop pool is beautiful as is the view and the skyline bar is a nice place to relax -- although a food menu would improve the appeal.  The location is good, away from crowded Piazza San Marco and correspondingly more quiet.  There is a free shuttle water bus between Piazza San Marco and the hotel to help with transportation which can be a complication.
