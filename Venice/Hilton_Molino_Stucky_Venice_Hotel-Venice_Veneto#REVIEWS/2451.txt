We stayed in this hotel for one night after the cruise, as it's in a very neighborhood of a cruise port. It's a massive hotel, with big, comfortable rooms, nice rooftop terrace with swimming pool. Prices of food in hotel restaurant are obviously high - it's Hilton after all. But we walked for a few minutes along the canal and found little restaurant with good Italian food and reasonable prices. Breakfast was one of the best I've ever experienced in any hotel. Plenty of food choices, hot, cold, anything you want. They had fabulous Nutella filled muffins - heaven! Breakfast normally cost I think 30 euro per person, however if you buy it at the check in, they offer it half price, which I think is reasonable for what you get.
