We were so well looked after during our stay for a long weekend. The front desk sorted us a great room with a view of the canal and as Gold Members we got use of the executive lounge which was great. We went in February so a bit cold for the roof top cocktail bar but the waiter brought us blankets and we braved it for an hour. Cocktails were €16 which was a bit steep but the norm for Venice. The hotel runs a free trip to the glass blowing factory, which we went on and it was very good (although you only get to go to the factory that provides the free taxi) The Hotel river bus to St Marks Sq was great and ran regularly until late at a single charge of a few €'s for the duration of your stay charged when you check out.
