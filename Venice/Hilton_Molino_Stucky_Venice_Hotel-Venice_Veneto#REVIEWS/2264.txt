I think this was my 4th or 5th visit to this lovely hotel. It's great being away from St Mark's Square where it gets very hectic, but the free shuttle water ferry runs frequently from the hotel to (close to) St Mark's when you want to go there. Stopping off at Zattare gives the opportunity to walk across the Academia Bridge and round to all the sites as well. The staff truly go out of their way to make everyone feel welcome and special. The rooms are lovely and the food excellent. Only adverse comment I would make is that it's much better from May onwards (only because of the weather) when it's possible to eat outside by the waterside and then (a little later) the pool on the roof (with another great restaurant for lunch and dinner) is also available.
