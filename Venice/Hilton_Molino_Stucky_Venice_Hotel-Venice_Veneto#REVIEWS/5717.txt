The hotel and location are lovely, and the Alialaguna water transport from the airport drops you off right outside (excellent value at  25 euros return trip each). The room was extremely comfortable, with all amenities. Our major drawback was the breakfast, at 40 euros each you somehow expect the best both in terms of food and service. However, we were left to find our own table, and many tables were just piled high with dirty dishes. The food was in another room across the hall, and although there was an hour left of service, many dishes were empty, (although to be fair, when my husband went for food 15 minutes later, a few had been restocked) However, coffee pots were empty and trying to find someone to refill them pretty hard.
