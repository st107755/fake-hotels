My wife and I stayed one night prior to a cruise.  We were skeptical staying outside the city, but the free shuttles ran often enough that this was not a problem at all and we had all the sightseeing time we could handle.  The room was adequately sized, comfortable bed, very clean, quiet and perfect for one night.  We did the buffet breakfast and it was quite good, albeit a bit pricey.  Not exactly an ordinary highlight of one's stay, but we received EXCELLENT service from the Concierge Staff.  Our two large pieces of luggage did not make it onto the plane coming into Venice, and given that we were scheduled to leave on the cruise the next afternoon, there was some amount of panic setting in.
