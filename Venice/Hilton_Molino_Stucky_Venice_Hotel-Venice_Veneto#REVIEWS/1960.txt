My family absolutely loved our stay! The staff is extremely helpful and kind. The place is very clean and beautiful. They have a great selection of food and beverage, also if you are a diamond member they have a lounge just for you with food and drinks for free. Also they offer a shuttle boat the the other side of the water. You do pay for that at the end of your stay. They have a roof top pool and bar which both have breath taking views. I do need to say that the pool deck is very very slippery and they make you take your shoes off. We can't forget that they have a Nutella bar. The bar has a big selection of food with Nutella. There is a cruise port right by the hotel so if you are lucky you may see on pass right by!
