I don't usually comment on other reviewers' writings, but I think the balance needs to be redressed here as the casual reader is NOT getting the full picture.  The hotel boat shuttle runs a free service from Giudecca (an island directly across a big canal from the main part of Venice). Two shuttles an hour operate in off-season, and more in high season. Both shuttles go to Zettere, and one goes on to St Mark's Square. The shuttles run regular as clockwork. If you cannot tell the time, then there is a simple solution - turn right out of the hotel, walk 100 yards down, and there is a vaporetto stop if you are in a desperate hurry.
