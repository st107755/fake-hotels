Recently returned from a 3 night stay and were really glad we chose this hotel! The Hilton shuttle was great but also the vaporetto stop was only a short walk away if the times of the shuttle weren't convenient. The rooftop pool was a lovely place to go mid afternoon after seeing the sights - what a great view and we always managed to get a sunbed. We got the number 5 bus from the airport and then the 4.1 vaporetto from the bus station and it was no problem - and faster & cheaper then the Alilaguna (about 50min total). We bought 72 hour ACTV travel passes online from Venice Connected before we went and paid 37Euros each and this covered all our transport, including airport transfer, for the whole time - even out to Murano.
