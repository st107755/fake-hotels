We stayed at the Molino Stucky for four fantastic days at the beginning of March. We were given a room that we felt was not adequate (it had a poor view and the ceilings were sloped fairly low in places) and decided to upgrade to a room with a view of the canal at the front of the hotel.  Upon mentioning that it was our anniversary, we given a discount on our upgrade and given access to the Executive Lounge which offers complimentary food and drinks all day, which was fantastic. This particular staff member also arranged for rose petals to be put on our bed while we were out in Venice which was a lovely thing to come back to.  Overall the hotel is extremely well looked-after. It is very large, and the location (not being on the main island) was not an issue whatsoever.
