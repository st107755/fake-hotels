Our family and my mother visited Venice for five nights and stayed at the Hilton Molino Stucky.  We did visit in an off season, so we did not experience some inconveniences.  In honesty, I believe that the timing of your trip to Venice will dictate many of your experiences and perceptions.  The hotel is absolutely gorgeous!  There is a hotel shuttle that makes two stops, both near vaporetto stops.  There is also a vaporetto stop on Giudecca that is not a long walk from the hotel.  The Executive Lounge is the absolute best part of the hotel.  Conveniently located, it offers hors d'ouerves, drinks, desserts, and snacks on a rotating schedule all day long.  It is perfect for a quick stop and refuel.  We had two rooms that were supposed to be connecting.
