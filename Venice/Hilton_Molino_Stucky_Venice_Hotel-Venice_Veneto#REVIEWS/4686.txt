The Hilton hotel is on the island of Giudecca, across a lagoon from Venice.  It takes about 10-minutes to get to San Marco square by boat (the only way).  The “Planaca” vaporetti stop is a 5-minute walk or the hotel has a frequent private (free to guests) shuttle boat.  The hotel is a beautifully refurbished historic building constructed about 100-years ago as a mill and pasta factory.  It is a massive building, especially in Venice.  However, it is still quite comfy and convenient for a pleasant Venetian Holiday.  Spending time on Giudecca is a delight.  There’s no crowds as over on Venice.  The locals and their dogs are fun to watch. We stayed from December 26 until January 2, which was slow until the New Year’s Eve weekend which was very busy.
