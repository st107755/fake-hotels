My husband and I stayed for 4 nights in mid February.  The hotel is set back from a busy street so is very quiet while it enjoys a private dock on the Canal.  The staff is very friendly and welcoming.  Everyone knew our names within the first day.  The breakfast is lovely with made to order omelettes and a wide variety of buffet items.  We often skipped lunch it was so filling!  Our room was large, comfortable and had a very large bathroom.  The only drawback was that it was very dark because the Windows had views of nearby buildings.  Front of the hotel rooms have great canal views so ask.  It really was only a minor detraction because the canal side terrace was so perfect.  To sit with a glass of wine and watch Venice float by.
