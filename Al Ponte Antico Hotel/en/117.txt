We stayed two nights in the Junior Suite right on level with the Grand Canal, with a fantastic view of the Rialto Bridge.  We arrived by water taxi from the cruise ship terminal, and were welcomed at the hotel's dock by Hotel Manager Alex.  He immediately introduced to the Hotel's owner, Matteo.  Throughout our stay everyone greeted us by name, and made us feel like family friends.  The room was VERY spacious, well-appointed, clean and quiet.  Breakfast on the second floor terrace was like wandering into a fresh bakery, and my omelets were very tasty.  We relaxed a bit each day sitting out on our canal-level terrace.  The location is about 10 minutes from the Rialto market, and a 15-20 minute walk to Piazza San Marco.  Gondola and water bus stations are very close.
