You can be assured of a very warm welcome at this lovely hotel and the best service from Matteo and his staff.  The hotel is very centrally located and only a stones throw from the Rialto bridge. You can watch the world go by on the Grand Canal from the hotel terrace. From the hotel you can easily walk around the city and enjoy the sights of Venice. The service is fantastic and Matteo is on hand  with lots of local knowledge if you need any advice. He will tell you about the best place to pick up a gondolas or recommend or book a restaurant for you if you wish. The hotel is exceptionally clean and smart and the home cooked breakfasts are very special. You can arrive at this hotel in style as the water taxi lands at the hotels own landing station.
