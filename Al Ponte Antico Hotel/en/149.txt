What an amazing start to our honeymoon! A really special place. Without exception all of the staff knew our names and we were greeted by the owner who took the time to show us around. Great location and even though right by the busy Rialto Bridge, the hotel itself is a peaceful haven. There is a beautiful terrace with fabulous views and breakfast is wonderful - freshly cooked pancakes and the most delicious cakes made by the owner's wife. Drinks in the evening on the terrace were the perfect end to the day - reasonably priced and generous portions!
