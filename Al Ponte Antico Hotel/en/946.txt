This hotel is nothing short of spectacular. True, it was expensive, but the suite right on the water we stayed in was beautiful and the service was impeccable. We had breakfast on the lovely balcony each morning, which has an excellent view of the Rialto Bridge. Our suite was truly spacious, they gave me copious quantities of ice every single time I asked, with a smile and a "thank you Matthew". Breakfast included made to order omelettes and crepes. Every single person learned our names the first day, and addressed us as such at every turn. I could go on, but you get the point. Hotel Al Antico Ponte is now my favorite hotel in the whole bloody world.  We too spent hours by the windows in the hot part of the afternoon just watching the world go by, which was delightful.
