We researched quite a few hotels in Venice, but ultimately chose Al Ponte Antico based off its reviews & location. We booked our reservation directly through the hotel’s website approximately 3 months prior to our arrival date. (We were honestly surprised to see availability as the hotel is constantly full!) We took the public vaporetto from the Santa Lucia train station to the Rialto Bridge – An easy 10-minute ride. From there, it was only about a 3-minute walk.  Upon our arrival, we were greeted by Matteo & the front desk receptionist Oliver. Matteo gave us a quick tour, filling us in on the history of the building & the restoration it had undergone over the years before showing us to our room.
