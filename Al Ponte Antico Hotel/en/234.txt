I'll add the same praise as those just before me: An exceptional small, intimate hotel experience, with great staff and owner. We were a little overwhelmed by the crowds in this central part of Venice, but step into the tiny alleyway to get to the hotel entrance, and you're immediately taken to a secluded, quiet oasis. Our room was on the ground floor, well protected from the Grand Canal and the outside streets. No view, but completely quiet, nicely appointed in period furnishings, and very comfortable. There are Canal view rooms, but we didn't see any other rooms in the hotel.
