My mom and I stayed at Al Ponte Antico for 2 nights this May and absolutely loved it. The hotel is beautiful with a wonderful bar and a terrace area overlooking the Grand Canal, but what we enjoyed most were the people. Matteo, the owner, and the rest of the staff were amazing. Everyone was so warm and helpful. We received very good restaurant recommendations for dinner, and in the mornings a lovely breakfast was provided courtesy of Matteo. Although I was there with my mom, and therefore not looking for romance, it would also be a great choice for a couple. Our stay at Al Ponte Antico was one of the most memorable parts of our visit to Venice.
