I stayed at this hotel for 4 nights. This was my first time in Venice and we decided on this hotel due to the reviews on Trip Advisor. I love small hotels as I feel they are a lot more personal than a chain or large hotels and this one didn’t disappoint.  Even before we arrived, we received personal emails from the staff asking whether we had questions and that they were looking forward to having us in Venice.   We decided to arrange our own transfer from the airport rather than book through the hotel. http://www.sharevenice.com/en/  the transfer costs € 19.00 per person which is extremely reasonable based on the other prices that were on offer.
