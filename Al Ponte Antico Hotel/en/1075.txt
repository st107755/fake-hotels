Our wonderful stay commenced with arriving by water taxi at the Hotel's private berth to be met by the charming Oliver. We were made to feel welcome from the very beginning.  To have a room opening up onto the Grande Canal with views to the nearby Rialto Bridge was like being in Paradise. All day there was activity on the Canal, from early morning when the tradesmen, etc would go past in their boats laden with building materials or large linen bags for the many hotels. Then all the ferries packed with workers and later on the relaxing gondolas.  The room was large, with beautiful furnishings. The entire Hotel was tastefully decorated.
