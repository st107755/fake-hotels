Booked Italian trip with Planet Rail. Arrived in Venice by train from Milan. Collected by water taxi from station and taken to the really beautiful hotel Al Ponte Antico that overlooks the Rialto bridge. We were met by Alex the hotel manager at the canal side and from the start were really made to feel welcome. The hotel is a lovely old palace with only 9 rooms so you get the personal first name experience. After checking in we had some superb red wine on the terrace which is on the bend on the Grand Canal just before the Rialto. This view is magical and so rare in Venice. We later met Matteo the owner who was equally as friendly. David and Oliver on the desk were really helpful suggesting and booking restaurants if needed.
