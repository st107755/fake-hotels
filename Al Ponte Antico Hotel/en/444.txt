My wife and I stayed at this hotel for 2 nights. I found this wonderful hotel on TripAdvisor and it was as amazing as advertised.   It is perfectly located on the waterway and has a fantastic view of the Rialto Bridge. The hotel has it's own pier, so this made it incredibly easy to get to the property on the way in from the train station and also made it very easy to get a water taxi to the airport when we were departing.   The staff is incredibly friendly and welcoming. They know each person by name and are like other reviews have stated are available 24 hours a day. Matteo was a fantastic host. When we first arrived, he greeted us and then showed us around the hotel while giving us some background into how he purchased this property.
