After staying at the Al Ponte Antico Hotel in Venice, we all agreed we would not be able to stay anywhere else in Venice! This hotel is very special. They only have 9 rooms, each one unique. The breakfast room / bar area is very beautiful and the food is divine. But the most spectacular area is the terrace that overlooks the Grand Canal, right above the Rialto Bridge. There is nothing like it anywhere else in Venice and it is worth every penny to stay here and be able to have that amazing view at every moment of the day, and many times all to yourself.   The hotel is owned by Mateo who pays extreme attention to every detail, and it shows. Alex, the Reception Manager, is also wonderful.
