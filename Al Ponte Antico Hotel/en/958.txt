We really enjoyed our stay at this hotel. The rooms were clean and comfortable, the staff went out of their way to be helpful and welcoming (they knew all of the guests' names, for example) and the terrace overlooking the Rialto bridge was a wonderful place to relax in the evening. I was very surprised to read some of the recent reviews of the hotel. It's decorated like a fourteenth-century palazzo, not a modern hotel, but that's because it is one. The beds are firm, but we found them very comfortable (it's a question of personal preference, really). The towels were absolutely fine. The breakfasts were delicious, especially the crepes, and there was plenty of choice. The free wi-fi worked perfectly well.
