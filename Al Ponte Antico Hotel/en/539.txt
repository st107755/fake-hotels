We spent four nights on a repeat visit to the Al Ponte Antico Hotel in Venice and while we thought they could not improve upon our previous stays - we were wrong.  The owner Matteo along with his family and staff do everything they can to make your stay memorable.  The location is ideal.  It is very close to the Rialto Bridge and easily accessed through their water entrance but the land entrance is hidden away down a side alley.  The building is a former palazzo and the rooms are beautifully decorated.in keeping with their history.  The breakfast buffet and cooked dishes are excellent as well as having a delightful setting which includes a balcony during good weather.
