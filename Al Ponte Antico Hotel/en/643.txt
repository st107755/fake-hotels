This review is long overdue - we stayed at this gem of a hotel the last week of August 2014 for a few days before a cruise.  The city of Venice was the highlight of our trip, and this hotel contributed greatly to our wonderful Venetian experience.    After reading the reviews, I was expecting a fantastic location and amazing terrance view and Al Ponte Antico certainly did not disappoint.  The view of the Rialto bridge was absolutely stunning and it was simply wonderful after a long day of walking being able to come back to the hotel and relax on the balcony with a drink (or 2, or 3).  Such a convenient location as well - impossible to get completely lost, as signs are almost everywhere pointing tourists back to the Rialto Bridge.
