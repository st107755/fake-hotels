From the moment of our arrival by water taxi at this superbly refurbished bijoux palace situated within a stone's throw of The Rialto Bridge, Matteo and his staff were incredibly welcoming.  Our room, on a level with the Grand Canal, with splendid views at night as well as daytime, was just beautiful - spacious, richly furnished and comfortable.  The upstairs terrace outside the breakfast lounge and bar was an attractive feature, and sitting here overlooking the Grand Canal eating Matteo's famed omelette breakfast was a magnificent start to every day.  Similarly the terrace was the ideal location for evening Bellinis.  The friendly staff could not have been more polite and helpful, recommending and booking different restaurants each night of our stay, all excellent.
