A lovely hotel located close to the Rialto Bridge with rooms tastefully decorated and a style that takes you back to a bygone era. All staff, including the propreitor, were extremely friendly, helpful and provided a first class service. We were allocated a bedroom that did not have a view of the Grand Canal and the view through the equivalent of prison bars was a concrete wall less than a few feet from our room. The hotel entrance was extremely difficult to find and the lack of any signs at the end of a narrow alley that leads to the hotel entrance clearly needs the owners attention particularly as the majority of the local shopkeepers that we asked for assistance either could not understand English or did not know how to access the hotel.
