We have stayed at this hotel twice, once during Christmas 2012 and in May 2017. Both times were just fantastic. I agree with all of the other points that the positive reviewers express: great location, views of the canal (you are on the Grand Canal), fantastic owner, Matteo, and staff (Oliver is extremely good at his job and is also a wonderful concierge), gorgeous hotel, beautiful dining room. The hotel only has about 7 rooms and is housed in an 800 year old Palazzo with traditional architectural details and decoration. The breakfast room is open for breakfast but you can get coffee and drinks anytime. The staff and Matteo are just wonderful hosts who will really give you an insider's view of Venice and where to go.
