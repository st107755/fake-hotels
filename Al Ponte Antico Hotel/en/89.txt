We visited Venice and stayed in Al Ponte Antico from the moment we arrived the staff were welcoming and brilliant. Greeting us by name on first arrival at the hotel and the attention to detail was amazing so caring and helpful recommending the best things to do and places to visit for food and activities. (Thank you GiGi especially)   The owner is often around the hotel which shows how much care and attention goes into everything. With only a handful of rooms you often feel like you’ve got the whole place to yourself creating a very relaxed atmosphere.   Breakfast is lovely eggs and pancakes made to order with lots of other options ready for you in the buffet.
