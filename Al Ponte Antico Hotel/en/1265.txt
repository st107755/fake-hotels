The view, the view, the view! The VIEW!!!!!!! I cannot imagine anything much better than sitting on a terrace 50 yards or so away from the Rialto, sipping a cocktail and watching Venetian life drift by on the Grand Canal a mere platform-dive away. Heaven. If this isn't enough, then the rest of the hotel should be - it is a charmingly restored Venetian palace which speaks directly to the decadent past-present of Venice. Matteo and his team are comparably charming - they are impeccable hosts. The rooms themselves are fairly standard, if perfectly comfortable, affairs - this is a choice made primarily on position and ambience.
