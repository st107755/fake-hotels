Another TA member here who carefully reviewed so many options for our stay in Venice and based on the reviews and email exchanges with the hotel decided to spend our first three nights in Venice at Al Ponte Antico. It was the perfect choice. Matteo and his entire staff cannot do enough for their guests. This is a very small hotel in a renovated, but still very elegant, palazzo. So much of the original decoration has been left in place and you really feel like Venetian royalty...I think it is a combination of the service and surroundings. Each room is unique and my review is based on a stay in the junior suite which is at the front of the hotel just under the famous terrace sharing the same views.
