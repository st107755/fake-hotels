We've just returned from a fabulous anniversary trip to Venice and had a marvellous time staying at this charming hotel overlooking the Grand Canal and Rialto Bridge. We stayed five days in mid April, a perfect duration to explore the city, though quite popular with tourists as it as the week before Easter. Matteo Peruch, the owner and manager was an excellent host and we were delighted with the helpful friendly service we received during our stay from all the staff. Decor was stylish throughout, the breakfasts were amazing - the wild mushroom and cheese omelette made by Matteo on our first morning must be the best I've ever eaten - and the views from the hotel's terrace overlooking the Grand Canal were a sheer delight.
