We stayed here for my wife's 60th birthday over Easter this year and I cannot list the superlatives associated with this hotel. The service is outstanding, the ambiance is amazing and the view from the terrace is wonderful. Matteo, Oliver and Rosealba were fantastic and they made us feel like we had known them for months not just hours. The breakfast was varied and tasty but the highlight is without doubt sitting on the terrace over looking the grand canal drinking wonderful Prosecco. All the staff made my wife's birthday very special indeed including the complimentary Prosecco on arrival and the rose petals. Matteo booked us a wonderful meal in a local restaurant to celebrate the birthday and all the staff were very knowledgeable regarding restaurants.
