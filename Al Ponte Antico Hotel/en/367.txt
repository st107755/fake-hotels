My partner planned a surprise trip to Venice for my birthday (and to propose!) and booked this wonderful hotel. From booking up until we left, everything was completely perfect. Despite the cold February weather we were still able to sit out there under the heater to enjoy a prosecco (or two)!  Our room was lovely, with a huge and comfortable bed.
