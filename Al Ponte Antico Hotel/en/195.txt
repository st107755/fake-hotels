I honestly think that anything I write here will not do this hotel justice. I have just come back from the most incredible break in an amazing city that was in no small way down to where we stayed.  The hotel itself is located within a palace from the 1500's and has been renovated to a very high standard. The whole place is really different and it is clear that there was some considerable time spent restoring it.  Our room was amazing. The décor but also the view. If you can, try and spend a little more to ensure a view of the grand canal. Our room looked out onto this and the Rialto bridge. I can't imagine that there are many better views in Venice.
