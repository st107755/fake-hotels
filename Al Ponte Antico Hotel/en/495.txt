Venice, itself is an incredible city and this hotel only makes it better. The location was great, walking distance to the great attractions of the city and close to many places to eat. The hotel is located right on the Grand Canal and affords beautiful views. We loved sitting outside watching the world go by on the canal.  The room was huge! We even had a separate seating area. I think it was the biggest room we've had and it was decorated beautifully. You really step back in time in this hotel. It was amazing to fall asleep to the sounds of the canal.  The breakfast was great. You get a breakfast spread and cooked breakfast. I'm picky about my eggs and the scrambled eggs were perfect! So much great food and juices!  The staff was flawless.
