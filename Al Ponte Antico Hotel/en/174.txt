The title of this review might seem a bit exaggerated, however I struggle to find a better experience in my many years of travelling. The level of service and attention to detail at this hotel is unmatched, in my view, by any larger and more famous venue, not just in Venice but around the world.   This time around (it was our second visit) I planned a surprise birthday celebration for my wife and the interaction I had with the hotel staff before the visit was spot on and full of excellent tips and expert recommendations from restaurants and bars to jewelry shops.  Upon arrival we were greeted like old friends and welcomed for a spot of breakfast on the terrace. You can sense Venice vibes from there like nowhere else.
