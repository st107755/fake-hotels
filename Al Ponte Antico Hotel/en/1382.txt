There is no doubt that the staff at this intimate, unique and charming hotel on the Grand Canal offer a service that is second to none.  Personally, as a shy and self-conscious English woman, I found Matteo's personal attention over effusive and uncomfortable but I realise that the issue is entirely mine and that Matteo's enthusiasm for attending to the needs of his guests and his passion for his city is both sincere and inspirational. His staff are equally helpful and friendly and welcomed us with genuine attentiveness.    The location of the Al Ponte Antico is perfect and the views of the Rialto Bridge from the terrace are unforgettable.
