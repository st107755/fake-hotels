My husband and I stayed at Al Ponte Antico Hotel for three nights.  We simply cannot say enough about this little hotel!  We would give it 10 stars if we could.  In fact, we would say this is our favourite hotel anywhere.  First, the personal service is fantastic.  The entire staff knows your name as soon you as get out of the water taxi  (the hotel has its own private docking area).  The owner, Matteo, personally greets you on arrival and even cooks you breakfast in the mornings.  He is the most charming and helpful man you will ever meet (as are all of the members of his staff).  The staff can't do enough for you and always with a smile.
