Definitely one of the most romantic hotels I've ever stayed in. The occasion was our 50th wedding anniversary and this was our first trip to Venice. The hotel could not have been better located with a perfect view of one of the most famous sites in the world - the Rialto Bridge. The proprietor, Matteo, and his small, hand-picked staff were super helpful and friendly although we did find it a little disconcerting being referred to by our first names on every possible occasion even if Mr. or Mrs. was generally added.The room was more than adequate, spotlessly clean, and it was obvious that much care had been taken to provide it with an authentic Venetian look.
