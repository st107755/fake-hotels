My husband and I celebrated our 25th anniversary last week with a Mediterranean cruise that ended in Venice, where we stayed an extra night at Hotel Al Ponte Antico.  The owner, Matteo, is the epitome of European charm and hospitality, and it is clear that the same service philosophy is ingrained in all staff members, who could not be more warm, welcoming, and helpful.  The "palace" itself is lovely and comfortable, with a fantastic terrace overlooking the Grand Canal, perfect for enjoying the fantastic breakfast (included in all room rates), or a refreshing beverage at any time of the day or evening.
