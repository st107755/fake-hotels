Al Pontico is perhaps the most ideally situated small hotel in Venice! A stone’s throw from the gorgeous Rialto bridge with its terrace facing the grand canal, one can spend hours seeing Venetians and tourists go by their lives! We stayed in the Junior Suite at the water level whose large windows open directly on the Grand Canal, to sit & watch gondolas pass by is one of the most luxurious things one can do in Venice. We wished we had more time to relax and enjoy the view, but of course one always wishes one had more time to spend in Venice!  Breakfasts are excellent, customer service is very good. All the staff makes a point to know all guests by name which is a pleasant surprise.
