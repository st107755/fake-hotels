My wife and I had the pleasure of visiting the Hotel Al Ponte Antico for the second time and we were once again absolutely thrilled with our stay. Matteo and his team of hospitality professionals are incredible hosts. We arrived via water taxi at the private dock and were met right away by Alex who immediately greated us by name and welcomed us. Matteo instantly appeared and treated us as though were were royalty. Our room overlooking the Grand Canal was splendid, the setting was like that of romantic Venetian movie. The room was always spotless, comfortable, warm and inviting. Each morning, Matteo and Rosalba prepare and present a fantastic breakfast which may be enjoyed sitting on what must be the most special terrace in all of Venice.
