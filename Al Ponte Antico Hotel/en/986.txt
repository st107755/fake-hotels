Much of what we experienced has already been said in many other reviews but we felt we had to share our experience at the Al Ponte Antico.  Arriving via water taxi from the airport right to the hotel entrance is quite easy and effortless and takes away any concerns with having to navigate through Venice's cobblestone streets with your luggage.  The hotel itself is quite charming and really has a lot of personality.  The front entrance is down a quiet little side street and the hotel is furnished and decorated with lots of personal touches.  The location is superb and allows easy access into all of Venice's great neighborhoods.  It is a stone's throw away from the Rialto Bridge and the outside terrace overlooks the Grand Canal and the bridge.
