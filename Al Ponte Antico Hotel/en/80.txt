We were fortunate enough to stay here for 3 nights at the beginning of may, to celebrate my partner's 60th birthday.We had a deluxe room , directly facing the grand canal.The room was very spacious and comfortable. In hindsight it may not have been necessary to have a room directly on the grand canal, because the outside terrace was a lovely place to sit, at any time of the day. The grand canal traffic was noisy at times and so a quieter room would may be a better option. The staff were all very friendly and extremely welcoming. The location of the hotel is perfect, right in the heart of Venice and only a few minutes walk to Rialto bridge.
