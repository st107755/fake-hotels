A very nice boutique hotel with great customer service and phenomenal location! The hotel is small and rooms can be situated just off the breakfast area which means you are subject to people's morning conversations; isn't a problem unless you plan on sleeping-in later than 7:00/7:30 am?  Again, my only complaint is that the bed was obviously old (you could see the dips in the mattress where people have slept on it) and the pillows were about as thick as the blanket.
