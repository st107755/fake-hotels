When you visit a unique city like Venice you’re pretty much guaranteed an amazing experience, so what will make it extra special will be the location and comfort of your hotel and what will make it truly great (and make you return) will be the service.  Al Ponte Antico has a perfect location fronting the Grand Canal by the Rialto Bridge (book a shared water taxi from the airport – via the ‘getyourguide’ website - and arrive in style at the hotel’s own private wharf). The palazzo is sumptuously decorated, quiet and comfortable but, what makes it truly outstanding, as other reviewers have mentioned, is the impeccable service.
