My husband and I stayed at the hotel for 3 nights at the end of May. We were greeted with a very warm welcome and then taken up to the terrace for a drink whilst we waited for our room. We had booked the suite on the ground floor that has windows that open directly onto the Grand Canal. The view was superb and probably one of the best in Venice. The room was very large and had a lovely lounge area where you can watch people go by on the gondolas and boats along the Grand Canal. We had breakfast every morning on the terrace and Matteo cooked amazing crepes and omelettes.  Apart from the amazing view and location of the hotel, the thing that sets this hotel apart from the rest is the service.
