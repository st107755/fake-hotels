I stayed here recently with my 12 year old son.  From the moment that we arrived by water taxi we were treated to the best service that I have ever experienced. This family run hotel as adjacent to the Rialto Bridge and entered via a quiet little lane. Everything that the Matteo, Bruno and their family recommended was spot on.  Whether it be an early morning visit to the fish market, riding the canal bus from St Marco for the full length of the canal or the best places to eat they were always full of great suggestions. When we arrived I asked them if they could suggest any tours that we should do.  They gave me a small map and gave me a basic outline of Venice and said we suggest that you go and get lost in Venice.  We did just this and it was amazing.
