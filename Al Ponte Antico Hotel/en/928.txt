Only one word can describe this hotel - Wow Warm welcome when you arrive and a very attentive staff with the great location makes this a first class hotel. This hotel is a little expensive but the nice balcony and great breakfast is worth the price of admission. We enjoyed breakfast every morning watching the canal traffic and supply boats delivering to the local restaurants and bars early every morning. We enjoyed relaxing and playing cards in the rain on the balcony while watching the canal traffic go by. We also enjoyed the local bar - Bacaro Jazz which makes a stiff drink and has assorted customer’s bras hanging from the ceiling.
