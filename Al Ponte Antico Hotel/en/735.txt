Have to concur with previous reviews that this is a fantastic hotel for a stay in Venice both for service and location. My husband and I stayed here for three nights at the beginning of June as part of a trip to Italy. Having taken a water taxi from the railway station to the hotel we arrived at their private landing on the Grand Canal to be greeted warmly by Matteo and this set the tone for the rest of our stay there. He showed us to our room which was Room 101 one the first floor, just off the main lounge/breakfast area with views on to the Grand Canal. We had been travelling all day and Venice was very hot so we were so glad to walk into a wonderfully cool room (great air conditioning).
