My girlfriend & I just left the Al Ponte Antico following a three night stay, and this  is, without a doubt, the most gorgeous & historic hotel that we’ve ever stayed in. Situated in a magnificently restored 14th Century palace, the hotel itself is a historic monument.   As soon as we walked in, we were greeted by the owner, who gave us tour of this small, 9-room hotel. All of the staff knew our names immediately, and were extremely gracious & helpful throughout our stay.   Perhaps the best part,  aside from the outstanding view of the Rialto Bridge & Grand Canal, from the ground floor suite, was that you can tell they truly cared about their hotel and took well-deserved pride in the immense effort it clearly took to restore it.
