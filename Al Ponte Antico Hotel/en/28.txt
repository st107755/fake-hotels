My wife and I stayed at Al Ponte Antico in November 2019, for one week.  I can say without reservation that it was the best hotel experience I have ever had.  The hotel is ideally located, in the center of Venice on the Grand Canal, with most major attractions within easy walking distance, including San Marco Plaza.  The view from the balcony is amazing; one can view gondolas and other boats traveling up and down, as well as seeing the glorious Rialto Bridge when you turn, which is not more than 230 meters away!  The hotel has a private pier, which permits water taxis to go directly to or from Marco Polo airport.   The hotel is so beautiful!
