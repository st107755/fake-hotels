We had the first night of our honeymoon at Al Ponte Antico in the junior suite on the canal. Our wonderful host Matteo had said the view was "breathtaking" but when he opened the windows I did actually lose my breath! Nothing like it! Besides the location the staff were all superb, happy to fire up the most exquisite looking coffee machine at past midnight and the buffet breakfast could not be beaten. Can't wait to go back.
