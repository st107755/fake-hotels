We chose Al Ponte Antico based on its Tripadvisor rating and we weren't disappointed. I would have no hesitation staying again. We were travelling with our daughter so chose a deluxe room (102) which has a sofa bed and views straight onto the Grand Canal. Yes, several of the rooms open straight onto the breakfast salon/bar (see earlier review) but this isn't an issue in my view - this is an old building so walls and doors are thick enough to keep the bedrooms quiet. The best aspects of this hotel are its location, its ambience and, last but not least, its staff, who couldn't be more helpful. Bruno and his colleagues try very hard to make sure everything is OK not just for my wife and I but also for my daughter.
