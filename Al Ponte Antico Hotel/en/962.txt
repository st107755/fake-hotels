Our first impression of this hotel was one of disappointment and not what we had expected. Whilst rated as a 4-star establishment, it seemed more like a 3-star hotel. This is a small establishment which seeks to promote itself through its personal service – this however amounted to addressing all guests by their first name at every encounter. On arrival we were given room 103 which is one to be avoided. The bathroom had two lights not working and the decor was a disgrace – a hole in the bath exposing glass fibre, a black and corroded shower hose, paintwork that was peeling and chipped and split chipboard cabinetry. The towels were thin. The bedroom itself had no natural light and the end of the bed was so close to the wall that there was barely enough room to get round.
