My husband & I had planned our trip at least a year ago but we had to cancel twice before finally getting to Venice last Sunday. We arrived by private boat, which was totally worth it, and waiting for us at the entrance were Matteo, the owner and Oliver, one of his excellent staff members. They welcomed us warmly and Matteo told us that we had been upgraded to his best suite! And what a suite that was!! We were blown away by its size & beauty! It was like stepping back a few centuries & imagining how a nobleman would live... except that it had all the modern features as well! It had a bedroom as well as a living room but the best part was its outdoor terrace... right on the Grand Canal!! We spent hours sitting outside & watching Venice come to life!!
