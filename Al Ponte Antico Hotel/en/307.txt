The best thing about this hotel was the service. There are only 9 rooms, I think, so, the service was very personal. The staff was always helpful in getting around Venice. They gave very good recommendations for restaurants and bars, and you could have quite long conversations with any member of the staff about your day, your experience in Venice or Italy in general, and they were always happy to make time. Also, all of our pre-trip communications were very thorough. They gave us plenty of information about transferring from the airport, which was done using a water taxi that pulled up to the hotel's private dock on the Grand Canal.  What a great way to arrive!
