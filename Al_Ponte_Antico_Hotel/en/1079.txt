Words cannot describe just what an unbelievable pleasure it was to stay at the Al Ponte Antico Hotel for our wedding anniversary. From the moment we arrived Matteo and his team treated us like royalty - a bottle of champagne in an ice bucket on rose petals with a lovely note signed by the staff was waiting for us in our room.  Matteo, the owner was always absolutley charming. We were consistently treated as though we were the only guests staying in the hotel, nothing was too much for them to do for us. On the few times we saw other guests (it only has 9 rooms) I noticed that they treated everyone in exactly the same way.  The hotel itself is quite simply beautiful - the attention to detail is amazing and you feel like you are living in an authentic Venitian Palace.
