Stayed at the Al Ponte Antico for three nights. I had booked the hotel based on reviews on Tripadvisor, and found that the place really lived up to our expectations. The warmth and hospitality displayed by Mateo and his team is truly exceptional.  The view from the balcony overlooking the grand canal is superb, I could sit there all day and watch the world go by.  Of course the best part of our stay was the breakfast. I will remember the delicious omelette and the crepes that I had for long time!  The location of the hotel could not be better, we had the signs to the Rialto bridge to guide us back every time we wandered out exploring the city.
