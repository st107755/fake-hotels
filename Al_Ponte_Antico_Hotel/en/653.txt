Hotel Al Ponte Antico is a fantastic hotel. My wife and I stayed here for our first visit to Venice and were extremely pleased. Matteo and his staff’s personnel interaction and outstanding customer service with their guests make this hotel a must stay. We had a beautiful room that overlooked the Grand Canal and Rialto bridge which is very eloquently decorated. Breakfast was a wonderful experience with Matteo himself doing the cooking, per order, most of the mornings. We also enjoyed his insight as to what places to visit while staying in Venice. There is something special about a hotel with which the entire staff takes the time to personally know your name and address you every time you pass them.
