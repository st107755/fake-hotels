We stayed at the Al Ponte Antico Hotel for a week in November 2014. I have nothing but happy memories of our time there. Everything detail was taken care of. The staff was beyond helpful. If we needed directions, or restaurant recommendations, or dinner reservations they were there with exactly what you're hoping for.  It is clear that all of the staff love the hotel and love Venice and want the guests to have the best experience. The breakfasts were top notch. You can't get a better start to the day. The hotel is in a great location. You can easily walk to everything and because it is right on the Grand Canal you can watch the flow of the city float by on the water.
