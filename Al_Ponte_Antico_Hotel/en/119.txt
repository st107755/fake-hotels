From the moment we stepped ashore from our water taxi from St Lucia Station, having just got off the Orient Express from London we felt like honoured guests in a family palace. We were greeted by Matteo and Alex who showed us around a building that they are rightly very proud of. We had arrived with another couple off the train and by the time we had been shown around and completed the registration formalities our cases were in our room - the junior suite with waterside patio on the Grand Canal. The room itself was large and sumptiously furnished and decorated with a door opening onto the patio. We asked Alex for a recommendation for nearby restaurants for dinner - something not too expensive and nearby as we had been travelling for nearly 36 hours by then.
