Without a doubt one of the best boutique hotels I have ever stayed in and certainly the only place to stay in Venice. Matteo and his team are wonderful - they gave me great recommendations on places to dine and some fabulous insights into Venice in general. The breakfast was superb - fresh eggs made to order and a sumptuous buffet. Try the tomatoes bought daily at the local market dressed with olive oil, salt, pepper and basil - amazing flavour. The breakfast room/bar is what really separates this hotel from the rest - stunning Venetian décor and a terrace that provides the best view of the Rialto Bridge on offer anywhere in the city.
