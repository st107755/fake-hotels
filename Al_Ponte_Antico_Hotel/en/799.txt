This gem of a hotel is located on the Grand Canal just a few steps from the Rialto Bridge. We arrived by taxi on a rainy afternoon. The hotel has a private dock that makes arrivals and departures a breeze. Upon arriving we were met on the dock by Matteo, the owner, who greeted us by our first name. It is this type of detail and warmth that set the tone for our three night stay. The staff could not do enough for us. Every morning we would have breakfast on the terrace overlooking the Grand Canal. I cannot imagine a better way to start the day. The room that we had was in what they refer to as the Noble Level, right off the cafe/bar. The room was spacious, impeccably clean and well supplied.
