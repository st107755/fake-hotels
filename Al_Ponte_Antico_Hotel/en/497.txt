Warning: a visit to Venice and to this hotel could change your holiday plans for ever. Within 24 hours my husband was already planning our next trip. I had brought my mother here for a few days 4 years ago - a hugely successful trip. When I arrived this time with my husband I was greeted by name and welcomed back like a favourite aunt. The location is stunning, it was warm enough to have breakfast on the terrace; each afternoon we returned there for a cup of tea or something stronger while chatting with other guests and after dinner we sat and watched the comings and goings of Venice over a grappa. Other reviewers have already written of wonderful service from a lovely team and the breakfast. I can add no more. Do not expect perfection - its too easy to oversell somewhere.
