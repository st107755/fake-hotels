We ended up at this hotel by default, as we were transferred from their sister Locanda Orseolo - but I was more than happy with this arrangement. Our initial dealings with the staff were friendly, and when we arrived early with our bags, having disembarked from our cruise at 10am, we were welcomed and shown around the property and amenities, with apologies that our room was not ready. We had never expected it to be, and were very surprised that we were able to use it within the hour - which we had spent catching our breath and using the hotels wifi to catch up on some urgent business, sitting on the breakfast balcony. We had an internal room, the door came straight off from the bar area - but we would never have known it - it was peaceful. No view, but a beautiful room.
