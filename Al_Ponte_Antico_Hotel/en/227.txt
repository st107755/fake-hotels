The hotel is situated in the center of the city with a view of the Rialto bridge (an important point given the few Grand Canal crossing points).  But, it is not the location or the view that sets this hotel apart, while those are still impressive and important. The aspect that sets Hotel Ponte Antico is the Service (with a capital S), the owner Mateo is what every hotel should strive for in personal Service.  Mateo personally greets the guests and provides explanation of the hotel. You can tell this is his life’s passion. Mateo has his employees learn your name and faces, you will be amazed when any one of the employees will greet you by your name even though you have never met them.
