My husband and I spent three nights at Al Ponte Antico and we could not have picked a better place to begin our first vacation in Italy. From the moment they greeted us by name at their dock to the moment we said goodbye, everything was first class all the way. The staff was the most attentive and friendly group of people I have ever encountered at a hotel.         Even though the immediate Rialto area was quite crowded, it was very easy to wander off through a little alley with no one else in sight. No matter where we ended up, it was easy to find our way back to the hotel because of the signs showing the way to the Rialto Bridge.
