My husband and I stayed at this hotel during our honeymoon for 3 nights. We originally booked the best room at this hotel. We were expecting the room that has a sitting room with windows that open right to the grand canal with a view of the Rialto bridge, called a junior suite. We were given the room across the hall which was a nice room, but it didn't have the sitting room in the front, no view of the rialto bridge, and it also had a leaky roof. The whole reason we paid extra for our room was so we could have the sitting room looking out to the grand canal. These two rooms should not be the same price!! BEWARE!!! If you book the jr. suite thinking you're getting this room you may not.
