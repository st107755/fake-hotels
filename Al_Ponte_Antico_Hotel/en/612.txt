We have just returned from an amazing 7 nights in Venice partly to celebrate a friend's 50th birthday. This has been a dream of my husband's for years and the view really was quite out of this world with every imaginable type of water transport sailing by.  We were greeted from the water taxi by Alex who called us by our names straight away and it just continued from there. All the staff were amazingly helpful and Matteo the owner was charming and ran an excellent team.
