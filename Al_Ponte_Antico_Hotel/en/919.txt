This was by far THE BEST HOTEL that we stayed at in Italy (we also stayed in Florence and Rome). The room was 3x the size of any other hotel room and was decorated in authentic Venetian style. This was important to us because we really wanted to get an authentic feel of the city where we were staying.  The staff here is impeccable. So friendly and helpful... The breakfast was one of the best parts of our entire trip. While sitting on a terrace overlooking the grand canale (BEAUTIFUL), Rosalba and Oliver served us a delicious breakfast that went light years past the typical continental breakfast served at other hotels. Rosalba made omlettes and crepes to order and was so sweet.  Oliver was also incredibly nice. We felt like we were staying with family while here.
