This hotel is on the Grand Canal close to the Rialto Bridge which is an excellent location.  We were able to go to the most popular areas in the morning and at night when they were not as crowded.  The hotel has their own dock and so we were able to take a water taxi directly to the hotel from the train station without having to wheel our suitcases around Venice.  Likewise when we left we were able to arrange for a water taxi to pick us up at the hotel and take us to the airport.  The staff including the owner are very friendly and had great suggestions for non touristy places to eat.  The breakfast was over the top compared to the breakfasts we had at other Italian hotels.
