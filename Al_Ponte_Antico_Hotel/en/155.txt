We absolutely enjoyed our stay in this lovely little hotel. The service was impeccable, the AC during a very hot summer was such a comfort, and the view from our room straight out onto the Grand Canal and the Rialto Bridge was breathtaking! Breakfast was good, and we often had takeaway lunch, snacks and late night drinks in the terrace, with a view that people pay top dollar for at restaurants and bigger hotels down the road. It was so convenient to arrive and leave by water taxi straight to the hotel’s dock, too. My favourite bonus: when you are right in the centre of Venice, as this hotel is, you can experience the magic of this beautiful city without all the crowds in the early morning.
