My daughter and I are spending 9 days in Italy on vacation. Venice was our first stop and Al Ponte Antico will certainly be a highlight of our trip. The reviews are all accurate and if anything, the hotel is even better than described on Trip Advisor. From the personal greeting when we arrived at the hotel via water taxi by Mateo, to the incredible hospitality of everyone we encountered, and the absolutely gorgeous view from the balcony overlooking the Rialto Bridge, I can not think of a better place to stay in Venice. The warmth of the staff and the quality of the lodging was superb. I travel all over the world and this was one of the finest experiences I have had in a hotel.
