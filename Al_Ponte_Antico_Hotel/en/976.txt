As others have mentioned we booked this Hotel purely on the basis of the reviews on TripAdvisor. Venice is not a location you tend to visit often - as such the reviews probably have more effect on selection than other places we've visited.   In summary we were disappointed with the quality of this Hotel for the rates charged and that really dominated all the other considerations. We had the Junior suite (as we had the kids with us) and this not remotely worth the price we paid. We accept that Venice is expensive but we just felt ripped off.  The location is good for visiting the whole City but the web site pictures do embelish the exterior view of the Hotel somewhat.
