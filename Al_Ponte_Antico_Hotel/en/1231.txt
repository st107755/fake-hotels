My wife and I stayed for 5 nights at the start of our honeymoon in June and we could not have wished for a better hotel. If we had been offered a free transfer to six star opulence elsewhere in Venice then we would have swiftly declined! Here's why...  As you will probably have already gathered by now, the hotel is located on the Grand Canal and is a stone's throw from the Rialto Bridge. Our arrival by water taxi from the airport late on our first night was magical...a dash across the lagoon before entering small quiet canals and arriving at a scene from a film. If you're undecided about how to transfer to your hotel from the airport, and you can afford your own water taxi, just do it.
