My fiancee and I stayed here for several nights in July 2018. From the moment we booked until the moment we checked out, everyone was incredibly friendly and attentive. The room was a bit small and dark for the price, but the bathroom was spacious. Matteo, Oliver, Alex, Rosalba, and everyone else at this hotel is friendly and super knowledgable about the city. The location of the hotel is both a blessing and a curse. It's nice to be so centrally located. But the location means it's crowded - really, really crowded - all the time. I knew that lots of people visit Venice, but I had no idea how bad it would be. I'm a New Yorker, so I'm with crowds all the time. Venice is like Times Square - everywhere!
