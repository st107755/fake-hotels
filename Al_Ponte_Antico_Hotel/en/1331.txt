We recently returned from a 2 week vacation in Italy.  We spent 3 nights in Venice and stayed at the Al Ponte Antico Hotel.  This hotel had received good reviews in the forums and I would like to provide another.  We liked the location of this hotel and thought it optimal for staying in Venice.  The hotel is on the Grand Canal near the Rialto Bridge.  I had read some forums recommending staying off the Grand Canal due to noise.  However, I think the positive aspects of staying on the Grand Canal outweigh any negative.  Our room had windows opening onto the canal and there was some noise from the water traffic, but this is nothing like that of a motor highway.
