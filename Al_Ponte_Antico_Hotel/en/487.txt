From the moment we arrived via the water taxi we felt at home.  Matteo and his staff are so welcoming. Nothing is too much trouble from providing directions and tips on where to go and what to see to tea and cake on the terrace overlooking the Rialto bridge. The hotel is an old palace and refurbished to a high standard with many original features. Rooms spacious and spotlessly clean. Good selection at breakfast with cooked eggs or pancakes made by the owner himself. They provided restaurant recommendations in advance and made all the bookings for us. Each of these were excellent and we had great tables  as well. It was our silver wedding anniversary and matteo provided a lovely bottle of prosecco for us to celebrate.
