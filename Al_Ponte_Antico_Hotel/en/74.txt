I don’t know where to start and how to do this hotel justice. Maybe the beginning would be a good point! I had been planning the perfect proposal to my girlfriend of three years, for obvious reasons I wanted everything to be perfect!   So obviously the hotel was an important part of that. We arrived via water taxi just short of Rialto Bridge and were greeted by name and helped out of the boat by Oliver who took our bags straight from the boat. He then proceeded to show us around the hotel which little did I know was an old Venetian palace! The high ceilings, gilded furniture and material covered walls looked very royal indeed. Our room was no exception, amazing drapes over the bed and very high ceilings. It was also a large room, not typically found in Venice. 10/10.
