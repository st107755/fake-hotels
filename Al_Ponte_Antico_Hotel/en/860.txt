We booked the grand canal room looking directly out on passing gondolas from our open windows facing the water. The room was gorgeous with the owner leaving areas in the room where you could see original pillars and ceilings. We only had one night, but within an hour of arrival our greeter had arranged for a private taxi boat to Murano where a factory staff member met our boat, took us to see a master glass blower do his work and then we were browsing and shopping. That evening, the same staff member set up a great dinner in the neighborhood which was casual but one of the best meals we had on our holiday. The next morning the owner cooked our breakfast to order. He also told us interesting bits of history about the palace and Venice.
