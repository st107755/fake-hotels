I recently stayed with my brother, sister and brother-in-law for three nights at Hotel Al Ponte Antico, located directly on the Grand Canal near Rialto Bridge.  To state that the rooms were beautiful and meticulously restored and that the location is fantastic is true.  But what made our stay here so wonderful was the personal attention and utmost professionalism given to us by the owner, Mateo, and his staff.  They were all so helpful and always asked what they could do to assist in our daily itinerary from breakfast, daily planning and logistics, reservations, history lessons and evening cocktail hours on the terrace.  We didn’t just “stay” at this hotel but more importantly we engaged with the staff totally from pre check through to departure.
