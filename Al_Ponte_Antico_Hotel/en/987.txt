We arrived by water taxi straight from the airport. As the hotel is right on the Grand Canal, you are dropped off right at the front entrance, effortless. We were welcomed by our first names, given a brief but informative tour of the hotel and left to settle into our room which was at water level looking out onto the Grand Canal. During our 3 night stay, we were always greeted by Matteo, Bruno and all staff by our first names, nothing was ever too much trouble for any member of staff. They gave us great advice on what, where and when to visit the places we wanted to see. This hotel is a very special place to stay because of the location, history and service.
