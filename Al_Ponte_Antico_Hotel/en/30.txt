After ending a two week organized tour of northern Italy, my wife and I spent three nights in Venice on our own. We booked the hotel based on a Rick Steve's recommendation and what appeared to be a great location. It was a great find. It's small, only nine rooms, but absolutely beautiful and very Venetian. The service from the Peruch family was excellent. We enjoyed watching the city come alive in the morning while enjoying a delicious made-to-order breakfast on the Terrace and the gondoliers in the afternoon while having a great Italian wine, or a pretty good manhatten. We had a interior room that was very comfortable and quiet. It appeared that rooms on the Grand Canal may have been a little noisy so we were very happy with ours.
