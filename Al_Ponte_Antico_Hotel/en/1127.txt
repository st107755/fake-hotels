This hotel has received such well deserved praise from fellow Tripadvisors that additional praise is superfluous.   The owner and staff could not be more attentive and the hotel's location is perfect.   My wife and I are frequent visitors to Venice and I'm sure we shall be staying there again in the not too distant future.  However, there is one comment I would make that travellers with a long stay or part of a large family might find useful.  This is a small hotel and the owner plus his excellent staff make a point of knowing the names of every guest.  And my point concerns the ordering (and ultimately paying for) the drinks.
