My husband and I stayed for four nights at the Al Ponte Antico Hotel and were treated as Venetian nobility by the entire staff of the hotel. It is a small boutique hotel located directly on the Grand Canal with a view of the Rialto bridge.The personal attention from the staff, who know everyone by their first names make it seem more like you are staying in someone's private villa. The hotel's location is perfect being close to all of the major sites and not very far from Piazzale San Marco which is a hub of activity. The owner, Matteo, and his staff (Oliviero & Tomas) could not have done more to make our stay exceptional. My husband  and I had a very spacious room over the terrace where breakfast is served and right off the gorgeous dining room.
