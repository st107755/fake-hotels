Venice is a bustling city with streets crowded with tourists. Although we were tourists too, I never like feeling like I'm one of "the mob". This little boutique hotel is perfectly situated, right on the Grand Canal beside the Rialto Bridge. From its small private terrace we were able to sip Prosecco and watch the boats and gondolas navigate the canal, feeling like we were still part of the action but removed enough to be able to actually enjoy it. In the evening, when most of the horde of sightseers were gone it was lovely to wander the streets and enjoy the music at St Mark's Piazza nearby. Everyone who worked at the hotel knew us by name, were warm, welcoming and the service was exceptional. We only stayed for one night but it was well worth every dollar.
