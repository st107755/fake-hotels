Prices on booking sites are a poor way to choose a hotel. If you’re in Venice, you should make your trip memorable. This hotel will ensure that’s the case for you. You don’t just get a room here, although the rooms are certainly special. You get a staff of devoted caretakers at your disposal who will bend over backwards for you. Matteo, the owner and proprietor, understands exactly how to make you feel like you are the most important person in the world while you are with him, and his staff are well-chosen for their same devotion.
