We spent three nights with Matteo and his fabulous staff in mid-December 2009 and from the moment we stepped through the door it was a dream come true. From the amazing, personalised welcome each and every time you enter the reception area, to the fantastically appointed rooms, not to mention the beautiful dining/bar area with original finishes, this hotel has everything one could wish for. Friendly and welcoming seem very poor choices as words to describe the staff as they honestly make you feel like you are their only guest in the hotel and like you are a member of royalty. Their local knowledge is astounding and they have fantastic advice for good food, wine, and activities in and around Venice.
