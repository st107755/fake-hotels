My wife and I recently stayed at this boutique hotel for three nights to celebrate a milestone birthday. From the moment we arrived the hospitality shown towards us was exceptional (which was personal without being intrusive) and we even had a bottle of bubbly waiting for us on arrival to celebrate my wife's birthday - a very nice touch!  The hotel was completely renovated a few years ago and has been finished to a totally uncompromising standard. You really do feel like you stepped into a palace. Perhaps the best part is the stunning terrace overlooking the canal, which surely has to be one of the best views in Venice that you can enjoy in your own privacy.
