Sadly we only stayed one night at this wonderful hotel. Believe me it is the most fantastic place to stay in Venice and that is due to the warm welcome & amazing staff at the hotel. The wonderful Matteo is extremely proud of his hotel and the staff fall over themselves to make you feel at home offering all kinds of useful advice and greeting you by name.
