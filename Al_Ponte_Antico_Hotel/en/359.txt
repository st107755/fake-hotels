It would be easy to just list many superlatives to describe this wonderful hotel, but that in itself would not do it, or the fabulous staff justice.  From the moment you arrive (by water taxi if you can) Matteo and his team make you feel special. Without exception, every one of the staff go out of their way (very subtly) to ensure your stay is as comfortable and pleasurable as possible.  The location of the hotel is amazing, we were lucky enough to have a suite on the ground floor and I felt I could almost touch the Rialto bridge from the terrace.  Every aspect and detail of the hotel has been carefully restored and the result is stunning and in keeping with the wonderful old building.
