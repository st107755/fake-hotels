We stayed in Venice the first 4 nights of our honeymoon and spent it at Hotel Al Ponte Antico. We were amazed by how friendly and accommodating the staff were to our needs. They were constantly giving us hints on where to go for sites, food, "cichetti", etc. They also spoke perfect english and were not offended by our lack of speaking Italian. We felt incredibly relaxed and it was a great way to kick off our trip. The hotel itself is also very nice - traditional Venetian decor throughout and a lovely balcony that is open anytime to sit out for a drink or during breakfast overlooking the grand canal. Our room was perfectly air conditioned when we arrived, but they also showed us to adjust if we had wanted to.
