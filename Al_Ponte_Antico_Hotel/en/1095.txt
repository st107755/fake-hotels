We arrived at the hotel by water taxi, docked right out front...the hotel is located on the Grand Canal. We were greeted by an extremely professional and hospitable young man, our bags were attended to and we were introduced to the owner of the hotel. He was extremely friendly and helpful and was eager to tell us all about the hotel and show us around before showing us to our room. We felt that anything we needed or wanted could be arranged.  We booked a Deluxe Room with a view of the Grand Canal for one night. The room was amazing, with a great view. The bathroom was very no-frills but clean and did its job well. The bed was hard, but we had no issues falling asleep. The free wi-fi was great.
