Just returned from four fabulous days in Venice and had the pleasure of staying at the Hotel Al Ponte Antico. I cannot say enough good things about this place. Matteo and Alex and all of the staff truly treat all the guests like family members. They are so kind and helpful with recommendations and assistance whenever requested. We even ran into an odd hiccup with our fancy dinner reservation and they came to the rescue with pleasure. Rooms are exactly as you would want them in Venice, traditional and outfitted with Murano glass fixtures. We loved that each room looked and felt different. Bathrooms are gorgeous! Breakfasts were delicious and Matteo's fresh-made omelets and crepes could not be beat. Cocktails on the patio overlooking the grand canal were a true treat.
