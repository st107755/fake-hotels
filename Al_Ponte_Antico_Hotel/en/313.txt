This was my first trip to Venice and we stayed at the Al Ponte Antico Hotel for 4 days.  We arrived from the airport to the hotel via water taxi.  There is a dock on the Grand Canal so you can enter the hotel from the water side.  It is a small hotel with only nine rooms and the attention given to us by the owner, Matteo, and his staff was first class.  Our room was on the second floor just off the bar/restaurant.  The room was quiet and comfortable.  The owner cooked breakfast each morning with a wide selection of breads, pastries, cereals, cheeses, meat and juices.  In the evenings, we sat on the small terrace overlooking the Rialto Bridge and enjoyed Aperol Spritzers.  The hotel provided excellent restaurant recommendations.
