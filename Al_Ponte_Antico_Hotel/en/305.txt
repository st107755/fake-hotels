A quirky little hotel located on the Grand Canal, just a couple of minutes from the Rialto bridge. It has a small terrace located on the Grand Canal, with the most incredible views - we spent many happy hours sipping wine and watching the gondolas, water taxis/buses and other canal traffic pass by. Very special and memorable moments.  A beautiful old building which has been renovated with love (and probably at considerable expense) by the owner. Breakfast is served in the first floor "salon" or out on the terrace.  Our room was quirky and comfortable, though perhaps would benefit from a "refresh". Service was excellent and very personal - nothing was too much trouble and every member of staff called us by our first names, even though we were only there for 3 nights.
