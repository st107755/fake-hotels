From early email conversations with Oliver and Igor prior to arrival to meeting the owner Matteo when we arrive and been shown around. Its an extremely personal experience where the staff all know your name with nothing ever being too much trouble. The décor is exquisite, the location is amazing with a door to patio with views direct to the Rialto bridge from our room (we stayed in Junior Suite with Patio) and the cooked breakfast to die for. Keeping things easy we took a water taxi from the railway station straight to their private wharf, which if you can is definitely the way to go.
