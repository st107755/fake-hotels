My wife and I stayed at Al Ponte Antico for four nights at the end November. This was our first trip to Venice and I spent quite a bit of time researching options online (including reading numerous TripAdvisor reviews). We were looking for a higher-end hotel with a room in the €400-500 per night bracket. In many locations this would not be a problem, but in Venice this was a bit of a challenge. Looking through all the well known luxury hotels I was disconcerted by the number of reports of poor service that guests received. I selected this hotel based on its good service reviews and then hoped for the best. Luckily we weren't disappointed. We caught a water taxi from Marco Polo airport to the hotel.
