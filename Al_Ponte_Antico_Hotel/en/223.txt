I have been fortunate to have stayed in many top properties around the world and Al Aponte Antico is standout among them.   It is in perfect location, a unique and beautiful building that I looked forward to returning to every evening.     The rooms are enormous compared to typical European hotel.  They retain the history and character of the building but at same time offer modern luxury e.g. newly remodeled shower / bath.  The breakfast is deliciously prepared and offered on the terrace overlooking the grand canal or inside in the wonderfully appointed lounge with impressive 14th century ceiling.     The owner Matteo and the staff at the hotel are what really makes this place special.
