I was determined that our first ever visit to Venice would be special for all the right reasons and actually postponed our trip by 12 months in order to stay at this hotel  on the dates we wanted. Our stay was for five nights and the hotel did not disappoint, sitting on the Grand Canal a couple of hundred metres from the Rialto Bridge and with views down the canal in the other direction as well. The hotel is small and the building was originally a 15th Century Palace. Be aware that the hotel is by definition not of modern design; the limitations of size, space and planning regulations, means that bathrooms are actually shower rooms and are very small.
