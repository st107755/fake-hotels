We stayed six nights at this hotel and it met  our every need for our first visit to Venice. The location is perfect  being very central with a balcony overlooking the Grand Canal. We had a "Classic Room " with a high ceiling  and traditional good quality furnishings. It was not overly large but came with a very high quality bathroom . The service was exceptional , each member of staff knows your first name and the owners are there most days to help. They do not serve any meals except breakfast but this is very good , can be taken on the balcony and is much more than the average " continental breakfast ". The staff know many good restaurants locally and we used their  recommendations each day for dinner without a single failure.
