A recent stay in Venice at this hotel highlighted the difference between a four star and five star hotel. Located right by the Rialto Bridge, the location was fabulous. We had a large suite with a view of the canal. Half of the room contained the bed and the opposite side of the room had two chairs, a small table and a couch with a broken leg. It looked and felt antiquated, and very much fulfilled the Venice "vibe" but was really was not comfortable or inviting. The washroom was very small and if you are large you will be quite uncomfortable in the shower. The staff, in particular Oliver, were warm and sincere. Oliver went way beyond in terms of sincerity and really reflected what all hotels should look for in their employees!
