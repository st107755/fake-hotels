We travelled to Venice with friends for a 50th birthday surprise for my wife and I chose this hotel due to its location and the glowing reviews.  From the moment I made the reservation, the contact with the hotel was exceptional. I travel extensively for both business and pleasure and until this time had become resigned that true customer service no longer exists. I received a lovely confirmation email from the reception manager and when I asked for recommendations on excursions and restaurants received a considered and thorough response. We visited during Carnival and most excursions were busy, so they instead arranged for a private tour guide to show us the hidden Venice. An incredible experience in itself!
