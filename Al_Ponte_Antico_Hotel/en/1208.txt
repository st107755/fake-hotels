We stayed here at the begining of November for 3 nights as a celebration for our's and our friends anniversaries and had a marvelous time.  It well deserves all the lovely things that are said about the hotel, the staff and the location.    We arrived by water taxi at the hotels own entrance from the Grand Canal, at the recommendation of Matteo, who I had spoken to before we arrived.  He also made a reservation for us at a fantastic restaurant for the night of our arrival, as it was a Saturday and we had heard that it might be difficult to get into restaurants if you hadn't booked on the weekends.  His courteous and friendly manner set the tone for the whole trip.
