Perhaps the finest boutique hotel I have stayed in after 40 years of travel. Matteo, the proprietor, and his capable staff led by Alex, exceeded every one of our expectations. In a hotel, you need competence and genuine love of hospitality. You often find one or the other, but you almost never get both. The Al Ponte Antico is a rare gem where you get competence and sincerity that is impossible to miss.  My wife and I stayed three days in the Junior Suite. Worth every penny and then some. It's a great feeling when every staff person you interact with is genuine, remembers your name, and sincerely wants to assure you have an exceptional experience in Venice.
