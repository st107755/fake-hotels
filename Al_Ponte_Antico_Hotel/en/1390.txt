Had I written this review immediately after our stay the hotel would have received my highest review. We stayed at Al Ponte Antico as part of our honeymoon in Italy. We had a large room on the first floor that looked out onto the Grand Canal. The room was very spacious, nicely decorated, and well kept. It wasn't the furnishings or the stunning location right across from the Rialto bridge that really sold the hotel, it was the staff. The staff were very accomodating and recommended only wonderful restaurants for us to visit off the beaten tourist track. Matteo, the owner makes delightful crapes and omlettes in the morning that you can eat overlooking the beautiful canal.
