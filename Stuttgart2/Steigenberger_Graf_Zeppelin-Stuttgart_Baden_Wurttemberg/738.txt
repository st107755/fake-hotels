I can’t say enough good things about our stay here.  From the moment we stepped in Bianca at the front desk gave a wonderful face to the hotel.  On our honeymoon, and as an Expedia Plus member (for one of these reasons) we received a double upgrade to the top floor into a lovely room.  It was beautifully appointed, spacious and utterly comfortable.  Walk in ward-robe, sitting around, large flat screen, ipod music player, Nespresso coffee machine, a bottle of sparkling water, a large bathroom and the most comfortable bed I’ve slept in for a long long time.  But back to Bianca for a moment – she prepared a map with great food recommendations and sites to see, explained the local cuisine (versus ‘German’ food) and also helped us out with how to get to Porsche and Mercedes Museums…
