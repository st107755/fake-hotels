On exiting the railway station in Stuttgart our first impression of the Graf Zeppelin was of a stark, severe building. However, once inside we were quickly reassured that we were in a luxury hotel. We were early for check in so had a drink in the chic, relaxing lobby bar until our room was ready. Our room was fantastic; spacious with a generous king size bed, a small sitting area and a sleek, modern bathroom. No frills but very high quality, simple lines. We had a perfect view over the station and hills surrounding the city. Breakfast was served in a light, spacious room on the mezzanine floor and provided an extensive choice of hot & cold food, including my favourite, Bircher muesli & fresh fruit salad.
