The Steigenberger Graf Zeppelin Hotel is located right at the main train station, just across the street. Rooms are nice and offer the usual amenties such as mini bar, TV, desk and chairs. There is one free bottle of water per stay. The bathroom is nice and spacious. Breakfast is great - there are just so many different kinds of bread, bread rolls, cereal, jam, cheese, meat, joghurt, fruit, juice etc. to choose from. Staff is very friendly and the breakfast room is nicely decorated.
