We generally prefer boutique hotels, but this location is right across from train station and walking distance to some great restaurants and shopping, so it's a nice trade off. The hotel staff were friendly, and the rooms were gigantic with very nice appointments - we could have had a party for 20 in our room and still stay comfortable. We stayed in two different rooms - one in the beginning of our trip and the other 9 days later, so we had good experiences in both rooms. The second room was actually even larger, with a walk-in closet with custom shelving I wished I had at home.The bathrooms were large as well, with separate tub and shower and nice vanity space.
