Located opposite the main train station, Hauptbahnhof, this hotel is centrally located and enables you to visit the places of interest either on foot or by train. The hotel has a welcoming feel to it – really tested this on our arrival after some delays in the station itself which is undergoing a major reconstruction at present. We were cold and hungry and our room was not ready, despite my husband informing them of our arrival time several weeks beforehand. So we had a snack and a drink in the bar/lounge to refresh and kill some time. It still took more than an hour but when we finally got our room, we were not disappointed.
