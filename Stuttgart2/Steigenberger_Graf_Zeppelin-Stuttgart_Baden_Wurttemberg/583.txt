This is a very comfortable and beautifully furnished hotel in the centre of the fine city of Stuttgart. Its location is a major selling point, opposite the main train station (and thus less than 30 minutes from Stuttgart Airport) and conveniently close to the city's main street (Königstrasse) and the principal sites.  My room was pleasant, if a little small for accommodation billed as a superior double. The bathroom, however, could fairly be described as luxurious. The air conditioning worked perfectly, which was just as well given the scorching 30 degree heat outside. The television had a good selection of international stations including most of the British channels and some American news channels. The room was spotlessly clean and meticulous cleaned each day.
