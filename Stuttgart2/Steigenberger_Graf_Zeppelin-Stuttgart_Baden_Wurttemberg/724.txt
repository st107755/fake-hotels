This hotel was recommended by Porsche as a place to stay while in Stuttgart to take European delivery of our car. We stayed two nights and it was a great introduction to the experience! The desk staff were very helpful and  friendly...also, very diplomatic Germans when I asked one of them to translate a magazine cover for me which ended up angrily saying that Americans were spying on them! :)   There's a small bar as you make your way to the elevator, which we never visited, but our room was very nice and quite spacious especially by European standards. Especially comfy beds and linens, very nice bathroom with separate toilet/bidet room, and very large closet area.
