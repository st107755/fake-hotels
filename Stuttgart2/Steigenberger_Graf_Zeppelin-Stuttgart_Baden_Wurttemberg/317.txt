The highlight of our stay was the reception staff, particularly one individual that I’m sorry I cannot remember her name exactly (Francesca ?).  She was extremely helpful and responsive to our needs. It started when we arrived early, before regular check-in, and were told our room was not ready. We indicated we had just flown all night from the United States and were very tired. She searched for another room enabling us to check-in early. Additionally, she gave us directions to the Christmas markets and to the Mercedes museum. While giving us directions to the museum she indicated there was a medieval Christmas market in Esslingen just a few stops beyond the museum stop on the S-bahn. It turned out to be a terrific recommendation.  The hotel is centrally located near the city…
