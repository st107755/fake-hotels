Highly recommend this hotel! It's right across from the train station and central to walk almost anywhere in downtown Stuttgart, you don't need a car. The hotel is old and beautiful but all the rooms are renovated and very modern. Our deluxe room had a 4 pc bath with double sinks, heated towel rack, separate rain shower and bathtub, a king bed, living area with a couch and chairs, lots of closet space, a desk and a tv that turned to face either the living area or the bed. Very spacious and the cost was right. The restaurants are all great, wifi was very consistent in the lounge and so-so in the room.
