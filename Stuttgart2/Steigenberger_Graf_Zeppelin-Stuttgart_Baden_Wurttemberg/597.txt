We stayed here for one night when we picked up our Porsche at the Stuttgart factory.  This hotel is one of a few offered by Porsche as part of your delivery experience and the company appears to have only selected hotels that represent the quality of their brand.  We enjoyed our stay here!  The staff and service level was both very friendly and professional.  The hotel is conveniently located directly across from the train station and there is a long pedestrian mall around the corner that offers shopping, dining and outdoor beer gardens. I don't know if we had an upgrade but our room was huge with a separate seating area and large well appointed bathroom.
