We had the privilege of staying here as part of the European Delivery program through Porsche.  We were not disappointed by this hotel.  Centrally located near train station and shopping, this hotel met our needs for 2 nights stay.  Beds were comfortable and the rooms were one of two locations with air conditioning on our 17 day trip through Europe.  The most amazing part about our room was the bathroom.  With speakers, towel warmers, and a large shower with waterfall shower head, this bathroom was, by far, the best on our trip.  Staff were friendly, accommodating, and helpful in giving tips for shopping, dining, and simply relaxing to people watch.
