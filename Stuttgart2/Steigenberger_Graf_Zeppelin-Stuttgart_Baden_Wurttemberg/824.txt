This is my second stay at this hotel and like I stated in my previous post, the location for this hotel can't be beat.  You can walk to nearly everything and if it isn't in walking distance, the train station is across the street and the U is right on the corner.    I was pleasantly surprised to find newly renovated rooms, offering a larger bathroom than in my previous stay.  The rooms, like before, continue to be comfortable, quiet and a good place to relax and get a good night's sleep.  However, not all rooms are renovated, much to the disappointment of my coworkers.  Granted, none of the rooms are bad-they just wanted the new spacious bathrooms!  I was disappointed in some of the staff during this trip.
