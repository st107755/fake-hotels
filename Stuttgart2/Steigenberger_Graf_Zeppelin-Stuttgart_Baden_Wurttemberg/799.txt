We have now been twice at the Graf Zeppelin Hotel in Stuttgart. We travel with our two children and booked the room always directly via phone. The Service was always helpful and the the employees try to get the best room for you.  The location is directly at the train station and a few meter away from Königstrasse Shopping paradise!  The rooms are very clean and we always asked for an extra bed in the room for the children who was given to us without extra charge.  Standard room on the first trip was 150€ and the deluxe room on the second was 230€ in total without breakfast.  Any extra request was done prompt and the employees are very friendly!  All rooms are very good noise isolated. No matter the side you get, there all silent - no noise from the road or shoppers.  Bathroom…
