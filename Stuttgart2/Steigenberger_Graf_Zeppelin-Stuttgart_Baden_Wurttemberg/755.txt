We stayed at The Steigenberger Graf Zepplin in a deluxe room for 2 nights in May and it was a very comfortable place to stay.    We had a car which meant we had to find a place where we could keep that, which was at a cost per night. There is WiFi but it is only free for 30 minutes and only one item per stay, not per night.  Breakfast wasn't included in our rate, but we had it one night and it was very good, champagne is included.  The room had heated towel racks which meant we could do some hand washing each night and we could be sure it would be dry in the morning.  We utilised room service and the food was good and the service was fast.
