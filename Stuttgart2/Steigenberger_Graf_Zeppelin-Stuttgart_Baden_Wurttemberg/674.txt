I had booked a Superior room on Advanced Pay rate, and a few days after booking, I received an offer which would have been a few euros cheaper for a higher category of room, coupled with the ability to buy breakfast at a 25% discount on arrival at the hotel. Upon arrival, we had to wait about 15 minutes to check in, as there were only two members of staff on the reception. I inquired if we could purchase breakfast at the discount price, and was gruffly told no, that is only for customers on the special rate (therefore if you see a rate with the discount offer, book that as otherwise you won’t get the chance).
