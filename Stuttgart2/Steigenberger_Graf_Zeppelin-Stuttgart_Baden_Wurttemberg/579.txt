We have just returned from a four night stay at the Steigenberger Graf Zeppelin and would happily recommend it to anyone.  I'm not sure whether the hotel's greatest asset is it's central location, just across the road from the main station, or the friendliest and most helpful staff I have ever encountered. The Reception staff were exceptionally skilled at remembering individual guests and couldn't have been more helpful. All the staff we met, in whatever role, were friendly and efficient.  The hotel's location is perfect for travelling around the city using the transport system and is just five minutes walk away from the longest pedestrianised street in the country.
