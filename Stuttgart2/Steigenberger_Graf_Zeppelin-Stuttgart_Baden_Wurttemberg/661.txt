I stayed here a couple of nights, as participant in some internal meetings.  The location is good, close to both to the pedestrian areas and the railway station.   The breakfast buffet was good, my room was spotlessly clean, the overall inpression was good and the lunches in the restaurant was OK as well. The emplyees were also polite an helpful.  What drags the impression down, was first and foremost a very sluggish WiFi service, I (and the other participants) have not seen such slow WiFi for many years... I also found the air in both the conference room and my own room too warm. But I was able to open the windows in my room, to bypass the problem.
