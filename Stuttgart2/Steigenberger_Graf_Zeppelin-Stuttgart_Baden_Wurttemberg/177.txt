When your classic convertible needs a new canvas top, there is a place near Stuttgart that does phenomenal work. If said classic carries a three-pointed star on its front, then Stuttgart makes particular sense, as Stuttgart is home to that very company. Well, this is what brought wife and me Stuttgart. Why Steigenberger? The name has been synonymous with top service houses.  Room: I believe we had a standard room, number 409. I’m not sure as it seemed unbelievably large for a standard. Upon entering, you find a large wardrobe along the wall, across from the door to the bathroom, with separate tub and shower! Straight ahead is the lounge, with a large desk and TV that rotates for viewing from the sofa or the bed, whichever you prefer. Speaking of the bed, it’s at least a queen,…
