Stayed in this hotel directly opposite main station and central for city centre. Took a regular double room on 4th floor at the back of the hotel. Slept like a baby with the window wide open on a warm summer evening. Bathroom was brand new and done with great retro-look taste. 20 min walk to Saturday flea market which was wonderfully situated in a square full of old buildings and under trees. There are great places for breakfast close to the hotel. Hotel does breakfast too but a bit expensive at EUR 26 if you just want a quick bite. Eat at a Michelin rated Italian restaurant for every meal during the whole weekend for around EUR 35 for 3 courses including wine!
