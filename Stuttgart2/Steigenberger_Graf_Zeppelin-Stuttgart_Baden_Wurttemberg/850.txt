We had a most pleasant stay at the Graf Zepplin.  The hotel is directly across the street from the main rail station so it took very little effort  for our jet-lagged bodies and minds to reach the hotel.  Our room was large,  oddly-shaped, and recently remodeled.   The bed and ammenities where all good.  My wife had a very relaxing massage in the hotel spa which helped with her travel related stress.   Stuttgart is a modern cosmopolitan cities with many foreign business travelers.  Remarkably,  it is difficult to find a traditional german restaurant in the central city.   The Zepplin-Stube restaurant on the street level is a good choice for sampling local cuisine.
