Ok - so this review is kind of long overdue since we visited Stuttgart in November 2015 as part of our overall tour to Germany.  We left Munich and took a train to Stuttgart Hbf or Central Station. The great thing about the location of the hotel is that is according to Google maps only 230 metres away from the Central Station and makes it a whole lot easier for us since we will be taking the train back to Paris to catch our flight back to KL. Certainly no hassle of hailing a cab down to get to the station given the distance.  Also, if you need to hire a car, there's plenty to hire from the car rental companies situated in the Central Station - no need to walk any further from your hotel to look for car rental.
