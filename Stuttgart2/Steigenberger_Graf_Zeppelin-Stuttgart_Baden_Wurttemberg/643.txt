I stayed one night at the Zeppelin for a dinner with a longtime friend in Stuttgart. He had recommended the hotel for convenience considering I was arriving that day via train from Heidelberg.  My friend was not joking, the hotel is literally immediately across the street from the station.  Looking somewhat tired from the outside, once in the lobby, the hotel showed signs of refurbishment.  The reception desk was well staffed, and they were efficient, friendly and helpful. There was a small bar and meeting space in the lobby, somewhat small for a hotel of its size.  Rooms were well appointed, maintained and quiet. WiFi was free and reasonably fast. The shower was great.
