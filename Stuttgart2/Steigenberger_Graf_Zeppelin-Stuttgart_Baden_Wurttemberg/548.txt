I had a lovely stay in the Zeppelin with my family for the Christmas market.  The check in was efficient and friendly.  While we were a little early for the rooms to be ready the reception made us very welcome and we had a lovely lunch in the bar.  All the service was excellent and very attentive.   My room was lovely with a large bed and a pleasing amount of space. There was a walk in shower and I particularly liked having underfloor heating in the bathroom.  The room was spotlessly clean and well appointed and had everything we might need.  The Wi-Fi was free and seemed to work reasonably well.
