This hotel is ok.  I wouldn't say it was luxurious.  Just a pleasant hotel.  Had to change rooms when I first arrived as the flush in the bathroom would not work. Apparently this is a problem known throughout the hotel and they are working to replace the flushes in all of the rooms.  Second room identical to first room. I was on the SPG floor in a superior room. Room was noisy due to traffic from the road (often woke with my heart racing after blasting of horns or sirens outside).  There are no tea or coffee making facilities although they do provide free bottles of water.  Had a bit of a disaster in the restaurant Le Cassoulet but the bar restaurant (I think it's called Lillets) is very nice.
