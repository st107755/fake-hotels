Le Meridien’s location is pretty good.  There is a new Train station under construction across the street, so getting to the existing station and the Schloss garten is a bit confusing at first but both are a 10 minute walk.    We were upgraded to a suite at check in and really liked our room that overlooked the park / train station construction site.    We tried Le Medoc restaurant on site for dinner one night and really enjoyed it.  Food and drinks in the lobby bar were good.  Breakfasts were outstanding, with my favorite feature being the honey bar that offered six different honeys / Honigs to choose from.
