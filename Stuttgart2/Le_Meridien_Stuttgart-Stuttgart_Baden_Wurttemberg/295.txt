We stayed here for 3 nights during the Fruhlingsfest, great location,  close to the main train station and minutes away from the underground. Its on a busy main road, but the soundproofing is so good you wouldn't know.   Reception staff were very friendly, and helpful, we received a very warm welcome from them. We arrived at noon and our rooms were ready for us... fantastic. The town Centre  is a very short walk, through the park ( you have to cross a busy main road, but that is taken care of by the foot bridge which is pretty much in the middle of the hotel). There is a really nice beer garden on the way to the town too, handy!
