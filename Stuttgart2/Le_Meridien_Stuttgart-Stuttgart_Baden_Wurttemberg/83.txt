A good indicator for me at any establishment is the mood of the employees. Happy employees generally lead to a great experience and vice versa. What stuck me at this hotel therefore were the happy laughing faces (that’s way above smiling). From Lisa who checked me in, Sarina who checked me out and the incredible Conceirge Gotz. The hotel itself is ideally located. In front of a large park and planetarium. And walking distance to the central station, subway and main shopping area. There is a major construction going in front of the hotel but there is no disruption to traffic and passages have been made to walk through to the market area. Checkin was smooth and being a Marriott platinum I was upgraded to an Executive room. Breakfast had a good spread including a live egg station…
