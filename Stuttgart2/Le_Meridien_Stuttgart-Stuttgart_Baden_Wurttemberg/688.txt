The hotel is right across the park from the train station and a block from a U-Bahn stop and within walking distance to the zoo, the large shopping area and several of the museums in the area, so the location was perfect. The rooms were spacious, clean and comfortable. We stayed on two separate occasions within a two week period and both times had large kings-size beds with one mattress, not two, as some European hotels usually have. We stayed in one room that had a large tub, while the other had just a shower, both had the rain shower heads and the bathrooms were large and clean.
