I stayed here on a work trip in October 2015 and was very impressed. The rooms were well equipped, the staff were very friendly, the buffet lunch was fantastic, the restaurant food in the evening was very good and the conference facilities were excellent. I didn't use the spa, gym or pool but colleagues did and they were impressed. There is a lot of construction work going on around the hotel but there is a great park just over the bridge which was great for an early morning walk to blow the cobwebs away. The only negative comment I would have would be in relation to the wifi coverage - firstly, it's ridiculously expensive at €6 for 24 hours and this is made worse by the fact that the connection speed is poor.
