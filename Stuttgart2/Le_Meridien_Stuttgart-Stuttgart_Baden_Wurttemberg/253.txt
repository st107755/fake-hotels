Spent Saturday to Monday in Stuttgart over a UK bank holiday weekend - didn't know what to expect from the city or the hotels. Very impressed with Le Merdien. About a 10-15 minute walk from the train station (was a bit longer than predicted and had to use Google Maps to find it due to the new train station building works going on in the city and little signage). If you take the bridge route it will take you directly to the hotel front door. Rooms are very nicely decorated, large bathrooms and some UK/US tv channels available. All items in the mini bar are priced. Robes and slippers are provided free. 24 hour gym is free to use and relatively small but you can make good use of the machines.
