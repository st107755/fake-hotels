We stayed at the Meridien for three nights on our way to Switzerland. The main purpose of staying in Stuttgart was to visit the Porsche museum. We only paid about £85 a night room only and considered this excellent value. The welcome was warm and felt genuine.  Whenever we dealt with hotel staff they were helpful and friendly. Our room was exceptionally comfortable and well soundproofed, so no problem with the outside traffic. The situation is excellent and, like some other reviewers, we did not have breakfast  in the hotel but walked the five munutes across the park to a small cafe for coffee and croisants.
