I had visited the hotel a couple of years previously, so was aware of the close proximity to the centre of Stuttgart. The hotel has convenient underground parking, but the curved ramp down to the garage is Very narrow for a large car, and although I was glad I wasn't the driver, the numerous paint marks on the wall, suggested other drivers had had similar issues. There was a minor issue at check in where the key card I was given was not activated for the correct room, but when I went back down to reception this was swiftly corrected. The room was functional and comfortable. My room faced away from the road, and was very quiet.
