This is a wonderful hotel.  My family recent spent one night here.  We had stayed here on a previously trip and had a great time.  I was worried that the hotel wouldn’t live up to my memories the second time around, but it did and possibly exceeded.  The service is wonderful.  On this trip, they greeted my 4 year old son with a chocolate dessert shortly after we arrived in our room.  Everyone was very friendly.  The rooms are very modern, clean, and pretty large.  Despite being located on a major street, there is little to no noise in the room.  The beds are oh so comfortable.  The spa area is very nice.  My son loved their pool and I enjoyed the hot tub.
