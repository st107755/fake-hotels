When we arrived at the Le Meridien, the staff were already expecting us. They made sure we felt welcome the entire time. The front desk staff and concierge were always willing to help us with directions and recommendations. They had a very good knowledge of not only downtown Stuttgart, but also the surrounding castles, gardens, and small towns that border it.  My spouse and I were in town for an American-German wedding. We had a lot of spare time and the Concierge made sure we made good use of it. They provided us with detailed directions, tips on how to navigate the train system, and advice on the best places to go shopping.
