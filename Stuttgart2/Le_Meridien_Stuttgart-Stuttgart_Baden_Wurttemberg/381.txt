Le Meridien is in a pretty convenient location, about a 10-minute walk from the main train station. The caveat is that it's not very obvious the first time how to make this walk, as there is a lot of construction and a very specific route that pedestrians have to take. Once you get your bearings, it's very easy to walk to the nearby U-bahn station and main shopping area.  The hotel and room were very comfortable. I would especially highlight the bathroom as a good size for Europe and nicely laid out (and strong water pressure in the shower). The room showed some small signs of wear, but was otherwise just fine.  I used the sauna, and fitness area, which were very nice. There's also a pool and jacuzzi, which I didn't use.
