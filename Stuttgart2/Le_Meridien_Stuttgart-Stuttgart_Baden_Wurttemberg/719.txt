The hotel is very convenient for the station- probably about a 5-7 minutes walk across the park if you know where you are going. But why on earth doesn't the hotel describe in their web site how to get to them from the station? The site map is appalling - the name of the street in which it is situated is not even on it! It is one of the most modernly appointed hotels in which I have ever stayed. I normally like traditional stuff but the room decor and facilities were great. The room was large, fresh and appealling with a big flat TV on the main wall. The lighting was superb (once I worked out which switch operated what!) with infinite combinations.
