I stayed in this hotel for three nights. This was my first visit to Stuttgart and I selected this hotel based on previous reviews and due to a good experience I had in another Meridien Hotel. I wanted a hotel that was centrally located and within walking distance to shops, bars, restaurants and most importantly the Christmas Markets.  The staff throughout the hotel from the reception to the bar were helpful, courteous and friendly. The check in was hassle free. The rooms were not ready but they stored our suitcases which allowed us to head off and explore the city for the day. It was raining heavily so they provided us with a large umbrella for the day.
