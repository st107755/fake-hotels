This is a very good hotel in the Stuttgart area. A short walk takes you to a conveniently located shopping district and train station.  The hotel has an ATM in the lobby but be prepared for the 6 Euro transaction fee. I recommend walking to the train station an using the bank ATMs or Western Union.   The overall decor is pleasing but I found my room to be a little small, even for Europe. The shower is fantastic. I had long showers. It's that good.  The spa and fitness center are well appointed. However if you are in a room with a number that ends below 50 you have to go down to the lobby, walk across the lobby, take another elevator to floor 1, walk down a corridor to another elevator and go go down to floor 0.
