The Le Meridien Stuttgart runs like the Mercedes Stuttgart is famous for; high standards, precision delivery, almost flawless performance!  Hotels are complex organizations to run, so it's quite difficult to be perfect, (and as guests I don't think it's fair to expect it!) but this hotel comes closer than most any I've stayed at.  From the front desk to the restaurants to the comfortable rooms to the housekeeping-- all delivered five star service on a consistent basis.  The hotel is located 7-8 minutes from the train station and you can cut through the park for a pleasant and easy shortcut.
