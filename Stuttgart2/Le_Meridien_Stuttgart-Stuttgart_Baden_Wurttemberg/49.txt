I called down and asked for it to be looked at and promised it would be fixed while I was at dinner.  Upon return to my room, it was still very warm but, as I was extremely tired left it. I had an extremely restless night due to the heat and when opening the window, realised that as it’s on a very busy dual carriageway, that the noise meant that wasn’t possible.
