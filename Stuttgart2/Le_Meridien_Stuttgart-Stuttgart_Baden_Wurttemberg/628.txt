I've stayed twice at the Le Meridien Stuttgart, my first time just before Christmas and the second time around when spring came along (and very nicely at that).   It's only fair to say that the hotel hasn't let the side down at all during either of my stays and even strangely grew on me (could be the onset of spring taking its toll though).  It's nicely maintained, the staff is as helpful as you could wish for, the rooms are very clean and spacious, in short all the basics are covered.   On the upside, the health club is a small gem in its own right, with its indoors and yet suitably large pool (not olympic of course, but still, you could do some laps) and collection of saunas and steam bath.
