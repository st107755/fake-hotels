I have stayed at this hotel several times, a few years ago it was associated to another group, but still  with a luxury brand. It is an excellent hotel, with great service, very good facilities and attention to details. Location is good enough: a few steps from the Staatsgalerie and a short walk to the railway station, where the Koenigstrasse (main pedestrian shopping street) begins.  If you arrive by car, there is an underground car park (20€ per night) with direct lift access to the lobby. Near the garage lift there is an automatic cash machine, which does not take credit cards, but you can always pay at reception.  The lifts appeared recently renovated and shining in marble.
