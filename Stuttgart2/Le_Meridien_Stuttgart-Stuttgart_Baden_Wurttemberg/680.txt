We've stayed at this hotel a number of times over the last few years, and on the whole it's a great hotel - good location, rooms are clean and  well furnished, and the facilities are good.  The hotel is located across the road from the park, which makes for a pleasant stroll to the main station or to the main shopping area. The hotel is also a 5 minute walk from the U-bahn (Staatsgalerie). There are no tea and coffee making facilities in the room which is a little annoying, but there are plenty of other places to get hot drinks. The rooms have flat screen TVs with a full range of channels (including standard UK channels - BBC1 &2, ITV, which is a real bonus).
