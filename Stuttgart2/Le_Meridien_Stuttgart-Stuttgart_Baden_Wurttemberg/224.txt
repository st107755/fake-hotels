I searched forever for hotels in this city because there are not too many nice ones. We finally decided to book the Le Meridien, and it was a good choice. If you're doing a weekend trip, especially with the car, you can park onsite.  This seems to be a great business hotel considering the location and the structure. The walk to the city center however takes 15-20 minutes...  The rooms are simple and comfortable. We asked to switch rooms because we got twin beds instead of a double, and they did that without discussion. The staff was also friendly and polite.  The only problem in this hotel is the breakfast.
