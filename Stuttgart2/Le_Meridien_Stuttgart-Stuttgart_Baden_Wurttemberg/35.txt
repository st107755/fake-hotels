I think it’s really the people who make a difference, and the Le Meridien Stuttgart team was excellent.   For starters, I checked in late on June 21 (around 11:30pm) and the lady at the reception was super warm, friendly, helpful and quick with the entire process, despite being on a late shift. Without me having to explain so much, she quickly offered to validate my carpark ticket so I could drive in and out for my entire stay. And that’s how you anticipate a customer’s needs :)   She promptly gave me my room key so I could rest after my 16 hour flight and 2 hour car ride.   Over the next few days, staff were super helpful, friendly and really try their best (from the doorman on the curb to the handyman from maintenance).
