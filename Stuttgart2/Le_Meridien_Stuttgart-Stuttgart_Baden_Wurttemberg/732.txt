Came to Stuttgart for the Christmas Market which was entertaining (the christmas market at Ludwigsburg, 10 mins away on the train had a smaller but more authentic feel about it though). We decided to go for a bit of luxury for the weekend and stayed at Le Meridien. Can't fault the room and the bed was excellent. Get a room at the back of the hotel. We left our window open all night and didn't hear a thing; the road at the front of the hotel is a bit like a motorway and hence can be very noisy. All the staff were very helpful as you would expect in this class of hotel and the breakfast was excellent.
