The room has spacious with a very comfortable bed, wonderful shower/bathroom and a large TV. From a practical point of view there was also lots of storage space, several hangers and an ironing board which was useful before a wedding. As long as you hang  your breakfast order on your door by 3am the night before then they also do a very nice breakfast room service.
