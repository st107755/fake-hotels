We stayed in the Executive rooms at the Le Meridian. I would highly recommend paying the little extra. Its the final touches which makes all the difference. They give you a free mini bar, free internet and a massive king sized bed. Im sure it is bigger than the standard kind size bed, as it was huge. The shower was big, with a very very large showerhead which provided a lovely shower experience.  The service is what you would expect of a 5 star Le Meridian Hotel, everyone speaks english which helped immensley. The concierges knew a lot about the local area.  We went to the restaurant for lunch and was hoping for a light lunch. The lunch menu didnt appeal to us, yet they were able to make up a caeser salad and a club sandwich to satisfy us.
