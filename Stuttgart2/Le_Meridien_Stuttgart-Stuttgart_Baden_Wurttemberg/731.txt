Stayed here to visit the Christmas market and we had a wonderful time.  The rooms were a good size, clean and quiet.  The staff v helpful, particularly the concierge who gave us excellent recommendations on restaurants, talking of which we enjoyed the Paulana (?) which is a Bavarian style micro-brewery serving traditional German food, fried potatoes, onions, cabbage and enough red meat to deliver an immediate cardiac arrest.   Also the Gluhwein and bratwurst from the market stalls were tasty and imbued a certain festive spirit.   We were warned about avoiding the walk across the park when returning to the hotel from town at night, but all we saw were some drunks who seemed v harmless.
