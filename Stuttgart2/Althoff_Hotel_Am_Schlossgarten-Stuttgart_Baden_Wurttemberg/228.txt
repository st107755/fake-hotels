We had booked one night in an executive room and on arrival we had been upgraded to a superior room. It was beautiful and very luxurious with quality bathroom toiletries - from Penhaligans. All the staff were excellent, efficient and very friendly. We had late supper in the Vinothek and the Maitre de was very helpful with selecting the local wines, and offered tastings to make sure we were happy with the decisions. Room service coffee in the morning was served from silver coffee pots, what you would expect from a 5 star hotel. If/when we come back to Stuttgart we wouldn't hesitate to stay here again!
