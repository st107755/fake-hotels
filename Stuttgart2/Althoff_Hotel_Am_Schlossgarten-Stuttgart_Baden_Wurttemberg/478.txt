We stayed one night in Am Schlossgarten as part of a pre-paid package trip to pick up a new car from the Mercedes factory, we paid extra to upgrade to this hotel and were a little disappointed.  The location is great, it sits on the corner of the park with it's museums/castles and the main shopping avenue. The service is fine but not special compared to other premium hotels and the layout /decor was comfortable rather than opulent but not unpleasant.  The let down points were the restaurant and our room. The restaurant was over priced and suffers from a chef who's trying to hard with too many ingredients and not enough attention to successful flavour combinations. Our rooms was a real howler.
