One of the most important reasons I booked this hotel was its location across the street from the main train station, because my daugher and I were  traveling by train.   You can reach the hotel through an underground passage via escalators on both sides of the street.   The hotel is located at the beginning of an enormous pedestrian shopping area, which makes it very convenient. The hotel's rooms were renovated in 2010 and the results are terrific.   Our rooms were in the superior class.  They were quite large, with a sofa, chair and desk at one end and the bed at the other.  The bed and linens were very comfortable.
