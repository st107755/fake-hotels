LOCATION: The hotel is only a three minutes’ walk from the station so was convenient for arrival and departure.   Set back from the street next to verdant parklands, it also proved a good base for sightseeing with galleries and the excellent Landesmuseum in the Altes Schloss nearby. Around the corner is Konigstrasse, a wide pedestrianised  street with shops, restaurants and cafes stretching into the distance as far as the eye can see.  ROOM: An Executive Park View room on the seventh floor had, as the name suggests, a pleasant outlook. On the evening of my arrival, the glorious strains of opera in the park could be heard but I’m not sure that the hotel guarantees such a wonderful welcome to all new guests.  The room was compact, plenty big enough for one or a couple who get on…
