When we arrived in Stuttgart I was a little put off by the exterior of the hotel.  Once we entered, to my relief, the hotel was wonderful.  Our room was very spacious and very clean.  Breakfast was included with our stay.  To say the breakfast buffet was fantastic would be an understatement.  It was truly elegant with white linens, fresh hot coffee, delicious selection of breakfast foods.  We were also welcome to special order anything we wanted, which we did!    The hotel is located on the Schlossgarten park, it's a wonderful open area with many activities.  Surrounding the park is wonderful shopping and great restaurants.  We couldn't have asked for more.
