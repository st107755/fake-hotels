In September 2015 we stayed for one night at the Althoff Hotel am Schlossgarten in Stuttgart. It was our second stay.   Since we have written about the hotel more detailed before, this is a short review.  We were in contact with the hotel before arriving, because we wanted to have a room on a high floor again and asked if the hotel could accommodate a low fat and low salt diet. We received each time a quick reply and were in regards to our dietary requirements directed towards the Zirbelstube - the hotel's one Michelin starred restaurant. A review is going to follow about our experience at the Zirbelstube.  At check in we were recognized as return guests and given a room on the 6th floor, the same floor as last time.
