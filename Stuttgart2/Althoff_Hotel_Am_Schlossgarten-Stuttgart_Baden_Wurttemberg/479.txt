My wife and I stayed 5 nights at this hotel recently. Our deluxe park-facing room was certainly worth the extra charge. The view was of the park and part of the new palace, as well as of the hillside and the beautiful buildings in the vicinity. Our room was spacious, and my only grouse was that there is no individual temperature control in the rooms (only to control speed), and therefore it got pretty hot at night. The staff throughout the hotel were consistently friendly and very helpful. They readily organized tour and train tickets for us and also sent a porter to assist us with our luggage at the train station.
