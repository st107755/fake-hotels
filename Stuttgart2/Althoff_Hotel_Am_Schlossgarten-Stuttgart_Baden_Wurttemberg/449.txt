Stayed here for two nights in a single room whilst on a business trip in November. Good hotel in a good location. The room was clean and well appointed, and although I only had breakfast in the hotel, the food was good and staff offered fried eggs and bacon if you wanted it. Overall the service was excellent and I enjoyed my stay. The only things I would critisize....and this really is being a bit picky.......is that there was no tea and coffee facility in the room. Also, my room was close to where the maids stock their trolleys in the morning and this can get a little noisy. I know some other reviews say about the lack of English channels available on the TV, but you are in Germany so can expect to see German telly!
