This is one of the very best hotels we have ever stayed in, and it is across the road from the railway station and really right in the city. We had 6 nights here and enjoyed every one of them.  We reserved a junior suite. The room was lovely, large, comfortable, and beautifully fitted. For a five star hotel, the price is very reasonable. And this is a five star luxurious hotel.  Nothing to look at from the outside, but pure luxury inside. The staff at reception really make it so enjoyable as well; you could really see that they loved being a part of this hotel.
