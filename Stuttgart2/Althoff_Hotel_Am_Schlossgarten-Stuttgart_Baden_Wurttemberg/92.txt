For a supposed 5 star hotel, we had a horrible time here. This was the most expensive hotel we stayed at during our 12 day tour of Germany, and it was our worst experience. The hotel is in the middle of a construction area, which made it a little tricky to find. The parking is quite nearby, but there are no signs showing where it is, requiring us to go inside to get directions. The elevator from the parking garage did not take us inside the hotel, but rather to the side street, forcing us to carry all our luggage around the construction to get inside the hotel. Staff spoke English well, but did not seem interested in spending time answering questions about the surrounding area, simply providing us a city map.
