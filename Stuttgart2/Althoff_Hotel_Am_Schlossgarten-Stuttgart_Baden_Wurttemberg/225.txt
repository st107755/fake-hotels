This very central Hotel, overlooking the park and Opera House, is to be highly recommended. We stayed on the 9th floor in a mini suite, with the view overlooking the park and Opera House. The room was clean and had the expected accessories fora  comfortable stay. Hotel Reception was prompt, attentive and very polite. The bar had live piano music during the evening after we saw the Ballet. On the Sunday we went to an Opera at mid-day. We had afternoon tea and drinks in the hotel cafe adjoining the park. The cakes are to be recommended. The only problem was that there are only two lifts and we arrived when a famous German football team  with all their staff were using the lifts.
