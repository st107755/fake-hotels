This hotel is located within a very short walk from the main train station in Stuttgart.  It is next to the large and beautiful Schlossgarten (one can do a 10km jogging loop within the park).  It is also next a long pedestrian zone with great shops and restaurants.  It is a great location for business or leisure in the city with the advantage of quick access to most places by train as well. Of course there is on site parking if you come by car.  Service is excellent in all areas: reception, bar, and breakfast.  The breakfast buffet is of a very high quality and includes some cooked to order items.
