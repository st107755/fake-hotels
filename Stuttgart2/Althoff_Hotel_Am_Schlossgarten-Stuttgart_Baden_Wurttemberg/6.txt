On 12/19/19 my family of 9 visited Stuttgart, Germany. We stayed at the very accommodating Althof Hotel. The staff was  very friendly. Anytime I needed something or a restaurant reservation made or cancelled it was done promptly and with a smile!  The Christmas market in Stuttgart was huge! It also had a children section with rides. I liked it much better than Munich’s. We also went to  the mid evil one in Essingen which was quaint. We ate at The Kleine Traube. I highly recommend this traditional adorable German restaurant. Also, we spent the day in ludwigsburg and toured the palace. That was very interesting and the pales is in extraordinary condition. The Bouroque Christmas market in ludwigsburg was fabulous!
