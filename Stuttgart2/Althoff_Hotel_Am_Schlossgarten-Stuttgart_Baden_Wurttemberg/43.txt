The location of this hotel is superb, especially for what was my first time in the city and wanting to be near the centre. It’s a really short and easy walk from the subway station and when stepping outside you’re almost immediately on the main shopping street. With the lake in the other direction and Opera House it couldn’t be beaten.  We arrived a lot earlier than the advertised checkin time and unfortunately the room wasn’t ready. However the friendly member of staff at reception completed as much of the process as possible and offered to call us once it was ready. This was fantastic and, as promised, the call came shortly after and we could quickly return to the hotel.
