We landed in Stuttgart after a very long flight from the U.S. and had our reservations set for the Hotel Schlossgarten.  They delighted us from the very beginning of our stay with having our room ready for early check-in… 10:00am.  After a short and refreshing nap, we ventured out into old town Stuttgard which is a stone’s throw from this lovely hotel.    The rooms are spacious and the breakfast buffet is an amazing array of everything plus eggs-to-order.  The Vinotek restaurant/wine bar is nice for a casual and delicious meal if you don’t feel like venturing out.  I am sorry we missed having dinner and the One Star Michelin but we had too many scheduled events to take advantage.
