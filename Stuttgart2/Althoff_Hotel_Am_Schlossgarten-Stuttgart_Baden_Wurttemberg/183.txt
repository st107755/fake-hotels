Very convenient location if you arrive by train. Just right out the hotel door is the tourist information center, shops, and restaurants. A short walk away is the Altes Schloss-Landesmuseum Württemberg.  We arrived at the hotel at 1100 and were allowed to check in immediately...much appreciated by weary travelers. The staff was efficient and friendly. Our room was clean, but the decor was a bit shabby chic in an old world fashion. The bathroom had double sinks, tub/shower combo, and a magnifying mirror. The bed was comfortable with wonderful down pillows. They provide turn down service with slippers and little mats at bedside so your bare tootsies never have to touch the floor.
