First of all the price was slightly ridiculous; nearly £182 a night without breakfast. To add breakfast it would have cost £25 per person per day - I don't think so!!! Even caviar and champagne wouldn't cost that much!!! Secondly there was no drawer or shelf space in the room and only 1 small wardrobe with hangers. We were staying for 4 nights so this was a bit of a pain.  Having said that, the staff were all very friendly and helpful, the hotel itself was lovely and the location was fantastic; right in the centre and opposite the train station which was great being as we were using Stuttgart as a base and visiting different places in France and Germany. There was free coffee in the room which was refreshed each day and we got water and chocolates every night along with a turn…
