The hotel is modern and centrally located, and very clean.   We stayed here for one night and asked the hotel to book us an Italian restaurant the week before we stayed there. I checked TripAdvisor for a recommendation and told the receptionist the name. It transpired that this restaurant was fully booked, and an alternative was found. When we arrived we were given a map with the address of the restaurant but no name and were told it was walking distance and very easy to find. The receptionist was nowhere to be found to clarify the restaurants name, so we ventured out on foot.   We walked a mile or so in the poring rain and could not find the restaurant for love or money!
