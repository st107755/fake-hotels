When are rooms were ready they brought our bags up.  The rest of our luggage didn't arrive until later that evening.  However, by the time we got back from dinner, it was in our room.  The staff was so helpful, calling Lufthansa for updates on our luggage.  The next day, the staff again allowed us to store our bags while we toured Stuttgart.
