I stayed at the Hotel Am Schlossgarten for 4 nights on a business trip and really enjoyed my stay. All the staff were super friendly and went out of their way to help and make the stay as enjoyable as possible. The hotel is in an excellent central location just opposite the railway station and around the corner from the Koningstrasse, the main shopping street.   My room was very large and homely (214). The bed was extremely comfortable and I was provided with a huge menu of pillows to chose from. Bathroom was also very large with a huge walk-in shower and twin sinks - it all felt very new and of high standard. The room also had a walk-in dressing room and wardrobe.
