I had to spend three nights in Stuttgart attending a convention taking place nearby at the university. Almost everything was as you can expect from a 5-star hotel in such a moderate price range. However, my "executive room" was pretty narrow, there was not much space left between the queen-size bed and the suitcase rack, perhaps a problem for "horizontally challenged persons". Fortunately I was on my own, for the clothes of my wife would not have been enough space in the wardrobe. There were no shelves or drawers for underwear etc., just a couple of hangers. WiFi was free and sufficiently fast only if nobody else in the hotel was using it. Even then it was hardly possible to skype.
