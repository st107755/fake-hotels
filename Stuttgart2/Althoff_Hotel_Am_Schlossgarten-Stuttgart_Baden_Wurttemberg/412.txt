Stayed here for three nights attending a conference. Staff were friendly and efficient, rooms well appointed if a little dated. I was attending a conference and had work to do and the room was good for this. Internet, as usual in the more exclusive hotels, was expensive, always seems ironic that cheaper establishments offer it as part of the deal.  Location, location. Next to train station - well across the road and to the left. Near the main shopping mall, which is extensive, and alongside a truly wonderous garden. What surprised me, once I crossed the bridge going over the main road was just how extensive it all was. I ended up at the zoo and saw a polar bear from not very far away; a rare sight.
