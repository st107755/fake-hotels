As other reviewers have noted, this hotel is in what I think could best be described as an area in transition. From my hotel room, I had a pretty good overlook on the park below and it wasn't really a pretty sight. Every night I was treated to screaming and mayhem and, quite honestly, it didn't really feel very safe in the immediate area surrounding this hotel after dark. The same could be said for many hotels right in the middle of a big city, so just keep that in mind.  On the plus side, the hotel staff were very nice and accommodating and I thought the breakfast buffet was excellent. The hotel is close to transport and many of the attractions of Stuttgart.
