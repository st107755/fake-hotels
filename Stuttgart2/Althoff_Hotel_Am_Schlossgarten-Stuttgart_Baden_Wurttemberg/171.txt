This is a great hotel with a fantastic location in Stuttgart.  We considered staying at the Meridien and Graf Zeppelin while staying in Stuttgart.  I am so glad we close Schlossgarten.  It has such a great location.  It's across the street from the train station and only a few short blocks to the Christmas Market.  The hotel offers parking (for a fee).  It's also only about a 10-15 minute drive to Porsche and Mercedes.    The hotel room was comfortable and a decent size.  The bed was very comfortable, the bathroom was large, however water got everywhere when you take a shower.  Our room faced the street, however it was very quite.
