We stayed in one of the larger suites in September, 2019, while visiting automobile museums in town.   The room was over 600 euros per night, which included a breakfast buffet.   The hotel is a tired building which has not kept pace with modern hotel amenities. In what was essentially the most expensive room in the hotel, there was no shower—only a rather crude attachment to the tub faucet that created a shower of sorts.  There was no closet—only an old creaky armoire. It could have been charming but wasn’t. Drapes were old and didn’t come close to blocking out light when closed.
