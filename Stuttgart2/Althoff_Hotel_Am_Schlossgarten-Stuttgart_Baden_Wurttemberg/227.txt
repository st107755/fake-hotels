The Althoff is a very high quality hotel. Everything is top quality and they have services to assist you in everything, naturally at a cost. It is near shopping, the train station is across the street, plenty of dining options and it's a quick drive to the Porsche and Mercedes museums. It is really a great location and property, hands down. Why didn't I rate it as excellent?  - the room was a bit small, but for more € that is easily fixed with a small suite or other class. Actually, this is a benefit because the small room allowed a lower price and thus my opportunity to stay there.
