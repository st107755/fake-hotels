I stayed here 3 nights for business reason and chose the hotel since I wanted to combine the city life with visit to Messe Stuttgart. The trains run approx. 4 times per hour and takes around 30 min. The hotel is conveniently situated close to the main railway station. It is also worth to bring a pair of jogging or walking shoes in order to explore the nice park just outside the hotel. There are construction work around the hotel, but this only destroyed the nice view of the park and was of no concern regarding noise. It is also easy to pass to come to the other part of the park. The breakfast is very good, with a lot of fresh and tasty selections, but expensive though (27Euro is quite much to pay). I missed the pricelist for the minibar in my room.
