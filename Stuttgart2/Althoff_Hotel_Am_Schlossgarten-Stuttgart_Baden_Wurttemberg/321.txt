I have spent 6 days at this hotel while on a business trip to Stuttgart. The hotel is located almost in front of the main train station and it's also very well connected with the subway and local trains. The check-in was smooth. But on other times, I had to wait to be served at the reception, which was served by at the most two staff and sometimes only one. I think there should be one more staff on peak hours. Remarkably, the room was ready although I arrived around noon. This is not common in Europe. The room was rather small but clean and with sober furniture. It was equipped with a larger safe than average, a minibar and some additional drinks on display on a chest of drawers.
