It's not the hotel's fault that Stuttgart is a massive noisy building site for the next few years. And it's not the hotel's fault that the once lovely garden it faces no longer offers a peaceful stroll away from the shops  (litter bins overflowing, pigeons, people hanging around drinking during the day). But the hotel does emphasise the fact it borders the gardens as a selling point and this was the reason we chose it instead of the Steigenberger. We had breakfast on the balcony (pigeon netting very unattractive) but didn't use the terrace in the evening for drinks as the view and noise were not that pleasant.
