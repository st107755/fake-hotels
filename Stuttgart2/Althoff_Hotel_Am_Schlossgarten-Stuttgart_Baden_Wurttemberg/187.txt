We ended up staying in this hotel for one night as there was a mix up with our booking at the first hotel we stayed in. We called in early in the morning and left our bags, the reception staff were kind and very helpful. We spent the day at the beer festival and arrived back late in the evening, our bags were in our rooms and my coat had be hung up.  The room I was in was not large but comfy. There were no tea/coffee making facilities, but there were two bottles of complimentary water. The bathroom only had a shower (no bath) that was really good, I don't do baths anyway!  The hotel is located right opposite the main railway station and at the bottom of the high street, so we'll located for shops etc. The "red" open top tour bus operates from right outside the hotel as well.
