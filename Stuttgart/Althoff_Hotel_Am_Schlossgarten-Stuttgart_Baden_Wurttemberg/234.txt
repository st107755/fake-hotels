We stayed here New Years Eve, and the staff helped us organise a booking for a room with a view.  We stayed near the rear of the hotel, in two rooms with balconies overlooking the city.  While it was a little more expensive than our usual hotel, we really felt we got our money's worth. We felt welcome from the first moment when the reception staff greeted us by name as we checked in!  The rooms were extremely comfortable. The views of fireworks all night long were spectacular, and we could alternate between being out on the balcony in the freezing cold, or watching the fireworks from inside the room where the huge windows looked out on the city.  The hotel is in a great location - just across the road from the station, and beside the shopping precinct.
