A truly lovely hotel in the heart of Stuttgart. On the right there is a beautiful park (hence the name of the hotel), on the left the main shopping street lies. Behind the historical centre is. Oh, and the main train station is opposite.   Though the hotel is in the centre of everything, the sleep is great, the city seems to be quiet at night.   The rooms are really spacious and very comfy.   The staff is absolutely great and doesn't make fuss of that the hotel is 5-star.   I would recommend the advance purchase rate to those who think that prices are a bit too much. In this way the stay is totally affordable.   There is only one drawback, to my mind: the wifi is payable. I think such great hotels should make it free of charge.
