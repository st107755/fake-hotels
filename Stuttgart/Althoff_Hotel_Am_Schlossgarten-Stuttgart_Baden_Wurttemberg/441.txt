We stayed in a Deluxe Park View Room for several nights and loved being right in the heart of everything.  The room was large, beautifully and artfully appointed, had two large windows (which opened), and a sitting area with a sofa and two chairs,  It was very comfortable and quiet.   The cafe restaurant served a wide variety of well prepared food with style. We enjoyed the calm and comfort of the bar as well. The staff was professional and friendly and knowledgeable. There was nothing we asked of them that they could not deliver.  There is nothing even mildly critical we could say about this hotel.  As others have mentioned, the location can't be beat.  It is very close to museums, performing arts halls, and quality shopping and restaurants.
