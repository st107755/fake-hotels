The hotel itself is in a great location in the town centre right in the centre of town, this was the best thing about this hotel. Although my stay here was not bad, I was however disappointed with this hotel as it is a 5 star Upon arrival we were lucky enough to check into our room early. The staff were friendly. The room itself is really small, especially compared to some of the other hotels in stuttgart, the decor was old fashioned yet the room was clean which of course is a must. The air conditioning was not very cool and it was 35 degrees out so it would of been nice to have a cooler room. The bathroom is tiny, and in need of updating, there was no bath only a shower, not really a major issue but would of expected both for what we paid for the room.
