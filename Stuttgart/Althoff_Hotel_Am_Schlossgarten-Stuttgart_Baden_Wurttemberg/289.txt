I spent two nights at the Althoff and found the experience enjoyable and relaxing. The very courteous and helpful staff contributed greatly to this. The location of the hotel, so near the station, the park, the theatre and the shops is enormously convenient. It is good to note that on preparing to leave the station you should go towards Königstrasse, not the Schlossgarten, otherwise you may miss the entrance, down a short passage on the left of Königstrasse. My room looked out mainly on rooftops with only a side view of the Schlossgarten park, but friends tell me that this was why complete silence reigned all night. The room, like the whole hotel, was in perfect condition and well decorated in a slightly traditional style. The bed was comfortable.
