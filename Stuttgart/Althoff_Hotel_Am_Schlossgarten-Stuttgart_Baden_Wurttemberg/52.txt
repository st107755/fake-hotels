My husband and I stayed here for 3 nights mainly to visit the Mercedes Museum and the Mercedes factory tour in Sindelfingen. The hotel is situated across from the train station which made it very easy to get to when we arrived from Brussels by train. It is also right on the shopping strip which made it easy to go out for food and shop. An impressive looking hotel on the inside, with a lovely looking foyer as well as helpful staff. Our room overlooked a park, was big with a sitting area. It was big but tired and the cushion on our chair sank! Bonus was that there was a Nespresso coffee machine in the room and the hot water in the bathroom was instant.  The negative we had was the breakfast buffet.
