Hotel situated right outside Hauptbahnhof (Königstrasse), very convenient location for me as I was arriving by train. Check in went smooth, had ordrered a Junior Suite and got one on the 6th floor facing Königstrasse, at least the direction of it..   Room was perfectly clean. Large room, 40 sq meters. The bed was a little to firm for me, but at least long enough! Nice furniture with a desktop, divan and a chair/couch. Bathroom with two sinks and a bathtub.   Evening turn down service, they prepare your bed for the night, slippers are on your bedside. They even open todays television program and put the remote control on your bedside table!  Breakfeast was expensive (Think it was 23€), instead I had breakfast at one of the many bakeries you find right outside.
