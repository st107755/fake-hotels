The hotel is in a fabulous location, with a beautiful park on one side and world class shopping on the other with a pedestrian walkway through the historic center.  And right next to the controversial train station, so easy to arrive by train from anywhere in Europe.  The rooms are the usual small European size, nothing special, but very comfortable and clean.  The service is what stands out - from the front desk to the cleaning people to the restaurant.  Everyone is trained to please the visitor.  In fact, I left my pants in my room, sent an email from back home asking whether they had found them and they mailed them to me by DHL, without cost and a gracious letter.
