I had a really lovely time at the Althoff Hotel in Stuttgart.  The staff were particularly friendly and helpful especially the delightful Room Service waiters.  Although it is quite a large hotel they all made me feel very welcome - the cleaning staff, the staff at breakfast and the staff in reception and any other staff I happened to meet in the hotel.  My room was very spacious and well appointed and had a lovely view over the park.  The bed was very comfortable and the room was very well cleaned and they even have a turn down service in the evening.  I chose the hotel for its location opposite the main station but it is also right next to the U-bahn and the main shopping street.  It was ideal for going to the Mercedes Cup Tennis - just three stops on the U-bahn.
