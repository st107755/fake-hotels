This hotel is located next to the central station. It's next to the garden where you can take a really lovely walk. And on the other side of the street is koenigstrasse which I understand is their main shopping street.  I drove there and it was very easy to find, and the turn off the street goes straight into the parking garage. The service is really good! When I got back to the hotel in the evening, the sheets have been turned, there was a little choc next to the bed. The tv was tilted a little towards the bed. The slippers was placed out next to the bed on top of a towel. Everything was super cozy, bed was good. It just made me feel really happy as a guest and I will definitely stay here again next time I come to Stuttgart!  Oh and the breakfast is also really good!
