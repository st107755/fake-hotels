This is definitely not the cheapest place in Stuttgart, but I have a hard time imagining it can be beat on anything other than price!  The location is unbelievably convenient if you're traveling by train (if you follow the underground signs to the Schlossgarden, you end up a few steps away from the hotel--a useful hint when it's raining!), or if you're going to the opera.  The hotel is located in the "old town" part of Stuttgart and there's a tourist information bureau, a wide array of shops and restaurants, and various sightseeing locations (churches, the old and new castles, etc.) within a 3 block radius.  The back of the hotel and their lovely outdoor restaurant face toward the Schlossgarten and the Opera house.    I knew most of this from the map before we arrived.
