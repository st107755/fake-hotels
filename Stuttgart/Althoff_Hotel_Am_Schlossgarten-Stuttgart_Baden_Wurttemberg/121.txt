We arrived without a reservation and were welcomed with great discreet service with a personal touch. Room 502 is a corner room with two windows. Great views of Stuttgart and the surrounding hills and the Schlossgarten on the other side. Despite being right next to the elevator, we were in no way affected by any elevator noise. Another bedroom door besides the entry door gives extra sound proofing for noise from the hallway. Nice feature. Everything was in working order. While the bathroom could have been a tad larger, despite double sink layout, it was comfortable. Beds were on the harder side, comfortable, fine sheets, plenty of pillows, typical for Germany two down comforter for the king size bed. Breakfast was a true highlight, buffet style, great variety.
