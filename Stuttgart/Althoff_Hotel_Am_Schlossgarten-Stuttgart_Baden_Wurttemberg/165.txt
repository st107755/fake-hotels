Family of three (two adults and one child) stayed in the lovely Schlossgarten Hotel for a Christmas Market weekend in December.  Found the staff to be super friendly and helpful, from the time of booking, arrival to leaving the hotel.  We arrived before noon, our room was ready - loved this. Having spent all day walking around Stuttgart and Eislingen Christmas Markets we thoroughly enjoyed relaxing in the piano bar that evening. My son had to do some German homework (he had school on the Monday)....which he did in the piano bar, one of the waitresses kindly helped him with his German - needless to say he got 30/30!  Reception staff  were also very helpful in providing information about trains / museums (e.g. Mercedes Benz).  The room, bed, pillows were all very comfortable.
