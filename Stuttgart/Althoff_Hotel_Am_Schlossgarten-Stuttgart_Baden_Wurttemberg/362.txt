The hotel was great and comfortable, the turn down service was also great. The hotel staff was good and obliging, even offering a tea/coffee when my room wasn't ready in time for my check-in. I had the pleasure of eating at the Berhard Diers restaurant and it was fantastic food, as you would expect from a Michelin starred restaurant, service was polite and efficient. The restaurant was slightly over priced for a one Michelin starred restaurant in my experience (perhaps this was the case in Germany?), bill of approximately £135 for a 6 course menu with two glasses of wine from the pairing list.
