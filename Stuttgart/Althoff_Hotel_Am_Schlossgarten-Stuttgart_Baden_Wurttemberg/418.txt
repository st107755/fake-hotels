This is the first time we have stayed at this hotel,having previously stayed at the Le Meridien and Park Inn in Stuttgart.The location of the Hotel am Schlossgarten cannot be beaten.It is on the  main shopping street (Konigstrasse) and only a couple of minutes by foot from the main railway station.Despite this the location is quiet (the hotel backs onto a lovely park). The hotel staff met us at the station to help with our luggage which was perfect as we had three cases and two young children in tow! We had two interconnecting rooms which was ideal for our needs.The bed linen ws changed daily and the room spotless.
