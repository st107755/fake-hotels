I stayed in a Junior Suite at the Althoff for one week in July, which is summer in Germany and peak season. There was some glitch on their website and I had to do the booking via email, but the manager was very efficient and when I arrived by taxi from the airport, everything was ready to go. The room was fairly expensive, but I went for that added bit of space and luxury, and it was definetely worth it. Breakfast is not included in any tariff as far as I could tell, so you either have to pay in their restaurant, or walk over the road to the next bakery. If anything, the room service was too efficient, and the daily non-optional bar fridge check almost felt a bit intrusive.
