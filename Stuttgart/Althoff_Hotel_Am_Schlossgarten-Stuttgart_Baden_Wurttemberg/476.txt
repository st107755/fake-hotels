We were in Stuttgart to visit the Christmas markets in Stuttgart, Ludwigsburg and Esslingen. We chose the Hotel am Schlossgarten because of its proximity to the train station, the palace square and Christmas market. The Koningstrasse location was perfect. The hotel is directly across from the train station, but you would never know it. The trains run underground and there is NO noise from passing trains. The hotel has the Koningstrasse shopping street on one side and the gardens with a view of the opera house and museums on the other side. We had a park view room, and were very happy with it. The park and view of the hillside were lovely. Our room was exceptionally large for a European hotel room. We were very pleasantly surprised.
