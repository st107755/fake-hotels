This was the first hotel we stayed at during our tour of Bavaria.  The staff were super friendly, and the room was quite nice.  I think the weather (which was close to 80 F) was unusual for September because they had already switched over to heat, so our room was very hot - even with the window open.  We asked for a fan for the second and third nights, and it helped, but not enough to allow us a good night's sleep.  Also, with the window open, there was a lot of noise from the park below.    The hotel is situated right next to the main train station and a popular shopping district so there are lots of food options nearby.  We enjoyed the ability to just leave the car in the garage and tour town on the metro.
