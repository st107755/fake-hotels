You can't beat a hotel across the street from the train station for location and this is where the Steigenberger is located. I had high hopes for my stay due to the Steigenberger name and in many respects, I was not disappointed. However, the front desk staff member, a gentleman, who checked me in was aloof and not at all engaged in greeting me or having any kind of conversation. He politely asked me for my information, but did not provide any indication of when the room would be ready or if my things would be placed in the room before I returned. The room itself was large, but dated. The bathroom was well appointed, but had seen better days.
