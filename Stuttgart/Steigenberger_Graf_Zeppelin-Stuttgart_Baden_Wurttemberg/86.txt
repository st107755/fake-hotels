The Graf Zeppelin Hotel in Stuttgart is a well located and generally comfortable property. I had some concerns regarding the location of our spacious and nicely appointed room. It was located on the first floor (2nd floor in US) on a busy street and faced the train station, which is undergoing major renovations. However, the sound proofing was excellent. Also excellent was the water pressure.  On the negative side were the bath towels. The Zeppelin wishes to position itself as a 5 star resort property. No self-respecting 5-star would offer their guests such thin, scratchy awful towels. In addition the toilet paper provided is shame. More like a combination of card board & sandpaper.
