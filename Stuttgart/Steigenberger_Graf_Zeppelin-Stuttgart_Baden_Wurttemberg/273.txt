We enjoyed two nights in the Steigenberger Graf Zeppelin and really couldn't have been happier for the stay. We arrived in Stuttgart by train and the hotel is literally across the road and unmissable due to its size.  The greeting at reception was warm and friendly and, although this was the first time we'd stayed here, we were immediately given a room upgrade - nice! On reaching our room, we were quite astonished - it was a huge suite with separate bathroom, dressing room (as large as some hotel rooms we've stayed in!), lounge area and enormous comfy bed. The room was on the front of the hotel but well soundproofed and road/rail noise was not noticeable for us.
