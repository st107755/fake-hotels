Finally made it back to Stuttgart after some years. Had 4 beautiful, sunny days. As we laid our stay on a bank holiday, we chose a hotel in the middle of the city with some wellness amenities. Did not use the spa area at all as it was around 25°C during all the days we spent there. Room was pretty big but they clearly saved space when designing the bathroom. In the shower we needed to be careful to not flood the toilet area when showering normally. Mirror and sink are ok for guys but there is too little space to put things for the ladies. Bed was very comfortable and there are plenty of chairs and small tables to put ones shopoing treasures on. Although the place is at the junction of two of the busiest streets in Stuttgart, you don't hear a thing inside.
