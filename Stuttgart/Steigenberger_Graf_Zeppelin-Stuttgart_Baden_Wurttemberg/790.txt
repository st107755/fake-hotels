The pros: We stayed in this hotel recently for 2 nights and were happy with the overall quality of the hotel in terms of the room.  We stayed in a spacious deluxe room with a newly updated shower and bathroom.  The bed was comfortable and the hotel itself was very clean.  The hotel is in a good location if you want to drive to the nearby Porsche factory/museum, which was the main reason we were in Stuttgart.  The cons: We didn't find the location next to the train station to be very atmospheric or convenient since we were not traveling by train.
