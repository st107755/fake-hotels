The evening 'tuck down' was around 5pm - before we showered for the evening. We needed the towels to mop up the bathroom floor, so no dry towels on our return or for the next morning! The 'bar' space was next to the front door and was usually full of people (and their luggage) waiting to take the train to the airport and making use of the free wi-fi. No residents only space.
