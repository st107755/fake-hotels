The Zeppelin is beautiful yet comfortable hotel nestled right near several great eats in Stuttgart. The hotel itself has a top notch Steak house which is pricey but well worth it. The Block House right across the street has amazing TBones and the best baked potato's I've ever eaten. Sorry I don't mean to rant on about the food but my girl friend and I ate there 3 days in a row and I'm craving it now.  The rooms at the Zeppelin are setup perfectly and quite spacious. They have new modern decor and nice bathrooms. For the price of the hotel rooms that you pay you'd expect free internet but here it's for fee which to me is tacky. The concierge was very knowledgeable and helpful. Some of the other staff at the front desk were a little rude in our dealings.
