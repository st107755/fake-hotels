Room was nice and spacious but I was disappointed by the bed. King size bed had 2 separate mattresses with a gap between them - during the booking 3 different bed types were offered so I booked king size expecting it is 1 big mattress. It would be helpful to provide more details about bed types during booking.   Pillows were very thin (around 5 cm) and even though I used two I felt like there was none. This was my worse bed experience in a 5 star hotel.   Bathroom was clean and nice but water from shower was leaking into the room so the carpet in front of the bathroom was wet. Bathrobes were a bit tired with visible sewing fixings.  Room service was great and food was nice. The service in the restaurant was great. The only downside was the dessert in the restaurant.
