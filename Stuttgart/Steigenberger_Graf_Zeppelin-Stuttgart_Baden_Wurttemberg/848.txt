I stayed here on business for 4 nights in mid-December.  Other reviewers are right when they say the outside is not attractive, but the inside is lovely.  The lobby and restaurants are nicely decorated.  This is a great hotel for a conference—the hotel provides good support.  The location is perfect—across the street from the main train station and around the corner from the pedestrian shopping area and the Christmas markets.  The rooms were decorated in a simple Scandinavian style and were clean and well maintained.  Plenty of room for suitcases, generous closet space, a well-lit desk.  The bathrooms were spotless, bright, if a bit small.  The only real complaint I have was with the bed.
