My wife and I stayed here while doing a European delivery of a Porsche. I stay at a lot of hotels for business and really enjoyed my experience. I find what differentiates most hotels (aside from location and amenities) was the quality of the staff. They were truly excellent. Among other experiences on my last day of the stay I mentioned to the concierge that I had paid for the breakfast in my package and had an early train so would miss it. His immediate response was "no problem" and with the front desk arranged for a (good!) boxed breakfast which I grabbed at 5:00am on my way out. The concierge was excellent with directions and helping park my new car in the garage. Will book my hotel there (or try another Steigenberger) on my next trip. A truly excellent experience.
