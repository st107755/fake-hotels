This hotel is located right between the main station and the shopping streets. The location will be my main reason to stay there again. The room (123) was spacious, including a walk-in closet. Breakfast was delicious and elaborate but pricey (26 Euros as far as I remember) and so were the spa treatments (very satisfactory, lovely staff, but expensive: 75 Euros for a facial treatment). I would recommend booking beauty treatments in the city at half the price. Also: the hotel only offers 30 minutes of complementary W-LAN per day. Any time over this limit costs about 5 Euros/hour. This, to me, seems embarrassing and unworthy of a quality hotel, especially since they advertise "W-LAN im gesamten Hotel".
