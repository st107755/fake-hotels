We have stayed at the Hilton Garden Inn and the Le Meridien in Stuttgart.  When we were booking for the World Gymnastics Championships a year ago, neither hotel was available.  We gambled on the Steigenberger based on the reviews and photos, and the gamble paid off!!  First off, the hotel is directly across the street from the main train station.  And close to shopping and restaurants.  Second, we requested an eUpgrade to a junior suite and it was fulfilled.  The room was huge, clean and well appointed.  Third, the breakfast buffet is absolutely amazing!! An extensive selection of food choices. Plus a menu to order from if you cannot find your choice on the buffet - all included in the price of the buffet.  Fourth - a major shout out to the ladies in the breakfast room.
