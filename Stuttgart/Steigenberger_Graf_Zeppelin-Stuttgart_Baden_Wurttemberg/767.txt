We have spent 5 nights in a junior suite. The hotel has a very convenient location, the hall is elegant and spacious; unfortunately we could not sit at the bar to enjoy a drink, since the smell of cigar (I suppose from the cigar lounge) spreads all over the place making it very unpleasant. The room was spacious and very comfortable, with all the toiletries you should find in such an hotel, but the cleaning and the room service were not at the level we expected. The furniture and the TV were under the dust, and the carpet floor seemed not to be cleaned carefully every day.
