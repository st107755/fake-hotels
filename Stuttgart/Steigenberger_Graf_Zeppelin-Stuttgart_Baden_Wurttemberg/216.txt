Like many German cities, Stuttgart was very heavily bombed during the war, so in general the city has a modern post-war look, and the Graf Zeppelin Hotel reflects this.   Its location is excellent - especially if you are travelling by train, as I was - as it is opposite the station, and is also only a short distance from the restored historic area around the Altes Schloss and Neues Schloss.   The staff were friendly and helpful, and the customer-facing staff at reception, the bar, etc. all seemed to speak English to a greater or lesser extent – sometimes, very good English, making my attempts at German appear, at times, rather pointless (although it seemed only polite to try...).
