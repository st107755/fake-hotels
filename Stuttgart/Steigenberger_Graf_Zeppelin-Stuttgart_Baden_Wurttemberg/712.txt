Stayed here with wife and 2 young children just before Christmas as we were visiting the Christmas markets. There were not a lot of hotels in Stuttgart that could accommodate 2 adults, a 4 yr old and a toddler in a cot bed, so we had to have a Junior Suite. yes, it was more expensive than we would have originally budgeted for, BUT, it was well worth the extra price! Junior Suite was nice and spacious with a large sofa bed for our 4 yr old and space for a cot bed. The children still had enough room to move around the room without feeling couped up! Nice big bathroom, spacious closet and even a nice entrance area before you even got into the room.
