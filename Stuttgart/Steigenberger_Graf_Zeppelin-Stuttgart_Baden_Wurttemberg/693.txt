Wow, were we ever impressed with this hotel.  You cannot find a better location in all of Stuttgart, as far as I'm concerned.  RIGHT by the Bahns (trains), just around the corner from shopping and convenience stores.  The staff was extremely nice and helpful. Back to the hotel.  The delicious buffet breakfast is only 9Eu each, which, by California standards for something similar is about half the price.
