First time visit to Stuttgart and it could not have been easier.  Train from the airport direct to the central train station located opposite the hotel - you cannot hear a thing once in the hotel unless you open the window. The train station also has access to the subway and the town centre is a 5 mins walk away. The information centre is located at the top of the main high street which you will pass walking from the hotel and we purchased a 3 day travel ticket for all modes of public transport for 2 zones [ as we visited both Ludwigsburg and Esslingen - highly recommended ] .  The interior of the hotel is very contemporary, the reception staff were very friendly and helpful with information about the hotel including the spa and swimming pool which are lovely.
