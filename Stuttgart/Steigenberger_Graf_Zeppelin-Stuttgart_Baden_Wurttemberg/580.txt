The hotel is located directly opposite the train station in Stuttgart, right next to one of the entrances to the underground subway area (shops, main trains and local trains/trams). It is also just one block away from the start of the main shopping street.  The hotel’s lobby was not huge, but nicely presented, and includes a lounge/café on the ground floor. The Reception staff were friendly and efficient and, of course, spoke very good English. The lifts to the upper floors were small and quite slow, but it’s not a high rise building so this wasn’t a problem.  We had room 312, which turned out to be a suite, rather than a normal sized room. It was right at the end of the corridor, and had a view directly over the large construction site opposite (next to the train station).
