Upon arrival, we had to wait about 15 minutes to check in, as there were only two members of staff on the reception. I inquired if we could purchase breakfast at the discount price, and was gruffly told no, that is only for customers on the special rate (therefore if you see a rate with the discount offer, book that as otherwise you won’t get the chance). Our room was comfortable and well presented, with a very modern bathroom, on the fifth floor near the Zeppelin Spa.
