I've stayed two different times in the Graf Zeppelin.   One was last year in May and was quite disappointed. Our room was right next to a renovation area and had to wait almost 50 minutes to bland cheese sandwich since the restaurant was already closed.  I stayed there this year again in July and had a much better experience. Stayed in a nicely renovated room and had a very pleasant time. The bathroom was great but had a little problem keeping the water inside the shower box. Service was good especially the blonde lady serving at a small cafe in the main lobby area. Although she spoke broken english she was very pleasant and very efficient (pity a didn't get her name). By the way the hotel location is great if you're arriving by train.
