I have stayed here several times over past few years for month-long stays. In comparison to five other Stuttgart hotels in the same class, SGZ ranks above them all. The hotel is a nice size, not too big or small. Rooms and bathrooms are spacious and clean with good views. The hotel lobby is an enjoyable area to chat and have a drink. Location is ideal. Restaurants offer a variety of food; there are many restaurants within a two-minute walk. Pool and sauna are clean, relaxing and stay open late. What makes this hotel such a nice place to stay are the people who work here. Each and every staff member is incredibly kind and accommodating. All speak perfect English as well. Julia, assistant front desk manager, is particularly exceptional.
