I booked an Executive room for a one night stay over a holiday weekend and received an excellent rate. I was assigned an enormous room with quite modern outfittings. Really nothing to complain about if also nothing to induce over-excitement. A nice well-designed room in an international chain hotel. The exterior is so hideous it is now trendy...do not be put off. I felt it well-represented this prosperous, no-nonsense industrious city. Check in was friendly and efficient. The spa seemed a bit busy. For those arriving by train (including the S-Bahn from the Airport), the location is ideal. It is a short walk along pedestrian streets into "downtown" Stuttgart...gaining character with age (i.e. mostly post War) if not beautiful.
