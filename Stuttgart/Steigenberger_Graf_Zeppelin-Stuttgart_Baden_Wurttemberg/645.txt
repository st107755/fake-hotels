Easy location. Just across the street a few meters from the Stuttgart central station. When you arrive at the station, take the exit near McDonald’s (on the right side), you’ll pass by lockers and will need to go down some flights of stairs to get to road level. There is another way through the subway station underground but it’s less straightforward. The hotel is a pleasant walk to the Schlossplatz with countless shops and boutiques along the way. Plenty of taxis in front, one of which we used to get to the Mercedes Museum (it cost just 1-2EU more than if we took the train).   The receptionist gave us a warm welcome and walked with us to the elevator.
