The staff makes this one of my favorite hotels and the best in Stuttgart. Professional, well trained, friendly, young, energetic and always helpful employees make the difference. The hotel is an easy ten minute walk to the main train station, plaza and shopping. The rooms are clean, modern and the beds are comfortable. The first floor spa is extensive with a sauna, steam room, whirlpool, pool, lounge chairs, with massage and facials available. The gym has hardwood floors, ballet bar, and is well equipped with a universal, bicycles, treadmills and elipticals, although a bit light on the free weights (pardon the pun).
