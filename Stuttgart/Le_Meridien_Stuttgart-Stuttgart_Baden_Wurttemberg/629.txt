Stayed for 4 nights and although part of a group, found no compromise in service. Excellent throughout. The whole hotel was spotlessly clean. Staff were always helpful and with a command of the English language that would put a lot of British hotel staff to shame. The room was spacious, with very comfortable bed.  The bathroom walls seemed to be made up entirely of mirrors, - nice in one respect, but not too flattering when you catch sight of yourself from an angle that you've not seen before. It certainly makes you think twice before having that extra Danish at breakfast in the morning.  The breakfast was as good as you'll get anywhere, although they did run out of Ceylon tea one morning.
