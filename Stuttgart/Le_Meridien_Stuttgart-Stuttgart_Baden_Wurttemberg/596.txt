Recently spent three nights at this hotel. It's a somewhat reluctant participant in the SPG program so as a plat was given an executive room (despite availability of junior suites), which is basically a standard room on a higher floor. One positive of these rooms is you can get a nice view across the park to the city without much traffic noise.  The standard rooms themselves are very nice.  A good size, well furnished and in good condition.  Housekeeping also does a very good job. I was pleasantly surprised that the rooms had a modern contemporary feel especially compared to the older style lobby.  The TV also has a good selection of english language selections.  A handful of resilient protestors remain in the park - but are no risk or threat.
