We had a wonderful 3-night stay in July 2017. We specifically booked a quiet room, facing the gardens because we were aware of the construction going on in the city. The room was very quiet and seemed recently renovated. We were in the Schloss Wing and our room was tight, but OK for 3 of us. The extra bed was actually very comfortable.  The service at this hotel is amazing. Very kind, knowledgeable and helpful front desk staff and very friendly staff at the morning breakfast.  The walk to the start of the pedestrian shopping district was very easy...about 7 minutes walk through the park. We also drove and used the hotel parking lot. We always found a space and it was easy to drive to the Mercedes Museum and other places around Stuttgart.
