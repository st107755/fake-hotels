I stayed at the Le méridien for one night during a visit to Stuttgart. The hotel is quite easily to find and offers a large parking lot. It is close to the park, train station and city center with it's shopping areas.  The staff was friendly and welcoming during our whole stay.  The rooms are quite spacious. Ours offered 2 queen beds, a large desk with office chair (not pressed against the wall as you usually find them) and a well sized bathroom. Everything was clean as it should be and there was no lack of any equipment. The spa and wellness area are worth a visit, nicely designed and a great place to relax.  Regarding the fact that the rooms themselves were a real bargain (115€ for a room with 2 queen beds) the value for money is fabulous.
