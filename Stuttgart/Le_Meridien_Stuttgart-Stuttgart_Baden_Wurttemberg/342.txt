Le Meridien, Stuttgart, is a luxurious hotel in the heart of the city. The facilities were great - lovely spa (though some nudity in the sauna / steam room - it is Germany remember!), big twin beds, great size bed room and good shower room. It felt like it had been renovated recently - not run down at all. The staff were really friendly and helpful, both beforehand and during our stay. The Stuttgart football team stayed here when we were - it's that nice! Close enough to the centre of town to walk, but far enough away to be quiet. It is on a main dual carriageway, but we were at the back of the hotel and didn't hear a thing.   The only issue was that I wouldn't feel safe walking back from the city centre in the dark through the park.
