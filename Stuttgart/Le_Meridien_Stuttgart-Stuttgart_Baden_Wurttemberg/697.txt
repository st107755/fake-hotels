My 13-year old son and I stayed here for two nights at the end of a (long) 3-week tour of Europe, and it greatly exceeded our expectations.  It was raining when we arrived in Stuttgart by train, and we took a taxi from the station to the hotel--unnecessary, but not expensive.  The hotel staff were wonderful, from the front desk staff to the concierge to the wait staff.  They were polite, spoke great English, and were there to help.  We wanted to visit the Mercedes Benz museum, and the incredible concierge spent so much time with us, explaining how to use the Public Transit (very easy, very efficient, very German!) and how to make the most of our time in Stuttgart.
