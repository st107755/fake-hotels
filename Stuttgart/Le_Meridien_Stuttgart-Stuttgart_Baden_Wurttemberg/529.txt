I was visiting Bosch for work and stayed close to the rail station at Le Meridien. It was a terrific choice. I arrived very late due to delayed trains. The hotel staff were extremely friendly and welcoming and checked me in very quickly. I had a comfortable room on the second floor, away from the street - it was quiet, well decorated and very clean. A particular mention should go to the pillows (which sounds odd, I know). For some reason, at many hotels I find the pillows too soft or too hard. These were perfect - don't know how they did it! :-) I slept very well both nights I was there.   Breakfast in the morning was excellent - lots of choice, good (Illy) coffee and fresh bread. The staff at breakfast were very efficient and helpful.
