The hotel is about a 10 min walk from the hauptbahnhof. Check in was very easy and the assistance received from the front desk staff was excellent. The (standard) room was a good size with a huge and very comfy bed. The Bathroom had a bath, excellent shower, robes and slippers. The room had a desk, ironing board, safe, flat screen TV and ample hanging space for clothes. The room overlooked a busy road, but this could not be heard from the room.  The breakfast was fairly expensive, but the service received was excellent and there was a good selection of food.  My wife and I have stayed in many hotels in Stuttgart including the GRAF ZEPPLIN and ALTHOFF HOTEL AM SCHLOSSGARTEN, the Le Meridien compares very well to these similar standard hotel.
