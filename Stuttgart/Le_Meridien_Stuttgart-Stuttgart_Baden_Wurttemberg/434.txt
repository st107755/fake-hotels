The Méridien Stuttgart is a pleasant modern business hotel in central location. On the one hand, this is a blessing. The city center with shopping, museums, the world-famous art gallery, opera house, and ballet are all within a short walk. On the other hand, one of the city’s main thoroughfares with heavy traffic is in front of the hotel. Just across is one of Germany’s epic building sites, Stuttgart’s new underground central station which is eating into the city’s “central park”, the Schlossgarten (castle garden) with its greens, ponds,  and old trees (much to the dismay of parts of the locals – in fact, almost all of the park is still there and very beautiful). In any case, the noise of ongoing construction and traffic can be deafening.
