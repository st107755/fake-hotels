The one night we stayed in Le Meridien is one that will not be forgotten nor be repeated. We were walk-in guests and the welcome at the reception was warm and nice. However, the room was dirty since there was an old cigarette but (non-smoke room) on the floor and long black hairs were found throughout the room. But the worst of all was the baby cod, for a 5 star hotel one would expect that also the little ones get treated like little stars but it was a disgrace. I had to wrap blankets around the so-called mattress to make it a bit more comfortable for the little one and fortunately I had brought his own blankets too. The breakfast was good though - but the plates weren't cleared as they should and we had to move them to surrounding tables.
