I have stayed in the Maritim a number of times (which is not a good hotel) and this Hotel is a big step up. Good size rooms, nice TV (with broader range of English channels), comfortable bed and good ambience. Shower is a bit pokey but clean and acceptable. Buffet breakfast nice. The closest restaurants are about 1 kilometre away which is fine usually but a little trickier with the rain. The staff were very helpful during the entire stay and they showed me a very nice park across the road for an 8 kilometre jog. All the front office staff were great; I just wish hotels could train their cleaning staff to be more friendly and cheery. I know it is a tough job but surely they would enjoy it more if they did it with a smile and engaged with guests.
