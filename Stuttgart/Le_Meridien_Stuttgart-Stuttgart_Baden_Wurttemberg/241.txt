Location: Great location, despite construction. Those two things might have swayed my opinion of this hotel alone…The bed was comfortable (spring) and the room was a good size, though I’d expect bigger for the price. The room has plenty of lighting which is rare in hotels I think. The bathroom was nice with a large tub/shower combo. The room was very clean at the staff did a fantastic job making the room feel very customized and cozy.
