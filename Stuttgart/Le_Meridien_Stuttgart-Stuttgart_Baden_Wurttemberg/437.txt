I had to book the Le Meridien in Stuttgart only because during the period of my travel there were few vacancies: the city was invaded by a enormous Chinese delegation touring Germany during the visit of China's president. Chinese had colonized the city for that week. I travel often to Stuttgart for business and I use another hotel with which I am very happy with its price/value offer. The rate I secured on the hotel's internet site was quite high (Euro 240) not including breakfast and only internet (luckily, because the rates are outrageous). The day will come when hotels will realize they cannot extort money to their customers for something that is just as necessary as water or electricity. Please, don't book hotels that do not include internet!
