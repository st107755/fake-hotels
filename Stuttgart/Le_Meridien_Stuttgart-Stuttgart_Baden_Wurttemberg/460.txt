The Meridien Hotel is in an excellent location, a very short walk over the pedestrian bridge, through the park and you are at the train station and the shopping centre. We went for the Christmas markets, it was fantastic, the largest and best so far! Stuttgart is a beautiful city. The staff at the hotel are extremely friendly and speak excellent English. We arrived early but they had a room ready for us. The room was facing the busy road at the front, but it was silent! Not even vibration from the traffic! The room was large and very clean, as expected. The bed was a proper very large double bed, not two singles pushed together, and one very large quilt, not two singles! The pillows were plump and firm, we slept for nearly 12 hours each night!
