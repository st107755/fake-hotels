We stayed at Le Meridien for 2 nights. It is a wonderful hotel. Check in was very quick, efficient and and the staff was very friendly; we were offered an upgrade on our room class right away. Rooms are very modern and very clean. Our room was on the fourth floor, overlooking a park and fitted with a real king size bed (not the normal twins pushed together). The hotel has a very nice spa and pool, free of charge with the room. The pool area is great for adults and part of the spa so children need to be very well behaved. The spa area looked wonderful. We tries the buffet on Friday evening and it was simply great. The other days, you have the regular restaurant which is excellent; but we must recommend the Friday's buffet!
