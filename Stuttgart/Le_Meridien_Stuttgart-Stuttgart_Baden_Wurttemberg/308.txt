We planned a short getaway with friends and booked us in for the weekend. The guest relation department did their very best and if you arent yet, subscribe to the SPG membership, to collect the points and make use of certain benefits.   Arrival, concierge and front desk team did a great job. We used the SPA, as well as the Wulle Bar - if you fancy German Bundesliga, get in there.. its a cosy place to watch the games and try some traditional beer. Ask for Chris, very hospitable guy, just in his apprentice, but doing his utmost to serve you well (getting snacks from the hotel, arranging cigarettes, refills automatically while keeping eye contact).  At night time you shouldnt miss the cocktails at the hotel bar either.   We will come back!
