My wife and I stayed three nights at this hotel in early October as part of a brief European holiday. We found it a very good comfortable hotel with friendly, helpful and knowledgeable staff. The amenities were of a high standard and well maintained. Location was excellent with only a 5-7 minute walk to the central station and the start of the main shopping street. There is a pleasant park opposite  (must use the footbridge to cross the road as it is very busy- quite a few stairs if carrying suitcases). The room was comfortably furnished with plenty of room. The bed offered an excellent nights sleep. Bathrooms were of a high standard with a large shower. The bar area was very relaxing (live piano music on some nights) and very popular.
