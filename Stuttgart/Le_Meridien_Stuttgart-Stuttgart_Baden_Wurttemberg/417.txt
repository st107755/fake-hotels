So Le Meridien in Stuttgart is pretty much the perfect place for a business stay, and pretty cool for  leisurely stays as well.  The hotel is extremely clean -- it just feels crisp.  The hotel staff honestly could not be any nicer or more accommodating.  Breakfast buffet is great, coffee is yummy... all good news here!  The location is a 5 minute walk to Königstrasse -- a long pedestrian walking strip with tons of shops and restaurants and sights to see in Stuttgart.  So a great place for business stays.    The only item I might add -- and it's certainly not a "knock" on this hotel -- is that it is not exactly filled with "character".  It's fairly modern -- but not "ultra-" modern.  So I wouldn't exactly call it "charming".
