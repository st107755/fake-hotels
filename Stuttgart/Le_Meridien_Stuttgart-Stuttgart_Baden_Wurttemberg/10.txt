We have stayed 2 nights in this hotel. I looked forward to staying in Le Meridien hotel before the trip, even knowing that there were few shops open during New Years eve and New Year’s Day holiday. Yet, I was quite disappointed in the arrangement of the hotel. First, it took 20 minutes walk to the city centre, and there were construction sites all around the hotel area, which took extra time to get to the city Center. I think it will be more thoughtful if the hotel can provide shuttle bus services to the city Center, which is quite common as a five star hotel service. On New Year’s Day, some of the museums are opened but generally shops are closed in the city Center.
