Stayed here for two nights as part of travelling Germany vacation and what a great hotel to start our holiday. Getting to the hotel was daunting due to the amount of building work going off. My advice is look for the Schlossgarten exit and follow the signs to the planetarium.   Check in and out was friendly and professional, staff are all so very polite in the hotel. The room was spacious, and the bed was one of the most comfortable I’ve ever slept in.  Breakfast was a great choice, everything you could want, the only niggle is the way the coffee is organised, could be better.  We spent both evenings in the hotel bar which was more like a posh members bar.
