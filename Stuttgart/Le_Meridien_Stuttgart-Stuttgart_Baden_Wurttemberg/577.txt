At first appearances, the hotel may seem very businesslike but once you meet the staff you will find it is a very welcoming place.  For any trip to Stuttgart, business or pleasure, Le Meridien is my first choice because of the friendly staff and the convenient location.  Every trip I took with my family, they treated my children wonderfully!  There was an instance where we mentioned we were going to have a family movie night and the manager brought my children milk and cookies.  The front desk staff are always so welcoming and always remembered my name. They are like a family away from home.  Their rooms are very accommodating;  the regular rooms facing the garden are peaceful and the suites are very roomy.
