We spent 8 wonderful nights at Le Meridien and had an absolutely fantastic experience. The staff was very efficient and accommodating. Our room was  extremely comfortable and the bathroom was spacious and well appointed.   The hotel’s amenities included a pool, hot tub and fitness center—all of which added to our already wonderful experience. Parking is a modest 20 Euro per day and a taxi to the airport generally runs about 30 Euro. The hotel is within a 5 minute walk to the Stuttgart Train Station and the city’s main shopping district. We found the breakfast buffet (30 Euro per person) to be quite enjoyable and a great way to start the day. During our stay, we had a chance to enjoy the Friday Evening French Buffet.
