I stayed at this hotel over Christmas 2013 It is a lovely hotel with great rooms and a really good atmosphere. The price over Christmas was quite reasonable. The closeness to the airport was very handy with downtown just a 30 minute train ride away. At 15 Euros for a three day pass it was also cheap to get around the city. I cannot review the restaurant or the bar and this is where there was a little disappointment. It seems that over Christmas this hotel turns into a high-end restaurant. On Christmas day there was a buffet that was quite expensive. They closed the bar/cafe for the whole period of our stay (four nights.)
