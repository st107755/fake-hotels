It's too bad that is only an airport hotel. It is SO nice. I'd really like to see a hotel like this in the downtown Stuttgart area. Also, I wish I had been able to stay here for the entire duration of my trip in Stuttgart and not just the night before our early flight. Having previously been at the Ritz Carlton in Aruba a week prior, this hotel is right up there with the Ritz. I consider myself a bit of a hotel Princess, and this hotel did not disappoint. The accommodations are luxurious and modern and the hotel room was immaculate. This hotel has a very trendy/cool vibe and reminds me of the Cosmopolitan in Las Vegas. Well done!
