Frequent user of this hotel (both solo and with family) with no negative experiences.  While I agree with other reviewers regarding high prices (compared with nearby properties), this hotel is a classic example of the phrase "you get what you pay for."   The location cannot be beat for convenience associated with early morning flights... A 5 minute walk to the airport (vice a taxi or shuttle).  The terminal has several food options (including a very good Asian noodle/vegetable vendor, as well as a small grocery store) for anyone wishing to save some money on hotel food service. The rooms have a solid feel to them and are much quieter than the norm; ambient noise from other guests is usually non-existent.
