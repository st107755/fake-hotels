I stayed at the Movenpick for business over a year ago, because it is virtually by the airport in Stuttgart.  I did not expect it to be so "grand" and luxurious. Clearly I had not stayed at a Movenpick before! The place is massive, from the halls, to the bars, to the restaurant, and even the rooms are large and comfortable.   The one thing i loved most about the rooms are the design and décor. Dark walls, dark wooden furniture, large comfy beds, large mirrors, beautiful large leather chair in front of a large flatscreen plasma TV, and a modern bathroom (also black tiled, clean, shiny, and very "funky").   The prices are not cheap, but for a guaranteed good stay at a prime location and at an excellent Hotel, the prices are fair.
