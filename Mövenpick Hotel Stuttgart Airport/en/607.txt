Stayed at this hotel for 4 nights whilst attending an exhibition at Messe Stuttgart. Although the hotel appears quite expensive when booking, this is not a case of paying extra money for nothing. The rooms are modern, decorated to a very high standard, spacious and spotless. Without exception, all of the staff were very pleasant, welcoming and professional. The restaurant had a nice ambience and the food was delicious and of top quality both for dinner and breakfast, and the breakfast choice was extensive too.  The bar was also a cosy and relaxing place to unwind. .   The location is great for visiting shows at the Messe - just a 3 minute walk away.
