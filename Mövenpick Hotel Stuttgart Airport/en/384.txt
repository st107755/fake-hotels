I am in Stuttgart for 1 day for business so thought I would take a chance and stay at the airport. The Moevenpick hotel is a little jewel. The lobby is bright and modern. The front desk staff friendly and accommodating. I had requested a room when i filled out my online preferences and was super happy to find out I was on the top floor. Room is updated, and very comfortable. The bathroom is gorgeous and as a woman I appreciate that this hotel finally put in a mirror and lighting so you can actually put your makeup on. The bed is very comfortable. A nice touch was the bedside card offering specialty pillows. Just dial #99 and you will be delivered a neck roll pillow which helps if you are sitting in bed reading. maybe you would like a neck supporting cushion.
