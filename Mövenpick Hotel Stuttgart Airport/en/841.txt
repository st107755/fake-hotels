I stayed in the hotel for two nights during a recent business conference also held at the hotel. The hotel was absolutely superb in every aspect. The conference facilities were perfect for the large group that we were in. The buffet lunches were just right for the middle of the day and the restaurant food was also excellent. The hotel is pretty new but everything has been buit to a very high standard. The room that I had was excellent. It was a twin bed room but the single beds were more like a UK double each. Others have commented on the glass wall between the bedroom and the bathroom which made the room quite unique. Could be some fun if you stayed with a partner !!
