I've stayed at the Stuttgart Airport Moevenpick on 8 separate occasions and I've found a consistently elegant hotel with a very professional staff, making the travel comfortable and efficient.   The rooms are amply furnished and the beds are very comfortable. The Internet service is fast and there is a good selection of television channels from around the world.   The restaurant serves a 12 euro breakfast buffet that is expansive and tasty. The coffee is wonderful.   The workout room is equipped with three treadmills, elliptically, and a rowing machine for a sufficient workout facility while on the road.   I choose this hotel because of its proximity to the airport when I have an early morning departure.
