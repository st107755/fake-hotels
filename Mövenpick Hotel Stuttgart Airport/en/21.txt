The hotel is only a few steps from the main airport terminal. If you have a very early flight and don´t like dealing with taxis or trains (or delays) in the wee hours of the morning, this is your best option. Rooms are a very decent size, well equipped and contain all the amenities expected for the price range. I had a 6 am flight and had a great rest and dinner.  Service is very friendly and helpful. The dinner options are excellent (incl. room service). Didn´t try the breakfast buffet as I had to leave before it opened (5:30 am). The hotel is aimed at early-hour and business travelers with the Stuttgart fairground a few steps away. Lots of space for business meetings at the hotel.
