This hotel is literally on the airport's doorstep, but is so much better than your average airport chain hotel.  Our room was almost of a boutique hotel style; fancy, opulent, stylish... (I think that's enough adjectives!) with an equally impressive sleek, modern bathroom with complimentary toiletries, powerful shower and fluffy towels (and 'fashionable' window from the bedroom into the en suite - I'm never convinced by this design feature!)  The bathroom also had blue low lighting to guide you in the middle of the night - a really good idea.  In terms of the room, the bed was large, firm but extremely comfortable, as were the pillows.  There was a coffee machine and hospitality cabinet (an impressive step up from the standard hospitality tray!)
