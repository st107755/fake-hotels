This is an excellent airport Hotel; well located for everything at the Airport.  On our way to visit the Mercedes Benz Plant in Stuttgart, we had the opportunity to stay in the Mövenpick Hotel Suttgart Airport.  The acoustical design is amazing; never heard a sound from outside the Hotel.  The staff is both friendly and helpful.  Our room was very spacious, beautifully decorated and the bathroom was large with very modern features.  We had dinner in the Hotel restaurant.  It had a large menu and wine list.  They were celebrating "The Asparagus Week" and the Chef's creations with spring vegetables.  The food and service were excellent, our compliment to the Chef and restaurant staff.  We had a great evening!
