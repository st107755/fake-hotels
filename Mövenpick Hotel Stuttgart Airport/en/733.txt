For a start, my experience with this hotel was a very good one. The location is unbeatable if you are coming by plane and even more so if your target afterwards is the exhibition and conference centre (Messe Stuttgart). Distances are 100 metres and 150 metres, respectively, with the hotel right in the middle. Having said that, due to excellent public transport (trains into town and trams once there) and the airport’s constant stream of taxis it is also handy for business and leisure in the city. For what it offers it is reasonably priced, with lower rates over the weekend.  The (executive) rooms are spacious and well appointed, the restaurant is very good and staff are a credit to their employer.   Gripes are minor.
