After a business trip in Stuttgart I stayed an additional day to visit the Mercedes Benz Museum. Since I had an early flight for return to the US I decided to spend the night at the airport instead of town. It was a great choice!  The Movenpick is a wonderful business hotel just a few steps from the airport terminal.  The staff were very friendly, the room very comfortable and quiet - all of which made for a delightful stay!  I was particularly impressed with how quiet the room was since it is so close to the airport. They have done a great job of soundproofing the rooms.   I have travelled quite a bit and I think this is by far the best airport hotel that I have experienced.  I highly recommend it and will certainly be back myself.
