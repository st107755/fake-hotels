After 8 hours on the road from Venice, we were well welcomed by Walter at reception. It was late at night but he was all smiles throughout check-in. We were three in a room and requested for an extra bed. They had already prepared the largest room on the floor so that the extra bed would be in a comfortable private corner on its own. Plenty of space for 3 of us and our large suitcases. When I called for extra towels for the 3rd person, Walter humorously exclaimed "Oh my, this is bad service, I am so sorry. Let me bring it up to you right away." Two minutes later he showed up, with towels and even the extra rubber duck. Talk about great service!   The room is clean, beds were comfortable. Bathroom was a good size with a modern design and functionalities.
