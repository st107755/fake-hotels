Stayed here for just one night and it was perfect. The staff were all friendly and helpful, the room was immaculate with a very effective blackout blind and a bed that had an electric control to alter the firmness. Didn't play with it as it was fine to start with. I slept like a log and heard nothing from inside or outside the hotel.  I ate in the bar and this was tasty and fresh and the bar staff were really friendly and chatty. The breakfast buffet was faultless. Lots of choice of both hot and cold selections with an omelette station all nicely presented and freshly prepared. Good coffee as well. The hotel is right next to the airport entrance and has a car park right next door that they offer a discounted rate for. Still expensive at 25 euros per night though.
