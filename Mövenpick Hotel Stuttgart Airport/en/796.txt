We were in Stuttgart in July 2011 and missed our flight.  We didn't want to head back to the hotel we had been staying at so decided to walk across the street to the Moevenpick.    We obviously didn't have reservations and it was before noon so we had no expectation that we would be able to get a room let alone check in early.  We were treated so pleasantly that after missing our flight it was a real treat!  We were informed a room would be ready in 20 minutes and offered water while we waited.    Our room was done in 10 minutes.  The room was spotless, well stocked and beautiful.  The beds (including a roll-in bed) were all comfortable.  I thought it might be noisy being right across from the airport but it was quiet.  The staff were top notch.
