This hotel is just a 5 minute walk from the Stuttgart airport terminal.  It offers an excellent breakfast buffet that is included in my room rate.  The room is nicely decorated with a dark and modern aesthetic that is cheerily offset with a yellow rubber ducky in the shower.  The shower has a glass wall between it and the bedroom.  Despite the hotel's proximity to the airport, my room is remarkably quiet.   The gym is small but well done. It has just two treadmills, two ellipticals, one rowing machine, and various weights. There are complementary towels and at least two large TV screens in the gym. Next to the gym is a wellness/sauna space which looks nice but does not appear to include a pool or hot tub.
