I've used this airport hotel several times now. Last week driving back from Austria, it was a very convenient overnight stay, adjacent to the autobahn and very convenient for the airport facilities as well as the exhibition centre.  Private underground car park is very convenient with plenty of spaces. Reception area is modern and well furnished. Check in was a bit slow because room wasn't quite ready but the staff were friendly ang gave me a luxury bar of chocolate as an apology for the delay.  Room very comfortable and well fitted out. Nice shower rather than bath which, for an overnight stay, was fine.  Best thing for me was the restaurant. A reasonable menu offering a range of local cuisine. Also some very pleasant local wines, both by the bottle or by glass.
