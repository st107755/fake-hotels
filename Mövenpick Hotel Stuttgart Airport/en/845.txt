We have stayed at the Moevenpick Hotel on a number of occasions. My husband and I travel frequently and have high expectations when it comes to accommodation and restaurants.  However, the Moevenpick at Stuttgart Airport is absolutely excellent.  The rooms are impeccable and nothing is too much trouble for the staff. For guests like ourselves whose German is very basic, the staff speak perfect English and will do everything to go the extra mile. The facilities are superb. However, the star of the show is the restaurant. Whilst buffet breakfasts are limited in terms of creativity in most hotels,  the lunch and dinner is exceptional, both in terms of quality, choice, and freshness of products. The restaurant staff are equally as attentive whilst not being intrusive.
