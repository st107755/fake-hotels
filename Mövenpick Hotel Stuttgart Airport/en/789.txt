I stayed at the old Movenpick in 2010.  This newer property is wonderful.  Location is right across the street from the airport.  At the airport there is also a train terminal that will take you right into Stuttgart very inexpensively and efficiently.  There is a fair sized bar and workout room on the lower level, a restaurant on the lobby level and being a fairly new hotel, the rooms are spacious and quite modern. I would highly recommend this hotel if you were getting into Stuttgart late, or if you had an early morning flight out.  I do not know if there are hotels closer to downtown Stuttgart, and if there are, I don't think that they would be as nice as this hotel is.
