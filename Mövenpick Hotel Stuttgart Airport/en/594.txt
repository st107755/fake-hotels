We stayed for three nights in this hotel and cannot fault the comfort or service.  The staff were always so accommodating, without being aloof or patronising. It really is the little things that count - for example, when we asked at breakfast if we could take a little bit of fresh milk back to our room for tea, they gave us a large pot full! The breakfast buffet was very good and the Sunday brunch seemed very popular - just wished we had known about it earlier!  The proximity to the airport terminal and S-Bahn station could not be better.  (For example, we left the room only 5 minutes before we knew the train would depart.)
