I would definitely book here again.  I loved proximity to the airport.  It is just a short walk (come outside of airport and there it is, just cross the taxi lane).  The Wellness room was so relaxing, I enjoyed the saunas, steam room, and foot soak area.  This was just what I needed to recoup after a week of physically challenging adventures.  The bartenders were really polite and accommodating.  The only reason that I'm not giving 5 stars is because I thought my meal in the restaurant lacked flavor and was a bit overpriced (sirloin with onions and spätzle).  I had a similar dish elsewhere and it was much better and 10 Euro less. Next time I stay I will probably utilize the grocery store in the airport for food or eat elsewhere.
