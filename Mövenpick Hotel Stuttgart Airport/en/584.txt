Moevenpick Stuttgart is stylish and modern... the lobby gives that immediate “wow” factor.  But don't be fooled:  no style over substance here.  Their customer service-oriented staff at reception immediately impressed me with their friendliness and professionalism.  The location a stone’s throw from the airport terminal (easily walkable by foot) makes it perfect for a layover or longer.  Rooms are pleasing to the eye with nifty features and include all creature comforts that a discriminating traveler desires: comfortable beds with down duvets; copious towels; tea and coffee maker; and anything else (such as their luxurious robes and slippers) just a phone call away.  One truly feels one could be at a luxury hotel in any of the world’s capital cities.
