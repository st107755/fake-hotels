Stayed at Moevenpick Stuttgart Airport during February 2015.  I was visiting an exhibition at the nearby Messe and the hotel is extremely convenient due to its close proximity.  It is literally a two minute walk from the entrance of the hotel.  The hotel is modern with a contemporary feel.    The room was very clean and comfortable.  The only issue I had was that the bathroom in the bedroom was not very private.  For some reason they thought it would be a good idea to have a window from the shower into the bedroom.  I have stayed in many hotels and at no time did I ever feel that I needed a window in the shower looking into the room.
