After an initial glitch (due to an error by a third party tour operator) we finally got booked in for three nights to the fabulous Movenpick. Anja & the team on reception could not have been any more helpful. We got a room on the 6th floor overlooking the airport terminals, despite being so close to the airport we never heard a sound. This hotel is simply wonderful. The bedroom & bathroom were immaculate. Perfect lighting & wonderful mirrors.  Food & service in the restaurant was brilliant. The breakfast buffet is 100% fresh - making for a wonderful start to the day. We stayed for three nights & the bar & restaurant staff remembered us on every occasion. I couldn't rate this hotel any higher, it made our stay in Stuttgart extra special. Thanks Anja & the team!
