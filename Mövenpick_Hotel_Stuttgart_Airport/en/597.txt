We booked this hotel for a weekend break visiting the Christmas markets, and what a good choice it was.  It's 30 metres from the terminal, and we arrived in the morning, but were able to check straight in. Our room was ready, including the cot we'd requested by email, plus little extras like toddler slippers, animal soap and a rubber duck! The room was spacious, and very quiet, despite the view of the runway.  As mentioned in other reviews, the bathrooms are fairly open, the door and wall to the toilet was opaque, and there's a glass partition between the shower and the bedroom, but it looked great.
