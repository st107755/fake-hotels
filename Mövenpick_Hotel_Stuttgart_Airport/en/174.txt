This hotel looks unappealing from the outside, in my opinion, but when you enter its portals you are greeted with friendly staff, spotless rooms with a funky interior . It is never our first choice to eat in hotel restaurant, but it was late, so we ate in main restaurant, it was such an enjoyable experience that we ate there on our second night too! OK, it wasn't the cheapest place but the food was interesting and well presented. We had opted for the breakfast package which certainly did not disappoint with an impressive array of beverage and bakery items plus an omelette to order station supporting the other hot foods available.
