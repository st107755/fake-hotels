The moevenpick is directly outside terminal 1 of Stuttgart airport  so you can actually walk there with your luggage. There is an S Bahn to the main station from the airport - the city center is about 20 minutes away. Despite being close to the airport its still amazingly quiet. I even had the windows open and did not really get disturbed by the noise. The guest rooms also have blackout curtains which help alot for a peaceful night's sleep.  There are 6 floors - the two top floors are the executive level - which is where I stayed. There are two bar/restaurant areas in the hotel  - one on the lower level which is more of a cafe/night bar (very professional waiters) and then one on the ground floor which is where breakfast is also held.
