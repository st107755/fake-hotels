The room is of decent size and the bed is very comfortable. The restaurant dinner is worth the money and the breakfast spread is excellent. It's clean and all nice. A word of caution, the shower area can be seen from the bedroom. Probably designed more for couples in mind, although there is no bathtub to support a full romantic design. Not sure if all rooms are like this. Also, I find it lacking in the personal services. During check-in, unlike in most other hotels I stayed, the receptionist did not even notice it was my birthday. On the first morning, we were not offered coffee until we asked for it. When my partner asked for refill, the staff filled his cup without offering to refill mine although mine was empty as well.
