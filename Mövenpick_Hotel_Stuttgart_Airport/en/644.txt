I stayed at this hotel for an early flight out of Stuttgart airport. I had recently stayed at several properties in the Unites States that are known for great design, but I've been disappointed in each because the design doesn't add anything to the function of the room and in fact often takes away from it.  Not so at the Movenpick! This is a hotel with great modern design, and everything WORKS! I ahd a basic room and every detail was well-thought out. The room was comfortable and the bed was great. The bathroom was ultra-modern and yet still warm and functional. I love the blue LED nightlight in the bathroom. I love that you can operate almost every light in the room from switches by the bed. I love that the minibar was CHEAP for a hotel minibar.
