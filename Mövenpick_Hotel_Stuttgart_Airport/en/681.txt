Located right across the airport terminal the Moevenpick Stuttgart is a very good business hotel which really stands out when compared with other airport hotels and hotel chains. I had made a similar remark after a stay at another Moevenpick hotel (Nuremberg airport) and now I can confirm it. They seem to pay a lot of attention to decoration, design and harmony of colors. This was especially true in my room. Spacious and well appointed, nicely combining white and black colors it also showed some surprising features such a a club style armchair that you don't really expect in an airport hotel room. Large and functional bathroom with a window to the room. Efficient and professional service. I had dinner at the hotel which was also quite good.
