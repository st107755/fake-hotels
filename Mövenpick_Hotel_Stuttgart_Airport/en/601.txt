This is a nice hotel. Really nice. I travel a lot but would now wish I had more business in Stuttgart so that I had a reason to stay here more often. The rooms are great, the people extremely nice. The breakfast is fantastic - the food in general good.  Why not a 5 stars rating? The dinner took a bit long (1 hour) to arrive. Location - if you're not using the airport it's probably a less great location. Don't think I'd ever give an airport hotel 5 stars. But do go if you can.
