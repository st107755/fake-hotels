I stayed at the Mövenpick Hotel Stuttgart Airport for one night on 28 August 2011.  I was on business and needed to stay very close to the airport.  This is a very nice hotel that is right across the road from the main airport terminal building.  Check-in was fast. The staff members were friendly and polite. The service was excellent and the room was clean, bright and practical.  The bathroom was spotless and the shower was wonderful.  The bed was comfortable and there was very little audible noise from the aircraft taking off and landing.  I had breakfast and dinner at the "Trollinger" restaurant.  Both meals were very good.  Check-out was very fast also. In summary the hotel was great, the room was clean and comfortable and the standard and quality of the food was high.
