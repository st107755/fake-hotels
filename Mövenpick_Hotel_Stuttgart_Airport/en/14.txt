Stayed at the Movenpick while attending a conference at the Messe.  Location is perfect.  Simply walk across the street from the airport.  I arrived around 9:00 AM after flying all night and they got me into me room by 10:00 so I could shower and get to work.  Very helpful and friendly staff.  The Messe is literally right next door so no waiting for taxis or fighting the traffic.  The breakfast was top notch.  They even had fresh papaya to go with the fresh pineapple, grapefruit, melon and oranges.  The servers were very attentive always keeping my coffee full and removing used plates.  The restaurant for dinner was also very good.  I never had to make a reservation at an airport restaurant hotel before but it was worth it.
