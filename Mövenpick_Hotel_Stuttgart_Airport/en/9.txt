We got trapped at Stuttgart airport due to bad weather and were held on our plane for 3 hours before being let off with no support from our airline (Eurowings). We decided to take ourselves to Movenpick which is literally just outside the terminal doors. Despite being wet and bedraggled we were warmly welcomed by reception and quickly settled in our room.  The room was super smart, well furnished, very quiet and comfortable. We both got sorted then headed downstairs to the bar - as it was now 23:00 and we hadn't had dinner - having expected to be at our destination by 19:00. The restaurant had closed but the bar was doing food and again we were met by a friendly waitress who was very happy to serve us dinner and drinks.
