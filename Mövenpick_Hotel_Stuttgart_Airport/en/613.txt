Don't let the dreary-looking building fool you. Once inside, it is like an oasis which took me by complete surprise. The rooms are spacious, with a contemporary but cozy decor. The bathroom didn't have the cold tiles on all 4 walls that you find in many hotels. Instead there was mosaic tile on the floor, wallpaper on the walls, shower with good water pressure... The bed was very comfortable with nice, fluffy pillows. The TV had Many channels, including more than 10 English/American ones (and not just the typical CNN, BBC...) The windows also opened a little for fresh air, and when closed, the room was very sound-proof.  Room service was efficient and surprisingly tasted really good. They had made a mistake with my order but they corrected it within 5 minutes.
