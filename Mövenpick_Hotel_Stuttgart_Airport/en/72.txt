We only stayed one night in this hotel as we were traveling from Amsterdam to Milan but it was a very handy mid-way point - easy to find and would be perfect for anyone flying via Stuttgart!  We were traveling with our dog and this hotel was so accommodating - dog bowl and treats were awaiting us in the room!  We also came across 3 other dogs so it is a popular spot for people with pooches!  We were also given the option of a "quiet" room which provides extra amenities such as soothing teas, eye shades, ear plugs, etc and totally sound-proofed rooms so you are not kept awake with noise from the Airport (it's virtually attached to the Airport).
