Although it is the closest hotel at Stuttgart Airport, it is a lot nicer than what you would expect from a typical airport stay. Nice big room and I liked the bathroom layout with a window to the bedroom (that can be closed if you want privacy) and a separate WC. Good selection on the breakfast buffet.
