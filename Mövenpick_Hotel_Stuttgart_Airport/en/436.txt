This was my first visit to this hotel, I booked directly on their website a standard room but used the upsell system they use to request a cheap corner room, subject to availability.  This was granted on check in.  The hotel is very modern, and I love that.  The room itself was truly a large corner room and everything was functional.   The spa area is very clean and yes, you'll see naked people, as is the way in German sauna's.  I had dinner in the bar area and thought it was very good value for money - I will return to this later as there was a nice surprise at check out when I got my bill!  The hotel wifi is good for free.  It worked fast enough for my needs although you can upgrade your speed for a small fee.
