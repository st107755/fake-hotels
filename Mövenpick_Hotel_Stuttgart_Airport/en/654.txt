My brother and I recently had an occasion to be in Germany and fly in and out of Stuttgart, although we only had one day in Stuttgart. I chose the Moevenpick based on its location and Trip Advisor reviews. I was not disappointed.  We arrived Friday afternoon and just rested and had dinner. Saturday was a trip to town and the Mercedes Benz Museum. The hotel is first class, rooms large and well appointed. They have a coffee kettle, which is a great bonus for one who likes his coffee first thing. The restaurant was quite good, and the staff there very gracious and helpful. Like others I was a bit put off by the 23 Euro breakfast, but found that you can order al a carte.
