I stay here approximately 10 times per year as the company I work for is headquartered in Stuttgart. I have stayed in several other places closer to downtown, which might be more convenient, but I keep coming back to the Movenpick.  Why? The ability to merely walk across the street from the airport means you will be in your room 5 minutes after the plane reaches the gate on arrival. For departure, it means an additional 45 minutes of sleep for those early morning flights as well as skipping the worry of whether the cab will show up at 4 am.  The hotel itself is well appointed and clean. The fitness center is small but nice, and the breakfast buffet is quite extensive. The staff is very helpful and friendly.
