I had a job to do at the airport so flew in the night before and stayed here as it meant only a 5 minute walk in the morning to get to the job site. As it is an aiport hotel the prices are going to be higher than other hotels as you are paying for the convenience. If you plan to go into the city there's a short train ride on the S-Bahn.  As it turns out this was an excellent hotel choice. As you can see by reading other reviewer's notes this hotel has all the right things in order to make your stay a pleasant one.
