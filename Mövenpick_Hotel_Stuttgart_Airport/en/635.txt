My experience with hotels that are located adjacent or next to airports have invariably been unpleasant. They are either not well maintained or are of lesser standard than their sister hotels in other parts of the city. The Movenpick is an exception - it is new, well-managed and highly functional. Staff (at all stations) were helpful and welcoming - the restaurant served "very good" food (for breakfast and dinner), the gym was well-stocked with equipment and housed working saunas and steam rooms. The room was spacious and designed to accommodate business travellers - viz. free wifi, a good size working desk and multiple power sockets. They also offer to print documents - for free - what a novelty!
