I flew in late and night and stayed for three nights. The location is very convenient, you literally walk across about 50 m to the hotel. This comes in handy for a morning flight where you dont have to rush, and has its merits. Similarly the S3 train stops at the airport and if you are coming in from town, that is convenient as well. My four star rating is because of the location primarily.  The prices I paid was a bit on the high side, however others may be able to find sub 200 Euro rates regularly.  The food at breakfast and room service is pretty good as well. My cons are about the staff - I think they can smile a bit more. I get the swiss and german attitudes in this hotel more so than others I've stayed at in the region. It does not feel very welcoming.
