This hotel is a destination in itself for me and I take every excuse to book a stay, usually before or after a long flight. It is such a gem that I kind of don't want to tell you how wonderful it is, because then my 8am early check-in will no longer be possible, as the volume of passengers might increase due to positive reviews. But I suppose it's best for the hotel that the world knows how amazing it is. From the stylish, clean, modern rooms, with flirty glass-window showers, to the beautiful lobby with mad comfortable chairs (you have to try them), this place has become a home away from home from me. There are always apples by the check out so I can get a healthy snack, and two restaurant options plus room service, with delicious dishes.
