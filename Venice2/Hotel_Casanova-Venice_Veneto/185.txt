The location of this hotel is prime, just a short stroll to everywhere you want to be in Venice and that is why you go to Venice [not to hang out in your hotel room or the hotel lobby]. A very convenient walking location to/from the vaporetto to/from the train station [if you take line 1, line 2 drops you off on the wrong side of San Marco and you will have numerous bridges (with steps) to cross on your way from the dock to the hotel] and a very convenient location to the boat service to/from the airport. It is Venice [it is slowly sinking into the lagoon] so all the buildings are old. The walls are too thick for Wi-Fi signals to reach your room, so it really only exists in the lobby.
