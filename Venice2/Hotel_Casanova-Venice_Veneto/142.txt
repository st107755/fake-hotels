Luckily our room was close to the elevator.  Ok, enough of that, this hotel in my estimation, is a decent bed and hot shower that's close to San Marco square.  Not much more than that...  There is a decent coffee place right in front of it, great for grabbing some bottled water (it was very hot this late into June) and a couple beers to take back to the room.
