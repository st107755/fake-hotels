We decided to splurge on this 5 star hotel on our once in a lifetime trip to Venice. We got to the beautiful hotel, only to be told that we were not staying at the actual Bauer hotel but at a "different property". The clerk was apologetic from the get-go once she saw our faces drop. Well this "different property" was in a completely separate building (Casa Nova), with no front desk, no concierge, NOBODY in the lobby. It was around the corner but on a creepy block with random individuals disturbingly watching us every time we walked in and out. There is no security and we found the front door wide open numerous times. The room was large but with a low ceiling and no windows - very dark and dreary - the calibre was 3 stars at best.
