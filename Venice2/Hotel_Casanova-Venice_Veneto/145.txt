I stayed in Venice for 4 nights with my wife and had booked a hotel which was recommended to us for 3 nights, but unfortunately it was fully booked for the 4th so we had to look around for somewhere to spend the last night of our trip.  We picked Casanova as it was close to St Mark's Square, and would be easy to reach by waterbus on the Grand Canal from our previous hotel.  I can't fault the hotel for its location, because it is a 2 minute walk from St Mark's Square, and a 1 minute walk to the Grand Canal. I wasn't impressed with the price though for what in my eyes was a very basic hotel.
