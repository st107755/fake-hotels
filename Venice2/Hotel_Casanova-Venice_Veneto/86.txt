Venice is undisputedly one of the most beautiful, romantic, cultured and atmospheric city's on the entire planet and this hotel is situated right at the heart of it, just a stones throw away from St Marc's Square. The Casa Nova is just a few feet away from anything a Venetian hungry tourist could be looking for be that gondola rides, rich Italian cuisine or tours through the countless museums and churches that the islands has to offer.   On top of that, breakfast at the Casa Nova is absolutely spot on, plenty to choose from and something to everybody's tastes. The staff are also all really friendly and willing to help whenever.
