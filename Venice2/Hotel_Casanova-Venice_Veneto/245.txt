Spent three nights at the Hotel Casanova and it was a lovely time. While some of the decore is dated it was extremely clean and the staff was absolutely fantastic. One of the best things was discovering a full sized bathroom with a shower that any person of any size could fit into. The location was wonderful a short walk from San Marco Square and a multitude of great resturants and boutique shops. Every morning there was a lovely continental breakfast with fresh tea or coffee. The Front desk were fantastic at helping you make arrangements for a tour, tickets to a show or to catch the vaparetto to the airport at 3:30am.
