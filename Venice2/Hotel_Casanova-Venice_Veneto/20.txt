This hotel is in a great location for all the usual places you would want to visit, it is very easy to find from the water bus stop, three minutes walk and you are there. Two minutes to St. Marks Square and all the restaurants and bars. We stayed in the suite, supposedly their best room on the upper most floor. The elevator only goes to the 4th floor then along corridors to a couple of flights of steep stairs to the door of the suite. Not at all easy when carrying suitcases and if you are mobility impaired. This is not mentioned when booking the room. The suite itself was lacking in the finish that you would expect from a 4 star hotel.
