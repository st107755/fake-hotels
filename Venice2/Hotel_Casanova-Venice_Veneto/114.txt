Having never been to Venice before and our time being limited to one night as we were joining our cruise the next day I wanted to book something central, and Hotel Casanova is definitely central. It is about 3 minutes from St Mark's Square and 4 minutes from the vaporetto stop San Marco Vallaresso (also where we got the alilaguna boat to take us to the cruise terminal). The hotel was very easy to find, check-in was quick and friendly and we used the small lift to get to our room.  This hotel is in a very old building and the decor is in keeping with this. Our bedroom was a good size and had a little shuttered balcony overlooking the street outside. Our bathroom was quite large with a shower and a bidet.
