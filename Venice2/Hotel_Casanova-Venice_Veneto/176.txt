Friday, 4 September 2015 Hotel Casanova Venezia Looking for a good hotel in Venice right next to San Marco Square? This hotel is very conveniently located just off San Marco Square and within a 3 minute walk from a ferry landing. Staff was friendly, welcoming and speak good English. The room was large with plenty of comfortable furniture, well air conditioned, very clean and quiet. A refrigerator is provided to keep your drinks cold. After a day’s sightseeing in the heat outside it was nice to enjoy a refreshing shower. There is a lift for this three story building. Getting travel luggage to your room is not a problem. Breakfast was provided in a nicely set out dining room with good views along two alley ways. There was plenty to eat for all tastes. Luggage storage was provided.…
