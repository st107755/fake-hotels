This hotel is close by St. Mark' s Square which is one of the sites that one would probably visit when they come to Venice. From the airport, the hotel can be reached through water bus to San Marco station and from there it would take at least less than 5 minutes slow walk. In terms of location this hotel got points for that plus their staff were very accommodating.  The hotel looks and feel old in a good sense which give this hotel its character. The room that I got was okay although the bed was not that really comfortable and the blanket was too thin in my opinion which I guess was just only fitting during the summer period.
