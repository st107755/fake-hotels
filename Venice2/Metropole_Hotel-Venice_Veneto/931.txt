It's been a long time since I went to the Metropole (July last year), but have been to a number of hotels since and none of them have lived up to the magnificent Metropole.  A minor booking mix-up over card details was smoothed over by Mr Aurelio Bon with a lovely bottle of Prosecco.  The room - junior suite - was fantastic, the garden breakfast, simply a delight, with sparrows flying round pecking crumbs.
