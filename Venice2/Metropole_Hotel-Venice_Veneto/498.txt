This was my first time staying here and it was lovely.  Not the old world elegance of the Danieli or the Gritti but lovely all the same.  They have a dock for a water taxi which is great if you have a lot of luggage, as we did.  Room was going to be ready in about 20 minutes so they gave us some cappuccinos in the lounge to relax with.  We had one of the new rooms they said, on the ground floor off the breakfast room with windows on a small canal.  As this was the end of a three week cruise, views were not the most important thing.  Bed was comfortable, room was large, bathroom had only a shower but was large and comfortable with heavy towels and good products.  Of course bathrobes and slippers were provided.
