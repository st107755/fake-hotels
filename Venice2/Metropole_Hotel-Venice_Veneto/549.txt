This hotel has a touch of the past with its amazing collection of "stuff"- fans, cigarette cases, other smoking items and its rooms with heavy brocade furnishings.  It is conveniently located about 10 minutes walk to San Marco and close to the vaporetto. However, it is just far enough away from the sights so that it is quiet.  They will arrange a water taxi from the airport.  It goes right to the hotel entrance, but at a hefty 110 euros some may consider other options.    The room was not terribly large, but certainly more than we expected in the heart of Venice. We prefer hard beds and soft pillows and found both.
