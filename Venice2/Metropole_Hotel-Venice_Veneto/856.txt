We arrived at the beautiful and somewhat edgy Metropole Hotel by private water taxi that delivered us to the hotel's private dock. The location overlooking the grand canal is perfect yet once you enter the hotel you are in an oasis removed from the tourist scene. The hotel is decorated with impressive collections of antiques from Venice's noble past. The effect is on a carefully decorated and orchestrated space not like a standard hotel more like a large villa. The rooms were exquisite with silk rippled fabric walls and a venetian glass chandelier.  Our room had a balcony overlooking a small canal with a view to the grand canal.
