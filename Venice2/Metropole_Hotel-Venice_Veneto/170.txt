We recently stayed at the Metropole for 2 days to do some sightseeing in Venice prior to taking a cruise.  The hotel is very easy to get to from the airport by walking to the airport dock and taking a water taxi direct – they have their own private dock on the canal and they met us to take our bags right off the boat.  The hotel is beautiful and the staff is so kind and helpful.  We arrived about 0930 and our room wasn’t going to be ready until around 1400 so we changed clothes, stowed our bags and took a nice leisurely walk of the city.  The location is excellent right on the opening to the Grand Canal next to Piazza San Marco, the Basilica and Palazzo Ducale (just next door).
