We chose to celebrate our 30th anniversary with a trip to Italy and Venice was one of our stops. The Metropole was the perfect hotel for us! As others have said, the location is wonderful and the views are spectacular. We were upgraded to a small suite with a roof top terrace which had beautiful views of the the basin and the side canal. The concierges were quite helpful with tours and restaurant suggestions. They answered all my questions even before our arrival which I greatly appreciated. We had lovely breakfasts in a beautiful dining room and would have enjoyed the outside patio area if it had been just a bit warmer.  Each evening we had cocktails in the cozy Zodiac Bar.
