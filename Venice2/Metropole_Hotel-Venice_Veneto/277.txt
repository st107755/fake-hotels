We visited for our 30th wedding anniversary, our first time in Venice (and what a magical city). The hotel was on the waterfront, five minutes walk from St Mark's Square. The staff were uniformly helpful, friendly and efficient so we would stay here again. We were given a bottle of prossecco to welcome us as we were there for our anniversary, a lovely touch. The rooms were clean, quiet and well equipped. One star dropped for the transfer service. I had booked a "private water transfer" from the airport at a cost of €220. In the event we were driven most of the way, only joining the canals for the final 20 minute portion of the journey. On the way back, we booked a water taxi from the hotel direct to the airport (there is a 1KM walk at the airport end) for €110 and it was much…
