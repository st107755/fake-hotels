The first time we stayed at the Metropole was in 2011, when we took a 12-day cruise from Venice to the Greek islands and Istanbul, starting and finishing in Venice. Prior to our cruise we stayed 3 nights at the Metropole. We arrived at the hotel by water taxi and entered the hotel through its private dock. It was very convenient and somewhat magical. The hotel is old, and most of the rooms are rather small, but the ambiance is very special. The decor is typical of old Venice with beautiful fabric hanging on the walls and glass chandeliers hanging from the ceiling. Our room faced the lagoon, and we watched from our windows every morning the activity that took place outside.
