The Metropole is a classic example of a luxury hotel with personalized character. It's been in the same family for ever the result being a unique place full of history. You notice how special this hotel is as soon as you enter the lobby. The public spaces are beautifully decorated with antiques and lots of comfortable sitting areas. In the evenings the light up lanterns which cast a warm glow over everything. The Met restaurant is Michelin starred and is simply fabulous. Just outside is a lovely outdoor garden where breakfast is served. It is wonderful place to hang out and relax. Like the rest of the hotel the rooms are individually decorated with lots of style and comfort. Walking floor to floor is fun because it feels like a museum with loads of art and artifacts all over.
