We happened upon the deal of the century and were able to book a room for three nights at a substantial discount.  The hotel is right on the main canal, a few minutes from St. Peter's basilica.  The hotel has a Michelin one star restaurant, to give you an idea of how yummy the breakfast is.  The room had antique furnishings and there is a somewhat creepy but interesting collection of crucifixes around the hotel.  This hotel was just so interesting, which can't be said about a lot of the places we have stayed in Europe in the year since we moved to this side of the pond.  We were really impressed with the fabulous customer service.  When our neighbor decided to party into the night, we called the front desk to report the noise.
