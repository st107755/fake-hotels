I stayed here for six days with my mother in early December, a beautiful time to be in Venice, when the crowds are gone and if you are lucky (like we were) you can enjoy the beauty of the city under crisp azure skies.  Having spent four weeks in Venice about five years ago, the Metropole was everything I would expect from a Venetian hotel.  The location is terrific and could not be more convenient.  We had a superb room at the back overlooking the garden - very spacious, with a sitting room and large bathroom with a huge tub.  The decor of the hotel is charming, not over the top cliche Venetian, but just the right ambience.  It has the feel of a well established club to which people return over and over again.
