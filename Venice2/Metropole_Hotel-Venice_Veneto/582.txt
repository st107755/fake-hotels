We had a most enjoyable stay at the Metropole, arriving late at night by water taxi to the private launch. Our room was elegantly furnished and of a reasonable size, with a side canal view and very effective air-conditioning.  The location of the hotel is excellent - so near to St Mark's Square, yet also close to more secluded and quiet areas which we enjoyed exploring.  The staff throughout our stay were friendly, efficient and helpful.  Breakfast was most pleasant, with a range of food on offer.  We dined in the restaurant during our stay and enjoyed the experience.  Whilst the food and service were very good, the lack of other diners made the atmosphere a little odd.
