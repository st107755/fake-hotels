This hotel came highly recommended and it did not disappoint. From the moment you enter the rear door off the canal by water taxi, you feel like you have truly stepped into the splendour of Venice. This hotel was romantic, classy, and truly Venetian. Located along the water's edge, one need only step outside to be treated to a splendid vista. Just a two minute walk from Piazza San Marco, you are able to walk to any corner of this wonderful city. The lobby, the sitting room, the lobby bar, the hallways, and the room were all decorated as one would have expected it to look 200 years ago. The bed was very comfortable.
