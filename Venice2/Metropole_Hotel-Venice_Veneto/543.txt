The Metropole Hotel is an odd duck when it comes to Venice. It is a (self described) 5 star property that oozes a 3.5 star vibe. The half star is because they are trying. It is not that the Metropole does not care: they obviously do. But some how attention is lavished upon the wrong things at the wrong times; the over all experience comes off more like a first rate hotel in Reno as opposed to a first rate hotel in Venice.  The Good: The location is close to San Marco Square and right on the Venice Lagoon.  This means that transportation by water bus, water taxi or Gondola is easily accessible.
