Although the Metropole was a little pricey for us, we thoroughly enjoyed every minute of our 3 night stay there.  The staff were very accommodating and friendly while still being professional.  Our room, #207, had a small terrace from which we could enjoy views of the small canal we were on and by looking to the right, the Grand Canal was also visible.  The antique furnishings were gorgeous, while the bathroom was thoroughly updated with a large tub and all marble.  You get the feeling that you have stepped into another time there.  The breakfast (included in our rate when we booked the hotel) was absolutely incredible.
