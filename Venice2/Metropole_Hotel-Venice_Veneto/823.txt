Venice as a whole was brilliant and I would recommend visiting there wholeheartedly to anyone, young, old, families and couples alike. If you want a hotel where the location, service from all the staff, rooms and facilities are top notch then the Metropole is for you. It is a little more expensive than some of the other hotels you can stay in, we actually got a very good deal by booking on Otel.com, but I would say it's definitely worth paying the extra.  The reception staff were excellent, very professional and very helpful, they went out of their way to make sure we had information about the boat service and what was going on and where to visit in Venice even though we hadn't asked for it.
