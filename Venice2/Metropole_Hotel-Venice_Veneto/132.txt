Our daughters and their husbands treated all six of us plus a little one to a four day birthday trip to Venice. Metropole came recommended and it didn't disappoint. What a beautiful place,full of charm, wonderful Venetian decor, splendid antiquities on display in glass cabinets in the corridors. Our room felt extremely hot on the first night and the reception sent a fan to help out. The following morning we were offered a beautiful suite with a large terrace, views over the lagoon, cruise ships passing after dark and splendid rooftops of Venice.  We didn't make use of the hotel restaurant and bar but we loved our breakfast there.  Vast variety of savoury, sweet, continental and Italian food and friendly, attentive service.
