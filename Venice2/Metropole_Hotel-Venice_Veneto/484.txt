We stayed at Metropole Hotel May 2015 for 4 nights.  While the rooms need updating, the superb staff's attentiveness, excellent service and location, I have to give this hotel a 5 star.  We had an issue with our reservation because our travel agent double booked us (reserved 4 rooms instead of 2). The staff was extremely accommodating and waived all of the duplicate fees.  Any other hotel would have charged for at least the first night.  The hotel staff arranged our private tours, our water taxis, and everything in-between.  Recommendations to restaurants were on point, recommending high end as well as local delights.  The Oriental Bar--A MUST!!! I cannot stress enough that this bar has the best drinks we have ever had.
