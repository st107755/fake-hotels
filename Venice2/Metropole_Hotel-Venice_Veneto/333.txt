We had booked for a shortbreak for our honeymoon with neither of us having been to Venice before. And the Metropole was wonderful. We took the water bus from the airport (which takes a very long time) which gave us the flavour of the water  borne city. The stop was right next to the hotel so very convenient. The staff were very welcoming and we left bags to go and explore until out room was ready as we were early. Armed with a wonderful map provided by the hotel we had a great day. On returning our room was lovely and the bed so comfortable we had an afternoon nap. And I  never nomally sleep during the day!  Breakfast was good.
