We stayed in this hotel, one night post cruise, because of a Frommer  recommendation. The hotel is located right on the Grand Canal 3 bridges from San Marco  and has a water taxi entrance.  The hotel had reserved a water taxi for us from the pier and when we got to the hotel the bellhop was at the door ready to take our luggage and addressed us by name. The rooms were ready within an hour of arrival which was about 11 o'clock.  Each of the 4 couples had a different size room, even though we paid the same amount.  All the rooms had different types of antique furniture.  All the beds were kingsize and confortable .  Bathrooms were large and modern with excellent amendities.
