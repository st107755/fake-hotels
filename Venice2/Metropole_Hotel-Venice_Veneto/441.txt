This is a very good hotel for the location and the city.  What we loved about it is the easy access to a lot of attractions in Venice and it very convenient that the water bus station is right in front of the hotel.  I would have given a 5 star except for a few things.  First, we paid lots of money to have a lagoon view - come to find that the only way you could see the lagoon from your room window is if you are standing at the window.  The hotel bed height is much lower than the standard height in the US and the windows were also high up, so you cannot see the lagoon out your window if you are sitting or lying on your bed (there goes the romantic factor).
