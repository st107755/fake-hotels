My partner and I spent our Christmas at the Metropole Hotel in Venice, and it proved to be a very festive choice.  Nestled on the lagoon and accessible by boat, the hotel is a true charmer.  Outside, it was festooned with brightly colored lights to mark the holiday.  Inside, the lobby was a riot of heavy red velvet brocades, twinkling lights, overstuffed antiques, rampant greenery, and large, glass cases filled with local curios, ranging from gilded cigarette boxes to a giant collection of corkscrews.  With remarkable ease, the hotel manages to seamlessly blend incredible whimsy with five-star panache.  Our room was resplendent pink, with four large, marble columns surrounding the massive bed.
