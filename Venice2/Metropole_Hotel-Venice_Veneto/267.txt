The Metropole hotel saved us from every independent traveller nightmare.  The hotel we had originally booked to stay in had cancelled our booking without informing us.  The Metropole found us a room, and a great one to boot.  The staff on reception were really helpful throughout our stay.  The hotel is in a great position just minutes walk from the most important sites - St Marks Square, Doge's Palace and the Cathedral.  The rest of Venice was all within easy walking distance.   The hotel has a old world charm about it, with antique furniture all over the place.    The room we had was a good size with a really comfortable bed and was spotlessly clean.
