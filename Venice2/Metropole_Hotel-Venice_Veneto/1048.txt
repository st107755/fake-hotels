We choose the Metropole partly due to the reviews read here and we had high expectations; also we arrived here after our stays in Rome and Florence at excellent hotels (Dei Mellini and Degli Orafi, respectively). However, we were bitterly disappointed. The hotel is very nice and location is superb, but it was the human factor that disappointed us. We had booked a standard double room and we were given a room that seemed to us rather small, but we thought maybe standard rooms were not so big in this hotel. Then we checked the emergency map and realised it was by far the smallest room on the whole floor, but then we thought the hotel was so full (it was busy indeed) that must be the only room left.
