My daughter and I spent 4 nights at the Metropole in April. The hotel is beautiful, the lobby and the restaurant areas are stunning. The room was nice and clean, but the walls were very thin  (we were awakened by the TV next door several times) and the bathroom tiny. There was no noise from outside, probably because the maid closed the heavy shutters each night. The staff was wonderful, very friendly and helpful. The concierge booked dinners and gave us directions everywhere. Breakfast was amazing, they had everything you could imagine including smoked salmon and desserts. We had a lovely stay during a very busy weekend, however, for the price the rooms at the Metropole are not what I would expect.
