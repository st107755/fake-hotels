Location & amenities  The location is very central, only 100 meters  away from St Mark's square and 50 meters from central waterbus station. The hotel creates a "good old days" atmosphere  together with a little bit mysterious scent  which is very charming and - at the same time - relaxing. The hotel has nice breakfast facilities and a very tasty breakfast. There is room service is available and the hotel has a two star Michelin restaurant (MET). The MET is very popular i.e. you need to book it in advance. The Oriental Bar is very charming and cosy, like cut out from outer world.   Service  The service was friendly and personal.
