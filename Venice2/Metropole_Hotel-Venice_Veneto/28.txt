We stayed for a few days, my husband had booked a package which included champagne breakfast in our room on the first morning, (breakfast in the restaurant on the other mornings) and also included an evening cocktail and dinner in the Michelin star Met Restaurant.   Everything was fantastic, the staff rang to check or preferences for breakfast and our hot drinks before bringing our amazing breakfast to our room. We also loved breakfast in the restaurant, which has a stunning garden.  We had our champagne cocktail before we ate in the Met however had we have know this came with a delicious selection of canapés we would have chosen to have this at a different time.
