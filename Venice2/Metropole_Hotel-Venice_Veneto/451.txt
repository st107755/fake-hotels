What an amazing Hotel.  It was like stepping back in history with the many displays of antiques throughout the premises.  The rooms themselves were large, only negative was they were dimly lit so would recommend the hotel installing brighter, stronger lighting.  The bathroom was well appointed but our room had a shower over the bath which was quite high to step in and out of.  A little dangerous possibly.  The breakfast room at the back of the hotel had both indoor and alfresco dining and either buffet or menu choices, both very nice.  We were pleased with the services provided by ALL the different staff we came into contact with.
