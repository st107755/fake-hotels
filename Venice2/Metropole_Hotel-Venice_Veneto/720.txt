Arriving by water taxi...having the bags offloaded...and then finding that we were given an upgrade to a suite simply started the break off perfectly. The staff were attentive...sometimes a little too much if that is possible...well spoken, courteous, knowledgeable and smiling.  The room was simply amazing and had a roof top balcony...overlooking the lagoon and the side canal. Sunsets up there with a drink were...well, perfect.  The bar was fun...the cocktails fantastic. Great atmosphere... Price...yes, it is not the cheapest hotel around but frankly you get what you pay for. It is Venice, Italy, middle of the summer, platinum hotel on the waterfront, excellent service, wonderful rooms...what do you expect? You don't expect to get a Ferrari for a Fiat price do you?
