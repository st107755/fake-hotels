This was our second trip to the Metropole.  In the 12 years since the last visit, it had improved greatly.  The hotel has been renovated and has a much cleaner atmosphere.  The bathrooms are impeccable with full services and lovely appointments. The somewhat stuffy feeling of the past, now has an elegant cast.  There were several public rooms on the main floor that were quite inviting.  We enjoyed an after dinner drink in the bar on the left.    Hotel staff were quite attentive and provided a very delightful entry to Venice by promptly and efficiently receiving our luggage from the taxi that we took to the dock.
