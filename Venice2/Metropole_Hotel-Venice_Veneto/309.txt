We booked this hotel without doing too much research. What a winner it turned out to be!  We caught a private water taxi (only my wife and I) from the airport where there was a booking office. It cost €110 for the trip to the hotel, but looking back, it was worth it. The trip lasted about 1/2 an hour and dropped us off right at Metropole Hotel's doorway on the side of the hotel.  The front entrance of the hotel itself opens up directly onto the Grand Canal waterfront esplanade and is literally only a couple of 100 meters from St Marks Square....which must surely be one of the most crowded places I have been to anywhere in the world!
