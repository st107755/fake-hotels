We stated at the Metropole hotel in Venice for 9 days, from the 7th to the 16th of april.We were 2 parents, 3 kids and a baby. The location of the hotel is a little far from the center of Venice, but as you walk kms in Venice, it's not a big deal. The hotel is very charming with all these antiques, even if beeing Europeans we are less impressed by old things we can find in our own family houses than Americans. Nevertheless it's very nice and charming. We had a brand newly renovated room with gorgious mosaïc bathroom and a superb laguna and San Giorgio view. We wished we could have had big windows instead of the typically venetian tiny windows !
