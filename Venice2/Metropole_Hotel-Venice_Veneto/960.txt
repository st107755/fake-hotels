When we arrived at the Venice airport, I checked my water taxi reservation and noted the taxi would drop us off at the ‘nearest dock to your hotel’. I asked the representative how far the my hotel was from the dock.  She laughed and said ‘Madam, you are staying at the Metropole’.    I didn’t press her any further and 25 minutes later when we pulled up to the front door of the Metropole and the doorman helped me depart the boat, I understood what she meant.   The Metropole is a throw back to a more elegant and formal time. The front desk area is decorated in reds, dark wods and silver trays. The lobby is well appointed with comfortable couches and dark wood tables (antiques).  Additionally, glass cases contain many collections of evening bags, religious items and cork screws. …
