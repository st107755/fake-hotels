I stayed at the Metropole for 4 nights during the Festa del Redentore weekend. The hotel is in a wonderful location on the water, with a very quick walk to St. Mark's and vaporetto stops. I had rented a suite with a terrace view to watch the fireworks on Saturday night and the view, though somewhat obscured by rooftops, was quite beautiful. My issue with the hotel was the extremely inconsistent level of service I received.
