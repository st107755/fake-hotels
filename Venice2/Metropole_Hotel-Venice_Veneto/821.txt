You are thrust into a historic , old world atmosphere right off the Riva in Venice.   All antiques and red velvet everywhere give you the feeling as if you are a grand lady in an elegant old world place. Rooms are ultra clean with great service with very attentive staff. Vivaldi was said to have come to this place as a music teacher when the hotel was a girl's school.  The owners have paid attention to detauls and it shows.   Comfortable and exceptional experience.
