Venice is such a special place to us, and we yearned to return.  We had no idea what crowds we would experience from the cruise ships passing through.  It would take more than crowds to ruin Venice for us.  We simply enjoyed less-traveled parts of Venice during the day, and enjoyed the Piazza San Marco and other busier areas later when the crowds lessened.  The Metropole Hotel met all of our expectations, with a beautiful, large room which was surprisingly quiet.  The staff was  fantastic, and helped with all arrangements, from the water taxi into and out of the hotel (very worth it, by the way), to directions and recommendations and reservations to restaurants.
