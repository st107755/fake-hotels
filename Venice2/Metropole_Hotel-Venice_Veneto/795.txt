This was our third stay at The Metropole and it was fab.  The staff are so friendly and polite. The hotel is so Venetian, quirky,  yet complete luxury. This time it was room 219 and the view was amazing. You can sit up in bed and look over the Grand Canal (you can even sit on the loo and take in the same view if you fancy!). It was really very quiet at night for a room overlooking the Grand Canal - due to shutters, double glazing and thick curtains. It was also really cool - you can keep the aircon on at night and turn the fan off to keep the room cool but quiet. The room has had a recent refurbishment I think so you still get the fabric lined walls and antiques but you also get a bathroom with all modern facilities - plus a hairdryer that actually dries your hair.
