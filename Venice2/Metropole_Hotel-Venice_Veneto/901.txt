I stayed at the Metropole Hotel for 4 nights, in a Junior suite with a view of the lagoon. The only major problem was the fact that they had a problem with the rezervations and when we arrived there were no double deluxe rooms with lagoon view available (that was what we had booked on the net and payed for), but they offered a better room with no extra charge. However we had to wait for it to be ready for about 3 hours. It was worth the wait, we had a great view of the lagoon, 3 large windows. All the rooms of the hotel are different from each other and we ended up with a great room.
