This hotel has a classic charm to it. It's decor is traditional, but retains with it a taste of the past. Our room was huge - even by American standards. Breakfast was great and plentiful - the cappuccino is the best in Italy! The location cannot be beat. It is right on the main walkway along the end of the Grand Canal and is a 5 minute walk from the St. Mark's Square. The only negative is the obvious kickbacks the hotel gets from local restaurants. Whenever we asked for suggestions, the hotel pulled out brochures of upscale eateries! When asked about other places to eat (even other expensive restaurants), we were met with negative responses and immediately redirected to the restaurants they had advertisments for.
