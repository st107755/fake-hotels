A fast taxi boat will deliver you directly to a discrete canal side entrance off the Rio Della Pieta. Here you enter the hushed eclecticism of a dimly lit world, reminiscent of times long since gone, rich in texture and colour, wafted with the bookish odours of an antiquarian's study. Similarly bedecked the rooms are well serviced with modern utilises, clean and comfortable. Staff are unfailing polite, knowledgeable and willing. The concierges perform miracles. Sat within an open leafy courtyard a full breakfast is taken without fuss, fueling the coming day. Emerging from the comparative sanctuary of the hotel you are immediately struck by the bustle of humanity journeying along the warm sunlit pavements of the Riva Degli Schiavoni.
