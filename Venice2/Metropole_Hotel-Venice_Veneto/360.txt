The hotel is in an excellent position just a short walk from St Marks Square. It has its own landing stage if you take a water taxi from the airport. A cheaper option is to take the Allaguna water bus, blue line and get off at San Zaccaria which is just in front of the hotel.  The building is very old and quirky, furnished with antique furniture and antique collections in cabinets throughout the building. The main lounge was lit by candles and was astounding. If you want a modern bog standard hotel room which is the same all over the world, then don't come here, only come if you want a different sort of experience.
