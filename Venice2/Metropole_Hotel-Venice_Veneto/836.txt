We booked a family room and received more than we hoped for: a huge square room, like 8 by 8 meters, with a very high ceiling, making it look even bigger !  The interior of the room was what one would imagine when thinking of Venice: two antique king size beds and a lot of old paintings, velvet, mirrors, chandeliers, etc. The room - nr 311 - was overlooking a small canal and with a bit of effort you could see the lagoon from the side. At night you could hear the sea rolling into the little canal what was very calming. The bathroom was a bit on the small side with only one sink but was neat and functional.
