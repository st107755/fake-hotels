After disembarking from the most glorious trip on the Orient Express from London we arrived at a The Metropole where we were warmly welcomed by the staff and who remained attentive throughout our stay.  The service was excellent throughout and the atmosphere in the hotel very gothic and eccentric which adds to the vencían charm.    The room was a little snug but very comfortable and the perfect temperature with the sweltering heat outside.   We spent a couple of nights relaxing with cocktails in the gardens/terrace which are very attractively deigned with different private areas to sit if you want some peace and quiet.  We did enjoy the Oriental bar on our first evening with a good selection of beverages and the Michelin star restaurant was  super with excellent wine pairings …
