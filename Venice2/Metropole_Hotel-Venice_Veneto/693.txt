Right, from the top. If you are going by train from Paris ask to go via Zurich as the views on the jounrney down to Milan are very special. When you get to Venice you could get a water bus to the hotel but if you can afford a water taxi, do it, a great start to any visit and it makes you feel very special. I agree with many other reviews in terms of for me the hotel was clean, the service was very warm and friendly as well as efficient, the location is perfect, the garden at the rear is great, the food was good, and I liked the decor, which has Moorish and Oriental flavours. However just like some people think Shabby Chic is actually just shabby, about 5% of people will probably hate the hotel as to some it will not have the traditional feel of a 4/5 star hotel.
