We stayed here for our honeymoon and it was a truly magical trip. Hotel staff were really friendly throughout our stay and made us feel completely at ease. This is such an individual hotel that manages to combine traditional, Venetian opulance with modern touches such as the chilled out bar and the beautiful little garden full of lanterns, candles and cushions - there's even a resident DJ playing ambient tunes on a Thursday night.  The location is excellent, just a two minute walk to St Mark's Square. I think the rooms must vary quite a lot as we didn't experience the huge rooms mentioned in other reviews.
