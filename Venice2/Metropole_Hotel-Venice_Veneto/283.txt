While 5 Star accommodation is so subjective and difficult to describe to every traveler, there is no substitute for excellent service and the Metropole was exemplary.  From Nicola (concierge) organizing our transfers - you must try to arrive by water taxi on the private dock - to the Bell Captain looking after our bags - every member of staff was keyed into ensuring that all of us had an amazing experience.  Bravo!  We particularly enjoy a hotel that embraces the city/country it is located in and The Metropole certainly left no doubt that we were in Venice.  From ornate decorations to the Canal views to the quirky but clever collections throughout the property this property - the last on our 2 week tour of Italy - left us with a lasting memory of Venice.
