The Metropole has always been our favourite hotel in Venice. It has recently changed from a 4 star to a 5 star, I guess with a corresponding increase in cost.  The ground floor has been re-organised: the lovely old breakfast room and the glamorous Zodiac bar have been knocked into one room and naffed up to produce a larger space for the very expensive Michelin-starred Met restaurant. The bar is now a sort of public bar just inside the main entrance where the Met used to be. Breakfast is in a rather soulless room in the garden, with, at least over this Christmas, awful piped musack. Two years ago we had a live pianist!
