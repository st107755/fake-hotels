We stayed at the Metropole for three nights last month, during the Biennale. We had 2 double rooms and one triple.  The triple room was very spacious and overlooked the basin of the Grand Canal.  We felt so fortunate to have a view of the Canal and San Giorgio Maggiore from the three large windows (which opened fully) in our room. Between our two double rooms, one was spacious, the other was very small. The hotel is very well-located – near Piazza San Marco and directly in front of the water bus station.
