For us this was an expensive hotel, but we were only there for three nights and got a deal on last minute so went for it.  So glad we did, from the moment we arrived it lived up to its pretensions of luxury.  The staff were courteous and helpful and also friendly but not too much.  The location is of course wonderful, we didnt have a view of the lagoon, but you could see it by sticking your head out of the window and we didnt have a bath because we were in a standard room, but the shower was excellent. Very comfortable and very big bed, lovely towels bath robes etc. Bar grand and comfortable and lounge/relaxing area fabulous, slightly kitch but none the worse for that and all smelling lovely with masses of pot pouri.
