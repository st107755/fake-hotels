The Hotel Metropole is bellissima! I cannot recommend the hotel highly enough. My girlfriend and I spent some nights at this magnicent jewell of a hotel while we were attending the Venice Film Festival during the first week of September. We opted for this hotel, rather than the Excelsior on the Lido (site of the festival) and the excellent Cipriani on Giudeca. All staff members were friendly and helpful; the chef is amazing; he prepared an exquisite platter of savory Venetian seafood delicacies.
