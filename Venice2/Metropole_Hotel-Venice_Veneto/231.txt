I had the pleasure of staying at this hotel last week and thought I’d write and say how delightful the hotel was.   We arrived by the public water boat €15 each way and got off at the San Zaccaria stop which is a few minutes walk from the hotel. To our delight and possibly because it was my birthday we were upgraded to a junior suite. This room had a large four poster bed, a separate lounge area and a large bathroom with bath and separate shower. The room looked out over the canal to the side of the hotel and if you leaned out a little you can see to the main waterway.   As soon as we arrived at the hotel the staff were very polite and charming; even using my name on occasions, which adds that certain homely touch. The hotel is a living museum with a large number of figurines…
