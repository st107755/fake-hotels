The service at Metropole Hotel is definitely 5-star and if you appreciate the precise detail put into your stay then pick Hotel Metropole. The location is also very central and near the main piazza so you can definitely get around with ease.   Now, to my main issue: the rooms. I understand many hotels in Venice try to maintain the Venetian feel but in my opinion, its quite scary and tacky. The room I stayed in was entirely red from the carpet, to the drapes to the bed covers. Did I mention everything was upholstered? Yes, even the walls. The furniture was outdated and the decor was just too much for one small room. If the rooms were brighter and had more white I would feel more comfortable. But the red everywhere was excessive.
