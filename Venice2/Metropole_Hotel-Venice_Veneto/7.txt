This was our third visit to the Metropole. And there were some serious disappointments. We were only informed that the Met restaurant was closed after we had booked directly with the hotel (and after we tried to book a table). And, just a few days before our stay, we were also informed that the lifts weren’t working. Nor did they work during the several days we were there. There was building work going on during our stay - again not something we were informed about. And, while some of the staff were absolutely lovely, especially the charming ladies who served our delicious breakfast, those on reception were cool and dour in a way that we, at least, associate with an earlier age of hotel management.
