The staff were extremly welcoming and helpful and we were delighted to find that we had been upgraded to a room with a roof terrace where we could see the lagoon. It was beautiful. We were also lucky enough to be bought a chilled bottle of prosecco to our room.  Breakfast was fantastic and delicious, we also chose to eat in the Michillen star restaurant on one evening which was a great experience.
