If you are a fan of minimalist boutique -style hotels, this is not for you. However, if you love the tradition of Venice, reds and golds, gilded mirrors, ornate carvings and cherubs, not to mention superb service, this is definitely the one. The bar, lounge, reception and rooms are all decorated in traditional style - we had the most amazing carved oak bed, complete with cherubs - and are most comfortable and luxurious. We had a side room looking onto a small canal, which was quiet, just the noise of boats passing by.  The restaurant, in contrast to the rest of the hotel, is decorated in tasteful greys and creams, you can eat outside in fine weather in the delightful garden if you wish.
