This was a spectacular place to stay in Venice. Either take a water bus from the train station (with a stop at San Marco a bridge or two away from the front door), or splurge on a private water taxi that actually takes to you the hotel's private dock! It's located just a few "blocks" away from the Piazza San Marco (crossing the congested bridge where all the tourists stop to take pictures of the Bridge of Sighs), and the hotel greets you with red velvet splendor. Each floor is filled with museum-grade collections of everything from corkscrews to crucifixes, giving this intimate hotel the feeling of a museum with elegant guest rooms.
