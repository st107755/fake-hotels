We stayed at Metropole Hotel for 3 nights. We are experienced travellers who have travelled all over the world, but we can really recommend this hotel for excellent service and location. We had booked 2 double rooms, and were lucky and got room overlooking the side canal of the hotel. The hotel is in an old building, and the interior is classic old and dark, but give a great atmosphere to the hotel. The location is perfect for everything Venice has to offer. Rooms were in same old style with comfortable beds, however the bathrooms were modern and clean. Breakfast buffet was not extravagant but had everything you need for a good breakfast, and the outdoor seating was fantastic.  We had booked private taxi boat through the hotel and thqat was perfect.
