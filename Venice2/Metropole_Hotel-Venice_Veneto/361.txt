We booked 5 nights at this hotel and was not disappointed. The reception area is very warm and inviting with welcoming staff who took care of us throughout our stay.We arrived by private water taxi late at night which took us straight to the front door off of the side canal which was such a nice thing to do. Our room on the first floor was very pretty, clean and had plenty of storage space. In our room there was a socket suitable for british plugs. The bathroom was well equipped with fresh towels every day. The room was serviced twice a day which included a turn down service. The decor throughout the hotel was amazing with different themes on each floor, displays of small antiquities in cabinets and on the walls were really interesting.
