Hotel Metropole was a great choice for our Venice stay. The old sophisticated villa faces the Lagoon and you arrive, James Bond style, in a water taxi from the airport through the side canal entrance.  The hotel used to be Vivaldi’s music school and feels authentic and historical. We had booked 2 rooms and paid extra for a lagoon view room for us (adults), but didn’t get one. They offered to change but the lagoon room on offer was tiny compared to the room we had been given and the view wasn’t what I had expected , so we opted to stay in the room they had given us (quite plush and large but zero view) and in return they gave us a much needed late check out...The kids room was spacious and had a great view of the beautiful back Courtyard.
