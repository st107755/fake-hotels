Given the hotel is 5 star rated, located on the Grand Canal in Venice, and the price we paid I had high hopes for this hotel.  The hotel is lovely, in the public areas which are filled with antique furniture etc but not sure whether the level of conform warrants 5 stars.  The room was ok, but had a weird cheap looking 1980's styled patterned carpet which did not match the antique style of the rest of the room (which was all green).  The shower head was plastic and something you could buy from Wilkinsons for £10.    Some staff were snooty, and others were very helpful and attentive (mainly the female staff at breakfast),  On checking in we were asked for our passports with no explantation.
