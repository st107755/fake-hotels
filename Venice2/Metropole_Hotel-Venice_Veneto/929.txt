I am just returning home from my first visit to Venice and as part of my overall Old World experience I feel compelled to write a ‘little something’ to express appreciation for the wonderful time I spent at the Hotel Metropole last week. My first impression of the Hotel was its grand archetypal and artistic style, with rare classic art collections throughout the grounds. In my humble opinion, this striking first impression was also on par with the professionalism I received at the front desk, upon check in, being ushered to my suite and throughout my two night stay.  My accommodations were charming and very clean to say the least.
