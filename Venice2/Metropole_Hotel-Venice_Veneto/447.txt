We were immediately cheerily greeted by the door man and then the concierge. When asked what he could do for us, we explained we were just looking around as we stayed there 15 years ago. He invited us on a free canal cruise for hotel guests. We declined but appreciated the gesture. That evening we went to the Metropole bar for drinks.
