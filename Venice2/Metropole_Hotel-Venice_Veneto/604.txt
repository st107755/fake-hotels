Stayed 6 nights so really had time to settle in and get to know the staff, all of whom were very courteous & professional. Angelo in the bar is the perfect host as well as everyone else. The rooms were nice, plenty of space and the bed & bedding very comfortable. This was our first trip to Europe so weren't sure what to expect. Everything in Venice is opulent, very "over the top" in regards to décor, etc. Not what we are used to in West Texas!   The location was perfect. Amazing water views across the canal. Very close to St. Mark's Square but just far enough away that the bulk of the tourists crowds was one bridge away.
