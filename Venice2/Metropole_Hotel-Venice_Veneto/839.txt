This hotel is outstanding. Every aspect is deserving of a five. The staff are friendly and efficient, the food beautiful and the service flawless. Where the Metropole really comes into its own is that it has a personality. In these days of dull, formulaic blandness, it is lovely to see a business that is comfortable with itself enough to do something different design wise. The rooms are beautiful, the public areas interesting and comfortable and there is a cohesiveness of design that I found enchanting. Arrive by taxi for that Venice sensation, and enjoy the capped porters assisting you onto the Metropoles personal quay. Allow an hour or so in the bar; sumptuous, relaxing and quiet.
