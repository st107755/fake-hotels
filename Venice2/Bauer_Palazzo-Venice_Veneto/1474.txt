The concierge and doormen made us feel particularly welcome with our luggage being transferred from the boat to the hotel lobby in an instant. We arrived earlier than expected and our room wasn't ready but we enjoyed a Bellini on the terrace overlooking the Grand Canal. Our room was comfortable and clean with electric shutters on the windows to keep out the bright light at night. The space felt sumptuous. All the staff we encountered were very courteous, helpful and seemed genuine, happily recommending places to visit.
