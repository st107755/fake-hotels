The online pictures of the Bauer Boutique Hotel looked quite impressive and elegant.  When we arrived to the beautiful lobby we were excited that our room would be equally as beautiful!  When we walked up to the lobby desk, there were a few people complaining about water taxi's not being called. That made us aware that we needed to put our request for transporation early and reconfirm the morning we left.  When our room was ready, my husband and I were puzzled we had to leave the main building and walk around a very dark corner to this old boutique hotel.  The elegance was gone!   The ancient buiding did have Gothic decor but no real charm.  The room decor was dark, cold with no real thought put into making it more inviting for the guest.
