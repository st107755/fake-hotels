Did a lot of research for our first trip to Venice.  Ultimately decided on the Bauer and was very happy with the choice.  The hotel is all it advertises - good food, helpful and polite staff and rooms with some character and style.    We chose two connecting rooms (538 and 541) with massive outdoor terraces (literally bigger than each room - really great for enjoying sunset wine and cheese).  The hotel had an Amex three-for-two deal which made the room price high versus very high.    Like a lot of things in Italy, the room descriptions on the website do not always match what is listed on the website's price configurator screen so it can be difficult to figure out which room or suite you are really pricing.
