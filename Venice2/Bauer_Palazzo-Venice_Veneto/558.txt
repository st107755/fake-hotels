We enjoyed this hotel and our stay in Venice but the view from the room was not what we expected when we were booking. We were told that we will get a partial view and thought that it meant we would be able to wake up and see the city. Instead, we had to lean really far out the window to see the water and take in the view. The staff in the hotel was very friendly and helpful. Concierge service (especially Marco) were very professional and recommended a lot of good places to see as well as good restaurants. The room had a bath tub with no shower and it was leaking from time to time. This property is very nice but it needs a little work and it is hard to preserve it as is.
