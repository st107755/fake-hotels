My husband and I have just returned from a 3 night stay - we booked a junior suite at The Bauer to celebrate our tenth anniversary, but we were lucky enough to be upgraded to a delux suite at Il Palazzo. Suite 158 (with complimentray prosecco) was fantastic with a view of the side canal and the charming sounds of gondolas passing by our windows. Staff were all very friendly and helpful, service is very attentive - I don't think we ever opened a door of pushed the lift button! The opulent style of this historic part of the hotel was stunning and very comfortable. Breakfast on the seventh floor roof terrace is a delight, we lingered over it for an hour every day, and I have to confess to finishing it off - again every day -  with some of their delicious cakes.
