My husband and I had a short stay in the Bauer Il Palazzo as a treat for me for a "special" birthday.  I cannot speak highly enough of the hotel and its staff.  The approach to the hotel on the Grand Canal was breathtaking, and the hotel itself is the prettiest of all the palazzi.  The staff are all efficient and pleasant, particularly Paulo, Head Concierge, who can be relied upon to give informed and helpful suggestions for visits, and can rustle up  anything you want with one flick of his fingers!  Although this hotel is apparently frequented by such luminaries as Sting and John Grisham, we were treated at all times like VIPs.
