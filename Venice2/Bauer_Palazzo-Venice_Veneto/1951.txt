We spent a fantastic week-end in Venice, with cold but sunny weather. We had chosen the Bauer Il Palazzo afer reading some reviews here, but I lacked some pictures. I try to post a few ones to give you a better idea of this property.  The week prior to our trip I requested the concierge to arrange transfer from Marco Polo airport to the hotel. At 210EUR it is expensive but worth it, especially when you arrive at night and when it's your first time there.  We stayed in 'executive panoramic' suite 727 with a view over San Marco. The suite was perfectly clean, with a nice marble floor, we had a large walk-in closet, an even larger dressing room, a beautiful wood-paneled sitting area, one bedroom and one complete bathroom with bathtub & shower.
