July, 2005: just spent two nights at the Bauer in Venice with wife and another couple; First, the location:  just off the Grand Canal with a small canal entrance on the side, fronting on a lovely square (San Mose) with a Church and some shops; about a 2 minute walk to San Marco Square; 15 minute walk to the Rialto Bridge; surrounded by small streets and alleys filled with shops and restaurants; about as centrally located as you can get; Note: while some of the other "famous" hotels" directly front the Grand Canal with their entrances (actually the Bauer Il Pallazo connected to the Hotel Bauer fronts the Grand Canal but without walkway access), it is no bargain in that the street in front of these other hotels are extremely busy and noisy.
