If you are looking for the absolute top in the combination of style, romance, location and views this is it. We stayed here in January 2005 and had a room with a balcony and view of the Grand Canal. Our room (and bathroom) was spacious, especially if you compare this to the standard in Venice. Even in the better Venetian hotels rooms are cramped and bathrooms are more or less terrible. Our bathroom had the size of a standard Venetian hotelroom and had a jacuzzi, a seperate shower cabin, two sinks and brass taps. Very nice. The tones in the room were classy (beige, white and ´marble´). Beds were superb, compared to what you normally get in good Italian hotels. Staff was very well trained and very helpfull.
