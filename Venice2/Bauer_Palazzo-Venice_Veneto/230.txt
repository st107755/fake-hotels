Walking into the lobby of Bauer Palzzo, with its combination of Art Deco furnishings and over the top glitz, it’s not clear whether you are in Venice, or The Venetian in Las Vegas..Franly, given the obvious difficulties in retrofitting an old palazzo into a hotel, it’s not surprising that the rooms leave something to be desired, but they are more than serviceable, just not 5-star quality.  I will add that our room only had a walk in tub/shower that if you were limited in your dexterity would be a challenge to get in and out of.  Regardless of all that, the location of Bauer is an A+++, and makes it worth staying in and of itself.
