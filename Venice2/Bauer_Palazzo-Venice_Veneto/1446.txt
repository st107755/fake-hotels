There are many luxury hotels in Venice and I have stayed in most of them, but the Bauer Palazzo is, by far, the best. On the grand canal (as opposed to the lagoon), the views are spectacular and the rooms elegantly appointed in period style. A sumptuous breakfast is included in an outdoor rooftop setting as well as a ground floor, both with terrific views. But the best thing about this hotel is the exceptional service provided by the front desk. They arranged everything during our stay, including tours, transfers, restaurant reservation. Just one example: when we left Venice, we had a car service picking us up at Piazzale Roma to take us to Verona.
