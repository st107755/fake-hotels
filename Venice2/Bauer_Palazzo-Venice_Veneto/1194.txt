'Settimo Cielo' is the name of this hotel's rooftop terrace - it means 'heaven' - and that's a pretty good way to describe the Bauer Il Palazzo.  I don't have much experience of luxury hotels, but this seems a great example of 5 star service.  On arrival Roberta gave us a comprehensive tour of the hotel and was informative, professional, and friendly.  Our every need was anticipated, from the front of staff taking care of franking and posting my card, to remembering how all four of us took our coffee at breakfast every day. Nothing was too much trouble for them.  A particular highlight was breakfast - all tastes are catered for on the rooftop terrace in a magnificent buffet involving cake, cereal, meat, fresh fruit and much more.
