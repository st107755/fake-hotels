Our stay was very enjoyable, comfortable and everyone particularly Paolo the Head Concierge and the Bell Staff were very helpful to us.   However, please note in order to visit Venice and stay in quality accomodation, given the strength of the € against £ and $, you must expect it will be expensive. We booked a Panoramic Double Room with breakfast included: note that otherwise it would cost an additional €44 each.    The room had a balcony overlooking a Gondola station and provided a view of the City and Grand Canal. It was too cold to sit outside but this would have been wonderful in warmer weather.   The room was spacious, the bed comfortable and the marble bathroom with separate shower was beautiful.   Breakfast was fine but we found the quality and service in the main…
