Spent the first 3 days of our honeymoon at the Bauer - loved the decor and mix of modern luxury furnishing and old style glamour. We were given an upgrade on arrival and found all the staff to be very helpful. Our room was constantly tidied and lovely turn down service. Our room was large, light and airy with lovely balcony and view of the grand canal. Lovely breakfast on the terrace - shame that the terrace is not open all day as its a lovely place to sit and take in the view. Found the concierge very helpful and gave us great advice on best walking route. We had a small incident where the air conditioning leaked all over my husbands shoes and they very kindly purchased two pairs for him and had all his shoes cleaned and dried.
