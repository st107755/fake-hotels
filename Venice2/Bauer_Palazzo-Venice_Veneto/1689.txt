We really enjoyed staying at the Bauer.  We were only in Venice for 2 nights so being near the crowded St. Mark's square didn't bother us because it made it easier to access the main sights.  I probably liked the "old world charm" of this hotel more than my husband, who would've preferred to more modern part of the Bauer hotel that the Palazzo is attached to, but like any hotel, it is all about what you personally prefer.  The hotel itself felt very luxurious to us.  We were given a beautiful suite with 2 marble bathrooms and because it was our honeymoon, a bottle of Prosecco upon our arrival.
