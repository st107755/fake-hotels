This is  probably not the most grand in appearance hotel in Venice, but it is definately one of the very best in service and attention to detail.  The lobby and public areas are very nice but not "knock you off your feet" showy by any means.  The rooms are very elegant and in the traditional venetian style.  The Bauer next door is much more contemporary for comparison.   My favorite experience at the hotel was the breakfast on the top floor overlooking the grand canal.  The breakfast was of the best quality and the view was amazing.  The most impressive quality of the hotel was the service.
