Our five night stay at the end of March was magical with full sun and temps in the 60's we had breakfast every morning outside on the terrace overlooking the Grand Canal! The last day two ocean liners sailed into the harbor dwarfing the churches and domes and throwing the 17th C into stark relief with the 21st. A wonderful location. Convenient to everything.
