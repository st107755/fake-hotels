On arriving at The Bauer II Palazzo by water taxi, you enter into a world of opulent decor and lavish furnishings, reflecting the beauty and wonderful history of this stunning city.   Our room was large, with elegant decor, silk wall hangings, rich fabrics and of course brilliant Murano chandeliers. The Bathroom was a good size, all marble, with both shower and bath.  The view was superb, looking over the Grand Canal, do not think you could improve on the view - just Venice.  We only had breakfast, as so many restaurants to visit. Breakfast was excellent, on the top terrace overlooking the orange tiled city, spectacular.   The staff were all so friendly and the concierge extremely helpful.
