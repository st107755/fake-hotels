Stayed here to celebrate our 25th wedding anniversary. From the moment we walked through the doors we knew we had chosen the perfect hotel. Luxurious interior, very friendly and professional staff. On checking in we received an upgrade on our room. The room we was upgraded to had views out to the grand Canal and was extremely comfortable. On our first site seeing day, we returned to our room to find an ice bucket with a bottle of bubbly and two glasses with a card saying "courtesy of the management on your special occasion". A lovely and thoughtful touch. The outside dining terrace area for breakfast and dinner overlooks the Grand Canal and the Santa Maria della Salute church. Beautiful.
