I stayed at what was then called the Bauer Grunewald with my parents many years ago and decided to stay again to see how much it had changed. Found a good deal with Expedia and can whole-heartedly recommend the hotel under its new ownership and management. Had one of the older rooms but well-maintained and elegant in a classic '50s Venetian style. The public rooms with Venetian glass and wood are unchanged and have an enduring glamour. Restaurant terrace on the ground floor is a magical place for breakfast with possibly the best view in Venice (so much better than the Danieli) giving a real sense of the morning bustle on the Grand Canal.
