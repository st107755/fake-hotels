I returned to Venice with my wife and daughter this past summer and stayed at the Hotel Bauer.  My wife and I stayed here on our honeymoon 25 years ago.  Upon check in we commented to the staff in regard to our past visit to the Bauer.  I don't know if this had any influence on our room, but when we opened the door to our room/suite, we were blown away.  We had reserved a standard room with additional bedding for our daughter.  What did we find when we opened our door?  We first noticed the front hall closet which was probably the size of a NY studio apartment.  The living room was beautifully furnished with a sofa, love seat, desk and several pull up chairs.  It also had a non working fireplace.  The guest bathroom for my daughter was beautiful.
