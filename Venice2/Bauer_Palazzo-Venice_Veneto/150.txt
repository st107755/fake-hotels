My husband and I stayed at Bauer Palazzo for 5 nights in late September of 2018. From the moment we drove up in the water taxi, we received outstanding service from the front desk. Our room was upgraded to a deluxe suite on the 3rd floor, which was beautiful and spacious and I loved the red fabric EVERYWHERE! Very luxurious. The bathroom was all marble and had a wonderful bath tub and adjoining walk in closet area. We were on the side opposite of the canal the gondolas operate in and it was very quiet at night which we enjoyed. This is one of the most beautiful places I have stayed, and we have pretty high end taste. My favorite thing was the guest only access rooftop terrace bar and restaurant.
