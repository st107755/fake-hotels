We have just returned from a fabulous 4 night stay at the Bauer Palazzo and couldn't fault a single thing! We thoroughly enjoyed our stay and hope to return! I saw a few reviews complaining about the noise of the gondola traffic but to us it made our trip hearing them singing in Italian in the mornings, it made us feel we were in Venice! It was a real treat! When our windows were shut we couldn't hear it! I would definitely recommend a trip to use the spa at the sister hotel, you can get a free boat over of which we were quoted €60 each way to use a local taxi to the same island. We had a Romantic Turkish bath which we had the whole room to ourself and the service we received was outstanding from the woman. They have a beautiful relaxation lounge which has a view over the water…
