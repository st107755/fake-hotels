I have made my fourth visit to Venice last month. My wife accompanied me rather reluctantly as she always thought that Venice could give unusually fantastic impressions only for the first visitors and much less so on the subsequent visits. I could agree with her but still wished to be there one more time to see more of historical heritage and to walk on ‘calles’ with the background of narrow canals and old architectures. I also wanted to stay at a hotel providing a room with a canal view and for dining a terrace looking out to Chiesa della Salute across Canal Grande.
