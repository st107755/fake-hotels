We booked 3 nights with the hotel directly, after sorting through all the usual online search engine and paid €231/night B&B, for a delux room. Not sure what made our medium-sized room 'delux', maybe it was the toiletries, bathrobes and slippers? In fact the bed was pretty uncomfortable and the Bauer would really benefit from offering a pillow menu. But to be fair the room is not the big selling point.   The hotel itself is the star attraction, with great facilities for a central Venetian location. Our favourite bits were the private landing stage, restaurants, Grand Canal Terrace seating and fitness facilities, with sauna and steam room. The Jacuzzi is STILL out of order :-(  On our last day, the restaurant room was full for breakfast, so we were asked to use the rooftop dining…
