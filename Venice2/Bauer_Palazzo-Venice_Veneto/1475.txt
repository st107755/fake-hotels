My wife and I just had a three night stay at the Bauer Hotel. Have been to Venice 7 times and belive that the location of Bauer is ideal. In addition its wonderful terass is larger than of any other hotel I know.   The public areas of the hotel are beautiful. We had a suit consisting of bedroom, living room, walk in closet and bathroom overlooking a side canal. Rooms were not large by our standards but perhaps for Venice. The only time we have had a much larger room was at the Excelsior in Lido. Room quailty was good but nothing extraordinary and certainly not what you could expect considering we paid almost euro 700 per night.
