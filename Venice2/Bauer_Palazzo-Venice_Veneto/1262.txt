I was privileged to return to the Bauer Hotel again this summer after my memorable stay last year.  Although my room was not as large as the room I had last year, It was larger than most European hotel rooms.  The room was beautifully furnished in what appears to be antique furniture.  The bed cover was gorgeous covered in small hand-tied bows and the room had a beautiful mural over the bed.  The room was also exceptionally clean and the bed was very comfortable.  We also had a nice closet, refrigerator, and safe.  Once again we were pleased to have a window overlooking the canal at the side of the hotel.
