This was my  second stay at The Bauer and my mother's fourth. Arrived by water taxi and as we arrived early morning we dropped off our bags, checked in at spent the day getting lost in what I think is the most beautiful city in the world.  We arrived back at the hotel at around 5.30 and went up to our room, which was really pretty, overlooking the side canal, but being on the 4th floor was far enough away not to be disturbed by the gondoliers calling etc. We had been given a bottle of iced prosecco which we enjoyed while getting ready for our meal. The room was tastefully decorated and as the room had the sun on it all day it had a wonderful warm glow from early morning until dusk.
