I originally tried to book the Baglioni when planning our trip to Venice, however it had no availability during our vacation dates. Well, I am sure glad it didn't because our stay at The Bauer was absolutely perfect! We arrived via a private water taxi from the airport which we arranged through the hotel and that was quite a fun experience! It was a very comfortable ride and took us through several canals. Since we already had our own private boat ride we did not feel the need to rent a gondola during our stay. As soon as we docked at the hotel the entire staff came out to greet us and made us feel very welcomed.
