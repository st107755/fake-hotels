If you are travelling to Venice you must consider the Bauer Hotel.  This hotel offers only the best of service, food and location.  Located on the Grand Canal just minutes from St Marks square, the location is ideal.  Gondolas and shopping are just outside your door.  The staff offer excellent service and the concierge desk is most helpful.  Rates can be determined by checking out the Bauer Hotel website.  The full breakfast offered each morning (included) sets you up for the rest of the day.  Easy access to the airport is available by water taxi or public water transportation.
