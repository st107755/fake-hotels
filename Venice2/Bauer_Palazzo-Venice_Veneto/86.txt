This was our fourth stay in 4 years and it is sad to see the decline, staff gruff and disinterested, sometimes downright rude. The quality of the buffet breakfast is not 5 star and never changes, it’s like the accountants have pruned the variety and quality. It’s a pity they have not pruned the price. As a Leaders Club member there was minimal acknowledgement and not like times of old. In this  21st century there needs to be a recognition of the need for powerpoints or USB sites. Positives remain the location, ample storage in a tiny room, and it’s nice and clean. As a regular to Venice it is a time for a change for what was a loyal customer
