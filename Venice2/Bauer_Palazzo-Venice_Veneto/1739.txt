Venice is always an expensive city and since we were in town for the festival of the Redeemer the hotels were heavily booked and charging a premium rate for the festival night.  The Bauer’s location is excellent.  The staff and service were professional, attentive and friendly. Our room and bath were fine but in need of updating and TLC.  We had a king bed in a very small room with very high ceilings.  The white marble bath was nice but when you took a shower, the entire bathroom got soaked.   A plus was the Juliet balcony overlooking a small canal. The breakfast buffet was very good and included in our rate, otherwise it cost 50 Euros.
