Definitely old-world, faded elegance-which I don't mean in a necessarily bad way. We requested a room with a view, as suggested in another review, but e800 was beyond our budget. Chiara, at the front desk, showed us 2 rooms: one with a "lateral" (little slice) view of the canal and another without a view, but a more luxurious room. We chose the latter and, with the exception of a leaky shower (which was somewhat repaired), we were not disappointed. Great staff overall. The breakfast was delicious and generous, with both Continental and American-style options, fresh fruits, salame, cheeses, juices, cakes...and Prosecco for Mimosas!
