Staying at the Bauers IL Palazzo Hotel in Venezia, Italy is truly a legendary and inspiring experience. It is distinctively heritage and Venetian crafted in architecture. The room decor is contemporary and the furnishing well maintained and clean. Lighting is warm and simply pleasant with the feel of romance in the air. It has its private dock for the water taxi to stop right in front of the hotel lobby.   A very comfortable stay with a homely experience. One can opt for an interior intimate or terrace rooftop seating restaurant that provides excellent cuisines great views of Venice and the skyline.
