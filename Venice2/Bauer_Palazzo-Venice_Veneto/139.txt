I had been to Venice three times before this trip but staying at Bauer Palazzo made my stay that much better! We were lucky enough to get a room that looked over the Grand Canal which was amazing and the hotel amenities and service was the best I've experienced in Venice!   There is a terrace (Canal Bar) where you can drink a bottle of wine and watch the sun go down. It's easy to waste several hours here as it doesn't get much better, anywhere you go in Venice.   The only fault I could pick would be that the terrace on the 7th floor (highest terrace in Venice) is advertised as "reserved for guests only", but is actually reserved for VIP guests only.
