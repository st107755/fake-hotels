This was a difficult hotel to review as we are not five star people!! Leaving that aside, the hotel is beautiful. We had a suite which was enormous - entry hall, 2 sitting rooms, 2 bathrooms, large bedroom, walk in closet, and windows overlooking a small canal full of gondolas! The breakfast is very extensive and served on a beautiful terrace overlooking the Grand Canal, and there is also a restaurant and a bar. The hotel also has its own spa on Giudecca and a shuttle service is available.  The service at the hotel is exemplary. The hotel is also very close to St Marks Square and the Vaporetto station.
