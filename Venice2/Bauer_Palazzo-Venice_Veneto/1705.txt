We stayed here for three nights following a recommendation, when we first arrived the hotel is really impressive very nice lobby and entrance area, our room was not ready which is OK as we had arrived in the middle of the morning, so we dropped our bags and went into Venice, is was clear from the outset that the position of the hotel commanded its pricing, it is surrounded by the best shops, When we got back we were escorted to the room which was a nice size and obviously had some refurb at some point, it also had a nice terrace which overlooked the piazza. The bed is somewhat hard but a large size; electric shutter blinds give you extra darkness at night.
