If you want a great hotel in Venice, one that will cater to your every need, is easily accessible by both boat and land, is a hop, skip and a jump away from St. Marks Square, the Bauer Il Palazzo is it!!!!! Just wonderful! The ambience of a Venetian palace with all the comfort a hotel can offer. The Bauers have two adjacent properties and while the Hotel Bauer is a beautiful hotel, the Il Palazzo is simply majestic and I suggest choosing it. I was in suite 352 and could not get over the lush and luxury surroundings with a great view. True, you need to understand the internal architecture so not to get lost but this only takes a short while.
