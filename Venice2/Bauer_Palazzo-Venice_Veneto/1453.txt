Known internationally and well regarded. We stayed at the Bauer this weekend for our 20th anniversary as both the children were on school trips. The service was excellent. The staff were very helpful. The concierge was great suggesting timings and schedules for us. Our room was in the main hotel overlooking one of the side canals of Venice, but with a view down to the Grand Canal. There was a bustle of gondolas with people having their music/serenade. Very atmospheric. We were able to access the balcony from out room so could sit, watch and listen. Breakfast was included and was superb, with plenty of choice and good quality, with prompt and attentive service.
