The Bauer Il Palazzo is simply magical!   I can't say enough about enchanting Venice or this hotel, in particular.  The location is ideal.  As you are greeted from your water taxi at the Bauer's dock, you know you will be in for a treat.  The location is primo and a bit a way from the crowds.  On exiting the hotel, you are literally on the shopping streets filled with upscale designer boutiques... and a mere two minute walk to San Marco square and all of the magic of Venice.    The hotel rooms are authentic Venetian style: large, lovely, comfortable and oh, so quiet. The view from our balcony was magnificant...a view overlooking the canals and the Venice rooftops.
