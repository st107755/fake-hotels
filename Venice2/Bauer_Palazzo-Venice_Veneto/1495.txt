Located in a 18th C. palace, features 35 deluxe double rooms and 40 suites. Most  overlooking the canal or with a view on the city of Venice and on St Mark's Basin. Each room is unique and individually adorned, sumptuously refined with antique furnishings or reproductions of genuine Venetian fabrics from Rubelli and Bevilacqua and Murano glass chandeliers by Seguso and Cenedese. Bathrooms are decorated in Italian marble and some have whirlpools.
