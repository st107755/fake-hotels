My husband, toddler and I spent two nights at the Bauer Il Palazzo recently.    First of all, the location can't be beat.  You are steps from St. Mark's Square and getting around the rest of Venice from this hotel is no problem.  The hotel has it's own dock, so arriving on a private water taxi was no problem, and definitely a plus for us as we were traveling with a 2 year old.    Our reservation was for the Bauer, and upon check-in we were pleasantly surprised to find that we had been upgraded to a larger suite over at Il Palazzo.  Check-in was easy and the reception agent personally escorted us to our room.    The room was wonderfully spacious and the decor was old-world.
