I stayed at the Bauer Hotel with my daughter to celebrate her 18th Birthday. I chose the more expensive hotel to ensure a good location but in fact it doesn't take too long to cover most of the main sites of Venice on foot so it wasn't really necessary. The hotel accommodation and service was 'okay' and the view from the terrace was exceptional, although our room had no view at all . The breakfast was very good and the staff efficient. However, if you want to experience the ambience of this wonderful city then I really would suggest a smaller more intimate hotel with a less sterile atmosphere (more like an airport lounge than a hotel reception!).
