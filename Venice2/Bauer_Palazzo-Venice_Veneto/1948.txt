The place oozes luxury but is also friendly. Unlike many hotels that are "all front" and bump up their star rating with in-room faxes and other geegaws, every aspect of this hotel was great. The decor in the rooms was faultless: ribbed wood panelling met fabric coverings and venetian cloth - think 1950s major refurb, updated with venetian textiles and a modernist/70s touch.
