The perfect Venice hotel, beautifully situated. A renovated Art Deco gem, with lots of original Art Deco pieces of furniture etc.... All this is so refreshing after the corporate faceless ness of the chains. Staff go out of their way to be helpful, and always with a smile. The rooms are comfortable, and stylish, and our huge bathroom was a marble clad delight. Our view was over the charming rooftops to Piazza San Marco, and the grand Canal. Breakfasts were excellent, and to be able to sit there on the terrace looking over the canal, listening to the gondoliers singing publicly and chatting privately was glorious.
