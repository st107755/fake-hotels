If you have dreamed of staying in a grand Italian house reminiscent of past glories of this Queen City, then this hotel is the perfect choice.  The interiors are richly decorated with antique furniture, lavish mirrors, and beautiful silks covering both walls and furniture.  The walls of our suite were opulently covered in red and gold silk brocade.   The bathroom walls, floor, and vanity were wrapped in beautiful white marble.  Sparkling lights make by craftsmen from Murano  illuminated every room and table.  It was like walking into a 17th century grand home when we entered our suite.
