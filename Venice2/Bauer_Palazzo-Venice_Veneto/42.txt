We spent three nights at this hotel from 17 October. I was quite apprehensive as the reviews are a mixed bunch. However I have to say that we were delighted. We had opted for a deluxe room and it was certainly worth it! The room was beautifully furnished (albeit traditionally), with it’s own dressing room and beautiful marble bathroom (great shower!) and really comfortable bed. We even had our own huge terrace overlooking the square and canal at the front of the hotel, plus superb views over the rooftops of Venice! We opted to include breakfast and were very impressed with the selection. It was a real treat to eat it whilst overlooking the Grand Canal. Both the terrace and roof top bars are great.
