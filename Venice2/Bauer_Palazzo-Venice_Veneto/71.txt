Having just arrived in Venice, and staying in Londra Palace(another luxury waterfront hotel), we wandered out in search of a waterfront restaurant that appealed. We wandered into the Bauer, where we found their delightful waterfront terrace. Despite having no reservation, we were seated at what i considered their best table, with a superb view of the lagoon and Grand Canal, and were served a superb dinner. Their risotto was one of the best i have ever eaten!!! The attitude and service of the waiters, including the Maitre D' were wonderful! So we booked on the spot to come back 2 nights later.
