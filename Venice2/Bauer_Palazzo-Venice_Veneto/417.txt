We stayed at this hotel with our three children and so had two interconnecting rooms. Being Venice, and despite the high cost of the rooms, we did not have high expectations... we just hoped that the hotel would be comfortable and clean. It certainly met this criteria and in fact our expectations were exceeded. Rooms were a good size, very Venetian in their aesthetic, and impeccably clean.   Staff were all wonderful and the hotel common areas were fantastic. Location could not be better with direct access to the grand canal at one entrance and another on the other side of the impressive foyer just minutes from St. Mark's Square.
