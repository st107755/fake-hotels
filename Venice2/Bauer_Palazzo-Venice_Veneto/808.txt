We enjoyed our 2 nights at the Bauer. This is a 5 star hotel in an excellent location fronting onto the beginning of the Grand Canal. If you want a room with a canal view, you will have to pay considerably more. The breakfast is sumptuous served on the terrace with the memorable views. We thought we would have a pre-dinner drink there but were informed that the terrace was out of bounds due to a "private event", somewhat surprising for a 5 star hotel. Fortunately we were saved by the very helpful concierge who suggested a little non-touristy bar nearby.
