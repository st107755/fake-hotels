We just returned from a four night stay at Il Palazzo. What a delight...a small boutique hotel within the larger luxury hotel, the Bauer. It was truly a special experience from beginning to end. A light-filled suite overlooking the Grand Canal, a truly luxurious bathroom, with separate shower and freestanding soak tub. The head concierge, Gian Paolo, is amazing: he set up a guided tour for us of the Palladian villas in the Veneto that was the highlight of our trip, and was quick to suggest ways to see the sights while avoiding the day trippers. The various elevators that one needs to use to get around, and where Il Palazzo begins and the Bauer ends is a little confusing...but it's truly an oasis amidst the crowds of Venice.
