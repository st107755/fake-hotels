We travelled to Venice (early 30's couple) for the first time and stayed in the Bauer Hotel. This is not to be confused with The Bauer il Palazzo (which is essentially the same hotel, just located at the front, instead of the back).  After reading some of the negative comments, particularly in relation to service, there was a little apprehension but all of that was quashed within 1 minute of walking into the hotel.   We booked a private boat from Marco Polo airport through "Motoscafi Venezia" (google it to find it), which dropped us directly to the door of the hotel where we were greeted by 2 of the staff to help us with our bags.
