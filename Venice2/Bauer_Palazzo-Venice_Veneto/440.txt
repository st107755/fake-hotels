Location is phenomenal, literally a 2 minute walk to St Marks Square with panoramic views across the Grand Canal to the beautiful Santa Maria della Salute from either the ground floor or roof-top terrace which are simply glorious. Imbued with classic, romantic Venetian elegance, it of course would not be complete without its own canal dock — so your arrival and departure via water taxi is a breeze (not to mention you’ll feel like a movie star…). The Bauer Palazzo is one of the most stunning hotels we have had the pleasure of experiencing.  We only had a deluxe room, but it was fantastic, on the 5th floor, overlooking rooftops and a small canal below.
