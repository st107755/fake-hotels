When I booked I was fully aware this would be in the annexe called Casa Nova but being part of the Bauer Palazzo I assumed it would be the same quality as the main hotel.  Upon check in we were took to the annexe which is around the corner from the main entrance in a small street, through a small door and up to our room. The annexe is very disappointing it is certainly not the same standard as the hotel which I have been in years previously it is more like there 3* sister hotel.
