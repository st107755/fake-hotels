One of the hotel which one has to try if opportunity arise.Gorgeous 5starts hotel,in central part of city. Accessible by motor boat or if using public water transport- allilaguna stopped at St Marco Vallerosa which is a short walking distance- with one one small bridge to go across if having luggages. The room we have- 245- is large- double bed with ensuite- bath and balcony with view of Grand Canal and the river. Room well served with seats, fridge and TV to rest in between outing. The facilities for guests are excellent- down stairs- multiple  places or nook  to rest, read and chill out.Breakfast has wide range of option, wonderful coffee options or tea To complete the stay, the staff are pleasant approachable and helpful.
