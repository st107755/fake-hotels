Our family just spent 3 nights at the Bauer Il Palazzo and found it to be an outstanding Venice hotel.  The location is terrific - facing both the Grand Canal and a smaller canal that is a busy gondolier waiting location. It is quite near the piazza San Marco, and while this might sound like a drawback because of the large number of tourists there, in fact it's a plus - as this is also an area full of great shops and restaurants, and a busy vaporetto and water taxi stop.  You wouldn't know about the crowds at all unless you walk that direction for 5 minutes.   It's not cheap by any means and we stayed in a “palatial” room that had room for 3 people. Our room was high up (on the 6th floor) and looking over the Venetian rooftops and down on the canals was breathtaking.  Breakfast…
