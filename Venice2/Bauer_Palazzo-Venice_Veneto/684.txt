We spent two nights at the Bauer whilst in Venice awaiting a flight home. The hotel is wonderfully located in central Venice, a couple of minutes from San Marco Square and in the middle of the high class shopping area. The room we had, on the second floor, overlooked a side canal and from the balcony the comings and going of gondoliers and passengers could be viewed, all day long if one so desired. The room itself was a good size, with ample wardrobe space as well as a mini-bar, a flat screen TV and a safe. The bed was king-sized and very comfortable, and the bathroom was also of a good size.
