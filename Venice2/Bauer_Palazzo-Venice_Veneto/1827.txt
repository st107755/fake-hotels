We had a standard room which was quite nice and roomy.  No particular view but you don't need one if you are out seeing the sites.  Breakfast each morning was outside on the patio overlooking the grand canal.  That was wonderful and they have a good assortment for breakfast.    This hotel is very close to the grand canal.  It is also an easy walk to St Marks square.  The hotel is next to a smaller canal and  they have a gondola stop right there.  I would suggest taking a ride on one at night even though it costs more.  Your concierge can set it up for you and they will pick you up at the hotel dock.  During the day the canal is extremely busy and at night it can be more romantic.
