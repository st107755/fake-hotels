This hotel was recommended to us by Leading Hotels of the World.  We were truly disappointed.  After spending 5 wonderful nights at the Byblos Art Hotel in nearby Verona (also a Leading Small Hotel of the World), we were expecting the same fantastic experience at the Bauer but it didn't happen.  Since we didn't know how to get from the Venice airport to the hotel, we called the hotel & asked them to arrange transport for us.  They charged us 264 Euros for the transfer when, we later found out, it normally costs only 100 Euros!  Check in took a long time.  We found that the staff in general was cold, snobby & not very welcoming.  Everyone seemed to only want to do the specific job that they were assigned, and did not go out of their way to help.
