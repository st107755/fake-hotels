We came in January 2015 and we were immediately given an upgraded room as they were not fully booked out, despite the fact we'd gone for a cheap package (£300 for four nights) deal so were probably due the rooms round the back. I'm writing this review late but had such a wonderful time I thought it worth posting about a year on. The bathroom was all marble and our bed so comfy, and the room was very Venetian, peaceful, tucked away and private. We were let into our room to find a bottle of complementary prosecco in ice waiting for us. The hotel lobby is beautiful and I LOVED the breakfast. The staff could not do enough for you in both the restaurant and the reception, where we were given lots of great advice and directions and they helped us out with our (rather poor) knowledge…
