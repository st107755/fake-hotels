First off let me start by saying we only like great hotels and top notch food. With the Bauer we got exactly what we had hoped for. A hotel in the best neighborhood in the city, right on the grand canal across from the most prestigious shops in the city. While many contend that the hotel is a bit old that is what it is supposed to be. Francesca Bortolotto Possati the owner of the Bauer maintains the hotel impeccably well with the care and love her grandfather had building it. We came to the Bauer wanting a hotel with Charm and history and we got it. Our room was a decent size with a great view of a canal that was a starting point for many Gondola rides.
