stayed here 3 nights in August 2016 the location is excellent 3 minutes walk to the Royal Gardens where most of the tours start and st marks square and water buses 3 minutes away also. We caught a water taxi from airport to hotel cost was around £220 return best way to travel there if you can afford it if not the water bus station in max 5 minute walk to hotel with luggage. Hotel was clean  housekeeping was excellent there no easy access to any plugs to charge phone etc breakfast was good did not eat evening meal there so can not comment on that. Bottle beer cost 10 euro pot of tea was 9 euros. If you ask hotel to order water taxi it was 70 euros 10 euros more than the the taxi rank outside royal gardens. The designer shop are right outside the hotel entrance.  Warning if you…
