My fiance and I stayed at the Bauer Hotel for three nights in Feburary to celebrate my birthday, and it was truly amazing.  The Bauer is a beautiful, classy hotel which is immediately evident from the moment you step into the gorgeous marble reception to be greeted by extremely friendly, pleasant and helpful staff.   Our bedroom was a lovely size, and as with the hotel in general, was fresh, clean and modern. The marble bathroom was gorgeous and spacious and contained two sets of dressing-gown's and slippers which were changed daily.   The location was absolutely perfect; we couldn't ask to be situated anywhere better. Literally a stones throw from St Marks Square, it took all of thirty seconds to be right in the centre of things.
