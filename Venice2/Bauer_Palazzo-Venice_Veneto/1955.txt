My wife and I stayed at the Bauer for 6 nights from 19th September 2005.  As it was our 21st wedding anniversary I arranged for a suite situated on the 4th floor.  I have stayed in many top quality hotels throughout the world in my time and was a little apprehensive after reading some of the views here! Due to the position of the hotel there are NO rooms with a canal view, (maybe some of the standard rooms above the water taxi landing stage have the side canal view), but the suites are central around a main courtyard with no view at all.  That being said our suite was breathtaking, two huge bathrooms with wall to wall marble and robes, an enormous sitting room, a large bedroom with king size bed, dressing room, baggage room, electric window shutters and two lots of air-con.
