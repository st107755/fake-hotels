What an amazing hotel! It was wonderful to stay here, and I can't really praise it enough. I was here for work just after the big flood that affected Venice in November 2019 - high tides of up to 150cm were still affecting the city, but the Bauer simply soldiered on. Although the water outside was literally thigh deep, and member of the team were working hard all day to vacuum up water leaking in, the only element that we couldn't use was the terrace and restaurant (although meals were moved into the ballroom).  The Bauer is beautifully designed - fabulous 1930s architecture, and a real feel of opulence - the corridors are wide, marble floored, and lined with mirrors; the rooms are large and comfortable, and the ballroom ceiling is gilded!
