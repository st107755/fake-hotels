We stayed at the Bauer il Palazzo the first week in June. It was a wonderful hotel with amazing staff.  Truly everyone there was accommodating..Even though we tested their patience during the first 24 hours they didn't miss a beat. First they took us to a beautiful very large red room with a lovely bath.  The staff noticed my disappointment that we had only a slight view of the canal. The next morning they responded by moving us to a smaller room with the most spectacular view, straight on the grand canal. Though I was in seventh heaven, my husband was bothered that we shared the terrace with two rooms and he preferred the larger room with more privacy.
