I read somewhere that in Venice, the number of stars a hotel has does not necessarily reflect the standard and quality of the hotel, rather it reflects the facilities available. However, that said, our room did not have an iron and I was not willing to pay extra for the housekeeper to do it as it was just a t-shirt.  We booked a deluxe double bedroom and although the room was slightly narrow, we had a small terrace that overlooked a canal which was great. We did not have any trouble with noise from the canal.  The room itself looked slightly worn and the carpets seemed slightly dusty. It was a bit dark in the room as well. Other than that, the room was OK.
