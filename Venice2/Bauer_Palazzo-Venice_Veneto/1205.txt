A beautiful hotel in an amazing position, right in the center of Venice.  The front of the hotel overlooks the Grand Canal so you get the most stunning views from your breakfast table whilst eating from the extremely full breakfast buffet. There are two entrances, one is from a canal reached by water taxi and the other from the street.  St Marks Square is 5 minutes walk from the hotel.  The staff in the hotel are extremely friendly and accommodating - nothing is too much trouble or a problem and every request is attended to within minutes.  We had a fabulous room with a small balcony overlooking the side canal so when we opened the doors we could listen to the Gondolas passing and the Gondoliers singing - an absolute delight.
