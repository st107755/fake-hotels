We spent a magical weekend at the Bauer Hotel in Venice - there was nothing I could fault about the service or the wonderful room we got.  I had booked a superior room but when we arrived at the hotel, the friendly and extremely hospitable reception staff showed us to a room which was an upgrade.  What an upgrade it was.  Our room was more like a small suite and although it didn't look out on a canal, I didn't care.  It was beautiful - we had a large main room with a huge Venetian glass chandelier, the furniture was very in keeping and their was a lovely seating area in front of a mock fireplace.
