My husband and I stayed here for 3 nights for our 10th wedding anniversary, we booked through 'lastminute.com' and paid about £350 for the 3 nights (for 2 people in a deluxe room INCLUDING breakfast!!!) - we emailed the hotel before we arrived to say we were there to celebrate. And when we arrived we were given room 236, a fantastic room, huge bed, fab marble bathroom and MASSIVE terrace over campo san moise. FANTASTIC, the service from the moment we stepped off the private water taxi (highly recommended) was first class. Breakfast buffet was included (a good thing as I think its about 40 euros each otherwise) wonderful choice and again fantastic service in restaurant overlooking the grand canal.
