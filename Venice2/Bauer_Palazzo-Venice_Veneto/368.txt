I stayed at the Bauer with my wife in July 2017. Not a huge amount to say amount to say about this hotel apart from; it is just fantastic. The location just 5 minute’s walk form St Mark’s square is brilliant, you’re literally right in the heart of Venice. The restaurant and terrace offer some of the best views of the Grand Canal basin and the Santa Maria della Salute in Venice. I loved dining on the terrace enjoying superb food, service second to none and with just the most amazing views. Want a trip on a Gondola? Just walk outside the hotel, 30 seconds over a cute little bridge and hey presto a Gondola station, it just keeps getting better and better. Hotel itself is very traditional, we stayed in the cheapest room they have and it was just excellent. Decorated very tastefully,…
