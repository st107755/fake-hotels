Our first time at the Bauer so I confess we were lucky rather than well planned. There are two parts to the hotel - the 'old' palazzo and the newer hotel. I would focus on what exactly you are booking and in which part of the building and view etc, almost down to the particular room.  We had a room in the Palazzo - great. There is a separate reception desk, lounge, pier (its Venice after all), all of which made a difference and made it feel exclusive and private. The main hotel seemed to be partly a staging point for folk heading on cruises and much busier.  The hotel is well located, well-run, serviced etc. We wouldn't say its quite a destination in its own right like some European hotels but definitely does its job well.
