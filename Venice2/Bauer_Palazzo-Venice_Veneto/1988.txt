The Bauer Hotel was a disappointment.  The hotel was conveniently located on the Grand Canel and was close to most of the major attractions of Venice.  However, the hotel itself had very little to offer.  The rooms were cramped and in need of a coat of paint.  The furniture was flimsy and needed to be replaced.  The hair dryer and mini bar in our room did not work.  The television was suppose to receive some 'English' channels.  When we asked about those, we were told the satellite was not working with those channels and they did not know why.  The pillows on the beds were like rocks.
