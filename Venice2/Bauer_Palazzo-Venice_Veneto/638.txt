Very disappointed with this hotel. I researched their webpage and it read very well, we were really looking forward to a memorable stay in beautiful Venice. Unfortunately the reality was far from the photos and glowing description. We had a 'deluxe' room for 3 nights that was booked and paid for months in advance. Our room was very depressing with a view of the laundry next door. The curtains could not be left open because of the lack of privacy and the room lighting was so poor we had to sit in bed to read. Our room 229 was in the 'newer' front section of the hotel..not the older palazzo. Sadly the unwelcoming room clouded our view of the whole hotel and it was easy to find fault everywhere.
