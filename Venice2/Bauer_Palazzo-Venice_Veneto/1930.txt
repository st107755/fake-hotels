My husband and I stayed for 3 nights at this wonderful hotel.  We sprung for the suite and it was worth every penny!  Our room was spacious, the airconditioning was great, the decor was luxurious and the service was outstanding!  We had 4 huge windows in our room and views of the canal and gondoliers passing by was standard.  When we closed the windows, the noise from the outside completely disappeared.  The Italian linen sheets on our bed where ever so comfortable and the nightly turn down with slippers placed at each side of the bed was a lovely touch. We were literally less than 5 minutes away from St. Mark's square and many key tourist places.  Great shops surround this hotel.
