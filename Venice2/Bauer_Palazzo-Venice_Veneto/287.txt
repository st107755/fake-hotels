We stayed here while visiting Venice in November.  We were welcomed by the very friendly and information concierge.  The hotel itself is very large!  It took a few trips outside of the room and back to figure out the layout of the hotel.  The breakfast room on the roof was lovely, they also have a nice breakfast/dinner room and bar located on the main floor.  The front patio overlooking the water was beautiful.  We were able to sit on the patio at night enjoy a drink, and watch the gondolas on the canal. When we were escorted to our room the concierge opened the door to a huge 3 room space.
