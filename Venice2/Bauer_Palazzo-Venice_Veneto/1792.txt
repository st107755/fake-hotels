I regret to say that this is not a very good hotel. It does have 3 key qualities: location, location, location. But after that it is lacking in so much. Anyone that writes a review about what a great place it is has never stayed in a real 5 star hotel. The rooms are VERY tired, the furniture (upholstery) is worn out and needs replacement. One night they turn down the beds and the next night they forget to do it. You order room service and the dirty dishes and glasses remain in the room for 2 days. The internet works sporadically. The concierges are inadequate and would never be tolerated in a 4 or 5 star hotel. No concierge would be better than what they have.
