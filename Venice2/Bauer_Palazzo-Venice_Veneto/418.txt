This hotel has beautiful outside areas on the Grand Canal.  The outside breakfast area, the outside terrace seating (for both breakfast and evening) and the rooftop bar are really outstanding.  A wonderful place to hang out in the evening or at night, after a long day of walking around.  Right on the water and the views are beautiful.  Just a warning, the hotel is not on the "boardwalk" of the Grand Canal, so you cant walk along the water to get to the hotel. You have to walk through St. Mark's square and down a couple side streets.  It's close to St Marks, so if this doesn't bother you, then the location is great.
