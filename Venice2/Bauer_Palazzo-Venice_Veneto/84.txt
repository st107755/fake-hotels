My husband and I stayed 4 nights in Bauer Palazzo. We booked "classic double room" and paid €443 inclusive breakfast & tax.  I am just going to attach photos so people can decide for themselves whether this room justifies as a 5-star hotel bedroom that costs €443 per night.  Amenities were similar to ones you see in motels. No hair conditioner, no soap dish, broken hair dryer.  I have been to much nicer Radisson Inn or Best western during my college days.  Forget nespresso machines! There isn't even complimentary instant coffees or kettles.  Not too mention our door was broken on the second day (key didn't fit in the keyhole because it was too old) and we had to call someone from downstairs to fix the door.
