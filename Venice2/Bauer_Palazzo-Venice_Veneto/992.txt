This was my second stay at the Bauer.  It was everything I remembered it to be more than 10 years ago.  The room was relaxing, quite, and comfortable.  I loved the bathroom marble.The location is superb-right off of St. Marks Square and it took us no more than a 2 minute walk to get there even with a toddler.  There was plenty of room for our little one to run around.  They had a crib already set up in the room and a complimentary bottle of wine and water chilling for us upon arrival.  We were given a complimentary tour and water taxi to a Murano glass factory; I liked that we were given the choice between a modern or classic factory to tour.
