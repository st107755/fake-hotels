When researching luxury hotels in Venice, I kept reading reviews of so-called 4 star or luxury hotels that did not live up to their rating, and also saw quite a few travelers' pictures of such hotels on this website that were scary.  However, a couple friends had heard of Bauer Il Palazzo, and I didn't find anything too scary about it, so we went with it, despite the hefty price tag.  The hotel did not disappoint.  From our arrival, the staff was consistently friendly and helpful.  We stayed in a deluxe double suite, which had a large king bed and walk-in closet, a nice sized sitting room (which we never used), a beautiful marble bathroom, and a spacious terrace with view of one of the side canals.
