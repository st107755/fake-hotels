We booked a room at the Bauer at the end of a cruise.  The hotel location was absolutely fabulous and we were impressed with the reception area.  We had heard nothing but wonderful things about the Bauer and were looking forward to our stay.  However, when we got to our room we were stunned at how small and poorly appointed the room was. The room was a very small square room with little room for more than the kingsized bed.  There was nowhere to sit other than a straight desk chair and very little room for that. The room overlooked three other rooms all over a lobby skylight with walls painted an odd brilliant green for some reason. It appeared we could pass things to the other rooms out the window.
