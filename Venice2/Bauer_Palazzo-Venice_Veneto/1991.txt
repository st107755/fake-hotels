My wife and I spent the last leg of our Honeymoon at the Bauer. Prior to Venice we visited Rome and Florence and enjoyed both. Having never visited Venice before we weren't sure what to expect. From beginning to end the Bauer was fantastic. The staff was fantastic, the rooms spacious, and the location could not have been better. We had booked with a breakfast package included and would strongly recommend doing so. The food was great and the patio view was exceptional. Our travel agent had emailed ahead and mentioned that it was our Honeymoon. Upon our arrival we were given an upgraded room and there was a bottle of champagne waiting for us as well.
