A very classic feeling hotel with lavish decor and an excellent location that does not quite deliver a top stay. Service was at times great, at times less so, amenities were nice but lack of individuality or personal touch and the lack of upkeep in the room makes the experience a bit forgettable. Obviously a lovely hotel in many ways, but for the money and the expectations for such a hotel the overall experience was not more than average. Still a nice hotel to stay at but would probably try others were I return to Venice before choosing the Bauer hotel again.
