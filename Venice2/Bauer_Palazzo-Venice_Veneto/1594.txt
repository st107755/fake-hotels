Actually I'm quite surprised to read all the good reviews of Palazzo Il Bauer.  My husband and I were very disappointed; the Palazzo Il Bauer is definitely not a 5star hotel. Although we had a great time together and we thought that our room was lovely (almost 1.000euro per night, so that's what we expected), we thought that the main part of the hotel did not give us the real Venezian Palazzo feeling at all.  The lobby of the Palazzo is small but the style of it is beautiful, it looks really old. When you enter it via the canal it looks good too, but the other entrance to that lobby, on the side, (this entrance you'll use much more often), is rather miserable; a small door coming to a dark and windy narrow alley.
