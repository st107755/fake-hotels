My wife just returned from our sixth visit to Venice and stayed at the San Clemente Palce (see review) and at the Bauer Palace. From the start, we were quite disapointed with the accomodation standards of the Bauer;  our room was old, small, and looked like in need of a good refurbishment. No internet access our in room information available. Bathroom was fine (had been refurbished...), but small and had no separate shower. Breakfast was held on the Grand Canal, in a very pleasant setting. Choices were limited for a hotel of such claimed standards.  Overall, we felt that we were staying in an old myth which had failed to evolve with the rest of the world.
