In the half-term holidays, my family and I went to Venice and stayed in the Bauer Hotel. It was excellent. We were treated like royalty; the staff were excellent especially when we asked them where the good restaurants are and when we went to the place the food was to die for! The rooms were very clean and posh but the downside is that they weren't  that big and could of had a bit more lighting but overall it was great! When we went down for breakfast, there was a gorgeous buffet filled with all types of breakfast foods: cheese and ham, sausages, scrambled egg, beans and potatoes, fruit, croissants etc. If you eat outside when having breakfast you can see the most beautiful view!
