We spent two nights at the Bauer Palazzo in July arriving with taxi boat from the airport. You make a stylish entrance with the boat directly into the hotel lobby which is stylish(but not as stylish as at The Danieli) with very high ceilings and comfortable chairs. The reception and concierge personnel are both friendly and helpful. We stayed in a junior suite which was nice. The hotel is located directly on the street with all the best luxury shops in Venice and only a stone throw away from the Piazza San Marco. The Bauer Palazzo has a very nice outdoor garden directly on the Grand Canal where you can sip an afternoon or pre dinner drink and enjoy the busy canal with all the boats and gondolas - in full privacy.
