The Bauer is connected to the Bauer il Palazzo, which is a much better (and significantly more expensive) hotel. Both are extremely well located, just a block from San Marco. Our room was overlooking a side canal, and was just a short walk through the hotel lobby to the incredibly beautiful il Palazzo restaurant, on the Grand Canal, with a great view of all the boat traffic, churches and other historic buildings. We kept looking around for James Bond (007) at one of the other tables! The included champagne breakfast was very well done, and there were flowers blooming everywhere. The only issue was that our room was getting just a little "worn looking". Could have used a remodel. Some of the furniture was chipped and dented.
