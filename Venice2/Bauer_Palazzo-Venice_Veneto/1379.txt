This was our first hotel stay in Venice. Overall everything was very good. My ten year daughter thinks it was excellent so we differ in opinion. The location couldn't be any better but I was a bit disappointed to learn that the lobby and restaurant are in a 15th century palazzo and the rooms are actually in a modern building connected to the lobby by a series of elevators. This can make getting to your room feel like quite a journey. We were upgraded to a full suite after booking a Jr. suite. The room was quite large with two bathrooms and again my daughter loved it. The decor was a bit too Elvis inspired  for my personal taste but was overall very nice and spacious. The service was good and the restaurant has breathtaking views of the canal.
