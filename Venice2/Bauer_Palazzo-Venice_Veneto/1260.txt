Our hotel room had a great view of the canal and was just a couple blocks from St Marks square.  You could open the window and hear some people singing on the gondolas, which was very lovely.  The rooms are a good size, and have an electrical system with many switches that takes some getting used to.  The front desk staff spoke english and the concierge staff spoke both english and spanish.  The hotel has a beautiful bar/restaurant that is right on the water with a wonderful view.  Breakfast was included with our stay, but we weren't able to go because we overslept.  WIFI was available, but not included for the price.
