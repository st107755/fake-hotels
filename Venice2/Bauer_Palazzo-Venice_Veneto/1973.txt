My husband and stayed at the Il Palazzo after New Year's and we were very disappointed.  We both love Venice and wanted to try a new place.  It is difficult to find really great places  to stay in Venice because of the hoardes of tourists, but we read great things about this hotel on this website and in travel magazines.  Il Palazzo is in a fabulous location, but that does no make up for its shortcomings.  The so-called renovation done in 1999 has not stood up to the short period of time that has passed.  The interiors are very tired and the "luxury" is absolutely not present.  The staff is indifferent to your presence and are absolutely not focused on service, as the Bauer group claims is their #1 priority.
