I was a bit apprehensive about the Bauer hotel especially after reading the reviews but I was wrong as I had the best experience during my stay there. The hotel is traditionally decorated but elegant and well maintained. The room was a bit small but I was offered a room overlooking the canal, which was lovely. Breakfast on the terrace was a treat and the food selection was excellent: cold cuts, warm food (sausages, bacon, scrambled eggs, hash brown), cereals and pastries etc.   The hotel is situated 3 minutes walk from San Marco square with easy access to designer shops and water transportation.
