A mandated flight layover after a cruise found us needing a place to stay for the night.  Holland America suggested the Bauer it was a bit pricy but because of the location on the Grand Canal my wife and I chose it.  The water taxi delivered us to the door a great entrance to the hotel.  We arrived at 08:30 check in was 13:00 so we left our bags and made a tour of Venice.   The Hotel is old, but well appointed with lots of staff in uniforms.  The bags were delivered to our room, but for some reason the bell captain thought he should show us our room. He showed us how to turn on the lights and made sure our bags were there and paused to let us know he expected a tip (Holland America told us the gratuities were included) then he left.
