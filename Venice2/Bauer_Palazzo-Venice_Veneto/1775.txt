We stayed here three nights after arriving from an overnight flight from the US.  The hotel is a hotel within a hotel (The Bauer), making it private.  We booked a grand canal suite - one of the more expensive rooms in the hotel.  We had a nice (but not spectacular) water view, and a very small balcony that one could barely step out onto. The room was very quiet, despite the noise of the grand canal below. However, of all the rooms we stayed at in Italy in this particular  price range, this was the smallest.    The best thing about this hotel is the rooftop terrace where they serve breakfast. And breakfast at this hotel was one of the best that we had while in Italy.
