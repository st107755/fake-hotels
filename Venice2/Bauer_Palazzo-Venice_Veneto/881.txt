We stayed here over New Year and were not disappointed with either the service or location. Just a few minutes from Saint Marks Square and with the terrace located on the Grand Canal, you could not ask for a better base. Drinks on the terrace were idyllic with the stream of gondoliers entering the Canal from the San Moise. The Staff were all you would expect from a 5 star hotel, attentive, immaculate and eager to accommodate every request. The only complaint would have to be the size of the room. We unfortunately chose the Superior Double, inferring that the term "Superior" implied a level of luxury.
