I visit Venice at least twice a year and on the last two occasions have stayed at the Bauer Hotel. The accomodation is excellent.  In the 50 or so times I have visited Venice I have always felt safe and secure but was sadly disappointed this time. While boarding the vaporetto at Fondamenta Nuove my purse containing around $500 was taken from my bag. Fortunately my cards were separate. My bag never left my shoulder at any stage. The friend who was with me had also gone to much trouble to buy tickets costing £150 each for La Fenice. We went to the box we had been allocated and unfortunately were unable to see the stage as our seats were behind two large ladies.  Fortunately I have a friend who works there and she was able to take us up to the fourth tier where seats were laid out.…
