You are greeted by harsh architecture as you approach this hotel. If this is not the ugliest facade in Venice I would be shocked. It's actually startling.   The lobby is large and a glorification of red marble. Okay, not my taste, but I wonder who chose the colour. I was greeted by a rude and not particularly helpful bellman. The reception staff were a mixed bag, some were friendly, others made me feel as though I was annoying them.   The hallways are stark, cold, clinical, lacking of comfort, all which created an uncomfortable experience.   The room is in desperate need of redecoration.
