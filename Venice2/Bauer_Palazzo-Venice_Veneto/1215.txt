Should you stay in this hotel? Yes, if you want the best possible location in Venice at a reasonable price.  You will be able to have dinner on a gorgeous terrace overlooking the canal, the views are breathtaking.  Step out of the hotel in the other direction and you have a canal to the left with cheerful Gondoliers waiting to take you in a Gondola, church to the right and all your favourite stores just outside.  San Marco square is just a few metres away. You can escape from the crowded streets into a quiet haven and relax.  Dinner on the terrace is magical with excellent food, friendly and attentive waiters.  All the staff in the hotel are polite and helpful.
