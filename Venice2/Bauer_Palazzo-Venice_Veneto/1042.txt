I booked a suite at Il Palazzo and was given a room on the 5th floor with a side terrace that was shared with the adjacent rooms. The view was lovely. I also appeciate very much that this hotel takes dogs (35 euro charge per night ) . The location is close to San Marco and access to other parts of the city is good too, either by walking or boat. The room was spacious with a very comfortable bed and plenty of storage for clothes and luggage. The bathroom looked quite nice with marble sink and pedestal and expansive mirrors.
