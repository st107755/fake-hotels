Following short break in Rome my wife and I arrived at the Bauers for a three night stay. The location is excellent being a short walk from San Marco but quiet. It benefits from restaurant, bar and breakfast on the Grand Canal where an early breakfast with the sun rising, or an Aperol spritz with the sun going down is a great place to be. The room we had whilst looking over the roof-tops rather than canal could not be faulted, being clean, traditional and cared for. What stood out for us however in a City that can often lurk for tourists to scare you with the price of a coffee and surprise you with "included" cover & service charges is the staff at the Bauers.
