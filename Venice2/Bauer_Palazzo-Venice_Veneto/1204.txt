This was my first visit to Venice, and although I knew it had canals, I had not realised that in the city centre, the only means of transport are feet and water. This means of course that you can stay in a central location such as this without traffic noise and danger. However, unless the hotel has a landing stage, you are going to have to trundle your bags through the streets. The BiP has a landing stage so your water taxi brings you from the airport right to the door. The arrangement of the hotel is odd in that the same structure houses two hotels under the same management, with the BiP facing the grand canal and the Bauer looking the other way.
