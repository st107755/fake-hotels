Go down passage way by Harry's bar see Omega watch place ahead then left and you will come to the Hotel. Opposite Hotel is Prada Miu Miu and Chanel Louis Vuitton further down. Loved Hotel. Staff really friendly and helpful.  Breakfast gorgeous stunning views with gondolas passing by. We sat outside and it was wonderful great breakfast with really good hot coffee and great to sit outside. Pigeons were fine as long as someone at the table they kept away.
