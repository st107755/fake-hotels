Ideally located on the Grand Canal a stones throw from St Marco Square. From the canal the Bauer is a classic hotel, yet from the pedestrian side it is decco. two hotels in one. Our room was huge and classical. Friends at the neighbouring Westin were paying a similar price for a much tighter space and much less interesting. The dining room on the canal is great but there's more. there's a roof top area offering breakfast and later free snacks and beverages, with a great view of the canal. this may have been only available to certain room classes. But there's much more. the sister hotel the Bauer Palladio a short hop across the water on their shuttle is an amazing hotel for lunch or dinner - set in a huge garden. we were the only ones dining outside and it was sensational.
