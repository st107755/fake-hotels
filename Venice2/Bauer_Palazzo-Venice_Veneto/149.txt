My husband and I checked in to the Bauer Palazzo after enjoying a Mediterranean cruise. The hotel is situated right on the Canal and was easy to reach via the Alilaguna water bus/vaporetto from the cruise port.   Upon arrival, we were pleasantly informed our room had been upgraded and included a beautiful view of the Canal. However, the room itself was not ready but the hotel happily stowed our luggage while we waited for it to be serviced by housekeeping. In the meantime, we enjoyed the rooftop lounge, which had the most AMAZING views of Venice. We also met with the Concierge who booked us a complimentary shuttle to the island of Murano.
