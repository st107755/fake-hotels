The title of this review is motivated, in part, by the fact that there is a Louis Vuitton store very close to the hotel. When we were in Venice, the LV store was displaying very expensive handbags decorated with images of works by famous artists (Monet, Dega, ..). The Bauer Palazzo is an elegant old hotel in a great location, just a short walk to the Piazza San Marco. Our room was large enough and there was adequate hanging and storage space in the closets. The bath was fairly large, and included a full tub with shower, as well as a toilet and bidet. However, there are problems with the bath design- the marble partition on the side of the tub that is intended to make it a shower is not long enough to keep the shower spray from wetting the bathmat.
