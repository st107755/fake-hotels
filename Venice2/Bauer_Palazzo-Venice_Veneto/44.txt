The Bauer Palazzo has perhaps the best address and position along the Grand Canal, and indeed it is a floating palace.   Inside the hotel boasts very large public spaces, rare for Venetian property, but adding to the grandeur of place. Murano glass everywhere and gilded trimmings. Opulent Neo Baroque styled rooms are a bit dated, but also quite sizable, considering, for Venice, though they do vary and here you get what you pay for. Rooms with canal views are the most expensive of course, but if you cannot book one of those rooms, the terrace bar and restaurant right on the canal will offer endless views and photos.
