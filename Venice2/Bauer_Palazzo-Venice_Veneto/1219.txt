Stayed at the Bauer Casanova over august bank holiday weekend with my boyfriend. The location is perfect- minutes walk from St Marks square in an upmarket area and on the Grand Canal. The hotel itself is beautiful! We stayed at the Casa Nova in a double room which did not disappoint! Large luxurious room with a marble bathroom and a spiral staircase leading to small living space. I have read previous reviews criticising the Casanova due to it being an annex to the main hotel. In my opinion the Casa Nova is 5* just as all areas of the hotel are. The entrance to the casa nova is 2 metres from the main lobby and although there are no canal views the room cost is compensated for this even though it is of no issue.
