Thhis was my 3rd time at this hotel, it continues to be my favorite in Venice. This time we had a great room with views of the rooftops and St. Mark's campanile from the bedroom and bathroom.  Having stayed there more than a couple of times (and multiple rooms with family) I can assure you that there are many different rooms, some with different charms than others. Make sure you py attention to the type of room you book and if you're not happy with the room they might have a different room in the same category that is completely different.  Service was great, from the moment our water taxi dropped us off and the bellman greeted us, until we checked out afte a couple of days of busy sightseeing.
