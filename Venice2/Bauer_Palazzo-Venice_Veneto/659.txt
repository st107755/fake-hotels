We found this hotel below standard and didn't feel that it deserved a 5 star.  On entrance to the reception there are large stains on the rugs which appeared to have been there a while.  The staff behind the reception and concierge desks lacked hospitality skills. We didn't find them very attentive during our stay and at times it felt they were dismissive. Breakfast was limited with only a small selection. We were disappointed to find a lack of healthy choices and no chef prepared options or fish such as mackerel. The breakfast was listed as €50 per person and definitely wasn't worth this amount.
