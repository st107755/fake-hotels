We stayed for 3 nights during Carnivale.  The hotels location is perfect; the staff numbers plentiful, well presented, polite and well used to dealing with international clientele with all the quirks and idiosyncrasy that must involve!  On arrival check in was faultless – and to use a real metal room key rather than electronic key was a novelty.  Two tiny lifts were available to access the rooms  – max occupancy was 2 people with cases Our room was on the 3rd floor and on exiting the lift  we were met by a long very wide  marble floored corridor which some may consider cold and unwelcoming.  I know it’s petty to mention it but this is a 5 star hotel after all – the large wooden clock above the lift was broken for our entire stay...I wonder if it still is?  Our room was…
