They had 20 guests mainly from Australia come to the wedding which was absoloutely amazing. It was huge! with a big bathroom, large living area, two walk in robes and the best part was the four terraces which were wonderful.  The view from our room was beautiful, we could see the gondoliers and also the square below.  Wifi was quite good, however it did drop out at times but wasn't much of a problem.
