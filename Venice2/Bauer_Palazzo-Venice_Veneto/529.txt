My husband and I were traveling with a friend. We had two rooms and were grateful to discover both had outdoor space with gorgeous views. If booking, I recommend making direct contact with the Bauer or LHW and asking for room details. The location is perfect and all the major sights are a short walk away. The hotel's outdoor terrace is spectacular. Breakfast is great, but skip the room service.   Though we loved our rooms, the level of hospitality in the hotel was inconsistent. Upon arrival we waited about twenty minutes before a front desk agent made eye contact or acknowledged us. Eventually a warm, cheerful woman checked us in and showed us to our rooms.
