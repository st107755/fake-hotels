We stayed here for three nights during the beginning of Carnevale. First, I can't say enough about the beauty and enchantment that is Venice! As far as accommodations go, however, you can't get much better than Bauer Palazzo. Gondoliers serenading their passengers right outside the window adds ambiance and charm to the luxury accommodations. The room, bathroom and closet are spacious- and we rented only a basic double room- and the bed was extremely comfortable, and the linens were very high end quality. Our room was next to the elevator, and it was still very quiet.   The location is top notch, as it a mere two minute walk to Piazza San Marco and the waterfront.
