Beautifully situated on The Grand Canal Il Palazzo is the Boutique hotel section of the Bauer. The two buildings co join as one. The Venetian Palazzo facade is on the canal whilst  the front entrance to the Bauer opens onto a Square and is elegantly furnished in a timeless Art Deco Style. We arrived by a water taxi that the hotel had arranged for us as the sun was setting. The small private wharf in front of the hotel leads into an intimate and stylish reception. The staff are polite but more than that also welcoming. We had chosen a deluxe king room with a view over the Grand Canal with balcony.
