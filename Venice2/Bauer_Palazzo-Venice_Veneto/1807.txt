We stayed at the Bauer for the last 3 nights of our honeymoon at the beginning of March. We would recommend this hotel to anyone. It is in the heart of Florence and easily accessible from both the train station and airport. We took the train from Florence and upon arrival located the public transportation boat which dropped us off 1 block from the hotel. From that point we never needed to take the boats again (with the exception of going to Mirano) since the hotel is in such a central location. The service was fantastic. From check in till check out, everyone helped make our stay perfect. We dined in the restaurant the first night in Venice.
