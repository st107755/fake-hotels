Stayed at the Bauer on the Grand Canal for a long weekend break. Our room was in the annexe next to the hotel, but the quality wasn't compromised. The bedroom was adequate, the bed was very comfortable and the bathroom, including walk in shower were excellent. Having breakfast on the hotel piazza, overlooking the Grand Canal was a delight. You need to accept that staying at the Bauer, most things you purchase, eg: drinks, food and the hotel itself are not cheap. You are also charged a city tax on departure of 10 euro per night. The hotel is situated just a two minute walk from St Marks Square and therefore, everything in the area is quite expensive.
