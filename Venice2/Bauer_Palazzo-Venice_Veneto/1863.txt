My husband and I stayed at the Bauer Hotel for 4 nights for our honeymoon in mid-June 2007.  Can't believe some of the terrible reviews I've been reading, as our experience of the Bauer was wonderful.  Ok, maybe I had the advantage that I took the time to learn some of the Italian language (something others don't appear to even consider!!) - however all staff on reception and concierge team seemed to be fully conversant with the English language and very friendly.  We paid 374 euros - (£260) per night, a rate obtained via Expedia for a Deluxe Double Room.  I telephoned the hotel directly about 5 days prior to our arrival and explained it was our honeymoon and that any special touches would be appreciated.  We were amazed when we realised we'd been upgraded to a Junior Suite -…
