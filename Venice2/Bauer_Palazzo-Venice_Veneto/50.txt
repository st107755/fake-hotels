I found the hotel to be in an excellent location.  We were able to walk out the door and find the gondola station and many high end shops. The lobby was large but the staff and the concierge were stand offish. When asking for a simple request like sharing an umbrella, I felt like they were doing us a huge favor.   When a question was asked to one, they often said we needed to ask someone else.  Our room was small with a tub/shower combination, a heated towel bar, and no washcloths. Luckily we found a maid who was willing to go to the spa to find some for us.  The concierge said they did not have any and never used washcloths/facecloths in their homes.
