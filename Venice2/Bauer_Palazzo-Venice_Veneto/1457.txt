My husband and I were celebrating our 20th anniversary, and everything about this hotel made our vacation just perfect.  The hotel itself has an intimate feel, the staff knew our names right away, and upgraded us to an incredible room on the 5th floor with a balcony and a welcoming bottle of prosecco.  Even before our stay, the concierge was very helpful in arranging restaurant reservations, reserving a tour guide, and recommending activities like "Music a Palazzo" which is a small opera company that performs in a palazzo on the Grand Canal.  Breakfast on the rooftop, "7th Heaven" as they call it, was a highlight, with the best view of the Grand Canal and the rooftops of Venice.
