We arrived by water taxi to the canal side door of the hotel - what an experience! The hotel has an impressive position on the Grand Canal with amazing views of the surrounding buildings and all the daily life around you.  They have a wonderful terrace over looking the Grand Canal, where we enjoyed a delicious lunch of fresh fish and salad with excellent service and the very best view; it is a bit pricey but worth the plash out and much cheaper than dinner.  The drinks terrace adjacent to the dining terrace is also a great spot for an aperitif and is where you take breakfast, which was fabulous.
