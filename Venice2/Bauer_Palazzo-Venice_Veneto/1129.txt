When we complained we were told this happens every day as they are delivering goods from the canal to the restaurants and hotels. So avoid a room that faces this side of the hotel. The room  was also dark and rather soulless so we were pleased that our new room, a superior panoramica was much better. There are a few things that let down the hotel.
