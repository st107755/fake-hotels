What a beautiful hotel! The enterance is grand and lavish, with its marble floors, leather sofas and AIR CON it really sets a standard of what you will experience in Venice! We stayed in room 245, the room is stunning, with its large bathroom, grand bed (soooo comfortable) and balcony our breath was taken away. The maid service is flawless, they change the rooms twice a day and it’s a real treat heading back to a cool, dark room (shutters are put down) at night. The breakfast is very good, lots of choice & fresh and Gluten free options too! The waiting on team are brill and the view is amazing no wonder we were at breakfast for hours!
