Just returned from a four night stay at the Bauer Hotel, we were allocated a room  in the Casa Nova which is an annex to the main hotel, this did not make any difference to our stay at the hotel, the staff and welcome at the hotel was very reassuring as this was our first trip to Venice. One of our cases had gone missing from Heathrow to Venice but we found it had been returned to our room on our second evening!!! The breakfast we had every morning was delicious even down to the bubbly available every morning. There was bacon, eggs, sausage, pancakes, lovely pastries and copious amounts of fruit all sumptuous which kept us going for most of the days.
