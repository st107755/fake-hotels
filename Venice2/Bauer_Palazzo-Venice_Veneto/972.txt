The hotel is centrally located on the Grand Canal very close to St. Mark's Basilica, high end designer shops and smaller stores.  A 70 Euro ride from the train station.  The staff was very friendly and helpful.  Breakfast is served indoors or on the terrace overlooking the water.  A copy of the International Edition of the NY Times was available at breakfast each morning.  Our room was spacious, well appointed and clean.  With the exception of church bells that inexplicably rang at 7:30 one morning (not a Sunday), the hotel was very quiet. The bathroom was, as described by my daughter, as big as her college dorm room.  The shower was strong.  The only criticism I have of the hotel is that its wifi is not very good.
