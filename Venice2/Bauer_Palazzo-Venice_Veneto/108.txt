We stayed here over Easter and were not disappointed.  All of the staff that we came across were friendly and welcomed you. The hotel is right in the middle of Venice. A perfect location. There is a gorgeous terrace out at the back of the hotel on the Canal where you can sit and watch the gondolas pass by.  Breakfast was delicious. Enough choice to please everyone.  Room service was excellent and couldn’t be more helpful.  Our suite was lovely. The bed huge and the bed linen like silk. Brilliant electric shutters so you could shut the morning light out if you wanted to sleep In a little.
