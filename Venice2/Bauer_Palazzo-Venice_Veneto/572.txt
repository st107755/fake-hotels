We spent 3 Nights in October in the Bauer Palazzo. The Hotel is located perfectly looking out onto the Grand Canal, side views over a small adjacent Canal where you can hear the Gondoliers maneuvering into position to pick up tourists for a beautiful ride along the winding canals through this stunning city. The Hotel was perfectly located for all the walking we did and a great base to stop off for refreshments before leaving again for afternoon and evening walks. The Deluxe Room we had was certainly large enough with a long narrow bathroom, separate dressing area - maybe missing tea/ coffee making facility for a 5* Hotel!
