We stayed at the Bauer for 2 nights at the beginning of September, a birthday gift from my husband. Having never been to Venice I was totally bowled over by the atmosphere and charm! Our stay at the Bauer was lovely-we found the service impeccable with attentive and welcoming reception staff as well as the concierge providing helpful advice and booking us a water taxi for our early start on the day we left. The hotel definitely has an air of opulence with textiles lining the walls and marble bathrooms. The air conditioning was great and a very welcome feature in the 30 degree heat.
