When we arrived a woman at reception told us they had good news for us and that they had upgraded us to a junior suite.  I was skeptical because I knew the hotel was fully booked.  To see this junior suite we had to leave the main hotel building by a back door, cross an alleyway and enter a third building.  The rooms were barely renovated, dismal and not connected to the main building, at least we were not shown a connection.  I was in shock.   I asked to see the original room we had booked.
