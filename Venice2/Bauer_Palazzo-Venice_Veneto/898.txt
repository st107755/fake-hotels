This hotel was lovely.  On check-in, I was offered an upgrade to a deluxe room.  My room was excellent - lovely décor, beautiful marble bathroom and with balcony overlooking side canal.  The bed was very comfortable with high quality linens.  Lovely breakfast room overlooking the Grand Canal.  The lobby was in art deco style and extremely comfortable.  All the staff were exceptionally helpful and courteous.  I visit Venice every two years and this was the nicest hotel I have stayed in.  I read a couple of complaints from other guests and it's true that the hallway (I was on the second floor) was a bit 'cold', no complimentary water, not enough electric outlets for chargers.
