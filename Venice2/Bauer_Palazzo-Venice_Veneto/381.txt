Our cruise agent booked us into Bauer Hotel for an extended stay following a cruise. What a wonderful surprise to find ourselves, not only in an elegant hotel, but just steps from St Mark's Square and so many of the sights we wanted to enjoy. The rooms are comfortable, the included breakfast is substantial and the staff, not only competent, but friendly and patient.   We weren't able to stay at the Bauer for the entire time we wanted to see Venice, as they were booked solid. Fortunately for us, Bauer has other hotels just across the Grand Canal, for which they operate a free water shuttle every half hour from 6 am to 1 am.
