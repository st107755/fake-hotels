This is the fifth time I have stayed at this hotel.  Usually we have a room in Casa Nova this time it was the main hotel.  The rooms have a different feel from Casa Nova no silk lined walls and antique feel it was very Art Deco.  Very lovely.  The service was exemplary as usual the food wonderful and the terrace is to die for.  Another bonus is the sister hotel Palladio across the lagoon which has a tranquil feel.  We had a wonderful lunch in the garden away from the hustle and bustle of St Marks Square.   I have stayed in Venice almost 30 times but since discovering the delights of the Bauer would not stay anywhere else.
