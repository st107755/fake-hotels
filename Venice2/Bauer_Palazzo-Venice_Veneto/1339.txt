We stayed two nights here. As we pulled up to the hotel at dusk, the view was pretty spectacular. I will never forget it. As far as the actual hotel and rooms, they were very comfortable but small. I'm not a fan of the old, rich tapestry look, so this was not the greatest for me- but I soon got used to the fabric covered walls and such. And, when in Venice.... Our room had a balcony right on the canal and an amazing view, but it was a shared balcony. Sooo, if we walked to the right, we were literally looking into someone else's room. But I digress...  The location is quite good- it's near all of the things you want to do in Venice and you will be doing a ton of walking anyway, so you want your hotel near that stuff so you can knock it all out before you see the real Venice.
