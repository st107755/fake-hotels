We had three rooms at the Bauer for our party of six.  They were all nicely decorated with stylish, high quality bathrooms.  One room even had a terrace overlooking the carvings on the San Moise church with two comfortable deck chairs.  My favorite part of the hotel was definitely eating breakfast in the morning on the grand canal, watching the boats go by and the gondolas get ready for the day.  There was also a lovely terrace at the side of the hotel where we were able to sit at the end of the day and play cards, order drinks, etc.  My one real disappointment with the hotel was the loud disco music that marred our final night.
