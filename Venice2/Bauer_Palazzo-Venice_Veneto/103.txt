Very well located in the Grand Canal and near Piazza San Marco, I expected this hotel to be a real 5 star palazzo, but not really... To start, the so promised rooftop is not open to public, amenities are very average for a 5 star, terrible hairdryer on the wall makes it very low budget kind of hotel (had to ask for a good one). Staff is very friendly specially on the breakfast (and the location for breakfast is actually very very good). The two old concierges are not very useful and arrogant: wanted to ask some questions to the concierge that totally ignored me (extremely rude), a young concierge called Simone that was there realized the bad manners of his colleague and was very kind and useful giving me informations I needed.
