The very young, old and indifferent will find this hotel one of the most beautiful places to book for a trip. Our silk wallpapered rooms with fire place overlooked the canal the gondoliers would roll by each day and the concierge was most helpful in directing us to where to dine (with the locals) and made all the car/boat arrangements to get us to and from the airport (when arriving) and train to Florence.  When travelling with a party of 8 one needs to have a helpful person to assist the coordination and they were great and patient in doing so.
