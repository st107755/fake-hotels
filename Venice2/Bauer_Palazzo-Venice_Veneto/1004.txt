Would highly recommend this hotel!  Great location near San Marco's Square.  The staff was very helpful, knowledgeable and accommodating - holding our bags and providing a generous space to freshen up and take refuge from the oppressive heat until our room was ready.  The room itself was beautiful with a gorgeous view towards San Giorgio.  From the rooftop deck (where the extensive American-style breakfast was served), the (nearly 360 degree) view was fantastic!  Just watch out for "Sofia" the seagull, who will try to snag your leftovers as you return to the buffet for more!  The wait staff helps keep the birds at bay w/the surprising help of....waterguns (too funny!).
