We came upon an article in a magazine (Marie Claire I think) that detailed a weekend trip to Venice in the off season last year, and ever since have been dying to go.  The author had stayed at the Bauer Hotel, and gave us goosebumps with the description of the luxury accomodations.  We did some homework on Trip Advisor and through the hotel's website, and were completely repelled by the price (we aren't in the habit of dropping 600 - 700€ per night for a hotel).  But the closer we got to our departure date, the better the prices got.  In the end we booked for 3 nights  and paid 210€ per night for the standard double room.
