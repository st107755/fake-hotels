The Bauer Palazzo Venice is, quite simply my favourite hotel in the World. Venice is a unique place and the Bauer is the perfect hotel from which to experience the magic of this place. The rooms in the Bauer are spacious and cosy but what really makes this hotel so special is the warmth and professionalism of the staff, especially those at the front of house. My wife and I try to visit on an annual basis and are always welcomed back with genuine warmth which is why we so look forward to returning. I happened to mention on our last visit earlier this month that I was celebrating my 60th birthday.
