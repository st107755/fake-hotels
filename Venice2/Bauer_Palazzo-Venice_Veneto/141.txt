We arrived in Venice at the time of severe flooding.  This hotel did everything possible to keep life running smoothly and pleasant for our stay.  We had a room in the old Palazzo overlooking the Grand Canal and Santa Maria Salute,  The room was exquisite and quiet with every comfort and an excellent bathroom.  This room allowed us access to the seventh floor for breakfast and drinks/snacks during the day where, since we were confined indoors during the flooding, Anastasio deserves special mention for looking after us and telling us interesting things about Venice.  We did get out on three of our five days.  On only one evening was the hotel under particular pressure to serve all of us who were stranded indoors and staff had to work extra hard.
