We arrived in Venice on the Orient Express from London, and got a water taxi straight to the dock. I couldn't believe it - the doorman was waiting to welcome us with glasses of prosecco whilst our luggage was whisked up to our room. When we were ready we were shown to our room - which was lovely. No Grand Canal view - which was probably just as well, as the waterway is busy all the time! - but we could see the Campanile from the window. The entrance is small, and I can see that if you arrived on foot you might be confused by the rather bizarre way in which the two hotels are blended together but yet not blended - but once you sort out which is which, the Palazzo is simply exquisite.
