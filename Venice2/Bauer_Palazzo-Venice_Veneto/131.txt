We just spent 3 nights here with my family. The rooms we had were amazing - spacious, top floor, views of the grand canal. Very clean, staff was wonderful and gracious. Room service was prompt and precise. Breakfast was on the top floor terrace/room. My only suggestion would be to add an additional “made to order” option for hot breakfast to the terrace as it seems to be adding the additional factor that all our other Europe 5 star hotels offered.
