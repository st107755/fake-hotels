My twin and I stayed at the Bauers Casa Nova annex midweek during February. When we arrived at the hotel and walked through the doors we were stunned by the opulence and could not quite believe our luck!  The Bauers is truly magnificent! Our room was beautiful with silk lined walls and a marble bathroom. Every morning we were treated to possibly one of the best views in the world as we watched gondolas float by in the sunshine. Breakfast was lovely with every option imaginable and the hot chocolate is not to be missed! The hotel is in a perfect location for you to explore venice and is footsteps away from St Mark's Square.
