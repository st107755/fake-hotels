A very upscale, but not flashy, hotel that treats customers as customers. The best part is the terrace right on the Grand Canal, overlooking the Salute Church directly across. Breakfast on the terrace, where you can take 10 minutes or an hour very comfortably, is super, as you watch the gondoliers right in front of you getting their boats ready for the day. To have a drink on that terrace in the evening is just as nice, and the illumination of the Salute at night is almost magical. Do stay here, especially if you get a deal from Expedia for one night free, as we did. But, do not just take the vaporetti and schlep your luggage as we did, to our regret.
