I stayed at this hotel in February 2013. I had arranged this trip as part of a ruse to 'pop the question' to my girlfriend (she said yes btw). We only stayed for a couple of nights and because of the occasion I obviously wanted it to be special. Upon approaching the hotel via a water taxi (which is a must even if it is a little expensive) the concierge was polite and helpful getting both the luggage and us on to the dock. The reception staff were equally as helpful and spoke english well. The lobby of the hotel is amazing, with marble and gold everywhere. It is exactly how I would expect a venetian hotel to be.
