Our stay at the Bauer il Palazzo was excellent.  We arrived by taxi and from the minute we docked till the minute we departed we were treated to exceptional service.  I was a bit concerned before going to Venice as I had heard stories of poor and arrogant service but this was never a problem.  The hotel is part of the larger Bauer hotel but the Palazzo is far more traditional and elegant.  The room was stunning as was the views.  We couldn't be in a better location, only minutes from St Marks square but far enough away to not be in the masses.  Every designer label shop is located right next to the hotel.
