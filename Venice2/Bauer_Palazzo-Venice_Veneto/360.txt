I've walked past the gopping sixties concrete eye sore of the Bauer Hotel entrance dozens of times. But that is only half the story. At the back of that building overlooking the Grand Canal is the Bauer Palazzo, a converted Venetian palace with both a ground floor dining terrace on the canal and a gorgeous top floor terrace used for Breakfast. This is a connected but separate hotel from the Bauer. The location is the best in town: A short walk to St Mark's, a gondola stop on your door step, rooms with views over the Grand Canal and Giudecca. Two of our party were not very mobile, but we walked everywhere they wanted, and at the end of the day the sound of musicians and singers drifted up from the canals below to our enormous and beautiful rooms.
