We stayed at the Bauer Hotel for two days prior to our 25th anniversary cruise.  It is expensive but worth a splurge for a special celebration.  We arrived in the morning and took the water taxi arranged by the hotel.  My DH had never been to Venice so the entire experience was thrilling.  Our room wasn't ready but they told us to have breakfast on them and come back to talk to the concierge about what to do.  They arranged a private tour of Murano and it was worth the price.  On the way there the private boat took us down the Grand Canal for pictures and on the way back he took a different route to show us more.  When we came back our room was ready.
