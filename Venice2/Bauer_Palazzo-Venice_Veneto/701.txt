This hotel was chosen for us by our tour company for a 3-night stay in Venice before joining our cruise ship,the Queen Victoria. And what a choice it turned out to be !!  Must be the best hotel in the world and we have stayed in some of the best.  We had  suite 249 with a balcony overlooking the canal and spent many happy hours watching the gondoliers taking their clients along the canal, playing their accordions and singing romantic songs.  Our suite was so comfortable and stylish I really could have spent the whole of our trip in the hotel.  So if like me you have a celebration, I had my 64th birthday here, there is no better place on earth to do so.
