Just a short walk from St. Marks and the San Marco vaporetto stop. Also 5 minutes to the San Marco stop from the airport which we walked from with luggage with no problems. Beautiful hotel, nice staff (especially Guilia in the terrace restaurant). I researched hotels for months before deciding on the Bauer. Rooms are updated (bathrooms are beautiful marble and granite). Clean - which is always my first concern. Nice crisp white bedding. Ask for soft pillows when reserving your room. Ground floor restaurant overlooks the Grand Canal. The included buffet breakfast offers plenty of options. Our room was in the newer section (room 241) which overlooked a canal and Campo Moise.
