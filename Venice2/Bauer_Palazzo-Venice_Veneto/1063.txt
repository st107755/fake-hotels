You couldn't rate this palace as a hideaway, what with its red-canopied and gold-embossed facade, trumpeting its elegance down the Grand Canal.  Its authentic 18th century pedigree, with an unbelievable location minutes from Piazza San Marco and footsteps away from everything luxurious, ranks this property as "the most exclusive'.     We budgeted at smaller hotels en route to Venice to crown our experience with this magic pile.  Its sister hotel, the Baure, can hardly be called a discovery, considering its size.  For us it represented an intimate hideaway minutes from when we felt like leaving this romantic idyll.
