We stayed here for our wedding anniversary and had a wonderful time. The hotel has its own jetty so if you have loads of money (and it helps to have loads of money in Venice!) then private water taxi (€100 single) it from the airport direct to the hotels jetty. Its located at the start of the Grand Canal. Having said that the alilaguna (public boat) offers an unhurried and much cheaper trip which makes a few stops but gives good views of Venice and is only (€12) with the stop being less than 5 minutes walk from the hotel, so the money you save you can spend on Bellinis at Harrys!
