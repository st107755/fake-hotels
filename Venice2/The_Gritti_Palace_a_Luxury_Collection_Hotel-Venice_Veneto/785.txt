I stayed at this Hotel with my daughter for a few days in January.  Since my last stay there has been a substantial amount of reservations and the hotel is back to looking its best, it is well located and the service really is at the top end of experiences.  Our room was big for Venice and comfortably appointed with both a bath and shower in the bathroom.  Food in particular breakfast was excellent and the bar service prompt and efficient unlike some of the other well known bars in Venice the staff realised it was there job to serve customers and did it happily and professionally.
