There are many very good things about this Hotel. A stand out feature was the service, particularly at the front desk. Staff were very friendly and efficient and gave the impression that they would go that extra distance to get things right. The concierge was good too.  Our room was also very comfortable. We had a room with a fantastic view over the Grand Canal which was well worth the extra charge (over twice as much as a standard room). The bathroom had more marble than I have ever seen before and the room itself was a decent enough size with a small balcony and (of course) that fantastic view. The bed was comfortable too.
