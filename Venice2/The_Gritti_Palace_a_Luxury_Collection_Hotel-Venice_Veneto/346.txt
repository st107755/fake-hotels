We stayed here twice in two weeks with a side trip in between, having stayed at this hotel many times in the past and known what to expect somehow they surprised me and managed to continue to improve the standards of service and comfort.  I stay in high end hotels every month and it takes quite a bit to impress me but the way that the team at the Gritti do 5 star is beyond what i have come to expect.  The spotless decor and public areas the immaculate work of the house keepers in the room cleaning, the ever friendly and helpful concierge and finally the very professional restaurant and bar staff all combine to provide an unforgettable experience, at times we felt guilty leaving the hotel to see the sites.
