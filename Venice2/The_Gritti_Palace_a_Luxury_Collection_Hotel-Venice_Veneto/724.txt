Staying at the Gritti Palace is an incredible experience. The service is wonderful (even before you arrive), and the rooms are sumptuously decorated. Even if your room doesn't have a canal view, you can go to other areas of the hotel (the restaurant and bar) to sit on a deck and enjoy the Grand Canal. Bathroom amenities are fantastic, and the sheets, towels, and other in-room amenities are equally high-end.   If you can afford it, I highly recommend arriving by private water taxi; it's a magical experience. The hotel has a private dock. We learned from the concierge that the private water taxi service to/from the airport is cheaper than the website indicated if you are willing to walk from the dock to the terminal on your own (without car service).
