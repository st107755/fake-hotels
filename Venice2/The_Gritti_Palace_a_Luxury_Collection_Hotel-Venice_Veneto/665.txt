The location is great, right on the grand canal, and it's fun to have drinks and watch the boats go by.  However, I found the staff and overall service experience was very cold and unfriendly, to the point where I was relieved when it was finally time to depart.   It started with our check-in experience. Our room was ready two hours LATE (after the guaranteed check-in time.) This was a significant inconvenience in itself, and was also handled very poorly by the front desk staff and the manager on duty.
