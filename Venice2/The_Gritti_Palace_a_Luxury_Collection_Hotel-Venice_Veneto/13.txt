This past week I returned from a long weekend stay in Venice at the Gritti Palace.  It was exactly what I needed and so much more.  Truth be told, I never thought I'd visit Venice as I can't handle crowds.  It is rather sad that it took a pandemic to make me want to visit and after doing so, I can understand the angst locals have regarding tourism.  Visiting now was nothing short of magical....  The staff at the Gritti Palace were top notch and the room we stayed in was stunningly beautiful (Landmark Grand Canal View).  In these rather uncertain times, I can attest that I felt safe and secure during my entire stay at the Gritti Palace.
