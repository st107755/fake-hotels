Since retirement nearly twenty years ago we do not routinely stay in five star hotels, however a British Airways package for four nights in Venice was too tempting to resist - especially staying at the Gritti Palace.  We were booked into the lowest grade double room, the Venetian, which although not large and without any outlook was well equipped with elegant and well designed storage and the bathroom, tiled in beautiful marble, was well proportioned with an excellent full length bath to soak in.   The decor was, of course, tasteful Venetian with glass chandelier, many wall lights, long antique mirrors and a very comfortable bed.
