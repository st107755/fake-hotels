Rooms were otherwise well draped and chandeliared, Lobby was beautifully marbled, functional and clean. Earnest Hemingway memorabilia fills another library type lounge room. I did not get a novel idea sitting there ;-(  For breakfast, we chose to try different local bakeries every morning, as there are too many wonderful options.
