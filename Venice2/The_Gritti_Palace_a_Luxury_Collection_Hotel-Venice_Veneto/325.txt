When in Venice, do as the Venetian do - celebrate the good life - and you can certainly do that in this lovely palace. You can have a coffee on the outside deck (if the weather cooperates) and then easily explore the city from this well situated hotel. It is an easy walk to Dorsoduro or the Piazza San Marco. The service was exceptional and they will gladly provide as much or as little assistance as you may choose during your stay. The breakfast room is bright and well appointed offering both buffet or selections off a menu. You may be tempted to simply stay in the hotel and enjoy your suite with beautiful Murano glass chandelier or lounge in the sumptuous library or enjoy aperitifs in the comfortable bar overlooking the Grand Canal.
