We were at the Gritti from November 22nd to November 25th 2013.   The hotel interior and exterior is beautiful. The facilities are what you expect in a hotel of this standard. The staff are wonderful and go out of their way to make your stay memorable.   The breakfast buffet as well as a la carte menu is appropriate for the class of hotel, and again, the staff will try and accommodate any requests.   We were a little bit disappointed by the Restaurant Club Del Dodge the first night we ate there. The atmosphere was fantastic but the food was not exceptional. At the end of the second course, the waiter asked if we enjoyed the meal and my husband made a very, very light comment that the steak was not really good, it felt like it had been boiled.
