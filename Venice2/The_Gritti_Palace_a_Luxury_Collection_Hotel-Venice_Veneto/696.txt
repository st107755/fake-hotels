We had an excellent time at this luxury hotel in the busy centre of venice. Unforgettable are the spectacular views from the hotel's terrace on the morning where you can enjoy a delicious breakfast buffet while barges, gondolieri and vaporettos just seem to navigate around you - we still wonder how they avoid collisions while moving in at least 5-6 parallel lanes and sprinting out of side channels seemingly without watching any other traffic. And at times with less traffic (which happens as well), you can enjoy the splendid view across the channel onto Basilica di Santa Maria della Salute and all the wonderful palaces nearby.
