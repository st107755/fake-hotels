My wife and I stayed at the Gritti Palace for a few days. Overall, we would recommend it highly. The location (near St. Mark's Square) is great and the staff is very helpful. A few more specific notes.  The Gritti Palace is a quite old hotel. However, it was extensively and successfully modernized a few years old. As a consequence, the hotel amenities are very up-to-date.   A good example is air conditioning. Many hotels and other facilities in Europe purport to have air conditioning. In reality, they don't. By contrast, the Gritti Palace has excellent AC, which is quite welcome after the heat of Venice in the summer.
