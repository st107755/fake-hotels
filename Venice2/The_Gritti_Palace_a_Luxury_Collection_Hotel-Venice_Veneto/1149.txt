While expensive, the Gritti Palace is an amazing place. First the location. It is not on the busiest, and noisiest, part of the Grand Canal as are other Luxury hotels.  Quite the contrary. One can sit outside and dine and look at the fascinating canal traffic all day, and not have to be offended by looking at or hearing the din from the many thousands of wandering, mega cruise ship tourists who have invaded and ruined the charm and beauty of "mainland" Venice in recent years. Second the accommodations in the newly renovated Luxury hotel are simply gorgeous. In both the public and private area.
