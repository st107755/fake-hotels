Outstanding sumptuousness This hotel really does deserve the 5* reviews. It is perfectly located on the Grand Canal, has opulent and exquisite decor throughout and exceptionally attentive staff. The service here is exceptional, as are the views. We didn't have a room overlooking the canal, but ours was still gorgeous with antique paintings, a luxurious marble bathroom and such a comfortable bed. Despite the antiques here, the technology available is bang up to date with a large TV in the rooms and a good range of different language options. I was particularly impressed with the complimentary bottle of wine, chocolates and two bottles of water that were replenished each day (the water that is!) according to our preferences (i.e, fizzy or still as per your requirements.)
