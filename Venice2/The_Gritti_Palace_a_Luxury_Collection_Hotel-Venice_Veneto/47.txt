I don’t think I can ever stay in any other hotel in the world and be satisfied after my stay at the Gritti Palace!  From the moment we arrived to the moment we departed we felt like royalty.  We recently stayed for 5 nites and it was the ultimate in luxury.  We arrived by boat and were met at the dock. One person escorted us to the check-in desk and another took our luggage.  Check-in was easy, smooth and friendly. We were then escorted to our room, the gentleman filled us in on the property and the area and showed us around our room. Our luggage arrived shortly after.  The TV in the room greeted us with our name scrolling across the top and we had a bottle of complimentary wine awaiting us.  This is a room you never want to leave. Italian décor at its best!
