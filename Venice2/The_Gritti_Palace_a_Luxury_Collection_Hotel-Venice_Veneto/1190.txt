My wife and I have stayed at the Gritti 3 of the 4 times we have been to Venice over the last 25 years. The only exception was when it was under this last renovation, It has taken me 2 months to think about doing a review as I have mixed feelings.   My first impression is that they upgraded the bathrooms to today's standard of a Luxury hotel and they added a new Bed and Television. And they jacked up the prices double before you even take into consideration of the weak dollar to the Euro. I think this was probably the most expensive hotel that I have stayed in and I have stayed in many top end hotels around the world. I still am having a hard time accepting what i paid to stay there.
