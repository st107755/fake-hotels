My husband who travels for business a great deal and generally does not like hotels, said it was his favorite hotel ever. The staff could not have been more friendly or helpful. Our rooms were beautiful. And the Grand Canal side restaurant and bar - a dream! We stayed on points and were never made to feel any less than a full paying guest. They even upgraded the second room that were arranged for our kids.
