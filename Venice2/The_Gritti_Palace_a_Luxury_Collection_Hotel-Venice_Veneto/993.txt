Think of Venice, and romance and the Gritti has to be included in your dream!  My husband took me there for a surprise Anniversary trip and it was truly divine.  We ate in the restaurant overlooking the canal on the first night and breakfast there each morning bathed in sunshine.  Our room was in the older front section of the hotel and while we didn't pay for the full frontal canal view (£££), we could see the canal from our window.  The bed was divine - big and a softness that just sucked you into the most blissful sleep imaginable.  The linen impeccable and a huge bathroom and dressing area too.
