This being our first real 'just us as a couple' trip for several years, my wife and I were looking for something exceptional.  So we splashed out on the Gritti Palace, and we are utterly and completely delighted that we did so. The memory of our five days there will remain with us until we die: this is one of the best hotels on earth.  So OK, it's not cheap.  In fact it's very expensive (but no more so that the Cipriani, and much cheaper than some of the top-end hotels in London).  And that leads us to our first tip.  Book a package including breakfast: ours did, but we could see people who hadn't thinking twice about having another cup of coffee.
