Having been to the Danieli the Gritti Palace wins hands down in comparison - and its down to the professionalism of the staff. They are attentive without being obtrusive and provide impeccable professional service. Special mention must go to the young waiter from Lecce who was so friendly and engaged with us when we sat out on the terrace in the evening having a couple of drinks and also the two gentleman on the check out desks when we left. Such friendliness doesn't get forgotten.  The hotel itself is gorgeous - a sense of history everywhere with a lovely library and lounge areas. Some of these paintings must cost a fortune.
