I wanted to surprise and delight my wife on our last night in Italy so I booked a room here for one night with my Marriott Bonvoy points. This turned out to be a great value as we were upgraded due to my Marriott status to a Sestieri Suite which had a room rate of 3,000 euros for our night!  The location of this hotel is perfect. It's very close to all the main attractions, but you can easily walk back to the Rialto bridge etc.   The highlight of our stay was the buffet breakfast. We've stayed in some other nice hotels, but the breakfast serving and sitting rooms create the most magical breakfast experience we've ever had.
