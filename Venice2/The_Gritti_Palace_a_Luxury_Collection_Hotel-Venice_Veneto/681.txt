Gritti Palace helped me fall in love with Venice. Concierges arranged private boat transport from the airport- which I highly recommend doing. They meet you as soon as you walk off the plane and whisk you and your luggage into a car and to the dock. The rooms were recently renovated  yet still maintain that old Italian charm. Beautiful carrera marble bathrooms, all Aqua De Parma products (which smell incredible), and the beds are beyond comfortable. Aside from morning cappuccino and a croissant I wasn't in love with the food there. We had lunch our first day on their deck overlooking the Grand Canal which was lovely but if you want a "to die for meal" this isn't the place to have it. It was good though.
