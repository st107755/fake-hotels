My husband and I  stayed at this hotel in November 2019 for 2 nights.  We stayed at the Gritti Palace for our 8th Wedding Anniversary, which was a surprise trip and I only learnt of the hotel upon getting into the Water Taxi to the hotel.   On arrival at the hotel jetty, we were taken to reception to check-in and showed around the hotel, which showed no signs of damage or flooding following the most recent severe Acqua Alta experienced in 50 sum years!  The Gritti is incredibly classic and romantic and luxurious. On arrival to our room, my breath was taken away. We had the most stunning views and balcony over the Grand Canal and the Santa Maria della Salute Basilica.
