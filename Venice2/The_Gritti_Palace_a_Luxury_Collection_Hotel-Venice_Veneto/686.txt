Get to Venice by private water taxi from Marco Polo airport to the jetty of your hotel, it is a highlight of visiting Venice.  Water taxi drivers drive just like other Italian drivers too!  Gritti Palace hotel is beautifully situated just off the entrance to the Grand Canal and has a wonderful terrace on the water which is excellent for just watching things.  Rooms Ok but you have to go for the massively expensive ones to get a canal view.  Service is good, food good and the hotel altogether a very interesting experience with a mass of antiques and paintings including one of the great man himself; if you can judge by his looks I am glad I never had to meet him.
