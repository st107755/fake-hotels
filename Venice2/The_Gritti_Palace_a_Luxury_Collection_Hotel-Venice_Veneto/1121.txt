We spent three nights  at the Gritti at the end of our most recent trip to Europe.  We were fortunate to receive a complimentary room upgrade from a Grand Canal view room to the Peggy Guggenheim Suite which also fronts on the Grand Canal.  To say that this accommodation is over the top would be an understatement.  The front room looks out on the Grand Canal and is beautifully furnished and contains a whole library of art books.  There is a powder room for guests, all marble (of course!) and the nicely furnished bedroom has a king size bed, plenty of closet space, and a writing desk.
