I don't know where to begin but the Gritti Palace is a magical hotel.  We had a great welcome to the hotel and we were upgraded to probably the most beautiful suite we have ever seen!  The Gritti has a real sense of place and everywhere you went at the hotel it was clear you are in Venice.  From the glass, to the art, to the furnishings.. it was all very Venitian! The service at the hotel was flawless.. from recommendations by the concierge to the wonderful service at the Riva Bar...  And speaking of the Riva Bar.. it would be easy to sit here for hours just to enjoy the sights.  There is probably no better view in the entire world.
