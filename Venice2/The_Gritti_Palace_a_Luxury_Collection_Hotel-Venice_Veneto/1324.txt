Great location on the Grand Canal. Great view of the Canal and Santa Maria Basilica. The fact that the restaurant and bar have outdoor seating right on the Grand Canal is a great bonus. I don't think most hotels on the canal offer that, but I'm not sure. Tremendous, first class service from everyone from the "front desk" staff, to the concierges, to the restaurant staff. All as good as it gets. The Gritti doesn't actually have a Front Desk. It's a little better than that. You'll actually sit at a desk when you check in. That's a nice touch that seems to becoming a trend at the best hotels. "Front desk" staff, concierges, and restaurant staff all speak very good English.
