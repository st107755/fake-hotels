First of all you must get a private water taxi transfer to the hotel from the airport. The service is provided by Blitz and includes fast tracking through passport control!  It takes about 45 minutes from collecting luggage to docking at the hotel, well worth it (€245).  We had a room overlooking the grand canal which was lovely and room was a good size with lovely high ceilings. Bathroom products by Aqua de Parma and was a good size too with a raindrop shower and bath.  Room service excellent (recommend the burger or the club sandwich) and the restaurant also lovely with an outside terrace on the Grand Canal. Breakfast had lots of choice with cooked options available. The bar was superb too with a fantastic bar tender.  Gym is small but was never busy when I used and hallway…
