We stayed at the Gritti Palace for two nights in May.  This hotel is unbelievably beautiful and luxurious!  We purchased the 48 hour water bus pass and used the pass to travel from the train station to the hotel.  We entered through the side door of the hotel and I think we surprised the staff.  I believe most of the guests must arrive by water taxi at the front entrance.  The staff could not have moved quicker to assist with our luggage and to check us in.  We used Starwood points for our stay.  In some of the Starwood properties, you are made to feel like a second class citizen if you use points for your stay.
