The Gritti Palace in all its glory definitely is the most romantic and authentic hotel in Venice.  Ambience at the Palace does not disappoint!  The décor in the common areas is magical and historic.   We spent several nights as their guests and they truly made us feel welcome with a personal (by name) greeting each and every time we came through their doors.  Personnel are friendly, accommodating and delightful, from our registration host to the concierge to the bell captains to the restaurant staff.  There was not a request too small to accommodate.  The restaurant was outstanding and delicious; breakfast on the terrace a must, nothing like Venice in the morning!   Celebrating our 40th Anniversary and they saw to every minor detail, making our special occasion just brilliant. …
