         Really, I would give this hotel a 4.5, the big knock here being the inconsistent service.  For location and luxury, the hotel is wonderful.  The location is on the mouth of the Grand Canal and is easy walking distance to many of Venice's attractions.  The rooms are well appointed, comfortable and luxuriously decorated.  Do yourself a favor and try to get a canal view room.  It's worth the splurge! Really it is a great hotel.  But, they can't call themselves 5 star because of the inconsistent service.  Some employees offered great service, and categorically, the younger males and females really stood out in terms of being friendly and helpful while the older males were more standoffish.
