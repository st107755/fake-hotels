We stayed 2 nights here and the room was really beautiful and just the type of accommodations I wanted to experience in Venice. We were upgraded per Platinum status upon check in, personally shown to our room which was beautifully appointed and smelled fantastic. Great bed and the bathroom was by far the most luxurious and clean bathroom I’ve ever had in a hotel. I actually took a bath which I never do in hotels but it looked so clean and relaxing. The hot water was fantastic in the shower and all amenities were promptly restocked. We were given a bottle of red wine and glasses to enjoy in our room during turn down which was a nice touch.
