We flew to Venice from Paris for a return to the unparalleled luxury of The Gritti Palace. This is the height of indulgence but the high price is forgotten because value is well delivered.   We returned to a suite we were in almost two years ago and were pleased to feel that it was even nicer than before. Elegance and old style comfort.   Breakfast on the deck overlooking the canal could, I think, extend one’s lifespan by a year for every morning begun there. If you cannot appreciate the splendor and intricacy of Venice while you are at The Gritti then you simply should not ascend to luxury.   For a special pleasure take the hotel’s Riva for a cruise.
