We just returned from 3 days in Venice for the Carnival. This was our 4th time in Venice. We had stayed at wonderful hotels on our previous trips, but decided to try the Gritti Palace this time around. So happy we did. The location is ideal. The room decor was stunning (427) with lots of space to store clothes and the heated bathroom floor so nice in the morning. There isn't any real view from the windows in this room, which didn't really matter in the end since we were out and about during the day. The staff is very attentive to the guests and the concierges are very professional and helpful. The Spa offered an excellent massage service.
