This ancient hotel is a living piece of history with modern features. Once you step inside, you feel as if you've been transported to old Venice at its best. The furniture and lighting, the floors, and other decor bespeak a sense of privilege and honor. The staff are all attentive. Without hurry or intrusion.they appear ready for almost any request, The food is wonderful, dining on the Grand Canal--morning or evening--is a delight.The Gritti is a wonderful place to begin to understand Venice.
