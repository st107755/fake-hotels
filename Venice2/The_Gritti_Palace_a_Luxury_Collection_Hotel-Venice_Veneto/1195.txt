From arrival by private water taxi to departure by same, this was by far the most gracious, welcoming, and service-focused hotel that we have ever visited. Our room was sumptuous - spacious, airy with high ceilings, appointed with lovely furnishings, all dressed with silk paneled walls, silk drapes, and Murano chandeliers. The closets were finished to the hilt, and there was even a compartmentalized dresser drawer for my bow ties and tuxedo studs. The bathroom is luxuriously finished with marble and mirrors and stainless steel, and everywhere there is the slightest nose of Acqua Di Parma.
