I had never been to Venice before, so we thought we would push the boat out and choose a more expensive hotel in a central location.  On arriving by water taxi at the hotel, the staff were fantastic.  Our bags were whisked away and we were taken to check-in.  The lady then took us to our room, showing us where the restaurant, bar and spa were en route.  We went straight to the Riva Bar for a relaxing glass of chilled white wine and watched the world go by on the Grand Canal for an hour.    Centrally located for St Mark's, taxis, gondolas, restaurants, etc., the location was perfect and we spent five days in glorious sunshine.
