My husband and I stayed at The Gritti Palace for our wedding anniversary in April 2017. We booked our transfers to the hotel via the concierge and were not disappointed; the private boat from the airport was the perfect start to our romantic weekend. We had a spacious room which was clean and very comfortable, as well as a bottle of Prosecco and chocolates waiting for us on arrival.  The weather was fantastic throughout our stay and the hotel is perfectly situated for exploring Venice on foot or by water taxi.  The concierge arranged an anniversary meal for us at the Restaurant Terrazza Danieli, which had an amazing view, but was very expensive.
