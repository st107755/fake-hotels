The Gritti Palace has become my one of my personal favorite hotel in the world, on par with the Four Seasons in Florence.   There is an air of discreet exclusivity about this place, filled with antiques and fine paintings. A fusion of culture and understated luxury.  The marble foyer with its mosaic floors is even regularly perfumed!  The hotel is relatively small, in fact I was told that only 9 rooms overlooked the grand canal. My wife and I were fortunate to stay in one of them.  The room was not very large, with a narrow window. It offered very few storage, bore a small bathroom..
