This hotel encapsulates the idea of Venice and what it means to be in a place which is unique, beautiful and exclusive. The hotel is right next to the Grand Canal and having breakfast in the outside patio while looking at the church and watching the Gondolas is one of the most relaxing things to do. The hotel is walking distance from the Main Plaza and there is a Water Bus stop very close to the hotel.   We were upgraded to a suite which was cozy and had a very Victorian feel to it. The hotel is full of portraits of all the celebrities who have stayed here in the past and there is even a Hemingway suite. Dinner at the hotel was good but Desert was the highlight.
