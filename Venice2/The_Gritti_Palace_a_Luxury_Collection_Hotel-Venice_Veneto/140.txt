The Gritti is one of those celebrated properties that you always read about in travel magazines and dream of visiting at least once. With a enviable pedigree, one of the best locations in Venice and great hotel chain offering backing, we are lucky to see it live up to its very high reputation. The worst thing at the Gritti having to leave because when you leave the Gritti, you feel like you left home.  The most notable thing from our four day stay is the incredible talent the property has. Besides everything that makes the Gritti great, like it’s heritage, it’s location, and it’s incredible suites, the real magic of the Gritti is in its staff! Every single person in the hotel was smiling and welcoming at us at all times and it was a great addition to our stay.  It’s amazing how…
