This was a return trip to Venice for us, having visited many years ago. Being over the Christmas period, and after some considerable research, we opted for the Gritti. Good choice. The hotel arrange the transfer from the airport ( Mercedes & water-taxi) so we arrived in comfort and style. Following a smooth check-in by an efficient concierge service, we settled down to relax. The hotel has classic Venetian style and charm. A beautifully restored palazzo with a stylish bar and riverside terrace, first class restaurant and excellent in-room dining. It is located about five minutes from St. Marks Square and a minute away from a water bus stop (This was convenient as it saved queuing with the St Marks tourists for a bus).
