The Gritti Palace is well-located on the Grand Canal, opposite the Santa Maria della Salute and Centurion Palace hotel, both of which provide nice vantage points when sitting at the GP's restaurant or bar, both located on its canal side terrace.  You really feel like you're getting the "Venice experience" at this place, which once was a private palazzo.  The décor is traditional, and beautiful, and there are Murano chandeliers and antique furniture throughout.    Our room was a Giglio Prestige (an upgrade!) and was large, well-decorated and comfortable.  We didn't have a canal view, but being able to enjoy that view from the hotel's restaurant and bar compensated.
