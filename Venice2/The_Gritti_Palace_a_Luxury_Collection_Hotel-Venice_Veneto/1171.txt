We spent three wonderful nights in August and had a most enjoyable and romantic experience in this classy hotel which is situated in one of Venice's best locations. Our room was immaculately clean and the decor exquisite. The breakfast selection was somewhat repetitive and the evening meal disappointing especially as it cost over £200. When we made the dinner reservation they booked us in an hour earlier than we had requested and then seemed somewhat put out when we turned up at the correct time. Our real gripe was on checking out. Our flight was not until 11pm, but the latest we could check out from our room was by 3pm. We were told that if we wanted to keep our room (even though it had not been sold) until we were due to leave at 8pm, we would have to pay the price for another…
