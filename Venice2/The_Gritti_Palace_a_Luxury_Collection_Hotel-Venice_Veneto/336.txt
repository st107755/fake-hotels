The room was spacious and truly 5 Star, as was every other aspect of The Gritti Palace. My Wife received a dozen red roses and chocolates on her Birthday which shows the staff attention to the finer details.  Every single member of staff were friendly, courteous, knowledgeable and assisted us with our every enquiry. Breakfast was amazing, we recommend ordering the poached eggs!
