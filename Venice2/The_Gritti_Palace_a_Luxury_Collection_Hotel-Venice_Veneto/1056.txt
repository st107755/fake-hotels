Our three day stay at the Gritti Palace in the first days of March was a joy from start to finish. The hotel was recently renovated and, while sumptuously decorated, does not feel austere or overly grand. The staff have a large part to play in this atmosphere of warmth and understated elegance. We found them to be helpful, professional and generous with their knowledge of the city. Every detail has been considered, from the linens to the bath toiletries (Acqua di Parma), opulent flowers in the lobby and the breakfast buffet which is exquisite in variety and quality. The only thing I would have changed is for the television in the room to be closed in a cupboard. The room was otherwise perfect.
