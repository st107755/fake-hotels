My husband and I stayed at the Gritti Palace for one night in December. From the check-in experience to the concierge service - everything was perfect. We even had free bottle of wine and some chocolates waiting for us in our room when we arrived. The room had a very tasteful decor. The Acqua di Parma products were wonderful. Breakfast was excellent. Unfortunately the weather was too cold, so we were not able to eat on the terrace, but I'm sure on a warm day it would be lovely. The hotel was also rather convenient to reach from the train station. We walked for about 20 minutes. They have water taxis etc. in Venice, but unless you have a lot of luggage, you can easily walk to places in Venice. One traveller advice: Ask what concierge what services they offer.
