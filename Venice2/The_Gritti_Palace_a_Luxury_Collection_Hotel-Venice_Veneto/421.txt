When in Venice we usually stay at one oft the Luxury Collection hotels, the Gritti, the Westin (Europa&Regina), and the Danieli. This time it was the Gritti, and again it was a true pleasure. Since I wrote a detailed review on this excellent hotel in June 2014 (“A Venetian landmark restored to its splendour”), I would only repeat myself by going into details. Arriving by boat, the reception was friendly as always, with the headwaiter from the terrace restaurant running to shake hands. As a Starwood member we received our due upgrade, however right to the top into one of their fantastic suites with antique furniture, oriental rugs, marble bathroom, and Venetian crystal chandeliers.
