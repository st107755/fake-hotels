My husband and I treated ourselves to this 5-star hotel on the Grand Canal for our first trip to Venice. The concierge helped us with restaurant and tour reservations before our trip. When we arrived, we received a complimentary upgrade to a canal-view room, which made lingering in the hotel a pleasure. They also provided us with a gift of fresh fruit, bottled water, and Prosecco. Our room had  a walk-in shower and a soaking tub. The hotel couldn't be more conveniently located, within walking distance of San Marco Square, the Rialto bridge, and many shops and restaurants. There is free WiFi, and a beautiful breakfast buffet--overlooking the canal--is included. One hallway is decorated with framed photos of the many celebrities who have stayed there.
