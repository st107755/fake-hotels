The Gritti Palace provides a discreet and comfortable hideaway right in the centre of the San Marco area. It's location behind the luxury stores right on the Grand Canal is hard to beat. Slipping into the hotel through the unassuming side door provides escape into an immediate sanctuary from the crowds nearby at Piazza San Marco. Service is efficient and courteous. The Venetian rooms are comfortable.   Special mention must be given to Rooms Manager Pedro Perestrelo who went out of his way to provide a friendly and exceptional level of service while I was waiting to check in at reception.
