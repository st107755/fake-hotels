As a special occasion for our 20th wedding anniversary, I chose the Gritti Palace. That was a great choice as I fully enjoyed our stay at this prestigious address in Venice. The welcome and treatment received by all management and staff throughout the 5 days was superb and fully justified my choice.  From the outside via the Piazza Santa Maria del Giglio the entrance is not indicative or impressive for a luxury collection hotel. One enters through a rather small not ostentatious door. From the Canale Grande you enter via a floating dock. Once inside the historic proportions of the palace convey being in a special place.
