Firstly, let me say we were thoroughly impressed with the Gritti and try as I may, I really cannot fault it nor do I really want to!). This weekend was to celebrate my husband's birthday as a surprise. He is a man of few words but even he said it was "awesome!"  We treated ourselves to the water taxi service form the airport and it was lovely to arrive in style. We stood at the back of the taxi observing everything and thoroughly enjoying this special method of arrival. Landing, our bags were swept away and we were warmly greeted and shown to reception (not your usual reception). We had room 206 which was beautiful and overlooked a side canal leading into the Grand Canal.
