Visiting Venice is truly an amazing experience and one that is up top 5 for the bucket list! A short getaway to celebrate our 16th wedding anniversary truly made for a liberating and an awe inspiring experience for both of us.   Our choice to stay at the luxurious Gritti Palace undeniably enhanced this visit with its wonderful location, ornate fittings and a true charming professional service.   Arriving at the hotel in the water taxi from the airport at its own landing pier kick started our stay. We had a wonderful room recently renovated we were told and in its own Venetian decor that appealed to us. The Acqua Di Palma toiletries enhanced the luxurious feel to the room.
