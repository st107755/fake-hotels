We stayed for 3 nights in October at The Gritti Palace, celebrating our 20th wedding anniversary and it was amazing, as always!  The Gritti Palace has recently been renovated and to a very high standard.  Each room has been  luxuriously refurbished with the walls covered in Venetian silk and the bathroom floor and walls in beautiful Italian marble.  Added to this, each bathroom now has a walk-in shower, bath and twin sinks.  It is fully stocked with luxurious bath/shower cosmetics too - just what you'd expect from a grand hotel.  The breakfast is delicious with plenty of healthy options and you can order anything you want and the chef will cook it for you.  They cater for every nationality, as you would expect.
