My wife and I spent four days, including New Years Eve, at the Gritti Palace in Venise. it's probably the sixth or seventh time that we come here and we always found the experience outstanding. Of course, the location on the Grand Canal just across La Salute is exceptional, and this doesn't change over the years. Arriving by taxiboat to the private dock is a feast in itself. The Gritti was already a beautifully decorated place before the major renovation a few years ago. It is now absolutely superb: they kept the charm and historic feeling while adding all the modern conveniences.
