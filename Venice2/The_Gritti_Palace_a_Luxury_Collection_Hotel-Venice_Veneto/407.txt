I find it difficult to review the best of the best. If you want to feel like some sort of royalty, the Gritti Palace is definitely for you.   We entered our room to find complimentary water and a (great) bottle of wine. The rooms were renovated but still had an enchanting baroque vibe to them. My favorite part was definitely the all marble bathroom - probably the most beautiful bathroom I've ever seen.   From what I've been seeing, the rooms can be priced from USD 700- 1,140 a night, and maybe more in high tourist season. To be completely honest, I would not have spent this money on the experience.
