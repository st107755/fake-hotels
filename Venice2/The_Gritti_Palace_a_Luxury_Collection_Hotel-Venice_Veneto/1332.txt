This hotel is worthy of many superlatives but to the uninitiated it is in the best position of the "top" hotels in Venice - not on an island outside the main city, not too near the tourist traps, and on the Grand Canal, but not too far up, not too far down.  Goldilocks would like it here!  It is galactically expensive of course, particularly if you opt for a Canal view room, but then you cannot wake up to that view anywhere else and it is Venice of course!  Once you get past the number and commit to staying then the room quality, service standards, location and general smoothness of it all make it pretty good value!
