A historic residence which went through a remarkable restoration to it's former glory, I am so happy we chose to stay at the Gritti Palace as it was the perfect place to start our Venice experience! As soon as you enter the quaint lobby to check in you know you are staying somewhere special!  Waking up looking out to the canal and seeing the gondoliers starting their day was magical. We enjoyed both breakfast and early evening aperitifs on their  beautiful floating canalside restaurant and it was so fun to people watch. Our suite was just spectacular, with particular mention to the stunning marble bathroom! So many pieces of art and history on the walls in the suite and throughout the hotel.
