Everything about the hotel was wonderful. The Gritti is a true Venetian experience, and is the best thing about Venice. The hotel is right on the Grand Canal, across the picture-perfect Basilica di Santa Maria della Salute. The new Riva lounge is a must-do for lunch and pre-dinner drinks.  The staff is wonderful, and I would like to mention Marcello at the concierge desk; he was the best ever, went out of his way to help us with arrangements for the water taxi to Piazzale Roma, called our car service repeatedly to ensure we were met on time. The most charming man. I would also like to say a special thank you to the very efficient and attentive Club del Doge staff, and the men at the hotel dock, who could not have been more helpful.
