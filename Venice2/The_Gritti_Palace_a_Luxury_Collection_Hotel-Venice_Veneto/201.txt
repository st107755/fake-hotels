We stayed in Venice for a couple of nights at the end of our 20th anniversary trip, and chose the Gritti Palace because of its classic status as one of the great palazzo hotels of Venice. Obviously the location is wonderful, and our room was absolutely glorious-- we had a very large, beautifully appointed room on the first (what Americans would call the second) floor-- with a view of the neighboring square, high ceilings, and a huge bathroom and entry hall. As befits a palace hotel in Venice, the bar (Bar Longhi) was wonderful, with an excellent cocktail and wine selection, and truly excellent barmen-- the older, head barman was fantastic (he knew our room number and preferences after our first visit), and the younger staff are well on their way.
