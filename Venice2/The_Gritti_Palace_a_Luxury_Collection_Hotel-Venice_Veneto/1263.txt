We were lucky enough to choose this hotel when staying in Venice for our silver wedding anniversary. We choose it through reviews and Trip advisor as we wanted somewhere special and were not disappointed. From the private water taxi dropping us off from the Grand Canal to being greeted by hotel staff we felt like we were in a movie set! It was fantastic. The room was beautiful and the Aqua de Parma products were abundant, changed twice daily. I had never come across bath towels that size and wouldn't want to be washing them back home. Breakfast was perfect and the view was to die for with the gondola, water taxis and boats passing up and down the canal.
