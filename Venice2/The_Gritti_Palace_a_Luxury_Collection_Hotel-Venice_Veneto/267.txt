My partner and I stayed at the Gritti Palace for five nights during a return trip to Venice on the Orient Express.  The hotel has been beautifully restored and is perfectly located on the best part of the Grand Canal.  Breakfast on the terrace overlooking the Grand Canal is wonderful.  Our room (a Giglio Prestige Room) was just lovely.  Other than breakfast we didn’t use the restaurant or the bar, so can’t comment on those.  We did however, make use of the hotel private yacht to have a tour of the Islands of Murano and Burano, and this was a really magical afternoon.    The only thing stopping this being a five-star review was a really upsetting incident with one of the concierges.
