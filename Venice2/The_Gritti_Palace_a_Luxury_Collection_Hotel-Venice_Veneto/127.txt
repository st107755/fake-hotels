We had a fabulous stay at the Gritti for my birthday weekend in February – and first day of Carnivale. The hotel is absolutely gorgeous and over the top in terms of décor – mirrors, detail, molding, fabrics, etc. For us, it was an “Italian dream” and this is one of my favorite hotel rooms in all of Italy and that is saying a lot because we had NO view at the Gritti. We didn’t even have the lowest category room but our room overlooked low rooftops and another building (apparently only a handful of rooms in the hotel have a view). From the beautiful bedding to the thick floor to ceiling fortuny curtains, to the generous closet space and the well thought out details – like USB adapters at the bedside.
