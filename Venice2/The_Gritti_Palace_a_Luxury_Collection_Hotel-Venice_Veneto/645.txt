Let me first start with the negative and say that which is bordering on unbelievable if you have not been to Venice; Venice is not that nice! I would not have believed it myself but the city is run down, full of garbage on the streets, hordes of day-trippers particularly massive amount of group tourists from parts of Asia who make navigating the extremely small streets, no more than alleyways, very very difficult. There are rats larger than cats at night. Many of the buildings are falling apart and are in severe disrepair and many seemed abandoned altogether. It is truly sad. Obviously it is a city that needs to be seen but 2 days is sufficient. In addition, everything and I mean everything is extraordinarily expensive.
