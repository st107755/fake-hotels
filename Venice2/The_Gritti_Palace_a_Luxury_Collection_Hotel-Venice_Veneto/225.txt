We stayed here on a family vacation.   The rooms are beautiful.   The entire hotel was restored in 2013.  If you care about newer rooms, especially given the natural decay of Venice, then this is a good choice.  We viewed 4 rooms before making our choice. Each was immaculate.   The entry level “Venice rooms” are small but like a jewel box.   The location is lovely.  It is a 10 min walk to st marks square.  You are on a canal and breakfast overlooking the canal is lovely.   We compared this to the other Starwood property- Hotel Danieli which was run down and old bathrooms. ( although the staff was lovely in both locations).  Danieli is right on st marks square.
