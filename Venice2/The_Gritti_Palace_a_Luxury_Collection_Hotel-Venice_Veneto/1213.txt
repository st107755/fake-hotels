We booked this hotel for a wedding anniversary in June. From the correspondence prior to our stay to the end of the week, we could not fault it. Having visited Venice previously, rather than walk to the  speedboat taxi, we arranged for the hotel to meet us at the airport with a short taxi ride to our speed boat taxi, which, of course took us to the door of the hotel on the Grand Canal. Checking in was easy and our luggage appeared in our room straight from the airport. Our room was beautiful - no view, but nonetheless stunningly decorated with a beautiful Italian marble bathroom and fabulous toiletries.
