While we only had the chance to spend two nights, our stay at the Gritti Palace was possibly perfect. We were warmly greeted, and it seemed as if the staff knew who we were even as they helped us disembark from the water taxi. Checking in was a breeze, and we were quickly escorted to our suite, despite minor computer snafus. As SPG Gold members, we were upgraded to a 1 bedroom suite with a view of a small canal. I admit I was a bit worried about sounds and smells, but neither was an issue.  We were greeted with a complimentary bottle of wine and a small box of chocolates. The room was well stocked with other beverages, too. The staff went out of their way to be helpful and their suggestions/recommendations were spot on.
