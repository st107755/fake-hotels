My wife and I stayed at the Gritti Palace for 3 nights in October 2013. We have previously stayed at the Hotel Danielli and also the San Clemente Palace and visited the Cipriani.  Without doubt this is our favourite. The hotel has recently been completely renovated and the good thing about it is that it does not really show. That is just because it has been done so well and to such a high standard that it looks like it was always this way.  The location is brilliant - right on the Grand Canal but away from the hordes that gather around St Marks Square and the Danielli.  The service is quietly efficient and the terrace overlooking the Grand canal where we had breakfast and evening drinks and dinner is just wonderful.  This is understated luxury at its best.
