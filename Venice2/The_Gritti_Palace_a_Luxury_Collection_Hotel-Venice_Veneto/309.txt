If you choose to stay in Venice stay here...  The hotel is beautiful perfectly located opposite the Santa Maria Church with the best views in Venice! Also, you can walk to locations very easily!  The best asset is the staff. We were looked after so well by Massimo on arrival. Ivan who is the best concierge is so polite and helpful he will endeavour to get you the best restaurant and all his recommendations were very good! In the bar we were looked after by Andrea who was very attentive and polite.  The rooms are stunning especially the Venetian Rooms!   Hands down one of the best hotels I’ve stayed in!   Recommendations: Try and get a table at La Zucca and then walk back to the hotel it’s worth it! Also go for a drink at Harry’s Bar and try their famous Bellini!   We will…
