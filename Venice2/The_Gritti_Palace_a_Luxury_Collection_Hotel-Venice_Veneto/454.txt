My wife and I came to Venice for a 3 day stay to celebrate a 'big' anniversary. We had not visited Venice together before. The Gritti Palace proved to be a magical hotel selection: arriving down the Grand Canal by river taxi, we were warmly welcomed on arrival, a presage of impeccable service throughout our stay. The hotel is a glorious 500 year old building, beautifully decorated and maintained and perfectly situated on the Grand Canal, within walking distance of most of Venice's top tourist attractions, restaurants and shopping streets. Our room was sumptuous. We booked a canal-facing room and despite the extra cost, we were absolutely delighted and really felt it was worth it.
