This was our first visit to Venice and we chose The Gritti Palace after reading so many fantastic reviews about it - of course, we were a little apprehensive as we were visiting with such high expectations after all the reviews, but The Gritti Palace did not disappoint. We arrived by water taxi and were immediately greeted by a member of staff as we disembarked our taxi who then took all of our luggage and walked us to a private check-in area. We were then introduced to the lady who would be checking us in and she ran through all of the details with us, showed us around the hotel before walking us to our STUNNING room (a Giglio Prestige Canal View room).
