Visited Venice for a birthday celebration and had a brilliant time.  Stayed in the Gritti Palace Hotel and really enjoyed all aspects of the hotel.  It has a very luxurious old fashioned feel, but is totally up to date in terms of facilities.  Our room was large with a modern bathroom with lovely toiletries and lots of towels.  Our room looked out onto the side street so was very airy and bright.   Breakfast was very enjoyable sitting out on the terrace looking onto the Grand Canal.  There was always something going on to watch and enjoy.  One morning a tour of four gondolas sailed past with a tenor on board singing, it was very atmospheric.
