First let me say that if I was rating this hotel based on location alone, I would give it 5 stars, however, this review is based on my overall experience during my stay.  I had very high expectations for this hotel based on reviews I have read. Upon arrival, check in went like usual. Was not offered a warm towel or even a drink to welcome us to the hotel. Strange! This had never happened to us before in any luxury hotels!  We decided to wait in the lobby until our room was ready. The view from the terrace restaurant was fantastic, absolutely loved it! However, it’s the only common area to get a view from. Gritti Palace should really consider converting the terrace used for the Redentore Suite into a common area lounge/bar for all guests.
