This hotel is fantastic, the staff are friendly and pleasant and the location is perfect (close to San Marco square). We were lucky enough to be upgraded to a suite on check in and this was lovely. Whilst we didn’t look out of the front of the hotel over the Grand Canal, we did have a side canal view which was great (picture attached).  We ate in the hotel one night and the waiters were extremely helpful with giving us recommendations on what to order which was gratefully received.  The breakfast was excellent with a wide variety of continental buffet items and you also had the choice of several hot breakfast items freshly prepared to order if you wanted.
