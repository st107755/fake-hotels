So many positives to report about my recent four night stay at The Gritti Palace.  Firstly, communication prior to arrival was really efficient and helpful.  We booked the arrival package to transfer from Marco Polo airport to the hotel and it could not have been easier.  A brief mini bus ride after being collected in person in Arrivals Hall, straight to the quay side to get on board the private launch.  20 minutes across the lagoon and arrival at the hotel's dock on the Grand Canal.    The room was a Prestige Giglio example. Very comfortable with beautiful furnishings, and a marble bathroom with Aqua di Parma toiletries.  Overlooked the square and the Grand Canal.
