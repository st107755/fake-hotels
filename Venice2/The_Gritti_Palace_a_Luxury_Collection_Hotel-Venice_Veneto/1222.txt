My wife and I visited Venice for the first time at the end of April.  We chose the Gritti Palace as it had rave reviews on here.  The reviews do not do this excellent hotel justice!  From the minute we checked in, the staff did their best to make us feel welcome.  Nothing was too much trouble for them, they even helped us print off our boarding passes for our return flight home.   The views are incredible (try and have breakfast outside, weather permitting, there is nothing like it) The room was gorgeous and the welcome bottle of wine is a nice touch.    I have to praise the gentleman who booked us in (I do him a dis-service by forgetting his name) but he patiently talked us through everything and looked genuinely pleased to be doing so.
