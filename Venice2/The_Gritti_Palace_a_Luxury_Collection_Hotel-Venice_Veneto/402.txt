We spent the last three nights of our honeymoon (and my birthday) at the Gritti Palace.   Things we loved: The location was fantastic and we were a hop and a skip away from ten best shopping and Saint Mark's square. The room and bathroom were beautifully decorated and clean. The view from the restaurant is in my opinion the best in Venice. The staff were very friendly and helpful and let me use the spa showers on check out day as we had a late flight and spent the day exploring Venice.  Things that could be improved: I didn't think the food was great at the restaurant. Not compared to some of the other places we ate at the same price point.
