We were celebrating our Anniversary and Second Honeymoon. After reading about Gritti, I changed our trip dates for when there was availability here in our price range (lower range). The hotel is amazing--the service is impeccable. Our room was small but perfectly outfitted and came with a view of the Grand Canal! There were fresh flowers, sparkling wine and fruit waiting for us for our celebration.  The room was gorgeous and the bath looked newly remodeled with great shower pressure. I absolutely loved it. Although it was May, it was unseasonably rainy and chilly, however the Gritti Concierge gave us umbrellas to walk around with--no room number or deposit needed.
