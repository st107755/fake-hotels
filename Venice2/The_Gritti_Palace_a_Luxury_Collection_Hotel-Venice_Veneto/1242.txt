We spent three nights at the Gritti Palace and could not have been more delighted with our choice. All staff were extremely friendly and helpful and whilst a five star hotel it was not in the slightest bit stuffy. Our rooms were serviced twice daily and we had an abundance of towels and goodies in the modern bathrooms. It was very easy and good fun to travel by water taxi from the airport directly to the hotel. Breakfast was delicious and all staff spoke good English and made us feel very welcome. I would recommend sitting out on the terrace by the canal and watching the very busy waterway.
