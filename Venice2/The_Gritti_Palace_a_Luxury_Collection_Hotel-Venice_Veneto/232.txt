This was our second extended stay at the Gritti Palace. Once again Paolo Lorenzoni and his staff proved how well the Gritti is organized and know how to offer warm and personal service. We had arrived early in the morning and were offered complimentary breakfast following the check-in. Relaxed and well rested we were then able to explore Venice. Upon our return we were advised that we had been upgraded and were guided to our suite on the second floor where a chilled bottle of Prosecco awaited our arrival. The Gritti Palace truly deserves the denomination luxury collection. In itself it can be considered one of the sights to see in Venice. Even the local Venetians consider it the “top”. The scenery during meals on the floating dock on the Canale Grande make for an unforgettable…
