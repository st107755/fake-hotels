The Gritti Palace is - without any doubt - one of the best and probably the best hotel I ever stayed in my life (till now). This is not a hotel but indeed a palace where you feel yourself king (duce) of Venice. It has everything you can dream of: the location is superb (just in the middel of the center at the main canal), the terrace is astonishing (what a view), the interior decoration is like a museum, the breakfast is enormous, the restaurant/food can't be better and the staff is highly professional but still friendly and giving the guest maximum personal attention. Okay, the room is good but not a palace - but that doesn't matter in relation to all the other fantastic aspect of this hotel.
