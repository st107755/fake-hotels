Our recent trip to Venice included a stay at the Gritti Palace. It was truly disappointing. Only 1, much smaller than expected room was ready, so we had lunch on the patio. To call the food ridiculously expensive is an understatement. A medium sized bottle of water is 10 euros. The Hemingway burger was more like meat loaf. Even though there are awnings, it was still uncomfortably hot. The rooms were still not ready after lunch, so we walked around the city. When we returned after the promised time, we were told yet again the rooms were unavailable, so we requested to speak to the manager.
