The best thing about this hotel is its location. It is set on the waterfront so that at any time of the day you can relax outside and be entertained by the constantly changing activities of the canals. Then when you decide to explore you are a short stroll to St Marks Square without any of the downside of being too close. The bedrooms are not particularly big and they don't all have a view. They are, however, very ornate and have all that you need. Bathroom is pink marble from floor to ceiling with lovely big showerhead. The breakfasts were excellent with a buffet of quality fruit, meats/cheeses, breads/croissants, as well as tasty hot dishes from the menu. Staff were attentive and spoke good English.
