Arrived by water taxi (60 euros) from the train station and was immediately met by the staff on the dock. We arrived around 10am and room was ready in about an hour. As a platinum we were upgraded to a side canal view and were able to hear and see all the action below. Also we chose the breakfast amenity and it was incredible. Might be one of the best views for breakfast in the world. All staff we encountered were friendly and informative. The room was on the smaller side but it was beautifully furnished and had a large bathroom. There was no place to really put our 2 suitcases. Not that we watched much TV, only about 4 channels in English. Location is perfect away from the crazy mob scene of St Marks but only a 10 minute walk to it. Very peaceful.
