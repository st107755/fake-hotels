Federica welcomed us very warmly, and every single person on staff we met after that was very helpful and extremely nice to us. We had booked 2 different types of rooms during our stay (we wanted to experience as much as we could!) - and not only was there complimentary champagne to greet us both times we entered a new type of room, but we were also very generously given an upgrade to the peggy guggenheim suite which was absolutely beautiful - SO beautiful that we didn't want to leave the room.
