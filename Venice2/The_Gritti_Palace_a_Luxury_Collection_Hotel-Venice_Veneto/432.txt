This was our first hotel during our 2 week trip to Italy with our son and daughter-in-law, and the one against which all others were compared. The hotel is elegant and perfectly located. We were upgraded to canal view rooms and enjoyed watching the gondolas go by outside our window. The rooms were beautifully furnished and the bathroom toiletries were heavenly. We enjoyed starting each day with the amazing breakfast which is served on the terrace overlooking the Grand Canal. We arranged our transfers (from airport and to train) through the hotel, and the service was outstanding – well worth the money and much better than any of the transfers we arranged through our other hotels.
