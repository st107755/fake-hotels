We've stayed at the best hotels in the world, and the Gritti certainly looks the part, but falls short when it comes to service where it does not compete with the world's top 5 stars.   The hotel's best asset is its location, beauty and decor. Things started well with a wonderfully elegant check-in lady who escorted us to our Patron suite. They were kind enough to give us our requested Punta Dolgana suite but I will note that while the website says all of these suites have 1.5 bathrooms, this particular suite does not have the extra powder room. Nonetheless, the suite is stupendously gorgeous with real antiques, exquisite decor, a gorgeous canal view with Juliet balconies and marble clad bathroom.
