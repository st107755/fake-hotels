The hotel really is stunning and in a perfect location on the Grand Canal, just minutes walk away from Piazza San Marco. The room and bathroom were incredible, decorated in true Venetian style without being "blingy" for lack of a better word. I would also highly recommend the hotel restaurant, Club Del Doge, for an evening meal. Moreover, the staff couldn't be more helpful. To evidence this, at 7pm on Saturday night, I asked the front desk to put rose petals on our bed for our return from dinner. They told me they would see what they could do but the florist was closed and it might be impossible. Not only did they manage it, they didn't charge me for the inconvenience.
