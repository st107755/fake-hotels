The hotel was recently refurbished. It is truly magnificent  The service is excellent. The Concierge Staff was extremely helpful.  The room we stayed in did not have a view but it was nicely appointed and extremely comfortable. Sitting outside on the terrace with its magnificent view of the Grand Canal and watching the Gondola's pass by was an incredible experience.
