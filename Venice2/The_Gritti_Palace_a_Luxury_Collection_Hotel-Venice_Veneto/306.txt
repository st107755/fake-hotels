Overall, this was a very solid hotel. Keep in mind this is Venice- a truly historic city- so no matter what, the building exterior will always show it’s age. They’ve tried to keep the original floors throughout. It’s pretty unfair to say this isn’t a luxury hotel. The rooms are very beautiful.  Service was excellent- they anticipate your needs so you don’t have to ask for much. Coffee was very good here and they have soy milk and gluten free food.  They have excellent champagne by the glass and they even let us take champagne with us to our gondola ride.  One thing I didn’t like was the lack of attentive to detail when we booked dinner at the restaurant one night.
