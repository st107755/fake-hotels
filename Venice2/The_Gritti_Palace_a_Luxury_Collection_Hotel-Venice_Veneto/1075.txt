Cannot fault this property.  I arrived on a water taxi from the airport (€95) to the private jetty and was shown to the bar where my partner was waiting.    Everything about our two-night stay was excellent.  Service was absolutely immaculate, friendly but relaxed.  Dinner at the restaurant was excellent although not cheap (great wine advice and knowledge).  Breakfast great - good buffet with fantastic fruit and pastries, plus standard hot options.  The bar mixes a great Negroni.  The concierge was very helpful.  And the room... well we were upgraded to one of the Patron Corner Suites - the John Ruskin Suite - which normally goes for €4500/night.
