We have recently returned from 4 nights at the Gritti Palace and have had the most wonderful time.  The staff could not be more attentive and friendly – we were always greeted by our names whenever we came in and this personal service was rather special.  The hotel is a delight – you have to accept that it is in the Venetian palatial style, which may not be to everyone’s taste, but it is beautifully executed and takes you to another world of years gone by both in service and style.  It is also in an ideal position for exploring Venice to the full, and the concierge staff are very knowledgeable on what to do and where to dine.
