My wife and I have fond memories of this beautiful hotel, having had our wedding celebrations there four years ago.  As such, we were saddened that the hotel closed and were greatly gladdened to learn that it has reopened in the last few weeks. We visited this last weekend.  The hotel is undergoing a "soft opening" period.  Clearly, the staff weren't quite up to speed and a lot of work is being done before receiving any volume of guests.  However, the property remains as beautiful as ever and we can't wait to return now that the hotel is run by the St Regis group. Prices for rooms seemed to be a great deal than they were before the closure and I hope that this will be addressed in time.
