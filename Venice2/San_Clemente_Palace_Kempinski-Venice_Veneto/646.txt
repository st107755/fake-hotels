The hotel is a beautiful and romantic oasis 10 minutes away from the excitement of San Marco and the tourist centre of Venice.  We arrived in a high speed water taxi direct from San Marco airport which in itself is an adventure and were welcomed on the jetty by the hotel staff who helped us off the boat and directed us to reception.  The hotel stands in beautiful gardens and there is a swimming pool and tennis courts. The interior is very impressive and the room we had was delightful with everything that you could want as a facility in a hotel. The décor was excellent.
