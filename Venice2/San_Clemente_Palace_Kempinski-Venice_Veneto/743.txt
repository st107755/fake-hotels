Porters and the Concierge on hand at the pier to greet us set the tone for a purely pampered stay, and as SPG Gold members, we were treated to a complimentary upgrade at check-in.  Breakfast in both the buffet and outdoor restaurant included Mimosa cocktails, juices and coffees and every form of hot and cold treats one needs to start the day in lovely Venezia!
