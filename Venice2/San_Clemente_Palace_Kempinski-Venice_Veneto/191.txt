This is a beautiful hotel with friendly, helpful staff. We were celebrating our wedding anniversary and very kindly were given a room upgrade. The hotel is large with beautiful rooms, gardens and public areas. The drinks in the bar are expensive but that’s to be expected in a 5 star hotel. The cocktails need work, my husband asked for a whiskey sour if it had egg white, when it was served it was basically a whiskey and orange so when questioned the bar man said he had forgotten the egg white. When it was returned an egg white had been tipped in so had settled at the bottom of the glass. It wasn’t possible to drink so we sent it back again. Not good for such a bar - cocktails should be amazing and not served undrinkable.    We had booked a celebration meal in the fine dining…
