I stayed at a grand deluxe room which was very well sized with very high ceilings.  All the staff were friendly with smiles on their faces.  The boat transfer to and from St. Marks square was fairly efficient and I was offered chilled bottled water on a hot day on multiple occasions.  The pool area was excellent and the staff were friendly.  However, it does not seem that the pool was heated at all.  I mostly live in Canada and I have swam outdoors at much colder temperatures and believe me the water was cold!  We had dinner at your main restaurant and the food was terrific.
