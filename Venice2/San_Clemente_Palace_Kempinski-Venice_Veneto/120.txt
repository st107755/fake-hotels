My husband and I returned ten days ago from the San Clemente Palace Hotel where we stayed for five nights.  We have previously always stayed at the Cipriani over the last 30 years, but this time we thought we would have a change.   We were lead to expect that it would be step down from the Cipriani, but nothing could have been further from the truth.  From the moment we arrived, everything was perfect apart from one thing, of which more at the end of this review.  Our room on the first floor was large, quiet, comfortable, the view from the two windows of the trees and gardens were unexpected and wonderful, and the bed was the most comfortable we had ever slept in when in a hotel.
