The little island and old monastery is beautiful and especially in summer it must be fun. Love the old monastery, the pool and the island. Breakfast was also great and well organised.   A few minus points for a luxury hotel:  The interior was not all in shape and you’ll find some broken mirrors and curtains. Not nice for a 5 star Hotel.  The Transfer from Piazza San Marco to the Island is taking a lot of time and during lunch they don’t transfer at all.  The staff at the reception was friendly but not professional. They have let us wait for 2 hours until the rooms were ready. The reasons was a problem with their computer . Unacceptable  And forget room service or concierge. They simply don’t pick up the phone. I’ve tried it for 60 minutes and called via cell phone at the main…
