This was our second visit to the San Clemente Palace before setting off on a cruise.  It is a indeed a haven from the crowds and heat of Venice. It is great to be able to relax by the pool after a hard day sightseeing. The restaurant by the water's edge is special even if it is expensive.   One thing that marred  our visit this time was that a couple of days after we left we received an email with a long list of items that were missing from our minibar and asking if they could charge our credit card.  We hadn't even opened the mini bar during our stay and had been asked at checkout if we had taken anything, to which we replied in the negative.
