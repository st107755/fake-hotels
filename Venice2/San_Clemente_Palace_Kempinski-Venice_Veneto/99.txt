My family and I (2 adults, 2 kids) stayed last weekend at the San Clemente Palace Kempinski in Venice. What a great experience! It started with the pickup at the station at Piazza San  Marco with the free shuttle boat taking us directly to St. Clemente Island. The kids loved this start and were looking forward to spend the rest of the day at the big outdoor pool and kids club. Kids club is open until 10:00pm so we could enjoy one of the best dinner meals we ever had Acquerello Signature Restaurant. The rooms or shall I say suites are very spacious with high ceilings and wide corridors with discrete luxury in every detail.
