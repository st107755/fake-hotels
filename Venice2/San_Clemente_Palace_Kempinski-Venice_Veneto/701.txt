This St. Regis is literally an isle of calm and one of the most unique luxury properties in the world.  The hotel takes up the entirety of the small island, with a stunning park like setting and views including of St Marks etc. over the water.   There is also a small church adjacent to the hotel.   We had two days of rain during our four day stay and found the hotel to be a sanctuary for relaxation during that time.  We basked in the luxury and spaciousness of one of their St. Regis suites, which was gorgeous in every way.   As the sun came out we thoroughly enjoyed walking around the grounds, enjoying good wine, all the while watching the boats and rowers go by.
