Since Venice has become a favorite cruise port, the city, especially around the Piazza San Marco, can become crowded and noisy when multiple ships arrive and depart. So whether you're looking for a quiet retreat from the crowds on a visit, or a pre- or post-cruise stay, the San Clemente Palace is an ideal oasis. The hotel is located on San Clemente Island and offers regular complimentary air conditioned shuttles to and from the Piazza San Marco 24/7.  The crossing takes about 10 minutes. Arriving on San Clemente Island from Venice is a wonderful retreat from the noise and summer heat of the crowds in Venice.
