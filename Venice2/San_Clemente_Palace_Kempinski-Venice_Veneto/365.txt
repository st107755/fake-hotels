Venice is crowded, we all heard that. But there are ways to avoid the crowds, I learned when visiting the city for the first time last month: Explore the city in the morning or evening hours, walk to the beautiful areas far away from the tourist spots and choose a hotel on one of the surrounding islands. The Kempinski is ideal if you want a special one, because every transfer with their own boat from busy San Marco to the resort (10 minutes every 30 minutes) transfers you to a quiet and relaxing luxury resort with lots of space and comfort. No masses, no noise, no hectic. Loved digesting the incredible historic sights of Venice during a walk in their park. Unique property!
