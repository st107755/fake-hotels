The guest rooms are clean and spacious and the beds are very comfortable.  The breakfast buffet is delicious and extensive.  The bar was a fun place to go have a drink.  Service at dinner was slow but the staff were very accommodating.  Maybe the kitchen is very far from the dining room?  It took ages for the waiters to get us anything, even condiments.  Overall the food was very good but a bit pricy.  We sat out by the pool on a warm September morning and it was nice and relaxing.  We had a lot of fun staying here and only wished we could have stayed longer.  Don't be put off by the 10 minute shuttle to St Marks.
