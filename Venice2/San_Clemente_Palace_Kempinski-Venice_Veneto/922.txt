We had some qualms before our arrival about the location of the hotel.  It being on a private island, I was concerned about getting to and from the main drags of Venice.  We hired a water taxi from the train station, which cost about 70 euros. The moment we set foot on San Clemente, we were taken by the beauty and serenity of the place that I didn't want to leave to explore the rest of Venice.  The grounds are well-kept and the palace is elegant, beautiful, and spacious.  The rooms here are probably much bigger than you would get in Venice.  We were upgraded to a very lovely and comfortable St Regis Suite, the size of which was accentuated by the high ceilings and tall windows.
