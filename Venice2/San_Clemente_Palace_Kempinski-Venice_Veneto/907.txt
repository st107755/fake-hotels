Having stayed for three nights at the St Regis I can honestly say that it is one of the finest hotels I have experienced. From being greeted at the dock on arrival to leaving, the service was impeccable. Staff are on hand at all times without being over bearing or intrusive and nothing is too much trouble. The property itself is magnificent and as alluded to by other contributors are haven from the crowds of Venice. The hotel boat service is efficient and runs at regular intervals so you are never hanging around. Standard of decoration is superb and clearly, no expense has been spared.
