San Clemente Island would be the perfect setting for a suspense thriller... think "murder under the sun" - in Venice.  Don't get me wrong, this hotel is superb: they have excellent amenities, a heated pool in a beautiful garden, tennis courts, a golf pitching court, the food is great, room service and housekeeping top notch but my imagination went wild when I read about the history of this place.  Back in the day, if you said to a local Venetian that someone's going to San Clemente, it meant the asylum. Yup, you got it - San Clemente was a monastic settlement and eventually a mental hospital.
