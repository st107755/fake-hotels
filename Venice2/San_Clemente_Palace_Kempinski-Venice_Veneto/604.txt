The San Clemente is a truly remarkable hotel, as noted in many of the reviews here on trip advisor.   Looking at a top five reasons why you would choose the San Clemente I would offer the following:  1) Location - a stunning 10 minute boat ride from San Mark's square (a free shuttle service is run by the hotel leaving every 20 minutes). After the hustle and bustle of a day in Venice the quiet surroundings of the San Clemente are simply perfect.  2) Staff - everyone is highly motivated, knowledgeable and as a result the customer service experience is outstanding. We came away feeling that there was nothing the staff wouldn't do for you.  3) Venue - the hotel itself and the grounds are stunning.
