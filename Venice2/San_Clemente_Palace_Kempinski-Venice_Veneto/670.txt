Beautiful San Clement island, beautiful buildings, very friendly staff, great arrival experience with the private shuttle boat. If you have chosen a room with view on the laguna and Venice, you will be overwhelmed with the beauty of what you see through your windows. San Clement is truly a lovely place to stay, when visiting Venice. However, should you go during a period when the nights are cold, even though the days may feel warm, your un-heated room will be icy in the morning and your bathroom with make you feel colder that if staying in an igloo! Despite the friendliness of staff, I do not recall being downgraded to a lower category room ever before in my lifetime, just for the sake or turning the heating on.
