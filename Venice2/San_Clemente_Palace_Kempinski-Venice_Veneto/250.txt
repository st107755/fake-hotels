We loved everything about San Clemente Palace Kempinski. We booked a stay of 9 nights for our honeymoon. The hotel on its own island was the perfect romantic getaway, combined with a city trip.   We booked a deluxe room and it was plenty big enough with a beautiful view of the gardens and water. The bed was so comfortable and the bathroom with separate shower and bath was ideal.   The island itself is beautiful with walk ways all around and views of Venice. The chapel is gorgeous and there are so many seating areas to watch the water. The food at breakfast was incredible and catered for all food intolerances.
