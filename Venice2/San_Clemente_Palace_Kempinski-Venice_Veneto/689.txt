We stayed here in August, 2015 with our two children who were both 10 years old, when the property was still a St. Regis. Arriving in the early evening, the property was absolutely stunning! The grounds were immaculate, there were large expanses of park like grounds (absolutely unheard of in Venice) and the hotel proper is exquisite.  We were celebrating our children's birthdays, so the room was already pre-decorated with balloons and cakes. All of the furnishings throughout the hotel were beautiful, including the enormous Murano chandeliers lining all of the hallways, absolutely gorgeous.
