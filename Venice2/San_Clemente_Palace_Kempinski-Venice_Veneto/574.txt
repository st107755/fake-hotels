We stayed for two nights and were in a junior suite. Huge - must be 1000sq ft and 5m ceilings, so very nice. Of course, it's Venice, you pay for such luxury but it's a fantastic hotel. Excellent welcome and reception is very efficient and helpful too. Free shuttle boat service to Venice operates twice an hour and works well - takes around 10 mins. One thing - breakfast is hectic if you want to have it around 9.30am - the same time as everyone else. You end up walking 50m to the buffet which is a pain - admittedly this is a first world problem kind you. Food is great but service can be a bit slow - my two soft eggs took 20 mins and were rock hard.  The room they service breakfast in isn't really big enough to handle the number of guest really.
