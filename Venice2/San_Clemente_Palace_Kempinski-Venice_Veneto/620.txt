The San Clemente Palace is an old Monastery situated on its own island about ten minutes across the water from Piazza San Marco.   This was our second visit and we loved staying here. Getting out of the craziness of Venice was great and the location meant you had a holiday within a holiday. The hotel is very comfortable and would be my first choice for staying the next time I return to Venice.   The hotel itself is huge, the main building has wide corridors, high ceilings and spacious rooms giving you the feeling of peace and tranquility wherever you are in the hotel.   The private launch shuttles you back and forth across to Piazza San Marco all day so you easily get into the City, or escape back to the tranquility of the island.
