Usually I’m quite lazy in writing a review but the wonderful memories of my recent weekend escape are so vivid that I wish to share them.   What I loved most of this beautiful hotel was the contrast and the sudden change of setting from the gilded and lavish town of Venice, with its crowded channels, to the peaceful wide green spaces of the hotel park and the peace of a former monastery (in just a 10-minute boat ride).   There you may choose your intimate corner, diving in a sofa amongst botanical treasures.
