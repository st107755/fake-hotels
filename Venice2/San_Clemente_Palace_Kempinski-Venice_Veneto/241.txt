San Clemente is an oasis of calm, beauty, luxury, fresh air, tranquility, great service and services, within a short boat trip across the lagoon from Venice San Marco, between Giudecca and the Lido. Only the hotel's private launches and water taxis can access the island (or your own yacht [up to 30m] can be moored at the pontoon during your stay). The service is provided on luxury teak launches with air conditioning in the interior and standing room for those that enjoy the sea breeze.
