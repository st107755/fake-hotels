Good things first : Very nice hotel. Staff were friendly and very attentive. Room was large and clean with very comfortable beds. I was pleasantly surprised that our room was cleaned twice a day. Excellent continental breakfast. Fast wifi Slight downside: On a private island (may be good for some people). We arrived late at night so the hotel kindly organised a private water taxi (120 euros) to pick us up at the airport. There are complimentary boats between the hotel and San Marco square which runs every 30 minutes and takes less than 10 minutes to get between those places. Our experience was that the boats were very punctual so do not hang around much at the jetty at San Marco Square hence if you miss it, you have to wait for the next boat.
