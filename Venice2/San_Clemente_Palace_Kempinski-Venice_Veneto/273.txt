      It was truly an unforgettable stay! I was waking up each morning feeling in paradise! The hotel is very luxurious with lovely, friendly, & welcoming staff!         The concierge is very helpful, helped me enough with all the must see destinations and all the film locations in Venice. The breakfast is astonishing, has lots, lots, and lots of variety of delicious food! Maybe one of the best breakfast buffet in a hotel!         The hotel is a small island about 10 minutes from San Marco, it offers a free transfer from and to San Marco almost every 20 mins.       The hotel has a big pool, very big garden, indoor & outdoor restaurants. The hotel is lovely by all means, even it’s corridors has a great scent of perfume.        If you’re willing to go to Venice then this hotel…
