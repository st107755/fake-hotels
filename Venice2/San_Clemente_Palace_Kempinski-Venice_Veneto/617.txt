We stayed in San Clemente after a cruise. Our fifth time In Venice and we usually stay at the Europa and Regina but this time I thought we would try an island for something quieter.   We were upgraded on arrival which was nice.  The women working on reception could not have been nicer. They were so helpful and polite making us quickly feel relaxed. That first impression continued as we entered our room which was very spacious and nicely decorated.   This place is quite outstanding. Beautiful gardens, spacious feeling, excellent pool area and al fresco dining.   I stay in hotels all over the world, and I don't always score then on trip advisor but this one had to be done.
