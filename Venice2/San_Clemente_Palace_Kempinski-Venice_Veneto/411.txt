After booking I was slightly put out about some of the reviews I read about the hotel and was concerned that our trip would leave us disappointed. Really, I do not know what some people find these days to complain about. It was utter perfection - the bit we enjoyed the most is that it was completely exclusive and felt like a relaxing holiday as well a a city break. We did not travel with our children, but had we done so they would have loved the pool area and grounds which were great to explore. And the water taxi, nice touch and good fun.  All the staff was so friendly and attentive, especially at breakfast. We have stayed in many 5 star hotels and it was up there with others.
