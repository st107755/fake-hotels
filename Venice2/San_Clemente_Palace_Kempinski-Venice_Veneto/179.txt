Located away from the madness near St. Marks the hotel is on the private Island of San Clemente in Venice,  just ten minutes away from the main area of by one of the hotel's complimentary boats. The boat ride is an exceptional experience (especially at dusk or at night) as you’ll cross the historic  waters of Venice. San Clemente Island is a private island and may not be reached by public transportation: only the hotel’s own boats and water taxi services may access this outstanding Venetian retreat. The hotel is like a palace, with super comfortable rooms that are clean and quiet and a hotel staff that is professional, polite, and caring making one feel like a honored guest.
