Just spent a few days here with my husband. Beautiful hotel and grounds on its own island in the lagoon.  Beautiful views of Venice. It’s my second visit and I didn’t consider staying anywhere else as it’s a fabulous hotel and rare to find an outdoor pool in Venice. Rooms large and very comfortable. Service superb. The only criticisms I would have are the bar prices being very steep (18 euros for a small glass of Sauvignon, 8 euros for coffee). The maid service was hit and miss, we don’t expect to return to the room at 4.30 to find the maid only just cleaning and then turn down arrive less than 2 hours later.
