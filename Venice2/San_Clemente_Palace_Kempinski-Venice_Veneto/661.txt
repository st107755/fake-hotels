Yes, this is an outstanding hotel in almost all respects. From the arrival by water taxi direct from the airport (140 Euros, booked in advance), through a beautiful garden to the reception and into an impressive, substantial palatial building, ( a former lunatic asylum), everything looks great. The service at check in is friendly and efficient ( pre authorisation of credit card for a huge amount). Apparently, there are 17 categories of room and we thought ours ( Superior?) were good, well furnished with a massive, very comfortable bed, courtyard view. The even more expensive rooms have a lagoon view but we couldn't detect many balconies..a direct result of the former life of this building, no doubt.
