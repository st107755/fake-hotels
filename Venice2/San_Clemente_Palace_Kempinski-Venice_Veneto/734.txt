Absolutely stunning resort and just as beautiful as all the photos. I traveled with my wife and 2yr old baby for 4 nights and found the resort quite family friendly with fairly large rooms. Importantly, the resort offers free ferries to and from the center of Venice twice an hour and usually takes ~15mins. We did not find this too inconvenient although you will have to organize your day around the times. However, after spending a fully day in over-crowded Venice, we loved coming back to quiet San Clemente. We were impressed with the lovely grounds, beautiful swimming pool (essential for a baby in the heat of summer).
