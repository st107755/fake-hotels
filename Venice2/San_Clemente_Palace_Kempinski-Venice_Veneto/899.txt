I was told this was one of the most prestigious hotels in Venice, which built up my expectations perhaps too much.   The room they reserved for me although good sized, hardly had any windows, there were two small windows high up on the high-ceilinged wall, so there was no view, and I was reminded how the place used to be an asylum.   We asked for a change of room and got a much smaller room, but at least with two 'normal' windows with a view of the garden. The room, however was so small that when we ordered breakfast in bed, the room service was unable to wheel in the small breakfast trolley and had to leave it right behind the door.
