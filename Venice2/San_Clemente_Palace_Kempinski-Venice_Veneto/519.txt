My wife and I spent 4 days here and I must say, this is as close to having my own private island as I will likely get. The facility and grounds are huge! If you've seen the movie The Shining... yeah, it's kinda like that. The boat shuttle to St Marks Square runs right on time an puts you in the heart of Venice. I get the sense that some of the staff are still learning their roles. Service in the restaurant is a bit uneven. They still need to work out some of the kinks. Food selections are limited and quite expensive. The hotel was very kind and upgraded us to a suite. The room was huge and well appointed.
