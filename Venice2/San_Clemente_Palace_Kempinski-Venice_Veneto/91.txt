My husband and I love Italy and whilst we find it hard to decide which is our favourite city, both agree Venice has something magical about it. It wasn't a great surprise then to learn he wanted to celebrate his birthday there even though the end of August wouldn't be a time we would usually visit. We decided we needed an hotel where we could not only enjoy all that Venice has to offer but also somewhere we could relax and retreat to if the weather and people became too much. As soon as the water taxi approached the San Clemente Palace we felt this hotel was going to be very special and it certainly was.
