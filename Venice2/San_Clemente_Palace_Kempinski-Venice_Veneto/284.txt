Located on a lush island in a gorgeous hotel, formerly a monastery which has been redone with incredible elegance, we found this incredible property.  Arriving by water taxi, we were surprised how lush and spacious this hotel was - particularly comparing it to the other hotels we have stayed at in Venice.    We enjoyed a quick lunch by the huge pool outdoors while our child swam, then took the water taxi into the city.  One of the big bonuses of staying here is the water taxi ride which takes you directly to St. Mark’s every 30 minutes.  The taxi is never crowed and has an elegance about it that is refreshing.
