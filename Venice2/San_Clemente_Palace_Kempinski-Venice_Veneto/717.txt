So for our 25th wedding anniversary my wife takes over the booking duties and finds us a cracking deal through Expedia. She had good communication with the concierge prior to arrival to discuss various airport transfer options pricing from reasonable to ouch. We being thrifty travellers took the Alilaguna water bus at 15 Euros each, its signed well at the airport and is accessed by a mostly covered walkway 750m away. It takes about 1hr 20mins to San Marco although the best stop for the St Regis free private water taxi is San Zaccaria.  The St Regis water taxi takes approximately 10 mins to get you to the hotel and runs every 30 mins from morning to midnight which is a great service.
