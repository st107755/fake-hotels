Spent 6 nights at this fabulous hotel. Perfectly located on its own private island just 10 mins by complimentary water taxi from the hustle and bustle of Venice. Great combination of relaxing by the lovely pool and dipping in and out of sightseeing.   Great rooms, high ceilings decorated in a traditional style with the odd contemporary twist. Lots of super, contemporary installation art dotted throughput the hotel and its well maintained grounds. We had excellent service throughout and I would especially like to mention Jessica and Damiano who looked after us at the waterside Acquerello restaurant, great attention to detail and they were brilliant with my daughter who has a nut allergy.
