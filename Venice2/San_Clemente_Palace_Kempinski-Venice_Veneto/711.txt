I stayed at the hotel for 2 days after the tour I was on concluded. It was excellent. One approaches the hotel either by taxi or one of their shuttle boats. Security is very good in that they know who is arriving. One couldn't ask for better service from reception to the breakfast room or housekeeping. The rooms were very large and extremely comfortable and beautifully appointed. Breakfast is either a buffet or off the menu. The service is excellent. There is a huge variety of fruits, pastries, etc. on the buffet. The pool was a nice place to relax for a bit each day before heading to San Marco Square on their private shuttle which runs every 30 minutes both ways. From there, Venice awaits your pleasure.
