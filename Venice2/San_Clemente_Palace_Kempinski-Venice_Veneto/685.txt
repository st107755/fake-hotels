I stayed here with my husband for one night. The hotel is a short ride away from st marks square by boat. The hotel offers a free water shuttle service twice every hour that takes about 10 minutes. The staff are very polite and helpful. However the hotel has a few things that could be improved for it to be worth the price that it charges per night.
