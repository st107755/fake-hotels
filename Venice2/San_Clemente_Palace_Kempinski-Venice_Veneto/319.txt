We’ve been to Venice several times before and were always staying at hotels on Grand Canal, this time looking at the sunny and warm weather forecast decided to try different experience - stay in San Clemente Palace - what an excellent and right decision we made! Everything started from excellent arrival experience – we took the hotel free shuttle boat from San Marco square. Very important to mention that unlike many shuttle services in the hotels at San Clemente it operates from 8.50 in the morning till 00.40 in the night without interruptions every 30 min we used it many times during our short stay and shuttles were always on time – very convenient and well managed!
