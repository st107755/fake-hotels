If you want to visit Venice but want a tranquil retreat away from the crowds, this is an excellent choice. It requires a 10 minute boat ride from San Marco but it is well worth it for the lovely grounds, swimming pool, and space. Not only is the property spacious with walking paths, outdoor art, and a restored 12th Century church but the rooms offer plenty of space. The hotel offers a boat that runs every 20 minutes. During peak times it may fill up so be aware and ready to board.  The services are mostly all top notch and I can personally vouch for the incredibly knowledgeable and helpful concierge desk.
