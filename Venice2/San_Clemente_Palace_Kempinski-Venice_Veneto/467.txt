This is a little slice of heaven in Venice.  Unremarkably, everything is incredibly expensive.  I recommend the speed boat from the airport to the hotel.  Our trip to the hotel was at night and it was very romantic!  Although it is 140 euros each way so be ready for the cost.  The disappointment came with the food.  We ate at the bar both nights (100 euros for 2).  Both nights we returned the food.  I had a bolognese tortellini one night and it was no better than a meal you would buy for a child at a cheap restaurant.  A small bowl.  Not very hot.  Flavourless.  The difference - it was 28 odd euros.  My partner had chicken - again it was flavourless and she returned it.   It was the same the second night.
