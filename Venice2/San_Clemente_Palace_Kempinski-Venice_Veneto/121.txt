I can’t speak highly enough of the hotel and its staff, especially Costi with his incredible cocktails and mind bending magic tricks! A group of us stayed for four nights for a work celebration trip. The hotel is very impressive, extravagantly yet tastefully decorated. Great facilities - the spa was smaller than anticipated but perfectly adequate, the massages were amazing & the gym looked very good. The pool and tennis courts were awesome.  The food was equally as impressive. Vast selection for breakfast, catering for cultures and cuisines. But the staff made our stay unforgettable.
