At first I wasn't sure I wanted to be on a private island and miss the hustle and bustle of downtown Venice, however this turned out to be one of the highlights of my entire trip.  I felt like I was staying at Buckingham palace.   We had a Costco package and had upgraded to this hotel.  It was totally worth it.  The boat ride back and forth was a fun addition and only took 10-15 minutes.  It was nice to get over to the island to relax after a full day of walking and touring.  We even decided to just stay at the hotel and use the pool and relax one day and then went into the city for dinner.  I can't even begin to explain how nice the breakfast buffet was.
