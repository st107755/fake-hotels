This hotel, located on a private island only 12 minutes boat shuttle from St Mark’s Square, is absolutely lovely. Don’t be put off by the fact it’s on an island...the shuttle goes every 30 minutes and ran like clockwork.   The rooms are very spacious and very nicely appointed (the bed was really comfortable). The (included for our package) breakfast buffet was an amazing choice of cooked, breads, pastries, cheese, meats, fruit and many other items.   The grounds are beautiful, with a lovely view of Venice across the water. The service was first class...everyone seemed really motivated and attentive. One evening, there was a little hiccup on a drinks order at the outdoor bar...no big deal but the waiter brought us a lovely selection of chocolates as an apology for the mistake. …
