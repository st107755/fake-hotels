I recently stayed at the San Clemente hotel and it was one of the most peaceful holidays I've ever had.   I was apprehensive about staying off the main island and having to get a boat to the hotel, but the instructions on the website, along with the picture of where to find the area to get the boat from San Marco, meant that all my worries were for nothing.   The boats to and from the hotel leave around every 30mins, and it takes about 15mins to get to the hotel.  The gardens and the actual hotel itself is stunning. It honestly took my breath away. We ate at the restaurant on the first night and the food was incredible. Everyone who worked there were so welcoming and friendly and funny! Thoroughly enjoyed myself.
