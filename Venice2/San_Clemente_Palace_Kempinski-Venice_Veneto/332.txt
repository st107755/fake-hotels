My girlfriend and I recently stayed for three nights at the start of our nine-day Italy trip, during which the San Clemente was our favorite property.  The grounds are expansive and well-maintained, with lush green hills and a large heated pool. The interiors of the rooms and common areas were impressively decorated, with local details including but not limited to Murano-glass chandeliers and paintings by local artists. The room was also comfortably-sized and impeccably clean.  What truly stood out and compelled me to write this review however, were the boat rides and the instances of exceptional service.
