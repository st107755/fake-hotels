I booked a trip to Venice as a special treat for our grown-up family and daughter's fiancé. The San Clemente Palace fitted the bill perfectly, with its beautifully appointed rooms, high-quality fine dining area and large swimming pool set in the island's gardens. It made for an ideal counterpoint to the crowds of tourists and hustle of Venice itself.  It was quite exciting to be taken directly from the airport to the hotel by boat, and arrival at the private island did not disappoint. A short walk took us to the elegantly appointed reception along a path shaded by some of the garden's large trees. We did our best not to appear intimidated while in our casual travel outfits.
