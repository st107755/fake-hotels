We have stayed at several St Regis property's around the world. The location and staff at The San Clemente Palace are top notch. They are professional, friendly, and could not be more professional. I am a lifetime platinum SPG member and the hotel really went out of their way to make us feel welcomed and appreciated.  The property itself is a nice break from the crazy tourist crowds in Venice proper, but this is only a short boat ride away if you want it. We never felt isolated or inconvenienced because of the location - on the contrary we appreciated the calm, peaceful quiet when we had had enough of the crowds.
