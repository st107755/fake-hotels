My husband and I went with our two year-old in the beginning of June. It was crucial that we not be directly in Venice, as the city is not very stroller friendly with tons and tons of stairs. It was very hot while we were there and it was so nice to be able to relax by the pool, which would not be possible at even the nicest hotel in Venice. Finally, my son's favorite activity was taking the boat between Venice and the hotel (10 minutes). He could have done that all day.  A few highlights - the grounds are gorgeous and it smells like flowers the second you get off the boat. The inside is beautiful and also smells like flowers. Loved that.  The food was excellent.
