I will absolutely stay at this hotel again.  There were a few bumps as to be expected as the employee and staff "find their groove."  For those reasons, I had to drop a star.  Getting there:  I feel it is completely worth the money to take a Water taxi directly to the hotel vs dragging luggage over the large bridge, and figuring out the Water Bus.  That, in itself, is an experience.  The Location of the hotel is perfect for walking to St Mark’s Square.    Check In:  We were acknowledged for being Titanium Elite and offered points or breakfast.  We selected breakfast.  (Upon check out, they had to verify we selected breakfast, as they said it was not noted in our reservation, which choice we had made.
