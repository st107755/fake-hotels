This hotel was recently opened in Sept 2019. If you are looking for modern decor with style and luxury at its finest this will be the property of choice. For this reason, this is also why you would expect to choose the St Regis over its sister brand hotels like the Gritti and Danieli because they represent the more traditional Venetian decor.  If you are coming for the first time, the hotel is right next to St Marks Square.  This is on the street with all the luxury shops and it is right on the river. I would recommend taking a water taxi as it stops right in front of the hotel whereas if you would like to take a waterbus it stops you a few minutes away but its more affordable.
