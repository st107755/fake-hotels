The new St. Regis is a much needed breath of fresh air among the Venice hotel scene. After visiting the city a few times, one grows tired of the over-the-top decor that seems to be the staple of all luxury hotels in town (think Gritti with golden drapes everywhere). Aman did a great job at offering high-end luxury with more of a contemporary taste, but its location is not as desirable. St. Regis ticks all the boxes: freshly redone but with a tasteful approach, the best location in town, an outstanding outdoors area, lots of rooms facing the canal, amazing drinks of truly international standard, top notch service.
