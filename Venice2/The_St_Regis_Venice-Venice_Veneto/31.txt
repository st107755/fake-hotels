If you're looking for somewhere cheap in Venezia, go elsewhere. If you're happy with lower quality rooms, a cramped feeling in the public spaces, go elsewhere. But if you like being treated well, like a lord, courteously, in excellent interior surroundings, and finding staff with beaming smiles on your holiday, this is the one. My wife tells me that the hotel has fairly recently been re-developed, too. They've done a good job.  It's also really close to Piazza San Marco, and the historic Caffè Florian. My wife says the shopping in the locality is excellent as well. Equally as good as she's found in Milano. So there!
