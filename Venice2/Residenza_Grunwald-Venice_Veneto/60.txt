My friend and I booked a 4 day Venice break through British Airways.  Initially, we were perturbed to find that we had booked into the Casa Nova annex, rather than the main hotel as this was not clear in the booking procedures.  However, we needn't have worried.  This is a wonderful place.  Beautiful decor, great rooms, lovely bathroom with bath and separate shower.  Breakfast is in the main hotel on the terrace overlooking the lagoon.  It is a gorgeous breakfast buffet with waiter service for beverages.  The main hotel is also beautiful with attentive and efficient staff who are clearly well-used to providing 5* luxury.
