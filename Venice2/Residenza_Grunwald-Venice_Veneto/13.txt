I stayed here for a 3 night mini break booked as a package via British Airways. I was fully aware the Casa Nova annex was not in the same building as the Baur itself. I arrived by water bus from the airport.The water bus stop & the valoretto stop are almost next to each other & once I worked out which direction I needed to walk in, the hotel was less than 5 minutes way & no bridges to climb. Check in was speedy & I was escorted to the Casa Nova building, which was a 1 minute walk way.  I had a very comfortable double bedroom, plenty of storage space with proper wooden & padded hangers.
