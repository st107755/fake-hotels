I have recently returned from a  3 night stay in Venice and stayed at Bauer Casa Nova the annex next to 5 star Bauer Palazzo Hotel. It was lovely and I happened to get an amazing deal on my room through Tripadvisor.  You have breakfast in the actual Bauer hotel which is just a few steps away.  Even on a cold morning it wasn't a problem. The hotel was quiet with few guests but that suited me fine. I liked walking into the hotel for breakfast as it was such a peaceful way to start the day overlooking the Grand Canal.  The food was lovely with a great hot and cold selection and a large choice of cakes.
