Arrived by water taxi to the Bauer Palazzo. Good check in and was showen to our room in the Casa Nova which is located back through main doors and down an alley. The suite was lovely. On entering there's a sitting room with a dining table, settee that turned into a double bed for my granddaughter. One wall was wood panels that concealed, a small kitchen, television, good wardrobe space with a room safe. The bedroom had a large king size bed and off the bedroom was a beautiful marble bathroom with everything you need. The suite was spotless. The hotel is not cheap, and a gin & Tonic from the bar is €23 which I think is extortionate. In the room mini bar there's some snacks, but please check price list first as the small box of Pringles that you can buy in the supermarket for £1…
