My wife and I returned to this great hotel last week. It was our second visit and we are looking forward to book for next year. It is clearly a 5 star hotel. Our room was in the annex and delightful. It was clean and has an excellent maid service. The beds are turned down each night and slippers left at the side of your bed. The bathroom is marble and has a huge bath and a separate large shower. Yes it is expensive, as are most of the 5 star Venice hotels. Breakfast is included in the room rate, with everything available that you would expect. The staff are amazing, nothing is too much trouble. English is spoken by most of the staff excluding the room maids.
