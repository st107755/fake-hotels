Just walking into the lobby of the Bauer Hotel makes you smile.  The polished marble floor, the grand piano, the comfy leather chairs, the Venetian chandeliers all in a cool Art Deco style.  From the moment you enter you know this is a special place.  The girl who checked us in was great.  She was amusing and efficient and gave us a condensed history of the Bauer as she showed us to our room at the Casa Nova which was out the front door and less than a minute walk to the left of the main hotel.  We took the elevator to the third floor and walked a small flight of stairs to arrive at our room at the top of the building.  It was fabulous.  The style of the room was both chic and relaxed.
