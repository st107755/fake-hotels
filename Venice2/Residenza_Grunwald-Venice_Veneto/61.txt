I stayed at the Bauer - Casa Nova with my husband and 2 year old daughter. We were allocated room 15 on the first floor. Similar to many of the reviews on here, we were also under the impression that we were staying at the actual Bauer hotel and were amazed by the beautiful photos when making the booking. It was only until I received the booking confirmation was when I realised that we were not actually booked into the Bauer hotel itself but the Bauer Casa Nova – the building next door to the hotel. Having read the mixed reviews on here I was quite concerned that we were booked into a horrible hotel but on arrival we were pleasantly surprised by how nice it was.
