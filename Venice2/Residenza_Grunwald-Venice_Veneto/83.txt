Stayed at The Bauer Casa Nova for 2 nights at the end of June on a trip with my teenage cousin and Nana. We actually booked a different hotel but were upgraded through BA holidays. The Bauer is a series of hotels near each other and Casa Nova is the most modern (i.e not a beautiful old building and not in the main part of the hotel) and consequently the least expensive.  Location is perfect, a few steps from St Mark's square and in a beautiful canal side building.   Breakfast is nice - a small buffet of mostly continental style, but the seating outside along the grand canal is fabulous.
