The Bauer Casa Nova. The Bauer's little sister. Beautifully appointed, very clean, and highly recommended. Just bring your ear plugs if you are a light sleeper as the local services and deliveries get going as early as 5.30am!!  The Bauer II restaurant on the terrace: Someone has done a lot of good work behind the scenes. The staff seem to naturally empathise to your level no matter where you are on the life ladder. I warmed to everyone I met here. The terrace is lovely and you just soak in the atmosphere. The food was beautifully presented, fresh, tasty, and even felt healthy. If you ask about the wines they will help you to choose a great accompanying wine... well worth the chat. OK its expensive but my Venice experience has taught me a lesson.
