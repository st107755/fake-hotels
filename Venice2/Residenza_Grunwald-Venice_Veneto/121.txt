The Bauer Hotel was recommended as a pre-cruise hotel, however we found it to be pricey (yes, even as Venetian hotels go). So with a little online investigation, we happened onto the site of the Bauer Casa Nova, which prices were much more reasonable than the main Bauer or other of its hotels. We booked online and had no problems, even with follow-up written communication in English. Our package for a hotel room included continental breakfast, with the option of upgrading to the buffet breakfast at half the usual price. How surprised were we to receive an upgrade to a beautiful suite--and with buffet breakfast included!   The suite was so well-appointed and decorated.
