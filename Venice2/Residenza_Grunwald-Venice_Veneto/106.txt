My partner and I had originally booked a room in Venice at a slightly cheaper hotel in a more remote location, but we were offered an upgrade to a room at the Bauer Casanova and we couldn't have been happier with our stay. When we first arrived, we were offered a further upgrade to a suite in the Casanova as we were staying for just over a week. The suite was beautiful, decorated in traditional Venetian style, and we found that we appreciated the quiet atmosphere that came from being in an annex of the main hotel.   The amenities of the main hotel were also fantastic. I really enjoyed starting my day with a succession of creamy cappuchinos on the terrace and thought that the breakfast selection was good, although it could have included a tad bit more variation day to day.
