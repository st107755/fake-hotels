Myself and partner have just returned from a 2 night stay at Casa Nova which we both thoroughly enjoyed. The hotel is in a perfect location situated a minutes walk from Saint Mark's square and all of the luxury boutiques. Upon arrival at the main Bauer hotel we were greeted pleasantly by the staff and showed next door to the Casa Nova. Our room was on the top floor and our bags were taken up behind us. The room was beautiful with nice wooden beams exposed and the size was  considerably bigger than we expected, more of a suite. The bathroom was also very large and had a double bath and separate shower and most importantly clean. The wifi signal was good and the room was cleaned twice a day.
