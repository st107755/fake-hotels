Fantastic hotel with very very good service, the staff are very courteous and helpful.  The room (number 22) was a good size furnished and decorated to a very high Venetian standard. The bathroom too is finished to a high standard with excellent complimentary products.  Although the Bauer Casa Nova is a separate building; the main hotel reception, bar & restaurant are literally around the corner...just a few seconds walk in fact so don't let this discourage you.   The buffet style breakfast is simply amazing with everything one could want for breakfast and good quality as well.  We also really enjoyed the bar which had live music in the evening with light snacks & meals available until late into the night.
