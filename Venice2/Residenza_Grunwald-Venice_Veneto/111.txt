I booked this hotel using Visa World Points thinking I was booking a suite in the main Bauer hotel. After check in at the main lobby we were escorted to the Casa Nova which is a boutique property next door. The property is very quiet and small. I did not run into one other person my whole four day stay and the room was extremely quiet despite the fact it is so close to the main tourist area of Venice. Our suite was beautiful and decorated in a high end Venetian style. Brocade everything, but in a light color, thank god. The bathroom although on the small side was a very modern white marble with clean lines. The ceilings were beamed and high. The living room offered a couch, kitchenette and small breakfast table.
