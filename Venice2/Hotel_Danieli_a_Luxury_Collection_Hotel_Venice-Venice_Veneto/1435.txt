We have chosen this hotel for a one-night stay during the carnival for its location, history and beauty. And I should say that the hall is fantastic, it really brings you back in time and amuses with columns, Murano glass chandeliers, historic stairs and golden ceiling.   The location is great, one building away from the Doge’s palace. We had a lagoon-view room with a balcony at a new part of the hotel and it was fantastic to wake up and to hear the sound of the sea and gondolas hitting the docks.  As for the room itself we were a bit disappointed. I don’t want to say anything about the room size. Yes, it wasn’t big but it is normal for historic European hotels. So it is fine.
