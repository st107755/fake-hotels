For our third anniversary together, my partner and I decided to spend a night at the Hotel Danieli after hearing my parents' review of the hotel when they stayed, over twenty years ago.  Arriving early on a sunny friday afternoon onboard a speedboat, we alighted at the steps leading into the hotel from the canals. Unfortunately, our first experience of the hotel was not what we expected - the concierge failed to open the doors for us or assist us with our luggage; his phone appeared to be more important to him the whole time. This experience was the same when we departed the hotel.  Having said this, the actual check-in experience was delightful and the lady who assisted us was very friendly and informative.
