We decided to treat ourselves to a room with a balcony for one night before our cruise. The reception was a bit chaotic when we arrived and the 2 staff, though doing their best were struggling to cope with the volume of customers wanting attention. The reception area is also a bit small and wherever you stand and wait you feel you are in the way. Once checked in however we were very happy with our lagoon view room and settled in for the evening and watched Venice go by. Unfortunately the dining room balcony was directly above our room and it was very noisy from 6am with what sounded like furniture being constantly moved around (possibly breakfast preparations).
