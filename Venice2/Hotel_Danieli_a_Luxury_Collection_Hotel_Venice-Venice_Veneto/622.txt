We chose the Hotel Danieli because we wanted to be able to stay out late and get up early to experience San Marco without all the tourists.  The Hotel Danieli certainly delivered in that aspect.  Staying on the island and in Venice proper afforded us a lot of magical moments that we had not been able to take advantage of by staying further out.  The Hotel Danieli was everything I hoped it would be and more.  The first thing I noticed about the room was the plush red curtains.  It was romantic and luxurious.  The wood floors, the mirrors, and the deep hues of the room had a certain mystery about them.  Then, I stepped out on to our terrace.  And I never wanted to leave.
