My first trip to Venice and my wife's second - begrudgingly I might add. For me it was love at first sight as we arrived by train and walked through to the hustling and bustling of water taxis and ferries with the backdrop of ancient picturesque buildings and quaint bridges. We jumped into our highly polished water taxi and took off for the fascinating trip through the canals to the Hotel Danielli located on the Venice lagoon and almost next door to Saint Marco Plaza. The property is quaint and luxurious and the service absolutely first class but not snobby. They have managed to capture that perfect balance between class and friendliness - very impressive.
