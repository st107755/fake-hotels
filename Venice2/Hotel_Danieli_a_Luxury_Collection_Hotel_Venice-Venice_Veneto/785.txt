Sitting on the terrace eating breakfast with a view straight to San Giorgio Maggiore in the sun must be one of the highlights of any visit to Venice. The breakfast selection is superb and there are even seagulls to keep you company which the staff have named.   The location is not limited to the view. Just a hop, skip and a jump away is the Doge's Palace. Around the corner from there lurks Saint Mark's Square and the Basilica. Vaporreto stops are outside so it is easy to reach all parts of Venice.   Rooms are strewn across three buildings and we had to cross a canal to access the one for us.
