I have stayed in the Hotel Danieli on many occasions over the past 30 years.  It never fails to deliver.  The Danielli is a Grand Hotel which has been kept up to date, without losing its character.  I can think of no better start to the day than eating breakfast on the Danieli's rooftop terrace overlooking the lagoon and the entrance to the Grand Canal, and in my opinion the quality has gone up a notch this year when compared with previous visits.  Lagoon view rooms in the more modern end of the hotel are spacious, airy and air conditioned, and a welcome refuge from the hubub of the City.
