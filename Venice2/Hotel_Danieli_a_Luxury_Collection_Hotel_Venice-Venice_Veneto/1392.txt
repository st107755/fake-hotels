My wife and I stayed here in mid-April.  This is a luxurious hotel with beautiful rooms and the best location next to St Marks square.  The water taxi can access the hotel on a canal and you walk right into the lobby.  The lobby and bar area is decorated with glass chandeliers and stained glass windows.  Although the outside of the hotel is very plain, the rooms are luxurious, comfortable and have excellent amenities.  Our room was 337 and we had a balcony and looked right out to the Grand Canal.  We were welcomed with champagne and biscuits in our room too.  We enjoyed breakfast each morning on the rooftop terrace overlooking the grand canal (be careful of seagulls).
