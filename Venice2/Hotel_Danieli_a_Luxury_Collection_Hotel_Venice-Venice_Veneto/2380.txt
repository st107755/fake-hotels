We stayed at the Danieli Hotel in Venice for 2 nights last weekend. On entry the hotel looks wonderful but for a 5 star hotel I was quite dissatisfied with a number of things. Firstly the bed linen was hard and scratchy to the point where we kept waking up uncomfortable. Secondly, the price of the breakfast is outrageous, they charged me 10 euros for half a gratefruit. I would not recommend the Hotel restaurant as it is very over priced for poor quailty food. The staff are ok but nobody goes out of their way to assist you.
