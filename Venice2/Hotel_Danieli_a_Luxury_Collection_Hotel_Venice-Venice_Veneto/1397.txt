I had one night at this beautiful hotel when I was on a business trip from Paris to Milan and stopped into the Danieli to celebrate my birthday.  I had stayed at this hotel previously and had loved the character of the old merchant palace and the staff was amazing then, so it seemed like a great place to stop for a birthday celebration.  There is nothing more exciting then arriving via water taxi at a hotel and the Danieli's beautiful lobby doesn't disappoint.  My friend and I were warmly welcomed as hotel staff took my hand to help me from the boat.  As we checked in, the front desk noticed from my passport that it was my birthday and wished me a happy birthday!
