The proximity to sights and San Zaccaria waterbus stop can’t be beat for quick hops/day away from the centre.  Very impressed with the Concierge team - recommended excellent restaurants - including good value gems.  The strawberries and Spumante in the room was a nice welcome gesture, as was a upgrade to the “lagoon”. We were able to enjoy the sparkling wine up in the terrace instead of the confines of our room - the terrace offers a much nicer view than our 1st floor “lagoon”‘ room in the main palazzo -  Kept windows closed as visitors on the bridge were conversation distance outside our window.  The wooden shutters kept the noise out so we slept well.   The bed was comfortable and the shower had good pressure. The room decor is reflective of building history and age. WiFi…
