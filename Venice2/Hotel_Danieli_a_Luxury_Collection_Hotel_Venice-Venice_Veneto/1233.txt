We visited the Venice recently as part of an SPG "Moment".  We had the pleasure of staying at the Hotel Danieli. We arrived by water taxi from Marco Polo Airport prearranged by the hotel staff. We never touched our bags once we landed at the hotel dock. The hotel is a venerable establishment, tastefully yet elegantly decorated and literally a less than 5 minute walk over the bridge to Piazza San Marco and the San Marco district with all it has to offer.  Stella at the front desk handled our check in smoothly and very courteously.  The Concierge, bell staff and Bar staff could not have been nicer or more helpful as we stumbled through our basic Italian...in fact all the staff we  interacted with spoke English quite well.
