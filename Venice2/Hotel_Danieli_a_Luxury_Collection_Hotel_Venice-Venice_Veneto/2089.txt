You must really look at this hotel, not judge it quickly.  The suite we stayed in was in the Excelsior section, non smoking and relatively recently renovated.  It was wonderful, fresh exotic flowers, marble bathroom, with everything anyone would want, a bottle of wine, chilled on arrival, everything perfect, comfortable beds, and in the most desired place in Venice, imagine.
