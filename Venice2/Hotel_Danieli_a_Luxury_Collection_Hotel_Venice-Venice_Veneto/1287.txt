Hotel Danieli is as beautiful as they come- its centrally located, the views from breakfast are unreal and the rooms comfortable. It was however a bit of a madhouse checking in. We arrived on a hot day and after fighting our way through crowds of tourists on St Marks square and the Grand Canal area we arrived to a crowded lobby with at least 12 people in line to check in. I'm sure it's unavoidable at large hotels that numerous patrons are checking in at the same time but it would have been nice if someone would have welcomed us, maybe offered something to drink and had us sit comfortably in the lobby upon arrival- I feel like most hotels in this price range manage to make this a pleasant experience. We got a €85 resort credit which is pretty standard when booking with Amex fine…
