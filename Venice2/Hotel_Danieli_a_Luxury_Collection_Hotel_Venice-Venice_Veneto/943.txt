A beautiful and historic hotel - right next to Piazza San Marco. Very central but this can be a good or not so good thing depending on what you are looking for. Good - central; not so good - crowded and full of tourists. beware being impaled by selfie sticks. However because we were there for only 2 days the location was perfect as we were in the middle of it all. The hotel has so much history, its worth spending the time with the hotel staff learning about this property. The check in and concierge services were pitch-perfect down to restaurant reservations and 4:30 am taxis to the airport. The rooms are in classic Venetian style and vary greatly.
