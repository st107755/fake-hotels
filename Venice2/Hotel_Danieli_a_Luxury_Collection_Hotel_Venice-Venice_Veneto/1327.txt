When arriving via water taxi you have the feeling of arriving at a grand European Hotel, this continues as you walk into the lobby filled with comfortable seating and light with gorgeous Murano chandeliers. I went to check in and my room was not ready yet. I was less than thrilled as I'd arrived via London after a long transatlantic flight and was exhausted. I sat in the lobby and had a Bellini or two and waited an hour. Room still was not ready. I watched as several other travelers were having the same problem and various displays of rage were performed for the entire lobby. I decided to take a walk to San Marco's Square which is steps from the hotel. After my walk I returned to hotel and room was still not ready.
