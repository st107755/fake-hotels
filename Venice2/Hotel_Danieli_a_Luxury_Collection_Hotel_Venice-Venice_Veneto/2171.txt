For location and beauty this hotel can't be beat but unfortunately the cruise ship passengers inundate the hotel on the weekends before they load onto the ships.  This means getting into the dining room for breakfast is a nightmare.  My husband likes breakfast and the choice at breakfast was very disappointing.  A grim-looking buffet or begging a waiter for a freshly-made breakfast.  I think the buffet was included for all the cruise-ship passengers and so all the guests are assumed to be on that plan.  The terrace on the top of the hotel serves a lovely lunch with the greatest view over the lagoon but again the service was terribly slow. Prepare to spend all afternoon waiting for your lunch.
