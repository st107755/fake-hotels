I stayed at the Danieli for three nights in October with my wife and fifteen year old daughter.   We asked for a room with a lagoon view when we booked which was extra but as first timers to Venice it was definitely well worth it. We were on the top floor of the newer part of the hotel and the view each morning was stunning.  I think this is technically a five star hotel but you need to remember that most of the buildings are over 200 years old. Our room consisted of a dual aspect bedroom with plenty of room for a king sized bed and a reasonable quality fold up bed for my daughter. There was still a lot of room to move around and sit on the little sofa and chairs.  The furnishings were 18th century style.  The bathroom had a good shower and bath combo and was roomy.
