If you're looking for a modern or refurbished glass, glitz and bling hotel then the Danieli is not for you. However, if you are looking for a Magnificent Grand Tour Hotel with good service then the Danieli is where you should be in Venice.  We flew into Marco Polo mid afternoon and had a water taxi transfer to the Danieli where the concierge and the porter were waiting for us.  The process was very attentive and seamless.  The receptionist was very welcoming and we were checked in and into our room very quickly. Our room was just what we expected spacious, clean and comfortable. The bathroom was well appointed, very clean and well stocked with quality product. We particulary liked the bathrobes and slippers.
