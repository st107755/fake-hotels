Arriving by water taxi directly into the lobby of the Danieli is an appropriate way to start.  When your eyes finally focus on your surroundings you realize you aren't in Kansas anymore !   An old world Italian ambience wraps around your person immediately.  This is a fairy tale hotel. Surviving masterfully amongst orchids that plunge from her interior courtyards.  You are swept up in the past and you love it. Our room was beautifully decorated to the period with etchings of Venice, high count Egyptian cotton sheets and large bathrooms with All the amenities. The maître d' in the hotel's restaurant, ( which was superb ), was welcoming and warm.  We actually decided to stay in and eat in the Hotel's restaurant because of him.  The food was outstanding!
