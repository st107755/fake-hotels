This is a fantastic hotel. There is nothing about this hotel that is cookie cutter and definitely gives you a sense of elegance. The bell staff/concierge team were great. They are the first hotel staff members you interact with when you arrive at the hotel and always greeted us with a smile and genuine greeting. This hotel is in a fantastic location- we walked to every attraction, restaurant, shop, etc. Our room was simply amazing- beautifully appointed, spacious, had a balcony, and a wonderful bathroom. We didn't check out the rooftop restaurant/bar but will do that the next time we are there visiting.
