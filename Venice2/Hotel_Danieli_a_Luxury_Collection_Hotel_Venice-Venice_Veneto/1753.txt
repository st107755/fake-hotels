Given the romantic backdrop of Venice many hotels can probably offer some history and romance but the location and pedigree of the Danieli make it very special. It's an expensive venue, even by 5 * standards but is pretty much unique so gets away with it! If you speak to your operator or the hotel you can use a private water taxi to take you to the private side entrance when you arrive with your luggage. With views out over the lagoon it is well worth paying the extra for "Lagoon View" given it's such a spectacular vista. You are very close to St Marks Square and have easy access to the entire city using the cheap ferry system that stops virtually outside the hotel entrance.
