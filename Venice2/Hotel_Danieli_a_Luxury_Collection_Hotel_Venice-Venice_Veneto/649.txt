They have their own private dock for your transportation, which is great since Venice does get crowded. The breakfast terrace with breathtaking views on consistently clear sunny mornings were the highlight of the beginning of our day. We've been to Venice numerous times over 15 years and this time it was incredibly crowded, which negatively affected wandering the streets during the day. Probably due to cruise ships with huge groups of tourists.  We went up to the terrace around 4 pm for drinks to escape the crowds.
