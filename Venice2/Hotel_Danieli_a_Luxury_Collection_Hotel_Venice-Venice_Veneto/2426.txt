It must be a joke that this hotel claims to be in the 5 star category. The opulent decor is well past its glory days and the place looks sad and faded. It's like an old courtesan who might have have once been beautiful but has stubbornly refused to relinquish her now garish clothes and retire gracefuly. The hotel is gloomy and smells musty and is in sore need of a good team of cleaning ladies.  My friends and I ate lunch on the 4th floor terrace fully expecting to pay top prices for the extraordinary view. What we did not expect was the totally unpalatable food and the surly almost reluctant service.
