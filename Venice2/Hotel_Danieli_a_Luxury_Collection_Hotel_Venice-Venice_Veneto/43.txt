This hotel is ideally located near San Marco Square and is figuratively and literally a palace. The interior design, decorations, tapestries, and art work are beautiful. Marble and stair cases are everywhere. The personnel are both helpful and pleasant as they greet and ask to assist you every time they see you. We were with a small/medium size conference and the dinner was just so-so, but the service was exceptional.  This is a very old building and some downsides include a very small fitness room and the air conditioning cannot be individually adjusted. Hotel Danieli had switched to winter mode which meant that only heat was available. The temperature in our room got up to 25C.  We were offered a fan and we opened our windows.
