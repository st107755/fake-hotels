Yes, it is highly expensive ! But, to be honest, months after we went, we keep an outstanding memory of our 3-night stay. To summarize : gorgeous breakfast from the terrace with iconic views on the laguna, Santa Maria della Salute and Doge Palace ; 5mn walk to San Marco Piazza, meaning excellent location ; iconic views each time you exit your hotel (quays, laguna, piazzas, palaces) ; magnificent rooms (we were very kindly upgraded in junior suite) and magnificent common areas : you are like prince & princess living for 3 nights in an old palace. If you can afford it, don’t hesitate : go to the Danieli. One very important point to add : while being a landmark hotel in the city, with tourist coming to see the main lobby, the staff has always been extremely friendly and helpful…
