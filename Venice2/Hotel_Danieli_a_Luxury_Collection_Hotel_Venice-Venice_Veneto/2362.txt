This is by far the most amazing hotel we’ve ever stayed in.  The sumptuous décor, the incredibly gracious staff, and the prime location of Hotel Danieli all provided the perfect backdrop/home base for a fun and memorable trip to Venice.  We booked our room with the Starwood points program and upon arrival we were told that we had been upgraded.  (It is part of the Starwood Luxury Collection.)  The room: The room was large, spotless, and beautifully decorated in soft tones in the Venetian style.  The walls of the bedroom/sitting room area were covered in embroidered silk in a delicate ribbon and floral pattern, and the draperies were made of the same silk.
