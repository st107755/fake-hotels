My friend and I decided to stop by at the famous Danieli for a cocktail.  It was always my dream to honeymoon here so I decided to try it out beforehand.  The location is nice but very chaotic outside with canal traffic and tourists.  Once inside, you are transformed into a whole different world.  The decor is typical Venetian with gold, chandeliers, heavy drapes and tassles everywhere.  Truly Breathtaking.  But from there, everything went downhill.  I imagined a member of staff rushing over to our aide but unfortunately we were left standing in the middle of the reception area. How strange, there were staff on the reception desk doing nothing and more standing around and not an ounce of customer care.
