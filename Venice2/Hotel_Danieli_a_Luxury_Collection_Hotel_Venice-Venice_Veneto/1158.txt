The location is perfect for sightseeing in Venice and one of the sights to see is the hotel itself.  One of the most beautiful palazzos every built in Venice, just walking to or from your room, sitting in the lobby for a drink is like time travel.  The rooms were beautifully decorated in a renovation just a few years ago, and kept immaculately.  Breakfast on the terrace is a wonderful way to start the day, as you look down upon the most beautiful sights including the Bridge of Sighs.  Service from the front desk, the concierge, housekeeping, at the bar, breakfast and on the terrace were perfect.
