Wanted to go there for a long time. Before I start though, one simple fact. If you go to Venice, and stay in one of the best hotels in the world, don't even count the cash in your wallet!!. Its not enough!!  The Danieli, is simply the most elegant hotel I have ever been in. Mind you at £700 per night for a lagoon view, ( The suits start at 5600 per night!!) it should be good. And it is. From the moment you enter, the hotels magnificence surrounds you. The ornate decorations, furnishings and artwork adorn the whole place. The staff accompany you to your room,  after check in, and give you the time you need, explaining the history of the place. You are made to feel important from step one.
