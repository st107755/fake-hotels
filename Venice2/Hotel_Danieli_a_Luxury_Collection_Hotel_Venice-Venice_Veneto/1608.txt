Very few things equal the magnificence of the Hotel Daneli. From the moment we checked in to the time we (with great reluctance) checked out, the staff of this hotel constantly and consistently went out of their way to make sure our every need was met. The concierge staff were the most well-informed and courteous we have ever encountered. They could not do enough for us. For example, one morning on our way out, I mentioned we were going to the Fortuny Factory (not the museum) and before I knew it he was on the phone setting up an appointment for us with the factory manager!   Rarely have we had the delight such as the breakfast each morning! What. A. View! The food choices were plentiful with great variety.  Our room was extraordinarily comfortable and impeccably clean.
