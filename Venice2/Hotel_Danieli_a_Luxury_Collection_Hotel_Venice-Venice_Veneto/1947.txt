I stayed at the Danieli for one night before a cruise in the updated, modern wing in a luxury double room.  This is a great hotel for vacationers to Venice.  Its very close to everything, the staff was polite and helpful, and the on site restaurant where breakfast is served has fabulous views.  When you arrive by water taxi, you are let off at the hotel's private canal entrance which is a charming start to a vacation in Venice.  I've read several reviews that highlight the original palace as the most desirable rooms.  While I did not see / stay in those rooms, I would caution you not to discount the newer part of the hotel.
