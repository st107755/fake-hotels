We have just returned from a 3 night stay at the Danieli hotel in Venice and cannot stress enough what an amazing experience it was! On arrival we were checked into the hotel by a very friendly female receptionist who took the time to personally escort us to our room while giving us a history of the hotel. She upgraded our room free of charge to one with a lagoon view which was simply breathtaking . It was a great touch to find that when we arrived back at the hotel after dining out each evening that the maid had made the beds, laid out complimentary slippers and left chocolates on the bed.
