There is no question that Danieli is one of the most luxurious historical 5 star hotels in Venice, and I was quite excited to stay there. The view from the restaurant on the 4th floor is magnificent, and the hotel is close to main attractions and very convenient for water buses to go to different areas. It is a beautiful hotel with marble columns, nice furniture and decoration, and Murano chandeliers in the main lobby as well as in each guest room. I visited my friend's room which was a large suite with a huge bathroom with black and white marble tiles, living room with nice couch, etc., and it was just gorgeous.
