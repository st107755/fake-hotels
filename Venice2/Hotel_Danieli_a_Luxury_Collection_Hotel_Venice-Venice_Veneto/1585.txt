Our first holiday as a married couple was at the Danieli so where else would I take my wife for our Silver Wedding than back to Venice and the Danieli? The hotel promised, "Luxury Romance Package Includes Daily Room Service Breakfast For 2, Flowers And Champagne Upon Arrival While In Residence Two Or More Nights, Celebrate Romance With Champagne, And Wake Up To Breakfast In Bed Surrounded By The Scent Of Fresh Flowers".  Our 3 night stay cost us £3,288 and worth every euro if the Danieli could deliver the service and the special romantic stay we were promised. It started well at reception and the room, with a balcony and view across the grand canal and lagoon, champagne and a modest flower arrangement was enchanting.  After that it went down hill.
