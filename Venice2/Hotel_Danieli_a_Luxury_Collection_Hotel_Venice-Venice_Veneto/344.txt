The Danieli is a luxurious treat regardless of whether you are staying at the hotel or stopping by for a drink and a light snack. Magnificently appointed, Hotel Danieli combines luxury and taste in a stately but not stuffy manner.  Going to the Danieli for a Bellini is a tradition for my family, and we usually sit by the bar downstairs in the grand salon. On this particular visit we were visited by Emil (in wait service), who asked if we’d rather have our drinks served upstairs on the terrace overlooking the lagoon. After a rather emphatic “of course”, we were escort to the elevator and taken upstairs to spectacular view of the lagoon.
