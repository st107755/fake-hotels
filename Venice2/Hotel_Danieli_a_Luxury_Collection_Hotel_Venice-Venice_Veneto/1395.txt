The Hotel Danieli is a gorgeous historic property in Venice that is directly overlooking the Grand Canal and in a prime location, just adjacent to St. Mark's (San Marco's) Square.  This allowed us to walk almost everywhere, versus requiring us to take a water taxi, which can be quite costly.  The hotel interior is glamourous, with clear Moorish influence and stately grace.  The staff is equally formal and courteous, yet many individual staff members went out of their way to help us.  I would recommend the "breakfast plan" at approximately 30 Euros/pp at the time of this post.  The buffet is better than most, and the restaurant has sweeping views of the gorgeous canal.
