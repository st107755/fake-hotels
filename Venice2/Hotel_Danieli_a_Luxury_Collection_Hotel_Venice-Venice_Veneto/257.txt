What's not to love :). Everything was excellent from the funny and friendly bellman who helped my family off the water taxi to the personable and patient staff who checked us into our beautiful rooms.  All four of our rooms had unobstructed views of the Grand Canal and islands across the way.  The rooms were large and well appointed.  I'm sorry, but I forgot to take pictures - I was so eager to unpack and get out and wander Venice! We ate dinner one night on the rooftop terrace - the food was great, but the view surpasses everything - so beautiful, especially at sunset.
