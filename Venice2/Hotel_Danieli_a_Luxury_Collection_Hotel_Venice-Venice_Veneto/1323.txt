From the very first time we stayed at the Danieli it has captured our heart and our imagination. Superbly located at the point where the Grand Canal opens up into Venice's lagoon, and within short walking distance from the Bridge of Sighs, the Dogi Palace and Piazza di San Marco, it offers some of the most spectacular views in this spectacular city. No experience is better than dinner on the terrace, seeing the sun set over the Grand Canal and the campaniles and domes of the grand city, leaning over to see all the gondolas and below as they mix with the faster traffic in what is arguably the world's most magnificent street.
