You will not find a closer accommodation to St. Mark's Square than this lagoon-front hotel, which is literally just around the corner from the legendary tourist piazza and St. Mark's Cathedral.   Comprised of three different conjoined palazzi from different centuries, it is sited next to the Palazzo Ducale whose dungeons are connected by the infamous Bridge of Sighs.    The head of the tiny canal beside the hotel makes for a very convenient lagoon-side  gondola pier for your de rigeur gondola experience.  Even more convenient is that right  in front of the hotel, there is a major vaporetto/boat embarkation pier servicing the Piazza San Marco.
