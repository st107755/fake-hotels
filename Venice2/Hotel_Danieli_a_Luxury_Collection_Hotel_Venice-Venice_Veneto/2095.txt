I plan excessively for travel.  A bit too much sometimes.  I am often disappointed or spend the first few hours second guessing my hotel choice when I arrive at a new hotel.  However, the Danieli did not disappoint.  I had been to Venice several times, and just wanted to be right off Saint Mark's Square.  The Danieli is a fantastic location.  If you are in the mood for more secluded or less pricey Venice, this is not the place.  If you want a grand experience and want to drink in Venice as it once was and now is, the Danieli is great.  Great service, great room, and quiet.  I would highly suggest booking a room with breakfast included.  Otherwise, the Danieli charges you an absurdly expensive breakfast charge.
