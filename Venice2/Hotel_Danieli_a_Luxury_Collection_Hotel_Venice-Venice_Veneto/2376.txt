Stayed in the Danieli from 8/3 to 11/3. I was slightly apprehensive after reading some of the reviews but had found a really good deal so went for it and I am so happy I did. On arrival the reception staff were extremely friendly and helpful - we were upgraded to a lagoon view room. It was explained to us that the room was slightly small - I have stayed in small rooms in Venice before and this was palatial - clean, warm, sparkling bathroom, robes and slippers provided, beautifully decorated - and with that fabulous view. We had breakfast in the hotel as it was included in the room price and it was vast with a huge choice of hot and/or cold food, sweet and/or savoury depending upon your choice and greed level.
