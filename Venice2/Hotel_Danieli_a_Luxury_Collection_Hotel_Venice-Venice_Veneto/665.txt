Venice is such an incredible place for so many reasons, and we were very thankful for our lovely stay at the Hotel Danieli. A beautiful lobby greets you and soon you realize this is going to be an excellent hotel experience. Well-appointed, luxurious rooms (we stayed on the older, historic side but I am certain all of the rooms are quite lovely).  The phenomenal breakfast buffet served on the rooftop terrace was an awesome way to start each day.  The convenience to the gondolas, the vaporetto stop, St. Mark's Square and the Doge's Palace all combine well with the beautiful hotel to make this a top choice for visiting Venice.
