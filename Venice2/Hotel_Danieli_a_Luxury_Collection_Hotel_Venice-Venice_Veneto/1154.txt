The elevators are small and the hotel is extremely traditional and clearly old.  Our HVAC was sufficient to keep the room cool but never could get is as cold as we liked it, and the bed was firmer than we usually like. We found the location of our room could be a little noisy at night as I think the alley it was beside could be busy.
