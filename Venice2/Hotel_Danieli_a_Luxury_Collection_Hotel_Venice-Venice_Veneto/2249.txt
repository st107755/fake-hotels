When I saw that the Hotel Danieli was included in my tour, I was ecstatic.I had stayed in Venice several times before in lovely small hotels at a reasonable, rate even with my single supplement added. When I was escorted to my room, I was very disappointed. The carpet was stained and extremely dirty around the edges of the room. The furniture was chipped,and the walls were scuffed. There was no dresser. The room itself was dark and cell-like. I expressed my disappointment to the receptionist who escorted me and was told that there were no other rooms! After several calls, my room was changed. It was much better, but not worth the price. At breakfast,I had to ask several times for coffee which was finally served,lukewarm.
