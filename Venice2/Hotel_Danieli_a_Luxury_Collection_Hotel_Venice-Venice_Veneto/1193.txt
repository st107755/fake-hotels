The Danieli is the charming personification of all that is great about Venice. The staff and the public rooms are wonderful. We particularly enjoyed the terrace restaurant for breakfast and for one excellent dinner. The concierge was very helpful while we were there and the transport arrangements made by the concierge to pick us up at the airport and then take us to our cruise ship were outstanding.  The proximity of the hotel to Piazza San Marco, restaurants and stores is excellent. Our room, off the Grand Canal, was quiet and peaceful. That is particularly important because the hoards of tourists start early.
