Placed perfectly on the Grand Canal within paces of Piazza S. Marco, this venerable hotel sets the standard for gracious and charming luxury.  Upon our arrival we were greeted warmly by the young desk clerk who promptly informed us that we had been upgraded to a suite.  He personally showed us to our quarters and explained all of the features of the suite, which consisted of a bedroom, huge bathroom, and living area.  Alothough the suite was not overlooking the Grand Canal, it was on a small canal overlooking the gondola entrance to the hotel.  This was splendid because you could fling open the casement windows and regard the canal below with its constant gondola traffic, most of the time with the gondoleers singing their arias in booming tenor voices.
