We had a wonderful 3-night stay at the Danieli in early September.   The stunning 13th century staircase, the beautiful lobby/lobby bar area with its elaborate ceiling, the stained glass windows, and the large public rooms on each floor of the original building really transport you back to 13th century Venice.   We had a large room in the original building, overlooking a small canal, and  I loved throwing open the windows to listen to the singing gondoliers going by below.   We could even see a bit of the lagoon off to the side.  (A real lagoon-view room here would surely be fantastic!)
