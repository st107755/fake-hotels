A lovely old hotel, with a beautiful lobby, and possibly the best location in Venice.  Great transport links and close to the main tourist attractions (which could be a downside for some) so very convenient. We stayed for three nights which was the perfect amount of time for unhurried exploring.  A wonderful place to relax after a day strolling around Venice.   Our room was a reasonable size and in good decorative order, although a little dark with all the red velvet. We didn't spend much time in the room, having come to explore Venice and ate out the entire time, so can't comment on the restaurant.
