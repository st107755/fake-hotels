We had the pleasure to stay in this delightful Hotel on a recent trip to Venice to celebrate our 30th Wedding Anniversary.   From the moment you arrive canal-side and enter through the short passage to the main lobby the staff are present and attentive. It is hard to concentrate on the checking-in process as it's impossible to ignore the gorgeous galleried stair-case and constantly find yourself gazing upwards to take it all in, it's just beautiful - and in addition the whole Hotel smells fragrant and simply delightful. Our room was very well furnished, the bed extremely comfortable and large and the bathroom well-appointed. It was all kept immaculately clean by housekeeping throughout our stay.
