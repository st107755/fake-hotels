We thoroughly enjoyed our stay at The Danieli. Location is second-to-none, right on the Canale Grande. We traveled with public river taxi from the mainland, and found that we were dropped off right in front of the hotel.  Rooms are nice - not too modern, and only offer you the basic in amenities. View from our room is nothing to write home about, as houses are built quite close together (we looked out to the right of the hotel). I am assuming there are also rooms facing the canal, but did not ask.  Service really isn't their forte - but that (in our experience) is pretty much the case with most of the hotels and restaurants in Venice (there are some exceptions).
