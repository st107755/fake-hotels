We took a weekend trip to Venice to celebrate my birthday and chose the Danieli mainly because of its central location but also because it looked so beautiful. We took a water taxi from the airport and so arrived at the Danieli waterside entrance--what a romantic introduction to the hotel. The staff, from the chambermaids to the concierge, housekeeping to reception, porters to waiters. could not have been more helpful and friendly. And yes, the hotel interior was just as beautiful as the photos suggested. Our room was not large, but was beautifully appointed, with all mod cons and a lovely big bathroom.
