We booked two luxury King size rooms here, celebrating my husbands 50th birthday with our daughter. We were given a lovely room at the rear of the hotel, with a huge balcony. We booked their motor boat arrival from the airport, and were glad that we did. My husband felt like James Bond, pulling up at the side entrance to the hotel! Whilst we were there they seemed to come every day with some little treat or other - prosecco, canapés, biscuits - there was no end to their hospitality. Initially we balked at the price of breakfast, but frankly it’s just standard for that area of town. The view from the terrace it just stunning, and breakfasting here became an important part of our holiday, with its constantly changing light and view. The food, and free-flowing prosecco is just…
