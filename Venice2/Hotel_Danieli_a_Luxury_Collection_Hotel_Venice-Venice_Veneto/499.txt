Visiting this hotel was like stepping back in time.  It was my husband's sixtieth birthday and we decided to spend it in Venice and chose to stay at the Danieli "a luxury collection hotel" and why not.  We were not disappointed.  We stayed at the oldest part of the hotel that has been renovated, large and spacious but a bit dark inside.   The hotel was immaculately clean, on arrival, we were checked in swiftly, taken to our room, showed us around the hotel with all its grandeur, the magnificent paintings, the golden richness of the walls and ceiling and the deep red velvety soft finishing of the furniture.
