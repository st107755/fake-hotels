We stayed at Hotel Daniele for a few nights after finishing a cruise from Venice on MSC cruises. The hotel is just wonderful and the staff are fantastic. The hotel is located right at the main waterway which makes it very easy to catch a water shuttle or book a ride on a gondola. It's also just steps away from Saint Mark's Square (Piazza San Marco) which is a great place to wander around and visit local restaurants, stores and cathedrals.   The small entrance to the hotel leads you into an enormous entryway with huge granite columns. There is a lounge inside to the left and a huge seating area outside of the lounge, the place is just stunning.
