We had a very easy early check in and a HUGE room with a great view of the Grand Canal. I asked to be moved to a higher floor and the staff was very accommodating and very polite. This is a gorgeous old hotel with lots of history.   Our room was very clean, comfortable, spacious and well appointed. Since we ended up moving to a higher floor, the room was a bit warm, but this is Venice in high summer, so all to be expected.   Location was perfect, a stone’s throw from everything.   I have nothing to fault the hotel for on any level----except for the breakfast buffet, which I found to be subpar and in need of a total overhaul.
