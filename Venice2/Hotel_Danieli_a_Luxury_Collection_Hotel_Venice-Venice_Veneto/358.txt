The hotel is located in central Venice - 2 mins walking from Piazza San Marco which makes it incredibly convenient but also extremely busy with tourists.   The lobby of the hotel is grand in typical Venetian style (which may not appeal to all....). Check-in was ok and we were shown to our room. The hotel has an illustrious history with gorgeous design details however it looks a little tired and dark and could really use a renovation to bring it back to the standards it deserves.  Our room had an amazing lagoon view which was fabulous, the bedding was exceptional and the bathroom gorgeous with a lot of marble.
