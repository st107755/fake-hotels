I had mixed expectations of what the Danielli would be like, but thought "why not" and was glad I chose it.  The public rooms are really quite beautiful as was our room in the old part of the hotel.  The furniture and decor were good antiques as opposed to the usual "5 star" reproduction tat most hotels think its OK to put in their rooms. The service was excellent, friendly, helpful and professional but the main benefit of this hotel is the rooftop restaurant, the view is amazing, eating breakfast there in the morning watching the equivalent of a venetian autobahn is truly breathtaking and something I would recommend to anyone.
