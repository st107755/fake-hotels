We had drinks at the piano bar downstairs and dinner on the rooftop restaurant. The bar had outstanding service with waiters doing everything right to make us feel welcome (we were not hotel guests). I would go so far to say they are some of the best waiters I have ever seen.  The restaurant has an absolutely fabulous view with good food. Service was not as good--I had to ask them twice to serve us wine as I refuse to serve myself at nice places, and I even saw one of the (busy) serving staff tell another we needed a refill and the waiter just ignored her (and more importantly: us). That annoyed me so I immediately called him over and told him to serve us wine.  But despite that small thing it was a very pleasant experience in a beautiful setting and I highly recommend it.
