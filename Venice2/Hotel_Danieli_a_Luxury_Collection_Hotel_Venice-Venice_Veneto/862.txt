Rooms were more spacious than any other hotels I've seen built in this time period. A converted mansion--hard to believe all of that was someone's home! Bathrooms were also spacious and well-appointed. Great water pressure and temperature was very responsive. Firm mattress and plenty of TV channels -- though some complimentary movies would have been nice after a long day of traveling.  The lobby walls look like outdoor walls, gorgeous chandeliers on the first floor. Can arrive there on a private boat straight from the train station to their waterway drop-off point. Located right on the Grand Canal--very central and easy to find your way back. Just a block or so away from Saint Mark's Square and the area of the water where you can pick up water taxis.
