Hotel Danieli is one of my favorite hotels in Italy. we stayed at the Danieli for the weekend in mid summer of 2005. The hotel  was built in the 14 century to meet the demands of Europe's traveling nobility. The hotel is beautifully restored to truly represent the splendor and romance of Venice, lavishly decorated with stained glass, ornately carved pink marble and gold leaf columns, murano glass chandeliers and high ceilings decorated with gold leaves. It tuely represents the reach artful Venetian culture.  The hotel is just steps away from Piazza San Marco, Doge's palace. We got the 'suite lagoon view ' room on the top floor with a beautiful balcony over looking the  water and the city.
