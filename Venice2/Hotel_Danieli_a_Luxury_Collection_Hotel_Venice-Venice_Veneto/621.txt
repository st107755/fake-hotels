This is a hard review to write. The Danieli is between average to very good. It just felt it should have been better for the luxury collection.   From past experience I'd go for the Gritti or Cipriani over the Danieli.   Pros: location can't be beat, great concierge desk, rooftop restaurant is excellent especially considering many restaurants with a view have food that doesn't live up to the view. Breakfast buffet on the rooftop was excellent as well and the staff couldn't be better.   Cons:  Grand canal facing room was average at best, sort of paint-by-numbers of what a Venetian decor should be with two uncomfortable chairs and a very small TV.
