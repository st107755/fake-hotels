Ever seen the movie "The Tourist" starring Angelina Jolie and Johnny Depp? If you have, then this hotel would sound quite familiar to you. Before our arrival, my husband and I could not decide between the two Luxury Collection hotels (this one and The Gritti), but we decided to stay at the Danieli which was a few minutes away from La Piazza San Marco. And this goes without saying, but this hotel lives up to its brand name. A true luxury collection hotel. Beautiful property, the rooms with its famous The Doge Dandolo Royal Suite (which we did not stay in unfortunately!) and The Dodge Suite (which we stayed in!). The suite was just beautiful.
