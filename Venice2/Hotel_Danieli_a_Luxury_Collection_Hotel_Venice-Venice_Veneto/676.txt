The hotel is very well located, has really nice roof-top restaurant overlooking the Grand Canal (both the breakfast and lunch were simply perfect) and the rooms are very spacious (even my bathroom was bigger then some hotel rooms in Paris or Brussels). The staff was professional and helpful. What should be improved: some parts of the hotel are now outdated (talking about the conference area) and the check-in and check-out procedures are too lengthy and formalistic (it took 4 separate signatures to check in:() When you pay the sky-high price they are charging, you should not be bother with formalism...
