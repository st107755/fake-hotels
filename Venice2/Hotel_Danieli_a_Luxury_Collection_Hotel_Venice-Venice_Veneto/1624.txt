Oddly the room seemed to have no hotel directory (setting out hotel services etc) and the concierge said I could find it all out through the TV intranet service.  I gave up after 20 fruitless minutes.  The public areas are amazing architecturally, if a little lacking in atmosphere. The hotel is in a wonderful location.  Even though it was March we were lucky with good weather and breakfast on the roof terrace was a delight.
