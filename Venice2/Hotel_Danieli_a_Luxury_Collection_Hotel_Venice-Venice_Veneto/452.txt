We wanted to be on the terrasse and see directly the fireworks, we got that. At a price, it was beautiful. The location just abutting the doges palace is premium. We got the smallest room/closet ( no view of course) we have ever gotten in ages. Everything in order but aged. The « free » water was not replaced. The extravagant new year dinner was beautiful to look at, service was good and the music too. The food was alas non-offensive ie tasteless. Not memorable. The view on the fireworks is memorable and worth the trip. Checking out will try your patience as they insist you sign so long lines with people trying to check in. ( no possibility to email the bill) Excellent concierge service, especially MARCO. The porter came on time and did a good job ( not easy with the high tide…
