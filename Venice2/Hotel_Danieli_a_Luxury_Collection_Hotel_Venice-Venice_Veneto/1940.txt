Hotel is very close to St Marcus square. Of course, a lot of people in this area, but if you turn left from the hotel, you will find areas with less tourists and a nice park.  We stayed in the new wing of the hotel. Rooms are in an old style, with dark red colors. A little to dark and red for my taste, but it felt correct for this type of hotel.
