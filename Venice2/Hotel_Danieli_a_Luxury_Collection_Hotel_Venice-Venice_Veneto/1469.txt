My husband and myself stayed recently in the beautiful  Danieli in Venice.  It was a fantastic experience.  Location could not be better. Service was very good and friendly - however, there was one annoying little glitch regarding the promptness of making up our room.  Despite being asked to do this as we left for sightseeing at 10.15am on our return at 1.00pm or thereabouts the room still had not been made up - very irritating and certainly not the service we expected from this or indeed any 5 star hotel.  We received an apology. Apart from this glitch though our three day stay there was terrific.
