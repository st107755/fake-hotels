20th Wedding Anniversary trip and first trip to Venice. We wanted to do a classic Grand Canal hotel, and the Danielli seemed like the perfect spot. The location is indeed fantastic. A beautiful spot overlooking the Grand Canal and the outer islands. Conveniently located by the primary tourist destinations of St. Mark's Square and Basilica and the Doge's Palace.   The rooftop restaurant and terrace offered perhaps the finest views in Venice, and the food was very good.  The areas around St. Mark's are very very crowded during the day with cruise ship and da-tripper traffic, but by late afternoon the area was quite and serene.
