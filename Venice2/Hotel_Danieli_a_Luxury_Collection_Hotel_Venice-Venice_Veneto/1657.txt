We came to the Danieli for a 2-night stay in January 2014. The location is a real bonus: next to St Mark's Square, a few steps away from a vaporetto stop, right on the Canal. The hotel itself is extremely beautiful, and the public areas were quiet and elegant. On arrival we were treated well and efficiently, and taken to our room straight away. The room was a generous size (and included a tiny view of the Canal, despite the fact that we hadn't paid for a canal-view room), very well equipped, nicely decorated. The bed was large and extremely comfortable. We did not have room service, though noticed that it was extraordinarily expensive (even for a five star hotel).
