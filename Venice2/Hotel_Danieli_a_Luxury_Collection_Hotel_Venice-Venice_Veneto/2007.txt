My Father and Mother spent their honeymoon in Venice and I remember my Father telling me that the Danieli Hotel was the best hotel in Venice and one of the best in Italy, how things have changed! My partner and I arrived at the hotel at 1pm and we were advised that the room would not be ready till after 4pm, this was our first disappointment, so we decided to go and have some lunch we returned at 4.30pm and still the room was not ready, the receptionist apologized and offered us a cocktail.  The room was nice and clean although the bathroom had not been updated for the last 50 years; the shower was connected to the bathroom taps and the water flow very weak!
