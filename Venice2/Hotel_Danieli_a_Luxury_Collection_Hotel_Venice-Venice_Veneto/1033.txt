We stayed for a short break two years ago at this 5star iconic hotel at a special winter rate.  The highlight of our stay....no, not the E45 breakfast (we went to the bar round the corner where gondoliers gathered)....was an eye-wateringly expensive Sacher Torte gateau, so rich we had to share and then lie down in a darkened room afterwards..  Never mind Proust's madeleines, this sublime cake with its gleaming chocolate coating and airy texture stayed in my memory and I was determined to repeat the experience when I returned for a few days this March.  Yes, I knew it would be a silly price for a cup of tea and cake, but why quibble over a beautiful experience.  The waiter took my order but corrected it to "chocolate cake".
