I had viewed pictures of the Hotel Danieli on travel advisory.  I knew of the reputation but was not sure of what to expect of an old hotel.  I can only tell you that when I got off of the motor boat and entered the hotel it just took my breath away.  It made you feel like you took a step back in time. The lobby was decorated beautifully and in the center of the lobby a  Christmas tree stood from floor to ceiling.    I reserved my room with twin beds since I was meeting my son who was backpacking in Europe for the Holidays.  Two rooms would just be to expensive.  Upon arrival I was instructed that I would be upgraded to a larger room at no cost.  I was taken to my room and when I entered it was not one but a two room suite.
