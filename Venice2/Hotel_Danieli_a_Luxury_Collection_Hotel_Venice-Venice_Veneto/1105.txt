My husband surprised me with an  trip to Venice! We stayed here for 5 nights! The hotel itself was wonderful and opulent. The reception areas are so grand and well kept, it was a pleasure to sit and admire the beautiful architecture. Check in and out was smooth and personal. We had cocktails at the downstairs bar every evening and the waiters would serve us canapés and have a little chat with us, they always remembered us and we were greeted with a smile as we sat listening to the resident pianist play! The waiters even said goodbye to us on our final evening which I though was a nice touch!
