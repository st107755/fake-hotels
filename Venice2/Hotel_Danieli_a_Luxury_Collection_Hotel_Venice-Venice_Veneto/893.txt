After reading the previous review I felt even more compelled to leave my contribution on the Danieli in Venice.  True, it is an iconic hotel which epitomizes the city of Venice.  True, there are some pretty tacky travellers, some of which may choose to stay there....but I really don’t know what the Danieli has to do with it!!! I am sure that if they could be more selective of their guests, many would be sleeping in a tent on Riva degli Schiavoni....but with the advent of online booking it is not possible to have any control over who comes to your hotel.  Now that I have gotten out of my system my little 5 minutes of irritation on account of the previous review, I would like to say that we found the experience at the Danieli to be a very pleasant one.
