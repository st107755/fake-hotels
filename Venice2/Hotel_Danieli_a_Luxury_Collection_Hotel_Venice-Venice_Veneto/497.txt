 Lovely premises, one of a kind, full of charm and in a most convenient location for all that Venice has to offer. The Restaurant on the 4th floor has an open terrace affording quite amazing views and is a glorious place to be over lunch in the mild early autumn sun. Service overall was not too shabby by the standards of old Europe and they did go out of their way to afford me that slightly later check out time.  Having said all that and with my hand firmly on my heart, i am finding great difficulty in recommending the Danieli whole heartedly. They have become perhaps a tad complacent.
