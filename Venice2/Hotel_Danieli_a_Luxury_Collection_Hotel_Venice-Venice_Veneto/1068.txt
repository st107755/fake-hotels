We had only one evening and day in Venice and were welcomed to the Danieli.  The foyer is Grand and Elegant, room spacious and decorated with beautiful tapestry fabrics.     A lovely treat for a quick get away.  Having stayed at several Luxury hotels of late, it was on par with the exception of a little bit of attitude from one of the Concierges. (Which is a shame as I would probably have given 5 stars had it not been for the concierge)  We have stayed in hotels with celebrities and Presidents in the last year and even with heightened security while Presidents were in residence we have never been treated with anything other than exceptional grace and respect as fellow guests.
