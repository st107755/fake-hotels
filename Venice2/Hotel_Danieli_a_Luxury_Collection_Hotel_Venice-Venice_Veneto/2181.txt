I had read all the reviews about this hotel and was expecting it to be pretty bad. I booked it basically to experience the location. Overall the hotel really is just average. Most of the staff made half-hearted attempts at customer service, sadly for me this was all spoilt by extremely rude staff in the restaurant. Given how much they are pushing the restaurant’s appearance in the film The Tourist, you think they might have bothered to actually bring the service standard up to 5 star. The location is an experience to have once in your life so we booked a canal view room. The room was quite nice. The furnishings are getting pretty old but on the whole it was fine.
