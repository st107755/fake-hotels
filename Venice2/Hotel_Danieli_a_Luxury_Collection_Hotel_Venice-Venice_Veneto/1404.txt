Used my starwood points for the first time to stay at Hotel Danieli over New Year's Eve.  It has been my dream ever since I was a poor college student studying abroad in Venice, to stay at the Danieli and I am so happy that it actually lived up to my dreams!  Even though I was using points for my room over maybe the busiest few days of the year, they upgraded my husband and I to a beautiful suite and gave us free sparkling wine.  Each evening we had delicious cocktails at the iconic bar, dazzled by the Murano glass chandeliers, opulent lobby and beautiful Christmas tree.  The concierge was at times a bit brusque, but we asked them for many recommendations and they always came through.
