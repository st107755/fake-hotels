My wife and I lived for a year in Vicenza a few years ago. Vicenza is only 45 minutes from Venice so we traveled there often and are very familiar with the town. Being so close however, we never stayed in Venice. We decided that for our return visit this spring we'd splurge a little and stay in the Danieli where we used to go for a drink on their rooftop terrace once and a while. While expensive for your average traveler, we enjoyed our three night stay at the Danieli more than any of our recent travels. The service staff were polite, gracious, and accomodating. The hotel itself is magnificent. The rooftop terrace provides a wonderful place to enjoy a drink while gazing over the lagoon.
