From the moment I step inside the Danieli, I am bewitched. The beauty of its lobby is overwhelming -  and the beauty of the interior of this elegant palazzo is accented every time I visit by magnificent flower decorations. The reception and the concierge desks are professional, experienced and exceptionally courteous. It is a joy to be with them every time because this is another thing this hotel does right- continuity of personnel, so you know when you arrive that you will meet a friend.  The restaurant also is outstanding - elegant and with a roof terrace that has the best view in Venice.
