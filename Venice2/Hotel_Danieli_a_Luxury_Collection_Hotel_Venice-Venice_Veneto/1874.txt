The view out of our room was mesmerising, overlooking the canal with Punta della Dogana and Isola di San Giorgio Maggiore just across. It feels as you have stepped in one of this 18th century paintings of Venice! If you decide to stay in this hotel, then I would definitely recommend you pay that bit extra and get a room with a view. The hotel itself is very classic in style, the lobby is absolutely unbelievable with its very high ceilings and very ornate. The rooms follow a similar classic style (but with all modern amenities),  and even if it is not what we would normally go for, it perfectly fits with Venice!
