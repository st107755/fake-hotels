The highlights:  1. Arriving in a water taxi at the private dock to the side of the hotel – a magical experience that made me feel like Sophia Loren.  2. The spectacular and very beautiful interior which, unlike many hotels, hasn’t been spoilt by naff modernization.   3. Eating a vast breakfast on the beautiful roof terrace while looking across at San Giorgio Maggiore – a fabulous start to the day.   4. Great people watching, both at breakfast and in the beautiful lobby bar. Had to explain to husband that man walking around in what looked like a petrol pump attendant's PVC jacket was in fact dressed head to toe in Vetements probably at a cost of several thousand pounds :)  5. The discreet, professional staff, all of whom were charming. Seamless service e.g. housekeeping answered…
