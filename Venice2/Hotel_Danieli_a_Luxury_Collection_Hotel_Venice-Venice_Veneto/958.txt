I felt like I was a guest of a Doge. The location is absolutely the very center of Venice. The streets might be full of tourist with folks going every which way, but then you duck in Hotel Danieli and all is calm with the staff there to take care of you. You get to drop your key off with the bellmen that get to know you and don't ask for your ID every time you return. The rooms are nice although the AC isn't always the best. We just asked for a box fan, opened the windows and we honestly felt more connected as we slept with the peaceful sounds of an ancient city that truly sleeps each night.
