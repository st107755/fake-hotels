We got back after a short break to Venice. We contemplated staying at either Griti Palace or Danieli but opted for the Danieli as it was our first time to Venice and the Danieli has a great entrance. Ideally we should have spent 2 nights at each.  We got a room in the old part of the hotel overlooking the Lagoon which had excellent views. As many people have commented, the rooms are showing their age but that didn't detract from the experience. The only negative I would say about having the view of the lagoon is that in the mornings, you can hear the hustle and bustle of people outside.
