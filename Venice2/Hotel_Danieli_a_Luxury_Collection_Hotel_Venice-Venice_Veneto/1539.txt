My wife and I visited here for her 30th Birthday meal. And from the moment we arrived we were not disappointed. The reception area is fantastic and can actually be seen in the film The Tourist. The restaurant felt a little dated to me and i would imagine that this place really come in to its own when you can sit outside on the balcony over looking the grand canal. The food and service were excellent and i was particularly impressed with the chocolate desert but i did feel it was little expensive and the atmosphere was a little amiss as there was almost nobody there.
