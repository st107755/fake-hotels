My husband and I stayed here for two nights and did not want to leave. We fell totally in love with this beautiful old palace, it's history, and of course, Venice. The hotel is absolutely stunning from the moment you walk in- the main staircase and lobby are breathtaking. The peach bellini at the downstairs bar is the best, and listening to the live pianist each night is such a treat. Our room made us feel like royalty- located in the main building, with huge windows, and with a view of the Grand Canal. The staff is always courteous and helpful. Breakfast each morning takes place on the roof terrace, with yet another beautiful view of the Grand Canal. We will definitely be back!
