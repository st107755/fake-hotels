Why Hotel Danieli has such a brand name is beyond me. I spent last weekend there with my family and it was simply a huge disappointment from start to finish.  No one greeted us when we entered the foyer although plenty of staff were standing around. They looked bored to death. Check-in took forever as the reception staff didn't seem to know what they were doing. Albeit spacey, the room was dusty, dark and noisy. The room service coffee pots were covered in what must have been a month worth of dust. The choice on the breakfast buffet was limited and the bread was not fresh. The fruit salad was good, though.
