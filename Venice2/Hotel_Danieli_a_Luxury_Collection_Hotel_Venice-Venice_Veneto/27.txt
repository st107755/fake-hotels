We stayed at the Danieli for 3 night this summer and although we had a rough start with our checkin and room, the Manager stepped up on the next day and made everything perfect.  The hotel lives up to its beauty and overall esthetics. The location is perfect and the rooftop restaurant provides one of the best views as well as a great buffet breakfast.  We are Ambassador Elite with Bonvoy and blew all our points on 3 nights including a Suite upgrade. We spend the 1st night in a very large suite but honestly it wasn’t very pleaseant. The layout didn’t make sense and the living room was overrun with furniture that didn’t make sense. On the morning we asked to change and due to exceptional Manager service we were given the room of our choice... not a large Suite but the very pink…
