Perfect location, adjacent to St Marks Square, plus so useful having its own docking place for boats, which then take you directly to the door.  Pay the extra for a deluxe lagoon view room as it will enhance your visit.  (Suspect our great experience of this hotel may have been marred if in a room with a lesser view).  Massive comfy bed, clean room and bathroom.  Ask for a plastic mat for inside the bath as can be slippery.   The Rooftop terrace is just such a romantic place to eat.  Great having breakfast there or afternoon drinks.  Very wide choice of food for breakfast, which was excellent in every way.  Caters for vegetarians.
