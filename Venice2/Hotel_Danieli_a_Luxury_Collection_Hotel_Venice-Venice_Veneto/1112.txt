Ok - so I will let you know that spending New Years at Hotel Danieli will not be for the budget conscious.  So if you can afford to splurge, Hotel Danieli is THE spot to be for New Year's Eve.  It will set you back 300 Euros/person.  There are two options for NYE.  There's the formal sit-down dinner in the terrace restaurant or the more fun "cocktail lounge" party downstairs.  I originally was a bit skeptical since it's buffet food in a ballroom.  But what the description failed to include was that the ballroom opened up to the main street where the fireworks were RIGHT IN FRONT.  So at midnight, we were able to leave the ballroom, go outside to see the fireworks but still hear the DJ music.  The DJ was amazing!  It was a beautiful Italian woman and she rocked it!
