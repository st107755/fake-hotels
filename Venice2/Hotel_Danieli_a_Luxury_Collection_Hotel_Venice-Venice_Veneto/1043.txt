My wife and I spent four wonderful nights in this palatial hotel and enjoyed almost every minute of it. The only slight fly in the ointment, apart from getting stuck in a lift, was the temperature in the room overnight. Even though it was only April the room did get a tad on the hot side and the air-con system would only heat, not cool. Did we let this upset us?   No because everything else was first rate. From the moment we stepped through the door on day one to the moment we left we were treated like honoured guests.  We were personally escorted to our room by one of the front of house staff, who gave a detailed explanation of the hotel's facilities and where to find everything.
