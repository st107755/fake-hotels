My husband and I recently stayed at the Hotel Danieli for four nights of our multi-part vacation. It couldn't have been a more wonderful and relaxing location for us!   I had read the other reviews regarding the staff not being friendly but found all of the staff to be very welcoming and check in went seamlessly upon arrival.   Our room (225, I believe) was gorgeous and overlooked the lagoon. There was a complimentary bottle of wine and cookies waiting in our room which were delicious! We spent wonderful hours sitting with the balcony doors open while reading and watching the boat traffic go by.
