The Danieli is a landmark renaissance palace overlooking the lagoon in Venice. I have stayed there before. There are many wonderful attributes to this hotel. The original building lobby is breathtaking. The location is unbeatable. The rooftop dining area is a fantasy come true. The room I was given however was in desperate need of renovation. Dowdy, and drab. The “king” bed was two twins shoved together which is commonplace in Europe but there was no connector to keep the beds from splitting apart and one night I found myself on the floor in the middle of the bed. I had a room on the 2 floor facing the lagoon but immediately right outside my windows were thousands of tourists at the same level as my windows on ramps and stairs, always looking in, and the view that I had was not…
