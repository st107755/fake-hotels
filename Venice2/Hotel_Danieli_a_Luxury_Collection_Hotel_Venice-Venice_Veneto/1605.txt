I have stayed in many places In Venice over the years, this is one of the best, pricey but worth the special occasion, if you only come once to Venice in your life time spend the extra and have a time of your life.  If you can secure yourself a room with a balcony overlooking the lagoon, do it you won't be disappointed, amazing views of the busy gondoliers, cruise ships coming past your window only 200 meters away and watch the venetian lifestyle pass you by.  Only one bad point is the Internet and the pay tv is not the best. It quite often stops working and after paying top dollar I feel that should not happen.
