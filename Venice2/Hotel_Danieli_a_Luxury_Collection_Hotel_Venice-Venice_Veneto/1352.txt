The Danieli has a reputation for elegance and service as well as it's prime location.  However, what we found was a hotel living on it's past reputation. While the location is prime being on the Grand Canal and so close to Saint Marks it is not enough to persuade me to stay there again. The first unpleasant experience came at the lobby bar/grill where I paid approximately $18 for a grilled ham & cheese sandwich and $11 for a cup of coffee--no refill! The next negative experience came at breakfast which is served on the beautiful 5th floor terrace overlooking the Grand Canal--spectacular view but service not so much.
