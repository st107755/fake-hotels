My wife and I spent 4 days in Venice at this hotel. The hotel is in part a palatial venue. We were upgraded to a suite and the furnishings were elegant. The room was as quiet as one would expect and enjoy. However to our minds the most outstanding thing was the service. My wife has allergies and I had forgotten to notify the hotel we needed feather and dust reductions. After getting to our room I called housekeeping and in less than hour everything in the bedroom was changed out. Also the shower door was hung incorrectly so that it would not open properly. A call quickly resulted in the door being fixed so it worked perfectly. All this service and splendid location comes at a price, but if you want to go first class this is the hotel for you.
