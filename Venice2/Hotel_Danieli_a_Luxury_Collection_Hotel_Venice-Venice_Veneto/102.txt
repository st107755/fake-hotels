If you have never stayed at the Hotel Danieli, you do not know what you are missing. It is one of the Best Hotel experiences you can have in Venice... That's saying a lot with the Gritti Palace just right down the Canal. When you arrive, the Water Taxi doesn't just drop you off at the landing... Oh No... The Taxi pulls up to the side of the Hotel where there is a Portal for Guests to directly enter the hotel from the Water Taxi. The Bellman takes your luggage from the Taxi Driver and you are in the Hotel Danieli. The property is classic and Beautiful. The front Door is amazing and the Professional Service is all you can expect.
