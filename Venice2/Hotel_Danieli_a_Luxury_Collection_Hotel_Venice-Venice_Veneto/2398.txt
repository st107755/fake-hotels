The Hotel Danieli has location, location, location, snob appeal, and somewhat disappointing rooms.   My main complaint was the uncomfortable (antique?) mattress.  From other TripAdvisor comments, I see we are not alone in this regard.  A Luxury Collection hotel, part of the Starwood properties, should  have beds of quality equal to the "heavenly beds" which make Sheraton stays so pleasant. We stayed in the main buidling and our room on the fourth floor was small, clean and very quiet (you can't complain about hearing the gondoleers... this is Venice after all).  The food and service in the rooftop Terrazza restaurant was terrific and they are not shy about charging for it.
