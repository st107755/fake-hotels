We were very apprehensive about staying here after reading some of the reviews. However, from arriving on a Sunday afternoon, staying 4 nights in a very comfortable bed and departing after breakfast on Thursday, we found all the staff to be helpful and courteous - we throughly enjoyed our stay.  The hotel building is very old and full of character, it is in a prime location, probably the best - which is why we chose this hotel after a bit of research. As it was July the hotel was very busy. Our room although not a lagoon view was pleasant and not a bad size, bathroom was also fine.
