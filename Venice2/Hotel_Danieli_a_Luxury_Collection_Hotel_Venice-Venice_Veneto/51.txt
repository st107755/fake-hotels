My wife and I stayed at the Danieli for six nights in September. We had a beautiful room with a lovely view of the Grand Canal. The decor of the room let you know you were in Venice. The public rooms, especially the lobby and the lobby bar are gorgeous. The Terrazzo Danieli restaurant where they serve breakfast has an outdoor terrace with a lovely view over the Grand Canal and the lagoon. On a sunny morning having your breakfast there is breathtaking. The buffet breakfast is one of the better breakfasts we have had at various luxury hotels.  The staff at both the reception desk and the Concierge desk are knowledgeable and helpful.
