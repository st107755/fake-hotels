This historical hotel is one if the icons of Venice hospitality. The guest list is an A+ list of the world's movers and shakers.   The hotel itself is actually three hotels in one: the original Danieli Palace hotel, a pretty invisible second building, and a newer, gauche modern-era wing. Historic buffs will prefer the original building, complete with inconsistent room sizes, high-low ceilings, irregular shapes, older bathrooms and some stunning views.   The second oldest building is sort of drab and in need of some TLC. The rooms here and also varied and some afford amazing views, and oddly dated floral spreads, walls and grandma colors.
