This hotel is living history; it’s a modern classic and a destination in its own right.  It is one of the worlds “grand dames” and it was a privilege to have been, even for such a short period of time, part of that!  We occupied a superior room during Carnivale.  It depresses me to read comments by others complaining about petty detail such as minor damage to furniture- get real please, these are antiques not Ikea!  I fully endorse all the many positive comments by others especially regarding the staff who were ALL outstanding!  Enjoy this hotel, spend time there rather than continually pounding the “streets”, enjoy a leisurely cocktail in the bar with the multitude of “freebie” nibbles the expert barmen provide,listen to the piano player and in the mornings  enjoy a magnificent…
