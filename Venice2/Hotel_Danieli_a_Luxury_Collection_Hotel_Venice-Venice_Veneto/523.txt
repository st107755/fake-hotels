This hotel is five star all the way!  The location can't be beat, as it is steps away from Doges Palace and St. Mark's Square , and the vaporetto (public water shuttle) stop is directly outside in front.  Restaurants and shops are right around the corner or on the promenade facing the water.  The beautiful outdoor terrace is a wonderful place to have breakfast or dinner, as the views are stunning!  The staff is helpful, and the concierges very accommodating in making dinner reservations or guiding you to the sights.  The hotel is made up of three buildings; the main building where you check in was the original palace of Doge Dandolo in approximately the 14th century.
