We stayed in this beautiful hotel for 5 nights and, even though we didn't have a water view, we delighted at the peace and quiet of our room.  The Danieli is right in the heart of the old city, a few minutes walk from St Mark's Square and with fantastic views across the lagoon to the S Giorgio Maggiore which can be enjoyed from the rooftop restaurant.  A sumptuous buffet breakfast is served there and dinner, of course.  We at as part of a group one night and were impressed by the speed of service and the quality of the food - no easy task for a large group.  Some hotels allow casual tourists in but not this one - you can get no further than reception unless you want to buy a drink in the decadently decorated bar and lounge.
