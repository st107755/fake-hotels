Two years ago we stayed here on our 20th anniversary and found our stay incredible.  Our return was even better, we had a flawless four days.  Both times we stayed in rooms with views of the lagoon from balconies that opened through French doors.  We travelled from Athens with good friends who stayed at other hotels, we all had dinner at the terrace restaurant, what a incredible experience.  The food was excellent, creative and the service impeccable.  Our stay wasn't inexpensive, but was worth every Euro.  One evening they put on a 45 minute fireworks display just for us and our friends just outside our room (our friends thought it was a celebration ending the plague, but I'm sure it was for us).
