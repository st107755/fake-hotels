We stayed at Hotel Danieli the day before our cruise ship was to depart Venice. This is a gorgeous hotel that dates back to the 14th Century. A beautiful lobby with a grand staircase and chandeliers. The neatest thing about the hotel is that it is located along one of the many canals in Venice, and they have a private dock for gondolas and water taxis!  Our room was excellent; it featured comfy beds, a marble bathroom (complete with a bidet), and a balcony that overlooked the canal and the surrounding area of Venice. Thte breakfast buffet in the morning was to die for: excellent sausages, pastries, omelettes--pretty much everything you wanted!
