We were woken shortly after 7am on Saturday 13 June 2015 by the rubbish boat and the noise of crashing glass. The boat was directly under our window (Room 103). The General Manger said that there would be no point in changing rooms as the following day was a Sunday. Unfortunately, as Philippe Back (Front Office Manager) explained on Sunday, the General Manager had not stayed in the hotel on Saturday night and was not aware that the rubbish boat does indeed operate on a Sunday. It was moored below our window on Saturday night and the noise started shortly after 8am – on a Sunday. The boat has a very noisy engine and it moved off at 8.20am.
