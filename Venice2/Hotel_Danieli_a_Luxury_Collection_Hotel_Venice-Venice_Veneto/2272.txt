I stayed 2 nights before and after a cruise out of Venice. First the good: gorgeous setting and palazzo,  comfy king size bed, free slippers, beautiful restaurant with gourmet food, nice concierge who says buongiorno. Now the bad: check in and check out was a nightmare both times, wait an hour in line, the reception desk are slow, surly and make a big show of escorting you to your room while 20 others wait in line. Can't the bellhops do this with your luggage? Reception staff stare at computer screens for an eternity and seem to be arguing forever in french, spanish, etc at the person checking in over prices...I had made my reservation online and I thought it would be a snap, but be prepared to stand there for 1-1.5 hours, no joke.
