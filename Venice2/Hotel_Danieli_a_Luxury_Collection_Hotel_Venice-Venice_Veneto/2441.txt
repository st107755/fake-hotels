Although well located with an impressive lobby, the place is clearly living on a reputation it no longer deserves. The prices are ridiculous and can in no way justify either the mediocre service, or the more mediocre rooms. Our room on the Grand Canal had a huge (I mean huge) water stain running down the wallpaper, a poorly working air conditioner, a chandilier with 4 of its 7 lights burned out, and a bed that clearly has not been replaced since the Danieli was built. There simply is no reason on earth to spend the sums demanded of this Hotel for a product that is not worth the price under any measure.
