A beautiful historical property and a renowned name. In the peak season it feels the staff simply cannot cope and the lobby is like a crowded railway station. Whilst one may expect elegant and cultured guests, it is surprising to see so many "shorts and tee shirt" and the atmosphere of a tour group hotel. It is an undoubted commercial success and its rates reflect the demand however, as someone who stays in 5 and even 6 star properties, I will be surprised if the percentage of return guests is high. The service and professionalism simply do not match the name or the property. I would not recomend a suite as there is no distinction whatsoever, in service or attention and if things don't work you must accept it as they are very busy and full.
