It was our first time in Venice and we spent two magnificent nights at the Danieli.  If I get a chance to visit the city again, it will be hard to stay anyplace else.  Service was magnificent and everyone I interacted with was exceptional - concierge, front desk, housekeeping.    I wondered if the hotel could be as spectacular as it looked in the photos on line, and it was.  We were happy to bring friends by to see the hotel and our room!    Prior to our arrival, the concierge had reached out offering to help with transfers or any other needs.  I did ask them to help arrange transfer from the airport to the hotel and it was very well worth it.
