My husband and I researched the net onboard our cruiseship and decided to simply walk in and book it after our cruise as a final splurge before leaving beautiful Venice. We wanted to be near the Grand Canal and were looking for 5 star luxury, so we were not disappointed. However, our room seemed to take a long time to get ready- nearly 2 hours after normal checkin time! If you don't mind spending about $700 Australian (400 Euro) for a night in a suite then this is the place for you. We didn't splurge on breakfast though- it was about $60 Australian (45 Euro) per person overlooking the grand canal, so we looked for a cafe.
