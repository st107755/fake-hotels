Hotel Danieli is in a great location a few hundred yards from St Marks Square and next to the famous Bridge of Sighes. We arrived by the hotels own Water Taxi which costs 180 Euros compared to the freelance Water Taxi which charges 70 Euros and there is no difference at all. The Hotel is impressive with great architecture and lovely rooms but it lacks any personalisation. We were celebrating a special anniversary and when booking they promised to organise little touches to make the trip special. This was forgotten and no mention was made. For a 5 star hotel little things like forgetting to make up the room, not replacing soaps and shampoo’s , a standard printed note of welcome all smacked of a formulaic rather than caring approach.
