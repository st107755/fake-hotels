A very beautiful historic grand hotel in a great location. This hotel is right next to Piazza San Marco on the Venice lagoon. So while it's a great location it is in a very busy tourist area.I recommend arriving in a Water Taxi. We paid 60 euro from the train station but it is a special experience. We used the Vaporetto (water bus) to return to the station which worked out since there was a storm and the larger water bus cuts through the chops easily. The Vaporetto was only 6.5 euro per person and since we had no oversize luggage there were no extra charges. Both the water taxi and water bus drop you off close to the hotel.
