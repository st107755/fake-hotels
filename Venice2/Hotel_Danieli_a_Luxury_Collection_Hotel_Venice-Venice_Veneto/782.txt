Due to a booking mishap, we had to leave our hotel we were staying at and made lemonade from lemons and found a room for two nights at the iconic Hotel Danieli. We found the booking easily via Starwood's site and prepaid for breakfast and the room, and it went very smoothly from there. I was excited to have the opportunity to stay at a hotel that is legendary.   Well, our stay was good, but I expected more from a hotel of this class. We had an upgrade (which was nice, thank you!) to a suite… room 94. And they accommodated early check-in which was also super great since we had to lug our luggage from the previous hotel to this one. Thank you! The room was clean and had old school elegance. However, there is an odd mix of old school with new technology with neither…
