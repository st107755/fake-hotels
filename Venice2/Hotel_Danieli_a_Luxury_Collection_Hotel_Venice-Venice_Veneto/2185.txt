2 nights at the Danieli were included on a recent tour to Croatia, and we were initially impressed that we would be staying at this glamorous and renowned hotel. However, we discovered their reputation to be undeserved in terms of value received. Our room was smallish and needed refurbishing in terms of the shabby furniture and drapes. The bathroom was fine, but certainly not spacious, and the windows looked out onto the walls of another building.  All this would be OK if the room rate wasn't outrageous. Our breakfast buffet was lovely and the view from the terrace magical, but it would have cost 68 euros if it hadn't been included in our tour.
