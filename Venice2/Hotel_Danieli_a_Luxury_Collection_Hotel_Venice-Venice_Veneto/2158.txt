The Danieli has a great reputation. My husband and I spent several days there in August 2011 and were very disappointed. We have traveled all over the world and stayed at wonderful, as well as mediocre hotels and The Danieli certainly did not meet our expectations of a great hotel. The staff is arrogant, the concierge shares the barest minimum of information and our room looked as old as it was, with chips in middle of the doors and scratched, torn wallpaper. The place needs an all around upgrade.  We also had to wait in a long line to check in. When we checked in, we were coming off a cruise ship and there was definitely not enough staff to check everyone in.
