We spent two nights at this hotel (Oct 11th and Oct 12th).    ROOM Lovely room. The décor is very "old world" Venice.  The room size was ideal.  Large, walk in closet with plenty of room for all of our bags and then some.  The bathroom was clean and good sized. Lots of room on the vanity for makeup and other sundry items.  The robes were super warm and comfortable.  Quite...we heard no outside noise unless we opened the windows.    PRICING AND LOCATION It is hard for me to rate this place on price because we booked it using points. I understand it is expensive.  I suspect it is because it is literally right on the Grand Canal and the water bus station is right in front.  The location is unbeatable.
