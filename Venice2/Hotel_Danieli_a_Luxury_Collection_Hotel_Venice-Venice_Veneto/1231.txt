We thank the staff for that upgrade but it was booked by my daughter, a  Starwoods platinum member. The hotel can only be reached by water taxi which adds to its charm. This hotel is expensive and acts it. Breakfast is $52 per person but it served on a lovely outdoor terrace overlooking Venice.
