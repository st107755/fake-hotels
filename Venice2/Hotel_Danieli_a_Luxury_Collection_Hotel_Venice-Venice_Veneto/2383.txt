Very centrally located, just a few metres from Piazza San Marco and very convenient for using the vaporetto boats.   However, the whole city of Venice is very beautiful and interesting, and staying a little bit away from the tourist hordes in the San Marco area may make a better Venice experience..   The building is impressive, beautiful and historic and the view from the roof terrace is wonderful.   I had a single room, which was OK, and not too small. No view, though.  Large selection of italian, american and international food for breakfast. Needless to say, eating on the roof terrace is very expensive.   The atmosphere in the hotel is rather pompous.
