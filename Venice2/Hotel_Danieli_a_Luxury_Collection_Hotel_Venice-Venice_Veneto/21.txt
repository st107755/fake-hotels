Just feel that I need to write my review now during our stay at Hotel Danieli. We arrived yesterday, for a few nights, from Verona and were greeted by the most warm welcome by Danieli staff. The Xmas decorations in the lobby were simply magical and stunning. The architecture of the hotel is stunning with intricate woodwork, frameworks, tiling, etc.  What made an impression to us about the Venetians and staff are their resilience, positive outlook and warm hospitality despite the recent events of one of the worse Aqua Alta they’ve experienced in the last 50 years.  We were humbled, amazed and touched as we got to experience a bit of Aqua Alta today!
