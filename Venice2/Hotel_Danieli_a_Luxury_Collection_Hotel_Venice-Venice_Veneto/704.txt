We went to Venice spontaneously, booked the room in this hotel in the very last minute, from the train station the boat took us to the front of the hotel. You can already see this beautiful hotel standing from far. At this moment you just can't wait to get in. The very friendly receptionist welcome us with a big smile, and as a platinum member of SPG we got upgraded to the LAGOON VIEW SUITES room. oh and, when we enter our room we got a bottle of Champagne on the table! This room has everything you could imagine, it has beautiful decoration for their concept (don't expect anything minimalist, this is not type of hotel), the bed was fantastic (very important), and what i really highlight from this room is that it has THE BEST VIEW TO ENJOY VENICE!
