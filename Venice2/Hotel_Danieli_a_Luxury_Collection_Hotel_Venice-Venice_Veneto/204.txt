The location is excellent on the lagoon and next to St. Mark's Square, but our room didn't have a view of any of that so other than when you walk out the front door that wasn't really a great benefit.  I love the history of the hotel, but there is nothing special about the quality of the rooms. Our room was relatively noisy...we could hear people walking in the alley way outside. It was very warm (I suppose we could have opened the window but that would have made the noise worse).  The rooms are very small.  The concierge was very friendly and we enjoyed talking to him but I am glad I did not follow his advice...he said we shouldn't leave for the airport until two hours before our flight.
