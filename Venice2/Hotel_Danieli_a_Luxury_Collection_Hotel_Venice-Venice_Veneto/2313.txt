We have stayed at the Danieli four times, from 2005 to 2008, as Venice is a favorite stop for us in Italy.  The hotel is in a prime location a stone's throw from the Doge's Palace and San Marco.  The staff has always been welcoming, and there is nothing quite like the experience of arriving at the front door of your hotel on a mahogany boat!  The lobby is a testament to the genius of Murano with stunning hand-blown chandeliers, and I enjoyed walking the stairs to each level where portraits and furniture adorn the space and leave you with the impression of palace life.  Rooms are elegant in the old building, and slightly larger in the "newer" annex.
