I have read a lot of the other not so favorable reviews of this hotel and almost cancelled my reservation because of them. I am so glad I didn't (maybe the poor reviews lowered my expectations and that is why I liked it). This is without a doubt a nicely furnished, luxury hotel. This hotel is what all the other chains (Intercontinental, etc.) aspire to be but fall short.  I will admit that there is no way I would spend 600 Euro a night to stay here. (more of a budget thing than a reflection of the hotel.) I used my rewards points to pay for a night at this hotel and it absolutely made my trip to Venice complete.
