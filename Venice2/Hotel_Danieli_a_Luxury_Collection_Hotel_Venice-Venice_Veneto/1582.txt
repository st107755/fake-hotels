Everyone can recall a special, thrilling moment in life.  To me, gently approaching by taxi-boat the grand facade of the Danieli is definitely one of them.  Then the boat will rather perilously (so it seemed) navigate through a very narrow side canal, swarming with gondolas filled with thrilled tourists taking 'selfies', maneuvered by shouting and/or singing gondoliers, all the way to a small side entrance to the hotel, where two porters await to unload your luggage.   Then into the main foyer, with the splendid hall and famous  staircase seen so many times in pictures, as always it looks smaller in real, but it is still spectacular.
