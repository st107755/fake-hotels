I really want to write a glowing report of The Danieli as my husband wisked me here for a surprise anniversary trip. We stayed 4 nights over a long weekend in a top floor room with private balcony overlooking the front of the hotel and the lagoon. OK not a suite in the old building but the next level from that. I'll précis the points: fantastic location for accessibility and views over the lagoon, beautiful historic building with impressive central lobby/staircase/ lounge, rooftop restaurant with same outstanding views, very comfortable bed, refurbished clean room with double glazing. However the room was small - only just large enough for the bed, side cupboards and a desk (small wardrobe & safe/fridge 'built in'), the hotel landings in the 'new' building were fairly dismal,…
