There are things that money can't buy - e. g. love, health, lifetime - and a stay in Venice at Danieli Hotel. Of course Venice is magic, and usually a 5 star etablissement offers good service and quality arrangements, but only at Hotel Danieli you can find history, culture, aesthetics and excellent gastronomy. The main house with breathtaking hall dates back to the 13th Century and the historical details are really fascinating, words are not enough. We even got an update and stayed in a room with the historical windows overlooking the laguna. The furniture is in the typical Venetian barocco so that you feel like Casanova just left.
