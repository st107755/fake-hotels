Arrived 7 pm from the Orient express.  Very slow checking, stood waiting and eventually the cashier checked us in.  Very nice room, quite dark in the evening. A mirror over the desk would be helpful for doing hair. Concierge excellent. I would advise you to pre book the restaurant if you want a table on terrace because we tried to book upon arrival and told it was full for next two days! We persisted and the fabulous concierge got us a dinner table for our second night and we were lucky enough to book the Sunday brunch. Very busy outside, I believe three cruise ships in town!  Overall excellent hotel. Room service food very good. Bar service downstairs very good. There is a sign as you come in to hotel saying residents only but no one seams to police this rule?
