A stunning hotel situated on the lagoon front, next door to the Doge Palace and just around the corner from Saint Marcs Square. San Zachariah is also very close by including some bars / hotels that you can get on the WIFI (acts like a sort of a budget hotspot if walking slowly past).  Tip. If stopping in the San Marco area, it's worth getting the waterbus (vaporetto) from Marco Polo airport (15€) as it saves you having to carry luggage / pushchairs up and down bridges all day whilst locating your hotel). The moment you walk through the revolving doors of the Danieli or step off the Gondola at the stop outside you know this place is special.
