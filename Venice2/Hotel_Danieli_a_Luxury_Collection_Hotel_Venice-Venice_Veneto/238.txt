Back at the Hotel Danieli since two years ago (for architecture biennale). This time, we arrived at the Danieli, and then moved our stay to another hotel. This was a better way to do it. The Danieli has its own private dock, so arrival (and departure) is easy. There was the 140 cm aqua alta three or four days before we arrived, so we were lucky that we arrived at low tide. When aqua alta hits, the hotel is near St Mark's square, so is subject to flooding. This affects the use of the hotel dock, and getting on and off your private water taxi. However, no issues with low tide. Check the city of Venice website for the easy to read chart with the tide times and heights, if you are worried. Very useful for planning your day!!
