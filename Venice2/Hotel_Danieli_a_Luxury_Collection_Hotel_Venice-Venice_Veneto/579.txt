Upon our arrival we took a seat near the bar and had some very tasty cocktails and bar snacks while we waited for our room it was a little pricey but not enough to stop us from continuing with cocktails even after receiving our Room keys. Our room had air conditioning, a large bathroom, a walk in closet, and a reception area when you first walk in ( a lot more than you get in London for similar money).
