What a fabulous hotel.  If you like old world charm and antique furniture and fittings then pick this hotel.  The room was surprisingly large, plenty of storage and the bathroom again was very large in comparison to modern hotels.  We had booked a Deluxe room, we were offered on arrival a room at the front of the hotel for an extra charge, to which we declined, and pleased we did as the room we had was on the small canal side and very peaceful, I can only imagine that although we would have had excellent views of the grand canal, it would have been noisy as it was very busy at the waterfront.
