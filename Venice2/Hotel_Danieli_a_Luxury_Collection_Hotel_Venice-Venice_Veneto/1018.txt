My wife and I booked a midweek getaway to Venice.  We booked a room in The Danieli. What a pleasant surprise when we arrived  there. It's literally 30 M from San Marco square.   The main lobby is a work of art and the room we were given (at a more than reasonable price) overlooked the lagoon. The balcony was more like a terrace!  The room was very nice and had all the facilities one would expect from a  Palace.  We arrived very late and had to order room service. The food was decent and arrived within 15 minutes. Worked out perfectly with the complimentary bottle of bubbly that was waiting for us on ice.  All the staff were polite and more than helpful.
