Expensive but worth a visit as the hotel is a true classic with its history and location close to St Marks Square.  Handy too if you are SPG loyalty member and decide to use your points to offset the cost. We also chose to split our stay with two nights here and at the Molino Stucky which can be reached by taking the free shuttle boat from the landing dock near the Danieli. Stayed in a luxury double overlooking the roof tops to the Palazzo Ducale and the room was truly refective of venetian gothic with Doge red drapes, gilt mirror and marble bathroom. Bed was comfy and room was more than adequate for our stay.
