We only popped in one chilly evening for a warm drink which was served with Biscotti. Longed to linger, but such luxury is prohibitive to me. We only saw the public areas, the foyer and lounge area. There was a good pianist who played to our requests.  The location is ideal on the Grand Canal and very central for exploring Venice.  I promise myself that one day I shall return to this opulent hotel. The service was punctilious, the surroundings are sumptuous. Sadly, I returned to our rented holiday apartment, which was adequate. But when I need some pampering and luxury, I shall know just where to go
