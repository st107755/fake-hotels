If you want to splurge and get the most incredible balcony in all of Venice, I highly recommmend, the Hotel Danieli room 101 ( I believe) It is on the top floor of the main part and historic part of the hotel. It has an approx. 30foot balcony overlooking the Grand Canal. The room is impeccably furnished and has a beautiful sitting room and full large walk-in closet. Every room in the suite has balcony access except for the bathroom which instead has a big window that you can view the Grand Canal in. The staff was extremely friendly, warm and professional. I have stayed at other hotels and this is by far the best. The restaurant is exceptional and you must have the breakfast!!
