The Danieli is a special experience. Arriving by water taxi from the airport direct to the hotel entrance is a treat in itself...and then when you get inside, you start to understand why the hotel has such strong reviews. It has to have one of the most beautiful hotel entrances anywhere in the world.   Considering the size of the hotel, we thought the service from all the staff was extremely good, given the pace of activities going on all around them. Our room was very good and reasonably spacious by European standards. We did not have a view, but I'm sure the cost of this extra would have been somewhat beyond our budget.
