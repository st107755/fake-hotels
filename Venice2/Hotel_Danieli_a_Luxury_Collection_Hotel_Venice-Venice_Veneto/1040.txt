We loved the Danieli; the historic building, the location, the staff, the room, the terrace, all wonderful. We had an executive suite on the 4th floor which was spacious, clean and beautiful. We had a view of the roof tops (on juliette balconies that opened) which was actually romantic and lovely.  Not the Grand Canal but we prefer to pay for a big suite (separate lounge/bedroom) rather than a small room with a view of the canal which were actually similar prices.  Be aware that a view of the canal in Venice from your room will cost you. Unless off course you can afford 3000 euros/night for a suite with a view!
