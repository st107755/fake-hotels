Of course nothing bad can be said about the palace itself, which is absolutely magical and leaves you speechless. We were escorted to our bedroom and explained the structured of premises, how it is made of three different parts.  Our bedroom was in the new part of the building, nicely decorated and spacious. With a small entrance, a walk in wardrobe and a big bed. In terms of services, I must say it was disappointing as in they do not provide anything "extra". During the 3 nights we spent there we weren't given particular attention and we did not notice any nice touches apart from small chocolates left in the bedroom in the evening.
