So i decided to book two rooms with lagoon view and connecting door to make our stay a bit more impressive. With a little smile the gentleman at reception decided to upgrade us and when we saw what he meant by that we were totally blown away. We were shown to one of their famous signature suites with a little balcony overlooking the channel.
