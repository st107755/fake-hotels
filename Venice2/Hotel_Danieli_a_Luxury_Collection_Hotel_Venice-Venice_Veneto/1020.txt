The arrival by water taxi makes you feel special. We had just moved from another hotel in Venice and it's a tough call to berate Danieli as it's location, staff and breakfast are excellent, however I'd sacrifice the location to seek higher quality in the future. The staff are excellent ( some if the best I've witnessed in Italy), but they need to be to make up for the tired furnishings and décor in the hotel.  I understand it has to maintain its traditional look, but you can do this with fresher newer quality.  It is true that the hotel has a great name, and the excellent concierge staff do their magic to doors open around Venice for their customers, but it could easily be the best if they sorted out a refresh program.
