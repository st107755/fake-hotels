Had some trepidation about booking our stay at The Danieli based on mixed reviews, but I am glad that we chose this hotel.  After lots of research and phone calls to Starwood, I was able to obtain the SPG Cash + Points rate for our 3 nights here. This rate is NOT advertised so call Starwood directly! Check-in was very good.  We had requested early arrival, and when we got to the hotel, we were told that we had been upgraded to a suite BUT our room would not be ready until after 2.  This was not a great option since we had come off from a cruise and had tour plans for that afternoon in Venice.
