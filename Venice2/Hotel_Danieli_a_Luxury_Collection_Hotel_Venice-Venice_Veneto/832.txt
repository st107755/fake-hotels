Recently returned from a two-night (26th - 28th Sept '16) - but all too short stay at this fabulous hotel. It is the second time we've visited and it could not have been more pleasant. We were upgraded to a suite, which was a lovely surprise and soon fully immersed ourselves in the relaxing atmosphere of the historic Danieli. The staff were - as before - polite, helpful and wonderfully obliging. Nothing is too much trouble and any requests are greeted with a smile and enthusiasm. Breakfast on the terrace was fantastic - as per our previous experience - and we were blessed with sunshine for the duration of our stay which gave us a great start in the morning.
