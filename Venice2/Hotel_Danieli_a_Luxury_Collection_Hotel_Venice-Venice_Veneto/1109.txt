I booked this hotel as a surprise for my fiancé, and what a pleasant surprise it was! Apart from what you can expect from a five star hotel ( excellent rooms, bathrooms, amenities etc) this hotel does really deserve an extra star. It's stunningly gorgeous, perfect location, but best of all, the staff were incredible. I've stayed at plenty of excellent hotels before and the staff is always kind and becoming, but at the Danieli they were genuinely kind. Everytime we came down all of them smiled wholeheartedly at us, they were eager to help with any query or folly that we had and they offered to take our pictures when we came down all dressed up for dinner :)  My fiancé and I had an unforgettable time in Venice, and the staff definitely contributed to that!
