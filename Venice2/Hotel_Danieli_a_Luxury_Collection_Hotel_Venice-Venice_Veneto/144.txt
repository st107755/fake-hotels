This was a special stay, until the OE cancelled our train! But there is so much about this hotel, the lobby and bar areas are, as others have stated, over the top but stunning. We managed to stay in the older Dandolo Palace, room 65 on the second floor and this part of the hotel is stunning. Never had a door to a hotel room like it!!  Check-in is special, like the Savoy, it is a personalised experience, however this was one of my quibbles. If you have pre-paid, you are required to present the original card on which the payment was made. Fine, unless like me you were caught up in the BA hack, and your bank says close that account and open a new one. So I have to pay for the room again and await a refund of my original payment.
