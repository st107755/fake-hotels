The Danieli was perfectly romantic for my first wedding anniversary.  My husband and I stayed three nights and loved the location (right on the Grand canal near st. Marks).  Upon arrival we were upgraded to a room overlooking the grand canal which was lovely and truly reflected the area.  The lobby is very impressive and upscale.  The location was great and had easy access to the Airport Vaporetto (stopped literally in front of the hotel to the left).  My only issue with the Danieli was the staff, I found them to be quite snobby especially the waiter in the lobby bar.
