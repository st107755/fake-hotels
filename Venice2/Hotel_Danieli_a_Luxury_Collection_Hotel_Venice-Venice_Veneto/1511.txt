This grand old hotel is impressive upon arrival with an incredibly opulent lobby in the former palazzo that faces the Grand Canal and is situated next to the Palazzo Ducale and Piazza San Marco. My friend had booked us here upon recommendation from a colleague, who now stays at the Gritti Palace - and we now know why. Our small, single rooms faced a side canal filled with endless lines of gondolas that I enjoyed watching 24/7. My bathroom was spacious and elegant and my bed was luxuriously comfortable.  However, we were apparently staying in the same hallway as cruise ship day trippers who want to come ashore and spend the night in this famous old grand dame of a hotel.
