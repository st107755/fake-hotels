I really wanted to love this hotel.  I had been sent here on business with a group of journalists.  The weather was unusually warm for March, but the hotel itself was acting as if it was December.  The rooms were unbearably stuffy and about eight of us failed to get any decent amount of sleep on the first night because our rooms were too stuffy.  We all opened our shutters and windows (which meant that light could enter, and noise).  We were then all awakened several times in the night (when we did snatch short bursts of sleep) by the sound of rubbish/recycling being collected from the canal outside.
