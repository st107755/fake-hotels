Just returned from a four day trip to celebrate our 27th wedding anniversary. I haven't been back to Venice for 40yrs and my husband, never has, I always said if we went, we would have to stay at the Daneili and it certainly lived up to our expectations. Arriving by motor launch is definitely an experience worth having. We stayed in a Dandolo Suite which was extremely comfortable (a little hot sometimes) graciously decorated in the Venetian style. As a disabled traveller, we were located close to the lift, which was helpful. The staff are knowledgeable source of information and always willing to assist me and my wheelchair.
