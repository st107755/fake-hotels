As a child I read lots of books about this Hotel as I was a big Agatha Christie and Graham Green fan. I fulfilled a lifelong ambition to stay here in 2004. (See my earlier review.) After a long illness and looking after a sick parent I am able to travel again and decided to come back to Venice. In the interim the Danieli had been refurbished as was even better than last time. I was upgraded to a Lagoon view suite which was enormous (really 4 or 5 rooms in a row) overlooking the lagoon and grand canal. The decor was lovely and the air-conditioning most efficient given Venice was very hot. I had the balcony doors open most times as I like to hear the hustle and bustle outside.
