Hire a private speedboat from the carpark and race to the hotel's dock - you can't beat it for a classy experience. Make sure you hand the hotel staff your camera so they can get a pic of you disembarking. The building kind of leans but the history here is palpable. We had two spacious adjoining rooms for the three children and ourselves, the hotel staff were kind and caring (if a bit remote). One advantage of staying here is that if you get lost all the locals know where your hotel is. There was no downside at all apart from ..... I think we are quite well off but the cost of the rooftop meal appalled me. Ditto the cost of breakfast in one of the cafes surrounding the square (nothing to do with the hotel).
