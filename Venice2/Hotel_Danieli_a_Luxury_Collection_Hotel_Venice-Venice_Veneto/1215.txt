When the desk clerk learned our accommodation was a birthday gift, she upgraded us to a lagoon view suite with doge red living room with murano glass decoration, comfortable king bedroom (though dimly lit) and marble bath with everything one could possibly need. It was, I believe, in the older section, which is beautifully maintained. There was already a bottle of chilled Spanish Cuve in the room. We enjoyed retreating there after a day at the Biennial, wandering the streets or visiting Piazza San Marco, sitting on our balcony and watching the tour ship droves pass by. We found the staff friendly, courteous and helpful with good advice about transport.
