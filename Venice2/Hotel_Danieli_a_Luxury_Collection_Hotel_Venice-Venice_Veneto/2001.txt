The best hotel we have been to so far. Danieli really became famous when one of the James Bond movies was shot at the hotel. You sail directly to the check-in, which is located to the side in the canal, and from the first moment, you are blown away by all the beautiful halls/rooms, where there are none of the usual hotel wannabe antiquities, but the real thing. It is certainly not every day that you get to sleep in a real antique 17th century gold bed.  The main building of the hotel is the original 14th century Palace belonging to Doge Dandolo.  At the top is La Terrazza, which is the hotel’s roof top restaurant, with a view of Grand Canal, and this is an experience in itself.
