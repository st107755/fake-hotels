Overall, my stay at the Danieli in May following a cruise was very special.  It  was my first visit to Venice, which made it especially amazing.    I checked in around 11am and my room was not ready but they said it would be ready shortly, so I stepped outside the hotel and was promptly approached to take the free boat ride to Murano.  I read about this here and there so I was prepared, and since I love art glass I wanted to go there anyway.  I actually loved the semi-private (me and another couple) trip to Murano.  We enjoyed the demonstration and toured the incredible display rooms and then we were whisked back to Venice without any problems.
