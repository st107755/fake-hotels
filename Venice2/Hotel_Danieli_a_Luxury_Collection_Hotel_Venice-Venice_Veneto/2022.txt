This was my second trip to Venice and my second stay at the Danieli.   The hotel is in possibly the best location you could wish for in Venice - a two minute walk from Piazza San Marco with beautiful views across the lagoon.  The hotel occupies a 14th century building traditionally Venetian in its decor both in the puiblic areas and in the rooms. If you want high tech plumbing and contemporary furnishing this is not the hotel for you. It exudes an air of faded granduer with mottled glass in the mirrors, traditional fabrics and hand painted furniture and traditional Venetian / Italian bathrooms.
