We spent three nights in a luxury, inner view, double. The location is ideal and St. Marks is a five minute walk. The exterior is grand and imposing. The interior is richly decorated and furnished in traditional style. Our room was of a decent size and well equipped. The bathroom was slightly small but adequate and well designed with plenty of marble. I felt the room would probably need to be refurbished in the next few years but was fine for now. The staff are an absolute delight and highly efficient. Breakfast comprised a buffet with a good selection of quality offerings. There are beautiful views of the lagoon from the terrace.
