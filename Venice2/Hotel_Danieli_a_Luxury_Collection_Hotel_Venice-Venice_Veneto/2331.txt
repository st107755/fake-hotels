The Danieli made our beautiful trip to Venice even more delightful. It was a one-of-a-kind hotel: charming, elegant and completely unlike anywhere we've stayed before. To top it all off, the location was perfect, just a two-minute walk from Piazza San Marco and Doge's Palace.   We enjoyed so many things about our room: the starched linen sheets, the gorgeous Murano glass chandelier, the entirely marble bath, beautiful parquet wood floors, and the cleanliness. This hotel used to be the palace of Doge Dondalo and it shows in the hotel lobby and stunning marble staircases. We attended a dinner in the Marco Polo room in the adjoining building, which is also part of the Danieli, and we loved the scent of leather and fine wood in that section of the hotel.
