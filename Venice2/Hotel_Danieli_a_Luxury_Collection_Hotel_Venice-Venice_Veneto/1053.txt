You'll love your stay at the Danieli Hotel! When planning our trip to Italy, my mom, who was in Venice in the 60s, said "If the Danieli Hotel is still there, you have to go to the rooftop restaurant!" To our luck, not only was the Danieli still there (as it's been since the 1300s), it's now a Starwood property!  You're immediately aware of the history of the hotel when you pass the Piazza San Marco and pull up from the Grand Canal. The lobby is a step back in time but fully luxurious. Venice was our first stop, so we were early arrivals given the AM landings of flights in Europe from the US.
