We visited Venice on a short last minute break and were pleased to find we could afford this hotel as we had recently seen the film The Tourist which starred the Danielli and had commented on how lovely it looked.   When we arrived at the hotel the porter took our bags and escorted us over to reception, where we checked in. The man on the desk gave us a map and booklet about Venice before escorting us to our room whilst the porter brought up our baggage.  Although our room was far less grand than the one assigned to Angelina Jolie in the film, it was very nice.The room was in the newer building and overlooked a small canal rather than the Grand canal.
