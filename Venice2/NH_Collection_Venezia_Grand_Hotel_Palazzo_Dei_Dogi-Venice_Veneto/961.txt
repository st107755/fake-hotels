Staff were very friendly. Very helpful in figuring your way out and planning your time there. The rooms were clean. This is a Marriott hotel. So if you would like to  pay more for that logo, sure. You can find similar hotels at a lower price.   To reach this hotel from mainland you have two options. Take a water taxi - over 70 Euros. Take a water bus - 7 euros per person. Water buses are very convenient. Best to just buy a water bus unlimited trips travelcard to cover your entire stay in venice. Worth it.
