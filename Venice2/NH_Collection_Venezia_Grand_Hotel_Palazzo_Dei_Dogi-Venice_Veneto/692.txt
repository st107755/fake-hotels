This hotel feels like you are staying in Venice from the moment you walk in with its large ceilings and glass chandeliers. They offer a water taxi tour to the glass blowing factory which I loved! We had two different rooms. One was stunning, charming, and elegant. The second was so bad I could barely deal with it so we requested to change again. I am not sure why the difference from room to room but maybe ask for one on the 2nd floor. The spa facility here is small but charming. Honestly, this location I found to be great for walking around and enjoying the city. I stayed at New Years Eve 2015/2016 and Venice was the perfect place to bring in the New Year.
