We enjoyed 2 nights here at the end of a cruise.  After early morning disembarkation from the Oceania Riviera, we took a water taxi from the cruise port to the hotel, maybe a 10 minute ride, and arrived several hours prior to proper check-in.  They are used to this and happily got us pre-checked in, stored our bags, made water taxi reservation for later and then allowed us to have breakfast in their quaint outdoor eating area.  This is located just out the doors at the beginning of their fantastic garden area and is under canopy.  Such a serene location to have a meal.  We enjoyed breakfast a total of 3 times this stay and the food did not disappoint.
