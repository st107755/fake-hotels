We did not stay at this hotel, which has five stars and is of very high quality, but six of us went for a meal there last Tuesday evening. The meal was fine and the service attentive, but things went downhill when we went to pay the bill, which for some strange reason you have to do at reception rather than at your table. The concierge, one Benedetto by name, was surly and uncooperative when we asked to pay by three credit cards, as we always do when out together, with never a problem. The bill was €300, so easy to divide. In a most unfriendly tone he said only one card would be acceptable, which results in us having to sort out between us later. No explanation, no apologies for inconvenience, just a demand for payment in a manner unbefitting of an hotel of this quality. Suffice…
