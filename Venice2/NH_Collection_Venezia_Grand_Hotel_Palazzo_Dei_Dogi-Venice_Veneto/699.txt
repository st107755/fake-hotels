I took my girlfriend away for a surprise 24 hours in Venice to propose.    Arriving - the landing for boats is a 2 minute walk from the hotel, it's very easy to get to from the airport via the public boat service (55 euros for two return tickets) or a private taxi was quoted at 120+.  On arrival, you're welcomed by the beautiful interiors and a very pleasant welcome by the staff.  Pablo was very happy to help, showed us to our room, gave us a map and some guidance about where to go.  As you're in the Jewish Ghetto, it's away from the tourists and lots of amazing local 'bars' and restaurants.
