The hotel occupies a long strip of land with water at both ends: the massive north lagoon at the garden end, and a narrow canal at the main entrance. The vaporetto stop 'Orto' (Madonna del Orto) is very nearby, on the lagoon, perhaps 5-10 minutes walk with your luggage - on wheels, I hope!. There is no hotel shuttle service, either from the airport, or to San Marco, despite what you may read in other reviews. The staff were pleasant enough, but not exactly helpful. I needed an adaptor, but was met with shrugged shoulders, 'no can do'. The breakfast area is very pleasing, in a permanent tented gazebo construction in the garden.
