We stayed here for a couple of nights in August. It was a city break with British Airways. We were a family of 4 with 2 rooms as our children are young adults. We planned the visit to celebrate my daughters 18th birthday. I emailed the hotel a few weeks before we arrived with a few questions and explaining the purpose of our visit. The service was first class. The hotel organised a private speed boat transfer direct from the airport to the hotel. It stops on the canal right outside the front door. They even presented my daughter with a beautiful complimentary birthday cake on the day of her birthday. The rooms were beautiful, very clean and spacious.
