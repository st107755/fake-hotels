Spent a few nights here and was very pleased. The room was spacious enough with a view overlooking the gardens out back. The staff were all very helpful with the questions we had.  While the hotel was away from what you might call the hustle and bustle, nothing is really that far away in Venice. Half the fun is wandering around and getting lost.  As noted by other reviews, the hotel offers a shuttle to St Marks. It is quite comfortable and pleasant. I found that it took roughly the same amount of time to get to St marks taking this shuttle or taking the public water bus down the Grand Canal. You can only go so fast on small boats.
