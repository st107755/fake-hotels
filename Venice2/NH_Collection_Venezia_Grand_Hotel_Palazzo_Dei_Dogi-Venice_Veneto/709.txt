The Boscolo Venezia is a grand old building in a very quiet area which has been wonderfully converted into a plush hotel. As I understand, the building started out as the Rizzo-Patarol noble palace but also served as a convent and embassy. The entrance and public rooms reflect this grandness but are also very welcoming with comfortable seating areas and a grand piano under the ornate Murano chandeliers and detailed wall murals. The hotel is easily accessed from the airport by water. It has it's own docks (canal side and lagoon side) and there is a vaparetto stop (Orto) which is reached without crossing any bridges.
