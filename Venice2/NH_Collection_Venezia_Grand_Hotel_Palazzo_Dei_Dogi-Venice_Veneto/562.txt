If you don't mind staying in the quieter Cannaregio district (as opposed to the more tourist-rich San Marco district), then Boscolo Venezia is a great choice!  The hotel offers great service, great food, and beautiful rooms that showcase Old World Venetian luxury.  And I can't forget to mention that the air conditioning/climate control system was amazingly reliable (you can't say that about other hotels in Venice and Italy in our experiences).  The rooms and common areas showcase Murano glass chandeliers, beautiful rugs, rich woods...just a very nice stay all around. Venice is a walking city, unless you're leveraging the water buses (we don't).
