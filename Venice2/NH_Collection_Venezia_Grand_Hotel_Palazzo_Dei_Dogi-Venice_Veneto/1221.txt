We stayed here for 1 night at the end of a cruise.  We booked this hotel bc of its affiliation with Marriott.  It is a beautiful hotel property.  Formerly a palace, it has been well-preserved and maintained.  It is not close to San Marco, which may be a good thing or bad depending on your perspective.  We had already spent one day touring around the square, so we kind of enjoyed being a little bit removed from everything.  The hotel does offer a free shuttle to San Marco, so it's certainly easy to use that and get there whenever you need to.  You are probably 4 blocks walk (over a few bridges, so not stroller-convenient) to get to restaurants, gelato, shopping on foot.  The building is lovely and the rooms are quite varied.
