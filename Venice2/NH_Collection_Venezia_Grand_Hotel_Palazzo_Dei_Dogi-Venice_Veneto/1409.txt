The Boscolo Venezia is located in a beautiful building with its own moorings, which enable free (but limited frequency) transport to St. Mark's Square. While the breakfast and dining area is quite pleasant (rain or shine), the overall service is somewhat lacking. The over-zealous room cleaning staff continued to make noise throughout the morning and early afternoon through my stay, which resulted in a request to change rooms as door-slamming did not cease. A basic request for a simple bottle opener, which was not present in my room, resulted in a bureaucratic and draw-out response. The "spa" needs some ventilation as it has a lingering and rather unpleasant smell while its amenities are not much better than your local gym.
