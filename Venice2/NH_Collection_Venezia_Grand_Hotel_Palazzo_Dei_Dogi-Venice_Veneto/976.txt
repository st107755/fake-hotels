The Boscolo Venezia is hands down one of the BEST hotels I've ever had the pleasure to stay at.  Imagine a 400-year old property that's been everything from a villa to a monastery with incredible attention to historical detail (the Murano glass chandeliers, hand-stenciled beams and stained glass are living art).  It is well off the main tourist track, but the northern neighborhood is very convenient and comfortable.  Very convenient access to the water taxis (the island of Murano is the next stop.)  It's pricey, but that comes with beautiful rooms in which everything worked perfectly.  The property was incredibly well maintained; the gardens were inviting and peaceful.
