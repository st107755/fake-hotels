We stayed at the hotel for three nights beginning on Feb 15th. We just booked a standard room but on arrival were upgraded to a suite. I have never seen such a grand room, clean, huge and very beautiful! We were on the top floor and had a lovely view over the garden and lagoon below. The man who greeted us on reception was really helpful, giving us a map and explaining where to go and how to get there. The hotel itself is situated away from the main sites but it's a lovely walk and if you want to get the water bus there are two stops situated between 5 and 10 mins away. Breakfast at the hotel was included in our package and was great lots to choose from, but at 30 euros (if you don't have it included) I would say it's expensive!
