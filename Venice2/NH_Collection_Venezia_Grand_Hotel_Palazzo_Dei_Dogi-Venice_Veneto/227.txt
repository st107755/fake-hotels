We recently spent 3 wonderful nights at the Grand Hotel Dei Dogi in Venice.  While it's true that the hotel is located a good 30 minute walk from the "action" (the tourist attractions, St. Mark's etc.), it is located in a very peaceful neighborhood, which we absolutely loved and very close to some great restaurants and bars.  The hotel itself is beautiful with historic elements and beautiful grounds.  Our room, located on a high floor, was large, beautiful, comfortable and well appointed - no issues at all.  Thankfully, our Marriott "level" afforded us the delicous breakfast buffet to be included - because frankly, it was a little pricey as was the hotel bar.
