I’ve read some negative reviews and nothing in them chimes with my experiences. I’ve always had excellent service - but I noticed this time it was exceptionally good and they issued questionnaires at the end, so I suspect Boscolo has taken to heart the negative reviews and they’re now bending over backwards.  Whether you’ll like it depends on what you want. This is a 17th century palace with an intimate feel, and a long, walled garden of century-old trees, winding gravel paths, roses tumbling over pergolas and a covered jetty to watch sunset over the lagoon. You will not get this anywhere else in Venice. The staff are efficient, attentive and kind without being stuffy. Anyone would enjoy the architecture, which is airy and sumptuous.
