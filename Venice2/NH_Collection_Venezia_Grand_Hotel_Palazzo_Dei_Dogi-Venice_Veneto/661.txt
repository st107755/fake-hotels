This hotel is located in a former palace, and from the moment you walk through the front door, its history is apparent.  The public areas are beautiful, and the staff was extremely friendly.  The hotel has what is claimed to be the largest private garden in Venice, which was extremely quiet and peaceful to stroll through.  The back of the hotel faces a lagoon with a private dock and the hotel offers a free water shuttle service to St. Mark's Square throughout the day.  We took it one day and the one way journey lasted about 30 minutes, which is about how long it would take to walk to St. Mark's from the hotel.
