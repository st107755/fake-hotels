Speaking as a hotel manager, I have high expectations when visiting a hotel, this hotel exceeded all of them. The service from check-in till check-out was excellent. The accomodations were not only very clean from a hotel this age, but also spectacular in their own right, one of a kind art work, italian chandeliers, historic pieces all over the hotel. The room was huge by European standards, and we found it reasonably priced in comparasion to many lesser chains in Venice (keep in mind this is the most expensive city in Europe for hotels). As many have mentioned the hotel does have a boat which we did not use and the tolitries were above what i expected.
