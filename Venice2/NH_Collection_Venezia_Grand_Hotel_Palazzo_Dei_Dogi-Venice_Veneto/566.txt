Magical is the word my husband and I most often use to describe our recent trip to Venice, and the magic began when we first entered the lobby of the beautiful Boscolo Venezia!  We arrived a little travel weary as most do, but the friendly, efficient staff and peaceful gardens soon left us just excited.  We LOVED the quiet location of this hotel away from the throngs of people that crowd the Piazza San Marco. Yet we had easy access to San Marco via the hotel boat. We used it numerous times and it was never crowded. Our room overlooking the garden was spacious and well appointed. The staff gave us wonderful recommendations for dinner at restaurants that were an easy walk away through quiet streets. The breakfast in the garden was a deliciously great way to begin each day.
