Great hotel! I don't think I have anything but good things to say about this place. The hotel is quite a ways away from the main attractions but being away from the crowds and noise was perfect. So they escorted my wife and I to the bar and offered free drinks until the room was ready. Now mind you, we were quite tired from our flight and really just wanted to take a nap but that went away after our first glass of Prosecco. The bartender was extremely friendly and attentive.
