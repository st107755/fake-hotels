We stayed for a few nights at the Boscolo Venezia prior to a cruise.  Prior to arrival I had a number of questions regarding our stay, such as breakfast, a gondola ride, tours, etc.  The great folks at the hotel made all arrangements and answered all questions.  In our case, since I am platinum status with Marriott, the lovely breakfast buffet was included.  Our room was on the separate building on the other side of the beautiful gardens, overlooking the lagoon.   It is located on the far side of Venice, away from the tourist masses, but within a few minutes walk of San Marco, etc.  We loved just wandering around Venice, especially in the out of the way, non tourist areas.
