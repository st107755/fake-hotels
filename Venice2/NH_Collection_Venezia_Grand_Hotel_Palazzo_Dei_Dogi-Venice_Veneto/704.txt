We had the pleasure of staying at the Boscolo Venezia Hotel last week in Venice.  The hotel staff was amazing and always greeted us with a friendly hello every time we came in and out of the hotel.  The room was plenty big for a family of 4 and a small dog.  The hotel has a beautiful private garden in the back of the hotel that overlooks the sea where beautiful sunrises and sunsets can be seen.  The onsite breakfast and restaurant is very good and the staff also very warm and accommodating.  It is so hard these days to find a place to stay where everyone appears to be happy at their jobs and when you have a question they never seemed bothered to answer us.
