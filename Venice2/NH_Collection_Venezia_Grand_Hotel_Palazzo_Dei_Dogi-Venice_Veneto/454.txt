At the heart of Cannaregio area, wonderfully located away from the crowds of San Marco this is a traditionally furnished Venetian palazzo with a garden,although the weather was a little too chilly to use it. Staff were all helpful and friendly. Our room overlooking the canal was comfortable and spacious although the lighting needed some improvement. The bath was fine,but the shower not thermostatically controlled and inconvenient. Breakfasts were well cooked but coffee cool and sometimes stewed. Could do better on coffee. Only blot on our stay was a wedding party with music and loud singing that occupied the entire hotel public rooms for a whole afternoon and early evening.
