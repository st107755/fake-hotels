We spent 4 nights here over Valentine’s week and had the most fabulous time. The hotel is based on Cannaregio and this is a beautiful area which the perk being that it has a more local feel than being in the centre. It’s a beautifully restored building and like so many places in Venice has a tremendous wow factor when you go through the doors! We chose a canal view room and it was huge with a dressing area and big bathroom. Breakfast was fabulous (with Prosecco) and the staff were lovely - very friendly and extremely knowledgeable. We were lucky enough to be in Venice for the opening of the Carnevale and also the Regatta the following day and both days the staff gave us brilliant tips for the best places to watch from.
