My husband and I stayed here on our honeymoon and found the hotel to be romantic, luxurious, and in an ideal location away from the crowds.  We loved Venice and we believe that it was because of this hotel, that we fell in love with the city. The area around St. Marks can be so congested and noisy, but staying off the beaten path helped us to explore more of the city and get away from the tourist section. Because of Dei Dogi we got to see the daily life of Venice and we got to spend time together exploring the winding streets and canals.  However, that doesn't mean that the hotel isn't in a great location! The hotel is near tons of restaurants, businesses, and a short walk away from the popular sights and streets.
