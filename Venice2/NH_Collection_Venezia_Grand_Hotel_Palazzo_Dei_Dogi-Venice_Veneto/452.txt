As promise on our last review on TA, here we are telling you about our stay at Boscolo Venezia this April/May. Lately, we have been staying at Autograph Collection hotel in other cities, like Madrid, Granada and Rome, which we appreciated, and influenced our choice for our stay in Venice: we choose this hotel with the announced “contemporary comfort marries with the luxury of the epoch” to spend 4 nights during our visit to La Serenissima. We were so excited with our choice, furthermore because we are Platinum members and very loyal to the Marriott brand. This hotel is a building of a noble family from the XVII Century, located in the sestiere Cannaregio, facing the lagoon and close to the Orto vaporetto stop. At the lobby you can see beautiful afrescos and Murano chandeliers.…
