The Boscolo Venice is a Marriot Autograph Hotel, not quite the exclusive Boutique Style Hotel that ONE of its two website purports, thus their poor performance during our 2 night stay becomes more understandable.   The Boscolo website has no mention of Marriot. It does however mention that "The Boscolo Venezia hotel has a free motor boat service” in 'Directions' which we misunderstood to mean that the hotel had a free motor boat service. In fact the service runs just to St Mark's and back a few times a day.  We arrived at the hotel around 7:00pm by water taxi. We were checked in by a pleasant, friendly young lady who commented that we had a garden room "so it would be nice and quiet".
