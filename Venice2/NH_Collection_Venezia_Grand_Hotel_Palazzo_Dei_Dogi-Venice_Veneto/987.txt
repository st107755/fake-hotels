We went to three cities in Italy. Our first stop was Venice. After 3 airplanes and way too many hours traveling, all I wanted was a comfortable bed...quickly. I was skeptical about taking a boat to an area unfamiliar to us in Venice, but my husband kept assuring me it was fine. We were the only ones who got off the boat at this dock, then we had to walk through a neighborhood and building courtyards, lugging our suitcases behind us. When we emerged on the Fondamenta Madonna dell'Orto, we looked to our left to see the beautiful hotel. The staff was so welcoming, and they walked us through the hotel and out the back, through a lovely, tree-lined park and to our room.
