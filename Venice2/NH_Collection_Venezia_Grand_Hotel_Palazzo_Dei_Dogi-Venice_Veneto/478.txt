A haven of tranquility and understated grandeur awaits you at this gem of a property located in the residential Cannaregio district to the north of Venice.  The location really does afford you the best of both worlds; peace and quiet in a charming residential neighbourhood, with the vibrancy of the tourist trail but 20 minutes walk away. The hotel boasts the largest private garden in Venice (complete with original subterranean ice house, and, on this visit, a delightfully friendly kitten).  I chose the Presidential Suite (on a special offer) which is in a separate building at the foot of the garden away from the main house, and with its own private jetty overlooking the lagoon.
