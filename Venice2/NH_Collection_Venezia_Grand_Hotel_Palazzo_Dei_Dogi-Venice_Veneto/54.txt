My wife and I stayed here for three nights prior to our cruise. This hotel is far from the maddening crowd's of tourists but is in easy walking distance of all parts of Venice. The staff is friendly and is always asking if there is something they can do for you. Check-in and check-out was very easy and efficient. We upgraded to a canal view room which was very large. The beds are a little firm but fine for the few days we were here.  Our bathroom was large with a tub ahower combinations. One challenge is the moisture that collects on the mirror. If you crack the bathtub door a little the mirror will clear up.
