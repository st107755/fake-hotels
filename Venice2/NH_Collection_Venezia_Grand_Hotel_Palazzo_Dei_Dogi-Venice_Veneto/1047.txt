My new wife and I stayed here for the first stop in our honeymoon, and it was an amazing experience. I travel a lot for work, so we were able to use my Marriott points. Because of my Marriott status, they included breakfast at no charge in exchange for some other usual Marriott perks, which was more than fine with me. The buffet is delightful, with meats, cheeses, pastries, and of course, Nutella. The staff spoke excellent English and were extremely friendly and knowledgeable.  The location was perfect--it was not located in the touristy area of Venice, but was close to public transportation to get there. For a few extra Euro, it was definitely worth the peace and quiet.
