If you are looking for an elegant Venetian hotel away from the hordes of tourists that occupy most of Venice during the summer, the Boscolo is for you.  This is one of the most beautiful hotels I've ever stayed in.  With its Marble floors and colorful glass chandeliers that are everywhere, you begin to feel very special.  The hotel is right on a canal and is in a very quite neighborhood.  We really enjoyed this in the evening for we were able to go out and dine with the locals.    I stayed here with my family in August and the room that was provided to us was very large an was able to facilitate two cots.  We had a canal view and the room was very comfortable.
