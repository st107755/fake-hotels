We booked this stay using Marriott points and had a junior suite. The building is 300 years old, so please ignore the reviews saying the hotel is dated. The furnishings are classical in style, and the rooms are not modern by any stretch of the imagination. But the hotel is clean, staff is amazing and the location is great. In the rooms, you will find a coffee maker, flat screen TV, hairdryer (bring your own - the one they provide has very low power), a mini bar and more. The shower, like most European hotels, was tricky to get going but serviceable. We received free breakfast because of our Marriott status, which was a full breakfast buffet with the option to order off the menu as well.
