We stayed here before and after our cruise and really enjoyed our time there. The staff were great and the rooms they gave us were amazing, right on the water by their private shuttle dock. This hotel is an old palace and is “off the beaten” path if you will. We really liked that as it allowed us to get away from the crowds. Our friends stayed with us at the front end and had a room in the main building. It was not as nice as ours, but still not a negative. You have to understand you’re not staying in a modern building. The quirks, etc., are all part of the charm. As others have mentioned, the garden area is lovely. The breakfast (included due to platinum level) was great as well. Only tip is be sure to explore beyond their complimentary Murano factory stop  as there are many…
