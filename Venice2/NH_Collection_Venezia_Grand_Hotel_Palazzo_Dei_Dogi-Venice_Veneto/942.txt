The weather for this time of year was very mild which I would not complain about, but the hotel rooms were very stuffy at night and made it difficult to breath.  My husband spoke to the reception as we were unable to get the air-conditioning to work and they informed us that this time of year it doesn’t work only the heating. ???? So the following evening we opened our windows, OMG! we got absolutely bitten alive by Mosquito’s which woke us up at 2am in the morning trying to kill them all. My husband got bitten at least 20 times and my hand doubled in size from the swelling of their bites. I cannot why a 5* hotel doesn’t have air-conditioning at all times of year ?
