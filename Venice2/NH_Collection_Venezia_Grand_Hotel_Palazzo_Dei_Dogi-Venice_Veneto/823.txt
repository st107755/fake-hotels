Looking initially for a Marriott, we were more than pleased to be introduced to their Autograph Collection. The hotel, in a canal-side palace with a lovely garden, is luxurious and wonderfully quiet, as is the whole neighborhood. A five minute walk to the vaporetto stop on the Lagoon, or a ten minute walk to another on the Grand Canal, put us within easy reach of all the sights. We had thought we'd take a water taxi from the train station, but it was so easy to find the proper vaporetto route, and then walk five minutes from the Orto stop, that we stuck to public transit for the entire trip.
