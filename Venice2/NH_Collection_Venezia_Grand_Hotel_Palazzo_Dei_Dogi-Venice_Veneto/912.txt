This hotel is located in a quiet neighborhood of Venice, but it is just a short walk to all the tourist locations.  The rooms are spacious, well appointed, and well maintained.  Beautiful Murano chandeliers decorate the common areas.  The service is friendly, prompt, and professional.  The concierge is very helpful in arranging for transportation and dinner suggestions. The dining facilities offers a wonderful buffet or menu options.  The bar as a wide assortment of wines and beverages. For a real treat, book a water taxi from the airport directly to the hotel for your arrival transfer.
