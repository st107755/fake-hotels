Just came back from a long weekend in Venice over the August Bank Holiday.. Arrived at the Dei Dogi expecting a 5* hotel.. The service of the receptionist certainly appeared to support this, as when they ascertained that this was our first time in Venice, there was an in depth explanation of how Venice works, and how to get about on the vaparetto service.. The lobby was also pretty impressive, with the garden restaurant visible through the lobby.   However, once we were shown to our room, I was rather taken aback by the size. It did have a King size bed (two singles pushed together, but this is usual in some hotels in Europe) and a dresser and chair that was so close to the bed that if you pulled the chair out you had no further room to walk round the bed.
