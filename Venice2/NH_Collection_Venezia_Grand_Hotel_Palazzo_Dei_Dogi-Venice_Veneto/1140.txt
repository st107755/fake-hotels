Spent one night before a Costa cruise and three nights after the cruise. Nice, clean hotel. In the Cannaregio district. On the surface it seems far from the main attractions but Venice is not a large city and most areas are walkable within 30 mins from the hotel. Nice restaurants nearby within a 10 minutes walk. The hotel offers a free shuttle service via water taxi to the San Marco area and the island of Murano. The hotel is charming but also has some awkward aspects. For example the windows lack screens and with them closed both rooms were warm and stuffy and the building lacks any air handling this time of year.
