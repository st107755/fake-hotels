The whole atmoshere of Venice in February lends itself to total romance, with less crowds and no unpleasant smells from the water. The weather is below zero which makes the water ethereal and magical. De Dogi is off the beaten track but they provide a water taxi service for guests and the alternative walk to St Mark's Square only takes 20 mins. Wrap up in your furs and enjoy the tranquillity of this quarter of Venice.  Leaning out of the balcony on a Sunday morning and only hearing single footsteps and church bells from the street below in  what is usually a crowded city was sureal.
