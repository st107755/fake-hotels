I've stayed in many hotels across the world and the suite at the Boscolo Venezia was truly the most magical.  The incredible view of the lagoon, wonderfully comfortable bed, spacious room, sumptuous bathroom, and amazingly tasteful décor were unbeatable. My only regret was only having one night there.  Aside from the wonderful, classic, and quiet room, there is a spacious garden with various surprises (and old ice house, lounge chairs, etc.).  The hotel is nicely tucked away from the crowds, but it was just a 5 minute walk to a wide variety of restaurants and other amenities.
