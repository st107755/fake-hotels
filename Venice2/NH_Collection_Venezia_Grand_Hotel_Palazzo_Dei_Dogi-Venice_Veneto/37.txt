The onsite bar is typically not busy and we were able to relax there with our fellow travelers. (We were there in November, a period of lower tourism.) The bar manager made each visit enjoyable.  The staff is very attentive, friendly, and speak excellent English.  We ate breakfast each morning in the hotel restaurant and found it to be excellent.
