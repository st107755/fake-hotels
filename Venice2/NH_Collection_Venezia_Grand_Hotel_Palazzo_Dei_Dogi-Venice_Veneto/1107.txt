The Boscolo Venezia provides a more authentic experience than many other Venetian hotels. A beautifully maintained palazzo with lots of murano chandaliers, marble and tapestry. Service was professional, friendly and efficient. The small spa provided the opportunity to relax tired muscles after a long day of sightseeing. We didn't try the restaurant but maybe should have as restaurants in Venice can be really hit or miss. Breakfast was nice with several original items you can order from the menu in addition to the standard buffet which was good but not more. The hotel transfer to San Marco was a bit long but we thought of it more as a water tour and enjoyed it but did it only once.
