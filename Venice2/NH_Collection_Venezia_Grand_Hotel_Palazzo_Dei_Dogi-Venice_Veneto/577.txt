The hotel is located in Cannaregio, the northern residential area of Venice.  It sits on a large piece of land for the area, a canal on one side and the northern water side of Venice on the other.  It is as far away as you can get from San Marco.  If you decide to walk it, about 11,000 steps in each direction.  The hotel offers a free taxi service that takes about 1/2 hour from the back door right to San Marco, about every 2 hours.  This is not a modern hotel by any means.  It is in the old traditional Venetian style.  The standard deluxe rooms are very small.  The superior rooms offer a lot more space.  We had a king superior.  The bed was very comfortable.
