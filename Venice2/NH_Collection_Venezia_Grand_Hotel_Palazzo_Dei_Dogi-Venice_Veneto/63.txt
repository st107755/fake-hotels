My wife and I just ended a 5-night stay at the hotel and were pleased with just about everything. The staff is uniformly responsive and friendly, creating a warm atmosphere. Special kudos to concierge Ivan, who was on point with his recommendations and always willing to help. The property is a beautiful, unique one with the garden being a great asset.  The breakfasts were a pleasant way to start the day, seated facing the garden and enjoying the buffet with its one complementary item, e.g waffle. The location is also great--near a vaporetto stop, not far the boats to Murano and Burano, and, most importantly in the Canareggio Sestiere.
