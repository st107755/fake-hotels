We stayed at the Boscolo Grand Hotel dei Dogi on our last night in Venice. The hotel was gorgeous-- I couldn't ask for a nicer atmosphere.  There was an amazing outdoor garden that was quite peaceful. Our room was nice-- we had a tub. Apparently my parent's room didn't have a tub which I though was surprising since we were paying big bucks for the rooms. I was also surprised that if we wanted to use the Internet we'd have to pay 10Euro an hour. For the price we paid, I expect Internet to be free.  Regarding other services-- we had the hotel reception guy show us our room and then the porter brought up our bags.
