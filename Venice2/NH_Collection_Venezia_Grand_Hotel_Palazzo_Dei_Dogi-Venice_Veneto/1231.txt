It was total exhilaration once we stepped into this Boscolo palace hotel.  It is full of character, ambiance, and charming.  It feels like a palace some times and as a museum at other times.  It was great to step into this hotel after staying at Rome Marriott Grand which was again a good hotel.  The staff upgraded us to a junior suite facing the canal. Our kids really enjoyed the two level suite with spiral steps.    The hotel is situated away from tourist spots. It is actually the other side of the island that is opposite to the side of the San Macro square. We enjoyed the quite evening strolls on the canal roads near hotel.
