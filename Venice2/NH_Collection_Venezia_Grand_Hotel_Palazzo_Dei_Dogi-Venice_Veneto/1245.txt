First time travel to Venice prompted me to choose a US chain.  The gardens of this hotel are amazing!  Although small, we kept finding other walkways to explore and enjoyed the gorgeous roses!  Our room was beautifully appointed but a little small for the price.  The hotel has convenient access to the vaporetto (water bus) and the Alilaluna (water taxi to the airport 16E/each one way-purchased onboard).  When we checked in the staff advised we had a "deluxe" room however the e-frame on the counter showed much more spacious and beautifully appointed rooms.  2 nights cost almost $1000 US.
