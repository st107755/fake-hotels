I chose this hotel for our 10th Wedding Anniversary and vacation. It is a Marriott property and therefore I burned some points. This hotel has excellent staff, location is away from all the tourism and noise, and free shuttles to St. Marcos Square (Piazza San Marco). We were tourists however, the staff and atmosphere at this hotel makes you feel like royalty. In fact, we were caught a little off guard by the genuine hospitality of everyone there. When they say, "It's no problem, no worries, of course" for whatever we asked for, they meant it and never failed. The only negative of this experience is the bathroom shower was a little tight and the sink was cracked. Understandable since this is a 17th century hotel.
