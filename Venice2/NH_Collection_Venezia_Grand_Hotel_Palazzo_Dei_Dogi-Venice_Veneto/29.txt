The Dei Dogi, part of Marriott's Autograph collection, was absolutely gorgeous. We were fortunate to have a room right on the Venice Lagoon at the back of the property, separated from the main building by a large, beautiful-even-in-winter garden. Every morning there was a delicious breakfast buffet with everything from the usual eggs to amazing Italian cold cuts, good fresh Italian bread and wonderful pastries. Fresh fruit, yogurt and muesli were also available. The staff were friendly and courteous, all spoke at least some English and many were quite fluent. There is a boat that will take you down to St. Marks, and even pick you up at the end of the day.
