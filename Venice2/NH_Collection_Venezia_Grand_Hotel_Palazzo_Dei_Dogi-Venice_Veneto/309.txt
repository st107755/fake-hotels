This is an excellent boutique hotel with a superb location, good breakfast, friendly staff and pleasant rooms.  There were a few things that could have been better, but the pros outweighed the cons and we’d gladly come back.     Breakfast -   Marriott Gold and Platinum members get complimentary breakfast in the restaurant, which was quite impressive. You’re allowed to order one item each from the menu plus a wide selection of cereals, bread, meats, and pastries in the buffet. The breakfast staff was always helpful and friendly and had no problem communicating with us in English.
