From the moment we walked in and saw the exquisite décor in the lobby we were thrilled!  The gorgeous Murano Crystal chandeliers adorning the ceiling added a touch of daintiness and from there on the whole experience was just great! I particularly enjoyed the fruit flavored iced water they always offer in the lobby, even though we were there in March and the weather was delightful, after coming back from a long walk to the Rialto Bridge or the Jewish Ghetto it was so welcoming to enjoy a cold glass of fruit flavored water!  The staff was very accommodating and courteous.
