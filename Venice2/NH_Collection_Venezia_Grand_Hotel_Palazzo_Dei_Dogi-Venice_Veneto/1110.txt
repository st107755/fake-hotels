The Boscolo sits on the outskirts of the main part of Venice, but is an easy walk (although first-timers will likely get lost once or twice.  It's quiet and charming and the staff was wonderful in helping us check-in, make restaurant recommendations and arrange a ride on their beautiful wood boat to the center of old Venice.  Breakfast was included in our rate and after a near 3 week trip throughout northern Italy in many hotels, this breakfast was by far the best.  Best room also, although I believe we got an upgrade to a room that overlooked the gardens.  Biggest room of the 3 weeks and most elegantly appointed.
