The Boscolo accommodations were far from over the top as the price would reflect. After getting my second room, the stay became very enjoyable. The staff was exceptionally helpful when I mentioned the room was not what I paid for. Not an argument or even the slightest but as they set out to ensure my stay would get on track for our three day stay.  The hotel is on the quiet side of Venice if there is such a place. There are several smaller local restaurants a short jaunt from the hotel. Our preferred type of place to eat. The Boscolo's free water taxi gets you to the main attractions with ease and in style, although it is only a 15 to 30 min walk to just about everything from the Train station to St. Marks Square.
