My wife and I recently concluded a 3 week trip to Italy with a 5 day stay at the Boscolo Venezia (in Venice).  The hotel is beautiful, and the service is outstanding.  It's a 5 minute walk to the bustling tourist parts of Venice, but the location is quiet and peaceful and there is a lovely garden behind the hotel.  Bathroom was very well appointed and the beds were very comfortable.  Plenty of storage space too (very unusual for a European hotel).  The hotel is elegant and retains the feel of the grand residence that it once was while not sacrificing modern conveniences.  We had room 401 with a view of the Madonna dell'Orto bell tower.
