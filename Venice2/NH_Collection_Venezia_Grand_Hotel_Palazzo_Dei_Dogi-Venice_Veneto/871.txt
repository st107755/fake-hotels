The Marriott Boscolo helped make Venice our favorite stop in Italy.  Venice is beautiful, but crowded with tourists.  It was great to be able to get out of the crowds and go to the Boscolo which is located in a residential area.  The breakfast was wonderful, with seating in the back near the beautiful garden.  It was such a relaxing way to start the day.  The room we had was perfect for sleeping.  The room was quiet, the air conditioning kept it cool and the dark out curtains kept out all light.  You would think you woke up in the middle of the night it was so dark in the room, but then we opened the curtains the sun shone in and we watched the activity on the canals.
