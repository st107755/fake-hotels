They boast the largest garden in all of Venice, that didn't matter much to us but the property was beautiful all around. This was the third stop for us on our honeymoon trip to Italy. At the prices they charge, I can't imagine they had people throwing parties. The free rides to San Marco square were really great just always remember to book ahead of time because they fill up AND remember to book a return water taxi. The location is a little far from the main touristy areas but we ended up liking that, there was still a ton to walk around and see and we had a lot of fun.
