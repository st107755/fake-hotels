We recently stayed here with our two teenagers. We were overall satisfied with our stay, and loved the location of the hotel. We had two rooms, one of which had an incredible view of the canal. We liked the decor of the rooms, which I think added to the charm of the hotel. We also found the staff to be more than helpful with our questions or concerns.   We only had two issues with our stay. First, was the key to one of the rooms. One of our rooms had a key card entry on the door, but the other room had a large, awkward skeleton-like key that was attached to a metal ball about the size of a ping pong ball.
