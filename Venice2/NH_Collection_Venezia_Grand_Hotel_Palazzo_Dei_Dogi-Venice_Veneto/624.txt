After a glitch in corresponding with the hotel in advance of our arrival, everything went without a hitch during our stays. My wife and I needed a hotel which enabled us to leave some luggage while traveling throughout Italy and the Boscolo was just the place. We stayed one night, and then went on a cruise, returning for 2 more nights, before traveling to Florence, and finally 1 last night before leaving Italy. The staff was very friendly and helpful, and went out of their way to provide an upgrade on each of our stays (we are Marriott Platinum).   Breakfast at the restaurant was very good, the concierge had excellent suggestions for restaurants and things to do, and each of our rooms was perfect.  The other nice piece was the location.
