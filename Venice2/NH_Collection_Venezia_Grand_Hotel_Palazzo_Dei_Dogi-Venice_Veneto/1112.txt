After a long search of a suitable place where to spend a long weekend in Venice with wife and other couples of friends, we opted for the boscolo as the chain had a solid reputation in spite of some financial woes reported recently in the press, many stars and good price. I had attributed the lower cost to the fact that this hotel was further away from the very center of Venice which was Ok with us as we were City regulars in town for the biennale.   The outside of the building is promising, in a quiet canal and with a prestigious font door. The first surprise hits you as you cross it: the breakfast tables where blended in the reception room.
