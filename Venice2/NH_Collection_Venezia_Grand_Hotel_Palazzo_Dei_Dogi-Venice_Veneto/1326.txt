It was a lovely place to stay in Venice. The hotel is located at the first stop on the alilaguna orange line from airport called madonna dell orto and very close to the church of the same name. You may not find the glitzy glass facade of modern 5 star hotels but the building has a history and thus an old world charm about it.  What I liked most was the personalised attention given by the staff particularly Georgia and martina at the reception. Mohammed helped us with the luggage till the pier.  Particularly useful was the free boat rides to st marks square and murano. The boat driver was a youngster who was keen to give us all the thrills of a Venetian boat tide.
