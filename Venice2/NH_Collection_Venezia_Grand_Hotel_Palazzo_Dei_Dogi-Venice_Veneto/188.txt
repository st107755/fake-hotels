Loved our stay at this autograph series hotel by Marriott! We got off to a rough start as the day of our arrival there was renovation being done to the hotel front desk so the check in was a bit chaotic and there was no greeting by a doorman  however there was an upgrade to our room accommodations! Our room was beautiful and large and the bed was very comfortable and the bathroom large and very nice. We found our hotel very easy to find and loved the Cannaregio area. Several people need to be spotlighted for their incredible service during our stay and that would be the bellhop who both assisted with our bags to our room and also to the water taxi the next day ( he was VERY friendly and gave us more information than the front desk workers) and Ivan, the concierge.
