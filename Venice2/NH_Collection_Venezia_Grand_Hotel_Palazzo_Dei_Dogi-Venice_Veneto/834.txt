This hotel is affiliated with the Marriott brand; however, it has its own distinctive personality. It's located about a 25-30 minute walk from the Plaza San Marco area. This means that you won't face throngs of tourists every minute; conversely, you'll be walking a considerable distance to the major attractions. If you enjoy being away from the crowds, you'll find yourself in a quiet area that has several very fine restaurants less than a five minute walk from the hotel.  The staff were generally helpful, and our Junior Suite was both large and elegantly furnished. One of the best sets of features was the restaurant and accompanying outdoor garden, perhaps the largest outdoor garden in Venice, and quite beautiful.
