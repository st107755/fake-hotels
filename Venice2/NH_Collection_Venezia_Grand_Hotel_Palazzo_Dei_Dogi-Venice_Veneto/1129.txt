As with allot of reviews I think it is hit and miss. The great aspects. Location, is out of the hustle and bustle which worked well, walking home you certainly felt liked you belonged more getting out of the throng of tourist, as commented there are some great restaurants close by, well recommended by the concierge. We stayed in a room overlooking the main water not in the house itself, we loved the view, the sunset from the little pier and sunrise over the water in the morning. Water taxi is a great option, and really is a value add. It's a great ride. Trip to glass showrooms was really worth it, especially if you would like to get a quality piece of Murano.
