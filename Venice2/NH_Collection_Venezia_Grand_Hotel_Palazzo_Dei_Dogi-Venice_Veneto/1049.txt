My family recently returned from a vacation to Rome, Venice and a beautiful town (actually a independent republic) called San Mario, Italy.  The Boscolo Venezia is a very nice hotel situated away from the hustle and main tourist attractions.  It's a 30 minute walk to St. Mark's square or the hotel provides a boat shuttle to and from St. Marks.  Times for the shuttle are limited and reservations are suggested.  The gardens behind the hotel are beautiful and you have access to a small dock overlooking a main "highway" in Venice.  As nice as the property was, it was a disappointing stay due to the poor service and attitude of the front desk.
