I loved everything about this hotel. We had a rental car and parked at the Piazzale Roma. It's about a mile walk from there to the hotel so we decided to take a water taxi. While this is an expensive option (about $100), it was very nice to have a private boat ride through the canals as soon as we got to the island. The city does provide a much less expensive water bus service. There is a stop very close to the hotel so this is a good option if you're looking to save a few bucks. Once we got the the hotel everything was perfect. The gentleman that checked us in walked us to our rooms and explained the history of the building to us on the way.
