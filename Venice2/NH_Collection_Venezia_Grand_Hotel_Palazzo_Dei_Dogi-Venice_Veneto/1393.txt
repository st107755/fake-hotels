Venice is crowded in July so we were thrilled about the location of this hotel away from the craziness. It's a very short walk from the Orte vaporetto stop. The hotel offers boat service to and from Piazza San Marco but make sure you know exactly where they pick up. We wandered around for 30 minutes and never found the hotel boat at one of the scheduled times. The rooms were larger than we expected and very comfortable. I was concerned when I read several reviews about air conditioning problems, but we had no such issues. The staff was incredibly polite and helpful. The gardens behind the hotel are amazing and our children loved getting lost in them. The hotel's restaurant recommendations were top notch. I agree with earlier reviews that the wifi is unreliable.
