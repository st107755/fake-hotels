Venice was our first stop on our trip through Italy, we arrived from the airport to the hotel via Aliguana water taxi (very easy aside from short line at the airport, but significantly cheaper than taking a private water taxi.)  The hotel is on a lovely, quiet canal on the north side of Venice.  I cannot capture in this review how absolutely beautiful the setting is. The hotel is on a large property with a beautiful garden in the back as well as a dock looking out into the lagoon which we walked out to in the evening to enjoy the view.   On arrival, we were warmly greeted by the concierge Ty (not sure if I am spelling that correctly).  He was very welcoming and made us feel right at home.
