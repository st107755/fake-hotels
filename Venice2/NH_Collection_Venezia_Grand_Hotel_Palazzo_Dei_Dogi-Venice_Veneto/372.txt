If you want to be in the middle of all the action in Venice, this hotel is not for you.  It is located at the north side of the island in Cannaregio and is about a 30-minute walk from the Piazza San Marco and about 20 minutes to the Rialto Bridge. The area is a quiet residential part of Venice and provides some wonderful restaurants in the area that are less touristy.  The hotel is pure luxury.  We were in two different rooms - one an excellent upgraded room and the other a very basic room that must have once been the servant quarters of the 400 year old pallazo.  Make sure you get the type of room you want.  The hotel has a fabulous outdoor covered breakfast area alongside the enormous private garden that extends all the way to the lagoon.
