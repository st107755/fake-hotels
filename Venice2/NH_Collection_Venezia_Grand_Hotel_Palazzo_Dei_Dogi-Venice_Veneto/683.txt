We visited Venice last week for our 35th anniversary, what a great trip. My wife and I  arrived early Sunday  to a rainy chilly morning, our hotel the Boscolo Venezia was gracious enough to allow an early check in. The room was spacious and had 3 great windows to open and take in the view overlooking a canal. Gondolas floating by right underneath our windows pretty cool. Venice is a very busy, crowded city over in the St Marks square area, the Boscolo is located in the Jewish Ghetto, which is a neighborhood. At night the hotel is peaceful and quiet. The garden is gorgeous, we just missed the roses blooming. The hotels' Vaporetta took us several places including St Marks Square and Murano.
