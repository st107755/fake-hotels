Romantically located on the canal of Venice, in an adorable neighborhood far removed from the usual hustle and bustle of the sinking city the hotel promises an unforgettable stay for the high-end traveler. The gardens are a wonderful change in scenery if one so longs for some sturdy grounds and lush greens after walking the intricate, liquids streets system of Venice. What the hotel offers on breathtaking scenery and old-world charm it lacks on true service that one expects from a 5 Star Hotel. Weather you arrive by air or train, there is no complimentary shuttle to and from the hotel.
