We had a wonderful stay at the Boscolo Venezia, Autography Collection in our 9 day Venice, IT trip.  The people were very friendly and went above &  beyond to make sure that you were happy.  They actually walked us around to several rooms to give us our choice. We ended up asking for one of the rooms that overlooked the canal in the front of the hotel.  The breakfast was excellent but we ended up just having an entrée only for my wife while I had the buffet which also included the entrée option.  We had one dinner there and even though I grew up in Oklahoma and know what good beef is, I had a steak that was surprisingly good.
