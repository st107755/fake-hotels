This is a real gem in the Marriott collection of hotels. It's a 5 due to the rooms, service, restaurant and location.  The rooms are typical Italian but not overly ornate like many other older Italian hotels I have stayed at. The lobby areas retain the old palace and former French embassy decor, but these areas have been modernized. The room we had facing the canal was spacious as well as the nicely appointed bathroom. Shower water pressure was strong and the AC worked very well.  The service was excellent at the front desk and restaurant. Alessio who checked us in was extremely nice and patient with all of our questions when we arrived. The concierge Ivan was extremely helpful planning our day trip to Murano, Berano and Saint Marks.
