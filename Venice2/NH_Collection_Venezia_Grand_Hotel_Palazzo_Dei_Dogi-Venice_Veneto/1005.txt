Family vacation through Italy.  Booked by a Travel agent.  It was only a 15 minute ride but 80 Euros. The hotel is right on the canal with their own dock so it was easy. When we got to the hotel, our room wasn’t ready so they held our bags and we went to their restaurant for lunch. Service was terrible.  There were only 2 other tables at that time.  It took an hour and a half to eat and the food was bad and over $100 euro.  There is private taxi that takes you to the Murano Glass factory. That was the most pleasant thing about the hotel.  We booked two rooms.  They were both old, semi clean and only average for the price.  Our bathroom floor had multiple cracked and broken tiles.
