We returned to the Boscolo Venezia this year because we enjoyed it so much last year. For us, the combination of the excellent service, lovely accommodations, and chance to walk through beautiful Venetian streets without crowds, is the draw. The staff is excellent. The garden is spectacular.  Breakfast each morning, especially outside, was a great way to start the day in Venice. The breakfast buffet has wonderful choices and the waitstaff is charming.   The rooms are elegant. The fact that the hotel is not in the middle of all the madness is what makes it so wonderful, a quiet and peaceful refuge to enjoy after a day of walking all over Venice.
