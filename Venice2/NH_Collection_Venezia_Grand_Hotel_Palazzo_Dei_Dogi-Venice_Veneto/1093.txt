My husband and I stayed at the Boscolo Venezia hotel for 5 nights the last week in March. The staff put us in an upgraded room in the casino building deep in the garden facing the lagoon. It was a lovely, quiet room with a marble bathroom and top notch amenities. The entire hotel is immaculately maintained and extraordinarily beautiful, in a city with many beautiful properties. The garden is unique, spacious and peaceful in a city with very few private green spaces. The dining room and bar offer a variety of interesting food choices and surely the most luxurious breakfast selection in the Marriott system.
