The staff is the reason our stay was so great.  We had many questions since this was our first time Venice.  Staff was eager to help with all questions.   My electric adapter did not work and the front desk loaned me an adapter and also gave me directions on where I can purchase one for the rest of our trip.  Staff knew that we were traveling by train to Tuscany and called ahead to make sure we were making the correct connections.  We were touched when the hotel staff offered water to the foot porter that transported our luggage by foot from the train station.   A big perk to staying here is that they offered free transportation by boat to the Island of Morano and also to St Mark's Piazza.
