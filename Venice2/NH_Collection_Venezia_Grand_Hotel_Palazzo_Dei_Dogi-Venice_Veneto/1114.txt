This elegant hotel, now accessible through the Marriott network Is at the dorsal part of the “fish” on the water in Canaregio.  In addition to being an elegant, old style hotel with great service , food and ambiance, it is located in a neighborhood so one gets out of the touristic hubbub and get to walk by restaurants where actual Venetians are eating and having fun.  There is an amazing large garden that stretches to the sea where they have a boat to take you San Marco, if you make reservations. We did it once for the experience but found it faster and more convenient to walk which, in Venice is usually the case.
