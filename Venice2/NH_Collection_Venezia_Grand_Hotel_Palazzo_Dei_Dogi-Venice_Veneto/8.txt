We stayed at this amazing hotel at the very end of January-early February 2020, just before the pandemic.  Staying in Cannaregio was a must for us to avoid the few tourists hanging about even though it was low season.  This hotel is a dream come true: it has a romantic, very large garden which our very young children absolutely adored playing in and which is a total luxury and a unique feature in Venetian hotels.  Staff were more than courteous, they were super-polite, professional and extremely nice to our kids.  The hotel has the best location in Venice- off the beaten track and yet very close to a vaporetto stop.
