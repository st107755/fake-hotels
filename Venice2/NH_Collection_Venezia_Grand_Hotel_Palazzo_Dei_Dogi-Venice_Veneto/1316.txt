Small, beautiful hotel. The lobby is well decorated with classy taste and the lamps are really beautiful. The room size is fine, the bed is quite comfortable and the location is great, Osteria L'Orto dei Morti is around the corner, there is a vaporetto station just two blocks from there and it is a quiet area of Venice, just 20-25 minutes walking where the crowds are in Piazza San Marco. Sadly the staff didn't make our stay pleasurable. It all started when we arrived to Venice, the instructions to get to the hotel were given without care and extremely quick, so you wouldn't understand anything but how much the taxi would cost.
