Stayed for 3 nights in this excellent establishment having disembarked from a wonderful leisurely cruise.  Its really important to note that this hotel is not close to the main and most popular part of Venice, (San Marco) but in the more residential region, Cannaregio. BUT DO NOT LET THIS PUT YOU OFF!!!!!!!! It is the most quiet, beautiful and real part of Venice and even though we know that Italy, by and large is expensive, pricing in bars and restaurants in this area are very good and value for money.  Having been to Venice on many occasions, i would now not choose to stay in any other area.
