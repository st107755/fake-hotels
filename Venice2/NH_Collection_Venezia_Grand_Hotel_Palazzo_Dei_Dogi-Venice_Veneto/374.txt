We stayed at Boscolo for two nights during our Euro-Honeymoon trip. Definitely a beautiful place. They pride themselves in having a garden, which is definitely beautiful. Everyone is friendly and helpful! The only caveat was the wifi was slow in the building by the lagoon. Also, tons of mosquitos- which is probably just a Venice thing.  We liked that it was quiet and away from the crowded areas. We first used the complimentary water taxi from hotel to San Marco, but we prefer the walk as it was shorter. Lol we are average walkers and did it within 20-25 minutes; the water taxi took 45 minutes. Yes, I timed it.
