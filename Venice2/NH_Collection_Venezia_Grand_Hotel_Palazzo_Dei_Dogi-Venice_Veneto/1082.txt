My wife and I stayed here two nights in April.  The most convenient way to get to the hotel is to take the Alilaguana (orange line) from the airport; the hotel is a short walk from the first stop.  I booked the smallest room available (referred to as Deluxe on the hotel’s website).  The room was adequate, and the hotel itself is quite beautiful.  As a Marriott Gold member we received complimentary breakfast, which consisted of an extensive buffet and a hot dish from the menu.  The breakfast was very good, but not likely worth the regular price (around 30 Euros).  We took the hotel shuttle boat once to St. Mark’s Square; it was a nice ride.
