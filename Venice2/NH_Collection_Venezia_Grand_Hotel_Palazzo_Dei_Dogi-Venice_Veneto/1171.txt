This hotel was a fourteeth or fifteenth century Palace that has been turned into a beautiful Hotel. It is in a residential section of Venice in a very quiet neighborhood. The room was Beautiful and well appointed. Expect a hike or an expensive water taxi ride to get to St Mark's Square and many of the more popular attractions. I reccomend walking, everything is flat (except the Grand Canal Bridges and, to a lesser degree, the smaller canal bridges.) Another option is the Vapporetto (water bus) but if you want to do that you should buy a multi day pass ahead of time or carry a lot of euro's (cash only).
