Hotel Boscolo Venezia  My wife and I recently completed a 5 night stay at the Boscolo Venezia.  In all aspects it was a wonderful experience.  We were greeted at check-in by Phillip who did a very thorough job of explaining what was available to us during our stay:  an included cooked breakfast, free boat shuttles to Murano and San Marco ("downtown" Venice), spa access etc.   Phillip then personally escorted us to our room (which was an upgrade overlooking the canal) with our luggage following shortly thereafter.  The room was very spacious, even by American standards, with picturesque beams (I suspect due to the age of the building - several hundred years old); room sizes and layouts may be different.
