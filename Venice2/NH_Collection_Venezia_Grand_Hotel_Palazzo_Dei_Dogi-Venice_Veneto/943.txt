This hotel is very nice, no doubt about it. The front desk personnel are pleasant and helpful, we were allowed to check in early after an inhuman 24 hours of flying (I live in Hawaii), the room (although compact) was tasteful, with windows that open and a view of the canal on the Madonna D'Orto side. The internet was included, and although it wasn't lightning fast, it was adequate for uploading photos and surfing.  The areas which were weak (and this is only relative, since these wouldn't be expected in a hotel of a lower category) are the dining - there is no way the restaurant could be considered five-star.
