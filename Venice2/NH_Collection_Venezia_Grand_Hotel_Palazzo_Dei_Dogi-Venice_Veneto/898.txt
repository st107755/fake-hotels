I liked the location of this hotel. It is in a mainly residential area but it is still close enough to neighborhoods that are tourist traps. It's a bit longer trek to all the main tourist locations but still very walkable unless you are not in physical shape to walk 5+ miles a day. You can do it in less but I found that we walked about 5-7 miles a day between wandering and seeing sites. There are a couple of places to eat very closeby and many more just about a 5 minute walk. There is a beautiful Venetian lobby with Murano glass chandeliers. The hotel itself is very tired but I think most hotels in Venice have this same type of tired decor so I won't dock points for that.
