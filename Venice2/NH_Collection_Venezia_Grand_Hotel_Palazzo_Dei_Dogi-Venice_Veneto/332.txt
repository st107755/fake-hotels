The boscolo was a nice hotel, we stayed for 3 nights. The room we had was huge , but the bathroom was kind of small and old, dripping faucets etc. we heard that the hotel had been recently sold, and it seemed true because it was showing a lot of wear and tear. The breakfast was not good, but if it is included ok, if not you could find better. The location was very quiet away from the crowds, the hotel offered a shuttle to st. Marks and we did use it. The ferry stop is also nearby. If you are going back to the airport by ferry give yourself plenty of time, this is the last stop before the airport and the ferries will pass by if they are full.
