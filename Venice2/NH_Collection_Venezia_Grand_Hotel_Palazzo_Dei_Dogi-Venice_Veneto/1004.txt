The Boscolo is a nice property in a not-so-tourist part of Venice.  However, the hotel is easily within walking distance to anywhere in the city (St. Mark's square takes about 30 min).  Alternatively, the hotel provides a free water taxi to St. Marks square.  The service is very good, and we had a wonderful room overlooking the water in the back building of the hotel.  Our room was spacious and unique, with a king bed and hardwood floors.  Breakfast was excellent (and free for Marriott Gold status).  We did not eat any other meal at the hotel, and there is no reason to with several restaurants close by.
