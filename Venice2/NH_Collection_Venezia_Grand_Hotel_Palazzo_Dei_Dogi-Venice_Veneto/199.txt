My husband and I visited Venice at the end of August for a two day stay before heading to Lake Lugano. First, our room was beautifully decorated with a view of the canal. The efficient front desk staff arranged a dinner for us at the hotel restaurant. The food was elegantly prepared and delicious. Our next day we took the water taxi to Murano and Burano upon recommendation of the concierge, Ivan Romanello. He was outstanding in arranging not only a memorable day trip with shops and restaurant recommendations but also in printing travel directions and documents for our trip to Lugano. When leaving he also promptly arranged a water taxi to the garage parking area. The young bellman, Sam, was so gracious and friendly as well which added a personal touch to the hotel’s atmosphere.…
