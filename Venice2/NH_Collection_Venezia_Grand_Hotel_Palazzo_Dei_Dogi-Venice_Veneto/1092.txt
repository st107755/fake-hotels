I have had some bad experiences being to Venice. The city is really too small for the number of tourists visiting this city. Since I don´t like being treated as a tourist, Venice is of course a hard place  to be positively surprised. The city itself with its history and cultural heritage if of course fantastic, but you easily run into tourist traps.   I made some research regarding hotel and the decision fell on Boscolo. The hotel is very nice and very helpful service. Prices are always high in Venice, but it was not exaggerated at Bascolo. I can really recommend to stay here at your Vencie visit.
