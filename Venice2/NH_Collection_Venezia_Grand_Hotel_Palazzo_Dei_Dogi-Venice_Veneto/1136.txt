The location of the hotel is great; away from the huge crowds in a rare part of the city that still represents traditional Venice life, yet easy walking access all the tourist sights. The hotel is clean with a rich, luxurious interior.  However, as so many people have written this is not a 5 star hotel. In reality this is a 3-4 star hotel with 5 star prices. Drinks and food are ridiculously expensive (€4 for a water, €6 for a cola etc.) with no in room tea or coffee facilities. The hotels courtesy transport did not work in the 3 days we were with them, no apology, no alternative options. Staff on main reception were unhelpful on all requests for help and information.
