Myself and my wife have just returned from a 3 night stay at the dei dogi, the hotel itself is situated a little off the beaten track approx 20min walk from st marks square but this suited u's fine.  Upon arrival the we were checked in promptly and presented with a map of Venice which was explained and quickest routes to various attractions highlighted.  Our room was a superior room situated at the back of the hotel by the hotel pier, I'm not sure what made the room superior but my mind wondered if this was a superior room then I would not like to stay in the standard ones, the sound proofing was appalling, the couple in the room above us were very athletic and loud of an evening this lack of sound proofing left little to the imagination so beware!
