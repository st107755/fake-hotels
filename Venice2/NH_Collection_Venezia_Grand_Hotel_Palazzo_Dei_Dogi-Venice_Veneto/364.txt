We stayed at this hotel for 2 nights in August and loved every minute of it.  We caught a water taxi to the front door, although you could get here by vaporetto if you wanted to.  There is a vaporetto stop about 25 metres from the hotel.  The hotel is ornately decorated in Venetian style with the most beautiful glass (presumably Murano) chandeliers in the lobby and throughout.  The staff were fabulous.  My husband's gold status was recognised and we were thanked for our patronage and given an upgraded room.  Our room was huge with a well appointed large bathroom.  The breakfast service was superb - both buffet and you could order hot dishes from the menu.  The restaurant has both an indoor and outdoor seating area.
