I was a bit nervous about staying in this hotel due to the reviews, but it was better than I expected, though unrecognisable from the 5 star reviews here!  We went in October so the air con was not an issue, but the hotel has thin floors so is very noisy internally - the people above us sounded like elephants walking across the floor so make sure you bring ear plugs!  The free shuttle boat was not working and we were not offered an alternative, but worse still the kitchens were closed for 2 weeks (!) so although breakfast was available, it was annoying to have to walk for 20 mins on our first night for a meal, as it was a bit remote.
