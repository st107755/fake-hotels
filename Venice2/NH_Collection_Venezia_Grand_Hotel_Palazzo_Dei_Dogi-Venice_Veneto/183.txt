The hotel is located just around the corner from the Madonna dell'Orto airport ferry terminal.  This is the first stop on the Orange ferry, just give allow waiting times of at least an hour before getting on the ferry.  Its $14 Euro verses about $110 Euro for a water taxi.  The hotel was beautiful from the lobby to the courtyard.  It is located off the beaten path so its quiet in a non touristy area.  The Jewish ghetto is a short walk away.  The water shuttle to San Marco was convenient although it probably only saves about 10 minutes verses walking.  Our room was definitely non-standard with lots of crevices and cutouts but spacious and provided a nice view of the canal below.
