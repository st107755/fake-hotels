We stayed here after a week traveling around Italy.  It was a wonderful choice. A great feature of many of the Autograph Collection hotels is that they are in old stately homes that have been beautifully renovated into hotels.  We traveled with three other couples and each of our rooms were beautiful and decorated differently.  The don't miss of this hotel is the property.  While the entrance is on a small canal in the Canareggio neighborhood, the property has the land all the way back to the bay.  Some rooms are at the back of the property which is beautifully maintained with different gardens, "secret" tunnels and a lawn.  We were told it is the oldest and largest garden in Venice.
