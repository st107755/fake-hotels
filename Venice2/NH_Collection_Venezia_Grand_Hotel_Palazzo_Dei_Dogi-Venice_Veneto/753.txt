My favorite thing about this hotel is its location. Venice can be a bit overwhelming with its hordes of tourists filling the streets wall-to-wall and absolutely daunting number of hotels, so if you're looking for something elegant in a quiet neighborhood that is still beautiful and picturesque / in walking distance of everything, this place is perfect. We walked about 5 minutes and were in the thick of things. It's also very close to the public boat line to the airport (a mere 12 Euros per person) which was great as we flew in and out of Marco Polo Airport.  Service was quick and friendly, and everything's clean.
