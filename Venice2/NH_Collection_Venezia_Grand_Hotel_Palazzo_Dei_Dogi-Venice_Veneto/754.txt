We stayed at the Boscolo Venezia during our recent trip to Venice and loved the hotel. I understand that the hotel is almost 550 years old and used to serve as the French embassy to Venice. The hotel is nestled in the quiet Cannaregio neighborhood, away from the hustle and bustle of the main attractions. and is 3 minute walk from the Madonna Del 'Orto vaporetto stop.  Once you enter the building you are welcomed by beautiful Murano glass chandeliers. The staff explain patiently all the features the hotel has to offer including the complementary shuttles to San Marco and the Murano glass works tour.
