We booked this hotel after receiving an email notification of a special reduced rate offer – after looking at the photo’s on the website we were expecting something amazing – our room was comfortable but I felt it was a bit small and a bit scruffy – the furniture and bathroom were of adequate standard but nothing like the luxurious rooms portrayed on the website.  Had I been in a worse frame of mind I may have asked for a better room, but as we did not spend too much time in our rooms and just wanted to get out and enjoy Venice, I didn’t feel the need to complain.  The hotel reception was very grand, the breakfast room elegant and tasteful and the gardens a hidden treat, which would be lovely in the summer.
