My husband travels a lot so for our tenth anniversary we splurged cashing in many of his earned points to tour Italy and Venice was our first stop. The Boscolo Venezia is off the beaten path, quiet with old world charm and the rooms attractive and clean. We arrived in the afternoon, checked in and stowed our stuff in the room to go off exploring with the little time we had there. I still had some American money on me and hid it in a special compartment in my purse and then hid my purse. When we arrived back later that night the purse had been moved and the money gone. I know there are safes in the rooms though in such a fine establishment I just hadn't thought it would be necessary. Management had little to say besides they would look into it.
