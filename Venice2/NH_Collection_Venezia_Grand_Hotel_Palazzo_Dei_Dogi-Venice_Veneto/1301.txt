We spent 4 glorious nights in January 2013 at this gem of a hotel located on a canal within  walking distance of all the main attractions but far enough from central area to enjoy a private oasis in a quiet neighborhood. Front desk staff were terrific from check in to daily chats and help. Excellent breakfast included with great cheese and smoked salmon and prosecco etc. We used the charming small restaurant one rainy night and had a well Cooked meal with one on one service. We had booked two rooms and the hotel provided us adjoining rooms with a private front door. Both rooms were large and luxurious. Hotel provides a free water taxi to San Marco and Murano.
