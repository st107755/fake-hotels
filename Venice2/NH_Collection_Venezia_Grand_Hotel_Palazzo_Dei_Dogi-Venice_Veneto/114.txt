My daughter and I stayed here last week and had an amazing time!  The hotel is beautiful.  The room was a bit small, but spacious enough for 2 people.  The bathroom was large with bath products that smelled wonderful!  The staff were all very helpful and friendly.  The breakfast buffet was amazing.  The restaurant overlooks a beautiful garden and fountain.  The hotel does provide a free water shuttle from the hotel to St. Mark's Square several times a day.  Because the hotel is on the north side of Venice and the boat was too big to fit through the smaller canals, they had to take the longer way around, which was about a 35 minute ride.
