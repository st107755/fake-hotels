I was torn between the presidential suite at the Dei Dogi or a standard suite at the Danieli as a Marriott loyalist...went with the Dei Dogi and couldn’t be happier with the decision.    We loved the location - far from the madding crowds which forced us to see more of Venice than we would otherwise have.  Also the hotel is a two minute walk from the first alilaguna stop from the airport, making it crazy convenient - twenty minute ride versus over an hour to San Marco.    The presidential suite was something - large, ornate and beautiful, with two baths and a nice loft upstairs where the bed is.  Fabulous views out the many windows.
