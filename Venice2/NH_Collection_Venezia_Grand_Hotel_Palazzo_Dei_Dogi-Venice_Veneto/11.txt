This was my third visit to Venice, but the hotel was the best yet.  I love staying in this area of Venice as there are so many good restaurants and bars here and it's a pleasant 20 minute walk to St. Mark's Square.  I also like that it's the first stop from the airport by waterbus.  The staff are amazing and the standard and quality of everything is superior. The breakfast is extremely good.  Hot hotel buffet food is usually such a disappointment, but not here. All fresh and tasty.  We didn't bother with any a la carte breakfast items as the buffet was so good and varied.  The pastries were fabulous!  My room looked over the garden and was very large and extremely clean, with a very well equipped bathroom,  The bed/mattress was so comfortable.
