Our 4 day stay at the Dei Dogi was spectacular! The lobby was in typical venetian style with Murano glass chandeliers and much ornate gold decor.  Our room was spacious and beautiful with windows facing the garden.  Upon entering our room there was a chilling bottle of champagne and Italian chocolates awaiting us. Housekeeping was wonderful leaving our room clean with fresh towels each morning.  We enjoyed the simple yet elegant breakfast buffet outside in the garden veranda every morning.  The cappucinos we ordered each morning were perfect! The strategic position of the structure on the lagoon, given its history, is a very fascinating aspect of the hotel.
