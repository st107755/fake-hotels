We booked our room using Marriott points.  We arrived late only to be given an attic room. It was hot, and the under-powered air conditioner was unable to cope. It severely interfered with my night's sleep. To be fair, I did not ask for a fan or to change rooms. Perhaps that would have been a better course of action than suffering in the heat.  In the morning, we decided to have breakfast even though it was not included in our room rate. There were no other options nearby. We paid $98 Canadian for a continental breakfast for two. It was not worth the price.  This hotel is far away from the main attractions.  I read many reviews which said it was nice. It just wasn't for me.
