Beautiful property, excellent staff.  I really enjoyed my recent stay here. Make sure to check out the secret garden in the back-- you might miss it if you are staying in the front and don't go looking.  The boat shuttle service was convenient; and I appreciated staying a bit away from the tourists.  The quiet gives space to appreciate the everyday beauty of Venice.    I was especially impressed and appreciative of the staff here.  In particular, a front desk clerk named Gaia gave us excellent restaurant recommendations that were exactly on point for what we were looking for.
