This was our first visit to Venice.  We chose this hotel because it was part of the Marriott family.  As we have found with other Autograph hotels, we found it to be luxury with comfort.  The hotel is located in a more residential district of Venice.  We arrived on a Sunday morning to quiet streets leading to the hotel - despite the fact that Carnival was just starting.  We were greeted wonderfully by Pablo at the front desk.  He told us of all the amentities in the hotel and escorted us up to our room.  A beautiful, large, very quiet room on the 2nd floor. The breakfast buffet featured lots of meats, fruits, breads and breakfast cakes and pastries, along with the choice of a cooked dish.
