We stayed here in March for our wedding anniversary and had a wonderful time.  Hotel staff were very friendly and helpful at all times. We loved the hotel's boat which had a regular service to and from St marks square area.  Our room was lovely with nice toiletries, we overlooked the canal but didnt have a balcony, just two large windows. We took a travel kettle, tea and coffee on the advice of previous guests reviews, and bought a small carton of milk from the local shop which did us fine. There is a mini bar in the room so we kept the milk in there, we also bought some champagne which was cheap and nice too.
