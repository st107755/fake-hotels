It is in a quiet neighborhood that can be reached easily via the vaparetto.  From near the front of the train station take the 5.2 vaparetto and get off at the Orto stop.  Just follow the little streets until you come to a canal.  Turn right and you can't miss the circled "B" logo of the hotel a few doors down.  No reason to walk and drag your luggage over the bridges. Especially since you should get a vaparetto pass for your stay. Get a local map with the vaparetto routes and you can get around the island very easily.  The hotel is a classic old building that's been done extremely well with nice furnishings and wonderful chandeliers.
