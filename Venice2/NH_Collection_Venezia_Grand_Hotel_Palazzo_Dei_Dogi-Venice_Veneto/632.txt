Wonderful hotel in a quieter part of Venice yet close to shopping ,restaunts and water ferry. Beautifully decorated rooms with old world charm,quality bedding and bathroom products. 24 hour tea, coffee and chilled water in private lounge. Huge garden at rear of hotel with secret pathways, fountains & nooks. Walk through garden to Venice Lagoon. Eat breakfast in a tented Pavillion in the garden while listening to the sounds of fountain.  Impeccable service. Free transfers to St Marks Sq. Total luxury
