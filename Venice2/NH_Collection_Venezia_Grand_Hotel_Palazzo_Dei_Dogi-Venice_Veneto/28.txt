I spent five nights at the hotel and had a terrific experience.  I have status with Marriott so enjoyed the very good buffet breakfast for free and felt my status was recognized with a large room overlooking the garden.  The staff are uniformly excellent, but special recognition to the breakfast staff and to the bartender Matthew for their exceptional professionalism.  The rooms were a little fusty, but well equipped with robes, slippers, a room safe and plenty of armoires.  I didn't try restaurant but did enjoy the bar - I thought the prices were a little high or I would have used it a little more.
