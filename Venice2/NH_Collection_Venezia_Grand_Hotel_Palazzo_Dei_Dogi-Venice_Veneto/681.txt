I am a Marriott Silver member.  I book Marriott as first choice hotel brand on my many trips.  I book Marriott for the comfort of the beds and the service.  This is a Marriott managed hotel so unfortunately I got neither.  Can I start by saying this is the first negative review I have given any Marriott in all the years booking with them.  Positives first,  the location is ideal for the public water buses. You come off the water bus and the hotel is literally round the corner, in fact you see the rear of the hotel from the launch.  So this saves a bit of money on private taxis.  There is a complimentary water shuttle from the hotel to St Marc's Square.
