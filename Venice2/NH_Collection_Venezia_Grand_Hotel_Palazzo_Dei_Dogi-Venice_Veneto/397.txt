A little late submitting my review, but this was my introduction to the Boscolo section of the Marriott Hotel chain, and I was perfectly impressed.  The hotel is away form the tourists crowds, which is great after a long day of exploring.  It has the only private gardens in the city, and they are simply beautiful.  The staff were all friendly, courteous, and ever-ready to assist with anything I needed.  The hotel also has a shuttle service to take you to San Marco Square.  On my second day there, because a wedding was taking place in the gardens and I couldn't go through the back, I was greeted in the front of the hotel by a private water taxi in lieu of the larger shuttle boat.
