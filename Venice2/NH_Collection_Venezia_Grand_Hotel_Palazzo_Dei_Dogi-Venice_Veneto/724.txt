We only stayed one night at the Boscolo Venezia because we were flying out the next day.  This hotel is one of the most beautiful hotels in which I have stayed!  It is a former French embassy with a lovely garden in the rear of the hotel.  Walk through the garden and you will find yourself at another dock with a gorgeous view of Venice.  Our room was spacious and beautifully appointed.  The bathroom was a little crowded but equally beautiful.  We ate a quick lunch upon our arrival at the hotel's restaurant and it was delicious.  And the breakfast the following morning was lavish with any type of food you would want!  I cannot say enough about the staff.
