On arrival at the airport beat the queues by taking a private water taxi to the hotel (80 euro).Go directly outside the airport and take the shuttle to the waterside. When we arrived at the hotel, the lobby waqs beautiful, we were greeted by the friendly staff who made us feel very welcome. Luckily we were upgraded to a suite the room was elegant, in a venetian style, big and airy, and very clean. We didn't eat or drink at the hotel as the prices were a little on the expensive side, however, there are lots of little bars and restaurants within easy walking distance and at very reasonable prices.
