The hotel itself is beautiful and a credit to the Boscolo Collections. Our rooms were perfect and the breakfast outstanding. The hotel is located in a quiet neighborhood on a small canal. At the rear of the property, through a magnificent garden, we found our rooms that overlook the lagoon. All of the excellent accommodations were made even better by the outstanding staff. Georgia, Gaia and Ivan were so helpful and pleasant that we immediately felt welcome.
