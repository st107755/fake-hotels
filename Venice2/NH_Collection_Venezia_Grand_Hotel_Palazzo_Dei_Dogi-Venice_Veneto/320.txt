This hotel is a hotel in a 300 year old Palaza that has all the old world charm of Venice but is not a cookie-cutter hotel like some of the hotels in some chains we have stayed out. It has magnificent Murano glass chandeliers and light fixtures throughout. It is the only hotel we have been to that also has a beautiful and well-kept garden and resident cat. It is accessed by water taxi or hotel shuttle or from the public pier nearby. We enjoyed going to  St Mark's on the shuttle boat and it is the first time we have arrived at a hotel in a water taxi and left for the airport in a water taxi.
