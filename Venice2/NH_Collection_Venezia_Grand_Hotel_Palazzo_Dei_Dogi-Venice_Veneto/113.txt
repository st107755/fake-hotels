I stayed in this hotel for three nights traveling with family. The hotel is located in a great part of Venice that is quiet and has many local restaurants and bars within 5 minutes of walking. The restaurant was excellent for dinner. We had the tasting menu, and it did not disappoint. The staff in the restaurant was superb. Also, the bar was excellent. The bar manager, Alessandro, was one of the best bartenders we had ever had in travels at luxury hotels around the world. He was extremely helpful, courteous, professional, and made us feel welcome. The concierge was also very helpful. The front desk staff was very unfriendly and quite apathetic.
