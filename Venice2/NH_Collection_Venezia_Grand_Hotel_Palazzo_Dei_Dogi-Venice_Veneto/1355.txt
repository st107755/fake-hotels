My husband and I were celebrating a special anniversary so we chose what we thought would be a special hotel in romantic city.  We were not disappointed.  This hotel was stunningly fabulous.  Every inch was opulently decorated as only the Italians can.  From the marble floors to the Murano light fixtures the whole hotel was a visual treat.  The hotel rooms were large and as lovely as the public areas.  Everything was spotless.  We were not well when we arrived after a cruise.  The staff could not have been nicer.  They took care of all of our needs and always were gracious and smiling.    A shuttle boat was available to take guests to and from San Marco Square.
