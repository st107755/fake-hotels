Boscolo Venezia was the perfect base for a Romantic weekend in this beautiful city. Located away from the bussle of St Marks, but still only 30mins to walk to there.   As a Marriott gold card holder I was offered plenty of extras during our 3 night stay: free breakfast for me and my partner (usually €36 each for hot menu and buffet), upgraded room and some free drinks on arrival. As well as the extras the staff were fantastic in helping us to get the most out of our stay, booking restaurants on our behalf and offering advice on tourist activities.   The hotel itself was splendid. Period decor in the room, which had an enormous bed and lovely marble bathroom. There was also a lovely garden with a fountain and a grotto that led from the rear of the hotel down to the lagoon. Free…
