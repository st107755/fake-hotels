This was a present for my 50th Birthday.  I have always wanted to visit Venice so I was obviously delighted to learn I was finally going!  The transfer from the hotel was smooth and even though it was night when we arrived, the hotel was easy to find.  The front desk staff were welcoming and friendly although the reception area is very small.  We were shown to our deluxe room, which was also very small.  We went out for supper and when returned found that the air conditioning in the room was not working and the room was very hot and stuffy.  There are mirrors everywhere in this hotel, and it became apparant the next day, when we had time to properly look around the room that the hotel is clearly designed for "adult" weekends!
