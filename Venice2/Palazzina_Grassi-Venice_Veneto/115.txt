This was my second visit to Venice but first stay at the Palazzina G. This boutique hotel can only be described as 'really quite cool'. I read many reviews prior to our trip and I do understand why perhaps the decor, mirrors everywhere, curious sinks in the bathrooms, TV's set in glass panels are not to everyones taste and may take a little getting use to. But this really shouldn't come as a surprise to new guests as the hotel does describe its unique,Philippe Starck designed interior very accurately so really there should be no complaint.  We add a very generous suite with private lift access and glad we ran to the extra expense as this gave plenty of space to relax after a long days sightseeing and also plenty of space to get ready for the New Year's Eve party.
