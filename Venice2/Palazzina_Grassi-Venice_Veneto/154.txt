We booked this with out knowing much about it. It feels like the hotel is hidden away down an alley way. When we got off the ferry we literally wandered around until we got our bearings and found it. When we approached the building we ran into a couple also looking for the same hotel, and felt like we were walking into a secret club as there is not a lot of signage out front directing you. When you walk in the hotel is really nicely decorated in a very artistic way. You see the lobby first then beyond that the restaurant/bar. There is also a lovely roof top area to enjoy breakfast which is really good by the way!
