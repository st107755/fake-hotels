We enjoyed staying at this property.  The design throughout is interesting, quirky and eye catching with mirrors, glass, art of different kinds, leathers, woods, metals and photography.  There are a lot of photo opportunities and large doors that open up onto the Grand Canal where you can step out and view.  We arranged for the full transfer service the hotel offers from the airport to Palazzina G via private car and water taxi.  This was a wonderful experience in that it was a very relaxing way to arrive at the hotel and although pricey (180 Euro), a real special way to arrive to the hotel.
