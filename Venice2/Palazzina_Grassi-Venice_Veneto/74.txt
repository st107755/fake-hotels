We had a second floor room facing the rear terrace so no view and hampered privacy, but it was a good sized double, light, bright and a beautiful bedroom and bathroom. A fabulous wardrobe exterior contained a stunning fitted bar with all the mod cons you could desire.  Having stayed at a very traditional Venetian pension the previous nights this was a philippe starck contrast! The mirrored room felt extravagant and chic and the service was simply superb. If I went again, I would go for a room with a river view and also experience the bar and restaurant which looked sublime.   The main flaw was that it was a bit of a walk away from the main sights, a bit too quiet in terms of location and the only 'local' restaurant was pretty poor and over priced.
