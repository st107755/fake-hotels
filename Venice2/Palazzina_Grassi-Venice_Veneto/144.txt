We recently visited the Palazzina G, which is a boutique style hotel with a great deal of style and panache! The hotel was designed by Phiipe Starke and is a minute; well seconds from the grand canal. With only 16 or so rooms and some apartments, it is never overcrowded and the restaurant and bar offer a superb, welcoming, intimate environment which draws you in, with soft lighting, soft and individual seating.  The rooms were bright with floor to almost ceiling back lit mirrors, which Offered elegant class without seediness. The only negative we have about our room was a lack of drawers, there were space for some but having none with limited storage was something we noticed.
