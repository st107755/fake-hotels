Stayed at the Palazzina over the weekend. We booked earlier in the year and were a little worried after seeing a few negative reviews. Happily however, we are able to report that it was a fantastic hotel and the staff couldn’t do enough to look after us.  We emailed a week in advance to ask if they could make some restaurant bookings and the front desk got back to us within the hour to confirm the tables were booked. A good start.  We arrived late on a Friday night to be greeted by an extremely helpful woman at the front desk (Long hair and glasses, I wish we had her name as she deserves to be recognised), who made us feel very welcome right away.
