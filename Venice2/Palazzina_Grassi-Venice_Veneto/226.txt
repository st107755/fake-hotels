Stayed 21-23 August 2010, Superior room #113. Certainly one of  the most beautiful hotel I have experienced. Absolutely amazing. Wow factor as soon as entering the hotel! Philip Starck design in an excellent mix with traditional Venetian atmosphere. Colors are rich and warm. The beautiful Venetian (Murano) chandeliers are spectacular. Fantastic mirrors in all sizes everywhere. Furniture, decoration, glass sculptures, vintage objects – all items are so thought through. And yet, it is extremely cozy and welcoming.  The Krug lounge is special with its furniture and décor combined with great views over the Grand Canal. The terrace on the second floor is charming.
