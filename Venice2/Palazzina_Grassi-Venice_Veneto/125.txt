We had booked the hotel in advance and opted for the hotel transfer from Treviso Airport for €200 in a luxury car and water taxi. The luxury car turned out to be a transit van people carrier (it took 25 minutes) and the water taxi 5 minutes. At a cost of €200 each way - an absolute joke! The hotel is clearly placing a large mark-up on the cost for this service so I would steer clear.  The welcome was a little strange. We were told how to shut a door by pulling it to, asked if we could see the television in our room which was right next to us and shown how to turn on a light switch and open a wardrobe door, hilarious!
