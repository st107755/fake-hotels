I took my husband for a surprise birthday weekend. We began by arriving on the hotel's vintage water taxi through the mist to our hotel and were shown to our room - all very slick but perhaps not entirely worth the E140 price tag. My major disappointment was the room itself, we paid almost £700 for 2 nights in a superior (standard) room, which was very small with only a shower room and a very overpowering smell of air freshener, which actually gave me headaches for our whole stay. Considering the price I was also surprised breakfast wasn't included. We had a great meal in the hotel restaurant on our first night and they recommended a fantastic local place for the next night.
