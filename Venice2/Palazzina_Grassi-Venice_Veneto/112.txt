I booked this hotel for a long weekend to celebrate my fiance's birthday. The location is excellent within walking distance of S.Samuel vaporetto stop - take the road leading to the left and then it is first left again (2 minutes) It is in a quiet district, but also within a 10 minute walk of the hustle and bustle of St Mark's Square, which is ideal. The staff were excellent - very welcoming and helpful. I booked a superior room, but we were upgraded to an executive room on the second day. We were much happier with this room as it was larger with natural light. My fiance also received a lovely little cake in the evening during our cocktails - it was a nice touch that they remembered it was his birthday.
