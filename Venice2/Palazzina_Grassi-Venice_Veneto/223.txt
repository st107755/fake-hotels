I dont usually write reviews, but the negative comments I saw on this hotel compelled me to contribute.   The Palazzina Grassi is an incredible hotel, so long as you know what you are after and in for. It isnt one of Venice's grand old hotels, but thrives on its uniqueness.   The hotel pickup from the airport was fantastic, prompt and easy and we had an impromtu tour of Venice en ruite to the hotel. Arriving we were ushered into the central restaurant/coffee area for complimentary coffee while they checked us in which was painless and efficient.   The room is spacious, beatifully decorated and incredibly welcoming. Yes, there is a shortage of storage space, but for a weekend trip it is perfect.
