We stayed at the Palazzina Grassi in April for my birthday weekend.  This hotel is absolutely amazing!  We were completely blown away from the minute we walked in to check-out.  It's very chic and modern... the rooms have a ton of mirrors and cool furniture.  We stayed in executive rooms -- ask for one with a private terrace.  The bar and restaurant are very swanky... a great spot to take a break from siteseeing for some drinks.  It never seemed to get crowded in the bar at all which was nice... we felt like we had the place to ourselves at times.  They also have a terrace on the second floor where you can have breakfast served to you!
