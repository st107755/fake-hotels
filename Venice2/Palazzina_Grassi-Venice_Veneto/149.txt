That unassuming little door in a narrow side street behind the Palazzo Grassi (Palazzina G is it's little sister I guess) leads you through to a paradise of varied designs that have a slightly naughty feel to it.  The staff are friendly and attentive without being intrusive, the beds are huge and comfortable but this is much more than just a luxury hotel. There is a sense of walking into a secret members' club which is available only to those 'in the know'.   From the low lighting of the bar which incorporates PG's restaurant to the stark (as in Philippe Starck...) white decor of the bedrooms - with mirrors everywhere so perhaps not for the body-conscious! - this is a hotel with STYLE.
