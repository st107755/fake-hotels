OK, even if he didn't design this hotel, he should have.  I stayed here with my boyfriend for a long weekend before Christmas and we loved it.  Very chic and contemporary, central location (once you know how to find it), nice rooms.  We were very content here and would stay again.  Just two things to note:  breakfast is crazy expensive at 40 euros and no a la carte menu.  I just wanted coffee and fruit and that meant leaving the hotel every morning if I didn't want to spend 40 euros for it.  Also, the lobby level can turn into a raging "party" late at night - both nights we were there, there were parties, one hosted by Venice Magazine.
