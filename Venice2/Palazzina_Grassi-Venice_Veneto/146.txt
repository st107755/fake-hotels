Stayed here for two nights mid April. Very easy to navigate to from the airport. Was slightly concerned by the very brief directions provided on their website but actually we found our way straight there, despite there also being absolutely no signage to identify itself!  Staff were very welcoming and the complimentary drink whilst we were checked in from the restaurant/bar set a good first impression. Our bags were taken to our room then once we were ready we were shown up to where was to become our base for the three days. It was lovely to be physically shown to the room and given a brief tour as to where things were and how things worked.  We had room 108, with a really cute view into a neighbours courtyard.
